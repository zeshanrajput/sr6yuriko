# River People (Faction Reference)

Source: Shadowrun Lore

## Overview
* **Connection Rating:** 7 (About 120 members, sprawl wide, limited finances, mundane, pervasive Matrix)

Taking their notes from the Walking People outlined in the Hack and Slash drop, the River People are a like-minded group of technomancers, punks, anarchists, deckers, and artificial intelligences. The group was originally formed to provide a refuge for the Emerged in the New Orleans sprawl, and the behavior of the corps toward these people led the organization to stage attacks against the corporations ranging from vandalism to hits for paydata.

The group has strict rules about maintaining secrecy, relying on word of mouth to find the right people with the same goals to invite. Any sort of initial meet with new people or prospective members happens away from the host they use as their home base.

They are considered a mixed-user group, and their current membership numbers are somewhere around 120 associates. 

## The Host
Their main hangout is the **Mississippi Renovation Society host** (Rating 9 Foundation host). 
* **Outsider Access:** Outsider access to the host would make you believe that it’s a nonprofit organization dedicated to cleaning up the Mississippi River, but this is just a front. 
* **User/Admin Access:** Accessing the host with user or admin status shows an ultra-real clubhouse that the River People own and manage.

## Leadership
The leadership of the organization consists of eight individuals and rotates every three to four years. An Emergent intelligence, who goes by **Indomitable Will**, is part of the core team and the one who does most of the reaching out to invite new members to join or hire outside resources.

> These are some pretty good people, and Indomitable Will is a solid sapient to know. I had done a few runs for them earlier on in my career and eventually accepted an offer to join up.
> — *Lanyap*

## Goals
Make the corporations pay for their transgressions against the Emerged.

## Restrictions
Live in New Orleans sprawl. Do not have a corporate SIN. Maintain the secrecy of the organization.

## Patronage
The group doesn’t have any patronage from a larger group or any single person. The resources for the organization come from the dues that their members pay as well as some paydata the organization is willing to sell on behalf of its members.
