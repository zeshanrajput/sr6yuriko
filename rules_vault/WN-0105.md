## Tough Tony’s

Tough Tony’s is a true speakeasy, named in honor of Manhattan’s most notorious mobster, Anthony “Tough Tony” Anastasio. Most people in Manhattan have never heard of it, and even if they know the location (a repurposed Washington Heights meat-packing plant), they couldn’t get in. You have to have connections with the New York Mafia to be given the passwords for that. According to rumor, Tough Tony’s is run by the Lucchese family, and the basement of the club has access to the Underground. The location is also a good spot to make connections with the smugglers and skimmers moving people and contraband around the island for the Mafia.
