## Terms and conditions

Subject gets +1 Edge when making Climbing tests.

While this spell is sustained, the base threshold for climbing tests is reduced to zero (see p. 93, _SR6_ ).

The subject may move along a ceiling at their normal climbing rate (so long as the ceiling can hold them). If the Structure rating (p. 113, _SR6_ ) is less than the Body of the subject, then the ceiling buckles or breaks under the weight of the subject, and Gecko Crawl fails to keep the subject connected.
