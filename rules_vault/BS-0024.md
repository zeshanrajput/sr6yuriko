## Matching the Shell to the Essence: Gene Therapy

We’ve had gene therapy for decades. It started out expensive and time-consuming and has become a bit less of both over time. It’s still not something that you can complete over a weekend, but it’s much faster than it was even five years ago. Corporate executives often go for sabbaticals or vacations and return with a different genetic expression.

The time and cost still depend on what you need to have done. Note that the following costs are a “treatment only” option. This will generally consist of a nanotic tank in a basement with a Matrix connection. Many people pay significantly more for treatments in spas and resorts.

Note also that this treatment changes your DNA and RNA. In most cases, this will not make any immediate physical change to your body, although your body will alter over time. This can also be triggered if, for example, you alter your genome to become taller; normally, nothing would happen until you use hormones to trigger a second puberty, at which time there will be quite a growth spurt.

This is also not the same as adding a genetic augmentation to your body (see chapter 5 for those).

The procedure itself is completed in a variety of ways. The Sixth World lost a lot of advanced nanite knowledge a few years ago, but the devices from back in the day do still work for those bold enough to try them. In addition to nanites, a similar procedure using a magical lodge can be completed to adjust the flesh. The costs and outcomes are the same, but people who do not wish to sully their bodies with mere technology and chemicals have an option.
