## Attack Rating (AR)

In combat, the Attack Rating is a measure of the accuracy, efficiency, and basic smoothness of the attack. Usually, it comes from a weapon, but sometimes you’re attacking with your bare hands, so you need an Attack Rating. It’s Reaction + Strength at the Close range, with no rating at the other ranges (see Attack Rating/ **Range** , p. 108). Some qualities and gear may affect this.
