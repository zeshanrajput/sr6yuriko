## **Caring for Health**

**==> picture [233 x 4] intentionally omitted \<==**

**Ostensibly, one of the things we do between runs is heal up from the previous one so we can be ready for the next one. Unfortunately, for too many of us, that healing process mainly involves over-sleeping and over-drinking.**

**And look, it’s not like I’m some sort of health nut or anything. I don’t run unless I’m chased, and I really try to avoid being chased. I still eat the hacker diet of mostly garbage. So I’m not preaching from an ivory tower or anything. I just know that, for all sorts of wounds, it can help to have someone who knows what they’re doing make sure it heals right, and I want to look at some of the ways this gets done. Namely, a choice so central that I’m going to make it a heading.**
