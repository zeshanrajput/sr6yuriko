## Functional Tail (Balance)

- **Cost:** 6 Karma

Usually between one and two meters long, a balance tail is not under your conscious control. It functions instinctively by twitching, swaying, and wrapping around things at random to improve your balance. You gain a bonus point of Edge for all Athletic tests related to balance, climbing, gymnastics, or jumping. This Edge must be used on the test or it is lost.
