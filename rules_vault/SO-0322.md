## Injector Pen

This item may appear to be an expensive ink pen, a cheap plastic ballpoint pen, or a stylus for use with any number of electronic and AR-capable devices. And in the right circumstances, it can be much mightier than a sword. While the pen functions as a mundane writing utensil, its true purpose is to conceal a one-use syringe loaded with a single dose of a drug or toxin. The syringe and toxin reservoir are hermetically sealed so that the chemicals are undetectable by sensors until they’re ready for use. These are single-use devices, designed to be disposed of after one use. They cannot be reloaded.
