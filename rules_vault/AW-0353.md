## The Great Mother

Long ages past, a dragon known to the Tandorans as the Great Mother took the first eggs from the doomed land and brought them to a place where they would be safe. She drew upon the power of the island, and it was protected. Only then did she carefully hatch the first generation of Tandorans. Most of the first ones failed to hatch, or died shortly after, but enough survived—just barely. The Great Mother recognized that the loss of even one of her new children might spell extinction for the species, so she stayed with them for the first ten generations. She raised them within her lair and watched over them with her own eyes. She taught them all they would need to know to survive in Tandora, and she taught them of the world she had taken them from. When the eleventh generation of eggs hatched and all was well, the Great Mother left with the promise that she would one day return in order to take the people out of Tandora and back to the world they had come from. When that world was healed and ready, they would join the other people there and take part in greater things. Until the Return, the Tandorans
