## the tiding

The Tiding is a quasi-independent operation normally performing missions that are effective but remain out of sight, normally not sharing their information with anyone outside of one of their trusted circles. Members of the Tiding are selected based on being able to think outside the box and make connections that others might miss. This means they are not cookie-cutter suits and rarely conform fully to corporate norms.
