## May 13, 2083

Shadowrunners in Seattle find the final key to deciphering the Disian language and codes. Defender codebreakers from Transys Neuronet begin working on a usable cypher. Meanwhile, Disian forces double down on their efforts to take out key Defender targets and cells. This includes using chimeras in larger numbers, often in direct assaults. In response, the Defenders initiate counter-offensives.
