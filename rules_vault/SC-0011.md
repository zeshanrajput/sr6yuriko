- **Attributes:** Charisma 4+, Intuition 4+

- **Knowledge skills:** Metaplanes, Parabotany, Parazoology

- **Positive qualities:** Polyglot, Will to Live

- **Negative qualities:** Gourmand, Indecisive, Injury Prone, SINner, Weak Immune System

- **Life modules:** Professor, Talismonger, Event: You benefited from the influence of a mentor
