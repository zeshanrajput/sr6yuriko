## Condition Monitors

Condition Monitors track the health of your character. Each character has two: Physical and Stun. Condition Monitors are a series of boxes set in rows of three (see sample at right). Your Phys- **Physical Condition Monitor** ical Condition Monitor has (Body/2, rounded up) + 8 boxes, while the -1 Stun Condition Monitor has (Willpower/2, rounded up) + 8 boxes. When a row of boxes on -2 a monitor is filled, the character takes a –1 dice pool penalty to all tests -3 except Damage Resistance. Each row filled on either monitor increases -4 the penalty by 1. When either monitor is filled; the character is unconscious. If the Stun monitor is filled, remaining damage becomes Physical; extra physical damage becomes Overflow (p. 121).
