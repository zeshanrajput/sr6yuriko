## Getting Burned

If the SIN attached to your lifestyle is burned, so is the lifestyle associated with it. In 24 hours, any assets associated with the SIN become unavailable: subscriptions are canceled, assets are repossessed or seized by collections, and it’s a safe bet that law enforcement will be stopping by your place to make an arrest in even less time. Unless you have a safehouse, bolt hole, or other secondary lifestyle, your lifestyle category goes all the way down to Street until you can set up a new lifestyle under a different SIN—or maybe one of your fellow runners will let you move in with them.
