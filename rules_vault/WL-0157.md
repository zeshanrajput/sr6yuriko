**The hog is identifable based on its size, coloration, and the presence of many skin lesions. These creatures are frequently a brilliant red in color with patches of black, short fur on parts of its back. Due to the toxic environments in which it lives, the skin is frequently covered in black skin lesions. The face of the hog has two sets of tusks, which are frequently charred as the animal has the ability to be wreathed in fames. These hogs also grow large and regularly reach 200 kilograms, standing 140 centimeters tall at the shoulder.**
