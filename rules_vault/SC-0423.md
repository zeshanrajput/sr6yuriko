## The Getaway Gauntlet

It’s a simple job—wait outside the bank for your crew, and when they come out, get them away, fast. The job starts out trickier when the police arrive faster than expected and need to be outrun. Then, when the team arrives at their safe house, they find it’s been compromised, and a rival team is waiting for them there.

Things don’t get better from there. The driver has to dash across the sprawl, from one end to the other, encountering obstacle after obstacle. These obstacles can include:

- A gang fight that breaks out in a street in front of them.

- A massive twelve-car pileup on a highway.

- A rampaging juggernaut that escaped from a zoo.

- A free spirit of air whipping itself into a tornado.

The runners, especially the rigger, should be tested by these events, with exhaustion eventually taking a toll as they can’t get to a place to rest.
