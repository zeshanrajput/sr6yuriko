## The Spikes

At one point, the Spikes, under a brilliant troll named Lord Torgo, were the dominant gang in the district and were threatening to explode city-wide, uniting all goblins under a single banner. Lord Torgo’s capture and subsequent murder in prison shattered them into a dozen subgangs, each led by a strongman claiming to be the true heir of Torgo, and they’ve never approached even a fraction of that old power since. Each branch has a related name (the Rusty Spikes, the Sundress Spikes, the Spiked Tuskers, and so on), but other than that, there’s no unity. There’s a continued hope that someday, a new Torgo will rise up and unify the gangs under one strong leader, but a generation later there’s no one worthy.
