## **Resources**

**Dadeson: I hear the big plans, but what makes you think you can accomplish any of them?**

**Sarkenbleck: Connections. Connections. Connections. Oh, and most importantly, loyalty. As we have controlled discourse and respect for the methods of our fellows, we do not have petty jealousy and rivalries. We work together, and we work globally. While our numbers are small based on the offcial membership of Asintmah, when we look at other, more forward-facing organizations, we estimate our overall connective, collective worth to be somewhere near 2.8 trillion nuyen. Yes, with a T. We are a megacorporation without the need to waste money on covering up public outcries. Sure, we can’t feld an army of tanks, but that’s just fash and violence to keep the morons entertained. The real deals go on in back rooms, with lines on maps, bank account numbers, and talks of just the right number of losses on each side to make everyone able to spin the tale they want.**

**We have our scholars in powerful positions in the world’s largest banks, every megacorporation, and many infuential smaller corps. We rarely own them (other than stock), rarely run them (except through infuence), and our names stay buried in the corporate rosters with a blip for a minor mention here and there to keep folks from thinking we’re hiding.**

**For example, if we needed to drastically decrease the population of a certain community of witches operating in the NEMAQZ, we could fund a team of highly cybered and Awakened runners and send them at each other for a high-casualty encounter on both sides. Follow that with an Ares fight losing power over the QZ and needing to dump a heavy payload that just happens to con-**

**sist of Serin-7 gas or the latest strain of FAB, and you’ve solved several problems.**

**Connections, accidents, direct assaults … all on the table. We make sure people we don’t want to come back, don’t. If the serin isn’t enough, we “inoculate” them with a nano-explosive, skimp on the extract, or, warn the witches that they’re coming! Ha ha. Devious! And, as you can imagine, every aspect is meticulously coordinated by one of our own, and we utilize very specifc assets for some of the more delicate work.**

**As we say, never send the undisciplined to do the work of the Alliance.**

**We also make sure to utilize the correct connections for the right maneuver. While the Asintmah Alliance must come together for our grander causes, our members are welcome to join other organizations in order to gain access and accomplices.**

**Our biggest asset is our people. People who work to achieve the top of their feld. Olympians, innovators, artists, and all others who excel but get cast aside for the arcanely enhanced and artifcially boosted. The best and brightest minds and the most skilled men and women on the planet claim membership.**
