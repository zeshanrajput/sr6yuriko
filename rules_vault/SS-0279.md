## Brief

My husband is missing. I’ve seen him, heard him. I’ve only caught glimpses. He is screaming, his skin is … they have done things to his skin. I’ve never seen visions like this, but I see them now. I don’t know exactly where he is, but I can tell he is near. I can give you a trail to follow. Please bring him back.
