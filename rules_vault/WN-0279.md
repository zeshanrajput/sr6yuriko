## The Job

Piper Jane’s interview questions can be found in the Interview Questions sidebar. Negotiations for

the interview payment and her contractor investigation can be done separately. For the interview, net hits increase the pay by 250 nuyen per hit. For the investigation, net hits increase the payment by 1,000 nuyen per hit.
