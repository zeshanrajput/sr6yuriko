## Obstacles

Since it’s one of the oldest tricks in the book, that means it’s been working a long time, and it’s something people are more likely to pick up on. The suspiciously alluring individual is just a little more suspicious when you just got Vermillion-level access at the Ares’ new Alpha Lab, or just acquired a genuine Rembrandt or Renoir. The soft touch is essential to allay their fears and overcome hesitation. Act natural. Be one more great thing happening to them lately if they’re riding high on success. Or the relief they need when things haven’t been going their way. Never press the point, but be the point they want to press.
