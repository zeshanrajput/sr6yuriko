## **What rating are the reactive plates in the Ares “Bug Stomper” armor?**

The armor does not come with the plates themselves; it has just been modified to have the slots, so the plates do not count against the armor’s capacity. You can pay for and install the level of reactive plates you want. This armor has plates for front, back, and sides, which represents the four slots.
