## Practiced Paranoia

Paranoia isn’t a disorder—it’s a survival skill you’ve perfected.

**Cost:** 4 Karma

**Game Effect:** You may add your ranks in Con to your dice pool on a Judge Intentions test—if you do, you must replace one of the dice with a Wild Die.
