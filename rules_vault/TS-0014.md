## **Types of Law Enforcement Providers**

**The term “law enforcement provider” covers many different types of organizations. The most common variety is the local police department operating within a municipality–that’s what most of this data dump will focus on.**

**These organizations derive their authority in one of four ways: municipal, regional/national, military, or corporate. Of course, corporate departments use the policing authority of a government entity that has been subcontracted to them. There’s also a lot of overlap and blurred lines between the different types. For local departments, the end results will all be somewhat similar, regardless of the type or source of authority.**
