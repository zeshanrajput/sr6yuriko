## RCC Case

Using a case from an RCC has some of the advantages of a commlink case, but they tend to be bulkier. You can sling one under your jacket or carry it in a satchel, but they don’t exactly fit in your pocket like a commlink can. That said, the extra bulk provides a lot of capacity for accessories and case mods. However, people will think you have drones available. If security becomes suspicious, you may have to pass a Con + Charisma test opposed by Judge Intentions to pass it off as harmless. An RCC case has a capacity of 8. However, if you would like the case to include a functional RCC (because drones are fun, too!), the capacity is reduced to 2.

**Cyberhacks:** This type of case can barely fit a cyberhack. If you use an RCC case to house a cyberhack instead, it will have no room for a functional RCC or cyberdeck and no capacity for case mods.
