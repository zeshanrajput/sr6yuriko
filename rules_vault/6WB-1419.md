## Diving gear

Underwater diving gear, including a wetsuit, partial facemask with snorkel, breathing regulator, an air tank with two hours of air (additional tanks cost 50¥), and an inflatable vest for surface buoyancy. The regulator and air tank protect against Inhalation toxins just like a gas mask. The wet suit provides rating 2 Cold Resistance (p. 266).
