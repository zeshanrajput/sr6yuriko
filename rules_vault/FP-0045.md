## **Aaron Sewell**

**As one of the youngest, he is also among the most interesting of these card hunters. He was only**

**a teenager when the SWT frst began appearing, but he became fascinated with the imagery, mystery, and unpredictable effects of the tarot. It started with hacking and tracking those who hunted the cards, but he got himself embroiled in the efforts of the Hands of Fate and dragged into the non-virtual world thanks to his virtual skills. Once he got a taste of the hunt rush, he was hooked. His mind gets thrills from fnding clues and tips on the Matrix, but his body is pure adrenaline junky. He’s coming up on his twenty-ffth birthday, and the rumors are already fying about his desire for a big hunt before and a massive event and celebration on the day of.**
