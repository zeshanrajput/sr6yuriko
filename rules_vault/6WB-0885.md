## Trap

This attack allows a sprite to lock down a target, preventing it from taking an action. The sprite makes a Cracking + Level vs. Willpower + Firewalltest, where success means the target cannot take an action as long as the power is sustained. This power takes the sprite’s entire attention; once the sprite goes to do something else, the target can act.
