## Utility and Support

Your spells are perfect for assisting your fellow runners, whether by healing, concealing them, enhancing their attributes, levitating them, or any number of other handy tricks you might know. Unless your team is flush with faces, you’re also likely to be the primary social specialist. This means negotiating with employers is your responsibility as well as running your cons and playing pranks. If there are other socially adept runners on your team, that puts you in a perfect position to run all manner of interesting and complex confidence games—assuming you all get along well enough, that is! Whatever the case, despite the chaotic and distant nature of your personality and the tendency toward seeming like a loner, the Coyote shaman exists best as a team player. When your fellow runners shine because of what you manage to do by working together, bask in that glow. There’s nothing better in this world, chummer.
