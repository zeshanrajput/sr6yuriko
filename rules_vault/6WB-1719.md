## Against The Horde

## Main Location: Gropiusstadt

Notable Characters: Horde Brutes (use Sons of Sauron Brutes, p. 205), Saif Alhazred

The Horde is one of the oldest gangs in Berlin. Through strategic alliances with other troll and ork gangs, it has now developed into a gang kingdom with many hundreds of members. In Berlin, the Horde has profited from longstanding conflicts within the Russian and Turkish Mafia and has even taken over the production facilities of the Shaders syndicate, where BTLs and persona chips are being made. This accumulation of power has aroused the displeasure of Saif Alhazred (see **Berlin Contacts** ), as he believes no group should gain the upper hand in Berlin’s crime scene.

**Original Message:** Here’s a job where you can get creative. Mr. Schmidt wants to cause strife within the Horde. And the quickest way to do that is to stop business from running as it should. Schmidt doesn’t want assassinations. He’s more interested in lost drug shipments, sabotaged chip production, theft of protection money, or incidents in the Horde’s brothels and at the big urban brawl matches in Gropiusstadt.
