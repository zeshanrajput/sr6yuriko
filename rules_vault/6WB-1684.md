## Vogelfreidorf, Strausberg Industrial District, 22:51:03

Zoé pulled her heavy vehicle through another tight curve. No doubt about it: The unmarked Roadmaster behind her definitely had it in for her. Two more clicks to the A10 underpass, the district border to Marzahn. If the pursuers were random raiders, they would veer off. If they were corporate killers or runners—tomatoes, tomahtoes—chances were that Marzahn’s many gangs would rather assault them than her. After all, even the riffraff of Marzahn needed transportation sometimes, and her cab label Black Flag had several agreements with the local neighborhoods and their respective warlords.

To the left and right of the street, collapsed, burned-out, or overgrown houses passed by. With a crash, the ram of Zoé’s kampftaxi hurled another car wreck out of the way and into a former front garden. Left drift, then full speed down the avenue to Marzahn. The Roadmaster stubbornly stuck to her.
