## **Emerged**

**Emerged critters—I prefer to call them technocritters—are a recent arrival to this mad world. There’s been a new and different sort of Awakening going on, parallel to and quite different from the magical one, which we call the Emergence. It makes sense that, since we get paracritters along with mages, we’d end up with some technocritters to go with our technomancers. Emerged critters have adapted or developed some capability to interact with the Resonance and the Matrix. Some of their powers or effects can seem to be passive by-products of their presence, most commonly extra Matrix noise. Many technocritters have powers similar to those of sprites and technomancers, and will use them to directly mess with, hack, or subvert devices and fles. I know some people who have had limited success training some technocritters, but they tend to be more diffcult to work with than most other critters.**
