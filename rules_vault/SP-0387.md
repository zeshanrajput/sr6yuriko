## **The MCT Devices: game rules**

**This technology allows a portal generator to operate at twice the efciency of the base Disian device. Opening a portal uses 3 Essence, and keeping it open uses one additional point of Essence for every two minutes. The technology is also the basis of the new style of Harvesters being constructed on the material plane.**
