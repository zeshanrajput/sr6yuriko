## **Takeaways**

**I hope this little tale has shown the value of doing proper legwork on every aspect of a run. That means not only who your client is and who they’re working for (that’s the bare minimum to be honest), but every little detail of the job, including people and corporations involved as well as locations. Do not leave any stone unturned and don’t take any info at face value. Question everything.**

**That saved his life, as he was relocated to another city than Detroit. The agent who spotted Ringmaster, however, died on the first night of the Battle of Detroit. Make of that what you will.**

- **Balladeer**
