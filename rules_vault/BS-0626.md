## Drug Tolerant

You have a higher-than-average tolerance against becoming addicted to drugs. Whether because you are genetically predisposed to tolerance or you just happen to be stubborn as a mule and just that tough, addictions have a harder time taking hold of you.

- **Cost:** 5 Karma

- **Game Effect:** You gain a point of Edge when making Addiction tests.
