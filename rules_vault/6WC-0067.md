## Courier

Getting things from one place to another is usually accomplished over the Matrix or by shipping company, but not when it’s illegal, sensitive, or too important to be trusted to risk interception. That was your niche as a courier. You took the thing to the destination, no questions asked or answered. You had to learn how to stay under the radar, spot trouble before it spots you, and get around without being tracked by whoever wanted what youwere carrying. Did you ever peek? If not, how did you resist the temptation?
