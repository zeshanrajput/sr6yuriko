## Backgrounds

From a very young age, you began to notice wrong things in the world. Pollution, poverty, violence, corrupt executives and politicians, sadness, and disease—almost all caused by people’s poor choices or malicious actions. At this same time, you felt a sense of what could be, or rather what should be, if only people would step up and do something to fix things. Instead of bringing about

the changes you hoped to see, you brought mostly disappointment and frustration upon yourself. But the passion to do something kept burning you up. And yet you felt powerless—until your Awakening came. Sometime during adolescence, Eagle came to you in a vision, a dream, or perhaps even a waking manifestation. The mentor spirit welcomed you, recognized you as a kindred spirit, and gave you secrets of power. Power you could use to do something about the injustice, the pollution, and the corruption. Initially, this got you into a lot more trouble, but you eventually learned to temper your passion with wisdom.
