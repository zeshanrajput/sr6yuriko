## Data Sprite

**Hyperthreading:** If you ask your data sprite to help you thread a complex form, add its Resonance to your dice pool on your threading test.

This command is also available to music sprites. **File Ken:** You can request assistance from your data sprite on any Matrix action that targets a file, adding its Resonance attribute as bonus dice on your test.
