## **> The Big Rhino**

This Ork Underground restaurant is famous as both the first and best place for authentic ork cuisine. Big, spicy food served with big, strong drinks to loud, awesome Goblin Rock, this place is an icon of the city, right at the entrance to the Underground. Get in good with the regulars, and you can find all kinds of contacts.

- Sounder
