## Friend

Having a face fatale on your side can be a great asset, if you know how to use them. Like any part of the team, if they don’t fit in or aren’t allowed to utilize their skills properly, they won’t be of much

use to anyone. They are a force multiplier, making all of their teammates work better, providing intelligence, and manipulating the enemy to the team’s benefit. Even if the face fatale isn’t a player character, they could be a very helpful contractor when the team needs an edge.
