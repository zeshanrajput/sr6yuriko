## Silver (human, male, 22, silver hair, blue eyes, thin build)

Talking is sufficient force for Silver. He’s got charm to spare and not a whole lot else going for him. He made friends on some early runs with Sliver due to the humor of their similar names but now rides on his partner’s coattails. He does the talking at meets and gets the team more nuyen, but once the bullets start to fly, he’s much better at keeping his head down than knocking down his opponents.

Silver is looking to set up a meet initially. He wants credit for drawing the team into a trap. He offers intel on Chapel and their target, or he tries to hook the PCs with bogus data. It’s a trick that will likely be seen through, but it’s his only chance to be the hero of this team.

During any action, he tends to stay under cover and takes potshots at cheap targets. He’s not opposed to slipping in for a finishing blow in order to take a target out for good once they’re down. It’s dirty, but it’s all part of the shadow game.
