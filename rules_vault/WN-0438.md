## Who’s Who

Here we’ll cover the forces arrayed against the runners, the negotiator they send, the conditions where they might be moved to attack the PCs, and the resources they send after them with some suggested tactics on the attack. There is also a line on how their bid might be increased and how they might interact with other interested parties. As the gamemaster cannot guarantee where events will take place, they might want to get familiar with the runners’ regular hangouts, dosses, or safehouses. The parties will intercept the PCs in public, invite them to meetings at their bases, or otherwise look to have conversations that might turn to violence. Good luck out there!
