## Visitors

While the Habitat might seem cramped to the ultra-rich, it’s still a luxury pad in orbit, giving it some caché. With a decent amount of empty luxury space, the Habitat has taken to welcoming exclusive conferences and tourists. It may be an old facility, but the interior trappings can still be spectacular. Want to cap off your business meeting by sipping on champagne chilled by frozen moon rocks while the sun rises over the earth below? The Habitat awaits.
