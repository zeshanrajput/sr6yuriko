## Radiant Hell Hounds

These badass toxic mutant hell hounds have improved physical attributes, regeneration, and an energy aura. They hunt in packs—though exiled lone hunters are not uncommon. Due to the savage competition of their chaotic pack hierarchy, few survive long on their own.
