## Infrasound Emitter (Rating 1-4)

Infrasonic sound waves elicit feelings of discomfort or fear on a subconscious level even when they lie outside the afflicted target’s audible range. This implant augments the user’s vocal tract to broadcast powerful sound waves on these frequencies when performing a low growl. An infrasound emitter grants the ability to use a non-magical version of the Fear critter power (p. 224, _SR6_ ) that affects all other metahumans and vertebrate critters within 5 meters of the user. The infrasound emitter’s rating is used in place of Magic. If a target lacks the ability to hear these frequencies, they do not identify the user as the source of this effect and would instead consider their current location as the source of their fear. If the user also has a vocal modulator (p. 285, _SR6_ ), an infrasound emitter also permits the user to perform regular speech that is imperceptible by those without augmented hearing.

**Cyberware overdrive:** An infrasound emitter’s range is doubled, and its rating is considered 2 higher, ignoring the maximum rating of 4. One of the user’s dice for using the Fear critter power is converted to a wild die.

**Wireless bonus:** The sound waves can be directed to exclude specific, potential targets.
