## Gammaware

Gamma grade typically appears when referencing theoretical or next-generation cyberware and bioware that is not mass-produced. Gammaware could be a unique prototype or one-off experimental implant that is deep in the development envelope. If the gamemaster decides to incorporate a gamma-grade item in the game, it should have lower Essence loss than delta grade and not be available for a regular purchase. Of course, untested experimental prototypes may also have some unexpected side effects that show up at the worst possible time.

For the purposes of the cyberadept quality path (p. 87-88, _Hack & Slash_ ), gammaware should be considered to have a 0.4 Essence multiplier, and the difference in Essence loss becomes an Essence hole.
