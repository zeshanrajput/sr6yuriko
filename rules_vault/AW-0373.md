## Brazilian Kiwi

While it should probably be called the Tandoran Kiwi, some extensive testing on my part confirmed that this is indeed the same plant that was mentioned in Dunkelzahn’s Will: “To the first party who successfully cultivates the Brazilian kiwi outside its native environment, I leave 10 million nuyen and funds for further research to be provided by the Draco Foundation.”

The Tandorans were unwilling to guide me to a location where they could be found in the wild, but they are commonly found in the gardens and orchards of Kyoko. They were more than willing to sell me as many kiwis as I liked, but they did not share any of their agricultural secrets. To be fair, I didn’t bother helping them cash in on Big D’s will, either. I wonder if the same techniques, fertilizers, or whatever it is they use to make it work would have the same effect on Earth, or if the magical metaplanar environment sustains them here in some way.
