## **Warpath Sympathizer**

**While you are not a full-fedged member of Warpath, you share their core belief: The Treaty of Denver was a farce, and all ancestral land should have been returned, not just part of it. Whether sharing your wealth, giving them shelter, or offering your service, Warpath knows they can count on your help. It is a risky affliation, however, and if it gets discovered, the consequences could be dire.**
