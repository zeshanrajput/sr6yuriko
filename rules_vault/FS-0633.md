## traditionalist

## Shaman

## (Professional Rating 4)

Magic isn’t common anywhere, but the First Nations attracts disenfranchised NAN youth, many with the Talent, like moths to a flame. They see a cause, a chance to rebel, and a way to learn without sweating in a lodge or freezing their hoops off on a vision quest in the woods. They have a bit of a wild streak, and most see their gift as a reason to feel superior—some even think they deserved to be worshipped. The gang has, on occasion, had issues with their shamans turning toxic from the lack of discipline and the abuse of their power.
