## Discombobulate

## [Con (Performance), Major Action] 5 Edge

You act so strangely and unexpectedly that it shocks a target into inaction, or at least makes it extremely hard for them to concentrate. Make a Con (Performance) + Charisma test opposed by the target’s Composure. If successful, the target gains the Dazed status for one round, plus one more round for every two net hits. However, if the attempt fails or results in a glitch, the one attempting the performance becomes Dazed for one round. On a critical glitch, this increases to one minute.
