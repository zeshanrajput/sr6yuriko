## Optional Rule:

## Obsolescence

For those looking for a grittier, more political commentary on the Sixth World, we offer a chance to see the shiny chrome they have installed decrease in value and usefulness over time. Note that this can be particularly harsh when applied only to those who are augmented, so we recommend that this apply to everyone.

When using this optional rule, a runner must stay reasonably up-to-date with current fashion/software/techniques and training or they fall behind those who do. This costs time and money, and even those who manage to keep up with recent developments can fall afoul of a sudden change in technology.

Each month, a runner with a lifestyle of Low or higher must make an Edge test.

Runners who have a High lifestyle or higher only suffer negative consequences on a glitch.

Runners who have a Medium or Low lifestyle suffer negative consequences on a simple fail or a glitch.

Runners who have a Street or Squatter lifestyle automatically fail this test.
