## Optional Rules: Effects on Various Creatures

## Non-Sapient Paracritters

Paracritters of a non-sapient nature seek to retreat to dens, kennels, or warrens, hiding from a cold and impassive world. Most suffer from one or more statuses: Confused, Dazed, Fatigued, Frightened, or Stilled.
