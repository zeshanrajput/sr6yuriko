## Wuxing: Corruption Over Harmony

According to our defector, out of all the megacorporations still actively allied with the Disians, Wuxing may be the one most likely to crack, and soon.

Because of their intimate knowledge of the manasphere and mastery of geomancy, Wuxing became a prime target for infiltration by the Disians soon after their existence was discovered. It was also this devotion to the manasphere that made it difficult for most to believe that Wuxing would side with the beings who were actively trying to destroy it. But as is often the case when metahumans are involved, if the offer is valuable enough,

someone will be willing to pay the price. And in this case, the Disians offered the Wuxing magicians and geomancers the potential to expand their knowledge into these realms far beyond what they could have ever comprehended. And for a select few, they made the standard offer of immediate power as a Faustian.

The defector also reported that the Disians initially made assurances that their mana-harvesting plans would not harm the material plane; that their methods were more akin to taking the fruit from a tree while leaving the healthy tree to grow more. In fact, they claimed that with their assistance, they could make the trees stronger than before. Whether this story was true or not, it was enough to convince the powers that be at Wuxing that a partnership with the Disians would be a beneficial one.

Wuxing’s main task was twofold: to help map the ley lines across the globe and determine where the Harvesters could be placed for maximum effect (known as the Divine Map), and to help solve the problem of harmonizing the material plane’s mana energy with that of the Disian metaplane, thereby enabling the transfer of energy from the material plane to the other.

As the two powers started working together, the lie eventually became more evident, especially when it came to the utilization of metahumans in the Harvesters, and the damage the harvesting would actually cause. By that time, however, it was too late. Wuxing’s leaders realized how deep they were in with the Disians. Also, several high-ranking officials within the corporation had already gotten a taste of Disian power and vowed to keep it, at all costs. Those who dissented were quickly and quietly disappeared.

Even Wuxing president, Sharon Chiang-Wu, fearful not only for those in her company, but especially for her five daughters, towed the line for a time and hoped that the Disians would leave once they got what they wanted. But then, without warning or explanation, the Disians went into what we would describe as panic mode and accelerated their plans for the Final Project.

It was at that time that President Chiang-Wu realized the full extent of the danger and that capitulation wouldn’t save anyone. She then began to covertly gather supporters to create an insurrection within her own corporation. And when the time was right, she charged one of her best security agents with enacting an operation that allowed him to extract himself from the corporation _and_ take with him everything Wuxing knew about the Disians, including the most up-to-date version of the Divine Map to give to the leaders of the nascent Defender Movement.

This operation was successful, and agents from Grey Cell were able to secure the defector and his data. But things didn’t stop there for Wuxing.
