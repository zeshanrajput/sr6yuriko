## Triceratops Bio-Drone Prototype

The triceratops bio-drone is designed and equipped as a heavy combat juggernaut, with an exoframe that mounts additional hardened armor plating and a heavy turret with an RPK machine gun for ranged attacks. When operating independently as a bio-drone, its turret fires independently as a separate drone that can make two attacks each combat round on its own. When controlled by riggers, two riggers jump in as a team, one controlling the dinosaur and the other acting as a gunner for the turret.
