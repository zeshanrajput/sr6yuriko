## Network Synergy

You’ve developed your network of contacts to the degree they’ve learned to work well together and rely upon each other, even without your direct involvement. As a result, they all become more effective.

**Cost:** 5 Karma

**Game Effect:** You must have the Contact Network and Network Connections qualities to take this quality. If you gain or spend a Favor Point with each and every contact in your network over the course of a session, the Karma cost to purchase this quality is not doubled.

Every contact within your network functions as if they have +1 to their Connection rating. This doesn’t apply when determining the cost to raise a network contact’s Connection rating.
