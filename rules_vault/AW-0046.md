## Roche

Similar to Damar, Roche has chains of stone rings that meander through hills of refuse and older mining pits. Tall, thin sheets of colored glass material create large conical spaces within the rings for their gardens and pastures. The Drumlins grow crystals in some and let their midden herd graze in others. Unlike Damar’s beautiful stones, Roche’s are more like a salt crust across the field. The midden herd’s pastures are not as aesthetically appealing either, with puce-colored asbestos material growing in patches.
