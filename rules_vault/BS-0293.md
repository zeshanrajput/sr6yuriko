## Food Poisoning (Gastroenteritis)

- **Vector:** Ingestion

- **Speed:** 4 hours (1)

- **Power:** 7

- **Nature:** Bacterial

- **Effect (Ongoing):** For three days, the infected individual gains the Nauseated and Fatigued II status conditions as they experience vomiting, stomach cramps, and diarrhea.

- **Effect (Final):** At the end of the three days, the individual resists (Power)S damage. Any glitch or critical glitch results in kidney failure and hospitalization. Recovery from kidney failure is through a replacement organ or through nanite treatments over the period of two weeks.
