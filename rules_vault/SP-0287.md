## **Interception**

**Evo hires the player characters to intercept a convoy that is transporting a mysterious cargo from one out-of-the-way location to another. They don’t tell the players that the convoy is carrying the main data-processing core for a Disian Harvester or that it is guarded by a team of chimera and led by an actual Faustian. This mission may take place somewhere that suits the gamemaster and players; it could be an overland convoy of ground vehicles, an aircraft, boat, or even a submarine.**
