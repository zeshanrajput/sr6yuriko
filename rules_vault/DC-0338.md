## Handling Mods

Handling is a measure of how easy a vehicle is to maneuver. It’s a combination of a smooth ride, effective turning radius, responsiveness of the controls, and the ability to handle the effects of rough roads or turbulent air. The total modified Handling value on any vehicle may not be below one.
