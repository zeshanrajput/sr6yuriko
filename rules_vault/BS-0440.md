## Chill BTL

- **Vector:** Slot

- **Speed:** 1 combat round

- **Duration:** (Rating x 5) minutes

- **Power:** 6 + (Rating)

- **Effect:** +1 Charisma, –1 Willpower

Chill is a great BTL for those who suffer from anxiety. It loads behavioral patterns to initiate calming breathing techniques and suppress some chemical responses that raise heart rate. Users of this BTL

will appear to have a general outward calm that others are attracted to. The user of this BTL gains a point of situational Edge on Social tests and a point of situational Edge when resisting Social tests. The Chill BTL does not have any crash effect.
