## Putting it Back Together

So now you know what’s broke and needs to be fixed. How do you put it back together? It’s not like you can twiddle your fingers and magically heal a car, let alone a top-of-the-line combat drone. It’s too technological, too processed to handle magic effectively. So it falls to the mundane to turn a wrench and get these things back into tiptop shape.
