## Oneiro

(Dreamsage, Delphi)

- **Vector:** Inhalation

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** 2D6 minutes

- **Power:** 12

- **Effect:** Stilled status, use of Divination metamagic (p. 117, _Street Wyrd_ )

This Awakened drug is burned like incense. When the vapors are inhaled, they produce vivid visions and dreams with prophetic insight. Addiction to this substance is common due to the tantalizing nature of being able to glimpse the future. Users of this drug gain temporary access to Divination metamagic, making Logic + Intuition test (threshold set by the Divination table on p. 118, _Street Wyrd_ ). If the user knows the Augury and Sortilege ritual, they may use it to interpret the dreams as normal. If they do not have this ritual, they gain the Confused 2 status for the next hour once the drug has worn off.
