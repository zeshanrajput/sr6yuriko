## Octahedron

The great city of Octahedron spreads widely, with long colored streams churning in a spiral. Each arm is a densely populated residential neighborhood, moving at various speeds. It’s the largest congregation of air elementals within the metaplane. Various vortexes drift from the hub of the Octahedron, changing the direction of the winds on the whim of the queen. The heart of the Octahedron is a storm mixing layers of denser gases. Wind shear prevents intruders from entering without an invitation. Within the eye of the storm is the court of Queen Candela. From here, she rules over her little part of the metaplane.
