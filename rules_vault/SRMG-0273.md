## **How does Astral Gateway (p. 66) and Metaplanar Gateway (p. 167,** _**Astral Ways**_ **) work in** _**SRM**_ **?**

For clarity about the powers and rituals, we’ve distilled down what each of these do?

1. An Astral Gateway only forces all to become dual natured and allows all subjects to voluntarily project. No physical matter can traverse this. And the ritual isn’t fast: six hours.

1. Metaplanar Gateways ritual and critter power allow for the physical transfer of materials from the physical plane to a metaplane. It opens a gateway for someone to step through. People and items are not pulled or drawn through. Transiting between the physical to a metaplane and back is not precise, making it impossible to set your return location to something other than the point you entered and instead ends up within 1d6 x 100 kilometers from any intended target. It doesn’t allow transit from between the two places on the same plane.
