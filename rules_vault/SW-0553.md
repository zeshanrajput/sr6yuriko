## Game Information

With this metamagic, an initiate learns to better protect themselves from this kind of mana backlash. It’s not perfect, but it helps. On a critical

glitch during Step 5: Craft the Focus, the Essence lost is reduced by Initiate Grade x 0.1 (to a minimum of 0).
