## Nagato Corporation

The average shadowrunner knows two things about Nagato: They’re affiliated with a meta-friendly branch of the Yakuza, and their elite security force, the Green Serpent Guard, rival the Red Samurai in skill, if not numbers. Nagato is a corporate conglomerate comprising seventeen separate corporations in industries ranging from consumer electronics to military weapons.

The corporation is currently headed by Akasuki Sato, who is also the driving force behind the relocation to NYC. The move comes as part of developing alliances with a number of corporations, including Saeder-Krupp and Shiawase.

- There are questions surrounding the inclusion of the Nagato Corporation in the MDC. The choice to vote for them over MCT put Renraku and Shiawase in a position to have to justify that vote to the larger MCT. Fortunately for those corporations, MCT has focused its attention on Nagato instead of them.

- Mr. Bonds

- Nagato’s meta-friendly stance makes them more like Evo than any of the other Japanacorps. At the same time, that stance puts them at odds with the larger Yakuza organization they remain a part of.

- Mihoshi Oni
