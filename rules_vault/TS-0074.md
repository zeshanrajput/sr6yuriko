## **—“Mr. Black”, Evo Corporation Consultant**

**Riot Control is like demolitions in that it is rarely an established department. Instead, when command believes that a riot is possible, or in the event one unexpectedly starts, patrol offcers will be pulled, issued riot gear, and temporally assigned to tactical. SWAT team members will then act as team leaders for units of riot shield- and stun baton-armed patrol offcers for the duration of a crowd control event.**

- **If it gets bad enough, it’s all hands on deck; detectives, desk sergeants, even data-pushers get issued body armor, a club, and a shield and go stand shoulder-to-shoulder.**

- **Hard Exit**

- **Worse, since almost no one is trained in proper riot operations and de-escalation, mistakes are common when other officers are deployed for riot duty. They panic, take swings, the crowd responds, and things go bad, fast.**

- **Bull**



- **You also get cops hungry to bust heads finally given free reign, so the clubs fly freely.**

- **Slamm-0!**

**Para-Critter Control units are set up to capture, remove, or sometimes kill dangerous Awakened animals encroaching on metahuman occupied areas. While more common in rural forces, some urban forces also have teams of “bug hunters” attached to the tactical division. Again, this is rarely a dedicated department and more often it is composed of SWAT team members who have had some training in animal handling and maybe parazoology. Specialists from magical operations and K9/PK operations are likely to be called out with these units.**

- **When they assumed all ghouls were feral, “extermination” teams were placed here to deal with us. We haven’t forgotten.**

- **> Hannibelle**

**Nobody does crowd control like Sternschutz. Organized to an almost sickening degree, utilizing an army of drones, and deploying a few Awakened personnel to really send shockwaves through the masses. Their polizisten act as the shield line and make up the bulk of the riot control forces, but the fabled Sonder Schutztruppe (Special Protection Force or “SST”) and Sonderabteilungen für Magie und Thaumaturgie (Special Department of Magic and Thaumaturgy or “SAMT”) units are extensively utilized as well.**

**The priority of any riot control scenario is preventing the amassing of bodies. Constant dispersal and the elimination of priority targets (often identifed as individuals who are rousing the crowd to more violent endeavors) is essential to shattering the mob mentality that has taken hold. Sternschutz is phenomenal at this.**

**Sternschutz shield lines are immovable and are supported by cadres of drones. These drones intercept thrown projectiles from the crowds, to protect the polizisten who are holding the line, and can simultaneously deploy less-than-lethal munitions into the crowd. Special Protection Force detachments are utilized like a scalpel in these situations. SST operatives identify agitators within the crowd, rapidly deploy, apprehend the person of interest, and pull back from the greater mob. This application of precise force allows the removal of dangerously infuential persons from the mob, thereby diminishing the crowd’s fervor and organization. SAMT squads are often deployed onto rooftops for overwatch, and act as an often-lethal deterrent to the worst of the mob. These operatives also support the SST with identifying and removing VIP targets from the crowd.**

**Sternschutz’s adept handling of mass civil disobedience, and overwhelming presence in Berlin, has become so well-known that anarchists and other agents of chaos have come to avoid the city whenever possible, preferring to take their love for depredation elsewhere.**

**—David Ahle, Retired Sternschutz Marketing Lead**
