## **You’re About to be Sentenced**

**Let’s start with an easy one. Have you gone through the judicial system, fought the good fght, and lost? Are you staring down a solid stretch in the clink? Then it might be time to get away. Don’t show up at that sentencing hearing, because you won’t be allowed to leave it. Unless you think you can handle some hard time, get out of there and start your new life.**

- **A little hard time doesn’t have to be a deal-breaker. You get the state to feed you for a year, money in your hidden accounts earns a year’s worth of interest, you probably don’t get shot at while you’re inside, and maybe you can build up some legal knowledge. You get out, act rehabilitated, and you don’t have to cut ties with the people who are important to you.**

- **> Kia**

- **Just know law enforcement is going to watch you a little more carefully, so you’ll have to be even smoother than before if you expect to continue a criminal lifestyle.**

- **Red Anya**
