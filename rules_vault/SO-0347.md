## Concealable Surveillance Gear

Many of the small and boring items that we wear can house sensors. This includes things like a hat, pendant, or maybe your favorite pen. The item is non-functional except that it houses the sensor and looks completely innocuous.

The device can house a single sensor and has a concealability threshold of 8.
