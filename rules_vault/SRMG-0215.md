## **Can I show up with zombies (p. 12-14,** _**That Old Voodoo**_ **)?**

Creating a zombie is inherently dangerous and fraught with peril. For every zombie a runner brings to a session, roll (2d6 – Force). If the result is under three, you have lost control of the zombie at some point during downtime and show up to the run with the Zombie's force in stun. If the result is under zero, the zombie becomes a grand zombie, does (Force x 3)S damage to the summoner, and escapes. When this occurs, the whole party gains +2 Heat modifier.
