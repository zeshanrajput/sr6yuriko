## What They Want

OmniStar is, on Earth, all about maximizing scope of services. From education to enforcement to cleaning up when things go wrong, their magical resources have been focused on serving those ends: training magicians, using magicians to enforce the law (public and private, criminal and administrative), and providing medical and other holistic services in addition to DocWagon’s famed armed-response abilities. They want information, expansion, and critical information they can use to concentrate control in their centers of power.
