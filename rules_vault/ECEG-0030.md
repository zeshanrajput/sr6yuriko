## Security Rating: B

While the Port of New Orleans may only be one port, the reality is that for us, it’s two. The nice clean legal port and the smuggler’s port that uses all the same shipping lanes to move its goods. The port here is perhaps the most important international port in the CAS, and it moves absolutely everything.

Much of this district is built on mutual aid, community, and the support of the Krewe of Zulu.
