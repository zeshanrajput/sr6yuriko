## Wuxing

Devil rats and ordinary rats are troublesome vermin in urban areas and are notoriously difficult to root out. Wuxing has developed an unusual solution to this problem. Enter the Wuxing Pied Piper, a genetically modified and lightly augmented demon rat that is designed and trained to use its animal control powers to gather up rats (both devil rats and ordinary rats) and lead them into large containers. The demon rat does not pursue the other rats into the container but rather orders them to remain in place until it is sealed. The rats inside the container are quickly disposed of, and the Pied Piper heads off to round up another batch. Pied Pipers are expensive to manufacture and require special training before they are ready, but they are also incredibly effective.
