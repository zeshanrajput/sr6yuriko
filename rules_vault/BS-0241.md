## Cognitive Shield (1-3)

The user’s neural network is augmented by implanted, engineered nerve clusters. A cognitive shield uses these clusters in aggregate to compartmentalize the nervous system and evaluate whether impulses are natural voluntary or involuntary muscle contractions, or whether they are anomalous with no explainable origin and to be rejected. Not only does this implant cure many kinds of dyskinesias, it has the added benefit of offering the user some protection against being puppeteered by hackers or magical forces.

The player may add +1 die per level of cognitive shield to any defense test against external control of your body. The player cannot choose when to use it, and once used, it takes twenty-four hours for the chemical to replenish within the gland. Additionally, cognitive shield adds to the resistance test for ripjacks (p. 125).
