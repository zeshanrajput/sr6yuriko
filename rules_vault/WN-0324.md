## 20: grey matters

The PCs have found information about research originating from a place called the Octagon. A little Matrix research finds that a structure called the Octagon was part of the Metropolitan Hospital on Roosevelt Island until 1955, when it was abandoned. It later fell into corporate hands, and information about the research that went on there is worth looking into. A trip to the island is in order!
