## Strategies

Try to anticipate as many possibilities as you can, but only invest any significant effort in the ones that seem to be leading to the results you’re looking for. Look for opportunities to work into your plans—don’t be too attached to any one idea. You should have a wide range of useful spells and always a spirit or two at the ready. Pick up a variety of additional skills to help fill out your general usefulness, which are often handy for supporting the other runners using teamwork.
