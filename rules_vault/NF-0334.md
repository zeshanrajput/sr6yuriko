## New Gear

They say it’s a poor craftsman who blames his tools. That might be true, but I’ve yet to see an album go platinum with a toy xylophone, a plastic drum set, and two broomstick guitars. The simple truth is that it takes money to make money, and in the world of entertainment and media, that’s as true as it is any place else. There are plenty of garage bands full of big-name talent out there, but without a sound system, no concert will take your manager seriously. And if you don’t have a manager? You have work to do.
