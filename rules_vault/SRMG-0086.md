## **If my equipment takes Matrix damage or is bricked, how long does it take to repair?**

Equipment that is bricked during gameplay is assumed to be fully repaired and between _Missions_ . To repair items during a _Mission_ , use the repair rules ( _SR6_ , p. 175). A bricked device can function again when it has even a single box restored to its Condition Monitor.
