## **When do I use Multiple Attack and when do I use Firing Modes? How do they interact?**

As stated in the corrected third printing, firing modes that grant multiple attacks don’t require the multiple attacks action. However, if you want to use SS, SA burst, or BF Narrow burst attacks on multiple targets, that will still require the multiple attacks minor action. And the expenditure of that additional ammo!
