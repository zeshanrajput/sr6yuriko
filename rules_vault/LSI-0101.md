## Hotel California

Like the song says, when you enter, you might never leave. Within your home, there’s a physical or magical manifestation that occasionally keeps you trapped within for 2D6 hours, unable to contact anyone until you are mysteriously released—no Matrix connection, no astral travel, nothing. No more than once per session, when you enter your home, the GM may roll 1D6, with a roll of 1 triggering this event.
