## **Pack Attack**

**Only critters that have been trained to attack upon command may learn the pack attack command. Critters can be trained to attack in groups, using the Grunt Group rules (p. 114,** _**SR6**_ **). You can order critters to attack as a pack or have them back you up—in which case you are the primary attacker, and your own Attack Rating and dice pool are increased based on how big your pack is.**
