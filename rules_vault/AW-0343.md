## Airship Weapons

## Ballistae

Ballistae are effectively large crossbows that are too big to be used without mounting them to a ship (or the ground, I suppose). Reloading a ballista requires two Major Actions: one to crank back the cable, and the other to place the bolt. To speed up reloading time, two characters may work together to reload the weapon.

If a high-explosive ballista bolt strikes its target, it does not deal extra damage from net hits. Instead, it explodes, dealing damage to its target and everything within a five-meter blast radius (characters and items below decks are unharmed). This explosion can ignite flammable objects and cause secondary explosions.

If an attack from a barbed ballista bolt does at least one point of damage, it becomes lodged in the airship. Removing it requires an Engineering + Logic (6, 1 Major Action) Extended test. If a rope is attached, reduce all attack ratings by –3 (to a total of –5).
