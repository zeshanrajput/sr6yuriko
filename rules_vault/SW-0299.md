## New Adept Powers Aid Sorcery

## **Cost:** 0.5 PP per level

**Activation:** Major Action (Minor to maintain) An adept with this power can channel their inner magic to aid a nearby spellcaster with their sorcery. The adept must touch the spellcaster and spend a Major Action to open a mana conduit between them, and they must maintain physical contact and spend a Minor Action every subsequent round to keep the conduit open. A spellcaster that casts a spell while a conduit is maintained in this way adds the adept’s Aid Sorcery ranks as bonus dice on any Sorcery test (also including Counterspelling and Ritual Spellcasting). After each time a spellcaster uses these bonus dice, the adept must resist drain in the form of Stun damage equal to their ranks in Aid Sorcery.
