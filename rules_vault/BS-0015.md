## Optional Rules:

## Essence Loss from Physical, Emotional, or Magical Trauma

If you are interested in playing a grittier, more detailed setting, then you might consider whether you want to include Essence loss from other sources. This is something to discuss with your whole group, as Essence loss can affect different characters in different ways, and some of these causes of Essence loss can involve sensitive topics that people may not want to include in their game. The optional rule doesn’t affect all archetypes equally—a mage is generally a lot more sensitive to Essence loss than a face or a rigger, and even a small loss could cause significant changes to adepts if it triggers magic loss. By the same token, some street samurai might not care in principle, but might be running pretty close to empty on their Essence so that even a small loss might have dramatic consequences.
