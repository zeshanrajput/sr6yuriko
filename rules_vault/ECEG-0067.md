## A Tale of Two Krewes

When talking about smuggling through the New Orleans port and delta, there are two krewes you need to know about. There are many small krewes also operating in the area, but few of them will take a job if they know either of these are already involved. I’ll go through both for you, but it’s worth knowing that they vary mostly in methods and location, not in what work they take. These are both heartless krewes that will smuggle anything, including live cargo, the worst drugs, and snuff BTLs. It can be profi table to work with them, but watch your back if you do.
