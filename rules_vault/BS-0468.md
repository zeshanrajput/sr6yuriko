## AI Host Device

While most AIs (and similar entities) prefer to make their homes in devices that have a permanent physical location, some have chosen to employ a special form of service animal that allows them to move about in the physical world. A specialized commlink, cyberdeck, or other similar device designed to house an AI can be implanted in a critter so that the AI can make their home in its headware. In most cases, this is also combined with a critterjack and critter comm augmentation, though some AIs utilize a biodrone harness in order to take direct control of their cybermount.

**Game Information:** This is the critter equivalent of an implanted commlink or cyberdeck, though the intended function is notably different.
