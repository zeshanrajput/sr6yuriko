Gabrielle Spinrad didn’t wait for her control to be secured before going on the offensive. Since she became CEO, she’s reviewed and taken over (or abandoned) more than a few crucial projects initiated or managed directly by her late husband. But she also seeded a few of her own along the way. And she is proving to be quite a savvy leader.

As said above, with the untimely demise of Johnny Spinrad, Gabrielle was simply too late to the Disian ball, yet instead of catching up on this front and joining the war against Dis—or selling their soul to our enemies—she went down another road. She decided to exploit the situation for Spinrad Global’s benefit.

- One of the things Miranda King did for Gabrielle was to hire some prime runners for a deep cover job in the Rhine Ruhr Megaplex. The goal was to unveil some S-K secrets, and the runners quickly followed clues leading to their plotting with Dis. After a year, the team pieced together a dossier, the contents of which are akin to the one we did for the **Slip Stream** download. It took a little while for this detailed report to make it to her desk, but it was the key to her really understanding what was going on. Especially as the Awakened on the team had already intuited that the Disians’ metaplane might be dying and that desperation was most likely what was driving its denizens. Of course, they didn’t figure out that their endgame would be the demise of the Sixth World, so it’s unclear if Gabrielle is aware of the ramifications of the Final Project. But in case we, as a species, survive the next months, it’s a nice reminder that the work we do in the shadows often matters more than we may think.

SG’s strategy against S-K in the Caliphate is two-pronged. The first one involves messing with the constantly uneasy alliances between the AAAs working with the Disians, or leaking intel to the faction opposing it, whether it’s Renraku, Evo, or even Grey Cell. This is coordinated with the second

prong, which consists of surgical strikes on related assets or projects of Saeder-Krupp. Of course, those massive operations generally include weeks of preparation and manipulation, plus a few jobs that need to be executed precisely.

In April 2082, S-K even announced the imminent launch of a new line from Buraidah to Medina.

- For years now, the New Islamic Jihad regularly tried (and failed) to strike S-K trains. It’s assumed that Spinrad Global (or the al-Shammar family) are using the terrorists against their competitor, but there is no proof of that.
