## Specialized Traits

The elements above are common to pretty much all _Shadowrun_ characters, but there are some things that are particular to specialized characters, notably Awakened characters and Matrix-focused characters. These include: the **spells** mages use (p. 131), a magician’s **Initiate Grade** (p. 166), **metamagics** (p. 167), **adept powers** (p. 156), **cyberdecks** (p. 174), **programs** (p. 184), **complex forms** (p. 189), and **echoes** (p. 195).
