## Portable Facility (CF Mod)

A vehicle with enough interior space can be made into a mobile facility. A vehicle modified in this way counts as being a Facility (p. 273, _SR6_ ), but because of the limited space available in a vehicle, it must be focused on one specialization of an active skill, like Medicine, Hardware, Armorer, or Automotive Mechanic. This specialization must be chosen at the time the mod is installed and cannot be changed.

50,000¥ of this price is the cost of the Facility, and that’s a fixed cost; if you do this conversion yourself, you only save 50 percent of the remaining 25,000¥, or 12,500¥.
