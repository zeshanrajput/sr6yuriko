## **GMC Pegasus**

**GMC are the kings of the LAV, so when LEP frms started making demands for a pursuit craft that could at least have a chance to keep up with the t-birds used by smugglers but not break the bank, GMC answered with the Pegasus. While not nearly as armed or armored as the Banshee or the Gryphon, the Pegasus is very much a contender for king of the sky based on speed and maneuverability alone.**
