## Therapeutic Nanites

**Anti-Rad:** Popular among spacers and workers in high-ambient-radiation environments such as the SOX, anti-rad nanoware scours the body via the lymphatic system for isotope traces, free radicals, and radiation-damaged cells, expelling them from the body. As a protective system against background radiation, an anti-rad nanite colony grants the owner bonus dice for Radiation Resistance tests equal to the nanite volume of the colony. For higher-energy exposures happening over a single combat round, the active nanite volume is added to the DR, and any level of active nanite volume provides a point of situational Edge that can be used in that Damage Resistance test.

- **Nanite Volume:** The nanite volume of the colony sets the level of protection against radiation, adding the active nanite volume to the Damage Resistance test or the DR.

- **Overdrive:** Using overdrive provides one automatic hit on the Damage Resistance test for each point of nanite volume put in overdrive. Such an aggressive use of this technology is a shock to the body, causing unresisted



Stun damage equal to the volume of nanites placed into overdrive.

**Nano Health Suites:** This exhaustive suite of nanotools is tailor-made to ensure that the owner has the best possible resistance to pathogens, toxins, and other foreign compounds. The nanites hunt down everything from cancer cells to ethyl alcohol. The nano health suite grants the owner bonus dice for Disease Resistance tests, Toxin Resistance tests, and Drug Resistance tests. The nanohive may be programmed on the fly to allow specific beneficial drugs to work normally.

- **Nanite Volume:** The nanite volume of the colony sets the level of protection against diseases and toxins, adding the active nanite volume to the resistance test.

- **Overdrive:** Using overdrive provides one automatic hit on the resistance test for each point of nanite volume put in overdrive. This is a shock to the body, causing unresisted Stun damage equal to the volume of nanites placed into overdrive.

**Oxyrush:** Banned from most professional sports, this nanite colony aids the user by increasing the amount of oxygen that is carried in the circulatory system and helps to scrub any carbon dioxide in the bloodstream. This nanoware can reduce or remove the Fatigued status.

- **Nanite Volume:** The nanite volume removes one level of Fatigued for every two units of volume active in the colony.

- **Overdrive:** Placing a point of nanite volume in overdrive provides a bonus point of Edge to use on Sprint or Swimming tests.

**Savior System:** Using the same nanotech found in the savior medkit, this system runs an advanced expert system on the nanohive, acting as a rating 6 medkit or a rating 12 trauma patch. The system consumes one unit of nanite volume when activated. Any remaining nanite volume active in a colony provides a dice pool bonus to this test. As the system is internal, the savior system only requires thirty seconds to make a diagnosis and begin treatment. The nanite-based healing process takes thirty seconds per box healed with a threshold of 5 – Essence.

- **Nanite Volume:** One point of nanite volume is used in the Healing test, and the remainder of the colony adds the active nanite volume to the dice pool of the Healing test.

- **Overdrive:** Using overdrive allows the nanite volume that would normally provide a dice pool bonus to become an automatic hit instead and changes one of the other dice to a wild die.
