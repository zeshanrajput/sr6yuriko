## Improved Matrix Privacy

You’ve developed Matrix security habits that a fugitive hacker would consider paranoid, and you regularly pay an illicit privacy service to cover your digital tracks. The threshold of all Matrix Search actions to find you is increased by one, and you gain a bonus point of Edge on any tests to resist Matrix Perception and Trace Icon actions that target you.

**Cost: 1 LP**
