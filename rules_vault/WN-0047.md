## East Village (B to C)

As the name suggests, this covers the territory east of SoHo both above and below Houston Street. The East Village butts up against Heritage, which is why the security rating here fluctuates. It is largely a bedroom community built around the middle-class corporate employees of a dozen AA corps that are vying for the attention of the Corporate Court.
