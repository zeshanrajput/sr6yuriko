## Medical Services

Arrangements with CrashCart remain in place as they have for the last twenty years, while emergency services and transportation remain primarily in DocWagon’s purview. CrashCart has expanded with their new Raphael line of transports into the high-rises and skyscrapers, going where a lot of the rotor- and vector-thrust craft simply cannot get to quickly and safely.
