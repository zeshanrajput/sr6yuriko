## \<06-02-83/2232:37>

Just inside the trees was a line of obelisks covered in glowing runes. According to Arcane, they were maintaining a ward of some kind. I passed through without any issue and the Court’s metaplanar translation stopped. I was once again my normal cybered-up self. Some of the others had trouble getting past it, having to drop spells and deactivate their foci. Once past the ward, the facility and the device could be seen ahead; a white tower of honeycomb crystal surrounded with all the hallmarks of an MCT zero-zone. I dropped into VR and started hacking the host, shutting down sensors, diverting the guards, and taking drones offline. Cat, Specter, and Angelica moved in to plant the demo charges while Arcane followed them, providing an assortment of spells and some help from his free spirit pals.
