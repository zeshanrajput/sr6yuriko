## **Resonance Wells and Dissonance Pools**

**In the simplest terms, a Resonance well makes it easier for a technomancer to connect to the Resonance in the area around the well. Conversely, being around a Dissonance pool makes Resonance tests more difcult. A twisted Dissonant technomancer will sufer the exact opposite efects, with a Resonance well inhibiting their access to the Dissonance (simply interchange the terms Resonance and Dissonance in the rules below).**

**The size of the area under the infuence of a well or pool is up to the gamemaster and may range from a few meters to more than a kilometer. The terms well and pool are also interchangeable, with Dissonance wells and Resonance pools both existing and having the same efects for a given size.**

- **Large Resonance well: Any technomancer* using the Resonance attribute for any test in the area of a large Resonance well automatically gains a bonus Edge before a test.*\*

- **Medium Resonance well: Any technomancer using the Resonance attribute in the area of a medium Resonance well spends 1 Edge less on Edge actions and Edge boosts than normal (to a minimum of 1 Edge).**

- **Small Resonance well: Any technomancer using the Resonance attribute in the area gains 1 bonus die on any test where Resonance is part of the dice pool.**

- **Small Dissonance pool: Any technomancer in a small Dissonance pool receives a –1 dice pool penalty on any test where the Resonance attribute is part of the dice pool.**

- **Medium Dissonance pool: Any technomancer in a medium Dissonance pool spends 1 Edge more on Edge actions and Edge boosts than normal on tests using the Resonance attribute.**

- **Large Dissonance pool: Technomancers cannot gain or spend Edge on tests using the Resonance attribute in a large Dissonance pool.**

- **Includes all other entities with a Resonance attribute**

- **such as EIs (Emergent Intelligences) and sprites.**
