## Today in Lagos

The city of Lagos is the backdrop for this story, but it is also one of the primary antagonists. While this comes in the form of specific local challenges, they are all part of the struggles of the city itself. This brief primer offers some of the flavor of Lagos in order to help the gamemaster build this setting into the tale they weave rather than it simply being a generic place. The city flavor is also a key component in the decisions the runners will make as the story progresses. How they see the city may very well sway who they help and how they handle the revelations of their investigations.
