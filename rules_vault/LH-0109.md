## Offworld

## Posted by: Polaris

- The work has proceeded well for several months, but new complications have arisen. More than half of the safeholds have been completed, but the rising occurrence of manastorms in the area has made the ritual work difficult. There have also been raids on the labor camps that have grown up around the area, taking anything from supplies to people. These are increasing in frequency, with many believing the metahuman abductions are due to Disian activities said to be centered in Beirut. Sphinx Security has started hiring additional security personnel directly from the population. Hourani Construction has spearheaded all the physical work, while Qur’anic Telesma is the magical coordinator.

- **>** Levantine Son
