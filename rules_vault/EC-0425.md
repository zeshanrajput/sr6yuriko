## Jimmy Kincaid

Got a problem? Odds against you? Call … er, sorry wrong PI. Let me start again. Jimmy lives in a beach house on Oahu in the Independent Kingdom of Hawai’i … no, wait, that guy can grow a mustache.

Sorry, I have to tease my friendly competition in Puyallup, especially when he brings up his master’s degree from U-Dub and his Magical Order of Moose or whatever. He was second-generation law enforcement before he became a private investigator. He mainly works the northern half of the district but has taken cases beyond it. And I hate to say it, but he is really good. Not just for his deductive skills, but he truly is a helpful neighbor.

- It’s a little surprising that he didn’t become a Ranger.
