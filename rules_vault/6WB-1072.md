## Pay a Bribe

This is the way the world works! Money greases the wheels of … well, everything. For every 1,000 nuyen spent in bribes, 1 point of Heat is eliminated. Each 1,000 nuyen bribe only affects the Heat rating of a single person, but multiples of 1,000 can be spent to spread the reductions around.
