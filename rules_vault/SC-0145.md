## The Gadgeteer

Whether you are an enchanter aspected magician or just have a passion for alchemy, you prefer using alchemical preparations to create magical effects over casting spontaneous spells. Ever since you learned that you could prepare magic to go off under certain conditions, you’ve been looking for ever more creative and inventive means of employing them. One of the big advantages that has helped you trick people is having a preparation go off without the spell having come from your direction. That particular tactic of misdirection never gets old, and it’s never failed you.

You use preparations for all manner of tasks, but the one drawback is how much time you need to spend preparing them. That, and the foresight to have the ones you need ready for the moment you need them. While many people see you as an eccentric magician, very few see just how much effort and intention you put into understanding the events and people around you. In order to be prepared, you feel the need to anticipate as much as you possibly can. You are constantly getting in touch with your contacts to keep up on the current rumors and gossip. If you are at all technologically inclined, you love to leave little bugs and other sensor devices where you think you might pick up something useful. Besides being a skilled alchemist, you are well-suited to do the work of an information broker or fixer. But it’s with a team of individuals that cover skillsets you don’t possess that you truly shine. Working together with a coordinated team provides a force multiplier to your gadgets, tricks, and schemes like no other. And you definitely like having friends you can rely on.
