## **If I am using Swarm and link-firing mode, what is the order of applying adjustments?**

The order to apply the bonuses is first to apply the link-fire mode on a single drone to create a “virtual weapon”. After that, you apply the swarm adjustment. For instance, if you have three MCT roto-drones with two weapon mounts, each bearing an Ares Predator VI, the total DV would be 4P with a near AR of 15 and +3 dice pool modifier.
