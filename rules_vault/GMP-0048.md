## **Using a Matrix Search during Legwork** 

**Step One:** The runners make an Electronics + Intuition (Extended, 10 Minutes) test, if multiple runners are doing the research and add the results together, they can continue the extended test until hitting the number of successes they want. Compare the number of successes to the Legwork Block to find out what information is gathered.
