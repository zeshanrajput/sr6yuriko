## **antarctic tunnels**

**Accidently discovered by Professor Robert Ito in 2061 during the passing of Halley’s Comet, a series of tunnels was found nearby via high-resolution ground penetration radar. At frst, it was simply thought to be part of the “city” near the Queen Maud mountains, merely an ofshoot vein of orichalcum that was once present in the area. Decades later, Professor Ito revisited his old data as part of another project and realized that what he had found wasn’t an ofshoot vein but instead could be a network of subterranean and subaquatic tunnels. If projections are correct, these tunnels may encompass the entire continent.**
