## Rane

Rane is one of several cloud cities that are tethered to the steam and smoke from Tulmult. Its normally white architecture is dulled and tinged by the influx of ash. The structures droop slightly, as the whole city is constantly forming precipitation, raining on all within and below. It can make for a miserable time, as even within the vaporous buildings, there are trickles and streams of water along the walls. Why I chose Rane as a point of interest is that for some JackPointers, Rane might seem familiar. Whether it was intentional or not, Rane mirrors the layout of Seattle, including a misty version of the Space Needle.

- Besides Rane, there’s Dunpur and Torrens, which mirror the cityscapes of Copenhagen and Singapore. Beneath the three cities is a flurry of storms that make travel dangerous at times. The storms help obscure smugglers entering Tulmult from the water side of the metaplane.
