## Skin Pigmentation

Skin pigmentation treatment can be used to paint the higher levels of the patient’s skin, giving a color scheme that doesn’t sweat away, instead remaining in place for roughly a week until it fades from standard abrasion (though it can be removed quicker with a skin solvent). Some choose to paint up their favorite sports team’s colors or logos, others dress up for clubbing, while still others simply follow artistic paths. A permanent treatment is also available, drawing on genetic sub-components to change the body’s natural skin color, ensuring that the selected pattern will be repeated from then onward. A second treatment can be undertaken to correct the first.
