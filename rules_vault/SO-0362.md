## Example One

Haze had a lovely evening with Ms. Tara Michaels and managed to acquire some very interesting paydata in the process. Without going into too many details, he secretly planted a data tap. While Haze and Tara (now on a firstname basis) were quite focused on each other, the decker working with Haze hacked into the building’s host and gathered the paydata. Some of it was sold to info brokers for nuyen, but one file revealed evidence of embezzling. Haze considered selling this information but decided to use it directly instead. He then set up a meet with Tara again under false pretenses. When Haze reveals the evidence to her, he gains leverage. This grants a point of circumstantial Edge to Haze when he makes Influence (Intimidation) + Charisma vs. Composure tests to browbeat and blackmail Ms. Michaels.
