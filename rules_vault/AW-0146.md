## Vadose

## (Water, Force 5)

The fluid forms of the vadose is similar in nature to the undine’s common form, but darker and less refined. Whether they don’t care for relating to metahumans or just chose a minimalistic form that suits their needs is still a matter of debate. Vadoses become denser as they work at greater depths. Vadoses aren’t creative problem solvers; they tend to take instructions literally, and they don’t bother trying to understand metahuman vernacular. Vadoses are the lowest caste of water elementals and are treated poorly by other water spirits. This is sometimes an advantage, as vadoses are overlooked and often under-estimated.
