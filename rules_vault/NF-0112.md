## Venues

Let’s talk locations. Sure, there are plenty of arenas to see your favorite acts. Big glitzy megadomes, packed with frenzied fans and merch vendors selling the hottest new holo-weave Ts for the low, low price of nana’s replacement liver. That’s not what we’re all here for. We’re here to discuss those cubbyholes of character, places the corps and the cops either don’t recognize or don’t bother, and where a man might be able to put his feet up or rage the night away with relative comfort.

I’ve been around a bit, and I’ve found that the best method of finding the real heart and soul of a city is to ask your favorite bartender where he goes when the shift ends. What place does he knock back a few in during the wee hours?
