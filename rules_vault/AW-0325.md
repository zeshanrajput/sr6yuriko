## Aztechnology

Aztechnology maintains a small token embassy deep within the Spire’s kindred district. Their primary activity within the Spire is lobbying the bureaucracy for mining rights and access to the Great Portal. They also maintain a small hangar at the edge of the docks that is capable of housing and maintaining up to four t-birds. Outside the Spire, Aztechnology engages in aggressive true element and reagent harvesting and has established small bases on a number of cloud islands as well as a significant presence on Pirate Island. Aztechnology makes use of a few local airships for transporting cargo and personnel from one island to another, but they also have at least six military-grade t-birds for use by execs and high-priority missions.
