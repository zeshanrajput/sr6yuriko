## Zurich-Orbital Habitat

Once the most feared set of astronomical coordinates in the universe, now it’s just another blip passing through the night sky, assuming you can even see the night sky where you are. The long and the short of it is that the Zurich-Orbital Habitat is one of the oldest continuously operating space stations around, and age has made it a liability. At this point, every piece of the station has been replaced or upgraded more than once so that there is no bit of the original _Freedom_ station there.
