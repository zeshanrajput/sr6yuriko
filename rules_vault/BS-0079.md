## CYBERWARE OVERDRIVE BONUSES

Many cybernetic modifications can make use of augmentation overdrive (p. 282, SR6). As is the case with wireless bonuses, when an augmentation in this chapter can benefit from overdrive, its bonus is listed. In some cases, the bonus varies slightly from gaining +2 Rating. Regardless of the nature of the bonus, it always costs an additional Minor Action to invoke cyberware’s overdrive; if the test glitches, the augmentation ceases to work for (rating) combat rounds, minimum of 1.

In addition, any cyberware with a rating can utilize the overdrive bonus as long as the rating can be applied to any test, not just to attributes. For example, a cybereye with vision enhancement can use augmentation overdrive when used to make Perception tests.
