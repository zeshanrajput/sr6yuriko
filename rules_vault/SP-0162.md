## **Rogue Elements**

**Unfortunately for Dis, not everyone got the memo, or perhaps some who did are ignoring it.**

**There are some credible rumors that SG corporate powerhouse Shaheed Zahir has for his own reasons taken this matter somewhat personally. Having power and position in SG analogous to Buttercup’s in Evo, Zahir has learned about the memo and the Disian incursion, and he has personally directed corporate shadow-ops expert Miranda King (no relation to Karen King of Ares Prime) to form a task group to “begin studies regarding certain hypothetical situations and formulate possible responses.” So far, King has stayed under the radar with her “theoretical**

**studies,” but she has reached out to several individuals and groups for their “expert opinions regarding confdential matters.”**

**As yet, King’s work has remained academic, but Zahir has been funneling tremendous amounts of money into her little task and studies group. And in turn, King has been quietly reaching out to multiple shadow contacts. Take that for what you will.**

**> It should be noted that one of these groups is none other than the elite mercenary company 10,000 Daggers.**
