## **What do Extravagant Eyes (6WC, p. 84) do to Hobgoblins?**

A hobgoblin’s eyes are all black in appearance which makes them more memorable to non-hobgoblin populations. This grants Edge on all Memory Tests and when a hobgoblin character would gain Heat, they gain 2 Heat instead.
