## Sylph

## (Air, Force 4)

Sylphs have a semi-translucent humanoid form consisting of clouds or smoky substance. More specifically, the opaque clouds edge and outline the sylph’s form. Details such as eyes, mouth, and ears are hazy. The same for then end of their limbs as they whip away from their form. When dealing with metahumans, some sylphs choose to be more opaque, adjusting their form with denser clouds and mimicking metahuman features. Long pointy ears are quite popular with them. Sylphs enjoy traveling to the material plane more than other air elementals.
