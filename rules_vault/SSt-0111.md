## **Postmortem**

**The postmortem here is simple and short: If the runners had put zero effort into fguring out what their mission truly was about, they would have failed. Giovanna’s troubles at the boat party could have destroyed their cover. Not looking into Milica could have led them to kidnap only her friend. Not realizing that she was in fact Anya Spinrad and that she would have no desire to go with the team would have let Anya manipulate them so that she could escape. And so on.**

**So in short: Don’t jump to any conclusions, and carefully inspect and research every possible angle.**
