## **From Krime Katalog, does a Krime Party grenade do both DVs at once?**

No. The grenade must be configured in either frag or flash-bang mode. It takes a (I) minor action to swap modes.

**From Age of Rust, can I purchase a Buzzard Transit?**

Certainly!
