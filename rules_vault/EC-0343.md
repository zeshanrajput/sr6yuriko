## McNeil North Tunnel

This is part of the never-completed system that has been mentioned in other spots. This one has become another Seattle home for the Sea Dragon, as she bought the property from the city shortly after they declared independence. The island got a healthy shake after the tunnel collapsed below the bedrock, and the tunnel entrance, a massive spiral drive, flooded. Lone Star is not exactly thrilled, but it’s at least one less escape route they need to watch.
