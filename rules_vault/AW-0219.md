## Chimera Types

## Baseline Doppelganger

Doppelgangers are chimera designed to be sleeper agents in the Sixth World. At first glance, they look exactly like they did before re-construction, despite being greatly enhanced. The only way to identify a doppelganger is by the purple color of their blood or through an Assensing (4) test.

( **Note** : While these stats represent a human, other metatypes exist; their attributes can be adjusted accordingly. Skills can also be added as appropriate.)
