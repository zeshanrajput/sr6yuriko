## Nanopaste Disguise

A thick paste that can be rapidly applied to a person’s face to hide or completely change their features. When activated, the nanopaste quickly forms a detailed mask over the original face, creating an instant and effective disguise. When paired with a commlink running the companion app, the user can reconfigure the nanopaste disguise as needed without a new application.
