## **Opposition and Enemies**

**The Big Ten megacorporations, many smaller corporations, and most national governments can**

**be counted among the enemies of the Denver Data Haven. The truth, however, is that their opposition is conditional, as most of these organizations covertly use the Nexus for their own purposes. As for true enemies who are dedicated to the destruction of the Denver Data Haven, only one stands out, though there may be a second one depending on your point of view.**
