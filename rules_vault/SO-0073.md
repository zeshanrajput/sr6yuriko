## Misdirection (I)

Every good performance, act, or solid lie uses something shiny to attract the viewer’s eyes, keeping them away from whatever tell you may have. While they’re not watching what you’re really doing, you can really work your magic. Make a Con + Charisma vs. Willpower + Intuition Opposed test. Your opponent gains the Misdirected status at a level equal to your net hits.
