## BTL

Better-Than-Life chips are a common street drug that give the user intense experiences beyond what can be felt through their normal senses. They grew out of the use of simsense chips, which allowed consumers to experience their favorite trids from the safety of a home unit. BTLs are created when you remove the biofeedback filters on those systems or chips. They require tapping into neural pathways using DNI, whether with trodes, a skilljack, a datajack, or cyberjack. BTLs are classified as either basic BTLs or personafixes, also known as p-fixes.

Some p-fixes and BTLs can grant skill ranks if the user has a skilljack and skillwires. These ranks do not add to existing skills and cannot exceed the limits of the skilljack and skillwiare systems. If you already have the skill, you use the higher of the two ratings.
