## Tracheal filter

Implanted at the top of your trachea, this organ can absorb airborne impurities and keep them from reaching your lungs. The filter adds its rating as a dice pool bonus to Toxin Resistance tests versus Inhalation-vector toxins ( **Toxins and Drugs,** p. 121).
