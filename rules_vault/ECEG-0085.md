## Colo Colo

Named after a mythical rat-like creature known for sucking the saliva out of its victims, this Emerged critter will steal all your data and make using the Matrix just generally more diffi cult. This nasty rodent is about thirty centimeters long with coarse brown fur, and it makes its home along the coastal waterways of the Americas. There is a standing bounty in New Orleans for 10 nuyen per pelt to remove this vermin.
