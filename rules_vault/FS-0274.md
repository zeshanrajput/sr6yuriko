## Ammo Sling

Sometimes the simple things work the best. The ammo sling is basically an inexpensive synth-leather or synth-nylon bandolier with micro-hardpoints that allow ammo pouches to be attached. The idea is for a shooter (or some poor non-combatant) to carry extra ammo for when the shooting goes beyond expectations, but the individual isn’t wearing tactical armor. Ammo slings can also be attached to a weapon—they’re extremely popular with shotgunners—or simply worn on the waist or over the shoulder. For an additional fifteen nuyen, the sling includes a tactical gear pouch for when you need to store your non-bang-bang stuff or even more clips.
