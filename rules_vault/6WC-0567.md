## Berserker

## **Bonus** _:_ 8 Karma

The character has a short fuse. They lack self-control and are prone to fits of mindless, nearly primal rage. They have no control over these fits once they happen, and they are just as likely to strike out violently against friends or innocent bystanders as they are at foes and enemies. When the character takes three or more boxes of Physical or Stun damage, they must make a Composure test (p. 67, _SR6_ ) or fly into this rage. While so enraged, they increase all their physical attributes by +1 (not beyond their racial maximums) and reduce all their mental attributes by –1 (to a minimum of 1). These fits of rage last for (1D6 + total current wound modifier) combat rounds. If the individual possesses an adrenaline pump (p. 291, _SR6_ ), it activates automatically.
