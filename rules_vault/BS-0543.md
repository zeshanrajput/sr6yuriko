## Eligible Critter Powers

**Armor:** The critter’s DR is increased by the rating of the genetic augmentation, up to a maximum equal to its Body attribute. This is compatible with normal armor as well as augmentations that enhance DR.

**Concealment:** The critter’s fur or skin can change color to match its environment. This provides the critter with the Invisibility (Improved) status equal to the rating of the genetic augmentation, up to a maximum of 4, and only functions while the critter remains perfectly still. The critter must stop moving and use a Minor Action to activate its concealment, and it must receive special training to do so on command.

**Corrosive Spit:** This is a mundane ability that is the result of special glands that produce a corrosive liquid, combined with a natural immunity to its own spit. The spit glands take one minute to replenish their ammunition after each use. Use the critter’s Body/2 in place of the Magic attribute to determine the DV and AR of the corrosive spit attack, and use the critter’s Athletics + Agility for the ranged attack test.

**Enhanced Senses:** This provides the critter with an enhanced sense, per the critter power (p. 223, _SR6_ ).

**Hardened Armor:** In order to receive this augmentation, the critter must first have the armor critter power (either naturally or artificially), which is replaced by the hardened armor. The maximum amount of hardened armor is the rating of its original armor power or Body/2, whichever is lowest.

On an opposed test to notice that a mimicked sound is false, use Perception + Intuition vs. the critter’s Charisma + Intuition.

**Noxious Breath:** Instead of using its breath, a critter may be engineered to spray noxious fumes from its other end using a dedicated gland similar to a skunk’s natural defensive measure. This functions exactly like the Awakened version of the critter power (p. 226, _SR6_ ), using the critter’s Agility + Reaction for the attack test and its Body in place of Magic.

**Venom (Vector: Injection):** Mundane venom can be just as effective as the Awakened version. In order to use the venom on command, it must be specifically trained to do so—otherwise, it will use the venom with every attack from its natural weapon. Injecting the venom requires the critter to score at least one net hit on a Close Combat attack using its natural weapon. Instead of being determined by the critter’s Magic attribute, the venom’s Power is equal to the rating of the genetic augmentation, up to a maximum of 8. After each use of venom, the critter may not use this power again until at least one minute has passed.

**Venom (Vector: Contact):** Critters without a natural weapon can be equipped with venom that is delivered on contact from a specific area of its body (usually a paw or tongue). In order to use its contact venom, the critter must use a Minor Action before making an attack. A critter must receive special training in order to deliver an attack using its venom on command. If a critter with this power is ever grappled or engulfed, it may use a Minor Action to excrete its venom. In order to deliver the venom to a target, the critter only needs to touch its target using Close Combat—on a tie, the venom comes into contact with the target. If the critter is grappled or engulfed, no test is required to deliver the venom. In place of the Magic attribute, the venom’s Power is equal to the rating of the genetic augmentation, up to a maximum of 6. After each use of venom, the critter may not use this power again until at least one minute has passed.

**Venom (Vector: Inhalation):** A critter can be equipped with venom glands in its mouth that can be sprayed at a target within Near range. To hit with the venom attack, use the critter’s Athletics + Agility vs. Reaction + Intuition. The venom attack is successful on a tie, requiring no net hits, but every two net hits increases the Power of the venom by 1 due to the effect of striking with a larger concentration. In place of the Magic attribute, the venom’s Power is equal to the rating of the genetic

augmentation, up to a maximum of 6. After each use of venom, the critter may not use this power again until at least one minute has passed.
