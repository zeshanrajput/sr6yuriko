## The Plane of Beasts

Another metaplane that is nearly as close to the physical plane as the Plane of Kin is the Plane of Beasts. This metaplane has been described as “the embodiment of primeval instinct,” and I can’t argue with that. Animals and beasts of all varieties are represented here in the forms of animus spirits—creatures that would not seem out of place on the physical plane, as well as fantastic beings otherwise only found in myths. It is from here that all beast spirits are summoned. Even the plant life here seems more animated and bestial in its nature—it’s best to assume that everything living here is hungry and wants to eat you. The cycle of life is the law of the land, and the food chain is the only hierarchy you will find here. It is not a civilized place, though there are many beasts that form social structures such as packs or even tribes. Nests, burrows, and dens are the closest things to buildings that you will find here.



## THE PLANE OF BEASTS

**Technology Level:** Stonepunk **Magic Level:** Normal **Base Level:** Beasts **Terrain:** Various **Population:** Mixed **Society:** Varied **Distance:** Adjacent




Beast spirits both great and small thrive here, in incredible variety. To the naturalists and the explorers, it is a place of endless wonder. However, few of these spirits have any interest in interacting with astral travelers. They are focused on the rhythms of their own existence—find food, don’t get eaten, repeat—and have little to offer metahumans in the way of information or items. Unlike in the physical plane (in which a spirit’s form dissipates upon disruption), a beast spirit slain within the Plane of Beasts remains as a corpse. Scavengers make very short work of such corpses, but if they are kept at bay, a skilled magician will find that they can be harvested for valuable reagents. Increased demand for reagents has drawn many hunters to the Plane of Beasts in the hopes of making an easy fortune. This plane is inhabited by some incredibly powerful and ancient spirits, some of whom take great offense at the intrusion of metahuman hunters upon their realm. They are quite capable of making short work of any hunting parties from Earth.

It seems possible that the Plane of Beasts is the plane of origin for mentor spirits. One of the items on my bucket list is to confirm this hypothesis, but it has proven difficult. Traveling within the Plane of Beasts without getting eaten is difficult—even purely astrally projecting travelers seem tasty to those ravenous denizens. Even when I have succeeded in making contact with a wise and ancient spirit here, they do not communicate clearly. I wonder if this is an intentional obfuscation, or if it is a result of having a completely different frame of reference. I suspect it’s a little bit of both. The next time I return here, I hope to bring with me a few associates who follow various mentor spirits.
