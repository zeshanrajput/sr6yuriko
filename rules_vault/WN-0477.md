## Indica Meiers

## (Operative, Connection Rating 4)

A long-time agent with the CIA, Indica is a serious operator with a serious tableside manner to match. She’s been assigned to Project Anima in its transitional years to become Animus Construction now and oversees security operations for the Manhattan locations as well as those elsewhere on the East Coast of the UCAS and CAS.

Indica has spiritual escorts to help ensure her security—two Force 5 spirits, one fire, one kindred. She looks human and does little to make people study her that hard to learn otherwise, but she is in fact an ork from the Manhattan Underground. When working with others, they quickly learn she’s just a bit too durable to be “soft and fleshy,” as she refers to it. She is just under a full two meters tall, with a solid build and obvious musculature, in her mid-forties with brown hair and blue eyes.
