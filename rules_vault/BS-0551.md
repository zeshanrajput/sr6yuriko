## Unnatural Immunity

A critter can be modified to be immune to a specific toxin or pathogen, which must be specified when the genetic augmentation is applied. The critter automatically resists all effects of the toxin or pathogen whenever it is exposed, requiring no test. This genetic augmentation may be applied multiple times to make a critter immune to a variety of toxins or pathogens, but the maximum number of immunities is equal to the critter’s Body attribute.

This may also be used to effectively undo an allergy or weakness. The most common consumer application for this among the incredibly wealthy is to create designer dogs that are able to safely consume chocolate.
