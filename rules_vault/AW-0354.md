## Miko

Roughly a century ago—in the 1980s, which would pre-date the Awakening by several decades—a human appeared on the island of Tandora. Her name was Miko, and she was from Osaka, Japan. She had no knowledge of how she arrived in Tandora, and she never found her way back home again. The Tandorans found her wandering the shore of the Great Lake. Despite her shock at finding herself in a bizarre new world and meeting such unusual beings, Miko was courteous, calm, and kind. The Tandorans took her in and learned her language as she learned theirs. They taught her about their history, and she taught them all she knew of the world she had come from. To the Tandorans, it was a herald of the Great Mother’s ancient promise that they would one day return to the world they had come from.
