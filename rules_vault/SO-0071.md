## Hustling the Mark (I)

In a confidence game, the goal is to inflate their ego to the point where they’re distracted. This lets you set them up to take the fall when the real payout is on the line—after all, pride comes before the fall. Spend a Major Action and make a Con + Charisma (4) test or a Stealth (Palming) + Agility (4) test. Succeeding at the test allows you to convince the other party of their superiority and there’s nothing you can do about it. This gives you the Ace in the Hand 1 status.
