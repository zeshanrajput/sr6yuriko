## Overdrive

## (X-Cyte)

- **Vector:** Inhalation

- **Speed:** 1 combat round

- **Duration:** (10 – Body) hours, minimum 1 hour

- **Power:** 9

- **Effect:** +1 Reaction, +1 Logic

This stimulant comes from the jungles of Latin America and affects the central nervous system. This drug is commonly used by hackers looking for an edge while hitting a host. When the drug wears off, the user needs to resist (Power)S damage. Users who reach level 5 or 6 of the Addiction quality also gain the Uncouth quality.
