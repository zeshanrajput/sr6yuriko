Numerous powerful spirits from the adjacent metaplanes have been making regular trips to the Place of Clouds via any of the Air cardinal poles, and by ascending some of the heights above the Plane of Water. Until the Awakening, the Place of Clouds was most often used as quiet little getaway among the spirits of the elemental planes. It was widely considered by most elemental spirits to be a very pleasant place to visit. The many cloud islands were incredibly diverse, so the elemental spirits found them to be both fascinating and rejuvenating, much like metahumans on the material plane regard tropical islands or the natural hot springs of Iceland. Any significant or highly important issues are resolved within the Mist Queen’s court in Korikyu—until recently, the deals made regarding the Place of Clouds have always been relegated to cultural exchanges and preserving the pristine nature of the hyper-metaplane. It was mostly considered a playground for the elementals—a safe and friendly place to relax, explore, make new friends, and improve diplomatic connections among rivals.

Since the Awakening, the Place of Clouds has been visited by metaplanar travelers from Earth. These people saw the Place of Clouds in an entirely different light. The many strange islands of clouds, each with a unique and strange biome and inhabitants, were fascinating and exciting not for their intrinsic value but for the resources that could be found there. Natural pockets or veins of true eleGETTINGments exist here and tend to be more easily accessible than on other metaplanes. Nearly every mineral, plant, and animal can be harvested for pure reagents that are especially useful for enchanting foci and alchemical preparations. One of the most hotly contested islands resembles a vast mountain range in the sky. Deep within the mountains are veins of many minerals, but the reason it has become a battleground is the existence of veins of orichalcum.
