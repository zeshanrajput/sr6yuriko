## Riggers in the Shadows

Now that I’ve given everyone a taste of how the SINners that make up civilized society use riggers, and why we’re entering the Age of the Rigger 2.0, I guess it’s time to get down to brass tacks. Just like magic, riggers can do almost anything. Yes, it costs some nuyen to customize or buy a drone that can do some jobs, but if you give any rigger worth their VCR a drone that can pull the job off, the job will get done. It’s a fact, as true as saying the sky is blue or the world is poorer without Dunkelzahn in it.
