## Sh ifter (Fins)

Dolphin, selkie (seal), shark and other aquatic animals fall within the fi ns subcategory, which includes freshwater or saltwater shifters—though the only known freshwater shifters are river dolphins. The largest fi n community is found in Amazonia and sprawls from the Amazon River delta south along the coastline. Every known aquatic adaptation of shifter is found here. The community is mostly land based, but the aquatic shifters have unique architecture built that goes from land to water in many places. Seal shifters can be found along any coastline; the largest mostly seal shifter group is outside of Seattle. Shark shifters are found everywhere sharks are—which is to say any ocean, anywhere. The largest wild grouping of shark shifters can be found off the coast of Australia, although they migrate out to different territories during the year. Ray and orca shifters have also been observed.

- **Minimum Base Cost:** 15 Karma

- **Gained Powers:** Dual Natured, Environmental Adaptation (Aquatic and Cold), Natural Weapon, Supernatural Recovery, Shift, Swim Speed 10/20/+2

- **Gained Weakness:** Allergy (Silver, Extreme) or Allergy (Gold, Extreme)

- **Attack:** Bite 3P, Attack Rating 6 + Strength.
