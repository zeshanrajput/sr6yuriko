## Hook

At the end of mission 26, the PCs learned that some Animus managers would be gathering for a meeting. The names of the managers were not, of course, included in the data. If the PCs want to find out who these higher-ups are and what they are talking about, they’re going to have to find a way to get eyes inside a meeting that is not meant to be seen by any outsiders.

Any Mr. Johnson the PCs have used on an Animus-related mission could hire them here, as they are sure to get valuable information from this meeting. They offer base pay of a 10,000-nuyen

lump sum, but they promise more based on the quality of the information uncovered.
