## Road Rage

You’ve braved the roads of the Sixth World long enough to be perpetually on edge when you’re out there. If someone does you wrong, you snap and can think of nothing more than getting them back.

- **Bonus:** 7 Karma

- **Game Effect:** If the vehicle or drone that you are piloting takes damage, you may only gain or spend Edge on Opposed tests against the target that damaged you until either the encounter ends or your target is defeated.
