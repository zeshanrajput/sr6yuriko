## Self-Trained

Some private eyes get by with no formal training whatsoever. They were born with a natural sense of curiosity and are keen-eyed, and their peers notice. They start off doing favors for people in their neighborhood—finding lost bikes, tracking down someone who may have run away from home for a time, that sort of thing. A few early successes build their reputation, and soon people beyond their immediate acquaintances seek them out, asking for their help. Sometimes, these people even come with money.
