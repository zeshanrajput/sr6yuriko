## Empyrean

## (Fire, Force 4)

If ardors are considered rural hippies, then the empyrean are urban goths. Their appearance is less humanoid, with torsos and legs replaced by a conflagrant cyclone. Their faces are a rapid flickering of light on smoke, creating a projected expression that metahumans can relate to. While the greater emperor wears the crown, many royal empyreans look for tall or extravagant headwear as they wait their turn.
