## Devil Jack Diamond

The devil jack diamond is thought to be related to either the muskie or the alligator gar and some of the largest specimens spotted have been over three meters long and weighing over 150 kilograms. The fi sh is named after its hard, thick scales, which are black with a deep red along the edges. The devil jack diamond is aggressive and will attack small boats when worked into a frenzy. They are often an ambush predator, hiding as a fl oating log.
