## Shift

- **Requirement:** Essence 6

You have the ability to switch between a metahuman and an animal form chosen during character creation. This is a Major Action (I). Your attributes remain the same between the forms (exception: see Shifter Might). Gear, armor, clothes, weapons tend to be dropped and torn during the change.

This power rejects any augmentations that are not delta grade or better. Any augmentations that are not deltaware are forced out of the shifter’s body when this power is activated.

This power is lost if your Magic attribute drops to 0 or if your Essence drops below 6.
