## **SIN Scanner**

**Having some sort of SIN is mandatory for a person to interact in any meaningful way in the Sixth World. If you are using a legitimate legal SIN, this has no more effect than leaving a data trail of everything you do. A fake SIN works much in the same way, as long as it passes verifcation, at least. The level to which a SIN is checked and verifed varies widely based on the environment and who is doing the checking. The sophistication of the system and how much information it can verify is defned by its rating. Low-end scanners will only perform the most basic checks, while more advanced scanners will perform deeper analysis**

**While corps don’t generally care about their employees, the law enforcement providers realized that having offcers (especially veterans) leave because of addiction or staggering injury and death rates is bad for the bottom line. Recently, OmniStar put some major R&D cred into the chemical augmentation game in an attempt to level the playing feld.**
