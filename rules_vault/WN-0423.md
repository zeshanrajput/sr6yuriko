## Hotspots

## The Topside Facility

After stepping through the wall of energy contained by the arch, there is a moment of disorientation. Anyone Awakened instantly realizes that something feels “wrong.” The manasphere of



Dis is radically different from the mana found on most other metaplanes. The team is standing on a stone floor in a white-paneled room with the portal’s sheet of energy hanging in the air behind them. Working at a nearby dataterm is a slightly built male human with greying hair, a white lab coat, and a name tag that reads Dr. Hernandez. He looks up from his work and glances at the team saying, “Ah good, you’re here already.” With a chuckle, he continues, “I don’t think I would have taken your job for any amount of scrip. But since you’re here and I still have a little time, what questions do you have?”

To fill in some background, Aztechnology has built a small facility around the location where the portal connects to Dis. When the portal first opened, it was inside the main floor of a four-story stone building used as a shop and residence. After acquiring the building, Aztechnology has steadily expanded and fortified the structure, digging down and building out, making it a well-defended forward-operating base for Aztechnology operations on the metaplane. The facility, codenamed Topside, has space to house thirty residents. Right up until the last few days, that included a mix of researchers, diplomats, and security teams. The megacorp has also installed a small framework host with wireless functionality, but it doesn’t reach past the facility’s walls. Topside is located some distance away from the center of the metaplane, but the looping-back displacement effect (p. 69, _Astral Ways_ ) makes it difficult to measure the distance exactly. Sometimes it’s an hour’s walk; other times, it may take several days of travel to get to the central areas of the metaplane.

Recently, something has been happening to Dis. The ambient mana level of the metaplane began dropping and then was redistributed somehow. The mana levels are now stable near the city center, but they drop off rapidly as you move out. The structure of the metaplane is also starting to collapse. Sections of the outer city have begun to crumble, leaving holes in the reality of the metaplane. As the edges of these voids expand, the frayed structure of the city can be seen slowly disintegrating, small fragments breaking away and drifting off to disappear into the void. The exact number of these voids scattered around the metaplane would be impossible to calculate due to the nature of reality in the city. But several can be seen from Topside including one that is very close, slowly expanding toward the facility.

The Topside facility is in the process of being evacuated. The decay of the metaplanar structure is leaving the future of the facility in doubt, and all personnel and most of the equipment is being moved out.
