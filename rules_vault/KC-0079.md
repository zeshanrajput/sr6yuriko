## How to Use this Chapter

This chapter looks at how shadowrunners might be involved in the sudden fortune enjoyed by Eniola Okoro and the examination into its source. It starts with **Today in Lagos** , which gives some of the basics needed to navigate this city. Next is **The Tale to Today** , which provides important background for the work the runners will be doing, including information on the major players in the story. **The Trail of Breadcrumbs** then provides a structure for work for the runners and what they might find as they start their job.
