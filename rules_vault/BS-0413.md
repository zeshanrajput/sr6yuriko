## Zombie Dust

- **Vector:** Contact, Injection

- **Speed:** 2 combat rounds

- **Duration:** 1D6 hours

- **Power:** 6

- **Effect:** Instantly prepares a vessel for possession

This mixture consists of powdered pufferfish, marine toad, hyla tree frog, and metahuman remains. Zombie dust is a refinement of an ancient voodoo formula and causes a target to become instantly prepared for possession. While this can be used on a willing ally, the use of it on an unwilling target will cause the summoner to take a hit to their reputation.
