## berlin at a glance

**Basic Information:** 1,845 km[2] free city state. Associate status with the AGS, but with no voting rights.

**Population:** 6.9 million (humans 63%, orks 16%, elves 8%, trolls 6%, dwarfs 5%, others 2%)

**Police Services:** “Berliner Polizeifreiheit” = Berlin Police Freedom. Every district has free choice in how to police itself. Basic police services are provided by the Sternschutz (owned by the Frankfurt Bank Association). Corporate Sectors usually employ their own corporate guards. Alternative Zones use any form of registered or unregistered “police” force including protection gangs or vigilantes. The BERVAG Directorate for Central Tasks acts as a top-tier criminal investigation and civil protection unit, relying heavily on bounty hunters and shadowrunners.

**Administrative and Judicial Services:** Berliner Verwaltungs AG (BERVAG), a state-owned corporation with many subsidiaries, including the Berlin Judicial Services GmbH (BeJuDi), which runs the courts of Berlin.

**Energy:** Ruhr-Nuklear (Saeder-Krupp)

**Water/Sewage:** Berliner Wasserbetriebe (Proteus) **Traffic Management:** ALI = GridGuide (Renraku) **Public Transport:** BERVAG-Verkehrsbetriebe (BVB), technical provision by Urbanys (ESUS), plus independent alternative companies (Stattbusse, meaning instead of buses)

**Main Expressways:** Aussenring (A10), Innenring (A100), Avus (A115), EuroRoute 4

**Waterways:** Spree and Havel rivers, many inner-city canals, to the North Sea; Elbe-Havel Canal, to the Baltic Sea; Havel- and Spree-Oder waterways.

**International Airports:** Berlin-Schönefeld International (BSI), S-K Tempelhof (SKT), Z-IC Tegel (ZIC).

**Long-Distance Train Stations:** Berlin Central Station (Hauptbahnhof), BSI Metro Station, Spandau, Westkreuz, Südkreuz, Ostkreuz, Nordkreuz.

**Ports:** BEHALA West Harbor (Mitte), BEHALA South Harbor (Spandau), S-K Tempelhof Harbor, Neukölln Free Harbor, plus the Schattenhafen Am Urban shadow port.
