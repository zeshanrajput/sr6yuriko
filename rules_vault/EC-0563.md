## > **Olga’s Tearoom**

On the ground level of this renovated townhouse, you’ll find a classy Russian-style tearoom with some of the best Eastern European varieties and rich Turkish coffee (yes, **real** coffee). If you know the right passwords and your cred is good, upstairs has a variety of Eastern European delights of the metahuman variety. Olga, a hulking, hatchet-faced troll, is part madame, part enforcer, all business, and has recently expanded her stable to include a few choice male and non-binary options for her clientele. Be careful rubbernecking in the lobby; the blackmail can be juicy, but Olga prides her establishment on its discretion. **>** Kat o’ Nine Tales
