## Gatekeeper

## [Influence (Intimidation), Major Action] 4 Edge

You openly mock and attempt to make someone feel rejected and out-of-place. Make an Influence (Intimidation) + Charisma test opposed by the target’s Composure. If successful, the target gainsthe Fish Out of Water status for the remainder of the scene or encounter, or until an hour has passed.
