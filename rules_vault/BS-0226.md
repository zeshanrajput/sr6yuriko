## Cultured Bioware

## Biolink

This is some drek, and we all know where the research came from, even though no one is ever going to admit it. The biolink is best described as a wireless datajack. A small cluster of tissue is grown across a section of the midbrain. This tissue acts in a fashion similar to the part of the brain in technomancers that allows them to connect to wireless devices without the use of a datajack. It’s what some call the first step in understanding and recreating the technomancer phenomenon, but anyone in the shadows with half a conscience knows this all came at the cost of hundreds if not thousands of technomancers. Getting this implant is paying back into the pockets of a mega that has hunted and killed more innocent technomancers than GOD.

This bioware doesn’t open the subject to the Resonance and doesn’t offer a Matrix persona, but it does allow a direct feed of Matrix data to the brain and the potential to use other Matrix-accessing devices like commlinks and cyberdecks via DNI, but without any wires. Biolink allows a character to activate a trode and access AR without a device.
