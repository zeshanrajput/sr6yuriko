## Travel

As previously stated, except for those residing at the cardinal poles, all fire elementals are on the move. The largest vehicles they use are the colonial tanks. Like football stadiums or irregularly stacked apartment complexes riding on tracks or enormous legs. Their engines belch fire and smoke as a crew continues to feed the machine with whatever they can find. For more personal travel, fire elementals have a fascination for combustion engines and are envious of metahuman machines like motorcycles and t-birds. Their ingenuity has prompted similar versions in the elemental plane, ranging from over-engineered beasts with multiple exhausts to make sure everyone knows of your arrival to sleek hovercrafts, glowing like they’ve just been pulled from a forge.

- There is one thing that engineering fire spirts and riggers have in common, and that is trying to build that connection to the machine. Spirits cannot interface with metahuman cybertechnology but have been working on their own version. Fire wire, as it were.
