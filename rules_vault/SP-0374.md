## **Mana Battery: Game Rules**

**These portal mana storage devices have a rating between 1 and 6 and can hold 50 times their rating in units of mana. As covered under “Mana Storage” (p. 170), a native Disian can move any quantity of units between devices or their internal storage as a Minor Action. Mana batteries are only usable by native Disians.**
