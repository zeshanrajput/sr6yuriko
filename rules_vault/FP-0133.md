## **> Sim-Eon**

**Since he left STV for Index-AXA in the ’70s, Fauchon has become their main fxer. If someone in the corp has a problem, he is be the one making it go away. Generally, he uses some nasty legal tricks for that, but he also has no qualms resorting to the shadows, and even wetwork … if cost-effective, of course.**

**He also brought millions to the Index-AXA, using expertly executed lawsuits to ruin the competition. To this day, he seldom loses in court, and after years, this reputation alone makes many parties settle directly.**

- **His success rate is also to be attributed to his ruthless use of runners. He is a master at making key pieces of evidence disappear, casting doubt on the opposition’s narrative, “convincing” a witness to recant their testimony at the last minute, or simply tampering with the jury or bribing the judge.**

- **Skarn-Ka**

**In comparison, his partner in crime, Adrien Saurat , appears to be a more scrupulous character. In his early forties, this entrepreneur is well known for being a rich philanthropist. Originally born in Marseille, he still has a little hint of accent and a very warm, friendly demeanor. He joined ESUS after his own “social startup” was bought by the corporation.**

**The venture marketed itself as giving the unemployed (and SINless) another chance to come back to society. In truth, it exploited underpaid construction workers. Previous employees have reported long hauls without the necessary resources, fourteen to sixteen hour shifts, and being forced to deliver projects faster and cheaper than the corp’s competitors. All of this ugly reality was hidden under the disguise of doing charity work, of course, which benefted the image of any other corporations working with him. Recently, he relocated to Lille to help coordinate several acquisitions for his small yet growing personal corporate empire.**

- **There are some persistent rumors that Saurat had some sort of religious upbringing. Some kind of a weird sect. He left this church on his own, but certainly learned from that experience how to doublespeak and manipulate people into doing his bidding.**

- **Skarn-Ka**

- **In short, meeting with Fauchon makes you want to have a shower afterward, while with Saurat it’s more like a weird feeling of having just been fragged over, but with a smile.**

It’s not the only one in this case within the consortium, but it’s one of the largest. This makes it an odd duck within the FT, because sometimes the AA’s best interests conflict with the interests of the French Touch at large.**
