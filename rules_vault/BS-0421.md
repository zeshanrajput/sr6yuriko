## BTL and Ripjack

Better-Than-Life chips and ripjacks take advantage of Artificial Sensory Induction System Technology (ASIST) to send signals directly along the neural pathways of the user. They take a technology that allows you to connect with recorded memories and experiences and then amplifies them, causing you to go into overdrive. Or worse, they scramble all the signals and inputs in your neural system, resulting in overloads and damage.
