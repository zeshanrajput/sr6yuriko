## Dis Magician

## (Professional Rating 9)

The Dis Magician has been given the honor of journeying to the Sixth World to assist with the harvesting. They carry no gear save the body they use to help anchor them to this metaplane, although this body degrades quickly outside Dis. Additionally, Sixth World mana is extremely potent for Disians, greatly enhancing their abilities.

**Note:** The spells and abilities here are only one possible combination. Disian Magicians can only use mana-based spells and abilities or those that affect living matter. They also cannot summon spirits on the Sixth World metaplane.
