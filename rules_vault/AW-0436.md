## Reaction

Your nervous system begins to misfire, causing unexpected twitching and spikes of intense pain. Sometimes your body even ignores you when you

try to move.

**Game Effect:** All Edge boosts and actions cost twice as many points of Edge on tests that use Reaction. If you receive a second Reaction mutation, instead of rolling for Initiative, treat all initiative dice as if they had rolled a result of 1.
