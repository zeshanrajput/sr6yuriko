## Smuggler’s Run

## Main Location: Spandau

Notable Characters: Any five archetypes (pp. 81–91), Sigma

In addition to Berlin’s large and well-secured airports, the Gatow airfield exists on the site of the former British military airport of the same name in southwest Berlin. Apart from a rudimentary runway and a few hangars, little exists here, and its proximity to the gated villa enclave of Kladow means that many professional smugglers prefer to use even more remote airfields. But a team of shadowrunners that has stolen the prototype of a critical object for Sigma wants to fly it out over Gatow, and Sigma has little time to prevent this.

**Original Message:** ALARM! ALARM! Incoming rush order. AAA-Schmidt is looking for a team to intervene ASAP at Gatow airfield and recover stolen hardware. Good payment on delivery, no questions asked.
