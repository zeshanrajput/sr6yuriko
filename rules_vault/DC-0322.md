## Run-Flat Tires

A common upgrade for vehicles owned by shadowrunners, these are a general-duty tire design that perform equally well on or off the road. They are an upgrade to standard tires and are designed to continue operating even when their air pressure is released due to a puncture. Run-flat technology may also be bought as an upgrade to racing and off-road tires.
