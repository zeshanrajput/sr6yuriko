## Sensor Upgrade

- **Cost:** 5 Karma

The AI is an expert at tweaking the sensor settings of devices linked to it. The AI adds a +1 to the Sensor rating of any vehicle, drone, or device that is linked to its icon. All functions of a single sensor array gain this bonus, if applicable. This bonus also applies to any device the AI is currently running on.
