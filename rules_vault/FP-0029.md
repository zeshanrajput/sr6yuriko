## **Arcane Collapse**

**The runners are hired to smear the reputation of a high-end restaurant that uses magic as its major selling point. The restaurant is avant-garde, with a reputation for dangerous, thrilling entertainment. The runners are instructed to sabotage the magic used to control a quartet of void wasps that are supposed to be used as part of a show. The boxes the wasps are kept in have a mild sedative that makes them susceptible to control, but the team is given a pheromone that stimulates their fear and aggression. After being released by the handler, the wasps wreak havoc as they panic, trying to locate the endangered hive their instincts tell them is nearby. The food accents fail in the chaos, as do the levitation deliveries and the illusion effects around the staff and bar, revealing it’s quite ramshackle and unsafe.**
