## Informed Opinion (A)

Waltzing into a conversation in which you actually know what you’re talking about really does give you an advantage. At least it sure beats talking out of your hoop! If you have a knowledge skill applicable to the subject at hand in any social situation, you can spend a Minor Action and gain 2 points of Edge.
