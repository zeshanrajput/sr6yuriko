## Blood Magic Resistance

For whatever reason, blood magic simply does not work well on you.

**Cost:** 12 Karma

- Yep. But the sad truth is, most Joe and Jane Publics are gonna buy this hook, line, and sinker.

- **>** Sunshine

**Game Effect:** Any blood magic practitioners have a –2 penalty performing any rituals or spells against you, and they can only gain 1 Edge in your presence per combat round (replacing the normal maximum of 2).
