## Undine

## (Water, Force 4)

Undines are curious and often gentle elementals. They commonly appear as a translucent humanoid figure made of water, but their bodies are mutable, allowing them to have a misty or snowy shape as they wish. Those who have worked with metahumans before present a relatable form, with misty hair and frost/foam clothing. While undinesare part of a lower-tier caste, they are tolerated more by the upper classes, possibly due to their mutable forms or inherent charm, allowing them to take menial jobs in the ice and cloud cities. Undines are less fazed by fire, as their form just moves to the next phase (ice to water to steam), but they are vulnerable to electricity like the sylphs, as their watery form ionizes and disperses.
