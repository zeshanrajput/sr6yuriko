## Demon Squirrel Chimera

The demon squirrel is between 1.5 and 2.0 meters tall and has an average weight of 80 to 90 kilograms. They have flat, curved structures along the sides of their skulls that feature extremely efficient audio-sensory organs. At first glance, the demon squirrel looks wrinkled, with excess skin folds along its side. These folds are skin-membranes that acts like gliding wings similar to flying squirrels (hence the name).

The demon squirrel also features extremely long and strong limbs; both arms and legs ending in hooked-clawed hands. These claws assist in both climbing and can be used as weapons. The demon squirrel has a set of razor-sharp teeth backed up by a muscular jaw for biting attacks. The demon squirrel’s saliva also contains a potent neurotoxin.

The demon squirrel has the ability to carry targets of equal weight through an ability that mimics the Levitation spell. They are also pack hunters. While some distract targets, others swoop in for the capture or kill. Extremely agile, the demon squirrel chimera have no kind of natural armor.
