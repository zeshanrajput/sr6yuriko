## Han Liu

Han Liu is one of those undesirable metahumans: an elf. He’s also dalaketnon, so the almost-albino look with white hair makes him further stick out. He has used that blank canvas to colorize himself with fiberoptic hair and tattoo sleeves of a blue dragon. He has a chip on his shoulder to prove himself to the Kenran-Kai, and he pushed himself to be the leader of the Blue Dragons. He is also in favor of the New Way, and if given the opportunity, he will take the Blue Dragons into confrontations that will force Tomizawa to choose between the ways.
