## **Powered Melee Weapon Research**

**Outside of magically powered weapon foci wielded by Awakened individuals, melee weapons have always had to rely solely on the strength and skill of the user. Overcoming that limitation and leveling the feld for mundane users has recently become a primary goal of close-combat weapon research. Numerous powered designs from multiple manufacturers are currently in development, with some already on the (black) market.**
