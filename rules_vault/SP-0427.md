## **Evo “Mr. Grey” Combat Walker Vehicle**

## **Game Effect: astral jammer**

**When the preparation is activated, it causes a penalty of –1 die per hit to all mana spells and assensing tests within its area of efect. This penalty applies to anyone casting mana spells or assensing from inside the area of efect as well as anyone casting spells or assensing from outside the area of efect but targeting inside it.**

**Anthro drone technology has become much more common in recent years, creating drones capable of walking on robotic legs. To combat the Disians, Evo has designed an experimental combat vehicle using walker technology, with grey manatech materials incorporated into its armor systems, specialized weapon systems designed for combat against Disians and their chimeras, and an advanced combat AI system to assist a rigger pilot that is protected by a rigger cocoon. The fve-meter-tall bipedal Mr. Grey CWV weighs fve tons. It is a highly effective combatant, but it lacks the speed and mobility of faster vehicles and is designed to be deployed by a larger transport vehicle to bring it close enough to a combat situation to be of use—though it does come equipped with a rocket booster system, it normally reserves using it for deployment via aerial drop or an emergency evac.**
