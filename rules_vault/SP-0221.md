## **Middle East**

**Compared to the rest of the world, the Middle East has been relatively quiet regarding the Disian Confict, with no known hot spots or fashpoints. The Week of Death barely affected the region, with relatively few (reported) incidents of violence and death. While there is no solid data indicating why this happened, two theories have been put forward.**

**The frst theory postulates that Caliphate security forces and providers got wind of Disian operations and were extremely proactive in stomping down any sign of trouble before it began. This has come credibility, but it also indicates that Caliphate intelligence somehow learned about Disian operations when no one else did.**

**The second theory says that there may be a bit of a quid pro quo with Spinrad Global. If the corporation does nothing, the Disians will do nothing in the area. While there is no direct data to corroborate this theory, it fts within patterns and other circumstantial information.**

- **Give it time, I’m hearing rumors about things in motion that simply haven’t come to light yet.**
