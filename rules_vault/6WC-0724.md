## Build Your

## Own Qualities

**Use this rule** if you want to adjust the cost of qualities or create your own qualities for your game. This optional rule sets up a standard set of guidance for the cost or benefit for qualities. In addition, there is an option for qualities that grant more than one level of effect. Your game could allow for Incompetent with levels that adjust the Skill cap down, or you could not have levels so that Impaired can only be selected once.
