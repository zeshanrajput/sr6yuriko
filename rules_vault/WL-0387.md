## **Comprehension Test**

**When a critter is issued a complex order, or if the situation changes while the critter is attempting to carry out a command, the critter must make a Comprehension test using its Logic + Intuition. The threshold for this test is usually 1, but if complex orders involve multiple commands or the GM determines that the situation is particularly confusing to the critter, the threshold increases to 2.**

**When a critter fails a Comprehension test, they stop what they’re doing and return to their handler or owner to await further instructions. On a critical glitch, the critter does something other than what it is commanded to do.
