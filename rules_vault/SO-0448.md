## Brittle Composure

When you lose your composure, it shatters completely.

**Bonus:** 4 Karma

**Game Effect:** Whenever you fail a Composure test, take a number of boxes of Stun damage or lose points of Edge equal to the number of hits you failed the test by. You may choose whether to accept Stun damage or Edge point loss. If you choose Stun damage, it can’t be prevented or reduced by any means—though you can recover from this damage as normal.

You may not take this quality if you have the Stiff Upper Lip positive quality.
