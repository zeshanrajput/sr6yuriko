## Aegis Cognito

Aegis has always been a mystery, as they have no desire to let outsiders know what is going on within the spy corporation. Since Johnny Spinrad’s assassination, the corp has become a black hole—a growing black hole that has been expanding rapidly while the rest of Spinrad Global is reeling and in a bit of a panic given all of the known and unknown schemes Johnny was using to keep his web intact.

Aegis is as wary of AIs as any group in the world. They have perhaps more wired hosts that are not connected to the Matrix than any other corporation. They know that the best way to keep people from reaching a particular destination is to make sure no road travels there. This, of course, comes with a weakness, in that data that is separated can’t communicate, and gaining revelations from data connections is essentially Aegis’ brand. That means sometimes their data has to mix, and finding out where and when is a pre-occupation of many in the intelligence field, whether they are Matrix entities or not.

Aegis also understands that the best defense against an AI is an AI you trust, so they have diligently recruited Matrix-based allies. Their efforts to find and profile various AIs means they have one of the best databases of existing AIs and sapient Matrix beings on the planet.
