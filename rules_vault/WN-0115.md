## Settling In

Terminal looks like what you might imagine if a rich person decided to dump money into a derelict section of town. Where the train and bus stations let out on 34th, the streets are relatively clean and free of AR graffiti. The buildings are in the process of being refaced, and people are happily shopping and taking pictures. NYPD officers drive past, eyeballing anyone who looks suspicious. The refacing of Terminal hasn’t progressed very far. A few blocks in any direction, and the district starts to fall apart.

The runners are given an apartment to use in the Terminal district. By the time they make the short walk to their apartment, it looks like they’ve wandered into a warzone. Gang tags on the buildings and in AR announce they are in Slaughterhouse territory. Eager’s present relationship with Slaughterhouse is not good. He tries to hustle the runners into the building quickly in order to avoid an incident. This is an opportunity for an optional encounter. A group of Slaughterhouse gangers, one for each runner, emerges from an alley and demands they surrender Eager (use Eye-Fiver Go-Ganger, p. 204, _SR6_ ).

Eager escorts the runners to their space and lets them know they have just over two days to gear up and plan out the job before the meeting is scheduled to take place. He’s happy to help them obtain

gear and show them where they’ll need to do surveillance as well as the general area of where they need to upload the footage.
