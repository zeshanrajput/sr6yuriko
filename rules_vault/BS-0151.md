## Retractable Skates

A simple modification for cyberfeet is to place retractable wheels into them. These wheels can be in-line or arranged side by side. Although one foot with retractable skates does not provide a meaningful benefit, if the user has both feet augmented with retractable skates they gain a modified movement rate of 10/30/+2. It takes a Minor Action to deploy or stow retractable skates.

**Wireless bonus** : Gain a bonus Minor Action after spending one to extend or retract the skates.
