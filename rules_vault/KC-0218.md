## Nguyen Rikki

## (Information Broker, Monad, contracting with Evo Financial)

Nguyen Rikki is a dwarf Monad with a side job of tracking down more difficult and dangerous Monads and AIs who have few scruples about taking over someone else’s body. He’s a bit far from his home in Korea, but he’s quick to adapt. He is not afraid of small confrontations once he has a good feel for his opponent.
