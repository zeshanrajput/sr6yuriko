## Redliner

Any cyberlimbs you have are overclocked and have their safety limits disabled, causing them to run at an increased capacity, but the additional stress takes its toll on your body.

- **Cost:** 10 Karma

- **Game Effect:** For every pair of cyberlimbs installed (two cyberarms or two cyberlegs), your paired limbs receive an additional +1 Strength and +1 Agility, up to a maximum for +2 toEDGEward the augmented maximum. Pushing your cyberlimbs this hard causes strain on your body, and the structural integrity granted by those cyberlimbs is not as effective. You only gain 1 Physical Condition Monitor Box per full pair of cyberlimbs, instead of 1 per limb.
