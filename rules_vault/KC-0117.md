## Adegonye Law

Okoro was in a good place in her life when she met Tobi Adegonye. His law firm was well known for making arguments that combined philosophical points with past precedent, and they saw good results. The pair worked several cases together, and when Okoro began to grow in power in Lagos, she asked Adegonye to come work for her. He declined the direct assignment but joined her coalition of firms.

Adegonye has some serious reservations about working with Okoro at the moment, questioning her philosophies and ethics now that she has money, but he sees it as an opportunity to help her rein in her growing moral flexibility.

He also owes a healthy debt to Kiniun the Lion and is being forced to spy on his behalf. He’s not very good at it, and the Lion knows not to push, but he is a weak link in the chain of law firms surrounding Okoro.
