## Knik

Those from Crystalwell are born hunters and trackers, as the untamed atmosphere is harsh. Knik, however, is an exceptional borea tracker, being one of the few air elementals to travel the Astral Sea. He was part of the queen’s army before volunteering for special assignment, the details of which are still unclear. The result is that Knik left the army and became a freelancer. Knik and a few other borea can be hired for hunting expeditions or rescue operations. They have even constructed a large airship to take on non-air passengers.
