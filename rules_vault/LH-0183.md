## Benandanti: Looking to Purchase

## Kurokawa: 90,000 nuyen

This katana was forged just over a decade ago using instructions found in an ancient text. The blade is made with traditional forging techniques except for a vein of pure orichalcum running the length. According to reports, the hilt is made from the tooth of an Eastern Dragon and the blade itself appears to be solid in astral space, regardless of the wielder. Since its forging, the blade has changed hands several times (in bloody fashion), and those who use it become fascinated by it, leading them to obsession. Recent reports place the sword in the hands of a young go-ganger who frequents Interstate 75 in the CAS, although given the weapon’s history that could change.
