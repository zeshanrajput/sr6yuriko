## Computer Virus

## (Illegal) Electronics + Logic vs. Data Processing + Firewall (Major)

## Outsider/User/Admin

The aliens are going to destroy the Earth, and the only way we can successfully stop them is by loading this computer virus into their system. The runner who deploys this attack against a device or host prevents the target from taking any actions for minutes equal to net hits on the test. This can be used to prevent the host from recording footage from cameras or for locking a building as none of the security locks will disengage.
