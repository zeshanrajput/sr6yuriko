## **“You’re Under Arrest!”**

_**During a work shift, contracted peace offcers may be required to arrest suspects in regard to criminal activity. The duty of each Lone Star offcer is to ensure that any arrest adheres to the legal requirements imposed by the applicable justice system, laws, and contract. When making the arrest, the offcer(s) must identify themselves by either displaying their badge and ID or by wearing the authorized uniform. The arresting offcer(s) will then inform the subject of their intent to arrest and of the nature of the crimes they are accused of. The offcer(s) may then subdue the perpetrator using necessary force. Once secured, it is the responsibility of the arresting offcer(s) to ensure their safety and wellbeing. The arresting offcer must then transport the subject forthwith and without unreasonable delay to their assigned, or in exigent circumstances, nearest Lone Star Security Services precinct, substation, or facility for processing. Once offcially charged, the subject will undergo a series of scans and sample tests to confrm their identity. These procedures include a cyberware scan, a blood test, a DNA scan, a fngerprint and hair-sample test, and a retinal scan. In accordance with contract stipulations and local law, Lone Star Security Services will immediately assign a criminal SIN to any subject who cannot produce an existing number. These**_

_**identity-verifcation procedures are performed in the biotech area by a licensed and qualifed medical technician under the observation of the arresting offcer(s) to ensure that the samples are taken correctly, that the subject’s health is maintained, and for general safety. Any illegal or dangerous augmentations the perpetrator has will be cataloged, disabled, and removed if necessary. Safety and security measures will be taken to prevent any Awakened subject or third parties from using their abilities.**_ **—Lone Star Security Services Procedure Manual, revised 2080**

**Once the investigating offcers have presented the necessary evidence to the proper judicial authorities, they are given a court order or warrant to affect an arrest. Most perpetrators will attempt to fee or resist arrest. Please don’t do this; all it does for you is slot off the pursuing offcer.**

**Vehicle pursuits are tricky. While most LEP contracts have a general operations fund to deal with collateral damage and loss of life, there is also a clause in that contract that states if an offcer is acting with blatant disregard for property damage or metahuman life, that offcer can be held liable for the costs of any damages or legal disputes.**

- **This right here is why most cops also have some kind of special on-the-job liability insurance. Granted, they’re often not charged criminally, because they were simply doing their duty, after all.**

- **> Old Crow**

**To avoid such situations, Lone Star’s policy also dictates that the offcers will try to stop or prevent pursuit by any reasonable means. This includes ramming and pinning vehicles with police cruisers or drones, or in some cases lethal force. Any damage to property or civilians hit by the pursuing vehicle requires a report. As such, vehicle chases are the worst for offcers, and they’ll either give up quickly or go too far and make you regret it.**

- **I know of peddlers who take the opportunity to throw their defective merchandise under the wheels of the cops in pursuit of getting their money back. I’ve also heard that some chipheads throw themselves directly at the vehicle to claim compensation.**

- **> Kane**

**While legal requirements vary widely from state to state, jurisdiction to jurisdiction, Lone Star offcers regularly move between states or, worse, in and out of corporate enclaves or extraterritorial zones. That can be tough, as laws such as the “Miranda rights” may differ depending on where an offcer ends up terminating the pursuit or affecting the arrest. If the procedure or legality of said arrest is called into question, especially in regard to extraterritorial rights, there’s a risk that the subject can be released on this sole technicality.**

**It’s not uncommon for offcers to attempt an arrest only to discover they pursued a corporate citizen onto corporate property. And the corps don’t like it when someone else messes with their people.**

**Also, I’m not going to give you all a lecture on the toys we use (magemasks included) when arresting an Awakened suspect. Just note that if you’re Awakened, you’ll be rendered defenseless because we aren’t taking any chances.**

From your socks to the inside of your teeth, everything will be inspected. If you have a vehicle, they’re going to strip it down. The same goes for any hideouts or safehouses they link to you.**

**Bail is also set at the time of arrest. A preliminary hearing is held at the police station, during which the offcer states the offense on which the defendant has been arrested. If it’s a misdemeanor, such as theft or public disturbance, a Lone Star offcer sets bail. It’s simple: if you pay, you’re free until your trial. If you’ve been arrested during a run, however, no chance in hell we’ll set a bail for you.**

**On that note, keep in mind that your cyberware alone constitutes another set of extra charges entirely! In fact, almost everything you shadowrunners consider standard tools falls into the criminal category.**

- **If they have to set bail, they can set it so high you’ll have no chance to pay for it. That’s where knowing some bail bondsmen will come handy. But watch out; they’re worse than the sleaziest prison guards.**

- **Hard Exit**

- **From time to time, officers will take the opportunity to snag runners from custody to hire them. Very Faustian.**

- **> DangerSensei**

**Depending on the charges against you and the conditions of your arrest, you may have to wait a long time in a detention cell before going to trial. If you’ve disrespected more than one extraterritorial entity, the judge has to look at the different conventions to decide how you’ll be tried and in front of whom. You may even be sent back to another jurisdiction, several times over, if everyone decides you’re a hot potato.**

**In the meantime, your equipment, magic, cyber, drone, vehicles, etc. are sealed, packaged, and sent to the authority that will judge your case for what constitutes evidence. To cover costs, the rest may be requisitioned by our services. And there’s no guarantee that it’ll be returned if you ever go out (through the front or back door).**
