## Entropic Conversion

Some protosapients have the rare ability to heal damage to their core code by reducing the ambient structural organization of the surrounding Matrix—that is, by increasing entropy and converting the siphoned “organizational energy” into its own structure through reverse entropy. With a Major Action, the entity heals 1D6 boxes of damage on its Matrix Condition Monitor and increases the ambient noise of the area by the same amount. Ambient noise subsides by 1 point per hour until it returns to normal.
