## **Uploads Given Straight**

**Each of the fles is quite detailed, in some instances going so far as to name specifc individuals, capacities, and resources. The summaries of the fles are given below.**

**Regarding Loremaster: Loremaster is a title given to a representative of the great dragons. It includes the names of the most recent three loremasters: Dunkelzahn, Lofwyr, and Celedyr. It also tells how certain draconic relics are capable of interacting with the Noosphere and the Matrix. Only the Jewel of Memory is listed by name, but four others are suggested, though their identifcations are redacted.**

**Regarding Pax: The fle describes the role that the Dissonant technomancer played in the events surrounding the confict between the AIs Deus and Cerberus and the fallout of the confict in Boston. Several leads about her last known whereabouts and her degrading status are included in the fle.**

**Regarding Cynthia Bills: This fle holds the results of a trace run by the Kechibi code in search of the earliest personalities exhibiting the CFD virus and their current status. The fle includes a detailed analysis of the rogue virus prior to its infection of the biological host. Psyche instructed the Kechibi code to pursue data trails that went beyond the Refecting Pool and into the Resonance Realms beyond it, displaying an ability unknown to the code prior to this.**

**Regarding Danielle de la Mar: De la Mar has served as a catalyst for events that have shaped and reshaped the current world. These files are a detailed intelligence portfolio, including information on many of her current associations, whereabouts, and several details regarding her forthcoming attendance on the Grand Tour.**

**The details indicate that Psyche is monitoring de la Mar’s actions closely and may consider her role equal to something it refers to as a “change nexus.”**

**Regarding Hengester: This fle is perhaps the most revealing. It highlights the processes used by the organization and how its membership has evolved and moved on to other organizations and corporations around the world. It goes as far as to name several of the individuals and the dates of their passing. It is the only one of the uploads that indicates what some would label as a blacklist, with the names of those deceased and those still declared functional. Of all the fles, it is the only one that indicates that Psyche may go out of its way to erase some entities, though it does not indicate what it uses to make such determinations.**

**Regarding the CCMA (Corporate Court Matrix Authority): The upload gives a listing of all known directors of the CCMA, the rulings they have overseen, and their roles as outlined by the Corporate Court. The fle includes information on contacts and connections who are actively watching those individuals. While not viewed as a blacklist, certain rulings within the past three years’ time are highlighted arbitrarily. Browsers of the fle batch can match those times to some of the citywide blackouts that took place, including those in Toronto and St. Louis. Individuals who succeed in a Judge Intentions (5) test may surmise that Psyche considers the CCMA a hazard or obstacle.**

**Regarding the Light of Spirits: The name of a tribal community near the city of Taos within the Pueblo Corporate Council territories (p. 96,** _**Hack & Slash**_ **), it is also the name for the spirit lodge and Matrix host that the tribe maintains. Current tribal leaders, founders, and certain projects that the LOS tribe is currently undertaking are highlighted in the fle. It includes numerous entries on a Matrix persona that assumes the form of Kokopelli, which both Emergent and Awakened individuals say they have encountered on quests for enlightenment.**

**Within the fle for the tribe are entries describing the free sprite known as Wraith, whom they recognize as an agent of Kokopelli acting in their universe. The community leaders and more active members have begun to realize that the being they see as Kokopelli is something different, for it can act as both a Paragon (p. 129,** _**Hack & Slash**_ **) for the Emergent and an Avatar (p. 117,** _**Street Wyrd**_ **) for the Initiated. Psyche has numerous agents that have ties to the tribe, including a handful who beneft from the strongest of Psyche’s endowments.**
