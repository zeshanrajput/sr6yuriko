## **critter corner: gomatia**

**Welcome to the jungle—it’s time for Critter Corner! I’m Mr. Johnson, here with the tips that might just help you live to enjoy retirement. Whether you’re headed into Amazonia or a corp facility protected by Petrovsky, you might just have a run-in with a gomatia—an aggressive Awakened chameleon that’s big enough to eat people. Due to their adaptive coloration, unless you’re exceptionally observant you’re not likely to see one of these critters before they strike. Gomatias are incredibly patient, capable of lying still for hours at a time with no motion to give away their position. They have also taken very well to training as security animals and have become nearly as common as hell hounds. Gomatias also develop an attachment to their handlers or owners and are fercely loyal and protective of them. They are sometimes used as bodyguard critters, most commonly as a kidnapping deterrent, protecting the children of wealthy and powerful individuals.**
