## Al-Hazad’s House of Wonders

A brightly colored store at the start of Neon City, this location is stocked with faux telesma and enough trinkets to appease any tourist. Despite the fluff, the current owner, Ephraim, operates as a Talismonger for the Red Dragon Triad. He has access to rare telesma and everything else a magic-user needs to function in the city.

- Ephraim is discreet as possible about his operation, but if you ask for an item he doesn’t have on hand, that means he needs to get it from the Triad or have it smuggled in. The rarer the item is, the higher the risk of the Triad asking about your business.

- **>** Jimmy No
