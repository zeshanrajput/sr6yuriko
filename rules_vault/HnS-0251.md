## Golden Screwdriver

Whether using a screwdriver or a soldering

iron, you are a hardware wizard.

- **Cost:** 5 Karma

- **Game Effect:** When you make a test to repair Matrix damage, the interval on the Extended test is 10 minutes (instead of 1 hour).
