## Dracogenesis Powers 

Unless otherwise noted, a power can only be purchased once. 

(Note that any time a dracogenesis or Critter power references Magic, use the drake’s Edge instead) 

- **Adaptive Coloration (Sea Drake only):** Gain Concealment (p. 222, _SR6_ ), self-only, when underwater. 

- **Animal Control:** per p. 221, _SR6_ 

• **Astral Projection:** per p. 160, _SR6_ . If you do not have a magical tradition, use Edge+Willpower to determine Attack rating in Astral Combat. 

- **Bursts of Power:** Swap in a Wild Die (p. 48, _SR6_ ) on all rolls to activate or use drake powers. 

- **Compulsion:** per p. 222, _SR6_ 

- **Corrosive Spit:** per p. 222, _SR6_ 

- **Dracoform Talent:** Increase your Edge score by +1 only for the purposes of calculating drake powers (this stacks with the usual +1 from undergoing dracogenesis). 

- **Draconic Power:** Increase your maximum Edge attribute by one (this may exceed the Metatype Attributes Table, p. 64, _SR6_ ) or, if you have one at 1+, increase your maximum Magic or Resonance attribute by one (similar to Initiation or Submersion). 

- **Dragonspeech:** as Mindspeech, p. 225, _SR6_ 

- **Elemental Mastery:** You may make Elemental Attacks with Electricity, Chemical, or Cold (choose one at the time this power is purchased), not only Fire. May be purchased multiple times. Also modifies any power based on Elemental 

Attack (Energy Aura, etc). 

- **Elemental Resistance:** per p. 71, _SR6_ (matching your Elemental Attack element) 

- **Energy Aura:** per p. 223, _SR6_ (matching your Elemental Attack element) 

- **Enhanced Sense (Wide Band Hearing):** per p. 223, _SR6_ 

- **Enhanced Sense (Low Light Vision):** per p. 223, _SR6_ 

- **Enhanced Sense (Smell):** per p. 223, _SR6_ 

- **Enhanced Sense (Thermographic Vision):** per p. 223, _SR6_ 

- **Improved Armor:** Increase your dracoform Armor or Mystic Armor by half your Edge score (rounded down). This may modify Hardened Armors as well. 

- **Improved Flight:** Replace Vestigial 

   - Wings with a flying rate of 20/30/+4. 

- **Improved Natural Weapon:** +1 DV with Natural Weapons. May be purchased twice for a total +2 DV. 

- **Fear:** per p. 224, _SR6_ 

- **Hardened Armor:** Change half of your dracoform Armor (round down) to Hardened Armor (may be purchased twice, turning all dracoform Armor into Hardened Armor), per p. 224, _SR6._ 

- **Hardened Mystic Armor:** Change half of your dracoform Mystic Armor (round down) to Hardened Mystic Armor (may be purchased twice, turning all dracoform Mystic Armor into Hardened Armor), per p. 224, _SR6._ 

- **Heavy Scales (Western Drake):** Gain +2 Hardened Armor, per p. 224, _SR6_ . 

- **Influence:** per p. 225, _SR6_ 

- **Magical Defenses (Eastern Drake):** Gain +2 Hardened Mystic Armor, per p. 224, _SR6_ . 

- **Magnificent Specimen:** Gain +2 to your Dracoform Adjustment Attributes to invest (may be purchased a number of times equal to your dracogenesis rating). 

- **Mystic Armor:** Gain Mystic Armor equal to Edge rating, as per p. 226, _SR6_ .

- **Nimble Claws:** Remove the Unsteady Hands flaw from your dracoform. 

- **Noxious Breath:** per p. 226, _SR6_ 

- **Penetrating Natural Weapon:** +2 Attack rating with dracoform Natural Weapons 

- **Quicksilver Dracoform:** +1 Initiative Dice and Minor Action. May be purchased up to four times. Does not stack with other similar effects, whether mundane or magical. 

- **Regeneration:** per p. 227, _SR6_ 

- **Resistance to Toxins and Pathogens:** Gain both Resistance to Pathogens and Toxin Resistance (p. 73, _SR6,_ for both). 

- **Spirit Affinity:** per p. 73, _SR6_ (matching your Elemental Attack element) 

- **Swift Transformation:** Changing from metahuman to dracoform costs three Minor Actions instead of 1 Major Action. This power may be purchased additional times, each time reducing the number of Minor Actions required to change forms by one, down to a minimum of 1 Minor Action. 

- **Tail Stinger (Feathered Drake only):** as per Venom, p. 228, _SR6_ 

- **Toughness:** per p. 73, _SR6_ 

• **Transcendence of Form:** Some drakes have learned to harness aspects of their draconic power while in metahuman form. Spend a Minor Action and take 4P drain, resisted by Body + Willpower to activate a draconic power. This may be a Basic Power, a Dracogenesis Power, or your dracoform’s favored attributes. Powers with a persistent effect (such as Armor or Natural Weapons) last for a number of combat rounds equal to your Edge rating (but may be renewed by taking another Minor Action, resisting drain as usual). You are able to receive the full, improved, version of any power that has been improved through dracogenesis upgrades (Improved Natural Weapons, Hardened Armor, etc.). These powers are accompanied by a clear physical transformation for the duration (the existence of actual scales, 

claws, or a lashing tail), or—if not—a visible magical illusion much like a shamanic mask effect.
