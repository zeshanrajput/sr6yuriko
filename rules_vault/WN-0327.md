## Hotspot

## The Octagon

The Octagon was once the main entrance of the Metropolitan Hospital, but it was abandoned when the hospital closed in 1955. Some efforts at renovation started in the early twenty-first century, but the disruptions that accompanied the Awakening slowed them down. The renovations picked up again in the 2040s, and the building seemed to be in regular use in the 2050s, but the trick was, no one knew who was using it. City government said

only that it was “privately owned,” and there have never been any identifying signs on it.

At present, the Octagon and attached buildings form a medical clinic engaged in secret research, much of it not approved or even known of by most medical organizations. Experimentation on the brain is their specialty.

The Octagon building is five stories tall. The bottom three stories are wider, then the structure narrows to a two-story cupola about four meters wide. Newer wings were attached to the south and west sides of the Octagon, and those in turn lead to larger buildings, each seven stories high. The south annex is used for patient care, while the west is primarily for administration, though the lower two floors hold some outpatient offices.

All physical security guards in this complex are Elite Corporate Security (p. 208, _SR6_ ). Two are in the lobby in the Octagon itself, and twenty others are spread throughout the complex. All can respond to an emergency call within minutes, but typically no more than half are called to any scene, just so that other parts of the building are not left unwatched.

For Matrix security, the building relies on two Professional Rating 8 deckers (p. 209, _SR6_ ). Each watches one of the wings, and they both check in on the central Octagon regularly. Since the most secure files in the building are not wireless, they are primarily looking for unusual Matrix activity in the building. If they see anything that arouses their suspicion, they’ll send physical security over for some quick questions.

A Professional Rating 10 shaman (p. 210, _SR6_ ) sits in the sixth floor of the west annex. They have summoned a Force 6 spirit of air to watch the secure room on the fourth floor and two Force 5 kindred spirits to roam the floors and watch for anything out of line. The spirits have free rein to materialize and question anyone they feel needs scrutiny.
