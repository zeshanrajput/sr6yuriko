## The Astral Quest

The most basic method for traveling the metaplanes is the astral quest. This method is only available to initiated mages, but with an initiated mage as a guide, other mages, adepts, and even mundane team members—using some method of forced projection—can travel along with the mage and assist on the quest’s trials. Although there are other ways to travel to the metaplanes, the astral quest remains the best option for some tasks, since the process guides the seeker directly to where the goal of the quest can be found.
