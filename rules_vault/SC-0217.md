## Highway Prison Break

You are contacted by a group that has a lot of cyber to make up for their relative lack of other skills. They need to get as much as they can from their ’ware, so they’re dedicated to leaving wireless on. That’s where you come in. They know their gear is vulnerable, and they know you know it. Your job is to keep their ’ware functional until the job is done. This might be the Crimson Crush or any other gang that has more augmentations than Matrix skills. The gang has a very important job to pull off, which is a hit on a prison transport convoy. The gang has their own plan, along with the vehicles, weapons, and numbers to pull it off. They’re going to need every edge, including reliable wireless Matrix access. Some astral support couldn’t hurt either, so any team with a mage can easily negotiate a bonus. But what the gang really needs is some serious Matrix defenses. The other runners are welcome to join in the high-speed hijacking operation for another bonus, but it’s all right if they’d rather guard the decker.

The actual operation will be a loud, messy, violent assault on a Lone Star (or other law enforcement) convoy, centered on an Ares Roadmaster prison transport driven by a rigger and supported by a security decker. Unless the runners stop the security decker, they’ll start bricking the gangers’ augmentations, and the attack will fail. If the decker cop is defeated, the attack will succeed. However, the transport also carries a conjuring adept. Unless the spirits they summon are defeated by the runners, the gang will take severe losses.

If the gang succeeds without taking severe losses, each runner gains a new ganger contact with the types Criminal and Street, with Connection 4 and Loyalty 4. If the gangers failed or suffered severe losses, the contact is not gained. If all the runners get into it and make a major difference in the operation, each runner may choose bonus Karma, nuyen, or increase both Connection and Loyalty with their new contact to 5 each.
