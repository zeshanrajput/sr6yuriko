## Flash Module

A mounted array of flash-paks provides an effective defense against enemies that depend on sight.

**Game Information:** This module can be activated remotely with a Minor Action or by a trained critter with a critter comm augmentation with a Major Action. Anyone who can see the critter gains the Blinded III status. Unless the critter also has the flare compensation augmentation, it gains the Blinded II status. The flash module’s battery allows it to run for up to 1 minute before needing to be recharged, which takes one minute when plugged into a power source or 1 hour via wireless induction. A critter with a critterjack may be trained to activate and deactivate the flash module on command, which counts as only one command toward its maximum.
