## Drone Rigging

Rigging with a drone is identical to vehicle rigging when jumped in. Be aware that noise penalties may occur if the drone is too far away from the rigger.

Most of the time, drone rigging is done by remote commands, which rely on the Pilot rating of the drone to carry out. Commanding a drone is a Minor Action. Once the command is given, the drone will use its Pilot rating as its combat attribute, and any autosofts it is running as skills.

Drones use the same statistical block as vehicles. For hacking purposes, consider their Device Rating to be equal to their Sensor rating.
