## Frog Tongue

The soldier gains a long, sticky tongue that they can shoot out of their mouth with precision. The tongue can pull an item weighing up to half a kilo (a full kilo of the individual’s Body is 6 or more). Someone must manually remove the object from the tongue, as the tongue cannot drop it on its own. Treat the effort to grab an object with the tongue as an unarmed attack with no damage.
