## shadowrun

## ADEPT POWERS

## Adept Accident

**Cost:** 0.5 PP

**Activation:** Minor Action

With a touch, an adept can disrupt the regular flow of mana in a person and cause accidents to occur, similar to the critter power of Accident. After successfully contacting a target, within (Magic) minutes of the contact, the adept can roll Magic + Willpower vs. the target’s Reaction + Intuition. If the adept has net hits, the target suffers an immediate Glitch. If there are 4 or more net hits, the target suffers a Critical Glitch with catastrophic results. The adept must resist Drain equal to their hits (not net hits) using Body + Willpower.
