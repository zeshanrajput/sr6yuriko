## Persnickety Renter

## • **Bonus:** 6 Karma

The AI with this quality is limited in what they can call their home devices (see Restoration and Realignment, p. 124). This quality is taken for a specific type of device (e.g., commlinks, drones, devices with a rating equal to or greater than 4, etc.). The device must be able to load apps, autosofts, or cyberprograms in order to be a home device.
