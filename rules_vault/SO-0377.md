## Stirring Performance

## \[Con (Performance), Major\]: 4 Edge

By pouring one’s heart and soul into a performance, you take advantage of your other social talents to boost its effects. Much like the Big Speech Edge action, this allows a player character to make an Influence (Etiquette or Negotiation) + Charisma test as part of a Teamwork test to boost their Con (Performance) skill test. For every hit on your Influence test, add a bonus die to the dice pool for a Con (Performance) test. This counts as a Teamwork test, so player characters are limited to gaining no more bonus dice than current ranks in Con (Performance).
