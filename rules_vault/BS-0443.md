## God Chip

- **Vector:** Slot

- **Speed:** 1 combat round

- **Duration:** (Rating x 2) combat rounds

- **Power:** 9 + (Rating)

- **Effect:** +1 Body, +1 Agility, +1 Reaction, +1 Strength, –2 Willpower

The God Chip gives users a feeling of euphoria and suppresses the moral centers of the brain. The chip was originally created to reward the followers of Winternight, granting them visions and memories from the Norns. New versions of this



BTL carry the same name but have been modified to reflect the values of the organization creating them. Whenever a God Chip user increases their addiction level, they must succeed at a Body + Willpower (2) test or gain the Scorched negative quality. When the BTL wears off, the user gains the Confused 2 status for the next (rating) minutes.
