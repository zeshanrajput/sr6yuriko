## Repetition

Repetition is another old but powerful technique that has only gotten easier and more powerful in our age of easy information repetition. Believe it or not, you can get a lot of people to believe a lie—even a big one—if you repeat it often enough. Blast the same “fact” in enough places, saturated throughout all the daily experiences of a person, and people will eventually internalize it. It becomes part of their world, turning into a fact as solid as the color of the sky(scraper) above their head. If you do this across an entire population group, then the intelligence of the herd takes over. It doesn’t even matter if the sources are credible—quantity has a quality all its own.
