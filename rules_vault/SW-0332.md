## Freefall

**Cost:** 0.25 per level (max 3 levels) **Activation:** Passive

Some adepts are light on their feet, preternaturally so. Every level of Freefall adds 3 meters to the distance an adept falls before calculating damage (see p. 113, _SR6_ ).
