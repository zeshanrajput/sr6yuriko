## Smokers Club

Assassins. Need I say more? This “club” of killers for hire varies across the ranks, and honestly each one is truly individual in their style, preferred methods, and contract requirements. They work all over the world, and sometimes runners work for them—or against them. Being on either side can be dangerous, because these lone wolves have no qualms sacrificing the pack in order to get to their objective.
