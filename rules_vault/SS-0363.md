## Slow Aging

The soldiers have not aged according the Earth calendar—they’ve generally aged at about a tenth of the expected rate. So those who have been gone a decade have only aged one year, and those who were gone a year have only aged just over a month. A soldier might have been gone since World War II and only aged fourteen years. A soldier from the Ming dynasty might emerge, having only aged fifty years.
