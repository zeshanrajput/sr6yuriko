## S pirit of Legba

## (Spirit of Guidance)

The lwa of Legba will traditionally appear as an old man with bent limbs, walking with a staff. Sometimes spirits will appear as a well-endowed young man with horns. Each of the nanchon has an aspect of Legba who opens the doorways for spirits of the different nations. Papa Legba is part of the Rada nanchon, and this lwa is sometimes called Legba
