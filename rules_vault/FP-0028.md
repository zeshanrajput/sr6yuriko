## **Cyber Sabotage**

**The team is hired to sneak into a cyberware production facility and add several small chunks of uranium to their alloying systems. After dropping the metal into the melting vats, the team needs to access the site’s host and insert a code into their quality monitoring software. This is a long game move and results won’t show for a month to a year, but eventually users of these wares will start to suffer minor effects of radiation poisoning and then face an increased cancer risk.**

**If the team is smooth and successful, they will be hired to repeat the process at facilities all over the world.**

**Unless the runner team are potential Asintmah candidates, Mr. Johnson won’t reveal anything about the radiation hazard or the long-term results and the material will be labeled zinc, a brittle metal that would reduce the structural integrity of the products.**
