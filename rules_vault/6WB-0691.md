## Cat

Cat is honored in cultures around the world. Cat is often seen as the guardian of mystical secrets, including those involving the afterlife. She knows many secrets but rarely shares them, and never with anyone who is not worthy (according to her own definition of worthiness). She is stealthy, sly, and arrogant. Cat toys with her prey—threatening, taunting, and confusing her targets—rather than going directly for the kill.
