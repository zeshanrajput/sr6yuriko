## Designer

- **Cost:** 6 Karma

Some AIs are experts at restructuring firmware to achieve maximum hardware efficiency. Their self-designed home device (see **Restoration and Realignment** , p. 124) grants them an additional +1 modifier to the Data Processing or Pilot attribute and 2 points of noise reduction.
