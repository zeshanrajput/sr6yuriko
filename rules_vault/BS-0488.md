## Essence Loss and Critters

Mundane critters handle Essence loss caused by modern bioware, cyberware, nanoware, and genetech with some difficulty. The threshold for any test to influence, train, tame, or otherwise control or interact with a critter is increased by 1 for every point of Essence loss—round up any fractional loss, even if it’s only 0.01 Essence.

If a critterjack is installed, the threshold increase due to Essence loss is reduced by its rating.

Awakened and Emerged critters lose Magic or Resonance when they suffer Essence loss, which can cause them to become mundane if the attribute is reduced to zero. Many critter powers use the critter’s Magic rating to determine a dice pool or some other aspect of their effectiveness, so the more heavily augmented a paranormal animal is, the less powerful its paranormal abilities become. This requires most augmentations of Awakened or emerged critters to be minimal, while mundane critters are the more popular choice for heavy augmentation.
