## metaplane of air

**Technology Level:** Nanotech\* **Magic Level:** Normal Base Level:\*\* Air **Terrain:** Open **Population:** Mixed **Society:** Strict **Distance:** Adjacent

Major City Octahedron

Cardinal poles **Air-Earth:** Foehn (p. 37) **Air-Fire:** Tramontane (p. 37) **Air-Water:** Korikyu (p. 38)

\*A shapeless cloud floats with the elementals and can change shape and density with a thought from the elemental to whatever tool they need.

\*\*The practice of magic related to the elemental plane is in a medium mana flow (p. 168, Street Wyrd). All other elemental magic is considered to be in a medium mana ebb (p. 168, Street Wyrd). Unless an individual is at one of the cardinal poles, only air spirits can be conjured.
