## Winter Systems

This security and defense corporation took a huge PR hit when they lost their NY security contracts, but that bad press was chased by good money. NYPD subcontracts pay better than the city contracts they originally held, and the financial boon didn’t end there. NYPD relinquished its contracts in Newark, Brooklyn, and Queens. Winter System’s typically low bid earned them two of those contracts, with the third being won by OmniStar.

Presently, Winter Systems is looking at OmniStar and Centurion Security as primary competitors. While Ares has long been a corporate rival thanks to their Knight Errant division, the two corporations have participated in joint-operations training on Governors Island, the purpose of which remains to be seen.
