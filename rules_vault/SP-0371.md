## **A Quick Summary of Faustian Crystal Rules**

- **Faustian Crystals have a rating of 1 to 8; they function as both a memory crystal and a mana battery.**

- **Faustian Crystals may internally store a number of units of mana equal to 20 times their rating.**

- **Units of mana can be converted into power points or bound into other efects by the patron. This lowers the maximum capacity of the crystal by that amount. Crystals can also grant critter powers or duplicate the benefts of cyberware.**

  - **10 units of mana may be used to generate 1 power point (5 units may be used for 0.5 power point).**

  - **Bioware and cyberware can be duplicated at 1 unit of mana per 0.1 Essence cost.**

  - **Critter powers may be duplicated at 1 unit for very minor powers and 10 units for the most powerful.**

- **The crystal is always controlled by the Disian patron, and abilities can be withdrawn, restored, or changed at will. The Faustian has no control over abilities set up in the crystal by their Disian patron.**

- **Any unbound units of mana left in a crystal may be used by the Faustian to augment spells or rituals, increasing the efective Magic rating of that one spell or ritual by the number of units added to it.**

- **Units of mana used in this way are expended and can only be replaced by a Disian transferring units into the crystal.**
