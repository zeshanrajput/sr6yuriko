## Hidden Pocket

This is a special little space sewn into the armor that blends in exceptionally well. There’s a limit to a tailor’s magic, but these little spots make great places to tuck away those precious items like credsticks or data chips, or even a hold-out pistol.

The Concealability threshold for items in a hidden pocket increases by 2. Retrieving items from a hidden pocket requires a Major Action.
