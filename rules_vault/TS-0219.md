## **Mancatcher**

**An oddit y of the Neo-PD, a Mancatcher is a three-meter pole topped with a lightly padded halfcircle with numerous small hooks designed to snag a suspect’s clothes. A small locking mechanism at the end allows for two Mancatchers to be interlocked around a target from two different directions. Rather than be carried in patrol cars, they’re tucked away in police supply doors throughout a district in case of an assailant going on a knife rampage. A single offcer can use the Mancatcher to hold a melee-armed opponent at bay, pinning them against a wall or the ground until help arrives, while two or more offcers can render a suspect utterly trapped, allowing them to be netted, tased, or otherwise brought down safely by other offcers.**
