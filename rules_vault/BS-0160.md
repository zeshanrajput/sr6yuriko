## Crab Claw

A crab claw cyber implant replaces the user’s forearm and hand with an oversized, pneumatically driven cutting clamp. These serrated blades have a bite strength similar to the jaws of life used by emergency responders. If the user wishes to damage their opponent during a grapple (p. 111, _SR6_ ), they deal this weapon’s DV + net hits rather than Strength/2. A crab claw doubles its DV when employed against a barrier that can be placed between its blades.
