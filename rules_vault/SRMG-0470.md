## **What type of damage do the Foamtek explosives do (pp. 146-147)?**

The Foamtek grenades and rockets don’t do any damage. The associated values are used to determine if the target is able to resist it using a damage resistance test. If they are able to fully resist the value, then they are not affected by the material.
