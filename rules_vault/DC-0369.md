## Standard Construction

## (Level 2)

This is the typical passenger-vehicle-style construction and is the default for most vehicles. A standard construction vehicle can be stripped down to open construction or upgraded to a totally enclosed construction by the method described under the Open Construction section.

Being inside a standard construction vehicle in a firefight provides protection similar to being behind a barrier. If the windows or doors are open to return fire, the occupants can use a Take Cover major action providing Cover II, III, or IV (Cover status level is up to the GM’s discretion, depending on the vehicle and the runner’s position in it). If the vehicle is closed, the occupants may not attack (unless it has gun ports) and everyone inside the vehicle counts as being behind a barrier. Vehicles use their Armor rating as the Structure rating, so incoming fire subtracts half of this value (rounded up) from the incoming unmodified damage, same as any other type of barrier.
