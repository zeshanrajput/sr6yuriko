## Assembling a Proficient Combat Team

Okay, this next part is not about assembling a proficient _shadowrun_ team. It is about building a _combat_ team where the individual members combine their skills and resources for the purpose of engaging in combat operations _._ While a shadowrun team should be proficient in combat, that’s still only part of what’s required during a run. I’m simply ignoring all these other aspects and focusing on when the bullet hits the bone because this is a data file about combat.
