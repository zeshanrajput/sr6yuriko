## **The Denver Collective**

**A group of technomancers living in the FRFZ, the Denver Collective and the Nexus appear to be intimately intertwined. For a start, Perri, the pri-**

**mary admin at the Denver Data Haven, is also the leader of the Collective. Many members of the Collective are believed to be on staff at the Nexus, but of course the Data Haven staff also includes numerous non-Emerged members. In the same way, not every member of the Collective works with the Data Haven. The recent uncovering of a hidden cult of technomancers operating in Denver, loyal only to Ghostwalker, has created some tension within the Collective. Many members believe that at least a few of their fellow members have actually been manipulating the Collective for Ghostwalker’s beneft instead of working in the best interest of the Collective.**
