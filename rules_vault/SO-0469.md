## Network Breakdown

The core contacts in your network will eventually get to know each other, which is part of what makes them so much more useful. The drawback is when you treat one of them poorly—they’ll all judge you for it. Whenever you lose a Loyalty rating with any of your network contacts, you lose a Loyalty rating with all of them.

If a network contact’s Loyalty rating ever goes below 3, they leave your network and cease to be one of your contacts. If you ever have fewer than three network contacts, your network collapses and you lose all the qualities gained in this quality path.
