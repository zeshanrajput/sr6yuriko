## Crimson Crush

Actually a Redmond gang, the all-ork Crimson Crush sent a few members south and have been recruiting in the heavily segregated areas of Christopher. But instead of sending these new recruits to join the main gang they seem to be building a new chapter from the ground up. It’s a good bet once they get a decent number of members, they’ll be trying to run the South Watch out of the area.
