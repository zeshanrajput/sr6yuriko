## **How Does Ryumyo Fit into All This?**

**We wish we knew. So far, Ryumyo has been** _**persona non grata.**_ **But a lot of dragons, both great and lesser, have been quiet on the subject of Dis. What we do know is that several of Ryumyo’s trusted metahumans have been busy meeting with some of the disenfranchised Yakuza clan leaders since the Week of Death began.**
