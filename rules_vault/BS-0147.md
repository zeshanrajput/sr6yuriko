## implanting gear in cyberware

One of the most useful features of a cybernetic limb is its capacity to interface with most kinds of technology. Many kinds of gear exist as cyberware, and in all cases where a cybernetic version exists, that version must be used in a cyberlimb. For example, you cannot implant optical low-light into cybereyes because cybernetic low-light takes precedence. The following sensors and gear from Shadowrun, Sixth World have no cybernetic equivalents and can be incorporated into cyberlimbs at 1 capacity cost. Note that a device that must go into a “hand” can be implanted into a full arm or forearm partial limb, since they too incorporate hands.
