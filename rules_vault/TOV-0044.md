## **Bokor**

When a Voodoo houngan or mambo is ostracized from their community, they often are left adrift. Some Voodoo priests eventually become more mercenary, selling their skills and service for nuyen. These former priests (bokor) or priestesses (caplata) don’t have the same support of their community. Some say that this lack of a community has caused them to lose their moral guidepost, but it could be the fact that they didn’t have an internal compass to begin with.
