## **Gear and Appearance**

**Sternschutz had its Barghest Squads and thaumaturgical corps back in the 2060s, but has since expanded on them. For drones, Sternschutz has a deal with Mitsuhama, which gives them access to the latest-model drones for patrol operations.**

**If you wonder how to recognize the Sternschutz employees, it’s easy. They typically wear black uniforms with a silver star on the chest, a black beret, and black combat boots. Depending on the service they belong to, their arsenal varies, but it’s usually better than the underpaid and underequipped state police you may encounter in the AGS.**
