## **Pick Each Other Up**

**The shadowrunner life is not for the weak, and that goes double for cohabitating shadowrunners. We work odd hours and stand a decent chance of coming home from work exhausted, wounded, or both. So what are you going to do when you and your housemates get home, completely exhausted and dripping blood, and there are dishes that need to be done? And the foor needs to be vacuumed? These are the times you need to step up. Give a little extra to the good of the household. Do a chore you really don’t feel like doing. Or use some of your pay on a drone that will do that for you.**
