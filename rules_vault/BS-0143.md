## Grapple Hand

A winch, a feed system with thirty meters of cable, and a pneumatic launcher that propels the entire cyberhand as a grappling hook are installed into a full arm cyberlimb or a forearm partial cyberlimb. The winch is strong enough to pull the user’s body weight the length of the cable over the course of three combat rounds. It takes a Major Action and a successful Athletics (Climbing) + Agility test to secure the grapple on a stationary target, or an Exotic Weapons (Grapple Hand) test to successfully grab onto a hostile target. It takes a Minor Action to unhook and retract the grapple hand.

**Cyberware overdrive** : The grapple hand can hoist or drag twice the user’s body weight the length of the cable over the course of one combat round. Roll 1D6; on a 1, the grapple hand system fails and cannot function until it is repaired.

**Wireless bonus** : The user retains control of the disembodied hand, allowing it to “walk” about on its fingers and reach a better grip to hang on to.
