## GodSlayer Inc.

GodSlayer is a new North American Evo subsidiary created to manufacture and market a brand of power-actuated combat hammers. The GodSlayer line currently consists of the Vulcan (power hammer), and its double-sided variant, the Thor. The line also includes the Zeus (power maul), and the Hercules (power actuated fist), which is marketed as the Herc. The newest addition to the line is the Kronos, a troll-sized double-sided power maul, advertised as the largest hand weapon on the market. As an option, one side of the Kronos can be fitted with a hardened armor-penetrating spike instead of the meat-tenderizer-like hammer face.
