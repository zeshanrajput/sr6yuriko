## beast summoner

## Plot Hooks

## Spiritual Assistance

One of the spirits you regularly summon mentions how poorly they are treated by another shaman. They struggle as hard as they can to resist his summonings, but the shaman has an old focus that holds a bit of their energy and makes it easier to summon them.

They want the summoner to steal the focus and free them from being summoned under his control.

The shaman in question has a terrible reputation with spirits but is significantly more powerful than the summoner (at least 2 higher Magic rating and all that entails). The shaman inhabits a toxic zone, where they have a collection of bound and rather unpleasant toxic spirits that thrive in the wretched place. Not to mention the potential for toxic critters that have been attracted or mutated by the mix of muck and mojo. Along with animate foes, the location itself is a hindrance that can be put against the team. Thick choking smoke from tire fires in barrels or piles. Radiation slowly sapping them. Toxic gasses that don’t affect the gangers because they all wear crazy respirator masks.

The character will likely need a team and thus can ask other runners to aid them, call in favors, or offer payment and play the role of Mr. Johnson.

With a little digging, the summoner’s team can discover that this shaman has a connection to a local gang that helped create the environment he thrives on. Whether this is littering, for a low-end setting, or stealing nuclear waste and storing it in a warehouse in the neighborhood is up to the gamemaster’s preferences and style of play.

The investigation and action should be generally straightforward with the already-existing initial connection. Legwork could involve some asking around, but most people in the area work with the shaman, are terrified of them, or think they are a barrens bogeyman.
