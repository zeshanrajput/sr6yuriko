## Brief

My wife is a hero, a patriot who has served our nation. She disappeared with the rest of III Corps. When I heard stories of people from III Corps starting to return, I built hope that I would see her again. And then I did. And then I saw her again. And a third time. Three different versions of my wife. None of them right. One barely had any memories of me. Another had an expertise in knitting and crocheting my wife never had (and a fierce temper to boot). The third had scales.

If these three have returned, I believe my wife has, too. I just haven’t found her yet. All three initially appeared in the Mid-Atlantic region, which is also where I live. I believe the real version is there, too. She’s an ace auto mechanic, a skilled rock climber, and a big Concrete Dreams fan. I think she’ll gravitate to those things. Please find her.
