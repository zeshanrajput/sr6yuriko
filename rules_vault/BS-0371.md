## Cereprax

(Brain Boost, Egghead)

- **Vector:** Ingestion

- **Speed:** 1D6 minutes

- **Duration:** (12 – Body) hours, minimum 1 hour

- **Power:** 10

- **Effect:** +3 Logic, +2 Intuition, gain benefit of Analytical Mind ( _SR6_ , p. 70)

Cereprax is a nootropic drug that is often used by corporations as part of their daily cocktail of drugs provided to knowledge workers. It works by boosting the speed of chemical change within the neurons and improving their connectivity with one another. This enhances reasoning and memory and enables leaps in logic and intuition. This drug is highly regulated in corporate environments due to the risk of permanent brain damage caused from too-frequent use. When the drug wears off, the user suffers –2 Logic for the next (12 – Body) hours and needs to resist (Power)S damage.

If the user takes a second (or more) dose of Cereprax within Power hours, their racial maximum for Intuition is permanently reduced by 1. If their Intuition reaches 0, the user dies, making Cereprax one of the deadliest drugs to get addicted to.
