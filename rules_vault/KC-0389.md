## Bobcat (elf, male, 38, brown hair, brown eyes, muscular build)

Always wanting to be more than just muscle, Bobcat finally found an extra niche when he discovered his affinity for small tech gadgets. He loves trackers and tracking devices as well as good old-fashioned (but new-fangled) spy-tech. He has worked on being inconspicuous and decently deft to plant bugs and trackers on the opposition but can still lay down the pain most of the time.

Bobcat will be looking for every opportunity to get a listening device or tracker in place in order to keep tabs on the runners and get ahead of them. If a fight breaks out, he is all about balance. If it’s a fist fight, it’s a fist fight. Draw a weapon, escalation ensues. His only exception to that is with trolls. He knows full well what one of their fists can do, and he’ll usually resort to Stick-n-Shock or drugs to try and bring them down.
