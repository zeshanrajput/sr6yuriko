## Becoming Indispensable

Having a network of contacts is not a oneway street that solely benefits you. To make this work, you’ll need to make yourself useful to your network. Progress on this quality path requires you to help one of your core network contacts raise their Connection rating by one. This costs time, nuyen, and cash: 1 week multiplied by the new Connection rating, 1 Karma multiplied by the new Connection rating, and (for ratings 4–6) 3,000 nuyen multiplied by the new Connection

rating. However, your work to help out a contact improves the whole network. You may purchase the Network Connections quality at its regular Karma cost (not doubled).
