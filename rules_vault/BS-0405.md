## Pixie Dust

## (Bleeder, Neverland)

- **Vector:** Inhalation

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** 1D6 minutes

- **Power:** 10

- **Effect:** +1 Charisma, High Pain Tolerance, memory loss

This is what happens when you mix traditional cocaine with leäl, and now you get high and can’t remember taking anything. Worse yet, a lot of times the drug is mixed with random bad drek like raw sugar, ground glass, or laundry detergent pods, which give it the reputation of causing nosebleeds. A user of pixie dust loses all memory of the last 1D6 minutes, including taking the drug.
