## Take Your Best Shot

Not everything has to be life-and-death for the gun-fu street samurai. Sometimes it’s simply about bragging rights can lead to a better rep. A local fight promoter has decided to branch out by creating a small tournament that (pun intended) targets local shooters. The concept is simple: A tactical obstacle course is set up with AR and physical targets. The shooter with the best time and score gets not only a cool 15,000 nuyen credstick but also gets the title of “best shot in the sprawl.” And of course, there may be some small side wagers going on as well. Before the competition, the gun-fu samurai is approached by one of the local crime syndicates and offered 20,000 to throw the match. If they take the 20K, they now have a small dark secret that could cause problems in the future, especially if they already have a reputation for having a code of honor.
