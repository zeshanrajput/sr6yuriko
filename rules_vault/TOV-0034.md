## Servit eur

Serviteurs are believers in Voodoo and can be possessed by lwa spirits summoned by houngans and mambos. People who wish to be possessed by lwa are interviewed by Voodoo priests to examine whether the character truly wishes to serve the lwa and live by the ideals of the religion. If you pass the interview, you undergo a ritual that is like the ritual that houngan and mambos undergo to initiate.

- There is some aspect of this which is related to low Essence. But I have seen them, and I put them in a class with blood spirits. Give them a wide berth or put them down if you’re capable.

**Cost:** 2 Karma

**Game Effect:** This ritual creates the magical link needed for the lwa spirit to be able to possess the serviteur. The spirit doesn’t need to make the test to possess the serviteur. If the serviteur breaks the tenets of the religion, they are unable to be horses for the lwa, losing the Serviteur quality. You then need to re-purchase the quality before progressing down the path.
