## **razor teams**

**As the number of incidents and encounters where Disian magic was present increase, there has also been a dramatic increase in casualty rates within Grey Cell and allied units, particularly among Awakened personnel. To combat this and take advantage of weaknesses of Disian magic against those with augmentations, a new combat formation was recently deployed.**

**Known as “Razor Teams,” these units are composed almost exclusively of highly augmented combat specialists and rigger drone/vehicle operators. Former magicians who have “burned out” are also becoming a common sight among these teams.**

**The working theory behind Razor Teams is that their extensive combat augmentations and skills, backed by drones and limited magical support, will be sufcient to overwhelm any Disians or Faustians while still having enough frepower and durability to handle any chimeras or standard threats.**

**The exact composition of a Razor Team varies, but they operate with no less than four primary combatants, one rigger for transportation and fre support, and a Matrix specialist (as needed). On rare occasions, an Awakened member, typically an initiate, may be attached. These individuals are considered more temporary mission specialists than permanent team members.**
