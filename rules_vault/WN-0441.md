## Aztechnology: J.J. Harvin

J.J. Harvin Jr. is one of two Aztechnology elements at work here, and the two of them are not aligned in their philosophy. The sect led by Harvin is concerned with the Animus memory-altering procedure, and they want to destroy knowledge of it and have Animus shut down. They view Disians, who can bring strange magic into the field, as a strong ally, and they’d be happy to point them at an assault on Animus-owned property.

_Sends_ : J.J. Harvin, board member, through a commcall.

_Resources_ : Harvin has a lot of money, and his main focus is throwing it around to get what he wants. But he also has four Force 6 blood spirits (p. 159, _Street Wyrd_ ) that he can use to enforce his will where he needs it.

_Tactics_ : Harvin slow-walks his offer. He doesn’t want to be the first out of the gate, and he’d like to act when the PCs feel some degree of desperation. His first offer will be to send assistance, either Disians or his blood spirits. Once he’s built some trust with the PCs, he’ll see if he can get them to level some Animus facilities for him.

_Offer_ : 100,000 nuyen and spirit assistance. _Conditions for Attack:_ Only if Harvin is directly threatened. Otherwise, he doesn’t think going after the PCs is worth the effort.

_Ways to Play off Other Factions:_ As noted above, Harvin wants to deal with desperate runners, and he’s not above making some deals to increase that desperation. If he can persuade other groups to increase their aggressiveness, he will. He also absolutely does not want any other corporation to have access to Animus’ technology, and he will raise his offer to 125,000 nuyen if he thinks that will keep the tech out of others’ hands.

He also doesn’t want Ding Ramos to get the tech, as he feels that will unleash harm on the world and also help Ding increase his standing within Aztechnology. He’ll pay 150,000 nuyen to keep Animus tech from getting into Ding’s hands.

Harvin wants to keep the Dis portal’s location secret; assistance in doing that earns 25,000 nuyen from him if the PCs bring it up with him.

_Aftermath Attack Risk:_ None. Harvin’s got too much else to worry about. Serving on a megacorporate board is a big responsibility that takes a lot of mental focus, which doesn’t mean every little grudge can be pursued.
