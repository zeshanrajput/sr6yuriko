## Nazca Geoglyphs

These ancient patterns etched into the ground are gigantic glyphs that can form both astral and metaplanar portals. The lines became active as Halley’s Comet passed, and even now, almost twenty years later, not all of the passageways have been researched well enough to ascertain where they lead or what triggers the formation of particular passageways. There have been a number of mishaps in recent years, leading to the whole area being sectioned off and high-security barriers being placed around a few of the geoglyphs.
