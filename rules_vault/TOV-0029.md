There are a few ways to get your hands on a fresh cadaver. The easiest and least socially acceptable method is to create your own. Doing this isn’t without risk, especially since the murder you literally committed is now walking around with your astral signature on it. Killing someone to use their corpse as a zombie increases your Heat by 1—in addition to any other Heat gained.
