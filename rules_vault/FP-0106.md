## **Grey Cell**

**The covert organization typically called the Grey Cell has recently become better known due to their hostile reaction and active aggression against visitors from the metaplane of Dis. It’s believed that the group has been using the Denver Data Haven to help collect and organize data on Disian activities, creating databases of their resources and allies. The Grey Cell has been using the information to conduct terrorist operations, and many that have been conducted against MCT properties. In return, it is believed that the patrons of the Grey Cell and its chief operative (known as Ringleader) are providing the Nexus with protection and support.**
