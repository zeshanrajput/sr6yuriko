## Painting the World 

Not every published adventure will have complete descriptions of the scenes or world. The players at the table may ask for specifics concerning what they see when they look at that run down warehouse at the end of Glow City in the Redmond Barrens. Having an idea of what the scene looks like in Astral and Augmented Reality will allow you to paint out the options that they have to solve the mission and more clear picture of what Shadowrun is.
