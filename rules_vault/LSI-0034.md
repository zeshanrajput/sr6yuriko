## Cleaning Services

Somebody comes by to tidy up the messes you leave behind (trash, dirty dishes, laundry, etc.), and keeps your home clean. Your common or seasonal allergies trigger at one level of severity lower, to a minimum of Mild (p. 75, _SR6_ ).

**Cost: 1 LP**
