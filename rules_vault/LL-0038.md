## shadow stock 

At a player’s discretion, they may have a cosmetic effect from these absorbed mundane/ metahuman enhancements, such as silver scales where chromed-up datajacks or cyberlimbs exist on their metahuman form, or darker scales where prominent tattoos would normally be. 

_Example_ : Our eastern drake is an augmented infiltration specialist, with enhanced articulation, muscle toner, cybereyes, synaptic boosters, and a few other expensive goodies that help her with her work. None of those carry over into her drake form (her dracoform Agility is based on her natural attribute score without Muscle Toner, her boosters don’t grant any extra Initiative dice, she doesn’t receive any bonuses from Enhanced Articulation, etc). Her player and GM decide that the sinuous movements of an Eastern Drake are stylish enough to show her slippery, nimble enhancements, and none of them carry over cosmetically to her dracoform.
