## **Inhibitor Bands**

**Most of the time, standard restraints (p. 278,** _**SR6**_ **) are good enough, but occasionally you need a bit more to contain and control your subject. Inhibitor bands are hybrid technology designed to cover all of the bases when an offcer knows they are dealing with an extraordinary but unknown perpetrator. They combine cyber restraints (p. 30,** _**Body Shop**_ **), Grey Mana, and localized jamming. Inhibitor bands come in two models: a cuff that can be applied to the wrist in the same way as standard restraints, and a collar that wraps around the neck. While an inhibitor band is applied, all cyberware is disabled and cannot be activated, wirelessly or otherwise, Magic is reduced to zero, and all wireless signals are jammed (rating 6) within 1 meter of the wearer. Removing an inhibitor band without the proper key requires a toolkit and an Engineering + Agility (10, 1 Major Action) Extended test. Failure to succeed will trigger the anti-tamper device built into the band. Cuffs have a taser that inficts 6S(e) damage upon the wearer, while the collar is equipped with a Narcoject (p.122,** _**SR6**_ **) injector.**
