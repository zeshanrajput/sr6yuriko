## p. 287, Wired Reflexes, sentence 3 

_Adjust the sentence to read as follows:_ 

When activated, each rating point of wired reflexes gives you +1 Reaction (with accompanying bonus to your Initiative Score) and 1 additional Initiative Die (with accompanying Minor Action). Wired reflexes are incompatible with augmentations that affect Reaction or Initiative.
