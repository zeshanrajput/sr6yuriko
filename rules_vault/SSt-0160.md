## **Avoiding Attention**

**There are a few steps you can take to make sure a cop won’t decide to show up at the wrong time and frag your entire run. When you throw out all the legal jargon, the threshold for a cop poking their nose into something is if they think it looks suspicious** _**in context**_ **. What that** _**doesn’t**_ **mean— and this is important—is “something that looks like you’re breaking the law.” A cop has the legal right to stop you and ask questions over something that’s legal but “unusual,” like wearing a parka on a hot summer day or walking around with a crowbar in the middle of the night.**

- **Or being an ork in a human neighborhood.**

- **Butch**

- **And given how much policing is done by corp security on extraterritorial soil, they can stretch these definitions pretty far.**

- **> Mika**

**So, the frst step to avoiding attention from the cops is to not look like you’re doing anything out of the ordinary. If nothing gives a cop a reason to think “huh, that’s weird,” most of them won’t go out of their way to look for one. If you’re scouting or shadowing a target, blend into your surroundings—in the “look like you belong there” sense, not the “make yourself invisible” sense. Wear clothes that match the neighborhood. **If you want to keep that cop from fragging up your whole run, you have to do the same thing the wageslaves do. Be polite. Be cooperative.
