## Optional Rule: Contact Keywords

Contact Keywords are an optional rule for contacts to help you organize NPCs and their expected behaviors. Each of these keywords acts as a positive or negative quality specifically for contacts, which can adjust outcomes in tests regarding their trustworthiness. These keywords don’t have any specific cost, and it’s easy to create new keywords to work in your game.

Creating new keywords is relatively simple. Select a dice pool modifier between 1 and 4 and relate the keyword to a specific trigger.
