## Striking Callus

Added to a user’s hands or feet, these are simply thick, hardy patches of callused skin, altered to be almost as sturdy as troll dermal deposits, allowing the user to strike dangerous blows with what are at first glance ordinary hands and feet. A relatively new development, they are, as yet, unrestricted weapons.

This provides 1 point of situational Edge for Unarmed Combat attacks.
