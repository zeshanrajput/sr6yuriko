## Australia

Since Australia is a massive area of expansion, the Yakuza have been wanting to get the land to effectively bend the knee for decades, seeing the island as a paradise for crime. Australia is a prime source of workers for hostess clubs, being far easier to gather than, say, Californians, but they tend to be recruited in an above-board manner, rather than trafficked like people from the Philippines or Tsimshian.
