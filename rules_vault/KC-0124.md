## Odu, Uba, & Kobu Law

These triplet brothers were fighting for the people of Lagos while Okoro was still learning to read. When she first came back to Lagos, she worked at the firm as an intern. She gained insight and respect for the fight under their tutelage. She reached out to them after her windfall and asked to join forces. They mistook her request, thinking she wanted a job, and denied her a position as partner in the family firm despite their deep respect for her.

A month later, after her growth spurt, they reached out with a case offer, only to discover she had suddenly become flush with business. A second meeting came with some muted apologies followed by a lot of laughter. They joined her efforts as Lagos experts and brought their connections to several influential individuals.

Odu, Uba, & Kobu Law is the quintessential street-level law firm, and they have a long history in the city. They have a nice little office building in the heart of Lagos Mainland that is well-known to the citizens of Lagos.
