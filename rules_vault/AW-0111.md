## Drumlin

## (Earth, Force 4)

Drumlins are conglomerations of jagged stone in a humanoid form, a stereotypical view of what an earth elemental should look like. There are no detailed features such as fingers, eyes, mouth, etc. on a drumlin, as much of what we would assume to be necessary sensory organs are spread across their whole form. They also don’t move in humanoid fashion; the stones shift into a more spherical shape and then roll to their destination. Communication is through the banging and grinding of stones in their form, creating a literal gravelly voice. A drumlinis the most common type of earth elemental summoned by chance, as it meets the formulated requirements of the magician’s imagination.
