## Prometheus Engineering

The way construction works is, you hire a bigname corporation to build your arcology, and then that company subcontracts the work to someone else for pennies on the dollar. Prometheus Engineering is that someone else. Prometheus is responsible for constructing nearly every post-quake building in the city. The corporation isn’t merely cheap labor, though, as they’ve won seven International Design Awards—including one for their arcology at Grand Central developed right along the fault line, as well as the double-helix spire on the westside.

- Construction means blueprints. That means that Prometheus has the floor plans to every building in the city—up until the point where post-construction changes were made, that is.

- Mika
