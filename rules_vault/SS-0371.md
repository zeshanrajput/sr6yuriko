## Spectral Hand

The soldier is capable of making their hand and part of their forearm insubstantial so that it can pass through solid objects. They cannot dematrialize an object and carry it with through a solid object, but they can partially rematerialize the hand, so that they may stick it through a door and open it the the knob or handle on the other side. A dual-natured weapon can sever the hand while it is dematerialized.
