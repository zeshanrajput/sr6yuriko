## Zephyr

## (Air, Force 6)

Zephyrs appear as great beautiful falcons with colorful cirrus plumage. They are the apex predator of the elemental Plane of Air. Those who get a closer view see that the zephyr’s beak is serrated with savage teeth. It is a fast flyer, striking from above to take out welkins, wafts, and any traveler who wanders too far into their territory.
