## Drone-Mounted Tag Sprayer

A pixel tag sprayer designed to mount on a micro-drone has a payload of only 1,000 pixel tags. A mini-drone pixel tag sprayer carries up to 10,000 pixel tags, the same as a handheld sprayer. These are available in two forms: an exterior mount that clips onto the drone, requiring a Major action to install or remove. When used on a micro or mini-drone, the exterior version cancels any benefits from the realistic features mod and increases Handling by 1. The second is a tag sprayer that can also be installed as a permanent modification, taking up a single mod slot and avoiding the drawbacks of the exterior version.
