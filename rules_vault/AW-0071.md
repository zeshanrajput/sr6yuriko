## Korikyu

In the cardinal pole of air and water, there is a large column of water capped by cylinder of ice. It slowly rotates on the winds from the elemental Plane of Air. A double helix stairway of ice descends from the ice cap, wrapping around the whole column. The ice cap is intricately built, consisting of multiple structures around a central castle. While the other cardinal poles are more villages or trade hubs, Korikyu is designed for diplomacy, as recognized metaplanes have ambassadors and the like here.
