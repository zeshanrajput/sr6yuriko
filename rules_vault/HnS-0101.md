## Keytar Case

Keytars are equal parts dorky and badass, which makes them the perfect fit for deckers with attitude. These cases are designed to house both a cyberhack and a cyberdeck, but more importantly include a musical keyboard, synthesizer, and high-definition speaker system. These things have been around since before the Awakening and were a popular inspiration for some of the first portable cyberdecks. _Plus ça change,_ eh, chummer?

A keytar case just for a cyberdeck has an available capacity of 10.

**Cyberhacks:** This type of case was meant for a cyberhack/cyberdeck combo. Available capacity is 4, but if you remove the synthesizer components, it goes up to 8. But seriously, why would you do that?
