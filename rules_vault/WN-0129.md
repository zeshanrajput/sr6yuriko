## Hook

There was a lot of trouble in Denver a few months back, enough that Ghostwalker is looking for someone to punish. He sent out his people to run down information—and proof, if possible— about what actually happened and who’s responsible. One of his operatives, a fixer known as Cat, has been pulling a thread that’s led her to Manhattan. The Denver office of Shibata Consulting, an MCT subsidiary, closed right after the Aurora Event, and three top managers were transferred to the AA-corp’s home office inside the Mitsuhama Arcology in New Harlem. She wants to find out what they know.

Cat brought along one of her regular associates, a decker called Spyder, but she is looking for local talent to do the heavy lifting. If any of the runners participated in _The Third Parallel_ and have Cat as a contact, she reaches out to offer them the job; if not, she selects a runner team based on a friend-ofa-friend recommendation.
