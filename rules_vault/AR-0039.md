## Satan in Missouri Valley 

Jimmy No is correct—the apparition behind the Missouri Valley cemetery is an alchera. It’s not Satan—it’s one of the Jotunn from the hyper plane of Muspellheim on the Metaplane of Fire (p. 16, _Aetherology_ ). The Jotunn can see the people watching it, but it has not yet figured out how to communicate with them or transmit anything other than visuals. It sees how they watch it and knows it holds them in thrall. It is working to enlist them in the glorious work of burning, regrowing, and burning again.
