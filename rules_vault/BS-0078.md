## Sense the Breeze

Martial arts masters are fast, even without the benefit of chipped reflexes. With the benefit of augmentations, they become faster still. After rolling to resist damage following the use of either version of bending of the reed (p. 103, _Firing Squad_ ) convert a number of unsoaked Physical damage boxes to Stun damage, equal to the amount of bonus Reaction from augmentations the user has. **Category:** Mobility, user must have at least +1 Reaction bonus from augmentations, user must know one or both versions of bending of the reed.
