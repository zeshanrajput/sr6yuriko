## Streets

- **Common life modules:** Activist, Artist, Barista/Bartender, Black Star NeoAnarchist, Blue Collar Laborer, Bodyguard, Bounty Hunter, Charlatan, Clairvoyant, Courier, Entertainer, Entrepreneur, Fixer, Ganger, Guide, Nurse, Pragmatic Magician, Salesperson, Servant, Smuggler, Stuffer Shack Clerk, Traveler

A lot of these backgrounds serve as a starting point for how social adepts purposefully developed their skills and abilities. With magic, however, and especially with adepts, survival can be just as effective a teacher as an academy. Once again, the social adept is a relatively new character type. The streets are definitely the type of place where the skills and abilities of such characters would develop out of sheer necessity, much like how the streets helped birth the otaku (proto-technomancers) in the previous generation.
