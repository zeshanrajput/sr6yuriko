## **Donald “Donnie” Boyd (6) [4]**

He’s a runner who goes by Taps. He usually hangs out at Hits and Heartbreaks losing money betting on whatever is currently hot. He was being held on a drunk and disorderly after he got into a scuffle with a bouncer while a local NYPD, Inc., officer was too close.

Everyone in Terminal knows Bella, and Bella knows everyone who does business in Terminal. She works as a joygirl to pay the rent, keep her ear to the street, and because she genuinely enjoys it. She wasn’t under arrest at the time of the Precinct 10 chaos—she was inside having a meeting with a few detectives she knows discussing some of her stable who had recently gone missing. After she left, she hung out at Claddagh and had a few drinks looking for her next client.
