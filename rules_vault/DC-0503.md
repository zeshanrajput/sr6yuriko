## Anthropomorphic

Anthropomorphic vehicles, usually drones, operate somewhat differently than most walkers. For starters, they come with a full set of two drone arms and two drone legs, which are usually Obvious but may be Synthetic. If the vehicle is Size Class 4 or less, it can go anywhere a metahuman can, with similar limbs and build. They can even use some metahuman equipment, such as clothing, weapons, or tools. This makes them far more versatile than most vehicles, but the price point reflects that. Cost Multiplier: x2
