## **Room and Board**

**Having your very own pet hell hound sounds like it would be pretty wiz—until they set your sofa on fre or try to eat your neighbors. Very few landlords are willing to allow pets of any kind, but even the ones who do are going to balk at letting you bring home anything exotic or dangerous. If you’re going to be taming and training critters, you’ll need to make special arrangements for housing and feeding them.**
