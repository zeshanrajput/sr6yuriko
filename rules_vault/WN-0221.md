## A Face I’ve Seen Before

Becky Wu Ping of White Clouds played the role of Alice in mission 7, **Forgot to Remember** (p. 58). White Clouds is a fashion subsidiary owned by SpIn, but even their records of her are now questionable at best. Spinrad and White Cloud executives can confirm who she is, her roles and past projects, but they cannot bring her memory back, and the time of the Gala is fast approaching.

The runners are being called in by Hadrian Williams-Lee (p. 143) again and asked to find a way to help Becky snap back to herself. This is a legwork mission and a challenge of social psychology. The challenge is to discover key information that might help Becky come back to her senses and present that information in the best way possible. Hadrian assures the runners that SpinGlobal has top doctors on the case, but he’s gambling that an unconventional approach may yield unexpected results.
