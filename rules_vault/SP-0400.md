## **Grey Manatech Innovations**

**==> picture [233 x 4] intentionally omitted \<==**

**To better equip individuals for fghting against the Disian threat, Evo is expending signifcant effort on R&D regarding new applications of grey manatech (GMT). This has resulted in some highly effective armor and cyberware mods and accessories that protect against magic, particularly against mana spells. The basic grey manatech mods and accessories provide defensive bonuses against all magic while dampening the user’s own magic. Some of the newest innovations are specialized helmets that provide improved protection against mana spells that invade the mind, such as Control Thoughts. Some grey mana protective gear is designed to be used by magicians, which provide a limited defensive beneft but do not dampen the user’s magic as harshly.**

**Lastly, ammunition designed using grey manatech interferes with the magic abilities of any target that it strikes by releasing nanoparticles into their body. These tend to be less effective than normal ammunition when it comes to penetrating armor and dealing lethal damage, but it makes up for this by interfering with a target’s ability to use magic. Besides being effective against chimeras, Faustians, and Disians, they’re also useful against magicians and Awakened critters, and they partially mitigate the hardened armor granted by a materialized spirit’s immunity to normal weapons.**
