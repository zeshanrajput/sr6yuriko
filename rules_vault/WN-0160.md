## Binge Watching

El Guapo gives the team eight days of video footage for twenty cameras. That is the week prior to the day after the disappearance. To review the footage, the team will need to perform a Perception + Intuition (15, 1 hour) Extended test. After



completion, give the players the following timeline.

- 3:30 p.m.: Lex returns home and goes to his room.

- 3:45: He leaves his room, goes to the kitchen, and eats half a large pizza.

- 4:00: He returns to his room.

- 4:15: A bedroom door on the other side of the house opens. It shuts again and reopens about fifteen minutes later. No one can be seen opening or closing the door.

- 4:55: The front door to the house can be seen opening and quickly closing. Again, no one can be seen.

- 4:56: The family dog can be seen running to the front of the house. It then casually walks to the end of the front yard and turns around and comes back to the house.
