## **Plot Hook**

**A rancher with a ground wolf problem has made a deal with a corp that studies paracritters—the rancher wants to be rid of the critters, and the corp wants some live ground wolves for study. Mr. Johnson represents the corp and hires the runners to capture as many of the ground wolves as possible—at least two, but ideally the whole den. This will be tricky, as the ground wolves can phase through the earth and are formidable predators, but if the PCs can trap them in something that isn’t made of natural earth, they should be able to collect a nice payday. The rancher may be convinced to provide some bait, but they will be reluctant to do so.**
