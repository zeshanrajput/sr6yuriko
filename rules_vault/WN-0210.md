## Hook

An antique vehicle has been stolen from a very powerful and connected executive of another company. If the players haven’t told anyone about their other job for El Guapo, he will contact them again. He wants the antique vehicle located and brought to him. He tells the players he plans on returning it to its owner in an attempt to build a positive rapport.

The truth is more complicated. El Guapo thinks Lex James is working in the shadows and still hopes to get him back alive and in one piece. He has narrowed his activities down to a few possible new faces in town, but he can’t pinpoint which one might be Lex. That was, until the recent theft of the vehicle, which sounds like a job Lex might have pulled off. He doesn’t expect the runners to be able to catch Lex, but the least he could do is return the car, which could gain El Guapo favor with a rival while also reducing the chance of possible legal issues for Lex and the rest of the James family.
