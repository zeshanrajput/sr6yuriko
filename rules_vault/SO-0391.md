## Terrify

## [Influence (Intimidation), Major Action] 4 Edge

By using visceral screams, gnashing teeth, a frightening appearance, and horrifying weapons, you attempt to frighten the target into inaction or prevent them from acting. Make an Influence (Intimidation) + Charisma test opposed by the target’s Composure. If successful, the target gains the Frightened status for one round, plus one round for every two net hits.
