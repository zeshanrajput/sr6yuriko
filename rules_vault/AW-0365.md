## The Great Desert

At the center of the desert is an ancient ziggurat that was here long before the Tandorans arrived. The Tandorans believe that it was once a temple, and that the long-forgotten gods who live there are mad and vengeful. They find the desert inhospitable and do not travel far enough into it to explore the temple. The region of the desert surrounding the temple is watched over by thunder beasts—picture komodo dragons about twice the size of a rhinoceros. This is where they live most of the time, and it’s in this place where the beast-riders from the camp find their mounts.
