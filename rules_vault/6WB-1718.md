## Dead End

## Main Location: Falkensee

Notable Characters: German Neo-Nazis (use Humanis Goon, p. 204), Cashmere

In the far west of Berlin lies Falkensee, a loveless concrete ghetto of dilapidated apartment blocks. Whoever ends up here has literally reached the end of the road. The runners are contacted by the residents of a block inhabited mainly by metahumans: A gang of neo-Nazis called Werewolf Kommando 13 is making itself at home in the area, harassing shopkeepers and extorting protection money, and the residents fear they are about to attack the apartment block or even set it on fire. In their distress, the residents have turned to Cashmere (see **Berlin Contacts** ),
