## Improved Pilot

Vehicle pilots incorporate the most sophisticated mobile hardware and software available; some even approach the level of a basic AI. Improving a vehicle’s Pilot rating takes more than just loading a new program. Hardware is replaced and upgraded, communication channels are enhanced, and the vehicle control systems are upgraded. Pilots may have an attribute rating from 1 to 9.
