## **Check on Conditions Regularly**

**Anything below zero degrees Celsius is cold, and sometimes it’s diffcult to feel the difference between, say, –15 and –20. But never forget that there is a real difference. Even a fve-degree change could shave ten minutes off the time before frostbite sets in. And while temperatures above thirty are hot no matter how you look at it, thirty-degree temperatures with high humidity will wipe you out more than dry heat (though once you get above forty, it tends to matter less how dry the heat is, as it can still bake you). While a difference between ten degrees and twenty won’t matter a whole lot to anything you’re doing, the difference between thirty and forty could be the difference between functioning and lying on the ground trying to recover from heat stroke.**

- **Everyone should follow this advice because it’s so damn easy. Everyone has AR, and the standard ARO that comes with every AR viewer is a weather bug. Odds are, a weather update is floating in front of your face all the time. Use it.**

- **Of course, some of the places we’re talking about have pretty poor Matrix service, so if your widget relies on that, you might not get anything. Spend a few nuyen on a thermometer to keep you current if you’re going to the wilds. And keep it far enough away from you that you don’t skew the readings.**
