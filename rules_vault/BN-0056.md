## Functional Tail (Prehensile)

- **Co** st: 8 Karma

This functions like the balance tail, except you can control it as if it were an extra limb. The tail can pick up items, though it lacks fi ne digits and has diffi culty with fi ne manipulation. Basic tasks that normally do not require tests such as pressing a button or pulling a trigger require a successful Body + Agility (3) test for you to pull off. The tail has an effective Strength attribute equal to half your Strength (round down). Snake forms are an exception to this and use their full Strength attribute. You can hang by your tail when desired. Prehensile tails do not provide extra actions or attacks.
