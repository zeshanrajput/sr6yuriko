## **Option 1:**

## **Your Case Gets Dropped**

**Prosecutions take time and money. Even government prosecutors have budgets. Going to a trial they can’t win is a waste of prosecutorial resources. On top of that, in countries where prosecutors are elected, the prosecutors also have to worry about making sure they look good for the public. If all the public sees are courts returning not guilty verdicts when everyone thinks it should’ve been guilty, that prosecutor isn’t getting another term in offce.**
