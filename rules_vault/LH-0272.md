## Update Five: The Grinder

Finally, we’re inside the main complex _and_ I don’t have to shout … much.

There’s a slight break in the fighting as we finally made it into the main complex, which it turns out has three sub-levels. Everything above was simply window dressing; _lethal_ window dressing, but I digress. I can still hear fighting in the distance; gunfire, explosions, and other noises I don’t want to know about.

Anyway. When we broke through the perimeter and made our way to the main complex, several hidden hatches in the ground flew open, and dozens upon dozens of chimeras—of every fragging kind— spewed forth, ripping into any Defender in proximity. One even managed to get too close to me, but Dash smashed its face in, _then_ blew its head off.

The Disian chimeras went into what I can only describe as a blood rage at the sight of the liberated, and focused on them; no idea why, don’t care. Using that to our advantage, the Iron Cav kept the chimeras occupied and allowed the rest of the forces to move up and into the main building. Our engineers quickly blew the main building’s doors, and thus began a painstakingly brutal close-quarters-battle as everything opened up on us again. But we quickly deployed another ace in the hole: cyberzombies.
