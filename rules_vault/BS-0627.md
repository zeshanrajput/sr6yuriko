## Essence Loss and Social Rating

Unless offset by training or a natural ability, losing Essence reduces the ability to read and project the subconscious empathic clues all metahumans use when they communicate. The impact of Essence loss is subtle but factors into Social Rating as a negative modifier. Every 2 full points of Essence loss causes a –1 modifier to the Social Rating number. This works out to 6.0 to 4.01 Essence having no effect, 4.0 to 2.01 is a –1 Social Rating modifier, 2.0 to 0.01 is a –2. Unlike the Social Rating derived from armor or cyberware, the negative effects of Essence loss always apply and do not use the opposite value for Intimidation tests.

As an additional note, any character with the negative quality Cyber Psychosis has an additional –1 Social Rating per level, stacking with the Essence loss modifier.
