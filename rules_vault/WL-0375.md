## **New Lifestyle Qualities**

## **Stay-at-Home Critter**

**If you’re keeping your critters at home with you, you’ll need to make some space for them. At Low lifestyle, this is limited to small mundane critters (Body 2 or less). At Medium lifestyle, this quality is available to accommodate mundane critters up to Body 4. High or Luxury lifestyle is required to allow larger or Awakened/Emerged critters.**

**Cost: 1 LP per mundane critter, 2 LP per Awakened or Emerged critter**
