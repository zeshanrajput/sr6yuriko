## Behind the Scenes

Tobi Adegonye is being forced to spy on Okoro by Kiniun the Lion, a powerful hawala unhappy with Okoro’s connection to Ayeowo. Adegonye has collected copies of the spreadsheets with Kechibi transactions, a slew of document copies linking Okoro to eco-terrorism, a copy of the “Kechibi” message with a note that Code Eco has a copy, and finally, a message and transaction details for Okoro’s funding of Ayeowo and their arrangement. The firm operates from a small office in a building with eight other offices in Oshodi-Isolo near the airport. Kiniun also has an office in the same complex, so his goons are always around to keep an eye on his prize spy.
