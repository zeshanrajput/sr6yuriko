## **Option 3:**

## **The Offer You Can’t (or Shouldn’t) Refuse**

**Like anyone, cops and lawyers need things done, but they have all these pesky rules to follow. Sometimes, they need a way get those things done** _**for**_ **them, while making sure there’s no trail back to them if anyone starts snooping around. Sound familiar? The fact is, both law-enforcement corps and attorneys (prosecution or defense) regularly hire runners to infuence a case one way or another.**

**The normal deal that a law enforcement Johnson makes is something like “do this job for us and we’ll make your charges go away.” That’s not exactly it, of course, because no cop or prosecutor who’s willing to hire a runner team is stupid enough to throw away the leverage they have over you. What it does get you is the opportunity**

**to avoid being tagged with a SIN, whether citizen (if you aren’t convicted) or criminal (if you are), and whatever penalties might come from being found guilty.**

**This might sound like a bad deal for you, but remember: The cop is relying on you to keep your mouth shut as much as you’re relying on them not to charge you. Nothing ruins a cop or prosecutor’s career faster than public backlash from fnding out they were involved in a criminal conspiracy (which is the legal term for them hiring you). Since they know you can bring them down with you by speaking out about who hired them, police and prosecutor Johnsons tend to be more likely to backstab you than their corporate counterparts.**
