## Downer BTL

- **Vector:** Slot

- **Speed:** 1 combat round

- **Duration:** (Rating x 5) minutes

- **Power:** 6 + (Rating)

- **Effect:** +1 Intuition, –1 Reaction

For a user of the Downer BTL, the world just slows down, and their mind seems more open to all the beautiful patterns existing around them. Users get a sense that they should observe before acting, and the mind is focused on that. They gain a point of situational Edge on Perception tests. When the BTL wears off, the user gains the Panicked status for (rating) minutes.
