## Lesser DemiGOD

## (Professional Rating 7)

DemiGODs are the agents working for the Grid Overwatch Division and are the SWAT team equivalent of Matrix security. These are the guys monitoring OS and just waiting to converge on you. They can be encountered out on the public grids, but can also be hired under contract to patrol in hosts.
