## April 27, 2083

Globally, manastorms increase dramatically, most notably in Australia. Played off as natural phenomena in the media, the increase in storm activity is a result of Disian Harvester and Mobile Harvester testing. Defenders are forced to concentrate on secondary targets instead of assaulting the Harvester Network directly.
