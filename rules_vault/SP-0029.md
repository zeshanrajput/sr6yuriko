## **Faustians: Bought and Paid For**

**Part covert ops and part feld generals, Faustians are metahumans who have gone beyond allying or collaborating with the Disians; they’ve literally sold and bound themselves to them. In return, the recipient is given a boon of some kind, which is often either a physical or magical enhancement, or simply something the receiver desperately wants.**

**Regardless, the Faustian is then ritually bound to a particular Disian, their “patron,” for the rest of their lives. Once the pact is sealed, the Faustian receives a rare crystal which is embedded into their body, usually in the sternum. Once the rite is complete, the Disian uses this crystal to channel their power (among other things) to their vassal, granting them (among other things) increased magical-like powers and attributes. It also allows the patron to use their Faustian vassal in similar ways great dragons use their metahuman “voices.”**

**The Disians frame this as a great honor, but it’s nothing more than another form of slavery freely accepted for personal gain. Despite Faustians and others (falsely) considering themselves “allies,” the Disians consider them nothing more than tools to be used and discarded as necessary.**

- **This, of course, also tracks with why the Disians surround themselves with so many “allies.” Such individuals not only provide a means to an end, but they’re also convenient shields and scapegoats.**

- **Frosty**

**While Faustians are nothing but tools, they are nevertheless powerful ones. Enhancements and boons aside, their most potent function is their placement within our society. Most often, a Faustian is a powerful individual or seated in a position of great power or specifc purpose. This allows the Fustian not only to facilitate their patron’s wishes and by proxy the Highest Council’s, but also to guide or manipulate people or global events to create favorable conditions or outcomes for the Dis. For decades now, such manipulated individuals have inadvertently assisted the Disian cause.**

- **Deadline**

**We know that not just anyone can become a Faustian, as the crystals required for such a joining are exceptionally rare. To receive such a “gift,” an individual must provide something extraordinary to the Disians or be a key component in their plans. As of now, we can only confrm the existence of ffty-one Faustians, but as data is further decrypted, that number is likely to grow—estimates now sit at nearly two thousand of the Faustian crystals available. Most of the Faustians we’ve confrmed thus far have been embedded in strategic locations such as national governments or smaller corporations, facilitating, clearing the way, or covering for other allies and Disian proxies to complete various plans and objectives.**

**As for Faustians in the Big Ten, we have our suspicions, but we haven’t confrmed anything. Surprisingly, Aztechnology appears to have the fewest, while there has been an uptick within both MCT and Wuxing; the latter is estimated to have the most and could explain why such a corporation would even work with the Disians in the frst place. Saeder-Krupp is reported to have at least fve, but we’re still working to confrm that number and their identities.**

- **Warning: Individual Faustians are not to be underestimated. In some cases, the Disian boons they receive give them tremendous personal power on their own. But I can’t reiterate**
