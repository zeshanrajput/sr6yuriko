## **From Whisper Nets, can I play a monad (p. 149)?**

Yes. There are a few addendums to clarify the use of monads in _Missions_ play. When selecting the Nanite Volume during character generation, the following table sets the NV number at each priority. Nanite Volume for monads is separately tracked from any nanites used from _Body Shop_ . Any reference to a Nanite Volume test requires you to roll the Nanite Volume of the character. Any time the character loses Nanite Volume, this must be re-purchased using Karma and cannot come from other sources of nanites. Nanite Volume



can be allocated between Mental Boosts, Physical Boosts, and Action Use. The total sum of the allocation cannot exceed the Nanite Volume of the character. Any monad powers are tracked separately from the allocation of boosts but when the Nanite Volume is temporarily decreased for one minute after boosting, the player must allocate which monad powers are temporarily offline.

Recovering NV that are spent or temporarily lost occur after one minute where the allocated NV is not active unless otherwise stated by the power.

Because the Monad attribute joins the Magic/Resonance column, a Monad cannot have Latent Awakening and cannot take Transhumanism. At character creation, the limit to NV is 6. This can be raised after character creation through the use of Karma. The maximum NV is two times the character’s unaugmented Body. When building a Monad using the Life Path method, you gain 1 Monad Ability for each NV at the end of the build.

Monads gain +1 Heat when the team gains Heat similar to other metasapients or SURGE. **Physical Attribute Boost** is initiated with a Minor (A) action.

**Action Use** is a separate power from Physical Attribute Boost. The gained Minor Actions can stack and end at different times.

**Modify Senses** costs 1 NV per sense and lasts five minutes. NV spent in this fashion recharge at a rate of 1 per minute after the sense turns off.

**Toxin Resistance** does not apply to the addiction test. This would provide a benefit to resisting radiation and BTL/Ripjack.

**Adrenal Control** allows the monad to make a Minor Action (A) to make a test to stay conscious. This means that the monad must have a Minor Action remaining or else they fall unconscious. If the monad is unconscious, they cannot take the Minor Action. If they succeed, they remain conscious until the end of the next combat round.

**Resculpt** requires a Con + Intuition (16, 1 combat round) test to change appearances.

**Obsidian Cloud** creates a Matrix entity that shares the monad's persona and is similar to an agent or sprite. The cloud’s Matrix Attributes are equal to those of a cyberjack with a rating equal to NV spent up to a maximum of 6. The Mental Attributes for the Obsidian Cloud are equal to device rating. The cloud has any skills as the monad (Cracking or Electronics only) and skill ratings equal to NV spent.

**Tech Blast** targets a single device per action.

**From Shoot Straight, does the purification straw (p. 115) reduce the chances that I get addicted to a drug?** Absolutely! The drawback is that the positive effects of the drug are also filtered out.
