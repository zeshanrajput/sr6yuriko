## Special Notes

The PCs may have enough knowledge to get extra information from the file they lift from the building. A successful Biotech (Medicine) + Logic (5) test made when reviewing the material shows that there are strong underlying concerns about the effectiveness of the procedures done in the Octagon. They have had some successes that motivate them to continue, but repeatability from one individual to another has been a challenge. Materials also mention the “creative restructuring of brain pathways” as a problem, as sometimes the things they are trying to hide have a disconcerting way of not staying hidden.
