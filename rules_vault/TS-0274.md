## **Shiawase Silverstar**

**Based on the popular Butler series anthro drone by Shiawase, the Silverstar is purpose-built to support LESP offcers in the feld. It can be seen being used as patrol offcers in districts where the workforce is in short supply, as backup in low-risk calls, or even assigned to investigation units to support canvassing neighborhoods after a crime. Overall, it is a light-duty general-purpose drone that can interact with the general population in a reassuring manner. Most people encountering a Silverstar in the feld have reported satisfactory interactions, and do not feel threatened in the same way as they do when interacting with standard mechanical drones.**
