## Koborokuru

The koborokuru variant maintains the thermographic vision, decrease in height, sharply featured angular faces, and toxin resistance of mainline dwarfs. They express more diversly in eyes, body hair, and overall musculature. Their eyes are commonly almond-shaped, and their body and facial hair are less than other _pumilionis_ types, which is often reflected in fewer or shorter beards and shorter or sparser hair. Muscle density is lower than the common dwarf, as is their overall bulk. They possess a slimmer, more muscular tone and a higher metabolic rate.

The koborokuru metavariant is most common in Japan, especially among the Ainu peoples of Hokkaido. They have had trouble finding acceptance in Japan, so many are moving to more accepting countries, such as the NAN, CalFree State, India, Yakut, Ecuador, Amazonia, and Vatican City. The latter’s status connects to the sanctuary provided to koborokuru children shortly after the Awakening.

**>** Koborokuru make fantastic models and are often seen in outlets such as Modern Metahumanity or Horizon’s Notable Peoples of the World netcast. Many koborokurus have also been finding ways to gain education in political science and the other social studies, giving them a foothold in international and intercorporate negotiations.
