**The law enforcement providers (or LEPs) operating across the Sixth World can be as diverse as their individual offcers. They exist to do just what the name says: to enforce the law set by the government. Laws can vary, so know what they are at a given location. Something perfectly legal in one area will get you swarmed by shields in another. Whether those laws and the government that sets them are enlightened or oppressive also infuences the police forces charged with their enforcement. For every open and enlightened society, there’s an opposing corner of the Sixth World that is despotic and authoritarian … with a police force that matches. Instead of existing to “protect and serve,” these organizations are there to “control and contain” the population.**
