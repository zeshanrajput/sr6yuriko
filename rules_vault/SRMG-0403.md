## **How many times can I take Neuromorphism (p. 129) at Character Creation?**

Similar to transhumanism, you can take neuromorphism one time during character creation, but the character must already have 1 Spark worth of codemods prior to undergoing the expenditure. This means that the highest amount of Spark that an AI with neuromorphism is 5.
