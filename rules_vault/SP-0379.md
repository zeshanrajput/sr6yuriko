## **Bio-Vat**

**These devices are large cylinders about three meters tall and a meter in diameter flled with al-**

**==> picture [36 x 208] intentionally omitted \<==**



**chemical compounds and connected to other devices with cables and pipes. A bio-vat is used to grow the homunculus bodies the Disians inhabit. Made to order for specifc Disians and designed to be used on a specifc metaplane, growing a new homunculus shell in a bio-vat a requires a “seed” from the metaplane that it is planned for—the seed being some fesh from one of the natives. Once the process is started, it takes about ten days to grow a new Disian shell. After it’s decanted, the new shell can be moved to storage, where it waits for the Disian it was grown for. As shells degrade quickly when used, a Disian active on a foreign metaplane will typically use one shell, have at least one spare in storage, and have the next being grown in a bio-vat.**

**Note: Bio-vats are a narrative device to be used by the gamemaster, so there are no specifc rules for them. Only the Disians and their servants have the knowledge required to make use of a bio-vat.**
