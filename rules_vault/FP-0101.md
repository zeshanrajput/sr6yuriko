## **Patrons and Alliances**

**The Denver Data Haven has numerous allies that include the other data havens, numerous shadowrunners, many criminal syndicates, and the other illegal hosts on the Shadow Matrix. There are also a few legitimate groups and individuals who are either patrons or close allies and can be drawn on for expertise, aid, or resources.**
