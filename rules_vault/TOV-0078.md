The size of the gris-gris limits the size of the spirit that can reside in it. Gris-gris that are three centimeters by three centimeter can house up to Force 1 lwa. For each increase in Force, the size of the gris-gris doubles, so a gris-gris (Force 3), the charm is nine centimeters by nine centimeters. At some point, the charm becomes too large to be toted around—such large gris-gris usually remain within the hounfour.

If you wish to have a different Force limit and number of services supported from voodoo stores, this is something that can be changed at your table. The numbers provided here are based on the idea that higher-powered magicians are snatched up by the corporations. They represent the average dice pools available to less-powerful practitioners working in those shops.

The second ritual is the calling forth of the spirit in the nature of the prepared gris-gris. This requires a Conjuring + Magic (Force) test to bring forth the spirit and convince it to aid others while homed in the talisman. The test takes fi fteen minutes, and any net hits are services granted by the gris-gris. Failing the test does not consume the prepared talisman, and the summoner may try again. On a glitch, the prepared vessel is consumed and on a critical glitch during any of the tests, the spirit summoned will provide aid to the opposing part of a test. A Conjuring gris-gris cannot be used to aid this task.

For example, if the user buys a healing charm (Force 2), it will have 2 services. When buying the charm, the recipient needs to take part in a two-hour ritual where the spirit is requested to aid the user. If the user is injured and needs to be healed, the charm uses a service and assists the healing test, rolling 4 dice.

When searching for a gris-gris in an area where Voodoo worship is common, the user is always able to fi nd a talisman, but if they are unable to meet the Availability of the specifi c fetish, they instead fi nd a fake product. These cost the same as the real thing but grant no boons.

The fi nal ritual is to implore the spirit to aid a specifi c user, which is covered under **Buying Gris-Gris** . If the creation of the gris-gris is for yourself, this step is not required.
