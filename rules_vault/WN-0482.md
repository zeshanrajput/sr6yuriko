## The Null Sect

The Null Sect is active in the Matrix of Manhattan at an assortment of locations. In addition to the normal haunts of Spin Global foundations, old NeoNET splinter hosts, and the Deep Foundation under MDC’s local grid, there is a lot of activity reported in several MCT hosts, including the ones attached to the Mitsuhama Arcology in New Harlem.

Dealing with the Null Sect should feel like dealing with a dragon. They don’t believe that nonNulls are their equals, so there is no guarantee that they will act in good faith. They don’t think like a metahuman, or have the same social clues, or even the same perception of reality, so it’s impossible to decipher what they may actually want. It’s also safe to assume that whoever they’re making a deal with does not have access to all the information they do, making it hard to be one hundred percent sure about the ramifications of what’s being agreed to.
