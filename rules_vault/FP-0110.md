## **Ghostwalker**

**As noted above, Ghostwalker has been both a patron and an adversary over the years. Currently, it seems that he is making an effort to mend fences with the Denver Data Haven, pouring money into the organization and providing less tangible but no less valuable support. Despite those efforts, it’s been reported that many of the leadership within the Nexus still consider the wyrm an enemy. They believe Ghostwalker is playing a long game, and they think that after two failed attempts to destroy the data haven, his recent support is a ploy to get them off guard so that his third attempt succeeds.**

**Of course, that belief is not stopping anyone from accepting money and assistance—it’s just being taken with a large dose of caution. The dragon’s envoy, the decker known as Spyder, is generally held at arm’s length and treated as an outsider, kept isolated from any critical information. Additionally, a great deal of effort is being put into hiding the location of any of the data haven’s physical facilities from Ghostwalker’s agents, and the identity of the staff is well concealed as well. Only time will tell if the White Wyrm is in fact an ally or an enemy.**
