## Step 1:

## Select the desired effect

The first step is to determine what you are building. You must select a vector, a duration, and at least one effect. All drugs, toxins, BTL, etc. start with Power 6 and a base threshold of 18. This list is not exhaustive, and you can work with your gamemaster to add effects that make sense.


## Step 2:

## Create the Substance

Once you have selected all the effects you’re targeting for the drug you’re building, it’s time to craft the drug or BTL. The type of thing you’re building will affect the type of test required. To build a drug or toxin, you need to have access to chemicals through a kit, shop, or facility (p. 273, _SR6_ ) and make a Biotech (Medicine) + Logic (threshold, 1 day) test. To build a BTL or ripjack, you need to have access to software tools and memory recordings through a kit, shop, or facility and make an Electronics (Software) + Logic (threshold, 1 day) test. In addition, if you have an appropriate knowledge skill that would apply to the test, you gain a situational point of Edge for the first interval of the extended test. Now that you’ve built your vice, it’s time to try it out and see how long it takes to affect you and how long the effects will last. First roll 1D6 to determine the speed and then roll a second 1D6 to determine the duration.

## Step 4:

## Roll Side Effects

Your home-created drug or ripjack is going to have side effects. The first step is to roll a 2D6 to determine the side effect. If the side effect is –1 to an Attribute, roll 1D6 to determine which Attribute is affected. If the side effect is to gain a Status condition, then roll 1D6 to determine which Status you gain while using the drug. For toxins and ripjack, roll 1D6 on the Side Effect table and apply the relevant effect. If the effect is to gain +1 or +2 to an Attribute, roll a second 1D6 to determine which Attribute. If the Power is 10 or higher, repeat this step for a second Side Effect.
