## Astral and Metaplanar Rifts

Similar to astral gateways and portals, astral rifts are seemingly natural phenomena that can cause forced projection and open astral passageways. Metaplanar rifts are similar, but they allow for physical transference—physical travel to the metaplanes—and do not cause forced projection. Like portals, the types of rifts that have been documented and the mechanics of their operation defy any simple categorization. Most seem to be a natural feature of a local manasphere, perhaps caused by ley-line interactions or the result of some geologic feature affecting the astral. Unlike portals, they do not have an anchoring object, and many drift within a geographic area. Some wander a few meters a day, others travel kilometers between openings, while a few remain in the same location year after year. Some rifts stay open or have predictable schedules, others need specific triggers, while many open and close at random intervals— or possibly in response to some unknown trigger. A few rifts connected to alchera have been documented, and these usually manifest and disappear with the alchera.

Astral rifts cause an area of forced projection, sometimes drifting across unsuspecting mundanes, forcing them out of their bodies. If the phenomenon

also includes a passageway to a metaplane, it may draw in the unwary and carry them off, leaving their unconscious bodies strewn across the ground. Many metaplanar rifts manifest out in the open and are easy to spot, with travel requiring someone to deliberately enter them. Some are more subtle in their aspect, however, residing within some other feature that disguises their nature. Entering a cave, walking a forest trail, or diving into a pool may actually transport the unwary traveler to another metaplane without any advance warning.

The rules for using rifts, including where and when they appear, as well as where they lead, are at the discretion of the gamemaster and should be decided on a case-by-case basis. Some rifts are well known and stable enough that they can be reliably used to travel to a given metaplane. Others will be chaotic and random in both the location where they appear and the duration of their manifestation. These may lead to a known destination but are still difficult to use due to their unpredictable nature. Of course, the locals who deal with these phenomena may know a special ritual or have a unique artifact that makes the rift usable.
