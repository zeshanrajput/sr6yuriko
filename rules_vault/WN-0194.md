## Johnson In Absentia

When the runners return for the next task, they find a different Mr. Johnson waiting for them. Mr. Johnson explains that he will be dealing with the runners from here on out. The terms of their arrangement are otherwise the same. He explains that this last objective is the toughest. They need to figure out what Alayna Gale is up to.

Alayna Gale is famous for their Met Gala red carpet reveal. They float through the city in a cloud of high-priced Evo security and keep the secret of the design near to or on their person at all times. Mr. Johnson knows that Gale will be appearing on the runway at an event at Lincoln Center. During that time, their design will be secured in



their dressing-room safe. Stealth is key here. The runners need to get a look at that design without Gale knowing their work has been compromised. If they complete the task successfully, Mr. Johnson will double the fee they agreed to.
