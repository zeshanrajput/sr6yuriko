## Betameth

(Buzz, Rigger’s Cocktail)

- **Vector:** Inhalation

- **Speed:** 1 combat round

- **Duration:** (9 – Body) hours, minimum 1 hour

- **Power:** 11

- **Effect:** +2 Reaction, +1 Intuition

Betameth represents a class of drugs that are derivatives of pseudomethamphetamine, which has often been abused as a diet drug among teenagers. It’s a stimulant that suppresses appetite and speeds up the user’s metabolism and thought processes. Users of the drug are often identified by their elevated energy while high. When the drug wears off, the user needs to resist (Power)S damage. If the user has an addiction above level 3 (p. 74, _SR6_ ), they are unable to spend Edge on Perception tests due to trouble focusing. If the addiction progresses to level 5 or 6, the user gains the Weak Immune System negative quality.
