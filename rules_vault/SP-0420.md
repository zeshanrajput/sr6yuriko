## **Game Effect**

**If an attack using a GMT arrow/bolt shaft deals at least 1 point of physical damage, the projectile is stuck in the target. An Awakened target with a GMT shaft stuck in them suffers a – 1 dice pool penalty to all tests that use their Magic attribute. Removing the shaft requires a Major Action and a Biotech + Logic (2) or Agility (2) test. After an attack using a GMT arrow/bolt shaft, roll 1D6—if the result is a 5 or 6, it may be reused if recovered.**
