## From the Schools

Many racing riggers come from rather mundane worlds, where driving or flying was something they pursued professionally or fell into while performing other work. Sometimes, it’s a hobby that becomes a career. This can be someone whowas into combat biking but developed their skills professionally because they showed aptitude and were able to get professional training through sponsorship, patronage, or independent financing. This scenario could also apply to other professional car, boat, or airplane racers.
