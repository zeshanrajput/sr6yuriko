## Dark Tech

The Disian Shadow War was, for some, the last stand; a time when corporations and organizations pulled out the darkest, most dangerous technology and magic ever discovered—and weaponized it. All these items are pyrrhic constructs as dangerous to their creators and users as to their targets.

Dark-Tech devices are prototype fusions of technology and magic with negative side effects that prohibit their use in any but the most desperate situations. Due to the extreme threat that Dis represented, short production runs were made to get them out into the hands of front-line operatives. These devices were never meant for regular purchase and were limited to specific missions, with the device being either returned to whatever black site it came from or destroyed.
