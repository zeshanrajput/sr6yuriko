## Last Crown of Atlantis: 450,000 nuyen

This tiara is said to be the crown of its last sovereign. It’s more likely to be a fabrication intended to fool the unwary, but there’s still enough magic in it to make it valuable. According to leaked documents, it was taken from a tribe of merrow and held in the desert for research. The tiara itself functions as a high-powered counterspelling focus, but with the centerpiece sapphire set in place, it bestows a functional glamour that appears as an extravagant blue dress that grants knowledge of the nearby tidal flow (manifesting as a feeling of pain in the lower abdomen for some reason). The last confirmed sighting was in the possession of elven media influencer, Kate Kson, approximately three years ago. I’d advise caution in dealing with anyone willing to flaunt this item.
