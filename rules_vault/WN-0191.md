## 6: cutting edges

## The Job

Press is not good for SpinGlobal. Following the death of its eponymous CEO, the corporation continues to receive negative attention about its future and its leadership. Gabrielle Al Thani Spinrad has a chance to turn that around with her upcoming appearance at the Met Gala. If anything is true of fashion, it is that clothes make the (wo)man. To that end, she’s deployed a trusted asset to gather intelligence on what the biggest names will be wearing so that she can do them one better.
