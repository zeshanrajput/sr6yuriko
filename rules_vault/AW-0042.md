## Cardinal poles

**Earth-Air:** Foehn (p. 37) **Earth-Fire:** Cupellation (p. 39) **Earth-Water:** Dovv (p. 31)

- \*Piezoelectric crystal technology

\*\*The practice of magic related to the elemental plane is considered to be in a medium mana flow (p. 168, Street Wyrd). All other elemental related magic is considered to be in a medium mana ebb (p. 168, Street Wyrd). Unless an individual is at one of the cardinal poles, only earth spirits can be conjured.
