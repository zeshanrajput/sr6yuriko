## Telescoping Modification (1-2)

Reach out and touch someone! Telescoping cyberlimbs allow a user to elevate themselves to see over walls, reach the top shelf in a Stuffer Shack, or do many other useful things with additional reach. While this modification is useful, it poses engineering challenges to maintain full structural integrity while extended. For each level, a telescoping limb can extend by one meter. However, while extended the limb suffers –1 Strength per meter. It costs a Minor Action to extend or retract a telescoping cyberlimb.

**Wireless Bonus** : Gain a bonus Minor Action after spending one to extend or retract a telescoping cyberlimb.
