## Shifter Barbarian

**The joke here is that shifters have none. When you spend most of your life naked,** clothes for modesty’s sake seem very silly. For some shifters, this is taken to an extreme.

**Game Effect:** Anytime you are wearing clothing or armor with DR or Capacity, you may not gain or spend Edge. Additionally, you tend to take a very casual or minimalist approach to dressing and will frequently grant Edge to anyone in an opposed Social test outside of the beach or a nudist colony, either due to lack of attire or your discomfort due to wearing a socially acceptable amount of clothing—except on intimidation and seduction tests. You also fail to meet any sort of dress code without signifi cant persuasion.
