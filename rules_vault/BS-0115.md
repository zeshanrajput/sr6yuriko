## Biowaste Storage

Not everyone can “hold it” indefinitely. With biowaste storage, you can! Each rating point allows the user to store a day’s worth of solid and liquid waste without needing to excrete. The waste can be removed via the usual methods or directly via an external port. Gloves are recommended.

**Wireless bonus:** Biowaste storage can communicate to a wireless biomonitor if there are any warning signs of parasites, toxins, or disease in the user’s waste.
