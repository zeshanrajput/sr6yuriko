## Ceramic/Plasteel Knife

Lightweight and nonmetallic, these knives have long been favored by divers, who don’t want their knives corroding from use in saltwater. More recently, these knives have also become a favorite of assassins and mercenaries who need a weapon that can pass undetected through an MAD. Manufactured knives are almost universally made of ceramic, but needy weaponsmiths have crafted knives from plasteel in a pinch.
