## members have any commlinks or ID.

**Main floor:** None of the maglocks in the building seem to be working, and all of the communications equipment is jammed. The few officers who were onsite are blockading the second-floor staircases, stopping anyone from reaching the armory.

**Basement:** With all of the maglocks off, the prisoners have left their cells. The doors to the impound locker are also open, and it looks like the escapees have already rummaged through looking for weapons, drugs, and anything else of obvious value. There is a dataterm in the desk with the last user still logged in; this can be used to provide access to the precinct’s host. A Hash Check Matrix action will give the location of the last few items checked in: two commlinks from “highjackers,” one commlink from “smuggler,” and a crate containing an “unidentified device.” The commlinks are still here, as well as a sixty-cubic-centimeter crate. The crate has already been unsealed, and the contents are a 45 kilogram, fifty-centimeter black metallic cube. It has no astral signature or Matrix icon.

**NYPD Host:** The two deckers who would normally be defending the host have been dumpshocked into unconsciousness, leaving the rating 9 host unprotected. There are also several dataterms around the building that are still logged in with access to the system available without hacking it. In addition to the building maglocks being turned off, it looks as if the records of everyone held in the

8: GETlockups have been erased. Other information still exists on the host—of particular interest are entry logs in the impound locker and the camera footage from the lot. If the video footage is checked, the PCs can see three Ancients heading for a Roadmaster van shot down by a second group of six metahumans wearing matte-black body armor and helmets, Two are dragging a large ork along, and the entire group gets in the Roadmaster and drives off. Shortly after being viewed, the video file disappears—it seems that all of the video files from the precinct are rapidly disappearing from the host.

If the runners start looking into the data erasure, they will see that even the entry logs are gone, and it looks as if none of these criminal records have ever existed. They may also catch a glimpse of ghostly translucent figures inside the host, but that’s the only thing out of the ordinary (beside the data disappearing without a trace).
