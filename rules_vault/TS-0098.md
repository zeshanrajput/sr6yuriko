## **Timeline**

**Another powerful tool for both you and the team is to build a timeline of the crime. As you are running the game, it will help you answer the runners’ questions. On their side of the table, constructing one as they gather**

**evidence and testimony will help them fgure out details of their investigation, like checking out an alibi.**

**Note that a common trick of professional criminals is to fudge the timeline. An Awakened killer casts a cold spell on a corpse after shooting them in the head so that the coroner believes the murder happened earlier than it did. When the runners discover the time of death is actually three hours later than expected, then suddenly a lot of ironclad alibis fall apart. It’s a good twist to use if your table is becoming quite thorough in their investigations.**
