## Physical Attribute Boost

Monads can boost their physical capabilities, including attributes and initiative, through the manipulation of their biochemistry using their nanites. These efforts are taxing on the nanites. Glitches on any roll should result in a decrease in NV.

Monads can trade available NV for additional Minor Actions. The NV used in this way temporarily decreases the NV attribute of the Monad.

Using Attribute Boost requires a Minor Action. The monad makes a Nanite Volume test. For each hit, the Monad can increase any Physical Attribute by 1 or add a Minor Action. Multiple hits can be used on the same attribute, and the hits can be distributed over separate attributes as the player desires, even on the same test. The maximum total boost is equal to the Monad’s NV, or the standard +4 maximum attribute boost limit, whichever is lower.

Any increase beyond the racially modified maximum results in a box of physical damage after the boost wears off. These boosts last for a number of rounds equal to the number of hits on the roll. After this time, the Monad temporarily decreases their NV by the total of the boosts, potentially knocking all the nanites offline, for one minute.
