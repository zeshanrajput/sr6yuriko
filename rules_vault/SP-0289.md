## **The Job**

**This mission is open-ended and straightforward: locate the convoy, intercept it, and either destroy or capture the cargo. The complication comes from the fact that its escorted and defended by a Faustian and a team of chimera—a very formidable obstacle. The Evo Johnson is aware of this fact but doesn’t inform the player characters both out of an abundance of operational security for this mission and to keep the price of hiring them as low as possible. He will not willingly reveal this information, as he has been explicitly forbidden to do so.**

**The convoy itself should consist of two vehicles: one with considerable cargo space, such as an Ares Roadmaster, a Maersk Shipyards Wavecutter MPAC, or an Ares Dragon, and one armed escort vehicle such as a Toyota Gopher, Aztechnology Nightrunner, or Ares Venture. All these vehicles are armed with machine gun turret mounts, and the larger cargo vehicle has a missile launcher loaded with AV missiles.**
