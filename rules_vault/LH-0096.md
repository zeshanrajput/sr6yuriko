## Amazonia

Amazonia is one of the largest Awakened Nations making its mark in survival ark construction. Their approach is unique, focusing on building a series of interconnected systems beneath the Tepuis Plateaus. These plateaus, among the oldest strata formations on Earth, offer a promising location. Additionally, Amazonia is constructing another site behind the headwaters of the Amazon River, bordering Peru. The nation’s militias, such as the Green Spears and Piranhas, are leading the security efforts, ensuring the sites remain free of Disian extractions.

The vicinity of Iguazu Falls has become a hotly contested area, with metahumans and metasapients working to establish havens near the falls. Several megacorporate and federal groups based in Argentina have fought to prevent illegal migration into the area. The determination of Awakened groups from Amazonia is evident, however, as they’ve directly invoked multiple great spirits to counter these actions.



The Great Anaconda M’boi is the primary coordinator of these operations, and aside from Yakut and Asamando, they’re among the very few national powers actively assigning leaders to these plans.
