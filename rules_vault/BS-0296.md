## VITAS-III

- **Vector:** Contact

- **Speed:** 12 hours (8)

- **Power:** 9

- **Nature:** Viral

- **Effect (Ongoing):** The newest variant of one of the deadliest viral plagues ever to strike metahumanity. The course of the disease lasts for 2D6 days, and the infected metahuman gains the Nauseated and Fatigued I statuses. On each day, the individual resists (Power)S damage, and any unresisted damage cannot be healed until the infection has run its course. There is no known antiviral for VITAS-III.

- **Effect (Final):** On a glitch or critical glitch, the individual suffers (Power)P damage.
