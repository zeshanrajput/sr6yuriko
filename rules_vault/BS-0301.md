## Nanotech Tools

**Nanoscanner:** During the height of CFD, several types of nanite scanners were developed. These are usually advanced millimeter-wave cyberware scanners augmented by a combination of radio frequency, infrared, and vision magnification to detect subcutaneous nanite indicators at a one-meter distance. Nanoscanners are also capable of detailed scans of tissue samples and can test blood, saliva, or other biological material for nanites.

To make a scan, the nanoscanner rolls its rating (1–9) once for each active colony present. A surface scan made from one meter away has a base threshold of 2. A tissue sample inserted into the scanner has a base threshold of 1. The base threshold is for a nanite volume of 5 or 6 and is modified as follows: a nanite volume of 3 or 4 increases the threshold by +1, and a nanite volume of 1 or 2 increases the threshold +2. Either scan takes ten seconds.
