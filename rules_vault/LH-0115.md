## Saeder-Krupp: Only The Dragon Knows

Something may be going on with S-K, and the problem is we don’t know why. Granted, there’s a lot of drek going down in S-K’s neck of the woods, and we’ll leave everything happening in the Allied German States for people better qualified to talk about _that_ situation.

But here’s what we do know. Despite what appeared to be an alliance with the Disians, S-K has quietly distanced themselves from that particular partnership, similar to Shiawase. But unlike that corp, S-K is doing it with less internal strife. There were never any indications, reports, or even rumors that the Disians managed to gain much (if any) ground in terms of corporate infiltration.

- Not entirely correct. There were some indications that the Disians managed to get some infiltrators into Lowfyr’s back yard. But it was nowhere near the numbers of say Wuxing or MCT. Combine this with other rumors that these individuals were in fact allowed in, and it seems like something Gold Scale would do if only to keep tabs on those who were trying to keep tabs on him.

- Thorn
