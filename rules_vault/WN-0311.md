## Buttercup

The free spirit Buttercup has lived through many versions of Evo. While she supports the direction of the corporation, she has her own agenda—one that is much older than Evo itself. Buttercup is interested in uncovering the role MCT is playing in opening pathways to threats from other metaplanes.

She appears as a pale-faced Japanese girl in her late teens with long black hair, wearing a kimono.
