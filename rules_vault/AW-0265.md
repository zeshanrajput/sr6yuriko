## Why Battle?

This is a question I’ve heard a great many times from students, as well as from colleagues within the faculty. The answer is simple: If you’re the kind of person who enjoys a good scrap, this is like a dream vacation resort!

**>** Assuming you don’t mind sweating like a pig. **>** Paladin

Yes, it’s hot and not very pleasant, but once you get into a fight, you’ll not need to worry about it. Assuming you don’t keel over after you’re done. We also aren’t the only people who have recognized the benefits of training on the Plane of Battle, even if you are shadow sparring or working on forms alone. The elder minotoads have started to loiter around the caves visitors tend to appear in front of, so you may well be able to bargain for some instruction if their styles of combat meet your needs.

- I would urge against bringing sugar or sugary snacks to the Plane of Battle to use for barter. Some of the elders are beginning to get leery of it after a couple of minotoad tribes were wiped out because they were all essentially sugar drunk. Sweet things work great, though, as long as they’re not made with sugar.
