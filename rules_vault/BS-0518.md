## Shock Module

This module consists of a shielded exterior frame that delivers a powerful shock to anyone the critter comes into contact with. Don’t pet it.

**Game Information:** The shock module damages anything that touches or bumps into the critter, providing an effective deterrent to close combat attacks. A critter with a critter comm augmentation can be trained to attack using the shock module in place of its natural weapons. Touching, grappling, and even making close combat attacks with melee weapons causes the shock module to discharge a powerful burst of electricity, dealing Stun damage equal to its rating. It is capable of delivering up to three shocks before it must be recharged. Recharging the shock module takes one minute when plugged into a power source.

The critter does not suffer damage from its own shock module, but it is not immune to the shock modules of other critters.
