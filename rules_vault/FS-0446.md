## Close-Quarters Firearms

When using a rifle, shotgun, or submachine gun in melee combat, that firearm may count as a Clubs weapon for the purposes of martial arts techniques. At the gamemaster’s discretion, striking an opponent with an un-reinforced weapon may damage or even break the weapon. **Category:** Ranged
