## Ai He Feng

## (Intelligence Gatherer, Financial Auditor, formerly Yellow Lotus Triad)

A man who should be of higher rank than he is, though does not seem to wish it. He is devoutly loyal to all the Triads and was first brought into the Yellow Lotus when he was a child. He was noticed by his Triad elders, especially the incense masters, for his ability to associate geometry and mathematics with language and pursuits of knowledge. At the age of nineteen, as his talents began to express to the point of trainability, he went on a spiritual journey. When he returned to Hong Kong at the age of twenty-one, he was fully trained, incredibly skillful as a mathematician, and initiated in what he refers to as “the Way of Coins.” But never let his quiet demeanor make you feel he is incapable of violence. He is incredibly dangerous, not just in his financial wizardry but in his ability to interrogate people.
