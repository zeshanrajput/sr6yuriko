## **// MEGALODON //**

**One of the most disconcerting facets of the megalodon is just how diffcult it can be to kill one. They are exceptionally hardy creatures, often requiring massive trauma to the brain stem to stop them. They also heal at an exceptional rate and seem to be resilient to most person-portable weapon systems as well as non-lethal shark repellants (both chemical and technological). This makes megalodons seem much more malicious that they truly are, as they rarely break off a chase once they’ve begun one. Megalodon watches on pleasure beaches are crucial; do them on the water, and metahumans and their small boats will be swallowed whole by these majestic creatures.**
