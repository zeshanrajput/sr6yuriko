## Cyberware Martial Arts Techniques

## Arm Bar Breaker

A cyberlimb uses actuators made of titanium, plasteel, or similarly durable substances. A martial artist may use this technique to unlock an option unavailable to grapplers with organic tendons: simply powering through a joint lock. This technique lets the user avoid the –4 dice penalty for attacks made while being grappled or to resist being restrained (p. 111, _SR6_ ). **Category:** Grappling, requires at least one full cyberlimb.
