## Vehicle Empathy (Pilot Specialization)

You seem to understand vehicles better than most people and can coax a little more performance through physical contact.

- **Cost:** 4 or 8 Karma

- 6: 2+ hours, but you found a new little eatery.

If both dice on the initial roll are 1s, you might have wandered into enemy/forbidden territory, and you’ll face significant hostility as you try to get out. Alternately, for simplicity’s sake, the gamemaster can just double the time rolled on the above table.
