## Weapons on the Metaplanes

“But what about my guns?” is the question that I expect you’re wanting me to answer next. Well, I’ve got good news and bad news. The good news is that on most metaplanes, your guns are going to function just like they would IRL. The bad news is that many spirits are highly resistant to most mundane weapons, including bullets and close-combat weapons. This means that without access to some sort of mojo, you’re going to be at a severe disadvantage in a fight against spirits. That said, I’ve seen people take down spirits with guns plenty of times—if it weren’t possible, we’d all be insect spirit hosts by now. You just have to hit them hard enough. Don’t expect to one-shot something nasty, even if you brought a Panther cannon.

The Plane of Faerie is going to do weird drek to your tech, and guns don’t get off easy here. Any firearms get turned into crossbows, blows, blowguns, and fancy-looking slingshots. The good news is that your skills and training with your firearms translates into an ability to use their transformed equivalent. And there’s more good news, because they don’t seem to have any trouble damaging spirits. Maybe the magical transformation makes the weapons themselves magical? I couldn’t say for sure, but the effect doesn’t last once you’ve left the place. Guns transform back to their original form the moment you’ve left. This seems to be the case on other metaplanes that cause equipment to be translated—the immunity that spirits have against normal weapons doesn’t help them when the metaplane adapts your gear. I’ve heard that there is a spirit named Gunsmith that offers to modify firearms so that they work better against spirits, and that they operate a boat on the Hudson Valley river. The catch (in addition to the high price they charge) is that once your weapon has been modified, it will disintegrate into dust if you bring it back to Earth with you.
