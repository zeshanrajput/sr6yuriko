## Tesla Coil

After Ares introduced the Scylla, it didn’t take long for other weapons manufacturers to reverse-engineer their marvel of directed lightning. Although the term “tesla coil” isn’t strictly accurate, it’s stuck as a brand-agnostic description of the cyber implant version of this weapon. A tesla coil projects electrical energy in a spray attack, much like a flamethrower. They prodigiously consume energy and require battery packs; the user’s natural bioelectrical current is nowhere near sufficient to power this weapon. A tesla coil is not designed to moderate its consumption of battery charges. Once a battery is drained by a tesla coil, it cannot be recharged.
