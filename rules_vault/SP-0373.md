## **Mana Battery**

**Mana batteries are crystals, typically worked into a piece of wearable jewelry so they can be kept close**



**==> picture [19 x 765] intentionally omitted \<==**

**at hand. They vary in size from small enough to be a ring’s center stone or mounted in an amulet to roughly the size of an open palm. These larger stones may be worked into a vambrace or chest plate.**

**Larger, non-portable versions of mana batteries exist at facilities controlled by Dis. Typically these are huge black granite boxes packed with racks of crystals bound together into a lattice. These devices contain thousands of units of mana and serve as a “refueling” point for the smaller portable storage devices. These large mana batteries have been charged from the building-sized versions on the metaplane of Dis, which contain entire metaplanes worth of mana.**
