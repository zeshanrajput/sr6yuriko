## Rave

- **Vector:** Slot, Wireless

- **Speed:** 1 combat round

- **Duration:** (Rating) combat rounds

- **Power:** 9

- **Effect:** –1 Logic, –1 Intuition, Confused

This ripjack bombards the senses with flashing lights, loud sounds, and other sensory inputs. This also inflicts the Confused status equal to the rating of the ripjack.
