## Air

The easiest way to reach New York undetected is to fly there. The four closest public international airports are Soze-MacArthur on Long Island, Newark in New Jersey, and Hitomi and La Guardia, both located in Queens.

Each of these airports provides air taxi service that can deliver you directly to one of hundreds of helipads throughout the city capable of handling most rotorcraft and small VTOL. Air taxi may also be the best way to move throughout the city undetected. Vehicles are logged while entering and leaving NYC airspace, but most airborne vehicles land at private corporate sites, keeping them off MTA’s radar.
