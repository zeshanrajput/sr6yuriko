## **Channel 12 Breaking News!**

**Good evening, this is D.C. Jimenez with a breaking news report.**

**Sad news in the corporate world as long-time Aztechnology board member Domingo Chavez was killed earlier today when his corporate suborbital appeared to sufer a catastrophic systems failure during its inbound descent. Corporate investigators have not commented as to the cause of the failure but say they will issue a statement once the fight data recorder has been recovered. Meanwhile, Aztechnology President Flavia de la Rosa has stated, “Domingo Chavez was one of the leading voices within Aztechnology. His insight, wisdom, and passion for his work will be missed.” Aztechnology will observe thirty days of mourning followed by memorial services.**
