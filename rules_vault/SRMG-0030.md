## **Can I carry my character over from** _**SRM**_ **2081?**

Yes. The follow up question is probably what has your character been doing for the last year. They’ve been living and just making do, but it looks like there are a bunch of hot jobs down in the Crescent City. Don’t worry about lifestyle costs during that time period. Any qualities or benefits that accrue during downtime do not accrue during this time.

Also note that if you’re bringing your character over, you may want to consider asking for increased difficulty at the table (see **Upping the Difficulty of Missions** ).

Any Reputation earned for the 2081 series of Missions does not carry forward to New Orleans. This should be denoted on your character sheet as Seattle Reputation. Reputation specific rules can be found in the **Sixth World Companion** section.
