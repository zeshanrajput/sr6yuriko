## Harley-Davidson Scorpion

Big, bad, and built tough. The typical heavy-bodied, chromed road hog, with some armor and a whole lot of attitude. Usually seen on the streets under go-gangers, the Scorpion is still quite popular with the regular population thanks to the reputation of Harley-Davidson.
