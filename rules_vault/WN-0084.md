## Matrix Assets Inc.

Much of the ownership of this organization is shrouded in cutouts. What’s clear is they are putting up a significant challenge to Vector Matrix Services, the localized Shiawase subsidiary responsible for handling the day-to-day security operations of many of NYCs small businesses. The corporation converts all its profits into real estate investment capital, which it then funnels through CitiGroup. There isn’t a real end goal for the organization other than to gain market share and a larger physical footprint within the city.

- From what scraps of information I’ve found hiding in dark corners, Null Sect owns this organization. Steer clear of runs coming from them. There is no telling what kind of mess they’ll pull you into.

- Perri
