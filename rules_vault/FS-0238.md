## Personalized Grip

Modifying a weapon to one’s personal specs is one thing, but personalized grip takes that idea one step farther. Available for melee weaponry and firearms, a personalized grip fits the weapon specifically for a particular user’s hands, making it more effective for them to use. The downside to this is that any weapon with a personalized grip modification is more difficult for anyone else to use.

- Almost makes me wonder why everyone doesn’t do this with all of their weapons.

- Treadle

- It’s a great weapon modification—right up until someone on your team needs to borrow it. For solo use, go for it. But it’s actually a bit of a liability for a team.
