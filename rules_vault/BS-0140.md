## Cyberfinger

Someone got your finger? No need to replace the entire hand when you only need to restore a single digit. Alternatively, a cyberfinger can be incorporated into an existing cyberlimb at a capacity cost of [1]. Cyberfingers do not have capacity that can be freely used, but if you want more than minimal functionality, you can incorporate one of the following features:

**Finger gun:** No longer just a hand gesture! A finger gun functions as an implanted hold-out pistol (p. 290, _SR6_ ). The pistol must be purchased separately.

**Finger key:** The memory-metal fingertip can reshape itself into pre-recorded key configurations or into lockpicking tools for Engineering (Lockpicking) tests.

**Finger lighter:** Available in electric arc or classic butane cartridge models.

**Frag finger:** Why flip them off? Pull your finger off and give them a proper “frag you.” This finger incorporates a HE grenade (p. 263, _SR6_ ), but its DV is reduced by 4 at all ranges.

**Torch tip** : A simple, small flashlight. Also available in black light, low light, or thermographic models.
