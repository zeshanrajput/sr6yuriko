## Arsenic

- **Vector:** Ingestion

- **Speed:** 1D6 hours

- **Duration:** 2D6 hours

- **Power:** 10

- **Effect:** Stun damage, Physical damage, Nauseated, Poisoned

Arsenic is a naturally occurring element that can be found in soil, water, food, and more. However, in large quantities, arsenic is poisonous. It acts as a cumulative poison, taking one week for a micro-dose to leave the system of the victim. A single micro-dose is equal to Power 1. Each micro-dose is cumulative and increases the Power by 1 if the subsequent dose is delivered before the previous micro-dose leaves the system. Each does also starts the clock over again. Those poisoned by micro-doses need to resist (Power)S damage if the current Power is less than 6, or resist (Power)P damage if the Power is 6 or greater. Victims of arsenic poisoning experience the Nauseated status if they fail a Damage Resistance test and cannot spend Edge on tactile Perception tests.



Arsenic can also be delivered as a single dose. The victim needs to resist 10P damage and gains the Poisoned # status equal to the amount of unresisted damage they take.
