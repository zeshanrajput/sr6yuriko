## Making Contact

The missions move the runners across the city, creating opportunities to revisit locations and reconnect with allies and enemies they’ve developed up to this point. Any of the runners’ fixers or Johnsons may contact them to get them involved in this day’s events. The first contact will come directly from Bailey West.

Keep the action flowing. At the conclusion of a mission—or even during one—have their commlink buzz, offering them the next job. This puts them in control of their interests. They get to decide what jobs they want or are able to take. The runs are organized into three time slots. They either take place before, during, or after the gala.
