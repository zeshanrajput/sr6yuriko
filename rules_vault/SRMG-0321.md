## **How do I value the cyberlimbs on a drone or vehicle?**

While the anthroform cyberlimbs are said to be treated under the same rules as the cyberlimbs for metahumans, this only extends on how to use them when determining strength, agility, and any specific capacity uses. This does not extend to the price of the limbs. Drones with this specific modification cannot be sold separately or modified. There currently aren’t rules to specify the capacity of the drone’s anthroform cyberlimbs and the price of the limbs does not line up with the price of the drone.
