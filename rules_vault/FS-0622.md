## Corner Dealer

## (Professional Rating 2)

Dressed in très chic fashion, the corner dealer is the lowest on the Cutter ladder. They act tough, try to prove themselves with slick “business” moves, and work hard to make every contact and connection they can in order to move up the organizational ladder. The problem is they often overstep the wrong folks, build up favors with the wrong people, and usually end up as the chum for the sharks they are swimming with.

Just below the leaders and masters of the gang are the masters of the flame. The descriptive phrase “a fire in their eyes” is usually literal with this lot. Whether cyber, bio, AR, arcane, or otherwise, virtually every Halloweener at this rank has glowing or burning eyes.
