## Hook

Cat messages the team and invites them to have lunch with her at the Waldorf-Astoria’s Bear Lodge. The reservation has “private lunch, party of seven,” and the end of the message says “dress appropriately.” Upon arriving at the art deco luxury hotel, the team is escorted to a private dining room in its Amerindian-themed restaurant. Cat and Spyder are already there. After a plate of superbly prepared antelope, traditionally spiced vegetables, and a few drinks, Cat is willing to talk about the job. She relates that Mr. Seven has sorted through all the data from the Aztechnology run and it believes the “Airlock” facility is indeed the portal to Dis. It was also able to pin down a location, a cave located under the Ares-controlled Governors Island. Aztechnology is using the submarines to access an underwater entrance to the cave system, but Mr. Seven wants the team to scout the island and see if there is another way into the cavern that bypasses Aztechnology security at the submarine dock. Spyder has been busy running stealth drones with LIDAR scanners over the island, looking for any natural cave entrances. He pulls up an AR map with four locations marked out as worth investigating.



Cat’s initial offer is 6,000 nuyen per team member to scout the island and find a way into the portal cavern. If the team fails to find a way in from the surface, they will need to scout the Aztechnology submarine dock to see how strong security is at that entrance.
