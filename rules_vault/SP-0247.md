## **Hotspots**

**The Evo cybermancy facility itself is a small campus surrounded by a tall fence. Landscaping drones cut several meters of dense foliage in front of the fence, making it more diffcult to sneak up to and climb over. The lawn within the fence is well maintained, and likewise provides no cover.**

**The center of the facility is a single three-story building that includes housing for the personnel who work here (they are not permitted to come and go on a regular basis) as well as a variety of recreational facilities. Located next to the main building is a hangar and garage large enough to house up to two Banshee-sized vector-thrust aircraft and a half-dozen trucks. A smaller outbuilding functions as a drone maintenance, repair, and deployment post with a short guard tower that extends from its roof. There’s also an outdoor recreational area with a tennis court and basketball hoops, but it is rarely used.**

**Primary work is done within the extensive basement located underneath the main building and hangar/garage. The basement is accessible via a stairwell and elevator from the main building, and via stairs and a cargo elevator located in the hangar/garage. There are two sub-levels: the frst consists of recovery rooms, a well-stocked medical clinic, a security monitoring station, and a dozen heavily reinforced holding cells. The deeper sub-level consists of two large sterile clean rooms, each equipped with magical lodges, blood magic implements, and delta cyberware clinics.**

**Despite the efforts of initiates with cleansing metamagics, the taint of blood magic is still present within the main building and garage and is obvious to any astral observer exploring the basement. The main building and frst sub-level are protected by separate wards, while the magic lodges provide additional wards that protect the clean rooms where the cybermantic rituals are performed. In addition**



**to the wards, a security magician specializing in conjuring provides spirits to patrol above ground (one Force 6 beast spirit and one Force 4 spirit of air). These spirits dislike the taint of blood magic and are reluctant to enter the buildings, so they’re used to patrol the facility’s exterior grounds. The spirit of air watches from above, while the beast spirit patrols along the fence, searching for signs of intruders.**

**Physical security is mostly provided by drones. Two security riggers are assigned here; one is on duty at all times. A single LDSD-64 Condor drone (p. 108,** _**Double Clutch**_ **) hovers a kilometer above the facility, and its mini drone carriers contain two Dragonfy-Alpha drones (p. 105,** _**Double Clutch**_ **) which can be deployed to intercept and destroy unauthorized recon drones. Closer to the ground, a dozen Evo “Splatter”-series drones (p. 104,** _**Double Clutch**_ **) patrol the immediate airspace; each is loaded with a dose of DMSO/Narcoject ( Duration: (6 – Body), Power: 15, Effect: Stun damage) that can be deployed against intruders.**

**The drone maintenance building stores four Steel Lynx combat drones (p. 302,** _**SR6**_ **), each modifed with mini drone carriers that are designed to deploy Evo Roller-Bomb (p. 92,** _**Double Clutch**_ **) drones equipped with fragmentation grenades. Also on standby are four Ares Black Sky drones (p. 302,** _**SR6**_ **) that can be launched to deal with aerial threats or combat drones, or stage airstrikes against approaching intruders. These and four Steel Lynx drones are kept in reserve and only deployed during security drills or in an emergency.**

**Facility maintenance and landscaping are performed by Evo Proletarian drones that double as surveillance units. They’re obvious and easily discounted as simple utility drones. The RCC controlling these drones is attached to a security console located in sub-level 1, which is directly wired to a retrans unit in the drone building.**

**Only twelve security guards and two supervisors are kept onsite on rotating twelve-hour shifts. Two are always on duty in the security monitoring station on sublevel 1, keeping an eye on cameras and other sensors. Another two guards are stationed in the guard tower that extends from the top of the drone building. The other two active guards are on patrol duty. These guards drive around in an armed and armored Toyota Gopher, sometimes within the fence, but usually on patrol in the surrounding area—always within two kilometers of the base, watching for suspicious activity nearby.**

**Matrix security here is formidable. Everything is protected under a local rating 6 host that is closely watched over by a small team of elite security spiders. There is at least one spider on duty watching over the host for signs of intrusion, with one on standby ready to join the defense within seconds, and two off-duty but onsite that can be called in for backup within one minute.**
