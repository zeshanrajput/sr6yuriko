## **GMT Dermal Plating**

**One of the frst uses of grey manatech devised for cybertechnology—and one of the most effective—has been to integrate it into the materials used for dermal plating. This does not reduce the physical effectiveness of dermal plating, and its impact on the metahuman body appears to be minimal. Upgrade kits allow individuals with pre-existing dermal plating to enhance their augmentation with GMT.**
