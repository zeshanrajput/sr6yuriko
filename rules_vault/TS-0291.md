## **First Responder**

**Uses: frst aid, hazardous environments, onsite counselor**

**Places to Meet: bar, crash sites, station Similar Contacts : beat cop, frefghter, paramedic**

**While not a law enforcement offcer, frst responders often work hand-in-hand with them (or at least should) and are sometimes part of specialized law enforcement units such as tactical teams. Or they’re kept on standby while high-threat warrants are served. Normally certifed paramedics, emergency med-techs, and even frefghters, frst responders serve as the front line of emergency services, swiftly and adeptly addressing a wide range of critical situations. Whether it’s providing immediate assistance to offcers or civilians, managing crisis scenarios, or ensuring client and customer safety, the role of a frst responder is marked by courage, quick thinking, and a commitment to helping those in need. Their diverse skill set, encompassing medical expertise, rescue operations, and crisis management, makes them integral to the well-being of communities. Establishing a connection**
