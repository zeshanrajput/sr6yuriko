## Clean Living

You grew up in the heart of the breadbasket and always ate the best food, and it made you nice and healthy, just like your parents promised.

- **Cost:** 10 Karma

- **Game Effect:** You can increase your Body attribute by 1 immediately upon selecting this quality, with no Karma cost, but you must increase all Lifestyle costs by 500 nuyen per month. Failure to keep up the cost of your food Lifestyle or extended periods in a place without quality food results in a loss of the 2 Body points (minimum 1) until your diet can be fixed or the food Lifestyle can be paid. This does not increase your maximum Body attribute.
