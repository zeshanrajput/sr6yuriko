## Shibata Consulting

The 29th floor is still the same as in mission 2 (p. 45), but if the run is attempted during the workday it will be full of employees. The rating 6 framework host is still accessible from any desktop dataterm, and it still hides a rating 8 foundation host sculpted as a parkland of koi ponds and pagoda-style Shinto shrines—see mission 2 for the host profiles. The Foundation portal is a decorative stone arch at the center of an elaborate rose garden.
