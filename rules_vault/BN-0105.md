## Renraku Pe lican 2 Delivery Drone

(Small Vectored Thrust Drone)

The Pelican delivery drone has been around for decades. The newest model has tilt rotors and moves much faster than the original model when in fl ight mode. In hover mode, these things can land a delivery of noodles right in your bowl. Even at top speed, these little guys can thread a needle. Flight mode is for wide open spaces. sacrifi cing pinpoint maneuverability for speed so it can get from point A to point B in a hurry. The engines and battery are a bit more than you’d expect—which helps carry the cargo—and the transition mechanisms take up quite a bit of room on the chassis. All of that leaves very little room for mods. But this answers the age old question of “how do I carry stuff without pockets?”
