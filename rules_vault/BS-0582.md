## Critters for Sale

Recent advances in critter augmentation and genetic engineering have sparked something akin


to an arms race among the megacorporations to claim the largest slice of market share. Every one of them has been experimenting with various critters and augmentations to create the most profitable options, some of which are designed for security and warfare markets, others for civilian consumers. There are countless possible combinations, and each mega has many more than I have room to show you here, so just consider this to be a sampleof the many different strange techniques that use mundane and Awakened animals as raw material from which to design a lucrative masterpiece.
