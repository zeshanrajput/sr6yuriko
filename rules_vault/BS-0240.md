## Biosenses

## Awareness

There are two versions of this modification. They both can be taken by one person; the difference is in the type and locations of the modifications.

The flesh around the skull is implanted with a variety of new receptors and a modification to the cerebellum. This doesn’t give the metahuman the ability to see 360 degrees, but they are able to feel slight changes in the environment—air currents, temperature, even some electromagnetic fields— then predict movement and potential dangers.

**Basic** : You have a sixth sense of danger, instinctively knowing when it’s nearby and preparing for it. Gain an Edge before making Surprise tests.

**Tactical** : This bioware gives you the ability to predict and avoid harm. Gain a +1 dice pool bonus per rating on any defensive tests. This bonus also applies to any surprise tests (p. 108, _SR6_ ).
