## **Professional Rating 3: Law Enforcement/ Security rank and file**

**This group represents the majority of those in law enforcement. You can call them an inexperienced patrol offcer, a new corporate spider, or maybe a seasoned armed security guard. Whatever the title, they’ve had enough training or real-world experience to give them the mindset needed to advance within their law enforcement service or security provider. Now they only need a little more heat and pressure to see if they harden and become stronger or break like so many others.**
