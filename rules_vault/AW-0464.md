## Wastes of Miggon Encounters Table

When rolling for an encounter in Hubris, roll 1D6 on the Wastes of Miggon Encounters Table. In the wilderness of Miggon, roll 1D6 + 2. In the power plant and its surrounding town, roll 1D6 + 4. In the glass desert, roll 1D6 + 6. As the gamemaster, you should create or choose specific encounters that relate to the key events or themes.

**Skills:** Astral 5, Athletics 7, Close Combat 4 (Bite +2), Influence 4 (Intimidation +2) Outdoors 5, Perception 2, Stealth 5

**Powers:** Armor 4, Dual Natured, Elemental Attack (Fire), Energy Aura (Fire), Enhanced Sense (Hearing, Low-Light Vision, Smell), Fear, Immunity to Fire, Natural Weapon (Bite), Regeneration
