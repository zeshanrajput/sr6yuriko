## **What is Work for the Man/Work for the People?**

Once between each _Missions_ adventure you may choose to **Work for The Man** or **Work for The People** . This will allow you to either trade 1 Karma for 2,000 nuyen, or to trade 2,000 nuyen for 1 Karma. You may trade away or gain a maximum of 1 Karma each time you do this, and you can only do this once between any given SRM, CMP, or PM.
