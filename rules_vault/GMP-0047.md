## **Using a Matrix Search** 

The advantage of a matrix search is that it costs nothing but time, however sometimes that can be the biggest disadvantage. Matrix searches have their own column in the Legwork Block and since this is an extended test the runners can keep at it until they find out everything there is to be found on the Matrix. Remember however, not everything “can” be found on the matrix, some information can still only be learned from contacts or known from personal experience.
