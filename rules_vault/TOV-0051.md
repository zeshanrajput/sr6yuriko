## G hede

Death and sex are the domain of the Ghede family. This is also seen as part of the cycle of life and death—but while held with reverence, this group is often seen as capricious, loud, and boisterous. They are part of a family instead of nanchon since their nation was taken away—but that is a long story. This family includes all the aspects of Ghede. Spells associated with Ghede are Combat, Illusion, and Health.
