## **The Leif-Killers**

**The Leif-Killers were formed by survivors of the Winternight betrayal, and their goal is to hunt down and kill every last one of them. For the most part, they are powerful shamans and adepts with a good support base. They are often seen in northern Europe, and they are also strong believers of the proverb, “the enemy of my enemy is my friend” . They are generally willing to provide aid and will even coordinate with you if you share this common goal. They’ve since broadened their scope and now also target members of Ex Pacis.**
