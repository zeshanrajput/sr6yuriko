## Pulmonary Arcane Arrester

Evo surprised everyone when they recently brought this product to market. Other corps were quick to start rumors that this product came about due to extensive testing of captives from the gnome and fomorian communities. Evo was quick to point out the similarities between its product and magical resistance creators.

The very small gland is implanted on the side of the heart just outside the pulmonary artery. As the heart pumps, the gland releases a small amount of a magically active chemical. It flows throughout the body creating what some call a very imperfect personal ward. Whatever it does, it affects magic cast on the metahuman.

You gain a bonus Edge for use on any Magic Resistance test; if you do not use it on that test, that point goes away. When a Health spell is cast on you, treat your Essence as if it were 2 points lower than it is. This number can never be lower than zero.
