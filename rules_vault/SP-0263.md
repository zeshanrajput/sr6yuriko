## **Hotspots**

**The MCT regional HQ where Hallow lives represents an insurmountable security situation. It is a Zero Zone where the public isn’t allowed, and intruders are shot on sight with no questions asked. The target’s destination is a future Harvester site, though it’ll be some weeks (or months) before it is operational. Hallow has been sent by her patron to check on the site’s progress, as it’s behind schedule. The harvesting site is also a Zero Zone and is defended better than a military installation.**

**The day of the trip, a large thunderstorm will move in, bringing with it lightning, thunder, and strong winds that make air travel extremely dangerous (+3 threshold to all Pilot tests) and will create signifcant interference (+5 Matrix Noise or more). Since the players know when the convoy is departing, the convoy makeup, and the route they must take, they may elect to set up an ambush. The players may describe the terrain they select within reason and make any necessary preparations that wouldn’t attract too much attention . The only solid requirement for the location is a two-lane highway, as that is the road the convoy is taking.**
