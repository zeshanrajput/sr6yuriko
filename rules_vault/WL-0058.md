## **Critter Corner: Ground Wolf**

**Welcome to Critter Corner. I’m Mr. Johnson, here to bring you the dirt on a unique paracritter: the ground wolf. Like its mundane predecessor, the ground wolf isn’t a canine, but rather an Awakened prairie dog. Unlike the relatively meek prairie dog, ground wolves are vicious predators that grow to be as large as a metahuman. Fortunately, they don’t typically attack metahumans, though there have been cases of attacks against people who have strayed too close to their dens. They do present a much more signifcant danger to livestock—even a small number of ground wolves can rapidly ravage a herd.**

**Ground wolves are made all the more dangerous by their Awakened powers. They are dual natured, can sense movement through vibrations in the ground, and appear to be able to pass through earth at full speed without needing to tunnel— though they are excellent at tunneling as well. These critters appear to be masters of the very earth, as they are able to cause it to reach up and engulf their prey.**
