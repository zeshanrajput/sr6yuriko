## Target Fixation

You are super-aware of who is coming after you in a fight—which limits your overall situational awareness and sometimes makes you forget who else is out there.

- **Bonus:** 8 Karma

- **Game Effect:** If you attack, you may not gain or spend Edge on Defense tests except against the target of your attack for the remainder of this combat round.
