## **Mitsuhama**

**All the knowledge you have acquired, yet you have truly learned nothing. Instead, you only take what others have built, to supplant those who have made the way for you easier when you could just as easily made the kingdom on your own! Keep looking down on us all, from your highest spot. But know that you can only remain vigilant for so long, that you cannot always see what stalks you from behind, that as assuredly as death comes for all lesser beings, so too will it come to claim you as well.**

**Nothing, not even your most powerful allies and patrons, can stop that.**
