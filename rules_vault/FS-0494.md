## Yea

## Dead People Don’t Wake Up

Overwhelmingly heard in the context of someone explaining why they blew that poor corpsec guard’s head off instead of just knocking them out. Having an enemy you thought you neutralized shake off their stupor and block your planned escape route is not fun, and sometimes leads to merciful souls getting shot in the back for their trouble.
