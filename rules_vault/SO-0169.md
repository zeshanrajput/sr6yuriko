## Salting Plot Hooks

the mark thinks it’s worth. Gold may have value anywhere, but the right somewhere is going to sell for much better. Maybe the market is high for gold right now, or oil, or reagents. Maybe local laws prohibit a massive oil derrick being set up, but subterranean mining is no problem. And who’s to say one or more of your team isn’t spreading the hype, posing as a rival bidder or speculator making promises to buy the land for a better deal and take on any additional expenses. Or they’re building rumors about the incoming demand for said resource based on events elsewhere in the world. Really pump that excitement.
