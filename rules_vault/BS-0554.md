## Critter Awakening

This causes the critter to become Awakened, and it gains a Magic attribute equal to the rating of its Logic attribute. Unless the critter is further enhanced to receive Awakened critter powers, this has no effect other than increasing the difficulty of training tests—add the Magic attribute to the critter’s dice pool on an Opposed training test. If the target critter already has a Magic attribute, this genetic augmentation can be used to raise its Magic attribute to a maximum of 6. The rating of this augmentation is equal to the desired Magic attribute.
