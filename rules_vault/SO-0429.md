## Dream Work

Being part of a team means working well with others. You’re talented in coordinating your efforts with others, amplifying their abilities while expanding your own usefulness.

**Cost:** 5 Karma

**Game Effect:** When you assist another character in a Teamwork test, increase the amount of dice they can potentially gain from your assistance (normally limited by their ranks in the skill) by 1. If multiple characters with this quality assist in a Teamwork test, the effects stack up to four higher than the normal maximum. A leader of a Teamwork test must have at least one rank in the skill used in the test to benefit from this assistance. Note that you still need to roll hits to provide the bonus dice, as this quality only increases the max potential dice pool bonus.
