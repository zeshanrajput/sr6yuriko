## examples of rifts

## Tswaing Crater

Located in Pretoria, South Africa, the crater and the area around it produce random astral rifts that can occasionally move through the city. The moving rifts cause forced projection and may leave mundanes stranded in the astral plane, needing the services of the on-call security mages to guide them back to their bodies. The rifts also occasionally generate passageways that can take the unwary mundanes into the elemental metaplanes.
