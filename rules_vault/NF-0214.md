## The Majors

With the addition of NABS and OTQ to the council, every major has a seat at the table. In fact, in direct reflection of the Corporate Court, you can now define a major network as one that’s on the ITCC, much like AAAs make up the Corporate Court. With control over consumer broadcast frequencies, cable access, satellite transmission, and Matrix content, they decide who gets what feed, at least legally. “But what about the indies? Don’t they push rebellious content?” Yeah, corporate-approved rebellion, like buying records from that renegade band that Renraku execs have hyped for months on their college radio stations, then fed into major broadcasts, stuck on MtD, and who have a major clothing line now available at “offbeat” online retailers.
