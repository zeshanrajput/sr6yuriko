## Killing Spree

## Main Location: Köpenick

## Notable Characters: Wjelkami Guerrillas (use Tír Ghosts, p. 209)

In recent years, the Awakened Spreewald swamp has spread further and further along the flow of the rivers Dahme and Spree and is about to enter Berlin-Köpenick. The runners are to travel upstream along the Spree from Köpenick Palace to the town of Beeskow in a small boat for the Berlin administration corporation BERVAG, collecting as many findings and samples as possible in order to assess the current threat. Encounters with critters, spirits, Awakened plants, telesma collectors, scattered units of the Sorbian Wjelkami guerrilla and Sorbian mystics are guaranteed. **Original Message:** Fancy a magical boat

cruise? Apparently, the Awakened swamp of the Spreewald is creeping toward Berlin from the south, and Mr. Schmidt would like to know more precisely how bad the situation is.
