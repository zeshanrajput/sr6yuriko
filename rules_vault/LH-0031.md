## The Disians: On the Defensive

After the Week of Death failed to yield the desired results of allowing the Disians to enact their Final Project unopposed, the Disians have adopted a defensive posture, and in American football terms, they’re running out the clock. Unfortunately for Project Nemesis, we still don’t know exactly how much time we have left. The primary methodology for this plan appears to be fortifying and defending the most important component in their plans: the Harvester Network.
