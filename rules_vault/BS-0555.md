## Awakened Critter Power

This genetic augmentation provides an awakened critter with a magical critter power. Only some critter powers are eligible: Accident, Animal Control, Binding, Concealment (Self-Only), Confusion, Corrosive Spit, Dual-Natured, Elemental Attack, Energy Aura, Fear, Guard, Hardened Mystic Armor, Immunity to Normal Weapons, Mimicry, Movement (Self-Only), Mystic Armor, Noxious Breath, Paralyzing Howl, Paralyzing Touch, Petrification, Psychokinesis, Regeneration, Search, and Weather Control. The following critter powers may not be gained through this genetic augmentation: Astral Form, Compulsion, Engulf, Immunity (anything other than Immunity to Normal Weapons), Infection, Influence, Innate Spell, Materialization, Mindspeech, Mist Form, and Sapience.
