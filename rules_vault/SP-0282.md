## **Faustian Double Cross**

**An MCT Mr. Johnson hires the players to smuggle a shipment across the border to an NAN location. The cargo is several large sealed and warded crates, and the players are under strict orders not to open them. Inside the crates are key components for Disian Harvesters. Along the way, the players will deal with another runner team that’s trying to intercept the shipment. Once they make the delivery (assuming they survive and succeed to do so), the player characters will be double-crossed by MCT as the Disians want to preserve their security and harvest any Awakened PCs. The runners that the player characters got away from earlier (or another runner team hired by the same faction) show up again, giving the players an opportunity to escape.**
