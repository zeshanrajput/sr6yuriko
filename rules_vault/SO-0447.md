## Bad Liar

Whether it’s because you deeply value honesty, or you’re simply inept at deception, you should probably stick to telling the truth (or keep your mouth shut).

**Bonus:** 6 Karma

**Game Effect:** You may not gain or spend Edge on Con skill tests to deceive someone.
