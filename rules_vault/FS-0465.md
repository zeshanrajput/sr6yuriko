## Multiple Opponent Defense

When you’re attacked by multiple attackers inthe same Combat Round, gain +2 to your Defense Rating on all attacks after the first. This bonus is not cumulative; if you’re attacked three times, you get +2 to your Defense Ratings against the second and third attacks, not +2 against the second and +4 against the third. **Category:** Mobility
