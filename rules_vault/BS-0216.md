## Chloroplast

The individual’s epidermis and follicular development have adapted to include chloroplast organelles that supplement the energy needs of the individual through photosynthesis. This gives the character a –1 LP modifier toward their Necessities costs (p. 179, _Sixth World Companion_ ). The character also receives +1 Edge to all Con and Influence tests when in partial or full daylight. They suffer a –2 dice pool modifier to those same tests when in deep shadow or darkness.
