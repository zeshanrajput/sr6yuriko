## Security

Security vehicles are the toys that most of the corporations keep to themselves, but they’re not too hard for us shadowrunners to get our hands on. Security vehicles have Ceramic Plate as their default Foundation, and they usually come equipped with at least Expert electronics. Cost Multiplier: x2
