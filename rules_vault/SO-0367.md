## Attitude

## \[Influence (Etiquette), Major\]:

## 1 Edge per +1 to Social Rating

Sometimes all it takes is the right demeanor and a sense of self confidence—real or feigned. Regardless of what the player characters are wearing, who they’re with, or how little time they’ve had to freshen up, they can still turn heads and demand attention and (possibly) some respect just by projecting their charisma. Make an Influence (Etiquette) + Charisma test. If successful, their Social Rating increases by +1 per Edge they spend for one minute per hit on the skill test. This duration can be extended by one minute per hit by taking the Attitude action again.
