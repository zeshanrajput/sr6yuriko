## The Exploding Boy

- **Vector:** Injection

- **Speed:** 1 hour

- **Duration:** Variable

- **Power:** 8

This drug attempts to suppress a technomancer’s Resonance. When taken, the user makes a Toxin Resistance test using the power of the drug. Any unresisted power reduces the Resonance of the technomancer. If the technomancer still has a Resonance of 1 or more, then the Resonance returns at a rate of 1 per hour afterward. If Resonance is reduced to zero by this drug, the technomancer loses their living persona and becomes mundane. Each week afterward, the technomancer can attempt to resist again, and if they successfully resist, they regain one point of Resonance. Their Resonance then returns at a rate of one point per day. During the time that the technomancer is hobbled, they can spend 2 Edge per round to act as if they have a Resonance of one. A technomancer on this drug is also a little unfocused and loses one die on any action that requires concentration or sustained interest.

This drug has not been around for long and may be in demand for people who have come to the Resonance in a traumatic way or who have a worldview that is incompatible with Resonance. It is also in desired by people who hate technomancers or who want to kidnap technomancers for whatever reason. It’s too early to say what long-term use will do to the technomancer, but it’s probably not good.
