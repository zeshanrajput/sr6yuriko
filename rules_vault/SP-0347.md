## **Slither Chimera**

**Slither chimera can appear as any species of snake. The majority are approximately two meters in length, but larger versions exist (boa and python). All Slither chimeras have heavily modifed tri-part jaws that can operate in one of two ways. First as a normal snake with fangs that can inject a paralyzing venom; second, an extended lower vertical section concealed in the body can hinge open for an attack with serrated teeth capable of causing large rending wounds.**

**Slither chimera are used primarily for covert entry and assassination. Additionally, they excel at general reconnaissance operations inside secure facilities. Their serpentine bodies allow them to traverse sewer pipes, communication conduits, and small openings in walls.**
