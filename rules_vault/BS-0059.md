## Omega Grade

Used/omega-grade implants are not necessarily prior implanted; this grade also refers to brandnew cyberware that is cheaply or shoddily manufactured. Omega grade still has the same x1.1 Essence cost, x 0.5 nuyen cost, and the –1 Availability modification. Additionally, it also imposes the Bad Luck quality (p. 75, _SR6_ ) on the user for any test that involves use of this augmentation, meaning both 1s and 2s count toward glitches on tests where the augmentation adds to a dice pool.

The long-term effects of cut-rate omega-grade bioware are said to include cell mutation and metastasizing tumors spreading through the body. Some runners, though, wonder if the ’ware will allow them to survive long enough to worry about long-term effects.
