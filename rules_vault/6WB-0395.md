## Outdoors

**Specialization:** Navigation, Survival, Tracking, by environment (Woods, Desert, Urban Areas, etc.) **Untrained:** Yes

**Linked Attribute:** Intuition

While staying indoors forever, watching trids and eating WhompSnappers, seems like a good enough life, sometimes you have to go outside. Outdoors is to help deal with those times. Whether you are following the tracks of someone or something, scaring up food, or figuring out the quickest way to your destination, Outdoors is the skill to use. Typically this is a Simple test, Outdoors + Intuition with a threshold set using the Threshold Guidelines on p. 36. Efforts to disguise tracks may set the threshold for Tracking tests.
