## Julie Lawdmore

## Horizon, Los Angeles

Julie Lawdmore is the PR manager for a star act at Horizon known as Two Minds or Kyley Ann Danes. She has been carefully cultivating a stable of country music stars and crafting her P2.0 and Consensus 2.0 scores, which gives her access to the best parties and lets her influence the direction of the Horizon Media Group. But things have recently gone off the rails, and she has found that she isn’t able to push some of her ideas through. If you are looking for Julie, you can set up a meeting by calling her office. Just make sure that you have a plausible reason to meet with her.
