## Doppelgänger

The soldier is no longer one of a kind—there are at least two of them running around. Both of them insist they are the real person, and they become tremendously confused when presented with evidence of their duplicate. All of these individuals have identical DNA, but they manifest different variants on their essential character traits (for example, both may have a certain innate caution, but one has learned to be watchful while in motion, while the other insists on taking things slowly to help them watch out for danger).
