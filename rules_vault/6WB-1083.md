## security rating Z

There is no organized security in a Z zone, mainly because there usually isn’t anything of worth. Cops don’t patrol Z zones and won’t respond to calls to crimes in those areas. Even gangs don’t provide informal security, because these areas are blasted wastelands without anything to protect. They are where the most forgotten parts of the world go to stay lost. That means things like ghouls and paracritters may be found.

**Types of Security Offered:** None **Typical Security Force:** None
