## **Black Tide**

**Vector: Injection Speed: Immediate+ Duration: 1 x 1D6 minutes**

**Power: 15**

**Ef ect: +1 Body, +1 Agility, +1 Willpower, +2D6 Initiative, High Pain Tolerance (p. 72, SR6 )**

**Just as Jazz has become essentially obsolete, so has Kamikaze. It’s no longer the drug of choice for the jacked-up crazed killers; now it’s a seasoning for soccer moms’ bagels. Black Tide is a next-gen combat stimulant that takes efect almost before the user takes it, thus possibly putting the user on a level playing feld with an augmented perp.**

**When Black Tide wears of, the user crashes and sufers -1 Reaction, -1 Willpower, and the Fatigued II status for twice the duration of the drug’s efect. They also sufer 6 Stun damage (unresisted).**

**So far, Black Tide does not seem as destructive to users as Kamikaze has always been. But the trial hasn’t even gone on half a year yet, so it’s way too soon to tell.**
