## Optional Rule

## Using ’Ware to Increase Essence

Note that filling a hole only applies to Essence loss that occurs due to the drifting apart of the aura and the body. This does not apply to recovering Essence for existing implants (see Optional Rules: Essence Loss from Physical, Emotional, or Magical Trauma).

## **If a runner has lost Essence in this way, there are only a limited number of implants that will restore Essence, specifically:**

- Purely cosmetic implants

- Replacement limbs

- Implants that are “fixed” in nature; for example, Cosmetic Alteration from the Cyberware chapter (p. 27) allows alteration of facial features in real-time, and would not be eligible due to its changeable nature

- Geneware or genetic therapy
