## **Can I load a single grenade with all the spirit allergies and weaknesses?**

No. In order to get sufficient quantities to affect the spirit, a gas grenade requires a full twenty doses of the allergen, or it dissipates too much. A spirit exposed to an allergen loses its Immunity to Natural Weapons. And because these things don’t really have a price:

| Grenade                  | Availability | Cost  |
| ------------------------ | ------------ | ----- |
| Grenade of ferrous metal | 4            | 250 ¥ |
| Grenade of gold          | 5            | 700 ¥ |
| Grenade of silver        | 5            | 500 ¥ |
| Grenade of wood          | 4            | 250 ¥ |
