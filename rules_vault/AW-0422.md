## alchemical potency

Alchemical preparations of any kind can be purchased from the Yasmal tribe. They cost 1 unit of scrap (or 2 bones) per point of Potency, up to a maximum Potency of 6. As long as the preparation remains within the Wastes of Miggon, its Potency does not degrade, but it will lose one point of Potency per minute if taken anywhere else. The Magic rating of the enchanter is always 6. The trigger for the preparation must either be contact or time.
