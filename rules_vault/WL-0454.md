## **Sonic Attacks in Water**

**Everyone knows that sound travels better through certain mediums than others, with water being better than air. When used underwater, increase the DV of sonic attacks by half (rounded down, minimum addition of 1) and increase the Attack Rating by 2. This can even give the attack additional range, if appropriate. Sonic attacks also have a minor area efect: anything within Near range (3m) of the target of the attack receives statuses as if hit by the attack, but the target does not need to roll Damage Resistance. Should you be hit by a sonic attack in water, you receive the Deafened I and Dazed statuses just for being hit. If you take any damage from the attack, you gain the Deafened II, Dazed, and Stilled statuses, though Stilled becomes Hobbled at the end of the following combat round.**
