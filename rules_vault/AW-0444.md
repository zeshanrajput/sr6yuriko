## bone trader gnawer

You begin to sense the background radiation and strange electromagnetic fields, and their chaotic fluctuations make it difficult to concentrate.

**Game Effect:** All Edge boosts and actions cost twice as many points of Edge on any test that uses Resonance. If you gain a second Resonance mutation, your fading DV is increased by one. If you have the Dissonant technomancer quality, you are not affected by Resonance mutations.
