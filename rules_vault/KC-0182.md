## The Word is Out

## Posted by: Mr. Bonds

I received a copy of Rikki’s notes and have been getting my grasp on what is shaping up in Hong Kong. Word is out, and the major and minor players originally involved in Yingyong have been joined by a great many other sharks, loaning or gorging. It seems as if more than a dozen weapons and defense manufacturers are now striving to gain the R&D materials and any working models. For now, the company is still standing, but I do not believe this R&D firm is going to stay independent for very long. Players from Ares Arms, Shiawase, SpIn, and Saeder-Krupp are now involved. If people are looking for work, this is certainly a good place to find it. Just be prepared and get yourself more than just a FreeTrader account or financial knowsoft. You’re going to need it.

As for the code itself, I am reaching out to people I know to find out if I can get a hold of one of the download keys that Rikki mentioned earlier. I’m hopeful, but not relying on getting it anytime soon.
