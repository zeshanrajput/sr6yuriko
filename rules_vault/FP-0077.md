## **Consulting**

**The main idea behind building up Convergence’s roster of hackers and diviners is to analyze what they have, but when you have this much talent in the roster, you might as well let them be put to use. In a short period of time, Convergence has built up a practice that serves a wide range of individuals, governments, and corporations. The great thing about people with money is they can often be insecure about staying at the top of the world, so they’re willing to fork out some cash to see what the future holds. Does that mean sometimes they act like a high-rent storefront psychic, only with real powers? Sure. But it builds up the organization’s fnancial war chest, allowing for more employees, and it also adds to their growing collection of data.**

- **Business is growing due to the uncertainty in the world right now. People trying to decide which side to be on in the growing Dis conflict would be happy for some signs about which way they should lean.**

- **Frosty**
