## **Quality Adjustments**

- **Damage Control** : Reducing Heat can only be done at the end of the run and it’s only for the person with the quality and spending the Karma. The extra box of damage healed is only on a successful healing test. If the test fails, no healing is done.

- **False Tell** : The creation of the False Tell is on the same action as the Con skill which means that you can only spend Edge on either the Con test or the Composure test, not both.

- **Fast Friend** : This quality is not legal for character creation but can still be bought after character creation for double the cost. When you buy a NPC using 1 FP to Loyalty 1, the contact starts at Loyalty 2.

- **Interrogator** : This quality only provides you with a point of Edge on tests to gain information using a social skill. It does not prevent the gain or spend of Edge on the defense test.

- **Master of Rhetoric** : Increase the cost to buy this by 2 per level (cost 7 Karma per level).

- **Poker Face** : To add your ranks of Con to the Composure test, you must spend a point of Edge.

- - **Practiced Paranoia** : To add your ranks of Con to the Judge Intentions test, you must spend a point of Edge.
