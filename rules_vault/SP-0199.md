## **Lagos, Kingdoms of Nigeria**

## **Status: Contested**

**While the Week of Death was a shock to many around the world, those in Lagos barely noticed; for them, violence of that scale is simply another Tuesday.**

**Up until three months ago, Lagos would have been classifed as under Disian control. A prime location for obtaining raw materials, and metahuman fuel for the Harvesters, it didn’t take much for Disian proxies to bribe or buy their way into positions of infuence with only the occasional use of strong-arm tactics to seal certain deals and make sure any gang or tribal leaders didn’t interfere too much.**

**The tide in Lagos began to change approximately nine months ago thanks to various charitable organizations under the “Helping Hand” initiative. Spearheaded by Evo with backing by local Horizon representatives, numerous clinics and aid stations were quickly constructed with the assistance of local religious groups to help give the hard-hit areas places to fnd both food and medical care.**
