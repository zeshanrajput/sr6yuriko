## 29: the gala

## Hook

The Met Gala is the social event of the season. Therefore, this isn’t so much a single adventure but a series of opportunities for the runners to get involved in various ways. The runners will be asked to take on multiple jobs with little time to prepare and practically no time in between. The rewards presented by the highest echelon of New York’s social society, though, should be worth the risk. They won’t be able to do everything, but there are plenty of chances to load up their credsticks.
