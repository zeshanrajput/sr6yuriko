\*Handheld and Mob Light systems can project either a narrow beam affecting a single target, or a cone that can affect several adjacent targets. The cone setting uses 3 carges from the power cells and covers a thirty-degree angle. When the Handheld emitter uses the cone setting, reduce its Attack Ratings by half (rounded up). The Mob Light version does not suffer from this reduction of effect.

† The level of the damage is based upon the setting of the device—low (5S) or high (10S). High power consumes one extra charge every time the weapon is fired. This damage is recovered much faster than normal Stun damage, allowing the individual a recovery test every turn instead of every hour. If, however, the individual is struck a second time before they have fully recovered, they recover from any additional damage every minute instead of every turn. Even if awake, individuals suffer from the Dazed status (p. 52, SR6) until they are fully recovered from the stun effect. This technology will affect individuals with cybereyes or those operating through a visual sensor device like a rigger, with the latter at a reduced effect of minus three (–3) to the DV.

SMALLpool modifier for the resistance test. If an individual is not facing the emitter, they gain an additional point of Edge to resist the test unless there is a mirror or highly reflective surface that can be used to bounce the light (gamemaster discretion).
