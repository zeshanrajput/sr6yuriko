## **Shiawase**

**Despite Shiawase, or at least elements within the corporation, offering substantial assistance on multiple pro-Disian projects and research, the corporation doesn’t seem to have offcially sided with or against the Disians. This, along with Shiawase’s ties to the Imperial Japanese Royal Family, has caused Renraku to (surprisingly) tread lightly. Some may say this is out of some kind of respect, but more likely the reaction is strategic. The idea is to take out or cripple MCT frst. Still, Renraku is offering substantial amounts of nuyen for information on Shiawase activities.**
