## Background (Ares)

Ares is coming in late to the party. After the events connected to the start of Project Lazarus hit the news, several top Ares executives quickly realized something big is happening. With the upcoming vote on who will replace the late Damian Knight as CEO, acquiring an asset like memory-altering tech would give someone a big advantage. The various factions vying for control throw all their available resources into Manhattan to discover what’s actually happening and who is involved. While the Animus technology would be a valuableweapon in the Ares arsenal, and different factions on the board will pursue it seriously, the paydata they end up being most interested in are the details about a metaplanar portal literally under their feet. If Ares gets the information on the portal location under their own extraterritorial property, they will immediately divert their military assets into the portal cave and force Aztechnology out, claiming it for themselves—perhaps eventually allowing other megas access and charging some insanely high fees.

As far as Ares’ own secrets, they will go to great lengths to conceal their past activities with the Disians. The fact that Damian Knight acquired the technology used in the metaplanar disruption devices for Operation Bottleneck and Operation Latvian Gambit from the Disians—and originally powered the devices with living, magically active metahumans—is a secret that no one in the corporation who knows it wants leaked.
