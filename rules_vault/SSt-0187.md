## **Posted by: X-Prime**

Shadowrunning is a team sport, and a whole lot of the time you’ll live or die based on the quality of your team.

I know that some people I’ve run with will be surprised to hear me say that, as I spent most of the runs we were on making fun of them, but none of them should take it personally. It’s just my nature. It doesn’t mean I don’t appreciate what they did on a mission or recognize how they helped keep me above ground and out of prison.

I’ve also spent most of my career in the shadows running with a guy who is fanatical about team-building. I’ve had a front-row seat to everything he does, and I’ve learned a lot. But at the risk of patting myself on the back, I’m a more fl exible thinker than he is. He has the One True Way of doing things, and he doesn’t vary from it. From my vantage point, I can see when his way works, and when it doesn’t. Sometimes I’ve even managed to convince him to make a tweak, or I

**just go behind his back and do my thing and see if h** e no **ti** ces.

**>** Maybe get to the point instead of this self-hagiography? **>** Cayman

Anyway, all that is to say I’ve learned a lot about team building in my decade-plus in the shadows, so I’m going to share it with you, organized into some critical points.
