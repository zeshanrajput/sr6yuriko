## Game Information

## Armor Clothing

When purchasing armor clothing, players can designate if that item matches any other type of outfit listed in this chapter, except for scavenged rags. For cheap clothes, blue collar, casual wear, and business casual, there’s no added cost. For a uniform, power suit, or Tres Chic armor clothing, players must also pay the outfit cost on top of the armor’s cost. No other armor types can be modified in this manner.
