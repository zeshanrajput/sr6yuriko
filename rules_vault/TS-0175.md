## **History**

**The Sternschutz originated from DaimlerBenz’s factory security and developed into a security company and police conglomerate under the leadership of the International Vehicle and Machine Union (IFMU) in the 2050s. It frst gained full police responsibilities in Stuttgart, except for those falling under BKA and LKA authority.**

- **For those of you wondering, BKA and LKA are criminal investigation agencies within the AGS. The LKA operates on a state level, while the BKA acts on the AGS level.**

- **Red Anya**

**While the IFMU had to downsize in the early 2060s, Sternschutz expanded its operations AGSwide. They secured contracts in cities like Cologne and took over security for various corporate facilities. During these years, Sternschutz provided not only personnel but also security concepts and technologies.**

**In 2065, IFMU ended. Sternschutz was purchased by the FBA and combined with Deutsche Sicherheitsdienst and ProSecuritas, companies they already owned. Following the merger, Sternschutz Security could get the law enforcement contract for Greater Frankfurt.**

**After the reunifcation process of Berlin in 2072, Sternschutz also gained the law enforcement contract for the sprawl. With this contract, Sternschutz replaced the former Berlin Police. The new Berlin Police Laws were a mix of Alliance laws and laws that Greater Frankfurt had in place. Berlin had a great deal of independence from the headquarters in Stuttgart and used it to employ several orks within the Berlin department. This was to overcome the racism within the Sternschutz, and at frst got some results.**

**Sternschutz secured law enforcement contracts in Baden-Baden and Augsburg, but missed out on contracts in Hannover and Salzburg, a fact that is not well received within the FBA.**

**Following the Bloody May Day episode on May 1 in Berlin, the contract underwent severe limitations. Planning tactician Bianca Voyé instructed Sternschutz to open fre on surrounded demonstrators, effectively ordering a massacre. Many Berlin police offcers declined to comply with the directive and were subsequently dismissed. These events, as well as the Berlin Police Gang Scandal, led to the Berlin Police Freedom. With this development, Sternschutz no longer has a full law enforcement contract in the city.**

- **It was a disaster for them. The relations between Sternschutz and Berlin’s population weren’t good before, but this incident was an all-new low. This led to a lot of newer, smaller security companies in the aftermath.**

- **Scythe**
