## Union Supporter

Everett has seen a massive resurgence in its local labor unions. You are either a member of one, have strong ties to one, or are just a regular at their hangouts. You often get some early information on work being done in the Everett district.

- **Cost:** 3 Karma

- **Game Effect:** Pick one of the unions described previously as your select group. You get a +1 Edge on all social tests when working with people who are part of that union.
