## **Can I transfer my character from a previous Season of** _**Shadowrun Missions**_ **?**

Characters created using 6[th] edition rules for _SRM 2081_ can play in any SRM or CMP built for Sixth World starting with SRM 2081. When SRM moves to a new city or starts a new story arc, your character can travel there, but be aware that your old contacts that can get you what you need probably don’t know a lot about



the city you just moved to. This does not include Season 9 or Season 10 as they were created for 5[th] edition and the Karma, nuyen, and difficulty aren’t balanced for 6[th] edition play.
