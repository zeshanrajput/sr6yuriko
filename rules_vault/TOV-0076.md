## Ritual: Gris-Gris Creation

**Mentor/Patron Spirit:** Thunderbird or War

Gris-gris are different from other types of enchanted items or alchemical preparations. Instead of anchoring magic into the talisman, you’re binding a spirit to the object. Within the tradition, the belief is that you are using the spirit of nature within the materials and asking for that spirit to intercede with the lwa on your behalf. Gris-gris are a type of fetish that is rare outside the Voodoo tradition and provide aid on different types of tests and can sometimes provide access to a spirit power.
