- **Vector:** Ingestion

- **Speed:** 2 combat rounds

- **Duration:** 10 x 1D6 minutes

- **Power:** 7

- **Effect:** Gain situational Edge to resist Manipulation spells

Ayao’s Will was originally created by MCT using bioengineered samples of an Awakened cotton plant, but the drug has since been replicated by all the megacorporations. The seeds of the plant are ingested and act as a mana blocker for a period. While on the magical compound, you gain 1 situation Edge for resisting Manipulation spells. When the effect ends, you gain the Nauseated status for the next hour.
