## **> Tickler’s**

A strip joint catering to all kinds, this place is popular with the corporate set, both for the show and for more intimate encounters. Mostly it’s notable for two things: the girls are treated pretty damn well by owner Jack Bellows, and one of the working girls is a top-flight info-broker. Word is she got Jack out of trouble when the Mob tried to muscle in on the place, and she’s got enough blackmail stored up to be quite the player. Ask around, and she can offer sweet intel and arrange introductions. Have fun. ;)

- Kat o’ Nine Tales
