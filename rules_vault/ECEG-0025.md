## lakeview

**Landmarks:** New Canal Lighthouse, Leviathan’s Bridge, Lakefront Corporate Airport

**Casinos:** Delta Queen, Golden Decadence **Shops:** Lakeview Boutique, Nu-Aristocrat Clothing

New Orleans wasn’t always as kind to its metahuman folk as it is now, and the result of that is Ereth’citi just west of Lakeview. The name is a bastardization of the Or’zet word “Ereth’cerri,” meaning home turf. During the 2050s, it was near to the only place in the city where they could escape prejudice and be allowed to live relatively normal lives. Once the population began to accept them about ten years later, they didn’t want to leave. They had formed their own cultural identity and felt a certain ownership of these city blocks after defending them from Humanis all those years.

Lakeview runs along Lake Pontchartrain north of Downtown and is the home of the affl uent in New Orleans. A wealthy city sprawl of malls, suburban neighborhoods, casinos, and nightclubs all with a beautiful view of the waterfront and its walkways. Its legacy of wealth is further cemented in the way its people live their daily lives, expanding their luxurious homes out and dressing like stylish aristocrats.

This district has never been too happy to be right next to Ereth’citi, nor has it been particularly pleased with the city’s recent tendency to play nice with its neighbor. Crime is near non-existent, but when there is any, it’s always blamed on that twin. Rumors abound that behind all the pleasant smiles and philanthropy, a not-insignifi cant number of homeowners in the area travel to the outskirts for Humanis Policlub meetings.
