## Good Times

- **Vector:** Slot

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** (Rating) hours

- **Power:** 5 + (Rating)

- **Effect:** Temporarily removes Bad Memories negative quality and reduces threshold on Flashbacks negative quality

This moodchip helps alleviate negative consequences for sufferers of past traumatic experiences. It does this by suppressing emotional responses in the presence of the trigger. These BTL are custom-designed for individual users and specific experiences. This is generally a prescribed drug for trauma victims to help them cope with flashbacks. When using the moodchip, the user is not impacted by the Bad Memories negative quality (p. 129, _Firing Squad_ ); if they have Flashbacks (p. 130, _Firing Squad_ ), the Composure test thresholds are reduced by 1.
