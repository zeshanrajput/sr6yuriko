## **When do I burn out if I have latent awakening?**

Once you have purchased the Latent Awakening quality, the first essence drop you take will cause you to burn out if you haven’t already purchased your magic up. For instance, if you buy Latent Awakening and then immediately buy a datajack (deltaware), you will immediately burn out unless you have bought your Magic up to 2.

## **When we want to increase an NPC’s Connection, do we use the rules in** _**SRM**_ **or the rules from the** _**Sixth World Companion**_ **(p. 162)?**

We will continue to use the rules contained in the _SRM Guide_ so that players are not required to retro fit any spent resources if they had previously increased an NPC’s Connection.
