## Return of the Nanoforge

With the new accords in place, nanotechnologies are making a comeback in all the right (and a few wrong) places. One thing grease monkeys should pay attention to is how they integrate a nanoforge into a shop or facility. There are three size classes of nanoforges on the market. Desktop forges can make a part up to the size of an average briefcase (20cm x 20cm x 50cm). Case forges are larger, coming in at about 1.5 meters on each side. The last line of forges is simply known as Cubes. These are capable of manufacturing entire complex components, up to and including entire PMVs, jet turbines, and passenger cockpits. Cubes come in various sizes, with the largest ones found only at the largest construction facilities such as the Yamatetsu Naval Technology yards in Vladivostok or, rumor has it, on the Martian moon Deimos (left there by the Monads).
