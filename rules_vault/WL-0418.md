## **Receptive to Training**

**This critter takes to training very well, increasing the amount of commands and special training they can learn.**

- **Cost: 3 Karma/level (max level = critter’s Logic or Intuition attribute, whichever is lowest)**

- **Game Effect: For every level of this quality, this critter may learn one additional command and receive one additional special training.**
