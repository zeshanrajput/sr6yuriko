## Additional character creation recommendations:

- **Attributes:** Agility 3+, Reaction 3+

- **Skills:** Outdoors 2+ with Specialization (Tracking), Athletics 1+, Close Combat 1+, Stealth 1+

- **Knowledge skills:** [Area] Knowledge, Notorious Cyber-Bullies

- **Positive qualities:** Bravado, Focused Ambition, Home Ground, Relentless Tracker, Teflon Coated

- **Negative qualities:** Bounty, Distinctive Style, Honorbound, Non-lethal, Trust Issues

- **Life modules:** Activist, Artist, Bodyguard, Courier, Fixer, Investigator, Mentor, Shadowrunner, Traveler, Event: You benefited from the influence of a mentor.
