## Fashion Accessories

Accessories turn an outfit into an ensemble. Various accessories such as jewelry, hats, scarves, purses, shoes, and more can be combined with an outfit in order to enhance the wearer’s appearance, status, or to make a specific impression. Only so much can be used at once without ruining the desired effect, so piling on as much as possible isn’t going to work. The outfit chosen determines how much the wearer can benefit from using accessories.
