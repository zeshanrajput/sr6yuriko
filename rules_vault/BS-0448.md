## Vulcan

- **Vector:** Slot

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** (Rating x 5) minutes

- **Power:** 8 + (Rating)

- **Effect:** +1 Willpower, +1 Logic, –1 Intuition, –1 Charisma

Pure thought and clean logic are sometimes required for the tasks at hand. This moodchip suppresses emotions, allowing the user to look at problems through a clearer set of eyes. The resulting drop in emotional response can be addictive for those who are dealing with emotional trauma. When the BTL wears off, the user’s Willpower and Logic are reduced to 1 for the next (rating) minutes.
