## Keeping the Peace Redux

As above, but with a twist: Someone’s OPSEC was foul, and Lone Star has gotten wind of the meeting between known underworld figures. They hang back and allow the meeting to unfold, but before it can conclude, they move in to arrest everyone caught in their dragnet. Ideally, the runners will detect the Star’s net before it’s thrown, but they are still, as representatives of the facilitating fixer, responsible for guaranteeing the escape of both parties without allowing either side to murder the other. And, ideally, without getting arrested themselves.
