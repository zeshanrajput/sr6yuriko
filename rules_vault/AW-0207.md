## Wuxing

Geomancy has been shown to be a strong component in the rituals being used by Disian allies to stabilize and maintain certain metaplanar portals. Additionally, Wuxing’s dominance in the transportation industry has allowed them to move people and material with ease. We’ve also received rumors that a top Wuxing executive recently met with and received “a most generous gift” from a Disian.
