## Rapid Transit

Take those globetrotter uniforms and strip off the corporate logos, and you get the Rapid Transit line. This is everyday armor for those who want to feel safe and a half-step fashion forward at the same time. The RT line comes in Basic, Elite, Platinum, and Diamond editions, offering a little variety for your plain fashion.
