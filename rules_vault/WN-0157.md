## Make New Friends

The best way to enter Dump Sh0ck is to walk in with the masses. The PCs will need to pass through MAD scanners (rating 2), olfactory scanners (rating 2), and millimeter-wave scanners (rating 2). However, the guards will readily take bribes of 100 nuyen to let non-lethal items pass.

Once inside, it will take a Perception + Intuition (3) test to spot one of the friends. Use Cutters Data
