## Interchangeable Heads

**Explosive** : These points carry a small, shaped charge that detonates on impact for +1 DV. They are inherently safer than explosive rounds for firearms and do not explode on a critical glitch.

**Hammer** : Using a wide, blunt front edge, these points change the weapon’s profile from Physical damage to Stun damage.

**Hunting Points** : The standard bladed point designed to cause Physical damage found on most bolts and arrows.

**Incendiary** : The Incendiary point is loaded with jellied white phosphorus contained in a hard case that breaks open on impact, igniting the phosphorus as soon as it is exposed to the air. The weapon suffers a –2 DV to its profile, but the target gains the Burning 1 status from a successful strike, with

additional strikes increasing the status by one for each point that hits a target (to a maximum of Burning 6).

**Injection** : Made as an interchangeable point instead of part of the bolt or arrow, Injection points are treated exactly as a standard injection arrow or injection bolt.

**Penetrator** : These long, thin, tungsten alloy tips are designed to penetrate armor at the expense of causing tissue damage. Add +2 to the AR of the weapon and –1 DV.

**Stick-n-Shock** : This point duplicates the effects of a standard Stick-n-Shock firearm round, increasing the AR by +1 and converting the damage to –1 Stun (electrical).
