## **Power Maul**

**A much larger version of the power hammer, the power maul has a fve-kilogram—or heavier—head attached to a one-and-one-half-meter to two-meter shaft. The weapon must always be wielded two-handed and requires a lot of body mass and considerable full body strength to use. Simply having a high-end cyberarm or two is insuffcient to properly swing one. The power maul holds twelve charges in the clip behind the strike plate; up to four charges may be set to detonate on a single impact. As with the power hammer,**

**the power maul may be purchased with a single or double strike plate.**
