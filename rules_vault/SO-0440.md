## Poker Face

No one knows if you have a tell because your face only betrays your true emotions when you let it. Sometimes you need to let your guard down to get your point across, but when you want to hide your intentions, you hide them very well.

**Cost:** 6 Karma

**Game Effect:** You may add your ranks in Con to your dice pool on a Composure test—if you do, the cost of Edge actions or boosts to affect your Composure test are increased by one.
