## Ruthenium Rifle Sheath

| **AVAIL** | **COST** |
| --------- | -------- |
| 4         | 1,000¥   |

This mod increases the Concealability threshold of the weapon by 1 level when trying to hide it from visual inspection. When used in conjunction with a ruthenium suit, it blends the gun right into the suit. When used with a melee weapon, the first attack made with the weapon gains a +4 to Attack Rating. After this attack, a Major Action is required to clean the weapon or reset the system.

A dropped weapon without wireless active has a Concealability threshold of at least 4, or +1 for all concealability ratings of 4 or greater. **Slot:** None

- Tough is right. I’ve seen troopers pulled out of the line of fire by nothing but their tactical slings, and one case where someone was kept from falling out of a t-bird when her teammates grabbed her rifle.
