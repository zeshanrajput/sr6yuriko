## Charybdis (Wild Zone)

CaribNet connects the various island members of the Caribbean League across expanses of water. The grid reflects exotic locations and high-seas adventures. Charybdis was initially put in as a joke as part of that grid closest to the Bermuda Triangle, where the Matrix goes a little crazy. Experienced local deckers use this instability to elude pursuers. Stories say that there are sea monsters here that can eat your comms. With CaribNet mostly inside the corporate Matrix box, Charybdis has taken a life of its own. It’s no longer upgraded, as hosts have been physically misplaced or lost (imagine that) within the triangle.
