## Armor Plating Module

Armor plating consists of multiple plates designed to be flexible enough to have a limited impact on the critter’s mobility. Lighter armor plating is made from woven kevlar similar to what is used in armor clothing, though it is generally much thicker than armor designed to be worn by metahumans.

**Game Information:** Armor plating requires one capacity per point of its rating. The rating is added as a bonus to the critter’s Defense Rating. The DR bonus from armor plating stacks with other forms of protection, such as natural armor, armor worn by the critter, or defensive augmentations such as dermal plating or orthoskin. The maximum rating for an armor plating module is 8. The module also gains a capacity of its own for standard armor mods (chemical protection, fire resistance, nonconductivity, etc.) equal to half the armor module’s rating (round up).

Initially, a critter finds an armor module to be distracting. Until the critter receives special training with the module, they receive a –2 dice pool penalty to all actions.

A critter cannot fly or swim while wearing an armor plating module with a rating higher than 2.
