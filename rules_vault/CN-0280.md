## Yin and Yang

The two groups found that they had a similar core structure, with a strong central authority, strict codes of honor, and semi-legitimacy despite being composed largely of outsiders. On top of that, their central focuses, while aligned, didn’t cross one another’s profit centers. They were mutually compatible and, within a few decades, had began merging into the first true gumi (meaning associations), creating the Yakuza as it still operates today.
