## Faustean Crystal

With only 2,000 in existence in the Sixth World, these crystals are bestowed only to those who have proven the utmost devotion and pledged their existence to serving Dis, having entered the Sacred Pact with them. While first and foremost a symbol of loyalty and allegiance, these crystals also provide several benefits to both user and their Dis masters.

Embedded somewhere on the user’s body, these crystals act as a conduit for Dis magical energies and provide benefits that function similarly to adept powers. However, these benefits do not combine or mesh well with any Sixth World technology—quite the opposite. In order gain the full benefits of such a crystal, the Faustean must have as much technology removed as possible. Magicians and adepts who become Fausteans find that their magical abilities greatly increase, but their connection to any spirits becomes strained and astral travel more difficult; their newfound abilities seem unable to completely sync up with the native manasphere.
