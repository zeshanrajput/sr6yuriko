## Encounter 1: Bad Day to be Infamous

The runner with the highest Reputation or Heat score is targeted by a trio of gangers (use Sons of Sauron Brute, p. 205, _SR6_ ) in the employ and company of Bailey West (use News Reporter, p. 169, _Sixth World Companion_ ). Bailey is a simsense reporter who records her own live feed to sell to screamsheets and data havens. She hangs back from the gangers as they try to knock out the runner and is hoping she can swoop in for the save, gaining the runner’s confidence and an inside track for her story. She’s charismatic, smart, and willing to play the long game. After the gangers attack, she offers to be a new contact for the runner, whether she saved them or just came to check on them after the “senseless violence.”

If the connection is made, she’ll call the runner in the next few days looking for information on

Animus Construction, saying only that she’s reaching out to all her street contacts and how happy she is she met them. She heard about the runners from a Cheli, the fixer who tried to take them out previously (p. 81).
