## Evo

Until recently, Evo has been actively snubbed by Dis. Exact reasons are unknown, but it’s likely because of Disians’ aversion to technology, which Evo excels at. There have been several meetings between Evo and Dis representatives in the last several weeks. I don’t see a partnership anytime soon—it’s more likely both sides are trying to feel each other out.
