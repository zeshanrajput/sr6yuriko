## Stability

A sprite can use this power on any persona or device for which it has either User or Admin access. Stability prevents normal malfunctions or accidents from affecting the target (including both standard glitches and those induced by the Gremlins or Accident powers). Ignore standard glitches and reduce critical glitches to regular glitches.
