## Defensive Secretion

The character’s skin exudes a defensive liquid when they are agitated or endangered, like the defensive secretions of frogs. These secretions are acidic, causing a burning sensation or other adverse skin reactions upon touch (gamemaster decision). If someone with unprotected skin touches the bare skin of the agitated character under the right conditions, that person suffers a –1 dice pool modifier for all tests made within the next twenty-four hours or until treated with a medkit with a successful Biotech (Medicine) (2) test. This quality does not work against spirits. Multiple touches raise the dice pool modifier, but only to a maximum of –4.
