## **>** Balladeer

As you pull off more and more successful runs, Shiawase has no qualms about giving you access to their tech; after all, a better shadowrunner is better able to do missions for them. Getting access to alpha-, or for aces beta-grade cyberware has always been an option, but Shiawase’s also able to produce proper bioware that’s tailored to your genetic code. Since they’re well-aware of what these implants are going to be used for, they don’t, as a rule, add markers or tracing devices. That would betray the respect between host and guest, after all, which is a shameful thing and would further undermine your usefulness to them. The same doesn’t apply to corporate citizens, however, who buy their cyberware from Shiawase with pride, happy to send back wireless updates on their activities all day long, for the good of the family.
