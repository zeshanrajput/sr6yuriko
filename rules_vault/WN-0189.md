## The Job

Villiers Tower is no easy nut to crack, but Piper is giving the team a couple days of lead time to look for or build a weak spot to exploit. They also have the guest list and can try to intercept a guest and take their spot.

Every floor is protected by wifi-inhibiting paint (rating 6) that helps limit access to office hosts from either the offices themselves or through the security host. Astral security consists of eight patrolling spirits of air (Force 4; add Guard) and two onsite security mages (use Lone Star Combat Mage, p. 207, _SR6_ ). Physical security consists of twelve security personnel (use Lone Star Patrolman, p. 206, _SR6_ ) spread all over the building.

The hosts of the party are Celina and Illyan Ardrin, twin brother and sister, well known in shadow circles to act as high-end fixers and Mr. Johnsons for any and all of the globe’s power players looking to work in Manhattan.

This particular event involves several mid-level corporate executives from among the members of the MDC invited to a party/auction. These young and eager executives are just the type to be willing to take the big risks, but not the type to have easy authorization for the big cash Celina and Illyan are asking. The Ardrins know this, and they’re under orders to throw this party for a collection of reasons. They want to present a pitch for some cutting-edge tech. They want the execs to party and possibly enter into unprofessional behavior. They want footage of the guests for the real demonstration of the tech. And most of all, they want them all to know someone else might beat them to this prize once the bidding starts for real.

The Ardrins provide an upbeat mingling environment in the c-suite as guests arrive. After about an hour, the twins take center stage and pitch their new tech: a BTL-like program that not only can implant a persona in an individual but comes with the added benefit of that person never remembering any actions they performed while “skipping,” as they call it.

The bidding starts at one billion nuyen—a sum at which everyone scoffs because none of them control that type of instant funding. Two make down payment offers with the addendum that they will need to talk to their R&D divisions (or other shadow assets) to see if funding can be made available. Most of the rest laugh it off as fairytale tech. When the twins refuse a demonstration tonight, the gentle jeers and drunken teasing commence, and Celina and Illyan politely retire from the party and thank the guests for their time. They then offer the entertainment present for all the guests to enjoy for the rest of the evening, announcing the suite is rented until 08:00 tomorrow morning.

Besides the guests, there are two dozen joygirls and joyboys, a plethora of drugs from the standard type all the way to BTLs and BADs, as well as sim module setups for full-body feel.

This allows the runners to gather some compromising footage as long as they can attend the gathering discreetly. Possibilities include acting as a joyboy (twelve of them are in attendance), joygirl (twelve of them are in attendance), drug connoisseur (there are six present), security (use Mafia Soldato, p. 207, _SR6_ ; only eight are present), or drink server (ten total present, all nearly identical Japanese women).
