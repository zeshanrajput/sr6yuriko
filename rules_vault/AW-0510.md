## Mana Level

**Very Low Mana:** Perhaps the mana here has been siphoned off or stripped away, or the native manasphere is unusually weak for some other reason. Mana still flows here, but it’s very difficult to manipulate, and the residents of the metaplane rarely use it. The entire metaplane is considered a non-aspected high mana ebb that affects everyone in it. Edge cannot be gained through magic use, and Edge may not be spent on tests that include the Magic attribute. Additionally, any test that includes the Magic attribute takes a –4 dice pool penalty. Entities from this type of metaplane will typically be mundane, but some have a Magic rating and can be summoned as spirits. These spirits will have the normal profile with a Magic rating, and while they do not use magic on their homemetaplane, they will use their magic abilities in locations with higher mana.

It’s worth noting that some metaplanes have no mana at all. While a few of these places are known to exist (Caelum, from Mission 30, _The Third Parallel_ , is one example), they do not connect directly to the Astral Sea, and traveling to them requires machines using technology currently incomprehensible to Sixth World researchers. This makes mana void metaplanes inaccessible by any normal gateway, portal, or rift.

**Low Mana** : Magic is difficult to use here, and while the locals may have magic, there will be little evidence of it being used. The entire metaplane is considered a non-aspected medium mana ebb that affects everyone in it. Edge actions and Edge boosts using Magic cost 1 Edge more than normal, and any test that includes the Magic attribute takes a –2 dice pool penalty.

**Below Normal:** Magic use and magical devices are uncommon here, but they can be found with a little effort. The entire metaplane is considered a non-aspected low mana ebb, and a –1 dice pool penalty is applied to any test that includes the Magic attribute.

**Normal Level** : Magic works as it normally does in the Sixth World. Devices that incorporate magic in their operation and entities that use magic can be found without too much trouble.

**Above Normal** : Magic is a common sight among the natives of the metaplane, with abundant devices that incorporate magic into their operation. The entire metaplane is considered a non-aspected low mana flow, and any test that includes the Magic attribute has a +1 dice pool modifier applied to the test.
