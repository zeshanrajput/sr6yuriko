## Guns

Let’s get it out of the way. Ares does guns. There, happy? That’s what they’re famous for, so that’s all we need to talk about, right?

Wrong!

The small-arms industry that you know is barely a tick in the general income of a megacorp the size of Ares, even one that’s had as many setbacks over the past decade as Ares has. You might wonder what they do, then? Space? Sure, they dominated everything from orbit outward for fifty years, but they have rivals worthy of the name now, and the income stream has shrunk over time due to that. Between the two, along with heavy industry, Ares is synonymous with the defense industry and military matters are a huge source of income. What you never think of are services and consumer goods, where they’ve been near the top for decades (more on that later), but more than anything else? They sell mythos.
