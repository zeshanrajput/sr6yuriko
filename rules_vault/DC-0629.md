## Evade Pursuit

## (Piloting, Chase Target Only)

Move ahead and away from all pursuit. Increase the range categories of all other participants relative to you by one for 4 Edge, or two for 8 Edge. Any pursuit that was already at Extreme range remains within that range category. You also gain a position advantage against all of your pursuers. **Cost:** 4 or 8 Edge
