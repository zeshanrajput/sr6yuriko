## Iaijutsu

Like the samurai of old (or at least the ones in the trids), the character may use the Quick Draw Minor Action (p. 42, _SR6_ ) with any properly stored melee weapon that is small enough to be wielded in one hand—no quick-drawing polearms, unless it’s dwarf-sized and you’re a troll. The character must still use the Quick Draw action together with the Attack Major Action. **Category:** Weapon
