## We’re Toxic

## Family Matters

## Main Location: Zehlendorf

## Notable Characters: Sigma

In stark contrast to Sigma’s tough business demeanor, the non-binary Schmidt is very happily married to their wife, Selina, and their husbands Lars and Mehmet (Berlin has absolutely no trouble with polyamory or any combination of genders in those relationships), with whom they have a total of four kids, all conceived by way of haploidisation.

Theoretically, even among the corpsharks of Berlin, there is a gentleman’s agreement that the family is kept out of the often brutal career maneuvers. Of course, there are always scumbags who do not abide by these unwritten rules.

**Original Message:** URGENT! One of the Schmidt’s competitors is trying to lend weight to negotiations by threatening the Schmidt’s family. The Schmidt instructs you to collect all family members as quickly as possible and bring them to the family’s private jet at Schönefeld Airport so they can be extracted onto the Schmidt’s yacht in the Baltic Sea.
