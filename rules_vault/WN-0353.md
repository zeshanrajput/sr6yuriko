## Jinxy Data

Jinxy, like both Piper Jane and Jon Nox, is a monad, with the special Matrix abilities monads receive (see p. 76, _Collapsing Now_ ). She generally communicates with Piper Jane only, but this is an emergency, so she reveals her true nature to the PCs. She does not talk but easily communicates with text messages.

Jinxy saw five men in nondescript body armor come into the hallway via the stairs. Someone was jamming the Matrix in the area. Jinxy ran for Piper’s door but was hit by a taser or something (runners find a Stick-n-Shock round in her fur if they look close).
