## **Food and food production**

**Let’s start with the most simple and common role that critters play in our civilization: food. Even with the advent of many alternatives to eating meat, metahumanity still enjoys eating animals. Growing livestock for meat is relatively expensive and resource-intensive compared to most plantbased food sources, but it seems that eating meat is never going to go out of style. Chicken, beef, and pork are still the most common options, though you may fnd cockatrice on the menu at certain fne dining establishments.**

**> What does cockatrice taste like, I wonder?**

**> Kane**

- **I hate to fall into a cliché here, but they really do taste like chicken. Their eggs have a distinctively unusual flavor, though.**

- **> Moreau**

**But using critters as a food source doesn’t always mean slaughtering them. Eggs and dairy are regular supermarket staples.**

**Megacorps exist largely to proft off our needs, and everyone’s got to eat. This makes food production one of the most lucrative and stable industries, and critters play a large role in meeting our need to feed. Though every megacorp has their fnger in the pie, Aztechnology takes the lead in this, devoting the bulk of their bio-engineering research and development toward food production.**
