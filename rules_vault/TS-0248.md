## **Honda Raptor**

**The Honda Raptor is purpose built for pure speed. When LESP go-gang task forces need a pursuit option, this is the machine they go for frst. The Raptor is the fastest production bike available on the law enforcement market and sees common use as a highway patrol option in rural areas, where its top-end speed and maneuverability on the open road can give an offcer the advantage they sometimes need when chasing down a go-gang.**
