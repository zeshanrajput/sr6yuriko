## True Elements

True elements, like all reagents, are measured in drams. Each true element “stone” is worth four drams when used as a reagent (p.153, _SR6_ ), and the material fades into nothing after use. When used in the crafting of a focus and not part of the focus (i.e., not part of the required reagents), the focus gains additional benefits.
