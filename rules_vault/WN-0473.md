## Gerald Ramius (“Gramius”)

## (Diplomat, Connection Rating 4)

Survivor of the Boston Lockdown, Mr. Ramius now works as a liaison between Evo and the Yakutian Ambassador-at-Large (Gregori Markovanich). In addition to a special quickened spell placed upon him by the ambassador, he wears a belt that holds a series of advanced preparations (ten Physical Mask spells in three distinct appearances).

Gramius is a human male in his mid-twenties. He has brown hair with a hint of auburn highlights, brown eyes, natural good looks, and average height, and he is in fair physical health. He seems younger than he acts and is guarded when it comes to discussions about his personal sponsor. He has two Force 6 elementals with him (any type) when doing direct business for the ambassador.
