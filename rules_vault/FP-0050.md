## **Kerry and Justyna Creager**

**This card-hunting power couple are some of the rare breed that actually go into the feld themselves. For good or ill, when you work** _**for**_ **them you also work** _**with**_ **them. They’re competent enough, but they defnitely make mistakes when the prize is in sight. It isn’t uncommon to end up working a protection gig along with recovering cards as they keep their collection on them, making them a constant target. They usually keep a security detail handy when they’re between hunts, but they’re smart enough to know certain skillsets are required for different jobs.**
