## Magic

The Citadel itself is a place filled with magic, perhaps more than any other metaplane, due to the role it has in astral quests. This magic permeates the very stone and air, causing the ambient mana to behave slightly differently than on the material plane. To reflect this, drain values are always 1 higher for all uses of magic within the Citadel. The exceptions to this are the orichalcum golems, as their entire bodies are more conducive to the flow of mana, which means all their drain values are reduced by 1, to a minimum of 0.
