## Tempo

Tempo is a highly addictive drug derived from a unique hybrid of angel’s trumpet and the Awakened twisted strangler fig tree. The drug is processed and distributed in popper, injection, or ingestible forms. Users of the drug will experience a general feeling of well-being, mild euphoria, and low-intensity hallucinations. In addition, the drug provides astral sight, which accounts for the mild hallucinations. Because of this, users are more in touch with the emotions of those around them. While on the drug, the user will gain 1 Edge for Perception tests and Influence tests; the point of Edge must be used on the test or lost. Technomancers who take this drug will find that it suppresses their abilities and will not be able to gain or spend Edge on any Matrix actions or Resonance actions. When the drug wears off, the user will suffer –1 Body, –1 Willpower, and –1 Charisma for the same duration as the high.
