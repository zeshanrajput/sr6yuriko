## Use the sample Contacts on p. 211, and also build your own

The sample contacts are a great starting point, but don’t hesitate to build your own contacts as the runners meet a variety of people. The range of personalities in the Sixth World help make it distinct and give it flavor, so make contacts that give spice to the setting you’re building and the story you’re telling.
