## Armorlink Upgrade

A commlink with an armorlink upgrade is more effective at protecting itself and the devices within its PAN against Matrix attacks. Add bonus dice equal to the armorlink upgrade rating to any test made to resist Matrix damage, which benefits the commlink as well as any devices connected to its PAN. The maximum rating for an armorlink upgrade is equal to the commlink’s device rating.
