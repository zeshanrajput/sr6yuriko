## Background (Evo)

While not directly involved with Animus Construction, Evo has welcomed more Monads into its corporate family than any other AAA—and benefited greatly from the nanotech advances derived from Monad-inspired research. When Buttercup starts hearing rumors of a nanotech-based memory erasing/altering technology—derived from old CFD research—she immediately understands this news could be another public relations nightmare for Monads and indirectly for Evo. She assigns Evo’s AI executive Taylor Dacopral to start collecting information on the situation. Dacopral hires a Monad data tracker named Rikki Nguyen who quickly locates some of the key players involved and sends the information back up the chain.

Unknown to Buttercup, the head of her security detail, Ms. Helena Chomsky—the late Johnny Spinrad’s chief of security—is actually working for Miles Lanier, and indirectly for Jonathan Blake. Helena is attempting to steer Buttercup into exposing Project Lazarus and the CIA while moving her away from anything connected to the involvement of Lanier and Blake.

Shortly after the meeting with the PCs, Taylor Dacopral is able to locate one of the primary researchers for Animus Construction, an AI using the name Vida Nova, and convinces them to join Evo. This gives Buttercup a great deal of insight into the possible beneficial uses for the technology. Vida Nova claims that in addition to treating

mental illnesses in metahumans, it can be used to stabilize and repair the minds of Monads that have never successfully integrated their personalities. Of course it could also be a dangerous weapon, making it even more important to Buttercup that Evo gets involved.
