## Social Test Example

Haze is hanging out at Dante’s Inferno, doing a bit of people-watching and searching for potential targets. His commlink is running the facial scanner and social HUD apps tied to the image link in his smartcontacts, identifying faces and displaying details about them in his field of vision. Tara Michaels, an Ares executive, catches his interest. He checks over her social media profile and finds her relationship status is set to “available.” When he approaches her, he uses his Influence (Etiquette) + Charisma test to break the ice and introduce himself (or rather, the false persona he chooses to present), and the social HUD app grants him a point of Edge. This app only provides an Edge on the first Social skill test, so Haze will need to rely on other advantages for the following Con (Seduction) + Charisma test to really catch her interest.
