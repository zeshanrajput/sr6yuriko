## Krime Heater

Fancy guns work, yeah, but are complex, have tiny little parts, and aren’t really that effective for what they consider “oversized” hands, especially designs with grip safeties. HA! Oversized my left hoop cheek! The Krime Heater is our way of making it clear that we don’t have conventional-sized mitts! Beefy and big, and able to put rounds downrange reliably while keeping parts sizable enough for easy stripping and cleaning! Why use the best-selling when you can use something that was actually designed for a proper size!
