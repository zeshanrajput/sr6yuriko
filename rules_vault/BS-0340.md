## Skeletal Pneumaticity

Derived from avian gene sequences, this augmentation causes the formation of hollow bones that are nearly as strong as the normal metahuman skeleton, yet much lighter. When combined using any device that allows personal flight (e.g., hang-glider, jetpack, cyberwings), flight range and speed are both extended by twenty percent. Not compatible with bioware wings.
