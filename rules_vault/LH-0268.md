## Update Two: First Strikes

## \<07-21-83/0804:07>

It’s like Mother Nature is losing her fragging mind …

Gunships and aerial drones were turned away after the first wave of strikes because of increasing storm activity and flying chimera attacks! Two drones and a gunship took silent lightning hits; I say silent because there was no accompanying thunder. Not freaky at all. The rest were chased away by a combination of spirit attacks and AAfire. The air strike took out a few weapons emplacements, but not enough for us to move in. El Cuadrilla and MET2000’s artillery is hammeringaway, and Triple Zero is responding in kind, forcing us to spread out.

Frag! More silent lightning strikes! One of them just smoked an armored MML! And another strike, and another! Damn, near misses, but this is getting out of hand!
