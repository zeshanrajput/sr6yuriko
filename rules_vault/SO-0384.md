## Enthralling Performance

## [Con (Performance), Major Action] 4 Edge

This performance draws all the attention toward the performer and creates a distraction. Make a Con (Performance) + Charisma test. Anyone who witnesses the performance and is within 50 meters must make a Composure test with a threshold equal to the hits scored on the Performance test. If the performance isn’t suitable for the location (per the GM’s discretion), the intended targets gain a point of Edge that must be spent on their Composure test, or it’s lost. Conversely, if the performance is in a uniquely suited venue, the performer gains a point of Edge, but it must be spent on the test, or it’s lost.

On a failed Composure test, targets gain the Misdirected status at a level equal to the number of net hits. This lasts for the duration of the performance, up to a maximum number of minutes equal to the performer’s Charisma (or until they stop, whichever comes first).
