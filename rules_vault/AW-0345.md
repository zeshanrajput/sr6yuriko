## Plot Hooks

The Place of Clouds is rich with opportunities for exploration and adventure. Shadowrunners might be hired by one of the megacorporations or magical groups operating there to advance their interests.

- Ares is currently building a weapons factory on Pirate Island. Your employer has hired you sabotage the manufacturing facility before it can be brought into full production. Alternatively, your team might be hired by Ares to take on some pirates near Pirate Island—close enough to be seen, but not within the Corsairs’ airspace—using Ares weapons to demonstrate how effective they are.

- Aztechnology has a problem on Pirate Island—a team of shadowrunners recently pulled off a casino heist, leaving the Azzies on the hook with the Cloud Corsairs. The runners may be hiding on a cloud island but are more likely somewhere on the docks, waiting for their chance to sneak their loot past the bureaucracy and escape through a portal or metaplanar gateway. The team might engage them within the Place of Clouds or possibly chase them down on another metaplane.

- Evo regularly hires freelancers and shadowrunners for its exploration and cartography project. The team is provided with an airship

and hired to travel away from the Spire in a specific direction, visit a specified number of unmapped cloud islands to discover their nature, and return home. The team is encouraged to locate and identify potential threats as well as resources and is paid well for any reagents or true elements that they wish to sell. Potential hazards include dangerous critters, hostile spirits, and airship pirates.

- Horizon is in need of shadowrunners to escort important guests to the premiere event on their new metaplanar island resort. The team is hired to provide bodyguard services and escort the guests through Horizon’s portal on the physical plane, then to the airship parked in their docks. The team will accompany the VIPs on their airship journey to the island destination. A passenger airship is rarely bothered by pirates, but a team of shadowrunners hired by a rival corporation isn’t going to play by the rules.

- Spinrad wants to hire a team of shadowrunners to do some metaplanar piracy. The team is provided transportation to Pirate Island, where their pirate airship awaits them. They are given a specific target to chase down and plunder, along with several locations where they might be found—and encouragement to take their own initiative. Unfortunately for them, another Spinrad pirate crew has decided they don’t want to share the skies by adding another crew to their faction, and they attempt to intercept and destroy the runners on their way back to Pirate Island.

- A team of runners is sent to the Place of Clouds by their talismonger in order to gather some true elements of a specific type. This contact is able to help the runners get to the Place of Clouds. Once there, the team will need to hire or purchase an airship to explore the cloud islands for reagents and true elements to harvest. The risk of encountering airship pirates is high.
