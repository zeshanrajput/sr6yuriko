## > **Taoist Temple**

Located in the winding maze of the heart of Chinatown, the Taoist Temple is where much of the Yellow Lotus Triad business goes down. Great bronze statues flank massive doors to an imposing, incense-smoky hall. Heavily aspected toward Taoist magic, this is where the Incense Master Su Cheng leads Yellow Lotus in nocturnal Tai Chi exercises in between cutting deals and running rituals on behalf of his “superiors.”
