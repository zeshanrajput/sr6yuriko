## Game Information

## Hong Kong Free Enterprise Zone (HKFEZ)

## About this Section

In pursuing the Kechibi Code to Hong Kong, runners will need some basic info about the sprawl. So here it is!

While this section is predominantly written in an in-universe style, it provides basic information about Hong Kong for gamemasters and players who want to bring their games there. Most of the events described in this chapter take place within the Downtown District of Hong Kong Island (Yingyong’s Offices), Kowloon City (Kanabo’s G1 production site) and the southern edge bordering it in Sha Tin (Kanabo’s G2 production site). One of the side hooks will take place in Neo-Tokyo during the recovery of hardware and files of a financial trader and decker for Shiawase (Keiretsu).
