## Ventriloquism

**Cost:** 0.25 PP

**Activation:** Major Action

An adept with this power is able to throw their voice up to (Magic) meters. While you do not appear to be speaking, you can make it appear as though the sound of your voice originates from any point that is within range and line of sight.
