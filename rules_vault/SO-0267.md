## Peacemaker

Deep down, all Peacemaker wants is for everyone to get along, even if they’re currently mortal enemies. She believes every argument can be solved in some way; on the other hand, she knows some arguments require force or bodily harm in order to get one’s point across.

## Advantages

**All:** Edge Boosts cost 1 less for Influence tests **Magician:** Edge Boosts cost 1 less for Detection spells, preparations, and rituals

**Adept:** Enhanced Perception power for free

## Disadvantages

You even see enemies as potential friends and go out of your way to keep from hurting anyone unless the situation truly warrants it.
