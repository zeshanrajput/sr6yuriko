## **How much can a flying creature or drone carry?**

Spirits and creatures, such as valkyrie and pixies, use (Strength / 2) for the maximum amount of weight they can carry while flying. Drones can carry (Body / 2) instead of Strength for flying Lift/Carry ( _SR6_ , p. 6768).
