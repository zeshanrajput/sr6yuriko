## Zapper Grenade

The zapper grenade discharges a massive pulse of electricity within its blast area. The capacitor that delivers this pulse is burned out after a single explosion, but when applied to the correct target, the electrical effects can be long-lasting.

Zapper grenades do their damage as Electrical damage (p. 109, _SR6_ ).
