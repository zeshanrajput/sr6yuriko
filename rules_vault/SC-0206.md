## DEFENSIVE DECKER

The corporate world makes wide use of deckers for defensive purposes, guarding hosts and files against intrusion, and monitoring devices for signs of tampering. Most government and corporate military teams have a defensive decker attached to them in order to protect vulnerable drones, vehicles, weapons, and augmentations. The easy access to expensive technology, high pay, and comfortable benefits available to people with such expertise makes them generally less likely to be found among shadowrunners. But shadowrun teams with a defensive decker on their side are able to handle a much wider range of jobs, without worrying so much about being detected and hacked by Matrix security. This makes you invaluable, especially to street samurai and riggers. Knowing how Matrix security works, and how security deckers think, also comes in handy when you have to hack into someone else’s drek.
