## Doomscrolling

## • **Bonus:** 4 Karma

With so much content on the Matrix to peruse, it is difficult for an AI or EI to properly take the time to do their restoration. Each day, the AI must make a Firewall + Willpower (3) test to successfully complete restoration. If they fail, they cannot gain more than 2 Edge from any source of Edge that day.
