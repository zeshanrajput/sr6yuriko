## Buzzard Transit 

More information about what Buzzard Transit is up to will come in future books. The basics of their business are legitimate; they really do have fifty vans making food, people, and package deliveries across the Omaha sprawl. Rides cost two nuyen per kilometer (minimum five nuyen), food delivery adds five nuyen to the price of your meal. 

_Note:_ The Buzzard van has two rooftop compartments that can hold Cyberspace Design Quadrotor drones, and a slot for an Aztechnology Crawler drone beneath the rear loading doors, but these drones are not included in the price of the van.
