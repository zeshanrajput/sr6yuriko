## Brume

## (Water, Force 4)

Brumes have a similar appearance as punkahs (p. 52), but they would find the comparison offensive. In their eyes, punkahs are there to serve, and brumes are to be served. They are very arrogant and do not like menial labor, as that’s for the lower castes. Most of the time, they float around the metaplane contemplating philosophy or what to serve at the next gala. One thing brumes are known for is fashion sense, relative to all the elemental planes. Brumes believe that fashion is an important gossip topic.
