## Winged

## MCT Gnat

Tiny and slow, but indistinguishable from an insect without a microscope or vision mag, the Gnat is often used in MCT’s Zero Zones, where a swarm of them all carry DMSO/Narcoject minislap patches on their feet, then land en masse on a target. They’re sold in packs of 10.
