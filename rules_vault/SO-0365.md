## Example Four

Jazz has been doing his best to convince the Halloweeners that his team had nothing to do with the killings they were blamed for. But whoever framed them did their job well, likely using the Trid Phantasm spell to create realistic illusions that were captured on video. It’s down to the last word, and Jazz’s final chance to make an Opposed Influence (Negotiation) + Charisma skill test. So far, he’s been skating by on just one or two net hits, and he’s almost out of Edge. He hasn’t been gaining Edge due to his Social Rating, his gear, or the circumstances, which have all been stacked against him. He desperately needs an advantage on this all-or-nothing, life-or-death test. But Jazz isn’t here on his own—it’s time for a Teamwork test! As part of the final test, Opti joins in to speak on their behalf. Opti has a bit of history with the Halloweeners (some good, some less so), and he’s picked up a Halloweeners Gang knowledge skill. When Opti makes his Influence + Charisma test to assist Jazz, Opti gets a circumstantial point of Edge due to his specific knowledge. This doesn’t grant Jazz any Edge on his test, but hopefully Opti can score some hits and get Jazz some bonus dice.

After the dice fall and the Edge are spent, Opti gets four hits, which grant Jazz four bonus dice. It’s a good thing, too, because Jazz barely manages to succeed on the main Influence test. The Halloweeners have begun to doubt that Jazz’s team is responsible for the killings. But if the runners want to completely clear their names, they’ll have to help the gangers find out who was really responsible. So much for their weekend.
