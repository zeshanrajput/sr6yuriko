## **The Important Guests**

**The Refugee : A being from a different metaplane who was working as a scout and intelligence gatherer for the Disian High Council throughout the ’60s and ’70s and into the early ’80s. An individual with abilities most similar to a technomancer, he sought his freedom and arranged for self-extraction when an opportunity arose in Denver. A team associated with IMA Hellcats, holding a contract in Seattle, eventually secured him. The ambassador broke the bonds on the Refugee, extracting as payment a period of service. Known to some as “Ref,” the being now puts in time working on tasks and helping with the creation of known metaplanar maps and knowable forms of navigation used by various metaplanar species.**

**Denizen: A former otaku, Donatello Villasnosquez was active online working in tandem with his tribe when he was taken captive by individuals working for Hengester, the organization that aided Danielle de la Mar during the formation of what would eventually become the Foundation-based Matrix in the ’70s. Denizen himself was not physically extracted, but he became separated from his tribe after escaping through its Resonance well during the struggle. The street doc he was stashed with at the time kept watch over his physical body and continued to do so even though his return kept taking longer and longer. His time away from himself and the Matrix of the world has caused him to act with extreme caution toward others, and he now fnds it easier to interact with Matrix and Deep Resonant entities. He is not an e-ghost, and his body has been transported to Taos in the Pueblo Corporate Council.**

**Onstage: The true guest, this is their frst time on JackPoint, but they are a long-time social media infuencer in music. Their real name is Cristo Montbalc, a native of Aztlan and lead vocalist and writer for the music sensation Metro Brix, a synthpunk/balladeer-rock band. During a dry spell, Montbalc was lucky enough to somehow garner the attention of Psyche, who aided with the inspiration for the album** _**The Tone of Tides**_ **.**

**Mr. Bonds: A long-time contributor to JackPoint, Mr. Bonds, along with the user Snopes, decided to intervene on behalf of a guest contributor who was doing a workup of his own discovery of the Kechibi code. While in possession of the code, the two users not only retrieved the user known as Rikki, but they also discovered the code has the ability to seek out and reconstruct damaged personalities of individuals suffering from significant psychological trauma. This is a detail they have shared only with Butch up to this point, and during the conversation here they do not elaborate on that to any length. As neither Mr. Bonds nor Snopes are Emergent individuals, they were unaware and thus unable to observe the full de-**

**tails. Butch, on the other hand, has several associates she has worked with during her own pursuits surrounding the CFD-causing nanovirus and its impacts.**

**The Orange Queen: The great dragon Hestaby is currently an outcast in dragon society. She has retained her presence on JackPoint through favors exchanged with others, including Marko. As she is currently not allowed to own any material forms of wealth, property, or servants, she can only do business through net-zero exchanges without suffering reprisal. Even Marko was surprised when Kechibi added her to the list of individuals he should get help from. An exchange was made as part of a bet. With other information he had from his use of the code, he knew that if he could get individuals with powerful intellects to join the forum, it would elevate the chances of appearance by a member of the Null Sect, with whom he could start a transactional relationship. What Hestaby didn’t know was the level of understanding and knowledge the Null had acquired on a subject very near and dear to dragon society: details regarding the Loremaster, much of which they got from their own attempts to study Psyche. This information is a form of exchange well worth the cost of one year’s talon clippings she gave to Marko after the forum, as he won the bet. She now has a copy of the uploaded fle.**

**Marko: Marko is one of the Yakut ambassadors at large, an owning partner of the mercenary group known as the Hellcats, and a veteran of the shadows since ’49. Marko is in possession of one of the famed tarot cards detailed in the** _**Book of the Lost**_ **. He indicates that he is in possession of the Matrix card (p. 113,** _**Book of the Lost**_ **) to the JackPoint admins in a private space. He plans to use it at the appropriate time to aid him in reconstructing the mind and spirit of his former companion, Atlas, who became lost during a journey across the Astral Sea (p. 3,** _**Aetherology**_ **). The physical body has been preserved, and reunifcation is his goal. Another source has informed him that Atlas was seen in the service of a member of the Seelie Court, in the role of a coimeádaí, a messenger individual who is compatible with certain Matrix technologies.**

**Explorer TU625: As indicated by the guest persona it presents, this is a member of the Null Explorer class of entities. The administrators of JackPoint have improved and secured the shadow host, with new protocols allowing them to detect and even identify utility operations of a Null that enters the host. What they haven’t been able to do is completely hide the access of the host, due in large part to the recognized user base. That’s something they will never be able to completely secure.**

The fles it provides in the upload are details it has learned of Psyche’s pursuits and interests in this universe. The fles include data gathered by other users of Kechibi, something that can be discovered with a successful Browse (5) action of each fle.**

**Cybil: Similar to how Explorer TU625 works, the monad who was once Cynthia Bills of Evo Corporation also attended the forum while diverting much of her nanovolume to improving her stealth capabilities. She has been attempting to trace numerous Matrix users, one of them being the monad known as Rikki, who works for Taylor Dacopral, a current member of the Evo board of directors. She was in the process of downloading a copy of the fles when the forum was shut down.**
