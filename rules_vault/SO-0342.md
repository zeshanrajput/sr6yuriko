## Game Information

The swindler stick appears to be a certified credstick with a specified value, though it actually contains no nuyen. When anyone attempts to transfer funds from the swindler stick, its true value will be revealed. Another way to discover its counterfeit nature is to analyze it using an electronics hardware toolkit, which takes one minute and requires only a single hit on an Engineering or Electronics + Logic test.
