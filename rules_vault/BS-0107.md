## Dream Link

A dream link creates simsense records of neural activity associated with thought. This augmentation allows its user to replay and thereby re-experience dreams, which is entertaining for some individuals and useful in psychotherapy for others. Dream links are sometimes used as an insidious control mechanism. Few people have control over what they dream, so regularly monitored dream links can give prisons, bunraku parlors, and other such equally distasteful institutions intel about the goings-on among their captives such as escape plots, gang affairs, and so on. Dream links can also be employed while awake to journal active thought for later replay. A dream link can store up to eight hours of generated simsense data.

**Wireless bonus:** A dream link can export saved thought to an external device such as a commlink or replay an externally saved recording.
