## July 09, 2083

All Disian forces are recalled and move into defensive positions around the Harvester Network. Using their new cypher, the Defenders are able to finally crack the Disian codes and develop methods to safely eliminate and shut down the Harvester Network. These methods will vary depending on regional forces, their capabilities, and the threat they pose, but MCT has also put a tremendous amount of money and material into the Harvesters and their defenses.
