## **Runt**

**This critter is particularly small, perhaps caused by a fuke of genetics or malnutrition in its youth.**

- **Bonus: 4 Karma**

- **Game Effect: Whenever this critter rolls Body to resist damage, ignore one of the hits scored.**

**Hardened Critter Armor: Training a critter to wear hardened armor requires an additional special training above and beyond armor training. This armor is bulky and obvious, and it restricts the critter’s motion in exchange for providing signifcant protection. Without the requisite special training, a critter wearing this armor gains the Immobilized condition. Even with special training, a critter wearing this armor gains the Hobbled condition and cannot fy or swim. Hardened critter armor provides +6 to Defense Rating, +4 dice to Body tests to resist damage, and two automatic hits on damage resistance tests. It can also accept armor modifcations.**
