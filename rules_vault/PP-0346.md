## Help Wanted

## Copyright Enforcement

That’s a euphemism. Spinrad Global doesn’t want people copying their stuff, and they don’t want to let them off with something as light as a cease-and-desist order or lawsuit if they make an attempt. Spinrad wants factories smashed, designs erased, and possibly careers ruined if the lesson needs to be severe enough. All of that, of course, requires runners. These jobs can be a lot of fun if you like breaking stuff, and who doesn’t?
