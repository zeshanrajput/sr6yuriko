## **How much Karma can I expect to earn from the average** _**Missions**_ **adventure?**

As with nuyen, Karma awards are based loosely on the **Run Compensation** guidelines ( _SR6_ , p. 243). The type of _Mission_ and its difficulty will factor into the Karma awards, along with various measures of success. The average adventure awards between 6 and 8 Karma.
