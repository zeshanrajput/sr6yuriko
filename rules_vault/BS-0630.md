## Negative Qualities

## Chronic Dissociation Syndrome (CDS)

CDS is the most common psychological ailment affecting those with excessive augmentation. The primary symptom is a sense of indifference and detachment toward one’s surroundings, relations, and interests. Even a functional Invoked Memory Stimulator will not stop the intense apathy and depression associated with CDS taking hold.

- **Bonus:** 10 Karma

- **Game Effect:** Must have less than 3 Essence. These characters suffer a –3 penalty on any Willpower tests other than Spellcasting and Damage Resistance tests.
