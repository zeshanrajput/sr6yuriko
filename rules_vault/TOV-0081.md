## shadows in focus

**ence + Charisma vs. Force x 2 test. The summoner gains net hits in lwa services, limited by** the net hits of the initial Conjuring test. When the spirit has completed their services and departs, the summoner makes a drain resistance test to resist (Force) damage. If the Force is greater than the summoner’s Magic, the damage is Physical. Otherwise, the damage is Stun.
