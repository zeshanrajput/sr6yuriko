## The Kingdom of Hawai’i

Seattle’s move has created a lot of unrest among the Pacific nations. The representatives of Hawai’i are looking to take advantage of the shifts and potentially pull more traffic through their ports. Most suspect it’s the influence of local dragons, but without ears inside those political meetings, we won’t get chiptruth, just screamsheet salaciousness.
