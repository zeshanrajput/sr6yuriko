## Hello World!

- **Cost:** 8 Karma

AIs with this quality have an ingrained set of sanity-checking measures that help prevent Fragmentation after disruption. AIs with Hello World gain a point of Edge on any tests to resist Spark loss. The point of Edge must be spent on the Resistance test, or it is lost.
