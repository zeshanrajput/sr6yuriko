## **Are gamemasters going to use Contact types (6WC, p. 160-162) when doing legwork or acquiring gear?**

Yes. Just because a contact doesn’t have a specific type, doesn’t mean they don’t know anything about a given topic or can’t help you out. They just aren’t as good at what you are asking them to do. Another way to look at it is that the areas listed are where that contact specializes. One missing connection type is Shadow, but this is implicit that all the contacts have at least one shadow connection which is you. The contact type for SRM connections can be found in Appendix C.
