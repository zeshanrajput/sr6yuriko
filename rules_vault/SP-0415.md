## **GMT Alloy Arrowheads**

## **GMT Alloy Coating**

**Arrowheads, crossbow bolt heads, throwing knives, and shuriken can be constructed using the GMT alloy.**

**A close combat weapon is coated with a thin layer of the grey mana alloy. This is less expensive than constructing a weapon using the alloy, but its effectiveness degrades quickly with use.**
