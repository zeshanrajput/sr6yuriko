## Trucks and Vans

## Toyota Gopher

The transformation of the Gopher from one of the most laughed-at trucks on the road to one of the pinnacle pickups of the late ’70s was a long road of design, power, and feature upgrades. Now flaunted as _the_ pickup to own, the Gopher offers off-road capability, great storage, and a roomy four-door cabin with plenty of creature comforts to go around.
