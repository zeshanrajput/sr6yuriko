## Fixing the Shell

If someone is in the situation where their aura doesn’t match their physical body, that mismatch causes an Essence “hole.” This has been known for years. In some cases, people remove cyberware and then put additional cyberware in the hole, which is efficient in that the aura is not damaged twice when an upgrade or replacement is needed. We have also found that the hole can be filled by using cyber- or bioware that re-aligns a person’s aura with their body.

This is revolutionary, so let me explain. When the implant brings the outer body into sync with the inner Essence, the Essence hole is reduced; in fact, the augmentation can fill the Essence hole, restoring the aura to its original state (or close to it). For example, if someone is, in Essence, a different metatype or gender, and they have an implant or cosmetic work to adjust them to their internal state, that might actually restore lost Essence rather than (as normally happens) creating a new hole.

Exactly what is covered here is still being investigated. Some of the easy day-to-day surgeries, such as elf-ears, tusks, dermal implants, or changing gender are definitely covered. Other, more complex modifications, such as implanted weapons, data-jacks, and bone-lacing, are definitely not. There are even situations where there may be a mix.
