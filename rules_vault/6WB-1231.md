## Hidden arm slide

Attached to the forearm and worn under clothing, this slide can fit a Hold-out, Light Pistol, or Taser. With a Minor Action and a specific movement, the slide moves the weapon into your hand. The use of a hidden arm slide provides a bonus Edge when first used. It also increases the Concealability threshold by 1.

**Wireless bonus:** You get a bonus Minor Action on any turn when you activate the slide.
