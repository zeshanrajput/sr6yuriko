## Crafting with True Air

True Air stone can be thrown into a kiln or desiccator as part of crafting a focus to dry it out. With this kind of preparation, the focus cannot be bonded to anyone else after the initial bond. Additionally, the artificer can imbue one additional attribute into it, selected from the list below.
