## Transmission Break

## Main Location: Lichtenberg

## Notable Characters: Broadcast Pirates (use Cutters Data Harvester, p. 205)

The Funkhaus Berlin was the largest radio station of East Berlin in the Cold War era. At the time of the Flux State, it was taken over by broadcast pirates, who reactivated the old technology and since then spread the neo-anarchist message all over Berlin and into the world. That does not suit the class enemy from the corporate sectors—above all, DeMeKo—at all.

// berlin city data


The now-named Free Funkhaus F-Ever (FFF) is well secured and has already survived various attacks. Maybe the runners can at least blow the pirate transmitters out of the air for the time being.

**Original Message:** Mr. Schmidt—who reeks of DeMeKo 100 meters into the wind—wants you to disrupt operations at FFF. Broadcast antennas, computers, recording studios, solar panels, generators—the more you break, the more pay beckons. But don’t let anyone know who you are—or book your ticket from Berlin already.
