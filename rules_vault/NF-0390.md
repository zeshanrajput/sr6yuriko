## GRANNY SWEETSPELL’S SNICKERDOODLES

These sweet and sticky cookies are so delightful that you should share them with everyone. Even your worst enemy will become a dear friend when you gather around these wonderful treats—enhanced, as always, by a touch of Granny Sweetspell magic.
