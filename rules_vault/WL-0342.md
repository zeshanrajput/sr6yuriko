## **North American gargoyle**

## **(Gargoyle saxi)**

**The frst type of gargoyle discovered was the North American gargoyle, which typically weighs 200 kilograms and stands 2.0 meters with skin like marble. Though called North American gargoyles, they can be found in both North and South America. Because they tend to be the most socialized of the gargoyles, their groups consists of three to six adults. Displaying an increase in Awakened abilities, they also are adept at protecting themselves when facing off against Awakened critters or metahumans.**
