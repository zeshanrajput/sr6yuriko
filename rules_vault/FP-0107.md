## **Ghostwalker**

**While the White Wyrm has a checkered history with the Denver Data Haven, he was one of the primary patrons from 2062 until 2074. After a four-year period of opposing the Nexus, even attempting to destroy it on two separate occasions, it now appears that he is once again offering to support the organization. It’s reported that tens of millions of nuyen have been funneled into the data haven through the dragon’s agent, a hacker using the name Spyder. Less direct support also appears to be back in place, such as operating without government opposition and the unoffcial protection from hostile outside organizations.**
