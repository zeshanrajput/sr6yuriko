## Wooden Gun (I)

The threat of violence can cause an opponent to react differently, though that can be tricky when you aren’t carrying a piece. Spend a Minor Action and make a Stealth (Palming) + Agility vs. Perception + Intuition Opposed test. If you succeed, you gain a +2 dice pool bonus on an Influence (Intimidate) test against them to imply you have a weapon on hand, ready to use, even if you don’t.
