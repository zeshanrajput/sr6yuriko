## Look and Feel

Lastly, the runners will need to discover the look that Becky was going for and the final feel as well. This is all about the fabric sensations of the dress. Gu can reproduce the material with the help of a nanoprinter and his software, along with Becky’s production notes, all of which he brought with him.

_“The fabric is art itself, we called it_ _**Memoryweave** . It has all the properties of electrochromation, photoluminescence, and memorythreads.”_ <noticing people being confused> _“It can change colors, shapes, and generate its own light. It reacts to the wearer entirely, using embedded simsense trodes, to obtain information as well as always scanning the wearer to better respond to biological needs such as warming and cooling. It remembers the skin and neural pathways of the wearer’s epidermis and encodes to them.”_
