## Liberty and Ellis Island

The Statue of Liberty, symbol of the UCAS, is brought to you by Horizon. They own Liberty Island and neighboring Ellis Island as well. In 2072, Horizon completed construction of a highspeed maglev that runs between the two islands. The following year, Horizon re-opened the two islands under the name of Virtual World Liberty Park, a theme park featuring AR reenactments of key moments from the American Revolutionary War. The islands have quickly become a prime NYC destination, drawing tourists from around the world. Local historians, notably those in

the employ of Ares, question whether the history being depicted is accurate to what happened during that time or a curated experience designed to highlight what the corporation would like to push as “American History.”
