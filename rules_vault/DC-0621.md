## Dead Stop

## (Outdoors \[Navigation\[)

When pursuing, make an Outdoors (Navigation) + Intuition vs. Outdoors (Navigation) + Intuition Opposed test. If you succeed, the chase target’s understanding of the area fails them and they make a wrong turn into a dead end. This ends the chase. At least one combat round must pass before a new chase can be initiated.

**Cost:** 5 Edge
