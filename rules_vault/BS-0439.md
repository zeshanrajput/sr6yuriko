## Casanova

- **Vector:** Slot

- **Speed:** 1 combat round

- **Duration:** (Rating) hours

- **Power:** 8 + (Rating)

- **Effect:** –2 Willpower, +1 Intuition, +2 Charisma, Con 2 (Seduction +2), Influence 1

The Casanova personafix makes the user more in tune with their passions and relaxes their inhibitions. While using the BTL, they are more susceptible to persuasion and more likely to indulge in additional vices, requiring a Composure (3) test to avoid partaking. They are interested in pleasing those around them and believe that everyone wants them. Users have a false sense of security that nothing bad will befall them. When the BTL wears off, the user gains the Dazed status for the next (rating) hours.
