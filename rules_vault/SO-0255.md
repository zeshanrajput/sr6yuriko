## Sharp Tongue

## **Cost:** 1.0 PP

## **Activation:** Major Action

This power allows an adept to fire off a quick barb that strikes a target so deeply that the mere words leave a mark on their aura. The adept rolls Charisma + Magic vs. Intuition + Willpower, with net hits becoming unresisted Stun damage to the target. The target must be within line of sight and able to understand what the adept is saying. Instead of inflicting damage, the adept may choose to stun the target before attempting the roll. If the adept gains net hits equal to or greater than the target’s Willpower, the target gains the Dazed status (p. 52, _SR6_ ).
