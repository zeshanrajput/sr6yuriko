## Triads

A few years back, the Large Circle Triad found themselves tied up in a nasty fight between Wuxing and Prometheus Engineering. That corporate war didn’t go Wuxing’s way. Prometheus, being on the MDC and friendly with NYPD, Inc., was able to bring pressure down on the Triad. Senior members of the Large Circle who survived the encounter found they were short on allies—until the Red Dragon Society swooped in to help.

- There are conflicting stories on what actually went down here. Some people say the Red Dragon Society helped break up the Large Circle Triad in order to expand their power. Others believe the Large Circle reached out first, offering fealty to Lung in exchange for protection from the NYPD, Yakuza, and Mafia who had all declared open season on them.

- Jimmy No

The New York Triad is now a mix of Large Circle members supported by the weight of the Red Dragon Society. They continue to be information brokers and supply BTLs exclusively from Hong Kong.
