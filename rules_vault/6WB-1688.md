## 1.2 Kilometers above the Anarcho Zone Kreuzhain, 23:26:45

Unlike Sigorski’s ground team, the drone had not lost the combat cab when a well-placed Molotov cocktail smashed into the front of the Roadmaster, blinding the team’s sensors. This would soon change: The cab had entered Kreuzhain and would dive into the maze of side streets covered by truck tarp, camouflage nets, and whatever the alternatives could muster to fend off any kind of real and imagined corporate surveillance from above.

Berlin city data //


For now, Sigorski could do little more than ping the point where the cab disappeared to his team. The drone would circle below the rain clouds and wait for the cab to resurface.

He didn’t have to wait long.
