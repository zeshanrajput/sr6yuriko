## Ocean (elf, female, 28, blue hair, blue eyes, thin build)

Nothing is ever as it seems with Ocean around. It’s a rare moment when she does not have at least one illusion spell active, if not several. She prefers trickery over direct force and regularly creates more problems by manipulating perceptions than Heller ever does throwing around lightning. When met in person, she’s aloof and flows in and out of conversations.

When the ambush is set, she prepares a series of illusions to distract her opponents and disguise her allies. She will create illusions of police or disguise the presence of authorities to capture or distract her enemies. She makes walls look like roads, alleys look like walls (often reinforcing the illusion with a physical barrier spell), and generally disrupts the world in a way that should keep an enemy or two from getting their bearings.

She will send spirits into the fray once it has started but usually commands them to stay safe and disengage if they become too injured. She does not risk them, as she prefers to keep a good relationship with the spirit world.
