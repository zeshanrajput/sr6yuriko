## **Bonus** _:_ See text

Individuals with this quality have expressed metagenes that catalyze and even feed on their darker emotions and negative feelings, disturbing the ambient mana in their vicinity. The character stands in the middle of a mobile low mana ebb (p. 167, _Street Wyrd_ ) aspected toward the individual. The radius of this ebb in meters is equal to the Essence + 1 of the individual. The ebb will expand over time, if the individual spends at least ten hours per day in a single location, at a rate of +2 meters per week, with a maximum of +10 meters. For non-Awakened characters, this quality has a bonus of 8 Karma. For Awakened individuals, this quality has a bonus of 15 Karma, and the radius is equal to their Magic attribute.
