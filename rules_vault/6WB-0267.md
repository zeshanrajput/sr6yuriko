## New Spells

Learning a new spell requires a spell formula, which generally can be purchased from a talismonger. You also need access to a magical lodge (see p. 129), some time to study and practice the intricacies of the casting, and a final ritual to lock the spell into the spellcaster’s aura. After about a week of study (p. 131), the spell is ingrained into the spellcaster’s mind well enough they’ll never forget it. The astral energies can then be used both for spellcasting (p. 131) and enchanting (p. 150). The spell costs 5 Karma.
