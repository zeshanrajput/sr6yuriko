## Orikomu Kumo, Mita-gumi, Neo-Tokyo

Orikomu Kumo is a relatively new member of the Mita-gumi clan. I don’t know much about his past, but from what I heard, he started in BTL distribution and moved into creation. BTLs are nasty things, but when you look at how they are crafting them today and what you could craft if you have an advanced data mining and manipulation program, you can see how tempting it might be. So Kumo got his hands on the code and was able to produce a few dozen prototype chips. Luckily, he wasn’t able to create any more, since someone went in and destroyed the code, but there are still some chips running Kechibi Code-generated simsense that could drop onto the streets of Neo-Tokyo.

- I have a Tanaka-san in Neo-Tokyo willing to hire a local runner team immediately to find those chips. Contact me if you are interested.
