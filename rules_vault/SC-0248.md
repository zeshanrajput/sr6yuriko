## Additional character creation recommendations:

- **Attributes:** Charisma 5+, Willpower 4+

- **Dissonance aptitude:** Any

- **Skills:** Influence (Intimidation)

- **Knowledge skills:** Art Appreciation, Mainstream Media, Underground Art

- **Complex forms:** Editor, Puppeteer, Tattletale, Static Veil

- **Positive qualities:** First Impression, Sprite

Affinity (the sprite type that matches your Dissonance aptitude)

- **Negative qualities:** Dissonance Rage, Dissonance Static, Dissonance Stigma

- **Life modules:** Artist, Entertainer, Journalist, Matrix Vandal, Event: You learned a harsh truth about the world.
