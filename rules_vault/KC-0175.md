## Wen Xun Lee

Their security chief is Wen Xun Lee, a younger human male with a few unusual traits. Some call him Nà zhi mao (the Cat) for his naturally gifted vision (he can see in the dark) and inhuman agility. Lee excels at his job of site security. Site personnel who know him have come to recognize a shift in his posture when he’s on the hunt for intruders, and they know to stay out of his way when they see it.
