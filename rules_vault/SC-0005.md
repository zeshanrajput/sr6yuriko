## academic mage

As an academic mage, you seek to understand and quantify the mysterious forces that are called “magic.” You approach your practice of magic with techniques and systems, seeking to make the process more predictable while gaining greater knowledge and mastery over the unknown. Many are scientists, and most follow hermetic traditions, but some shamans have been known to walk this path.
