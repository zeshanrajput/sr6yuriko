## shadow stock 

## Loyal Saeder-Krupp Executive Protection Agent 

## Metatype: Troll Professional Rating 5 

Everyone thinks being an adept is a piece of cake, but it’s _never_ easy growing up a troll. Everyone underestimates you mentally, even if they appreciate you physically. Esmerelda Takagi eventually found a place where she’s admired for both her brains and her brawn: close protection work. Executives enjoy the intimidation factor and status that having a troll bodyguard on retainer brings them, but _smart_ executives understand that a good specialist is more than just muscle. Saeder-Krupp has treated Takagi well, and she’s treating her assigned corporate agents well in return. She knows she’s got it good—she just has to keep proving that she deserves it.
