## Expanding the Network

Adding more contacts is essential to expanding your social network, but it takes special effort. The maximum number of contacts you may include in your network is limited by the number of ranks you have in the Influence skill. You must have a Loyalty rating of 3 with a contact before they can be incorporated within your network, and their Connection rating may not be higher than the highest Connection rating among your network contacts, or lower than 3.

You must then do some sort of important favor for the new contact that demonstrates the benefits of having you as a contact. Work with your GM to see if they have an idea for a special run you might partake for the contact. Alternatively, raising their Connection rating would certainly count (which you might have to do anyway for them to be eligible). If you and your GM would rather satisfy the requirement without taking up precious game time, two weeks of time and spending 4,000 nuyen and 2 Karma would be sufficient to cover this effort.
