## **anti-disian pows**

**Being a hotbed of metahuman trafcking, Disian agents over the past year have used Lagos and the surrounding areas as POW camps for those who have been captured by Disian forces or have proven to be problematic to the Disian cause. Through Evo and Horizon’s eforts, Grey Cell has learned of the existence of these camps and is currently working on obtaining actionable data for possible rescue missions.**
