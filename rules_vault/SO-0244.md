These spells are a less intrusive form of Mind Probe (p. 135, _SR6_ ). The caster gives the subject the ability to scan a target’s surface thoughts for a particular subject (word, phrase, sound, or image chosen when the spell is cast). This spell does not dig through a person’s mind for information, it merely verifies if a target is actively thinking about a particular subject. Roll Sorcery + Magic vs. Willpower + Logic. The spell is often used in conjunction with interrogation or conversations to determine if a target has knowledge related to a particular subject. For example, an investigator could use it to determine if a target knows anything about a murder weapon, or a con artist could use it to determine if a target is reacting.

Thought Recognition is used on one particular target, while Area Though Recognition scans the minds of everyone within the area.
