## Behemoth

If you ever wondered what would happen if you crossed a hippopotamus with an alligator, then wonder no more. The behemoth is a paraspecies of the common alligator and stands three meters high at the shoulder and roughly fi ve meters long. These creatures are dangerous when encountered due to being territorial. Besides their unusual shape, they are known to have a shorter snout with large tusks and horns protruding from their face.
