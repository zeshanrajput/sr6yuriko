## **Tremor Sense**

**==> picture [228 x 20] intentionally omitted \<==**

**----- Start of picture text -----**<br>
TYPE ACTION RANGE DURATION<br>P Auto Self Always<br>**----- End of picture text -----**<br>

**Creatures with this ability can detect subtle movements of the earth. The critter makes a Perception + Intuition test to detect movement even when in complete darkness just by sensing vibrations through the ground. The critter can sense when something is moving nearby (within Intuition meters), but not exactly where, and not when they’re standing still.**

**The only way to remove the Irradiated status is through medical treatment in a facility. This can be treated as an outpatient service, and it reduces the Irradiated status number by 1 per treatment. This treatment is covered under DocWagon Gold or higher, or at a cost of 500 nuyen per treatment.**
