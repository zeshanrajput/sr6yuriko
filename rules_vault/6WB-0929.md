## Professional Rating 2

Ancients. Cutters. Sons of Sauron. Halloweeners. These are names feared for something more than just random violence (well, maybe that’s exactly why the Halloweeners are feared). They are professional gangs and organizations designed to be the more militant arm of nicer policlubs. They’ve gotten more time under fire, but they’re still not pros, and they all have enough ego and rep that they push themselves a little harder to keep in the fight.
