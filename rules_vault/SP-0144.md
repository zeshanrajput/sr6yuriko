## **Evo**

**Renraku’s staunch Japanese conservatism and Evo’s progressive stances mix as well as gasoline and fre. There are also many within Renraku who see the corporation that was once Yamatetsu as having turned their back on the Japanese homeland, which is still galling to a lot of Renraku board members even to this day. But the fact that Evo stepped up and is actively working against the Disians has earned them at least the smallest shred of respect and cooperation from Renraku. And despite Evo offering all the support it can to other anti-Disian allies, Renraku has politely (but** _**frmly**_ **) refused any such aid.**
