## Quality Path: Chwal

Some adherents of Voodoo head down the route of becoming a horse, channeling the divine lwa to help communicate with other devotees. This is called being a _chwal_ or “horse” because it refers to the lwa “mounting” the serviteur. This is a vital role in the rituals of Voodoo allowing the lwa to guide and channel energies on behalf of the worshippers. This is their avenue to assisting humans as a guide or healing. **Game Effect:** You must have the Serviteur Quality from this path. Spirits who possess a chwal gain an additional service and do not need to make a possession test. If you break the tenets of the religion, you lose the Chwal quality, but this does not result in the loss of the Serviteur quality. You will need to re-purchase the quality before progressing down the path.
