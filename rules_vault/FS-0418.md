## Jeet Kune Do/ Mixed Martial Arts

The style of Jeet Kune Do was first developed by the legendary martial artist Bruce Lee, combining elements of many existing martial arts styles under a philosophy of “whatever works”— the same philosophy applied by practitioners of mixed martial arts, who often study both grappling and striking styles (jujutsu and Muay Thai is a popular combination) to ensure their effectiveness in the ring.

_Similar Styles_ : Hapkido (Korea) _Categories:_ Grappling, Striking _Signature Technique_ : Sweep the Leg
