## A Note on Drones and Seating

All drones are vehicles, but not all vehicles are drones. One difference is Seating, but it’s more than just the actual seat. Drones, even large drones, have a minimal suspension that’s nothing like the suspension that goes into passenger vehicles. Drones also do not move with any regard for metahuman comfort or G-force tolerances. Seating may never be added to a drone of any size. The only exception to this is inside a Valkyrie Module on a drone with medevac modifications.
