## Metaplanar Districts

The Spire has been divided up into various districts, organized by originating metaplane—Kin, Beasts, Faerie, and the elemental planes. The air and water districts are the largest, each taking up nearly a third of the city, and the remaining planes share roughly equal representation of the remaining third. The district of kin is slightly larger than the other less-represented metaplanes, but it is usually the busiest part of the entire city. This is due to the recent influx of metaplanar travelers, which began as a trickle early in the Awakening but has begun to attract the interest of megacorporations and powerful magical groups. All of the Big Ten megacorps, as well as the Draco Foundation, the Atlantean Foundation, and the Astral Space Preservation Society, have been granted permission from the bureaucracy to construct and occupy an embassy within the Spire—most of these are located within the kindred district.
