## Manasense

## **Cost:** 8 Karma

The individual possesses the ability to sense any type of active or passive aetheric (magic) energy, including spell usage, in their vicinity. The individual uses an Astral + Intuition test, using the Detection Spells Results table on p. 134, _SR6_ . The range of this sense is (Magic + Intuition) meters, and it can be increased with a simple Willpower test, adding one meter for every hit.
