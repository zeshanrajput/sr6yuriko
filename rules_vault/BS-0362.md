## overdose example

Orbital Burn is headed out to a social event later in the evening. Knowing he needs a little chemical assistance at the party, he grabs a couple doses of novacoke. He takes the first dose now, assuming it will hold up for the entire time he is there. He spends some quality time at the party and loses track of time. Before he knows it, he feels the crash coming on, so he heads to the bedroom to grab a second dose right before crashing. He rolls the Body (2) test, getting two hits and avoids overdosing. When the novacoke crash ends up hitting, it drops him to Charisma 0 and Willpower 0, but his second dose brings that back up to 1 for each. If he had failed the overdose check, he would have had to resist 12S damage from the novacoke.
