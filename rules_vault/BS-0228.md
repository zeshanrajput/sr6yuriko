## Frog Tongue

A spool of muscles is placed under the tongue, not unlike a frog’s tongue, only more dangerous. With a range of only one meter, the frog tongue is a melee weapon but is covered by the Exotic Weapon Skill rather than Unarmed Combat.

Frog tongues are often connected to chemical reserves but not always.

The individual gains a long, adhesive tongue that extends in a spring-loaded fashion, shooting outward to strike at a target and then retract. Not prehensile enough to use anything as a tool, the tongue is strong enough to snatch an object, provided the weight is less than a quarter kilogram. Because of the tongue’s sticky secretions, the object will stick to the tongue until manually removed (Body + Strength [2]). If the character also possesses the Natural Venom quality (p. 119, _Sixth World Companion_ ) the tongue can be used to apply the substance per those rules with an Exotic Weapon (Tongue) + Agility attack, AR 8/–/–/–/–.
