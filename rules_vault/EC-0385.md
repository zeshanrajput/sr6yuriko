## orting sites

## Orting Underground

Large pockets of space from buried barns, warehouses, schools, etc. created a catacomb of sorts beneath the ash field. Transient squatters have found shelter here, through abandoned sewage tunnels or rappelling down via the “headstones.” Orting’s dark and buried pockets have also attracted ghouls and the like, as it’s a close enough den for hunting in Puyallup.
