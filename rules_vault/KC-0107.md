## Omahla Adegoke

Omahla is the Yoruba representative on the Lagos Council, taking the place of the venerable Olabode Lekan just last year. He was placed on the council by the Yoruba Oni, Adegoke, his father, but few are foolish enough to call it any form of nepotism. Omahla is a social adept trained at the schools of Horizon. His return to Lagos saw him gently take over his predecessor’s network of spies and informants. He is an attractive ork in his mid-twenties, and while young, all are looking to him as the next possible Oni. But even if he does not get the throne from his father, he will be a force on the Lagos Council for decades to come. In the deal that forced his predecessor to step down, Omahla also gained the elder statesman’s little black book, full of knowledge that could be used to blackmail other council members into agreeing with his plans, ensuring the Yoruba maintain their powerful position. He is generally liked thus far,

but he will almost certainly build a lengthy list of enemies in the years to come.

Omahla does not want to see the power of the Yoruba threatened, and he sees Okoro as a potential threat to the longstanding relationships they have with the corporations around Lagos.
