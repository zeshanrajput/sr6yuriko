## **> Sounder**

**So to prevent trawling through all of JackPoint looking for the** _**Howling Shadows**_ **download, I shall simply say that meistersingers have the ability to trigger our reptile brains and produce a heightened fear response, can control fsh and marine mammals, and use their immense bulk and sonic attacks to destroy ships. And they do destroy ships, those that harm the balance of the world’s oceans, either through contaminating/polluting the seas or by taking too much from the waters.**

**Meistersingers move in pods of up to ffteen members as they hunt or mate. Females have a gestation period of twelve months, and they can produce offspring every two years. Normal humpback whales have been found to live over two hundred years. Meistersingers are posited to live longer than that.**
