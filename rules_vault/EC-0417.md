## Black Rains

The all-ork gang of Carbonado have seen better days. Their leader, a wily ork named Billy Mura, finally met his match in Ranger Wallace. Being publicly dragged out of town put the rest of the gangs on notice that the law has come to Carbonado. The second in command, Torque, took to the tunnels into Hell’s Kitchen to regroup, but Ranger Wallace hasn’t given him a chance to breathe. One by one, Wallace has been taking out Torque’s scouts, pushing him deeper into Hell’s Kitchen.

- Much of what the Black Rains did was bully the people and control the illegal market within Carbonado. Even the Chulos had to give a cut to sell here. With the Black Rains chased out of town, there’s a bit of a power vacuum. Granted, the Gianelli family has their own arrangements through Carbonado, but that was before the train was operational.

- LSPD
