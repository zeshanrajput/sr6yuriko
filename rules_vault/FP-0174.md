_**Arthur Vogel**_ **: You have to think that the man whose job Bishop took has some bad feelings. He feels betrayed, he has cash in hand, and he’s known to hold a grudge. He’s not granted any interviews since his ouster and is just stewing in North Virginia for now, since he can’t even get back into Ares’ space facilities. The unanswered questions are these: What does he want to do next in his life, how long will it take him to recover his strength, and who does he blame for his situation? More - than a few people think Bishop is just a sacrif cial pawn, pushed to make a play on the board while the queen, Sharon Aurelius, remains safe and sound. Let Bishop take the heat, fre him when he fails (or dab away a tear when he dies), and replace him with whatever pawn’s next in line. This is a minority view, but it’s out there. But does Vogel agree with it? If he does, he’ll blame Aurelius, not Bishop, and his wrath will follow. Then again, maybe he’d just lost sight of the goal once he was in the seat of power, and a few months away from that pressure and obligation to get his mind and body healed will show him that he’s been freed, not fred, and he can go back to enjoying life. Only time will tell.**

_**Clayton Wilson**_ **: He was in the running for CEO, but he got outmaneuvered by King at every turn. Once he found out that his big fear was going to come true, with Knight Errant being folded into Ares Services rather than remaining a division of its own, he turned in his gun and badge. He certainly blames King for this, and he’ll be after her frst and foremost. Bishop? He doesn’t care for him, but being beaten by a man is something he can handle. Losing to a woman? No, sir, he cannot abide that. He’s been forced out of Lone Star by his own brother, now he’s out of Ares, but he’s too stubborn to just retire and enjoy his wealth. He’s moved back to Texas, where he’s already agitating for the Republic to reform and to take Texas back from … well, he used a few slurs. Maybe he’ll take a break from corporate raiding and turn to politics? Not sure.**
