## **Billie Baker**

**This woman has a murderous streak several kilometers wide. Which is also the width of the swath of destruction she will leave in order to get a card she wants. Whether it’s fnding a new card or acquiring one from someone else, she has a reputation for paying more for the runners to do her bidding than she would have paid for the card in the frst place. She kills to show her power over life and death. Other than that, she’s a really nice person—just don’t have something she wants in your possession. She operates out of the CAS—Texas, specifcally—and has a preference for CASian runners.**
