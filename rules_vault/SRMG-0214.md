## **From Scotophobia, is Grey Mana Technology subject to the +4 limit for dice pool modifiers?**

Yes. In addition, the GMT boosts for DR applies to magic DR and not to physical DR.

**Does the DR I gain from Threatening Edge count towards damage reduction from the Sixth World Companion optional rule?**

No, although DR from Cover does count.



**Can I use Gris-Gris (p. 8-11,** _**That Old Voodoo**_ **) for downtime activities?** No.
