## **Track**

**If a critter has the Outdoors skill (ideally with a Specialization in Tracking), they may be trained to put it to use upon command. Tracking using critters most often is based on scent, and requires something with the target’s scent to put the critter on the trail. Then the critter may take the lead on the Outdoors (Tracking) + Intuition test, or provide assistance via a Teamwork test in support of another critter or character. When tracking with the assistance of a critter, in addition to any bonus dice, you also gain a point of Edge that must be spent on the Tracking test, or it is lost.**
