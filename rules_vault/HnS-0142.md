## Stealthlink Upgrade

A commlink gains a Sleaze attribute equal to its stealthlink upgrade rating. This Sleaze attribute is limited to Matrix stealth, and only applies when resisting a Matrix Perception action. This makes the commlink much more effective at running silent but does not allow for other Matrix Actions that would require a cyberdeck or living persona. The maximum rating for a stealthlink upgrade is equal to the commlink’s unmodified Data Processing attribute.
