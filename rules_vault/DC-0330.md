## Extra Entry/Exit Points

Wherever you happen to need that door, it’ll be there. It may be gun doors on the side of a rotorcraft, a well-placed escape hatch on a submarine, a sunroof in a limo that you can stand out of so you can shoot at your pursuers, or a removable panel in the floor of your Roadmaster that lets you drop down into an open manhole cover undetected.

This mod is only available for passenger vehicles, not drones (obviously). With this mod, any occupant of the vehicle is assumed to have a convenient entry or exit point accessible to them whenever they need it, no matter what state the vehicle is in (including upside down), or from what

position they are attempting to access the vehicle (such as from underneath or on the roof).
