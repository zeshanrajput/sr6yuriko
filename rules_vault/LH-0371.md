## AFTERSHOCKS & DISIAN LEGACY

> I feel exhausted and numb. I keep looking at all the newsfeeds and screamsheets and I’m equal parts proud and disgusted at how the world is reacting to the end of the Disian threat. It reminds me of a tornado strike; wide swaths of destruction next to areas that were never touched. And it’s a fitting allegory for the world. Those forced to deal with the destruction are doing their best to go on with their lives, while those spared couldn’t care one fragging bit because it didn’t happen to **them.** Whatever. Still, I am grateful. A lot of people I know both in and out of the shadows are dead, or at least missing. The Sixth World took some heavy blows, and while we’re still standing, we’re going to be dealing and coming to terms with what happened for a long time to come. I’ve done the courtesy of assembling some of the most recent posts dealing with the aftermath of the Disian Shadow War. Because even after everything we went through, Mother Nature herself wasn’t quite done with us. **>** Glitch \<08-16-83/1222:21>
