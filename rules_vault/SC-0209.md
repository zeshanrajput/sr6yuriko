## Former Corpsec

You lived the sweet life for a while. You had a corp job, SIN, and living quarters, and not even one of the bad ones. Your skill in protecting corporate assets and thwarting attackers earned you a certain degree of respect and privilege. You worked when and how you wanted, and you benefited from excellent corporate training and gear.

Then it all fell apart. Maybe some hackers finally got the best of you, overwhelming you with a brutal, multi-pronged assault. Maybe you were set up by a rival in your department who saw you as a competitor and undermined your code and your commands, making you more vulnerable. Or maybe you started seeing what your corporation did, who attacked it, and why, and you started wondering if you were on the right side of this fight.

Whatever the cause, you found yourself out of the corp that had provide your livelihood, training, and gear. No more salary, no more nice pad, and no more corp-issued cyberdeck. But they couldn’t take your skills, so you took them out in the street. Now you look at where you can put your protective skills, hoping to defend people who are about more than ever-increasing profits. You fit right in with a shadowrunning team.
