## Aziz Ibn Yusuf al-Shammar

## Posted by: Skarn Ka

Global Sandstorm’s director is at the front and center of an ugly spat with Saeder-Krupp. S-K’s infrastructure in the Middle East and Asia is often targeted by the New Islamic Jihad, and this has only intensified in recent years. Lofwyr’s PR lackeys exploited that and have done a thorough job of putting the blame on GS, implying strongly that the terrorists have the secret support of al-Shammar’s clan and the AAA.

At the last meeting of directors, he brought proof that Dis was trying to undermine Spinrad Global, and he denounced spies within the corporation. The board, trusting his word, dealt with them quickly and gave Shaheed funds to develop a new intelligence department. He is currently spending that money building magic lodges where he can get access to his rivals’ secrets through ritual spellcasting and spirit summoning.

- We don’t know if he is loyal to Gabrielle or not. He agrees to dig up whatever she asks of him, but some intel he finds never ends up on her desk. Rumors say the lodges he is building are part of a plan in case Gabrielle has to be removed from her position.
