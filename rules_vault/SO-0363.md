## Example Two

Jazz (barely) convinced the Halloweeners to hear him out, so they take him and his team back to the Jackal’s Lantern, where he will plead the team’s case to the gang’s leaders. The Jackal’s Lantern is the Halloweeners’ headquarters and main hangout at the center of their turf, and definitely a position of advantage for the gang. When Jazz makes his various Con and Influence tests to get his team out of this mess by convincing the Halloweeners they aren’t enemies, the opposition gains a point of circumstantial Edge due to their positional advantage.
