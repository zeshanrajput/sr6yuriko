As more data became available, organized groups started to form (Grey Cell being the most prominent) in response to this new threat. It wasn’t until the willing extraction of Aztechnology board member Oliver McClure and subsequent breach of Aztechnology’s ultraviolet-rated data vaults that the pieces finally came together, and the Disians’ existence was revealed. They were extremely slotted off about that and acted.

- There’s more to it. Yes, the Disians were forced to act, but not just for the previously stated reasons. During the climax of the Battle of Detroit, witnesses who fought at Ares Tower reported a massive energy discharge that was sent through what was later identified as a metaplanar opening. There are still conflicting reports and rumors, but my personal research suggests this energy was diverted into the Disians’ metaplane. The how and why are still unconfirmed, but I’m certain this was an attempt at eliminating the Disians or at least sealing off their metaplane. Of course, it failed, and now the Disian metaplane is deteriorating at an extremely accelerated rate; hence why the Disians accelerated the initiation of their “Final Project.”
