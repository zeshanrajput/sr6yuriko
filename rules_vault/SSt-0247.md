## **Relationships Matter**

**If you fnd a good street doc, then they’re** _**your**_ **street doc. That’s who you’ll see. They know your medical history, and hopefully they care about you a little. Those all can have a positive effect on your overall medical care. Plus, if you feel comfortable talking to your street doc, you’re more likely to share the full, honest details of anything you’re dealing with, and that’s helpful, too.**
