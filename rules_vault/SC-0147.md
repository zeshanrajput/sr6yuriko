## Plot hooks

This section is primarily intended for use by the gamemaster, but players are also encouraged to read through them. These are not clearly defined plots, but are a set of idea prompts for an adventure along with a menu of suggestions that provide a wide range of potential story ideas. There are no spoilers here, and as a player you are encouraged to recommend the ones that interest you most to your gamemaster, along with any character themes or background ideas that you want to involve or explore. Then leave the final details up to the gamemaster and the action that occurs within the game sessions.
