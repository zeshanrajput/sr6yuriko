## The Job

There could be a job here, or the runners could walk away from a solid contact. Piper set up a meeting but isn’t present when the runners arrive. The PCs need to track down where she is and why she didn’t show up. If the runners don’t go looking and locate her, all future Piper jobs are off the table. Without the runner’s help, she will disappear forever. See the **Clues in the Office** sidebar for hints on where to start looking.
