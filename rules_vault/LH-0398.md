## Current Status

Now I get it, the Defender Movement needed all the help they could get, and groups like Grey Cell were already weakened before the war and didn’t have the combined strength to keep Renraku in check. But when it was all over, the rest of the corps were more than happy to circle the wagons and pretend that nothing happened. Their gains during the war were so great that, unofficially, Renraku is looking to claim the top of the corporate rankings and is already starting to throw their weight around within the CC, pushing for a new audit as soon as possible to confirm it.

In the meantime, they’re consolidating their gains and integrating them into their corporate structure while moving forward with new product lines, as well as eyeballing some smaller corps to see what else they can add to their war chest.



And then, there are rumors of some possible issues with Shiawase, but I’ll let someone else handle that drek.
