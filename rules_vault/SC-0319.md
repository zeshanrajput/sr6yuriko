## The Maverick

You are an adept or mystic adept gunslinger who shoots first and never misses. An avenging angel, you are quick to react and shoot down any sign of danger. Do you use your powers to defend people and take down the corrupt or sell your skills to the highest bidder? Either way, you’re easily as dangerous as any street samurai. You can outdraw and outgun anyone, or so you believe. If it ever turns out that you’re mistaken on that point, you won’t have to think on it much. As long as you have your confidence and don’t run out of ammo, that’s just not going to happen. Unlike the other backgrounds in this archetype, you’re more of a combat specialist than a mage, filling the same role as a street samurai or weapons specialist. You tend to take less time dwelling on things and look for the most direct path of action that will lead to achieving your objectives. Why waste time on work, when there’s plenty of life to be lived—while it lasts?
