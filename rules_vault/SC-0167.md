## coyote shamans

## playing advice

## Roleplaying

The Coyote shaman is a magician who has chosen, or been chosen by, the most iconic of trickster spirits. Such people are natural pranksters, believably and habitually deceitful, and they are usually far cleverer than they are wise. As a Coyote shaman, you delight in entertaining people as well as tricking them. Even your closest allies find it difficult to discern when you are speaking honestly or being serious, and not even your own mother would recognize you when you disguise yourself. This ambiguity suits you, and you love the flexibility it grants you. Some people plan endlessly, but you are a master of improvisation. You may prepare ahead for a number of things, but that’s really more like placing your bets on some of the possible outcomes—some likely, many unlikely. When they work out, you take credit for your brilliance and foresight. And you can always downplay and deflect the times that they don’t. Always be watching for opportunities, not just to profit, but also to have fun doing it. And the greatest service a trickster spirit such as yourself provides to the world is when you take someone powerful and pompous down a peg. Getting paid for doing such tasks is just icing on the cake. You love being a shadowrunner!
