## Lifestyle Basics

## Lifestyle SIN

Any lifestyle category of Middle or higher requires a SIN—you must either tie it to their real SIN (which requires the SINner quality, p. 84, _SR6_ ) or have a fake SIN with a rating equal to or higher than the lifestyle category rating (rating 3 for Middle, 4 for High, and 5 for Luxury).
