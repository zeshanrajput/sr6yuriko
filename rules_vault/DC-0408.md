## Zapper

This modification directs an electrical discharge across the skin of a vehicle with the intent of knocking out an unauthorized individual in contact with it. The system can be actively controlled by the pilot or linked to the anti-theft system (if its rating is 3 or better). It does 6S(e) damage to anyone in contact with the skin of the vehicle; those who do not resist the damage suffer the Zapped status (p. 53, _SR6_ ) for a number of rounds equal to the unresisted damage value.
