## **If a drug doesn’t have a crash effect, is there not one?**

No. For _SRM_ , any drug in which there isn’t a crash effect gains the Nauseated status condition for a time equal to the time the character was impacted by the drug. This includes BTL. For drug interactions (p. 111, _Body Shop_ ), when you roll a second Nauseated condition on the interaction results, you gain Dazed condition instead on top of the original Nauseated condition.
