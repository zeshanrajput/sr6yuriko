## K ongo

This nanchon has a strong relationship to ancestors and also rebellion. As such, some of the spirit family Erzulie, Damballa, and Obatala are connected with this nation. This group is often considered closely connected to the Petwo as the rebellion it represents is not with words, but with hot action. The spells associated are typically Combat, Detection, and Health spells.
