## **The Mafia: It’s Just Business**

## **Additional Background Information: Null Sect**

**The Null Sect have indeed found a way into a Deep Foundation layer of Dis through the Resonance Realms. They are currently building a colony between the Disian noosphere created by the proto-Matrix information systems there and the true Resonance Realms. Although they have been slowly exploring some of the upper data structures on Dis, to date they have had no reason to learn much about the Disians or their language.**
