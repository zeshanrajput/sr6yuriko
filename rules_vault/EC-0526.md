## Suburbanite

The suburbs have some rules of behavior to follow if you want to fit in, and nobody really wants to be an outsider, do they? What to wear, how to talk, and especially what things you never mention in public. Luckily, you happen to know all those unspoken rules—maybe you grew up in a suburb, or you’ve just spent a lot of time in one. Either way, you know how to blend in and not draw any unwanted attention from your fellow suburbanites.

- **Cost:** 5 Points

- **Game Effect:** You gain a point of edge during any social interaction with people living in, or from, a suburban setting.
