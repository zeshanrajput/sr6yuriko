## Junkyard King

It’s a Harley … compatible … thing … basically the same, only with parts not made by the original manufacturers. You could spend more for the name-brand parts, but between you and me, it’s the same subcontractor out of Atzlan, just taking a detour through Hong Kong.

- **Cost:** 4 or 8 Karma

- **Game Effect:** The PC has more options available to repair or customize their drone or vehicle with less than adequate parts. At 4 Karma, reduce availability of the parts by 1 when looking for used parts (see p. 162, Edge test). At 8 Karma, reduce the threshold penalty by 1 when using used parts for repair test (see p. 164).
