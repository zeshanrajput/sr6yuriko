## **Critter Weakness: Purple Blood**

**The true nature of chimera used for covert missions is actively concealed by their creators. However, if a chimera has the Purple Blood weakness listed, it means the nature of the chimera can be determined by seeing its blood—after a wound that does at least one box of damage—or with an Astral + Intuition (4) Assensing test.**
