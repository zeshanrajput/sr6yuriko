## **Ares Seattle**

**One division seems determined to chart their own course, however.**

**Karen King of Ares Seattle has always seen her little corner of the corporation as a frontier outpost, doing what needs to be done to protect her people and her post, despite what the home offce says (especially when given orders she considers to be “idiotic”). With the current corporate climate, she’s doubling down on that, turning her outpost into a fortress in the Free State of Seattle.**

- **King is well known and respected in the Seattle Shadows. She’s been making deals and facilitating operations for a long time now. She’s known to be a hard-core true believer and was one of the few people who condemned the purging of the Firewatch teams and the move from Detroit to Atlanta.**
