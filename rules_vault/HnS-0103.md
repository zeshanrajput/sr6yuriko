## Wearable Case

Instead of having your cyberdeck components all together within a compact case, you can break them up into a number of smaller modules that are designed to connect via data cables. This has led to the innovation of designing clothing and armor with cables, connection ports, and shielded pockets for components. A cyberdeck can be designed to slot into a special armor modification with the drawback of zero additional capacity. The benefit is that unless you are thoroughly searched, your illegal cyberdeck gains complete concealment. Even on sensor scans, this does not stand out as unusual or suspicious technology, usually mistaken for commonly worn biomonitor systems. This case requires specially modified

clothing or armor. In the case of clothing, add the nuyen cost to any outfit. If you’d like to have it built into your armor, it takes up 1 capacity (armor capacity).

**Cyberhacks:** This case type is not available for cyberhacks. A wearable cyberhack is a cyberhack stuffed into a backpack.
