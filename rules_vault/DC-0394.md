## Propulsion Systems

The base method of propulsion is determined by the class of the chassis. Ground vehicles, boats, subs, choppers, LAVs and airplanes all start with different types of chassis. Of course, nothing says that a creative mechanic can’t take some liberties with the factory specs and make a vehicle move in ways it was never meant to.
