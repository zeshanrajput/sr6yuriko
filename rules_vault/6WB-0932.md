## Professional Rating 3

Beat cops and basic corpsec have the training and the mindset—all they need is a little more time under fire. A situation they all avoid like the plague, but who wouldn’t? Whether the streets call them a pawn (Knight Errant) or a tinman (Lone Star) they’re some of the most frequently encountered opponents for a shadowrunner. Whether it’s a random stop-and-frisk on the streets of downtown or a claim on the highway your transponder isn’t broadcasting, you’re going to meet them, and they aren’t going to flee just because you flash steel.
