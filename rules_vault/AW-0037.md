## Paying Spirits with Karma

Spirits don’t care much about nuyen, but they love Karma! With some notable (and terrifying) exceptions, they can only acquire it from someone who willingly chooses to give it to them. When attempting to hire a spirit, gain a point of Edge if you are offering any Karma as part of the deal. Most spirits will offer a single, short-term service (such as using one of their powers, like Astral Gateway) for a single point of Karma. Services that would take a spirit more time or put them at great risk are likely to cost more Karma than that. A good rule of thumb for how much Karma a spirit is likely

to charge for a dangerous or Extended service is Force divided by two.

Multiple characters can chip in to pay a Spirit’s Karma price. Karma does not transfer as easily from mundanes as it does from the Awakened or emerged. A spirit receives only 1 Karma for every 2 Karma that comes from a mundane character.
