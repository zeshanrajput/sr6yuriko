## **Can I take Incompetent (Hacking) when I am not a decker?**

Yes, but it needs to be a skill that you can take. Just realize that this will put your character into positions where you need to use that skill. If you are Incompetent (Firearms), during combat, the things you do while hiding still hurts your team, and opponents gain Edge. In the case of Hacking, maybe you just can’t help yourself when your commlink pops up that link for "HOT MILFS IN YOUR AREA" or Nigerian Prince scam and the GM grants temporary Edge to the other side when they are hacking your PAN or your team’s PAN. Essentially, if you are on the receiving end of a test where someone is using a skill you are incompetent in, they will gain Edge.
