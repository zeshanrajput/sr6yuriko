## Game Information

## Miggon Mutations

Metahumans who spend more than a day in Miggon are likely to begin to mutate. After twenty-four hours pass, you must make a test to resist mutating: Body + Willpower with a threshold equal to your Essence (round up) minus the number of Miggon mutations you already have. If you fail this test, you gain a mutation. If you succeed at this test, the threshold for your next test will be increased by one. The threshold increases by an additional 1 every time you succeed. If you fail a test and gain a new mutation, the threshold increase resets to zero.

Every time a character suffers any damage from a fire-based elemental attack while in Miggon, increase the threshold for their next test to resist mutating by 1. The threshold is increased once foreach instance of damage.

When you leave the Wastes of Miggon, the mutations disappear. However, if you return to Miggon, all of your previous mutations immediately reappear—and you’re likely to pick up more of them the more often you visit.
