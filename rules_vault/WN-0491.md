## Rikki Nguyen

## (Fixer, Connection Rating 4)

Rikki is uncommon—he is a Monad given a personalized body, a clonal blank, to reside within. His original hosting body is uncertain, but he believes it was Gerald Ramius (a detail Ramius does not confirm or deny). His own recollections are that Korea is the “birthplace” of his body, and he came to re-awareness within a MetaErgonomics research facility after being extracted from a host he had taken in Boston during the Lockdown crisis. He works primarily for Taylor Dacopral of Evo Corporation, helping in field research as well as tracking down more difficult and dangerous Monads and digital intelligences. A year prior, he was overwhelmed by an encounter with the Kechibi Code while following up on another project in Hong Kong. Taylor has been grooming Rikki ever since the Evo AI became aware of Animus Construction, hoping Rikki would serve as one of his primary proxy agents when doing operations with that agency. As a DI in a biological form, Rikki uses his Spark attribute as if it were Essence, but he himself is immune to Essence Drain, and he cannot personally gift Karma to a spirit.

Rikki considers himself on good terms with Gerald Ramius and is aware of Mr. Ramius’ relations with the Yakutian Ambassador, who also possesses a sizable portfolio of shares of Evo and several of their subsidiaries.
