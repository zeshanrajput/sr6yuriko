## **Astral Sensitives**

**Also known as the Aware, these individuals have a Magic rating of at least one, the ability to astrally perceive, and can train in the Astral skill. They are dual natured while perceiving, gaining all the advantages and liabilities associated with that state. Astral sensitives have no other magical abilities. Astral sensitives may bond with a weapon focus and use astral combat in the same way as an adept with the power of astral perception.**
