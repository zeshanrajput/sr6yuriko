## Lofwyr: Forever the Long Game

I hate to sound like an audio file stuck on repeat, but … we really have no fragging clue as to what Lofwyr is currently doing. And I’m guessing that is by design.

Even before the Week of Death, Lofwyr was rarely seen, even in his human form. After, he was completely absent, with various underlings or his primary agent, a drake known only as Scale, acting as his proxies within S-K. His next confirmed appearance was during _Operation: Cold Fury,_ where he, along with several other equally absent dragons, took part in an extremely powerful magical ritual that dealt with the harvested mana by apparently re-infusing it back into the material plane (and saving us all).

Since then, Lofwyr—along with all the other dragons who participated in that ritual—have once again disappeared completely from the public eye. However, that doesn’t mean he’s not active. Following the Disians’ defeat, S-K has been making a lot of internal moves on Lofwyr’s orders, conducting their own internal audit to see where they stand and what assets they still have. Some see this relative quiet and passivity as an indication of weakness. I think it’s the exact opposite and believe that we should be worried about S-K’s silence.
