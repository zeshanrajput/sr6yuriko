## Smuggling compartment

For items that won’t fit in a fingertip but you still can’t afford to have found, smuggling compartments can be placed in parts of the body that can be easily hollowed out/replaced. This includes ribs and the space between them, hip bones, shoulder blades, and so forth. These compartments allow the storage of small and miniature items (nothing bigger than a light pistol typically; your gamemaster will let you know if something’s too big). The compartment has a Concealability threshold of 8. Inserting or retrieving an object requires a Major Action.

**Wireless Bonus:** Inserting or retrieving an object takes a Minor Action.
