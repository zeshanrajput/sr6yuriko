## **3. Develop a Strategy Everyone Will Agree To**

**If the mere concept of cooperation is an incredible challenge, the next step of getting everyone to agree on a strategy feels like an impossible dream. Again, some of the simpler tasks, like rooting out Dis infltrators, don’t have complicated strategies. Other tasks, like learning the patterns of Dis spirit names, involve both deep arcane studies and metaplanar quests, which opens doors wide to many types of disagreements—the sort of disagreements that can rend fragile coalitions. Can Horizon hold whatever they build together? Lots of people have doubts, and I’m one of them.**
