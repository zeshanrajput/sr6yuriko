## Built Tough (1 to 4)

- **Cost:** 4 per level

Both animal and metahuman forms gain the Built Tough quality. See p. 70, _SR6_ . This stacks with Built Tough levels granted by metahuman qualities or with levels taken as a normal quality, up to a maximum of 4 levels from all sources combined.
