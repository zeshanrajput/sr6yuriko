## Weather

Hong Kong remains something of a sweat box in the summer, no matter how much Wuxing has tried to affect the city’s feng shui. The sprawl’s wet climate becomes even more humid between May and August. Typhoon activity is an ever-present reminder in the autumn months that Mother Nature remains in control. Sea travel has become more predictable due to significant improvements in observation and analysis, as well as new adjustable barriers that rise and sink in order to reduce stronger wave action while doubling as tidal power generation (thank you, Gaeatronics).
