## Physical Location

In Ikega, near the airport, there is a former bank that now acts as a TerraFirst! hangout den. Inside the heavy safe, currently used as a private room and office for Embiro Gontabanya, is an old-school comm terminal used for occasional downloads to store valuable information offline. The terminal is only operational when a download or upload is in progress, and it draws power from a generator located in the alley behind the bank rather than the building’s power supply. Heavy power cables come in to the terminal through the building.

The interior of the bank has a big, open floor plan directly inside the door with solid stone surfaces. Folding chairs, makeshift seating, and several tables occupy the space but do little to take away from the airy feeling. To the right side ofthe open area are four offices that were converted to sleeping areas. To the left are several desks and filing cabinets shoved against the walls, their drawers filled with documents for planning attacks as well as physical files stolen over the years. Straight ahead is the teller counter, behind which is the heavy safe, a room full of safety deposit boxes that have all been pulled out and dumped, and restrooms for the staff.

None of the doors have functional locks on them. The safe cannot close because of the wires running into it. The gate that closes off the safety deposit boxes doesn’t lock, but it does close.

Feather, the runner with a vendetta against Embiro, is found either on the streets of Lagos or in her small apartment located in the Ajegunle Slums. The apartment is a single room buried in a massive maze of other single-room apartments all crushed together.
