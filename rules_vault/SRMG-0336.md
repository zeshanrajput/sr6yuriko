## **Does a nocturna (6WC, p. 78-79) have Keen Eared or does it have poor hearing?**



The nocturna has the Keen Eared quality and does not suffer from poor hearing as stated in the text description of the metavariant.

## **Does the nartaki (6WC, p. 78-79) have boosted Agility from Shiva Arms, while the metagenic quality doesn’t include it?**

Shiva Arms does not increase Agility. The Agility racial maximum for nartaki is 6.
