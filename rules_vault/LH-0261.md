## Kenneling the Dogs of War

Across the globe, individuals of _similar persuasion_ (saboteurs, spies, and assassins) are conducting similar ops. Some are being quiet, others … not so much. The overall objective is to keep as many regular military forces as possible out of the fight by any means necessary. Last thing our people need is for an armored infantry or mechanized battalion to roll in hot when they’re assaulting a Harvester, especially if the military’s orders come from a Disian puppet. I don’t know how much we’ll accomplish, but if we can buy our people even a few minutes, it’s worth it.

But it’s not just the military we’re fragging with.
