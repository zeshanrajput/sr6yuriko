## Giant 

If you can’t guess the primary difference between most trolls and giants, then you’re either over-thinking or under-thinking. Giants average three meters in height, giving them challenges fitting into even troll-sized habitations and vehicles. Their excess height spreads out their muscles, making them appear more slender than other trolls. They have bark-like skin
