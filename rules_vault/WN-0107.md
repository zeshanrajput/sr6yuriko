## The Shogunate Club

The name of this club marks it as the dwelling of a Shogun, an ancient military leader of Japan. The Shogunate Club uses the title somewhat euphemistically to refer to those who have great wealth and lead many. The Shogunate Club was originally founded by the Four Oyabun as a place for _komon_ to mingle with and learn from leaders of business. Over time, the club grew to include anyone with considerable wealth and influence. Still, it remains a place frequented by members of the Yakuza.

The club is located at the site of the old YMCA facility at the corner of East 92[nd] and Lexington. The interior is modeled to resemble the classic home of a sixteenth-century shogun.
