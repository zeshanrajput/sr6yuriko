## Vampire’s Invitation

## Posted by: Slamm-0!

Since the category is access, we’ll put this here. There’s a lot of ways to play this, but the core version is setting yourself up as a repair crew. You’ve just completed some repairs in the neighborhood for another client, and you’ve got a whole bunch of leftover resources and a reason to offer them to others. The mark, hopefully, senses a good deal and lets you in, where you can do damn near whatever you like. The goal is, of course, to overcomethe hesitancy of someone to let you into their secure location (home, business, so on). You’re making your own invitation, just like a vampire!
