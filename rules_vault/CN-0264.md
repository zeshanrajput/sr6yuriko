## **>** Enigma

As written in the memo I uploaded, STV did not engage with the Dis at the time (the memo is about 2 years old) and did not pursue it further. Since the Blackout and the disappearance of the 125K people (if you count both the UCAS III Corps and the city of Dutchville) this has again raised their interest. Based on the evidence I gathered, some of their spin operations in the different regions have been geared toward that end to collect information from whatever source is available. Apparently, they reached out to some of their metaplanar clients here on Earth directly to get some insider information from beings who had dealings with them in the past. They had to burn some markers in the process, which in itself is surprising, given the usual altruistic nature of the firm.
