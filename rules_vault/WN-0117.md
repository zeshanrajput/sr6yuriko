## Getting the Lay Of The Land

Eager doesn’t own a vehicle. He gives the runners a crash course on local transit options including the specific bus and train routes that go to the places they need to get to for the job. Eager tells them they need to get at least ten minutes of footage, because Mr. Johnson thinks that’s how long it will take for Horizon to shut down their feed. He suggests that the Goldwater Club is close enough to Central Park for that area to be an ideal escape route if needed. He doesn’t have any suggestions if they get caught in Times Square.
