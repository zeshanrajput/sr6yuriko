## **Honesty Is Important, But So Is Minding Your Own Business**

**This is going to seem contradictory to the previous point, but that’s part of the delicate balance of living with other people. Sometimes, you might fnd out that someone you live with is in a Situation. Perhaps even a Sticky Situation. You may or may not have experience with this kind of Situation. You may have an interesting plan for how to get out of the Situation. In fact, you may see a path where you could take the situation in hand and just solve it. Here’s my advice: Talk to your housemate before you take any action, and don’t feel like you have to share every plan that pops into your head. Just because your housemate is in a Situation doesn’t mean they need you to solve it. Stand back, be supportive, be ready to help, but let them take the lead in their lives. And if they want to take care of it themselves, step back and allow it to happen.**

**While maybe conducting a little Matrix overwatch just to make sure they’re okay.**
