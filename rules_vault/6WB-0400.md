## **Linked Attribute:** Agility.

Shadowrunning isn’t supposed to be obvious, as you should be able to guess by the name, so Stealth is a very valuable skill. Stealth covers all the activities characters perform that they do not want

others to notice—sneaking into a research facility, slipping a credstick out from under a Mafia don’s nose, blending into a crowd of Lone Star officers, and so on. Typically, a Stealth + Agility test is made at the start of such an effort, with the net hits being used as a threshold for others to notice what the character is up to. Sometimes the attempt at stealth happens spontaneously, so it is a Stealth + Agility vs. Perception + Intuition Opposed test.

In some circumstances, but not all, the size of a character may play a role in how stealthy they can be. If the gamemaster judges it to be appropriate, the Body of a character can be used as a threshold for their Stealth + Agility test, or the threshold can be increased based in their size.
