## **Foes**

**No matter how you want to split it, our main opponents are the nations created by the invading Anglos. They’ve overstayed their welcome and yet refuse to leave.**

**Thankfully, the events of 2080–2081 have seriously weakened the UCAS’s ability to deal with us. They are still reeling from the fnancial loss they suffered due to the departure of Ares and its im-**


**pact within local law enforcement and the military. They are not as responsive as they once were, and we can more easily slip through their nets.**
