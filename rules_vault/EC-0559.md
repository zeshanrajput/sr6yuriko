## > **Humana Hospital**

Humana is almost entirely owned by Shiawase (the rest belongs to the Seattle Health Maintenance Organization) and is basically a new, well-run hospital that plays ball with their cyberware research division. Not the worst place to snatch some experimental tech or lift some paydata, but watch your ass, as you probably need to wear a patient smock to get in that deep. > Nephrine
