## Point Zero

What was the event that made your character go into the shadows?

All of these worlds and all of the common paths throughout them tend to have some grey areas overlapping into the shadows. For some, Life Events (p. 33, _Sixth World Companion_ ) are the pivot point from being part of a world, institution, or community to being a non-entity, a deniable asset, a flicker between light and darkness. Social adepts are one of those character types that have so many legal, legitimate, or institutional employers and occupations—all of which would be more than happy to exploit their particular abilities—that most reasonable people in the Sixth World, even the ones born and raised in criminal underworlds, wouldn’t take that risk. A social adept who has developed some awareness of their skills and abilities could sell out and leave the worst environs behind in a nanosecond for the security and stability any of these worlds offers.

A social adept is a golden goose. They could make a lot elsewhere, but they stay in the shadows. Take advantage of their presence while you can!
