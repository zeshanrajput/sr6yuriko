## **Do: Stick Your Neck Out for Important Relationships**

**You’re defnitely going to want to do casual favors for people in your network, but if you really want to score the big reputation points, then doing a big, stick-your-neck-out favor for your contacts on occasion will help a lot. Rescue their dog from Mafa kidnappers. Escort them through a gauntlet of hired killers. Find a missing relative. Take a big swing, don’t take any pay, and you’ve cemented your bond.**

- **Getting someone out of trouble with a local gang is always a good way to curry favor, and it’s usually not that difficult. Any decent shadowrunner should be able to convince a minor gang that they can cause more trouble for them than their minor grudge is worth. So you do a little intimidation and gain a grateful friend.**

- **Mihoshi Oni**
