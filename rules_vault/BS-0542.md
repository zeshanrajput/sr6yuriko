## Additional Critter Power

The critter is granted an entirely new capability through genetic engineering. This augmentation provides the critter with any critter power that does not make use of a Magic or Resonance attribute, though some critter powers are available in a mundane version. This new critter power may also benefit from the critter power augmentation.

Some augmentations have a rating that determines its effects. If no rating is mentioned in its description, its rating counts as 2 for the purposes of determining any applicable costs or drawbacks.
