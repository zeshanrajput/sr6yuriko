## Meridian Strike

By combining their Awakened talents with their martial arts expertise, adept practitioners of hapsum-do may disrupt the flow of mana in their opponent’s bodies. When making an unarmed attack against an Awakened opponent, if the adept has the Killing Hands power active, they may choose to reduce the dice pool for their target’s next Magic-linked test by 1 per net hit instead of inflicting damage. **Category:** Striking; requires Killing Hands
