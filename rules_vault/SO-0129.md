## Industrious Grift

All grifters are predators, and a predator is defined by prey. Rather than looking for marks and figuring out the right way to hit them or baiting a trap and snapping on whoever trips it, the industrious grift creates marks. There’s still an element of finding a mark with the right resources, but the difference is that instead of them coming to you with openings you can exploit or a lure that hooks them, you put them into a position to create cracks and weaknesses to exploit however you want.

- Big industry in this stuff. Some people make their whole careers out of building marks. Some even set them up for someone else to make the proverbial kill, selling the plans like other architects do with heists.

- Copperfield
