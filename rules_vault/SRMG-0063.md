## **How much do rituals cost (karma and nuyen)?**

It costs the same as the type of spell for normal spellcasting. For rituals that don’t fit in a type, the cost is 1,000 nuyen and 5 Karma.

**Capsule rounds don’t have stats for Sixth Edition, so if I steal some, what are the stats? Type Damage Avail Cost** Capsule 0S N/A N/A

Capsule rounds never do damage and are only attempting to deliver their payload. These are not for purchase and because of their short shelf life, they expire and are no longer good after one run. Stolen or leftover capsules cannot be filled with other toxins.
