## Site 000 Security Trooper: Elite

## (Professional Rating 7)

Considered to be the best of the best among the troops assigned to Site 000, the Elite Security Trooper is the one who holds the line when everyone else has given up or failed. Exceptionally fit and augmented with some of the best ’ware available, they are also exceptionally trained and chosen for their skill and determination in a fight. Any one of these troops would be considered top-tier operators in any national military, corporate, or mercenary unit.
