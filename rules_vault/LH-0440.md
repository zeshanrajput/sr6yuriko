## June 14, 2083

Tragedy at Skyhook Space Elevator. Initially reported as an accident, later reports classify it as a terrorist attack. Aztechnology president Flavia de la Rosa and two other board members are killed. Aztechnology doubles down on offensive operations against Defender targets in retaliation. Thisleads to some of the most brutal fighting of the shadow war.
