## **Antwerp, Belgium**

## **Status: Contested**

**Home to some of the largest ports in the region with access to the Atlantic and Arctic Oceans, Antwerp has always been a key logistics point in any European confict. The Disians know this and their enemies know this, which is why Antwerp is such a vital location.**

**It’s also currently one of the most contested. When the Week of Death occurred, Disian agents were quick to secure the ports both during and after the initial attack. But unlike other parts of the world, local Grey Cell and allied agents learned of the attack before it occurred and had counter-strikes ready. Unfortunately, they did not realize in time that this was part of a much larg-**
