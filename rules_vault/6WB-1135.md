## Polearm

Take a long stick, add any of a dozen varieties of blades to the head, and you’ve got a polearm.



Designed to keep distance between attacker and defender or strike at a mounted target, they’re not common street weapons. They’re nearly impossible to conceal, even for a troll, but they’re great at keeping problems at a safe distance.
