## Death Train Conductors

The conductors are bound to the Death Train, and are either incapable of or unwilling to leave it for any reason, though they are willing to chase their prey onto the roof of the train cars in their efforts to slay them. Traditionally, only one conductor will attack each passenger, though some of the more dangerous or troublesome passengers are sometimes assaulted by groups of them. The conductors are unfailingly polite, except when they are trying to murder you.
