## Keycard copier

The keycard copier allows the user to copy a keycard in seconds. A new keycard can then be manufactured with an Electronics Kit and an Electronics + Logic (2, 10 minutes) test. The keycard passes for the original, but some security systems may note the unusual usage of duplicate keys, like when “Dr. Scientist” accesses a lab that he just accessed and hasn’t left yet.
