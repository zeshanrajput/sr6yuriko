## **Rescue**

**A critter that is large and strong enough can be trained to rescue and retrieve an incapacitated or injured metahuman (or critter). The trigger must include some instructions to designate the target. Once commanded, the critter will move to their target and assist them with moving to safety. If necessary, the critter will drag or carry the target back. Otherwise, the critter may support the target as they move to safety. An augmented critter may also be trained to apply a trauma patch or stim patch, though this requires that they have appendages capable of administering the patch.**
