## Fearless

A critter can be engineered to have no sense of fear, making them immune to the Panicked and Frightened statuses—whether the source is magical or mundane. This makes the critter much more difficult to train, however. No Edge may be used on any training test to train the critter, and one of the dice is replaced by a wild die that only adds 1 hit on a 5 or 6, but still cancels all 5s rolled on a 1.
