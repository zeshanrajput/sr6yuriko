## Arcane Taser Plus

- **Formula:** Lightning Ball, Verbal

- **Drain Value:** 9

- **Cost:** 600¥/charge

This is the next step up from the standard Arcane Taser, when you have a whole group of people

who need to be subdued. The plus version expands beyond a single individual, but it risks including you in its electrical blast, so be sure to select the initial location carefully.

Touch the small marble (even if it’s still concealed), pick a spot for the party to start, say “tamed lightning,” and get ready to run from some stunned predators.
