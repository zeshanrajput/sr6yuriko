## Corrosive Spit

A gland much like one of the spitting cobra varieties is grown and implanted in the roof of the mouth. A person causes the venom to shoot out by forcefully pressing their tongue to the roof of their mouth.

Like the Corrosive Spit ability (p. 222, _SR6_ ) of critters, this ability allows an individual can make a ranged combat attack with Exotic Weapon (Spitter), DV 2P, AR 4/-/–/–/–. The damage is Chemical, and any damage also inflicts the Corrosive status with a value equal to the rating of the bioware.
