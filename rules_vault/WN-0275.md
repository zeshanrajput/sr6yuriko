## Ill-Gotten Gains

If any of the commlinks from the physical agents are recovered by the PCs or hacked and their files decrypted, information will show that the agents have recently been someplace called the “Octagon.” The PCs can also track where the commlinks have been recently, and information shows they were at the north end of Roosevelt Island (see p. 23). All the commlinks are specialized devices (device rating 7).
