## Explorer Primary 7 (Mister Seven)

## (Null Sect Xenosapient EI)

As a high-ranking member of its cast, Explorer Primary 7 has had extensive contact with the creatures of the outer world and the reality they live in. Using the pseudonym Mr. Seven, it spends its time residing in a customized anthrodrone, jumped-in to allow it to fully experience the world around it. While still slightly arrogant and condescending toward biologicals, it has enough experience in the outer world that the hardest edges of its prejudice have been worn away. This makes it easier to work with than most of its species. Unlike a typical member of the Null Sect, Mr. Seven travels alone, unaccompanied by other members of its caste or any golem constructs.
