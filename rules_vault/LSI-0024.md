## Lifestyle Category Details

Street/Squatter: Basic hygiene kit, occasional access to a sink with a mirror, public showers, clothing from thrift stores and vending machines

Low/Medium: A fully functional bathroom at home, decent street or work clothes, regular access to professional hair and makeup services

High/Luxury: Automated walk-in closet, luxury spa membership, fancy wardrobe subscription service
