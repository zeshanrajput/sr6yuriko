## New Qualities

## Positive Qualities

## Cyber Singularity Seeker

You believe that implanting more cyberware is bringing you closer to a state of true harmony between man and machine. Instead of feeling like you have sacrificed a portion of yourself, a set of replacement limbs makes you feel unequivocally whole.

- **Cost:** 10 Karma

- **Game Effect:** The runner gains +1 Willpower for each pair of cyberlimbs (two arms or two legs) they get implanted, up to a maximum of +2 Willpower.
