## **B. For 3 or 4 infusions**

- Select infusions

- Add negative qualities associated with infusion/ bioware mod

- Calculate cost in nuyen per mod

- Calculate Essence loss per mod

- Add moderate transgenic cosmetic modification for free and –0.2 Essence loss

Alternatively, for those who want only the cosmetic properties of transgenic infusions, simply choose Minor or Moderate Transgenic Cosmetic Infusion (–0.2 or –0.5 Essence loss respectively).

- **Game effect:** When exposed to an inhalation vector drug/toxin/gas, characters receive a –2 dice pool modifier to any test involving a physical attribute in addition to any penalties suffered due to the presence of the toxin. The character also cannot gain Edge on Perception tests involving scent.
