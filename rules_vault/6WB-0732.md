## repairing matrix damage example

Glitch gets a cyberdeck from Mungo the Decker, who claims that the “magic smoke” got out of it. It isn’t quite bricked, but it’s barely a cyberdeck anymore. Glitch’s Engineering + Logic dice pool is 12, and he gets 4 successes. This fixes 4 of the 8 boxes of Matrix Damage that Mungo’s cyberdeck had suffered.
