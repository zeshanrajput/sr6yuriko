## shadows in focus

**primary spirit associated with this type of grisgris, but Erzulie Dantor, Papa Legba, Ghede, and** Obatala are also associated with it. The appearance of the fetish matches the symbols of the spirit to be appeased. Charms of protection for Papa Legba will appear as a skeleton key. Charms to Erzulie Dantor appear as a lattice-work heart with scrollwork around the outside edge. Another symbol often used is that of a skull with the favored symbols of Ghede carved into it.
