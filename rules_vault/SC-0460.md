## Matrix

- **Common life modules:** College/University, Counter-Hacker, Data Liberator, Engineer, Hacker, IT Support, Matrix Security, Matrix Vandal, Research Scientist, Technical School, Think Tank

Growing up online, one might not think about how _social_ social media actually is. The most effective hacks can and often have been performed primarily through social engineering with a side of Matrix hacking, and not just pure digital exploitation and brute-force hacking. All the programs and cyberweapons in the Matrix combined are less effective than one effective social exploitation of a corporate spider or executive over email and DMs. Social engineering is a backbone of computer hacking and has been since the first telegraphs and difference engines were built.
