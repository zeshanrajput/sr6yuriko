## examples of portals

## Arcane Gateways

Created by teams of enchanters, arcane gateways are foci that aid in the astral gateway ritual. Typically taking the form of an engraved arch or false door, the devices add their Force as a dice pool bonus to the astral gateway ritual. They may also be created to provide access to a specific preset metaplane, even if the ritual leader has never been there. These devices can also simply create an area of forced projection without opening a passageway, allowing anyone in the area of effect to astrally project, leaving their bodies in a secure location.
