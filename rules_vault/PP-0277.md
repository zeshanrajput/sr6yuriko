## Game Information

These qualities are often possessed by people within the Saeder-Krupp, showing their particular corporate culture. Runners who were former or current S-K employees/citizens are also known to have these qualities. In other cases, runners may have adopted parts of S-K’s culture, without them ever being part of it. In other cases, they may have done something for the corp, earning them some respect.
