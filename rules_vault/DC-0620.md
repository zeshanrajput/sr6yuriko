## Chicken

## (Piloting)

Choose a target vehicle that is involved in a chase with you. At the end of this round, whichTHEever of you got fewer net hits on their Piloting test must make a Crash test or crash. You may not use this Edge action within an open environment.

**Cost:** 6 Edge
