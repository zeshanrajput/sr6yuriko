## The Alchemist

While most magicians prefer the instant gratification of spells and rely heavily upon the assistance of spirits, you have chosen to go all-in with alchemy. Few full magicians devote themselves to such an intense focus on alchemy, but it has become an especially popular field with aspected magicians who would otherwise be relegated to gathering reagents and enchanting foci. Whether you’re an aspected magician who chose to dive into alchemy as a means of casting spells that were otherwise unavailable to you or a magician with other talents who favors alchemy by choice, you are a master of preparation. When you completed your education, you found yourself working in the equivalent of a magical sweatshop, expected to craft the same alchemical preparations over and over again every day. The pay was decent, but every day’s work left you thoroughly drained, and your employers never seemed pleased by the quantity of your output. After realizing the impossibility of ever fulfilling the terms of the contracts that had funded your education, you utilized the assistance of some black-market contacts to escape into the shadows.
