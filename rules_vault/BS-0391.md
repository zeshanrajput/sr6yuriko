## Snuff

## (Aztech Chew)

- **Vector:** Inhalation, Ingestion

- **Speed:** 1 minute

- **Duration:** 10 x 1D6 minutes

- **Power:** 9

- **Effect:** +1 Reaction, Toughness ( _SR6_ , p. 73)

This legal drug is traditionally made from an ancient Native American formula including tobacco leaves, cannabis resin, and willow bark. The more modern formulation forgoes the natural ingredients and instead manufactures the drug by directly combing the active ingredients of THC, nicotine, and salicin (lthough many purists still prefer the flavor of the original, natural ingredients). Once the drug wears off, the user experiences –1 Intuition for a period equivalent to twice the original duration.
