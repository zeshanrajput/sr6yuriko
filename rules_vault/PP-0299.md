## Rivals and Rebels

## The Shiawase Family

It goes without saying that the main adversary of any Shiawase is another Shiawase. Everyone out of power wants in, everyone in power wants to stay there, and paying favorites and pulling strings is as natural to a Shiawase as breathing. The family hosts petty ambitions, personal fiefdoms, sleights real and imagined, and vast piles of nuyen to put their desires and feuds into motion. It’s rarified stuff, way outside of the air that most of us breathe, but that kind of drek rolls downhill and gets in everyone’s hair sooner or later.
