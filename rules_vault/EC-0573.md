## > **Tacoma District Courthouse**

Busy, but otherwise unremarkable. The real business takes place in the parking complex across the street, where the lawyers cut their deals in between meeting freelance contractors (that means runners, informants, and detectives). By the time you get into the courthouse, the work is done.

**>** Mr. Bonds
