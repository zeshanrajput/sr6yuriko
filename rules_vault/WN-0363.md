## Now What

It should be clear to the players that this is all he can offer. Therefore, there is no negotiation unless it’s for future favors. The players only have to decide if they’re going to help Silver Nail or hand him over to El Guapo. El Guapo will pay a lump sum of 50,000 nuyen if they turn him over. If they decide to help Silver Nail, the players have the freedom to create any cover story they want as long as it will not give Lex James or Spinrad Global bad press. He could have been kidnapped by a rival corporation, someone attempted to murder him to get back at the family, he was minding his own business at the park and got mugged, was in a bad car wreck, was in a coma—something along those lines. If the gamemaster thinks the cover story is really good, Silver Nail will appreciate their effort and will become a contact with Connection 3 and Loyalty 4.

The next day, there will be a big news blitz about how a Spinrad Global executive’s child


was happily reunited with his family. The story will either be what the players created or what El Guapo created, which is that a local go-gang that kidnapped him and attempted to ransom him off. Fortunately, an SG strike team rescued him, and he is home recovering. El Guapo will officially take responsibility for the lack of security, and he informs everyone that new measures are now in place to protect Spinrad Global personnel.
