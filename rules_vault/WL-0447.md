## **Mana Well**

**==> picture [228 x 20] intentionally omitted \<==**

**----- Start of picture text -----**<br>
TYPE ACTION RANGE DURATION<br>M Major Self Sustained<br>**----- End of picture text -----**<br>

**Some creatures have an ability to attract mana. When near these animals, the fow of magic is stronger, as they act like a gravity well on magic. They are living mana wells similar to the returned found in** _**Slip Streams**_ **(p. 141).**

**Creatures with this power can create a low mana fow with a Major Action. The low mana fow extends out two meters from the creature. The critter power will specify the tradition it is aspected toward (if any).**
