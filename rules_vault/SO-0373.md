## Improvised Disguise

## \[Con (Disguise or Impersonation), Major\]: 4 Edge

This action involves quickly improvising a disguise, using mannerisms, lighting, a spare napkin, attitude, and whatever else happens to be at hand. A player character may attempt a disguise or impersonation without access to a disguise kit or other tools, but it won’t hold up for long. Make a Con (Disguise or Impersonation) + Charisma test, opposed by Perception + Intuition or Judge Intentions—if there are multiple targets, use the highest dice pool to resist this action. If successful, the disguise lasts for one round per net hit (minimum one round). After this duration expires, others will automatically see through your disguise. Anyone who later reviews recorded footage of the act gains a point of Edge on their Perception + Intuition or Judge Intentions test to see through the disguise in the future.
