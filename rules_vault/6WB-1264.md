## Rockets and Missiles

Rockets are launched projectiles with stabilizing fins, a propulsion system, and a warhead. Missiles are rockets that carry internal guidance systems, with sensor packages adding their rating to the attack roll, since they can adjust their trajectory in flight (also reflected in their Attack Rating).

Rules for firing rockets and missiles are on p. 114. Rockets and missiles are set to arm when they have traveled 10 meters from their point of origin and explode on impact or with an airburst link (p. 259). This safety feature can be disabled with an Engineering + Logic (4, 5 Minutes) Extended Test.
