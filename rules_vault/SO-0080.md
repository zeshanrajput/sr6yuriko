## Zanshin (A)

Sizing someone up can help when negotiations break down. Spend a Minor Action to make a Judge Intentions vs. Judge Intentions Opposed test. With 1 net hit, you can determine if the target has ranks in the Close Combat skill. With 2 net hits, you can learn which Close Combat specializations or expertise they have. With 3 net hits, you can determine whether they are an amateur (1–2 ranks), welltrained (3–5 ranks), or highly trained (6+ ranks).
