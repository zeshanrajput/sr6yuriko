## Hotspots

**The Corner Swing Highrise:** This is a newer, high-rise, multifunction building situated at the east corner of West 150th and Broadway. It holds several clubs, bars, and cafés at the street level, with more than thirty stories of apartments and condominiums above them. Rikki’s safehouse is located on the seventeenth floor, apartment 1718, facing 150th[h] Street (southwest). The building is owned by an Aztechnology-controlled Manhattan real estate firm.

**Cartella Chocolates and Cigars:** One of the pubs at the ground level of the Corner Swing, Cartella is a place where the PCs could observe traffic going into and out of the elevators of the building. It is normally patronized by thirty-something professionals who appreciate the atmosphere offered by expensive South American chocolates and cigars.
