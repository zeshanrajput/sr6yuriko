## **Can I negate the Wild Die effect with Edge?**

No. You can spend Edge to re-roll the die, but the effect doesn’t change. If you roll more than one Wild Die, then each of the effects are still in place. So, you could roll a 5 and a 1, and you would have the 3 successes but other non-Wild Dice showing 5s don’t count as successes.
