## Conventional(ish) Technology

## New Weapons

Some rare types of conventional weapons proved to be effective in the shadows, especially against chimeras and other Disian forces. Flamethrowers and other chemical projectors were some of the Defenders’ favorites, while advances in energy technologies moved “tesla guns” into the field. Both pro- and anti-Dis corporations were in a race to arm their operatives with any weapons available.
