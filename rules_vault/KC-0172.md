## Extractions Are a Fact of Life

There have already been a few attempts to transfer Gu Guanyu out of the corporate reach of Yingyong. At this stage in the project, he is not quite the nova-hot property he might have been during development. However, Gu retains his grip on his research as the licensure and patents for the technology are in his name as well as that of the company. Remarkably, he has been the one to thwart several of these attempts, with at least two of the attempts handled by using the Guizhen technologies he keeps on his person.

This has not protected the other members of the R&D group, however, and Shun Yu Chen (the lead on the emitter technology the Guizhen I uses) has been secreted away somewhere already. Her role was critical to the versatility of the technology and the integration of it into everything from fashionable clothing to crowd control vehicles.
