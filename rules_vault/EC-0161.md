## Enola Thorne

Blake may be the face of the city, but Thorne is the one actually running things. This eightyyear-old woman feels like your favorite grandma, but underneath the old-school charm is a former Maersk executive who came out of retirement at the behest of the city council. Thorne is a no-nonsense leader who is willing to color outside the lines in order to ensure that her city follows the plan being laid out for it.
