## Misdirected

Just like a game of three-card monte, the flashy moves and sly talking have diverted your attention, leaving you vulnerable. They’ve got you looking at the wrong thing, and they can take advantage of the situation while your attention is on the shiny monkey. You take a –1 dice pool penalty on Perception tests per level of the status condition.

A con artist can apply this status with a Stealth (Palming) + Agility test for physical misdirection, or Con + Charisma for a mental misdirection.
