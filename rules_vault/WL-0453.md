## **Sonic Projection**

**==> picture [228 x 20] intentionally omitted \<==**

**----- Start of picture text -----**<br>
TYPE ACTION RANGE DURATION<br>P Major LOS Instant<br>**----- End of picture text -----**<br>

**Some critters, especially marine mammals, have learned to master sound much as they might other forms of elemental energy. Make a ranged combat attack, using the critter’s Magic + Agility. The attack has a DV of (Magic)P, Attack Ratings of Near (Magic x 2), Close (Magic x 2) – 2, Medium (Magic x 2) – 8, Far (Magic x 2) – 10. If the Attack Rating drops to 0 or lower, the elemental attack cannot reach those ranges.**
