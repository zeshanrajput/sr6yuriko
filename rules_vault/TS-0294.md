## **Public Defender**

**Uses: additional contacts, contacting inmates, legal advice**

**Places to Meet: prison, out-of-town restaurant, their offce**

**Similar Contacts : attorney, corrections offcer, former inmate**

**Employed by an agency or government to work inside the system to represent those who otherwise can’t afford an attorney, public defenders are licensed professionals who act as advocates for accused persons. They join meetings with clients and witnesses and attempt to research the case in question. They will negotiate charges with opposing attorneys and provide support to the accused by giving advice and answering any questions their clients may have. If a case makes it to court, the public defender will cross-examine and defend their clients while also maintaining their innocence.**

**Public Defenders fnd themselves in peculiar situations. As is the case with undercover agents, public defenders work with and understand those who belong to the questionable parts of society. This gives them a diverse set of contacts to pull from.**
