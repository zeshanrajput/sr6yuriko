**All three species are omnivorous and tend to be more active at night. The Lo Shu turtle is interesting in that it generates its own magical background. This turtle is considered to be good luck, and when one passes away, people will often build around the shell, and attempt to align the room to the defned feng shui of the turtle shell. Most people who have encountered these rare creatures have mentioned they have felt the urge to continue on and leave it alone.**

**Skills: Astral 2, Athletics 2, Close Combat 1, Outdoors 1, Perception 2, Stealth 2**

**Powers: Compulsion, Dual Natured, Guard, Hardened Armor 2, Mana Well (Taoist)**
