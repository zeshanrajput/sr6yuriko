## \<<Recording Begins>>

Thank you for inviting me to your discussion of the magical changes over recent years. I think it’s important to keep up with current thinking on magic and how it affects us and our environment. In fact, it’s a very fertile area for investigation and discussion. The mana in the world appears to be rising, and as it does, we find that there are unexpected changes in unexpected places. It’s fresh and new! So much metahuman knowledge is based on hundreds of years of learning and experimentation, but for magic we are at the very beginning of our journey to knowledge. In addition, our understanding of the impacts of magic on the non-magical parts of the world is evolving and changing as we study it more. Admittedly, a lot of that research is happening behind closed doors, but even the most secret research seems to leak out eventually. Between people changing or leaving the corporate research powerhouses, accidental data leaks, and the very few nonprofit organizations around, there

Augmentations, Essence, and the Meaning of Lifeis quite a lot of information out there if you’re willing to go looking for it. At this point, I want to put in a plug for the open-sorcery movement, spearheaded by my society, the Sorcerers Institute. We believe that when more people know about magic, it will become an important part of how metahumanity sees ourselves as people, instead of arbitrarily dividing along Awakened and non-Awakened lines. Please consider joining us if you feel you want to work in this area.

As you can see from these slides, my personal specialty is on the aura, or Essence, of a person. Leaving aside the finer distinction between those two things for now, I hope to bring recent developments to you, as well as discuss the effects of bodily changes on your essential self. I will also touch on current events and some of the major players in the area. For those of you who wish to find out more, I have published these notes, alongside the research that led to them, on ShadowSea.

Bear in mind that much of this is very subjective. I will be referring to Essence and auras largely interchangeably today, but they are not quite the same thing. Even the term “Essence” is not universal—what I refer to as Essence, some traditions call a soul, others an aura. Some traditions don’t distinguish between Essence and the body itself, such that a body is not complete without an essence, and an essence is not complete without a body. In fact, this is a very useful perspective to take when the body or the Essence are too damaged or diminished to survive. Living, however, in this crass and material world, we find we can approximate and estimate how much Essence someone has. It is a marvel in a way— we can know how much Essence can be damaged before the body just gives up, and vice versa. Unfortunately, like almost everything, the voracious appetite for profit drives megacorporations to exploit this as a resource, pushing their employees _right_ up to the edge of oblivion. That most people can’t easily see the auras or essence of themselves and those around them only makes it easier to implant someone with cyberware. When damage, injury, or sickness is not visible, it is natural for people to ignore it and put it out of their minds; we grow complacent to damage that is happening when we do not see it with our own eyes.
