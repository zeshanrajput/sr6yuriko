## Local Character

## Heart of Humanis

You have connections in the Humanis Policlub and Human Nation that you can call on to apply some “meta”physics.

- **Cost:** 3 Karma

- **Game Effect:** You gain a special 5-Edge action where you can call on help from a group of four Humanis Policlub or Human Nation members. You can schedule a time for them to deliver help; the soonest possible time is half an hour anywhere in Seattle but Snohomish (five minutes in Snohomish). Policlub members will act as a distraction. Nation members will become violent for you.
