## Rule One: Equal Names 

If the name of a thing (meaning weapon, gear, spell, quality, or whatever) is the same in _SR6_ as it was in _SR5_ , keep the name and use the new rules as stated in _SR6_ . No Karma or nuyen is subtracted or added in this process, but check your ’ware and adjust your Essence if that cost has changed. 

_Jane glances over her character sheet, thinking about what will move to the new version of Trix. The details of her armor vest in SR5 are completely different from the armor vest in_ SR6 _, the Sixth World Edition, but they play the same role within each ruleset, so it transfers to the new version of Trix as an Equal Name item. Jane will just use the new rules for it._
