## Hearing Expansion

A more unusual advancement expands the sound spectrum that normal humans can detect, allowing them to hear both infrasonic sound and ultrasonic within a band ranging from 5 Hz to 50 kHz. The primary advantage of this is hearing ultrasonic devices. This quality also results in minor visible changes to the size and structure of the outer ear. Like hearing enhancement, this modification is often paired with the biosculpture moderate modification to give the patient animal-looking, functional ears.

This enables the character to hear into the ultrasonic or infrasonic ranges. This does not give the ability to choose or control any part of the frequency spectrum in question. As this kind of hearing is always active, noises inaudible to others may sometimes distract or deafen the character. This bioware is incompatible with any implanted cyberears or hearing augmentations except the hearing dampener as a standalone implant.
