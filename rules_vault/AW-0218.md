## disian magic limitations

While Disian magic is extremely potent on the Sixth World metaplane, it does have some limitations. Because Disian magic is directly tied to life energies, it’s less potent against those who are augmented by technological or artificial means (both cyber and bioware). Those who have such enhancements have a built-in defense against Disian spells. In game terms, this means two things:

- The lower their target’s Essence, the worse the magic. A Disian Magician loses a number of their hits based on the target’s Essence rating. If the target’s Essence is between 4.00 and 5.99, they lose 2 hits. If it’s between 2.00 and 3.99, they lose 3 hits. If it’s between 0.01 and 1.99, they lose 4 hits. They do not lose any hits against Essence 6.0 or higher targets.

- For every two points of Essence lost to augmentations, the target gains an additional point of Edge when fighting against a Disian magician.
