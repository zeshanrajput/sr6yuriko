## Granite

## **Cost:** 15 Karma

Extensive calcite and keratin formations make the individual’s skin appear greyish and hard as stone. It grants +4 Defense Rating and the Hardened Armor ability (p. 224, _SR6_ ) at rating 2. Not cumulative with other worn armors. Incurs a –1 dice pool modifier to fine manipulation of small items.
