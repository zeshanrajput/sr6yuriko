## **New Orleans, Louisiana, CAS**

## **Status: Friendly**

**Unlike other locations that offer the Disians some kind of strategic beneft or furtherance of their goals, New Orleans presents a different kind of threat: knowledge.**

**Since the confict began, anti-Disian magical practitioners of all kinds have held multiple secret conferences in New Orleans to discuss strategy and share information on all things Dis. With the wealth of magical knowledge and multitude of expert practitioners living within or around the city, these impromptu conferences have led to numerous breakthroughs in understanding and developing methods to defend against or counter Disian magic. As such, New Orleans has become the prime clearinghouse on all things Disian-magic related.**
