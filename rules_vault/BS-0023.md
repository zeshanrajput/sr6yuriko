## **Explicitly excluded are:**

- Any implant that adds to an attribute

- Headware

- Implants that use capacity; e.g., hidden compartments in a cyberleg or a fingertip compartment

In addition, as these implants are intended to re-align the outer body to the inner Essence, they should fit with the runner’s personality and background. This is something to discuss with the gamemaster, who has final approval on any Essence change. It is also unlikely that a runner’s Essence will change significantly over the course of the runner’s career (unless they are very lucky), so this conversation will usually happen at character creation.

This rule can apply to cyberware taken at character creation. This can allow for an Ork Poseur to have cyberware to become more of an ork while not reducing Essence or Magic.

This optional rule cannot increase Essence that is reduced because of an implant currently in use.
