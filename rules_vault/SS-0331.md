## Professional Cleaner

## (Professional Rating 6)

Business in the shadows is often messy, both metaphorical and literal. And sometimes things happen that bring attention to the mess. Professional cleaners are those called into make sure such messes are properly tidied. Whether it means destroying evidence (especially bodies), silencing witnesses, deleting troublesome data, or simply making sure problems simply go away (by whatever means necessary), the cleaners are the one to call to tidy things up and take care of loose ends.
