## Expanded Teamwork Test Examples

When assisting an Influence (Negotiation) skill test, instead of using Influence (Negotiation) + Charisma, player characters might:

- Watch the opposition’s reactions for tells: Judge Intentions or Perception + Intuition

- Look up information to support the negotiation: Electronics (Computers) + Intuition

- Make the target feel uneasy or uncomfortable: Influence (Intimidation) + Charisma

- Perform a stunt to show off or demonstrate competence: almost any skill test, provided it can be practically demonstrated within the target’s presence. If the action seems inappropriate for the situation, the GM may rule that it has no effect, or even provide a bonus to the opposition instead of the leader of the Teamwork test.

- Bring up specific points based on a relevant knowledge skill: Memory or Perception + Intuition

- Assense the target to read their aura for emotional reactions: Astral + Intuition. This requires astral perception, of course.

- Most of these examples require a means for

the team to subtly communicate, such as texting over comms via DNI, using subvocal microphones, Mindlink spells, or even using subtle gestures, facial expressions, or body language in some cases. Being able to assense a target’s aura to see what they’re feeling doesn’t help a negotiation if you can’t share your impressions with the leader in real time—and speaking them out loud so the target can also hear isn’t going to help.
