## The Job

The runners are startled by a loud banging on the door. When they look at the monitoring camera, the runners see none other than Fluffy Duck banging on their door. He is carrying two large bags of freshly cooked food.

Once the runners open to door, Fluffy Duck pushes his way in and launches in on the story of the job. “Silver Nail was doing some investigating at the Columbia University Morningside Heights campus. and he needed to talk to some professors. As he was walking between buildings, he spotted some people following him. He can’t shake them, and he needs some help. He gave me this address— he got it by following you after you dropped off the car. He wants you to know there are no hard feelings. I need help—he’s my friend, and if you won’t come with me, I’m going to have to do this alone. I can pay you 2,000 nuyen apiece.”

Fluffy Duck is emptying out his savings here—the only concession the PCs can get through an Influence test is some free food. He also promises to keep his ears open in case he hears something the players might want to know. The PCs gain Fluffy Duck as a contact with a Connection 2 and Loyalty 2.

Once the players agree to take the job. Fluffy Duck will say, “Great! Let me get some things out of the van and I’ll be ready to go.” He will follow them in his van if necessary.
