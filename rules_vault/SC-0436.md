## sniper

## Plot Hooks

Lone-wolf snipers are trideo characters or amateurs who won’t last long in the shadows. Professional snipers work as a part of a team, even if that team’s primary job is to serve as patsies who can be blamed for their actions. This means that even shadowrunners who don’t ordinarily work alongside a sniper might be paired with a freelancer for the odd job here or there. Worse still are the occasions where shadowrunners have slotted off the wrong party. There are few ways safer to express hostility with professional shadowrunners than from extreme range. Several hooks, seeds, and plot twists incorporating snipers are provided below.
