## The Bayou

## Security Rating: Z

## The Barrens

The bayous are the swamps of New Orleans, collections of walking and boating paths with a small spattering of houses on the water. You can divide the bayous up into three sections. The shallow bayous are where most tourists and even locals go or live. Lots of walking trails are beaten into the ground, and wooden foot bridges make the swamp walkable enough that the only real danger is Awakened insects and a few snakes.
