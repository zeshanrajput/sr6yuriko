## Remodeling Ring

- **Formula:** Ram [Wall], Verbal, Somatic

- **Drain Value:** 4

- **Cost:** 400¥/charge

Often the only thing between defeat and victory is a poorly placed wall. This preparation, made in the form of a ring, is a decent solution to that problem. Lay a hand (wearing the ring orhaving it on your person) against the wall, utter the activation phrase, “make a hole,” and poof! Obstacle overcome.
