## Marsupial Pouch

## **Cost:** 4 Karma

The character develops a pocket-like pouch on their chest or abdomen, just like the poucha kangaroo uses to carry its young. This pouch is equivalent to a Smuggling Compartment (p. 287, _SR6_ ).
