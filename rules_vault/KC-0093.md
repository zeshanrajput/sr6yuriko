## Disease

Lagos is built over swampy stagnant water that is filled with garbage and refuse from the city. This water is a petri dish of diseases, both mundane and Awakened. The drinking water is as likely to make you ill as it is to hydrate you. Rarely does a gamemaster track every drink and meal a character takes but here, where those drinks can kill you, some kind of monitoring can help set the stage. Runners who spend “active” time in the water, meaning performing any “action” while in the water, gain the Poisoned (4S) status. Drinking the water, being forced to drink it, or being drowned in it means they face the Poisoned (6S) status.

To provide some level of protection, characters need to have access to clean water to drink. Possible ways to get clean water include buying filters or bottled water (40 nuyen/day) or having a Middle or higher lifestyle in Lagos.
