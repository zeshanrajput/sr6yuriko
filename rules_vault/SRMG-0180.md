## **From No Future, can I fit three dwarfs in a trenchcoat?**

No, only two dwarfs can fit in a single trenchcoat.

## **From No Future, the Cybereyes Rating 5 and Cyberears Rating 5 have different ratings than in the Core Rulebook. Which should we use?**

For purposes of Shadowrun Missions in the Sixth World, consider the systems in No Future to be rating 6 and 7, respectively. Thus, the Rating 5 systems use the Core Rulebook, the Rating 5 systems in No Future are now Rating 6 and the Rating 6 systems in No Future are rating 7.
