## Piper Jane

## (Journalist, Connection Rating 4)

Piper Jane is slim (because she often forgets to eat) with red hair, green eyes, and hint of Boston accent in her fast-paced newsie talk. She’s a newshound to the core and loves a good juicy story, especially one that tosses mud at the corps or any figure with too much money and not enough compassion. She’s been on the job since she graduated journalism school in Boston back in the ’70s and even took a trip back behind the wall to get the real scoop. She got more than she bargained for, but her accompanying Monad personality fits right along with her own, even if they keep talking about bringing down all the culprits behind CFD and finding AJ.

For NPC Monad rules for Piper Jane and Jinxy, see p. 149.
