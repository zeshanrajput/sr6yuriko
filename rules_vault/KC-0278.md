## Bando Keita

## Shotozumi-gumi, Seattle

Bando Keita is part of the Shotozumi-gumi in Seattle. He’s been living in the Downtown area for seven years working at the Double Down casino in the International District. Keita worked his way up from bouncer to pit boss and is known for his keen eye and his willingness to take direct action. His affable and charming nature puts the guests at ease, and he knows just when to comp some chips to a struggling newcomer and who to send afree drink to. You can find him most days working the casino. He doesn’t stand out in the crowd, but asking around at the casino will find someone who will point you in his direction.
