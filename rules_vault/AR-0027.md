## Dreamland 

Just north of Downtown, Dreamland holds some of the most notable history of the sprawl. Several minority groups made this community their own in the early years of the city, and the 

variety of cultural organizations and restaurants speaks to that ongoing diversity. This area has done a good job resisting the Sixth World tendency to strictly divide neighborhoods by wealth, and people of a wide variety of incomes mingle here. The historic **Dreamland Ballroom** has gone through a number of incarnations, and it’s currently a bar on the lower level, concert venue above. It has a few good spots for conversations that shouldn’t be overheard. The **Suede Lounge** provides a more upscale dining opportunity, with full private rooms for those who can drop enough coin to use them.
