## **Special Notes**

**When the convoy departs, the player characters receive a message from Lucia with details about the**

**convoy vehicles’ upgrades and tactics and wishing them good luck. Once the player characters have their target, they’re to call Lucia, and she’ll direct them to a handoff location. Dana Hallow won’t come willingly and will need to be rendered unconscious or otherwise restrained.**

**If the runners confront Lucia about the added danger of the target and her chimera, Lucia will pretend that she was unaware of the hazard and agree to pay them a bonus for their trouble.**
