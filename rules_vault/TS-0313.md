## **Professional**

## **Rating 2: armed Security**

**Similar to the unarmed security guard but with more testing, an armed security guard has completed all the requirements for an unarmed security guard and then taken another set of coursework. Once all the coursework is completed, they take a marksman test to prove they have a basic understanding of frearms. Most require an eight-hour training course that concludes with a simple test. These courses can be easily found on the Matrix. Once the test is completed, the applicant must submit their information to the accrediting body to obtain their license. In most areas, an unarmed security guard has none of the powers of a sworn offcer and therefore has no more rights than the average citizen. They will often still follow the “observe and report” mentality. Their additional training, however, allows them to carry non-lethal hand-to-hand items, which are most often batons.**
