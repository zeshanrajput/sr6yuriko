## **“I Want to Talk to My Lawyer”**

**First, get a lawyer. If at all possible, get one who works with runners regularly, like Kimberley Hightower in Seattle or Mitzi Goodman in San Francisco. Failing that, any good criminal defense lawyer will do. What you actually want is someone who can negotiate well on your behalf and who knows the law well enough to keep from making any stupid mistakes. Don’t worry about their trial record, because chances are, you’re not going to trial anyway.**
