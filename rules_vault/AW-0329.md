## Renraku

Renraku’s embassy is a small building located near the center of the Spire, yet still within the kindred district. Renraku has very little invested in the Place of Clouds and is primarily engaged in studying and expanding the hyper-metaplane’s Matrix. The megacorp embassy recently purchased a large, well-armed airship, but it has yet to depart from the Spire’s docks after the transaction. Renraku is preparing to send an expedition to Pirate Island with the intention of improving its Matrix infrastructure.
