## **Don’t: Bargain after Your Initial Meet Unless Things Drastically Change**

**Want to look unprofessional? As soon as a little hitch emerges in the run, go ask Mr. Johnson for a ten-percent raise. If you didn’t build at least some allowance for the unexpected into your initial bargaining, then you’re going to look like someone who has never been in the shadows before. If you low-balled yourself, then you have to live with it—suck it up and bargain better next time rather than asking Mr. Johnson to cover your mistake.**
