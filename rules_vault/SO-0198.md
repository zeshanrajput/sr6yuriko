## Reading a Lie from Auras

To sell a lie, the target really needs to believe in whatever falsehood you’re peddling to them. This is especially true when attempting to fool someone who can read a target’s mood and health simply by looking at their aura. For example, it’s a little suspicious when your target is grieving over the loss of their spouse, but reading their aura shows you their happy and relieved mood. Granted, information gathered like this can be considered subjective and open to interpretation based on what the target NPC is actually feeling or what they know. Rather, an assensing test will show possibilities and inconsistencies in statements made by an NPC.

Any character with astral perception can read the target’s aura using an Astral + Intuition test (p. 159, _SR6_ ). The net hits can be used as part of a teamwork test for resisting being conned. This teamwork test can be led by the astrally perceiving player character, but they’ll take a –2 penalty for viewing both astral and meat space.
