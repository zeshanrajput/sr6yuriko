## Saeder-Krupp

Saeder-Krupp has plenty of magical resources, and its Awakened critter R&D department is probably the most robust among all of the megacorps. Barghests are commonly used as security animals, but their paralyzing howl can cause havoc for the security forces that are meant to respond to the alarms. S-K has developed the Astral Guardian model of barghest, which is primarily designed to spot and respond to astral intruders. Its Paralyzing Howl is dampened unless it is given a specific command, at which time it can be amplified considerably.
