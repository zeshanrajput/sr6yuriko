## Game Effect

The place just gets on your nerves horribly. Also, the shower occasionally spits out blood instead of water, although it sometimes comes out of the kitchen sink. Every week, make a Willpower + Willpower (4) test, or end up Frightened (p. 52, _SR6_ ) for a full day as the haunting effects stick with you even after you leave the horrorshow.
