## **>** Orbital DK

- The Union Views—A MetaErgonomics project. That’s the name of the hotel being constructed at Lake Union and linking into the Underground; it’s not alone, and it’s a race to the finish between the competitors. Washington Depths is a rival project being spearheaded by S-K. Problem is, they’re using a lot of workers who live in Snohomish for the project. It’s creating some tension, but it adds extra security.
