## Toxic Mutant Animi

Miggon is populated by numerous creatures, some of which are recognizable as mutated versions of earthly creatures. Others are pure nightmare fuel—unrecognizable horrific abominations bristling with too many teeth and claws, oozing pus from weeping sores, and lashing out with tumor-covered tentacles. None of these are truly sapient spirits but rather are expressions of the nature of the Waste of Miggon itself. They embody corruption, mutation, suffering, and wasteful destruction, and channel their constant rage and pain into hostility against anyone and anything they encounter. They are dangerous, but at leastthey’re straightforward about it. No trains to trick you—just murderous masses of disease and terror coming right for you.
