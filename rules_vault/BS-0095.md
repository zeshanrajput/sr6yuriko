## Orientation System

An orientation system augments the user’s inner ear with a gyroscopic navigation system. The user reduces any Outdoors (Navigation) test thresholds by 3, to a minimum of 1. The system also displays the user’s current and prior positions on a mapsoft.

**Cyberware overdrive:** Add two dice, one of which must be a wild die, to an Outdoors (Navigation) test.

**Wireless bonus:** The orientation system connects with commercial global positioning systems, allowing knowledge of the user’s location anywhere on the globe. Furthermore, the user’s position can be displayed in AR to the entire team PAN or on a mapsoft.
