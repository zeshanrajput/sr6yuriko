## drug interactions table

Roll 1D6 for each drug beyond the first and total the results. Modify the result by the grade of drug taken and consult the table.

**Street-cooked drugs:** +1 to the roll result for each street-cooked drug used.

**Tailored drugs:** –1 to the roll result if all the drugs used are tailored drugs.
