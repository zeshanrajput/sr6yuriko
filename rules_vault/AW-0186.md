## Caste System

Disian society is based on two things, in this order of importance: 1) replenishing their manasphere and primary “energy” source, and 2) maintaining their social caste system;.

We’ve identified three castes thus far. Each one is responsible for maintaining some aspect of Disian society. Castes aren’t technically stratified, but some have a higher status and more esteem than others.
