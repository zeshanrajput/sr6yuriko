## **Harvester Network**

**From what we’ve determined, the Disians’ next step in their incursion is the establishment of a network of mana “Harvesters.” These massive devices will be the primary means the Disians will use to siphon mana from our metaplane. The Harvesters are derived from devices called, creatively, “the Devices.” These Devices served as prototypes and later testbeds that under certain conditions create stable portals between metaplanes; specifcally, the material plane and Dis. These evolved into the current Harvesters.**

- **Few years back, a massive mana disruption occurred in Ford’s Theater in DeeCee. It was later discovered that a “rogue” Ares Firewatch team used one of these prototype Devices to disable a known bug metaplanar entry point as part of Operation: Bottleneck, the precursor to Operation: Latvian Gambit in Detroit months later. A lot of people forgot about it during the Blackout Crisis and subsequent fallout, but the mana warp is still active and under quarantine.**

**Once a Harvester’s components are completed, it’s transported to selected sites for local assembly. These sites—which should be a surprise to no one—are active or semi-active ley lines, other locations of magical power, or areas where the metaplanar boundaries are thinnest. Once activated, these Harvesters siphon and transfer mana to the Disian metaplane, leaving the source to die a slow death.**

**That alone should be enough to warrant action, but there’s another revolting part of the harvesting process. The initialization of the process utilizes the life energy of metahumans to harmonize the metaplanar “frequencies” (best translation from the Disian language) to facilitate effcient mana-energy transfer. And yes, this process kills the individuals in question. Although “kill” is too benign a description. The process is extremely agonizing—the individual is literally peeled apart molecule by molecule to remove their personal essence at maximum potency.**

- **Awakened individuals are optimum, but anyone in large enough quantities will do. For those who haven’t been paying attention, THIS is what we’re up against.**
