## Special Delivery Services

You have an arrangement with a special delivery service used by shadowrunners and other criminals. You can remotely purchase any item up to Availability 4 and it will be delivered to your location within an hour (1D6 x 10 minutes), regardless of legality. All purchase and delivery records are deleted, and they don’t give a frag whether the cargo is illegal or not. **Cost: 2 LP**
