## Control

Your particular talents as a Dissonant technomancer allow you to more easily and thoroughly undermine devices connected to the Matrix. While most technomancers largely affect things within the Matrix, you turn your attentions to how you can affect the physical world of devices connected to it. Elevators, doors, environmental controls, appliances, drones, vehicles, and augmentations are all vulnerable to one such as you. And while you can so easily brick such objects, you find it much more useful to turn them away from their original owners and over to your own will. Machine sprites are your favorites—their Override power allows them to jump into and take direct control of a device. The wireless world is designed to serve megacorporate masters and the slumbering masses

- **Negative qualities:** Dissonance Confusion, Dissonance Gremlins, Dissonance Stigma

- **Life modules:** Drone Technician, Engineer, Matrix Security, Event: Your hard work paid off.
