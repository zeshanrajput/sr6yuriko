## Influence

This power gives the critter the ability to implant a suggestion in the target’s mind. Make a Magic + Charisma vs. Willpower + Logic Opposed test. If the critter succeeds, its target acts on the suggestion as if it were their own idea. If the target is confronted with the wrongness of the suggestion (such as “shoot your spouse in the face”), the subject can make a Willpower (critter’s Magic) test to overcome it. Any hits on this test allow a dice-pool penalty on whatever action must be performed. If the character fails at the Willpower test, Edge Boosts and Actions can be used in reverse, i.e., 4 Edge to subtract your Edge from your dice pool or 5 Edge to create an event like the gun jamming.
