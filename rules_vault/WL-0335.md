## **African Gargoyle**

## **(Gargoyle saxi sabulo)**

**The African gargoyle is regal and aloof. Tall and lean, they stand about 2.5 meters tall and weigh 200 kilograms or more. Their skin color and texture match that of yellow-to-brown limestone. Their wings are very bright white and look like clean, white linen. When not in fight, they will wrap their wings around their body, making it appear as if they are wearing a shendyt or kalasiris. Their appearance often resembles Egyptian gods from ancient hieroglyphics. African gargoyles are the most docile variety and prefer to remove themselves from a situation rather than get involved. If they are attacked and unable to get away, though, they will go into a berserk-like rage.**
