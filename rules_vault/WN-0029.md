## The Manhattan Matrix

A chummer of mine once described New York’s Matrix host as “Hacking Hell.” She might not have been far off. NYCNet is built to be a point-topoint representation of the city itself. This means that wherever you are physically in the city, there is a Matrix representation of that point in cyberspace. This strategy is standardized throughout the exterior spaces of the city. So, if you want to visit the Aztechnology pyramid, the point in cyberspace you’ll see when you arrive will look exactly like Midtown where the Pyramid physically exists. If you don’t pay attention, you can forget whether you’re in the real world, experiencing augmented reality, or in the Matrix itself.
