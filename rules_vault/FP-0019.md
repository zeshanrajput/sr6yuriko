## **Goals**

**Dadeson: What does the Asintmah Alliance want? Sarkenbleck: We seek a return to the Golden Era, a world as pure as the birth of Asintmah. A place led by those with the knowledge and skills to create a better world, rather than oppressed by those who won a genetic lottery or have chosen to ruin their natural form and the very integrity of their minds with artifcial and mechanical replacements. We want all who are willing to work and strive for true excellence to achieve success, instead of being surpassed by those who cheat the natural order. We want a world returned to the safety of a time before our primary sources of information and communication were manipulated and falsifed by aberrations able to corrupt databases with their minds.**

**Dadeson: Describe this utopian world that you envision.**

**Sarkenbleck: A world without magic, or technomancers, or the augmented. A world where those with skills honed through practice and study reach the pinnacle. A place where the information we read is vetted and true, not compromised by deviants out to corrupt the world in their twisted image. A return to the purity of the world before the so-called Awakening, as well as Emergence, and transhumanist views.**

**Dadeson: What are you willing to do to accomplish these goals?**

**Sarkenbleck: What we are** _**not**_ **willing to do is a smaller list. This world needs a cleansing of this deviant one percent that blithely push those truly deserving of the other ninety-nine percent into obscurity. We will take whatever action and seek whatever enlightenment and education are necessary to relegate these charlatan rogues to the basal social niche they belong to. Don’t get me wrong, we would never stoop to the level of accepting aid from those who should not exist, but they can serve our cause as mercenary fodder for the conficts to come.**
