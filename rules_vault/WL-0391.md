## **Fetch**

**The trigger for the fetch command must include some way of pointing out what you would like the critter to fetch for you. The target must be within line of sight unless the critter is already familiar with the object and its location. Retrieving an item that the critter cannot currently perceive counts as complex orders, requiring the critter to make a Comprehension test.**
