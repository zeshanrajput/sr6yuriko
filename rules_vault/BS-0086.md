## Metagenic Cosmetic Alteration

Minor cosmetic alterations such as eye-color changes and bosom enhancements predate cybernetics. A wide variety of subcultures from cosplayers, furry fandom, fetishists, and all manner of sports and music fanatics embrace more extreme SURGE-like enhancements. The following metagenic qualities from _Sixth World Companion_ can be purchased as cybernetic enhancements:

- **Animal Pelage:** Camouflage, Insulating Coat, Quills

- **Metagenic Morphisms:** Bone Spikes

- **Negative Metagenic Qualities:** Striking Skin Pigmentation, Unusual Hair, Feathers, Insectoid Features, Scales

The nuyen cost for these cybernetically gained metagenic qualities is 1,000¥ per point of Karma (bonus or cost), but the quality neither givesnor costs any Karma. The augmentation costs one tenth of this Karma value in Essence. For example, replacing one’s hair with artificial feathers costs 4,000¥ and costs 0.4 Essence, and the mechanical effects are as described on p. 129 of _Sixth World Companion_ .
