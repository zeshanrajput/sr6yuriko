## **Are the Expanded Rules for Contacts at Character Creation (6WC, p. 161) legal for** _**SRM**_ **?**

Yes, but the hard limit for Connection and Loyalty is 6 for _SRM_ . Using this rule, a character with Charisma 1 could get a Connection 5 contact at Loyalty 1 with their base Charisma x 6 pool of points. In addition, characters can spend Karma to gain additional points.
