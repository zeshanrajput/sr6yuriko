## Magic Inc.

## Gaines (human, male, 26, brown hair, brown eyes, lean build)

As the social adept face of the team, Gaines is always looking for the opportunity to talk his way out of trouble or into money. He’s not averse to violence, but he prefers to apply it only to situaFREE CITY BLUES // LET’S MAKE A DEAL OR TWO


tions as a last resort, even preferring to disengage and regroup. As a planner, this is always a part of the plan. Often, this part of the plan will involve disengaging and moving back to a stronger defense point, possibly to launch an ambush.

Gaines typically leads with a con. He rarely plays himself and instead pulls out a new persona for every situational need. His disguise skills make him a social chameleon. He will likely play the bait for the ambush by playing at being a bad actor. He’ll keep tabs for a bit but leave dangling clues until the team sees through his act. Then, he’ll lure them into the trap by running to the nearby ambush point, a nearby parking garage or an alley if a garage isn’t feasible.

He’s not going to leap into combat in any instance, and if the runners get him before he can launch an ambush, he should have the skills to talk anyone out of killing him or even harming him. If the dice don’t roll his way? Then it’s time to rely on fast feat and luck.
