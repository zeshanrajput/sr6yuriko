## **The Job**

**If the player characters take the job, Ms. Johnson gives them a datachip containing all the information she has thus far. What the players may or may not know is that Ms. Johnson is head of a local Grey Cell network and is investigating the possibility of another “Dutchville” type incident where an entire town (people, buildings, infrastructure, and all) were taken by the Disians. Furthering that worry is the fact that there have been possible chimera sightings in the area.**

**Note that if the players happen to already be affliated with Grey Cell, then Ms. Johnson will also give the information presented above. If not, then she will simply hire them to investigate the area for any unusual activities and have them check in as necessary.**

**The town in question is known as Stony Plains, a small corporate community of about 700 people. Owned and operated by Happy-Go-Lucky Farmers LLC (a subsidiary of Aztechnology fve times removed), the town is a major producer of soybeans and other crops. Two days ago, all communications traffc ceased, but because of its remote location and frequent storms (including mana), no alarms have been raised yet. Combined with initial reports of chimera in the area, Ms. Johnson wants to know what’s happening before anyone else does.**
