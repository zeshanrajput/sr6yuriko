## The Criminal Network

Up until the ’20s, New York belonged to the five families of La Cosa Nostra. That was when Akira Watada, with the support of Ryumyo, began to push his Yakuza out into the wider world, targeting the world’s most recognizable cities. New York became a battleground between the Mob and the Yakuza. The hold the Mob had on New York cracked, and all sorts of underground figures started to trickle in. Fast forward to the ’80s, and the city is divided between four syndicates that maintain an uneasy peace. Between the handful of other syndicates trying to get a foothold in the city and the ever-present NYPD, there’s a delicate balance in place, and any change is bound to have dramatic consequences on everyone.
