## **Building Influence**

**While Convergence has a large enough endowment to support their operations, it’s still a pittance when you compare it to the wealth of Big Ten, or even large nations. They are currently not in position to shape world affairs, and they believe they will need to be, so they are working on it. As it turns out, diviners and hackers are well-positioned to build up a large store of favors. Give someone a**

**heads-up about an event coming their way, defend them from an aggressive hack, or otherwise provide useful information, and you earn yourself a considerable favor. They are also looking at more tangible kinds of infuence, including placing people friendly to them in pivotal positions and building up stock positions that, while not being majorities by any stretch, can play the role of key swing votes in divided shareholder elections. They don’t know how much time they have, but they suspect it’s not much, so they’re operating like they’re in a hurry.**

- **Which means Kechibi code. They don’t want the full-on aggro version of it, though—they don’t want to bring down a nation or anything. But if they can combine insider information/prophecy with some Kechibi meddling, they can build stock wealth very quickly.**

- **Dr. Spin**

- **They should be careful, though, to make sure their investments are supported by actual value, otherwise they could end up holding hoards of worthless virtual paper.**

- **Mr. Bonds**
