## Cars

## Chrysler-Nissan Jackrabbit

These little electric subcompacts are everywhere. Built with only the slightest styling changes for several decades this vehicle’s popularity and anonymity draw runners to it for stakeouts and tailing targets. Just don’t get into a car chase.
