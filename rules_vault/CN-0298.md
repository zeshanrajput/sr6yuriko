## The Lieutenant

The right hand of the boss, the first lieutenant ( **wakagashira** ) is the direct head of the strong arm of the Yakuza, making sure that BTLs and shabu are moving, debts are paid, and so on. They rarely, if ever, get their hands dirty directly (they had plenty of that when they were younger) but, in a pinch, they can be counted on to take care of matters personally in order to save face for the oyabun. They are, to a person, harsh taskmasters and strict disciplinarians, and they are treated with fear and awe by those beneath them. When an oyabun retires, it’s generally the first lieutenant who takes their place.
