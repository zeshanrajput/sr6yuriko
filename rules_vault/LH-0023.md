## Field Update \<03-16-83/1829:02>

News from our Wuxing source isn’t all good. While the data was incomplete, there was enough to indicate a new weapon has been developed and is being field tested by Aztechnology. Exact specs are currently unknown, but data indicates this new weapon could be used to augment or supplement Harvesters. A special task force has been assembled to investigate and, if necessary, deal with this new threat.
