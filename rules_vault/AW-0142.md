## Suttee

## (Fire, Force 5)

Suttees are predatory, feline elementals. When hunting, their fur is coal black, blending into the ash wastes. As they approach their prey, they unsheathe glowing-hot, scythe-shaped claws and fangs. Suttees then leap with ferocity, manifesting a great flaming mane.
