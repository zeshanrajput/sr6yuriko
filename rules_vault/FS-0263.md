## glowcoat

| **AVAIL** | **COST** |
| --------- | -------- |
| 2         | 40¥      |

**Wireless:** You gain a bonus Minor Action when you turn the illumination on or off. You can double the range of the lighted area with a Minor Action. **Note:** Glowcoat reduces any Blinded penalties originating from darkness for you and anyone standing within the glow of the light (5 meters) by 1 level. Turning the illumination on or off requires a Minor Action.
