## Type II Infected

Like ghouls, the some Type II infected go into viral remission on July 26. They begin to recover some of their previous biological functions, but their gross physical changes from their Infection do not disappear. They no longer require metahuman flesh to sustain themselves but are still physically adapted to a raw meat diet. When the virus begins to re-emerge on August 3, they take on the full effects again, although some show variance in their expression that was not present before the remission.
