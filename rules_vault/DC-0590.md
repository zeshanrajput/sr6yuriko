## Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, T-bird

When one side of a chase has an obviously extreme advantage, don’t bother rolling dice. They get to keep up with, outrun, or outmaneuver the opposition due to their superior capabilities. If the disadvantaged party wants to get away, they fail. Alternatively, if the advantaged party is trying to escape, they get away.
