## Fast Friend

Whenever you make a new friend, the initial bond is strong. People who like you seem to really like you. **Cost:** 8 Karma

**Game Effect:** You must have a Charisma of 2 or greater in order to take this quality. When you gain a new contact, their Loyalty rating starts at 2 (instead of 1). If you take this quality at character creation, add +1 to the Loyalty ratings of all your contacts up to the maximum Loyalty allowed at character creation (Charisma).
