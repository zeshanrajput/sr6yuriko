## **From No Future, both AutoVoice and Vocal Expansion refer to 5[th] Edition rules, how does it apply in 6[th] ?**

For AutoVoice, the rating provides a dice pool modification equal to the rating for any skill test using Con (Singing). For Vocal Expansion, adds its rating to the Social Rating for social encounters using Influence or Con.
