## Ti Khao

You’ve been trained to use your knees to attack an opponent while grappling, whether by kneeling on them to choke them out or striking them for a bit of extra oomph. Add +1 DV to damage you inflict while grappling (see **Grapple** , p. 111, _SR6_ ). **Category:** Grappling
