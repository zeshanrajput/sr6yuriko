## crafting with true air table

- **Animated.** The focus has the ability to move as per the Animate spell (p.139, SR6). It can be mentally commanded to do so if within (owner’s Magic Rating + [Force of focus x 2]) meters of them. The owner may also quick draw (p. 42, SR6) the focus, making it magically leap into their hand. The focus must be within 1 meter of the owner for this to happen.

- **Disruptive.** The focus can temporarily damage itself to create a low mana ebb (p.168, Street Wyrd) centered on its location. The mana ebb is (Force x 10 meters in diameter and lasts for Force in minutes. Focus recovers 1 point of force every 24 hours.

- **Elemental Mastery.** The focus can gain +2 dice in its use in Conjuring tests on the related spirits of that metaplane (Earth to earth, etc.) or gain +2 dice in spell effects related to the true element.

- **Secretive.** The focus is loyal and resists giving away information. The focus makes a poor astral link (p.161, SR6). Add (Force + 1) to Astral tracking tests to follow this focus to the owner. It also hard to assense, adding (Force + 1) to the focus to resist assensing (p. 152, SR6).
