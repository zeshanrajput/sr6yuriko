## Phobia (Object)

You have an immediate negative reaction to a certain object, thing, or phenomenon, to the point that you will do whatever is in your power to avoid the thing in question.

**Common** : Ants, cats, dogs, heights, metatypes, the number 13,

**Uncommon** : Awakened people, confined spaces, snakes, specific gangs

**Rare** : Awakened critters, Dissonance, the Infected, spirits, technocritters, technomancers, types of telesma **Very rare** : Dragon scales, vampire fangs
