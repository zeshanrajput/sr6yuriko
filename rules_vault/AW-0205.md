## Saeder-Krupp

S-K is an enigma on many levels. We have documents confirming Lowfyr’s knowledge about Dis and that S-K has helped secure the facility known as Site 000. But other than that, the great dragon’s claws appear to be clean. I also find it hard to believe that a great dragon like Lowfyr would let a threat like Dis continue. Unless this is part of another game he is playing.
