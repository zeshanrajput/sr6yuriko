## Reputation 

For the purposes of SRM 2081, Reputation represents the runners reputation within the shadow community. Each mission calls out any Reputation gain for post-mission “Picking up the Pieces.” Having a Reputation that is 10 or higher, grants the runners a point of Edge when dealing with other NPC in the shadow community. If their Reputation is -10 or lower, they grant edge to NPC in the shadow community. Future seasons may align Reputation with specific factions.
