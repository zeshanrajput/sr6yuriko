## Puyallup Sites

## Euphoria Memorial

This memorial was made famous by the sim _Against the Hive Masters._ The Strice Foods-Amber

Gel facility has become a shrine to Euphoria and her music. The facility is still abandoned, though the first floor and surrounding grounds have been masterfully wired up with a larger-than-life trid projection of Euphoria and AROs of her music and photos projecting inside and out. While the artist is unknown, the memorial art is simply called Euphoria.
