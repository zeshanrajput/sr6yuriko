## Obstacles

The most obvious problem is that plenty of people won’t even stop to see what you dropped. If you’ve ever taken public transportation at rush hour in a plex, you know damn well what I’m talking about. Pick your place as well as your mark, somewhere you can harness a little shame if you’re pitiable, isolation if you want to scare them and there’s no one around to help or witness.
