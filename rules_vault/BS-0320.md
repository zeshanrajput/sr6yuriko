## Genetic Optimization

This is a broad set of genetic modification procedures based on the genetic profile of individuals with exceptional attributes and talents. By tweaking the genes responsible for the development of a given attribute, the physical and mental potential of an individual is increased. The same techniques can be applied to increase the maximum potential for specific skill sets. Each time genetic optimization is used, either the metatype maximum of one chosen Physical or Mental attribute is raised by one point or the maximum rating that can be obtained in a skill is increased by one point. Each attribute’s metatype maximum or each skill’s maximum rating can only be increased once, but any number of different attributes or skills can be chosen with different applications. This procedure only increases the potential maximum—it does not raise the attribute or skill. This genetic modification is fully compatible with and stacks with both the Aptitude (Skill) quality and the Exceptional (Attribute) quality (pp. 70–71, _SR6_ ).
