## Okoro’s Vanguard

The anti-environmental might of the megacorps is considerable, and no one law firm is going to be able to take on everything corp lawyers can throw at them. In her ongoing environmental crusade, Eniola Okoro has built a phalanx of law firms, a collection that boasts disparate talents and a lot of ways to solve the various problems her work presents.
