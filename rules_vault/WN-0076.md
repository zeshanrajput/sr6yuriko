## Sony Corporation

From its humble beginnings as a post-war radio repair shop to console video game champion to multinational keiretsu, Sony has always done business in Manhattan. Sony Plaza is approaching its one-hundred-year anniversary, and the company hopes to celebrate the occasion with a record year of growth.

The corporation’s closest AAA-level ally is Horizon, who keeps the Sony name out there by using a lot of Sony technology in their operations. Recent conflicts with MCT, who seems particularly riled about the role the corporation played in keeping them off the MDC for a second time, have hurt the company’s market share in other parts of the world. For this reason, Sony is pressing their role in NYC’s government and considering moving their world headquarters to the Big Apple.

Sony Personal Butler drones are the top-selling automated assistants in the city. This, alongside end-user electronics, keeps Sony’s name on the lips of its consumers. That isn’t enough for Sony, who continues to put their brand out there using advertising drones and zeppelins that circulate throughout the city.
