## Vector

Like traditional drugs and toxins (p. 121, _SR6_ ), the BTL or ripjack must be delivered to the target in one of two ways: Slotting and Wireless. The main difference is that the Wireless method reduces the Power by 4 (or, if the attacker has Admin access, reduces the Power by 2).

**Slotting** : This method injects the code directly into the system, either by slotting a chip or connecting a sim console.

**Wireless** : This approach attempts to deliver the payload via cyberattack, accessing the system remotely.
