## Evidence/Clues Available

Both the bank and Feather have an abundance of evidence on Okoro’s activities against the corps. Embiro also has evidence of Okoro’s funding of Ayeowo but does not have any data on the Kechibi Code.

Feather’s data has all of Embiro’s from the terminal, plus it contains notes about Embiro’s activities and a bunch of crazed ranting journals about her daughter and what she could have been.
