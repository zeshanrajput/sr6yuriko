## Astral Bluff

This metamagic technique allows the initiate to quickly adjust his aura to give a temporary false impression. The initiate can make a Minor Action to make small adjustments to their emotional state or health to help sell any lie they are trying to tell. The character makes a Con + Magic (3) test, then they are able to match minor aspects of their aura to the falsehood for a number of minutes equal to their net hits. If anyone assensing the character stays within visible range for longer than the duration, the viewer automatically sees the lie when aura reverts. When the astral bluff is active, the character gains a point of Edge when used in conjunction with a Con test.
