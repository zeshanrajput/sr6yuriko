## The Job

Rikki has Donovan James “DJ” Eastling (from mission 12, p. 69) stashed at a secure apartment, but he wants some additional measures. He has two Evo Proletarian IIIs (p. 94, _Double Clutch_ ), both having two integrated cyberlimbs instead of one, and he is willing to share operation with the team’s rigger. He keeps a Whispering Death (p. 107, _Double Clutch_ ) on call in a hidden ceiling panel in the front room. Several Cyberspace Dragonfly Alphas and Halos (six each, p. 105, _Double Clutch_ ) stand ready outside and inside the building for surveillance. He’s had a friend provide him with a warded space to help with magical defenses (rating 6 with flexible signature properties, causing it to appear as rating 2, p. 145 and 167 respectively, _SR6_ ).

The job is simple: assist Rikki in finding out why and how DJ performed the actions he did. (If the runners killed DJ, then Rikki has the backup sleepwalker instead.) Unfortunately, an interruption will make the job trickier.
