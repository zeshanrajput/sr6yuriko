## Geography 

## Downtown 

Between the river and a bend in highway 480 is the core of the city. It’s not as dense or as tall as the core of a lot of sprawls, but it still has a decent share of buildings that will let you see beyond the city limits and to the vast flatlands beyond. **Creighton University** is in the northwest section of the area, and the **Heartland of America Park** along the river gives downtown denizens a taste of nature and beauty.
