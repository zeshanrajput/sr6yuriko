## Knowsofts

Knowsofts replicate Knowledge skills, actively overwriting the user’s knowledge with their own data. Knowsofts must be accessed with a skilljack, and the number you can use at once is limited by the skilljack rating. Knowsofts, like Knowledge skills, do not have ratings.
