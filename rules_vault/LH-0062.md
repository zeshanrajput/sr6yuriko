## Chimera Liberation!

## Posted by: Iron Horse Actual

The time for the liberation of oppressed beings is at hand! Most of us fighting against the Disian scourge know the chimeras only as monsters, weapons that the Disians use to hunt prey down like dogs. But what if I told you there are some who managed to hold on to their metahumanity and remember who they were despite the best Disian efforts to erase all that?

I speak the truth! Even now, those who’ve managed to throw off the shackles of Disian enslavement have joined the ranks of the Defenders! Many of them have been welcomed into my mercenary command, the Iron Cavalry, where they have served with valor and honor against the Disian threat. Others … they’ve also found new purpose within our cause.

We must find more of those who can be liberated, while at the same time giving release to those who cannot.
