## (Chief of R&D at Yingyong)

The current leading research director, Gu is a forty-three-year-old non-binary elf of unmistakable intellect and sense of purpose in their work. Originally from Jiujiang, Jiangxi Prefecture, Gu has always been a striking and complicated being. They are known for their ability to bring unrelated technologies together to create new synergies, and forging new processes and technologies to make whole new applications for daily and task-specific uses.
