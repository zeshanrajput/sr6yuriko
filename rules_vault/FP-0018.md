- **Let’s preface this with some stuff that doesn’t pop in later. The Asintmah Alliance is a secret society that has done an exceptional job of staying off the global radar, mainly by accomplishing nothing major but apparently being linked to insane events around the world.**

**The name comes from Asintmah, the Athabaskan earth goddess who was the first woman on earth. The group has a weak Native American connection based solely on some membership and the name, but their anti-magic stance seems counter to any kind of totemic relationship; they apparently use the name to represent a “purer” time.**

**They are anti-enhancement, pro-“simpler” times, meaning no magic, no augmentation, no Matrix. They attempt to live in some kind of utopian global society that never really existed, but these guys can certainly sell the story that it did. The secret society hides behind several layers of other private and “secret” organizations that it uses for recruitment to its inner circle.**

**All its members are mundane, non-cybered, anti-tech, heavily skilled individuals who see the world before all those “special” aspects as a utopia worth returning to. Many members are at the pinnacle of talent in their fields (sports, science, politics, etc.) yet see those who surpass them as “cheating.”**

**Their membership numbers aren’t known, as they blend with so many other groups, but the actual Alliance still seems relatively large, though spread thin. I’d speculate membership is over ten thousand but less than one hundred thousand. While this seems huge compared to some secret societies, remember that membership here isn’t limited to magical ability or any other rare or limited asset. These are mundane people from all walks of life who just share a higher-level delusion.**
