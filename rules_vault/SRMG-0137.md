## **Can Edge Boosts and Edge Actions be reduced to cost 0 Edge?**

No, unless the Edge cost reduction is specific to a single named action or boost, such as Ballestra ( _FS_ , p. 103) and the Shank Edge Action ( _SR6_ , p. 48). Abilities that reduce cost in general, cannot reduce the cost to 0.
