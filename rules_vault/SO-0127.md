## Obstacles

The biggest risks are being found out by regulators, but it’s possible to get lost in the crowd. That said, you’re depending on chance for your anonymity, and that’s not the best tactic. Mind, when I say regulators, I mean federal, corporate, and syndicate. Each of them has different arenas and responses. Some may simply piggyback on your con, while others will demand you continue while paying them the profits. Independent revenge is a little smaller in scale, but the personal touch means someone may come after you.
