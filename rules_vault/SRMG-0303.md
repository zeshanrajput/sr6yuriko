## **Are there any clarifications on Signature Maneuver (p. 170)?**

A signature maneuver consists of two specific Edge boosts or actions. Each of these actions or boosts is only executed once. So, if you select “Reroll one die” and “Anticipation”, you cannot have the “Reroll one die” multiple times on the same maneuver.

Any modification of the cost based on Edge boosts or actions are applied to only one of the two effects. For instance, the Positive Quality Determination would reduce the cost of “Maniacal Slipstream” (the combination of Drafting and Redline) to 5 Edge instead of reducing it to 4 Edge.
