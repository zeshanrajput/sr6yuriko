## Teamwork Stealth and Perception tests

While many encounters have a strong potential for combat, keep in mind that they merely present the possibility of a direct encounter. When encountering a hazard or a potential threat, make a teamwork Perception test to determine if the group notices the danger in time before stumbling into it. If the Perception test is successful, then the group has the option of attempting to mitigate the hazard (at least partially) or make a teamwork Stealth test to avoid an encounter. Participating in the teamwork Perception test is optional, but everyone must participate in a teamwork Stealth test—especially if they’re awful at stealth. **Hazard (collapsing building, bridge, or street):** Make a teamwork Perception test, with a threshold of 1D6 + 2. If the Perception test fails, all characters must make a defense test using Reaction + Intuition with a threshold of 2D6 to avoid falling or being struck by debris: 3P damage, +1 DV for every hit below the threshold on the failed defense test. If the Perception test succeeds, the group notices the first signs of the hazard, reducing the threshold for the defense test to avoid taking damage to 1D6 (instead of 2D6).

**Encounter (atomic devil rats):** 2D6 atomic devil rats. Make a teamwork Perception + Intuition test with a threshold 7 to notice the concealed swarm. If the Perception test succeeds, the group may attempt a teamwork Stealth + Agility test with a threshold of 3 to avoid the encounter.

**Encounter (bone traders caravan):** 2D6 gnawers accompanying 1 or 2 carts or wagons. The bone traders are not initially hostile. They can be a valuable source of rumors and are willing to trade equipment in return for bones or bartered goods.

**Encounter (Crimson Carnage patrol):** 1D6 + 2 Crimson Carnage ghouls, led by a vampire lieutenant. They patrol Hubris and the wastelands of Miggon on motorcycles. Make a teamwork PerWASTESception + Intuition test with a threshold of 2 to notice them. If the Perception test succeeds, the group may attempt a teamwork Stealth + Agility test with a threshold of 3 to avoid the encounter.

**Encounter (toxic mutant horrors):** 1D6 – 2 (minimum of 1) toxic mutant horrors, stalking the Wastes of Miggon in search of prey. Make a teamwork Perception + Intuition (3) test to notice them. If the Perception test succeeds, the group may attempt a teamwork Stealth + Agility (4) test to avoid the encounter.

**Hazard (radiation spike):** Make a teamwork Perception (4) test (gain an Edge if using a radiation sensor). If the Perception test fails, all characters suffer 4P fire damage and must make an immediate mutation resistance test (see p. 152). On a success, the group notices signs of the oncoming radiation spike in time to take precautions to reduce exposure. No one suffers damage, but increase the threshold for everyone’s next mutation resistance test by 1.

**Encounter (toxic kin spirit marauders):** 1D6 Force 5 toxic kin spirits, wearing armor jackets and armed with a mix of Ruger Super Warhawk pistols and AK-97 assault rifles. Use the stats for normal kindred spirits, with Con and Firearms skills, and the optional powers of Fear and Innate Spell (Flamestrike). Make a teamwork Perception (4) test to notice them. If the Perception test is successful, the group may attempt a teamwork Stealth (5) test to avoid the encounter.

**Encounter (pack of radiant hell hounds):** 1D6 + 2 radiant hell hounds, stalking the land in search of prey. Make a teamwork Perception + Intuition (4) test to notice them. If the Perception test is successful, the group may attempt a teamwork Stealth + Agility (5) test to avoid the encounter.

**Encounter (fallout ghouls):** 2D6 fallout ghouls. If encountered within power plant or the town that surrounds it, make a teamwork Perception + Intuition (6) test to notice them. If the fallout ghouls are encountered anywhere else, the threshold to notice them is only 2. If the Perception test is successful, the group may attempt a teamwork Stealth + Agility (5) test to avoid the encounter.

**Hazard (sudden flash of light):** A sudden flash of bright light erupts. Each character must make a Perception + Intuition test with a threshold of 1D6. On a successful Perception test, you notice the sudden flash in time to close your eyes, and suffer the Blinded I status for 1D6 hours. On a failed Perception test, you suffer the Blinded III status for 1D6 hours, which is then reduced to Blinded II for another 1D6 hours, after which it becomes Blinded I for 1D6 additional hours. Characters with an allergy or vulnerability to sunlight are affected as if they were exposed to sunlight, regardless of whether or not they shut their eyes in time.

**Hazard (glass storm):** The winds pick up, carrying with them clouds of millions of tiny glass shards. Make a teamwork Perception + Intuition (4) test to notice the approaching storm. If the Perception test fails, the group is caught out in the glass storm. Everyone caught in the storm suffers 8P damage, which is reduced to 4P if the character is fully clothed and is wearing a respirator or gas mask. After the glass storm passes, all gas masks, respirators and armor that were worn have now become damaged and provide no further benefit until they can be repaired. If the Perception test is successful, the group is able to get under cover in time. The storm passes or dies down in 1D6 minutes.

**Encounter (Kr’u’Ba):** A Force 6 Kr’u’Ba, which is a bad-tempered toxic fire spirit. Make a Perception (5) test to notice it. If the Perception test is successful, the group may make a teamwork Stealth + Agility (6) test to avoid the encounter. If defeated, the spirit leaves behind a Kr’u’Ba heart—a valuable stone with a core of True Fire (p. 41).
