## Null Sect Activity

new activity in the hosts would draw them out like moths to flame, so I thought it would be a good place to start looking.

Before the _Scotophobia_ data drop, I learned that Rickman’s team had made a big breakthrough, developing a cypher that allowed for some basic Disian translations. Once I started looking around and dug into her team, I found there were two AIs who had recently joined. They were the ones responsible for the breakthrough, so I tracked them down for a chat. Since I knew what to look for, it was easy for me to peg them as Nulls. After they realized the digital cat was out of the bag, they simply revealed who they were to Rickman. I figured that would get them kicked, but to my surprise, she just added more Nulls to the Transys Neuronet payroll. Seems they made a pitch about how that first breakthrough was proof of their trustworthiness and capabilities, and she bought it hook, line, and sinker.

And it didn’t stop there. Once word got out about how _helpful_ the Nulls could be, a few other corps quietly added them to their own teams as translators and researchers. I’m pretty sure that right now, about a dozen of them are working for various organizations, and I’m thinking there are a lot more out there. Of course, you can take their story at face value if you want, but not me—I’m certain they’ve got an angle. This new routine is making me suspicious about what they’re actually up to.

I’ll keep digging and let everyone know if I uncover anything.

- One thing you can be sure of with Null Sect is that they’ll be keeping their true motives secret. Even if you think you’re making a good deal, you can be assured the Nulls will certainly be getting the better end of it.
