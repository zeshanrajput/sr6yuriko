## Game Information

Wearing cheap clothes provides a –4 penalty to Social Rating.

The penalty to Social Rating provided by this outfit does not apply when having a lower Social Rating grants Edge (such as Intimidation tests).

In locations with a Security Rating of B or lower, they grant a point of Edge on Con or Stealth tests to blend in with a crowd. This includes shadowing targets.

Cheap clothes are low-cost and are provided with a lifestyle category of Squatter or better.

Cheap clothes have zero capacity for fashion accessories.
