## Slab

(Coeur D’Hiver, Ghulpille, Reaper)

- **Vector:** Injection

- **Speed:** 1 combat round

- **Duration:** (10 – Body) hours, minimum 1 hour

- **Power:** 10

- **Effect:** Suspended animation

Slab and some of the street derivatives like ghulpille or coeur d’hiver were originally designed to be surgical aids. The drug places the user in a state of temporary hibernation, slowing breathing and heart rates. Observers require a Perception + Intuition (6) test or a Biotech + Logic (4) test to determine the character is still alive. If the runner is hooked up to proper medical equipment such as a medkit (Rating 6), it will automatically deterGAMEmine they are alive. Reaper is an Awakened variant where the drug is suffused with mana-enriched mycotoxins that cause the target’s aura to appear dead, increasing the threshold to assense by 4.
