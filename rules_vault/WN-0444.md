## Ghostwalker

Sick of all the astral meddling and the possibility of events that, if left unchecked, he knows will eventually lead to a rather unpleasant place, Ghostwalker is willing to simply pay to gain access to the info and bury it in the deepest hole he can find. He makes two different offers (see below), and if neither is taken, he resorts to the same thing that all be believes dragons should when they don’t get their way with the little races: destroy them and eat them as snacks.

_Sends_ : John Denver, a corporate Mr. Johnson with a distinctive name so you remember who you’re working with.

_Resources_ : Ghostwalker hires a local crew to take out the runners if they refuse to sell access to him. The crew will be partially acting as guards for Denver or blending in with the crowd at whatever

Denver (use Mr. Johnson, p. 214, _SR6_ ), Aries (use Street Samurai, p. 87, _SR6_ ), Gemini (use Street Shaman, p. 88, _SR6_ ), Libra (use Rigger, p. 86, _SR6_ ), Leo (use Technomancer, p. 89, _SR6_ ), Sagittarius (use Covert Ops Specialist, p. 83, _SR6_ ), Scorpio (use Combat Mage, p. 82, _SR6_ ), and Taurus (use Weapon Specialist, p. 90, _SR6_ ).

_Tactics_ : If neither of the offers below is taken, Denver leaves, and his team attacks. They’re skilled and relatively coordinated.

_Offer_ : First offer is 200 acres of private land in the Front Range Free Zone, along with all structures and property therein. The land currently produces annual net income of 20,000 nuyen, and this could be increased with more development. The second offer is the land along with a Z-O bank account with 150,000 nuyen.

_Conditions for Attack:_ As described above, Ghostwalker’s team attacks if his offers are rejected.

_Ways to Play Off Other Factions:_ If the PCs connect Denver to the directors at Animus, they can deal together and support each other, making them formidable against any other force. Ghostwalker will reward them with his second offer, and he will promise development subsidies on the land to make it more valuable.

_Aftermath Attack Risk:_ Low. The shadowrunners can be useful to Ghostwalker, but if they fail to be, then he will look to get what he wants from another source and will forget the PCs entirely.
