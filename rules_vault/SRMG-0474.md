## **Are extreme cyberweapon enhancements (p. 45) legal in** _**SRM**_

No.

**Are the new martial arts techniques for polearms (p. 48) legal in** _**SRM**_ **?**

Yes, except for Area Awareness. Whirlwind Slash does require the use of Agility and isn’t replaced with Strength.
