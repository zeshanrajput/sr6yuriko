## Drone Rigger

## Backgrounds

From as early as you can remember, you have been fascinated with technology. Mechanical and electrical gadgets held most of your attention, but the Matrix drew plenty of interest as well. As you grew up, your fascination revealed significant technological aptitudes. Adults often marveled at your understanding of the technology they barely understood and clearly took for granted. You were interested in understanding how it all worked, and how you might be able to make it all work better. One day, something happened that changed your life forever: You got your first drone. How you acquired it depends more upon your specific background, but the common thread among them all is that acquiring your first drone was a life-changing moment for you. You had no expensive rigger command console or control rig augmentations yet, but anyone with a commlink can operate adrone to some degree. Using the commlink interface wasn’t enough for you, however, and neither was having just one drone. Your first drone gave you a taste of an entirely new realm of capabilities and experiences. So many different kinds of drone designs in existence, and you wanted to own them all. You spent years researching and pining over different RCCs and wondering how you might ever afford control rig headware. Eventually you found a way, but it didn’t come without a price. Whether you paid off that debt or not, you made it happen. You took your expensive augmentations and hardware, along with a lovely collection of drones, with you into the shadows. Now it’s time to make some real money.
