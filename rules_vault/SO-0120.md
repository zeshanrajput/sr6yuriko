## The VIP Room

Clubs do this all the time. You pay more for the sheer exclusivity of a private room, the kind you’ve probably met Mr. Johnson at least once in your career. Of course, a big shot like you wants the finest drinks. And oh, chummer, how they sell them to you. But, and this is important, you’re not selling a bottle they can take with them, oh no. This is Bottle Service. It’s open once your cred clears, and, well, you bought it. No refunds, schmuck. Not our fault you can’t appreciate it. And if you think it’s a scam, watered down or cheap swill passed off as the good stuff? Well, you clearly have a drek palate and wouldn’t know Dom from Dos. Would you care for something else?
