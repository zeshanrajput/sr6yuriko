## **GMC Commander**

**GMC has produced some of the best workhorse vehicles money can buy, and the Commander is just another fne example of this. The Commander is a mobile command unit used when LESPs needs to put boots on the ground in situations requiring a more hands-on command structure. Building upon the Bearcat, the Commander provides a centralized platform for LESPs to coordinate and execute operations in the most effcient way possible. It features an advanced electronics suite enabling real-time communication and data sharing. The Commander is outftted to support a staff of four, including the commanding offcer, pilot, and two spiders or riggers.**
