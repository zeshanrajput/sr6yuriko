## Wanda Dato

## Fixer (Magic) in Lake Youngs

An elegant elf woman in her late sixties, Wanda knows just about every talismonger, enchanter, mage, and magical supplier in Renton. If you need to hire some magical backup or just need to track down an exotic reagent, Wanda is your woman.
