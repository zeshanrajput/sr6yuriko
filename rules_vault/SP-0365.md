## **Disian Conjuring**

**Disians may not learn the Conjuring skill and are unable to use the normal rules for conjuring spirits. They do, however, have access to a ritual they call Spirit Contracting that can be used to summon and bind spirits.**

**While unable to summon, any Disian may use their stored mana for Banishing. Because they do not have a Conjuring skill, the banishing test must be attempted using only units of mana, which become the caster’s Magic rating. Any drain incurred may be offset with units in a one-to-one ratio, resisted with two times Willpower, or a combination of both in the same way as with Sorcery tests.**
