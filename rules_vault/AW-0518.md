## Distance

The “distance” of a metaplane is a subjective ranking that describes how difficult a metaplane is to get to and how different it is from our own reality. This could be rolled randomly or simply assigned.

**Adjacent** : This category is limited to the Plane of Faerie (with its fractured metaplane of the Seelie Court), the four elemental metaplanes, and the various hyper-metaplanes contained within the metaplanes of kin, animals, and plants. The Astral Sea itself also falls into this category.

**Near** : These can be thought of as next to the eight adjacent metaplanes and are usually iterations or combinations of them.

**Intermediate** : The vast majority of the thousands of known metaplanes fall into this category. Near enough to be discovered and relatively easy to travel to, they can still present a very strange version of reality.

**Distant** : Filled with strange spirits and alien entities, the distant metaplanes can be both difficult and dangerous to explore. The Metaplane of Shadow and its hyper-metaplanes belonging to the brothers Gaf, Tak, Dru, and Obe are the bestknown examples.
