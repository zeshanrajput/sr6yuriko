## **What (Who) Does Psyche want?**

## **Posted by: Refugee**

**A common enough question that arises when anyone hears that something is moving about and they are not a part of it, directly or indirectly. The reports that have been compiled to this point would certainly lead to one possible conclusion that Psyche is probing, investigating the Matrix of this world and perhaps on other planes, to believe there might be a motive driving it. We could surmise that if we few have arrived at this possibility, others abroad have also learned of this and been compiling their own information.**
