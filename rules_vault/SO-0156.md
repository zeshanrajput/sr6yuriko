## Scaling

The best thing about this scam is that it scales up and down with ease. You can hit anything from your local city council to a Lone Star station to an Ares subsidiary. It’s the level of risk that you need to consider. And you can set things up for a one-and-done, or a chain of attacks. But no matter what you do, make sure that your plan is thought out. You don’t want to “engineer on the back end,” as it were. Decide what you want to do before you start, or else something will come up that you didn’t consider on the outset. You’ll blow your current op and lose the opportunity to go again.

- There’s nothing wrong with coming up with other things to do. But stick to the plan while you’re working. Once you’re finished, start planning again. You’ll be able to secure yourself properly before the next op.

- Rigger-X
