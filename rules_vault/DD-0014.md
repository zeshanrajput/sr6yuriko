## POSITIVE QUALITIES

## Cool Under Pressure

You’re a tough nut to crack; even under fire, you tend to keep your cool.

**Cost:** 4 Karma/level (max level 4)

**Game Effect:** You gain one extra die per level on Composure tests and tests to resist

intimidation. This does not apply on tests to resist spells or critter powers that cause fear.
