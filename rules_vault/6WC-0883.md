## Privacy Invasion

Some places are great for keeping the lookyloos from observing you. This place isn’t one of those. No matter how you drape the curtains, no matter what wi-fi defeating systems you put into place, you’re on view for the entire neighborhood. Hope you put on clean underwear.

**Cost:** –1 LP
