## Honorbound: Corporate Bushido

This is a variant of the Honorbound: Bushido quality found on p. 77, _SR6._ Holders of this quality honor the basic values of righteousness, courage, compassion, respect, honesty, honor, loyalty, and self-control, but filter all those values through the needs of their parent corporation. That means their loyalty is defined as loyalty to the corp—if they have to betray other people to maintain that loyalty, so be it. Similarly, they are honest to people from their corp. but they will lie their hoops off to people outside the corp if it furthers corporate interest. This can make them difficult to deal with sometimes, but also predictable, as you always know where their ultimate loyalties lie.

**Bonus:** 10 Karma

**Game Effect:** You cannot spend or earn Edge for twenty-four hours after you break a tenet of your code. If the same tenet is broken multiple times or broken again during the twenty-four-hour period, each infraction adds another forty-eight hours onto the initial twenty-four. If a different tenet is broken, it’s twenty-four hours for that one added to any current infractions, and the same rules apply for additional violations.

**Note:** A former Renraku employee may have permanently broken this code, thus starting them on the Boundaries of Honor quality path (p. 130, _Firing Squad_ ).
