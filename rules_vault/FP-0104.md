## **KivaNet**

**The Denver Data Haven has had common cause with KivaNet for over a decade. Created by the PCC in the autumn of 2070 as a techno-shamanic organization, KivaNet is supported by the PCC government and has a mandate to work toward the acceptance of technomancers within global society. The leadership of the Nexus is actively working toward the same end, and the common goal puts the two groups together on numerous projects. The Denver Data Haven has been known to call on KivaNet members for extra support when needed, such as the attack on the Nexus host by the Null Sect last year.**
