## **What’s in the Barn?**

**Eventually, Randy will fnish his scavenger hunt and retrieve his bicycle. He rides to an old farm approximately two kilometers southeast of town. Bypassing the boarded-up house, he goes directly to a dilapidated barn. But on further inspection, this is a façade. The barn in actuality is a small fortress with reinforced doors and walls (Structure Rating 16), with “kill slots” for weapons use.**

**But it’s the active jammer that extends fve meters past the barn’s perimeter that’s the frst indication that all is not what it seems.**
