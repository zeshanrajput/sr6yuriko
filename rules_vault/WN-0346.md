6 5 7 8 5 2 2 **IC:** Patrol, Acid, Blaster

The inside of the building contains rows of shelving up to the roof fifteen meters overhead. Automated cranes and cargo haulers move around the floor during the day but are idle after hours. The exterior camera network continues inside the building, arranged to provide line-of-sight everywhere inside the warehouse. Four rail-mounted drones, matching the exterior turrets, provide interior firepower (treat these as turrets that can be moved to cover any interior location). The center of the warehouse has a three-story building containing offices, restrooms, maintenance, and other support services. There is a large freight elevator on the side of this building that seems out of place; it also looks like the only possible way into a lower level.

The elevator door has a rating 6 maglock operated by both a keycard and keypad; neither are connected to the rating 5 building host. The elevator is operated by the wired rating 8 framework host serving the lower level, and the host is protected by a security technomancer (use DocWagon HTR Support Engineer, p. 208, _SR6_ ). Once past the door, the team discovers the elevator control only has a single button paired to a retinal scanner. A camera tied to the framework host covers the elevator’s interior. One of the MapMaster drones activates on the way down; apparently, Mr. Seven is rigging it.
