## Hyetal

## (Water, Force 5)

Graceful hyetals appear as a pair of cresting waves that can leap a meter or so out of the water as they crash on the surface. They stay on the surface of the water to avoid larger predators and skim the surface, feeding on smaller prey. The wave span of a hyetal can reach more than three meters, so they could accidentally catch a bystander in their wake.

Where earth gnomes have muted colors and rough texture, kaolins are grey/white with a smooth, ceramic texture. The concept of clothing is akin to body paint. Kaolins are twice as tall as earth gnomes and are thinner in proportion to other earth elementals.
