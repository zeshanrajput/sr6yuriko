## Strategies

Perception is one of your greatest strengths. In addition to Magic, Willpower, and Charisma (which are crucial for all shamanic magicians), Intuition is your most important attribute because it is used for Perception as well as Astral (Assensing) tests. You’re very perceptive using mundane sensEAGLEes and technological sensors, but your team likely relies on you heavily to keep an eye on astral space. Your own astral senses or summoned spirits should always be watching over you and your teammates, gleaning useful information by assensing auras and astral signatures and watching out for astral threats. You are also quite capable of noticing and assensing the presence and strength of magical security: wards, patrolling spirits, astrally projecting magicians, and even alchemical traps. At least some of your spells should be detection magic.

The key drawback of being an Eagle shaman is your allergy to pollutants. Make sure to keep a respirator handy for regular use in any urban areas and a gas mask for short-term use for really nasty jobs. Chemical protection for your armor is also highly recommended.
