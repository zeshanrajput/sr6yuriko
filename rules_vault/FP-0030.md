## **Blackout**

**Inspired by the blackouts and the massive infux of potential candidates they brought, the Asintmah Johnson hires the runners to set up a collection of small but powerful jammers around a sporting event. The runners are instructed to activate them as the patrons begin to leave via the Matrix-driven systems to fnd transportation, purchase souvenirs, post on social media, and other essentials that will suddenly be unavailable or pumping out incorrect or outdated information. In the resulting chaos, tipped-off Asintmah-controlled media will show live footage of this “failure on the part of the Matrix providers,” pontifcating on the pathetic, fragile metahumans who need these electronic crutches for basic functions. To create even more drama, low-level Alliance members will be planted among the crowd to tout the problems with addiction to Matrix access.**
