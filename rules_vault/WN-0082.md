## Drift Technologies

It isn’t often you hear about a video game company on our boards. Drift Technologies produces some of the best MMORPGs in the world. They work out of a tiny, ultra-secure research facility in the middle of SoHo—they also do it without the help of, or funding from, any megacorporation. The homegrown atmosphere surrounding Drift has made the corporation a fan favorite. It’s also the prime reason why so many young designers flock to Drift straight out of college.

> Drift is a hotbed of talent. The group is a mix of researchers, developers, and technomancers who are all dedicated to creating the best immersive gaming experiences possible. What they’re creating borders on a BTL-level experience. It might not be literally addictive, but try to quit after one game.

> Netcat
