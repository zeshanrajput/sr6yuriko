## **Check the Walls and Floors**

**Even though you should try to keep your shadow life out of your home, you still want to make sure sounds in your apartment stay in your apartment. Listen carefully to what you hear when you see a place, and bang on the walls to see how sound travels. If you fnd a great unit with thin walls, bulk them up with some dense material and fabric panels to give yourself some extra privacy.**
