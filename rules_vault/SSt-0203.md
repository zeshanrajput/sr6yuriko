## **Sometimes, you find a shadowrun. Other times, the shadowrun finds you.**

**Chicago. You could say the city and I have some history, some good and some bad. Mostly bad, if I’m honest, which I try to be with varying degrees of success. I was there when the bugs happened. I was there when they happened again. I’ve been through everything Chi-Town’s thrown at me, from Lockdown to FAB III and everything in between.**

**I’ve lost a lot in Chicago, more than I ever want to talk about, but I still have a few friends there. I don’t keep in touch with them as much as I should, I suppose, but I still drop by the Can-Can’t sometimes, and I’ve been known to darken the doors of Konnichi-Waagh on Canal Street. I hate the bad joke of the name, but I love their sake shooters. But enough of that, let me tell you about someone**
