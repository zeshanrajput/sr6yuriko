## Freelance Journalist

## (Professional Rating 3)

Freelance journalists in the Sixth World come in all forms, from every background imaginable. Some may consider themselves heirs to the noble news reporters of old, while others revel in muckraking and mud-slinging. However they get the job done, their whole reason for existence for living boils down to one thing: finding that one big scoop that will make them famous (or infamous).
