## **Attack (Lethal or Non-Lethal)**

**Ordering a critter to attack is fairly straightforward. In addition to telling the critter to attack, the trigger must also point out the target of the attack. In most cases, this is accomplished by pointing at the target, though augmented critters can be directed using AR objects. Lethal or non-lethal attacks each require a separate command—by default, a critter will usually use its most powerful lethal attacks.**
