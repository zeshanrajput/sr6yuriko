## Cybernetic Shells

A shell is a complete cybernetic body that has been purpose-built to accept a CCU and provide full functionality of a metahuman body. A base model without modifications comes equipped with the equivalent of a full cyberlimb replacement, plus voice modulator, cybereyes 2 with image link, low-light vision, thermographic vision, and cyberears 1 with sound link. The Physical attributes of a shell can be modified and upgraded similar to cyberlimbs. In all cases, the augmented maximum for synthetic shells is 10, while obvious shells can go to 12.
