## **Street Docs vs. DocWagon (and the Like)**

**When you need medical services, where do you go? To your local street doc or to the safety and reliability of DocWagon or CrashCart or one of their competitors? If you’ve followed my pattern so far, you should be expecting me to recommend a solid DocWagon contract attached to a good SIN. There’s a lot to recommend about that approach— the medical corps care enough about quality of care to ensure at least basic competency in their personnel, and the global reach of the large corps means that pretty much wherever a job takes you, you should be able to fnd treatment.
