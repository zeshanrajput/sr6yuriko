## Vida Nova

## (Emerged xenosapient, Connection Rating 2)

This is an update to that profile from _Assassin’s Night_ , with Vida’s true make-up and capacity now closer to understanding. Their interest in the technologies involved with Animus Construction appear to be their foremost agenda, to advance those to heights above and beyond what is currently possible. Their drive for comprehending not only the spark of virtual life forms but the essence of metasapience, however, can quickly escalate to something entirely discomforting. Vida seems prepared to pursue all forms of individual transference and expansion, with prolonged conversations indicating they believe events like reincarnation and resurrection are fully within the grasp of the science.

They are not a member of Garmonbozia, Ex Machina, or the Null Sect, but their capabilities put them on par with individuals from those groups at any time. They are friendly to the cause of Garmonbozia, and allies to them who prove true gain a bonus Edge in social tests with Vida.
