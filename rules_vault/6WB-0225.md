## Wet

Whether it’s a pouring rain, a sudden wave, or the efforts of a rampaging water spirit, you are soaked from head to toe. You take a –6 dice pool penalty to Damage Resistance tests against cold and electricity damage.

Cancels the Burning status, is canceled by the Burning status, and may affect the Corrosive status.
