## Scaling

Salting is a pretty big con to start with, and scaling it up generally means the nature of the find, the size of land, or difficulty of extraction involved. Which means deeper pockets. In truth, scale is something not to mess with when it comes to salt. The big buyers are going to want independent verification, which they can arrange without rattling the cages that bring other prospective buyers sniffing around. As always, know your mark and the balance between opportunity and hesitation. The sheer power of real estate is dangerous stuff to play with, and if you aim at giants, there’s a good chance they’ll squash you.
