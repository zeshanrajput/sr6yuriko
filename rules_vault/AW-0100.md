## Spiritual Guides and Characters

## Bergy Seltzer

Retired bubblehead from her majesty’s service, now a civilian bathysphere pilot, Bergy is an undine who does delivery jobs, such as moving machine equipment. When things are slow, he falls back to taxi services. It’s a few steps away from when he was firing torpedoes at water bug hives ages ago. Not that he wants to go back to those days, but serving as a tour guide to the cloud cities surrounding Tumult is not very exciting.
