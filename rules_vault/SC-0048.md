## Strategies

Prepare for as much as you can reasonably anticipate, but take care not to over-plan. Use your contacts, astral scouting, the assistance of your fellow runners, and any applicable avenues of divination to gather as much information as you can about the situations you expect to face. With that knowledge, communicate with your fellow runners to devise a plan. Take into account their strengths and weaknesses, and use your spells, preparations, spirits, and skills to bolster them.
