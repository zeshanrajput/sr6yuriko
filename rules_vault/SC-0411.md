## Types of Racing Riggers Stoppo

This is the classic version of the racer rigger in the shadows. When the team runs out of the build-

**==> picture [287 x 765] intentionally omitted \<==**

RACINGing where they staged a job, the stoppo is there, sitting in their vehicle, ready to gun the engine and make a getaway.

Stoppos seem to have been born with a steering wheel in their hands. They are highly instinctive drivers—when they talk about traffic flow, they mean something different than the rest of us do. We just mean how it moves, they mean how it lives and breathes. All the vehicles, working together, form a kind of organism to them, and they are in the bloodstream, feeling the flow and trying to beat it.

Stoppos hate steering remotely and absolutely love being jumped into their vehicles. To them, even just sitting behind the wheel instead of being jumped in is too remote, like playing a video game about driving instead of actually driving. They crave the feel of the road, the smell of burning rubber and exhaust, and the sight distant objects coming closer quickly as they gain speed.

Stoppos do not have to find the shadows—the shadows find them. If you have the ability to get someone out of a sticky situation before law enforcement catches up to them, someone will notice. And the shadows will make you an offer you can’t refuse.
