## Hyper (Amp, Blitz)

- **Vector:** Inhalation, Injection

- **Speed:** 1 combat round

- **Duration:** 10 x (6 – Body) minutes, minimum 1 minute

- **Power:** 9

- **Effect:** –1 Reaction, +2 Intuition

Hyper acts on the sensory cortex, amplifying sight, sound, taste, smell, and touch. Users of hyper cannot spend Edge on any skill tests except for Perception due to being bombarded with intense sensations. If they are hurt while hypersensitive, they take additional, unresisted Stun damage equal to half the original amount (after the resistance test).

Once the drug’s initial duration ends, the user gains the Dazed status for the next five minutes.

- **Effect:** –1 Reaction, –1D6 Initiative Dice

Monoamine Oxidase is an enzyme that rapidly oxidizes adrenaline, removing its effects. This counters the effects of an adrenal pump. When injected, the drug lowers the target’s Reaction by 1 and removes one Initiative Die. If the target has an adrenaline pump, all bonuses are reduced by 1, effectively negating a rating 1 implant and lowering ratings 2 or 3 one step.
