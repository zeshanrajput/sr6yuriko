## Open Sesame

There are plays and cons aplenty designed to extract something of value, but the quickest, the most accessible, and probably the most common and useful to any runner are the ones that grant access. In shadow work it’s all about where you can go, who and what you can get to. That’s what gives value to who you know, often touted as the most important aspect of power someone can have. Access is freedom, lethal and profitable possibility. And it’s so damn easy, so damn common, that almost every person in the Sixth World has done it more than once. And the crazy thing is, despite being so prevalent, it just keeps working.
