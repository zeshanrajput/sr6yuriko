## Game Information

Each application of lipguard seals in lipstick and protects the covered location from contact vector drugs and toxins for a number of hours equal to its rating x 2. During this time, the protected area is immune to all toxins that it contacts. Each use of lipguard covers three to four square centimeters of skin, enough to cover the lips, the center of a palm or back of the hand, or most of a cheek of a medium-sized metahuman and smaller. Trolls and larger metavariants will require two applications to cover their lips, palm, or cheek.

When a contact vector drug or toxin is applied on top of the lipguard, it doesn’t affect the wearer unless they accidentally touch the chemical with an unprotected part of their body. When worn on the lips, eating or drinking will result in dosing yourself. The GM may call for an occasional Memory (2) test (perhaps one per hour) to determine whether or not you forget about the toxin for a moment and accidentally dose yourself.

Lipguard only remains effective for twice its rating in hours. If the lipguard expires before the drug is deployed or washed away, the user will dose themselves.
