## Natural Weapon

A critter that normally has no natural weapon can be provided with horns, retractable claws, sharp teeth, talons, or some other equivalent so that it can make deadly attacks.

**Game Information:** This augmentation is only available for critters that lack the natural weapon critter power. Attacks using the natural weapon use the Close Combat skill, with a Physical Damage Value of Strength/2. The AR is the critter’s normal unarmed AR (Reaction + Strength). The natural weapon augmentation can be enhanced by other augmentations, such as the bone density augmentation.
