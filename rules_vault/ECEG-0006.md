## Louisiana Justice

**Population (SINless not reported):** 1,567,500 **Human:** 53% **Elf:** 5% **Dwarf:** 5% **Ork:** 27% **Troll:** 5% **Other (Goblins and Vampires):** 3% **Other:** 2%

There are a few things that separate this region from the rest of the country, in particular its law enforcement and legal system. Much of how the law works in New Orleans and Louisiana at large is based on old French law instead of the English example most everywhere else follows. What that means for those of you not familiar with legal history is that things like family are much more codifi ed into the legal system. Much of this doesn’t make a difference in how you do work in the shadows, but knowing that a family name may have actual legal standing as well as infl uence can change how you approach a job. It certainly can change how the police respond, depending either on who’s making a report or who ends up wearing handcuffs in the back of a patrol car.
