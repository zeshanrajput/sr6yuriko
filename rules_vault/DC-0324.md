## Shredders

These may earn some style points out in the barrens, but they’re illegal everywhere else. Why? Mainly because shredders are named after the ring of sharp metal blades or spikes built into their outer edge. These tires add the vehicle’s Body to the Attack Rating when ramming non-vehicular targets, one-half Body if it’s an attack made against another vehicle.
