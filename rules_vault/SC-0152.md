## The Artifact Forgery

This adventure hook works best with the gadgeteer background, but is easily adapted to any team with an alchemist.

One of the shaman’s contacts, preferably a fixer or talismonger, reaches out with a job offer. A recently discovered artifact believed to have magical potential has been stolen by a team of runners. The shaman’s contact has a lead on where the stolen artifact might be located. They want the shaman’s team to retrieve the stolen artifact from the thieves, but that is only part one of the job. A significant reward has been offered for the recovery of the artifact, and the shaman’s contact hopes to double-dip by receiving the reward for turning in a forgery and also selling the artifact back to the people who hired a team to steal it. They provide the shaman

with a formula for a unique alchemical preparation—one that will create a false aura that matches the original—and task the shaman with preparing the forgery. If the gadgeteer wishes to learn such a trick, they may do so for 5 Karma. If the shaman is uninterested in learning the formula, they will be asked to help out and watch for trouble while their contact prepares it instead. The last part of the job requires making a deal with the people who stole the artifact. This will likely be a tense social encounter, but the opposition would much rather make a deal and get the stolen artifact back than settle a score. The contact leaves all of the details up to the shaman and their team, and they take a cut of the nuyen. If this run is successful, the Loyalty and Connection ratings of the contact who hired them increase by 1 each.
