## Increased Overflow Lethality

**Use this rule** to add an inherent element of mortal danger to overflow damage (p. 121, _SR6_ ).

Upon suffering any amount of overflow damage that doesn’t immediately kill the character, a death’s door countdown begins, and the character will soon die unless stabilized. Healing at least 1

box of overflow damage by any means stabilizes the character. If the character does not become stabilized before a number of rounds equal to Body, they either die or must avert that death via the Not Dead Yet use of Edge (p. 48, _SR6_ ). Averting death in this way also counts as stabilization. If, after being stabilized, the character suffers more overflow damage without dying, a new death’s door countdown begins.
