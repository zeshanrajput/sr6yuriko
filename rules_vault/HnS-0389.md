## Corruptor

## • **Bonus:** 10 Karma

Virtual lifeforms with the Corruptor negative quality suffered fundamental defects to their proVIRTUALgramming during their evolution. The critter has an unfortunate tendency to trigger malfunctions in other programs sharing the same device. Treat this as if the life form has the Gremlins quality (p. 76, _SR6_ ). The gamemaster should make use of this quality for dramatic effect as best suits the story.
