## Intrigue and Metaplanar Interaction

## Uploaded by: Magister

Air elementals are impatient—they like to keep moving. This includes conversation. Pauses are like a vacuum, and they abhor them. In order to fill the silence, especially with metahumans, they multitask, enjoying a few conversations sequentially, allowing the metahumans to take their normal thought pauses before answering. This pause is the center of the animosity air elementals have with earth elementals. The staleness of conversation is easily seen in Foehn, as the city segregates earth elementals from air. Fire elementals are more up to speed with them, and they converse in what could be called music. Their form of native speech is much too fast for human comprehension—the vibration and tone to us sound like an orchestra tuning.

Back to G-Nome’s comment on the air elementals’ omnipresence. Air elementals, especially the greater ones, hear, see, and taste things on the wind. It’s how they know where things are before they arrive.

- I guess you’d have to be downwind from them if you’re hunting one.

- Slamm-0!

- You don’t know the half of it.

- Marko

These senses give the air elementals an advantage against other spirits (insect, shadow, etc.), both in knowing where the threat is and in coordinating with other air elementals. A murderous murmur if you will, as the flight of an air elemental can be coordinated at a significant distance. Armies of air elementals would be considerably formidable even against the hive mind. This is also a good answer for why the insects have not taken the elemental planes even as metahumans keep opening rifts.

That reminds me of another thing to mention: Air elementals don’t normally sense the flow of energies like the geomancing earth elementals, but they can feel the natural effluvium of their own metaplane. Some are becoming agitated by the incompatible effluvia outside their metaplane. They naturally assume metahumans are responsible, and rightfully so. Fragments of mystical ordnance showed up in Foehn a few days ago—Queen Candelia has kept the discovery quiet, but you know me. I had to get myself a souvenir. I heard that she sent a borea tracker out to trace the signature on the ordnance back to its source.
