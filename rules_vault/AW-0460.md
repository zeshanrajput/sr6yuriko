## Toxic Mutant Critters

Most toxic mutant critters have one or more additional powers than their equivalent on Earth. To make up a toxic mutant critter, choose the base critter type (such as devil rat). Then add 1D6 additional options or powers from the following list. You may choose the options that fit your idea best or roll randomly.

If you roll/choose the same option more than once, the attribute increases are cumulative. A toxic mutant critter’s attributes (including Magic) shouldn’t be increased by more than +4 total—unless you have something special in mind.

If the base critter type you are using does not have a Magic attribute, start with a Magic attribute of 2 (plus any increases from options and powers).
