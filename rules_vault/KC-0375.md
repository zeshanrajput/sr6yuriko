## Cinder (elf, female, 25, auburn hair, hazel eyes, thin build)

Cinder’s arcane talent pales in comparison to her social legerdemain. She’s an initiated mage with plenty of tricks up her sleeve. She’s amiable and not physically large, and she uses those things to make others underestimate her. Behind that smile is a mind constantly keeping track of the angles she is playing and looking for new avenues to manipulate.

If a fight breaks out, she calls the team to retreat and uses whatever means possible to get away safely, usually playing scared and seeking help from unsuspecting victims about to get pulled into her tangled web.
