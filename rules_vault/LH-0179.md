## **\<06-20-83/1048:33>**

Approximately thirty minutes ago, a massive manastorm surge has covered almost the entire continent and cut Australia off from the Matrix and most other forms of communication. Just before this communications blackout, emergency messages were broadcast throughout the country, warning everyone to take immediate shelter or evacuate. Ships, aircraft, and suborbitals ready to depart were given emergency orders to immediately do so. We are unaware of the disposition of those who attempted to evacuate or those still on the continent at this time.

All operations bound for Australia are hereby ordered to abort and return to their point of origin to await new instructions. Exceptions can be made if rendering emergency aid. Regardless of the situation, do not get within forty kilometers of the Australian coast.
