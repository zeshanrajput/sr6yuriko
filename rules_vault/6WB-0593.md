## Glitches

A glitch on a ritual in any step could result in extra Drain (e.g., +2 Drain Value), an increase of 1 to the threshold of the ritual, or the leader being forced to seal the ritual on their own. On a critical glitch, anything goes (prepare to put down a rogue minion, or find that you missed your target, or find that what you intended to be a healing circle really is an astral gateway and everybody just got dumped on some metaplane, or something equally unpleasant that your gamemaster thinks up).
