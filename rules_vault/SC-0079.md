## Strategies

Hitmen are meticulous, at least the successful ones. Scouting out the zone, researching the target, blending in and avoiding notice are their hallmarks. You are a spy with a lethal bent, and any crew is going to profit from that. With regeneration, you make a good bullet sponge rather than your teammates, and they’ll appreciate that. Mist form makes positioning and retreat a snap, offering unparalleled mobility so long as there’s no sunlight or molecular filters in your path. This doesn’t invalidate your tactical acumen, but rather it opens up many more variables in your favor. Always have multiple ways to execute the objective and just as many escape vectors, if not more. Redundancy and deniability are your friends. Just remember that time isn’t on your side outdoors, and even a cloudy day won’t stop the sun from scorching you.

Remember that you aren’t on your own and pick skills that make you a specialist in your approach. Are you a social predator or a stealthy one? Do you get up close, stay at range, or lay traps? Work in concert with your crew, and you’ll be more effective than you ever were solo.
