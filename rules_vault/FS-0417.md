Most twenty-first-century fencing is based on techniques that originated in France and Italy, although the Spanish style of _la verdadera destreza_ has gained quite a bit of traction in recent years. European duelists often paired their rapiers with an off-hand weapon like a dagger, a tradition that’s survived into the modern day.
