## The Scribe Speaks

One of the biggest names to try to explain it all was Ehran the Scribe. And it should be noted this wasn’t the first time he’s made speeches like this, and there are some striking similarities between those topics. Previously, he’d spoken to a group known as the Young Elven Technologists and was apparently also trying to support fundraising for plans to direct the growth of the group and theirambitions. The long and short of his speeches was to explain how magic comes and goes from the world and that humans can be clever about their creations, but don’t live long enough to really understand how to take care of the planet or make real progress.

I realize some people don’t believe his claims of age, but I for one have seen enough to believe it. If he’s showing signs of aging, then perhaps the lapse in magic has affected him in some way that the recession of the Fifth World did not. If so, the same is very likely to be true of any others like him and even, possibly, dragons.
