## El Guapo (Jay Houston)

## (Fixer, Connection Rating 4)

El Guapo is a middle-aged white human who stands about 1.8 meters tall with a solid build, shaved head, and a neatly trimmed goatee. During his time in the UCAS Special Forces, he specialized in interrogation and South American activities, earning the nickname “El Guapo” from his unit. While operating in surveillance with Amazonian forces during the Az-Am war, he became disillusioned with the military. After he left, he got his bachelor’s degree in data intelligence from the University of Michigan before pursuing his master’s in Matrix protocols at the University of Seattle. While in Seattle, he interned with Microdeck, where he met Mr. James. They became good acquaintances and built a trusting relationship, and they stayed in touch after they departed the company. When Ervine James moved to Manhattan, he knew he could benefit from Jay’s talents, so he offered him a position as head of his security team. His friendship with Mr. James is the reason Jay is handling the disappearance of Silver Nail personally.

In the cruel world of corporate politics, however, nothing is perfect, and Jay has a family secret, one he doesn’t know. He wasn’t planning on shadowrunning, but someone of his size tends to get pigeon-holed into certain roles. He was lucky and fell in with a good team who, over a few years, had more than their fair share of well-paying jobs. With money in his pocket, Fluffy Duck bought his food truck and started doing what he enjoys.
