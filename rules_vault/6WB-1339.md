## ID and Credit

These are the basic mechanisms used to show who you are and pay what you need. Many civilized urban areas check for SINs regularly, so a fake SIN is valuable for making sure you can go where you need to go without raising alarms. And many runners like to use credsticks instead of bank

transfers to make yourself less traceable. Here are some details on each of these.
