## **Disian Adept Powers**

**For native Disians, each power point worth of adept abilities requires binding up 10 units of internal mana. It takes 8 hours of light meditation, split up as needed, to bind 5 units into one half of a power point. This quantity of mana stays bound up in the creation of power points and may not be used for any other purpose. This means that the total number of units converted to power points is taken away from the maximum internal storage capacity. This lowered capacity stays in place until the power points are dropped and the internal mana capacity is once again free. As an example, a Disian with a Willpower of 5 has an internal storage of 250 units, converting 50 units into power points drops the maximum usable quantity of internal storage to 200 units and creates 5 power points.**

**Converting the power points into adept abilities requires focus and concentration. Each 0.1 power point worth of adept ability requires one hour of meditation to manifest. While it is possible to “forget” an adept ability and learn something different,**
