## **Claws in the Underworld**

**One cannot talk about MCT and not mention their ties with the Yakuza. Since Tiger Mitsuhama formed the company, the two have been linked. And while many Yakuza clans disapproved of any involvement with a corporation, the Yakuza have played a prominent role in MCT’s recent successes.**
