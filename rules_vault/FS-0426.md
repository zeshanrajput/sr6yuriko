## Kyujutsu

Kyujutsu is a Japanese form of archery using an unusually long bow called a _yumi_ , the largest of which were sometimes taller than their wielders. Originally practiced by the samurai, kyujutsu fell out of mainstream use with the introduction of firearms, but it has regained popularity in the Sixth World for both cultural and practical reasons.
