## Dual Nature

The cybermantic procedure never fully rejoins the body and spirit, leaving a cyberzombie trapped between both planes. As a result, cyberzombies become dual natured, but their nature prevents them from having a clear perception and understanding of astral space around them. While they do have an astral presence, they cannot spend Edge on Perception tests involving purely astral phenomena and receive a –3 dice pool penalty for attempting to astrally perceive. The cyberzombie may see a fleeting image, and if they are successful, may be able to interact or defend themselves from such a presence. Older generations of cyberzombies were completely unable to see the astral plane despite being dual-natured, but newer pharmaceuticals included in the auto-injector cocktail have allowed them to see smaller parts of the astral plane.

The fact that the body is now effectively possessed by its original spirit means that a cyberzombie becomes, in some senses, a magical entity. In addition to their Magic attribute of 1, they are subject to being locked out of places equipped with mana barriers such as wards. Of course, when the cyberzombie can just tear the building down, a ward is the least of its worries. This Magic rating cannot be increased with Karma or any other means. If the character already possessed a Magic attribute, it is permanently reduced to 1.
