## **If an EI doesn’t have a Physical or Stun Condition Monitor, where does fade damage go?**

Fade damage hits their Matrix Condition Monitor.

## **Can I use Reconfigure Matrix Attribute Minor Action to always set my Firewall as my highest Matrix Attribute after doing any attack?**

Yes, but the Reconfigure Matrix Attribute action can only be done on your initiative turn and is not an anytime action.
