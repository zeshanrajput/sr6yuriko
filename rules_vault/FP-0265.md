## **The Enemy of My Enemy Is My ...**

**It was supposed to be a simple job: Track down and eliminate a Dissonant technomancer who has been targeting the wrong people in the shadows. It turns out, though, that you aren’t the only ones hunting her. Luckily(?) for you, the opposing group is Ex Pacis, the current prey of a team of fve Leif-Killers (two shamans, two adepts, and one Coderunner technomancer). The Leif-Killers propose a tentative alliance to help deal with this problem. Together, the Dissonant will be more manageable, but that still means getting on Ex Pacis’ wrong side and working with known terrorists—and toxic ones to boot!**
