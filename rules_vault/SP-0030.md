## **Chimera: Monsters Unleashed**

**If the Faustians are the generals in the Disians’ shadow army, the chimera are the elite shock troops.**

**The term “chimera” in this context is an umbrella term used to describe any sentient being or animal that has been taken and modifed by the Disians to serve their needs. They also serve a practical role. If an adversary cannot be overcome by magical means or force, chimera are then deployed.**

**When frst encountered, these walking nightmares were thought of as nothing more than brute-force patchwork monsters. But after subsequent encounters, different variants were revealed. While too many to list here, we have encountered everything from hulking monsters to small rodent-sized creatures, to infltrators that look almost exactly like unmodifed metahuman beings, and everything in between. If a specifc niche needs to be flled, there is (or soon will be) a chimera to fll it. Some chimeras are stereotypical unintelligent brutes, others seem tailor-made for specifc purposes, while a rare few have been allowed to retain selective parts of their personalities and/or intelligence and skills to perform specifc functions or missions.**
