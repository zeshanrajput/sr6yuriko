## The Hook

In this mission, the players will be introduced to some of the participants for one of the subplots of this campaign. During this mission, a new Mr. Johnson going by the name El Guapo reaches out to the runners. He needs help investigating a missing child. Due to the high-profile nature of the family, whatever they discover will only be reported back to him.
