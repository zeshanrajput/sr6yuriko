## ara Pulse (dwarf, female, 32, blonde hair, blue eyes, average build)

Sometimes, Tara feels like an oddball among all the cyber and tech, but then she reaches out and makes that connection to the gear and feels right at home. She joined the team because they needed arcane support, and she fell hard for CPUs mix of brains and charm. She tends to watch over him a bit better than the rest of the team, but she knows that a successful run is one where everyone comes home. She actually believes that not only for herself and her team but other runners she may encounter. She understands that for most shadowrunners, this is just business, nothing personal. She doesn’t think anyone should die just for making money. She also doesn’t think people should kill for it, either, and she promotes that with her teammates as often as possible.

During recon, she will send over several spirits of kin to keep tabs on the runners. She’ll call them in rather mundane looking forms in order to have them blend in with the street rabble. Her other method is to use air spirits that she tells to keep some distance away and blend in with the background, though on more than one occasion her air spirits have been spotted when a cloud just keeps following a target.

When the fighting starts, Tara uses non-lethal methods on everything. She will also order spirits to take fallen team members out of the fight and get them to safety. Her spirits of kin are summoned with Innate Spell (Heal) in order to support fallen comrades or her if she falls. She has an astral reputation with her spirits, and they will help her even if she falls unconscious. She uses her 2 sustaining foci to sustain Increase Attribute spells for team members while usually sustaining Increased Reflexes on herself.
