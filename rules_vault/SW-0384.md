## Somatic (+2 Drain)

## Special: 5 drams of reagents

Magic has always been connected to finger wiggling or grand gestures to make the mojo flow.

This is a particular movement action that needs to be performed in order to make the spell work. The coding is built into the preparation and needs at least 1 minute of study and practice (from the alchemist, an instructional trideo, or pamphlet) in order to properly perform the gesture. The gesture requires a Minor Action to perform once learned. This trigger can be used with Health spells.
