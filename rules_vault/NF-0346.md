## Clothing

## Trenchcoat

The unarmored version is a timeless classic that’s making a comeback. Available in any color, they’re found most often in a dark grey or black, for the goths, samurai, and vampire-wannabes, or tan for the reporters and gumshoes. They don’t provide any armor, but they do provide an additional –2 Concealability modifier to items hidden underneath. Plus, they’re stylish!
