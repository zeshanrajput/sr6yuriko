## Masking

You learn to change the appearance of your aura (and astral form). You can make it look mundane or make your Magic rank look higher or lower by up to your Initiate Grade. When someone tries to read your masked aura, the Assensing test becomes opposed by your Magic + Initiate Grade—if they get any net hits, they see both your mask and your real aura. If you can use astral perception, you can even make your aura look like a different type (like a spirit or a focus—great for astral costume parties). You can also use this metamagic to mask the auras of a number of bonded foci equal your Initiate Grade.
