## Krime Kaltrops

A business that operates as fast and loose with accepted manufacturing practice as Krime is bound to have excessive waste. Krime executives realized that they had good slag metal just lying around the shop floors and decided to find a better use for it. Thus, the Krime Kaltrop was born. While a regular caltrop is made of dainty, hard-tospot metal slivers, the Krime Kaltrop takes all subtlety out of the equation.
