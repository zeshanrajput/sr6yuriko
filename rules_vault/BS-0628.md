## Empathic Retention

Either through a natural ability or by training and practice, you have maintained your awareness of the subtle non-verbal cues metahumans use when communicating with each other, offsetting the atrophy of social awareness usually caused by Essence loss.

- **Cost:** 4 Karma per level (max level 2)

- **Game Effect:** Each level of this quality negates 1 point of negative Social Rating caused by Essence loss. This quality cannot generate a positive Social Rating modifier and only counteracts the negative modifier caused by Essence loss, having no effect on other negative factors.
