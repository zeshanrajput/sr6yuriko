## Ghouls

Beginning on July 26, some Infected with Strain III go into viral remission, with feral populations suffering from full psychological distress, cardiac arrest, or neurogenerative failure. Some sapient ghouls begin to recover their biological functions, even to the point of regaining normal sensory functions. This also means they require normal methods of sustenance according to their base metatype expression. On August 3, the virus begins to reemerge, with full viral expression by August 5.
