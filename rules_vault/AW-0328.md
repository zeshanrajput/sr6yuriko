## MCT

MCT built the largest modern aircraft hangar on the Spire. In addition to being capable of storing at least a dozen vector-thrust aircraft, it includes manufacturing and repair facilities capable of constructing new vehicles if necessary. Unlike most other visitors, MCT does not make use of locally constructed airships. Instead, they use rigid dirigibles not unlike zeppelins, which make use of helium gas for lift (helium is readily available within the Place of Clouds). MCT has laid claim to six islands and is in the process of constructing outposts with mining and processing facilities. MCT declares its territory within the Place of Clouds to be Zero Zones and deters potential threats and trespassers with lethal force. Their transport and mining operations are always defended by heavily armed combat drones and powerful magicians and their contingents of spirits. MCT could give the Disians a run for their money, but the two factions have avoided each other thus far.

In addition to the reagents and true elements that are found in abundance in the Place of Clouds, MCT harvests wood and mines minerals that are used to produce as much of its material locally as possible.
