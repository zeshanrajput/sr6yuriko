## Government Allies

Governments are no longer the dominant force in the world, but they still wield considerable power and can still influence world events. They also provide corporations with the consumers they need. Most also have access to armed forces or intelligence services that can be co-opted with the right influence. Most importantly for Dis, governments can provide large tracts of land where experiments and projects can be carried out without interference.
