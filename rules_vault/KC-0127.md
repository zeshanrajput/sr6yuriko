## Gaia Investments

This company sprang up virtually overnight. Bintana Obukanwe is the founder, president, CEO, CFO, and every other title at the top. Mainly because she needs to keep the company’s assets as well hidden as possible. She’s hired half a dozen staff, including an office manager, a clerk, and four agents. The agents seek new business and make the company look legit while concealing the ultimate value of the firm. They have no multinational resources and no plans to do so, because then they’d gain extra scrutiny from the Corporate Court.
