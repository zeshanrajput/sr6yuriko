## Long Live The Defenders

We know the truth. We know what you are. We know what you want. We will not go quietly into the abyss. We will fight like demons to our last breaths. We will protect what is ours. We are Defenders!

**— Anonymous** , broadcast across multiple Matrix sites and VPNs, 11-23-82/01:23

Before we talk about the anti-Disian side of this war, I need to touch on something first.

While the Week of Death _almost_ succeeded, it failed on several fronts, most notably by not finishing the job. They gave their enemies a chance to regroup. And this led to the creation of what is being called the “Defender Movement” within the shadows.
