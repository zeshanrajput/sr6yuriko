## Chase Edge Actions

These Edge Actions are only used during a chase and are combined with the required Piloting test Major Action.

All of these Edge Actions must be combined with a Major Action for a Piloting or Sprinting test. Unless stated otherwise, the Piloting or Athletics test must succeed in order for the Edge Action to take effect. You may wait to see the result of your roll before committing to spending the Edge.
