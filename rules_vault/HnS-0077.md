## Enhance

## (Legal) Electronics + Logic (Minor)

## User/Admin

Sometimes the trid footage is unclear or maybe the audio is coming through garbled, but a quick shot of Enhance, and suddenly everything becomes clear! This Matrix action can be taken on the same turn or the turn immediately after performing a Matrix Perception test. Hits on the Enhance Matrix action are added to the previous Matrix Perception action.
