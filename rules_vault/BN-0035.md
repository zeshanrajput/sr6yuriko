## Sh ifter (Furry)

Canine, leonine, lupine, panthrine, tigrene, ursine, reindeer, vulpine and more. This subcategory is for mammalian shifters. These are typically four-legged and furry, but primates are also an option. Polar bear and reindeer shifters can be found in the Arctic circle. Lupine, great cat, bovine and ursine shifters are found on every continent. Brown bears, black bears, and lupine are the most common North American shifters with some panthrine, coyote, or cougar forms also occurring in the natural animal populations. In Central and South America, shifters of all species are found living in Aztlan and Amazonia sprawls, Ocelot, jaguar, primate, and pantherine are the most common mammalian shifters found in natural animal species. Yakut is home to tens of thousands of shifters of any variety listed. Lupines are found in the wilds of Russia, while vulpines are most common in Japan and China. Tigrine can be found in India, China, Russia, and Yakut, and they have the largest territories of any shifter furry. Canine shifters are found mostly in cities and are likely the most populous of any shifter population due to the large pre-Awakening pet population.

- **Minimum Base Cost:** 15 Karma

- **Requirements:** Body 3 or more, Strength 3 or more

- **Gained Powers:** Dual Natured, Environmental Adaptation (Hot and Cold), Natural Weapon, Supernatural Recovery, Shift.

- **Gained Weakness: Allergy (Silver, Extreme) or Allergy (Gold, Extreme)**

- **Attack:** Claw, bite or horns as appropriate.
