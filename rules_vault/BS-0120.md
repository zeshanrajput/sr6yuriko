## Foot Anchor

Gas-driven tungsten spikes are implanted in the user’s feet and are designed to punch into the floor the user is standing upon. Each foot may only have one anchor implanted. It takes a Minor Action to fire or retract a single foot anchor. The anchor cannot function on materials with 13 or more Structure Rating (p. 113, _SR6_ ). While the user has at least one foot anchor deployed, they gain the Immobilized status (p. 52, _SR6_ ) and they cannot gain the Prone status (p. 53, _SR6_ ). When making ranged attacks, they do not suffer the –3 dice pool penalty and instead reduce all AR penalties for firing mode attacks by half (p. 108, _SR6_ ), rounded down. Note that a foot anchor is not restricted to use on a horizontal surface; if the user has an anchor in each foot they can potentially, and very carefully, walk up a wall.
