## Flashlight

## Thermite burning bar

Thermite is an incendiary material that burns at extremely high temperatures. It is applied with the help of a burning bar—a rod of thermite and oxygen mounted on a handle and in a frame—that can be used to melt holes in iron, steel, and even plasteel. The thermite burning bar inflicts Fire Damage with a DV of 10P. It has to be set carefully, so a thermite bar can’t be used as a weapon, unless your target is incapacitated somehow, you cruel fragger.

**Wireless bonus:** The burning bar can be activated and deactivated wirelessly.

By 2080, flashlights are long lasting and super bright. Flashlights are also available in low-light and infrared versions, allowing even clearer visibility for low-light and thermographic vision, respectively. A flashlight can also be mounted to a weapon’s top or under-barrel mount ( **Accessories** , p. 259).
