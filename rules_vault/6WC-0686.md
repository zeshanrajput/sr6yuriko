## More Lethal Anti-Vehicular Rockets and Missiles

**Use this rule** to allow anti-vehicular rockets to more reliably knock out targets tougher than a scooter.

When firing an AV rocket or missile (p. 264, _SR6_ ), designate exactly one primary target, which must be a barrier, motorcycle-or-larger sized vehicle or drone, or a large critter (10+ Body). If that designated target is in Ground Zero range after scatter is resolved, then that target is considered directly hit and suffers the full force of the shaped charge, resulting in double GZ DV. Any other targets in the blast suffer effects as normal.
