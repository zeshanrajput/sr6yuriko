## _Deerslayer:_ Noriega. 

_Scope:_ Stop it. He didn’t pivot to governing, he just used drugs as an extra revenue source. And a pretty damn good one. 

_Deerslayer:_ We’re getting off track. It doesn’t matter what Vicar wants to do. Whether they want to govern, build power, expand their empire, whatever. I’m just saying they’re going to be a force to be reckoned with, and almost no one’s going to be in a position to stop them. Jump on the train now, before it gets up to full speed. 

_Scope:_ I’m not going to be security for drug caravans. 

_Deerslayer:_ You always lacked this much imagination? Everything’s changing. Don’t think about how you can fit in with what Vicar _has_ done. Think about what he _will_ do. And how you can build a place in that. 

_Scope:_ Why are you telling me this? 

_Deerslayer:_ Because when the time comes, I want you shooting next to me instead of at me.
