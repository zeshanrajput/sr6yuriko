## gm’s secret

The original fabric was found by others in SpIn but its discovery has been withheld. The original material has a pattern already obtained from Gabrielle, which includes the beauty-mark the player characters should have discovered. It was the discovery of the mark by the material and its cataloguing of such that has set into motion several events, including Becky’s abduction and her own memory alterations.
