## Eager

An former drug dealer and shadowrunner, Eager is new to the fixer side of things. As such, he does the jobs nobody else will do. He is the only fixer willing to touch Sutton. Even so, he desperately hides his connection to Sutton, because he knows it would kill his chances of growing as a fixer. Eager’s connections are presently limited to the Terminal district, where he grew up and did his dealing in the early years. While those connections run deep, he isn’t on good terms with everyone. His disconnect with Terminal’s Slaughterhouse gang has led to several scuffles in the street. The gang feels he abandoned them by becoming a runner and now fixer. Eager, on the other hand, calls the move his evolution. He remains a valuable contact throughout the campaign. His stats are on p. 141.
