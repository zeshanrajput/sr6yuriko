## The Pirates

Most pirates are news-focused, and you can read about them in that section. Pirate entertainers are something else entirely, focused largely around bloggers. Small-time entertainers who record skits, social commentary, or “reality” shows they then upload and allow to be passed around, becoming “Matrix Famous” without corporate backing. Due to an old law signed by President Dukakis, broadcasts of up to 10 km are unregulated, allowing anyone who can assemble the transmission gear to create their own pirate channel. Getting enough content for a 24/7 station is beyond them, but an hour a week? Ten minutes a day? Easy enough. You can see some walking around, broadcasting from a mobile uplink backpack while their CU3 drones whirl around them, getting good angles as they carry on their day. Others rig up a car or van, advertise the channel on the vehicle, and when you’re stuck in traffic you can tune in and enjoy what they have to offer. True pirates also exist, hijacking corporate transmissions to send their ownmessage out, but they tend to get caught quickly and are never seen or heard from again.
