## Smokescreen

## (Activate Smokescreen, Minor Action)

Maneuver to spread the smoke launched from your vehicle’s smoke launcher for best advantage. Gain one level of Cover for each Edge spent (Cover I for 1 Edge, Cover II for 2 Edge, etc.), but only against attacks from behind you.

**Cost:** 1–4 Edge
