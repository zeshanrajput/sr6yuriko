## Neo-Aztec Ascension: History

Currently the only AAA-rated national megacorp (Aztlan) in existence, Aztechnology’s genesis traces back to 2007 when the David, Massaya, and Medellin drug cartels joined their legitimate business interests together to buy out an old Mexican corporation (the name of which has been lost since the ’29 Matrix Crash). Records are unclear as to what the cartel leaders originally intended to do with this new corporation, now known as ORO, but very quickly the new venture took off and began outperforming the cartel leaders’ narcotics trade.

Eventually this newfound capital and influence allowed ORO to expand.
