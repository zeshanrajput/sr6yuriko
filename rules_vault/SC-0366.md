## Qualities

We’ve all heard what a great first impression can do. The First Impression quality helps the face fatale make that impact. While they will likely spend some time with a serious mark, they’ll meet a lot of them over their career, and First Impression gives them a very helpful edge in winning them over. In addition, there are those quick impressions (such as those with a shopkeeper or friend of a target) who the face fatale will likely only meet once in their life, but First Impression will go a long way to getting them any advantage they need.
