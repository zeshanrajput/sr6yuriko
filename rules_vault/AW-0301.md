## Finding Vaults

The lore and knowledge that can be found in the Citadel are within the vaults below the keep. In order to narrow down the thousands of options, Awakened characters in the catacombs can roll an Astral + Intuition (24, 1 hour) extended test to locate a manageable number of options. If the Astral Quest Rating to obtain the knowledge/information sought (or the Force of the free spirit being hunted) is more than 6, use the Astral Quest Rating/Force x 4 as the threshold for the search test. If a player wishes to substitute Astral for another magical skill, they may use the relevant skill for the knowledge being sought. Sorcery can be used when searching for spell or ritual formulas, Conjuring when looking for true names or home metaplanes of spirits, and Enchanting when looking for alchemical formulas, Enchanting designs, or the bonds of a focus to its owner.
