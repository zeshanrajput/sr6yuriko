## **Detectives**

**Most of the time, a detective will know about you before you know about them. Instead of keep-**

**ing public order on a beat, their job is to investigate potential crimes and collect evidence to be sent to the local prosecutor. Usually, if a runner meets a detective, it’s because they got sloppy and left a trail during a run. While they might not be the most immediately dangerous kind of police offcer, detectives make up for it in persistence and being able to take the initiative. I’ve seen more than one runner team get swept up by HTR without a fght because a detective tracked them back to their safehouse.**
