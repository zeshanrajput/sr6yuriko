## Zapper Strip

The zapper strip is equipped with hundreds of tiny wires extending up, which brush against the chassis of any vehicle that passes over. When this happens, the strip sends a powerful electric pulse into the chassis of the vehicle. A vehicle that drives over a zapper strip suffers 8S electrical damage. A zapper strip has enough power for ten discharges before needing to be recharged.
