## Smitten

Someone has stolen your heart and you just can’t help yourself. Just like all those classic noir films where the hard-bitten detective falls for the lethal dame, you know you’re going to make bad decisions and you simply don’t care. You can’t use Willpower to defend yourself in social tests against characters you’re Smitten by.
