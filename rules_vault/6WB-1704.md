## Tunnel Rat

Uncharted sewerage tunnels, forgotten bomb shelters, old escape tunnels and secret passages connecting old tenant basements – there are countless

connections under the streets and fortified walls of Berlin’s many compounds, corporate hubs and walled-off neighborhoods. You grew up in these mazes and know them like the back of your hand.

- Districts: All inner districts (AGC Siemensstadt, Chawi, Kreuzhain, Mitte, Renrakusan, S-K Tempelhof, Z-IC Tegel)

- Cost: 10 Karma

- Game Effect: You gain one bonus die and one bonus point of Edge whenever you try to find a secret underground connection within the stated districts. The bonus Edge is lost if not spent on that test. If successful, you have found or remembered an obscure tunnel that will bring you into the vicinity of your target destination (range of 1D6 x 10 meters).
