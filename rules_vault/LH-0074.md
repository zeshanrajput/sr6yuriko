## Renraku

How fragged up is it when one of the strongest links in your chain is also one of the most problematic, to the point they’re threatening to compromise the entire operation? This pretty much sums up Renraku within Project Nemesis.

Additional problems came when the head of all Renraku’s forces, Tai-sa Minoru Kenji (himself a legendary Red Samurai brought back from retirement) almost refused to coordinate with anyone regarding strategy and operational planning. For Kenji, he was steadfast in that it was his way or no way. It took a lot of negotiating and in-fighting, but eventually the Red Samurai agreed to a compromise. They would pick and prosecute their own targets and only work with other units under the direst of circumstances.

It was less than ideal, but at least it finally got the Red Samurai off their armored hoops and into the fight. And then holy drek…
