## Bartitsu

Known as the “gentleman’s martial art” and made famous by literary heroes like Sherlock Holmes and John Steed, bartitsu teaches the use of a cane or umbrella—back in the day, those were objects people might carry on an everyday basis— as a self-defense tool.
