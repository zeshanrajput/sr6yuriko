## **The Job**

**The factory is in a remote location, which means that an external security response is unlikely. However, MCT has solid security, consisting of drones, sensors, guards, security mages with spirits, and maybe some chimera. But the players don’t need to take over the site or stick around for long; gaining a direct connection to a manufacturing device and downloading its fles should only take a few seconds. If the players can sneak a data tap in and connect it to the device, their team’s hacker will be able to access it remotely. Some completed Harvesting devices are located within the same building, ready to be loaded into crates. Grabbing those and making a clean getaway will be a much more diffcult task. They’re large enough to make a quick smash-and-grab unlikely, as the players will need a vehicle with lots of cargo space and at least a full minute or two to load them up.**
