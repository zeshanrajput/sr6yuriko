## Hooked Forever

Cyberzombies may not age, but they require constant medications to keep from developing cancer or worse side effects from the procedure. Essentially, a cyberzombie’s lifestyle costs are increased by 2,000¥ a month for medications. Further, these medications aren’t exactly available over the counter. Producing them requires a chemical facility. Receiving them from other sources requires the good graces of the entity that originally performed the surgery (or overcoming an availability of 9).
