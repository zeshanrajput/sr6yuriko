## July 12, 2083

Manastorms across the globe increase in frequency and intensity. Information from an anti-Disian source in Wuxing indicates that the Disians’ “Final Project” is set to begin within ten days. The same day, several great dragons meet with Arleesh in person at one of her lairs to discuss their part in the upcoming battle with the Disians.
