## Making it Shiny and Like New

Detailing is the fine art of making what you’ve got look rad! This includes things like finishing work, new paint, chroming some parts (not replacing them), or smoothing out and pulling the dents and dings from the superficial damage mentioned earlier. Keep in mind this is one of the methods vehicle manufacturers use to put that extra bit of flair that builds a vehicle’s appeal. Detailing a recently repaired vehicle costs vehicle Body x 100 nuyen and takes 1 day.

You can skip this step if you don’t want to pay for it. Everyone does love a dark blue Jackrabbit with a school bus yellow door on it, after all.
