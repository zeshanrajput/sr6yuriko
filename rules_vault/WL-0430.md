## **Untrainable**

**Whether due to stubbornness or a lack of intelligence, this critter is particularly diffcult to train. • Bonus: 2 Karma/level**

- **Game Effect: For each level of this quality, the number of commands and special training this critter may learn is reduced by 1 (to a minimum of zero).**
