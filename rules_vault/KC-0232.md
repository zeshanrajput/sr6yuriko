## **>** Wait, what?

## **>** /dev/grrl

I’d already run into the Code while I was on a job. I was planting a falsified document in some middle manager’s office, and happened to notice he was running a program on a dataterminal that was pulling a _ton_ of power. I got curious about it, but since it looked like it was just a market analytics aggregator at a glance, I figured it wasn’t important and left it alone.

- Netcat



And before anyone says anything—yeah, bad call on my part, and I’ve been kicking myself over it.

So, because I’m still mad as drek at myself for coming face-to-face with the most highly demanded set of zeroes and ones on the net and dismissing it as disinteresting and routine, I’ve tried to make up for it by spending tons of time and effort stalking the back alleys of the Matrix, trying to figure out just who’s after this code and what it is they want to do with it.
