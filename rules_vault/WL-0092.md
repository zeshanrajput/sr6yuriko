## **Death Leopard**

## **(Panthera mortis)**

**When I frst started looking into this particular species, I will admit I was digging and seeing clues based on my own personal bias. I was positive this paracritter was not truly a critter at all and was simply a shifter who had taken a dark, dark road after seeing too many atrocities. I got it. I had seen the same. I think I almost wanted that to be the case. I wanted this to be an avenging angel of some kind rather than a nasty paracritter with a solid purpose but less-than-eco-friendly methods.**

**The death leopard is an Awakened species of the snow leopard, a species driven to extinction (or so we thought). Their actions arose frst in the 2040s when a hunting camp in the mountains of central Asia was found slaughtered. Most of the bodies were left behind, mutilated, with a few others dragged out to traps and used to set them off.**

**It certainly seemed like the actions of a vengeful metahuman group rather than those of an Awakened leopard. Similar scenes were discovered regularly in that same region and then slowly spreading out over a wider range. While the frst camp was specifcally hunting rumors of leopards in the mountains, later camps were everything from monkey poachers to cattle ranchers moving the herd.**

**When I fnally came into contact with a death leopard (the only I have ever seen), I was working with a group of chummers on the prowl for a young warmth of the mountain to bring to another area for repopulation. The frst night, Lady Strange, a Chaos mage, was on watch and went missing. All we found in the morning were paw prints and a long strip of the snowy ground that has been split when she got slammed a good ten meters and then dragged off. We all stayed up**
