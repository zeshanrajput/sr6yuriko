## Sur Pw en

The sur pwen are often referred to as junior priests in the congregation. The full name of the title is houngan sur pwen or mambo sur pwen. They are responsible for all the activities as the hounsi, but also have some additional responsibilities to perform some of the smaller ceremonies. The sur pwen can even use the asogwe’s asson. The path to sur pwen is when one of the hounsi participates in the initiation ceremony within the congregation.

**Cost:** 12 Karma
