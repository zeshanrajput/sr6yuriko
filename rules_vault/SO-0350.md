## Gaining Edge on Social Tests

Edge may be awarded to one side or another whenever an Opposed Social test takes place. A Social test is any test involving the Con or Influence skill. Most Con skill tests are resisted by Judge Intentions or Con + Charisma, and most Influence skill tests are opposed by either Composure or Influence + Charisma. Before the dice are rolled, determine who (if anyone) gets Edge.
