## Term (dwarf, male, 34, brown hair, blue eyes, thin build)

Magic is rare, and those who possess it often develop a strong sense of privilege and elitism. That was Term, right up until a wild night at a bar led to a beating, and Term decided that humility was the better route. But humility goes only until the gloves come off and it’s time to use his magic. Then, it’s showtime. His understated looks allow him to blend into the crowd, and he prefers to use his wide gamut of spells to best advantage.

When the fight ensues, he’ll direct a spirit into the fray and then decide between something flashy and violent or just flashy and distracting, depending on how the runners react. He responds to lethal violence with lethal violence, but if the runners display a softer touch, Term will use more distracting and team-boosting techniques. He is quite fond of making ridiculous phantasms that, while not realistic, are big and distracting or obscuring.
