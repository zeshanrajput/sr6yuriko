## November 23, 2082

Birth of the Defender Movement and implementation of _Project Nemesis_ . Pro-Disian forces transition to a defensive posture in the wake of Defender operations, focusing on protecting the Harvester Network. Although some pro-Disian

groups and units remain on the offensive and continue to actively hunt Defender forces and allies.
