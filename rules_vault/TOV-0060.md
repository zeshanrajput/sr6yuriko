## (Spirit of Water)

Agwe spirits appear as tall, strong, black men glistening with water and draped with seaweed and shells. They usually act dignifi ed and regal. Some of the Rada nanchon in the Agwe family are Met Agwe (lwa of direction), Agwe ‘Woyo (captain of the ship who carries the dead to the afterlife), and Papa Agwe (sailor embodying bravery and provision). In the Petwo family are Agwe Flambeau (bad luck at sea and underwater volcanoes) and Agwe GeRouge (sailor who is starving and fears death).

**Mentor/Patron Spirit:** Coyote or Mountain
