## **Does omega grade (p. 28) replace the used grade in the** _**Shadowrun Sixth World**_ **core book?**

Yes. That means that previously purchased used ‘ware gains the Bad Luck quality (p. 75, _SR6_ ) for any tests involving the augmentation.

## **The price for electrochromatic hair (p. 33) mentions price is by length, but the table provides a stock price no matter the length. Which do I use?**

Use the cost in the table, and the base length can be any length you desire with a max of 100 cm.
