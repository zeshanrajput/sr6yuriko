## ALPHA & OMEGA

## Posted by: Mr. Bonds

When we attack our enemies, we take two prime things into account: What we can do well, and what will hurt the opposition. Both of those things matter. I may be handy with a firearm, but if I’m firing at a de-materialized spirit, I can’t expect to have a good result. No matter how accurate my aim, the bullet will do no good. If, on the other hand, the bullet is aimed at a red, beating heart, it can put a stop to its motion and life in short order.

We often fight against the machinations of the megacorps with the weapons we have—bullets, spells, hacking, and street smarts, namely. The problem is, the corps have plenty of those things at their disposal, and they use them to quickly heal any wounds we inflict.

Their beating heart is the bottom line, the unending series of profit and loss statements that define their existence. When the corps are hurt, it is because they are hit there. The problem we have

is that they have erected so much armor around their finances that we have great trouble doing true damage to it.

That might be changing.

Rumors are bubbling of new weapons being developed, weapons that might directly strike at the financial heart of the corps. Questions abound— who developed the weapon? What does it do? Will it work? How will it be deployed?

I have no answers, only hunches. Experience has shown that when corps take devastating, unexpected, even fatal blows, it comes through equity markets taking away the foundation they thought they had. From Black Tuesday in 1929 to the major recessions accompanying the Matrix crashes of 2029 and 2064, large-scale economic upheavals have wiped entire companies off the books, or transferred their ownership to others. Sometimes, there are smaller activities, where a sector or a single stock is crashed or sent to dizzying heights, making sudden fortunes and steep losses. With every such activity, the governing bodies try to makechanges to prevent such things from happening again. In response, the forces of the world adapt, as they always do.

To understand how this back-and-forth game works and what its rules are, we need to set up a foundation for how this battlefield developed and the way the conflict has gone.
