## ZZZZZ DSEC I

## (Spacecraft)

I tossed all those Zs up when I saw the program alphabetizing things and saw no reason to put a flame war in the middle of this excellent discussion.

I felt this craft needed a mention and a place here, though, especially with the news I’ll pop in at the end.

I designed and got to fly this during testing. Its tech is beyond most of today’s engineers, so talking about the specifics is silly, but she moves smooth and can take the errant celestial object on the chin with a smile. She’s not a space dancer, but she can get the job done of taking metahumanity out into the stars for exploration and colonization.

I’m truly talking about this to give a glimpse of the future. DSEC is no small craft. Her final measure was 318 meters drive to debris shield, with five major living rings and four smaller lab rings. She left with a complement of eight smaller craft docked between those rings as well.

Very few can comment on this craft in any positive way, but I welcome anyone to do so if they made the same choice as I. Before I go, I owe you this:

We’re making more.
