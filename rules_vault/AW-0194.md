## Travel

Travel to Dis used to be relatively straightforward, as far as metaplanar travel goes. But in recent months, travel has become more difficult. While it is still possible to get there via other metaplanes such as the Seelie Court, travelers have commented on there being a recent shift in the barriers leading to Dis that have all but prevented travel to that metaplane. Some scholars speculate about a grand eclipse, with the Moons of Shadow blocking the direct astral ways between Dis and the material plane. Whether this is a naturally occurring event or something under Dis control has yet to be determined.
