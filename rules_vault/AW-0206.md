## Shiawase

Next to Aztechnology, Shiawase has the longest-running relationship with Dis. Known Disian agents and the Auditor himself have visited the

main corporate HQ on multiple occasions. Shiawase R&D sites have also been instrumental in developing the biotechnology for keeping Dis captives alive so they can be sacrificed later.
