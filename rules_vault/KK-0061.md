## KRIME KATALOG

## Krime Barco De Pesca

Krime’s entry into nautical products is this basic fishing boat that can operate on its beefy Diesel Demon multi-fuel engine or, after raising the powered screws, a basic sail design. Designed around troll physicality, it offers a 50 percent larger freezer than any of its contemporaries, which has gotten quite a bit of notice in the field and more than a few fleet purchases.
