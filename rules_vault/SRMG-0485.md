## **For Best of the Rest (p. 174-183), are there any changes needed to make them legal for** _**SRM**_ **?**

- Armored Backpack: The impact to the character is that they are able to carry gear as if their Strength

- was increased by 1.

- S.O.L.D.I.E.R.: Increased fragility means that on a glitch, critical glitch, or if the opponent has 4 net

- hits or more, the gear becomes inoperable until it is fixed.

  - ParaPlate: Only one of these can be worn at a time, and it takes two Minor actions to swap out a plate. Improvised Medical Supplies (Optional Rules): This is legal for _SRM_ .
