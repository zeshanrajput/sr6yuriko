## Krime Cleaner Grenade

It isn’t easy cleaning house—sometimes you just want to throw a grenade into the situation and collect the insurance. Well, Krime has an answer to that demand! The Cleaner grenade is a concentrated collection of cleaning solvents that don’t affect the color of fabrics! Just toss the grenade into a room, wait five minutes with a nice cup of kaff, then wipe everything down clean! Krime: more than just weapons! Now banned by international agreement.
