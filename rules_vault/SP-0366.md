## **Disian Ritual Magic**

**Disian rituals have the same mana and physical limitations as Disian spells, and the units used in a ritual contributed by the ritual leader are limited to 2 x (Willpower + Initiate Grade). Any of the participants in the ritual may also contribute additional units of mana, up to their Initiate Grade, toward the Magic rating used in the sealing test. Any participant may use units to offset their own drain in a one-to-one ratio. Reagents and a lodge are not required for Disian rituals.**

**In addition to all standard rituals, Disians have access to many of their own. These include Spirit Contracting and a very powerful version of the Metaplanar Gateway ritual described on p. 167,** _**Astral Ways**_ **, but with a radius measured in hundreds of meters. To represent an open Disian metaplanar gateway in games, use the rules outlined on p. 63,** _**Slip Streams**_ **.**
