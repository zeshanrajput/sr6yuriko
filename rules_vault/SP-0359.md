## **but what about a magic attribute?**

**Some may have noticed that the Disian Magician presented in “Astral Ways” has a Magic attribute. That number simply represented ten units of mana being used whenever a spell was cast. Any test initiated by a native Disian, that uses Magic as part of the test, will require a quantity of units to be expended to represent the Magic rating.**
