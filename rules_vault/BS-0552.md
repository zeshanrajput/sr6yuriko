## Magical Genetic Augmentations

Through the use of a recently developed ritual sorcery formula combined with experimental genetic manipulation, mundane critters may be enhanced with a Magic attribute and abilities. This can only be accomplished on an embryo, and the ritual must be performed once per day during its gestation.
