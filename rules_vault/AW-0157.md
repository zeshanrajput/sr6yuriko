## Sandcasting with True Earth

True Earth stone can be ground up in a special mortar to use the sand to cast a focus. With this kind of preparation, the focus cannot be bonded to anyone else after the initial bond. Additionally, the artificer can imbue one additional attribute into it, selected from the list below.
