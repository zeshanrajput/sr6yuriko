## **From Astral Ways, can I summon elementals (p. 46) or take Dinosaur Summoning metamagic (p. 141)?**

Yes. Dinosaur spirits are special beyond just having a set Force based on the type. They don’t have the power Astral Form which means that when they are summoned, they will appear only on the physical plane with a successful summoning. Because of this, they cannot be channeled.

**From Astral Ways, can I use true elements or true element foci (p. 57-59)?**

No.
