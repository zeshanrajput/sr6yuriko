## Strength of the Damned

Once a body has been pushed beyond death, pushing its metahuman limits is comparatively simple. To reflect this, add the cyberzombie’s total points of Essence below zero (rounded down) to all its maximum natural physical attribute values and Physical Condition Monitor (for instance, a dwarf cyberzombie with –2 points of Essence would have a new maximum natural Body of 9 rather than 7 and 2 extra boxes of damage they could take). Further, a cyberzombie is inhumanly resilient and is immune to Stun Damage. It also does not take wound modifiers as it becomes damaged. A cyberzombie gains Hardened Armor (p. 224, _SR6_ ) equal to its negative Essence value (rounded down) +2.
