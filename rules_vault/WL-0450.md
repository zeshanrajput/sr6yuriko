## **Radiation Engulf**

**==> picture [228 x 20] intentionally omitted \<==**

**----- Start of picture text -----**<br>
TYPE ACTION RANGE DURATION<br>P Major Touch Sustained<br>**----- End of picture text -----**<br>

**In addition to the existing engulf secondary effects on p. 223,** _**SR6**_ **, there is also Radiation Engulf. This requires a Close Combat attack and causes (Magic)P radiation damage; net hits increase the damage of the attack. Success on the test engulfs the target, and they gain the Immobilized and Irradiated statuses. On the target’s turn, they may use a Major Action to attempt to escape by making an Athletics + Strength vs. the critter’s Body + Magic Opposed test. On the critter’s next turn, if its target is still engulfed, it automatically inficts (Magic)P damage, which the target can resist normally.**
