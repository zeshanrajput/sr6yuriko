## Disguises and Espionage Gear

## Blur Mask (Rating 1-6)

This tiny device is worn somewhere on the user’s head. Typically, it’s built into a user’s glasses, a hood, bandana, or jewelry worn on the head or face. When active, the blur mask uses a combination of electronic warfare and hacking technology to cause visual sensors and processors to perceive the user’s face as an indistinguishable dark blur. Blur masks are legal in most public spaces but are highly restricted within most extraterritorial corporate property and secure locations. Using one also tends to attract the attention of law enforcement and security types.
