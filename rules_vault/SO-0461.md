## The Plot Thickens

You’ve set the hook, and now it’s time to reel them in. During this process, you gain the mark’s confidence over time. This takes at least one week and requires an Influence (Etiquette) + Charisma test, and even though this is not a Con skill test, you may use Edge from the Long Con Pool on the test. The number of hits you scored on the Influence test serves as the limit for the maximum number of hits you may keep on your following Con skill test. To successfully sell the con, you must make another Opposed test using your Con (Acting) + Charisma vs. the mark’s Judge Intentions. If you succeed, the mark will fall for your con and do what you ask of them (per the GM’s discretion).
