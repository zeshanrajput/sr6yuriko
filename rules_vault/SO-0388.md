## Quicker than the Eye

## [Stealth (Palming) or Con (Acting), Major Action] 3 Edge

You create a sudden distraction that draws an opponent’s attention away from what’s really going on. Make a Stealth (Palming) + Agility or Con (Acting) + Charisma test opposed by the target’s Perception + Intuition (vs. Stealth) or Judge Intentions (vs. Con). If successful, the target gains the Misdirected 1 condition. The level of the condition increases by +1 for every two net hits.

In a combat situation, the Misdirected condition decreases by 1 at the end of each successive round. Outside of combat, the Misdirected status decreases by 1 at the end of each minute.
