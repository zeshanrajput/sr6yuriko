## Heavy Implanted Weapon

The heaviest personally portable weapons cannot ordinarily be implanted, but with enough engineering virtually anything is possible. A heavy implanted weapon system provides the necessary volume and reinforcement coupled with retaining the field of motion necessary to make implanting a large weapon worthwhile. Rifles, machine guns, assault cannons, and missile launchers can be housed in a heavy implanted weapon mount. These weapons do not gain any concealability (p. 246, _SR6_ ) when housed by this system.
