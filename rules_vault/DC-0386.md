## vehicles and magic

When a spell or power grants a Defense test but invokes attributes that a vehicle or drone does not have, there can be confusion about what, if anything, to roll in their place. Rest assured that just because a drone doesn’t have Reaction or Intuition, they don’t roll zeroes on that defense test.

Remember that mana-based powers and spells have no effect on vehicles and drones. Additionally, the gamemaster is empowered to rule that certain Physical magical effects might have no effect at all on a non-living, non-biological target (e.g. Paralyzing Touch, Heal, etc.).
