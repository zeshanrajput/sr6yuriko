## Awakened Bug Spray

Exactly what it says on the tin, as they say. This Awakened bug spray will repel Awakened critters out in the bayous of New Orleans. When rolling an Outdoors (Survival) check, you gain one Edge. You also gain a point of Edge when defending against an attack from an Awakened insect. Each can contain enough spray for 24 cumulative hours of use.

- You shouldn’t be “clashing” at all, this is a critter-slaying tool, pure and simple.

- Marko

- A perfect weapon to bond—plenty of exotic components and a little orichalchum makes it easy to be one with. I could practically hear it singing to me before I even touched it.
