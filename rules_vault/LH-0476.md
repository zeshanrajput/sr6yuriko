## April 30, 2083

Buttercup hosts her infamous press conference, and the mainstream media reacts negatively. Horizon uses the chaos to obtain more intelligence and identify targets. Beyond this date, fighting intensifies as the hunt for Mobile Harvesters and the key to the Disian codes ramps up. Aztechnology’s Special Projects lead, Armando Salazar, begins plans for an internal corporate coup.
