## Spiritual Guides and Characters

## Queen Candelia

She is a storm of her own, an anthropomorphic whirlwind shifting around a dragonesque shape. Candelia’s tantrums of throwing furnishings are only hampered by retained punkahs who keep mobile, catching the housewares before they break. Part of it is theatrics, throwing anyone seeking an audience off their game and allowing the queen to do the unexpected. And part of it is the nature of air elementals, as the queen will execute multiple seemingly random orders. In the end, though, they work together to achieve a greater goal.

- Third part is that she really has a temper. If you curried favor from Lord Vendaval or Sigh-on-Wind, and not her, expulsion from the plane is the least of your worries.

- Lyran
