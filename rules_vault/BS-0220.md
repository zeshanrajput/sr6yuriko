## Granite

Extensive calcite and keratin formations make the individual’s skin appear greyish and hard as stone. It grants +6 Defense Rating (not cumulative with other worn armors). Incurs a –1 dice pool modifier to fine manipulation of small items, such as a trigger. The user gains one bonus hit when resisting an attack that does 6 or more modified damage.
