## Stinger

The stinger is a simple spike with a hollow tube that allows the use of a chemical gland to inject a target. Stingers come in three sizes: tiny, which is barely able to pierce the skin; medium, which is akin to a knife blade; and large, which is roughly the size of a forearm and quite illegal. Stingers are, in essence, non-retractable hand blades and spurs.

Stingers can use the Close Combat (Unarmed) skill. Damage Values and Attack Ratings can be found in the Bioweapon Table on the next page.
