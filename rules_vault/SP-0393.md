## **Chain-Sword**

## **Vibroblade**

**A larger version of a chainblade, the chainsword uses a one-meter-long blade attached to a hand-and-a-half hilt, long enough for one or two-handed use. The weight of the weapon requires a fairly strong individual (Strength 3 or higher) for single-handed use.**

**A vibroblade is a micro-serrated knife blade paired to an ultrasonic generator built into the knife’s hilt. The vibrations along the blade’s edge oscillate at thousands of times per second, allowing it to cut through fesh, bone, and body armor with ease. The vibroblade’s distinctive hum is fairly loud and makes the weapon’s use obvious. The ceramic edge of a vibroblade is somewhat fragile and susceptible to damage that can reduce the effectiveness of the weapon until the edge is replaced.**
