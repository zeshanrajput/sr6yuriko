## Job Board

Not every runner waits around to get that call from their fixer. If you’ve been in the biz long enough and made the right connections, you may get access to one of the many shadow VPNs that host job boards. Fixers post jobs, and some techs on staff go and dig up a little data on who and what these offers might entail. Sometimes they’re way off, but more often than not, they get enough right to keep the runners who come on coming back for more. Here, I’ve pulled up a bunch of jobs that point to MCT. Take a look at what they’re up to right here.
