## Ares Dragon

By churning these out year after year for corps, nations, and individuals, Ares has made the Dragon the most commonly identifiable cargo helicopter in the world. The double-rotor design gives it the lifting capability to handle heavy cargo, Firewatch strike teams, or an ordnance load that would give its namesakes pause.
