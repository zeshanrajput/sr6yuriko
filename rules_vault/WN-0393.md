## Encounter 4: Truce or Dare?

The reporters who have survived their encounters, along with Piper, want to meet the runners and talk about what’s been happening. They let the runners set the time and place in order to feel safer after all the ambushes.

All of these reporters have been under surveillance by the UCAS CIA, and the meeting plan is compromised. The CIA aren’t keen on all of these snoops getting together and realizing they’ve been used. They send a hit squad (use two DocWagon HTR Officers, p. 207, _SR6_ ; Lone Star Combat Mage, p. 207, _SR6_ ; DocWagon HTR Support Engineer, p. 208, _SR6_ ; six Mafia Soldatos, p. 207, _SR6_ ) to the meet site to target the reporters, including Piper.

The runners need to protect everyone so they can sit down and all have a nice chat about who they got these tips from and start solving the mystery of who is playing them.
