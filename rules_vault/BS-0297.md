## Bioamplifier Nanites

**Augmented Neural Network:** This nanite colony creates parallel neural connections in the sensory processing areas of the brain as well as nanostructures to augment the neural signals between the brain and major muscle groups. The overall effect is to shorten the response time between sensory input and physical action. The augmented neural network grants a +1 dice pool modifier to skill tests that are linked to Reaction as well as a +1 dice pool modifier to all Defense tests.



- **Nanite Volume:** The structure created by this colony requires two points of nanite volume.

- **Overdrive:** Using overdrive generates additional bonus dice to Reaction linked tests. Each point of nanite volume put in overdrive adds an additional +1 dice to skill tests linked to Reaction. Bonus dice from overdrive do not provide any bonus for Defense tests.

**Bio-Response Override:** The nanites in a bio-response override colony construct a series of nanostructures in the hypothalamus, parietal lobe, and amygdala. These structures allow the user to dampen selected stimuli or physiological responses, effectively lessening the sensations of fatigue, pain, fear, hunger, and thirst. This increases the Willpower attribute by one point and adds one box to the user’s Stun condition monitor.

- **Nanite Volume:** The structure created by this colony requires three points of nanite volume.

- **Overdrive:** Using overdrive reinforces the sensory override structures, completely blocking a portion of the sensory input instead of dampening it. Each point of nanite volume placed into overdrive offsets a single point of a negative modifier for one hour. For example, the –1 modifier for filling 3 boxes of the condition monitor can be removed.

**Extracellular Reinforcement:** By reinforcing the connective tissue between the body’s cells with strands of a tough, resilient biopolymer, the extracellular reinforcement colony increases the body’s ability to resist damage. The Body attribute is considered one point higher for soaking damage and for any test to resist magic where Body is used. One box is also added to the user’s Physical condition monitor track.

- **Nanite Volume:** The structure created by this colony requires three points of nanite volume.

- **Overdrive:** Using overdrive temporarily increases the strength of extracellular connections. Each point of nanite volume placed in overdrive is removed from the Damage Resistance test and automatically soaks one point of physical damage.

**Limbic Neural Amplifier:** This nanite colony builds supplemental neural connections that increases the parallel processing ability of the brain throughout the parietal, temporal, and occipital lobes. These are the areas that produce sensory perceptions from neural sensory stimuli, and the limbic neural amplifiers augment these systems.

The limbic neural amplifier nanostructure grants a +1 dice pool modifier on skill tests that are linked to Intuition.

- **Nanite Volume:** The structure created by this colony requires two points of nanite volume.

- **Overdrive:** Using overdrive generates additional bonus dice. Each point of nanite volume put in overdrive adds an additional +1 dice to skill tests linked to Intuition.

**Neocortical Neural Amplifier:** The neocortical neural amplifier colony creates a nanostructure in the user’s frontal lobe that amplifies abstract thought and enhances problem-solving ability. The nanostructures created by a neocortical neural amplifier colony grant a +1 dice pool modifier on skill tests linked to Logic.

- **Nanite Volume:** The structure created by this colony requires two points of nanite volume.

- **Overdrive:** Using overdrive generates additional bonus dice. Each point of nanite volume put in overdrive adds an additional +1 dice to skill tests linked to Logic.

**Neural Pattern Reinforcement** : By creating clusters of enhanced muscle memory, a neural pattern reinforcement colony increases the user’s ability to refine and repeat specific movements—such as a perfect trigger pull or sword stroke—thereby minimizing errors. The nanostructures created by a neural pattern reinforcement colony grant a +1 dice pool modifier on skill tests that are linked to Agility.

- **Nanite Volume:** The structure created by this colony requires two points of nanite volume.

- **Overdrive:** Using overdrive generates additional bonus dice. Each point of nanite volume put in overdrive adds an additional +1 dice on skill tests linked to Agility.

**Neurochemical Regulator:** A lot of what is perceived as “charisma” or presence can be chalked up to projecting self-confidence and composure while dealing with others. A neurochemical regulator colony constructs a series of nanostructures in the brain that minimizes the emotional responses associated with self-doubt and enhances those associated with self-confidence. The net effect of the colony is to increase Charisma by one point in any social situation. This effect also extends to composure tests.

- **Nanite Volume:** The structure created by this colony requires two points of nanite volume.

- **Overdrive:** Using overdrive generates additional bonus dice for skill tests using Charisma. Each point of nanite volume put in overdrive adds an additional +1 die to skill tests linked to Charisma.



**Neuromuscular Amplifier:** Similar to extracellular reinforcement, a neuromuscular amplifier colony adds a strong biopolymer between the body’s cells, but in this case it only affects the skeletal muscles. The nanostructures created with this nanite colony augment the body’s maximum power in short bursts, increasing the impact energy of melee attacks. This adds +1 DV to all melee attacks, either S or P as appropriate for the type of attack. The nanostructures created by the neuromuscular amplifier colony provide no benefit to cyberlimbs.

- **Nanite Volume:** The structure created by this colony requires two points of nanite volume.

- **Overdrive:** Using overdrive increases the power generated during an attack even further, pushing past what the biological systems of the body can normally handle. Each point of nanite volume put in overdrive adds an additional +1 DV to the attack but causes 1DV of unresisted Stun damage to the user from the stress of channeling the extra power through the body.
