## defiance dairy

At Defiance Dairy, we understand how precious our products are to our clients. Knowing this, we treat our cows to the finest foods and cleanest living facilities alongside beautiful fields where they are allowed to roam free between their spa treatment sessions.

During each cow’s spa session, they’re tended to personally. The facility is temperature controlled, handlers make sure to warm their hands properly, and each bovine is treated as a queen with a massage and a soothing bath. What does this mean for our milk? It means everything.

Free of unnatural ingredients, our products come from the purest source.
