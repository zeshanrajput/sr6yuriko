## **Specialists**

**Instead of spending half this fle describing all the different divisions in a police department’s org chart, I’m going to lump most of them under “specialists.” These include police deckers from the Matrix Crime division (often called “NetWatch” by people who still live in the 2050s), police mages from Arcane Investigations, police riggers from certain parts of the Patrol Division, the bomb squad, fur missiles (aka K-9), forensics, dispatchers, and so on. These are all the people who you aren’t likely to meet during your runs, unless the run is targeting the police department itself.**
