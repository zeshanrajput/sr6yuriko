## **What do you tell your kid?**

**The truth. People act surprised when I tell them this. “You tell your child you’re criminal?” Yeah, I do. Look, the way I fgure it, two of the most important lessons you can teach a child is how the world works and that you should never play the other guy’s game. In our world, playing by the rules is playing the other guy’s game, and therefore is for suckers. If we do our job right as parents, Jack will understand why we made the choices we made and the merit of them. He doesn’t have to agree, but he should understand. And it’s a lot better if he fnds out from us than getting surprised later.**
