## S pirit of Erzulie

## (Spirit of Kin)

Erzulie spirits take on the appearance of charming, sensual, and seductive women. In the Rada nanchon are Erzulie Freda (vain and fl irty), Erzulie Mansur (maternal love), and Granne Erzulie (grandmotherly wisdom). In the Petwo nation are Erzulie Dantor (protects women and children and deals revenge for those that wrong them), Erzulie Balianne (keeper of secret, lost loves), Manbo Zila (pain of childbirth), Erzulie Yeux Rouge (revenge for unfaithful lovers), and Erzulie Toho (aids the jealous).

**Mentor/Patron Spirit:** Cat or Mystery
