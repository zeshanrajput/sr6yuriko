## Covert-Ops Specialist

Some think of covert ops as crawling through vents, others as slinking slowly through the underbrush, but often, the covert ops specialist performs covertly in plain sight by looking like nothing more than an electronic-clipboard-carrying wageslave, a focused executive intent on a destination, or who wants to give off the air of authority to keep people from wanting to make eye contact with him. The thing about this is, all of it is to avoid starting a fight, because the covert-ops specialist prefers to not leave a trail of bodies (or even a single one) so that no one is the wiser for their arrival, and they can depart with whatever they were looking to acquire.
