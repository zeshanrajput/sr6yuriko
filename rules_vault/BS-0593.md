## Evo

Evo’s greatest contribution to the critter augmentation market is the development of the critterjack and critter comm augmentations, and—largely due to their cooperation with monads—nano-critter augmentations. They were the first megacorporation to create nanotech Matrix relay implants that are designed to modify plankton in order to overcome the obstacle of transmitting Matrix signals within the ocean.
