## **Doug Powell III**

**The third is often seen by the fourth and ffth as squandering the family fortune, but it would take a lot of card collecting to put a dent in this old-money bank. He is a classic style of old-money huntsman who respects etiquette and rules just as much as he desires acquisition. That doesn’t mean he always fghts fair, but it means when he fghts dirty, he cleans up the mess or expects operatives working for him to do so. His son and grandson know his desire to keep face, and they often work against his efforts in hopes of eventually embarrassing him enough for him to quit card-seeking.**

**Within the cartomancers, he is one of the few who will sell his prizes to other members. He usually approaches members with less resources or successes and ingratiates himself to them with the sale. Rumors inside the society refer to a divination Powell got from one of his frst cards. They claim that he thinks he will come into possession of a full deck by working within the society and making a single great move at just the right time.**
