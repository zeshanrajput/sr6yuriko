## Venom Sprayer

Drugs or toxins with the inhalation or contact vector can be delivered by a critter that is implanted with a venom sprayer installed in its mouth. This is a risky maneuver which requires specialized training, and a mishap can easily result in the critter being contaminated by the drug or toxin.

from inhalation vectors, while full body armor and helmet with the chemical seal improvement is the only sure defense against contact vectors. The venom sprayer carries three doses of the drug or toxin, but all three doses are required for a single attack. A venom reservoir augmentation allows the critter to be equipped with additional doses, but three doses are used for every attack—so there is little reason to use venom reservoirs with a rating that is not divisible by three.

**Wireless Bonus:** The venom sprayer can be disarmed or armed again with a Control Device Minor Action. It must be armed before the critter can attack with it.
