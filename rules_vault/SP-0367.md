## **Ritual: Spirit Contracting**

## **Threshold: Spirit’s Force**

**This ritual—only usable by native Disians—is used to summon and bind any type of spirit. It takes four hours to complete. The threshold of the sealing test is equal to the contracted spirit’s Force and there is no opposing roll by the spirit to resist the ritual. Hits on the sealing test past the threshold become services owed to the ritual leader. The number of services owed by any one spirit may not exceed the ritual leader’s Willpower, and these may be used as either normal or bound tasks. The number of spirits that owe services to a native Disian may not have more total Force than 3 x (Logic + Intuition).**
