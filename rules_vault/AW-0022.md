## Adjacent Metaplanes

Quite a few metaplanes are very near to the physical plane, meaning that they are very close within the space of the Astral Sea. But there are eight that are actually adjacent to the physical plane. The four Elemental Realms plus the metaplanes of Kin, Faerie, Beasts, and Plants, many of them overlapping one another in places. Several of them overlap the physical plane as well. These eight metaplanes are among the easiest for metahumans to explore, as their physical features and characteristics are very similar to the physical plane. Space, time, and the laws of physics apply in most cases— though there are enough strange phenomena and bizarre realms to provide exceptions to this.
