## Fixing the Game

The Dissonant technomancer has been contacted by an employer with an interest in sports betting. They wish to hire the team to influence the outcome of an important sporting event, and do it without implicating the team they wish to see win. The team can approach this a number of ways, but the dissonant technomancer will likely find that messing with the devices being used in support of the sports team during the game is where their talents should make the most impact. The other runners should also be involved, as device-tampering alone is not likely to be worth it. They might find a way to sneak some runners into the game, convince (probably by blackmail or extortion) some of the athletes or referees to play poorly, or even find some way to enhance the abilities of some of the athletes. There are many ways to approach the task. For rules to resolve a sports event, see _Sixth World Companion_ ( **Sixth World Sports** , p. 17), where they are included as part of a sixth world sports alternate campaign concept.

If the team successfully fixes the game, they gain a new contact (Connection 4, Loyalty 2) with the types media and criminal. This contact might represent the employer who hired the team to fix the game, or it could be another individual who also benefited from their unsportsmanlike interference.
