## Milton (elf, male, 16, white hair, grey eyes, thin build)

Being a drek-hot decker in the barrens can certainly expand a person’s head and that’s the case with Milton. He is exceptionally intelligent, but that mind is contained in an atrociously gaunt face

atop a frame of little more than skin and bones. He grew up in the Aurora Warrens in Denver and developed some solid skills against barrens-trash electronics. When trouble hit the FRFZ a few years back, he left and settled in St. Louis. He’s been doing some legit work for awhile but got back into the shadows recently in order to pay off some gambling debts. His efforts are blunt and obvious with very little subtlety. He figures he is in the best position to locate the code, since naturally it would be stored on something electronic.

He tries to hack the characters’ commlinks and look for data, but if he can’t find any, he is more than willing to destroy them in order to start hindering communication and opening up PANs to reveal more sensitive gear.
