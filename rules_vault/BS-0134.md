## Cyberlimbs, Attributes, and Overdrive Bonuses

The online Shadowrun, Sixth World FAQ made some clarifications and minor changes to the rules regarding cyberlimbs, which are repeated below for convenience:

Partial cyberlimbs and cyberskulls do not possess Strength or Agility attributes independently of the rest of the character. The presence of any kind of cyberlimb does not prohibit the character from also having other augmentations that affect the bones or muscles; these augmentations simply don’t add their effects to activities performed by a cyberlimb. Lastly, full cyberlimbs can make use of augmentation overdrive. Since they have no rating, they instead gain +2 Strength and +2 Agility, which may exceed the augmented maximum. One added die is converted to a wild die.
