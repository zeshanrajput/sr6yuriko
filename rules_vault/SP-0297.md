## **Breadcrumbs**

**A mysterious representative of Transys-Neuronet corporation needs to hire the player characters for a high-priority locate-and-extract job. The principal in this mission is a T-N company linguist and cryptographer working on a top-secret project who went missing and was presumed dead during the Week of Death. Then only seven hours ago, the analyst managed to make contact, confrming that she’s on the run and needs immediate assistance. What the runners don’t initially know is that this cryptographer has managed to decipher and translate the Disian language … and that a team of prime runners has been dispatched to fnd her.**
