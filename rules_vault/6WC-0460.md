## Corrosive Spit

**Cost:** 4 Karma (per level)

Like the Corrosive Spit ability (p. 222, _SR6_ ) of critters, the individual can make a ranged combat attack, with Exotic Weapon (Spittle), DV 2P per level of attack (maximum 4), AR 4/6/4/–/–. The damage is Chemical, and any damage also inflicts the Corrosive status with a value equal to the quality’s level (p. 52, _SR6_ ).
