## **Europe**

**While Asia is the largest front in the Disian confict, Europe is easily the most condensed, an almost close-quarters battle by comparison. With Saeder-Krupp’s world HQ located in the Allied German States and being the top of the megacorporate ladder until very recently, the corporation has traditionally had a great deal of infuence within the continent. But recent events have shown that S-K’s infuence may not be as absolute as Lofwyr had hoped. More and more elements are not just rallying against the Disian threat but fghting against the status quo.**
