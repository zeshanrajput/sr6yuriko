**The bank is in the old western portion of Denver’s plane. Folks know the sheriff has something dangerous in the safe but not what. The runners could go in hard, or they could use the citizens’ fear and suspicion to help get the artifact more subtly.**

**The artifact is actually a sample of void rock, or foveaium, as it is initially being called. The element completely cancels all mana around it. It is rumored to be from a metaplane that nearly died but is now simply mundane. Other rumors claim that it is a dreary, dying realm, but in the small pockets where foveaium is not present, there is still some joy and happiness. Those who have been to the plane itself say it heavily refects our own world, had the Awakening been stalled.**
