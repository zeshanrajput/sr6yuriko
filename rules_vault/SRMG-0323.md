## **What can link-firing (p. 143) apply to?**

First, this is usable for firing all the guns on a single vehicle or drone. Second, this is used for dual wielding. Third, this can be used on a set of cyberlimbs, but each weapon must have an Articulated Weapon Mount. Whether the weapon grants +1 DV or +2 DV is based on the final damage of the gun, ammo, and firing mode.

## **If I jump into a drone or vehicle and make an Athletics test to sprint, am I limited to the acceleration and top speed?**

No, as seen in the Shadowrun Sixth World FAQ, the amount of speed is increased or decreased by up to one meter for each net hit.
