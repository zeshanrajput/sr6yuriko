## 8: Get Out Of Jail Free Card

## Hook

Cat calls the team early in the evening, and she’s excited enough that her Scottish accent is on full display. She needs the team in the Lower West Side to secure a Roadmaster full of contraband before anyone else can get to it. The catch is that it’s in the impound lot of NYPD, Inc., Precinct 10. Cat offers 5,000 nuyen per runner to retrieve the van and a 3,000-nuyen bonus if the team can extract and bring her the driver, who is in a holding cell at the same location.

If the team asks for more information, Cat will tell them what she knows. She has a network of contacts on the lookout for unusual tech being smuggled onto the island in the hopes of finding some of the devices used in the recent incident in Denver. She just got word that an up-armored Roadmaster full of unidentified tech was impounded by the NYPD. The van was attacked by the Ancients in Lower West Side, and the driver was knocked out. NYPD arrived just as the gang was cracking open the cargo hold. Several gang members were arrested at the scene. The vehicle didn’t have the proper paperwork, so the driver was also arrested, and the vehicle was towed to the impound lot.
