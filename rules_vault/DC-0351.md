## CF Mods

All of the mods that use available CF are grouped together here. They must either use the available cargo space or use the extra cargo space mod to generate more CF. They have both the CF cost and a cost in mod slot points listed; the mod slot cost represents the CF generated with the extra cargo space mod. Other systems that are modifications of seating or cargo space only have a mod slot cost listed—or, like accessories, they may not cost any mod slots at all.
