## Attention Coprocessor (Rating 1-4)

Implanted tactical computers have been available since the 2050s, and modern systems are more efficient than ever. Cybernetic processors expand the biological brain’s capacity for multitasking while sophisticated algorithms parse sensory data fed to the brain for potential threats, warning the user via AR or neural tug. Each rating of an attention co-processor grants +1 die for Defense tests.

**Cyberware overdrive:** Add the attention coprocessor’s rating to a Perception test. One of these dice must be a wild die.

**Wireless bonus:** The attention coprocessor also adds its bonus to Surprise tests.
