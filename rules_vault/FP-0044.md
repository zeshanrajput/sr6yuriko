## **Cromwell Reeves**

**He lost his wife and daughter in an astral rift due to an incident with a card, and now he hunts them to fnd a way to get them back. He has been hunting too long, and the strain has worn him thin. He has as much a death wish as he has hope of actually fnding them, so working for him is dangerous. He doesn’t fear death and therefore doesn’t play fair. If he makes a deal, he keeps it, but if you try to cross him, he has little issue spending more of his fortune to make you pay. And remember, he hasn’t lost his competitive ego yet. Squaring the deal is enough to get him off your back, but we all know how well most runners back down.**
