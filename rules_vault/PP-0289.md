## Shiawase Manufacturing

It’s easy to overlook their industrial arm, but Shiawase’s got a massive one, and with the recent purchase of Toyota, you’ll be seeing more of it on a street-level view instead of a macro view. The consumer-products arm handles big hits like Hello Puppy and assorted mechanized toys, but they also have big numbers in heavy industry thanks to Hephaestus Industries and Shiawase Motors, who will now have a flagship brand to rally behind. Shiawase also slots their fashion industries in here, as well as computer tech; as noted elsewhere, Shiawase’s never been able to draft the local talent that MCT and Renraku can, so their computer tech has lagged behind both of those two. But being in third place behind two of the three best computer manufacturers in the world isn’t a terrible place to be.
