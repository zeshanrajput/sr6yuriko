## Gear Stats

The key functions of any piece of gear in _Shadowrun_ are expressed in its game stats. The exact categories of stats depend on the item’s type. Cost is a universal stat—everything costs money—and all items also have Availability. We’ll cover that and some of the more specialized stats.
