## Railspur Apartments

The Terminal district is in the midst of gentrification. Several blocks away from construction of the Zeta-Imperial Chemicals Arcology, Railspur is one of a few dozen buildings the gentrification hasn’t reached yet. Squatters still linger on the doorstep, AR graffiti fills the streets, and police response time is comically slower than it is a few blocks away.
