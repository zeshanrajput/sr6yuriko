## Pills and Pillage

## Main Location: Z-IC Tegel

## Notable Characters: Z-IC Flughafenrigger (use Minuteman Security Rigger, p. 206)

For many years, the hidden AI Apex has siphoned off millions of Vory money and redirected it for the expansion of the Berlin cable Matrix and diverse operations to defend, strengthen, and eventually reinstitute the Flux State. To finance her operations and to unite as many Vory factions as possible under her control, Berlin’s new tsarina Nadjeska “Drakova” Girkin needs money fast— and she has some bold ideas on how to do it.

**Original Message:** A very influential Schmidt is looking for a team to infiltrate Z-IC Tegel airport and take control of a heavy-lift drone carrying tons of pharmaceuticals. Since the drone will be hit with heavy jamming signals as soon as it veers off course, causing it to automatically land, the presence of at least one cable-linked rigger aboard the drone is unavoidable. Fierce opposition is to be expected. The drone and its load of four standard shipping containers must be directed to Northern Pankow, where it is welcome to crash.
