## **Strictures:**

- _Solidarity:_ Stand with your fellow members through thick and thin. Must be a member of the Shadow Chapters.

- _Community:_ Spend time and resources helping the local communities.

- **Specialties:**

- Usage of magic for shadowrunners.

- Non-lethal and non-area effect spell casting.
