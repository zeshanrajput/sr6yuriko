## **Mutual Backscratching**

**Offcially, it’s policy that all subjects and suspects are to be treated the same. As such, shadowrunners are to be treated as just another type of suspect, albeit a highly armed and dangerous suspect. According to the books, it makes no difference if what you’re doing is some kind of clandestine operation for some shady overlord or taking out some gang scumbags harassing the local convenience store. The law is clear: break it, and you’re a criminal. It’s then up to the local law to drag you in kicking and screaming to face what amounts to justice.**
