## Grey Biotech

Grey is the division that deals with environmental applications, biodiversity, and the removal of pollutants. It tends to work closely with green and white biotech on projects. The “Green Roof” projects are an example of this, helping urban environments, sustainable forestry, or transgenic pets. When working with white biotech, you will see grey used in air scrubbers or processes that remove sewage or chemicals from water or soil.
