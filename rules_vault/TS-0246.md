## **Evo PET (Persona l Electric Transport)**

**Personal transports are nothing new and there are many options on the market, but when Neo-PD approached Evo about designing and contracting their signature transport, Evo jumped on the chance. While underestimated, and often overlooked and scorned, the PET actually utilizes advanced tech in gyro-stabilization and AR controls that make any LEP offcer stuck on “foot” patrol glad they have it. The PET is a two-wheeled scooter board that you steer by shifting your weight. The PET features emergency light hubs on its wheels and AR broadcast for pursuit scenarios.**
