## The Job

The information retrieved in mission 26 mentions a meeting, but it does not give an address or even coordinates where the meeting will take place. The first job is to find out where the meeting will take place.

The contents of the message are as follows: “Mercury will move forward, and so should we. We begin where Tammany ended, 7 p.m. Dinner will be served.”

The first part of the message indicates the time. Any PC with an Astrology- or Astronomy-related skill can make a Logic + Intuition (2) test, or PCs can make an Electronics + Intuition (4) test to get the Matrix to spill the information. The reference is to the planet Mercury coming out of retrograde, which will happen the very next day.

The second part of the message is a historical



reference to the Tammany Hall political machine. This requires a History or New York knowledge skill and a Logic + Intuition (3) test, or a Matrix Search (Electronics + Intuition [4]). The reference is to the last building that housed Tammany Hall, which sits at 44 Union Square.

The PCs now know the time and place of the meeting.
