## appendix: PC monad rules

Some of the characters encountered in _Whisper Nets_ are Monads, people who experience cognitive fragmentation disorder (CFD) and are sharing their mind with another personality with AI origins.

The worst of CFD is over, cures are available, and the worst of the headcases have either been captured, capped, or took a ride on the DSECI express. Those who are left behind to be portrayed by players are the ones who still seek a balance with humanity. They have come to accords with their multiple minds, or one mind has simply wiped the other from existence, leaving only jealous memories and confused loved ones.

Being a Monad is not a turmoil-filled punishment, but a gift that comes with its own set of troubles, no different than being Awakened or Emerged. Some will idolize you, some will fear you, some will hunt you, and some may not even believe you exist. But you know you exist. You know _why_ you exist, and in the shadows is where you feel it is best to exist.
