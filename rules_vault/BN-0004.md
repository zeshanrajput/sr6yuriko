## **Sixth World** Shifters

## Posted by: Triple Flip

Hiya, Triple Flip here—but most folks just call me Trip. Shifter by birth, rigger by trade. I’ve been asked to provide a little different perspective on shifters than most folks have heard.

Shifters start out life as their animal form, and most will spend their formative years (months?) in that form. Shifters born to shifter parents have a much easier time. Shifters born or Awakened from animal parents have a bit tougher time of it. As it turns out, being born in the woods and raised by wolves has an impact on early childhood development. As these were the fi rst shifters encountered by metahumanity, those fi rst impressions have stuck around—but attitudes can be changed.
