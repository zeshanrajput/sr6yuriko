## Trigger Removal

A popular weapon modification for shooters who (for whatever reason) have difficulty manipulating a standard trigger, the trigger removal modification replaces the normal mechanisms with a remote trigger assembly. This assembly is linked directly to a smartgun system and allows the user to fire and otherwise operate the weapon with a mental or pre-programmed verbal/audio command. The major downside to this modification is that if wireless connectivity is lost, then all a shooter has is a hunk of metal and plastics.
