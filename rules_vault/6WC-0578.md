## Extended Overdrive

You can push your augmentations beyond all normal limits to eke out an extra burst of whatever you need.

- **Cost:** 6 Karma per Level

- **Game Effect:** When you engage augmentation overdrive (p. 282, _SR6_ ), it lasts for one round per level of this quality (instead of a single test). You must include the wild die on every test that uses the boosted attribute.
