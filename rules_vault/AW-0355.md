## Dinosaur Spirits

The Island of Tandora is populated by powerful beast spirits that resemble dinosaurs. These beast spirits are sapient, but they think and act as beasts rather than metahumans. Huge bipedal carnivores with comically short arms, sauropods with massive bodies and incredibly long necks, even triceratops and stegosaurs with their bony plates and spikes, and more. Bandit does not believe that they truly die when disrupted, but rather they are reincarnated when they hatch from an egg at a later time. Whatever the case, this means that you can harvest their corpses for meat, leather, and other useful purposes. I half expected the items that had come from spirit bodies to dissipate when we returned to the physical plane with them, but they stuck around. This presents some interesting possibilities for trading goods with the Tandorans, should such an arrangement ever become possible.
