## Natural Venom

- **Requirem** ent: Must be a venomous species.

- **Cost** : The Karma cost of this quality equals the sum of the Vector, Speed, Power and Effect costs.

Typically only found in snake forms, your animal form has poison glands—usually located in your mouth. Choose one selection per category:

- **Vector:** Contact (Cost: 3), Exhaled (Cost: 3), Injected (Cost: 1), Spat (Cost: 5)

- **Speed:** 1 Turn (Cost: 3), Immediate (Cost: 5)

- **Power:** 2 per level, maximum 4 levels (Cost: 2 per level)

• **Effect:** Stun (Cost: 3), Physical (Cost: 5) The Effect always includes Dazed and Poisoned statuses, the latter at a level equal to the power. Whether it’s Stun or Physical damage is determined by the selected effect.

Injected requires a successful melee attack with Natural Weapons.

Contact requires touch attack (Close Combat [Unarmed]) or a Natural Weapons attack.

Spit requires Exotic Weapon (Spittle), Attack Ratings Agility/(Agility/2)\*/—/—/—.

\*Maximum range is 10m.
