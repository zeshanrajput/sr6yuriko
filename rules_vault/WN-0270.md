## Countering Extraction

If the PCs do not talk to a Tiding magpie before 11:11 p.m. on the second night they are assigned to watch DJ, the Tiding will attempt to extract DJ from the apartment. They arrive by way of a maintenance elevator while their Matrix assets provide digital cover. They will breach the apartment door, utilizing a combination Ram (Door) and Silence to keep a lower area profile. During the extraction attempt, the Matrix assets will attack any and all wireless devices the PCs have to assist the physical assets.

Should the extraction attempt fail, including being halted outright by the PCs, the Matrix assets will attempt to parley, but they obviously will be operating from a weaker negotiating point. If parley is not agreed to, Matrix assets will withdraw as if possessing the quality Fade to Black (p. 81, _Hack & Slash_ ), and they will notify NYPD, Inc., of a hostage situation (arrival in seven minutes).
