## Ram Plate

Ram plates come in a variety of shapes and sizes, and they must be attached to a standard hardpoint. When making a ramming attack (p. 201 _SR6_ ), add the vehicle’s Body to the AR of the attack and halve the DV of the return damage suffered for the collision.
