## Surge Dermal Alterations

The dermal alterations process started with orthoskin. That augmentation proved there was a market for those wishing to have such advantages, and the true dermal alteration market was born. Depending on the modification, patients will go through a series of injections, microdermabrasion, laser therapy, and chemical peels or chemical baths.

The following modifications can be added to anyone, but typically a metahuman can only have one. Due to the strengthening process the epidermal layer undergoes for orthoskin, a character’s orthoskin can add modifications equal to their orthoskin rating, but may never take the same type of dermal alteration more than once.
