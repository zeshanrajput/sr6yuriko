## AA-Rated Corporations

AAs want that third A more than anything in the universe. The Megacorporate Audit proved it’s possible to earn an invite to the most exclusive club on, or off, the planet. The Corporate Court decides who’s in or out, and since their dirtside home is here in New York City, you better believe that any corp with two As next to their name maintains offices in Manhattan with the specific goal of lobbying their interests to the Corporate Court. Of the complete list of AAs to survive the Megacorporate Revision, only six are officially headquartered in NY. They deserve a mention here.
