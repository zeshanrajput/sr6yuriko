## Close-Quarters Firearms (Handguns)

When using a light pistol, heavy pistol, holdout pistol, machine pistol, or taser in melee combat, that firearm may count as a Clubs weapon for the purposes of martial arts techniques. At the gamemaster’s discretion, striking an opponent with an un-reinforced weapon may damage or even break the weapon. **Category:** Ranged
