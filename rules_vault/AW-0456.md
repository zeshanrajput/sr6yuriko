## Kr’u’Ba

Treat Kr’u’Ba as Force 6 spirits of fire. Some are more powerful (up to Force 10), but these are rare. Note that each time a character is damaged by a fire-based attack within the Wastes of Miggon, it increases the threshold on their next test to resist acquiring a mutation by 1 (see p. 152).

When a Kr’u’Ba is disrupted, it leaves behind a glowing core that is very warm, but safe to touch. Kr’u’Ba hearts are highly sought-after trade goods within Miggon in their own right, as they are useful for powering generators and as fuel for vehicles. A skilled alchemist may attempt to harvest the True Fire within a Kr’u’Ba heart. This requires an Enchanting + Magic (Force, 1 hour) Extended test on a disrupted Kr’u’Ba. Success yields 1D6 drams of True Fire. A critical glitch causes an explosion equivalent to a high explosive grenade blast (with the unfortunate alchemist at ground zero).
