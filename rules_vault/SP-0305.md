## **Special Notes**

**If the player characters are already members of or have a challenge coin (p. 48,** _**Collapsing Now**_ **), skip this next part, except for the praise. If not, then Mr. Johnson gives all surviving player characters gold-bronze challenge coins; one side has what look to be dragon eyes, the other side is adorned with writing that is unrecognizable to any metahuman.**

**Mr. Johnson then says to the player characters, “You have done my allies, myself, and the world a great service today. These will act as markers should you need them. But be warned that there are those who would use the coin to mark you as enemies and kill you on sight. Use them wisely.”**
