## **All Others**

**Again, this is where Renraku’s position can be succinctly summed up: Those who oppose Dis are worthy of at least some honorable treatment. The rest are either cowards for not standing up against the Disians, or they’re collaborators who need to be put to death as swiftly as possible. Can’t get much simpler than that.**
