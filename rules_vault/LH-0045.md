## Aztechnology Threat Assessment

## Mitsu Misdeeds

## Updated \<04-13-83/0603:47>

Despite the indications of internal strife, a possible power play, and relatively defensive posture, the discovery of the Mobile Harvesters has raised Aztechnology’s threat level considerably. Not only must we contend with the threat of normal Harvesters, but we must also prioritize the location and neutralization of these Mobile Harvesters. Especially since they don’t just harvest mana, but the manner in which they do so could severely damage, if not destroy, local manaspheres. They are weapons of mass destruction that can also be employed in a final retaliation situation in case of the Final Project’s imminent failure.
