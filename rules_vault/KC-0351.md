## Dust (human, female, 22, brown hair, green eyes, average build)

A hot mess on wheels, Dust is in the shadows for the rush. She’s got no problem being the team’s getaway driver but prefers to be chased and occasionally pulls off hot dog moves just to draw a little attention. She’s not particularly happy that the team wants to do this quietly and she has to run surveillance at the start, but she’s still good at it and just uses the time to get a feel for the opposition.

She sends out drones and offers to move the team around but quickly discovers that only Silver and Sliver ever take her up on the offer. The other two always drive themselves around, as if they don’t trust the rest of the team.



When the trouble starts, she goes after whoever is available but won’t attack anyone who is unarmed. If she sees someone who is a skilled brawler, she may direct something non-lethal their way, but for the most part she just focuses on more threatening targets. She sends in both Roto-Drones from the Roadmaster and orders them remotely.
