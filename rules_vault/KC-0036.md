## The Blob

## Posted by: Kay St. Irregular

What is the value of the state in the megacorporate world? Does it have any role?

The joke answer is that _someone_ has to pick up the garbage. The reality is that the waste disposal industry is so commercial that even the Mafia incorporated its sanitation industry assets to maintain its share of the market alongside its more legitimate competition.

The real answer about the role of government is that corporations cannot exist without nations and states. The Corporate Court and national governments exist because the corps know that without them, things dissolve into chaos. It’s just not a good business environment where every dispute is solved by all parties competing to see who brings the most guns to the table. So yes, governments exist at the whim of the corps, but part of that whim is their desire to maintain some sort of society, and to keep their rivals from getting away with things that they themselves are not getting away with.
