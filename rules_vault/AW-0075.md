## Cupellation

The cardinal pole of fire and earth. Here the elemental Plane of Earth is cooked by high temperatures from the Plane of Fire, forging and cooling into metallic planes. Rolling hills of iron, copper, and other elements, freed of oxidizing agents, abound here. A strong magnetic spike juts from the landscape as a beacon to the great marketplace of Cupellation. The city is shaped like a giant gear ringed by a shiny wall. Each tooth is a gate that leads to a pathway of like-minded vendors and craftsmen.
