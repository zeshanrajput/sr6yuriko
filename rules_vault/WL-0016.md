## **Using Critters**

**Throughout the history of metahumanity, we’ve been using critters for a wide range of purposes, from food sources to transportation, hunting, tracking, and keeping an eye out for danger. That tradition continues to this day, because let’s face it—critters make great companions, and they can be very helpful.**
