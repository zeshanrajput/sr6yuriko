## Boats

## Samuvani CrisCraft Otter

This five-meter-long craft is pretty, but also boring as the day is long. She’s sold kitted for a variety of uses, including pleasure boating, fishing, light hauling, and utility work, but her open-hull design leaves only a small cabin to duck into in inclement weather. Best thing about her is that every body of water in the world has these things all over it.
