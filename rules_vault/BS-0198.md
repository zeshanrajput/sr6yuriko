## Joint Replacement

A simple surgery where connective tissues and bone are regrown and strengthened. Each replacement focuses on a single joint structure, such as a knee, a shoulder, or a wrist, and includes minor work with bone and ligament strengthening at the junctures. The finished, all-organic joint is returned to prime strength and is expected to last as long or longer than the original.

If a character has sustained an injury to their legs that created a permanent or long-lasting Hobbled status (p. 52, _SR6_ ), this procedure can remove the hobbled status.
