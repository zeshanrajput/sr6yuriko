## Renraku

When it comes to augmentation culture in Renraku, obvious ’ware is preferred, while hidden augmentations are classified as dishonorable. The critter exoframe design originated from Renraku, and they employ it with most of their augmented critter products. Their most popular product is a police dog that is trained and equipped to support security forces in dangerous situations by using its cover module to protect them while adding to their firepower with an attached turret mount.
