## 5. A weapon of last resort

Doesn’t matter but have something, _anything_ for that last-ditch effort when every other option (or weapon) has been exhausted. I’ve even seen grenades being used as such.

- Firearms are simply the most common weapon in combat. There are exceptions to every rule, but that’s for you to discuss with your team when planning.
