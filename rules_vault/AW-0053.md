## True Earth

True Earth, also known as longpi (dragon skin), can be found naturally in extreme depths of the earth metaplane. A few places include the deep mines the Drumlins abandoned eons ago near Roche, beneath the great sand seas of the Argillaceous lands to the east, or the muddy depths surrounding Dovv, though Captain Akrose would be the best guide for that.

- Rumors also persist that Gaoling geomancers can use their harmonics as some sort of sonar to find True Earth, though none have given up their secrets.

- Lyran

Longpi often look like shaped obsidian, though the color of longpi can take on the hue of surrounding material, making it harder to find. One exception to its properties is that it’s always warm to the touch, no matter the surrounding temperature.
