## Setting the Stage

Now that you know who you’re going after and why, it’s time to get things rolling. Before you even think about making contact with your mark, you need to set things up. A very long con is all about establishing a narrative making your mark believe in it—that way you can predict and guide their perceptions and behavior. Depending on your mark and objective this step will vary, but it generally involves staging events the mark might notice, such as creating props, forging media, and otherwise planting the seeds to make your con more believable.

You must spend at least one week working on this project, during which time you must raise the Long Con Pool quality by one level, up to a maximum of level 3. If you also spend at least 2,000 nuyen toward setting the stage for the con, you do not need to pay double the Karma cost to raise the Long Con Pool quality.
