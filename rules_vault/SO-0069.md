## Drill Sergeant (I)

You don’t always need someone who can think for themselves when the chips are on the table and time is limited. By spending a Major Action and succeeding at an Influence (Intimidate) + Charisma vs. Willpower + Logic Opposed test, the person you’re influencing gains the Follower status.
