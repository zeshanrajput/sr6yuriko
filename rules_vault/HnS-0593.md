## The secret war

Sadly for the runners, the NFA is not the real twist of the story here. The runners are about to become active targets in a secret war being waged for the control of Berlin’s old, cable-based Matrix infrastructure. Indeed, many hardware devices and networks, dating back from before the wireless Matrix, still exist and have been repurposed a lot by the inhabitants of these alternative districts.

But one actor in the shadows has been working for years to get its hands on as much of this infrastructure as possible. It’s a powerful AI from the ’50s that very much depends on this computing capability to survive and grow, and it’s called APEX. It has already secured this location, and the runners are about to unwittingly become the AI’s enemies.
