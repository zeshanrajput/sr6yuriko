## **Are there errata for weapons in** _**Lethal Harvest**_ **(p. 160-167) or are any banned?**

Note that none of these are available prior to September 2083. That means SRM 2083-16, or CMP 2083-13, or later. In addition, the use of anti-magic technology is not seen as favorable in New Orleans. The use of these chemicals and weapons result in the loss of 1 Reputation with all magical factions for each mission where it is used.

Krime Sprayer does not have a damage code. The damage is based on the chemical.

Shiawase Incinerator, Fire Bug, and Fire Starter include the price of “spare” containers for attacks. The

ammo column sets the number of shots it contains. The price is for the fuel or the item. If you want to buy a backpack and the fuel for the backpack, it costs 800 nuyen.

rEVOlution Arms Gorgon Vapor Gun and rEVOlution Arms aerosol grenade are not _Missions_ legal. FAB III and IV are not legal for _Missions_ .

Krime Arson rounds have a cost of x8.
