## Character creation recommendations

- **Attributes:** Agility 6, Charisma 5, Magic 5

- **Skills:** Close Combat 3, Con 5, Influence 4, Sorcery 4, Stealth 4

- **Knowledge skills:** Arcana, Arms Dealers, Safe Houses, Weapons

- **Spells:** Ice Spear, Physical Mask, Powerbolt, Silence

- **Positive qualities:** Aptitude (Close Combat, Con), Exceptional Attribute (Agility, Charisma),

- **Negative qualities:** Addiction (Jazz), Honorbound (Assassin’s Code), Social Stress

- **Life modules:** Academy Training, Astral Explorations, Bodyguard, Security Guard, Shadowrunner
