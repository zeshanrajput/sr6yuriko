## Anthro

## Aztechnology Crawler

If you don’t have a fear of insects/spiders before you see a Crawler, you will after. Designed for skittering up stairs, across floors, and through the underbrush, the Crawler is a half meter wide, multi-terrain drone with a hearty (and terrifying) design.
