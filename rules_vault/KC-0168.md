## Among the Law Firms

There are six firms associated with Okoro’s efforts, including her own firm (see p. 50). Each one has different data that could be incriminating to Okoro, information the corps might want in order to keep her quiet and get her to stop her shadow war against them. This information is included in each firm’s description in the **Okoro’s Vanguard** section (p. 50)
