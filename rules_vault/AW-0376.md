## Unique Qi Focus: Raptor Skull

One of the rare items of value that is produced by the Tandorans of Kyoko is the skull of a disrupted raptor spirit, bleached, carved with images and symbols, and ritually sanctified. This fits comfortably over the head of a Tandoran, like a helmet or a mask, but can be worn with some difficulty by other species. These items are a special qi focus that provides the adept who bonds it with an effect similar to the concealment power of an Awakened critter. The greater the Force, the more powerful the concealment effect is.
