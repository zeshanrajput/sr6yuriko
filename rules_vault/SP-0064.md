## **Wuxing**

## **Posted by: Mr. Bonds**

**As one of Dis’ premier corporate allies from the start of the current harvesting process, Wuxing fnds itself in the horns of a dilemma. They cannot abide anything that would harm our manasphere, which means they cannot assist any steps Dis might take in that direction. Their entire corporate strategy is based on feng shui and the appropriate use of ley lines, and if Dis does anything to interrupt the patterns their corporation is built on, the damage could be deep.**

**But they also can’t directly act against Dis because they still consider them an ally. They have a diffcult path to walk, and it will take all their strength to travel it. They obviously will use their abilities in mana manipulation, which seems like a ftting way to counter the manipulation Dis seems to be attempting. I intend to explore some of the ins and outs of how they will try to do this.**

**Note that my expertise is more on the corporate structure side of things than on the magic-use side, so I hope you will forgive me if my analysis gravitates to business.**
