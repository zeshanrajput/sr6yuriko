## Existing Qualities 

Drakes have all of their usual qualities (including metaracial ones) regardless of their form (a troll who has turned into a western drake still has dermal deposits, for instance, but they will showcase as particularly tough scales, while an elf who has turned into a feathered drake can still see with their low-light vision). Some qualities, especially social or physical, will need to be modified by common sense and circumstances by players and gamemasters, but by default all qualities remain (it is hard to have Blandness when you’re a fraggin’ dragon, miniature or not, and nobody in dracoform is Human-Looking or an Elf or Ork Poser, but a unique dracoform could still allow for Long Reach and have longer limbs than most drakes). 

_Example:_ A young (human) woman transforms into a drake for the first time. She still has to deal with her Bad Luck and Honorbound qualities, and she is also remains Catlike and nimble in her new form, and her Analytical Mind doesn’t go away, either, nor her Will To Live. Her gamemaster rules that she no longer grants Edge against new Influence opponents for being an Elf Poser, though, because nobody’s looking at the prosthetic ears on a drake (but NPCs who already know her and know she’s embarrassingly enamored of elves would still receive the benefit).
