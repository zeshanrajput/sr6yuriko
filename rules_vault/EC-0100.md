## The Blue River Society

This is a small group of environmental activists who want to clean up the local waterways. They’re named after their slogan, “Let’s turn the Green River blue,” pushing for environmental regulations and taxation for corporations to pay for the clean-up. They don’t want anything to do with any toxics, but that also cuts off some of the more passionate environmentalists who’d otherwise support their cause. Good kids, but they ain’t gonna win.
