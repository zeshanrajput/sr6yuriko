## Dash

**Cost:** 0.25 PP per level **Activation:** Minor Action

Fleet of foot is putting it mildly! When an adept makes a Sprint test (p. 44, _SR6_ ) and invests a Minor Action to focus themselves, they add +1 meter to their movement for every rank in Dash.
