## **Who’s Who**

**Mr. Johnson is a monad who has worked for Evo ever since their CFD infection turned into an amicable arrangement. They are loyal to Evo and are keenly aware of the Disian threat. And while they’re withholding key information about the cargo’s nature and its lethal guardians, they honestly wish for the players to succeed, and will warn them that the convoy will be well-defended by an elite security team.**

**The convoy’s defenders consist of an Awakened Faustian and four to six chimera. The vehicles are self-piloted and have all been upgraded with rating 6 Pilot systems. Each vehicle is running three autosofts at rating 6: Maneuvering, Evasion, and Targeting. The cargo vehicle has a separate Targeting autosoft for its missile launcher and will switch autosofts as needed—which means that only the machine gun or missile launcher will be fred within the same round. The vehicles are protected by the Faustian’s PAN, which is a rating 6 commlink with an M-TOC Mark III. This increases the threshold for Cracking tests against the network by +3 and increases the threshold for the players’ Stealth tests to set up an ambush to 4.**

**For the chimera, use a mix of metahuman-based ones and some of the more monstrous beasties from the “Rules of Engagement” chapter. It should be clear when the drek hits the fan that the players are facing some unique resistance.**
