## **Can all targets spend Edge on LOS(A) attacks like full auto, spells, or grenades?**

For spells and full auto, each person can spend Edge on the test. This also means that if there are three targets, they can each spend 1 Edge to have the caster or shooter to reroll successes.

Blast Attacks ( _SR6_ , p. 114) have no defense test and therefore Edge cannot be spent against the attack.
