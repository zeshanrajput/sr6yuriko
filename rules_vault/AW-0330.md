## Saeder-Krupp

The Saeder-Krupp embassy on the Spire is located in the beasts district and is large enough to comfortably house a great dragon or two. The only Saeder-Krupp employees that you are likely to encounter in the Place of Clouds are drakes, and they spend very little time outside their embassy. So far, Saeder-Krupp has not become involved in resource mining or piracy. As far as anyone can tell, they do not make use of airships. When an S-K drake needs to travel outside the Spire, they seem perfectly capable of flying under their own power—along with an escort of powerful spirits.
