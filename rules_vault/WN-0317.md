## Hotspot

**Bailey’s Pub** is a quiet little sub-basement dive bar just a few blocks off Central Park at Lexington. The entrance is located via a downward-angled neon arrow with a matching glittering ARO. A two-story flight of stairs leads down to a steel door that used to be the rear entrance of the bar before the quake. It’s now the front (and only regularly known) entrance. The bar has a thin layer of stage smoke present during operating hours (19:00 to 05:00). It’s centered around a three-sided wraparound bar surrounded by a series of high-top tables, with the walls lined in booths. The former windows (seven windows in total) have been neatly bricked up but both of the windows on the front wall hold hidden doors to a pair of private meeting rooms. One of those rooms, decorated entirely in clocks, has a hidden door built into an oversized grandfather clock in the bar.
