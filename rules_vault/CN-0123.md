## Public Knowledge

Dr. Thomas Shalbermat was a pioneer and world expert in treating and curing of people suffering from Artificially Induced Psychotropic Schizophrenia (AIPS)—most of whom were innocent victims, guilty of nothing more than being online when Crash 2.0 happened on November 2, 2064. His work led to various treatments and cures that have been successful worldwide, and are still used today. He did not, however, live to see his life’s work accomplished, as he was killed on June 30, 2070, by Subject ROM #138 at the Queen Elizabeth Hospital in Hong Kong as part of the Technomancer Riots of 2071. When the hospital was rebuilt as the Queen Elizabeth II Hospital, a physical and digital memorial was put in place honoring his many achievements in understanding and treating the disease. Despite that, nary an hour passes without his digital memorial being defaced (and then restored from the archives) by the heartfelt and healing MCT Matrix administrators. All donations for Dr. Shalbermat go toward the Shalbermat AIPS Treatment Fund for administering treatments to those who do not qualify for corporate or government medical benefits.
