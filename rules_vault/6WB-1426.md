## Rappelling gloves

These gloves are made of a special fabric that allows you to get a tighter grip on a grapple line, giving you a bonus Edge on all tests to hold your grip on the line. These gloves are necessary in order to use microwire or stealth rope (p. 280) without gruesomely slicing your hands apart as you slide down it.
