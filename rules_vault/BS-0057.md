## New and Revised Implant Grades

Cyberware and bioware have five grades listed in the core rulebook; one of those—the used grade—is redefined here as omega grade. A sixth augmentation grade—exoware, available only for cyberware—is also presented here as a new option. While not a grade available to PCs, gammaware is also mentioned. It remains in the realm of prototypes and special circumstances.

Exoware adds ten percent to the base Essence cost, and users suffer –4 Social rating if they have one or more exoware augmentation. However, the nuyen cost is multiplied by 0.8, and its rugged construction adds a bonus box to the user’s Physical Condition Monitor for every full point of Essence it costs (after modification), which stacks with the bonus box gained from full cyberlimbs. Modify any Build/ Repair threshold to repair exoware by –1. Exoware grade can only be applied to cyberware.
