## **Eyes Are Watching**

**If you start seeing the same sedan with tinted windows or a white van with no windows in your neighborhood, watch really carefully. If someone is watching your neighborhood, it could become trouble for you, even if they aren’t specifcally watching** _**you**_ **. If they—whoever it is—care enough to put a regular presence on your street, they probably also care enough to make sure that presence has backup. If you mess with them, you’ll have more people to worry about in short order. So basically, if your ’hood has become the sort of place to attract attention, it might be time to move on.**
