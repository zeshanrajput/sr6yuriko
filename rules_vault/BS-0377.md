## Fugu

- **Vector:** Ingestion

- **Speed:** 30 minutes

- **Duration:** (10 – Body) hours, minimum 1 hour

- **Power:** 11

- **Effect:** Physical damage, Nauseated

This poison is derived from the pufferfish. It is a tetrodotoxin, a potent neurotoxin that inhibits neurons from firing, preventing the nervous system from carrying messages to the muscles, causing respiratory failure. Victims of fugu poisoning must resist (Power)P damage and receive the Nauseated status.
