## Wrist Shield

A wrist shield is mounted in the user’s forearm and hidden until needed. When activated, spring action propels a spiral of plates to form a rigid disc one meter wide, interlocking into a usable shield in a fraction of a second. While the wrist shield is deployed, the user gains +4 Defense Rating. The outer rim of each panel is edged, allowing the shield to tear through ordinary clothing as it deploys, but armor prevents the shield from opening unless the forearm is uncovered. The edges of a deployed wrist shield can be employed as a slashing weapon, and of course the shield can be used to bludgeon with as well. A deployed wrist shield still leaves the hand free to perform tasks. The shield can be deployed anytime as a Minor Action, but it requires a Major Action to crank it back into the forearm. Only one wrist shield can be installed per arm, and a user’s Defense Rating can never benefit from more than two shields at the same time.
