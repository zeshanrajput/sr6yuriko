## Genesis Consortium

The biotech producer, and once-challenger to Aztechnology, was revised downward to a single A by the Corporate Court. The revision cited the corporation’s lack of diversification as a key reason they did not deserve the second A. Since then, the Consortium has focused on expanding their operations worldwide, dipping into interests ranging from eco-friendly food production to recycled cybernetics. Their presence in New York is another way the Consortium is trying to remindthe Corporate Court they exist. The corporation is spending money in the lower-income sections of the city, investing alongside a number of A-rated corps looking to make the jump.
