**Skills: Astral 5, Athletics 5 (Running +2), Close Combat 6, Outdoors 6, Perception 5, Stealth 4**

**This odd status leaves them with a less-thanpleasant disposition. They’re still rabbits but tend to bite and threaten more often, and small groups of them have been known to bring down slightly larger Awakened animals. Including at least one attempt on a gnome’s life. They are still primarily herbivores and often focus and feed on Awakened plants. It was quite interesting to watch the damper bunnies go out with groups of their mundane kin. The dampers would neutralize the powers of the plant species, and the standard bunnies would eat them. The reverse has not appeared true with mundane bunnies partaking in their Awakened kin’s carnivorous habits, but I cannot see what occurs within the warrens. I have done fecal analysis all around the area and found no sign of the mundane bunnies becoming meat-eaters.**
