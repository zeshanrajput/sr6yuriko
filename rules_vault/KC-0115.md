## Earthguard Law

While Okoro dips her toes in the shadows to get things done, Earthguard dives in headfirst. The lawyers here are constantly busy defending and helping ecological avengers who may or may not have made their initial connections with each other through Earthguard.

This firm operates out of the home of its primary partner, Solden Holmes. Holmes’ home, a converted warehouse on the lagoon, complete with a boat dock, is in Lagos Mainland. He has a revolving door of ecological avengers coming through looking for someone to defend them in court.
