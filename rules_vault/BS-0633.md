## Emotion Leak

Sometimes those with large amounts of cyberware have a difficult time containing their emotions; other metahumans know instinctively how these characters feel. This is disadvantageous during negotiations, cons, gambling, and any other face-to-face interactions where deception is important.

- **Bonus:** 10 Karma

- **Game Effect:** The runner cannot use Edge on Con tests or Influence tests.
