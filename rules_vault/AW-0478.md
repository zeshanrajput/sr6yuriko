## Astral and Metaplanar Portals

This category covers a wide variety of objects or locations with a single common feature: They all

open passageways that function in the same way as astral or metaplanar gateways. Exactly what a given object is, and the rules it operates under, are almost as varied as the number of known portals. The most common type of portal are devices created by teams of enchanters, often as part of a megacorporate research project. Others are ancient artifacts unearthed at archaeological sites, maybe linked to Atlantis or some other legendary Fourth World culture. Sometimes the site itself is the portal—the Nazca Geoglyphs being the best-known example of this. Portals may also take the form of an enchanted feature of the natural world, such as a cave opening, a pool of water, or a rock formation. Lastly, there are the devices with more mysterious origins, rumored to be carried here from other metaplanes by entities who have their own agendas and motivations for bringing them to the Sixth World.

The rules for activation of a portal, and where it leads, are up to the gamemaster to decide on a case-by-case basis. The most basic astral portal— usually called an arcane gateway—is used with an Astral Gateway ritual. Other portals, such as those created with advanced enchanting techniques or those with metaplanar origins, may be activated with a simple command or a special spell created to trigger them.
