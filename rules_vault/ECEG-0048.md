## shadows in focus

## **Strictures**

**prospective members happens away from the host they use as their home base.**

**==> picture [137 x 12] intentionally omitted \<==**

- **Fraternity:** Support fellow members on their journey to deep magical understanding.

They are considered a mixed-user group, and their current membership numbers are somewhere around 120 associates. Their main hangout is the Mississippi Renovation Society host (rating 9 Foundation host). Outsider access to the host would make you believe that it’s a nonprofi t organization dedicated to cleaning up the Mississippi River, but this is just a front. Accessing the host with user or admin status shows an ultra-real clubhouse that the River People own and manage. The leadership of the organization consists of eight individuals and rotates every three to four years. An Emergent intelligence, who goes by Indomitable Will, is part of the core team and the one who does most of the reaching out to invite new members to join or hire outside resources.

- **Secrecy:** Strictly maintain the secrecy of the organization, inviting only the most promising members.

- **Arcana:** The study of magic and its nature is the priority of members. Note: Members are required to have four magic-related Knowledge skills.
