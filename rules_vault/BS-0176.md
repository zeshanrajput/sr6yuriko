## Brown Biotech

This field helps to improve the cultivation and management of deserts and arid lands. It started with bioengineering high-quality, disease-free seeds that can resist the high heat and other extreme environmental issues these areas endure. This includes seeds that require less water and additives to the soil that increase water and mineral retention.
