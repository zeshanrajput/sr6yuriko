## BEAST SUMMONER

**>** I am often asked why I have chosen the limited path I walk, and I simply respond, what are my limitations?

No spells—who needs the drain and strain when I can ask companions to lend the same aid?

No artificing—why make goop to power others when I can summon aid to fight by their side?

I don’t walk a limited path, I walk a limitless path. On my path are allies in an abundance no other runner on the street can count on. When I’m on the run and need to doss down in an unfamiliar flop, I simply pay my respects and ask the spirit of the structure to cover my six.

Get into a scuffle in a bar because I don’t hide my ears or my tribal tattoos—wrong day for those Humanis slags, because I always travel with unseen protection.

You lose your guns at the door, street sammie—but I don’t. My guns get pulled through the astral, and it’s a rare street spot that has the nuyen to keep up that kind of warding.

You see, I’m not limited. I’ve got strengths in abundance, and I can focus my attention at being the best and brightest. While my spellslinging counterparts are out hunting down their next

formula, I’m offering my spirit allies fine, mana-infused teas and making friends so I don’t need to force the manasphere to work for me, because I have allies to work beside me. **>** Irwin
