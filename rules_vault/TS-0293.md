## **Matrix Emergency Help Specialist**

**Uses: case information, local rumors, local politics Places to Meet: dispatch center, park, parking lots Similar Contacts : beat cop, police aid, snitch**

**A Matrix Emergency Help (MEH) specialist is the Sixth World version of the dispatcher (and is often called that) of the previous century, with many of the basic skills being handled by AI or Matrix agents. When a call is so unique that a live metahuman is needed, the call gets sent to a MEH agent who serves as the communications link between the public and law enforcement service provider, playing a pivotal role in emergency response systems. Their responsibilities extend beyond answering calls and require prompt coordination between multiple organizations. Additionally, a dispatcher’s ability to remain calm under pressure, gather crucial information, and make quick decisions directly affects the usefulness to emergency services.**

**Establishing a connection with a MEH operator can be valuable for individuals or organizations seeking a direct line to emergency services, as these professionals are instrumental in initiating swift and organized responses to incidents, ultimately contributing to client and customer safety and well-coordinated law enforcement efforts.**
