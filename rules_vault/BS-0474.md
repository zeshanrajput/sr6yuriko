## Heuristic Processor

This headware augments a critterjack’s direct neural interface and allows critters to creatively adapt their instincts and training to react to unexpected events.

**Game Information:** A critter must have the critterjack augmentation as a prerequisite for a heuristic processor. The critter receives an Edge attribute equal to the rating of the heuristic processor. The critter may only gain or spend Edge on tests that relate to their training and commands.
