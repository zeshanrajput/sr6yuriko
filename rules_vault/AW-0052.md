## Spiritual Guides and Characters

## Captain Akrose

Captain Akrose is very animated and quick compared to other earth gnomes. He wears a great purple tricorn hat and long black beard woven with small beads and stones. Stories are that he was bitten by a bole, and instead of dying, he bit the bole back. Akrose captains an iron freighter called the _Trilobite_ . It’s one of a handful of cargo ships that sail the sandy seas. It’s a bit of a throwback compared to the cargo trucks that speed down the Nazca slipstreams, but he’s also taken the _Trilobite_ out into the Astral Sea. He can tell you tales of his travels from Arie to Ogrothan. He’s even seen the golden dome palace of Thalarion, rising from the horizon like a second sun. He is always interested in helping others in their travels. His motives are less about seeking treasures and more about the journey. As they say, a rolling stone gathers no moss.
