## Higher Expectations

You hold yourself to higher standards, and so does Eagle and the spirits whose assistance you depend on.

- **Bonus:** 10 Karma

- **Game Effect:** You may take this quality at the same time as you take either Fresh Air Spirit or Breath of Binding, in which case the bonus Karma covers the Karma cost of the positive quality. Whenever you conjure or bind a spirit, you must offer it a dram of reagents or a point of your own Karma as a gesture of respect and remuneration. This must be in addition to any reagents spent for any other reason. If you choose to offer a point of Karma instead of a reagent and successfully conjure or bind a spirit, that spirit will grant you a single additional service. If you ever have a negative reputation among spirits, you may not conjure or bind any spirits until your reputation is restored.
