## Doldrum

The Doldrum is to them what a dense, scary forest is to us. The elementals say that the Doldrum is a graveyard of lost souls, as great simoons use it as a hunting ground. To the first- (often last-) time visitors, the Doldrum is a vast, dense cloud. It reeks of sulfur and ammonia. There is no breeze to clear the air, as it were. The Doldrum is more tangible, with crooked tree structures feeling like wet gauze interspersed within clouds that partially obscure any illumination. There are bursts of light and slow sizzles as volatile gases mix and collide with every disruptive step a traveler makes through the place. Even without the disorientation of trying to navigate, there are rumors that the Doldrum itself is one great organism that will move to secure its snack in despair. If you are lucky or important enough, outsiders who know of this plane’s hazards might dispatch a party of borea trackers to get you out of the Doldrum.
