## **How do I strengthen Loyalty?**

Simply spend as many favor points as the next level of Loyalty. (NOTE: You can immediately raise the Loyalty of a Contact that you gained favor points for during your most recent Mission without spending a Downtime Activity, as long as you have the requisite Favor Points (to include the one you just earned). You can buy up to maximum 9 Loyalty this way.
