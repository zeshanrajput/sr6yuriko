## **2 Edge: Assembled with Love and Bondo**

Using Juryrigging on adding a modification to a vehicle or drone allows for the modification to last for (net hits) hours with a successful test. Once the repaired object stops functioning, it cannot be repaired, only rebuilt from scratch. Only 1 modification can be juryrigged to a vehicle at a time.
