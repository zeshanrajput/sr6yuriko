## Megacorps in the Place of Clouds

## Ares

Ares offers mercenary security services to metaplanar travelers, accepting both local currency as well as cold, hard nuyen. Their embassy is located near the Spire’s docks. You can purchase weapons and ammunition there, but expect to pay incredibly high prices—as much as ten times the listed retail price. Hey, at least you can buy guns on a metaplane!
