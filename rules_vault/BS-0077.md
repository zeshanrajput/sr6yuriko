## Plasteel Shirt

Many traditional martial arts have techniques to toughen the body against strikes. Plasteel shirt is an update to these ancient secrets, employing cybernetics’ components as a protective shell and conduit to harmlessly redirect impact energies. When rolling Body to resist damage from attacks or indirect combat spells, gain +2 dice. **Category:** General, user must have a cybertorso or at least 2.0 Essence spent on cyberlimbs and bodyware cyberware.
