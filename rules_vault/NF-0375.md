## Teen Diva

You’ve practiced music, singing, and dancing since you were old enough to crawl, and it paid off. You got noticed by the right people, and now you’re a teenage rock star! Thousands upon thousands of fans want your autograph, your attention, your body, and whatever other piece of you they can get. I just hope the dream is really all it’s cracked up to be. You can trust your manager and the corporation that holds your contract—right?

**Attributes:** Agility +1, Charisma +1
