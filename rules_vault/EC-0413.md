## Top Hat

Top Hat is one of the Yakuza’s hot-drek deckers. Born in Neo-Tokyo, Top Hat is seen by many as an apex predator of the Matrix, or at least within Puyallup. The Reality Hackers do not attempt to engage Top Hat, unless they don’t care for their deck getting bricked. With such skills, some wonder why Top Hat hasn’t gone corporate.

- While the Top Hat symbology implies wealth and success, it is just iconography that masks Top Hat’s true self. There are still social prejudices that prevent Top Hat from being successful in Imperial Japan. It’s sad that they like the persona, not the person.

- Netcat
