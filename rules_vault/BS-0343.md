## New Positive and Negative Qualities

## Positive Qualities

**I’m Into It** : There are certain types of alterations that you find fascinating, and it shows up in interactions with those individuals.

- **Cost:** 2 Karma

- **Game effect:** Pick a particular transgenic alteration type. You receive +1 Edge for social tests during your meetings with that group of people.

**In a Previous Life, I Was a Unicorn:** Transgenic treatments haven’t changed you, they returned you to what you truly are, or were.

- **Cost:** 10 Karma

- **Game effect:** When this quality is selected, choose an animal with which you identify. For all transgenic treatments, characters only take half Essence loss as long as the transgenic process selected is related to the same animal type (e.g., tiger claws, tiger fur, cat-like reflexes).

**Chimeric Soul:** When you look down the path of evolutionary history, you see all the creatures that you were and could have been.

- **Cost:** 15 Karma

- **Game effect:** Characters who have chimeric souls are allowed to get one additional infusion from one other animal outside their initial choice (for example, leonine body with serpent venom sacs), raising the maximum number of possible infusions to six. Chimeric Soul cannot be taken in conjunction with Misaligned Souls.
