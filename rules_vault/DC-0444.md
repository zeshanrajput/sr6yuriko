## Pop-Out Concealment

This allows a weapon mount to be stored out of sight under the skin of a vehicle. These weapon mounts and their attached weapons can be deployed on command, requiring a Minor Action, then retracted again with another command, also a Minor Action. Pop-out concealment is much harder to detect than blow-away panels, and characters need a Perception (4) test to suspect something is under the panel. They are also completely re-usable, but the mechanisms are cumbersome and expensive, with the pop-out concealment systems requiring the same number of mod slots as the matching hardpoint.
