## **The Clayton Years**

Wilson was the founder of Lone Star, after all, and brought with him several experts, a corporate shark’s hunger, and the raging desire to beat his brother at the police game. You can see his touch in the revamped uniforms (the trench coats were all Wilson, the neon his engineers), the change toward more cars and fewer Citymasters, and the revamped training programs. Oh, KE was still about peace through frepower, but Wilson is a businessman frst and a Texan second. **Clayton took the most gung-ho offcers and tossed them into Hard Corps or Wolverine (lower-end Ares subsidiaries) and pushed better police training into the mix. Oh, they were still aggressively militarized, but offcers were taken out of seven-man fre teams in Citymasters and restructured into pairs driving patrol cars. The mil-spec armor and heavy weapons were held back just for response teams while offcers saw their gear replaced by more standard police loadouts. Those who were good soldiers, but unhappy with the situation, were moved to the now-expanding Firewatch Division, with Wilson giving control over them completely to Knight. In effect, he agreed to take on the “boring” police job while leaving Knight his soldiers.**
