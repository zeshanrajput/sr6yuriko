## **Cyberdocs**

**The four cybernetic specialists working in the cybermancy facility are some of the best Evo has, but they are not trained for combat. When threatened, they’ll surrender and beg the players not to harm them. If necessary, use Street Doc (p. 214,** _**SR6**_ **), but increase their Logic to 6 and Biotech to 9.**
