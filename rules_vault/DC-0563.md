## Driving Style: Stunt Driver

Starting with the legendary Evel Knievel, you idolize many of the vehicle stunts performed on the trid and no one is going to tell you that it’s all AROs and illusions.

- **Cost:** 9 Karma

- **Game Effect:** You only pay half the required Edge cost for the following uses of Edge:

  - Bootleg Turn (1 Karma)

  - Tokyo Drift (2 Karma)

  - Pickup (4 Karma)

  - The Exit (6 Karma)
