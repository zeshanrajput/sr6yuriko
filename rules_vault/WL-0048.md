## **Hellcow**

**Skills: Athletics 4, Close Combat 4, Outdoors 4, Perception 5, Stealth 3 Powers: Dual Natured, Engulf (air, earth), Enhanced Senses (vision enhancement, vision magnifcation), Infuence, Natural Weapon (beak, talons) Weakness: Fragile (2)**

**The frst hellcow (** _**Bos malus**_ **) was discovered in 2061. The frst ones discovered were tested and found to have mad cow disease. What was more surprising was that the animals born of those frst ones also shared the same traits, leading to it being classifed as a separate species.**

**Weapons:**

**Beak [Close Combat, DV 2P, 6/—/—/—/—] Talons [Close Combat, DV 2P, 7/—/—/—/—]**

**You can easily tell that the hellcow is related to the cow. The basic body shape and most of the head resemble the family it belongs to, but there are a couple of differences. The frst is that the cow is covered in red and brown scales. Its head is dominated by a large mouth with conical teeth and an elongated tongue. Their hooves end in curved claws.**
