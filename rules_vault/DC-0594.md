## Positional Advantage

The term “positional advantage” describes a variety of circumstances where one participant in a chase has placed themselves in better position than another. In practice, this can mean a variety of things—maybe having positional advantage means having higher ground, or better sight lines, or even in a less obstructed lane of traffic. The gamemaster can decide how best to represent positional advantage.
