## Bolt Hole

For 4 LP, you have a well-hidden and secure bolt hole located somewhere within ten kilometers of your residence. A bolt hole isn’t a comfortable living space and offers very few creature comforts beyond a place to sleep, a mini-fridge or cooler, and a microwave for heating up your stuffers. You may not gain or spend Edge on natural recovery tests while you are living in a bolt hole.
