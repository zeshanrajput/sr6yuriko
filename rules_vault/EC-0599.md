## Rickshaw Dan

There’s a saying in Seattle: Everybody knows Rickshaw Dan … _everybody_ . Said to have gotten his start with a rickshaw, this strong-jawed Irishman clad all in black is an icon among both the grey- and black-market set, legendary for his smallscale smuggling and incredible networking capability. It’s been said he drove the first Black Cab, that he’s got connections with everyone worth knowing in the shadows, and that anyone who meets his premium fees can see anything or anyone transported across the ’plex with total safety and speed.
