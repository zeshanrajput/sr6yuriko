## Attribute Codemods Attribute Augmentation

This codemod permanently increases the effective value of one of the AI’s basic attributes by the rating of the codemod. The ASDF attributes are much easier to increase than Willpower, Logic, Intuition, or Charisma, and this makes the ASDF attribute augmentations both less expensive and less invasive than the remainder of the AI’s atattribute augmentations. ASDF increases from an atattribute augmentation codemod do not stack with ASDF increases from Resonance or Echoes.
