## All Eyes On Me

Spinning into the spotlight is the latest surprise player: Ares! When Leónization Type II was introduced, Damien Knight swooped in and bought Type I at a steal. When Type II collapsed a decade later, Ares stood tall as the lone holder of the rights and quickly licensed it out, knowing full well that desperate billionaires facing terminal lifespans would have stopped at nothing to wrest copies of it away. That massive windfall continues to pay dividends, and the absorbing of Cross Biomed into the greater Ares whole pushed them higher in biotech. Will new CEO Michael Bishop keep the beat or stumble?
