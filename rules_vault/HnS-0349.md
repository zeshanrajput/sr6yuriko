## Clear

These constructs operate in packs and only exist to erase information. They will not engage in combat, and if attacked, they will attempt to flee, returning to their assigned job as soon as it’s safe to do so. If you manage to spot one, they appear as a colorless, translucent version of the other basic constructs. In game terms, Clears use Probe and Backdoor Entry to gain access to devices. Once inside a device or a host, they run Edit File, with Crack File if necessary, until all the data is erased (or they are destroyed). They always run silently and execute Hide actions when spotted or attacked.
