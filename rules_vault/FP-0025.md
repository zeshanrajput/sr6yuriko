## **Bases and Hideouts**

**Dadeson: Where are you headquartered? Sarkenbleck: We aren’t some policlub. We don’t have a clubhouse or a secret lair, you imbecile. You do not keep yourself away from prying eyes by having a place they can look for you.**

**Dadeson: That’s fragging useless. Where do you gather??**

**Sarkenbleck: Conventions, dinners, backrooms, offces, penthouses, parks, clubs …**

**Dadeson: Shut up. New question: How do you spread the word of these gatherings?**

**Sarkenbleck: Word of mouth, mainly, but we also have a code the chosen know. When there is an event or a desire for a gathering, an advertisement will make mention of a “fair world,” “level playing feld,” “true future,” or “a place for all” and provide a contact. These phrases let members know we are getting together at an event. From there, individual meetings are arranged in person or via message on our private network.**

**Dadeson: What is this private network? You claim to detest the Matrix.**

**Sarkenbleck: We detest the depravity and idiocy of the Matrix. The misinformation and destruction of civil discourse caused by anonymity is the problem. The abominations who use and abuse the tool to feed their ego and desire for chaos.**

**We have an ordered network. A small series of hosts implanted across several other systems which we access with Pocket Secretary’s Prime device. The hosts reboot on a random cycle frequently enough to dump any intruders before they can access or intercept anything important. Calls are twinned between hosts and roll over to a new twin whenever a host is about to reboot. None of our people use it as the weak-minded use the Matrix. It’s part of our protection and quite an ingenious system of defense, if I do say so myself.**

**Dadeson: And that’s your only defense? Anonymity?**

**Sarkenbleck: Of course not. We’re not fools. We know the power arrayed against us is supernatural and thus we need a protection greater than secrecy. Our homes are protected by natural barriers their astral forms cannot cross or spy upon and we have removed the ever-present spying devices and their connection to the Matrix. Our weapons are devoid of these abusive accesses and skill-devoid cheats.**

**We have learned that by making our tools, devices, and weapons with certain techniques and specifc materials, we can create pieces that mana struggles to touch. It is another sign that magic is not meant for our world.**
