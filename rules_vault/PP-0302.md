## Horizon

The youngest megacorp just rubs the oldest the wrong way. Horizon’s entire business style clashes terribly with the traditional Shiawase style, and having been beaten to the punch on the River Wind agricultural industry, Shiawase’s made a note that they need to take the upstart down a peg and let the puppy know whose house it’s playing in.
