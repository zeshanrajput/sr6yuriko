## Cal Free State (CFS)

To be blunt, Scattershot and company made a lot of noise in Shasta, which got a lot of attention. Now magic-users are flocking to the area to see for themselves, and the CFS government has been hiring like crazy because Sacramento just can’t get over “those damn elves.”
