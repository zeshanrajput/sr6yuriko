## Precise Throw

**Cost:** 0.25 PP per level **Activation:** Passive

The adept can throw objects further and with greater accuracy and precision than they realistically should. For every level of this power, increase their AR by 1. On thrown weapons with scatter, add the number of levels as net hits for determining scatter reduction.
