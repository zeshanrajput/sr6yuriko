## **Are Loyalty and the Law, and Blackmail legal in** _**Missions**_ **?**

Yes.

## **The gear and weapons listed in Tarnished star mention that these are restricted to law enforcement. What is the game effect of carrying and using it in** _**Missions**_ **?**

Any use of gear from _Tarnished Star_ increases the Heat modifier by +1. This effect is cumulative. This does not apply to vehicles with an availability of 6 or less.
