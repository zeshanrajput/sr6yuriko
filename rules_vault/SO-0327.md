## Game Information

Micro stealth tags have a rating that determines their size and subtlety both in the Matrix and in physical space. The default threshold to notice a micro stealth tag with a Perception test or a Matrix Perception test is its rating + 3. Attempts to plant a micro stealth tag using Stealth (Palming) gain a point of Edge that must be used on the test or is lost, and the threshold to notice the palming attempt is increased by the micro stealth tag’s rating.

These tags have a device rating of 1 and a limited capacity of 1. If a sensor is included, the tag must be purchased with the sensor installed. These devices are too small to modify after production. Any amount of Matrix noise disrupts their wireless connection, though they’ll continue to collect and store any corrupted data.

If no sensor is installed, the only information the tag records and transmits is its location. This makes them very useful both for actively tracking someone or for seeing where the tag went after the fact.
