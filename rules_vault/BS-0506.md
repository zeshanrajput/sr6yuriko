## Aqua Jets

Normally applied to aquatic critters, this exoframe accessory provides a powerful aquatic propulsion system.

**Game Information:** A critter must be equipped with a critterjack and receive special training in order to use this accessory. Aqua jets provide a critter with a water-based movement speed, with a Handling of 3, Acceleration 10, Speed Interval 20, and Top Speed 100. The aqua jets can be used on the surface, much like a speedboat or jet ski, but also work underwater. They do not provide the ability to breathe underwater, however. Its Pilot rating is equal to the rating of the aqua jets accessory, is hardcoded with a maneuvering autosoft of the same rating, and is incapable of running any other software. For all Piloting tests, use Pilot x 2 for the dice pool—no Edge may be used to influence this test, even if the critter has a heuristic processor. Using the aqua jets requires a Minor Action from the critter each round, which counts as its movement action.

The aqua jets accessory provides enough power to function for up to 30 minutes before needing a recharge. Additional battery arrays take up 1 capacity and provide 30 additional minutes of propulsion time each.
