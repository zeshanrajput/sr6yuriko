## The Great Jungle

Covering roughly one quarter of the Island of Tandora is a thick jungle. The trees of Tandora’s jungle are not as tall or thick as those of its forest, but they are much more numerous. The ground cover is also incredibly thick, which makes overland journeys exhaustive and tedious. The largest of Tandora’s beast spirits are too bulky to venture far into the jungle, but it’s perfect for the smaller bipedal predators I call raptors. Tandorans mostly stick to the South River that winds its way through the jungle. Far from the island’s center, the South River reaches a ravine of epic proportions, and there it becomes a waterfall on the scale of Niagara Falls.
