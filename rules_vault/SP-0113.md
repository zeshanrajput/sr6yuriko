## **Suspicious Activity Alert 06-14-82/1423:04 Zulu**

**We have a report of unusual activity near the Volga River inside either the Ulyanovsk or Samara Oblast. The target area is approximately 900 kilometers southeast of Moscow, agrarian with a low population density. Reported activity is that heavy construction equipment and building material is being quietly transported into the area by an unknown organization. There is no Matrix activity that would indicate the construction of a corporate black site. Additionally, the area is known by researchers to have abnormally high levels of ambient mana, so the probability of Disian involvement is high.**
