## Enthralling Performance Example

Johnny Banger and his chummers have been hired for a smash-and-grab at an MCT zero zone, which is going to require one hell of a distraction. With the help of some contacts to get the word out, Johnny arranges a flash mob of music fans to show up just down the block from the MCT facility. As the rest of the runners sneak into position, Johnny cranks up the volume loud enough for the MCT guards to hear and starts playing his guitar, spending 4 Edge and taking the Enthralling Performance action. The GM rules that this doesn’t count as an appropriate venue, but due to the legwork Johnny’s team put into setting up the flash mob it doesn’t count as being particularly out of place. Johnny aces his Con (Performance) test (natch), scoring six hits. Any guard who gets less than six hits on their Composure test gets the Misdirected status. Johnny doesn’t have a permit for this performance, however, so the runners had better move quickly to take advantage of the circumstances.
