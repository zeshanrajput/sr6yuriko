## Basic Drake Powers 

- **Armor:** per p. 221, _SR6,_ gain Armor equal to your Edge attribute. 

- **Dual Natured:** per p. 223, _SR6_ . 

- **Elemental Attack (Fire):** per p. 223, _SR6_ . 

- **Natural Weapon:** per p. 226, _SR6,_ close combat, (Str/2+1)P damage, AR (Str+Rea/—/—/—), reflecting an assortment of claws, fangs, horns, and tail swipes, varying from drake to drake. 

- **Underwater Adaptation:** You can breathe underwater (fresh and salt) as easily as on land. Increase base swimming speed by 1 meter/combat round. Do not divide your hits on Athletics tests in half when increasing swimming speed. Do not increase your swimming threshold by 1 for every fifteen minutes. Pay 1 less for Athletics test Edge Boosts related to swimming. Add your dracogenesis rank to your dice pool for all swimming tests.
