## Information

## and the Future

Multiple groups covered in this book—namely Convergence, the Echoes, and the Tiding—are interested in gathering information for various purposes. Often, those purposes are about getting ahead of future events by trying to anticipate or



**predict them. This can create a dilemma for a gamemaster who wants to involve these entities in their game: How can they provide information about the future when they don’t know how the future of the Sixth World will unfold?**

**The key here is to provide predictions that focus on personal levels. This is the area where gamemasters have the most control—the fate of PCs and NPCs. Information about their future will be of interest to the PCs, and while groups like the Convergence may be focused on averting the global disaster they believe to be looming, they’ll still listen to information that affects them individually. So while a Convergence diviner might be interested in learning about, say, who wrote the Kechibi code, they’ll pay attention to a vision that shows one of their members running in panic from Lone Star, or one that shows them relaxing in some luxury penthouse. Those kinds of personal visions can provide a path for runners to follow as more worldwide events of the Sixth World unfold.**
