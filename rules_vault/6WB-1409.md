## Maglock passkey

This maglock “skeleton key” can be inserted into any cardreader’s maglock, cleverly fooling it into believing that a legitimate passkey has been swiped. See **Maglocks** , p. 241.

**Wireless bonus:** The passkey receives a +1 bonus to its rating.
