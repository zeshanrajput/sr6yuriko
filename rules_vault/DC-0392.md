## GridLink

When a standard electric powered vehicle is connected to the GridGuide system, the operational time is effectively infinite. GridLink is a standard feature on all modern vehicles that use GridGuide. In the event that a vehicle does not have a factory GridLink unit, one can be easily installed with an aftermarket kit.
