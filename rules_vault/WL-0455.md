**Radiation is an insidious form of damage. A creature or person who is irradiated may not even realize that they are suffering from damage caused by radiation. This makes it different in that the damage done is measured in days instead of combat rounds.**

**A toxic critter uses the Taint power to pollute the manasphere. Use the rules for standard Cleansing (p. 117,** _**Street Wyrd**_ **), but consider any hits the toxic critter gets as increasing a mana fow (toxic) by 1 for every two net hits. The range of this effect is a two-meter radius.**

**The status is noted with a number that indicates the amount of Physical damage the character must resist at the beginning of each day due to the status. Any time the character is unable to resist all this damage, they also gain the Nauseated status. For attacks that don’t specify what the status number should be, then the Irradiated number is equal to the Magic of the creature. If the victim suffers a critical glitch on the resistance test, reduce their maximum Body by 1. A normal glitch increases the damage taken by 1.**
