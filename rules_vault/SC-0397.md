## Plot Hooks & adventure seeds Missing a Member

A runner team is ready to start their new job. They’ve received some advance pay, they have the details of the job, and they’re armed and armored up. One problem: their decker is missing. Gone without a trace. They call up a private investigator to help them find the decker as soon as possible.

The private investigator looks into the decker’s recent activities and discovers a flurry of contacts with a nightclub in their town. Looking into the club shows that the owner was a friend of the decker and had been contacting them about defending them from repeated hacking attempts. In fact, the club owner claims the decker was traveling to meet them when they disappeared. The club owner was being blackmailed for overlooking human trafficking going on in the club, and they wanted the decker to find out who was doing theblackmailing. But now the decker is gone—and the private investigator has a lot to look into.
