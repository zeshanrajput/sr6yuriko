## Thermal Dissipation

## (Rating 1-2)

Special fibers combined with new breathable materials allow the user to fight or stave off the effects of heat in hot and/or humid climates such as the desert and jungle. While it doesn’t help against fire damage, it’s handy when you’re spending long periods in the field.

Each rank of thermal dissipation eliminates one level of being fatigued due to overheating or grants +1 resistance versus heat damage.
