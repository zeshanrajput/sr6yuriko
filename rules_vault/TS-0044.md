## **Cops and Robbers**

**From the point of view of a go-ganger, few cops represent an actual challenge, opportunity, or even a rite of passage. Too many offcers in the area simply means moving on and joyriding somewhere else.**

**For runners, security and police are obstacles to bypass or overcome, though some of the cannier crews have been known to fnd ways of utilizing them for their own beneft. The right alarm at the right time can open a surprising number of doors. But the danger remains in all circumstances. Underestimating even a solitary beat cop has put more criminals in the ground than backfred rounds or black ICE, and how you deal with the warm bodies on the force not only separates the amateurs from the pros, but the living from the dead.**

**No, I don’t have sympathy for criminals. The system is fragged, but you’re not fghting the system when you pick up a gun and knock over the mom-and-pop Stufer Shack, are you? You’re just feeding corpses into the system. Rubbing shoulders with them is easier when you hate them. After all, deep down, they hate themselves. When they go down, you’re doing them a favor.**
