## Will to Die

An individual with very low Essence is continually walking the line between life and death, as their spirit’s hold on their body has become tenuous.

- **Bonus:** 10 Karma, must have Essence lower than 2

- **Game Effect:** Characters with this quality find themselves slipping into real death easier than normal. The character has two less damage overflow boxes.
