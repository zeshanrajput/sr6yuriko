## Quadmod (1-3)

This simple procedure strengthens the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and calves. Hence the name quad meaning “four,” not “quadriceps.” The heads of marketing must have been out of the office that week. The research was originally for running, but it turns out that we use the same muscle to jump as we do to run. Upon that discovery, it became a popular modification for athletes and parkour influencers.

This bioware requires a Minor Action to activate the muscle. When attempting a Jumping test (Athletics + Strength), a metahuman can make a running jump of 2 meters per hit, a standing horizontal jump of 1 meter per hit, and a standing vertical jump of 0.5 meters per hit. They also get +1 die on the Jumping test per level.

When attempting a Sprint test (p. 44, _SR6_ ) and investing a Minor Action to engage the bioware, they add +1 meter to their sprint movement for every rank.

This bioware cannot be combined with hydraulic jacks or any other cyberware or bioware that affects jumping or sprinting.
