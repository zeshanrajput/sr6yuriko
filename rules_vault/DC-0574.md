## Negative Qualities

## Accident Prone

When bad things happen, you’re a little worse than others at finding your way out of it.

- **Bonus:** 3 Karma

- **Game Effect:** If you roll a glitch or a critical glitch, you may not influence the outcome by spending Edge.
