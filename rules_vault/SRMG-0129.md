## **Do datajacks require a fiberoptic cable?**

Datajacks can operate as receivers for wireless signals, effectively acting as an antenna in your brain meat. However, some devices will require a direct fiber optic connection (throwbacks or devices that have had their wireless turned off or disabled). But under normal circumstances, you can use a datajack wirelessly. The datajack does include one meter of retractable micro-cable.
