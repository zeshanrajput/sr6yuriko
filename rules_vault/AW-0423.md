## Crimson Carnage

One of the groups you’ll want to avoid in Miggon is the Crimson Carnage, a marauding gang of Infected led by a powerful nosferatu who goes by the name of King Carnage. The majority of the Carnage are ghouls, with each pack led by a vampire lieutenant. You might think of them as a metaplanar go-gang, but King Carnage is more of a warlord than a gang boss. The Carnage tear around Miggon riding distinctively evil-looking and heavily armed vehicles—mostly motorcycles, but sometimes trucks and vans. Because ghouls and vampires require their food to be fresh, most of the weapons are designed to capture rather than outright kill: net and harpoon launchers are the most popular choices. The Crimson Carnage maintain a major lair within Hubris, from which they mount raids on the Yaggil within the city or ride out to harass the Yasmal, but they are also known to travel out into the Plane of Kin to find fresh prey.
