Additionally, a shifter must join an animalism ritual group (see the **Animalism Ritual Groups** sidebar). The ceremony takes a number of hours equal to the new grade of animalism. Without the help of other shifters, increasing grades of animalism is impossible. Shifter runners with a Reputation of –10 or lower are unable to improve their grades of animalism until their reputation improves.

Any grades of animalism are lost if a shifter’s Essence drops below 6 or if their Magic attribute drops to 0. Additionally, if a shifter’s Magic attribute drops to 0, any animalism powers are immediately lost.

Adept, magician, and mystic adept shifters may not gain grades of animalism. Only characters with an “E” priority in Magic or those that choose mundane for the Point Buy or Life Path character creation methods may gain grades of animalism.

Neither adept powers or cyberware/bioware modifi cations stack with animalism powers. Drugs and spells stack as normal, however.

Each grade of animalism allows the character to choose two animalism powers. Unless noted, these powers function both in animal and metahuman forms.

Unless noted otherwise, the maximum level of an animalism power is equal to the shifter’s animalism grade.
