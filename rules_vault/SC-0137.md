## The Charlatan

You might think that the existence of magic would make superstitions a thing of the past, but you couldn’t be more wrong. The public’s understanding of the Awakened world is already filled with more mysteries than answers, but imaginative fictional representations, marketing campaigns, and outright misinformation all contribute to fertile ground for any would-be charlatan to plant their own claims of magical expertise. You are a student of superstition, myths, and legends, and you use these as building blocks for your own scams. The fact that you have actual magical abilities makes your lies even more believable. That’s the truly brilliant nature of your approach: You use mundane lies and trickery in combination with true magic. You learned early on that you improved your chances more by exaggerating your magical abilities than by being honest about their limitations. Sure, this backfired a few times, and those were hard lessons to learn. You picked yourself back up and moved on, leaving many burned bridges behind in your past. Those who get to know you over a long period of time learn to distrust you, and you much prefer it that way. You would rather they never understand the precise extents of your capabilities than know you completely. There are so many options left open that way. Your teenage and earliest adult years were an absolute train wreck, and ones that you’d prefer to forget. Trial and error is a brutal

method of education, but you were determined to do things your own way, and every failure motivated you to try something different. Unfortunately, some of those failures resulted in vengeful consequences. You tend to be lucky and are confident that you can spot trouble and get out of its way. But you’re also very good at making people very angry with you, and some of them are just as willing to take it out on your friends or family if they can’t get to you. You carry the grief of those tragedies with you, but you’ll never let it show on the outside.
