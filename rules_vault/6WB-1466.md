## Voice modulator

This implant gives you perfect pitch and enormous vocal flexibility and distortion capabilities. You can speak with an increased volume (up to one hundred decibels) without strain, shift your pitch to make perfect bird calls or mellifluous singing, and most importantly, uncanny vocal impressions. The modulator can also play back (and more or less perfectly imitate) a recorded or captured voice. The user gains a bonus Edge for sound-based Con skill tests.

**Wireless bonus:** Add the rating of the modulator to your dice pool for sound based Con skill tests.
