## **>** Ethernaut

The first and largest regions is a great ocean. Here the water becomes denser and the light dimmer the further you move down. The pressure is somewhat uncomfortable even in astral form, as it tries to condense you into a single magical thought. Down into the depths, you find the Dovv (p. 31), gathering at the southern cardinal pole as heavy water laps against the perpetually eroding beaches. It is ear-popping moving from the ocean depths into the cardinal pole. So much so that the water elementals have a warning sign posted, as vadose will balloon out when they’ve been working in the depths for a long time.

- Other water elementals will make fun of and shame the vadose as they expand when they move out of the depths.
