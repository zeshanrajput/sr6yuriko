## Tools of the Traders

The audits also indicated access keys that seemed remarkably similar, and those keys were traced to their cyphers.

High-level tools were needed for proper analysis, so skilled analysts were brought in. As they were applied, they revealed the purpose of the program under analysis. The application they were examining is incredibly complex, and in the opinion of this operator, beautiful to behold on par with the Core of many of my kindred entities, free associative or flesh bound.
