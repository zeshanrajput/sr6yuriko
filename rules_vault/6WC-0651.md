## Stacking Advantage

**Use this rule** to allow strong advantages to generate more Edge than marginal ones.

When comparing Attack Rating to Defense Rating, this optional rule permits 2 Edge gained for a +8 advantage, rather than only ever gaining 1 Edge no matter how much more than +4 the advantage may be. Even with this rule in play, no more than 2 Edge can ever be earned for comparing AR to DR. This optional rule can be used whenever AR is compared to DR, so this includes hacking and spellcasting, as well as comparing Social Ratings (see p. 50, _Firing Squad_ ).
