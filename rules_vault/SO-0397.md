## Paranoid Mindset

[Trigger: Failed Judge Intentions or Perception Test, Action: Minor (A)] 1 Edge per +2 Dice

It’s not uncommon in the biz to have been burned before—and shocked, shot, and probably stabbed (figuratively and literally). When a Judge Intentions or Perception test has failed, a player character can spend a Minor Action to heighten their paranoia (or think of it as prudent situational awareness). For every point of Edge you spend, you gain +2 on all Judge Intentions or Perception tests. However, this also results in a Glitch in addition to any other results rolled. This lasts for the remainder of the scene or encounter, or after an hour passes (whichever comes first).
