## Coordinated Effort

## \[Influence (Leadership), Major Anytime\]: 3 Edge

Expertise at coordinating a team effort improves the chances of success at a Teamwork test. When a Teamwork test is attempted, player characters assisting the leader may take a Major Action to make an Influence (Leadership) + Charisma test. The amount of bonus dice the leader of the Teamwork test can accept from teammates is increased by one for every two hits on their Influence skill test. This action must be performed in the same round as the Teamwork test, except when the action takes longer than one round to complete. If the player characters assisting the leader have an additional Major Action remaining, they can use this to improve their own potential for the Teamwork test or use the Inspire Edge action to provide additional bonus dice for the leader of the Teamwork test.
