## Goring Horns

These cranial implants can take many forms, from a singular unicorn spiral to double bull horns to a complete crown of antler points. No matter the number or appearance, they are alike in performance. Add +4 AR to an attack made by goring horns if the user moved at least 3 meters toward the target as part of the attack. They are not retractable, so the user cannot wear helmets or other headgear that are not adjusted for their altered anatomy (increase cost by ten percent).
