## Crowd Ghost

The anti-surveillance paranoia of Berlin’s neo-anarchists and alternatives has seeped deeply into all lifestyles of the city, influencing fashion, makeup, and hair styling trends from the havenots to those who have way too much. Having lived in Berlin for many years, you have adopted habits and movements that let you blend in with the moving urban crowds.

- Districts: All inner districts (AGC Siemensstadt, Chawi, Kreuzhain, Mitte, Renrakusan, S-K Tempelhof, Z-IC Tegel) and anywhere there are lots of people milling about

- Cost: 5 Karma

- Game Effect: Whenever you move within a large crowd, you gain one bonus point of Edge when you are hiding from pursuers or try to avoid detection in general. This bonus Edge is lost if not spent on that test. This quality can be combined with the Under the Radar quality.
