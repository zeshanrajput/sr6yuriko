## What To Eat

After waiting in line for about sixty minutes, the players will meet Fluffy Duck (see Character Trove, p. 141). The ork is the size of a small troll but has a very cheery attitude and refreshing optimism that are rare in the Sixth World. It seems to infect the crowd, and you can see why people like his place so much. He takes your order, and so long as the tips keep rolling and the players aren’t holding up the line, he will keep talking. He knows the car and the person who drives it. He will tell the players the guy is called Silver Nail, is a good tipper, and shows up every few days and places a large order.

If the players stake out the food truck, Silver Nail will show up in two days. He will pull up next to the truck and yell something at Fluffy Duck, who comes out and hands him a large bag. Silver Nail will drive off—he is easy to follow to the storage unit.
