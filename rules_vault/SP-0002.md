## **Introduction**

**==> picture [202 x 5] intentionally omitted \<==**

_**“ Scotophobia (noun), 1) An irrational or disproportionate fear of the dark. 2) Can also be a fear of possible or imagined dangers concealed by darkness.” — New Oxford Dictionary, 2079**_

**Metahumanity has always had a primordial fear of the dark; it’s where dangers and nightmares reside. Deep down we know** _**something**_ **is there, waiting to strike when we let our guard down. Such fears are often irrational, and there aren’t really such things as boogeymen and demons stalking us.**

**Sometimes, however, there really are boogeymen and they have a name: the Disians. Now, they’re striking out from the deepest shadows against all threats to realize their ultimate plan: stealing life essence from the Material Plane itself. Sometimes, the fear is not misplaced.**

_**Scotophobia**_ **is the latest sourcebook detailing the current state of events regarding the metaplanar beings known as the Disians. While the Disians had been content to remain in the deep shadows and act as puppet masters, recent events have forced them to take a more proactive approach towards their goals. The result is an all-out shadow war, with nothing less than the fate of the Sixth World in the balance.**

**Chapter One, Just How Fragged Up Things Are , gives a brief primer on the events leading up to this point regarding the Disians, the allies, and those opposing them.**

**Chapter Two, Those from the Deep Dark , discusses the Disians, their current plans, and those allied with them. It offers information on just how far Disian infuence goes, as well as information about their most heinous creations: the chimera.**

**Chapter Three, Shining Light into the Dark , offers a look into the resistance: those who are actively working toward thwarting the Disians and their allies in an effort to save the Sixth World.**

**Chapter Four, Wildcards , shows that despite the very real threat the Disians represent, there are always those who play the waiting game, hoping to fnd an angle or a way to proft before committing to a side—or perhaps they try to play both sides.**

**Chapter Five, Flashpoints and Hot Spots , is all about battle lines and boots on the ground. It details specifc operations, campaigns, and their current outcomes in various locations across the globe for both sides.**

**Chapter Six, Mission Briefs , are mini-missions that represent some of the operations currently underway and highlight some of the actions being taken by both the Disians and the opposition.**

**Chapter Seven, Blast Radius , discusses events that are not directly related to the Disian confict but are happening parallel or as a result of the current confict.**

**Chapter Eight, The Dis-Connect , is an in-character meet between various power players in the shadows as they work to join together to provide a more unifed front against the Disian threat.**

**Chapter Nine, Rules of Engagement , contains stats, game rules, and explanations for Disian magic, new chimera, and new weapons, gear, and cyberware.**
