## Interim report 15

I’ll start by addressing the question in your last communication. Yes, it’s entirely possible that the ass-dragging of the Disians when dealing with us is part of a bargain made with someone else. I can’t rule that out. But it’s also possible that they don’t like us, or in general they’re closed to outsiders, or we breached protocol early on in some way I haven’t figured out. If I knew what the problem was, I could work on it. But I don’t have clear evidence of anything, so I just have to keep working on general trust-building, rather than addressing a specific cause.

- What’s interesting to me is that this is diplomacy conducted by an amateur. There are literally centuries of guides on how to do this sort of thing, but I get the impression this person is just making it up on the fly. These guides give you advice for when your sponsoring entity is recognized where you are and for when it’s not. The fact that that whoever sent this person couldn’t find someone who knew how to do the job is telling.
