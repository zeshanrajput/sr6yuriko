## The Contessas

Lastly are the smaller players, each waiting to take over for one of the bigger players, sniffing for weakness while being used by the others as proxies. To wit:

**Yakashima** has always wanted to move into the Big Ten, but fear of Japanese domination has always kept them outside. They’ve struggled for years with Shiawase, Evo, and even Renraku sniping their research and researchers but remain wellknown for their raw production numbers, if not advanced research.

**Tan Tien** , by contrast, has no production of note, being focused purely on research. They license or sell off their designs for other corporations to use, keeping themselves safe from the big fish … for now.

**The Genesis Consortium** took hits during the Az-Am War and wound up having to sell some stocks to Horizon to stay afloat. They’ve not sold enough to lose control of the organization, but having Horizon execs on your board signals that interest. Genesis facilities are all south of Aztlan, and it has unique agreements with Amazonia to do research and handle agribusiness there.

Lastly are **Zion Amalgamated** and **Proteus AG** , two corporations that keep their research internal and never sell it. No one’s sure what they know, and both are rock-hard in terms of security, but there are those who’d pay top money to look at their research.
