## Impeccable Style

Most people wear clothing, but you bring it to life. Somehow everything just looks better on you. If this shadowrunner gig falls through, maybe you could make a living as a model.

**Cost:** 5 Karma per level (1 to 4)

**Game Effect:** Each level of this quality increases your Social Rating, but only while wearing clothing/armor that provides a bonus to your Social Rating.
