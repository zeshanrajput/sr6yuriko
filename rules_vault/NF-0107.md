## Umsakazo, Klein Seuntjie (translated)

Wipe the sweat from my brow, I ducked out We’ve got dead messengers, You’ve got puppets, We’ve got dead messengers, And you’ve got puppets Specters in the corner,

Specters in the room, Specters in the corner, Specters are hiding in my room, Follow my tune from the city, Follow my pipe far away, Let’s go far away



There are few enough bands in the world that can take so little and create so much, but Umsakazo seems to exist solely to fill this role. From the trash hills of their country and the remnants of their ancestry, they are forging a new patchwork future.
