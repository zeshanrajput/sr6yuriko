## **Training and Oversight**

**While the LMPS is, just like all constabularies, answerable to the Ministry of Justice and other regulatory bodies, they’re responsible for training, certifcation, and oversight of frearms and magical activities for offcers from anywhere in the country. I should point out that while bobbies carry tasers, their basic twenty-week training doesn’t include much in the way of frearms profciency. And other than some defensive measures, it doesn’t include magic—certainly not combat magic. Legally it’s considered the same as frearms in the UK. And so the training and certifcation needed to use anything more than astral perception and healing magic while carrying out offcial duties is as extensive as carrying a pistol.**
