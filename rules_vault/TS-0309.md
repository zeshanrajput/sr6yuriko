## **Non-Player Characters (NPCS)**

**As a GM, life is easier when you have basic stats to work with while running a game. This section provides a list of non-player characters (NPCs) that have a law-enforcement background. Keep in mind that the stats and information for a Watch Guard could just as easily be a low-level thug standing on a street corner keeping a lookout for the police. If you want to know more about NPCs, please refer to the core rule book (p. 202-210,** _**SR6**_ **).**
