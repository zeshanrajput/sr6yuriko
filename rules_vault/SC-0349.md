## Combat

Your perceptive nature makes it much more likely that you’ll be going into most fights with some awareness of the risks. If you are capable of summoning spirits, you should always have a conjured spirit at the ready in astral space with instructions to warn you of any signs of danger. Assuming you have a service or two left after that, at the first sign of combat you can use a Minor Action to command them to aid in

the fight. A materialized spirit is a serious combat threat and very likely to draw fire from you and your companions. Don’t throw them away, except in the most desperate circumstances. Spirits don’t like being uselessly sacrificed in a hopeless fight.

After calling in backup, your second action should be to move into cover. From there, observe in detail to spot any hidden dangers and assess the most dangerous threats. If possible, switch to astral perception to identify any Awakened threats among the opposition. Geek the mage.
