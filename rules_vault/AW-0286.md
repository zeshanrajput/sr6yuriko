## Quest Examples

- A traveler might have to face their fears, so the Dweller designates part of the Metaplane of Kin as a Place of Fear, transforming the landscape based on the darker memories of the traveler and using spirits from the ID (darker side of this metaplane) as antagonists.

- The traveler might be looking for a spell formula or metamagic technique, so the Dweller creates a bubble metaplane with doppelgängers of the traveler (versions of them from roads regrettably not taken). The traveler then competes against themself in completing or perfecting the designated task.

- The traveler may be hunting down a dangerous spirit and want to eradicate it, and the Dweller drops the traveler into an arena as a Place of Battle on any metaplane, to see if the Awakened is truly ready to face the spirit within its native metaplane.

The Dweller also has a place called the Citadel where certain quests are completed. Success brings the traveler to this location. Astral forms of the Awakened’s magical items can be stored here to hide their astral link, and Awakened individuals may be offered tomes or scrolls of astral denizens to glean insight of the spirit’s true name or details on a spiritual formula for allies It’s still a mystery as to how or why the Dweller on the Threshold has such knowledge or why it picked the Citadel as a constant structure for many of the quests.
