## Sirrurg

## Posted by: Wyrm Watcher

Just when things couldn’t get any more fragged up, Sirrurg the Destroyer returned. But in a massive plot twist, he was on our side. Of all beings to come to metahumanity’s aid, I never would have expected him.

Strange times indeed.

Before anyone askes, there is _zero_ data on where he’s been all these years. Of course there are plenty of theories, but none of them make any sense. What we do know is that before the final battle with the Disians kicked off, Sirrurg returned with a bang, going after Disian targets we didn’t even know existed. He was next spotted in Antarctica, mixing it up with several Disian war forms and protecting the circle of his fellow great dragons as they performed the ritual that reinfused the harvested mana back into the material plane.

After the ritual was completed and the manasphere was (mostly?) restored, Sirrurg once again disappeared.

- What?! Have you not been paying attention? Sirrurg sightings are all over the news, and the shadows are abuzz about how the Destroyer is hunting down anything remotely related to the Disians. Any leftover data, tech, even collaborators.
