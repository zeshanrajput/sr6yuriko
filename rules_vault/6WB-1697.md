## Agent of the Flux State

You are an active member of Berlin’s neo-anarchist underground or even of extremist groups like Letzte Front or the terrorist network Axis F. Not all people living in an alternative district agree with you or political activism in general, but all alternatives appreciate the support of the neo-a movement and its fight against the megacorporations.

- **Districts:** Gropiusstadt, Köpenick, Kreuzhain, Lichtenberg, Marzahn, Pankow, Spandau

- **Cost:** 10 Karma

- **Game Effect:** You gain the Kiezspeak (N) language skill at no cost plus a bonus point of Edge on any social test with neo-anarchist activists and alternative residents of Berlin. This bonus Edge is lost if not spent on that test.
