## Krime Cocktails

So one of our inventors drank a little too much lunch one day and declared that he was going to make the absolute best cocktail that Krime had ever sold! Five skin grafts and a concussion later, he had it—the Krime Cocktail Incendiary! As smoky as white phosphorus but far more humane, this napalm-like secret mixture is useful for military actions or just starting the bonfire for the weekend getaway! Still not banned by any major country or megacorporation!

- A Molotov cocktail in a metal grenade form that emits an oily smoke from the darkest, most horrific pits of Hell itself! Thermal smoke, actually, and “humane” because people will pass out from this before they burn to death. Still, I’m glad they stopped broadcasting that “Napalm Sticks To Kids” advertisement that they started for this product.
