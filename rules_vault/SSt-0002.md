## Branching Out

## “Why would you say that?”

X-Prime gestured around the small wood-walled room. Everything was wood here in what X-Prime felt was a fairly ludicrous treehouse complex in Salish-Shidhe territory. “Because once you presented our identities, they escorted us here! This is not a waiting room, you know. We’re not being treated like honored guests.”
