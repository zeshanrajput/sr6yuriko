## Leadership Role

Because of their experience in this war, Grey Cell operatives and operators are seen as de facto leaders within Project Nemesis and within the

greater Defender Movement. To their credit, Grey Cell does their best to deflect any undue praise or hero worship and instead lead by example. Other groups will consistently try and assert dominance over the tactical narrative; however, Ringmaster and his subordinates have done a decent job of keeping the peace, or at least planning operations where the problematic elements become less of a problem.

- This was mentioned in another download, but Renraku continues to be the primary thorn in Project Nemesis’ side, specifically the Red Samurai. Despite early pledges to better coordinate and cooperate, Red Samurai arrogance quickly reared its ugly, armored head. It got so bad that units within Grey Cell, specifically the SixtyFirst IR, almost started shooting at each other.

- Sam-R-Eye
