## Jetblack (human, male, 27, black hair, black eyes, average build)

It takes a runner with some moxie to make use of the visage of legendary rocker Jetblack. When out in the world, this version of Jetblack often keeps an illusion or two handy to keep from ending up in screamsheets or “I saw Jetblack” hosts, but not always. Playing it up in a world where there are so many lookalikes for Jetblack lets him use that as a great cover. He has not only the look but can also manage the swagger and even pulls off a song or two with a bit of auditory magic.

When it comes to working against the runners, he will use illusion magic and the distraction of his appearance to create commotion when possible. If it comes to a conflict, he’ll use invisibility and disappear like the real Jetblack. He keeps a kindred spirit on hand to help with the illusions and to point him out at times in order to start a fan rush. These rushes are often perfect times for Bobcat to plant devices on the runners as a crowd moves past them in a group.
