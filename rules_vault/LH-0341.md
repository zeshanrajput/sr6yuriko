## rEVOlution Arms Specialty Archery Products

Sometimes stealth is more important than firepower. For those situations, rEVOlution Arms Specialty Archery Products provides a full range of options for bows and crossbows. These weapons have less ammo and a slower rate of fire than firearms, but they make up for it with silence, lethality, and now, the flexibility of loadouts.

**Game Rules:** Arrows and bolts with interchangeable arrow (or bolt) heads have the same cost and availability as standard arrows and bolts. The cost of these interchangeable heads is in addition to the cost of the arrows or bolts themselves. Interchangeable heads can be removed or installed in the field relatively quickly, taking an Engineering (armorer) + Logic (3, 1 minute) test per 5 heads changed.

rEVOlution Arms branded interchangeable heads do _not_ require a wild die to be included in the attack roll.
