## **Overview**

**Sternschutz Security is a private police and security service provider based in Stuttgart. The corporation is owned by the Frankfurt Bank Association (FBA), an AA-rated bank conglomerate from the Allied German States (AGS). Although their law enforcement contracts are in the AGS, the company has an excellent reputation internationally and holds law enforcement contracts in Lille and Zurich. Besides their full law enforcement contracts, Sternschutz also has various support contracts, ranging from border protection to security contracts focused on specifc neighborhoods or buildings within a city.**

**Currently the Sternschutz is led by René Demiroglu, who faces the hard task of reorganizing the company and improving the public image. Of course, the changes that come with that task don’t appeal to everyone, and the far-right employees of Sternschutz oppose the efforts.**
