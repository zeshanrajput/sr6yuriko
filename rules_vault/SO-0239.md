## Mental Fortitude

(Health)

**==> picture [227 x 20] intentionally omitted \<==**

**----- Start of picture text -----**<br>
RANGE TYPE DURATION DV<br>Touch M S 3<br>**----- End of picture text -----**<br>

Targets of this spell are able to shake off supernatural influences. They may add +1 per net hit to any test to resist magical mind control or emotional manipulation, in addition they can make a Composure (2) test to shake off any unwanted social status (p. 44). The caster rolls a Sorcery + Magic (5 – Essence) test. They may choose how many net hits to apply, up to a maximum of +4, with each net hit applied beyond the first adding 1 to the Drain Value.
