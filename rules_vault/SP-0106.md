## **History Lesson**

**For those of you who have forgotten or are too young to remember, the foundation was created as a result of some interesting times. The great dragon Dunkelzahn had just been elected president of the UCAS, and on the night of his inauguration, he was assassinated in a mysterious explosion of magical energy. That led to some interesting times, as everyone from UCAS authorities all the way to individual armchair detectives tried to determine what happened. The interesting times continued when it was revealed that “Uncle Dunkie” had a secret will with scores of benefciaries.**

**Including myself, but that’s neither here nor there.**

**Part of his will called for the establishment of the Draco Foundation, primarily to act as executors of said will but also to act as caretakers of Dunkelzahn’s vision to continue various works in his name. And while that sounds noble and made for great PR at the time, thirty some years later the Foundation now faces more than a few problems.**
