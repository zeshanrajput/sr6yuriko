## K-10

(Blood of Kali)

- **Vector:** Injection

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** 5 x 1D6 minutes

- **Power:** 12

- **Effect:** +3 Body, +3 Agility, +4 Strength, +1 Willpower, +2D6 Initiative Dice

When kamikaze isn’t powerful enough, there is a “kamikaze grade 10” to take it up to eleven. K-10’s formula was originally hacked from a research lab’s medical database back in 2071. The fact that all the original research subjects died because of the drug or because of indirect damage when they flew into a rage was noted and possibly not taken into account enough. Users of the combat drug automatically become enraged and go berserk when they take Physical or Stun damage and continue in that state until they succeed at a Logic + Willpower (4) test. When the drug wears off, the user must resist (Power x 2)S damage and gain the Fatigued II status.
