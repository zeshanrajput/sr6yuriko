## **Current Events**

**With Damien Knight’s death, Arthur Vogel tried to detangle some of Knight’s operations and KE was the top knot. The Firewatch teams have fared terribly under his watch, having their special status removed, then seeing their budget slashed and bodies moved back to Knight Errant proper. Combined with the ongoing astral experiments, Ares suffered several disasters, in terms of Detroit’s own bug invasion and the following failures in predicting and dealing with Disians, who, in theory, Firewatch would have been perfect for snuffng out. Vogel would have been hard-pressed to do worse intentionally than he did by accident, and it’s little surprise that he was voted out.**

- **Icarus**

**With the rise of Michael Bishop, Ares has another tumultuous time ahead of it. His frst action was to push Knight Errant, a division in itself, into Ares Services, a massive reduction in status. His second was to accept the resignation of Clayton Wilson, who wasn’t about to let himself be treated that way (or be there when the books were audited). Several potential replacements resigned with Wilson, loyalists all, leaving KE headless for a week until Bishop called up Bernie McGinnis out of Boston. McGinnis, a lifelong offcer who’d worked himself up from patrolman to commissioner, had retired just a few months before Boston was locked down and had been tearing himself up over what’d happened to his city. He’s a cop’s cop, dedicated to the law and to the very ideal of serving and protecting, and he signed on as a chance to do for Ares what he couldn’t do for his hometown. He’s not corporate in the least, and the structure’s alien to him, but he has enough political experience that he seems to be navigating decently enough at this stage. His big goal is to try and turn KE into a “real” police force.**

- **Lots of old hands want to see Bishop fail out of loyalty to Knight, but with KE’s general demotion, most are focused on disrupting other areas. That said, KE has been Wilson’s rebuilt arm for a decade now, and while McGinnis wants to push them further in the direction Wilson was heading, it remains to be seen how much pushback he’ll see.**
