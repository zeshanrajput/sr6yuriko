## The Komon

Specialists of the non-combat arm, these are the BTL programmers, the tattoo artists, cybersurgeons, photographers for idol mags and pornography (often the same person in the same studio during different hours!), and so on. If you have a talent that doesn’t involve fighting, the Yakuza can find a place for you.
