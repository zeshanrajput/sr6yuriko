## Using Overdrive With Nanite Colonies

Driving a colony of nanites into overdrive will amplify some function or feature of the colony and typically creates some negative side effects. Any overdrive function that adds bonus dice or an automatic hit must use a wild die on the test. Unless noted otherwise, any nanite volume placed in overdrive is removed from the active nanite volume at the start of the overdrive action. That nanite volume quickly burns out and cannot be reused, only reclaimed for precursor compounds.
