## Gorilla Chimera

Designed to be walking tanks, the gorilla chimera is named for the common modification that increases their limbs by 1.5 to 1.75 times. Most gorilla chimera also have greyish skin and a bald head, with the nose replaced with two slats before the nasal cavity. Similar to their simian counterparts, gorilla chimera also appear to be quick-tempered, aggressive, and sometimes territorial. As described in the chapter, Disians have the ability to imbue some creatures with magic-like abilities without the subject necessarily having a Magic rating. In these cases, the chimera can use their abilities but do not register as Awakened when they are assensed.

The powers the chimera have do not require a test using the Magic attribute to use, so they function as normal in their rulebook descriptions.
