## Chwal

When a serviteur has worked to continue growing their alignment with the lwa, they become better mounts. This eases the ability for the spirits to speak through them and provide guidance. The spirits lwa’s comfort with their horse convinces it to provide additional assistance to the houngan or mambo.

**Cost:** 7 Karma

**Skills:** Astral F, Athletics F, Close Combat F, Outdoors F, Perception F **Powers:** Astral Form, Binding, Essence Drain, Fear, Possession **Weaknesses:** Essence Loss (1 point per day)

**Optional Powers:** Concealment, Confusion, Guard, Movement, Natural Weapon (claws/bite) **Weapons:**
