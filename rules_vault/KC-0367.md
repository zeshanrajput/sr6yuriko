## Zero One (troll, female, 22, no hair, grey eyes, muscular build)

The sister of RAM, Zero One is fierce on the battlefield. She knows she is not near as bright as her teammates, but she doesn’t care. She knows that once the trouble starts, she’s in charge and keeping the team safe and on the winning side are her top priorities. She grew up rough and shows the signs and scars of her youth, but her mind is keenly aware that the world can be a better place. She respects Tara’s philosophies on killing and has adopted her code of non-lethal combat, though non-lethal and fully automatic are often at odds, but that’s a matter of weak enemies or slow emergency service responses.
