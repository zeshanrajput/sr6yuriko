## TLE-X

TLE-X stands for temporal lobe epilepsy with complications. Once thought to be related to the implantation of move-by-wire systems, it has since been found to occur in anyone with invasive implants. Those with TLE-X sometimes fall into seizures when under pressure due to the stress caused by these implants.

- **Bonus:** 10 Karma. Must have at least 3 Essence worth of augmentations, as the effect arises from the metabolic and neurological stress of excessive cyberware implantation.

• **Game Effect:** When a character with TLE-X experiences a sufficiently stressful situation, they must make a Body+Willpower (4) test. Failure of this test means the runner falls into epileptic seizures for (5 – hits) minutes. TLE-X is treatable, and the biomedical AEXD grants +3 dice on the resistance test to prevent the onset of TLE-X seizures. The damage to a character’s system remains present even if the implant that caused the condition to manifest is removed. It may be fully cured with brain surgery or gene therapy, but removing the actual quality still requires Karma expenditure.
