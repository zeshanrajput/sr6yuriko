## **Gamemaster Tips**

**Bullyphants are a hazard, while Grimm’s elephants are a threat. If the PCs are working in a savanna, a herd of elephants is not something they can safely ignore. The bullyphant is unlikely to attack or interfere with the PCs unless they get close to the herd, but the Grimm’s elephant will come after them looking for blood.**

**These elephants could make for an interesting complication on a run—perhaps a herd is nearby when the runners need to go after their target. The PCs could use their presence as an advantage or a distraction, but it’s equally likely that they’ll be in danger as well.**
