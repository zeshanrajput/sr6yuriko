## Blue Biotech

Blue biotech is focused on the oceans and seas of the planet. Be it aquaculture, underwater farming, or indoor krill production, if it lives in the water, it belongs to blue. Those of you in coastal areas should know how big this market is, but landlocked types might be surprised. You really shouldn’t be, as seventy percent of this planet is covered in water. Most people don’t live there, however, and out of sight is out of mind. Underwater habitats wouldn’t exist without blue biotech giving them sustainable air supplies and potable water, and bioware has let us push past even those boundaries. In this section, I’m going to list all the gear you would need to live under the sea. We already know the corps have created entire communities of aquatic metahumans who never need to bother with air. Past overfishing devastated fish crops worldwide, and blue biotech is busy trying to reverse that course as well.
