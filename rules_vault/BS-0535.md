## Genetic Augmentations

Advances in genetics and biotechnology have made it possible to customize an embryonic animal to specification. This allows for the design of critters with improved attributes, but some of the more interesting—and expensive—advancements allow a designer to add some of the capabilities of one critter to another.

Gene treatments can also be applied to a critter that’s already been born or hatched, but they are less effective.
