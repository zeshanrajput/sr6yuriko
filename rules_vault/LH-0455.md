## Spirits

Only spirits with the Inhabitation power remain in the physical world, though they’re restricted to normal form limitations. All other spirits retreat to their home metaplanes or Hidden Life objects if they possess such. If they try to remain on Earth after July 25, they suffer disruption or destruction. Wild and free spirits begin being spotted again after August 5, but many are still unaccounted for.
