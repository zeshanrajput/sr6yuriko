## Orkish Cuisine

A hearty fusion of soul food, barbeque, Creole, and Germano-Slavic influences, with an emphasis on portion size, spice, salt, heat, and the use of otherwise discarded elements of cooking as preparation elements and even primary components. Often high in protein and carbohydrates. Sometimes incorporates “shock” elements such as live worms, though this has fallen out of style with the rise of Or’zet and MOM efforts to foster acceptance between metahuman groups.
