## Negative Qualities

**Conspicuous Alterations (1 or 2)** : Your alterations have taken you away from what is generally accepted as a human or metahuman appearance, and you pay the price for this.

- **Bonus:** 6 or 8 Karma

- **Game effect:** Depending on the degree of transgenic modification taken, individuals suffer penalties (increase the cost of Edge Boosts and Actions by 1 or 2) on social interactions with non-transgenic metahumans. Additionally, other individuals (NPCs and PCs) get a +2 dice pool bonus when conducting a Memory test to recall they have seen you before. The Inhuman quality can never be combined with the Human-Looking quality.

**Misaligned Souls** : This was a bad decision. You can feel it in your bones, in your soul. You were never meant to be changed like this. Whatever transgenic infusion you chose, it’s making you feel wrong on the inside.

- **Bonus:** 10 Karma

- **Game effect:** Lose 1 point of Essence permanently when you are infused. Additionally, spirits (particularly animal or nature spirits) do not like to be around you. Individuals cannot spend Edge on social interactions with spirits. If you engage in a teamwork test to assist a social test against a spirit, no one can spend Edge, and the opposing spirit gains 1 point of situational Edge. Misaligned Souls cannot be taken in conjunction with Chimeric Soul.

This quality can only be selected when the character is about to receive a transgenic infusion, and they must go through with receiving it.

**A Lifetime of Rejection (1 or 2)** : The transgenic treatments did not go well. Your body is rejecting the new RNA code and you are now stuck with a lifetime of non-rejection medication.

- **Bonus:** 4 or 8 Karma



- **Game effect:** Characters must pay (quality level x 500¥) per month in medication. Failing to pay for the medication runs the risk of cancer, organ failure, or death.

**Fine Young Cannibal** : You have acquired a taste for metahuman flesh due to your transgenic infusion. You are not an Essence eater like a vampire, or even an obligate cannibal like a ghoul—you’re just in it for the meaty bits.

- **Bonus:** 4 Karma

- **Game effect:** Characters with this negative quality who are in hand-to-hand combat must make a Willpower (2) test to successfully resist trying to eat their opponent during or after combat. If any blood has been shed in hand-to-hand, take a –2 dice pool penalty on this roll. Reduce this penalty to –1 if the character has fed on metahuman flesh in the last twenty-four hours

**Doesn’t Quite Fit** : Your transgenic alteration makes it difficult for you to use or wear certain unmodified items.

- **Bonus:** 2 Karma

- **Game effect:** Add 25 percent to the cost for clothing and equipment that would be used in that transgenic area: pants for altered legs (or tail-holes), helmets for horned heads, gloves and weapons for clawed hands, etc.

**Sensitive Nose:** People with this quality are particularly vulnerable due to their acute senses of smell.

- **Bonus:** 4 Karma
