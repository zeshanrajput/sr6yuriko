## Patchwork Chimera

While other chimeras are created around a specific purpose or design, patchwork chimeras are unique. These chimeras have been observed using different biomechanical limbs that can be detached and swapped with other patchworks. These limbs vary in size, strength, and function but often feature natural weapons such as a crustacean pincer claw or long bone spike similar in function to a sword. Some patchworks can spit corrosive, acidic saliva.

Their torsos and heads feature dermal deposits and enhanced musculature that makes them stronger and resistant to damage. Patchwork chimera have higher-than-average intelligence and have been observed using tactics, deductive reasoning, and problem-solving skills.

**Note:** The weapons and augmentations listed below are examples or proxies. They can be swapped out for any bio-mechanical versions of standard melee weapons but can only employ two at a time.
