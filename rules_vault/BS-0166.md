## Oral Slasher

A weapon that is as concealable as it is impractical, the oral slasher hides a telescoping spike under the user’s tongue. A compact air compressor launches the weapon through the user’s open mouth in a vicious, lashing stab before spring-like polymers retract it back in place with almost as much quickness. If the oral slasher is employed against an unaware opponent who is mouth to mouth with the user (for example, someone engaged in a kiss) and the attack is successful, then the weapon automatically fills the victim’s Physical Condition Monitor with no Body roll to reduce the damage.
