## Unknown Metatypes

One thing that has come out of this research is that some people have restored part of their Essence by getting implants and surgery, but not in a way that has made them match up with any known metatype. Mostly these are one-of-a-kind people and modifications. One person had a black ceramic shell installed, their legs extended, and all hair and other external items of flesh removed, and their Essence became slightly more bright and vibrant, but with a distinct flavor that was not the same as any metahuman that I’ve seen before, and shocking given how much augmentation they had.

I’m not sure what this means, exactly. We’ve seen the variety of ways that metahumanity can express itself with some of the more obscure or exciting changes that happened around the Year of the Comet with Sudden Unexplained Recessive Genetic Expression (SURGE), and things have only become more diverse since that initial burst of change. Some of these have become more or less common, although not yet nearly as common as the base metatypes. It may be that as the mana curve rises, other metatypes are lurking in our Essence, looking to emerge. That next phase of emerWHAT IS ESSENCE? // Augmentations, Essence, and the Meaning of Life


gence will happen gently, over time, instead of as a traumatic event in the way that Goblinization occurred.

What it has brought up is the very rare situation of someone having an unexpressed metavariant. In a way this isn’t new—there have been elf and ork poseurs almost as long as there have been elves and orks. What we have newly noticed is sometimes, a person might have the Essence of a troll, ork, elf, or other metatype, but it has not been expressed. We have seen some brightening of auras with the use of surgery to make the outer body fit the inner being.

Saeder-Krupp has a research facility in the rebuilt Istituto di Genetica e Biofisica where they are gathering a selection of these cases for further study. This project seems to have been kicked off by the Golden Dragon himself some time ago, but he doesn’t seem to be involved any more or less than any other part of the organization. Perhaps Lofwyr sees it as pure research, perhaps it’s a hedge against some other kind of event, or maybe he’s just personally interested. I would say that time will tell, but given the time scale that dragons seem to operate on, it might be nothing for four hundred years and then abruptly will fit into some complex plan. This research facility has standard security, but information leaks out regularly as researchers come and go, and nothing untoward appears to be happening there.

It’s even possible that there might be some metavariants in metahumanity that cannot be expressed without some sort of augmentation or surgery. We haven’t encountered it yet, but this could be the start of a truly unique future for us, a future that might blur the lines between what we think of as natural or unnatural. I suppose it’s not that wild a proposition—as metahumanity evolved, we never evolved to live in apartment blocks eating only vitamin-boosted nutrisoy or kelp for years. We never evolved to sit in small cloth boxes working on the Matrix, or to build moon and Mars bases, yet here we are. Perhaps what is natural is for us to keep growing and changing, and as we incorporate this technology into our lives, perhaps our Essence will change to reflect that. This is quite a change in mindset, as we are used to metatypes presenting from creatures of myth and legend. Will our next stage be metatypes that express their true selves with cyberware? It would be a strange metatype that has ducted fans, ruthenium fibers, or a datajack, but who knows? And what happens if someone’s truth is something that cannot be expressed with our current technology? There is a lot of open research here.
