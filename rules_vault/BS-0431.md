## Pacifier

- **Vector:** Slot, Wireless

- **Speed:** 1 combat round

- **Duration:** (Rating) hours

- **Power:** 9

- **Effect:** –1 Reaction, –1 Intuition, –1 Willpower, –1 Charisma

This moodchip is used to make targets more compliant during an extraction or during situations of high stress. The program encourages compliant social responses like those of a codependent individual. Users become passive, acquire a glassyeyed appearance, and may behave drunkenly.
