## Sh ifter Shadows

Shifters frequently intersect with the shadow community. Shifters make excellent runners themselves, but they also fi nd work as security that runners need to work around. Any place where shifters are not welcome, they are interacting with smugglers and black markets, and they usually have multiple bolt holes for when the heat is on. Even in shifter-friendly communities, their unique needs and perspectives often have them at odds with metahuman laws and customs.

There is often a thriving grey market of shifter laborers who are too young by metahuman laws but still need to work. These markets also supply shifters with age-restricted items and fake SINs.
