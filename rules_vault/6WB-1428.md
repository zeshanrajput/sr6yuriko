## Survival kit

A rugged bag full of survival essentials. The bag includes a knife, lighter, matches, compass, string saw, lightweight thermal blanket, seven days’ worth of soy ration bars, a water-purification unit, fishing line and a hook, and a smattering of other survival equipment.
