## Naheka-Rengo (Yakuza)

In recent months, the New-Way Kawaru-gumi has used its smuggling operations within the Golden Triangle to its benefit. These operations have assisted others in constructing shelters in multiple locations along the western Pacific coastline from Magadan (Yakut) to Oahu (Hawai’i), to Manokwan (West Papua), and down to Darwin on the northern edge of Australia. It’s believed that some of the items being smuggled are to reinforce certain beneath-the-surface assets held by the Sea Dragon, Renraku, and Maersk.

Most of these have been framed as temporary habitats being reinforced with more permanent materials and placed in areas where tidal solid influences are diminished. Oyabun Naheka-sama has been remarkably forthcoming with these operations, treating them as extended security services for their clients and families. Several Awakened within the Naheka-rengo have been instructed to use certain detection formulas that are normally good at discerning the presence of even delta-grade bioware as a way of screening for Disian chimeras. At these sites, the entrances to such are said to be blocked by wards modified with this technique.
