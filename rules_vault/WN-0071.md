## Trans-Orbital

Want to be invaluable? Make the rockets that get the materials and technicians to space so the corps can keep their tech running. Trans-Orbital specializes in satellite uplink connectivity. They are a largely corporate-facing organization, and most of their earnings come from repairing or upgrading satellites for other corporations. T-O services everyone from the Public Broadcasting Service to the Corporate Court. The corporation is embedded in NYC, where they maintain a launch facility upstate of the city.

- Trans-Orbital maintains a research facility in Washington Heights that is often the target of run attempts; however, I’ve never met a runner who successfully broke in. The facility is guarded against all forms of intrusion, such as astral or brute

force attacks. The host system is layered and air-gapped in a way that prevents hacking intrusion. Whatever they’re hiding costs a lot of nuyen to keep secure.

- You definitely do not want to know what they are keeping there. Hint: It ain’t from here.
