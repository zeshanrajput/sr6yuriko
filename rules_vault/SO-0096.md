## Effects

The ultimate point of Paranoia Protection is to weaken the connection between the mark and their security. If successful, there’s a range of potential effects—from suspicion and hesitation in dangerous moments to firing the whole detail and replacing them. A new team will have less experience and familiarity with the terrain and client, which you can take advantage of.

Even if unsuccessful, the data you obtain about security responses has value, whether you’re extracting the mark or acquiring an item. However, that’s outside basic social engineering and more about testing hard targets if you’re not taking a soft approach.
