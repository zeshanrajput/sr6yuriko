## Shinto Summoner

## Prerequisite: Must be able to summon spirits

Your focus in the ways of Shinto give you a particular affinity with spirits connected to that tradition.

**Cost:** 2 Karma

**Game Effect:** Gain +1 to Summon rolls while in Shinto shrines.

Corrupted spirits will always select you over any other target.
