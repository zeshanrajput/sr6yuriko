## Plot Hooks Everyone’s A Mark

## Premise

This hook could be for either a starting or established social adept character. The character follows the _Learning How to Deal_ quality path (p. 134, _Firing Squad_ ) after the character has become burned out due to the personal toil of manipulating and deceiving people. This hook amplifies the consequences to the PC for the people they have used their skills and abilities against.
