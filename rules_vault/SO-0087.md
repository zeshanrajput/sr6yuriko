## Fire Drill Plot Hook

With the team stymied on how to get through a corporate obstacle, they observe a rival team using a Fire Drill to get through. Do they blow their cover to prevent the opposition from beating them to the punch? Or do they find a way to hijack it for themselves? Either way, the other runners aren’t gonna like someone stealing their score. The race is on.
