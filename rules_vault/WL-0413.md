## **Bonded Critter**

**With the Permanent Improvement option in the Critter Bond quality path (p. 139,** _**6WC**_ **), you can purchase or upgrade qualities for your critter by spending your own Karma. Whenever you purchase a positive quality for your critter in this way, you may choose one of the negative qualities from this chapter as well—only the negative qualities in this chapter are eligible for this. Bonus Karma from negative qualities reduces the Karma cost of positive qualities before the Karma cost that you must pay is doubled.**
