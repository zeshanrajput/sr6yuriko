## Local Actors

**Broken-Reed Triad (BRT)** . Born out of an early division with the 14K Triad in the late ’50s, BRT has become a considerable player in the many Triad circles within the divided Chinese governments. Among their more enterprising success stories is the watchdog-like recognition of rising agencies and those that comprise them, or will comprise them, during their infancy years. BRT has had their eyes on Yingyong since the beginning, and even went so far as to place Gu Guanyu with the rising company.

- I’m still working on the details, but Guanyu has a brother or an uncle in BRT who saw his talents while they were growing up. Names have been scrubbed skillfully, and since I cannot reach the Great Archive, I am unable to recover the details at this time.

- Rikki

BRT has been watching a significant number of these up-and-coming agencies since before my time, going back to their split with the 14K Triad.

**14K Triad** was formed in 1945, within the Guangzhou province of China. The ‘K’ represents “Kowloon,” and the “14” is for the original fourteen members, though there are historical variations of this. They are among the largest of all

the Triads on earth, with membership in excess of twenty thousand individuals pre-Awakening, while current estimates say they have upward of twenty-five thousand members. Regarding criminal activities, they still do it all: trafficking, drugs, extortion, identity theft (and brokerage) as well as money laundering, etc. The list is too long to cite here. Broken-Reed Triad broke from them in 2058 after a kidnapping and child-trafficking incident included more than a dozen children and young adults from their own members.

- There are reports in police archives that the 14K Triad was involved in heavy metahuman trafficking for unknown agencies at the time. Broken-Reed Triad had a higher-than-normal Awakened birthrate among its members. It is thought this was a sort of culling.

- Gramius

- The upheavals in the years following the founding of the Red Dragon and Yellow Lotus Triads have never really settled down. The war in the ’60s that wiped out Yellow Lotus showed how a war of attrition versus one of heritage can play out. The Gentlemen are a good example of this.

- Lei Kung

- Red Dragon Lotus may have the Great Incense Master as their head, but their members arose from within 14K during the latter part of the twentieth century. Many of the older triads of the last century fell during the years of the Awakening, but 14K survived.

- Yei Limu

14K has significant interests in Yingyong after they won a contract to provide smart aerosol deployers to the Hong Kong and Shenzhen police departments. They want that access and control, and they began to take steps toward more overt methods of getting it. These include attempted buyouts of housing agreements for company families.

**Kanabo** is a production company based in Kowloon City that often works with small agencies to manufacture technical and production equipment. They are a newer player in the field of small-arms production and often are contracted by local divisions of larger companies wanting to produce short runs of items such as personal sidearms, light-armor technologies for fashionware, and similar applications. Their management staff is entirely filled by locals.

- This is a nice, sweet introduction for a shill. Kanabo is, in truth, a manufacturing arm for small arms and armors for 14K. I have it on good faith that the production group is mobile, able to relocate their operation should they need to. Someone has helped keep them from being disheveled for a while now. Watch your back.

- Lei Kung
