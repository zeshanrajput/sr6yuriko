## Repairing Matrix Damage

What’s broken can often be fixed, with the proper tools, time, and talent. Repairing a damaged device is an Engineering + Logic (number of boxes, 1 hour) Extended test. The device cannot be used while it is being repaired, and repairs cannot be performed without a toolkit.

A critical glitch on a repair test means you messed up good—the device fails totally and will never work again.
