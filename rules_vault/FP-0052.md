## **Conrad Dominguez**

**Operating out of Bogotá, Dominguez is a mover and shaker for the cartel. Unlike some of his peers who live the perpetual high life, he keeps a modest villa with top-tier security and spends the bulk of his wealth on his quest for the tarot. His occupation allows for a lot of international access, and he has little trouble moving people and items to the places he needs. He’s also a sociopath, so be careful when dealing with him.**
