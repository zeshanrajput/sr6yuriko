## shadow stock 

- **Unsteady Hands:** Negative quality, per p. 79, _SR6_ . 

- **Vestigial Wings:** Gain basic flight at your standard metahuman movement speed (but able to be increased with Athletics tests and the Flying specialty). Add your dracogenesis rating to all flying tests.
