## The Whomp-Snapper Rally

The rich and amoral cause a lot of hell and misery in the Sixth World, but sometimes their particular frame of mind opens up an opportunity. A small coalition of the ultra-rich want to hold separate rallies across every continent of the world, including Antarctica. There will be a start time, a registered list of racers, a designated finish line, and those will be the only official procedures in place. First person across the finish line wins—and collects five million nuyen.

The racing rigger can either join the contest themselves or get a sponsor—someone who will help them get the ride need in return for half of the prize money. The rigger is told to draft a team, and if they’re smart, they’ll get one. The race will be a whole lot easier with a shooter, a hacker, a spellslinger, and a face at their side. But the rigger will be taking the lead.

Any number of obstacles can be thrown at the rigger and their team as they make their journey. Violence from other drivers, aggressive law enforcement efforts, stampedes of rampaging critters, even simple traffic jams can all afflict the team as they make their way across the route. If they want to succeed, these obstacles should be encountered at high-speed—any slowdown could cost the team five million nuyen.
