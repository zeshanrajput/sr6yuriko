## **Mental Manipulation**

**While freballs and the like are impressive, those in the know understand that perhaps the scariest thing magic can do is directly mess with your mind. Ask anyone who has had a spell like Control Actions or Control Thoughts on them. The loss of control of the most central aspects of yourself is terrifying, and some people report having trouble being able to trust their own thoughts after the experience. It can be seen as a tremendous personal violation, but the fact that spellcasters keep using these techniques despite the red fags that come with them attests to their power.**

**This kind of manipulation is so powerful that many have sought ways to do it that do not rely on magic. Tech-based mental manipulation would be qualitatively different than magic-based manipulation. If technology to control thoughts is even possible, it’s nowhere close to implementation, so no one should be preparing to use it anytime soon.**

**One area, though, where technology has made several strong steps forward is brain mapping. We are far**



**beyond the stage where we loosely say what functions happen where. Individuals’ brains can be mapped with great precision. Especially if they actively cooperate, thinking of specifc memories or concepts while hooked up to brain-monitoring machines.**

**The technology to use this information is still developing, but rumors say people have started on one approach, with at least some success: memory erasure. This can’t be done in a short-term way— you cannot, for example, erase the memory of the security guard who saw you sneaking into a building—but it still shows potential for high benefts and nightmares. On the plus side, imagine if you could erase specifc memories from a Mr. Johnson you double-crossed. Handy, right? Now, imagine they could do the same to you. Pretty terrifying!**

**The Tiding is quite interested in who is attempting to develop this technology and where it stands. A shadowy group called Animus Construction has drawn their attention to this area.**

- **Animus has drawn a lot of attention and some investigation. One of the interesting facts that emerged: They’re an outgrowth of the UCAS CIA.**
