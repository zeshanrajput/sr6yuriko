## Truth Seekers

Whether people know about it or want it to be true or not, the lines have been drawn in the war over the truth. On one side are the cyclopean giants of the corporations with their lie peddlers and truth twisters. On the other side are the ones they call “pirates.” We are the crusaders for truth, and we are getting more numerous and more organized. Together, we are standing stronger and fighting harder than ever before, punching above our weight and giving those bastards a black eye.
