## Bonded Chwal

During the times a chwal has spent working as a mount, you have made a special relationship with one of the lwa. Whether you have shared views on the direction of metahumanity, or maybe you have spent the time unconscious just absorbing the traits of the lwa, you have reached an agreement with one of the lwa. You remain aware while mounted by that spirit.
