## Aztechnology

No sacrifice is too great for Aztechnology, though in recent decades they’ve cultivated a reputation as the world’s greatest provider of food that has begun to overshadow their bad PR from their (alleged—ha! ha!) human sacrifices and blood magic. Sure, they have a few cybered-up combat monsters on the market, but their most popular application of genetic engineering is for enhanced livestock. Meat is back on the menu!
