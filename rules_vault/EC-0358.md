## The Vibe

**Ketron** was a chill little place to hide out and live quietly until a serial killer got snagged there, and now it’s a tourist trap for Mayan Cutter fanatics. The Mayan theme all over gives it an almost Aztechnology feel, but the number of lawsuits they’ve got pending says they want nothing to do with this place.
