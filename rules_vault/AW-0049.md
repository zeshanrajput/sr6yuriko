## Fettering and Offerings

Earth elementals are easygoing compared to offerings requested by other elementals. While the wealthy magician offers semiprecious stones, gold,

EARTHor silver to call upon greater forces, they forget that practicality can be more appealing to the denizens of this plane. Quartz, for instance, is useful to them as currency because it can be used to repair their machinery.

- Collecting an aggregate sample of earth and preserving its temperature and humidity can be very heady to them. Earth elementals can feel and smell the age and origins; the mystical terroir, as it were. Terrariums are a bit of an overkill, but there should be some thought and care taken in preserving the soil.

- **>** Ethernaut
