## Current Status

The world is still reeling from recent events, so it’s difficult to discern Spinrad Global’s next moves. Especially as it seems not everyone inside the corporation is aligned on how to wield its newfound influence. Gabrielle is determined to keep the peace she fostered with S-K during the Grand Tour, but Mohammed Sudayrin and Aziz Ibn Yusuf al-Shammar are more likely to want to double down on their plan to kick Lofwyr out of the Caliphate. As for Shaheed Zahir, who knows?

- It may sound like an impossible divide to reconcile, but I actually think SG will win on both fronts. They will preserve the armistice in Europe yet battle to the death in the sandy lands of the Caliphate.

- Sim-Eon

- I tend to agree, because I believe the truce between S-K and SG might be longer-term than we may have thought. I suspect the arrangement is akin to the relationship between the old USA and USSR during the Cold War. As far as Europe and northern America are concerned, neither AAA will go down a path that could take them to full-blown corporate war.



Other parts of the world—in particular, Africa and Asia—are a completely different story. **>** Mr. Bond
