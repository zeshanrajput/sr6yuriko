## Q-bars

(3-quinuclidinyl benzilate midazolam)

- **Vector:** Injected

- **Speed:** 1D6 combat rounds

- **Duration:** (9 – Body) hours, minimum 1 minute

- **Power:** 10

- **Effect:** +2 Body, –1 Reaction, –1 Agility, –2 Willpower, –3 Intuition

This drug is often used on the street as a truth serum, reducing a recipient’s resistance to interrogation techniques. Many with more nuyen will opt to go with gamma scopolamine, but this is way easier to get your hands on. The drug causes hallucinations and delirium, and inhibits cognitive functions while also acting as a downer. The real drawback is that it can knock the subject out. When high on the drug, the user grants situational Edge on Influence tests. If the user’s Willpower or Intuition reach 0, they pass out. When they crash, they gain the Fatigued II status.
