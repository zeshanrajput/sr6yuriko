## **Personal and Property Protection Group (PPP)**

**The PPP usually provides police protection for important persons and objects within a specifc area. This may include politicians, judges, specifc homes, or government buildings. Bodyguards of the PPP usually come from Sternschutz ProSec, while drones support object protection.**
