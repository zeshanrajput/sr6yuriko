## Wen Xun Lee

(“The Cat”, Security Chief, Kanabo production facility)

Originally from northern Malaysia (part of the SAW), Lee keeps his origins exceptionally low key to the point of not speaking about it all. As part of his job, he has access to an array of gear as necessary while on site, including manufactured products of the GI technologies. He always keeps a set of the full-wrap glasses on his person. There are individuals aware of his nature within the Broken-Reed Triad, but they do not concern themselves with trivial nonsense.
