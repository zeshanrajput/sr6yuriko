## Beat Cop

- **Typical Connection Rating:** 2

- **Type:** Government (also any except Criminal)

The Beat Cop also includes dispatchers and paralegal clerks, with a lower Connection rating it could be a snitch, rent-a-cop, or a security guard. With a higher Connection rating, the doors open to undercover cop, desk sergeant, detective, agent (as in FBI or CIA), commander or even as someone as high as the director of a national bureau. Specialists within a department may focus on a specific activity like magical investigations, Matrix crimes, or public relations, and could be any other type except maybe Criminal.
