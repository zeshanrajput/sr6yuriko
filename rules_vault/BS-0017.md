## qualities

## Negative Quality: Leaking Essence

Your aura is changing in such a way that it is beginning to diverge from your body. This is not immediately debilitating, but is certainly a long-term concern. Note that this divergence can occur at any age; it is only when the divergence becomes too great that the connection between the body and Essence starts to break. This is uncommon enough that an aura with this condition will be strange and unusual to anyone who can sense auras.

**Bonus:** 0 Karma

- At the beginning of each month, you lose 0.1 Essence.

- At the end of each month, you gain 3 Karma.

- At any time, a player can declare that their Essence leak has stopped. This removes this quality.

- Optionally, if a runner has undergone significant stress, the gamemaster may have a conversation with a player about whether they would like to take this quality.
