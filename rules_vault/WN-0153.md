## 4: dropping dark

About a year ago, the James family was living the corporate dream. Mr. and Mrs. James had key roles at Microdeck Industries, and their careers were advancing. That all changed when their oldest child was diagnosed with a rare disease. Neither magical nor modern medicine were able to offer a cure, only a treatment. The disease is not life-threatening, only life-changing, but it provided an opening for Spinrad Global to come in and leverage their love for their child. On a promise that Spinrad Global could help their son if they helped reverse-engineer a piece of technology, they moved to Manhattan.
