## Additional Chimera Types

## Doppleganger

Ultimate infiltrators that look completely normal. The only way to identify a doppelganger is by their purple chimera blood.

**Note:** While the stats below represent a human, other metatypes exist, and their attributes can be adjusted accordingly. Additional skills can also be added as appropriate.
