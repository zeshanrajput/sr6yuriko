## Smuggler/Coyote

In some ways, this background is like a stealth courier. They still want to get things from point A to point B, but it’s about more than blinding speed. Sometimes speed is important, but it can also be tremendously useful to pick routes that will not be seen. While a full-on interceptor or stoppo may not have taken the time to learn all the tunnels and waterways of Seattle’s Underground, the smuggler absolutely will have. They often benefit from partnering with a blocker to take eyes away from what they’re doing, but they don’t always have that luxRACINGury. So sometimes, they have to rely on their skills and their route. Knowing the gaps in security surveillance, squeezing through places no one thought they could, and relying on routes everyone else forgot about are some of their key tricks.

Smugglers and coyotes don’t have to find a path to the shadows—they’re already there. They just need to find someone who will pay them what their skills are worth.
