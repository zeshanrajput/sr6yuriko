## Humanis Paramilitary Sergeant

## (Professional Rating 3)

Weekend warriors who have proven themselves to be dedicated to the cause, effective in combat, and capable of keeping their cool under fire are provided with more expensive augmentations and positions as sergeants. They are also given the responsibility to lead and train a squad of soldiers, and they are responsible for keeping the violent tendencies of zealots in check. Managing public perception of events is more important to the cause than killing enemies.

Unless ambushed away from their buddies, the Humanis Sergeant will fight alongside a squad of Humanis Grunts. Their first actions in combat will be to move to cover, take cover, and ready their weapon. In subsequent rounds they will fire from cover, using the smartlink to negate the disadvantage. They will spend any additional available Minor Actions to Take Aim. They are unlikely to panic if a fight goes poorly; instead, they will attempt a measured retreat and escape. If the sergeant is killed, the grunts under their command are highly likely to panic. Whenever possible, combine the attacks of other grunts to assist the sergeant’s attacks using Grunt Group rules.
