_Example: The Fragging Unicorns are playing a single gig at Dante’s Inferno. The band has five members tonight; with hits on the Performance tests as follows: Rust-bucket: 5, MBT: 4, Dark ’n’ Stuff: 6, Abilene: 4, and Rev. Blackfeather: 6, for an average of 5. They are performing songs from an older album,_ Bug City Sushi _, which had a rating of 6. Multiplying their average Performance tests (5) by the rating of the album (6) gives a Performance rating of 30, making it an average performance._
