## Induced Dormancy

Some condition or substance can force some critters into a coma-like state of suspended animation. The condition can be a lack of air or exposure to a certain rare substance such as orichalcum. The length of exposure needed to cause the critter to become dormant varies and is mentioned in the critter’s description. The critter will awaken quickly, usually within a minute, once the condition or substance is removed.
