## Tests

In a _Shadowrun_ game, you will at some point attempt something that either other people, the laws of the universe, or both do not want you to accomplish. To see if you succeeded or not, you’ll need to perform a test, where you generate a dice pool, roll to get a result, and then see if you succeeded. There are three basic types of tests: Simple, Opposed, and Extended.
