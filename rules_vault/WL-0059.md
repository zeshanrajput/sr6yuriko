## **How to Deal**

**The best way to deal with ground wolves is to stay out of their way. Unless you go looking for trouble or fnd yourself in the wrong place at the wrong time, they’re not likely to attack you. If it does come down to fghting them, fnd solid ground. They can sense you through the earth, pass through it as if it were air, and even cause it to rise up and engulf you. The ground is their greatest advantage. If you can get up onto a vehicle or some paved ground, or better yet, up into the air, you’ll fnd them much easier to deal with.**

**Fortunately ground wolves aren’t particularly tough, lacking any form of natural armor or unusual resistances to damage. Almost any weapon, toxin, or spell is likely to be effective against them. The trouble is hitting them in the frst place, since they can just sink down into the ground to escape. Ground wolves are also particularly vulnerable to fre-based attacks.**
