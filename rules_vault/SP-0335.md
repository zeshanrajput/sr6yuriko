## **Game Rules for Chimera**

**The process the Disians use to create chimera utilizes a simplifed form of their mana-storage technology. This stored mana can imbue their creations with magic-like abilities even if the subject itself does not actually have a Magic rating. The powers can duplicate critter powers, adept abilities, or spells that will function as per their rulebook descriptions.**

**If the power granted to a chimera requires a Magic attribute for a test and the chimera does not have a listed Magic attribute, assume a Magic rating of 4. Any innate spells use a Sorcery skill of 4.**

**In addition to powers that duplicate critter powers, spells, and adept abilities, anything listed as an augmentation will function as the listed piece of cyberware, bioware, or any other form of technology, but are actually magical abilities that duplicate the features and provide the same benefts.**

**Some of the newly discovered chimeras also have the critter weakness Purple Blood, exactly matching the Doppelganger chimera; this makes them slightly more detectable than chimeras that do not have this weakness.**
