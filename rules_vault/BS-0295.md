## Rabies

- **Vector:** Injection

- **Speed:** 1D6 weeks (3)

- **Power:** 12

- **Nature:** Viral

- **Effect (Ongoing):** For 1D6 days, the individual gains the Fatigued I status. Detection during incubation or during the ongoing period of the disease means the individual can take an antiviral, which provides a +4 dice pool bonus for the Disease Resistance test. If the resistance test is successful, then the disease is treated and cured.

- **Effect (Final):** After completing the ongoing effect of the disease, the individual enters the final phase, where death is certain. The only way to avoid death, which occurs in the next 1D6 days, is by burning Edge.
