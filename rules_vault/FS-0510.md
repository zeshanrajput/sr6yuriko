## Vandalism

Everyone’s seen thugs break store windows to convince the owner to sign up for a protection racket. (I’m sure a few of you have even been the thugs doing the breaking.) For our purposes, “vandalism” includes anything that involves wrecking the target’s stuff instead of wrecking the target (or their ability to harm you) directly. Usually, vandalism serves to both inflict harm by making the target pay to replace things, and stoke their fear that
