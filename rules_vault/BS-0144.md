## Lower Body Replacement

Also called liminal bodies or one-half shells, a lower body replacement involves the restructuring of the user’s body from the abdomen down. The augmentation incorporates a free rating 1 biowaste storage system (see p. 40) because of the requisite removal of the user’s genitalia and the terminal end of their digestive system. The user gains four Physical Condition Monitor boxes. The lower body replaces both legs with one very large cyberlimb that can come in numerous different forms. As with other cyberlimbs, a lower body replacement begins with 2 Strength and 2 Agility. Any augmentations that must be implanted as pairs in the cyberlegs—for example, hydraulic jacks, must have two copies installed into a lower body replacement.



**Centaur:** A quadrupedal lower body is often equine in styling, but it can also take other forms such as feline or draconic. No matter the appearance, a centaur lower-body replacement sets the user’s base movement rate to 10/20/+4 and doubles the amount of weight the user can lift or carry.

**Serpentine:** The lower body is replaced by a mermaid’s tail, an alligator body, or a simple snake body with or without dozens of centipede-like segments. With this lower body, add 3 meters to the distance for each net hit on a swimming test.

**Wheels:** A wheeled lower body can be arranged as a two, three, or four wheeled chassis. With a wheeled lower body replacement, their movement rate is replaced with rules used by vehicles: the user has an Acceleration equal to the augmentation’s Agility x 2, and a Top Speed equal to its Strength x 10. The user still rolls Athletics rather than Piloting for any relevant tests. The user is treated as a vehicle if targeted by the Movement critter power (p. 226, _SR6_ ). Wheels are incompatible with speed boosters (see above).

**Spider:** This kind of lower body replacement is short and wide with numerous bug-like legs. This augmentation grants a bonus Minor Action when the user spends a Minor Action to cancel the Prone status, and gains Edge on any climbing tests.

**Cyberware overdrive** : The lower body replacement gains +2 Strength and +2 Agility. These bonuses may exceed the augmented maximum.
