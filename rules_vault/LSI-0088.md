## Training Room

What do you need to practice your skills or hone your mental and physical well-being? Each training room (gym, firing range, study, etc.) can be used to help with advancement of one skill or attribute that may be redefined each month. If you purchase multiple Training Room assets, you can apply it to an additional skill or attribute. Public assets are outside of your home, and must be shared with other members or subscribers (p. 68, _SR6_ ), and reduce the training time for the advancement by 20 percent. Private assets are located within or near your residence, and reduce training time by 50 percent. In order to gain any benefit from this asset, you must maintain it (pay LP each month) throughout the required training time.

**Cost: 1 LP each (public), or 2 LP each (private)**
