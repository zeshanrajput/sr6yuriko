## Oddities and Anomalies

With the players set up, let’s take a look at some recent events.

Upon learning of a prototype known as the “Guizhen I” (Sleeper I) project and the opportunity and market it would open up, an audit of the company and research team was performed. Within this audit, evidence of significant, aggressive, and extremely efficient stock manipulation was found. Very efficient and very precise, but too far-reaching for such a small corporation as this. It was also apparent that these transactions were happening from no fewer than two parties. The effort to just keep up with these transactions forced more direct investigation into the trading agencies, and that iswhen it became evident that significantly nastier plots were afoot.

The first agency discovered was Hohokam Financial Services, a small agency specializing in futures trading, portfolio management, and small business and personal loans. It was discovered this was a front for the Broken-Reed Triad. Specifically, the transactions were traced to Wang Jing; her pair of cyberdecks had logged nearly two hundred hours in the previous week.
