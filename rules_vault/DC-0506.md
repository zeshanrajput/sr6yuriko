## Environmental Adaptation

All vehicles, regardless of type, are meant to operate in a fairly limited environment. With this enhancement, you can choose one extreme environment, and the vehicle is adapted to and functions with no penalty as well as providing basic life support for its passengers. Environmental types include underwater, Arctic, volcanic, high altitude, and outer space. This enhancement can only be purchased once. Cost Multiplier: x2
