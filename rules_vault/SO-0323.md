## Game Information

An injector pen can be loaded with any drug or toxin that has the contact or injection vectors but can only hold a single dose. Loading the pen requires access to a biotech kit or medkit with a rating of 4 or better. Loading by hand requires a successful Biotech + Logic (3) skill test. Failure results in ruining the pen and the dose, while a critical glitch results in the person loading the pen dosing themselves.

Pre-loaded injector pens are also available for sale.

When attempting to dose a target, a user can employ brute force, trickery, or stealth. The brute force approach requires at least one net hit on a Close Combat + Agility attack with an Attack Rating of 2. If successful, the attack deals no damage, but the drug or toxin is injected.

The other option is to approach a target unobserved utilizing a Stealth (Sneaking) + Agility test opposed by the target’s Perception + Intuition. Or the attacker can attempt to lull the target into thinking they mean no harm with a Con (Acting) + Charisma test opposed by Judge Intentions or Con + Intuition. Once in position, the attacker makes a Stealth (Palming) + Agility test opposed by Perception + Intuition to inject the target. Only one net hit is needed to dose the target. A target may roll a Perception + Intuition (2) test to notice they’ve been stabbed.
