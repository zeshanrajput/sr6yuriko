## Mass Produced

Henry Ford knew what he was doing when he developed the mass production assembly line. Even though much of the technology has changed, it is still the most economical means of production. Corporation utilizing the massive facilities this requires can produce a vehicle much faster and cheaper than any mechanic in even the best shop. Cost Multiplier: x0.5
