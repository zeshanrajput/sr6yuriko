## **Retreat**

**Knowing when to keep your critters from recklessly endangering themselves can easily make the difference between life or death for**



**them. The trigger for this command is usually a verbal shout, as it is likely to be given in a chaotic situation in which the critter may not be able to notice gestures or other cues. Once commanded, the critter will return to their handler or a designated safe location or vehicle.**
