## **Is the Probe action’s hits subject to the +4 Augmented maximum?**

No. Any accumulated hits can be applied on the Backdoor Entry. As part of the Probe action being an Extended test, be aware that the defense dice pool doesn’t reduce by 1 on each subsequent round, but the attacker’s dice pool does reduce by 1 on each round.
