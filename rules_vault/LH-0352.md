## Prototype Tech

The Disian Shadow War caused a lot of cutting-edge prototypes to be introduced to the world. Some were ready for field testing, while others were still in the “will-it-work?” stage. None of these items are available for purchase through normal or even shadow channels, but they do exist, and the people who have them control who else has access.
