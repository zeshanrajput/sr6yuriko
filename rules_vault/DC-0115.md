## Powerboats

Powerboats are smaller craft, from one-person pleasure craft like wetbikes and jet skis to smaller security craft. These are almost exclusively opentopped, are always on the small side, are fragging fast, and often use waterjet technology to achieve speed and maneuverability unmatched by anything else on the water.
