## Pantheon Industries

This Detroit-based startup came out of nowhere just a few short years ago, and they’ve been making serious waves lately with their expanding catalogue of Matrix software products. Their first series of products, known as “Tac-Apps” and meant for military Matrix use, was light-years ahead of anything that heavy-hitters such as Renraku or MCT were putting out. To accompany their increasing success, Pantheon is rumored to be scouting out locations for new offices and research facilities in Seattle.
