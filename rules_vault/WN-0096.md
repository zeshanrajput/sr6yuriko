## Seoulpa Rings

New York’s three major Seoulpa Rings manage to stay in power the way any good business does— they provide a service that nobody can or is willing to provide. The Rings deal exclusively in sex trafficking, bodyware smuggling, and exotic drugs. From time to time, they’ll drift into the more traditional syndicate activities like racketeering, but the Yakuza, or more often the Mafia, are quick to put a stop to it. The New York Rings are small operations. The largest of the three is the Min-Park Ring, which clocks in at around three hundred members spread across the city. The Gangjun Ring is slightly smaller but also more concentrated in Battery City while occasionally drifting uptown as far as the International District. The Yeong Ring is fewer than one hundred members and stays contained on the West End.
