## Naming Gives You Power

While working on the extents of the exchange-based warfare, I decided to monitor the code to determine its capacity for improvement. Like many modern forms of soft and hard programming, it is stronger when connected with wireless access to the Matrix.

To get deeper into its functions, we should discuss its name. In the Kochi Prefecture of Japan, a legend exists about a phantom light that is said to be the ghost, or the soul, of a metahuman. It supposedly manifests with the capacity to do great harm to others and enact vengeance, directed or otherwise. Similar names pop up in other cultures around the world, “Kai qi bi” being one in Chinese culture. This has been Anglicized as “Kechibi,” a word we’re quickly becoming familiar with.
