## The Party Itself

The theme of this year’s Gala is Convergence: Where Magic and Technology Meet in Fashion. All of the guests will be draped in couture that has both a magical and a high-tech element in play. These priceless bits of fashion will likely contain never-before-seen technologies that every company worth its rating would love a closer look at. However, the Gala is a closed event. No one not on the guest list is allowed inside. No video or photography of what happens inside the Gala is made available to the public, except footage from professional and accredited journalists. Being on the list is the only way to find out what the elite do when nobody is watching.

Though arrivals start at 5:20, the Met Gala goes from 6 to 9 p.m. Several of the most famous people in the world are in attendance, so security at the event is airtight. Anyone getting through needs to have the proper clearances tied to their CityPass. Fortunately for the runners, the first call of this stage comes from a Mr. Johnson who can provide those clearances. Begin with **A Rather Ghoulish Event.** Once that run is underway, have the fixers reach out simultaneously with requests for **Save the Last Dance** and **Leaving So Soon** _._ Given the timeframe, the players will need to choose between those two options. They will be unable to accomplish both without splitting up (a famously bad idea).
