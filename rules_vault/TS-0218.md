## **Ares Foamtek**

**In a concerted effort to broaden their brand and gain some important public image credit, Ares recently reinvested some R&D time into their old Fogger Glop Cannon technology. The results are the new Ares Foamtek products, a quickexpanding and hardening foam that is designed to contain a target without causing any damage. They have three delivery systems: man-portable carbine, grenade, and vehicle-mounted cannon. While each delivery system has its own advantages and uses, Foamtek itself works the same in each. Make a standard attack for the weapon type to determine effect. One net hit inficts the Hobbled status on the target; if net hits equal or exceed the target’s Agility, or Body for vehicles and drones, they are Immobilized (see p. 52,** _**SR6**_ **). The net hits from multiple attacks are cumulative if applied before the foam hardens. Foamtek will completely harden at the end of the following turn. Once hardened, the status effect is increased. Hobbled becomes Immobilized, and Immobilized becomes Stilled. Hardened Foamtek has a Structure rating of 10 (pg. 113,** _**SR6**_ **). Foamtek will dissolve and break down on its own after one hour, but dissolves in one turn if the catalyst solvent is applied. Ares**
