## Elements

The essential element of pulling a Badger is understanding of the mark. Not everyone is vulnerable to the same shame. This isn’t the twentieth century; monogamy is a 50/50 shot, so infidelity might not be a factor, assuming they’re in a relationship at all. Sure, a politician or someone with a politically motivated marriage might be threatened, but it’s contextual.

You’ll be hearing that a lot. Context is everything when it comes to blackmail, and it boils down to two parties: the sensitive points for them and the party you threaten to reveal them to. Take sex for example, which is probably the most traditionally scandalous. It might be what they’re into, or cheating, or the fact they’re seen in a sexual context in the first place. It’s got to be something antithetical to how they present themselves to the world, how it ties into something they value, such as their reputation. A conservative politician can’t be seen to be outside the confines of how their constituency sees them on a moral or behavioral level. Or money, where there’s proof of them embezzling.

- The situation doesn’t need to be inherently compromising, but it does have to have the appearance of impropriety. But if we’re speaking honestly, it’s easier to get a mark to fork over the money if they’re truly doing something they’re not supposed to be.

- **>** Fianchetto

Who their reputation affects is the second element. That conservative constituency that reviles non-traditional exploits? Another mark might have a reputation as a tough guy to maintain, a brutal killer with no weakness or sentiment. And you’ve got proof positive he’s a big softie in some way. The embezzler is probably keeping some for themselves, but maybe that’s their job for someone higher up. Figure out who will be disappointed, alienated, or enraged at the information.

To catch up, the blackmail is the bullet, and the audience is the head.

So how do we load the gun?

It’s a pretty simple honeypot op, really. What makes it the Badger is that you are actively soliciting the mark to do the thing you’ll blackmail them with. You set the stage, seduce the target (sexually, traditionally, but it could be anything), and makesure it all gets recorded as well as possible, receipts and all. The pastor or politician who is running on temperance has to indulge in drugs or booze. The committed family woman has to do something that violates the trust she sells herself on. Corporate or syndicate loyalty has to be compromised, maybe to a rival or oversight authority. Find who trusts them, then create “proof” they broke that trust with the people they depend on for what they value (fame, credit, their job, their family, etc.). Everyone has pressure points. Find someone or something that’s important to them and squeeze.

- Pretty brutal way to take someone down. Bodies heal, but violating someone like that can do a real number.
