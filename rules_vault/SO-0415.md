## Professional Note 09: Trust

Being a professional whoes job it is to basically manipulate others, the face must, more than another person on the team, take precautions to ensure that they do not betray the trust of their team. Every operation, even the simplest of milk runs, is an intricate house of cards, and trust is the element that holds that house together. Each member relies on the others not only for the execution of their individual roles but also for support, cover, and protection. If that trust is lost, it can lead to tension, suspicion, conflicts, and grudges, and eventually the team imploding. Or worse. And part of maintaining that trust is knowing how each member of your team operates in regard to how much they want to know about certain thing as well as how they would react if certain things weren’t disclosed, or worse, they were outright lied to. Some people may not care, others may see that as a breach of trust and a deal-breaker.
