## **From Emerald City, what are the benefits of Advanced Combat Training (p. 110)?**

The game effect could be confusing. When you use a combat maneuver (FS, p. 86), you gain +1 Minor Action and +2 dice pool on the combat maneuver test. In essence, this is a refund on spending the Minor Action to be able to make the combat maneuver test.
