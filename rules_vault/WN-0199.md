## Alayna Gale

Perhaps the most famous trans troll designer of the era, Gale burst on to the fashion scene eleven years ago by arriving in nothing but black stockings, red heels, a sheer top and a long red nano-fiber scarf that morphed into a form-fitting dress as they posed on the Met Gala red carpet. Since that debut, Gayle has been a staple of the Gala, breaking boundaries and providing jaw-dropping looks each year.

Lithe with short spiraling horns and a mane of shock blue hair, Gale resembles a fomorian. They always wear black with one additional color added to the palette. Gayle is easygoing but extremely talkative and easily distracted. Their favorite topic of discussion is the recent partnership with Evo creating the new fashion line Blü.

Gale’s Met Gala design is an homage to the Lenape people who founded Manhattan. They’ve designed a column dress with a etched with circuitry that mimics mutable tribal lettering, writing the story of the Lenape people across their body. The magical element includes golden bracers tastefully highlighted with raw chunks of orichalcum. A set of drawings at the back of the safe suggest some sort of watcher spirit will be perched on their shoulder during the Gala.
