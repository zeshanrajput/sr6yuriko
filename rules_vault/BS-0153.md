## Sinister/Dexter Coprocessor

Because cyberlimbs are interconnected with the user’s neural network, any natural handedness they have carries over to the artificial limb. A user’s deficiency in using their “off-handed” limb can be compensated for via specialized electronics added to the cyberlimb’s standard neural interface. A sinister or dexter coprocessor allows a cyberlimb on the left or right side of the body to avoid any penalty for off-hand use (p. 110, _SR6_ ).
