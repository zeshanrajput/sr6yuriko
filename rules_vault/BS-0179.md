## Red Biotech

And this is what everyone’s here for. Red covers the medical field, bioware, pharmaceutical, and my personal favorite, bio-sculpting. In short, if it deals with the metahuman body, it’s red biotech. It isn’t a steady moneymaker due to volume like grey or green, but it’s blatantly marketed and highly profitable. Because who wouldn’t spend every nuyen they had to save their parents, themselves, or to make either look like they were twenty again?
