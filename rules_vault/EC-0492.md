## Renton Center Mall (Downtown Renton)

Catering to the upper middle class, the Renton Center Mall advertises the best shopping experience and the hottest brands in Renton. Having only two floors, it’s smaller than many other shopping malls, but with boutique shops like Whole New You and quality merchandisers like Tjang’s Lore Store, the Renton Center Mall manages to keep its status as a prime shopping destination. The mall’s small paranatural zoo and fun park on the second floor also make this a prime location for family outings.
