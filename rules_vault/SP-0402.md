**Notes : The power maul and power fst use the same basic rules as a power hammer with the following exceptions. A power hammer must be used two-handed unless the user has Strength 4 or higher, then it may be used single-handed.**

**A power maul requires Body 4 or more and a full-body Strength 4 or more to use. Simply having high-Strength cybernetic**

**limbs is insufcient to use a power maul.**

**A power fst is always a single-handed weapon.**

**The base DV listed is for a strike without charges—from either the foating or fxed striking face.**

**Using charges under the striking plate increases the DV by +1 and the AR by +2 for each charge used. With the power hammer and power fst, the maximum is two charges for +2 DV and +4 AR.**

**With the power maul, the maximum is four charges for +4 DV and +8 AR.**

**A smartgun system may be added to a power weapon but provides no benefts beyond an ammo counter and the ability to wirelessly eject spent clips.**
