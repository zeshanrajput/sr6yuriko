## Grease Monkey (Engineering Spec.)

When you were young, you would much rather make a trip to the junkyard than the toy store (possibly because kids in the barrens never have enough money to buy anything at the toy store). As you got older, the allure of discarded vehicle and machine parts on the heaps in junkyards was far stronger than the promise of some new toy.

You not only loved monkeying around with these items, but you could often get the devices to work.

- **Cost:** 4 or 8 Karma

- **Game Effect:** Choose an Engineering specialization. At the 4 Karma level of this quality, the Base repair time is reduced by 10 minutes. At 8 Karma, the Base Threshold is reduced by 1.
