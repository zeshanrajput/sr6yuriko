## Part One

The first job is contracted via a virtual meeting at Sunken City. This virtual hacker hangout is well-known as neutral ground, good for meeting virtual Mr. Johnsons or hanging out anonymously with your hacker pals. A specialized program overwrites every persona that enters the club, changing their appearance to some character from the classic _Dawn of Atlantis_ game series. As the gameplay swaps between adventures in ancient Atlantis and a modern hunt for the sunken city, the overwrites can be wide-ranging.

The meeting is set for 22:00, and Mr. Johnson tells the runners they will send the team a message so they know which persona to look for. Mr. Johnson’s overwritten persona looks like a red-robed cultist wearing a plain silver mask with a cherubic face.

The job offer is 10,000 nuyen for the team for a single day, twenty-four hours of visual surveillance on a single target. Negotiations run as normal, with Mr. Johnson having an Influence dice pool of 12; net hits increase the payment by 1,000 nuyen each, to a max of 15,000 nuyen. Mr. Johnson offers no other specific info but will tell them the target is no one famous, the runners must not get detected, and they must not interfere or interact with the target.

Once a price is settled on, Mr. Johnson sends over a datafile with a name, home address, commlink number, and picture of the target. Surveillance is to begin at midnight and run for twenty-four hours. The runners are told they need to meet back at Sunken City before 01:00 after the surveillance to hand off the footage for the pay.
