## Ordo Arcanoarchaeologist

## (Elf, Professional Rating 6)

The Ordo seeks arcane power through any means possible and has found the study of mystical relics, ancient or modern, to be a profitable path to advancement. Whether found at a field dig at odds with the Atlantean Foundation or bidding at an auction, these specialists and their retinues acquire their coveted prizes by all means necessary.
