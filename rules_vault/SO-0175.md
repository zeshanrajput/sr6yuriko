## Effects

Different marks respond to different stimuli. Some will need the tough sell, “You better pay for that or I’m breaking my foot in your hoop,” or, “Do you know who this was for?” Others go for pity. “This was food for my kids. This was all we had, down to the last nuyen. How am I gonna feed my family?” Whether it’s fear, pity, or charity, they transfer the funds, maybe a little extra to make it right. You let them know you’re square, and you vanish. Easy.
