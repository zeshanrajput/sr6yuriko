## **Posted by: Bull**

**has mentioned something called “Moneyspace”** and social dynamics, but that’s a deeper dive into psychology than I care to make. People listen to all kinds of wild stories about Those People, building up a sort of golem composed of half-truths and fl at-out lies, then get skittish and want to stay away from Those People. When they run into someone who doesn’t match the golem in their head, they make a separate category for “good ones” who aren’t like Those People, those who have managed to overcome their inborn corruption somehow. This lets them still distrust Those People but leave room for the “good ones.”
