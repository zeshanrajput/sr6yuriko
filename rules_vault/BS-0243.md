## Electrosense Organs

This piece of bioware is based on the platypus. It consists of three implants of distinct receptor cells on the front and sides of the skull. This cannot be used with a cyberskull or the awareness bioware.

This augmentation replicates the ability critters have to sense electrical fields, such as when they are near certain electronic devices, engines, or security systems. This organ sense functions at a distance equal to (Body + Intuition) in meters. A Major Action is required to use electrosense; roll Perception + Intuition and check the hits against the Electrosense Table. This is a passive sense, with no broadcasting noise originating from the individual.

A more sensitive version of this bioware is available. In addition to having access to the electrosense column in the chart below, the character also gains the information presented under the technosense information column.
