## Horizon Freedom

The Freedom range of sailing boats by Horizon are little more than enlarged dinghies, capable of holding a handful of people depending on the size in question. They are great for kids and new sailors to learn the ropes (pun totally intended) and get a feel for the sea, and for feeling the exhilaration of skimming across the water with nothing but some ropes and a sail to keep you going. It’s a wonderful feeling, something every sailor dreams about.

Too small to have an inboard motor, sturdy enough to mount one of the small mini outboard motors (roughly the size of a watermelon), and small enough to also row with oars if you wanted to, these make great stealth craft. I once spent a very amusing night watching what I can only assume was a team of runners use a trio of these to gain entry to a waterfront corporate facility. A dinghy is not the ideal getaway vehicle, as the poor slots found out, but if they’d not had to make a hot getaway, they would have been using the ideal craft.
