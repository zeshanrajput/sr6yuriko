## Raptor Foot

Another form cyberfeet can take is an avian appendage that has four taloned toes, with one facing backwards. It is very hard to find footwear for raptor feet, but the augmentation allows for great traction on all kinds of surfaces. If a pair of raptor feet is combined with digitigrade legs, they double the digitigrade legs’ bonuses to movement. The talons also serve as non-retractable hand razors (p. 290, _SR6_ ) and cyber implant weapons. Raptor feet cannot be combined with any other foot augmentations.
