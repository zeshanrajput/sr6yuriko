## The Memory of Water

One of the reasons that the ambassadors from other planes come to the Northern Pole of Water is that the citadel is also an archive of events. Witness statements and testimonies are recorded in millimeter-thin layers of ice within the structure of the citadel. This recorded concept is found in all structures and landscape throughout the plane, though it’s a little more difficult to find the correct information in liquid and gas form.
