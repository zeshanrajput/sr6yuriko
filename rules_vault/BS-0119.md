## Cyberwings

Gravtech vehicles have only been available to the impossibly wealthy for a few years, but that amount of time is an eternity when it comes to state-of-the-art. Evo was the first to successfully miniaturize the technology for deployment on apersonal scale, but it wasn’t long before knockoffs became available.

When extended for flight, cyberwings feature wide and flat lift panels that are laid out similarly to the feathers of an avian wing. Folded cyberwings are about the size of a worn backpack and prevent the user from gaining or spending Edge on tests to squeeze through tight spaces. The wingspan of extended cyberwings is almost four meters, so walking through a standard doorway is usually only possible while entering sideways. Extended cyberwings allow a flying movement rate of 10/30/+3. Cyberwings can only store enough power to generate flight for (Body) minutes.

**Cyberware overdrive** : When using cyberware overdrive with the Sprint Major Action while flying, gain +2 dice for the Athletics (Flight) test. One of these dice must be a wild die.

**Wireless bonus** : Cyberwings recharge one minute’s worth of power per hour when they are wirelessly engaged.
