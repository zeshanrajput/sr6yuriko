## **> Bandit**

**The main point I want to make about mundane animals is that they are easy to overlook, which is something you can use to your advantage, but also a set of assumptions you ought to challenge. These critters aren’t likely to pose a major threat like a wyvern or hell hound might, but they can spot you, sound alarms, and serve a wide range of other functions. Don’t ignore them just because they don’t have any supernatural or technological mojo.**
