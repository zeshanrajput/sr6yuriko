## optional rules

## Self-Improvement

Self-Improvement assets represent therapy and special training that helps you work through your personal problems or help you get closer to who and what you want to become. For every month that you maintain this asset, choose a quality and reduce the Karma cost to purchase or buy off that quality by one Karma. This can only reduce the cost to the quality’s original Karma cost (or bonus Karma). If the Karma cost of purchasing or buying off the quality is not doubled for any reason, this asset has no effect.

**Cost: 1 LP per quality**
