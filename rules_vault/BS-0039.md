## optional rule: state of the art

Some players enjoy greater depth for their games. For those players, we offer these optional rules for maintaining their equipment, cyberware, and other items as state of the art. This is intended to represent the effort and cost needed to stay on the bleeding edge.

Note that these rules are intended to be used in consultation with the gamemaster. Without moderation, it is easy for state-of-the-art rules to punish heavily augmented runners, while leaving unaugmented runners less out of pocket.
