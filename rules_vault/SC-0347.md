## eagle shaman

## playing advice

## Roleplaying

Eagle shamans hold themselves and others to high standards. You perceive and understand so much, and you take it all in with a completeness that others find astounding. Your supernatural aptitudes lean toward improving your senses, both physical and astral, but you are also more sensitive to magical and mundane pollution. Your mentor spirit is driven to seek out and eradicate sources of corruption and pollution, and this wars with the everyday sensibilities of an urban metahuman. You are constantly surrounded by examples of pollution as well as corruption, and to act against them all would be ultimately fatal as well as futile. And so you have learned to find some sort of balance, allowing you to set aside some passions so you can actually have a life and get drek done. Life ain’t easy for an Eagle shaman. You have a lot to watch out for, and knowing when and how to act is often your greatest difficulty. Constantly on alert, you watch as carefully as you can, but you also realize that you can’t notice everything. Does the thought of what you might be missing cause you anxiety, or do you put it out of your mind with ease so that you can focus on what you can observe?
