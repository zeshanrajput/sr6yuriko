## **Other Locations**

**As they grow, Convergence is opening satellite offces so Convergence can attract talent without forcing them to come to DeeCee. Currently, they have offces in Hong Kong, Johannesburg, Metrópole, and Amsterdam, and they are exploring sites in Sydney. Those offces are currently simple affairs, suites with a few private rooms, and not the luxurious diviner haven that their DeeCee building is. Still, you can fnd quality hackers and diviners at each spot, and if Convergence continues to grow, you can expect these offces to grow and become more interesting as well.**

- **These offices are about more than just not making new recruits travel to the UCAS. Each city was selected because it had nearby alchera and metaplanar gateway activity. Convergence clearly believes these phenomena are part of whatever is going to happen, so they’re keeping an eye on them.**
