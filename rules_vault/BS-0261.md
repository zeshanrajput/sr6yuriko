## Natural Venom

A set of venom glands requires a way to deliver the venom. Therefore, this must be combined with biospikes, fangs, frog tongue, quills, stingers, or thorns. If used as an injector, the attack must score two net hits, which must be used for the injection rather than to increase damage.
