## Medical Facilities

There are six clinics and hospitals, including Deireadh an Tuartheil, within the district. Half of them won’t touch you unless you have a DocWagon contract (which means you need a SIN). Deireadh an Tuartheil is for elves only, leaving Good Samaritan Hospital in Loveland and Guardian Angel Health Care in Puyallup for everyone else. Good Samaritan is a UCAS-contracted, military-run hospital that trains medics. Care there is cheap and impersonal, and long-term care is not available. They patch you up and give you clean bandages and water before sending you on your way. Guardian Angel is a collection of street docs working in the same complex.
