## People Waking Up

To the world at large, the Disians, the Shadow War—none of it exists. Everything they saw, the destruction incurred, and the lives lost were nothing more than the results of freak yet natural phenomenon, or the coordinated acts of brutal terrorism. That narrative, those so-called facts have been rammed down the throat of every person with access to modern media. And for the most part, they’ve been successful. The world at large is more than willing to buy into all this drek if it means they get back to their normal lives that much sooner.

And yet …

For some this narrative blitz for one reason or another didn’t work; the powers that be might have overplayed their hand this time as more and more are starting to question what they’re being told. Granted, there aren’t enough to overthrow the system, but enough where those who aren’t swallowing the lies are finding others of like mind (for better or worse). More and more people are doing their due diligence and determining for themselves if they’re being fed a load of drek or not.

These same people are also turning to alternate methods of information, which in turn reveals the shadows they’ve been told were only urban legends or the work of fiction. And being curious fraggers, they’re looking, and more importantly they’re _questioning_ .

I honestly don’t know what will come of this, or if anything ever will. But I remember something I was told long ago: sometimes an avalanche is caused by only a few stones set in motion.
