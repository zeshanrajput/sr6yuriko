## Drug Interactions Example

A couple of weeks later, Burn is on a run where he has been tasked with capturing a barghest. He’s done the research and knows that they can invoke fear, so he picks up a dose of brass balls to go with his normal hit of jazz. He takes the popper of brass balls and heads into where the barghest is. When he sees movement, without thinking he takes a hit of jazz. Time to roll on the Drug Interaction table. He rolls 1D6 and gets a three, so luckily there is no side effect, but that could have gone sideways.
