## Push

(Nanohi, Rush)

- **Vector:** Ingested

- **Speed:** 1 minute

- **Duration:** (15 – Body) minutes, minimum 1 minute

- **Power:** 11

- **Effect:** None

Push works as a no-frills drug that gets you high by releasing a flood of endorphins and serotonin. The effect has been compared to eating large amounts of chocolate or having an orgasm. The active drugs that create the effect are theobromine and cathinone. Excessive use can lead to hyperactivity and potential genetic mutation. When the drug wears off, the user suffers from apathy for 1D6 minutes before returning to normal and craving their next fix.
