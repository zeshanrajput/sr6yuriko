## T-Rex Bio-Drone Prototype

Due in part to its resistance to training, in order to harness the power of the T-rex, Ares has resorted to using the bio-drone harness. Imagine being the rigger who gets to jump into this giant! If the T-rex is operating independently as a bio-drone, the turret mounts can each fire independently with a dice pool of 12. Each turret mount can fire twice per combat round. If riggers are jumped into the bio-drone, three riggers work together: one to pilot the T-rex, and one gunner for each turret mount.
