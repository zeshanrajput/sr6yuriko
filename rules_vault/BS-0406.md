## Rock Lizard Blood

- **Vector:** Ingestion

- **Speed:** 2D6 minutes

- **Duration:** 1D6 hours

- **Power:** 7

- **Effect:** Grants Immunity (diseases and toxins)

This blue-green paste is created from the pulp of the North American desert’s weeping tree. The compound protects the user from diseases and toxins. Once the drug wears off, the user resists (Power)P damage.
