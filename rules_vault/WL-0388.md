## **Critter Command Actions**

**Many possibilities exist when it comes to critter commands. The options presented here are examples, not a complete list of every option. If you’d like to train your critter to perform an action that isn’t covered here, discuss it with your gamemaster. Not every command is appropriate to every critter—teaching most prey animals to attack isn’t likely to work well, and a hamster isn’t going to be able to fetch you anything much larger than a marble, for example.**
