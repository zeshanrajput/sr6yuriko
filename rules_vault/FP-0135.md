**So, nowadays, the French Touch sponsors the events discreetly attached to the Grand Tour and caters it, but in exchange, it sends a happy few execs among the members to partake in the fun. And some of them get a lot of attention during those events. Louis Belaran, a known ally of the consortium, just got his invite for next year. Édouard Merise has gotten a lot of international attention since persistent rumors from last year speculated about a secret relationship with Lady Rhiannon Glendower. Hard to say if there’s any truth to this particular story. For sure, this would put her at odds with Celedyr, but maybe that’s the point of these rumors.**

**But the fact remains that it fts a pattern. Every year, a few execs from the French Touch members appear on the Grand Tour and become celebs. Whether it’s through salacious scandals (like this German ESUS exec who got taped having sex with two high-end prostitutes while knee-deep in novacoke) or just glossy exposure (Ghost knows, the tabloids love Belaran’s silly look), one way or another, someone tied to the French Touch gets a lot of attention during the Grand Tour.**

**Which always leads the media to talk about the consortium indirectly. Which, here again, I think is the point. Note that I can’t pin that particular strategy on Saurat, but I’m pretty sure he is the architect behind it. Contrary to Fauchon, who attends only the most snobbish (and clean) events of the Grand Tour, Saurat fully embraces the debauchery every year. However, no proof of his bacchanalia has ever leaked.**

- **Saurat has been recruited by the Ordo Maximus, and the organization ensures that no one can discover the perverted hobbies of their members. In exchange, he is giving them the key to the Grand Tour and even access to French Touch intel.**
