## CitiGroup

CitiGroup doesn’t make anything. What it does is handle the money of other corporations, and specifically individuals in those corporations, so they aren’t entirely beholden to the will of their employer’s bank. CitiGroup finds itself in the precarious position of being the golden parachute of a large number of corporate executives across the spectrum. Thanks to the financial rules established by the Corporate Court, megacorporations have no clear right to examine customer records without a warrant from the Corporate Court.

- That’s led to an endless string of runs on CitiGroup to pry out the sensitive ownership data on personal accounts. Corporations want to know where their employees are spending their money, especially if that spending might indicate an employee is about to be extracted.

- Icarus
