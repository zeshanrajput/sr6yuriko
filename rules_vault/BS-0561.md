## Hiring a Laboratory

Genetic engineering and gene-therapy laboratories are commercially available, but most countries restrict access to these services. That’s no problem for a megacorporation with extraterritoriality, but individual consumers will need to apply for a special license in order to hire a laboratory to customize or create a critter to their desired specifications—and lots of nuyen. The availability and nuyen cost listed in the Genetic Augmentations Table only applies when hiring a laboratory—if you own a lab and are doing the work yourself, you only need to pay for the formula for each augmentation, the laboratory, artificial womb, growth vat, and supplies. The actual augmentation process doesn’t cost any additional nuyen unless you are hiring genetic engineers to work in your own laboratory.

When you hire laboratory services, you do not need to purchase any formulae for genetic augmentations.

If you are using a fake license to purchase genetic engineering services, the rating of the fake license and the SIN that it is attached to must equal or exceed the highest availability of the desired augmentations.

No skill tests are required when you hire a laboratory to design or modify a critter, but there is a chance of failure. Roll 46 on the Genetic Mishaps table for a mundane critter, and add +6 if the critter is Awakened (whether naturally or by design). If thDe total is 9 or higher, your critter will come with one or more weaknesses. If the total is 23 or higher, the critter dies during the process. You are still expected to pay full price for a critter with weaknesses, but you are only charged half price if the critter dies.
