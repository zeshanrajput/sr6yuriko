## Fire Wire

Fire wire is the elemental version of a VCR, manifesting in fiery red chains down the character’s arms. Through the character’s contact with an elemental planar vehicle, the character can “jump in” (normal rules of rigging can be applied). The character may make cosmetic changes to the vehicle that they are jumped into. Most often they change the color of the flames, or make it start projecting flames.

A character can make a fire wire focus, allowing for the additional ability to brand their own vehicle. A character can brand only one physical vehicle with this focus. If the character travelled astrally (and thus was translated to the Plane of Fire), then the primary character’s vehicle is considered branded. The character then appears with an astral form of the vehicle, unchanged in appearance, except blazing with St. Elmo’s fire. A character who has created a fire wire focus will always have the VCR translated into fire wire in the elELEMENTALemental planes, regardless of method of travel. A character with fire wire who has traveled physically to the metaplanes can bring their branded vehicle along with them, regardless of the size of the portal/rift. The physical vehicle gains elemental armor protection against fire and St. Elmo’s fire of any color is an optional feature. The vehicle also can be powered by any consumable absorbed by the St. Elmo’s fire.

Fire wire is considered an unusual metamagic focus, with a formula that only fire elementals know and won’t give away for free. The fire wire focus requires 1 dram of True Fire, and the Karma is spent by the rigger as the focus is bonded/implanted in them. As part of the ritual, the character “jumps into” their vehicle, mystically connecting the two together. The Force of the fire wire determines the size of the vehicle that can be brought with the rigger.
