## Effects

Once you’ve obtained the paydata, it’s up to you on how you use it. Make a few purchases with their accounts and then ditch it. Slip into their systems and make whatever changes or downloads/uploads you like. Frame them with community standards-breaching posts on social media or do a bad job concealing criminal activities. You have the digital keys to their house (possibly literally). Go wild.
