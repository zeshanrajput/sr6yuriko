## Current Status

Like other corporations, Wuxing is going into a rebuilding phase, even though most of their corporate infrastructure was left intact after the war. This is more like paying a sort of penance, as well as trying to stay under the radar of the other Big Ten, while they continue their purge and regroup.

The biggest news coming out of Wuxing is that one of the famous Wu quints, Moag, is taking up a management position within the corporation when she receives her master’s degree in business in the summer of 2084. Some in the media are already crying nepotism, despite Moag graduating cum laude and her multiple works with charities while she was in grad school. But rumors suggest that after what happened with the Disians, Sharon Chiang-Wu is possibly grooming her daughter for higher aspirations because she doesn’t trust anyone else.
