## Professional Notes 01: The Ultimate Team Player

A face is more than just the person who squeezes more nuyen out of the Mr. or Ms. Johnson. A good face can help in almost every aspect of a run, from talking passwords out of marks for the decker to subtly enhancing a mage’s illusion spells by schmoozing up corporate guards. The sidebars in this chapter will help illustrate how a face can focus on assisting team members in a variety of ways.
