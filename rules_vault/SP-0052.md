## **profile: miriam ito**

**Full Name: Miriam Akari Ito Birthdate: February 23, 2057 (age 25) Sex: Female Citizenship: Corporate (MCT) Height: 1.7 meters Weight: 55 kg Hair: Varies Eyes: Blue**

**Born in Kyoto, JIS, Miriam attended MCT corporate schooling until her Awakened gifts manifested. She then attended the corporate magical academy and earned respectable marks. In March 2073, during a summoning class, something went wrong, and the instructor was forced to use combat magic against the summoned entity; Miriam was caught in the spell’s blast radius. Ofcial records say she went into a coma, but uncovered data indicates that Ito died of her wounds. Three days later, “Miriam Ito” awoke from her coma and went on to graduate cum laude from the MCT training program. From there, she served on several magical research boards and supervised several high-value research projects.**

**That’s the ofcial record. Recently uncovered data indicates that Ito is actually Disian under the direct supervision of the Auditor and assigned to “guide the hands” of MCT leadership.**
