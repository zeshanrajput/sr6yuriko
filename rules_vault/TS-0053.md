While on patrol you remain available for any nearby call but are also expected to “keep the peace.”**

**It’s not always like this, though. Most of the time, beat assignments are shifted around so offcers learn the entire district or contract area. But not always. Some savvy offcers manage to keep a certain beat and learn how to engage with the local businesses and community to make their mark. They become well-known and in some rare cases, actually well-liked.**

**The size of a patrol beat (sometimes also called a patrol zone) depends on several factors: the number of cops assigned to the district, the tech available at the precinct house and in the offcer’s cruiser, and how big the contract area is. But generally, most beats are within a fve-kilometer radius of the precinct house in more densely populated areas, and within ten kilometers in areas outside of cities. The more advanced the technology, the larger the area as drones are great for expanding patrol coverage.**

**Offcers used to keep in touch with one another via radio, but radio is now just a backup to an extensive Matrix interconnectivity between precinct house, vehicle, and individual. In more rural areas, this distance can be far greater, but in those instances, the area covered is less a “beat” and more a rural jurisdiction and handled on a per-call basis. Most patrols are handled in pairs, but it’s also not uncommon for LEP offcers to work solo (especially on rural or low-priority contracts).**

**Each shift, assignments are divided up at a daily briefng, with the choicest (and often the safest) assignments going to the offcers with the most seniority. Still, no offcer is ever more than a couple of minutes away from any sort of backup, even if that backup is nothing more than a fying drone— or maybe an astral mage.**

**Depending on personnel, some shifts are assigned additional backup, like during certain hours where there’s more vehicle traffc, or during so-called “peak” times. Like I said before, where people can’t be, drones are becoming much more common, so even if the regular cops are absent that day, it doesn’t mean something isn’t watching.**
