## **Resources**

## **Loads of Money**

**I’ve mentioned this one repeatedly, but it’s important. Nonproft reporting laws in the UCAS are not as transparent as they used to be, especially if you know how to manipulate the law and lawmakers. This means fnding the size of Convergence’s budget is no easy task. We can make a few inferences, though. A business their size would easily be spending around ffty million nuyen annually. That means the endowments of the foundations funding Convergence has to be around a billion. That’s a considerable sum of money, and it’s likely stashed in dozens of places. A good portion of it, as I mentioned before, is invested in stocks. They’ve become a large investor in OmniStar, as they believe the three pieces are not settling comfortably together, so they might be able to play different factions off each other. They’ll keep looking to diversify, but they’re learning one of the hard truths of the Sixth World—a billion doesn’t go as far as it used to.**
