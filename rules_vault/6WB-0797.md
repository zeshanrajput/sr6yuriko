## Outsider/User/Admin

A successful test gives you information about thetarget. With a tie, you can perceive the item’s icon. A single net hit will give you basic information, such as device rating, or whatever name the device or icon calls itself. Two net hits will give you more specific information, including individual attribute ratings, and what programs it is currently running. Additional hits will give you more information based on what the gamemaster wishes to reveal.

This test can also be used to attempt to spot any icons in the vicinity that are running silent.
