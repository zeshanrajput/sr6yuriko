## Silap

## (Air, Force 10)

A silap is a great beast that lives on the edges of the metaplanes of water and air, presumably crossing via the cardinal pole. Its shape is that of a dire bear, approaching five meters tall when standing on its hind legs. Massive fangs and claws arc with energy as it charges its prey. It sometimes uses a storm to separate prey from the pack, though it is

known to use the storm tactic to lure hunters off cliffs.
