## Suckered

You got played, hard, and you’re feeling all the embarrassment that goes with it. Maybe you should have seen it coming, or perhaps they were exceptionally skilled; either way it doesn’t feel great. Whenever the person who suckered you is in the same space—or someone with details of you being suckered—you take a –1 dice pool penalty per level for all tests associated with Logic or Intuition due to being more defensive.
