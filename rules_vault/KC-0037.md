## Sovereign Wealth

Plainly stated—states have, control, and can exploit massive amounts of money. Land has value because of its scarcity and the relatively exclusive nature of control. Land within boundaries has value so long as it is protected and maintained, and everything on that land has value derived from that original monopoly on control. States can leverage outright ownership and the economic value leveraged by taxation and regulation to accumulate wealth and create money, and they invest and spend that money in things like transnational megacorporations. The megacorps exist to accumulate wealth and sell products and services, and states and subsidiary entities and institutions are their largest consumers. They are also the megacorps’ biggest protectors in both the use of violent force (economic, physical, social) and everyday force (economic, physical, social). The main takeaway is that states can borrow or create wealth based

not just on direct state assets but on the potential value of everyone and everything within their borders because they have the (theoretical) power to seize everything by violence (physical arms, taxes, eminent domain, etc.). Governments change, but states don’t (compared to a few decades ago), so someone can always be selected to make those seizures to satisfy creditors. They always will because they always have.

Let’s consider some examples: some free cities, newly integrated states, and Russia, because Russia is up to something.

There are some new free cities you may have heard about, and they have the markets atwitter. That makes them ripe for exploitation, and they have drawn particular interest because of the rumors of whatever is happening on the Matrix. Without turning this into an economic textbook, it’s important to note that Seattle and St. Louis are special. They operated under larger regimes where they had some independence, but not like now. Now, they are forming new treasuries and archives and systems that they couldn’t and wouldn’t be allowed to operate previously, and they need to do it quickly.

One aspect of these new free cities is the delicate matter of reissuing or integrating everything from SINs to property records to the assets that those records identity. They are now able to draw debt in a way that they were not legally allowed to before. There are archives and datastores and systems being modernized and built from scratch, and that gives hackers and corps and random weirdos alike new ways to interfere at the ground level. It also gives them a way to access and manipulate economic, sociopolitical, and other systems in ways that are of interest or outright concern to powers as high-ranking as the Corporate Court.

Other entities have these systems in place, but they’re now irrelevant. This is the case across the CAS, which ended up being the biggest beneficiary when the Big Ten put the UCAS in its place. We’ll leave the greater ramifications of that for later, but it also includes the re-unification of much of Florida after more than half a century. These states and territories have to integrate, which means that a lot of information is going to be moving quickly across parts of the Matrix, mostly but not entirely within government systems, which aren’t built to scale up, because why would they be? There is so much information being shared and processed that if it’s anything like the formation of the UCAS, they’ll be finding errors and misplaced or missing data into the next century. That’s just one country where the systems were at least similar. The integrations with the NAN or Quebec are going to be much more volatile. And that is creating data and processes that are being exploited by everyone from SIN counterfeiters to traffickers to political agitators and money launderers.



And Russia. Russia is in an interesting spot. The war’s over. A conspiracy among the Red Army, certain Vorys, Z-IC, certain church bishops, and Brackhaven has found their stalking horse to lead a challenge against Lofwyr’s and Evo’s influence within the country, in no small part because the war has officially ended. With the war debt and peacetime expenses coming due, they managed to shove through a debt and financing plan that looks far different than the economic horrors that has come with these plans every time Russia has tried to change course for the last century. Given the terms and scope of our analysis and speculation here, it conjures up all of the possible demons—financial and economic manipulation, internal corporate and political manipulation, media and public/social influence, and the mass of vague uncertainties and fears that tend to follow anything involving Lofwyr, Russia, and their enemies. Oh, and speaking of Golden Snout, this all was announced just two weeks after his nemesis got whacked in Barcelona.
