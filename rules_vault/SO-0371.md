## Don’t Shoot!

## \[Con (Acting), Major Anytime\]: 3 Edge

Acting can be used for more than performing on a stage, such as dissuading others from harming their targets. As a Major Anytime Action, player characters may hold up their hands, plead for mercy, make a cute pouty face, or whatever else they think might break down the will of someone pointing a gun at them. Add any ranks in Con (Acting) as bonus dice on a single defense test against an attack or combat spell. Don’t Shoot! doesn’t stack with other bonus dice, such as from Dodge, Block, or Full Defense.

attempt an Influence (Etiquette) + Charisma test in place of a Con or Stealth skill test to blend in or act like they belong somewhere. On a success, people will overlook their presence, and even guards are likely to assume they belong. This lasts for one minute per hit, or until something happens that exposes you. If player characters still have the Edge to spend, they can take this action again before the time runs out to extend the duration by one minute per hit.
