## resistance, at all costs

**>** We talked about the bad guys at the forefront of this fight, now it’s time to talk about the good guys, or at least those fighting for our survival. “Good” is a relative term in this cluster-frag. I also want to reiterate something from the first part of this download. This section represents the high-profile players in this fight. There are unknown numbers of independent individuals and groups, often working at the street level, who are sticking their necks **way** out to fight for our very survival and contribute however they can. So, to those who are literally fighting alone in the shadows, we feel your presence and thank you for your sacrifice, whoever and wherever you are.
