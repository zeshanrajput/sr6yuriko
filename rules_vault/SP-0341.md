## **Boogeyman Chimera**

**When these nightmares-made-real frst appeared, it didn’t take people long to associate them with boogeymen from their local folklore— names given to them range from Baba Yaga to Jumbee to Skinwalkers. Where the Biter is the blunt object of psyops for Dis, the Boogeyman is the scalpel. Boogeyman chimeras are used to sow fear, doubt, and anxiety among the opponents of Dis.**

**Although they rarely allow anyone to see them clearly, what reports exist say they wear very dark clothing, and their fesh appears pale and emaciated. When using their abilities, their eyes glow red. The glowing red eyes aren’t hidden by their Concealment ability and are often the only feature reported.**

**They spread fear and uneasiness, with some people reporting an unshakable sense of foreboding. Some cower in fear while others run screaming. Boogeymen will also use Mindspeech to terrorize, taunt, and threaten their victims, slowly eroding people’s sanity.**
