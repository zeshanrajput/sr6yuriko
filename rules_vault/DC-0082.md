## Suzuki Sporter

Taking a cue from their Transit motorcycle, the Sporter is Suzuki’s first entry into the mini-van market. At face value, the Sporter looks completely average and unassuming in every way. And like the Transit, it has the same semi-modular systems/ parts with easy access that make maintenance and repair (and modification) significantly easier. The Sporter’s other main selling points are its ample passenger seating and storage space in the back.
