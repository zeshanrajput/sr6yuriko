## Knives in the Dark (Operation: Seraphim)

## Posted by: Thorn \<07-19-83/0717:51>

I’ve done my part, for now.

I just spent the last three weeks infiltrating a Pennsylvania National Guard base. It wasn’t hard. Their security is adequate, but nowhere near good enough to keep out someone like me. My part of this little operation is to disrupt and keep the units stationed here out of a fight that’s coming to another part of the state.

All I had to do was masquerade as a supply officer, make my way into the mess area for “inTHEspections,” and compromise all the food and water with a toxin. Nothing fatal, although they may wish it was. And I honestly feel bad for whoever gets latrine duty for the next several days. Then I took out their commanding officer and his XO. Again, nothing fatal (for a change), but they’re not going to be reporting for duty anytime soon. And from what data I’ve gathered, the next in line is a walking clusterfrag of an officer.

But if the troops _are_ able to muster, hidden demo-charges in their armory, fuel depots, and motor pool should handle things. I hope I don’t have to use them. And if I do, I hope no one gets caught in the blast. If they do, that’s just how it goes.

Still, there are others who aren’t as … restrained as I am.
