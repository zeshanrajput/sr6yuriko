## Evil Eye

## (Illusion)

**==> picture [227 x 92] intentionally omitted \<==**

**----- Start of picture text -----**<br>
RANGE TYPE DURATION DV<br>LOS M S 4<br>Paranoia<br>(Illusion)<br>RANGE TYPE DURATION DV<br>LOS(A) M S 6<br>**----- End of picture text -----**<br>

This spell projects feelings of imminent danger, fear, and unease on a target emanating from the caster. Targets simultaneously feel chilled and nervous, as panic begins to set it. Roll a Sorcery + Magic vs. Willpower + Logic Opposed test. Targets who fail to resist the spell gain the Frightened status (p. 52, _SR6_ ). If net hits exceed the target’s Willpower, the target gains the Panicked status (p. 52, _SR6_ ).

Evil Eye is used on one particular target, while Foreboding affects everyone in the area other than the caster.
