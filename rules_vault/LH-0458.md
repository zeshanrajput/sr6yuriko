## Special note: Immunity

Entities with an Immunity of any sort lose access to the Immunity until magic begins to come back. For those with Immunity to Age, time catches up with them rapidly. They seem to age the normal human equivalent of 10 years each day, though they don’t die from old age. When magic begins to recover, the Immunity returns and their age begins shedding itself, making them appear younger again, albeit at a slower pace than it caught up to them.
