## Hyper BTL

- **Vector:** Slot

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** (Rating x 5) minutes

- **Power:** 6 + (Rating)

- **Effect:** +1 Reaction, +1 Agility, –1 Intuition, –1 Charisma

The hyper BTL amplifies the signals in your brain pertaining to the fight-or-flight response, without triggering the adrenal gland. Instead, the digital signals amplify the messages that go out to the rest of the body. The result is that the user of this chip can’t seem to do anything other than twitch and bounce through a room, giving the impression of an overly caffeinated squirrel. When the BTL wears off, the user gains the Fatigued I status for the next (rating) hours.
