## **Does a channeled spirit have its own initiative?**

No. The spirit doesn’t have specific actions while in the summoner’s body and is instead lending its abilities and power to the summoner. The summoner uses their own skills and has full motor control of their body. The magician has access to the spirit’s powers but at the cost of a service. Because two minds inhabit the same body, powers and spells are resisted by the lowest Mental attribute. Finally, the spirit cannot leave the summoner’s body until dismissed, there are no remaining services, or time expires per summoning rules.

A spirit can take over the summoner’s body at the summoner’s command, but this lasts for a single combat turn. During this turn, the spirit has control of the body and uses its skills.
