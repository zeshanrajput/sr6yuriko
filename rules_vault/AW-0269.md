## Lava Bombs

The volcanoes around the valley are in a state of constant eruption, and they spew lava bombs randomly. Every combat round (or at a suitable point when not engaged in combat), the gamemaster can call for an Edge (1) test. If a player fails this test, a lava bomb heads toward that character. This threshold increases by 1 every 5 combat rounds, or when the GM feels like it outside of combat, until a lava bomb targets a character. At that point, the threshold returns to 1. In the event of a glitch, critical glitch, or when a wild die rolls a 1 or a 6, a lava bomb targets a random character in the group in addition to the normal effects of the dice rolls.

When a character has been targeted by a lava bomb, treat it as a blast attack (p. 114, _SR6_ ), with the exception that the scatter distance is a fixed 2D6 meters. Damage is as a fragmentation rocket (p. 264, _SR6_ ), with anyone taking any damage from the lava bomb gaining the Burning 3 and Corrosive 3 statuses from the lava.
