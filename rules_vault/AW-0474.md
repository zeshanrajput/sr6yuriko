## shade

**Cost:** 1,000¥ / dose **Duration:** 3D6 hours, maximum 12 hours **Effects:** Astral projection

**Exotic Ingredient:** A natural herbal extract of pollen from the red orchid of Southeast Asia.

This compound allows the user, even a mundane, to astrally project. This counts as a single occurrence of forced projection and grants all users, including mundanes, access to the metaplanes if they are accompanying an initiate on an astral quest. Characters must return to their bodies before the duration ends or suffer 10S boxes of stun damage (resisted by Body only) and 10P – Essence boxes of physical damage (unresisted) as they are violently forced back into their body. If a user returns to their body before the effect expires, they still suffer the stun damage. Returning to the body will end the effect of the drug regardless of the duration left. Shade does not allow users to retain their astral form for longer than normal—mundanes still lose 1 point of Essence every 2 hours, while magicians begin losing 1 Essence per hour after Magic x 2 hours—and users are still subject to death if they exceed the normal time limit for astral projection.
