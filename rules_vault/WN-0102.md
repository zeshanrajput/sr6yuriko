## Lincoln Center

New York’s flagship performing arts center is located on the West Side between 62[nd] and 65[th] Street. The center comprises thirteen separate halls and stages surrounding a central plaza. The center was rebuilt following the quake, with the original MDC working to make it a “conceptually artistic reflection” of the council’s office space on the other side of the island. Lincoln Center hosts a number of local and national award shows, including the Draco Foundation annual competitions for Magical and Technical Advancement, which are held in February each year.
