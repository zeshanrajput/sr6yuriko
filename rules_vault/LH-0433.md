## March 24, 2083

Wuxing operative, known only as “Agent Xin” defects to the Defenders and brings information regarding all known Harvester Locations (a.k.a. The Divine Map). Defender leadership decides on four operational plans and begins preparations while continuing to search for the Disian code cypher.
