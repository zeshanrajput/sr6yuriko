## **Marcy Keane**

**While Kripa Grover tried to maintain at least cordial relations with Aegis Cognito when he left, Marcy Keane burned every bridge connecting her to Tír Tairngire’s Information Secretariat when she departed. She has embraced the Tiding’s information-frst approach to their work, as she became disgusted with the way nationalism (and occasionally racism) twisted everything the Secretariat did.**

**This means that while she might think she is** _**more**_ **neutral now, she is not being** _**neutral.**_ **She has a particular affnity for information that could undermine the largest powers of the world, be they megacorporate or governmental.**

- **She’s definitely not neo-anarchist in her politics, but she enjoys taking power from the powerful, so we can find some common ground with her.**

- **Old Crow**

**Keane is a skilled hacker who can go in either fast or sleazy, depending on what the job calls for. Sleazing might be her better strength, as she is quite capable of avoiding spiders or IC while looking for what she wants.**

**There was recently some noise coming out of Manhattan about corporate secrets swirling around there, and Keane is enthusiastically chasing down any information she hears about. If you know anything, reach out to her—she might pay you for what you have and offer you cash to get more.**
