## **Glamourworm**

## **(Vermes saxi arcanus)**

**After I got the frst scientifc brief on these from Dr. Greene, I knew this was going to be a target for runners, often as that personal pot of gold that gets them fragging killed. As you can tell by the name, this subspecies of rockworm has some illusion abilities that it uses to defend itself from predators—metahumans in particular. A good defense is a necessity, as its hardened keratin ring is a repository for precious gems, such as diamonds, rubies, and sapphires, that accumulate as it ages. The glamourworm is the same general size as the basic species and tends to favor geological regions with high mineral wealth. A typical specimen will have upward of 10,000 nuyen worth of raw materials, which can be processed to increase that value tenfold as gemstones or reagents. Larger or older specimens may have ten times that amount.**
