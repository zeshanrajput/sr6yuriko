## Sea Dragon

This one’s speculative, but Shiawase’s second-most-profitable arm is aquacology, and while much of it is done on land in aquariums, there are some aspects that only be done in the ocean proper. That’s Sea Dragon’s territory and while she’s more concerned with the Atlantic than the Pacific, her recent activities in Hawai’i and now Seattle have Shiawase on alert. They’d prefer negotiation, but if it comes to a fight, well, they have Aztechnology dragonslaying tech on speed dial …
