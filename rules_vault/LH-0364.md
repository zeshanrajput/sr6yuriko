## Gorilla Chimera

Gorilla chimeras feature oversized limbs, typically between 1.5 to 1.75 times larger than standard, with extra bone deposits on their knuckles for additional damage potential. Their hips and shoulders have also been enhanced with extra musculature and tendons. The gorilla chimera is reported to be extremely strong and resistant to painful stimuli. The torso has also been enhanced with smooth bone-like structures similar to dermal deposits on trolls, but under the skin. Most gorilla chimeras have greyish skin, bald heads, and nasal slits. Similar to their simian counterparts, they are quick-tempered, aggressive, and extremely single-minded.
