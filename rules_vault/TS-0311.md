## **Professional**

## **Rating 1: Unarmed Security**

**Most areas require an eight-hour training course that concludes with a simple test. These courses can be easily found on the Matrix. Once completed, the applicant must submit their information to the accrediting body to obtain their license. In most areas, an unarmed security guard has none of the powers of a sworn offcer and therefore has no more rights than the average citizen. They will often still follow the “observe and report” mentality. With their extra training, however, they are allowed to carry non-lethal hand-to-hand items, which are most often batons.**
