## Physical Travel to a Metaplane

There are several important differences between materializing a metaplanar form by astrally projecting into a metaplane and going there in physical form. First and most important, the physical body is on the metaplane—it’s not a manifested form, and dying results in death, not being sent back to your home metaplane. Also, since travelers are there physically, not manifesting, there is no immunity to normal weapons, no dematerialization, and astrally perceiving requires an action to shift perception. Leaving the metaplane to return home, go into the Astral Sea, or go to another metaplane requires a metaplanar passageway of some type to make the trip. Astrally projecting operates normally while physically on a metaplane, and it’s even possible to astrally project out of the metaplane and manifest somewhere else—but that leaves your body behind in a strange metaplane.

The advantage of physically being in the metaplane is that you can pick up items and return them to the physical realm when you go back. Recordings made with cybereyes or cameras transfer the actual data back, data acquired by hacking metaplanar data systems can be taken back to the physical realm, and valuable items like true elements or artifacts can be physically transferred. Just be careful about what you allow to be translated during the return. A data file of untranslated data in a format used on a metaplane isn’t worth much, while carrying a sample of True Fire through a portal without protection will have explosive results.
