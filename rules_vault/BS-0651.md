## Karma Hazing

Often referred to as “Astral Hazing,” Karma Hazing is a phenomenon that occurs with all cyberzombies and is a byproduct of their creation and existence. Because of the nature of the magic binding their spirit to their body, and the forestalling of entropy preventing the subject from simply dying, astral energies tend to pool and warp around a cyberzombie. Some say it’s the result of a tortured soul condemned to be bound to a machine, but others will insist the dark magic used in its creation linger on after its perverse “birth.” Karma Hazing affects all attempts to cast magic on, at, or in the vicinity of the cyberzombie. Whatever the ambient mana conditions are, the cyberzombie always stands at the heart of a high mana ebb (see p. 167, _Street Wyrd_ ) affecting all magicians that extends a number of meters from its body equal to its total negative Essence. If the cyberzombie remains in one place for long, the mana ebb will expand at the rate of about a meter in every direction every three hours.

Once a cyberzombie has left an area, the mana will shift back to its normal conditions in a day.
