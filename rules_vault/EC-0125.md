## Where to Shop

Shopping is a contact sport in the Belle. On the one hand, shoppers are looking for the best and most unique items. On the other hand, shopping anywhere outside of your micro-arcology means you are trying to be noticed. Bellevue malls are wired to the max with AROs, personalized shopping algorithms, and scores of attendants hungry to give you a “to die for” experience.
