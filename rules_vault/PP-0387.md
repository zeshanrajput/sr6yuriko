## Frankfurt Banking Association

**Focus:** Finance

**Headquarters:** Frankfurt, Allied German States

**Key thing to know:** They have their fingers in a huge percentage of the world’s transactions, so your money has likely passed through them at some point. You might not have noticed, but they did.
