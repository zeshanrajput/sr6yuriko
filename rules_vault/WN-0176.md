## Ashlee Corsair, Horizon: Talent Management, North America, Eastern Seaboard

**Description:** Female human, with blonde hair cut in a sharp down-angle bob, dressed scantily in high-priced fashions from Zoé’s not-yet-released line.

**Possible Party Missteps:** Joins Jackson Ito for a private encounter where she enjoys with wild abandon. One of the joygirls approaches her quietly at first, asking about possible jobs and talking about her “special” interview. Ashlee denies this while others are around but quietly gives her “personal” commcode for a conversation at a later time. The commcode is real, but it’s her assistant, who also participated in the “interview,” and the runners could talk to the joygirl for a cool 10,000 nuyen blackmail bit if they’ll play the muscle.
