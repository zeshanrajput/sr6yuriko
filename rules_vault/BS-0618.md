## Ares Deinonychus

T-rexes and triceratops are impressive, largely due to their size, but they can’t exactly hunt down targets within a building—unless they level it first. The deinonychus is a smaller bipedal carnivore equipped with hooked talons on the end of each foot, and they are just small enough to fit through doorways that were designed for metahuman use. They may be smaller dinosaurs, but they are also much more agile and better suited for hunting down targets in urban environments.
