## **Special Operations**

**These chimeras fll the niche of special operatives for Dis. Building on the foundation established by the Doppelganger chimeras and the Demon Squirrel chimeras, these new elite units are capable of conducting activities in a highly specialized, unconventional, and discreet manner. These units are characterized by their high levels of skills, versatility, and ability to operate in environments where conventional tactics or units cannot be used.**
