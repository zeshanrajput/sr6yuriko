## Definitions of Matrix Terms

**Base:** This is an alternate term used for the basement of a framework host.

**Basement:** Operating files, ownership structure, and archive of a framework host.

**Brick:** Slang term for an optical stack mainframe.

- **Cyberterminal:** Desktop computer combining the functions of a cyberdeck and cyberjack. These units are not portable.

- **Dataterm:** Common home computer, home entertainment system, and communication device. These units are not portable.

- **Deep Foundation:** The Foundation layer of the Matrix itself, connecting all host Foundations.

- **Foundation:** Substructure of a Foundation host that runs the host’s operating code. This layer sits between the Resonance Realms and hosts.

- **Foundation-Tech:** The technology used to create and maintain Foundation hosts.

- **Framework:** A host generated by physical processors instead of a Foundation. These hosts consist of optical stack mainframes. Sometimes called legacy hosts.

- **Mixed User Groups (MUGs):** Acronym to describe most Matrix groups in existence.

- **New Matrix:** Shorthand for changes made to the Matrix in 2075 by Danielle de la Mar (not personally, but under her direction). At this point, it’s not really new, but surly old deckers always have a chip on their shoulder.
