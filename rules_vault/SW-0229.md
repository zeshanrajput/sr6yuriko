## Resist Drain

When casting a spell, the conjurer may choose to have their ally spirit take a portion of the spell’s drain. Boxes of Stun damage from spell drain can

be transferred directly to an ally spirit as Stun damage, one-for-one, or two-for-one as Physical damage (i.e., two Stun for one Physical). Physical drain can only be transferred one-for-one as Physical damage. This service is painful for the ally. Frequent use will foster hatred over time.
