## El Canto, Flores Verdes (translated)

I watch the way you move, you stretch, Be here by my side, white blossom, Now I see the tears in your eyes, I can turn them to gold, Or we can run faster than the flood, Your love is worth more than gold to me Let the sun cast through me, See my intention, look closely, But throw me in the river of destiny, They’ll call Ullil against me, Put your faith in me, we’ll be just fine Meet me near the green flowers, Where we’ll run careless and free, Meet me near the green flowers, Wipe your eyes and see, Take my hand, Take my hand

Black snake in a suit and tie, You weave within me, You may have the biggest heart I’ve ever seen, How can this be? Take your scepter and take your chair, Now tell me, what do you feel? Fire and flames, our houses will burn, And I will rise to the sky beside you, The embers will fly, All I see is your eyes Meet me near the green flowers, Where we’ll run careless and free, Meet me near the green flowers, I need your eyes to see, Take my hand, Take my hand
