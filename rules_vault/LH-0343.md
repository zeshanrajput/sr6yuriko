## Branded Powered Weapons by Evo

The first round of powered melee weapons to hit the shadow market were prototypes “released” for field testing that proved to be popular enough for regular production.

Many of these early prototypes were made in Evo’s R&D labs, perhaps suggesting that Evo is capitalizing on their investment with a rapid manufacturing ramp-up. Unlike the core rEVOlution line, these weapons are being produced and branded under an assortment of subsidiaries scattered around the globe. In addition to ramping up production, Evo has also started pouring resources into marketing and advertising for these subsidiaries in an attempt to solidify their early advantage and establish these brands as market leaders.

**Game Rules** : The branded versions of powered weapons follow the same rules as their prototype versions (p. 177, _Scotophobia_ ). The increased production of the branded versions has lowered the cost significantly and made them slightly more available. The new weapons in these branded lines follow the specific rules common to the other weapons of their type. While these weapons are made by Evo, they do _not_ follow the rEVOlution wild die rules.
