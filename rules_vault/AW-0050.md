## Travel

Besides riding slumgullions (millipedes the size of a horse), earth elementals have other ways of travel. Amphibolite has straight lines of crystals along a paved surface set in the landscape. These are the metaplanar Nazca slipstreams, radiating out to major settlements such as Dovv, Cupellation, Gaoling, and Damar. Electric vehicles glide unusually quickly (for earth elementals) along the lines, hovering slightly above the ground.
