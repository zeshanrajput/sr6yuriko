## Binder

## Defense: Willpower + Data Processing

Where Acid IC wants to reduce your defenses, Binder IC wants to slow down all of your functionality—and keep you from running as many programs as you would like. When Binder IC hits in cybercombat, the target’s Data Processing attribute is reduced by 1 per net hit. When Data Processing hits 0, no Matrix actions can be performed until it hits 1 or higher. Lost points are restored after leaving the host, at a rate of 1 point per minute.
