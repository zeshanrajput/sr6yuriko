## Exotic Style

Berlin is known for its incomparably high tolerance of personal idiosyncrasies of individuals. Screeching colorful outfits, all-revealing cuts, glowing facial tattoos, garish hairstyles, or completely ripped outfits full of patches are common sights. Generally speaking, it can be said that in Berlin it is impossible to trigger public outrage through clothing, makeup, and hairstyles (exceptions such as the arch-conservative Emirate in Kreuzhain or the higher echelons of the Renrakusan confirm the rule).

Evo and Proteus further fuel this permissiveness and right to personal design in their Zehlendorf district: People with special needs of all kinds get not only the best surgeries and implants here but also the appropriate psychological guidance for dealing with any kind of self-transformation. The same is true for the Renrakusan youth scene, where neo-furries and other cosmetic alterations have been heavily in vogue for years and have spawned a lively scene where absolutely nothing is too crazy.

- Districts: Renrakusan, Zehlendorf

- Cost: 5 Karma

- Game Effect: Choose any three strictly ornamental modifications to your hair, eyes, nose/ snout, teeth, or skin that do not provide any additional game benefits at no Essence or money cost.
