## Acceleration, Speed, and Movement

The online Shadowrun, Sixth World FAQ made some clarifications to the rules regarding vehicular movement in tactical, non-chase scenarios, which are repeated below for convenience:

The Move Minor Action serves to govern vehicles and drone movement in most contexts. Rather than moving up to 10 meters, they move as many meters as the vehicle’s current speed, plus or minus the Acceleration rating. A change in movement rate for the turn in excess of the Acceleration attribute (very hard braking, for example) is possible by spending the Sprint Major Action.
