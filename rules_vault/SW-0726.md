## Solitary

## Beetle

Though beetle spirits come in various forms representing different species of beetle, they tendto have the same behaviors and characteristics and are considered to be the same type of spirit. They occasionally gather in hives, which are overseen by one or more mother spirits, and over which they are extremely territorial, attacking intruders with little to no warning.
