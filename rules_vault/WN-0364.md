## what did he find?

Silver Nail found a data cache that was sent out by a now-dead JackPoint poster going by the street name of Double A. The original purpose of the information was to warn JackPointers that MCT was working with the Null Sect.

Within the tens of thousands of pages of documents, text, and messages in the cache, there is something even more damaging. A group within MCT knew the Four Oyabun Rengo would not approve of the plans to work with the Null Sect. A large group of shareholders discussed in detail how they could trigger a buy-sell clause in the original contract to force the four Yakuza groups out of MCT.

The cash necessary for the buyout will use a large amount of liquid funds, making MCT’s cash flow challenging. After the Four Oyabun Rengo are removed, the group plans to overinflate inventory values to hide the cashflow issue from rivals and other competition.

The documents are dated and by themselves wouldn’t be considered relevant. Recently, though, MCT surprised everyone by removing the Four Oyabun Rengo from the organization. The information Silver Nail found, combined with a thorough review of MCT bookkeeping, could give a small group of rivals the ability to damage the mighty MCT.
