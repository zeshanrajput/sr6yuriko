## Game Information

The Citadel represents a treasure trove of magical lore, if players are prepared to go and get it. Every piece of knowledge, every true name of a free spirit, every metamagic (except sacrifice), every spell and ritual known to metahumanity (and probably some that aren’t) is stashed away somewhere in one of the thousands upon thousands of vaults within the catacombs. If someone on the material plane has ever carried out an astral quest for the knowledge, then there is a vault with it inside. The Citadel also offers gamemasters an opportunity to go wild with traps and puzzles, facets that traditionally do not play a large role within _Shadowrun_ .
