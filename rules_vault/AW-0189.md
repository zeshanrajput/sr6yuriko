## 02-C Chimera and “Lesser” Beings

Within Dis society, chimera do the bidding of their Dis masters, known as patrons. They’re used as laborers, troops, or in extremely rare occasions, advisors.

Chimera are taken from their metaplane and put through an intensive re-construction process where their bodies are repurposed to fit their new role. In most cases, the chimera’s memory is (supposedly) wiped clean, although some are left with some memories and skills as deemed necessary by their patrons.

The majority of chimera encountered in the Sixth World have been of the troop variety. Trying to classify or categorize them is almost a useless endeavor because of the vast variety already encountered. What we do know is that combat chimera boast enhanced strength, speed, and senses, and they come with some form of implanted bio-weaponry or armor.

- For every type we catalogue, three new variations complete with new surprises pop up. There’s no easy way to fight them except do as much damage as you can, as fast as you can, as far away as you can until they’re nothing but bloody chunks of meat.
