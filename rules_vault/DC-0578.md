## Dead Stick

This driving thing—it may not be for you. We’re not saying you’re bad at it, but cockatrices don’t even try to cross the road when you’re behind the wheel.

- **Bonus:** 4 Karma

- **Game Effect:** –2 dice pool penalty on all Crash tests.
