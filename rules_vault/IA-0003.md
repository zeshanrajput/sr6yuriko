## Welcome to Shadow Stock! 

**Shadow Stock** is a new line of PDF products for Shadowrun, Sixth World that provides a group of NPCs built around a common theme as well as new character options connected to these characters. So whether you’re a gamemaster looking for new NPCs to introduce to a campaign or a player exploring new options, this line has something for you!
