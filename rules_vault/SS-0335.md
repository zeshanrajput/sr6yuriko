## Professional Mercenary, basic

## (Professional Rating 4)

Sometimes the job requirement calls for a quiet professional who is proficient in combat and tactics but does not necessarily possess the flash and attitude of your hotshot street sammies. What they may lack in ’ware, they make up for in skill and knowledge. Considered to be consummate pros with their own moral and personal codes of conduct, the mercenary is a professional soldier first and foremost—their contract is their bond.
