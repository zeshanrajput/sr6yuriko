## Assault Cannon Blasts

**Use this rule** to grant assault cannons some additional tactical flexibility.

Under this rule, assault cannon ammunition creates a blast attack (p. 114, _SR6_ ) with DV GZ 6P, Close 4P, Near —, and Blast 3 m. Assault cannons also gain the ability to also accept standard and APDS ammunition using machine gun pricing. Assault cannons do not make blast attacks when using standard or APDS ammo.
