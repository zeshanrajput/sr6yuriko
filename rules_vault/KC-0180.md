## What is the New Toy?

As I stated earlier, I was originally tasked with exploring the development of the non-lethal technology being developed by Yingyong. The Guizhen research has reached the stage where it can be produced with little worry. This is a device (as “weapon” is an unusual descriptor) that directly inhibits the metahuman body’s ability to regulate its otherwise natural sleep cycle. If you want to get technical, here’s how it works: The device projects photons and other particles that parallel the function of zeitgebers, the external cues that synchronize the suprachiasmatic nucleus of the hypothalamus which then projects to the pineal gland and thus release, or cease the release of, melatonin to the body.

The device has an emitter, regulator, and battery system that can draw from power cells available on the open market today. The system is completely scalable, allowing for it to be used against single targets or for localized crowd management. It even affects most mammals in a similar fashion, though the exact effect on different species remains uncertain. It is non-lethal, though the usual disclaimers apply—making someonefall asleep while driving or when they’re walking on the edge of a cliff could be just as fatal as shooting them with a firearm.
