## April 30, 2083

Buttercup hosts her infamous press conference, and the mainstream media reacts negatively. But Horizon uses the chaos to dig up more intelligence and identify targets. Beyond this date, fighting intensifies as the hunt for Mobile Harvesters and the key to the Disian codes ramps up. Rumors suggest that Aztechnology’s Special Projects lead, Armando Salazar, begins his plans for his internal coup.
