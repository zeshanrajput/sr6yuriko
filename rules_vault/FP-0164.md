## **Mitsuhama Computer Technologies**

**All of the corps have Humanis members in them (yes, even Evo), but none are as thick with them as MCT. Why? No clue. But they’ve always been big backers of racism in general and anti-goblin in particular. They were the guys who were the steel behind General Saito in California who cracked down on Orktown, they’re the ones who back racist political fgures worldwide, and they’re the ones who’d sooner toss us in toxic barrels than treat us fairly. Most of the corps use us as disposable assets or a good source of income, but MCT would be happiest if we were scoured from the face of the planet.**

- **Speaking of General Saito, his brother Kenshin’s still angling for a seat on the board. It’s taken him twenty years (and two leónizations), but he can see his goal ahead.**
