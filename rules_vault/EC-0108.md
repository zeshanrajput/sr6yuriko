## Union Man

You’re not just a supporter but a card-carrying member of a union. You’re kept abreast of all the union news, are aware of what strikes are on, and can always rely on other union members to have your back when corporate overlords get greedy. You can’t cross picket lines and you never, ever, support a scab.

- **Cost:** 8 Karma

- **Game Effect:** Gain +1 bonus Edge for any social interaction with a member of any union.
