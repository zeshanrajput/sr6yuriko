## Just Be Social

The PCs are escorted to a waiting limousine flanked by a pair of 2080 Range Rovers and what appears to be a twelve-person contingent of highly trained security officers. These are Green Serpent Guards, the elite security force of the Nagato Combine (use Tir Ghosts, p. 208, _SR6_ ). The GSG doesn’t speak to the runners. They drive them directly to the Empire State Building, where they are escorted to a private elevator that takes them to the 71st floor.

Buttercup’s suite is a testament to wealth and eccentricity. The flooring is done in pink ivory panels polished to the point they shine like glass. The walls and ceiling appear to be a seamless cut of marble. Throughout the space are rare sculptures and stands containing priceless comic books and other collectables. The runners are led to the center of the room and invited to sit on one of several couches. Three women are seated across from those couches in large chairs. They do not stand to greet the runners. At the center of the group is Buttercup. To her right is Ahma Yoshida. To the left is Ms. Helena Chomsky.

Helena Chomsky is the first to speak to the runners. She introduces herself, though declines to explain whom she works for. Then she introduces Ahma Yoshida as a representative of the Nagato Corporation and Buttercup as simply Buttercup. She tells them they’ve been invited here to answer several questions from the other two women.

Ahma Yoshida will begin the questioning, with Buttercup thoughtfully listening throughout. If the



conversation branches into some of the areas Buttercup wanted to ask about, they will step forward and ask the question. It should be clear that Buttercup is in control of this operation. Ms. Chomsky will be the last to ask a question, and she will only ask one, which will be phrased as a gentle follow up: Would you care to share with us your understanding of the term “Monad,” and whom if any of these individuals you have interacted with since entering the city? After asking the question, she will meet eyes with both of the executives present and nod.

Each interrogator’s questions should should become increasingly difficult to answer, as the runners only have small pieces of information about events unfolding in the city. They haven’t seen enough yet to give thorough responses. Ultimately, the three executives present are trying to find out information that the runners do not know they have or might not have pieced together thus far. They are aligned in their interest to know more about MCT’s motives, but beyond that they all have separate reasons for needing answers.

Ms. Chomsky is Blake’s eyes and ears in the room. If the runners reveal her role, Buttercup will become enraged. After a few moments of visible anger, she will calm down. Ms. Chomsky will clear her throat and suggest that they deal with the Blake issue separately. Buttercup will agree and resume questioning.

If Blake’s involvement is exposed, he will act vengefully. After the runners are released, he will send the Seraphim team after the PCs with orders to kill them all.
