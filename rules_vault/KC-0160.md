## TerraFirst!

This group has data implicating Okoro in contracts against the corporations in order to expose their ecologically destructive methods. It also has data on those methods and on the corporations. The primary reason for this connection comes from Okoro’s family connection through her father. Growing up around many members of TerraFirst!, Okoro knew them as family and friends rather than terrorists and criminals. Anyone looking for dirt will find plenty of it at two separate spots related to TerraFirst!.

The first spot is a former bank they hang out in. The second is a runner who works extensively for TerraFirst! but has a personal vendetta against its local leader. She’s been building evidence and has even gained access to the comm terminal in the headquarters a few times.
