## > **Tacoma Mall**

At over a century in operation, this is the oldest mall in the metroplex, with a focus on middle-class customers. In the four floors, you’ll find a surprising number of antique shops frequented by mages looking for interesting trinkets that tend to pop up, and there’s money in scouting for them, as well. For the more technically inclined, the Body+Tech facility is a great place for no-questions-asked upgrade and repair service for cybernetics.
