## gm’s secret roll

1D6 rounds after discovery of the cellular markers occurs, something that can only be described as Gremlins will happen to the devices, but only if the devices are wireless enabled. A successful Reaction (3) test can prevent the data from being lost, otherwise it means starting all over. Sprites with Stability can prevent this from happening if at least Level 3.
