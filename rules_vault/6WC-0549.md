## Mood Hair

## Unusual Hair

## **Bonus** _:_ 4 Karma

Mood hair changes colors according to the individual’s emotional state, allowing those observing them to make a Judge Intentions test (p. 67, _SR6_ ) with a +2 dice pool bonus. Mood Hair is incompatible with any hair modification and the Feathers and Scales qualities.
