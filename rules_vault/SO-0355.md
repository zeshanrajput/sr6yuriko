## Circumstantial Edge

This is where things are less clearly defined, and player characters will need to rely on the narrative circumstances rather than a specific game attribute or item. Deciding whether to award circumstantial Edge might seem daunting or confusing at first to a gamemaster, but it’s really quite simple. If one side or the other has an obvious advantage (other than Social Rating or specific gear or qualities), award them a point of Edge. If it’s not obvious, don’t award the Edge.
