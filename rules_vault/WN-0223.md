## The Model and the Beauty Mark

Were the circumstances any different, it would never be possible. There are however several deals at stake to be hammered out at the Met Gala, so everything must go perfectly.

As such, the measurements Becky took of Gabrielle are part and parcel for the package. Some of the measurements were not the norm, including some unusual ones of her fingers, toes, even the angle of her jawline. What is important here is not only to figure out what was unusual, but to obtain those measurements again, while in the presence of her personal bodyguard, Nazih Sultan (use Renraku Red Samurai, p. 208, _SR6_ , raise Edge to 6, add Kukri, p. 12, _Firing Squad_ ) at the insistence of the Board. These actions require an Influence (Interrogation) + Intuition (10, 1 hour) test to be able to figure out the odd locations of measurements, and then obtain them. Collecting these can be performed with a medkit or commlink and appropriate software (cost of 50¥), using a Perception (Visual) + Intuition (16, 10 minutes) test. Those individuals with knowledge skills in Anatomy, Fashion Design, or Armorer gain a point of Edge to be used once each.
