## Driving Style: Interceptor

Sometimes you need to chase someone down. You know how to make sure they don’t get away.

- **Cost:** 8 Karma

- **Game Effect:** You only pay half the required Edge cost for the following uses of Edge:

  - Redline (2 Edge)

  - Focus (1 Edge)

  - In the Zone (2 Edge)

  - Double Down (1,2, or 3) Edge on any test made while chasing or shadowing a target.
