## **Holographic Concealment**

**When there is a signifcant amount of AR in an area, the critter can use a Major Action to place a hologram over themselves that makes them hard to see. The critter’s Resonance becomes a negative dice pool modifer on any Perception test to see it, and ranged weapons tests against it receive a –2 dice pool penalty while the power is active. This power does not affect individuals who have no active Matrix connection or ability to perceive Augmented Reality.**
