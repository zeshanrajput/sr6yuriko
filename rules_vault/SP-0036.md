## **Internal Dissension: Not Quite Unified Front**

**As fragged up as this sounds, the Aztechnology board now considers their penance paid and the matter settled. President Flavia de la Rosa has doubled down on assisting Dis, sending more and more assets into play, thereby signaling the board’s reinvigorated commitment to the cause. But like in most corps, not everyone (especially the rank and fle) knows of their complicity with Dis.**

**Enter Special Projects Division head Armando Salazar .**

**Now, full disclosure: Salazar and I have some serious history. But despite our personal blood feud, I think saving the world takes precedent (I know, I’m odd that way). Rumors are piling up that Salazar is royally slotted at de la Rosa and most of the Aztechnology board because two of his family members and one trusted lieutenant were selected as penance. And considering our feud is over spilt family blood, this is a major blunder on Aztechnology’s part.**

**Being a blood mage, medical physician, former leader of the Blood Daggers, and developer of several Aztechnology bioweapons (including Blue-227), Special Projects Division was specifcally created for him to solve (among other things) the problems with the initial prototype Devices. Which he quickly did, without Disian help. But worse, he’s also a vindictive bastard who never forgets or forgives someone who wrongs him (ask me how I know).**

**We’ve had reports that Salazar is working on a ffth column, creating a secret power base within Aztechnology. If he’s working against the Dis, that could be in our favor in the short term. The problem then becomes, if we win, what will Salazar do then?**
