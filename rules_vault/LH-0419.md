## Dragons Great and Small

## Posted by: Wyrm Watcher

Ask someone what they think of dragons, and you’ll get a thousand different answers. During the Disian Shadow War, most of them played a key role in events, even if it was only at the end. For some, that simply wasn’t enough; they felt that if dragonkind simply acted sooner, a lot of this could have (or would have) been avoided. Personally, I don’t like or even subscribe to that idea becauseit ultimately makes metahumanity dependent on dragons for our survival, which is exactly what a significant number of them want. So no, that’s a situation none of us want to be in.

Still, no matter what your opinion on dragons is, some of them played a significant role in the recent shadow war and people want paydata on those who played the biggest parts and what they’re doing now. Ask and ye shall receive.
