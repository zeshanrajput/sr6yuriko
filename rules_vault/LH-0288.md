## Cue the Destroyer

And so it begins.

Everyone on board freaked out when Sirrurg suddenly appeared out of nowhere and buzzed the fleet. The ship’s weapons turned in his general direction as he passed by but stayed silent. Good idea. The next day we passed by the remnants of a naval battle, correction, “slaughter.” Pieces of ships, drones, and bodies were scattered everywhere. My Aztlaner Spanish is rusty, but I think I saw a life preserver with “ANS _Chupacabra_ ” on it. I heard that normally we’d stop for any survivors, but there weren’t any.

Sirrurg’s presence confirms a feeling I’ve been having. They, as in the other dragons, are out there. Maybe on one of those other ships. Regardless, I can _feel_ them, in the back of my mind, as if they’re … looking through my eyes.

Fragging overgrown lizards.
