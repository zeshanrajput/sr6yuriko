## Cynic

The contact has been burned so many times that trust is hard for them. They’re always waiting for someone else to betray them. That doesn’t necessarily mean they’ll betray you at the drop of a hat, but if you want a strong relationship with them, you’re going to have to work that much harder.

**Game Effect:** Improving the contact’s Loyalty costs 1 additional Favor Point.
