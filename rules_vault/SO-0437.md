## Master of Rhetoric

Your arguments are highly persuasive and rarely faulty.

**Cost:** 5 Karma per level (1 to 3)

**Game Effect:** Reduce the cost of your Edge boosts (not Edge actions) to affect Influence (Negotiation) skill tests by one per level of this quality, to a minimum cost of one Edge.
