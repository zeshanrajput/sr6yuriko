## **Plot Hook**

**The PCs are hired to extract the teenage son of a high-ranking executive and politician. Petrovski Security has the security contract to safeguard the extraction target, and they employ a number of augmented gomatia that have been raised to be bodyguard critters. The target never goes anywhere without at least one gomatia, though normally several more are present in hiding nearby as well. In addition to the gomatia, the target is watched over by a small security team of two bodyguards with remote Matrix and drone support.**
