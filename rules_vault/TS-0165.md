## **“The Isms”**

**Neo-PD has an odd relationship with racism. In an operational sense, they’re nearly race-blind, with similar arrest rates for every metatype and no history of racial profling or physical abuse of**

**metatypes. On an organizational level, “You must be this cute to apply” is the rule, so other than a 10 percent elven minority, metatypes are almost impossible to fnd wearing a badge.**

**Sexism, however, is worse but also a mixed bag. Patrol offcers have near parity, with 49 percent male, 47 female, and 4 percent everyone else. Meanwhile, support services have a three-toone female majority. This plummets like a meteor at the leadership level, with only 10 percent of management chairs flled by women, and a tenth of that in upper management.**

**Women are hired at high rates but are treated as second-class offcers. They’re expected to support male offcers, serve them tea, and more. There’s a strong undercurrent that women should use their time on the force to look for husbands and should expect to leave the force by twenty-fve, or thirty at the latest, to start a family. If a female offcer hits thirty and continues, they get treated like a male offcer from then on, since they’ve obviously abandoned their life for their career. But the pressure to get out before then is enormous.**
