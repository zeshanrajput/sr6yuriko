## Knowledge Infusion

In time, information that is temporarily filed in the hippocampus transitions to the neocortex section of the brain. It is at this point that it becomes scientifically recognized as general knowledge. With this augmentation, the sulci and gyri of the neocortex are amplified and stimulated while RNA transference of a knowledge infusion strand takes place. The core concept is easier to illustrate than detail. It can implant basic knowledge right into your brain! Go in, get a shot, and suddenly you can perform close-up magic!

A knowledge infusion gives characters a single knowledge skill they did not possess or a level in a language skill (chosen when the infusion is purchased).
