## **Game Effect**

**Frangible ammunition is far less effective than normal ammunition at penetrating armor and dealing damage. AR is reduced by 4 and the DV is reduced by 1. However, an Awakened target that is struck by GMT ammunition (even if all damage is reduced) receives a –1 dice pool penalty to all tests that involve its Magic attribute. This penalty is cumulative for multiple attacks. If the target is wearing armor or clothing, the penalty can be removed if the target removes all its armor and clothing. If the target is not wearing armor or clothing, such as a critter, materialized spirit, Disian shell, or chimera, the penalty remains for at least 1 hour or until it can be thoroughly cleaned off. A materialized spirit that returns to its astral form leaves the GMT material behind and is no longer affected, even after it rematerializes.**
