## Gun Ports

Sometimes you want to be able to shoot out of a vehicle in a safer manner than rolling down the window or standing up through the sunroof. A gun port provides a firing position from inside a vehicle for a hand-held weapon fired by a passenger while they are still fully protected by the vehicle’s armor. This modification allows a single passenger to fire out of the vehicle in the direction the gun port is facing. If you want more than one passenger to fire in the same direction at the same time, you need more than one gun port installed.
