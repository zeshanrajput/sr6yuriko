## Local Character Kuàizi (Chopsticks)

## **Cost:** 3 Karma

You’ve been in the HKFEZ long enough to know how to talk with one of the Cho Hai or even the Miàntiáo dàshi (Noodle Master) at either of the two Noodleboy locations. You can arrange to have funds transferred and delivered through the network of Triads or laundered in under four hours. This allows for any financial transactions between people (not in AR/VR) in the HKFEZ to have only a two percent transaction fee on up to 50,000 nuyen and be completed nearly instantly. A minimum fee of 100 nuyen still exists.
