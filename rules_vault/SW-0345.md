## **Activation:** Passive

Some adepts are capable of a level of focused concentration that unaugmented mundanes cannot match. The Momentum power helps them move from success to success, regardless of how long they work at a task; every level of Momentum lets them ignore one level of the –1 dice pool modifier that progressively shrinks their pool when making an Extended test ( _SR6_ p. 36). For example _,_ Momentum 2 would allow an adept to use their full pool for the first, second, and third Intervals of an Extended test, and then roll with only a –1 penalty on the fourth, –2 on the fifth, etc.
