## Critter Training

A tamed critter can be trained by a character with the Critter Trainer quality (p. 133, Sixth World Companion). Only wild critters need to be tamed—critters that are raised by metahumans automatically count as tamed critters. Otherwise, taming a critter requires one week of effort and a successful Training test.

A Training test is an Influence + Charisma test vs. the critter’s Intuition + Willpower + Magic or Resonance (if applicable). If the critter has any augmentations, the Influence test suffers a negative dice pool penalty equal to the amount of Essence loss—rounding up even the slightest fraction. In most cases, a training test requires one week of effort. If you fail a Training test, you may try again with a cumulative –1 die penalty for each consecutive attempt. Taking a week off from training removes this cumulative penalty, allowing you to try again with your full dice pool.
