## True Water

True Water, or longlei (dragon tears), was the first of the true elements to be found. It was discovered in 2064 along the bottom of the South China Sea. Longlei are translucent hexagonal crystals, three to four centimeters in diameter, and they can be more than twenty centimeters long. They can easily be mistaken for diamonds or quartz, and they typically form in the deepest parts of the Metaplanar Sea. Vadose engineers use these crystals in the construction of the great engines that can withstand underwater pressures.
