## Aztechnology Sunrunner/Nightrunner

After gaining too much of an illicit reputation, the Nightrunner got an overhaul and a bunch of government contracts in places very friendly to Aztechnology, including Aztlan. It was retagged the Sunrunner, but there’s no hiding that distinctive sealable canopy and the excess of cargo space (which make ample berths if you actually want to use it for cruising).
