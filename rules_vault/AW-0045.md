## Damar

On the loam planes of Drumlin are the spherical stone homes of the residents who share the area’s name. Their settlements are also pre-industrial, with concentric rings of stone. The outer stones are three times as high as the internal structures, giving protection from predatory spirits. Drumlins are more farmers than fighters. In the vesica piscis between the outer walls of the settlements are orchards that sprout crystalline elements. The brilliant colors would put our gemstones to shame. These however are for consumption and technology instead of vanity. While it’s the best place to grow such crystals, it’s one of the more difficult with gabbros sneaking around in packs, looking for opportunities to raid fields. A security detail protects the gates that must be opened for travelers along the freeway.
