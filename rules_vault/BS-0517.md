## Quadrotor Flight System

Four vectored helicopter-style rotors provide a critter with limited flight and hover capability. Because it is nearly impossible to train a critter to directly operate the system, these are primarily controlled by a limited drone pilot system that responds to simple directions from a trained critter or wireless commands from an RCC, rigger, or equivalent.

**Game Information:** A critter must be equipped with a critterjack and receive special training in order to use this accessory. When activated, the quadrotors provide the critter with a flight speed,

with Handling 3, Acceleration 10, Speed Interval 20, and Top Speed 120, along with the capability of hovering in one spot. When landing, a critter must make an Athletics + Agility (2) test or fall prone. The quadricopter accessory can be controlled by an RCC, rigger, or equivalent. Its rating determines the Pilot rating, hardcoded with a maneuvering autosoft of the same rating, and is incapable of running any other software. For all Piloting tests, use Pilot x 2 for the dice pool—no Edge may be used to influence this test, even if the critter has a heuristic processor. Using the rotors requires a Minor Action from the critter each round, which counts as its movement action.

The included battery array provides enough power to fly or hover for 10 minutes before needing a recharge. Additional battery arrays take up 1 capacity and provide 10 additional minutes of flight time each.
