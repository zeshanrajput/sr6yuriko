## Fish Out of Water

Whether you’re not dressed for the fancy soiree, or maybe you’re a troll in an elven rights convention, you really don’t belong here. You take a –3 dice pool penalty to all Influence or Con tests while affected by the status. The only way to remove this status is to remove yourself from the situation. If you encounter someone later who witnessed your faux pas, they can invoke this status by recalling the circumstances.
