## **The Good(ish) Guys**

**Despite the Disians’ best efforts to remain hidden, their plans to strip-mine the material plane would require a lot of effort and material to make happen. Such plans and moves rarely go unnoticed; the trick is whether or not someone actually does something about it.**

**One of the frst to keys to dealing with the Disian threat was the great dragon Arleesh. Given her propensity toward combating magical threats to the Material Plane, she quickly realized something** _**bad**_ **was happening. And being a great dragon with who-knows-how-much resources at her disposal, she did something by actively researching and hiring other professionals to assist. The deeper they went, the more they discovered just how deep the Disians had dug themselves and what their goals were. Arleesh knew that even she and her immediate allies could not do this alone. From this, new alliances were formed, new assets recruited, and direct-action groups such as Grey Cell were formed. Since then, they’ve been active across the globe, doing whatever they can to thwart or at least hinder the Disians whenever possible.**

**But despite winning some spectacular victories, the good guys are still losing the war.**
