## Social HUD

The social HUD app links to a user’s social media profiles and provides a visual overlay that includes public information about anyone observed. Supplementary (such as private information) can also be added to these profiles. Name, contact information, and social media profile are displayed for anyone with a commlink that’s not running silent and broadcasting a SIN. Combined with the facial scanner, this app will attempt to display information about individuals even if they’re not broadcasting their identity or SIN.
