## Gaoling

High in the mountains is the country of Kaol. The jagged terrain gives way to geometric terraced settlements with pyramid-shaped arcologies. These are the homes of the kaolin. The settlements have a more contemporary look to their structures, with various-sized pyramids of polished stone. Geomantic religious orders look to harmonic resonance as a force. Basaltic obelisks, rings, and stairs litter the landscape surrounding the pyramids. Each is struck at various times as part of multiple ceremonial events, holidays, or just to gain blessing for the day. The kaolin of Gaoling can be a bit zealous when it comes to outsiders. To them, geomancy is not just visual spacing and alignment, it’s also about pitch and echo of sound. Blasphemous are those whose words bring discord.
