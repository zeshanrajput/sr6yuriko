## Evo Pet Pals

For as long as metahumanity has had pets, we’ve been talking to them. With Evo Pet Pals, your pet can now hold a conversation with you! These adorable critters are implanted with Evo’s critterjack and critter comm augmentations. And, so you never have to worry about those unpleasant odors, they now offer an option with tailored pheromones.
