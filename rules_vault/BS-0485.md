## Venom Fangs

Critters with natural weapons can be augmented with specialized injectors that can deliver any drug or toxin with the injection vector. The base augmentation carries enough of the drug or toxin for a single dose. This may be used for offensive combat purposes, but it is sometimes employed to deliver life-saving medication (in which case the augmentation is rebranded as an emergency medicine injector).

**Game Information:** The first time the critter deals damage using their natural weapons, the drug or toxin is delivered as well. If the damage is completely resisted, the toxin or drug is not injected but is saved for a later use. An unsuccessful attempt to damage a target does not waste the loaded “venom.” Because the drug or toxin is injected, even full body armor and helmet with a chemical seal will not prevent a target from being dosed bya successful venom fangs attack. Natural weapon shielding is not required for the venom fangs augmentation, but it improves the chances of dealing damage, which improves the chances of successfully injecting the drug or toxin.

**Wireless Bonus:** The drug or toxin is not released unless a Control Device Minor Action is first used to arm the injectors. Unless this feature is activated, the payload is automatically delivered with the first successful attack the critter makes with their natural weapon. If the critter has an implanted critterjack, they can be trained to arm the venom fangs upon command—the advantage of this approach is that the augmentation does not need to have wireless functionality enabled (and is less vulnerable to interference from the Matrix) in order to selectively employ the drug or toxin.
