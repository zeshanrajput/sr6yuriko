## **The République du Québec**

**Back in 2014, the Québec government signed the** _**Traité de Kahnawake**_ **(Treaty of Kahnawake). This was a gamble and granted the Québec indigenous people self-governing autonomy on the ancestral land within the Republic. Since then, Québec has mostly upheld its end of the bargain and left the indigenous people alone. So long as we are not causing trouble on Québec soil, the government pretty much ignores us or, at worst, has a “Let the tribal leaders deal with them” attitude.**

**But while they will provide some assistance and will accept our help against anyone infringing on their land, the Québec indigenous population doesn’t tend to fully support us nor to fully agree with our ultimate objective. Having gained their own autonomy and living by their rules and traditions, they just don’t see the need. This attitude has led many within Warpath to feel that they have essentially sold out to the white men and not asked as much as they should have.**
