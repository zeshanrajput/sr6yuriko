## Governors Island

Speaking of Ares, they have complete ownership of Governors Island. The corporation continues to use the island as a military training facility and testing ground. After losing their Manhattan contracts, Knight Errant redeployed a large portion of their force here to undergo training for future missions. From what observers can tell, that training includes simulated street-to-street battles with mercenary groups that are brought in and paid to serve as the opposition.
