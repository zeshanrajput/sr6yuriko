## Game Information What Grows Back, What Doesn’t

Some metagenic qualities involve parts that stick out in odd or possibly awkward ways, which increases their chances of being somehow cut off. That can leave the gamemaster needing to decide what grows back and what does not.

The basic guideline is that anything keratinous in nature (such as spikes, bones, or hair) can be broken or even snapped off and will recover in time. Any such parts that are lost contain genetic material that can be used as a Material Link (p. 144, _SR6_ ). The additional costs for recovery from these kinds of incidents is part of the overall changes to Lifestyle costs (p. 178).
