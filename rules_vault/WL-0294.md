## **Megalodon**

**The megalodon (** _**Carcharodon neomegalodon**_ **) is a huge pelagic variant of the great white shark, growing up to sixteen meters long. Its dorsal side is slate-grey, shading to white ventrally. Its dorsal fn is highly prominent and wicked sharp. An irregular pattern of black stripes is unique to each specimen and provides some additional camoufage. The shark’s mouth can be three meters across, and it is flled with rows of jagged teeth that are constantly lost and refreshed.**

**The megalodon quite rightly has a fearsome reputation. Sharks are some of the most effcient predators in the water, and the megalodon is a cut above even the great white. Megalodons will eat literally anything in the water; some specimens have been found with parts of cars or whole motorcycles and small boats in their guts.**

- **If it’s in the water, it’s fair game for a megalodon.**
