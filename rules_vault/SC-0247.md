## Shock Rocker

To you, the concepts of Resonance and Dissonance aren’t just ideas about the Matrix, but they also apply to your art and how you connect with the people and cultures around you. The megacorps would love to think of art as something that is made to serve their marketing, reducing it to nothing more than a tool to understand and motivate their target demographics. Whether your art form is film, music, theater, painting, digital sculpting, or any other art form, you are sickened by its commoditization. You are disgusted by the shallow and capitalist nature of the material that is produced in such a barren climate. You’re here not just to show everyone how it’s done—you feel the need to show everyone how to separate real art from corporate banality. You use your art to criticize the status quo, shake things up, and satirize and mock what others hold arbitrarily sacred. You also use your unique abilities as a Dissonant technomancer to sabotage and undermine the media that offends you.
