## Laid-Up Brawler

You tore your way through your youth, beating and brawling through any obstacle you encountered. It worked great, until it didn’t. You made too many people mad, left too many scores to setDECKERtle, and eventually it came back to bite you. Some of your previous victims pooled funds together to hire someone who gave you the beating of a lifetime. You put up a good fight and inflicted some deep scars, but eventually you went down and were subjected to a long series of kicks and stomps. The resulting list of injuries was long: concussion, fractured jaw, broken ribs, shattered kneecap, and more. You were laid up for a while—bedridden for more than a month, slowed and weakened for much longer. You had plenty of rehab work to do, but also a lot of time where you couldn’t do much physical activity. You needed something to do, and you turned to the Matrix.

At first it was just simple idle information-gathering and trid watching, but the more time you spent in that environment, the more you wanted to make it your own. It started with custom avatars, but before long you decided you wanted to re-shape things you weren’t supposed to control, and the door opened to hacking. In battling security systems and other hackers, you found some of the same thrill you found in brawling people face to face.

As your body healed, you regained many of your fighting skills, but you were reluctant to give up on the new arena you had entered. You now follow both paths, dedicated to not backing down from any attack that comes your way. And to never being caught off-guard and outnumbered again.
