## **Ward Foam**

**Standard Equipment: Ceramic plate armor, dual-brain pilot system, chameleon coating (rating 2), electromagnetic shielding, external communications system, grey mana warding (rating 6), increased structural integrity (rating 6), integrated cyberarms (external, Agility 5, Strength 10, retractable spurs with GMT alloy construction), life safety system (rating 2), nano maintenance repair system (rating 4), passive stealth features, reinforced cargo compartment (4 CF), rigger cocoon, searchlight, secondary propulsion system (rocket booster), totally enclosed construction, winch (rating 1)**

**An alchemical preparation anchored to a foam-producing liquid stored in an aerosol spray can. To use it, spray foam on the area that you would like to be protected by the mana barrier, and then trigger the preparation by touching the foam. The spell then activates and provides a temporary ward on the surface that is covered by the foam.**

**Hardpoints: 1 huge hardpoint (large weapon turret: RPK HMG w/ expanded ammo bins), 2 large hardpoints (large shoulder weapon mounts: one missile launcher, one grenade launcher), drone rack (10 mini drones: Evo high explosive roller bombs)**
