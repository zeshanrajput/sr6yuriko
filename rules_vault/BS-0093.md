## Increased Spectrum Hearing

This augmentation expands the user’s hearing beyond metahuman capability. The user is capable of hearing infrasonic and ultrasonic frequencies from as low as 5 Hz to as high as 120 kHz. Having access to a wider range of audio frequencies enables the user to hear sounds that are ordinarily drowned out by distance or muffled by intervening barriers. Not only can a user hear dog whistles and ultrasonic motion detectors, they reduce the cost of Edge boosts by 1 (minimum 1) for any audio Perception tests.

**Cyberware overdrive:** Gain +2 dice, one of which must be a wild die, for an audio Perception test.

**Wireless bonus:** Increased spectrum hearing can sync with cyberware that augments the user’s voice to permit speech solely in infrasonic or ultrasonic frequencies that cannot be heard by those with standard hearing range.
