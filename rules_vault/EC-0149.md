## Gaeatronics Mountain HQ

Dubbed “The Mountain” because of its revolutionary design, the Gaeatronics facility is largely underground. Nine stories are above ground level and covered in earth to create the appearance of a living mound. Trees, grass, and flowers, grow on the facility, and the company recently built a children’s park at the very top to further the usable green space.
