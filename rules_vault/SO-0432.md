## Flirt

You have a knack for attracting and cultivating romantic interest.

**Cost:** 5 Karma

**Game Effect:** Gain a point of Edge on social skill tests that involve romance, including (but not limited to) seduction. If this Edge isn’t spent on that test, it is lost.
