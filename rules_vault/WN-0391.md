## Encounter 2: It’s a Trap!

The runners are contacted by May O’Shay (use News Reporter, p. 169, _Sixth World Companion_ ) for a job meet. If pressed, she claims to have gotten their number from Swen Rellik. The truth is, she hacked Swen’s commlink and stole their contact info, but Swen isn’t aware of that. If the runners check with him, he’ll tell them he knows her, she’s a solid reporter, and he may have mentioned them over drinks with her at some point.

May wants to meet the runners around 23:00 at Club Neo in The Pit. She mentions that it’s currently closed for renovations, but the owner still lets her use the back room for meetings. She says she’ll leave the rear entrance in the alley open and meet them inside at 23:00.

Club Neo is closed for renovations thanks to some recent gang violence. May is waiting inside to meet the runners, but she has hired another runner team to take them down so that she can question them about what they know.

The other team has a similar make-up to the PCs’ team (generally, their skills are 2 ranks lower than those of the PCs, and their attributes 1 rank lower). They have two extra members, both of them muscle (use Mafia Debt Collector, p. 205, _SR6_ ). After the runners have come in to meet with May, the other team emerges from a building across the alley and comes through the rear door, non-lethal attacks blazing. They use guns and spells, avoiding anything with an area effect so as not to harm May.

May seems suspicious because she takes cover a little too quickly when the attack occurs (Perception [3] test to notice). If the attack is successful, the team wakes up in restraints with May asking questions about their work in Manhattan. If the attack fails, May claims the attack is the runners’ fault and they’re too hot to hire right now. She



says, though, that she’d love to buy some data on any big work they’ve been doing lately. She’ll try to keep their attention on making money from her. If she notices they are searching commlinks, May will try to get away as quickly as possible before the runners can find her contact info on their attacker’s commlinks.
