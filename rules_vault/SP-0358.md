## **Mana Storage**

**Units of mana are used in place of the Magic rating for any test initiated by a Disian that would normally use that attribute. Once expended this way, the units are gone, and another test will require more units of mana to be used.**

**Units of mana can be stored in internal or external pools. Any native Disian can internally store a number of units equal to 50 times their (Willpower + Initiate Grade). The amount of externally stored mana available depends on the mana storage method (see p. 175 for these devices). A Disian may move any number of units between their internal storage and any external storage within line of sight—up to the maximum capacity—as a Minor Action.**
