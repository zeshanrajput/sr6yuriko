## New Social Moves

In addition to the social maneuvers listed in the _Sixth World Companion_ (p. 157), there are a number of new actions available. These can be used with the optional extended social tests or basic tests. There are all manner of tricks and cons a socially capable person can use to bend a situation to their will.
