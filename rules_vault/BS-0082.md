## Electrochromic Hair

The user’s hair follicles on the scalp, and optionally the face, are replaced with thousands of receptors that are each mated to a myoelectric fiber optic filament. These filaments can broadcast any color, mix of colors, or even digital imagery in the same manner as the electrochromic clothing feature (p. 265, _SR6_ ). The filaments can extend or contract their length by up to fifty percent. It takes a Minor Action to alter the color or style of electrochromic hair, but the change takes two combat rounds to complete. Cost is based on length per ten centimeters (minimum 10 cm, maximum 1 meter).

**Wireless bonus** : Gain a free Minor Action after spending one to perform an alteration or download a new pattern.
