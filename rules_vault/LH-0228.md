## \<06-02-83/1819:58>

We just got our first look at the territory surrounding the Disian compound. It’s a multi-kilometer ring of blasted dirt and mud with the occasional charred stump of a giant oak tree. The entire area is crawling with packs of chimeras, including a really high percentage of destroyers. The big ones, not the little ones that are only the size of a Roadmaster. Our Unseelie guides tell us that the chimeras aren’t very disciplined and will move to any point in the perimeter where there’s an attack. So, we’ve decided to set up a distraction, then sneak across with the explosives.
