## **Security Systems**

**and cross-reference biometrics as well. The SIN scanner rolls its rating x2 versus a threshold equal to the rating of the fake SIN. Licenses must be attached to a SIN; as such, whenever a fake SIN is checked, the licenses attached to it are as well. If the fake SIN passes the initial verifcation, then a second check is made against a threshold equal to the lowest rating of all fake licenses attached to it. When a fake SIN is checked and found to be false, the system immediately reports it to the authorities and the SIN is considered burned. Any further use of the fake SIN will automatically fail.**
