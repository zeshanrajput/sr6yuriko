## Sjörå

## (Water, Force 4)

Like undines, sjörå are water elementals with features that metahumans would consider beautiful. Both have the same misty hair and foam clothing, but the sjörå have a tail, similar to that of mermaids of legend. Sjörå are more carefree than the other elementals, and they buck the caste system to do what they want. Socially, only the undines and vadoses interact with sjörå.
