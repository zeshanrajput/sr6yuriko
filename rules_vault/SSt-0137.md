## **Your Network**

**Along with your teammates, you have a network of people who make your runs possible. The dealer who sells you weapons. The talismonger who sells you reagents. The fxer who connects you to jobs. The bartender who listens to you and tells you what the word on the street is. And so on. Your rep with them is important. You’re going to need special help from them at some point in time. The question you keep needing to ask yourself is, why would they be willing to do anything for you? If you don’t have a good answer to this, then you have a problem.**
