## Wanda’s Witchery (Lake Youngs)

This family-style restaurant may be known for its comfort food, but the owner, Wanda Dato, is a well-known Seattle performer and magician. Wanda’s features an Open Circle Night several times a week that is the real draw for the place. A variety of local artists and magicians show up to perform anything from music and poetry to magical illusion shows. Wanda is also known to take center stage from time to time to entertain her guests.
