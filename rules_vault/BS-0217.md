## Climate Adaptation

The individual is adapted to better survive in extreme climates (hot or cold; choose one). This quality grants +1 Edge to all Outdoor (Survival) tests and reduces Fatigue due to exposure by one level when in the selected climate. An advantage in one climate is a disadvantage in the other, raising Edge costs and increasing Fatigue status by the same level. These adaptations are not compatible with implanted dermal plating or skin modifications.
