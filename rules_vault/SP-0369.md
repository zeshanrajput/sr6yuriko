## **Game Rules for Faustian Crystals**

- **Power Point: This metamagic may be gained by any Disian. Each time this metamagic is taken, it allows a Disian to store an additional 10 units of mana above their natural maximum. This bonus capacity must be converted to a power point.**

**At a fundamental level, Faustian Crystals allow a metahuman to use stored units of mana gifted from their patron. They function as a memory crystal, allowing mental communication with their patron, coupled with a mana battery that powers the magical abilities gifted to the Faustian. These devices are created with a rating from 1 to 8 and can hold 20 times their rating in units of mana. Typically, these units of mana will be locked into pre-set abilities by the patron when the crystal is gifted. The Faustian holder of the crystal has no control over what abilities are generated; these are controlled by the Disian and can be withdrawn from the Faustian, or restored, at will.**
