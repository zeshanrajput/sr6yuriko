## **Hostage Rescue Team**

**Uses: equipment, rescue, tactical knowledge Places to Meet: bar, local hangouts Similar Contacts : negotiator, SWAT**

**A hostage rescue team (HRT) member is a highly skilled law enforcement professional trained to respond to and resolve critical situations involving hostages and barricaded individuals, and occasionally operate as part of anti-terrorism task forces. All members of the teams undergo extensive training in tactical operations, crisis negotiation, and various specialized skills to ensure they’re well-prepared for any situation. A hostage rescue team member’s role extends beyond magical, Matrix, or physical prowess, encompassing the ability to make split-second decisions, adapt, and work seamlessly within a team. Unlike SWAT, all members of an HRT must have the same physical profciencies, meaning an Awakened member of an HRT will be just as effective with a frearm as any other member of the team. HRTs are the epitome of combat and tactical maneuvers as well as profcient communication and strategic thinking.**
