## Magic

Your eyes and the veins beneath your skin take on a soft, eerie glow. If you have a Magic attribute, you become acutely aware of the presence of so much toxic radiation magic.

**Game Effect:** You find it difficult to use your own magic. All Edge boosts and actions cost twice as many points of Edge on any test that uses your Magic rating (unless you are a toxic magician). If you gain a second Magic mutation, the DV of all of your drain tests is increased by one (unless you’re a toxic magician).

When encountered in the Wastes of Miggon, they are usually wearing armor equivalent to an armor jacket and are armed with at least one firearm. The most common guns are scrappy locally scavenged or scratch-built equivalents of the Ruger Super Warhawk and AK-97.
