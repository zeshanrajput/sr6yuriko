## **The Followers of the Ancient Way**

**Unable to trust the hackers at the Nexus, Ghostwalker decided to use the ancient information at his disposal to create a cult of loyal followers as his personal Matrix support. Scrolls in his possession described an ancient religion centered around what is called the Seed of Knowledge and the ancient ability to manipulate stored information in the same way as modern technomancers. By carefully crafting the message and providing personal guidance to a few technomancers, Ghostwalker created a new religion catering to the Emerged, with himself at the leading position. While the number of followers was small at frst, and the group was very secretive, the numbers have slowly grown by recruiting technomancers from around the world and bringing them to Denver. Ghostwalker has also started construction on a massive structure called the Temple of the Ancient Way at what was once the Alpha Site of the Nexus. This visibility of the project seems to indicate that he will soon go public with the new religion, expecting it to grow in the same way as the Children of the Dragon.**
