## **Can prepared vessels be re-used?**

You must prepare the vessel for each summoning of a spirit. You can prepare a vessel during your downtime (minor). Once the vessel has been possessed, you must re-prepare the vessel prior to another summoning. Preparing the vessel is done by buying hits and is not subject to Edge use.
