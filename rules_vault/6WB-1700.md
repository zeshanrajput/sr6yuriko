## Factory Blood

You come from a family of AG Chemie factory workers or dwellers on one of Strausberg’s toxic waste dumps. Not only are you used to all kinds of toxins in the air, food, and water, you are built to endure the most awful environments.

- Districts: AGC Siemensstadt, Strausberg

- Cost: 10 Karma

- Game Effect: Characters with this quality gain the critter power Immunity (Pathogens, Toxins) with a rating of 2, but also gain a noticeable sickly appearance (greyish skin, patches of missing hair, bloody cough, slight limp, etc.) that decreases Charisma by one (to a minimum of 1).
