## Tiding Agents

**Matrix Assets:** Use Seal Team 6 Counter Electronics Commando (4), p. 209, SR6. **Physical Assets:** Use the following NPCs found on pp. 207–208, SR6.

- Lone Star Combat Mage (2), raise Astral and Sorcery to 6, add Physical Mask, Ram (Door), and Silence (Area) to spells.

- Marine Corps Special Operations Forces (4)

- Add Datataps, Matrix Relays (rating 3, p. 41, Hack & Slash), and Tiding Commlinks to gear.

- Add silencers or sound suppressors to all ranged weapons. All ammunition is gel rounds.
