## Augmentation Integration

You gain an Essence hole of 0.25 Essence per level of this power (see **Essence Holes** sidebar). This Essence hole allows you to be augmented with delta grade (or better) augmentations—augmentations of a lower grade are rejected by your body. Note that if your Magic drops to 0 or your Essence drops below 6, you lose this power. If this happens, you immediately and permanently lose Essence equal to the Essence cost of your installed cyberware. If your Essence drops to 0 because of this, you die. If this power is taken at character creation, delta-grade (or better) augmentations can use the essence hole created—though most augmentations will be unavailable at character creation because they may exceed an availability of 6.
