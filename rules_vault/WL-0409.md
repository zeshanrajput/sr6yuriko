## **Spread the Love**

## **(Minor Action: Command Critter)**

**You command your critter to use its critter power against multiple targets, turning a single-target power into an area-effect power. The base radius is two meters, and each additional Edge you spend increases the radius by two more meters. All of the targets of the power gain a bonus point of Edge on any test to resist the effects, as the power is mildly weakened by spreading it out over multiple targets.**

**• Cost: 4 Edge (2m radius, +1 Edge per +2m radius)**
