## Eel

(Deltameth, Duck and Weave)

- **Vector:** Ingestion, Inhalation

- **Speed:** 1D6 combat rounds

- **Duration:** 10 x 2D6 minutes

- **Power:** 13

- **Effect:** +4 Reaction, –3 Strength, +1 Willpower, + 3 Intuition

Somebody looked at betameth and thought, hold my soyweiser. Eel is typically sold in a crystal form that can be ingested or smoked for a quick hit. While high, the user experiences amplified sound and light, and their brain feels like it is running on overdrive. This drug is popular with some combat specialists, but it is very addictive. When the drug wears off, the user resists 6P damage and gains the Hobbled status for the next ten minutes.
