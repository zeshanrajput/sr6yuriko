## The Lower Level

Twenty meters below the warehouse is a large underground complex. It consists of a maze of hallways connecting meeting rooms, storerooms, laboratories, hermetic lodges, libraries on esoteric magical topics, and the full-time residences of about eighty scientists and support staff, with another twenty dedicated security officers (use a mix of Lone Star SWAT Officer and Lone Star Combat Mage, p. 207, _SR6_ ) and Matrix support for the framework host. The complex buzzes with activity twenty-four hours a day, so the team had better have a good story ready for why they are in the facility. The most notable feature is a submarine bay open to the Hudson that contains three small dry submersibles similar to the Proteus Marianas but lacking the deep-sea capacity (rated for 200 meters depth).

Noise to the surface is 6, but it doesn’t seem to be a problem for Mr. Seven, who will occasionally send a text suggesting a good location for a transmission tap or park a MapMaster drone in a hidden location and then jump into another one. The team will soon discover the facility is called “Launchpad” and it exists to serve another nearby facility referred to as “Airlock.” This other facility is under Governors Island and it is the location of the portal. This fact can be discovered in several ways. It can be deduced from the geomancy charts in the hermetic lodge or gained from social interactions with the facility’s employees. Jumping into one of the submarines will reveal where the ships have been traveling to, and the information can also be gained directly from files in the offline framework host. If the runners do not gain the information on their own, Mr. Seven will eventually dig it out of the host through the transmission taps and pass it along.
