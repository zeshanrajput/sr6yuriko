## Hell’s Kitchen

The literal hotspot of Puyallup District. It’s relatively within the Mowich Lava Flow’s crumbing

In a failed attempt to create Crimson locally, the Yakuza brought some Awakened Dante bees for pollination. The bees escaped out into the wild after the little jolt of 2043. Some managed to survive south of the city in the remains of “wine country” and thermal vents. A few locals have managed to create an apiary to collect honey for brewing. What they got was what could be described as a knockoff Tír wine, complete with a dopamine stimulant.
