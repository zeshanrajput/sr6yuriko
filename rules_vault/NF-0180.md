## **Episode 4: Not So CalFree Now**

Watch as Chase brings his own brand of two-gun, two-fisted, red-blooded Old West justice to the West Coast! BTL-heads and sprawl gangers alike will find themselves under the gun, and Chase brings back a fan-favorite ammunition option, Wild Card™, where the only guarantee is that his straight-shooting Ares Carnivore tactical revolvers will feature mixed ammunition. Chase won’t know what he’s shooting until he pulls the trigger!
