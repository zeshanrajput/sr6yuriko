## The Job

The players receive a commlink message that says, “It’s Silver Nail. I have a job for you all. The pay is good, and you’ll be able to do something that makes a difference. I will be at Fluffy Duck’s truck this afternoon at 4:00 p.m. Whatever your decision, the food is on me.”
