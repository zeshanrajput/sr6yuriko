## Damaging and Repairing Cyberware

Cyberware takes Matrix damage just like any other device and ceases to function if bricked. The Matrix Condition Monitor is determined by its grade (see sidebar). Under normal circumstances, cyberware does not take physical damage because its structure is integrated into the character’s Condition Monitor. There are certain special cases, however, where a piece of cyberware can suffer physical damage, such as using the optional Absorb the Blow action (p. 149, _Sixth World Companion_ ) or the new Edge Boost below. When cyberware takes damage in such ways, it is considered broken until repaired.
