## The Cadence

The cadence is a phenomenon whereby a group of combatants synchronize their movements. And potentially start singing. The cadence also prevents anyone who is engaged in combat from becoming Fatigued, which lasts until 10 combat rounds (thirty seconds) after combat has finished, at which time characters gain the Fatigued III status until they have rested for at least 5 minutes and (if possible) drank some water or used some other means to rehydrate.

For every combat round that a character successfully attacks an opponent and is not successfully attacked, they gain one point of cadence. For every full 10 points a character has, they gain a +1 bonus to Attack tests, Defense tests, Attack Rating, and Defense Rating (i.e., +1 at 10, +2 at 20, +3 at 30). This continues to accrue until the character has the maximum bonus of +3.

When a character is part of a team or group in a fight, cadence points are only accrued in combat rounds where everyone meets the requirements (successfully attack and not hit). Should a group get to the maximum cadence bonus of +3, the characters will magically begin to sing. Players are encouraged to work together to identify a song known fully by one character in the group for them all to sing. Singing it out loud is not required but is encouraged, as the pulse and beat of the battle has gripped the hearts of their characters and propels them to new heights of skill, bravery, endurance, and camaraderie. And it’s also a fun thing to do around the game table.
