## Flying Carpet

This is not something that can be purchased, but rather is a type of free spirit found occasionally on the Astral Sea and a few desert-like metaplanes. Stories have circulated of artifacts and rituals that can summon these entities, but for now those rumors are unconfirmed. Any services obtained from these entities would have to be gained by negotiation or friendship. A flying-carpet-type free spirit always takes the form of an elaborate Persian rug of a variable size. Some are as small as a half meter by one meter, while others can comfortably seat a dozen passengers. These entities use the profiles of an air spirit but always have an additional special power: the ability to levitate at a walking speed and carry a number of metahumans—up to their Force—along with them. Of course, they can then use their Movement power on themselves and everyone riding. Flying carpets are non-verbal but understand the intent of a conversation and will attempt to communicate with movements that mimic a metahuman’s body language—at least as closely as an animated rug can.
