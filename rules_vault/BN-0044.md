## Dual Natured

- **Requirement:** Essence 6

This is only available when specifi ed by the Shifter quality and may not be selected as an optional choice. Both animal and metahuman forms gain the Dual Natured critter power (p. 223, _SR6_ ). This power is lost if your Magic attribute drops to 0 or if your Essence goes below six. You may learn the Astral skill.
