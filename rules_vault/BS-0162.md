## Fangs

Cosmetic fang implants can be installed by the same mall kiosks that do ear piercings, but cybernetic fangs are far more capable than simple replacement teeth. Cyber implant fangs can retract back into the user’s maxillary bone to conceal their presence. They can also deploy an injection toxin or drug when the user successfully bites a victim in a close combat attack. The fangs’ reserCHROMEvoir holds two doses, and it can be refilled via an external port.
