## Obstacles

Here’s the problem. Sometimes folks are a bit cautious (especially if they’ve been fleeced before). They may open the box and realize the con. You’ll need to make a choice here, but the easiest to do is just leg it. This is where investing in a wheelman pays dividends.

However, this may not be viable. Some folks are fine with lying, but running over folks is a line they won’t cross. You’ll have to talk your way out of it. My recommendation? Act all indignant. Make it seem that you’re out nuyen, too, and you fell for the same con you’re perpetrating. If they buy it, return their credsticks and take back the box. Youcan always try again on a different day, somewhere else. Preferably far away.
