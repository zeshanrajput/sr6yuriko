## **Can I spend money for bribes (6WC, p. 195) to reduce the Heat Modifier while operating in an area?**

Yes.

**There are a lot of different options to dealing with Heat and Heat modifiers spread across the books. Is there a right answer to the values?** This is a replacement for all the rules that appear in the various books on how to deal with Heat. It’s not meant to eliminate the option to lie low, but just a simplifier for the various monetary values. As part of preparation for a run, you can spend 1,000 nuyen in bribes to reduce Heat modifier. If you want to bribe them after you’ve done something illegal, then it costs 2,000 nuyen. For dealing with Heat, you can immediately spend 1 Karma or 2,000 nuyen at the end of the run to reduce the Heat gained on the run by 1. Any Heat removed at a later time period will cost 4,000 nuyen per point of Heat.
