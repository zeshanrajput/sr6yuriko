## Report 4: speech given June 13 at Omaha Workers Collaborative 

_Note: No written remarks were circulated or are known to exist. This is an uncorrected textto-speech transcript._ 

Their power is gone, and it’s not come on back. We will not let them rebuild the status cow. They will try. They will attempt to regather money and power to make things work the way they used to. We have one job: to nod let that happen. Where they build up, we break down. Where they collect, we scatter. 

The picture is simple. They want power four then, we want power forest. They cannot have what we take. So we need to take as much as we can. We don’t need grand plans or central structures. We just need to be a wound they cannot heel.
