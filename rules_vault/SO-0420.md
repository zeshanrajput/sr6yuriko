## Game Information

## Complementary Skills

The following character-building advice is meant to be used either during character creation or when spending Karma. Pick up a rank or two in Influence and Con if you can. Even though a point or two might not be effective on their own, they give you an opportunity to assist the team’s face with Teamwork tests. Or in a pinch, and with the help of some Edge, a player character might be able to talk their way through a critical moment.
