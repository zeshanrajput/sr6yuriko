## **Serious Divining Talent**

**I don’t know that I need to detail this anymore. If there is a type of divining you’re interested in, Convergence has someone who is really good at it. Whether you’re analyzing crystal balls, cow entrails, or patterns on suits of armor, Convergence has someone who can help you understand what you’re looking at.**
