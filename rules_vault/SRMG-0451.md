## **For power training (p. 182), what does that entail if they don’t have the skill or power?**

For _Shadowrun Missions_ , this prevents them from getting more comfortable being around that spell, skill, or power but doesn’t allow them to actually use that power.

## **Can I buy a critter with the positive quality Exceptional (p. 184) for Magic, Resonance, or Edge; or to add a skill they did not already have?**

No. Magic, Resonance, and Edge are considered Special Attributes and cannot be used for Exceptional.

**Can I get the positive quality Low Maintenance (p. 184) and High Maintenance (p. 185) at the same time?** No.
