## **The Hook**

**The player characters’ fxer reaches out and asks them to meet at a nightclub for drinks and a job offer. Ms. Johnson’s name is Lucia Quipp of Horizon. To sweeten the deal, she’s thrown in VIP passes that’ll grant them premium access for a year—just for agreeing to the meet. They won’t need to stand in line there anymore, even if they turn down the job. (Note: if the game setting is Seattle, the club is Dante’s Inferno.)**

**When the players arrive, Lucia strings them on for a bit, telling them to enjoy themselves. Drinks are on her tab, and she spends most of her time enjoying the club while her security team spreads out and tries to do their job. With a live band playing, she dances during most of their set. Only after does Lucia call the team into a meeting room to talk business.**

**The job is an extraction. The target is a VIP working for MCT. The target is known to be Awakened and will be traveling with a security team. Lucia knows the time and the route for this trip and needs the players to intercept and capture.**
