## **Australia**

**Regarding the Disian confict, the continent of Australia is currently one of the most active and hotly contested regions on the planet. Not only does it have some of the highest concentrations and convergences of ley lines on the planet, but its relatively isolated geographical location and lower overall population make it relatively easier to control when compared to other continents and countries. It is also one of the few locations where the location of a Harvester has been confrmed.**
