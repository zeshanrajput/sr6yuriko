## **Marketing and Branding**

**I’m going to touch on this division to wrap things up. Marketing makes certain that cameras are on the Knights when they do their patrols, and even more when the Cavalry rolls, collecting vids for future clients like it was some Vancouver blockbuster. They make certain that whatever tech the Knights are using, a civilianready version of the same gear is for sale in Ares stores in the region, cross-promoting heavily for a greater fnancial fow. Ares guns, Ares drones, Ares toys, all being marketed by the massive Ares PR machine. It’s one area that no other law enforcement provider can match.**
