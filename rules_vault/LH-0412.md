## Dutchville, PA: The Lost Town

Once a sleepy town of about thirty-thousand people, Dutchville became ground zero for a nightmare when the Disians first opened what would become their main portal back to their home metaplane. As a result, the entire town and its inhabitants were taken to the Disians’ metaplane, followed shortly by the entire UCAS III Corps. A fortress to protect the portal was created, but eventually, in the final battle of the Disian Shadow War, it was taken and destroyed by a Defender task force.

The manastorms and disruptions caused by the Disians that used to plague the area have since dissipated. But the entire place is simply empty. No trees, just enough grass to prevent erosion, and no animals to be seen for kilometers. There’s also no wind, just a complete, eerie silence that stands as a monument to the lives lost and insanity visited upon it.
