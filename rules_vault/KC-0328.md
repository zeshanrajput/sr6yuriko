## We Will Ren-Rock-You

On the adjacent property is a quarry that has been dug down a full 120 meters at its lowest point but has several shallower layers at one hundred meters, eighty meters, sixty meters, forty meters, and twenty meters. Each shelf has several hidden caverns that have been turned into hangars, each with a smaller tunnel that leads to a large cavern beneath the Defiance Dairy warehouses.

The quarry property also has a large operations building with a small office and a large storage warehouse for equipment. The office is no longer in use, but the smugglers keep a small security team (use Mafia Soldato, p. 207, _SR6_ ; add wired reflexes 1 and Ares Predator VI) hidden here in case of trouble at the quarry. The security team has off-road quads (use Yamaha Growler, p. 301, _SR6_ ) to move around the location quickly.
