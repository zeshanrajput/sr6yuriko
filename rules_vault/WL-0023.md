## **Mess with the Food**

**If a place has a critter, they have to feed it, and if they spent good money on it, they probably want to keep it healthy. That means doing more than just feeding them scraps from the corporate cafeteria. Detailed monitoring can probably help you learn when food is delivered and who delivers it. Find a way to put a little sedative in the food, and your run might encounter sleepy security. Tough to do that to a drone!**
