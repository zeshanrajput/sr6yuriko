## The Trail of the Asp

An assassin known as the Asp has racked up an impressive series of kills in Neo-Tokyo, Shanghai, Hong Kong, Mumbai, Beijing, and other Asian cities. Leaders of the Pacific Prosperity Group believe the Asp has gotten out of hand (and they’re worried the assassin might eventually target one of them. The cyber ninja is hired to track and eliminate the Asp.

The hunt starts in Singapore, where rumors say the Asp is lining up their next target. A face and a decker might help the cyber ninja find out where the Asp is currently there, but getting to the right destination will be all up to the ninja.

Sadly, the Asp has sources of their own, and they get word that the cyber ninja is on their tail. They are gone before the ninja arrives, and the chase is on.

This should be a long chase—taking the train to a port, jumping onto a boat, traveling to Indonesia, and attempting to make it to Jakarta. The cyber ninja needs to do more than keep up—they need to close on the Asp to catch them before the Asp can access their network and disappear.
