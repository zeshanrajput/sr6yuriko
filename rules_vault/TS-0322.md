## **Professional Rating 4: Security Agent**

**These sharp-dressed professionals usually stay quiet and pose a stark contrast to the extravagant environments they work in. They do their best to prove themselves worthy of their high price and put themselves between their clients and any danger. As these agents prefer to solve issues discreetly, keeping it cool is the best way to not have them dash for their SMG lockers. Historically (such as with Aigle Royal’s personnel), security agents are mostly humans, but more companies and outfts have started hiring from all metatypes.**
