## Arcology Shutdown

## (2059-2061)

On December 19, 2059, the Renraku Arcology was sealed off from the inside, cutting off all contact with its inhabitants and the substantial late Christmas shopping rush. UCAS military cordoned off the area, and shadownets eventually got word from inside that an insane artificial intelligence had taken over the arcology and was experimenting on its populace in horrifying ways. According to the news, Renraku operatives and execs provided a solution, though the building was crawling with hostile drones for a long time, and survivors of the nightmare were left scarred, physically and mentally. By 2063, the arcology had been transferred to the control of the Metroplex Guard.
