## **Agents of Psyche**

**Psyche often uses sprites, with a preference for Music and Data sprites. Free sprites, such as Wraith, are also encountered. Within the scope of this chapter, the user Denizen is also an agent, though acting more independently. Many members of the Light of Spirits tribe are also agents and can be met in any setting appropriate to the story. Among the more impressive abilities possessed by some agents are Mindspeech (p. 225,** _**SR6**_ **) and Restore Spark . The Mindspeech ability possessed by some agents cannot work across mediums, but it has been exhibited by both Emergent and Awakened individuals.**

**Those agents with the strongest ties to Psyche have also displayed the ability to rebalance an individual’s mind and personality. To explain in simple terms, Psyche has empowered certain individuals with the capacity to bring back into balance a being who has become lost to mental disease or extreme psychological trauma. Those agents of an Awakened nature, using their Mindspeech abilities, can rebalance the mind of a toxic magician or the being of a toxic spirit. Those agents of an Emergent nature can restore continuity to a Dissonant technomancer or a mad AI. The use of this ability and the conditions characters must meet to beneft from it are left to the gamemaster, but it should not be easy to access.**

**Psyche is not capable of restoring a being that has undergone mutation caused by exposure to toxic substances such as dangerous biochemical or radiation hazards, but it can restore the sanity of such an individual provided they are no longer exposed.**
