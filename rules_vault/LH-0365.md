## Demon Squirrel Chimera

While this chimera is between 1.5 and 2.0 meters tall with an average weight of approximately 80—90 kilograms, the moniker “squirrel” is misleading, though the demon part is accurate. Flat, curved structures along the sides of their skulls house extremely efficient audio-sensory organs. Along with skin-membranes that act as gliding wings, these chimeras have extremely long and strong limbs ending in hands with hook-shaped claws. These claws assist with climbing and are also used as weapons. Demon squirrels also have razor-sharp teeth, muscular jaws for biting attacks, and saliva that contains a potent neurotoxin. Finally, the demon squirrel can lift and carry away victims who are equal to or lighter than their own weight by using an ability that mimics a Levitation spell.
