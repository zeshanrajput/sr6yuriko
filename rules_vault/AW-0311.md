## Guardians of the Spire

The airspace surrounding the Spire is heavily patrolled by a fleet of airships flying a white flag,

which signifies the authority of the bureaucracy rather than surrender. These airship crews have been hired by the bureaucracy to keep the peace near the Spire. The guardian airship crews are mostly kindred spirits, though every officer is a powerful air spirit. At least four guardian airships roam the Spire’s airspace at all times, which extends to the nearest cloud islands—a distance of 4 to 5 kilometers (depending on the island). Any violence that occurs within this space draws the attention of the guardians, who swoop in to put an immediate end to the conflict. Normally the arrival of a guardian ship is enough to ward off pirate attacks and cause hostilities to cease; if it isn’t, they offer a warning shot before engaging. Any combatants foolish enough to continue fighting are attacked until they surrender or are killed. Spirits who are disrupted by the guardians are permanently banned from returning to the Place of Clouds.

The guardian airships are powerfully armed, and the movement power their captains provide make them incredibly fast and maneuverable. Each guardian vessel carries a complement of at least twelve Force 6 spirits. The ships are armed with cannons, grapple launchers, and boarding ramps. Their preferred tactic is to weaken a vessel with at least two direct hits before attempting a boarding action. They do not take prisoners—if you stop fighting, they’ll let you go with a stern warning (and you can bet there’ll be a guardian ship nearby whenever you’re within their airspace). If you keep fighting, they keep fighting. The guardians of the Spire fight fearlessly, as they know that the bureaucrats will provide them with a hefty bonus if they are disrupted in the line of duty. Their deaths here are not permanent, and they’re usually back on duty about a month after being disrupted.

The duties of the guardians of the Spire are limited to preventing violent conflict close to the Spire. They do not have any other duties or requirements, and will not provide any other assistance or services. They do not care if you were stolen from or if someone was kidnapped. The entire purpose of the guardians is to end fights.
