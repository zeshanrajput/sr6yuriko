## Devil Rat/Demon Rat/Glitch Rat

The devil rat is completely hairless and measures approximately a meter in length, including its tail. A standard specimen weighs three to four kilograms. The demon rat is a subspecies and measures about one and a half times the length and double the weight of a devil rat, while the glitch rat, another subspecies, is about two-thirds the length and weight of its parent species.
