## Fear

**==> picture [228 x 21] intentionally omitted \<==**

**----- Start of picture text -----**<br>
TYPE ACTION RANGE DURATION<br>M Major LOS Special<br>**----- End of picture text -----**<br>

This power allows a critter to fill its victim with overwhelming terror. The victim flees in panic and

doesn’t stop until they are safely away and out of the critter’s sight, at which point they gain both the Panicked and Frightened statuses. The critter makes an Opposed test using its Willpower + Magic against the target’s Willpower + Logic. The terror lasts for 1 combat round per net hit scored by the critter, while the status lasts double that number of combat rounds. Even once the fear fades, the target must succeed in a Willpower + Logic (Critter’s Magic/2) test to gather the nerve to face the critter again.
