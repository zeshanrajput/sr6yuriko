## swen’s questions

- What’s your specialty?

- Any corps you won’t work against?

- How long have you been in the shadows?

- Where’d you grow up?

- You ever mess up a job so bad you had to leave the country on the bounce?

- What keeps you here? Hooding? Cash? Vengeance?

He wants the runners to plant some data on a well-protected system. It’s easiest to access the system directly, but if their hacker is good enough, they’re welcome to handle it themselves. But Swen is hiring the team because he’s sure a little B&E will make the swap easier.

The pay is 15,000 nuyen total. Swen has an Influence dice pool of 13 dice. Each net hit increases pay by 1,000 nuyen up to a maximum of 5,000 nuyen.
