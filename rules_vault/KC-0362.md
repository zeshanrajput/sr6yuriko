## **Weapon Mount:**

Ingram Smartgun X [Submachine Gun, DV 4P, SA/BF, Attack Ratings 13/11/8/—/—, w/ smartgun, explosive ammo] ArmTech MGL 6 [Launcher, DV —, SA, —/8/8/3/, w/ smoke grenades]

- **Activated By:** Contact with Talia Rodrigo (Aztechnology), Alsiya (Horizon), or Hiteki Taktikaro (Renraku)

The Teching Crew is focused less on stealing the code and more on just thwarting the runners’ efforts to sell this item off to an opponent in order to keep

LET’Sthem from catching up. The Teching Crew focuses on keeping the runners from completing any assignments given to them by those megacorporations but will leave their efforts to work for Novatech or the government alone. Knowing this information will require the Teching Crew to spy on the team but they have several bits of tech that will do the trick. The intel gathering goal is to collect massive amounts of incriminating data on the PC team.

Their tactics and techniques are listed with each runner. They operate well as a team, but each of them is very independent in their methods, but they all feed their data back to the team’s data coordinator.

The team consists of CPU (Decker, Face, and Data Coordinator), RAM (Combat Decker), Tara Pulse (Tech Mage), Hard Drive (Rigger), and Zero One (Combat Heavy). During the intel gathering phase, CPU is a director and hub, but once combat ensues, Zero One is the lead, and CPU goes over to support hacking.
