## **Jillian Jewels (3) [2]**

Not quite as well-known as Melody, Jillian has been trying to work in the same area in order to get some working girl tips on the sly. She watches Melody often to see what it is that earns her the big bucks. She has seen some strange things but still doesn’t know her nature. She’s back in Terminal working if the runners want to talk to her.
