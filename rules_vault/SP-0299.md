## **The Job**

**The principal target is Dr. Julia Rickman, lead cryptologist for Transys-Neuronet. She was last seen working in her lab when a group of unknown individuals raided the facility, killing everyone in sight while simultaneously raiding data fles and fnally destroying the building.**

**In the chaos, Dr. Rickman managed to download all her project data and escape thanks to the heroic sacrifce of on-site security offcers and several of her colleagues. With the data secure on her personal cyberdeck, Dr. Rickman (barely) evaded the follow-up pursuit for the next several hours before making emergency contact with the T-N home offce.**

**Dr. Rickman’s last known location was ffty kilometers from the meet coordinates with Mr. Johnson, in an old industrial district that has since fallen into disuse and dilapidation. Her last message indicated that she was safe for the time being, but she’s certain that she’s being tracked and doesn’t know how long that will last.**

**The runners are also instructed to use the code phrase** _**“Scientia sit potentia”**_ **to identify them as friendlies and are given a secure commcode to notify Mr. Johnson once Dr. Rickman is secure.**
