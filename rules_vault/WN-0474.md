## Hadrian Williams-Lee

## (Fixer, Connection rating 2)

Williams-Lee can date his family’s New York Heritage to the 1800s, and he does so regularly. He believes his destiny is to inhabit a c-suite for SpinGlobal NY. Unfortunately, all of his efforts have done little more than turn him into SpinGlobal’s local go-to for all things shady.

Williams-Lee is a white elf with black hair and an ever-present emerald earring in his left ear and a diamond earring in his right. He is ever-alert for any opportunity for advancement, and he leans heavily on what he believes is his charm to take him where he wants to go.
