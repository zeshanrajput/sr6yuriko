## Spell Stripper

(Spirit-B-Gon)

- **Vector:** Inhalation, Injection

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** (12 – Body) hours, minimum 1 hour

- **Power:** 11

- **Effect:** Nauseated, dispels magical spells and banishes spirits

- **Effect:** +2 Agility

Derived from a North European mushroom species, woad is a cheap and legal combat drug. Users of the drug must make a Charisma + Willpower (3) test when injured or fly into a berserker rage. The +2 Agility is only active while the user is currently raging. Side effects include frothing at the mouth, fever, and a desire to bite. When the drug wears off, the user suffers –1 Charisma and cannot gain Edge on all Social tests for the original duration x 10.


(Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitors, or SSRIs)
