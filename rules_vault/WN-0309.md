## Who’s Who

The runners will encounter several influential Sixth World power brokers in this mission. Each is a major player in the corporate world and dangerous in their own right. Each of these operators has their own agenda, which does not necessarily align with that of anyone else in the adventure. This conflict of interests may provide space for the runners to get out of this alive, and perhaps even gain allies along the way.
