## **Are all the Adept Powers legal to use?**

Yes. Don’t forget that Commanding Voice doesn’t mean that the target will automatically do what you tell them to do. Orders may never result in the target directly harming themselves, though they may freeze up for one full round per level of Commanding Voice.
