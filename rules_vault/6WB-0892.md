## The Basics

If you want to run vehicles, there are a few key things to know, namely:

1. You can control vehicles remotely, or you can “jump into” them for a full immersive experience. You can also drive them like an ordinary citizen.

1. Vehicles and drones can only have one driver/controller at a time, regardless of method. You can’t be both jumped into a drone and controlling it remotely.

1. A vehicle driver or remote operator rolls Piloting + Reaction to defend against attacks made on that vehicle or drone. Naturally, an immobile vehicle or drone makes no defense tests against physical attacks.

1. Rigging can be done in AR and VR. Coldsim VR makes your Initiative Dice 2D6, while hot-sim makes it 3D6.

1. If you’re rigging remotely, noise penalties apply; see p. 176.

1. Vehicles and drones have an Attack Rating of the driver’s Piloting skill + Sensor and a Defense Rating of the driver’s Piloting + Armor.

1. With the exception of electricity damage (see pg. 109), vehicles are immune to stun damage with a DV equal to or lower than their Body.

With that covered, let’s talk about two specialized pieces of gear: vehicle control rigs and rigger command consoles.
