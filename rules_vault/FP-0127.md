## **Expert Program Service**

**Expert programs are specialized host-managed agents paired to a technical database for a specifc topic. These agents are semi-autonomous, and when given a question will conduct their own research in an attempt to provide an answer. The rating of the expert program is the dice pool used for the research, and depending on the complexity of the question, fnding a solution may take minutes, hours, days, or even months (see the Interval Scale chart on p. 312, SR6). Whether a question is solvable by an expert program is up to the gamemaster.**
