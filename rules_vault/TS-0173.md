## **Public Image**

**Since the 2060s, Sternschutz Security was known for its zero-tolerance policy and its notorious racism against metahumans. While the FBA may have tolerated the zero-tolerance part, they had a genuine problem with racism and pressed for changes. After getting rid of its former CEO, Klaus Daimel, Sternschutz’s international reputation saw a remarkable improvement in 2081. This improvement has shown in Sternschutz getting the law enforcement contract for Lille in France.**

**Another change that has a certain impact on public recognition is the new code of conduct, which Demiroglu put in place. Alongside this came Kim Loeffer, the new PR Manager and press spokesperson. The elf has been constantly pushing the corporation’s new politics in the media. Of course, she’s not too popular within the corporation’s far-right factions.**

- **She has a crosshair on her forehead. Some of Daimel’s remaining friends within the corp wouldn’t hesitate to hire someone new and finally get rid of her.**

- **Ecotope**

**The public image may have improved, but changes have yet to reach every precinct. There are still meta-racist employees on the streets, while others were transferred to offce services.**
