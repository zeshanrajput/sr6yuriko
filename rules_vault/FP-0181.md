## **The Nous(e) Relationship**

**Technomancers, AIs, EIs, e-ghosts, the Null, and many more all have something in common: each of them can interact with the Matrix and the Deep Foundations of this world, made possible in part by the Noosphere, a concept that was introduced as part of the** _**Hack & Slash**_ **upload. Of these, the most diffcult ones to imagine are the technomancers. Something about them confers a connection to your Matrix without some kind of hardwired object. Part of the problem lies in the Awakening of your world and the impact it has had upon your collective consciousness, and because people are taking the easy way out by saying, “it** _**must**_ **be magic.” There are certainly comparisons that can be drawn between technomancers and the Awakened, but I believe something in their genetics makes them receptive to nous felds in a way that the Awakened are toward mana, the astral plane, and the metaplanes.**

**This leads me to the theory that nous energies are more aligned with sapient energies and accumulation of sapient thoughts and constructs.**
