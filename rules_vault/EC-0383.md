## Neon Lagoon

Return water from the hydroelectric damn pours into deep lakes on the outskirts of town, where wild crawdads have managed to survive. Fishermen come out at night with lanterns to attract them to the surface and catch them in their net. Seasoned with green salt, this is another local delicacy only available in Neon K City.

- They don’t tell you that there’s bigger things in those lakes that also are attracted to the lights.

- Winterhawk
