## **Nezumi Chimera**

**Rats have been trained to use their sense of smell and memory to map out and navigate complex environments, including mazes, for well over a century. Disian artifcers have enhanced those abilities. This species of chimera was frst discovered in Neo-Tokyo and was designated “Nezumi.” Where the Aves chimeras are used to map or locate targets outside, the Nezumi chimeras ft the same skill sets and abilities in urban settings and inside structures. Additionally, all Nezumi chimeras exhibit the ability to fnd specifc items or targets. There are reports of them using psychokinesis to open doors, take key cards, or hide objects.**
