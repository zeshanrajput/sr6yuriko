## Owning Your Own Vehicle

New York maintains a vehicle cap, though you wouldn’t know it given the obscene number of cars jamming up the streets. The MTA offers two types of vehicle passes: Commercial and Personal. The bulk of both types of passes are issued to corpoESCAPINGrations, who then reissue them to employees. The result is a resale market of vehicle passes that often cost more than the city suggests you should pay for one.
