## Game Information

Blue-collar clothing provides a –2 penalty to Social Rating, but also provides a point of Edge on Con or Stealth tests made to blend in within locations with a Security Rating of D-A.

This clothing type is provided with a lifestyle category of Low or better.

Blue collar clothes have Capacity 1 for fashion accessories.
