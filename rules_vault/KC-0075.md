_Bungo:_ How high?

_Salem:_ If I wanted to tell you that, you would already know.

_Bungo:_ So why tell us this? What do you want me to do with this information?

_Salem:_ Understand that we’re all on the same team, don’t interfere, and sit back and enjoy the show!
