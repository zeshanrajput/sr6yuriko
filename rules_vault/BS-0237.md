## Tremor Reducer (1-3)

The tremor reducer, also known as the sniper’s best friend, uses a simple neurological buffer to handle the small tremors that the body naturally produces when trying to be still. Small “sparks” are sent to extremities to ensure that they don’t go numb, while general body shakes are nullified. In addition to allowing a character to remain still for eight hours per point of rating, the tremor reducer increases the maximum dice pool bonus for Take Aim (p. 42, _SR6_ ) by its rating.
