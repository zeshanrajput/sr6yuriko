## **AR Vertigo** 

Basically, AR is the default for everyone who's not in VR.  Opting to drop "down" from AR doesn't have much of a mechanical impact by the rules, but it is a roleplaying issue.  If you have to physically pull out your commlink and swipe on the display screen to do things like make commcalls, accept payment from Mr J, and so on, it can have implications on social tests, as well as any kind of technical task where speed is relevant. If a runner is dealing with a highly-technical Mr. Johnson and they opt to use their commlink like their grandparents did, Mr. Johnson gains a point of Edge. During combat, runners wouldn’t be able to take advantage of what others have seen and marked in AR unless they take the penalties of the quality.
