## p. 143, Spend Reagents, paragraphs 1 and 2 

_Adjust the text as follows:_ 

The leader must expend the number of reagents specified for the ritual, or an amount equal to the threshold if none are specified. The leader may expend more reagents to reduce Drain when sealing the ritual (step 6). For every dram of pure reagents spent (after the initial offering), the Drain Value is reduced by 1, to a minimum of 2. 

These reagents are used to seal the foundation for the ritual.
