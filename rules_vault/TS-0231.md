## **Miscellaneous Tools and Gear**

## **Badgecams**

**While the exact shape and capabilities d iffer between law enforcement service providers, badgecams are similar enough in capability to be represented in one entry. All of them can be touch-operated, turned on or off with a Minor Action, with all but Lone Star having wireless operation as well. In addition, Knight Errant badgecams self-activate whenever the offcer’s holster is opened and when a safety is switched off, while the Neo-PD badgecams activate as soon as an offcer leaves the station and switch themselves off when entering the station. By default, badges are set to record locally, then be uploaded via the patrol car, or when returning to precinct, but live transmission can be selected if needed. Lone Star badgecams are notoriously prone to shutting down whenever metahumans are approached.**
