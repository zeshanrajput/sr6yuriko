## The Job

The first step is hacking nearby hosts with cameras that show the precinct. While the precinct cameras are useless, as they were deleted in the data hit, there are six nearby cameras that face the right direction that can provide footage on those who left. Thankfully, they haven’t been wrecked by the hooligans running out of nearby Terminal.

Once they have the footage, the runners need to hit up the Matrix and some contacts to get some IDs. Then they can look for a good way to have a little face-to-face to chat with some of the more interesting characters who slipped out scot-free.

Hosts with cameras aimed at the precinct include a credstick machine across the street, the department store on the corner, the bar that shares the back alley, and the three bars across the street.
