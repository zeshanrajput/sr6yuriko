## Augmented Cyberlimbs

Cyberlimbs designed to enhance a critter’s capabilities are packaged as a set and are normally not available as individual replacements. For critters that walk on all fours, all four limbs are replaced as a set. For critters that have arms and legs, augmented cyberlimbs come in pairs.

**Game Information:** Augmented cyberlimbs must be purchased as a set, and all capabilities and modifications must be identical. Otherwise, these function much like cyberlimbs designed for metahumans and come in both obvious and synthetic versions. Synthetic cyberlimbs have a capacity of 8, and obvious cyberlimbs have a capacity of 16. They can accept the following standard cyberlimb accessories (p. 289, _SR6_ ): armor, attribute increase, and hydraulic jacks—these have the same availability and nuyen cost as their metahuman equivalents and take up the same amount of capacity. The only cyberweapons that a critter may use are close combat weapons, and these require special training in order to be effective. If a critter doesn’t receive special training to use a cyberweapon, it may only use its Agility as its dice pool, and no Edge may be gained or spent on behalf of any attack test. Ranged cyberweapons can be implanted in a critter cyberlimb, but they can only be used by a critter with a bio-drone harness.

The base Agility and Strength of an augmented cyberlimb match the critter’s natural attributes, and the attribute increase accessory is limited to a maximum of 4 per attribute.

The cyberskull and cybertorso options are also available for critters. Critters that normally have wings can receive cyberwings, but applying the armor attribute increase removes their ability to fly.

Each augmented cyberlimb (including cyberskulls and cybertorsos) provides one additional box to a critter’s Physical Condition Monitor.
