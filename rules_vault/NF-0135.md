## Recognition

This is the chance someone on the street will recognize the artist—not very handy when running the shadows. This number represents the threshold the average person on the street must hit in order to recognize the artist. If the artist is recognized while on a shadowrun, decrease Reputation by 1 and increase Heat by 1.
