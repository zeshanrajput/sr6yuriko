## **Gabrielle Al Thani Spinrad**

**It’s known that the Tiding was in Barcelona before and during the assassination of Johnny Spinrad, and it’s known that Gabrielle Al Thani Spinrad is not pleased with the organization. What’s not known is why. One theory is that she thinks they might be connected to the perpetrators—that their presence in Barcelona was gathering information about Spinrad that was fed to his eventual assassin. Another theory is that the Tiding is investigating the assassination, but Al Thani Spinrad is not happy with how they’re going about it, possibly because she disagrees with whatever theory they’ve developed. Whatever the reason, Al Thani Spinrad has not only been vocal in her low opinion of their skills, but she has also engaged in some sabotage, planting fake information in places where the Tiding is thought to be working so that they are led off course. So far, they haven’t bitten on anything she’s dangled in front of them, but that has just made her more determined to fnd the right tactic.**
