## Corrosive Spit Glands

The critter is augmented with special glands that can release a corrosive substance designed to affect inorganic materials on command. This substance does not corrode the critter’s own flesh but is toxic over time, dramatically reducing the critter’s lifespan unless they are also implanted with a toxin extractor.

**Game Information:** A critter must have a critterjack and receive special training in order to activate their corrosive spit glands. When used as part of a successful Close Combat attack, this permanently reduces an armor’s DR by 1. The critter may also attack a held object—if at least 1 net hit is scored, the target object is destroyed. No attack test is required to use the corrosive spit on an unattended, inanimate object. When attacking a drone or vehicle, add +4 DV.
