## Ozaki Nakazo

## Fujiwara-gumi, New Orleans

Someday Ozaki Nakazo hopes to be known as the king of New Orleans, but for now he has settled for being oyabun of the Fujiwara-gumi in the Crescent City. Nakazo has lived in New Orleans his entire life, and he dominates the local Yakuza through iron control and a cutthroat focus on business. But you don’t get to be the king of New Orleans through just prostitution. Nakazo brought back the art of the sokaiya racket to boost income. You can find him at the Summer Snowflake hotel in the Tangipahoa Parish. He’ll be the one wearing a Panama straw hat and a wide, colorful tie.
