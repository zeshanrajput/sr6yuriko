## BTL chips

Better-than-life chips offer an experience beyond what the average person can hope for. Jack in with your full ASIST activated, and you can feel everything being cast through the chip experience. This isn’t just a high, it is full sensory dive into a life a lot better than yours. Or anyone’s. BTLs lack the safety protocols built into standard simsense chips, so you feel exactly what the original creator felt, regardless of whether your body can handle it. BTL-related deaths are second only to murders in global causes of death.
