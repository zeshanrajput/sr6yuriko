## dweller on the threshold

## Posted by: Magister

You can’t discuss the Citadel without the Dweller on the Threshold. The Dweller sits in the space between our physical world and the metaplanes. Any magician who attempts to reach the metaplanes through traditional methods will encounter the Dweller. The Dweller is often the entity Awakened individuals can go to for higher knowledge. The Dweller has no set form. It is neither malevolent nor benevolent, and its attitude changes as much as its form.

As previously mentioned, when performing astral quests, nothing is hidden from the Dweller. It knows your thoughts, your mind, and your desires. This is purposeful in creating the challenges to accomplish before receiving your reward. The Dweller has the ability to mark places on metaplanes or create a bubble metaplane to tailor challenges based on the travelers. “Places” is the terminology used since the first publication of the Manual of Practical Thaumaturgy in 2036. It describes the type of challenge (Place of Battle, Place of Charisma, Destiny, Knowledge, etc.).
