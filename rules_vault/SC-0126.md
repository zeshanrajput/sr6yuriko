## Combat

Never forget you are an apex predator. You can regenerate mundane damage, enhance your attributes, or turn to mist for incredible mobility and stealth. Cyberware is very unlikely for you, but you can enhance yourself in a number of other ways with sufficient stolen Essence. It’s quite likely you are magically active, which opens as many options for you as any mage or adept. Regeneration means you can earn your teammates’ gratitude and appreciation by taking hits for them, but don’t carry it too far. Remember that you cannot regenerate magical damage or drain, so moderate what you attempt. It’s easy to forget that immortality does not equal indestructibility.
