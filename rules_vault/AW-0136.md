## Phlogiston

## (Fire, Force 6)

The phlogiston has a deer-like body with a long proboscis, similar to a tapir or large anteater. It has the appearance of soft lava, slowly cooling with many wrinkles across its form. It lives in the ash wastes, using its trunk to grab fruit from the higher branches or reach deeper into the boiling muck for other tidbits.
