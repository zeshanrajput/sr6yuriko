## **Can I build a character with Quality Path: Priest (Houngan or Mambo) (p. 16,** _**That Old Voodoo**_ **)?**

Yes, you may be part of the community and act as a guide to those around you if you honor the hidden world and call upon the Iwa to guide you.

Only the Hounsi quality is available at character generation. Mission runners need to choose from the League of Laveau (5 Karma) or the Order of the Golden Dawn (6 Karma) (p. 16-17, _Easy Come, Easy Go_ ) for their group contacts and pay the additional karma listed. Runners can gain Sur Pwen, Asogwe, and Bokor qualities by using a major downtime activity for each.

At the gamemaster's discretion, if the runner participates in or is part of a team that harms another member of the community or gains a negative reputation with the group, they are permanently expulsed from the group, lose the group contact, and permanently lose all of the qualities in the path.
