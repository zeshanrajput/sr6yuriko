## Co-Pilot

This app allows a team member to take control of a vehicle or drone linked into the network, so a rigger can focus on other tasks. Controlling a vehicle or drone with this app isn’t considered optimal.

**Rules:** When controlling a vehicle with this app, the new operator/driver can do so with their commlink or AR gloves, but at a –1 dice pool penalty. When controlling drones, the new operator can only issue commands as if in the captain’s chair.
