## **Time**

**Convergence formed based on the members’ belief that something was coming, and they need to come together to stop it. The big problems are that they don’t know what that something is or when it’s going to arrive. That means they are driven chiefy by the fear that whatever they’re doing right now to prepare won’t be enough, because the something is going to catch up with them before they are ready. Despite the laid-back attitude of many staffers, a few hackers and diviners working for Convergence have burned themselves out because they’re so worried about trying to get ahead of whatever’s happening that they don’t allow themselves any rest.**

- **And if people who have some perception of the future are worried about what’s coming, perhaps the rest of us should be a little concerned, too.**

**> Elijah**
