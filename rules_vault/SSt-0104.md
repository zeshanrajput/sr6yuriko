## **Alice in the Shadows**

**This second story is interesting, because it reminds you how the Sixth World is a weird place, where runs often go with some twists. This one in particular was diffcult to see coming. Indeed, here the client was sincere and forthcoming with the runners, but unbeknownst to himself, he hid crucial, game-changing information about the job.**

**I hope this will be a good lesson on why one should do one’s own legwork and never count on whoever is hiring the team to provide spotless intel. Because even if Mr. Johnson doesn’t want to conceal something, he may still be doing it.**
