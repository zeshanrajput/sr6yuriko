## Game Information

## HIPster

You understand the Horizon Internal Persona score system and are able to manipulate it with any fake Horizon SIN.

**Cost:** 6 Karma

**Game Effect:** When using a Horizon SIN on Horizon property, you gain an Edge on all Social Tests.
