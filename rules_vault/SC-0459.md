## Magic/Astral

- **Common life modules:** Academy Training, Adept Training, Astral Explorations, Clairvoyant, College/University, Magical Studies, Pragmatic Magician, Technical School

It’s easy to overlook, but probably the most important attributes for social adepts are also the most important for astral combat and astral-space-focused characters. A dedicated social adept with high Charisma and Willpower can go toe-to-toe with all but the most powerful spirits and dual-natured creatures. The social adept is not a discrete character class. There are mystic adepts who possess and develop social and mental abilities alongside their Spellcasting, Conjuring, and other magical abilities. Indeed, a mystic adept specializing in spirit or astral matters would be well-served by developing social adept abilities because of the overlap with all things astral and spirit-related.

The character grew up in a magic or astralspace-heavy environment. This could be within a specific magical society or community, like the Illuminates of the New Dawn or the Black Lodge. It could be a setting with a large amount of astral and spiritual activity, like Hong Kong, DenSOCIALver, or the Hudson Valley in New York, where the boundaries between worlds are thin and spirits (and other dual-natured entities) are able to function openly. Or perhaps the character lives almost entirely in astral space (e.g., a Blind or Infirm character whose waking life has been spent almost entirely astrally active) and became attuned to the necessities for survival in that world. Because the character is strong in mental attributes like Charisma and Willpower, they have skills and experiences that translate to meatspace.

There is a direct connection between this background and a conventional espionage one called extraplanar intelligence (EXPINT), which is the term for espionage among the metaplanes and directed at astral entities and astral communities like the Seelie Court, Dis, or even magically active locations on Earth like Denver or Hong Kong. This field has only existed for about twenty years and was originated by the UCAS military and Lone Star, with Knight Errant following. In the last ten years, the field has grown as the number of astral-related events that have inflicted physical damage on the world increase in number and intensity. The UCAS military has the most need, but recent events have gutted its capacities and spread them among other patrons … and into the shadows.

The combination of mystic and social adept also has a ready-made background for developing the social part of their role. A charismatic and strongwilled character, especially one already magically enhanced, is likely to find certain roles thrust upon on them by circumstances or simply by the player’s or gamemaster’s awareness of their social abilities.
