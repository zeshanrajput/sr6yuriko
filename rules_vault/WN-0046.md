## SoHo (A)

South of Houston Street is how NYC does quaint. The area bills itself as extremely family-business-friendly, with no major chain stores in the village and no corporations larger than A-rated Drift Technologies calling SoHo home. The area is racially diverse, touting the highest percentage of orks and dwarfs outside of Washington Heights. The local elven community is extremely insular, with most of the elven residents living in a six-building Telestrian-owned complex at the southern end of the neighborhood.
