## Wild Spirits

Dinosaur spirits are wild, beastly entities that favor physical activity and acting on instinct. They can be difficult to control and even harder to communicate with. Whenever a wild spirit shares their thoughts, they are always animalistic impressions rather than clear communication. Whenever a wild spirit must make a test while performing a service, add a wild die to the test. If the wild die rolls a 1, the spirit will stop following orders immediately after the action. They won’t go free or berserk, but they are likely to wander off as they wait for further instructions.
