## **Don’t: Think you Have to be Everything to Everyone**

**People can read the previous point and think it’s telling them to exhaustively build a model for everyone they know of how you going to build a relationship with everyone you know by discovering and fulflling their communication needs. That’s not the case. This is not about exhausting everyone on your contact list with endless communications. This is about making a few small touchpoints and then cultivating some crucial relationships. Remember that with some of these relationships, you’re not looking to become a best friend or intimate companion or anything. You just want to be someone they have friendly thoughts about. Pitch yourself appropriately.**
