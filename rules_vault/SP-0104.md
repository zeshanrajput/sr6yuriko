## **Allies**

**The simple part of this is, the Black Lodge will not have any allies who know they are working with the Black Lodge. They absolutely will hide their activities and try to build partnerships using other fronts. Their best tactic is to give nudges in directions people already want to go rather than try to force strange bedfellows together. That is to say, I would not expect to see Lodge operatives trying to bridge the Aztechnology-MCT divide, but I would not be surprised to see them trying to build support for the S-K people who want their corp to take more action.**
