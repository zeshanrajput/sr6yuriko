## Sorbian Blood

You are a member of the Sorbs, an indigenous Slavic ethnic group that has been living in the Spreewald region south of Berlin since the Middle Ages. With the coming of the Sixth World, the Spreewald—a vast network of canals, swamp areas, and deep woods—Awakened and quickly became very hostile to humans. The Sorbs’ extremely conservative Catholic faith and the return of magic gave birth to its own shamanistic school of magic, which the media dubbed “German Voodoo,” and it was one Sorbian priest of that faith by the name of Swonimir Kalauk who formed a pact with the Awakened Swamp on behalf of all Sorbian people. As long as they behave in deference to the pact, the critters, spirits, and plants there will leave the Sorbs alone.

- Districts: Köpenick

- Cost: 10 Karma

- Game Effect: Characters with this quality will be ignored by critters or spirits in the Spreewald, unless the character attacks them first or blatantly disrespects the ways of the Spreewald (i.e., starting a forest fire or driving a roaring jet ski for fun). Also, the character gains the language Sorbian (N) at no cost.
