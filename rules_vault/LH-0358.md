## Aetheric Sink

Similar to an aetheric jammer in construction, an aetheric sink appears as a slightly larger duraplast box with molded handles, but it operates on a completely different principle and creates a very different effect. At the core of an aetheric sink is a cyberzombie with no cybernetic enhancements, or at least nothing beyond the minimum required for life support. It’s a severed head in a box kept alive with machinery and enchantments binding its life force in place. The mind inside is effectively insane and the extreme despair generated by the condition amplifies the astral hazing associated with cyberzombies. This effect is magnified by the enchantments embedded in the device. and a high mana ebb is generated in the area around the Aetheric Sink’s physical location.

This device draws what electrical power it needs from any nearby wireless power sources. There is a Matrix interface associated with its power coupling, but it provides no information regarding the device. Assensing will reveal the nature of the device and the horror the prisoner is being subjected to.

**Game Rules:** Like other types of cyberzombies, this device causes Karma Hazing (p. 175, _Body Shop_ ), but on a larger scale. The aetheric sink has a rating from one to six, based on the duress the “occupant” is under and the enchantments holding the life force in place. This generates a high mana ebb in a rating x five-meter radius of its physical location. This mana ebb also expands by one meter for every three hours that the device is left in a single location. An aetheric sink has no on or off function and will generate a mana ebb as long as its occupant is “alive” and the enchantments are intact.

As with other types of cyberzombies, spirits refuse to go anywhere near this device. Because the victim inside is a metahuman, its use doesn’t cause a drop in the user’s Astral Reputation.
