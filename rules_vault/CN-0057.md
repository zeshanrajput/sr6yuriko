## Anti-corporate Eco-Terrorism

The primary targets of GreenWar operations are still megacorporate citizens and installations, especially of those corporations that exploit the Earth by mining or oil and gas extraction (Saeder-Krupp, Tanamyre Resources, Spinrad Global), are polluters (AGC, MCT), or mass producers (Ares, Shiawase). Tactics involve bombings of all kinds, chemical warfare (use of ricin and other poison gases), killings, demolition of office buildings (while people are still inside), using hacks to turn drones and automated guns against their owners, and other atrocities.
