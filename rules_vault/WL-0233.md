## **Ozian Baboon**

## **(Papio aerobaum)**

**I’ve written on this Awakened chacma baboon before and have since had a few more encounters, one of which happened while studying the bullyphant. Lyran had mentioned an elephant being lifted with the aid of some kind of manipulation spell, and while tracking a small herd of elephants that I thought had a bullyphant protector, I saw a fight of ozian baboons snag a calf elephant. Thanks to my familiarity with the spell, I was able to identify the ability as an innate Levitation spell that they can all use and even combine efforts.**
