## The Job

Anderson Chapel is the district mayor of Downtown and he needs to regain some political clout. He needs the runners to handle a data recovery inside the Deep Blackout Core. He tells the characters about a government office that has not been high on the recovery and repair list, which happens to hold some important information that Chapel needs in order regain some lost favor in the new national government. He offers the team access to his connections and a 10,000¥ payday if they will head into the blackout zone and recover the data. A Negotiations test nets an extra 500¥ per hit, up to 5 hits.

The data is actually blackmail material. Chapel kept it stored on a personal commlink kept in a hidden compartment in his desk at a small office he had for meeting with individuals he did not want to be seen talking to at city hall. The office is on the books for the government, but it does not have Chapel’s name on the door. Instead, it is listed as an office for City Parks Planner, Scarlett Dawson.

Dawson was given the Parks Planner position by Chapel as a favor while she and Chapel were having an affair. During the blackout, Dawson was at her office and went into hiding in the building. The whole building was taken over by a gang calling themselves the Night Crusade. They have been holding firm to the building and expanding their membership from other gangs or individuals who are being displaced or mistreated by the government and corporate forces that are trying to rebuild the infrastructure of the area. Dawson was found early on, held captive and put into forced labor, and she now spends her time suffering all manner of abuse.

The office building held by the Night Crusade is four stories. It still has no power, and the technology of the building itself no longer works. Personal commlinks and devices on the gangers are the only active electronics in the area. The base Noise in this area is 3. All the doors are unlocked, and lighting at night inside is provided by flashlights, glowsticks, and flares out on the street. The ground floor is a small grocery store, a cafe with a dozen seats, and a small foyer with stairs and an elevator to the upper floors. The second floor is two offices; Scarlett Dawson, City Parks Planner and Dr. Marshall Dawn, M.D., Family Practice. The third and fourth floors each hold two one-bedroom apartments and one two-bedroom apartment.

The grocery store and café are now a hangout for the Night Crusade. They’ve dragged in seating and boarded up broken windows. They use the grocery coolers as food storage even though they don’t have power and keep six to twenty gangers around, depending in the time of day.

The doctor’s office on the second floor is still used as a “medical facility,” but the ganger handling the medicine is more about cauterizing wounds and prescribing bliss for the pain. There are usually a few gangers in the office who are too high to leave.

The Parks Planner office is a headquarters of sorts where Gideon, the leader of the Night Crusade, holds meetings and hangs out. Dawson is kept on this floor as a personal servant to Gideon. When she is not going to get food or being used for ill purposes, she is kept in a closet, where she sleeps on a pile of nice suits. If she sees the runners go into the desk drawer and the hidden compartment, she asks if Chapel sent them to save her.

Chapel will have told the runners about the compartment, but it still requires a little skill to get access. An Engineering + Agility (5, 1 minute) test is needed to get it open, providing the possible time for Dawson to notice and test the character’s moral compass. Even if the runners have killed Gideon, Dawson still asks about Chapel and begs the characters to help her get out of the blackout zone.

The apartments on the upper floors are used as housing for the Night Crusade. The gang has thirty-seven standard members (use Mafia Debt Collector, p. 205, _SR6_ ; increase Professional Rating to 4) and Gideon (use Mafia Soldato, p. 207, _SR6_ ; increase Professional Rating to 5). They sleep and hang out here in shifts. Other times, they are down on the first floor partying or out in the rest of the blackout zone, scavenging for supplies and working against the government and corps as they attempt to rebuild.
