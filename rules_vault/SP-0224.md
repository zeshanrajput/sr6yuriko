## **“Agent Pedro”**

- **Aztlan didn’t stop there—we’ve received additional information about a potential Flashpoint Event in Amazonia. Files will be attached below.**

- **> Torrent**

**While Rio is frmly under the control of the Disians, Grey Cell has managed to embed a deep-cover observation team into the city. Known simply as “Agent Pedro,” the team consists of eight individuals who were handpicked by Ringmaster and whose records and identities have been thoroughly erased. Their mission is to obtain as much information as possible about the city and the Harvester located within it in order to facilitate its eventual disruption. Should any of the operatives be captured, they have a custom alchemical implant in their brains that once activated will trigger a special spell that will wipe their minds.**

- **To back up a bit, Aztlan didn’t leave the citizenry completely alone. They were given an option: swear fealty to Aztlan or end up as fuel for one of the Disian Harvesters. Or worse.**

- **> Marcos**
