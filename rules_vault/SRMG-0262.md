## **Why is Scholastic Mage limited to mages and why is the cost weird?**

For SRM, Scholastic Mage can be taken by any runner, even mundane runners. The cost of buying the quality is 2 Karma or 1,000 nuyen per level. If you have In Debt, the cost changes to 2,000 nuyen per level. Each level gains a single knowledge skill or increases the proficiency level of a language skill. There is no limit to the number of levels that a runner can take. The cost after character creation is double for either the nuyen or the Karma option.

**Why would I get the Masking or Extended Masking metamagic when there is False Impression or Manascape (pp. 37-38)?**

False Impression and Manascape are not _Missions_ legal.
