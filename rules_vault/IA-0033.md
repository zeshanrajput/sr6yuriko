## Dermal Alteration 

This shift in skin structure is even greater than just the normal bone deposits of trolls. This is far more extensive and takes on the appearance of rough tree bark. This quality provides a +2 Defense Rating bonus, cumulative with armor, but increases the cost of all armor and clothing by ten percent.
