## **Lowering Heat** 

Heat makes life miserable for shadowrunners. Lowering your heat in Shadowrun Missions has a few options. The first option is to work a contact and use their connections and some of their reputation to help. If their connection is 3 or higher, they can lower the Heat of your crew by 2, however your Loyalty is lowered by 1 or you have spent 2 points of Favor. If you don’t want to

involve your friends and contacts, you can use the universal language of the Sixth World. Nuyen. For every 1,000 nuyen spent in bribes, a single person can reduce their Heat by 1. Finally, shadowrunners can lower heat by laying low. If they spend a major downtime activity on lying low, they can reduce their Heat by 1. 

The option to find a fall guy is unavailable for Shadowrun Missions.
