## **>** Lanyap

Now, what does this have to do with a meet? It’s all part of the game, and one’s mask is another tool to employ. I know a few people who can wear their emotions on their sleeve and still be effective, but this is far from the normal way that most people operate, even in the shadows. Some simply prefer to be who they really are, or at least who they _think_ they are. But that’s a psychological rabbit hole for another time.



The best strategies in reading someone involve hidden emotions. That can mean determining what emotions are hiding under someone else’s mask, or the flip side of that coin, controlling your emotions during a meeting so that you can’t be read. During any meeting, or any interaction for that matter, people are going to project a specific persona that is useful to them at that moment. And if they plan to meet with someone more than once, that persona needs to be consistent.
