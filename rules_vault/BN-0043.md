## Constriction

- **Cost:** 6 Karma (Snakes only)

You may use Strength or Agility for any grappling test, and you gain a bonus point of Edge on any grappling test. When as an attacker you may damage your opponent as part of your grappling action, you gain a bonus Minor Action. This can represent the classic boa constrictor, or perhaps a bear hug or a great cat’s jaws on their prey’s throat.
