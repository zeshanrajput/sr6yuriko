## Spinrad

Spinrad’s embassy in the kindred district resembles a nightclub more than a logistics office. Club Johnny is the first proper nightclub to exist within the Spire, and it’s a popular place for visiting sky pirates. Spinrad maintains its embassy here primarily for the purpose of using its portal, as well as the many other metaplanar rifts within the Spire that lead to other metaplanes. Their other activities within the Place of Clouds are based out of Pirate Island.
