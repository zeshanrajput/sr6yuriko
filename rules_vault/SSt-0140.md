## **Do: Engage in Small, Quick Touchpoints**

**Look, people, interpersonal communication is easier than it’s ever been. You can literally think something to say to someone and have those words foating in front of that person’s eyes in seconds. So use it. If an article makes you think of someone, send it to them. If you remember you haven’t contacted someone in a while, send them a quick hello. Small bits of contact with no favors asked and nothing behind them but pleasant feeling can solidify your relationship.**

**Of course, you need to be careful. This doesn’t mean that sending repeated messages to someone is an automatic path to a closer relationship. You need to read the relationship, which is important enough to be its own point.**
