## **Can I sustain puppeteer and maintain control of a device?**

Yes. Puppeteer is used in place of Control Device Matrix action changing the test the technomancer does to be an Electronics + Resonance test instead of an Electronics + Logic test. The result of a successful test grants control of the device until it is lost.

If the hacker loses control of the device due to the control hierarchy, what they need to do to reassert control depends on the way they had gained it. If the hacker used the Control Device Matrix action, they need to make a new test later to re-establish. If they had gained it by using a sustained Puppeteer, they can just take it back without a test.
