## Making New Metaplanes

The possibilities represented by the metaplanes are nearly endless; even the closest and bestknown ones such as the elemental planes and the Metaplane of Kin may as well be infinite when compared to the physical realm. This section can be used as a way to make a new hyper-metaplane within an existing metaplane or set up an entirely new metaplane to explore. The choices presented can be randomly selected, chosen to define a particular idea, or some mix of those two options.
