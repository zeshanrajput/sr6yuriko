## Haze Grenades

Connected to the MCT metaplanar experiments in Denver that led to the Aurora Event a few years ago, these weapons were created by a renegade researcher following her own agenda. As the situation unwound, the original creator was killed and the prototype weapons, along with the information about their manufacture, fell into other hands.

Haze grenades are around the same size as a conventional grenade but made of smooth black metal. They’re purely mechanical with no Matrix icon, though in the astral they have the same shadowed aura as other Dark-Tech devices. Each of these grenades contains the last, tortured shred of a spirit, riddled with dimensional pinholes and held in place by an alchemical prison. No physical

damage is caused when the weapon “detonates,” but the fragment of the spirit imprisoned inside the device is finally destroyed. As the energy inside is released, dozens of dimensional pinholes are spread through the immediate area. The magical energy of the material plane floods through these and drains all mana from the area. The spot where the weapon detonates immediately becomes a three-meter-radius mana void, and the ten-meter area directly surrounding that becomes a strong mana ebb. This disruption to astral space lasts for a night and a day and then collapses in on itself.

Using this device, carrying one, or even being near it when it’s detonated, causes a drop in Astral Reputation (p. 63, _Street Wyrd_ ).
