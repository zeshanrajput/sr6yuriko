## The Teching Crew

## CPU (dwarf, male, 42, brown hair, brown eyes, thin build)

Mentally meticulous—that is the best way to describe CPU. He operates not just as a decker for the team but also as the team’s face and a central point for coordinating and analyzing data. He has a mind for the details and loves finding what’s in between the data, and he’s usually correct about where the clues point. He is a direct and polite communicator who doesn’t like to threaten or boast but rather gives facts and data.

When combat starts, CPU plays target coordinator, often putting teamwork effort behind the efforts put forth by his other team members. He’ll take pot shots with his pistol that just help his allies get angles; he’ll send a little static at a target to make them susceptible to an incoming hack, or he’ll make calls to local authorities and bring down trouble on the runners.
