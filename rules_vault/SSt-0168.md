## **snitches get stitches**

## **Posted by: Rigger X**

**Another danger of working for a police corp comes from within the shadow community itself. Runners might not have a syndicate-style code of silence, but you can bet that if anyone fnds out you’ve taken a job from the people in blue to save your own hide, they’ll start wondering how far they can trust you not to throw them under the bus. Don’t be surprised if your shadow contacts stop returning your calls after one scans you leaving the cop shop.**
