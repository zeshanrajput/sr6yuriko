## **The Coderunners**

**This is the most recent faction within Warpath, and they are shoring up one of our biggest weaknesses: the Matrix. We’ve always had some deckers, but they were few and far apart. Their functions were always to provide support, and cyberspace never really factored into our strategy. This new faction is rewriting the book on those strategies.**

**Composed of indigenous technomancers and the odd Native decker, they have brought the battle to the Matrix. They gather intel, cover our tracks, hunt for members of Winternight and Ex Pacis, and otherwise engage in online sabotage, - vandalism, propaganda, document forgery, and f nancial crimes.**

**While still mostly in a support role, they have become essential to our activities. They tend to be younger and more willing to work with others, including Matrix entities. For them, your online skills (or lack thereof) and willingness to commit digital crimes determine your worth to our cause more than your origin.**

- **One thing the author failed to mention in all this is the large number of toxic shamans and adepts they have in their ranks. At least they seem to have dropped the back-to-nature spiel.**
