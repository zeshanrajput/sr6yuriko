## Game Information

## rEVOlutionary

You’re familiar enough with how Evo works and have handled enough prototypes that you know how to make them work smoothly.

**Cost:** 10 Karma

**Game Effect:** This quality allows a user of one of the rEVOlutionary Arms weapons (p. 39, _Firing Squad)_ to choose to not use the wild die.
