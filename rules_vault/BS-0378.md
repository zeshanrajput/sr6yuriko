## Green Ring 3/8

- **Vector:** Contact, Inhalation

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** (3 or 8 – Body) hours, minimum 1 hour

- **Power:** 8 or 10

- **Effect:** Stun damage, Nauseated

Green Ring 3 was originally developed during the 1970s as an odorless gas meant to incapacitate rather than kill the opposition. Green Ring 8 is an improved version created by MCT to attempt to bypass standard chemsuits while still retaining the original colorless and odorless properties. Green Ring 3 victims must resist 8S damage and receive the Nauseated status. Green Ring 8 victims must resist 10S damage and also suffer from the Nauseated status. If the victim of Green Ring 8 is wearing a chemsuit, they also gain the Corrosive 4 status as the chemical dissolves the suit’s protections.
