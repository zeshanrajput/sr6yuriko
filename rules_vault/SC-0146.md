## Additional character creation recommendations:

- **Attributes:** Agility 3+, Intuition 4+, Reaction 4+

- **Skills:** Enchanting 6 with a specialization in Alchemy; Sorcery and Conjuring are lower priorities, if at all

- **Knowledge skills:** Magical Research Corporations, Pranks, Talismongering

- **Positive qualities:** Discreet Smuggler, Rote Alchemist, Spell Components, Team Player

- **Negative qualities:** Distinctive Style, Gear Acquisition Syndrome, Gremlins, Twitchy

- **Life modules:** Alchemist, Fixer, Mentor, Event: You became extremely interested in unusual or mysterious topics.
