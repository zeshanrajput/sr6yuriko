## Hook

After the last job she hired the PCs for turned into a dead end, Cat decides to try a different approach. She wants to meet the team at Tough Tony’s at 1:00 a.m. to talk about some more work. Tonight’s password is Swordfish. Cat is all business tonight, after the team arrives she gets right to what she needs while Spyder sits quietly smoking. The apparently unused state of the Shibata Consulting host has her suspicious, and she wants the runners to get back into the Mitsuhama Arcology, access the framework shell host, and then go all the way into the hidden host’s Foundation. She believes that there may be files still in the Foundation archive. She is offering 4,000 nuyen per runner to get into the archive and retrieve whatever files may exist that relate to MCT’s activities in Denver. Cat also mentions that the hatch from the Underground was sealed off after the last visit, so they will need to find another way into the building.
