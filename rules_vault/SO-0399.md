## Slapback Example

Rumble is undercover at a fancy corp gala, attempting to get close to a particular exec so he can subtly dose them with the drug, Slab, as part of an extraction. This is going to take some careful social maneuvering, however, and one particularly nosey snob isn’t making things easy for Rumble. They open with veiled insults and proceed to openly mocking his fashion sense, taking the Gatekeeper action. Rumble fails his Composure test and gains the Fish Out of Water condition, but he’s not going to take this lying down. He takes the Slapback action, spending a point of Edge to hit back. This counts as if Rumble had taken the Gatekeeper action against his target and scored one net hit. While it doesn’t do anything about the Fish Out of Water status Rumble now has to suffer through, at least the person responsible also gains the same condition.
