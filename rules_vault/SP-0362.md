## **Counterspelling and Boosted Defense**

**In addition to their use in casting, units of mana are used for counterspelling. They directly replace the Magic attribute when dispelling or when set-**




**ting up a Boosted Defense. Units also take the place of reagents for increasing the area of effect of a boosted defense, with each additional unit spent increasing the radius of the defensive effect by one meter.**
