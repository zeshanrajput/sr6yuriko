## **The Person Who Knows**

**The guidelines here are a lot like living with another shadowrunner. Be honest about dangers, don’t use the job as an excuse not to give support at home, don’t overshare, that sort of thing. Make some extra effort to give them time, because they’re going to worry about you when you’re on the job. You also may need to think about an exit plan from the shadows, as that lifestyle can grate on any partners.**
