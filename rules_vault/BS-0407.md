## Shade

- **Vector:** Inhalation

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** 3D6 hours, maximum 12 hours

- **Power:** 6

- **Effect:** Grants Astral Projection

Made from the pollen of the red orchid found in Southeast Asia, this compound forces the user to astrally project. This counts as a single occurrence of forced projection and even works on mundane subjects. Users of this drug also gain access to the metaplanes if they are in the company of a spirit guide or initiate. Users must return to their body before the end of the duration or suffer 10S damage (resisted by Body) and (10 – Essence)P damage (unresisted) as they are violently forced back into their body. If a user returns to their body before the effect expires, they still suffer the Stun damage. Returning to the body ends the effect of the drug, regardless of remaining duration. Shade doesn’t allow users to retain their astral form for longer than normal—mundanes still lose 1 point of Essence every two hours, while magicians begin losing 1 Essence per hour after Magic x 2 hours, and users are still subject to death if they exceed the normal time limit for astral projection.
