## rigid stock

| **AVAIL** | **COST** |
| --------- | -------- |
| 3         | 250¥     |

**Note:** The rigid stock replaces any other stock or adds a stock. When replacing another stock, it alters the AR as follows: —/+1/+1/+2/+1. When adding to a weapon without a stock, it alters AR as follows: –1/+1/+1/+2/+1 and decreases the concealability threshold by 1. If a weapon has no Attack Rating in a given range category before this modification is applied, that category is not affected.
