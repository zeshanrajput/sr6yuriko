## Ripjacks

A ripjack is the use of a BTL or other cyberattack that injects toxic code into devices and gear. While software and hardware manufacturers work to create good defenses against these attack vectors, they remain a risk. Ripjacks require tapping into neural pathways using DNI from trodes, skilljacks, datajacks, or cyberjacks.
