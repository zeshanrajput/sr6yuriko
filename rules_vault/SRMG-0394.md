## **Is permanent and ally sprite creation (p. 77-79) legal?**

Permanent sprites are not _Missions_ legal except in the case of ally sprites. An ally sprite cannot be a modular sprite. When an ally sprite crashes, it comes back one day later. When creating an ally sprite, you can



only add sprite powers for those sprites you have access to.
