## Wei Hong Yu

Wei Hong Yu is the facilities director at Kanabo, with a background in product manufacturing and mass production versatility. The ork has made lengthy strides with his position both at the facility and within the White Lotus Society, having earned the nickname of Shushu, or “Uncle,” after several successful deployments of desirables for the Triad. He’s decently augmented—mostly with bioware, so as not to be noticeable—so whatever you do, do not underestimate the speed or mental acumen of the man.
