## **Running with Critters**

**the nuyen, they can help you train your critter, and it’s defnitely cheaper to pay for training assistance than to replace furniture.**

**Know when to bring a critter on a run and when to leave them at home. Critters are incapable of comprehending the bigger picture and understanding the risks they take. You are responsible for those risks and deciding whether or not to put your critter onto a potentially dangerous task—take that responsibility very seriously, because it’s often a lifeor-death choice.**
