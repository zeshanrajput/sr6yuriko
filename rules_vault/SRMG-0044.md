## **English Core Rulebook versus French versus German?**

There are minor differences between various translated adaptations of _Shadowrun Sixth World_ Edition, but SRM is built on the English version of the rulebook. Your local region may use other language versions, but that character is not instantly portable to other regions. Your character must conform to the English rules when at a table using the English language rules. Similarly, characters must conform to the French rules when playing at a table using the French language rules.
