## 28: the cost of the press

## Hook

Piper, recovering from her recent abduction, contacts the runners with a warning. It seems the interview contractor has fed tidbits to the presssharks, and they’re ready to come after the runners to get the scoop.

Problem is, they’ll also be nipping at each other, and as much as Piper wouldn’t mind seeing her muckraking competition dwindle a bit, she’s never in favor of losing good data diggers over some black-book scheme.

Her warning comes just in the nick of time, as the first of a series of problematic encounters target the team member with the highest Reputation or Heat. After that, the encounters occur sporadically over a few days until the runners get a chance in Encounter 4 to come together with the snoops and look at what’s really going on.
