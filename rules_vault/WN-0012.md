## Vital Statistics

For all intents and purposes, Manhattan Island _is_ New York City. A landmark ruling by the UCAS affirmed that the island had the legal rightto the city name, forcing the other four boroughs to re-incorporate themselves as independent jurisdictions. The results of that case, put forward by the Manhattan Development Consortium (MDC), were far-reaching as it also created precedent for dual reporting—localized corporate citizens could also be reported as New York residents, stabilizing the 33,000 per square kilometer population density New York has come to be known for.
