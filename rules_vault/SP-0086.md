## **Week Of Death**

**On November 3, 2082, the Sixth World was rocked by a series of events that the media at large called “unrelated.” Of course, we know better. This was in fact the beginning of a massive operation consisting of an orchestrated series of assassinations and asset denial raids. Orchestrated by the Disians through their proxies, the main objective was to eliminate, cripple, or at least severely damage the opposition.**

**Their primary target was, of course, Grey Cell. Now, I don’t have exact numbers, and I doubt any of the Grey Cell members or affliates posting here are going to confrm anything. But rumors backed up by reliable data estimates at least forty-seven cells, seventeen caches, ten general storage sites, fve allied/contracted mercenary units, three black sites, and one research facility were destroyed during the week alone. And that’s not counting the untold number of targeted individuals who ended up dead or are currently MIA.**

**And as always, there’s the multitude of those who ended up as collateral damage,**

**But that was only the beginning. The Week of Death was merely the opening act. What most don’t realize is that the carnage in the shadows continues as we speak.**
