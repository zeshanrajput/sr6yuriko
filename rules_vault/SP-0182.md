## **Where Be The Other Dragons?**

## **Posted by: Wyrm Watcher**

**One would think that, with an impending apocalypse, the rest of the great dragons (and plenty of lesser ones) would have gotten of their scaly hoops and done something about the Disians. Granted, we’ve seen some of them, Arleesh and Celedyr most notably, being active in the fght. But what about the rest? If the dragons are as powerful as we’ve been led to believe, then theoretically they should have taken care of all this by now, obliterated the Disians, and sealed of the metaplanes, ending the threat.**

**But they haven’t.**

**This raises the question: Why? Are they playing another one of their long games with us lesser beings as pawns (yet again)? Or … have even the mighty great dragons have fnally met their match in the Disians?**
