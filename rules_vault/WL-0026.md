## **Natural Encounters**

**No matter where you run, you may encounter a critter in the wild. City, desert, forest—well, read on in this download, and you’ll get the drift. The good news is, most of them don’t care about you. Humans, by and large, aren’t their natural prey. We’re just too diffcult to bring down reliably, so they get accustomed to other food sources. That means if you steer clear of them, they are not likely to care about you.**

**Except for when they do. Some of them are curious, some of them are mean, and some of them don’t really know what they’re doing from one - moment to another, which makes them very diff cult to deal with.**

**The most universal tip I can give you (other than “steer clear”) is that you should not think that any one approach is always right. Sometimes you want to be intimidating, sometimes you want to be small. Sometimes you don’t want to move,**
