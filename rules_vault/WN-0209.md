## NYPD inc Precinct 10 (Foundation Host)

## Special Notes

The runners can take the items from the impound locker back to Cat with the video footage, and she will pay out 2,000 for the contents of the crate and another 1,000 nuyen for the three commlinks. If the team gets back to her in a few hours, she will tell them the crate contains a medical nano-forge, used for making high-grade nanoware, not one of the Denver devices. The driver is a small-time independent smuggler and the commlink has orders to pick up fourteen crates at a Lower West Side dock—owned by an MCT subsidiary—and deliver them to an automated storage facility in New Harlem. One of the commlinks from the Ancients has orders from Eager to intercept the truck and deliver the cargo to a Terminal warehouse instead. A record search will discover that neither of the two possible destinations have an owner listed. If asked about the job, Eager will claim the arrangements were made at a Matrix club, and he has no idea who Mr. Johnson really was, but the nuyen was paid out of an old digital account rumored to have funded NeoNET’s shadow ops for decades.
