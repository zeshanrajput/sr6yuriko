## Echolocation Receptors

The critter is implanted with additional hearing receptors specifically tuned for the purpose of echolocation, allowing them to use reflected sound to perceive their environment in great detail.

**Game Information:** The critter gains the Enhanced Senses (Hearing, Ultrasound) critter powers, effectively replacing the function of the Dog’s Ears augmentation. Additionally, the critter gains the benefits of the Ultrasound sensor function (p. 277, _SR6_ ).
