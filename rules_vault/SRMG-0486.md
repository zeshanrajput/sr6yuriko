## **SECTION 19: DEUS EX ARCANA**

**Would it be easier to ban the whole book?** _Deus Ex Arcana_ is the Advanced Magic book for _Shadowrun Sixth World_ . As such, significant portions of the book provide levels of play that are not compatible with _Shadowrun Missions_ . Rather than go through each section and provide whether it’s legal or banned, here is a list of all the legal items in the book. • New Spells (pp. 102-107, 113) except for the following: `o` Binding of Peace `o` Cleansing `o` Force Multiplier `o` Shapechange `o` Vacuum `o` Messenger • New Rituals (pp. 107-109) except for the following: `o` Manifestation of Desire `o` Sacrament of Changed Form `o` Sealing Ritual `o` Stories of Our Ancestors `o` Telling of Tales • New Metamagics: **Anchoring** and **Fixation** (pp. 110-111) • New Metagenic Qualities (pp. 113-114). P.E.R.D.E. is not legal though. • Minor Awakened Talents (pp. 115-116). Knacks are not legal for players. • New Quality: **Hardened Will** (pp. 129-130) • New Gear: **Ukkru** (p. 180), **Fool’s Mithril** (p. 180), and **Mellow** (p. 183)
