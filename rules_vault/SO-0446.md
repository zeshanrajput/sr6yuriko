## Negative Qualities

## Anti-Augmentation Bias

It’s not that you’re a bigot or think people with augmentations are bad (or maybe it is in your

case), it’s just that the idea of cybernetics, bioware, and even genetic alterations creeps you out on a deep emotional level.

**Bonus:** 13 Karma (6 Karma if you also have the Sensitive System quality)

**Game Effect:** If you take this quality, each point of your own Essence loss (round up) provides a -1 dice pool penalty to all tests that involve Charisma, Intuition, Logic, or Willpower. When you make a social skill, Composure, or Judge Intentions Opposed test against anyone with obvious augmentations (even just a datajack you can see), they gain a point of Edge.
