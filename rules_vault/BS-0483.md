## Lockjaw

The critter’s jaw and teeth are replaced with a hardened polymer, and its jaw muscles are replaced with high-strength cybertech myomer fibers. Once this mouth comes to grips with you, you’re not going anywhere.

**Game Information:** The critter gains a natural weapon bite attack with a DV of (Strength/2 + 1) Physical damage. This can be further improved with natural weapon sheathing but gains no additional bonuses from bone lacing. Once the critter successfully establishes a grapple, no Edge may be gained or spent on actions to break free of its grip. Teaching the critter to grapple a target requires a specific command, whether or not the critter has this implant.
