## Negative Virtual

## Life Form Qualities

## Cascading Failure

- **Bonus:** 9 Karma

The AI suffers from additional system failures while operating with damaged code. AIs who have this quality and are wounded increase their wound penalty by 1 point. This means one wound will cause the first modifier.
