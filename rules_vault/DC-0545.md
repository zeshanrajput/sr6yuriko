## Edge and Doing Stuff

It is important to understand that benefits gained from having better tools than what is required, such as having a shop to change a car tire, are present at every interval involved. This means that in larger operations that go beyond the first interval of time, the Edge bonus is present each inTHEterval passed. Having schematics for the part or vehicle grants +1 Edge that can only be used on the current project.
