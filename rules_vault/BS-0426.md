## Effect

BTLs and ripjacks can affect the runner or the device being used. Some just cause damage, but others will provide additional impact, causing statuses, dice pool modifiers, or changes in the gain or use of Edge. The effects of a ripjack will always trigger to the full degree unless the Power is reduced to 0.
