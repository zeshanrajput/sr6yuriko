## Novelty Hill Sleep and Eat

When it comes to lodging, Novelty Hill is the gold standard for Redmond. You get a clean(ish) sleep coffin in a safe(ish) warehouse. The wall of sleeping cubes overlooks a cafeteria section where they sell edible(ish) food. It’s where the desperate, the poor, and the cheap hang their hat. Nothing more, since you can’t fit anything else in those cubes. And that’s after the improvements the Finnigan mob made to it a few years back.

- Don’t expect any contacts to be made here. The Mob may own it, but other than collecting their share, they barely think about it.
