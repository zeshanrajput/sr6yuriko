## Free Spirited: Buttercup’s Influence

It’s no surprise to anyone that Buttercup is the driving force behind Evo’s participation in the current shadow war. And while Evo has proven to be extremely valuable to the Defender Movement, it’s not always been a smooth partnership.

Numerous reports indicate that Buttercup continues to behave in an extremely erratic fashion. One report said, “if she was metahuman, she’d be diagnosed with whatever disorder you wanted to throw at her.” Several times, Buttercup has disrupted strategy meetings, lashed out at allies, and has consistently pushed for direct assaults on _all_ enemy corporate HQs. No one knows for sure the cause of Buttercup’s behavior, but the consensus seems to be that the current mana disruptions may be affecting her in ways we cannot fully comprehend. Thankfully, members of the Evo board have been able to de-escalate the situation thus far.
