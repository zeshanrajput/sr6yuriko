## Cosmic Senses (Psychometry and Sensing)

True Air as a reagent makes an Awakened individual much more sensitive to the mana harmonics. With Psychometry (p. 120, _Street Wyrd_ ), True Air reduces the dice pool penalty for time by 4. With Sensing (p. 120, _Street Wyrd_ ), distance is increased by 4 kilometers, and details into the harmonics are more profound. An initiate can sense mana lines, astral gates, metaplanar gates (and if they are “local” vs. deep astral), mana barriers, and mana storms. This ability can be added to a metamagic psychometry or sensing focus.
