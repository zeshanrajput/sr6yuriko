## MCT-Sikorsky-Bell Seahawk

Saved during the acquisition of Sikorsky-Bell by MCT, the Seahawk program was headed for the dumps after MCT’s troubles in the Algonkian-Manitou Council. The aircraft firm found the project, took it as theirs, and now offers a high-speed personal amphibious jet, capable of hauling six passengers or a decent stack of cargo at high speeds. Great for restocking remote spots or ducking off into the middle of nowhere to hide from the heat. This craft is amphibious and comes standard with buoyancy and flotation upgrades, meaning it can take off from land and water.
