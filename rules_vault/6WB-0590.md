## Ritual Spellcasting

While spellcasting is the quick manipulation of mana, ritual spellcasting shapes mana over a longer period of time. More time allows you to manipulate mana in ways that normal spellcasting won’t allow. Rituals, like spells and alchemical preparations, are learned individually, and they all have the same Karma cost as spells. Rituals can be performed by either one person or a group. Spells learned through normal channels can be incorporated into some rituals, such as a Combat spell in the Prodigal Spell ritual (p. 145); in those cases, they do not cost additional Karma to learn separately.
