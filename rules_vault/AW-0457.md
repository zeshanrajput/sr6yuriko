## Toxic Fallout Ghoul Animi

These feral, skinless, ghoul-like animi inhabit the towns and ruins of the ancient power plant. They are fierce, and their hunger for metahuman flesh overrides all sense of identity and capacity for logic. When no food is available, they either lie in a dormant state of torpor, appearing to be lifeless corpses, or hunt in packs in the wilderness of Miggon. Any sign of life, particularly the sound of approaching prey, draws their attention and hunger instantly. While they appear to be ghouls, they are manifestations of the Wastes of Miggon and are not infected with HMHVV. They are not blind, and their senses of hearing and smell are sharp.
