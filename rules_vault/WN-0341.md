## Dr. Sonya Peters

Dr. Peters was a respected expert on neurology and brain functions, often sought after by other experts. She was known for her ethics almost as much as her brilliance—she was very aware of the potential misuses of the knowledge she was acquiring, and she was reluctant to take any move that would put control over the metahuman mind into the wrong hands. She received many offers for partnerships that she turned down because of her ethics.

She was approached by the CIA (though they did not identify themselves as such) about working on something called Project Anima about a year ago, and she turned them down. Not long after, she was diagnosed with an extremely aggressive cancer and passed within months of her diagnosis. By the time she passed, though, she was ready. She had mapped her brain into the Matrix and uploaded it as an e-ghost. Because she used university property to do the mapping, NYU gained control over the e-ghost, which means she spends almost her entire existence in the secret lab—which, truth be told, is probably what she would have wanted anyway.
