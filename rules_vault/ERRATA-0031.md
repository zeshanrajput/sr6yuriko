## p. 183, Reboot Device 

_Adjust the description of the action as follows:_ 

The target device shuts down and immediately reboots, coming back online at the end of the following combat round. The device disappears from the Matrix until its reboot time is over and any Overwatch Score, exploits, or backdoors are reset.
