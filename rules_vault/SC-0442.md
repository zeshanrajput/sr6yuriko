## Keeping the Peace

A big meeting between two hostile shadow factions is going down. A respected fixer is brokering the meet and responsible for enforcing the peace between the two sides. The fixer has chosen a junkyard maze of stacked streetcars as the venue, and they have hired the runners for security. They have several hours before the parties are notified of the venue to make their preparations and to prevent either side from establishing an ambush of their own. During the meet, the runners must ensure the agreed-upon security details are dealt with if they breach the peace.
