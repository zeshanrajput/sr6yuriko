## Peddler

- **Vector:** Slot

- **Speed:** 1 combat round

- **Duration:** (Rating) hours

- **Power:** 9 + (Rating)

- **Effect:** +1 Willpower, +1 Intuition, +1 Charisma, Con 2, Influence 2

Just remember the mantra that coffee’s for closers. You are here to make a deal and get the most for your nuyen. This chip is loaded with behavioral cues to react to the responses of those you’re trying to bargain with. The memories are loaded with useful arguments to be made for the buying and selling of goods and information. Just remember, it’s bad form to use it on your supplier. The user of this p-fix needs to make a Composure (3) test to resist the urge to sell an item that they have on their person that they aren’t currently using. When the p-fix wears off, the user will always take the first offer for the next (rating) hours.
