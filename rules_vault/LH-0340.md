## FAB IV Infection

**Vector:** Contact (both physical and astrally active bodies)

**Speed:** 15 minutes (until mana is drained) **Power:** 10

**Nature:** dual-nature bacterial

**Effect (ongoing):** Mana Drain (10 units per interval from a pool of Disian mana as the bacteria colony feeds). There is no test to resist this drain. Drained powers are removed at random.

The mana drain may also cause Stun damage to Disians and Faustians. At the end of each Speed interval, make a Willpower x 2 Disease Resistance test (as this is always an astral infection), and if the Power is not reduced to zero, take one box of unresisted Stun damage. The unresisted Stun damage may not be recovered / healed until the power of the disease is zero.



As long as there is a mana pool to feed on, treat each ongoing Speed interval as if it were the first— the Power resets to 10 and any previously unresisted Power is not added in. If the Power is reduced to zero for three consecutive speed interval tests, the infection is fought off and the ongoing effects end, even if there is mana remaining. If the disease is not fought off, it ends naturally after the mana pool the bacterium is feeding on is drained, the Power no longer resets, and the next Speed interval test will be the last.

**Effect (final):** There is no final effect for FAB IV; once the mana pool it is attached to is drained, the effects end. However, the infected aura is contagious for one Speed interval (15 minutes) after the final test, then the colony dissipates, and the infected aura can no longer transmit the infection.
