## Diagnostics

The Diagnostics power allows the sprite to evaluate the inner workings of an electronic device. The sprite can assist someone using or repairing the device. The sprite makes an Electronics + Level test; the character gets a +1 die per hit to the character’s dice pool to use (Electronics, Engineering, and Piloting skills only) or repair the item. This power takes the sprite’s entire attention; the bonus lasts until the sprite drops it or does something else.
