## **Structure and Organization**

**In 2075, the Sternschutz split into three smaller corporations. Sternschutz Police Service is the company that people associate with law enforcement, Sternschutz ProSec protects objects and individuals, while Sternschutz Transport Security focuses on secure transportation services.**

**Following this, I will focus on the structure of the Sternschutz Police Service.**

**Within the ’plexes Sternschutz is active in, it follows a decentralized approach to organization. The code of conduct and collaboration with government agencies are decided in Stuttgart, but the local departments handle the rest.**

**In a single metroplex, the Sternschutz organizes into precincts, following the typical police pattern. Each department has a department manager on site, who reports to the chief of police of that specifc ’plex. The chief then responds to the section manager, who coordinates activities. That person also installs and controls the chief of police in that sprawl.**

**Within a precinct, the employees belong to different services. While many of these services can be considered standard services, not every precinct or even sprawl has every service on-site. This means we must investigate which parts of the Sternschutz we encounter when things go downhill.**
