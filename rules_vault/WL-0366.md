## **Carnivore**

**Carnivores are more expensive to provide food for than pets that can digest processed plant matter. If your lifestyle category is Medium, High or Luxury, the cost to feed your carnivorous critters is covered under your lifestyle cost. If your lifestyle category is Street, Squatter, or Low, you must pay an additional (critter’s Body x 100) nuyen per month to feed your critter.**
