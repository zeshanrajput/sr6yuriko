## Mnemonic Enhancer

The mnemonic enhancer is a popular choice when it comes to cultured bioware for critters, as it makes them much more responsive to training and is relatively inexpensive.

**Game Information:** Add the mnemonic enhancer’s rating as a dice pool bonus to all training tests to train the critter. The number of commands the critter can learn is increased by the mnemonic enhancer’s rating x 2.
