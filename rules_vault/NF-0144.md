## Expression Point Exchange

Expression Points aren’t worthless. They can get an audience to change in a meaningful way, like only art can. They can get an audience (not an individual) to:

- Adopt an attitude

- Agree with a point of view

- Support a cause

- Give money

- Make the audience feel something

- Make a target more open to negotiation

- Collect various pieces of clothing

- Praise or criticize a local establishment

- Get a mob started, or aim them somewhere

- Any other action the gamemaster thinks is it is reasonable.
