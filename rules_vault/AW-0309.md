## The Kindred District Matrix

The kindred district has a functional Matrix, and though there is no access to the Matrix back on Earth, it allows all of your tech to function as normal. The kindred district’s Matrix can be accessed from a distance of up to ten kilometers from the city. All noise penalties due to distance outside of the Spire are doubled, however. The best news is that there’s no Grid Overwatch Division here, which means no Overwatch Score. Your devices, PAN, and the Matrix hosts that belong to the various megacorporate embassies must all provide their own Matrix security.

In addition to the bureaucracy, many spirits from the nearest metaplanes visit the Place of Clouds, mostly to enjoy the scenery. The vast majority of these spirits spend very little time in the Spire, preferring to visit the cloud islands—or perhaps one favorite island. To the spirits, the Place of Clouds is a pleasant and relaxing place to visit, which means that most of the spirits that you are likely to encounter outside of the Spire are on vacation.



The Spire itself is a center of trade and transportation. Airships carry passengers and cargo to the cloud islands and back to the city, and they are a constant sight in the airspace surrounding the Spire.
