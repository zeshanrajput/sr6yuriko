## **Opposition Report**

## **Everyone**

**Yes, that’s an unusual and broad header, but that’s a hazard of the info business. Everyone wants their secrets protected, which means they (quite reasonably) are suspicious of and hostile toward those whose entire career is about discovering those secrets. The Tiding was able to fy under the radar for a few years without anyone at the top of the food chain knowing much about them, but now that some bigwigs have hired or been targeted by them, they’re being mentioned more. More to the point, descriptions of their methods and their principal members are circulating around the top corporate security groups, which means the Tiding is going to have to adjust their methods if they want to keep their track record going.**

**The Tiding and their clients want to succeed in their work. No one else in the world wants that. That will be a challenge going forward.**
