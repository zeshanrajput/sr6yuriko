## Share Expertise

## \[Influence (Instruction), Major\]: 3 Edge per Skill Rank

By communicating the most helpful and relevant information quickly and concisely, player characters can provide an ally with ranks in a skill they possess. Choose a skill you have at least three ranks in and make an Influence (Instruction) + Charisma (3) test. If successful, spend 3 Edge per each rank in that skill you provide to your target. The target is limited to a maximum of three ranks in the skill you share. These skill ranks are temporary and will fade away after one hour per hit on the Instruction test. If the Share Expertise action is taken again, the duration can be extended by another hour per hit.
