## Shifter Naivety

- **Bonus:** 10 Karma

As it turns out, grabbing Mr. Johnson by the throat in your jaws until he submits is considered bad form. This quality is for shifters who struggle with metahuman society in general—for those who don’t understand technology, see Shifter Barbarian.

**Game Effect:** You grant Edge to your opponent in any Social test that isn’t related to intimidation. This Edge gain is not subject to the two-per-turn limit.
