## Guts

(NoFear, Brass Balls)

- **Vector:** Inhalation

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** (12 – Body) hours, minimum 1 hour

- **Power:** 9

- **Effect:** Gain immunity to Frightened status

Guts was originally designed to deal with anxiety, but it was too effective. It deadens the lizardbrain functionality that triggers the fight or flight response to fear effects, both learned and innate. While the drug is active in the user’s system, they cannot gain the Frightened status from magic or mundane sources. The negative side effect of the drug is that it becomes difficult for the user to understand when they are doing something that should frighten them and must make a Logic + Willpower (3) test to avoid doing something risky, dangerous, or otherwise inappropriate.
