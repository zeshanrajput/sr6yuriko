## **Use Critter Power**

**Just because your pet barghest has a paralyzing howl, that doesn’t mean they’ll use it on command without training. If you want to have a critter use its power at your orders, you’re going to have to train it to do so. If a critter has multiple critter powers, they need to be trained with a separate command for each one. The trigger for this command must include some way to communicate how you want the power used—or what you want the power to target.**
