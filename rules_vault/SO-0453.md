## Supportive Follower

You prefer not to be the center of attention and you’re only at your best when you back someone else up in a social situation.

**Bonus:** 5 Karma

**Game Effect:** You must have at least three ranks in either Con or Influence to take this quality. You may not gain or spend Edge on any social skill tests you make on your own, and you can’t benefit from a Teamwork test when using a social skill. However, when you support another character with a Teamwork test, gain a point of Edge that must be used on your test to grant bonus teamwork dice, or it’s lost.
