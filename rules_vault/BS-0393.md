## Void Burst

(Blackhole)

- **Vector:** Inhalation, Injection

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** (12 – Body) hours, minimum 1 hour

- **Power:** 11

- **Effect:** Stun damage on magic use

Magicians and adepts who have been poisoned with this insidious toxin become aware of it only when they try using their magic. This poison interacts with the victim by increasing the magical backpressure when they attempt to spellcast, summon, or use their adept powers. This doubles the amount of drain that the magic-user needs to resist. For adept powers that do not have drain tests, such as a magically boosted skill or attribute, they need to resist drain based on their magic and associated foci.
