## Deployable Cover Module

A retractable folding shield can be mounted on an exoframe, which can be quickly deployed to provide cover for the critter and one other individual. Simple and lightweight cover modules provide a slight amount of protection, but larger and heavier versions provide substantial cover.

CRITTERter. One other individual may take cover behind the deployed module, but that individual receives half as much cover as its rating (round up).

While the cover is deployed, the critter gains the Immobilized status. Note that this means that the critter’s Reaction is not used for its defense tests, which may well mitigate the module’s usefulness as a defensive option.

The only deployable cover module that can be mounted on a plastic exoframe is rating 1. An aluminum exoframe can mount a cover module with a rating up to 3. A rating 4 cover module requires a titanium exoframe. This module takes up exoframe capacity equal to double its rating.
