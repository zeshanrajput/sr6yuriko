## **Activation:** Passive

Some adepts shield their psyches with mana and certainty, making themselves impressively unflappable. For every level in Cool Resolve, they may add +1 die to all opposed social tests to resist someone else’s initiated Influence skills, and +1 die on all tests to resist the Fear critter power. (They _may_ add these dice, not _must_ , allowing them to benefit from friendly Leadership or Instruction rolls, etc.).
