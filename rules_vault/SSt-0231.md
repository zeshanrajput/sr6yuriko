## **You Have a Bad Feeling**

**I know this is really iffy, but I’ve learned not to mistrust gut instincts. Sometimes we unconsciously notice small details that our top-level mind decides to disregard, only noticing the sneaking suspicion that something is wrong. If that suspicion is continual and growing, it’s not a bad idea to decide it’s right before you get an especially challenging kind of proof. Be bold, cut ties, and move on.**
