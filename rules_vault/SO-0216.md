## Steadfast

There are people who put a huge amount of stock in their relationships. Contacts who are steadfast put a higher stock in maintaining that loyalty and remaining true to who they know even when the pressure is on. They’ll work hard to find a way to maintain their relationships.

**Game Effect:** The contact acts as if their Loyalty is 2 higher when resisting an attempt to influence or con them into giving up information on who they’re loyal to.
