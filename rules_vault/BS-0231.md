## Myonuclear Permanence

A fancy way of saying muscle memory. This is similar to a reflex recorder. Where the reflex recorder focuses on the addition of extra nervous tissue, myonuclear permanence creates a mechanism that creates muscle fiber that is more efficient in performing a trained task.

When installed, one of the following Minor Actions must be selected: avoid incoming, block, dodge, drop object, drop prone, hit the dirt, intercept, quick draw, stand up, take cover. When performing that Minor Action for the first time in a combat round, the myonuclear permanence provides the player with a free Minor Action. Myonuclear permanence may be taken for multiple different Minor Actions.
