## **From Collapsing Now, can I buy the GreenWar Gear?**

Yes, but you must have a contact that is associated with this faction. In addition to the contact, Asphyxia Spores have an availability of 6I and costs 60 nuyen per dose. Hyperallergenic has an availability of 5I and costs 90 nuyen per dose.
