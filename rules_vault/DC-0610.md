## The Exit

## (Piloting)

With a successful Pilot test, the character reduces speed enough to safely exit the vehicle as an additional Minor Action. At 5+ hits, the vehicle comes to a complete stop without crashing; otherwise, the autopilot must make a Crash test.

**Cost:** 6 Edge
