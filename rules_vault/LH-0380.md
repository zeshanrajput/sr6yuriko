## Scrambling For Answers

Even now it seems everyone has a theory regarding the Un-Awakening.

The Draco Foundation, in conjunction with the Dunkelzahn Institute for Magical Research, was one of the first to have a press conference to try and explain the situation, as well as calm the masses without mentioning the Disians (because thank you, fragging Buttercup). Their calm, reasonable, and rational approach was exactly the kind of thing the Sixth World needed.

Unfortunately, it wasn’t what they _wanted._

The media circus that followed frankly boggles my mind, even by current standards, and I’m as jaded as one can get. Everyone was either trying to debunk the DF or push their own theories. And of course, others tried to make a few nuyen on the suckers or increase their own prestige and influence.
