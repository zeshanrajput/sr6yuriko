## Addicted (1 to 4)

The contact is addicted to something. Maybe that addiction is to pills, shopping, sex, or something more exotic, but that addiction has power over the contact’s life, which means someone else can wield that power against them.

**Game Effect:** When someone tries to con or influence an NPC using the source of their addiction, the NPC loses (Level) dice on Resistance tests.
