## **Status: Disian Stronghold, Known Harvester Location**

## **Cajamarca, Peru**

**One of the frst and largest Harvester locations to be constructed was in Rio, a city without hope. Not only are most of its leaders and politicians doing the Disians’ bidding, but Amazonian police and security forces are more than happy to round up random citizens and ship them off in Disian cargo containers in exchange for their freedom.**
