## **Special Notes**

**If the PCs let the rogues and Disians fght it out, the gamemaster may choose to roll dice to see how it goes, decide which side makes the most sense to be the winners, or do a simple roll-off to see who wins. Whichever side comes out on top will be weakened and make for easier prey.**

**If the PCs decide against Arleesh’s advice to attack the shipment near the Spire, determine whether the rogues or Dis wins the battle, which will take place well before the shipment is expected to arrive. If the Disians win, the shipment won’t show up. If the rogues win, they’ll be in rough shape, but any attack will bring swift retribution upon the players for breaking the peace.**

**Once the players turn in the stolen shipment of True Elements to Arleesh’s agents in the Spire, they may return home or choose to spend some more time exploring this wondrous and unusual place. It might be a good opportunity to gather some True Elements of their own!**
