## Quality Path: Networking

## Establishing your Network

This quality path covers the acquisition and improvement of a network of contacts. To begin this quality path, you must have at least three contacts with Connection and Loyalty ratings of at least 3 each. You’ve got to start somewhere, right? These three contacts will form the initial core of your network (whether they know it or not). You will rely upon their knowledge and services, and they’ll come to rely upon you—not just for the business you bring them, but also because they know you, and you’ve done good things for them.

Once you have your three core network contacts established, you may acquire the Contact Network quality. If you expend three Favor Points from among your core network contacts, you don’t need to pay double the Karma cost to acquire the Contact Network quality.
