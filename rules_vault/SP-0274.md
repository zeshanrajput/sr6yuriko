## **The Job**

**Tracking Seimanth won’t be terribly diffcult. Not long after arriving within the dragon’s territory—a rainforest environment—the player char-**

**==> picture [18 x 765] intentionally omitted \<==**



**acters will be attacked by several waves of critters. These critters are under the effects of the Animal Control critter power, controlled by beast spirits that have been summoned by Seimanth, who is using them to ascertain the players’ capabilities.**

**The beast spirits will observe from stealth while in their astral form and will report pertinent details to Seimanth. After three waves of critter attacks, the beast spirits will materialize and attack. Assuming the player characters survive these encounters, Seimanth will take the next day to consider what he’s observed. He will then contact the player characters using the Mindspeech power to ask them why they have invaded his lair and what they plan to do. Seimanth will explain that Aztechnology is allied to dark powers that seek to destroy the Gaiasphere and that he’s working to thwart this disaster. He’s also willing to overlook their transgressions and reward them richly if they agree to a counteroffer and join him in an attack against what he calls a Disian Harvesting site that’s currently under construction near his borders.**

**If the team refuses Seimanth’s offer, he’ll attack them directly and attempt to kill them all. He’ll be aided by three Force 6 spirits: a beast spirit, a spirit of air, and a spirit of earth. If the team used ranged weapons against the earlier critter attacks, Seimanth and his spirits will get in close. If the team is strong with close combat attacks, he will keep his distance and use ranged combat spells to attack. If the team used spirits, his own spirits will focus on attacking their spirits. And if not, the spirits will materialize and join in the physical assault.**

**If the team accepts Seimanth’s offer, he agrees to provide them with a large payment that consists of refned orichalcum coins and foci worth double the pay they were offered to kill him. He will also provide them with some of his feathers, scales, and blood, which they may present as proof of having slain him. His plan is to have the team head to the edge of the Disian harvesting site and proclaim their success. When they’ve presented the false proof and the Aztechnology forces lower their guard, he will attack. That will be the signal for the players to join in the assault. Their job is to provide the element of surprise and neutralize as many guards and soldiers as possible while Seimanth slays the leaders and dismantles the Disian harvesting devices. The location is not yet operational, and no Disians will be present, but a Faustian and several chimera will be there overseeing the fnal preparations. The Faustian and most of their chimera will be attacked by the dragon, but at least one of them will directly face the players.**
