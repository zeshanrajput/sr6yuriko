## **Taming the Wild**

**Taming a wild critter requires one week of effort, during which you must have regular access to the critter—at least one hour per day is required. This is much easier if the critter has been captured, but it can be accomplished in the wild if the critter will tolerate your presence. In order to begin taming a wild critter that has not been captured, you must frst succeed at indefnitely appeasing the critter— this requires as many net hits as the critter’s Willpower attribute (see “Please Don’t Eat me” above).**

**After you have spent at least one week attempting to tame the critter, make a training test. If this test fails, you may try again (with another week of effort) with a cumulative –1 dice pool penalty unless you take at least a week off before making another attempt. If the test succeeds, the critter has been tamed and can now receive further training.**

**Sapient critters cannot be tamed or trained. If you are able to communicate with a sapient critter, the normal rules for social interactions apply (p. 94, 96-8,** _**SR6**_ **).**
