## Beasts of the Bayou Posted by: Lanyap

The area around New Orleans has more than its fair share of threats. Everything from Awakened to Emerged make their homes out in the swamps, graves, and slums of the city. Get out deep in the bayou and you’ll even fi nd some nests of mosquito spirits. Beyond the creatures, New Orleans has the two-legged threats as well. Humanis has their tendrils outside the core downtown and have made friends with some of the remnants of the vampire hunter guilds. Just remember to shoot straight and conserve ammo.
