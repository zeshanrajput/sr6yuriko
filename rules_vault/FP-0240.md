## **The Old Guard**

**The quintessential Warpath members, they’re the ones who are still involved in our original sabotage-and-demands tactics, even though they have yet to yield any substantial gains. If a brutal attack is claimed by Warpath, it’s generally the Old Guard’s work. They still have plenty of shamans and adepts in their ranks, but they also have the most cybered fghters and drone riggers in Warpath.**

**Most of them also strongly believe that if you’re not a native (or mostly native), you have no right to live in the Americas. So unless the situation is dire, they are the least likely to cooperate with non-indigenous people.**
