## Vashon: Still in Fashion

Pretty people and dirty deals. That’s the best way to describe Vashon. Named for James Vashon, a friend of George Vancouver (it pays to have powerful friends), but now best known for the Vashon Island fashion line, this place has a storied history that no one cares about unless it’s being worked into this year’s look. As the largest island (thanks to Maury being part of Vashon), it’s got sprawl space. Thanks to being governed by taste, they don’t have big ugly skyrakers, only thin towers made for art more than efficiency. That’s Vashon!
