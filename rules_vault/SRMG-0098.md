## **Can non-awakened characters take Astral Beacon or Spirit Bane?**

Absolutely. With Astral Beacon, characters may need to roll a separate ‘untrained’ stealth test against astral observers. For example, a character with 8 Agility and 6 Stealth only uses 7 dice. NOTE: Astral observation uses Intuition + Perception to spot characters using stealth. Assensing does not ‘find’ auras, it reads them.
