## **Cyberjacks**

The table on Page 177 is the correct table for Cyberjacks, not Page 284. The Matrix Init Bonus for cyberjacks is the extra _dice_ you get when in VR. Cyberjacks are able to perform all functions of both a datajack and a commlink, _except_ voice communication because they lack any direct audio output component.
