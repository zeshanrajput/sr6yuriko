## Game Information

## Metaplanar Gates

An metaplanar gate allows travelers to enter and exit a metaplane in their physical form, including bringing items into the metaplanes, and bringing items from the metaplanes onto the physical plane. These are most commonly created by a free spirit with the metaplanar gateway power, but more permanent metaplanar gates exist. Some are natural occurrences (or more likely unnatural unintended consequences of a magical action or event), but others have been created by magicians. Creating a metaplanar gate requires vast resources,

such as millions of nuyen worth of orichalcum and other radical reagents, as well as a series of complex and numerous rituals carried out by initiates trained in the metamagic of geomancy. A gate can’t be constructed just anywhere—it must be built at the intersection of two or more ley lines. And then it must be recharged at least once per month with another geomancy ritual. Every one of these rituals consumes an exorbitant amount of radical reagents. In short, creating and maintaining an astral gate is something that only megacorporations and very large magical groups can hope to accomplish.

The Dweller on the Threshold does not appear when a metaplanar gate is used to travel from the physical plane to a metaplane.
