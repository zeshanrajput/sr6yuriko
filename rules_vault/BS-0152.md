## Simian Modification

Cyberarms are not required to exactly duplicate the size and shape of a user’s natural limb. Simian modification extends the forearms significantly. With this added length, the user’s hand reaches the ground with only a slight stoop. When making a Close Combat attack with a simian cyberarm, the user gains the Long Reach quality (p. 72, _SR6_ ). Only partial forearm and full arm cyberlimbs can employ simian modification.
