## Shifter Preference

Shifters have a reputation as primitive and not understanding modern life. This quality is for those shifters who do not grok tech. For those who don’t understand modern social intricacies, see Shifter Naivety. You may not gain ranks in Cracking, Electronics, or Engineering, and you may not default on tests that require these skills. You may not gain or spend Edge on any test where you are operating machinery more complicated than a hammer or an axe. GMs may require a skill test for normal day-today things other runners do automatically, such as answering a comm call, using the Matrix to fi nd the physical location for a bar, commanding an autopilot, or operating a device.
