## WUX (Wuxing, CFS)

The newest player in the game, Wuxing’s obviously named media network operates out of San Francisco in the CFS. They have little original content yet, focused mostly on rebroadcasting shows from Hong Kong or working with assorted SimStudios in California that have slipped out of Horizon’s control. Their home improvement shows, featuring gardening, landscaping, and, of course, _Feng Shui_ are hugely popular, while “Find Your Center,” the early morning tai chi workout, is a breath of fresh air in a world filled with young, corp-perfect bodies doing intense athletics everywhere else.
