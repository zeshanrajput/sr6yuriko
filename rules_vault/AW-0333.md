## Wuxing

Instead of conducting resource mining operations, the representatives of Wuxing have entirely focused their efforts on the Spire and its bureaucracy. Initially their efforts met with little success, but they eventually paid off when Wuxing was allowed considerable access to the Great Portal within the Spire. While most of the other megacorporations have some form of gate or portal access, none of them are large enough to allow cargo vehicles to pass through. Currently Wuxing is focusing its efforts here on studying the Great Portal in an attempt to use it to connect to a location on Earth.
