## **critter training test**

**To tame or train a critter, make an Infuence (Critters) + Charisma test opposed by the critter’s Intuition + Willpower. If the critter has any Essence loss, your Infuence test sufers a –1 dice pool penalty for every point of Essence loss—round up any fractions. If the critter has a critterjack (p. 135, Body Shop), reduce the Essence loss penalty by its rating.**
