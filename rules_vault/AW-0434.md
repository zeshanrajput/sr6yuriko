## Body

The mutation affects your endurance, resilience, and overall toughness. This could manifest as weakened bones, weeping sores, a burning sensation as you breathe, a racking cough, or chronic fatigue.

**Game effect:** All Edge boosts and actions cost twice as many points of Edge on tests that use Body. If you receive a second Body mutation, increase the threshold on all tests made to heal you (first aid, medkit, Heal spell, natural healing, etc.) by 1.
