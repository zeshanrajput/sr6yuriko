## Saving Face (A)

Sometimes you need to deal with failure in a way that helps put you back on solid footing. Maybe it’s being graceful, or perhaps pointing out someone else’s flaws—either way, you need to defuse the situation. If you gain the Suckered # status condition, you may spend a Minor Action and immediately make an Influence (Etiquette) + Charisma (3) test. If you succeed, the level of Suckered is reduced by net hits.
