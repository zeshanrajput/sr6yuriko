## Russia

The current issues between Russia and Yakut are being worked out here on CI. It’s a nice neutral spot, though Russia feels the Yakut are getting favorable treatment due to their Awakened nature, and the NAN respect for arcane nations. It’s a little ridiculous, but it makes a great excuse as to why Russia’s shifter bounty is being labeled as cruel. It’s all because the NAN are playing favorites and has nothing to do with metasapient rights.

- Shadows are hot with Japan talking to Denver and St. Louis. The empire is looking to support all the little free states in order to keep the continent divided.

- They’re also talking to the UCAS and looking to reverse their earlier relationship. This is mostly a matter of making inroads for their megas to take more and more chunks of the UCAS, but it could help stabilize the decline of the former world power.
