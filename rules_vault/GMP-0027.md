## Handing out Edge 

One of the major aspects of Shadowrun Sixth World Edition is a more complex Edge economy. Edge actions are expensive but that is based on the expectation that the runners are able to gain more by setting themselves up for success. This means that you will need to look out for your players and make sure they have opportunities to generate more Edge. Missions which include higher-powered NPC combatants, make it unlikely for the runners to gain Edge from combat encounters and more likely to use Edge. One potential way for them to gain more Edge would be if they do smart planning prior to combat or social encounters. If they have organized the situation in their favor, grant them each a point of Edge. Another place where you can grant Edge is if they have a knowledge skill that matches the current situation. So if the crew is breaking into a corporate facility and someone has the corporate security knowledge skill, grant them a point of Edge. The main punchline is that runners will need your help in finding ways to gain Edge which helps the game feel more dynamic.
