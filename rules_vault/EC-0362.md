## Local Character

## Vashonista

Born and raised on Vashon, you have a deep understanding of fashion and its social impact on those around you. You know how to read the fashion choices of others along with how to adjust your own fashion.

- **Cost:** 10 Karma

- **Game Effect:** You gain a point of Edge for all social encounters when face to face.
