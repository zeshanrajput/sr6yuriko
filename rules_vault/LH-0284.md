## Update Six: We’ve Failed

\<07-21-83/2141:21>

We’re getting nothing but static from the main comm channels, but some of the secondaries are still active. Torrent is doing his best, keeping the lines of communications open, but I don’t think it matters.

Across the globe, multiple cells and groups have indicated that they’ve failed … that their targets have fully activated, and they’re fleeing the area as best they can. I don’t know how many, I don’t know …

I’m now looking up into an ominous sky. I’ve seen the colors of a manastorm before, but not like this. Right now, everything above looks like a kaleidoscope from my nightmares. Last message I got was about the Harvesters being activated. And now, an eerie quiet, no nature, nothing. But the lights … I can’t help but be overwhelmed with a sense of doom. The few mages suddenly feel tired, others have fainted.

I can’t help but wonder if these are our final moments.
