## Enhanced articulation

A number of procedures like joint-surface coating, re-lubrication, and tendon and ligamentaugmentation lead to more fluid muscle and joint action. Enhanced articulation provides you with a +1 Agility and a bonus Edge when trying to move through cramped or confining spaces.
