## Hit the Dirt (A)

A character can quickly drop to the ground in a defensive manner, gaining +2 dice on the Defense test of an incoming attack. They gain the Prone status (p. 53), but due to the awkwardness of their fall, they suffer a –2 dice pool penalty on any subsequent tests using active skills until the end of their next turn, unless they use the Stand Up action.
