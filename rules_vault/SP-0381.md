## **stasis tube: game rules**

**The original Disian device contains an alchemical soporifc that duplicates the efects of the drug “slab” (p. 71, Body Shop ) and enchantments similar to the spell Nutrition (p. 31, Street Wyrd ) to keep the tube’s occupant alive and unconscious for extended periods of time.**
