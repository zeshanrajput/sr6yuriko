## Microflare launcher

The flare launcher shoots colored flares 200 meters into the air, illuminating an area the size of a city block for a couple of minutes and negating the advantage of poor or low lighting. If you shoot it at someone, it’s an untrained skill check; the micro flare deals 3P Fire damage.
