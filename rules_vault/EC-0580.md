## > **Tacoma Purple Haze**

You can get Texan, Aztlan, and Pueblo cuisine here, at a place Lone Star officers love. The tostadas are simply amazing. Don’t forget the food comes on the spicy side, so if you need it mild, you’re going to have to say so.

- Traveler Jones
