## Special Notes

Once the runners have dealt with the assault team, they are definitely the focus of all the muckrakers, and they start offering pay for paydata—they pool together 30,000 nuyen and pay it out based on how much quality information the PCs decide to share (GM discretion). Conversations here can also start to go both ways and fill in more details for the runners to get them ready for the final aspects of this campaign where they need to decide what to do with the data they have.
