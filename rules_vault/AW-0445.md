## Edge

A sense of loss and despair settles over you, filtering all of your thoughts and sending your mind down dark and melancholy paths.

**Game Effect:** All Edge boosts and actions cost an additional point of Edge. If you gain a second Edge mutation, you may only gain one point of Edge per round.
