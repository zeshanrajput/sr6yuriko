## Meridional Agronomics

Europe’s primary player in the food market, **Meridional Agronomics** focuses mostly on flora but has a large fauna research arm as well. They have a surprisingly deep well of grey biotech due to the Euro-focus on environmental damage and cleanup, but they have little in metahuman modification.
