## Commonalities

## Memory Gaps

While the soldiers have different levels of memory of their time away, none of them have actual memories of how they left Earth. Their time away was like a bad dream, where they didn’t know how they disappeared or where they went. They were just there. Similarly, they don’t know how they got back. They just found themselves in a different place. Many of them were working on plans to escape where they were when they returned home, but they don’t know if those efforts led to their return, or if they escaped some other way.
