## Krime Party

A frag grenade and a flash-bang had a baby after getting freaky with some fireworks and, um, streamers? The Krime Party Grenade is a party in a small package that geeks the slots in the room, frags up the ones that survive, and drapes light and brightly covered paper over the tripwire traps that might be in the room. Now with MCT’s AR-Push Technology to force the music of your choice into the ears of anyone in the target area! Have yourself a KRIME PARTY!
