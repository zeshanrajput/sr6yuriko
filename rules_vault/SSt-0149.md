## **Don’t: Drek Where You Live**

**Want to get along well with your community? Don’t bring your work to you home. It may happen sometimes, because heaven knows there are things that are out of our control, but as far as you can, keep things from coming home with you. Do everything you can to not let people follow you home, and since you should be able to shake a tail, that should be doable. Avoid taking formal jobs from people in your neighborhood (as opposed to doing favors for them), and if you do any work in your neighborhood, try to be smooth and not execute it in ways that will build a need for vengeance.**

- **In other words, faces should earn their keep, and sammies should take a step back.**

- **Ms. Myth**
