They set on hiring a few prime runners to accomplish the impossible: persuade Dis to discuss an alternative to their Final Project.

A few teams took the bait, because the money was too good. They met with this little cabal, generally during or near Grand Tour events in Paris or London. Most politely declined this suicide run, and the rest, who were never heard from again, accepted. Yet, in early April of this year, some influential Disians agreed to hold a summit of sorts.
