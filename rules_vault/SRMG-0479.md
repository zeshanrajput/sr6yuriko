## **Is Mil-Spec Comm Systems (p. 67-69) legal for** _**SRM**_ No.

## **What vehicles and weapons are (p. 132-149) legal for** _**SRM**_ **?**

Ares Model 301 and 502: Yes. The legs are changed to be propulsion and are not considered as cyberlimbs. Remove the milspec comms. Using this gains a +1 Heat modifier.

Wheeled Vehicles: Banned Tracked Vehicles: Banned Battle Tanks: Banned Rumors and Ghosts: Banned Vehicle Mounted Weapons: Banned Chemical Weapons: Banned Naval Weapons: Banned
