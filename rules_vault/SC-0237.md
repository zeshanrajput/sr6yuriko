## Dissonant Technomancer

## Backgrounds

Most dissonant technomancers do not start out that way after Emerging. Perhaps you were simply drawn to the Dissonance because it aligned with your nature, but for many it is a response to trauma that comes from ill treatment by technophobes. But perhaps we should begin with an attempt to describe, if not define (for that would be impossible), what the Dissonance is. It seems to be a force of nature born from the Resonance that alters, corrupts, and deletes data on the Matrix—perhaps even in the Foundation. Not encountered as an entity, it is perhaps a living collection of impulses with

a will of its own, something like a mentor spirit for a magician. Matrix fauna that have been affected (some would say corrupted) by Dissonance act erratically and often garble files and other data simply by being near to them. Sometimes the effect spreads, and it has been known to mangle an entire host. A dissonant technomancer is an emerged individual that has connected to the Dissonance. They are bound to the Dissonance somehow, and some gain access to unique abilities that make them extremely effective at messing things up in the Matrix. There are drawbacks—those affected by the Dissonance often find it difficult to focus on one thing for very long, are easily distracted, and sometimes have erratic emotional responses.

You are a Dissonant technomancer, and this means you are driven to undermine the status quo of the Matrix. To show the users they aren’t simply operating enslaved devices, but rather participating in a living ecosystem. To make sure that no one feels truly safe in the Matrix. Most other technomancers are content to deepen their connection to the Resonance and seek to understand its greater mysteries, while deckers treat the Matrix as a synthetic playground to be molded to their whims. You exist to shake things up, to introduce chaos and entropy, and you are very, very good at it. But why are you drawn to sow chaos in a place that most people believe exists to bring order to the world? Are you a naturally chaotic person, doing it for the thrills? Did you have a semi-religious experience that connected you with the Dissonance?
