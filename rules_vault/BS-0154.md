## Snake Fingers

A hand with snake fingers can rotate the fingers at the knuckle and extend or retract fifty percent of their length, providing a wider range of motion than is ordinarily possible. Snake fingers reduce the threshold for Escape Artist tests involving that hand by 1 (to a minimum of 1) and grant bonus Edge when performing sleight of hand. Snake fingers cannot be combined with other finger augmentations.
