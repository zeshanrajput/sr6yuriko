## **Arrival On-Scene**

**Getting to Dr. Rickman’s last known location is easy; the player characters meet no resistance nor have any encounters. However, it should be noted that Matrix signals in this area are spotty—the large decrepit metal and concrete structures create a Noise Rating 2 effect.**

**On arrival, they notice signs that others are in the area (footsteps, vehicle tracks, etc.). Eventually, the player characters make their way to the exact coordinates, which are on the third foor of an old, decrepit offce building; an old and faded sign reads “Room 306: Maintenance Supervisor.” Broken furniture and old cleaning supplies fll the former offce while half-fallen, rotten tiles and old wiring hang limply from the ceiling.**

**Other than some “clean spots” where it’s obvious someone sat down in the flth (and multiple footprints), a search of the room reveals no other signs of Dr. Rickman or where she may have gone next. If the players choose to investigate, have them make a Perception (4) test (teamwork tests are allowed). If they succeed, they notice that if they stand in a particular spot in the room and at**
