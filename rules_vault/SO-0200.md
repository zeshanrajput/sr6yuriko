## Heat: When the Pressure’s On

No one likes it when the police or corporate security are brought to their front door. And when player characters are out breaking and entering, murdering, kidnapping, and perpetrating all the other illegal activities they do, a call to a fixer for a quick bolt-hole has the chance of bringing security down on the contact’s neck. That puts the runners at risk of getting the heat blown back on them because the contact snitches on the team, or just decides to make a quick profit. How the contact responds to security or gangs that come knocking will depend partially on their Loyalty and Favor Points, but also the level of pressure that comes looking.
