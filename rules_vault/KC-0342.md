## Allen (human, male, 22, no hair, grey eyes, muscular build)

Allen is named for the way he moves joints—by wrenching them, Allen is all up close and personal. He teams up well with Ocean for a nice barrier spell or illusion for cover as he charges into battle. He’s concise and deadly. He goes for the quickest method to drop an opponent and does so in silence, with an eerie calm.

During the early operations, Allen will hang back, often drawing a little attention to himself in order to pick fights. He rarely starts them and usually begins with a bit of defense before going on the offense.

When the ambush kicks off, Allen will be hidden by Ocean, or Ocean will toss a barrier up for Allen to stay behind to close the distance. He’ll attack precisely and try to drop an opponent as quickly as possible, usually focusing on the team’s weaker members in order to get numbers in their favor and force the enemy to worry about their allies.
