## **The Griffons**

**I have had drek luck on too many research expeditions going after these majestic predators. But then again, I am pretty much a snack-size morsel—I just pack a lot of fght. Big problem is, all that fght isn’t terribly useful once they’ve snatched you from your sleep and have hauled you up several hundred meters. Since I’m a glutton for punishment and also one who seeks to learn and improve on the mistakes of the past, I’ve looked about at several different relatives of the griffon, and I have a decent snippet of knowledge for each.**
