## Mystic Weave

## (rating 1-4)

The key to Parashield’s Mystic Tough armor is this stuff. This add-on accessory can be woven into existing armor to provide a little protection against incoming mojo.

Mystic Weave creates a threshold equal to its rating for all mana-based spells cast on a target but also imposes a negative dice pool modifier on all Magic-related tests equal to double its rating for any magician wearing the armor.
