## Epic Toxic Mutant Horror

Some toxic mutant horrors get bigger and nastier over time as they absorb others into their mass. These can become extremely large, nearly unstoppable forces of destruction—everything in their path is either destroyed or subsumed into the monster’s mass. Try to avoid these if you can help it. If one ever notices you, you’d best run.
