## Vomeronasal Gland

This augmentation combines a boost to the neural pathways between the nose and brain, and the implementation of additional olfactory organelles that augment their sense of smell to levels that rival a bloodhound.

The recipient can learn to identify individual people and animals by smell and can detect basic emotional states. This gives the individual 1 point of situational Edge when making smell-based Perception tests and Social tests when they can smell their target. Strong odors and intense background odors incur the Nauseated status (p. 52, _SR6_ ) if the individual fails a Body + Willpower (2) test.
