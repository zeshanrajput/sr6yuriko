## Cybernetic Augmentation

Critters with a natural Body attribute of 2 or greater may be augmented with cyberware and bioware. The nuyen costs of all augmentations for particularly large critters—critters with a natural Body of 8 or higher—are increased by ten percent for every point of Body above 8.

Critters with a natural Body attribute of 1 or less are too small to receive traditional cyberware or bioware, but some nanotechnology applications allow for a limited range of augmentations.
