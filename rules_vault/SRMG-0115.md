## **Do physical augmentations carry over into non-physical spaces (e.g. astral or VR)?**

Yes, regardless of sources, augmentations to Attributes and Skills (but not other traits, such as Initiative Dice) carry over. Keep in mind that many attributes are replaced in those situations.
