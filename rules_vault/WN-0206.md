## The Job

When the team arrives at the precinct, they find the entire block is in a state of chaos. Both physical and AR alarms are activated, and a steady stream of people are running out of the building, some still in NYPD-issued orange jumpsuits. None of the exterior doors seem to be secured, and there aren’t any officers in sight. If the team decides to stroll into the impound lot or the precinct building, no one shows any interest in stopping them. As they approach the building, all wireless access is cut off, and any devices that depend on a wireless connection stop working (the entire area has 18 noise).

Ten minutes after the team arrives, a patrol car comes back in for a shift change. The officers see the situation and drive out of the noise zone to call for backup. Within another ten minutes, the place



is crawling with NYPD, Inc., officers who attempt to get the situation under control.
