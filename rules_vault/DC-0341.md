## Removed Manual Controls

Almost all modern passenger vehicles are capable of being piloted by either AR or manual controls. With this modification, the owner simply removes the manual controls, leaving behind only AR analogues. This mod is used by people who want to free up some interior space or who don’t like using anything but AR. This mod frees up one Chassis mod slot and removes the unadapted gear penalty for dwarfs and trolls, since AR controls are equally convenient for everyone.
