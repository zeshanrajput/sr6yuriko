## **Surfst ar Marine Seacop II**

**Surfstar Marine has revitalized their line with their new Seacop II. Marketed toward LEP clients in coastal and heavy river regions, the Seacop is generally utilized for routine search-and-seizure duty; its speed comes into play when criminals think they can outrun the smaller craft.**
