## **Stasis Tube**

**A relatively simple device, the stasis tube is typically a two-meter-long, one-meter-diameter glassand-metal cylinder. Similar to a Valkyrie module, these devices are designed to keep a metahuman alive and sedated for storage. They produce an alchemical soporifc and enchantments to keep the tube’s occupant alive and unconscious for extended periods of time. Recently, stasis tubes have been found that appear to be manufactured on the material plane using Sixth World technology. These versions incorporate a modifed Valkyrie module instead of magic.**

**The truly sinister nature of either version of a status tube is revealed when it is connected to another Disian device such as a portal generator or a Harvester. The metahuman contained within the tube becomes an energy source, their Essence drained away and used to power up the connected device.**
