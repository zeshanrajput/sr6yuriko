## Hot Mess

Some people clean up good, but not you. No matter how well dressed you are, you always manage to look rough and disheveled.

**Bonus:** 2 Karma per level (1 to 4)

**Game Effect:** Each level of this quality reduces your Social Rating by 1. This penalty to Social Rating does not benefit you when a lower Social Rating provides an advantage, such as making Intimidation tests.
