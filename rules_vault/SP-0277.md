## **Head in the Clouds**

**Arleesh hires the PCs to travel to the Place of Clouds in the metaplanes so that they can gather information about reported Disian activity there. While investigating, the runners have the opportunity to acquire a shipment of collected True Elements. However, some Disian chimeras are after the same target.**
