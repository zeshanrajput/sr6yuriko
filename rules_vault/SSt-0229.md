## **Too Many People Are After You**

**Is one person trying to kill you? Must be Tuesday. Take care of them and get on with your life. Did you get a whole organized crime outft mad at you? Are you on a megacorporation’s hit list? Then maybe it’s time to throw out your old life and start anew.**

**Everyone has their own defnition of “too many”—people like Balladeer might not ever think there’s a number they can’t handle. But the rest of us don’t want to spend our lives looking over our shoulder, and buying into a new life can be worth the security it provides. Just make sure your SIN is good and your break with your old life is complete.**
