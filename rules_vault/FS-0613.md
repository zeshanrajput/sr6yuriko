## Neophyte

## (Professional Rating 2)

The newest among the Halloweeners are usually the most dangerous, even though they’re not the most skilled. Attracted to flames and fires, they are desperate to make a name and filled with visions of burning the world to fit the gang’s image of the ideal future. Many of these youngsters don’t make it far, as fire is an unforgiving mistress, but those who do slide up to the next rung of the firebug ranks.
