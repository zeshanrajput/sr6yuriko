## **Spirit/Sprite Bane** 

For Spirit or Sprite Bane, replace any spirits or sprites in the mission to match the character’s bane. If this mission does not contain spirits or sprites, add them in at inconvenient times. If the team is in a firefight, a random spirit of air (Force 4 or highest Magic on the team -2) joins the fray or grants their opponents Concealment. If they are sneaking, it might Influence the guards to check where the team is or it could cause an Accident. A random sprite could make an easy data steal into an absolute nightmare as the datafile is Camouflaged or Hashed, or the decker could become the target of an Electron Storm. A runner targeted by an angry sprite while they are in a firefight will find their equipment hacked or their opponents equipment boosted.
