## The Guizhen R&D

The technological breakthroughs of Yingyong created the Guizhen I device that can be incorporated into an array of tools. At its most simplistic analog, it’s a flashlight. It can be fashioned to look exactly like a normal version of the device, with an added kick. It uses high-capacitance power cells or can connect to another power source. Anyone caught in the visual scope of one of the devices must roll Body + Willpower vs. the setting on the Device. Those with flare compensation gain a bonus Edge toward resisting the effect. Individuals who suffer from the Blinded III status are immune. Individuals with low-light vision suffer –2 dice
