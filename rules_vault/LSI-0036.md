## Neighborhood Rating

What security protocols are a part of your everyday life? This is more about how strict the rules are than about how closely you follow them. Typically, you know the rules of the neighborhood and either how to obey them or bend them without attracting too much heat. The Neighborhood Rating asset defines your local Security Rating (p. 238-240, _SR6_ ). Because of the expense, it covers both the general protection of your residence and the law enforcement status of the whole neighborhood. The default rating of your home security devices (maglocks, cameras, motion sensors, alarm systems) is equal to the LP invested in this asset. You may also choose to augment this with the purchase of specific gear. If you adjust this asset to a different rating, this change represents either a move to a new place or a major change in your neighborhood.

**Cost: 0-6 LP**
