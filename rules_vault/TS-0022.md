## **Minor Awakened Talents**

**Not every Awakened individual is able to sling spells and call spirits. In fact, those few lucky individuals are in the vast minority of the total pool of magically active metahumans. Far more common, by several orders of magnitude, are those labeled as Sensitives or the Aware. These individuals can perceive astrally and with training can read auras and emotional states, detect spells, pick out astral signatures, and see entities in astral space. While perceiving, they can also engage in astral combat the same way an adept with astral perception would. Although not as impressive an ability as those wielded by the more powerful Awakened, astral perception is a tremendously useful tool for any investigator to have. Astral sensitivity gives the aware a huge advantage over the strictly mundane detective and opens up entirely new avenues of inquiry. While not directly admissible as evidence in court, reading auras and emotional states allows for the fltering of suspects and witnesses, while reading residual astral signatures may sometimes result in new suspects or leads.**

**Less common than astral sensitives, but still far more common than full or even aspected mages, are astral explorers. In addition to simply perceiving astral space, these individuals are able to leave their bodies and astrally project. When their astral abilities are trained, they have the full range of assensing skills along with astral projection. Many also become quite profcient in astral combat. While projecting, explorers may carry along the astral form of a weapon focus, making them a deadly opponent in astral space. Explorers are often employed to patrol the astral, watching for illegal activity and protecting designated areas from astral intrusion. To make up for some of their limitations, spirits summoned by other members of a force are often assigned to work with explorers, relaying messages between the astral and the material worlds.**
