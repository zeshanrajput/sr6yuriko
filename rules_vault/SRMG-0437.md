## **Can I play a cyborg or cybernetic shell (p. 176-179)?**

Yes. Cyborgs are not compatible with Transhumanism. In addition, the CCU is a packaged deal, and trade-offs have been made in the design of the components. As such the CCU cannot be upgraded in any way.
