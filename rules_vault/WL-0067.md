## **Pixiu**

**There are times where I wonder if a people’s belief in something is enough to create a thing where nothing was before. For example, there have never been any lions in Manchuria, but yet the local population has long included lions as part of their dance, art, and culture. After the Awakening, much to the surprise of everyone except the natives of the land, lions were seen to roam the grassland steppes of Manchuria. But these lions are far from ordinary, as they can fy on their golden wings.**

**The most dangerous time to deal with peryton is during their breeding season in the spring. During this time, the males become extremely aggressive. Anyone wandering through their hunting grounds is at risk as the animal will typically increase its protein intake during this period.**

- **The old stories talk about these creatures having their rectum sealed by angry emperors. Does that hold for what we’ve seen in the wild?**
