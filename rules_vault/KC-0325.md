Architecture and Structure: This host looks like an oldtime slaughterhouse, complete with virtual bloody carcasses and all. It’s quite unsettling.

The hangars have a heavy smart-canvas that matches the surrounding rock. The smart-material drapery opens to let in smuggler vehicles that are pulled into the caverns with a winch system. The vehicles are also pulled out by another winch that is hidden in the lip of the rockface. Each hangar has a camera hardwired to a wireless station near the center of the living quarters in the underground complex. This network allows each of the smugglers to access their hangar’s camera with a special access code from their commlink inside the underground complex.

The hangars and underground complex have a total of 76 workers (use Mafia Debt Collector, p. 205, _SR6_ ; add gel ammo). These workers perform loading and unloading duties, act as security when trouble arises, and handle the operation of the winches, including running out to a craft as soon as it lands to get it pulled underground as quickly as possible.
