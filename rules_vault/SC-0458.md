## Independent Professionals

- **Common life modules:** Academy Training, Adept Training, Astral Explorations, Bodyguard, Bounty Hunter, Coach/Trainer, College/University, Courier, Covert Ops, Entrepreneur, Financial Services Broker, Fixer, Intern, Investigator, Journalist, Lawyer, Magical Studies, Matrix Security, Medical Doctor, Mentor, Military, Nurse, Politician, Pragmatic Magician, Small Business Owner, Smuggler, Technical School, Think Tank, Traveler, Veterinarian

As noted, the social adept is so new that many of them likely do not know they have magical talent. They could exist outside megacorporations, taking on roles such as lawyers, businesspeople, doctors, nurses, or other jobs that require licenses and credentials (including professional magicians).

The character has a generally quiet background, where they have not been thoroughly indoctrinated by the life within an institution, although this can easily be combined with any of these other worlds to reflect the universal need for trained and vetted professionals. Of course, the Sixth World has a couple of independent professions that are of particular relevance here: institutionally independent military and intelligence professionals (mercenaries and spies).
