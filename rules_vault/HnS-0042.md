## Ownership

When you buy a piece of electronic gear, the device’s internal ID chip becomes registered to

your SIN, allowing you to interact with it in the Matrix. This registration hooks the internal ID of the device to your SIN. Changing ownership without the cooperation of the legitimate owner involves replacing the ID chip (that, or altering records on a series of hosts). Changing the ID chip on the device requires an Electronics (Hardware) + Logic (24, 1 Hour) Extended test. The device must remain connected to the Matrix, and if a glitch occurs, the police are notified of the attempted illegal change.
