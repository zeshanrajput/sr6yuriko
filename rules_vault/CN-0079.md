## Open File: 0304-A \<decryption complete/opening transcript/ translating from French>

## Speaker: “Jean-Claude”, smuggler

Yes, I think I may have some information. My former partner Serge disappeared six weeks ago. Serge was a magician, not very powerful. Could not cast a spell to save his life. But he had a talent for—what is it called, the sight? No, assensing! He was also very good with animals, especially Awakened ones. Together, we did excellent business transporting live and not-so-live specimens. Then one day, we were asked to transport some kind of creature I’d never seen before. One of my crew jokingly called it a chimera, like the beast of legend. Even Serge did not want to get close to it. Throughout the trip, he was extremely nervous, and more than once I saw him making commlink calls. When I asked about it, he tried to play it off or became angry. But Serge is very nervous and emotional normally, so I think little of it.



We finish the job, and our client said they have more work and wish to continue using our services. I’m overjoyed, but Serge wanted to vomit. When we returned home, three individuals I have never seen before were there to meet Serge. One had the trappings of another magician, while the other two had the bearing of military or mercenary. He would insist to speak to them alone and I was concerned, wondering if he was in trouble, or something else. <chuckles> But I underestimated Serge, and that was my fault. Two weeks later, we had a similar job. But this time, we were ambushed at our re-fueling point by not just one but two groups. The first, I had encountered before—local shadowrunners. They are competent, but we were more than capable of handling them. But before we could, all of them were shot dead by snipers. I am dumbfounded, even more when Serge pulls his pistol on me and the rest of our crew. It was the first time he’d ever pulled his weapon. With tears in his eyes, he begged forgiveness and for us to not resist, or we would die. A minute later, a tilt-rotor with stealth baffled engines lands, and its crew, wearing dark uniforms, offload our cargo. Serge went with them but left us a credstick containing double our original payment. I still do not understand what happened, but we got paid, and now I have a new magical associate. Such is business.
