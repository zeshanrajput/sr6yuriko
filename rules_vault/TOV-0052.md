## P etwo

## I bo

The Petwo are considered the hot-headed nation. The lwa associated with this group are more likely to be aggressive or quick. The practices are often considered darker and warlike. Nearly all the lwa have some aspect that is part of the Petwo nanchon whether that be the jealous lover, Ezili Dantor, or death at sea, Agwe Flambeau. Spells are typically Combat in nature, but also include mental Manipulation and Detection spells.
