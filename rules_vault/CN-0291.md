## The Associates

Sometimes, a syndicate has to look for outside help. These could be specialists, like a high-powered lawyer, a crack shot, or it could involve hiring assets from another rengo, but when it really counts, they hire shadowrunners. Deckers and magicians are, of course, always in demand, but riggers get hired to amp up security in an oyabun’s house when two rings go to war, faces might get hired as negotiators when peace is needed, and, of course, it’s a rare street samurai who hasn’t had a working relationship with the Yakuza in return for some upgrades.
