## Firearm Diplomacy

Ares is more than Knight Errant and gun manufacturing, but those areas set a lot of the general corporate culture. This means that some current and former Ares employees are a little over-reliant on firearms as a means of conflict resolution. They don’t always fire their weapon, but they’re willing to bring it out to emphasize whatever point they’re making at the moment.

**Bonus:** 3 Karma

**Game Effect:** Any time when an argument becomes heated (gamemaster discretion), a character with this quality must make a Composure (2) test. If they fail, they draw their preferred weapon and point it at their opponent, regardless of local laws or consequences. If they have no weapon on them, they prepare for unarmed combat by making fists or something similar.
