## Cloud Islands

Thousands of cloud islands fill the sky in the Place of Clouds, for as far as the eye can see in every direction. These islands exist upon dense white clouds, and upon these clouds are every environment imaginable—and some that defy imagination. Some of these islands are not much larger than a city block, while others could be considered small continents. Upon each cloud island is a unique biome that is not repeated on any other cloud island. These include forests, deserts, mountains, lakes, rivers, jungles, plains, magma fields,

THEeternally raging storms, erupting volcanoes, ice fields, and many more bizarre environments. Each island is fully stocked with appropriate flora and fauna, and so far no one has definitively determined whether these are actual biological entities or animi of this strange hyper-metaplane. Many of the islands are also inhabited by spirits that find the biome pleasing. With the exception of engaging in casual amiable conversation, these spirits tend to ignore metaplanar travelers. If you encounter such a local spirit who is open to conversation, ask them as many questions as you can think of about the island, for they know it well and will not hesitate to answer you.
