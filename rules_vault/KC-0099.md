## Critters

There are more than just two-legged predators in Lagos. Crocodiles inhabit the waters all over the region along with their Awakened kin, the afanc. Awakened and mundane wild canines, rodents, monkeys, wild cats, birds, and insects along with plants are commonplace and are a fact of everyday life for the locals. They should be somewhat terrifying for anyone from cities where devil rats are horrors in dark alleys. This adventure shouldn’t be some kind of safari facing a myriad of local critters, but a few scattered here and there as a fear factor in the shadows or as a sudden attack in a quiet part of town could set an appropriate mood.
