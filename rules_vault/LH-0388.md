## Evo: Wandering, But Not Lost

Despite the revelation regarding Evo’s motives for siding against the Disians, they still played a significant role in winning the shadow war by providing much needed weaponry, matériel, and most importantly, medical support to the Defender Movement.

As a result, Evo’s corporate HQ in Vladivostok came under heavy attack by pro-Disian forces during the final phases of the war. The main building’s automated defenses were disabled, and if not for the actions of Buttercup herself, it would have been completely destroyed, accompanied by a staggering loss of life. The Vladivostok HQ is currently abandoned, literally not figuratively, as the new Evo board considers its options. After setting up a temporary HQ in Kyoto, the board is debating over whether it wants to repair the old HQ or set up a new location in Africa, Asia, or Europe.
