## Glitches and Critical Glitches

Glitches and critical glitches present prime opportunities for gamemasters to add flavor, excitement, and plot twists to your _Shadowrun_ game. That doesn’t mean it’s easy to come up with glitches on the fly, so we’re here to help—the Glitch Table has a bunch of glitch and critical glitch suggestions for a wide variety of _Shadowrun_ situations. Note that they tend to get more severe as you get farther to the right.
