## Zone

## Awakened Drugs and Magical Compounds

## Animal Tongue

- **Vector:** Ingestion

- **Speed:** 1 hour

- **Duration:** (12 – Body) hours, minimum 1 hour

- **Power:** 6

- **Effect:** Immunity to the Frightened status

SSRIs are another drug created to help users deal with crippling anxiety or phobias. This drug will help you deal with those times when your phobias would put your run at risk. The negative side effect is that it makes users restless and sensitive to light. Users receive a Mild Allergy to light. When the drug wears off, the user suffers from the Panicked status for 1 hour.

- **Vector:** Ingestion

- **Speed:** 10 minutes

- **Duration:** 1D6 hours

- **Power:** 8

- **Effect:** Animal Control (p. 221, _SR6_ )

Animal tongue is made from the pulp of the manzana cactus found in Aztlan. The drug grants the user the Animal Control power. Warriors and hunters in the area use the compound to protect themselves from predators and scout through the eyes of desert birds. When the drug wears off, the user gains the Phobia quality concerning all animals that were controlled, lasting for eight hours.
