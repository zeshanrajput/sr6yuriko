## > **Gianelli’s Restaurant**

You like big, traditional Italian cooking, served with exquisite sauces at portions designed for whole families? Of course you do! Come to Gianelli’s, where the head chef is former Gianelli Mob leg breaker and current war consiglieri Tony Gianelli. Marvel at sitting a booth away from big players in the family as you savor a marinara so good you can’t help but come back again. Baffle at the kind old man giving balloons to kids whoused to dispose of bodies. Cringe in paranoia as you bite into your luxuriant tiramisu, knowing that this place could get hit by the Yakuza at any second, kicking the latest Mob War into full gear. And tip fraggin’ well.
