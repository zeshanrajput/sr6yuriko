## Command and Control Comms (C3)

A C3 unit is a system designed to manage a huge number of communications channels simultaneously, coordinated by simple artificial intelligence to allow a lone metahuman operator to manage the network effectively. When a PAN includes a C3 unit, that PAN can protect any number of devices, subject to gamemaster discretion.
