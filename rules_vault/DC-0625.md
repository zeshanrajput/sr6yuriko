## Redline

## (Piloting)

When you change position in a vehicle chase, you may move up to two range categories relative to the chase target. Your vehicle takes 4P damage (unresisted). If you are the chase target, you instead prevent any pursuers from gaining on you for the remainder of the combat round.

**Cost:** 4 Edge
