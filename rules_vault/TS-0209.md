## **Loyalty and the Law**

**Your contact with one of the security providers will hear about ongoing investigations and operations. If the offcer is aware that law enforcement is operating in the same area the runner team is, or if they’re aware that the team is a potential target, the contact may proactively reach out to warn them. If the team’s current Heat is less than or equal to the Loyalty of the contact**

**Favors given or received in the interview room skew 1 point in favor of the detective, to a minimum of 1 or a maximum of 3. For instance, if the detective is going to make a minor infraction go away, which would normally be a 1 Favor Point**

**or if there are 3 unused Favor Points owed, that individual will reach out to the team.**

**When the team is asking favors of the law enforcement service personnel contact, this can have an impact on their reputation. If the actions affect the organization negatively, the contact loses a point of Reputation with that group. Any time this happens, each member of the team who is connected with that contact loses a point of Loyalty. If the contact’s Reputation drops too low, they will reject the team’s request for aid.**

**If the team has hurt the relationship with their contact enough, that person will look to cut ties with them. If the team owes the contact Favor Points greater than 2 x the current Loyalty, then on the next request for help, the contact will begin the process of cutting ties with them. If the team’s Heat is greater than 10, the offcer will organize a sting to trap them.**
