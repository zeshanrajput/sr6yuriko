## **Don’t Be Afraid of Police Response Times**

**Sometimes, when we look for a place to live, we think like criminals and worry about how quickly the police will arrive when called. We tend to like response to be slow, so we can make things look good for us or just get out of there. But at your home, if you’re doing things properly, you’re a citizen, not a criminal. Police response times might work in your favor if some drekheads decide to carry a personal grudge to your doorstep.**

- **Really? What self-respecting runner would ever rely on the cops to solve their problems?**

**> Mika**
