## Corp-Shill

Sometimes it’s obvious when the person you’re working with is tied at the hip to one of the big corporations—they wear the branded shoes and shirts and talk the company lingo. Other times, they keep that relationship on the down-low and try to hide how deep they are in the pockets of corporations that only desire control and power. While that doesn’t make them easy to turn, you know that anything you discuss with them is going straight back to their handlers.

**Game Effect:** Anything the contact learns about the team automatically goes back to the corporation and reduces any thresholds by 2 when the corporation is attempting to find the team for the duration of the current run.
