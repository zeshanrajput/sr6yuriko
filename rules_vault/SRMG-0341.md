## **Can I fly with Functional Wings (6WC, p. 125)?**

Both Type I and Type II wings require at least Magic 1 in order to fly. If the creature has no Magic attribute, then they can still glide. An additional note is that Type I wings do not “transform” but are a permanent replacement for the creature’s arms and hands.
