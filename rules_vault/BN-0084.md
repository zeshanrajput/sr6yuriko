## Increase Physical Attribute

place of Charisma to determine the duration of this power or the number of critters you can affect.

Specify a physical attribute when you select this power. You gain a +1 bonus to this attribute for each level of this power while in animal form, up to your augmented maximum (+4). You may take this power multiple times—each attribute counts as a separate animalism power, each with its own level.
