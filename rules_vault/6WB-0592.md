## Ritual Failure

There are a number of rare things that could cause a ritual to fail prematurely. If a participant leaves the foundation after Step 3 of the ritual but before the ritual is complete, the ritual fails. If the leader is incapacitated (or killed) before Step 6, the ritual collapses and fails. If the foundation is disrupted or broken at any point after Step 3, the ritual fails.

When a ritual fails, every participant immediately suffers drain. Make a test by rolling dice equal to the ritual’s threshold; the drain is equal to twice the number of hits on this test in Stun damage. Any reagents you’ve already spent in Step 4 are wasted.
