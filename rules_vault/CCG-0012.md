## Conjuring 

Rules for binding spirits are not in the _SR6_ core book. If your character currently has bound spirits, refund 500 nuyen times the total Force of all bound spirits to repay the cost of reagents used in binding. If you have an ally spirit, take a Karma refund, or if your GM agrees, you may take the spirit through this conversion process to make it a full _SR6_ entity.
