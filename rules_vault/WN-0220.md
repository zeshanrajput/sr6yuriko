## What Makes Her So Special?

The star of any ball is the person who stands out the best and can work it to their advantage. This is what Gabrielle Al Thani Spinrad is still hoping to do. The gown had not been crafted yet, and time is getting incredibly short. The lead designer not only has forgotten the design, but she’s forgotten who she is. If the design is to be recovered, so is Becky Wu Ping’s mind. The runners have their work cut out for them.
