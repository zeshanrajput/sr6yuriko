## Effects

Once you’ve made the score, vanish. Make sure it’s going through all kinds of escrow, covered by the idea that it’s for tax breaks or something. Press the idea of maximizing profit for everyone. One of the most potent elements of Salt is that it’s free money sitting in the ground, waiting to be picked up. People are willing to pay a little to get a lot, especially if it’s right under their feet. In a way, you’re the one helping them to understand that they’re rich, and all you’re asking for is the finder’s fee.
