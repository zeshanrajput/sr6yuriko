## Momentum

If a player character is following up from a previous stage in a con or negotiation that went well, it makes sense for them to gain circumstantial Edge from the momentum of that success. Typically, this is up to the GM’s discretion, but a good rule of thumb is that if the previous skill check succeeded by four or more net hits, circumstantial Edge may be applied to represent that momentum.
