## Searchlight

The searchlight modification installs a high-powered light mounted somewhere on the exterior of the vehicle, capable of all-around movement from wherever it happens to be mounted. It can be operated remotely or manually if it’s physically accessible. Its illumination goes three times as far as a standard flashlight and can be bought in low-light or thermographic versions for the same price.
