## St ep Four:

## Spend Customization Karma

All shifters receive 50 Karma to spend on character advancements, such as attributes, skills, qualities and additional funds for gear

**and lifestyle. The Shifter quality and any other qualities purchased in Step Three come out of** this pool. Animalism or Initiation can be taken during character generation. You may begin play with no more than 5 Karma remaining unspent.

Shifters may not take Transhumanism (p. 146, _Sixth World Companion_ ). Adepts, magicians, and mystic adepts may initiate as normal, but may not take levels of Animalism (see below).
