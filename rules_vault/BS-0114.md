## Biomonitor

A cybernetic version of a standard biomonitor (p. 281, _SR6_ ) factors other augmentations into itscalculations. For instance, it will not mistake an active adrenaline pump as symptoms of shock or cardiac distress.

**Cyberware overdrive:** If a biomonitor is wirelessly synchronized with a medkit, the user’s Essence is considered 1 point higher (maximum 6.0) while the user is receiving medkit healing (p. 119, _SR6_ ). One die on this test is converted to a wild die.

**Wireless bonus:** When a predetermined amount of Physical and Stun damage has been accumulated, a wireless auto-injector can automatically trigger itself, or call DocWagon if the user has a contract.
