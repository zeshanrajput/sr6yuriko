## **Herbivore/Omnivore**

**Critters that eat plants as part of their diet are the cheapest and easiest to feed. If your lifestyle category is Low, Medium, High, or Luxury, the cost of feeding your critters is covered under your lifestyle cost. If your lifestyle category is Street or Squatter, you must pay an additional (critter’s Body x 50) nuyen per month to feed your critter.**
