## Physical Attribute Enhancement

The lowest-impact and easiest-to-apply genetic augmentation is an increase to a critter’s physical attributes. Each physical attribute—Body, Agility, Reaction, and Strength—must be improved with a separate augmentation. The critter’s natural attribute is increased by the rating of this augmentation, up to a maximum of double its original natural attribute. This does not count toward the augmented maximum, allowing the critter to be further enhanced by other methods and augmentations.

If a critter has an attribute that starts at 0, it can be increased to a maximum of 1. If a critter with a Body of 0 is enhanced to have a Body of 1, its Condition Monitor becomes 9 (instead of 1).
