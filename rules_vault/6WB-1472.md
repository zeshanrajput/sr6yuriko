## Retinal duplication

This highly illegal modification can be loaded with a recording of someone else’s retina to create an almost perfect reproduction. Make an Opposed test between the retinal duplication rating and the retinal scanner rating ( **Sensors and Scanners** , p. 241). The system can actively store a number of retinas equal to its rating. Changing retinal patterns requires 1 minute.
