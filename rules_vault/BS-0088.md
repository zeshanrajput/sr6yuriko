## Sensor Dazzler

A sensor dazzler is a specialized version of the general-purpose jammer (p. 270, _SR6_ ) that targets wireless sensor Matrix traffic without interfering with other signals. While the sensor dazzler is active, the user and anyone within two meters of them benefits from Invisible (Improved) 4 (p. 52, _SR6_ ) against cameras, millimeter-wave sensors, motion sensors, metal detectors, and chem sniffers that are both wireless and within five meters of the user. Sensors roll Rating + Rating against this threshold, and if they fail they cannot perform their usual detection function while the implanted jammer is in range. Anyone monitoring a jammed camera feed will only see static. Drones and cyberware are not blinded by this sort of jamming. It costs a Minor Action to begin or end the jamming, and each combat round the sensor dazzler is active it consumes one charge. A fully charged sensor dazzler has 10 charges and regains 1 charge per hour when attached to a power source.

**Cyberware overdrive:** Increase the benefit to Invisible (Improved) 5 until the beginning of your next turn, and the dazzler consumes 2 charges instead of 1.

**Wireless bonus:** A sensor dazzler must be wireless enabled to function at all.
