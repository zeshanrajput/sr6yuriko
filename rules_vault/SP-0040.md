## **Information Manipulation**

**One of the key components to Aztlan’s ultimate victory against the Amazonians in 2074 was propaganda support from Aztechnology. Clever manipulation of facts before, during, and especially after helped shape public perception of both nation and corporation as heroic entities who defeated a great evil.**

**And now they’re doing it again, but this time to help obfuscate and facilitate the Disian agenda.**

**We’ve already seen it during and following the Week of Death as Aztechnology-owned media outlets have not only whipped the public into a frenzy against the “terrorists,” but simultaneously created a groundswell of support for law enforcement and security forces pursuing them. Additionally, previous testing of the Devices has already caused some noticeable effects in local manaspheres across the globe. Already media outlets have been quick to calm the public. Most of these outlets have been pushing the narrative that these local mana-effects are “concerning, but natural” phenomena and that “emergency disaster assistance will be available if needed.”**

**As predicted, people are concerned but not frightened enough to question what is being fed to them.**
