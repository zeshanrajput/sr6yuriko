## Geneware

## Posted by: KAM

The geneware industry can generally be divided into three broad categories: therapeutics, augmentics, and transgenics, classifications that are based on the enhancement provided.

First off, the use of therapeutic geneware is usually a rich person’s game. The fact that the changes are permanent puts geneware at odds with the corporations that would rather offer up regular treatments. This keeps the price of genetech high, but for those with the means, it’s a simple matter of finding the right treatment. The most desirable of these include leónization and genetic revitalization.
