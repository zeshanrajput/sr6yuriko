## **Security Wand**

**When having a full-body scanner is inconvenient or impossible, there is the handheld scanner that features cyberware, MAD, and olfactory functions. Security wands don’t have the full sensor capacity of the big units, but their relatively low cost and easy use make them a mainstay in the security industry. Oftentimes they are the only security scanner a small establishment such as a nightclub may have, but even the professional companies have these available at security checkpoints. When used in conjunction with a full-body scanner, the wand enables more focused screening on a suspect, allowing an inspector to more accurately locate contraband that a larger scanner may have just missed.**
