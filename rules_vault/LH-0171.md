## Downfall: The InnerGerman Refugee Crisis

From November 2082 to April 2083, there were hundreds of thousands of refugees within the AGS—and after the Collapse of large parts of the RRP, possibly millions—all fleeing persecution, cold, homelessness, and a lack of economic prospects.

Those who believed their metatype would grant them special admission were bitterly disappointed. Elves turned to Pomorya on the Baltic coast, but the elven state closed its borders. Orks and trolls turned to the Troll Republic of the Black Forest and were also turned away. German Catholics suffered the same fate when trying to cross over into the church state of Westphalia. Even Berlin admitted it was full, but it lacked the means to prevent people from entering. The AGS Federal Border Guard of the Bundesgrenzschutz lets everyone pass into Berlin and focuses on keeping the anarchist “plague” contained within the city.
