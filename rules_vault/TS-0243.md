## **Pyrrhic Victory**

**Vector: Injection Speed: Immediate Duration: 10 x 2D6 minutes Power: 22**

**Ef ect: +4 Body, +3 Agility, +3 Reaction, +3 Strength, +3 Intuition, +4 Willpower, +3D6 Initiative, High Pain Tolerance (p. 72, SR6 ), Toughness (p. 73, SR6 )**

**While trying to make better versions of Kamikaze and Jazz for their front-line patrol ofcers, the bofns at OmniStar decided to “improve” K-10 as well. What purpose this drug is supposed to fulfll, and how it got into feld trials, no one seems to know. Pyrrhic Victory is the name the rank-and-fle call this drug … because it truly is. The drug boosts the user to a tremendous level, allowing them to perform feats many street samurai can only dream of, and unlike K-10 and several others like it, the user retains all their mental faculties. No raving berserkers, and in fact quite the opposite; users are calm and focused on the job at hand. When the drug wears of, the toll this has taken means the user dies. They simply sit down, and within roughly a minute they slip away while feeling the satisfaction of a job well done. No magical or medical care can prevent this outcome.**
