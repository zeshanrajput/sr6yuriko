## The Trail of Breadcrumbs

This investigation has no linear path, but a series of clues and evidence that need to be gathered together in order to make any kind of significant change in one direction or another. The evidence and clues are distributed among several different locations and individuals. Descriptions for many of these people and places in Lagos are provided, but the story progression of these encounters is in the hands of the gamemaster. Pay for the contracts is also up to the gamemaster. A general starting point for any party hiring the runners would be 10,000 nuyen per runner, though corps will pay more and local organizations less. There is also the chance that the runners can be bought off by Okoro, as she can easily double any offer made to the team in order to turn the jobs around on her rivals.

As the runners follow this trail, the Watchers (p. 57) should interfere with them, sometimes directly blocking them, other times intimidating people who the runners might want to talk to or stealing evidence the runners recently obtained. They can add action when the plot needs a kick.
