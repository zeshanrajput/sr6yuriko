## Fuel To The Fire

## Main Location: Reinickendorf

## Notable Characters: Saif Alhazred, Sigma

In 2080, Reinickendorf district representative Sofia Nordin sold off the district’s only valuable land to the neighboring corporate district Z-IC Tegel and made off with a senior post at just that corporation, Zeta-ImpChem. This ignited citywide riots, but the hot hatred has since cooled and given way to a sense of dull resignation in the abandoned parts of Reinickendorf. Sigma, through Saif Alhazred, recruits the runners to reignite the dying embers in order to disrupt Z-IC’s business at the time of a takeover pitch of smaller biotech companies in the district, in which Sigma has huge stakes.

**Original Message:** Schmidt wants you to cause a ruckus in the border area between Z-IC Tegel and Reinickendorf and blame it on the anarchists in Reinickendorf. The operational goal is to get Z-IC to “crack down on anarcho-terrorists,” thereby exploding the powder keg that is Reinickendorf. A dirty job, but someone has to do it.
