## Welkin

## (Air, Force 2)

Welkins appear as living soap bubbles, spherical in form as they float and bob around the air. They don’t have features, though the gas within the bubble can change colors, offering insight to its mood. They are attracted to smoke. If it’s not from pollution, they will feed, siphoning the smoke into their spherical belly. Welkins can defend themselves with an eruption of noxious fumes at the threat. They can also quickly inflate themselves to engulf a metahuman and then exhale oxygen from their body in an attempt to suffocate the threat.
