## Plot Hooks

## Kr’u’Ba hunt

The team is hired to travel to the Wastes of Miggon and hunt the dangerous Kr’u’Ba. Their employer is willing to pay a high price for each Kr’u’Ba heart they can bring back. This is a fairly straightforward mission requiring the runners to get to the Wastes of Miggon and go looking for some nasty toxic fire spirits. The most likely place to find Kr’u’Ba is the glass desert, but they can be found nearly anywhere within Miggon. To search for a Kr’u’Ba, make an Astral or Outdoors + Intuition (6, 1 hour) Extended test. There is a chance for an encounter each hour. On a success, the runners locate a Force 6 Kr’u’Ba. If searching the glass desert, the threshold for the Extended test is reduced to 4, but one of the dice must be replaced with a wild die. Each time the wild die rolls a 1, 5, or 6, add 1 to the Force of the Kr’u’Ba that will be found when the test succeeds. Of course, getting to the glass desert requires a long trek through the open wilderness unless the runners are willing to risk a ride on the Death Train.

The employer for this run might require a minimum number of Kr’u’Ba hearts (perhaps one per player), or just be willing to pay a premium price for whatever they manage to bring back.
