## Sympathy

Sympathy is often used as an appeal when you are not going to directly benefit from the proposed transaction. This means that instead of selling you aproduct, the message wants to sell you a good feeling, making you feel that the money or time you contributed led to improvements in someone’s life.

There is a basic appeal here, but also several obstacles. Tapping into this well too often can make it run dry, and dishonesty in your appeal can backfire. Once people get to a point where they feel their donation was not used well, that can reduce their willingness to give for a long time, perhaps the rest of their lives. This means you can occasionally take advantage of people, but don’t expect to be able to do so regularly.

Another obstacle is that appealing to sympathy depends on people valuing the well-being of others. If they don’t, then your appeals go nowhere. As people increasingly see society as a zero-sum battle for resources, and the more they develop an attitude to get what they can and protect it, an appeal to sympathy will generate ever-decreasing responses. That is to say, if you have a long spiel intended to appeal to the sympathy of Lofwyr for his fellow beings, you are better off saving your breath.
