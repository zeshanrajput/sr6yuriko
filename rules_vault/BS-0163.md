## Flametosser

The functional difference between an implanted flametosser and a military-grade flamethrower is the size of the fuel canisters. Both weapons are essentially a liquid sprayer that draws from two separate fuel tanks that are filled with chemicals that explosively combust when mixed. Each external refueling port is color-coded to ensure chemical residues are not improperly mixed during refueling. Flametossers make spray attacks (p. 117, _SR6_ ).
