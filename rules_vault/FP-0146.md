## Hate.

It’s not a word I use lightly. I’ve swum in it for most of my life, from being a human child warned about Those People to Goblinization, to becoming the best ork decker you never met and reading through Matrix fi les or listening through cameras on peoples’ supposedly private conversations. Despite what you’ll see here, it’s not limited to just humanity, either; frag, there have been days where I hate myself—so I get it, I really do.

But I have to stand against it all the same. I owe her that much. So, let’s begin.

And that’s it. That really is all it is. Sure, some will try to paint it differently—they were abused by Those People when younger, oh you know how Those People act, I saw a bunch of Those People up to no good, and so on, and some will attempt to back it up with pseudoscience, but at the end of the day, that’s all it is: Those People look different than me, and I don’t like it.
