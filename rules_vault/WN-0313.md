## Ms. Helena Chomsky

Mrs. Chomsky spent two decades as the personal assistant of Johnny Spinrad. Wherever he went, she was a few steps away, holding his commlink. Chomsky disappeared for a time before Spinrad’s assassination. She reappeared equally suddenly with a new employer as the director of personnel operations for Marsh and Hound Ltd. She is in charge of executive security services for a list of c-suite clients that currently includes Buttercup. Behind the scenes, she is actually serving as a proxy for Miles Lanier, working through a number of cut-out corporations to hide this connection.

Officially, her role in this group is to assess any threat the runners may pose to Buttercup. In truth, she has actually arranged to be there so that she can manage Mr. Lanier’s interests. Specifically, she is present to gather information and make sure Mr. Lanier’s involvement, and that of his associate Jonathan Blake, remain off the books.

Chomsky is neatly dressed with large blue-eyes, and she has a British accent. She does not carry a weapon, nor is she magically active. Use stats for Mr. Johnson (p. 214, _SR6_ ).
