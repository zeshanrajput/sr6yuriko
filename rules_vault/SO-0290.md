## New Clothing Brands

## FaceBlind Trucker’s Cap and Glasses

This cap and glasses contain reflective coatings and light-emitting devices. This doesn’t necessarily make your face invisible but does make it extremely difficult to discern any microexpressions.

Using this interferes with the use of cameras as a means of detecting body and facial movements as part of a Judge Intentions test and negates the use of visual sensors like those included in the Wakabayashi Technologies Emotional Analyzer 7000. In addition, this can be used to partially obscure you from camera feeds, but not fully remove you.
