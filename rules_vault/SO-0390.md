## Suggestive Gesture Example

Haze needs to make an appointment with a Horizon executive so he can plant a bug in their office. He approaches the executive’s secretary and opens with a few compliments, then bats his eyes suggestively, spending 5 Edge to take the Suggestive Gesture action. He scores three net hits, and the secretary gains the Smitten status toward him. This should make it much easier for Haze to get that appointment scheduled.
