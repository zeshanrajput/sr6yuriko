## **I almost died.**

**I was checking out a report on a breeding event and carefully exploring the area for the den. I was so focused on fnding a hole I didn’t even consider that some specimens might try to hide their gestation spot. It doesn’t help that hiding a hole that large isn’t easy either. The frst thing I should have noticed was the smell. It was wretched, but I assumed I was just downwind of a defecation site. I actually moved toward the scent in order to maybe check a sample or get a better track on the female. Turns out they were using a mix of dirt, fecal matter, and partially digested food to create a cover for their hole. I should have seen it, but I may have been tearing a bit too much inside my respirator mask.**
