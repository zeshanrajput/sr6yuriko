## **Disian Devices**

**Disian “technology” is based frmly in enchanting, and many of the devices used by the Dis are purely magical in their function. Other devices combine Sixth World technology with Disian enchanting. It’s currently unknown if this is a standard practice with the Disians or if it’s evidence of the rushed nature of the attempt at harvesting the material metaplane.**

**Following is a selection of both hybrid and pure devices used by Dis and their allies on the material plane. This list represents some of the most common devices encountered, but it is far from exhaustive. Numerous other tools and weapons have been encountered, some varying wildly from the devices described here.**
