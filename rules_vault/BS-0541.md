## Mundane Critter Power Augmentations

The critter’s natural talents and abilities can be enhanced and improved through genetic customization. In order to receive this augmentation, the critter must either already have the critter power or be granted the power through genetic augmentation.

You must specify which critter power receives the augmentation, though multiple instances of it may be used to improve multiple critter powers.

When the critter uses the enhanced critter power, increase its dice pool by the rating of the augmentation. Even magical critter powers can be enhanced in this way.

These augmentations are mundane in nature but may still be used on Awakened specimens—but only for the purpose of enhancing a critter power that does not make use of its Magic attribute.
