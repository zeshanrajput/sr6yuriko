## Perfect Pitch

This audio analytic augmentation is an ear implant that measures pitch and timbre. It is widely used by musicians and sound technicians. Perfect pitch grants a point of Edge for tests involving musical performance.

**Wireless bonus:** The implant can sync with wireless instruments and sound equipment. This reduces the cost for Edge boosts by 1 (minimum of 1) for musical performances.
