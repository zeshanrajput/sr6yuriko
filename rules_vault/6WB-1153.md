## Shock Gloves

Insulated gloves with an integrated system that discharges electric current with any contact after they’ve been activated. The gloves inflict the Zapped status (p. 53) and are good for ten charges before they need to be plugged in and recharged (at a rate of one charge per ten seconds). The charge is activated with a muscle command or via wireless signal, which takes a Minor Action.

**Wireless bonus:** You gain a bonus Minor Action on a turn when you activate a charge. The shock gloves recharge by induction, regaining one charge per half hour of wireless-enabled time.
