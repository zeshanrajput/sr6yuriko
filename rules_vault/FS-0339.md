## Crossfire

## Threshold: 3

The Crossfire maneuver is meant to position friendly forces in such a way that the enemy takes fire from at least two different directions. Before the maneuver is enacted, the team leader must first determine where best to position their people. Best

utilized as an ambush, the Crossfire maneuver can also be used during combat.

( **Notes/Benefits:** Before SUT maneuver test is made, the team leader makes a Perception + Intuition (2) test to determine participant placement. If maneuver succeeds, attackers gain a +2 to AR and defenders have a –3 penalty to DR.)
