## Handheld Pixel Tag Sprayer

Placing pixel tags by hand can be tedious work, so they are most often deployed using a handheld sprayer device that is roughly the size and shape of a mini-welder. Replaceable cartridges are capable of storing up to 100,000 tags and can deploy up to 10,000 tags per Minor Action at a range of up to two meters.
