## Areynn de Catelano

Areynn de Catelano is around to promote Aztechnology’s interests at any cost. She is a thirty-six-year-old female dwarf shark shaman blood magic practitioner, but what else would you expect from the Azzies? She’s as feared as her totem and has very little difficulty making sure any physical evidence is disposed of—unless it needs to be used for more arcane purposes. She knows just how dirty Aztechnology’s practices are and therefore knows just how much material Okoro has to work with. She keeps close tabs on Okoro and those in her inner circle to determine what they know and then makes sure that any potential loose ends and dangerous leads get muffled. Which will probably, at some point during this investigation, include the runners.

De Catelano is patient and a planner, but when it’s time to act, she is swift, deliberate, and efficient, though along the way she sometimes likes to play with her food. She is the hub of a small network of Aztechnology informants who act as her eyes and ears on the streets, keeping tabs on the runners. The informants are just data sources, but de Catelano likes to play occasional games, and they’re not the fun kind. If the runners catch her eye, de Catelano will send a blood spirit after them. It will be quick, dirty, and vicious, but the spirit will focus on one target.

Once any solid leads or real information against Aztechnology ends up in their hands, de Catelano will turn her informants into spies. She will keep an eye on the runners and get their measure with a few testing attacks before setting a trap for them by luring them in with the possibility of information.

De Catelano’s resources are some of the most abundant among the enemies of Okoro the runners will need to navigate. She has ten informants she can shift to keep perpetual eyes on the runners, though if she loses one, the others become more careful and potentially skittish.



De Catelano has the ability to summon blood spirits (see p. 158, _Street Wyrd_ ). These are both competent support and painful tests of the runners’ defenses. She has no moral qualms with creating them and thus gladly summons a new one when one is lost.

De Catelano has access to not only Aztechnology security personnel, but also a budget for contracting runners from an abundant pool of Aztechnology operators. They’re still shadowrunners, but they aren’t squeamish about working for Aztechnology.

Lastly, de Catelano has her own considerable arcane potential, which she uses with the deviousness and ruthlessness one would expect from a blood shaman with the shark mentor. She starts with invisibility magic, gives others orders, and assists with spells, often trying mind-reading magic to get clues and insights. If she hits the streets, she brings a security detail consisting of six grunts (use Weapon Specialist, p. 90, _SR6_ ), two mages (use Combat Mage, p. 82, _SR6_ ), a rigger (use Rigger, p. 86, _SR6_ ), and a decker (use Decker, p. 84, _SR6_ ), and keeps herself in a command position and invisible to make the runners’ lives hell.
