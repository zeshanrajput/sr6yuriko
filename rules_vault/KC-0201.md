## Ebb and Flow of the Undercurrents

The real and entirely serious part of Hong Kong’s tumultuous life is tied with the criminal forces (organized or otherwise) that constantly struggle and compete for the larger pieces of any pies out there. The more powerful White Lotus Society and Red Dragon Triads have the deepest pockets, but they are not the most numerous nor do they represent the full spectrum of Hong Kong’s people. The 14K Triad has survived giving birth to many of the earlier, richer Triads, but they lack the mythology of the White Lotus Society. Yet it remains the most numerous when it comes to sheer metahuman muscle. Smaller groups like Broken-Reed Triad and the Diànzi Rén Society have made their place through provision of uncommon capabilities.

- Diànzi Rén are only just now coming into their own. They have a large number of hackers and technomancers among their members, and they often subcontract their skills out to the other Triads in exchange for recognition and funding. Their incense masters are also probably the youngest on average— barely thirty years old at this time.

Adepts of the ways are numerous here, and they include no fewer than four drakes among their numbers. They provide astral and other Awakened services to several of the larger, richer Triads. It is believed that Lung has extended tutelage by way of the free spirit known as Chí shuí zhí (or “Water Bearer”) to many of the families.

Now, before everyone sits back in their cozy chairs and screams about us not mentioning all the other Triads, don’t panic. We know they are still there, with many of them doing well. The 289s remain in Yau Tsim Mong district, with Cee-Cee still in the chair, though with the strengthening of Broken-Reed Triad and their more skillful Awakened members, she’s been looking for better tutelage for her people. Smoke Circle has diminished some, as the Red Orchid fad proved to be just that, and they are still looking for a revenue source of equal power. Ten Thousand Lions of Kwon Tong remain in control of the Kai Tak Night Market, but both Red Dragon Association and the Black Chrysanthemums are beginning to push into the area and will likely force a power struggle within the next year or so.

Hanging below the Triads in power are the familiar Yakuza Clans as well as a Vory v Zakone outfit. The Vory have made strange bedfellows with several smaller shipping elements that use the ports in the Tai Po district, and they have managed to cement a firm grasp within.

this is piracy. Real, seafaring pirates. Pirates and pirate locations abound along the shores of the South China Sea and have plagued modern commercial shipping, luxury craft, and cruise ships. Many of the present-day Awakened pirates occasionally do business with legit operations along with the usual unsavory types, just to confuse law enforcement. Pirates flying the flags of the Red Tide and Razor Wind fleets generally stay outside HKFEZ patrol waters, except for Sai Kung and Sha Tin districts on the far eastern edge. Those harbors allow pirates to trade quietly, resupply, and repair. Alongside these relative newcomers are some experienced groups like the Black Dolphins and Wuji Crew, both continuing to find significant success. The one group that appears to have slipped beyond the regional waters is the Hell Thunder Crew.
