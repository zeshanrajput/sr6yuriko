## **What magical effects/items are blocked or can be disrupted by passing through a mana barrier?**

Active foci, alchemical preparations, all spells, sustained critter powers, activated adept powers that have a ‘duration’ (such as Attribute Boost), spirits, and dual-natured creatures.
