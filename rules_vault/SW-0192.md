## power index

For purposes of magic, different substances are given a Power Index to delineate their different strength and effects. The Power Index is used as the damage value when the substance is animated, and also shows the resistance the animated substance offers if someone tries to impede is movement. The person trying to impede or alter the animated material’s movement rolls Body + Strength with a threshold of the net hits on the Spellcasting test plus the Power Index of the material.
