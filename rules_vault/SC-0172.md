## Backgrounds

When people worry about what kind of super-augmented person they might come up against in the shadows, their thoughts often first turn to a tank—a dermal-plated troll slowly, clumsily plowing ahead, bullets bouncing off of it while it mows down the opposition. This is a dated vision, like something out of a twentieth-century flatvid. That’s not to say this vision doesn’t happen, but it’s far from the only way to get cybered up and do damage. Cyber ninjas use their augmentations to disappear, move unseen, and then deliver a lethal blow. They are fast, silent, and quite deadly.

Cyber ninjas are made, not born. To really be elite, they need to spend six or even seven figures of nuyen to enhance their physical forms. They can’t, though, assume that the ’ware will do the work

for them. A chromed-up person with little or no physical or mental skill to help use them is someone who has wasted a lot of money. If they pull off the right combination of ’ware and ability, though, they become one of the most lethal people in shadows—all the more dangerous because they seem to unite with the darkness.

Cyber ninjas don’t always knowingly start off knowing what they will become. Some are very intentional about what they intend to be, but others add a piece of ’ware when they have enough nuyen, and they often choose based on what’s available and what they can afford. Some people on that path end up a mishmash of disparate gear, but others get a few things that point them in a very ninja-focused direction, and so they pick up on that and run with it. And they keep adding and adding, because they learn how critical the edge that augmentations give them is.
