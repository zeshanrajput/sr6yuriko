## Elite Airstrike Drone PACK

## (66,400 nuyen)

The Ares Black Sky is kind of like your own private fighter jet. When you need to call in an air strike, this is something that could make that happen. Just keep the drone moving for quick hit-andrun strikes. It’s too expensive and fragile for heavy combat.

**Ares Black Sky** [w/ heavy weapon mount: Onotari Interceptor w/ airburst link, 8 high explosive rockets]
