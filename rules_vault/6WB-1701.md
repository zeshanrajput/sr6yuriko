## Marked by Kassandra

The latest surveillance tech introduced by Berlin’s megacorporations is the Kassandra system, a joint initiative by police director Yilmaz Wojenko (PSiAid), traffic director Aiko Kuizumi (Renraku), and the corporations Müller-Schlüter Infotech (MSI), Messerschmitt-Kawasaki (M-K), and Renraku. For some reason, you have been identified as a potential threat by the system, which now gives finding and following you an elevated priority. Luckily, the system has not yet been fully integrated everywhere in Berlin, mainly because of concerns of the other megacorporations that are not too keen to share access to their own surveillance systems.

- Districts: Chawi, Mitte, Renrakusan, S-K Schönefeld, Z-IC Tegel

- Bonus: 5 Karma

- Game Effect: Anyone with access to either Berlin’s Kassandra or France’s Marianne system gains a +4 dice pool bonus to identify and track

Berlin city datayou via biometrics. Obscuring your face with a mask or using Jynx-style facepaint to spoof face recognition systems will halve this effect.
