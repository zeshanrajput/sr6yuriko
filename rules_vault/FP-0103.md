## **The PCC**

**For decades, the Pueblo Corporate Council has managed the information infrastructure of Denver, including the current iteration of the Denver Matrix. At frst this was for the Council of Denver, with the tech-savvy PCC maintaining Matrix infrastructure in all of the sectors. Ghostwalker allowed that arrangement to stay in place after removing the Council and dissolving the sector structure. It’s believed the PCC was the original major patron of the Denver Data Haven, allowing the Nexus to set up in their sector and connect to the Denver Matrix. In addition, they covertly provided equipment, money, and technical support in exchange for collecting information on the PCC’s competitors. They are still believed to be a primary patron. A suspected PCC operative, Thomas Whitefeather (who uses a white eagle icon and the name CloudMaster on the Nexus), is believed to be the primary liaison between the data haven’s leadership and the spy agencies of the PCC.**
