## “Xiphos” Tactical Gladius

Katanas are so cliché. Not to mention bulky. The discerning street gladiator prefers to keep the Xiphos tucked beneath their trench coat and ready for battle. Designed for both offense and defense, the Xiphos is a thick but lightwieght and razorsharp tool for those who care about the kill more than the flash.
