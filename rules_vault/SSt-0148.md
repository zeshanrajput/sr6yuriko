## **Do: Spread Money Around Occasionally**

**We all have something we’re saving for. New augmentation, new car, better house, bigger gun, more powerful spell formula, etc. It’s tough to accumulate those savings with all the other expenses that are part of everyday life. You watch your nest egg build slowly, and you manage other expenses because you really, really want to reach that dream. That means it won’t be easy to hear this: You need to break into your reserves and spend money on your people sometimes. Buy dinner for your contacts. Help fund a block party in your neighborhood. Chip in when one of your contacts needs a new car. People remember when you’re there in a time of need, and since we all know how scarce cash is, sharing it can be meaningful.**
