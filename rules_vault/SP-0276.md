## **Special Notes**

**This is a very dangerous mission and wouldn’t be a good ft for newer characters. Your players should have at least 100 Karma earned before trying something like this. The most dangerous aspects are the dragon’s hardened armor and natural weapon with a very high DV—only one hit would be enough to take out most runners, and very few could survive two.**

**If the runners accept Seimanth’s counteroffer, they have a much better chance of survival, but gain 3 levels of the Hunted quality and won’t be hired by Aztechnology again—except as a trap with a double cross at the end. If the runners take**

**the gear provided for the job and run without fnishing it, they also gain the Hunted quality.**
