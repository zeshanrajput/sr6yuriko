## Jump Jets

Why not strap a pair of vectored rockets to your critter so that they can leap incredible distances? There may be some critical, pragmatic reasons why not, but let’s just set those aside for now. The jump jets accessory provides a critter with the ability to trigger a rocket-assisted leap that is primarily controlled by a built-in simplified drone pilot system that fires the jets at the appropriate angle, then swivels the jets for a quick reverse thrust to reduce the impact to a manageable amount of force.

**Game Information:** A critter must be equipped with a critterjack and receive special training in order to use the jump jets accessory. This requires a Major Action and an Athletics + Agility (3) test; failure results in a crash that deals 8 Physical damage to the unlucky beast. Success allows the critter to leap up to 20 meters horizontally and 10 meters vertically. If the critter leaps at a target, add +4 DV to their Close Combat attack.
