## 14: no comment

## Hook

The runners get a call from Piper Jane for a meeting at her office at 13:00. It’s strange to have an afternoon meeting, but the meeting isn’t the strangest part. When they arrive, she tells them the job is actually an interview. She offers each member of the team 2,500 nuyen for a short interview.

After the interviews, Piper makes a second job offer. This one is a bit of data digging on her contractor for the interviews. She doesn’t know who is behind the money and she wants to know who’s checking out her favorite team of runners. She offers a 10,000 nuyen lump sum for the investigation. She just wants intel. She’s not looking for the runners to rough anyone up or stir the pot, she just wants the scoop on who’s looking for a scoop on the team.
