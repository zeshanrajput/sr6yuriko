## **New Echo: Project Persona**

## **Prerequisite : Skinlink**

**With this echo, a technomancer can function as a cyberdeck for another individual. While in contact, the individual operates as if they are using a cyberdeck with ratings and attributes equal to those of the technomancer’s living persona. The individual uses their own skills for all Matrix actions they take. The technomancer providing this ability can communicate with the individuals they are so connected with and provide access to their various forms, but they do not perceive conversations or interactions without performing a Snoop test on the persona. All Matrix attacks and combat utilize the technomancer’s attributes, though those actions and attacks that cause biofeedback damage, such as Black IC, or impact the user in a similar fashion (p. 168,** _**Hack & Slash**_ **) will target the individual and not the technomancer providing this ability.**

**If combined with Aura Link (p. 67,** _**Hack & Slash**_ **), this ability can be used on a number of individuals within range, not to exceed the Resonance attribute of the technomancer.**
