## The great drone robbery

This adventure hook is designed for any shadowrun team with a drone rigger and a competent hacker.

A major drone-racing event is scheduled to take place soon, and the PCs have been hired by an opportunistic individual who wants them to steal as many drones as possible. They offer to provide shielded bags, containers, and cases to shield the drones from Matrix detection and tracking, as well as a map of the course. The drone course is set up in an urban location, ideally a barrens such as Redmond or Puyallup in Seattle. The PCs will have a short time to recon the course and prepare any traps or ambushes they wish, but security forces will have surveillance devices of their own. Any interference that is observed by security will be undone and will make the guards more vigilant, which means the guards will gain a point of Edge on any Perception tests against the PCs for the rest of this run).

During the race there will be twenty high-end racing drones. Whether the PCs snag them through hacking, nets, traps, or some other contrivance, their employer will be very pleased if they succeed at stealing at least three drones. If the runners are quick, clever, and stealthy, they might steal a lot more. But once it becomes known that drones are being stolen, the security response will be swift and very strong.

If the PCs steal more than three drones, they may keep one or more of them or cash them in. Each extra drone nets the runners a twenty-five percent bonus payment. If the PCs choose to keep them, they are all of the same model, but they come with 5,000 nuyen worth of mods (no weapon mounts). Or they may gift a drone to any contact with the engineering type and gain +1 Loyalty rating. Multiple drone gifts are cumulative, up to a maximum of three per contact.

The PCs may not cash in more than ten drones in this way. Any excess drones must be kept or fenced through a contact at low rate (ten percent, plus two percent per net hit on an opposed Negotiation + Charisma test) due to how hot the merchandise is.

If the PCs keep and use drones stolen from this race, add +1 to the Heat modifier at the end of any run in which they are utilized. This can be prevented by spending 2,000 nuyen per drone to make the necessary cosmetic modifications to make the chassis unrecognizable.
