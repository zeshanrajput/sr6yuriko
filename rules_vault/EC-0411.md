## Oyabun Kosuke Tomizawa

Leader Kosuke Tomizawa has been building up the syndicate, but it’s been slow. Many within the organization would challenge his leadership to go back to the old way, but that would leave them vulnerable. He also doesn’t want to push the New Way, as things haven’t been as profitable as he would have liked. With the building of Meeker Park, he sees opportunity for prosperity.
