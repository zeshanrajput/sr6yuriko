## Game Information

## Random Cloud Islands

To generate a random cloud island, roll for the technology level, magic level, base environment, and terrain on the Random Metaplane Generator in chapter 11 of this book (p. 177). When rolling on the population table, roll only 1D6 (instead of 2D6), so that the maximum population is “light.”



When rolling on the society table, add +2 to the result (treating any result higher than 12 as a 12). Use the distance table to determine how far the island is from the Spire—unless its position is already established. An adjacent island is five kilometers away, near are up to twenty-five kilometers away, intermediate is up to fifty kilometers away, distant is up to one hundred kilometers away, deep is up to five hundred kilometers away, and very deep is any distance greater than five hundred kilometers from the Spire.
