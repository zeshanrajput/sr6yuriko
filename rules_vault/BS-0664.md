## Cyborgs Vs. Full Cyberlimb Replacement

Cyborg technology was developed to circumvent the prohibitive Essence and nuyen costs of full body cybernetic replacements. The latter—full delta-grade limb, torso, and skull replacement—is also available in the Sixth World and can be achieved for less than a million nuyen, with Essence left over for additional cyber- and bio-implants.
