## **Who can use Edge on teamwork tests?**

Only the leader of a teamwork test can use Edge with one exception. Runners who are assisting may use the 2-Edge Boost to give an ally 1 Edge. During combat, helpers spend an Assist (A) Major Action and can spend the Edge at that point.
