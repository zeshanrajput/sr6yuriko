## They Don’t Deserve This

One of the most common retorts to the Dead People Don’t Wake Up argument, usually combined with lines like “they’re just doing their jobs” and “everyone is someone’s child.” The implication is that since it’s _possible_ to neutralize someone as a threat without killing them (putting aside whether it’s actually _feasible_ to do so), and nearly anything short of killing them can be treated by modern medical science, deciding to go ahead and kill them anyway is cruel and unjustifiable.
