## South America

The few times that the Yakuza have dabbled in drugs, they invariably come from South America. The Yakuza here are more middlemen, trading Korean arms for Peruvian chemicals, and working on low-end real estate trades. They were looking at making bigger moves in 2060 but, well, Comet. They’re in expansion mode now, however, so they should be up to something here, I just don’t know what. Anyone have eyes on the region and can keep us up to date?

**>** No promises.
