## Paranoia Protection

## Submitted by: Lei Kung

A protection racket is, generally speaking, built around the promise of security. Sometimes this means actual protection and a hand-off policy in exchange for a regular rate, like a real security operation. Most of the time, it comes with the implicit threat that it includes protection from the one offering it as well as roughing up the business owner, their family, trashing their property, and so on. Those who refuse get demonstrations regarding the consequences of their resistance. This is a neighborhood-wide operation, sometimes divided between gangs and syndicates on a block-by-block basis, and turf wars are often waged over this kind of income source.
