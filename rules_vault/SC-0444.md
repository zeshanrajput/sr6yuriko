## Taking the Tooth

A sniper of renown in the shadows has made enemies in a magical lodge. Unfortunately for him, they have managed to steal his cherished and deeply personal Hog’s Tooth (see Quality Path: Hunter of Gunmen, below). For all the skills an elite sniper possesses, resisting whatever ritual magic they’re about to cast onto them through the item is not something they are equipped to defend against. He needs allies to track down his enemies’ lodge, disrupt whatever fell ritual they’re performing, and return his priceless totem before it can be used by anyone else against him. Quickly!
