## Social Rating Example One

Jazz has a Charisma of 6 and is wearing an Armanté suit, for a total Social Rating of 16 (he’s a top-notch face after all). He’s sitting down to negotiate payment for a run with Mr. Johnson, who has Charisma 5 and is wearing a rating 6 power suit for a total Social Rating of 11. If the meet was taking place in a corporate environment, Mr. Johnson’s suit would grant him an additional +2 to his Social Rating, bringing it to 13. But this meet is taking place in a back room at Club Penumbra, and so that bonus doesn’t apply. That’s a good thing for Jazz, because his Social Rating is five higher than Mr. Johnson’s. Jazz gets a point of Edge on the opposed Influence (Negotiation) + Charisma test.
