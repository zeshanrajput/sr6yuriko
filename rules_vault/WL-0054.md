## **Gashadokuro**

**Japanese folklore recognizes a type of spirit known as yokai, which could be called ghosts, demons, or simply “monsters” in other parts of the world. Some are beautiful and treacherous, some are animalistic and mischievous, some are terrifying and brutal. What they all have in common is they are all deadly forces of the supernatural world.**

**Weakness: Vulnerability (sunlight, Severe)**

**Weapons:**

**Claws/Bite[Close Combat, DV 3P, (F x 2)/—/—/—/—] Astral Strike [Close Combat, DV 2, AR (F x 2)]**
