## **Personal Weapons**

## **Defiance T-290 “DoorBuster”**

**As a variation of the T-285, the DoorBuster takes a novelty shotgun and turns it into the best breaching tool on the market. With its compact frame and powerful three-barrel capacity, the DoorBuster is specifcally designed to break through barriers. The T-290 takes the three-barrel T-285, shortens the barrels, adds a foregrip, and modifes the choke for maximum power at the expense of range. The T-290 is most effective at Close range and is completely ineffective for anything beyond Near range. For purposes of breaking through barriers, the DoorBuster is considered a specialized weapon (p. 112,** _**SR6**_ **), adding the hits of an Agility test to increase its DV.**
