## **Trauma Response System**

**Silver Shiel d’s Trauma Response System (TRS) is the linchpin that makes the Guardian Angel more than just a collection of armor plates and accessories slapped together to protect a body. The TRS uses the latest in nanotech and an advanced expert program designed to keep an offcer functional … or at least alive until EMS can arrive on the scene. When combined with a biomonitor, the TRS can monitor the user’s biometrics and administer automated medical aid based on programmed thresholds. The TRS rating sets the systems Logic and Medkit rating (p. 119,** _**SR6**_ **). The rating also sets how many applications it holds. Each application can be used for a frst aid test or used to administer the equivalent of a slap patch (p.282,** _**SR6**_ **) with a rating equal to the TRS system if applicable. Reflls are 500¥ each.**
