## Ground Fighter

You’re used to fights ending up as a mass of tangled limbs rolling around on the nearest flat surface. You never suffer penalties for attacking or defending while you have the Prone status (p. 53, _SR6_ ), and the penalty to your Defense tests while restrained is reduced by half (rounded down). **Category:** Grappling
