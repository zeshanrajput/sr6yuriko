## Scorch

This is a particularly nasty form of convergence aimed at deterring the hacker from ever using the Matrix again in any such illicit manner. When the hacker’s OS hits 40, the hacker is link-locked and assaulted with a massive Nonlethal Biofeedback attack, much like Nonlethal Black IC. The hacker is hit with (host rating x 3) boxes of Stun damage, which are resisted as normal with Willpower + Firewall. Should the hacker fall unconscious from this assault, they will be dumped from the host in 3 combat rounds. At the end of this time, the Matrix condition monitor for the deck (if present) is filled, and the hacker will then gain the Scorched negative quality (p. 78, _SR6_ ). This quality may be bought off with Karma as normal for a negative quality.
