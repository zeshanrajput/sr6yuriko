## Game Information

Spending one hour and 100 nuyen grooming oneself (or being groomed by others), grants +1 to the subject’s Social Rating for the rest of the day or until one or more boxes of Physical damage is taken.

Spending two or more hours and 250 nuyen represents a greater investment in appearance and provides +2 to the Social Rating for the rest of the day or until Physical damage is taken.

With a lifestyle category of High or better, nuyen expenditure is not needed to gain the benefits, but a time investment is.

Alternately, spending five minutes to downgrade one’s appearance provides -1 or -2 (player character’s choice) to their Social Rating. Taking damage doesn’t ruin the effect.

Grooming stacks with outfits and fashion accessories.
