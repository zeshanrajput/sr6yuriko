## Finding Faults

The megacorporations spend a lot of resources trying to fill any cracks in the Matrix they discover. Happily, the very need for them to do so often leaves more cracks behind. Crash 2.0, with AIs dueling while the Jormundgandr virus rained down on the Matrix, had repercussions far and wide. It also sent out shockwaves that tore into the fabric of the Matrix—a Matrix that at the time did not utilize Foundation programming. It still worked. It was still lethal; it still prevailed, usually. It still had UV hosts. So why the new Matrix? Why the need for a Foundation-based system, and how was this accomplished? All of that is known now. Danielle de la Mar led a CCMA project that linked the minds of one hundred technomancers and formulated a base—the first Foundation, in fact—as part of the Global Matrix Initiative from which now (nearly) all Matrix activity takes place.
