## July 23, 2083

All Defender operations have concluded, all Defender forces are ordered to stand down but stand by for possible retaliation; none occur. All known Faustians, chimeras not originally from the material plane, and any Disians trapped on the material plane start to die. All manastorms worldwide fade approximately twenty hours later. Date is retroactively declared the end of the Disian Shadow War.
