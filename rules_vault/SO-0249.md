## Authoritative Tone

## **Cost:** 0.5 PP per level

## **Activation:** Major Action

The adept’s voice is infused with uncanny authority and power, making the adept a primal force of leadership so that others naturally want to follow their commands. The adept gains +1 die per level to all Influence (Leadership) tests. Any targets of Leadership or Teamwork where the adept is the acting leader using this power gain the Follower (p. 44) status.
