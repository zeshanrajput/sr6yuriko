## Adepts 

As is the case with spells, most of the adept powers from the core rulebook transfer over, but the Power Point costs for many adept powers have changed. Recalculate your Power Points after you transfer over your powers and make any adjustments that are necessary. For those powers that are not in the Core Rulebook, either negotiate how they should work with your gamemaster, or take a refund of the Power Point value and pick something equivalent.
