## **To-Do List**

## **Games of Skill**

**Holding onto these cards is often as much part of the fun as getting to use them. Members will often create elaborate traps and protections, even going as far as creating a “dungeon,” “haunted” house, or other adventurous endeavor for other Echoes to overcome. Part of the society’s clout is actually participating in these adventures. Hiring a team of runners to help is totally acceptable, but an Echo does not gain their full credibility if they are not present for the acquisition of the card.**

**Runners who they hire are considered special and touched by fate. It’s nuts, but it’s their way of letting you play in their games without needing to look at you like you’re nothing.**

**Those who possess the cards fall into this “touched by fate” category, as do those depicted on cards even if they don’t want anything to do with the tarot. Echoes regularly drag these people into their drama kicking and screaming (sometimes literally).**
