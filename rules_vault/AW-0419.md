## Denizens of Miggon

## Yaggil and Yasmal

The few non-toxic kindred spirits living in Miggon are the Yaggil and Yasmal tribes, who live in reinforced enclaves. The Yaggil enclave is within Hubris, near the train depot, making this the only relatively safe stop within the city for those riding the Death Train. Within the enclave walls, the people of the Yaggil tribe grow stunted crops, tend to their herds of sickly goats, manufacture and repair scavenged weapons and tools, and guard their walls against mutants and toxic spirits. Though they seem to be metahumans, Yaggil are kindred spirits tied to Miggon. They are a resilient community that shares their resources and looks out for each other.
