## Strategies

You are ruthless: play to this. Aside from Aztechnology, no one matters but you. Body count? Unworthy peons unfit even for the sacrificial altar. Collateral damage? Just one more hit against the competition. Your anonymity as a runner is paramount, but there’s plenty of spells and disguise options to help preserve that. Just remember you’re a SINner, and somewhere in AZT files, your face appears. All it takes is one badass decker to stumble onto that info and it’s in the world, and there goes your plausible deniability. Preserve that anonymity as well as you can.
