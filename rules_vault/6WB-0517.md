## Deal with drain

Each spell has a Drain Value, which may be

increased by any adjustments the caster makes. The caster rolls Willpower + their tradition attribute—Logic for hermetic mages, Charisma for shamans—and compares the hits to the Drain Value of the cast spell. If the hits are equal to or greater than the Drain Value, the drain has no effect. If the Drain Value is higher than the hits, the caster experiences Stun damage equal to the difference between hits and Drain Value. If the damage after the resistance test is higher than the caster’s Magic, the damage becomes Physical. Drain damage cannot be healed by magic or medkits.
