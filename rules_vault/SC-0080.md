## Combat

Assassins are killers, but not direct combatants. They kill at oblique angles, by surprise. If a direct engagement is inevitable, try to back up the heavies. Take control of the battlefield as best you can. Reposition so that you contribute in a way that plays to your strengths. You’ve got regeneration, which means you can take more mundane punishment than most, but remember your vulnerabilities, and that magic won’t heal fast, whether it’s the fireball some wizworm tosses your way or the drain of throwing your own. Alternately, use your skills to set the enemy up for an ally to take down. Provoke getting cornered and grappled, and then mist out so your pals can open up on full auto and take out the lot. Never stop moving unless you’re a hundred meters away and watching the fight through a scope. Never leave traces. Be death, get paid.
