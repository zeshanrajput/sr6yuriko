## Explosive

These slugs carry a shaped-charge explosive. Explosive rounds misfire whenever you roll a critical glitch. When this happens, the attacker must resist damage equal to the Damage Value done by the weapon (and don’t forget the modifier for the




explosive rounds). Naturally, the attack misses its intended target, and the weapon firing the bullets is destroyed.
