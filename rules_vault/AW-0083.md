## Absolom: Ardor Trailblazer

Absolom is the default leader of the elemental trailblazers, even if he doesn’t call himself one. Trailblazers make their own road through the world instead of following the path of least resistance. Their wagon trains move through the elemental plane without a destination in mind, trading with other migrants they cross paths with. His wagon is a great iridescent one, as layers of scorch marks have left a story of his travels. Absolom himself is getting on in years as the normal charcoal black appearance has turned mostly grey. Absolom doesn’t turn away from strangers in need. For a story and some work filling the boiler, he offers a meal and a ride to the next destination.
