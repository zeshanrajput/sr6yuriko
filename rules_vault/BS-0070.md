## New 2-Edge Boost

**Oh, that’s replaceable:** It turns out the metal took some of the damage in place of your meat! Heal one box of Physical damage. One cybernetic augmentation per box of damage healed that costs at least 0.2 Essence (before modification for grade) is broken and will not function until repaired.

Repairing damaged cyberware is a Build/Repair test using a Biotech(Cybertechnology) + Logic (6, 1 hour) Extended test. The device cannot be used while it is being repaired, and repairs cannot be performed without a toolkit. A critical glitch on a repair test means you messed up good—the device fails totally and can only be harvested for spare parts.
