## Expanded Cybersenses

While many corporations have moved away from R&D in cybernetics, a few continue to expand the possibilities. Recently, some expanded cyberoptics and cyberaudials have hit the market. Unlike standard eyeware and earware, these expanded systems are unmistakably artificial, requiring a large build up outside the bone to house the extra options. In return, you get a higher grade of implant with an expanded capacity.
