## The Rigger’s Advantage

## (Piloting or Defense)

Riggers feel that the way they control vehicles and drones gives them a certain intangible advantage over others. Use this action to show that’s the case. Add your control rig’s rating to your total hits on this test.

**Cost:** 1 Edge
