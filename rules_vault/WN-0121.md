## Hotspots

This opening adventure invites the runners to check out the city on their own for a day or two as they prepare for the mission. Scan through the Landmarks and Locations (p. 37) section as well as the other adventures to become acquainted with the locations used throughout the book. Feel free to incorporate any of them into this adventure if the runners are looking for a place to hang out or sights to see while they are in town.
