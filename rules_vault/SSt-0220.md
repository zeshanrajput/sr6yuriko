## **Pay Your Rent on Time**

**I know I sound like your mother here, but this is just practical. You in no way want your landlord angry with you—or heaven forbid, looking for you. While you may want your neighbors to like you, you want your landlord to forget about you, and paying them regularly will make that happen.**

**But how do you manage your money in a career with extreme lurches in income? That’s the subject of the next section.**
