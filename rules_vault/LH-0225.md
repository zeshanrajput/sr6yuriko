## Extraplanar: The Battle of Brocéliande

Posted by: Frosty \<06-03-83/0428:39>

I’ve been keeping this quiet as things were rapidly moving toward a conclusion and I needed to maintain operational secrecy. Back in March, the Disians sectioned off a valley near the Seelie Court and started building something there. They began with two portals, one to Dis and another to the material plane, then somehow, they altered the fabric of the metaplane, stopping the normal metaplanar translation of the Courts. This allowed MCT to start installing computer systems, like those used in the Harvesters. While the device being constructed bore little resemblance to a Harvester, I suppose it works on similar principles.

As the facility grew, the Emissary spent less time in the Court—and as a result, the opposition factions had time to build up strong anti-Disian support. As time passed, more members of the Court began to believe the Disians’ intent was, in fact, malevolent. When the Unseelie started engaging in hit-and-run attacks against the Disian facility, many in the Seelie Court provided covert aid. The situation became a stalemate; even with the added support from the Unseelie, they were unable to break Disian security. The Ebon Queen decided to try something the Disians wouldn’t expect: sending an outside force in to destroy the device.

Putting a team in place didn’t take long, and instead of an after-action summary, I’ll just forward the last few reports from Spyder, as he’s a frequent guest poster here anyway.
