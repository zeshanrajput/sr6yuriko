## **Enhanced Senses (Vomeronasal)**

- **Cost:** 6 Karma

Individuals develop additional olfactory organelles that augment their sense of smell to levels that rival a bloodhound’s. You also receive expansions to the amygdala and hypothalamus, empowering you to understand the smells you can now detect. These in turn improve not only your sense of smell but also your ability to discern and react to individuals by scent, including many of the base emotional responses (attraction, fear, etc.) This grants you a bonus point of Edge when making smell-based Perception tests and Social tests when you can smell their target. Strong odors and intense background odors incur the Nauseated status (p. 52, _SR6_ ) if you fail a Body + Willpower (2) test. The threshold for this test can be increased per the GM’s discretion for particularly strong or vile odors.
