## Background (SpinGlobal)

Prior to the merger of SpIn and Global Sandstorm, Gabrielle Al Thani had big plans—plans that included having teams explore the newly discovered Deep Foundation hunting for information she could use to her advantage. What they found was the Null Sect, and under her guidance the Null Sect assisted in the rise of SpIn and the mergers that led to Spin Global. Those same allies also gave her information about an organization able to help her with the next part of her plan, getting rid of Johnny Spinrad. The Null Sect had passed along information about Animus Construction and what it could do. After the implementation of Gabrielle’s plan, the Null Sect continued to monitor data flow around her to make sure the facts surrounding Johnny’s assassination remain undiscovered.

During the fitting for the gown Al Thani commisioned for the Met Gala, designer Becky Wu Ping discovered cellular traces of Al Thani’s Animus treatment. The watching Null Sect noted the entry in her commlink and on Gabrielle’s standing order (made before her memory alterations) ordered Animus Construction Clinical Operations to pick Becky up and have the memory of finding the mark removed.
