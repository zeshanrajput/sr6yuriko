## **Tsunami**

**Once considered a rival to MET2000, Tsunami was similarly ravaged by the Az-Am War. But unlike MET, they haven’t quite recovered. Currently considered more of a security force than a paramilitary one, Tsunami has been surviving on garrison and protection contracts, staying far away from anything resembling warfare.**

**Still, there’ve been rumors that the newly installed company CO, Colonel Maki Anderson , has been quietly recruiting as well as looking for a break-out contract to reestablish her command and become a major player in the mercenary trade once again.**

- **There are also rumors that Colonel Anderson has been secretly approached by representatives from Evo to serve as “field testers” for new weapon systems and equipment. But officially, no contract has been made.**
