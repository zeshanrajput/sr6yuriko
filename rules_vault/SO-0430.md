## False Tell

You know a few tricks to turn a false start into an advantage for later use. **Cost:** 5 Karma

**Game Effect:** When you fail a Con skill test, you can make a Composure (3) test to set up a false tell—some gesture your mark is likely to believe will show them you’re lying. If you succeed, you may incorporate your false tell in your next Con skill test that targets the same character. This prevents them from gaining or spending Edge when opposing your Con skill test.

You can also choose to make a Con skill test just to set up the false tell. If you fail in this case, you may still make a Composure (3) test to improvise a different false tell, but you may not gain or spend Edge on the Composure test.
