## RACING RIGGER

Racing riggers are all about the need for speed. The racing rigger doesn’t go for that spider life, controlling drones and other remote vehicle operations. They don’t (typically) jump into buildings, aerospace platforms, building-sized craft like LTAs or trains (tractor-trailer or locomotive), or anything classified as a ship. The racing rigger jumps into the vehicle and becomes, in that moment, the fastest metahuman alive. Which is all they want to be.

racing rigger

Anything that takes away from speed should be viewed with suspicion. Sure, a mounted gun might be useful— _one._ Any more is worse than a luxury—it’s an impediment. And why would you need more, anyway? With your speed and maneuverability, you should be able to gain positional advantage on anyone and put a shot where it hurts. Or better yet, just outrun them.

Some runners look sideways at the racing rigger. Speed is great, but that’s mainly for getaways. What about the work on the inside? What good are they?

Racing riggers exist to teach you.
