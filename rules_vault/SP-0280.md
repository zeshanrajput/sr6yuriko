## **Who’s Who**

**Arleesh is a great dragon with a vast network of metahuman and spirit agents and operatives who work for her both on Earth and in the metaplanes.**

**For the crew of the airship, use Force 4 kindred spirits. These spirits have the following additional skills: Athletics, Engineering, and Pilot (all at rank 4). They also have one of the following optional powers: Innate Spell (Levitate), Movement.**

**The rogue MCT crew includes several soldiers, two mages, and a rigger. For the soldiers, use the Lone Star SWAT offcer stat block (p. 207,** _**SR6**_ **). For the mages, use the Combat Mage archetype (p. 82,** _**SR6**_ **). For the rigger, use the Rigger archetype (p. 86,** _**SR6**_ **), but replace the vehicles and drones with the large transport airship and six MCT-Nissan Roto-Drones armed with FN-HAR assault rifes [Rife, DV 5P, SA/BF/FA, 3/11/10/6/1, 35(c)]. The rigger’s RCC will be running the following autosofts, all at rating 6: Clearsight, Maneuvering (Roto-Drone), Evasion (Roto-Drone), and Targeting (FN-HAR).**

**For the Disian attack force, use the Dis Seeker, which is crewed by a mixture of chimera with soldiers from the same stats as the rogues. Since this is one place Dis doesn’t care at all about blending in, the gamemaster can use some of the weirder and nastier chimera included in this book.**
