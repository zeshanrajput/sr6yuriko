## **KYP**

**Know your personnel! Design your plans for the team you have, not the team you’d like to**



**==> picture [19 x 765] intentionally omitted \<==**

**have. You might do your legwork and fnd that as long as you hack one tricky subsystem, things will go a lot more smoothly. Only problem is, your only hacker just barely learned what a frewall is, so they’re probably not ready for anything more diffcult than hacking a small-town traffc camera. Right then is when you need to let go of your dream plan and design a plan using the skills of your team. Don’t ask a collection of thugs to impersonate a high-level group of corporate execs. Don’t expect your team of faces to claw their way through a nest of ghouls. You may have to get creative, and some of the solutions you fnd may mean traveling indirect routes to get where you’re going, but you’re more likely to arrive when you play to your strengths.**
