## **Mutation**

**==> picture [228 x 20] intentionally omitted \<==**

**----- Start of picture text -----**<br>
TYPE ACTION RANGE DURATION<br>M Major Self Special<br>**----- End of picture text -----**<br>

**This power gives a critter the ability to rapidly undergo physical change and receive new abilities. Critters undergoing such mutations often show changes in their attributes, more commonly physical in nature. The act of Mutation is a Major Action most often uncontrolled in its nature. Critters can only undergo a single mutation at a time, but it is possible to undergo one mutation after another, with the total number allowed not to exceed the Essence or Magic of the critter in question, whichever is higher. Most mutations are temporary in nature, lasting until the triggering event is over, but they can remain present for longer periods of time.**
