## Taken Together

So what does all this mean? It means that a truly strong market manipulation campaign would take all these methods into account, rather than simply relying on watching numbers. It would understand how to play on people’s sympathies and affection for a particular brand or company, and it might build a narrative portraying a particular corp as an underdog to build sympathy for it. It would know that people will leap to engage with trusted brands given half a chance, so its manipulations will keep that in mind. And it will understand how people panic when the price of an equity plummets, and it will anticipate that reaction, building that into its actions to stay one step ahead of the mob. In short, the ideal market manipulation campaign would understand and use marketing. Though since this is merely theoretical at this point, I cannot say _how_ it would deploy these techniques. I can only share my opinion that it _would_ .
