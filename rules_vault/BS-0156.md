## Tentacle Modification

Most cyberarms have three points of articulation: the shoulder, the elbow, and the wrist. Tentacle cyberarms have hundreds of articulated jointsand have infinite options available in how they move, twist, or undulate. This flexibility is hugely advantageous when grappling with foes. Reduce the cost of an Edge boost by 1 used during a grapple test (p. 111, _SR6_ ) for each tentacle cyberarm the user possesses. This modification cannot reduce an Edge cost below 1. The downside of these flexible limbs is they terminate in very basic grasping hands. The user may not gain or spend Edge on any delicate work using a tentacle limb, such as picking a maglock or firing a gun. Only full cyberarms and additional cyberarms (see p. 43) can be modified into tentacle cyberarms.

**Cyberware overdrive:** A tentacle cyberarm gains an additional option when using overdrive: instead of the cyberlimb’s usual Strength and Agility bonus, a tentacle cyberarm can instead gain 2 dice for a grapple test, one of which must be a wild die.
