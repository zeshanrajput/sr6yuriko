## **Don’t: Be too Cocky**

**Yes, we get it. You’re a big, bad shadowrunner. You can take on anyone and anything. You have no fear. No one does whatever it is you do better than you.**

**The truth of it is, no one is going to hire you because you’re so good at talking about how good you are. Which means the more you try to prop yourself up by talking about how good you are, the more suspicious other people will be of you. So tone it down and let your actions do the talking.**
