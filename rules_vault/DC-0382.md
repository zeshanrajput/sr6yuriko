## Elemental Hardening

This is a group of similar skin modifications that can provide protection from specific energies or specific conditions. Elemental hardening is equivalent to the armor mods (p. 266, _SR6)._ Chemical, cold, fire, and electricity resistance can be installed on passenger vehicles and drones.
