## Sapient Dual-Natured Creatures

Sapient entities feel an instinctual desire to retreat just like non-sapient critter do, but may choose whether they follow this desire. Losing access to their magical abilities puts many of them in a position they are not used to, and they suffer from one or more statuses: Confused, Dazed, Fatigued, Frightened, or Stilled.
