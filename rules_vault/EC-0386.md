## GrubYum!

Instead of dairy cows, Farmer Eldon turned to Witchetty Grubs. Feeding them a mix of soy and microbe-generated sugars makes them grow very fast and plump. GrubYum! sells dried whole or powdered grubs on the black market, and their products are often found at the Crime Mall.

- What Eldon doesn’t tell the customer is there is a mix of Awakened grub as well. It’s mainly as a security protection in case someone steals live grubs. If they grow up, instead of ghost moths, you get wraith moths. Wraith moths are not vegetarian.

- Khan-A-Saur
