## Elemental Denizens

## Ardor

## (Fire, Force 4)

Ardors are humanoid, textured like the trunk of a tree turned into charcoal. Their facial features resemble knots and holes in the tree, colored in burnt shades, accentuated with ash whites and burning reds. Many are dressed in errhine or phlogiston hides cut in frontier/rural style, rather than the more modern/urban look of empyreans. Ardors are the most mellow of the fire elementals, as they travel across the metaplanes.
