## Interview Questions

- What’s your street name, and how did you earn it?

- What’s your role with the team?

- How long have you been in the shadows?

- What pushed you to this rough life?

- Who is your idol or role model in the shadows or in the regular world?

- Where would you like to retire to once you get that big score?

- Why do you still run the shadows?

- What did you want to be when you grew up?

- Do you have a favorite moment in the shadows?

- How does Manhattan compare to other ’plexes you’ve run in?
