## District Status and Concerns

As with any transition of power, Fort Lewis is currently experiencing multiple complications and issues that district leaders are being forced to deal with; in addition to creating and maintaining a brand-new military force. Some of these issues could have far-reaching—and potentially deadly— consequences, not just for Fort Lewis itself, but also for Seattle as a whole.
