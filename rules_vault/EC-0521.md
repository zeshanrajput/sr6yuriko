## Fixer (Muscle) in Spring Lake

Big Jeanie is a Goblinized ork lady in her early fifties who has owned the Hole in the Wall tavern for decades. She knows all the roughnecks and farmhands in East Renton, plus a lot of shadowrunners. If you need some muscle for a job, she is the ork to talk to about setting it up.
