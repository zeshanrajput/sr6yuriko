## Warp Bubble

- **Vector:** Ingestion, Inhalation

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** 1D6 hours

- **Power:** 7

- **Effect:** Astral Chameleon (p. 70, _SR6_ )

This magical compound is created from an Awakened yeast. The yeast needs to be alive when consumed or else the compound is not effective. One of the negative effects of the compound is that users have reported excessive amounts of burping while under its influence. Once ingested, the summoner gains the Astral Chameleon positive quality.
