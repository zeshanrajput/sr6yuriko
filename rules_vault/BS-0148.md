## Prehensile Foot

Also called “monkey feet,” these cybernetic replacements are popular with those who work in orbital microgravity. A user can do anything with a prehensile foot that they can do with a hand, such as fire a weapon, enter a code into a keypad, and so on. They are extremely useful for climbing tests, reducing thresholds by 1 (minimum of 1) for each prehensile foot. On the other hand, they are not as well suited for running as regular cyberfeet, suffering –2 dice for sprinting tests per prehensile foot. A prehensile foot cannot be combined with other foot augmentations.
