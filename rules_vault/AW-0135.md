## Punkah

## Névé

## (Water, Force 4)

Névés are cousins to the firns. Their forms are much softer, like a collection of freshly fallen snow. They’ve accepted the metahuman nickname of snowmen, though there are few that will take advantage of the childhood relationship. The névé’s lighter form allows them to work in the highest structures and craft extravagant details in cloud palaces for brumes.
