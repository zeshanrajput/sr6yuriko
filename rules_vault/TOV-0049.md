## R ada

The Rada nanchon are often seen as good-natured and helpful, treating others with respect. There is a strong sense of community focus that has made the Rada nation one of the most commonly revered. This focus on others is often seen as opposed by Petwo, leading the nation to be considered cool or calm. Rada include lwa from most families including Papa Legba Atibon to Ogou Badagri. Combat spells never do Physical damage, and the most common sets of spells include Illusion and Health.
