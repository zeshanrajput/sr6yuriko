## Clearing Presence

Your presence repels astral impurities and restores the natural balance within astral space.

- **Cost:** 5 Karma

- **Game Effect:** You must have Sense Corruption in order to take this quality. Whenever you are in the presence of a mana ebb caused by pollution, toxic magic, radiation, HMHVV infection, shedim, insect

spirits, or any nasty astral threat or source of corruption, you do not suffer any penalties from the ebb. Additionally, any entities attuned to that ebb may not gain or spend Edge on any actions taken directly against you. If you remain within the ebb for at least one hour, you may spend 1 Karma to clear it permanently within a radius of your Magic attribute times 10 meters. This quality has no effect on natural astral phenomena.
