## Optional Rule: Accelerated Instruction

The Influence (Instruction) + Charisma or Logic test can be used to reduce the time it takes to learn a new knowledge or active skill, and other character abilities. The default rules reduce the time by one day per net hit (p. 96, SR6). As an optional rule, the time it takes to learn is reduced by net hits multiplied by the difference between the skill rank of the instructor and the next rank of the student (net hits x [Instructor’s skill rank – Learner’s next skill rank]) in days. Specializations and expertise do not count towards the differences in rank. For learning knowledge skills, as well as learning an expertise/specialization, the difference in rank is considered to be two for the purposes of the learning test. The minimum time it takes to learn a skill is one day.

For example, Diva has knowledge of Trid Studios that she’s teaching to DangerSensei. She makes an Influence (Instruction) + Charisma test and gets three net hits. This reduces the time it takes for DangerSensei to acquire the knowledge of one month by six days.

Or we can watch Buckshot attempt to teach Firearms to the newest member of the team who has no ranks in the skill. Buckshot makes an Influence (Instruction) + Logic test and gets four net hits. The difference between his skill rank and the newbie is 6, reducing the time it takes to learn the skill by twenty-four days. This reduces the time it takes by four-to-six days, depending on the month.
