## Cyber, Bio, Gene, and Nanoware

Evo is the only corporation to jump back into nanoware and geneware head on. They have happily continued to develop and distribute previous innovations while pushing the fields forward. Their transhuman agenda and willingness to modify ware (both bio and cyber) for every shape and size of meta, already offers an edge in those fields. They are not the top producer, as other larger megas also operate in this field, but they innovate out on the cutting edge. While their market share may not be the highest, they are making bank by selling the best and newest.



- Conspiracy theorists are all over the Matrix talking about the gene and nanoware coming out of Evo. Claims are all over the board. Some say it’s about spreading monad code—the muchfeared brain wipe that the monads have promised not to use. A favorite is global brainwashing with just a tiny bit of code to make everyone favorable to monads. Other theories claim that this tech offers some kind of advantage to monads, or that they can manipulate stuff that has this tech in it.
