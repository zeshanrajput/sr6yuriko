## Project Nemesis (a.k.a. “The Plan”)

Posted by: Point Guard \<11-24-82/1630:00>

- In case anyone is worried about bona fides, Point Guard is XO for the Sixty-First Independent Rangers, a.k.a. Rifleman’s command. I originally asked him to do this primer for the new people (and to update everyone in general), but he said he was “unfortunately unavailable,” and that “she’s better at this drek than I am anyway.” Below is an edited briefing document and primer, and even though some sensitive information has been removed, it still works for our purposes.

- Glitch

Originally conceived in the early 2080s before the true scope of the Disian threat had been discovered, Project Nemesis (known then as “Option 3”), was one of several contingency plans created. As additional information became available regarding the Disian threat, updates and amendments to Option 3 were made. But it wasn’t until after the Week of Death that Option 3 was officially green-lit and redesignated as Project Nemesis.
