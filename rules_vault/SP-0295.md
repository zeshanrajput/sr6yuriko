## **Who’s Who**

**The harvesting device factory is protected by several security riggers, each of whom has control over a network of drones, mostly MCT-Nissan Roto-Drones (p. 302,** _**SR6**_ **) armed with FN-HAR assault rifes, with Steel Lynx combat drones armed with an FN-HAR and a grenade launcher loaded with H-E grenades. These drones are supplied with rating 6 Maneuvering, Evasion, and Targeting autosofts being run on the riggers’ RCC. Two security mages are on-site, with one on duty at all times, providing an astral patrol with two Force 5 spirits of fre. A dozen security guards are also on site, six of them on patrol, and the other six on standby. It’s up to the GM’s discretion whether a pair of chimera are on site as well for an added challenge.**

**The location is defended by a rating 6 Matrix host that monitors all the sensors and controls door locks and other building infrastructure such as the HVAC system and power. Very little information is stored on the host, as its purpose is utilitarian.**
