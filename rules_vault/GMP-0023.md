## **Running The Adventure** 

Gamemastering is more of an art than a science, and every gamemaster does things a bit differently. Use your own style when it comes to preparing and running the adventure and do whatever you feel is best to provide the best Shadowrun game you can for your players. Shadowrun Missions adventures are designed to run in a standard four-hour convention time slot. 

Please keep this in mind when running the adventure. You should leave at least 15–20 minutes at the end of the time slot to complete any necessary paperwork and pass out the players’ Character Journals if they don’t already have one. (Make sure that you have enough copies of the Character Journal to give one copy to each player after running the adventure.) This section offers some guidelines you may find useful in preparing to run any Shadowrun Missions adventure.
