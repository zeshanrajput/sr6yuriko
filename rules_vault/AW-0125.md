## Jökulhlaup

## (Water, Force 10)

Or as most Awakened call it, an ice dragon: huge wings, frosted scales, and breathing a cone of cold. The beast is over ten meters in length from snout to tail. Its wingspan is almost twice that. This great beast occasionally raids a cloud palace or ice city, eating whomever it can. Luckily, sightings of this beast are rare.
