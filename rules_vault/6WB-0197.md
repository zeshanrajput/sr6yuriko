## Social

Shadowrunners with social skills—often called _faces_ , since they function as the face of the group in negotiations and other discussions—are useful everywhere there are people, and even some places where there aren’t—some runners have enough charm to stop a charging rhino in its tracks. Whether they are negotiating for a bigger payday, talking their way past security, or figuring out how to fit in at a posh social gathering, shadowrunners with social skills are extremely handy to have around.

The uses of social skills are set out on p. 94, 96, and 97 of the **Skills** chapter.
