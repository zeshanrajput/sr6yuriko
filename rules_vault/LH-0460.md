## Spell Locks &

## Exclusive Spells

Generally not available for decades, exclusive spells can be sustained without a penalty to Magic, but while sustaining one, no other use of Magic can be attempted.

Treat Spell Locks as sustaining foci built for a specific spell, rather than a category. They allow a spell to be sustained without a penalty to Magic.
