## Game Information

In a corporate or business environment, the power suit adds its rating to a player’s Social Rating. When making an Opposed Negotiation test against corporate types or in a corporate situation, the wearer adds an additional +2 to their Social Rating. When making an Intimidation test in a corporate environment, this suit provides a penalty to the wearers Social Rating equal to its rating.

Power suits have three capacity for fashion accessories.
