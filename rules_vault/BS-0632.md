## Emotional Flatline

All these implants have made you more machine-like. You have become cold and calculating in social situations, so much so that it’s off-putting to others you interact with. This can be an advantage for scaring people.

- **Bonus:** 5 Karma

- **Game Effect:** When attempting any opposed Con or Influence test (except for Intimidation), your opponent always generates a point of Edge for each test due to your inability to appeal to them. When attempting to Intimidate, you generate a point of Edge for your callous disregard for your subject.
