## 3: Swap & Drop

## Hook

The team is contacted by a fixer, Swen Rellik, who has gotten wind of some new talent in town. He and his associates, including a reporter named Piper Jane, may want to use them for more work, but they need to get them on a test run first. Fortunately, a sweet gig has fallen into Swen’s lap.

Swen sets the meet for 18:00 at the Central Park North and Adam Clayton Powell entrance to Central Park. He brings one “assistant” (use Street Samurai, p. 87, _SR6_ ). They’ll run a white noise generator and have a leisurely stroll through the North Woods area of the park, giving them a chance to take in this historic site in New York.

Part of the meet is to see how the runners handle certain situations, so Swen has arranged two encounters. There are two other unplanned encounters as well, since this is Central Park in the Rotten Apple (see sidebar for details).

The job offer is long-winded but incredibly simple. Swen likes to chit-chat, ask interview-like questions, and generally learn all he can as he’s working on the details of the job.
