## Dragon Hide

While many expect this mod to involve scales, that would be a simple cosmetic modification unconnected to the actual upgrade. Dragon hide weaves flame-retardant materials into the skin, which distributes thermal impact, prevents blistering, and reduces overall burning as energy dissipates across the larger surface.
