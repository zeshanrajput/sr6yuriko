## The Great Savanna

## Arbora

Along the North River as it flows through the forest, there is a place where the river widens and branches around a long island before converging upon at the other end. This is where you will find the forest town of Arbora. Arbora is made

Bordered by the Great Forest to the north and the Great Jungle to the south are the open plains of a savanna. These rolling grasslands are broken up by very few groves and small forests, and on a clear day you can see all the way to the volcano on one side, even if you are near the outer edge of theisland. You’ll also be able to see the largest beast spirits that call this place their home, as well as the carnivores that hunt them.
