## **Spell Generator: Game Rules**

**Spell generators have a rating between 1 and 6. This rating represents the maximum number of single spells (efectively spell charges) that the generator can hold. All spells held in a spell generator are considered to be a hermetic tradition and are not limited by the Disian Magic rules. These devices require more units of mana to operate than an equivalent spell directly cast by a Disian. When using a spell generator, the limit to the units that can be applied to a spell is still 2 x (Willpower + Initiate Grade), but each die to boost the Magic rating costs twice as much, requiring 2 units of mana instead of 1. Additionally, Combat spells cast through a spell generator may not be Amped Up or have the area of efect increased. Drain is also much harder to ofset, with 2 units required for each 1 point of reduction, but any remaining drain is still resisted with 2 x Willpower as normal. Spell generators are only usable by native Disians.**
