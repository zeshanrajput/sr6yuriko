## Boxing

Ah, good ol’ fisticuffs. Boxing is the ancient art of punching someone until they fall over, hopefully before they punch you until _you_ fall over. The styles of individual boxers vary greatly—some are highly mobile and throw flurries of punches, while others focus on taking punishment until they can knock their opponent out in a single blow. The chief difference is that unlike other styles of unarmed combat, boxing relies on fists alone.
