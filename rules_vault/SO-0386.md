## Mocking Taunt

## [Con (Acting) or Influence (Intimidation), Major Action] 3 Edge

You ruthlessly and viciously taunt your opponent, making them appear foolish and weak. Make a Con (Acting) + Charisma or Influence (Intimidation) + Charisma test opposed by the target’s Judge Intentions (vs. Con) or Composure (vs. Influence). If you succeed, the target may not spend Edge on any action that does not target you (area effect spells and grenades count as targeting you in this case). Additionally, if you score as many net hits as the target’s Logic or Intuition (whichever is greater), they gain the Suckered status toward you.
