## **Aztechnology and Wuxing**

- **Buttercup**

**Wuxing is closest thing Aztechnology has to an ally in this Disian endeavor, at least on a group or individual level. Certain Wuxing scientists and geomancers have been working closely with their Aztechnology counterparts to perfect and perform the rituals and techniques used in the newest versions of the Harvesters. This collaboration has in turn created a close working relationship between those involved and seems to have translated to corporate politics in general. Still, given the status of Wuxing in this operation, it’s unknown if this will last.**
