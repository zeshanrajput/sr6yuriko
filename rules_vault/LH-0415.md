## Atlantean Foundation

As one of the Disians’ first targets during the Week of Death, the Atlantean Foundation was reportedly wiped out to the person, although that was later proven to be false. Foundation leader Sheila Blatavska managed to survive her assassination attempt and found refuge within the Draco Foundation, which later sheltered any AF members they could find. It turns out the foundation had planned for just this scenario and had emergency protocols in place for their members to go to ground, as well as secure any artifacts in their possession.

- Only difference was they anticipated attacks from metahumans, not extraplanar weirdos.

- eX-Professor

During the war, the AF lent their expertise to help Defender magical researchers in exchange for protection. Although DF and Defender leadership had hoped that Blatavska could be convinced to reveal the locations of several artifacts under foundation control for use against the Disians, she ultimately refused.

Still, the foundation provided valuable insights into Disian magic and even helped out on select operations against Disian forces. But on July 25, every AF member disappeared just ahead of the Un-Awakening. Since then, they haven’t been seen and have likely gone to ground per their emergency protocol.
