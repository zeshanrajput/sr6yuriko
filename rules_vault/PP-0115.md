## Rivals and Rebels

Given Aztechnology’s unapologetic and aggressive nature when it comes to pushing their technological and magical boundaries as well as achieving their goals, it’s easier to say who _isn’t_ out to get them. And even then, it depends on what Aztechnology is up to or who they’ve slotted off lately. Still, most have the good grace to be subtle about it, unlike a select few who make it their mission to be a thorn in Aztechnology’s side.
