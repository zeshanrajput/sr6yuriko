## Expanded Rules For Astral Projection

While in the astral plane, a projecting metahuman creates an astral form, or astral body, that is derived from their mental attributes. This body typically manifests as a reflection of the traveler’s real body. Initiates with masking can choose to make their astral form appear as nearly anything, as long as it’s within 50 to 150 percent of the size of their physical body—even with masking, a projecting mage still can’t appear as a dual-natured mosquito or a great dragon. Once in the astral plane, physical attributes of the body no longer matter, and the character’s mental attributes become all-important. To see how mental stats are used in tests on the astral plane, refer to the chart on p. 161, _SR6_ . For any tests that require the equivalent of a physical stat, outside of the ones listed, use the following conversions to determine the attributes of an astral form:

**Astral Body** = Willpower **Astral Agility** = Logic **Astral Reaction** = Intuition **Astral Strength** = Charisma

Any projecting metahuman is free to wander the astral plane for a limited time, but they remain connected to the physical form with an indestructible and nearly undetectable silver cord. Perceiving the silver cord is the same test as a traveler locating their body after it’s been moved (p. 160, _SR6_ ) as they are basically following the silver cord back. Astral projection creates one living body in two locations, not two different bodies, so any damage to the astral form is instantly reflected in the physical form and vice versa.
