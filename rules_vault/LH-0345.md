## Yamatestu Tsurugi

With a long history of weapons manufacturing, Yamatestu Naval Industries is marketing the Tsurugi brand of vibroblades, or myakuken, as the premier option for this type of weapon and is becoming the emerging market leader as a result. The lineup includes the very popular Hagami (vibroblade), the Kengami (vibrosword), and the Daikengami (vibro-katana).

Yamatestu has also just released the Neko-tegami, a heavy glove with retractable claw-like vibroblades at each fingertip. Advertised as vibro-claws, these are quite popular and typically purchased in pairs.
