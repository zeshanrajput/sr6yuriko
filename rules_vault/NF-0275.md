## Wandervogel

The largest group of those dedicated to the truth, of which I am a proud member, is _Wandervogel_ . I don’t know who came up with the name, but roughly it means “wandering bird.” The moniker is apt, as we are a loosely affiliated group of independents who rove about, gathering our own stories, but sharing information and resources when we need it. Although now bearing a German name, the origin of the group goes all the way back to the west coast of North America, in the megaplex of Seattle.
