## **Special Department for Matrix Security (SDMS)**

**In the binary world of the Matrix, Sternschutz brings the SDMS to the table. The department comprises experts in network and Matrix security, who handle all related tasks. This covers security measures against hackers, as well as preventing virtual vandalism. To fulfll their duties, they work closely with local Matrix corporations, like Ætherlink (S-K) or SecureNet (FBA).**

**The Special Department for Analytics and Forensics (SDAF) handles the medical and forensic examinations of Sternschutz. This department is in development, so most tasks are outsourced to forensic service providers, like BuMoNa Forensics. For most civilians, the standard services are the police forces they will encounter, and that’s it. They are the ones doing the boring everyday tasks within a ’plex, solving everyday crimes. But for us, there are more units to monitor. These are the specialized people who can make our lives a literal hell if we need to deal with them.**
