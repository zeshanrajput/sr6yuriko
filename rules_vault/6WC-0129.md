## Event: You got away with a lucrative theft

You got a tip that something very valuable would be vulnerable to theft, and you took advantage of that tip. Perhaps this was your first crime, but even if it wasn’t, this represents an escalation in your criminal career. They say crime doesn’t pay, buy you know that’s a lie.



- Resources: +75,000 nuyen

- +1 to any attribute or special attribute

- Contact Points: 4

Acquire a negative quality that represents a complication caused by the crime you committed, such as Hunted (p. 137).
