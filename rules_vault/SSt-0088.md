## **Anti-Technomancer Countermeasures**

**When it comes to threats to your drones, nothing out there is scarier than a technomancer. Sure, deckers can mess up your day, too, but in my experience they have nowhere near the capacity to frag up your drones that technos do. Deckers exploit or bend the rules of the Matrix, while technomancers just break them left and right. Between their complex forms and sprites, they can take over your drones and turn them against you without you even knowing it, or brick them before you have much time to react. So what can be done about this?**

**Your frst line of defense is a solid frewall. Get the RCC with the best frewall you can afford—I know, more data processing means being able to run more autosofts, but trust me on this. Next, make sure to run the encryption program, which enhances your frewall. If you can afford an M-TOC, you can use it to enhance your frewall even more, which for my part makes it worth the expense even if I never used any of its other features.**
