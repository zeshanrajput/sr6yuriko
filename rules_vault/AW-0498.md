## Metaplanar Translation

Getting to a metaplane by any of the various methods listed above is the first step; then you need to worry about what you do once you’re there. Astrally projecting into the metaplanes grants the Materialization power and creates a metaplanar form with material drawn from the metaplane itself. Methods that operate by using physical transference carry the actual physical body there and then alter it to conform to the reality paradigm of the metaplane. Both methods use the phenomenon of metaplanar translation to allow a non-native to survive in a perhaps hostile environment and even blend in with the locals.
