## Bar 3: Claddagh

**Theme:** The host is a massive Claddagh ring, matching the bar’s name, with seating along the interior edge. The staff and IC are all covered in AR layovers to make them as “authentically” Irish as possible.

**IC:** Patrol, Scramble

**Notes:** This is mostly just a gossip and dating spot for the local cops and badge bunnies. The real bar hosts the dates, and the virtual bar is usually set up as a pre-date chat or maybe a spot to hang out when off shift (or on) and just looking to catch up.

Once the PCs have the footage, the runners can start pulling identities and looking for interesting parties via some legwork. Matrix searches using the images will result in some info, and so will asking the right contacts, given the right amount of time.

Contacts with government, corporate, or security corp connections can take an image and do a search for it, though it may take a little more time and a decent bribe to coax the results out of them.

Everyone knows her as Tickets. She’s a well-known car thief in Manhattan who always leaves a pair of tickets to a local sporting event at the site of every theft. Street types know her because she can get into just about any car on the continent and has no problem driving them like she stole them if need be. She was picked up tonight for getting into a bar brawl at Claddagh, where she knocked out an officer’s cybereye with a tire iron in the parking lot when she found out he was two-timing her. The street connections know she runs a body shop called Whiz Wheels just outside the east edge of The Pit.
