## social disposition

Use this rule if you want to provide the character the ability to sway an NPC before attempting an Influence or Con test.

An optional attack in Social Combat or as an Influence Simple test can be done in an attempt to change their opinion instead of doing any sort of attack against their Social Condition Monitor. Success on the attack improves the NPC’s disposition by one category.
