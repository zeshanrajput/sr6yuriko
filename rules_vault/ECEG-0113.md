## **Former Zombie**

**A** t an ear **li** er po **i** nt **i** n your **lif** e, a **V** o **d** ou practitioner did something to you that made you permanently receptive to possession spirits. Your body is a natural home for spirits, and they will latch on and not give up easily.

- **Cost:** 3 Karma

- **Game Effect:** When you take this quality, you must select a contact who is your black-market pipeline. This contact gains +2 to their effective Connection Rating when attempting to acquire licensed or illegal gear.

- **Bonus:** 6 Karma

- **Game Effect:** You are permanently a prepared vessel, which grants possession spirits a point of Edge on possession attempts. In addition, the spirit gains an Edge to resist any banishing attempts to remove it from you.
