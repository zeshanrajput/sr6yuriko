## Social Rating

Social Rating works much the same way that Attack Rating and Defense Rating do on an attack test. A Social Rating is determined by the Charisma attribute plus any modifiers from outfit, gear, qualities, and other miscellaneous sources. On most Opposed Social skill tests, you’ll compare the Social Ratings of each side. If one side has a Social Rating that is four (or more) greater than their opponent’s Social Rating, that side gets a point of Edge.
