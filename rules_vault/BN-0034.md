## Ne w Positive Quality: Shifter

The Shifter quality is more complex than typical qualities. It is one quality with many options. The intent is to allow players to create a custom animal form. A player selects one, and only one, sub-category from furry, feathery, fi n, or scaly. The listed minimum cost is the starting cost. You may then select from the positive or negative add-ons. The positive addons increase the total cost of the shifter quality, and the negative add-ons decrease the total cost—but not below the minimum cost listed. A player may not use other qualities to modify the cost of the Shifter quality. Shifters may take other qualities as normal during Step 3.

**Natural populations of shifters are noted in the individual descriptions and are by no** means complete. Given the global population movement of shifters, they can be found anywhere—including zoos, private exotic animal collections, traffi ckers, or just simply shifter families moving around. Shifters’ metahuman forms will look like the indigenous people found near the naturally occurring shifter populations. Almost half the known shifter population has an Evo SIN, and these populations are typically integrated with metahumans. While there are certainly small informal groups of shifters inside Evo, large shifter-dominated populations only exist outside the corporate enclaves and housing.
