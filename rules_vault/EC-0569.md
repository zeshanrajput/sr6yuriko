## > **Sea-Tac Mall**

At five levels, this is the largest shopping complex in Tacoma, specializing in Salish imports, commerce, tourism, and cuisine. Hits by supposedly patriotic wannabe terrorists and thrill gangs over the years have led to ever-escalating security. Ironically, this makes it a decent spot for meeting Johnsons.
