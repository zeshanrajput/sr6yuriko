## Hotspots

## Quik-E-Nap

This string of coffin hotels is famous for its noname-asked, certified-credsticks-only policy. They are, of course, quite popular with criminals or those hiding something from the world. Slot a certified credstick, pay upfront, and a keycard slides out with a tower and pod number. Walk over to the tower (there are five) and slot your card. A few moments later, a fresh clean pod arrives. Climb in, and it takes you up to your slot for the duration. All your basic needs are handled by the pod. Rates are very reasonable. It’s only 10 nuyen per hour for a basic pod or 15 nuyen per hour for a troll-size unit (which is still a bit cramped).
