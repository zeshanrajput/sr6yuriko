## Strips

## Spike Strip

The most common type of strip consists of sharp, penetrating spikes that damage the tires of any vehicle that drives over it. The vehicle’s tires immediately blow out unless they use run-flat technology (see run flat tires below), forcing the driver to make an immediate Handling test to avoid crashing. Even if the crash is avoided, flat tires increase the Handling rating of a ground vehicle by 3.
