## Acolyte 3-7-2

## (Null Sect Xenosapient EI)

Acolyte 3-7-2 is among the lowest-ranking members of the Hierophant caste and is assigned to assist Prelate 3-7. The Null is very young and inexperienced, and it has almost no knowledge of anything outside of the Null Sect homeland. It is fanatical in its belief of the Hierophants’ dogma and the propaganda of the Disseminators. This will be evident in all its interactions with the players. Its attitude will be very arrogant and dismissive, making clear that it considers the runners to be inferior to the Nulls in every way. A technomancer, AI—or worse, another EI—in the group will be subject to outright hostility from it.
