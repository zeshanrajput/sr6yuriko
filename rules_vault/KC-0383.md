## Kinnick (human, male, 23, brown hair, brown eyes, muscular build)

Life as a CalFree bodyguard was easy until he started getting mistaken for the simstars and their egos just couldn’t handle it. It was even worse when their girlfriends made the same mistake, and Kinnick found himself blackballed from the work. He tried to just move to another sprawl and start up a new career, but the global Matrix kept his reputation right behind him so he ditched his former identity and joined the shadows. When he found this team, he knew he was home. He’s a handsome con artist and has funneled just about every nuyen he’s ever made back into his body for the best bioware he could afford. He has made sure to avoid marring his perfect visage and therefore can still run cons without being easily recognized by racks of metal. In fact, to most people he looks like just another pretty-boy meathead, and his slight bit of cyber-psychosis just adds to it.

In the initial surveillance, he will be working as close to the characters as he can, recording them via the camera on his glasses. As long as the setting works, he’ll be out in public. If it doesn’t, he will try to stay hidden in alleys and foyers.

If a fight breaks out, he hangs back behind his team and covers their departure before retreating. He makes an effort to deter and slow the pursuing characters rather than injure them.
