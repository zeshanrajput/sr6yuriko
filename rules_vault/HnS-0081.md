## Outsider

You’re the fastest hacker on the planet, and there isn’t an IC program out there that can catch you in the act. You can slice through a network’s defense so fast that you can hack your way in and out in a single turn. With a single net hit, you gain User access; with four additional net hits, you gain Admin access. With each additional net hit beyond that, you can manipulate one device or file. You immediately exit the network after executing this action.
