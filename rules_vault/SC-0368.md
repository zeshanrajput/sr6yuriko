## Contacts

For the face fatale, contacts are weapons. For each identity they slip into, they need a data trail to back it up. To latch on to a new target, they need a friend of a friend (possibly of a friend) to vouch for them and get them an introduction. Perhaps most importantly, they need a wealth of information on the target, likes and dislikes, their past and what they want from their future. The face fatale is out to shape themselves in the image they desire, not what they are. There’s also some muscle, in case the drek hits the fan (or even a fake attack that will set the target up as a hero, saving the face fatale for a nice case of Florence Nightingale syndrome. Luckily, with a high level of Charisma, the face fatale will have a large selection of contacts. A wide selection is important, but at least a few should be highly loyal. The face fatale shouldn’t be afraid of a little redundancy—they’ll need a wide web to dig up details on their marks. Those with a large social reach should be prioritized over specialized experts—a bartender will help the Face Fatale more than, say, an enchanter.
