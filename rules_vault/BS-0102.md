## Spider Eyes

Spider eyes walk the evolutionary path forged by animals that employ primitive eyes in clusters. Spider eyes are simple sensors that individually have poor perception for detail or color, but when deployed in number they allow for excellent motion detection. Spider eyes deploy clusters of these basic eyes around the head and neck. They do not replace the user’s primary sense of vision; they instead augment it without distraction. Users with spider eyes reduce the threshold for Surprise tests to 2. If they are not wearing a helmet or other headgear, they also reduce the cost for Edge boosts on Surprise tests by 1, to a minimum cost of 1. Spider eyes do not consume cybereye capacity, but they can be placed in cyberskulls.

**Cyberware overdrive:** Spider eyes grant +2 dice, one of which must be a wild die, on a Defense test.

**Wireless bonus:** The user gains Edge on visual perception test.
