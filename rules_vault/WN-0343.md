## Hook

Cat sends out a short message to the team: “Meet me in one hour, Clinton Castle, Battery City.” She doesn’t provide any more details over the commlink and will not answer any direct calls. After arriving at Battery Park, Cat can be seen just outside the walls of the old fort with an average-looking human male in a cheap business suit. Everything about this person shouts corporate wageslave, but Cat’s body language makes you think she’s uncomfortable being near him. A quick astral look will reveal the truth—this is an anthrodrone with high-grade realistic features that are actually vat-grown human tissue. The question then becomes, who (or what) is rigging it?

The team gets a message in all of their commlinks as they approach. “Greetings, our designation is Explorer Primary 7, but you should refer to us as Mr. Seven. Your associate Cat has accepted our offer of information exchange, and in that spirit we have shared intelligence pointing to the location of a portal to the metaplane Dis. In order to further narrow the search area, we require a thorough investigation of the Aztechnology facility located just north of this green space. Specifically, we require detailed maps of the lower levels and transmission taps placed in the facility’s off-Matrix host. We leave the customary negotiations to Cat.”

Once the team reaches the place where Cat and Mr. Seven are standing, she gives her companion a sideways look and makes the team an offer of 5,000 nuyen each (negotiation raises the amount by 100 per net hit, maximum 6,000 apiece). Once a final number is agreed to, Mr. Seven hands over a small backpack containing four Horizon MapMaster drones and six devices that appear to be combination commlinks and data taps.
