## **Astral Explorers**

**These individuals have a magic rating of at least one, the ability to astrally perceive, to astrally project, and the ability to train in the Astral skill. Explorers follow all the normal rules for astral perception and projection and have no other magical abilities beyond these two. Astral explorers may bond with a weapon focus and use astral combat in the same way as an adept with the power of astral perception or as a projecting mage.**
