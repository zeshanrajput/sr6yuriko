## Signature

## Maneuver Upgrade

Your signature maneuver is so well-practiced that it comes easily to you.



**Cost:** 6 Karma

**Game Effect:** You must have the Signature Maneuver quality (p. 170, _Double Clutch_ ) to take this upgrade quality. If you have more than one signature maneuver, choose which maneuver this quality applies to. You may take this quality multiple times, selecting a different signature maneuver each time.

You can perform your signature maneuver even if you have a condition or circumstance that would otherwise prevent you from being able to spend Edge.

When you perform your signature maneuver, gain a point of Edge that must be spent on that test, or it’s lost. You may gain and use this Edge even when you’ve already gained the maximum amount of Edge, or when something would specifically prevent you from gaining Edge.
