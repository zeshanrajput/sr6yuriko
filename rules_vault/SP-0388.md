## **Harvester**

**The gigantic devices known as Harvesters appear to be the tools Disians use to siphon mana from other metaplanes. Their creation and startup are the end goal of the Disian plan for any targeted metaplane. It’s currently unknown how many devices will need to be constructed, where they will be located, or how long they will take to drain the**

**Sixth World of its manasphere. All the devices currently under construction are a hybrid technology using a mix of Disian enchanting and modern machined material. The hybrid devices incorporate powerful framework hosts using cutting-edge optical stack mainframe modules, apparently there to speed up the mana transfer. Like other powerful examples of Disian technology, these constructs are powered by life Essence, having ports for dozens of stasis tubes around their exteriors. Due to the rushed nature of this harvest, the Disians are leaning heavily on MCT, using a version of the Denver devices that Dis helped them refne (after the failure in Denver), to create a hybrid form of Harvester blending Disian enchanting with MCT computing power, producing the fastest effective result.**

**Note: These devices are the primary plot driver and will be the method the Dis will use to drain the mana of the Sixth World. As such, they are there to move the story forward and do not have—or need—any specifc rules.**
