## Closer Contacts

The rigger is developing a deeper bond with their racing friends.

- **Karma Cost:** 6 points

- **Game Effect:** Increase the Connection and Loyalty of the rigger and mechanic contacts from The Society of Speed by 1 apiece. Additionally, when the PC and these contacts use the Give ally 1 Edge (p. 46, SR6) Edge Boost, once per encounter it costs only 1 Edge instead of the normal 2. This comes with responsibility, though; for every month of in-game time that passes, the PC owes 1 Favor Point to each contact.

The character takes the next step in the quality path when they have paid off a total of 20 Favor Points between the two contacts.
