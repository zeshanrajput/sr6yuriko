## Sony Plaza

Home to the Sony Corporation, the Sony Plaza is a collection of three buildings—two eighty-story towers flanking a historical, thirty-seven-story tower in a formation that puts the historical building at the apex. The shorter building is the original New York offices of the Sony Corporation. Over the years, the company continued to remodel the location, adding buildings to support the company’s expansion, as well as a centerpiece Japanese garden that is home to the city’s yearly Matsuri festival.
