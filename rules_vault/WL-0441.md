## **Euphoria**

**==> picture [228 x 20] intentionally omitted \<==**

**----- Start of picture text -----**<br>
TYPE ACTION RANGE DURATION<br>P Minor Touch Sustained<br>**----- End of picture text -----**<br>

**Some creatures can create a feeling of deep bliss or feelings of well-being. In most cases, this is a tool used to help them keep their victim still for an extended period of time while they feed upon them.**

**The creature can as a Minor Action make a Charisma + Magic vs Willpower + Logic Opposed test to leave the target in a state of euphoric bliss. If the attacker is successful, the target gains the Immobilized status and takes a dice pool penalty equal to net hits. The victim can attempt to break free using a Major Action on their turn by making a Willpower + Logic (net hits) test. If the victim is successful, they have broken free of the infuence of the Euphoria power.**

**Even after shaking the effect of Euphoria, the victim must make an Addiction test against Power (Magic of the creature).**
