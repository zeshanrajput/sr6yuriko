## Bioware Augmentation

Most bioware augmentations can be redesigned for augmenting critters, with a lesser impact on Essence than cybernetics. The only reason this isn’t more common than cybernetic augmentation is that it’s more expensive—in terms of nuyen, at least. Most people and megacorps are less willing to invest a large amount of money on augmenting a critter, and they prefer to utilize more cost-effective alternatives, even when those alternatives have a more significant negative impact on the critter. Muscle replacement has a much greater impact on Essence than its bioware equivalent, but the nuyen cost is roughly a third—and when it comes to megacorps, nuyen is what counts the most.
