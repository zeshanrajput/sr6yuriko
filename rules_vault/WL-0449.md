**Some creatures have the ability to pass physically through solid earth without the need to slow**



**down. A creature with the Phase power is able to use their full movement while going through natural earth. This does not allow them to see, and they must rely on other senses to be able to perceive where they are. In addition, they cannot move through constructed materials such as brick, concrete, metal, or plastic.**
