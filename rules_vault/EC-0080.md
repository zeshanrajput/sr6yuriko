## Bowman Metal Works

Despite being a fraction of Federated Boeing’s size, Bowman’s produces more pollution than all of the larger corp’s plants combined. It’s the largest employer of tuskers in the region, providing high pay due to the risky working conditions. Turnover’s high, as even orks can only take so much toxicity before they have to step down, but the good pay means that openings are always filled. They’ve been hit with fines galore over the decades, but somehow, they power through and keep pumping the air full of smoke and the river full of runoff. Not sure how that works, but it keeps churning all the same.
