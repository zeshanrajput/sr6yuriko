## **Brandan Kean (4) [2]**

Brandan is well known on the BTL dealing circuit. Despite his exceptionally white ancestry, his people call him “the Egyptian.” He got picked up on a random sting of a BTL den and played it cool and quiet but was extremely happy when the hack happened. He’s currently down in Terminal taking the night off from illegal activity.

This last video file would be especially interesting if the team had run through mission 8, **Get Out Of Jail Free** (p. 60) and went back for Cat’s legwork information. Just as everyone else is running from the Precinct, the file shows a black Ares Roadmaster driving toward it at speed. It drives past the bar a minute later with a second Roadmaster right behind it. Both stop in front of the Iron Horse, and a large black-armored ork steps out of the second van. From the build and armor, he appears to match one of the runners seen in the vehicle compound video file. He removes his helmet and throws it in the van before it drives off. He walks into the XXXotica club directly across the street from the Iron Horse. The pics recovered are clear enough to get a good match.
