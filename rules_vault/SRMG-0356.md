## **Does the Bounty (6WC, p. 136) quality work the same as Stolen Goods?**

For _SRM_ , the Bounty quality operates similar to Stolen Goods where the character starts a mission with 2 Physical Damage and 4 Stun Damage whenever their Heat exceeds 2, in addition to starting the run with 0 Edge. This damage was already healed, and so the only way to heal this is with natural rest.
