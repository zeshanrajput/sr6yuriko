## The Villages of Lower Manhattan

- **Council Member:** Nicolas Stanford

- **Security Rating:** A to C

- **Typical Host Rating:** 3/2/2

- **Primary Corporate Funding:** Various

Contrary to what Horizon’s PR machine wants you to believe, the village isn’t one place; rather, it’s three separate areas stretched across lower Manhattan below 14[th] Street. The villages are where the city starts to break away from its predictable gridblock structure. Compared to the rest of the city, below 14[th] is a maze of angled streets and bisecting avenues that don’t often extend the length of the island. Houston represents a dividing line for several of these neighborhoods, and those who live below the line view the world very differently than those who live above it.
