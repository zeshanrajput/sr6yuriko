## Slow Travel

Lagos is not a well-maintained city. After so many lives were lost to VITAS, the infrastructure spent most of the century decaying. Between streets ruined by weather and water, buildings crumbling due to lack of maintenance, and scads of locals limited to moving on foot, bike, or inefficient motorized transports, everyone moves around the city in massive clumps of metahumanity during the busiest parts of the day—between 0600 and 1800. From 1800 to 2200 (0200 on weekends) the traffic is a little lighter but tends to have more troublemakers. The early morning hours tend to be thinner traffic, meaning the city’s crumbling nature is the only thing slowing the runners down. All of these factors,

plus the limited access to faster motorized vehicles, will slow travel. This also opens players up to more situations on the streets, including ambushes from friends of anyone they may have been violent toward, attacks or surveillance by drones that aren’t hindered by traffic, and more difficulty evading any trouble on their tail.
