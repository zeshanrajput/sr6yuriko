## Game Information

A uniform provides its rating as a bonus to Social Rating, but only when the wearer is behaving within the proper context. Wearing an appropriate uniform provides a point of Edge on Con or Stealth tests to blend in or play a specific role. Wearing a uniform outside of this context prevents the wearer from gaining or spending Edge on social or Stealth tests.

Uniforms have one capacity for fashion accessories.
