## Special Services

Special services function just like a contact, but with Connection rating equal to the LP cost and Loyalty rating of 1. You must specify the type of service (armorer, mechanic, deckmeister, fence, etc.). Per the gamemaster’s discretion, a service can be converted into a contact through roleplaying and spending Karma and/or nuyen.

**Cost: 1 or more LP**
