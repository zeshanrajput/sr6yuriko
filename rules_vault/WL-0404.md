## **Edge Actions**

**In most cases, you may not use Edge boosts or actions on behalf of your critters (the Critter Bond quality path is a notable exception to this). If your critters gain a point of Edge as part of an action, they will use it immediately to re-roll a failed die on their test.**

**The Edge actions in this section apply when in-**

**teracting with critters. The Edge must be spent by the character using the action—not a critter.**
