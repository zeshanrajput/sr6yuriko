## **Creating a** Shifter

## You’re a Shifter, Hairy!

Creating a shifter is primarily done by selecting the Shifter positive quality (p. 12) during step three of Priority or Sum-to-Ten character creation, which counts as one of your character’s six starting qualities. The Shifter quality can not be purchased after character creation.

Shifters also have additional restrictions. If a shifter’s Magic attribute drops to 0, they immediately shift to animal form. They lose the Dual Natured, Shift, and Supernatural Recovery powers, as well as any benefi ts of Animalism. The maximum level of Animalism is 2 at character creation. Shifters may not be technomancers or have a Resonance attribute. Metasapients may not take the Shifter quality. There are no confi rmed cases of a shifter with HMHVV, nor any shifters that have expressed any SURGE qualities.
