## They’re Too Dangerous to be Left Alive/Capable

A combination of Dead People Don’t Wake Up and They Deserve to Suffer/Die For What They’ve Done, on the grounds that destroying someone’s ability to carry out certain acts (including by killing them, since death tends to severely limit one’s options) is the only way to prevent them from doing something in the future that’s morally wrong or dangerous to the person making the argument.
