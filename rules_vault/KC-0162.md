## Encounters

Embiro Gontabanya is the leader of the local branch of TerraFirst! and has been for about six years. Before that, he spent half a decade doing work around the globe after growing up in Lagos under Okoro’s father’s tutelage. He knows Okoro well and is a bit jealous, so he keeps information that could be damaging to Okoro. He’s usually found at the bank.

This branch of TerraFirst! believers (use Lone Star Patrolman, p. 206, _SR6_ ; remove jazz inhalers and armor vest) consists of about fifty people. Only fifteen or so are active, with the rest doing intelligence gathering, data analysis, or just sitting around to talk about the evils of the corporations and how much they need vigilant warriors like themselves. Many of them live or stay in the bank, with at least a dozen there at all hours of the day and night.

Feather (use Covert-Ops Specialist, p. 83, _SR6_ ; remove Lagos-inappropriate gear) is an extraction specialist trained by Fuchi. She had a daughter, Gemma, and the need to raise her made Feather leave the shadows. Her daughter found her way into TerraFirst!, and from there things went as they too often do. Gemma was on an operation with Embiro and died. Feather blamed Embiro and doesn’t care that the truth of the story involves a noble sacrifice.

Feather works with Embiro and is waiting for the chance to burn him down, so she has tons of incriminating evidence.
