## Connection with Sixth World Technology (devices),

## By Prof. H. Pierce <excerpt>

“… known as ‘the Devices,’ they’re designed to open and stabilize a portal to another metaplane by synchronizing with the target destination, allowing the controlled breach to be opened with minimal damage to both metaplanes. The only thing necessary for this is a drekload of power—and the sacrifice of metahuman life …

The Devices can also be used as weapons. By causing a massive disruption, a Device can cause a massive mana warp, making any magic use or metaplanar travel impossible while in proximity. Fortunately, the effect is temporary, as the local harmonics will eventually stabilize and return to normal.

Even if it may take several decades or centuries.”
