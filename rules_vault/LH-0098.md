## Tír Tairngire and Tír na nÓg

The information coming out of these two nations seems almost the polar opposite. Tír Tairngire appears to have been building some locations now for over a year. They aren’t community-scale operations, but as many as a dozen havens are built deep into the hardest stone escarpments. By comparison, my information from Tír na nÓg is fragmented, perhaps indicating political problems in coordinating anything significant.

- I have reviewed the projects within the Tír Tairngire nation. Those draw upon inspiration from older developments in our world’s history. I have also learned that some of this information was communicated to concerned groups within Amazonia and Azania. It is possible they were shared from there. As for the other Tír, I am concerned as I learned that a member of the House of Stewards was a plant for Disian operations.
