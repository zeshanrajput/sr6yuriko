## **Can I get an Ally Spirit (p. 59-61)?**

Yes. Creation of the Ally formula requires the use of a Major Downtime Action and must be done by buying hits. Summoning and Binding don’t require any additional Downtime Actions, but also must be completed with buying hits. Ally spirits in SRM cannot have the Possession (except for Broussard Vodou) or Inhabitation power.
