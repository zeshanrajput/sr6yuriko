## Speed Booster (Rating 1-2)

Speed boosters are a derivative technology developed from research based on the new generation of move-by-wire augmentations. Rather than improving Reaction and Agility, though, all of the cybernetic processors are focused on pure movement. This augmentation does not alter the user’s movement rate but allows the user to use the Move and Avoid Incoming Minor Actions an additional time per combat round per rating point.
