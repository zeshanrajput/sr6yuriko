## The Cost of Low Essence

## (Game Information)

In the world of _Shadowrun_ , augmentations can make the difference between a living, breathTHEing runner and a smear on the pavement. Many runners who seek to go the route of augmentation do so to an extreme degree, often dropping their Essence below 1. This is in large part due to heavily invasive implants such as muscle replacement, wired reflexes, or bone lacing, or simply a combination of augmentations.

In basic terms, Essence acts as a measure of how many augmentations a runner can take. A runner with a low Essence rating will have less room for new augmentations and will be harder to heal. The qualities Augmentation Acclimation and Cyborg (pp. 132-3, _Sixth World Companion_ ) and the optional rule Transhumanism (p. 46, _Sixth World Companion_ ) give more space to install augmentations but do not increase Essence.
