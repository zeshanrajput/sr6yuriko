## Peer Pressure

If your friends all jumped off a bridge, you’d be right there with them.

**Bonus:** 5 Karma

**Game Effect:** When you are outnumbered, all Edge actions and boosts that affect your Composure, Judge Intentions, and social skill tests cost one additional point of Edge.
