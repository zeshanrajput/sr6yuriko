## Spirit Strength

- **Vector:** Ingestion

- **Speed:** 1 combat round

- **Duration:** 1D6 hours

- **Power:** 10

- **Effect:** +2 Agility, +2 Reaction, +2 Strength, grants situational Edge on Perception tests

This magical compound is created from small black mushrooms, which are ground into a pastelike mixture. This was often distributed to warriors prior to a battle or hunting expedition. Spirit Strength improves the physical attributes of the user, and they gain a situational Edge on Perception tests. When the drug wears off, the user suffers –1 Agility, –1 Reaction, and –1 Strength for the same duration.
