## Saeder-Krupp: Playing By Their Own Rules

Throughout the Disian Shadow War, S-K had always been a mystery. Early on it seemed, for some unknown reason, S-K was siding or at least working alongside the Disians. Then, support for them all but dried up with no explanation or reason as to why. All S-K assets suddenly pulled back and simply stopped working with the Disians. Normally, this would have caused quite a … problem, as the Disians weren’t known to be the understanding types. But not this time. Maybe it was because they didn’t want to risk Lofwyr’s wrath or S-K actively joining the Defender Movement. Or maybe it was something else we simply don’t know about.

Currently, there’s no solid data and only a few rumors to indicate why S-K, and old Gold Scale by extension, did anything they did … or didn’t.
