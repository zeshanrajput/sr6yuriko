## Meat Surrogacy

Not all bioware can be grown in a vat. Some of the most cultured bioware must be grown inside a living host, and you volunteered for such a procedure. You have a mysterious piece of inert bioware growing within your body, and once it is removed, you stand to make some decent money. Because the bioware is not for you, it takes a little out of you to carry it around. But hey, it’s worth it for the sweet, sweet nuyen you stand to make.



- **Bonus:** 2 Karma per level (max 3 levels)

- **Game Effect:** Characters may take this negative quality in up to 3 levels. Each level taken costs 1 point of Essence. That Essence is considered occupied until this quality is removed. Six months after this quality is taken, if bioware still is in the runner, they report to the doc who implanted it, have it removed, and receive 20,000 nuyen per point of Essence used in this way. When the procedure is complete, the character is left with an Essence hole equal to the levels taken in this quality. Once completed, this negative quality is removed.

There is a risk that the incubating bioware could be damaged or removed forcibly. If this is the case, the runner is responsible for the damaged bioware, and its total will be taken out of the amount received at the end of the period. Should the runner take more than 6 boxes of Physical damage from a single attack, make a Body test with a threshold equal to the level of this quality. If the threshold is passed, the incubating ’ware is safe and undamaged. If the test is failed, reduce the final payout of this quality by 10 percent per instance of 6 boxes of damage from a single attack. If the bioware is forcibly removed, the runner will not receive any payment.

If the runner opts not to go to the original source to get the bioware removed and instead sells it to another bidder, or simply buys this quality off with Karma, they receive the Hunted negative quality at the same equal as Meat Surrogacy, which is removed.
