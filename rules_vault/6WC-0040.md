## Adept Powers, Complex Forms, and Spells

Magicians (both full and aspected) gain a number of spells (including formulas and rituals) equal to the rating of their Magic attribute. Additional spells may be purchased for 5 Karma each, with a maximum number of them equal to Magic x 2.

Adepts gain a number of power points for adept powers equal to the rating of your Magic attribute.

Mystic adepts must choose whether to gain a power point or spell for each point of Magic. You may purchase additional power points and spells for 5 Karma each, with a maximum number of power points equal to Magic, and a maximum number of spells equal to Magic x 2.

Technomancers gain a number of complex forms equal to Resonance and may purchase additional complex forms (up to a maximum of Resonance x 2) for 5 Karma each.
