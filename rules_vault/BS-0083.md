## False Face

Although it was invented for use in theater, spies and shadowrunners quickly appropriated false face technology. Facial bones are replaced with ceramic plates, gel reservoirs, and cybernetic servos. A false face allows the user to resculpt their facial features to any possible expression for their metatype. It takes one minute of concentration to complete a facial transformation. This augmentation grants +2 dice and bonus Edge for disguise or impersonation tests.

**Wireless bonus:** A false face can match the facial biometrics matched to a given SIN or fake SIN.
