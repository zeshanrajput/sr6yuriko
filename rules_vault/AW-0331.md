## Shiawase

Shiawase’s embassy resembles a small warehouse, and is located on the outer edge of the Spire within the kindred district. Since arriving within the Place of Clouds, they have been sending many small teams through their portal. Each of these teams quickly departs for a cloud island, where they either begin establishing a permanent outpost or join an existing one. Shiawase’s outposts each include a small visitor’s area to appease vacationing spirits, but do not tolerate metahuman travelers who come close enough to observe them. All of the Shiawase cloud island outposts are far from the Spire and the most populated islands, which makes the arrival of an approaching airship likely to stand out. They’ve attacked and destroyed several airships that belonged to Spinrad, as well as Evo. Numerous recent reports claim that Shiawase airships have made contact with Dis and were not destroyed.
