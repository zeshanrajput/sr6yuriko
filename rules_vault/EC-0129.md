## Where to Kick Back

While meets can happen at bars and clubs, I prefer to save those spaces for my days off. Local bands have a hard time breaking out with so few spots catering to new music, but the ones that pop go big quickly. Pick your poison. You might find ARO Dreams spinning techno down the street or Suzie T dropping coffin-club vibes at the local bar.
