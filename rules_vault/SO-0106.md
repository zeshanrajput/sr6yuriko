## The Honeypot

## Posted by: Kat o’ Nine Tales

Tale as old as time. To the romantic, they’ll do anything to be close to the one they desire. To the mercenary, it’s simple economics that you have something (or are something) they desire, and there is an investment cost in acquiring you, transactional or otherwise. Some people make a living as Babies using the same basic tactics at work here, except they’re generally more open about what they’re doing. The subtle ones are Honey Babies. The difference? We’ll get to that. A Honeypot is a manipulation. You are seducing someone with lies and playacting to get them to be more pliable to what you want from them. Once they’re putty in your hands, it’s up to you how to use what you’ve got.
