## **How many people can help on a teamwork test?**

GMs may limit participants in any given teamwork test based on conceivability of assistance. For instance, a shadowrunner with Athletics could not assist another runner with an Athletics + Agility or Athletics + Strength test if they were not actually present. Only two, maybe three, individuals could work together to open a mechanical lock without getting in each other’s way. These should pass a commonsense check. Otherwise, the person who received help can only take help up to the ranks in the skill.
