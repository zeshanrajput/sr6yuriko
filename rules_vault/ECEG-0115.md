## Thick Accent

You’re nearly impossible to understand, such that even the locals have trouble making out what you say.

- **Cost:** 12 Karma

- **Game Effect:** While listening to jazz music (including using earbuds or headphones), you gain an additional Minor Action, but you may not gain or spend Edge on Perception tests. In order to engage in verbal communication, you need to turn the music down enough to lose the additional Minor Action.

- **Bonus:** 6 Karma

- **Game Effect:** You cannot gain or spend Edge on Infl uence or Con tests.
