## Negotiator

When deals need to be made, it always helps to have an expert negotiator hammer out the details on your behalf. You always had a knack for finding points of compromise or making the other person see how your way really is the best option. Some people just thought you were charming or gifted, but you know differently—negotiation is a skill that takes practice, dedication, and a variety of techniques and resources. You spent some time developing that skill and finding ways to profit from it.
