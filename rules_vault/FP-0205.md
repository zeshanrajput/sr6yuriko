## **Psyche as a Paragon (Kokopelli)**

**As a Paragon, Kokopelli can function as either the Architect or the Oracle (p. 130,** _**Hack & Slash**_ **). Those with Psyche as their paragon will not use Intimidation or seek to destroy anything of an intellectual nature, requiring a Composure (3) test to be involved in any such action.**
