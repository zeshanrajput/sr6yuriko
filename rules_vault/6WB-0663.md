## Mystic Armor

**Cost:** 0.25 PP per level **Activation:** Passive

Mana forms a shield around you, enhancing your protection. Gain 1 point of Armor for each level of this power, cumulative with worn Armor, which also increases your Defense Rating. Mystic Armor is also active on the astral plane.
