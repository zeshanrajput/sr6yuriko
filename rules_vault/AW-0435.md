## Agility

This mutation affects your mobility and kinetic accuracy. This might manifest as an inconvenient extra limb, muscle spasms, new bone growth, or swollen hands and feet.

**Game Effect:** All Edge boosts and actions cost twice as many points of Edge on tests that use Agility. If you receive a second Agility mutation, reduce your movement speed by half.
