## **Episode 10: So Sioux Me**

Enjoy the hilarity and the high-octane action as Chase returns home to the Sioux Nation. But with his Apex Predators, his usual Ares Carnivores, and his rugged new Ford Phalanx™ helping him out, there’s no way they’ll try to put him back on an Anglo Reservation! It’s not revenge that’s on his mind, but frontier justice, as Chase tracks bounties through his old stomping grounds, and stomps skulls along the way. The drama intensifies as this reunion episode highlights our WildCard™ ammunition option once again. Gel rounds or APDS? Nobody knows until the hammer falls!
