## **Unwelcome Visitors**

**If and when the player characters decide to infltrate the 200- x 100-meter barn, they’ll have to bypass a rating 6 maglock or otherwise force their way in.**

**If they decide to enter by stealth, subterfuge, or strongarm one of the townspeople, the player characters will discover that the barn is indeed a bunker designed to protect sixty of the townspeople and twenty-fve children.**

**If the player character enter by force, they will immediately discover the secret the town is hiding.**
