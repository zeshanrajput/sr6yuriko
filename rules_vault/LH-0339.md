## FAB IV

Although the very existence of FAB IV is still shrouded in secrecy, rumors suggest that much like the discovery of FAB III, this was an accident. One story is that an Evo junior executive was visiting a research facility protected by a FAB II barrier and the executive became extremely ill after entering. An examination revealed that she was, in fact, a Faustian. A mutation of the FAB II used at the facility had created a new strain that attaches to, and feeds from, the undifferentiated mana pools used by the Disians. Dubbed FAB IV, the new variety will attach to and drain Disian mana storage devices and Faustian crystals. While untested, it will theoretically have a similar effect against actual Disians as well.

**Game Rules:** Only auras containing pools of undifferentiated Disian mana can be infected with FAB IV (such as mana storage crystals, Faustian crystals, and the Disians themselves). The infection can be acquired by being in contact with the physical bacterium, from being in direct contact with a carrier—including an infected Disian device, or astrally contacting the bacteria.

If an aura with a Disian mana pool comes into contact with the bacterium (physical, carrier, or astral), the FAB IV will attack the aura and feed on the mana pool contained in it. The astral bacteria drain ten units of mana every fifteen minutes, starting with unbound mana and then draining the mana bound into powers, causing the loss of those powers and abilities (chosen randomly). There’s no test for this drain as it feeds on the mana pool. A device with an active infection is contagious and after its mana pool is depleted, the FAB IV bacteria will remain in the device’s aura for one hour before dissipating and the device is no longer contagious. This infection can be removed from a Disian device at any time with a Sterilize spell.

For Disians and Faustians, the drain on the mana pool also causes a secondary effect. At the end of each ongoing Speed interval, make a disease resistance test, and if the Power is not reduced to zero, the infected Faustian or Disian suffers one box of unresisted Stun damage. Unlike a normal infection, the unresisted Power from the first test is not added to the Power during the next interval. But if the bacteria are actively draining mana from a pool of undifferentiated mana, the Power resets to 10 at the beginning of each new interval, even if the Power was reduced to zero in the previous interval. Once the mana pool it is feeding on is depleted and reduced to zero, the Power of the infection reduces as normal, and once the Power is zero, with no mana to draw on, the infection is fought off.

The FAB IV infection in a Disian is attached to its internal mana pool and will follow the Disian. If it abandons its homunculus body, the FAB IV colony will still stay attached to the Disian’s astral form. If the infected Disian manages to move to another metaplane, through a gateway or portal, the effect ends in the same way as any other spirit infected with FAB crossing a metaplanar barrier.

FAB IV is an extremely rare, highly secretive, and exceptionally well-controlled substance. It’s not available for purchase and can only be obtained through special circumstances determined by the GM.
