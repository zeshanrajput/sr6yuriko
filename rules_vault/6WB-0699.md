## Shark

Shark is a cold and relentless hunter. His power is known to all who live near the sea. When Shark strikes, he does so without mercy, driven into a frenzy by the blood of his prey. Shark magicians tend to be wanderers, always on the move. They are fierce and deadly warriors. A Shark magician believes the only good enemy is a dead enemy. If challenged, he does not waste time with threats or boasts—he strikes to kill.

## Disadvantages

A Rat magician must make a Willpower + Charisma (3) test to not immediately flee or seek cover whenever caught in a combat situation. If there is nowhere to flee, they are forced to fight.
