## Academic Mage

## playing advice

## Roleplaying

As an academic magician, you tend to be very focused on whatever puzzle, problem, or task is most important to them. You prefer careful preparation and decisive action, rather than improvisation and spontaneity. You are a planner, and when things go according to plan, you are extremely effective. On the other hand, when presented with sudden unexpected obstacles or a lack of preparation time, you feel out of your element. However, academic magicians are nothing if not resourceful, and planning to be able to deal with the unexpected is something you do. That said, you can be expected to grumble about unpleasant surprises. The stereotypical academic magician seems to be emotionally aloof and socially stiff, but there are also a number of emotive party animals among them.

- **Cost:** 15 Karma

- **Game Effect:** You must have a talismonger or alchemist contact with Connection and Loyalty ratings of at least 5 each. If you ever lose access to that contact, you still keep any academic alchemy techniques but may purchase no more of them. You gain access to three new alchemy techniques, which you may apply in any combination to your preparations. Each technique has a drawback, and the drawbacks for multiple techniques are cumulative if more than one are used together.
