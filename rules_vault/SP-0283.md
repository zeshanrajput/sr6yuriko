## **The Hook**

**The players are contacted by their fxer, who informs them that a Mr. Johnson would like to hire their services for a quick smuggling job. The meet takes place at a rented conference room in a local hotel, and Mr. Johnson is all business. He wants to hire the team to smuggle several sealed and warded crates across the border to a location in the nearby**



**Native American Nations. The player characters must not under any circumstances break the seal or disrupt the wards on the containers, under pain of death and worse—no pay. Mr. Johnson is terse and provides very little other information; only an extremely successful Infuence test will goad him into revealing that he expects an attempt to intercept the crates by a rival faction. The target location is out in the middle of nowhere, within the wilderness far from any town or city. Mr. Johnson has been instructed to offer ffty percent of the fee up front as a gesture of good faith, which is meant to lower the PCs’ guard against the upcoming double-cross.**
