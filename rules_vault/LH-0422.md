## Schwarzkopf: Fortress Prague

Considered the keystone of helping keep Prague, and the Czech Republic as a whole, free from Disian aggression and influence, the dragon Schwarzkopf has become something of a national hero. Officially, he has been recognized for helping aid the country in its time of need by providing aid and shelter during natural disasters, as well as being instrumental in rebuilding efforts.

Unofficially, he spearheaded the fighting against the Disians in eastern Europe, working alongside but not directly a part of the Defender Movement, and was responsible for amassing of the largest collection of magical foci on the planet. Nobody’s sure yet why he did so, but it’s rumored that several of these items were given to various individuals and groups for use against Disian forces.

Unlike most dragons, Schwarzkopf has been front and center in the public eye, making statements regarding the recent rash of manastorms and doing his best to quell the growing discontent regarding magic in general, as well as meeting with representatives of the Draco Foundation.
