## Fear Free

- **Vector:** Slot

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** (Rating) hours

- **Power:** 4 + (Rating)

- **Effect:** Reduces threshold for Compulsion tests for Phobia negative quality

This was an experimental moodchip created to help patients overcome their fears, but it wasn’t fully effective. It tried to do this by activating endorphin and serotonin centers when the specific trigger is present. While it wasn’t completely successful, researchers discovered that test subjects would not freeze up as frequently as the control group. When users of the BTL encounter the object of their Phobia, they only need to make a Composure (1) test every five minutes instead of the usual Composure (3) test (p. 130, _Firing Squad_ ).
