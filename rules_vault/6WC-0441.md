## Changelings from Metagenics

These expressions are commonly referred to as _metagenics_ . Individuals, critters, and flora, regardless of paranormal that possess these expressions— regadrless of paranormal or mundane status—are commonly known as _changelings_ . These are a further classification for what we call _metavariants_ .

Since 2061 and the passage of Halley’s Comet, the idea of Sudden Unexplained Recessive (or Rapid) Genetic Expression, or SURGE, has been with us. In truth, it had happened occasionally before that year, but corporate media was still unwilling to discuss the phenomenon, as it was one more thing they could not explain. A definition of _metagenesis_ can start us off.
