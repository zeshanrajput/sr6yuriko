## Hotspots

## Fluffy Duck’s Gourmet Chicken Biscuit Truck

Anyone who has a commlink can find information about this food truck, as it is one of the most trendy and sought-after food trucks in the area. It’s not especially healthy, innovative, or exotic, but it’s really good. There are soy- and gluten-free options on the menu, but the truck is best known for its fried chicken biscuit. There is even a sign that says Fluffy Duck makes the biscuits from scratch each day.
