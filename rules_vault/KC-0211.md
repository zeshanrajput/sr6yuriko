## Guizhen I Shields

All forms of the technology can harm organic material if it remains within Close range for more than one turn. The Pulse Hammer can also damage organic matter at Near range on its higher settings. The Hall of Tears is a building installation used to help create hyper-restricted areas and always operates at Close range.

(Visual Filters and Lensing)

There are counters to the technology. The most obvious, and possibly equally dangerous, is the Blinded III status, regardless of how it is achieved. Additionally, optical devices (glasses, goggles, or helmet visors) can be outfitted with specialized filtering technology.

These shields negate the effects of being struck by the device. Standard contacts are not able to cover the eyes of most individuals to the extent necessary nor do they have the structural capacity to fully incorporate any shield modification.
