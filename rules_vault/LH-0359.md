## Faustian Oracle

Reportedly developed in secret by Aztechnology’s Special Projects Division, the Faustian oracle is a magical construct that can be used to detect implanted Faustian crystals. These devices are all ornate daggers powered by blood magic. These are usable by any magician and don’t require knowledge of blood magic. Using these devices will, however, taint the user’s aura with the indelible mark of blood magic as they require blood to function. How strong a mark left behind is set by the method of obtaining said blood.

**Game Rules:** This device must be “charged” with blood spilled from a living metahuman. A Faustian oracle is treated as a DV 2P, AR 7 knife. Each box of physical damage inflicted by the device, either voluntary or otherwise, creates one “charge” that can be used to try and detect a nearby Faustian crystal. A Faustian oracle can hold a maximum of twelve blood charges, but they only last until the next sunrise. Any unused charges dissipate.

Treat the Faustian oracle as a Detect Magic spell, but specifically keyed to detecting implanted Faustian crystals. The number of held charges expended in a test (determined by the user) stands in for the user’s Magic rating, and the rating of the Faustian crystal stands in for the target’s Body. This makes the Opposed test [Sorcery + Charges Used vs Rating + Willpower], with a single net hit confirming a crystal is nearby, two net hits revealing its general direction, and three net hits showing the crystal’s exact location.

Activating a charged Faustian Oracle is a Major Action requiring the user’s full attention and a small amount of blood drawn from the user. After activation, a detection test may be made, also requiring a Major Action. The oracle’s effect lasts for a number of rounds equal to the number of charges used. A new test may be made each round for as long as it is active.
