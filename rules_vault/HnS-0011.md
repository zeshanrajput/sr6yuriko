## Control over the Emerged and AI

The scariest part of the growth of native Matrix entities for the people who believe they are in charge is the lack of control. Having a part of this new world that is not theirs to command is anathema to them. So any change they make that they feel gives them more control is something they want. The de la Mar Matrix fits this category.

How this was managed and who did it, including the cult-like group known as Hengester along with one hundred dead technomancers, isn’t what I’m talking about yet. What I’m going to describe is the goal and how the control sought to be managed.
