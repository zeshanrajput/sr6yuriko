## Corporate Connections

## Posted by: Gramius

In addition to the biological samples found during G-Nome’s mission, there’s significant supplemental information linking certain corporations to the events in the Awakened Yakut.

Intercepted data traffic passed along the Trans-Siberian grid yielded several Matrix intercepts. Combined with biometrics and biological sampling taken at the black site, it indicates the technicians working there were former Shiawase employees. And by former, they’re officially listed as “deceased.” Additional information revealed that Sumitomo Chemicals was responsible for most of the on-site hardware. Unfortunately, onsite cyberterminals couldn’t be accessed, but images the team had taken indicate the units were less than four years old.

Additional information was intercepted in Irkutsk, which serves as the main rail leading to and from the Trans-Siberian Rail Corridor to the south. As such, that rail corridor and other shipping routes throughout the region are well-protected by hardened, dedicated military or paramilitary forces; however, they were recently reinforced by Awakened Protective Initiatives, a Wuxing subsidiary.
