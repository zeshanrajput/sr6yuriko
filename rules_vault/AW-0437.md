## Strength

The mutation reduces your physical strength. This manifests as large and heavy tumors, aching muscles, and a pervading sense of fatigue.

**Game Effect:** All Edge boosts and actions cost twice as many points of Edge on tests that use Strength or the Close Combat skill. If you receive a second Strength mutation, the DV of your close combat attacks is increased by +1 for every two net hits (instead of +1 DV per net hit).
