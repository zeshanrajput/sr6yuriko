## **How much capacity does the MEMS system have?**

The MEMS harness has a capacity of 20. The breakdown for the system is 12/8 which is the number for front capacity (12) and back (8). Integrating a MEMS harness into your armor consumes 10 capacity, but you can just put the harness over the top of your armor. If you chose to put it over the top of your armor, you don’t have access to any of the gear on your base armor, and during combat you grant Edge to your opponent as the harness can slide while you’re moving around.
