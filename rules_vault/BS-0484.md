## Natural Weapon Sheathing

Natural weapon sheathing is a relatively inexpensive and low-impact option for improving a critter’s natural weapons. This requires modifying and reinforcing the paws or jaws as well, and regular maintenance is needed to keep the weapons sharp. If the critter already has the bone lacing or exoframe augmentation, the nuyen and Essence costs for claw or tooth sheathing are reduced.

**Game Information:** The maximum rating for natural weapon sheathing is 6. The Attack Rating of the critter’s natural weapons is increased by the augmentation’s rating, and the base DV is increased by half of the rating (round up)—to a maximum of +3 DV for rating 5 or 6. If the critter has aluminum or titanium bone lacing, the nuyen and Essence costs of the natural weapon sheathing are reduced by fifty percent

**Maintenance Requirement:** The critter is no longer capable of maintaining the sharpness of their natural weapons. The natural weapons must be maintained monthly, or they begin to degrade. Once per month, an extended Biotech (Cybertechnology) test is required—with a threshold equal to double the rating and an interval of one hour. A cybertechnology toolkit is required for this task; using a shop or facility grants a point of Edge for each step of the test (which must be used on the Extended test or it is lost). A shop grants one point of Edge throughout the entire Extended test, while a facility grants a point of Edge for each roll.

For every month that passes without performing the proper maintenance, the rating of the natural weapon sheathing is reduced by one and the threshold for any future maintenance tests is increased by two. Proper maintenance restores the rating to its original value.
