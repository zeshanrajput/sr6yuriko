## Fettering and Offerings

One word: fuel, meaning anything that can be used in combustion. Hermetic circles with blazing braziers to call upon elemental forces from the other world is still a typical media view of conjuring. Much like air elementals, preferred formal offerings are easy enough to procure, such as candles, incense, and high-proof alcohol. Fire elementals are simple and carefree. Many enjoy the offers given and do not dwell on the value or whether all the conjuring protocols were met.
