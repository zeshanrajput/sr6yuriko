## Combat

It also pays to watch the preferences and tendencies of attackers and carry your tactics accordingly. Some will be sent away by a sharp rap on their virtual knuckles—others will find that to be more incentive to fight. And that may be okay— based on the lesson you want to impart, it may be okay to repel people quickly, or it may be okay to turn it into a longer fight so you can see how much of your opponent’s strength you can sap away through attrition.

A defensive posture can feel reactive, but sometimes it’s time to go on offense. When it’s time to roll initiative in a real-life firefight, you need to find some cover and start bricking enemy hardware.
