## Linguasofts

Linguasofts replicate language skills, allowing a user to speak a foreign language. Linguasofts must be accessed with a skilljack, and the number you can use at once is limited by the skilljack rating. Linguasoft ratings are equal to Language skill abilities as follows: 1 = basic language facility, 2 = language specialist, 3 = language expert, 4 = language expert. Linguasofts cannot go above rating 4.
