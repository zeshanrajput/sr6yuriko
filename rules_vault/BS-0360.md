## Drug Interactions

Using multiple drugs at the same time has the potential to cause drug interactions. While the trid commercials tell you to go check with your pharmacist, the type of drugs that runners are likely to take are a lot more volatile, resulting in potentially harmful interactions, even if you have used those drugs together in the past. The less pure the drug, the more likely you are to have unexpected side effects. One common case is when someone on a drug is hit with a stim patch or a trauma patch. This requires a drug interaction check.

The effects of drug interation can range anywhere from headaches and nausea to damage and full organ shutdown. Sometimes, it will cause the user of the drugs to immediately crash instead of lasting the normal duration.
