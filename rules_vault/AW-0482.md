## Metaplanar Gateway Critter Power

| **TYPE** | **ACTION** | **RANGE** | **DURATION** |
| -------- | ---------- | --------- | ------------ |
| M        | Major      | LOS (A)   | Sustained    |

The spirit (or other entity) can open a temporary metaplanar gateway 2 meters in size, allowing physical travel to the destination by stepping through the opening in reality created by the power. The size of this opening cannot be changed and objects that can pass through are limited by the size of the gateway. Typically, only metahumans and drones can pass; very small vehicles may fit through at the discretion of the gamemaster. The gateway can connect to any metaplane the entity has visited previously.
