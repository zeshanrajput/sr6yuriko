## Ends Justify the Means

Despite the stunning tactical victories, there was a cost. As a whole, Renraku forces don’t give a single drek about civilians or collateral damage. Targets hiding in a building? Bomb it. The people living in it should have already gotten out. Shoot through civilians just to get a better shot at a group of chimeras? No problem. And that’s just the incidents that have been uncovered. Several times, there were simply no witnesses left to refute Renraku’s after-action reports.

Additionally, Renraku wasn’t shy about taking spoils of war. If there was anything of value to be taken, it was. Attempts were made by Project Nemesis to investigate these rumors, but this only led to more infighting just as the war was ramping up. Eventually it was decided for the time being to leave Renraku to their own devices because the threat of the Final Project still loomed.

- And not just material. Rumors are still circulating that Renraku used anti-Disian raids to cover unwilling extractions and assassinations as well.
