## Krime Ripper

In our partnership with Radio Shack, Krime has worked hard to develop an anti-air drone system for the international market, a compact and quick-to-deploy system able to handle all forms of anti-air drone combat! Incorporating the KPX-7 series of radar system with a TRS-8000 computer to run the targeting feeds, including the latest in smartgun technology as developed by Radio Shack, combined with a pair of Krime Wave machine guns! The system is able to fold flat in our patented Martrend trailer package that can be towed by any multi-purpose utility vehicle (MPUV) ever put into market, as well as by heavier vehicles! With its own dedicated multifuel power system, this is the self-contained anti-air system you didn’t know you needed!
