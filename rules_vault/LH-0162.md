## The Ebb and Flow of Conflict

Despite what some may think, war isn’t a constant state of sustained fighting. Eventually, there comes a time when forces on both sides need to refresh and resupply before going back to killing each other. The trick is taking advantage of the downtime in order to prepare for the next round of action while doing your best to prevent the enemy from doing the same.

This shadow war is no different.

The Week of Death provided the highest operational tempo of this war so far. In the following weeks, that tempo has slowed as the Disians have taken up their current defensive posture. We still don’t know if this is a strategic pause to reinforce, or if it’s an indication that they’re moving on to the next phase in their strategy.

Regardless, while their tempo has slowed, it has _not_ halted.
