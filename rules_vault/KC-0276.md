## Cast of Shadows Agano Kuniko

## Kawaru-gumi, Australia

Members of the Kawaru-gumi in Australia call Agano Kuniko _midori no oyayubi de tororu,_ or “the troll with the green thumb. “She was born in Australia in a Japanese enclave in Brisbane. Her initial years with the Yakuza were spent as a bodyguard working some of the clubs and adding to security. When the local oyabun decided to set up a grow operation outside the city, Kuniko was assigned as part of the security retinue. She worked closely with the botanist heading up the facility and was put in charge of a couple of the Crimson Orchid fields. She picked up the job quickly, and within a couple growing seasons, her fields were outproducing the others. They switched her to other fields, and she succeeded there. After twelve years, she now oversees the entire grow operation and has been keen on tracking various experiments and attempting to get even better results. She can be a little hard to track down, but if you ask around at some of the Yak bars downtown, they can point you in her direction.
