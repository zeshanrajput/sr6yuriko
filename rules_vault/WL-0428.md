## **Powerless**

**items—anything that would require a metahuman to use two hands counts as a large item. A gear harness provides no bonus to Defense Rating, but it can accept armor modifcations.**

**The critter is less capable than others of its species, lacking one or more of its natural powers and abilities.**

**Light Combat Armor: A simple vest designed to protect the critter’s vitals, made of the same material that is used for armored clothing. Without special training, a critter will be distracted and uncomfortable while wearing this armor, requiring a Major Action to issue commands to them (instead of a Minor Action). Light combat armor provides +2 to Defense Rating and can accept armor modifcations.**

- **Bonus: 3 Karma**

- **Game Effect: Only critters with critter powers are eligible to take this quality. Choose one of the critter’s powers—that critter power is lost. A critter may take this quality multiple times, with each instance taking away another critter power.**

**Heavy Combat Armor: Heavier armor consisting of polymer plates attached to armored fabric. This also includes some additional pieces to protect the head and limbs. Critters wearing heavy combat armor cannot fy or swim. Without special training, a critter wearing this armor gains the Hobbled condition, and issuing them commands requires a Major Action. Heavy combat armor provides +4 to Defense Rating and can accept armor modifcations.**
