## NEW GEAR & DARK TECH

As the Disian Shadow War ramped up, all sides of the conflict put weapon production into overdrive to get said weapons to the forces in the field before the opposition could. Such increased production also meant that hard-to-find weapons and gear practically flowed into the shadows. This included a record number of experimental prototypes sent out for “field testing.”

While there wasn’t enough time for new tech to emerge in response to the Disian threat, several systems that were already in the pipeline were quickly rushed into service. Incidentally, it became clear that weapons and tech to counter the Disian threat had been in development for quite some time before the war began.

Some of these systems were so dangerous, destructive, or repulsive that only the insane (or very desperate) would even consider using them. Unofficially labeled “Dark-Tech,” these terrible devices were normally hidden away at corporate black sites or secure research vaults and were kept around “just in case.”
