## **Side Note: Sapience**

**Some critters—usually paracritters or technocritters—possess cognitive capabilities that put them a step above what we normally consider to be “animals.” It’s probably ofensive to call them critters, but the terminology at large hasn’t changed much over the years. They aren’t metahumans, but they should be considered (and are in many places) to be people. Dragons and nagas are some of the most widely recognized sapient critters, but you could include various Infected such as vampires and ghouls in this category as well.**
