## **How do I summon or channel a great form spirit (p. 66, 122)?**

For _Shadowrun Missions_ , Great Form Spirit Hit Results table is not legal. Instead, a great form spirit gains the Great Spirit Power for the type. Otherwise, summon the great form as normal.

If you choose to channel a great form, remember that the Regeneration power would cause the body to begin rejecting any cyberware and you begin taking unresisted physical damage equal to Essence worth of cyberware. Minimum great form force is 5.
