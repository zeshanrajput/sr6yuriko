**Gotha: British elf female, 29, sits at one of those royal ascension junctions that means there still exists a real possibility that she could become queen, but it would require such a massive die-off that no one truly pays attention. Except the shadows, because there are rumors of a bulk contract that would be paid out should Gotha be coronated. Real deepshadows stuff but important to have here because it means she has the connections to set that up, even though most of her business in the House of Lords focuses on dealing with the aftermath and repercussions of unmanaged arcane activities and rampant tech abuse.**

**Ecological disasters that have created toxic creatures are a shining star in her campaigns petitioning the control of both. Gotha is often heard quoting fictional scientist Ian Malcolm. “Your scientists were so preoccupied with whether they could, they never considered whether they should.” She’s not just talking about cloning but Matrix tech, manatech, Emergent tech, and even augmentation systems that on one hand could preserve a life but are more often abused to make life-taking easier. She lost two daughters to violence when they were caught in the crossfire between a team of shadowrunners and the cybered-up personal security of a fellow lord.**

**Mtoumbo: Zulu ork female, 48, who sees a world that will burn itself out one way or another if those who understand restraint and moderation don’t come forward and lead. Watching her nation rise with the power of magic should have been a source of pride, but she felt stepped on and abused by yet another powerful people. The Awakened were no better than the colonists and were often worse, because they shared blood. The separation of cultures within her people did not feel like the old ways of honoring the wise and powerful who had learned lessons and lived life but rather the powerful placing a different form of boot on their neck. Internally, she grew in power with her point of view, but when Evo came along with an exceptionally sweet deal, they added another cultural rift that cost her much of her support. Access to Evo-tech was supposed to bring more balance and power to the nation but instead added another class of citizens to wear the boots of oppression while picking fights with the other powerful faction. Mtoumbo sees all this as a precursor to the collapse of the Zulu and eventually as a collapse of metahumanity.**

**Ditmoor: Japanese troll male, 35, never fit in the Japanese culture but at least he avoided Yomi Island. He has found a life back in Neo-Tokyo operating as a fixer for runners all over the massive metroplex as well as up and down the South Pacific. If a job has the stink of mana void creation or manipulation, it’s probably a Ditmoor gig. On top of those, he heavily funds anti-transhumanist organizations, especially those keen on not just destroying next-gen tech but getting in and corrupting production and archive files for regular tech as well. When NorthAm cities were getting scagged with blackouts, Ditmoor rallied the troops and rolled in with a bunch of old-tech. The people loved it, and he recruited scores of new Asintmah allies with rhetoric pinning all the problems on our tech addiction. He may be a troll, but he’s charismatic as frag and he can rally troops to the cause like no one’s business.**

**Rockefeller: Aztechnology dwarf male, 46. His family name may be inherently connected to old European/United States money, but this Rockefeller has Aztechnology citizenship, complete with voting rights. He has abundant wealth within AZT but also has holdings with companies outside his corporate nation. He has friends in every fovea research project going on, uses private money to make moves to get them the information they need, and makes sure it gets used the way he, and the Asintmah Alliance, wants. While his SIN says AZT, he has property and holdings all over the world. He can more often be found outside his home nationcorp than within.**

**Joseph Sarkenbleck: UCASian human male, 50, seems like a regular, everyday Joe (pun fully intended). Sure, he got radical after Chi-town went Bug City, but he came back swinging with plan after plan to strip the city of mana and kill off all the bugs. While he says it was Awakened athletes that took his chances, most recruiters will argue that with copies of his arrest records. Nothing major, but it was stacking up, and no matter how fast he could run, how well he read and memorized plays and defensive schemes, or how many shots he could drop on the court, no organization wanted a kid who already had red flags on his SIN as a juvenile. He’s disgruntled but highly rational, and he’s less a proponent of genocide and more an ardent supporter of neoslavery. He knows well enough that if plan A doesn’t work and mana cannot be cut off from the world, then all those capable of using it should be used for the betterment of the world. > Snopes**
