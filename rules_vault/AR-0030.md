## Millard 

This is the southwest area of the sprawl. Many of the people who do the actual work of the sprawl live here, along with a fair amount of the younger Lone Star officers. Don’t dress too showy and keep to yourself, and you can fit in just about any part of this section. The district borders **Zorinsky Lake** , which was rendered toxic thanks to the questionable practices of some early-century agribusinesses. The water quality is a little better than it was twenty years ago, but it’s still bad enough that if you’re looking for toxic mages in the area, this is the place to start.
