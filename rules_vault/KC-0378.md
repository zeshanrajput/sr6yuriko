## Alta (elf, female, 26, white hair, brown eyes, muscular build)

Attitude from altitude—that’s how Alta got her name. She was a prime P2.0 diva who regularly did her posts and tales from high altitude or remote spots that only she could reach via her piloting skills and remote control of her drones. She was the highlight of the social scene until a scandal over her brother’s death sent her spiraling. Her bad-girl streak led her to the shadows, and that’s where she stayed. She found this crew of other social skillsters and enjoys her niche. She even secretly records and edits a lot of their runs and posts them anonymously.
