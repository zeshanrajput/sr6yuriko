## Improved Astral Privacy

You are good at hiding your astral tracks and have a few simple mana barriers around, or maybe you just take steps to make your doss stand out less from astral perception. Add Lifestyle category rating to any astral tracking modifier to track you when you are in your home (p. 161, _SR6_ ). The Force of the astral barriers protecting your home is equal the your lifestyle category rating (1 for Squatter, 2 for Low, 3 for Middle, etc.).

**Cost: 1 LP**
