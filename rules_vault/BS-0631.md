## Delusion

The character has a firmly held belief that has no basis in fact. No amount of logical argument or persuasive charm will dissuade the character from this belief.

- **Bonus:** 10 Karma

- **Game Effect:** The delusion will result in the character gaining the Confused 2 status in most social situations or during a shadowrun when encountering the subject of the delusion. Anytime they are confronted with reality contradicting their delusion, the character needs to come up with an explanation as to why the delusion appears to be false. Examples of a delusion include an imaginary friend or foe, belief in the power of a lucky charm, or knowledge of a global conspiracy between dragons and elves. Should this delusion be forcibly broken, the character who is unable to rationalize this is put under extreme stress. They must make a Composure (4) test or suffer a mental breakdown, which may lead to a new delusion or other equivalent negative quality to replace the Delusion.
