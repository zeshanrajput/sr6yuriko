## Dragons

Dragons (and other dracoforms) are affected similarly to other dual-natured creatures. They receive the same status-related penalties but are driven to their lairs by powerful instincts. Adult and great dragons can choose to ignore this drive, but very few do, opting to retreat to their lairs and wait out this lull while under guard. They do not fall into torpor, but many choose to conserve their strength anyhow. After August 5, most of them have recovered enough to venture back out of their lairs.
