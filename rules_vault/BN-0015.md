## **Shifter augmentation**

Animalism (p. 20), Shift, Dual Natured, and Supernatural Recovery all have a required Essence of 6. Minor augmentations can be accommodated after picking up the appropriate Animalism power, which grants an Essence hole. Augmentations outside of the Essence hole granted by Animalism will reduce the characters Essence and result in the loss of those abilities. Obviously, this is not a common choice, but some shifters embrace the chrome. The noted combination of cryogenics, blood thinners, and immunosuppressants is accounted for in the standard cost of an augmentation. This locks a shifter into their animal form, which causes all manner of inconveniences. It’s impossible for a chromed-up tiger to function in metahuman cities. The larger animal forms will— sooner rather than later—fi nd themselves in a cage. Smaller animal forms can pass as pets or possibly as exotic animals, but most shifters fi nd that degrading and are usually quite frustrated by the near-complete dependance on another. Still, it can be done.
