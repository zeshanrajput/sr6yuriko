## **Ryoko Iseri**

**Any good intelligence operation needs a spellcaster who can eavesdrop on people at a distance and channel mana into interrogation skills, and Iseri is it for the Tiding. She’s certainly not the only spellcaster the Tiding uses, but she’s the one who directs all the other ones.**

**Iseri rose through the ranks of Mitsuhama Magical Services, starting in human resources where she screened hiring candidates and eventually making her way into the troubleshooting line of work, looking into problems both internal and external.**

**She is an extremely skilled interrogator because she knows good interrogation is not about yelling and bullying. She uses both people and magic skills to get a read on her target, then works to identify what they want. Once she knows, she engages them in light conversation and slowly moves toward hinting that she might be able to deliver it. Her friendliness and magic help people open up, and she doesn’t even need to get them to reveal their secrets out loud. Sometimes, they only have to think it, and she plucks it from their head.**

**She doesn’t do all her work in interrogation. Casual conversation can work just as well—it’s amazing what Iseri can learn if you set her loose in a corporate facility and let her talk to people for a day.**

- **It’s not just office secrets she comes away with—she usually gets enough host passwords to make a hacker’s head spin. Just doing a quick Mind Probe when people are logging on to some site is enough. Even if the password is entered automatically, the individual will often think about it when their device logs them on.**
