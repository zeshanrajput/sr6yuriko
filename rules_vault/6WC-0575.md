## Critter Dominator

You use your aggression and will to dominate creatures.

- **Cost:** 10 Karma

- **Game Effect:** You may spend a major action to attempt to dominate a non-sapient critPEOPLEter, using Influence (Intimidation) + Charisma vs. the critter’s Intuition + Willpower. If you succeed, you gain control of the critter as if you had the Animal Control critter power. This lasts for one minute per net hit. Afterward, the critter resumes its normal behavior, and you gain a point of Edge on any tests made against it. You may only ever attempt to dominate an individual critter once.
