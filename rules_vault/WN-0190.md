## Other Interested/Interesting Parties/Events

The following encounters are suggested for gamemasters who want to spice up the party with some third-party action. A mage (use Street Shaman, p. 88, SR6) with a Force 8 spirit of air (p. 147, SR6; add Fear, Guard) using its Concealment power rushes through the party recording footage. Security for the party is slow to respond, as they have no magical assets on site. The Tower’s magical security (eight air spirits and two security mages; see above) have been avoided so far, and the mage is quick to depart via a Levitate spell and an open window.

A hacker (use Technomancer, p. 89, SR6) hides a floor below with a hole drilled through the floor and wifi-inhibiting paint all around them. They randomly hack commlinks looking for paydata.

Security discovers Vera is an imposter. They quietly engage and remove her, but she appeals to the runners for rescue and sympathy, figuring the guards are possibly going to just kill her when they get her from the room.
