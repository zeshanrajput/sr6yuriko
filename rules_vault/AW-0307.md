## The Spire

On the largest towering cumulonimbus, second only to the Storm, is the city called the Spire. This city is ancient, some believing that it was built prior to the hyper-metaplane and was just absorbed into the cloud. The brumes might take credit for its construction, or a few sylphs, but no one really knows of its origins. The ancient and immortal spirits now reside within and manage the city of the Spire, managing the approval of new construction within the hyper-metaplane. The spirits are collectively known as the bureaucracy, and among their number are a diverse mix of kin, water, and air spirits. The bureaucrats oversee all city planning arrangements and have absolute authority over any building projects or modifications. The air spirits among the bureaucrats manage the docks district that rings the full circumference of the Spire. Many of the docks extend out as far as a kilometer, allowing room for as many as a dozen airships to dock at once. Air spirits ferry passengers and cargo to and from the airships and thecity, and they keep a running tally of all docking and porter services fees for the bureaucracy. No trade is allowed upon the docks, with the sole exception of purchasing passage on, chartering, or buying and selling airships.
