## Current Status

Evo, like some other corporations, is in a state of transition. With Buttercup currently MIA (allegedly) because of the Un-Awakening, and Evo’s board deciding what to do with their corporate HQ, as well as their newly gained knowledge, exAFTERSHOCKSpect some rebranding in the future, with the possibility of newer, even weirder advancements in the realms of cybertechnology.
