## Tandoran Player Characters

If your gamemaster is willing to allow you to play a Tandoran as a player character, choose Tandoran as your metatype. There is no additional Karma cost. You must decide whether or not your character is an actual Tandoran from the Island of Tandora who has somehow made their way to Earth, or if you are a changeling who has always lived on Earth. Simon Andrews, a fixer working for Saeder-Krupp, might be an example of the latter.
