## Sherlock (human, male, 36,

## black/grey hair, blue eyes, thin build)

Facts and even conjecture rarely stay hidden from a decker with the moniker Sherlock. He loves digging up facts and taking on the status quo, but he finds even greater pleasure using and manipulating those facts to run long cons. It reminds him of the good ol’ days of hacking that didn’t require much actual code and instead just took calling the right old lady with the right story. A simpler time. He is generally reclusive, preserving his visage from becoming too well-known. He is quite capable of running his social efforts over the Matrix, as his persona is frequently modified to suit the mood.

He provides great hacking and digging while they’re running the con. He’ll pull together anything incriminating on their commlinks while setting up back door access.

If a conflict erupts, Sherlock is going to head for the hills but not before putting every camera in the vicinity on the PCs and setting them on the defensive for a bit.
