## Rigger Interface

The rigger interface is what allows a rigger to jump in and actually feel as if they become the vehicle. All drones include a rigger interface, but it is not an automatic feature of passenger vehicles. Without the rigger interface, a rigger can still operate a vehicle manually or remotely without Jumping In.
