## **Feral Frenzy**

**When this critter goes into combat, they lose sight of who is an ally and who is the enemy.**

- **Bonus: 5 Karma**

- **Game Effect: When this critter engages in combat, whether in self-defense or if commanded to fght, they will continue to fght even after the enemies have been defeated. When no enemies are available, they will attack the nearest critter or character. Stopping the critter’s frenzy requires a Major Action and a successful Infuence (Critters) + Charisma test opposed by the critter’s Willpower + Intuition.**

**This quality is only available to critters with a natural weapon or at least one critter power that deals damage.**
