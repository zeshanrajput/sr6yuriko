## Wuxing Threat Assessment

With Wuxing dealing with internal strife, they have, for the most part, withdrawn from active participation in the current conflict. The Disians are not done with them just yet, however. They remain somewhat of a wildcard; it is unknown what would happen if they were given the order to intervene when the current conflict eventually escalates. If Chiang-Wu can regain control of her corporation, Wuxing could possibly become an ally against the Disians. If not, they could easily be taken out of play by leaking information about the loss of the Divine Map to the right sources. Either way, there is a real opportunity available to us should we somehow link up and coordinate with President Chiang-Wu.
