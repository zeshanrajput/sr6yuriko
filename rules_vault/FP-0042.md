## **Goals**

**How do you defne the goals of a group of people who fit on the whims of paper treasures? Their simplest goal is easy: acquire cards. Whether they murder, steal, or machinate, gaining possession and command over a card or collection of cards is their most base desire. After that, it’s all about gloating to other members about what they have, plotting the best (often meaning most extravagant) use of their card, and fending off those trying to take what is theirs.**

**As much of the tarot is a mystery, even with a card in hand, their efforts are not done. Deciphering what a card can do and then fnding how to apply that to best ft their agenda or entertainment is no simple task.**

**Cards used for entertainment are the most dangerous to runners, because one rich slob’s entertainment could very well cost the life of a good chummer. I know six different runners who’ve paid that price and a dozen more with expensive injuries.**

**At least if there is some form of agenda, the cartomancer might want runners available for multiple aspects of their work. It’s not always true, and sometimes it’s just a delay of the death sentence after the cartomancer wants to tie up loose ends. The good thing is, most of these cartomancers aren’t tactical tycoons or even very thorough on their follow-through. Still doesn’t make them safe, just makes them exploitable and avoidable.**

**When it comes to talking about how far they’ll go, it’s all based on the individual, but as all of them are a few fries short of a meal, it’s usually farther than you would guess at frst sight or meeting.**



**Violence isn’t promoted as a frst choice, but most of them are a bit morally skewed and have little issue making violence one of the steps even if they seem the squirrelly or squeamish type.**

- **Cards are their goals for sure, but not just any card. Members are always looking for something specific to complete or start a set. Some are working on multiple efforts at a time, but that’s the rare exception. Echoes are usually laser-focused on bringing card sets together. If a random card is placed on their path, they may make the purchase and immediately post it to the other Echoes, but they usually just post on its presence and leave their peers to acquire it on their own. Rare occasions happen with a unique major arcana that sets them on a new course.**
