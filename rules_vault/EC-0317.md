## Port Madison

Why do I talk about this gilt-lined harbor? Because it’s a regular target for runners looking for a soft place to hit the rich. At the north end of Bainbridge is a nice, deep inlet that is lined with overpriced mansions and their equally excessive yacht accessories. Security is usually staring at the eye-candy, so as long as you look like crew on a tender, you’re in like Flynn.
