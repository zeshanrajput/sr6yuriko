## Denizens of Dis

The citizens of Dis do not fit into any of the core spirit types from _SR6_ , but they have similarities, and of course use similar powers. Three basic citizen types are presented here, to accompany the descriptors given in the _**Bad Mojo**_ chapter (p. 77); each of these is a variant on a type of spirit on p. 147, _SR6_ . The template used was for Force 4 spirits.

Since spirits like to have flexibility in materializing and de-materializing, they tend to not carry weapons, letting their powers and spells take care of any harm they need to inflict.
