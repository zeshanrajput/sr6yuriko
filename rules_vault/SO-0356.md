## Qualities and Social Test Example

Jazz has the First Impression quality, which grants him two points of Edge on the first Social skill test when he meets someone for the first time. This meet involves a preamble: a tea ceremony, which is itself a test of etiquette. When Jazz makes his Influence (Etiquette) + Charisma test to behave appropriately and pass Mr. Johnson’s test of skill and finesse, he gains two points of Edge from his First Impression quality. Because you can gain only two points of Edge per round, no other sources of Edge are relevant for Jazz on this test. We still compare Jazz’s Social Rating to Mr. Johnson’s to see if the opposition is awarded Edge, however unlikely that may be. Mr. Johnson has a high Social Rating, but not high enough to net him a point of Edge against the likes of Jazz.
