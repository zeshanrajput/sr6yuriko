## Magician’s Way

Followers may resist adept drain with their tradition attribute instead of Willpower, and they may either swap in a wild die on any roll made for Astral, Conjuring, Enchanting, or Sorcery (the choice of one skill is permanent when this way is taken, but you may choose to include a Wild Die or not from roll to roll), _or_ they may choose to receive both the Adept and Magician benefits of their mentor spirit (purchased normally). The selection of which benefit to gain is made when the way is chosen.
