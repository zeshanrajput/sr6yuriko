## Current Status

Other than losing a lot of nuyen and material when Skyhook was destroyed, the corporation has come out of the war fairly unscathed. S-K has also been surprisingly quiet, not just about this, but other current events in general. In Corporate Court sessions, the current justice has been surprisingly passive about anything, barely contributing to proceedings and only speaking when absolutely necessary.

The one rumor regarding S-K is about Lofwyr himself, that after he and the other great dragons made their appearance in Antarctica, none of them have been seen sense. Of course, it’s not uncommon for dragons of any kind to have extended periods of absence, but given current events, rumors persist that Lofwyr and the other greats are now missing or even dead. If he is, that means that unless Lofwyr is using a metahuman voice as his proxy (for the first time), someone else is now in charge of S-K and acting as a steward until the golden one returns.
