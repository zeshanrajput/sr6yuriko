## Power

Power is the measure of how potent a BTL is. For ripjacks that do damage, this tells you how much Matrix damage it does. Resisting a ripjack uses Firewall + Willpower + the rating of appropriate gear to reduce the damage and Power. This is different from toxin resistance; gear that is specific to toxin resistance do not apply to ripjack resistance. Reducing the Power to 0 results in no damage being done and none of the effects occurring.
