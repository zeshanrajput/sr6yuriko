## The Midnight Club

It wouldn’t be ultra-rich without a private club geared toward the people who make more money in an hour than most of us pull together in a year. Those people are members of the Midnight Club. I don’t know what it’s like inside. From the outside, it is a nondescript four-story building in the heart of the downtown area covered in IR reflective glass. Centurion Security officers guard the front entrance kitted up in heavy security armor. However, the only people going in and out that way are the workers. Anyone who matters arrives by private limo and is escorted in through the basement garage.

- I dug down a little and discovered the building is owned by JRJ International. You might remember the company is the official holder of the Big Ten seat that has belonged to SpinGlobal, NeoNET, and Fuchi, to name a few.

- Mr. Bonds
