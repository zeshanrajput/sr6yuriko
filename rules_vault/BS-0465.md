## Unlearning

Once a critter has reached its maximum available commands and special trainings, it cannot learn more. If you wish to make a critter unlearn a command or special training in order to make room for learning a new one, this requires a successful Training test and one week of effort. You may then take another week to make a Training test to teach the critter a new special training or command to replace the one it has unlearned.
