## Meet the Johnson

The parking spot is far enough from the building that no customers or employees would willingly park there. It also allows for a quick getaway if needed. When the players approach, the driver gets out and opens a rear door. The car has enough Metahuman Adjustment modifications in the back to accommodate the players.

Once everyone is inside, the new employer starts to talk. “You can call me El Guapo. What I’m about to tell you is not public knowledge. My employer’s son, Lex James, disappeared yesterday, and we’re trying to find out why. Lex is Awakened, a hermetic mage who has been educated by some of the best tutors. He’s considered a prodigy in some circles, with skills across a range of spells. We’ve been looking for him, but all our efforts have come up empty so far.”

“As far as we know, nothing was taken from the house. Even his commlink was still there. I’ll provide you with security video for the house. Additionally, you should talk to his two closest friends. They’re inside—that’s why we’re meeting here. I think they may have an idea of what’s going on.”

“Time is of the essence—I need you to conclude your investigation within the next thirty-six hours. I’ll pay each of you 2,500 nuyen.” Each net hit on an Influence + Charisma Opposed test increases that figure by 250 nuyen. Once an agreement is made, El Guapo will send the team the security footage, names and pictures of the two friends Morgan Schnepple and Madison Greene, and a picture of Lex James.

After everyone is out, El Guapo says one last thing, “If you find Lex and get him to me uninjured, I’ll have a nice bonus for you.” The driver shuts the door, walks around the car, gets in, and drives off.
