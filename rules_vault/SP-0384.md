## **The Ares Devices**

**One of the frst adaptations of Disian devices attempted by metahumans started as a research program ordered by Damien Knight, the late CEO of Ares. Knight saw the potential of the Disian portal generator as a metaplanar weapon in his crusade against the insect spirits (Invae). While portal generators bridge the frequencies of two metaplanes, the devices envisioned by Knight did the opposite, erecting a wall of a disharmonious frequency between the two, making it next to impossible to travel between them. Early versions used Awakened metahumans to power the devices, but it was soon discovered that explosives made from refned radical compounds can power the same disruptive effect. These devices formed the backbone of Operation Bottleneck and Latvian Gambit, which were designed to limit where insect spirits could manifest to a single secure location.**

**These devices were used on numerous conjugation points between the Hive and the material plane, shutting off travel between the two. Detroit was the last accessible point on Earth, but was closed in 2081, leaving the portal on Daedalus Station as the only viable access point left to the Hive, possibly for the next few decades.**
