**Places to Meet: crime scene, mass transit, police station Similar Contacts : attorney, corrections offcer, former inmate**

**A crime analyst is another civilian offce worker that provides case support for investigators. They may also study trends in various crimes or criminal organizations. They analyze and research data, input data, and prepare reports that focus on statistical information. The crime analyst position provides crime intelligence to the police department, as they analyze and identify crime patterns and draw the attention of the relevant staff to them. They also assemble suspect and victim profles and offer action plans for fghting and preventing crime.**
