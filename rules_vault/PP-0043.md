## Attack!

After many years in operation, Attack! still isn’t well organized, and that’s the way they like it. They have no over-arching philosophy besides “corps are bad”; if you share that, you’re welcome to help their operations. Their actions have included industrial sabotage, datasteals, exposing corporate secrets, and anything else that will make a corporation look bad. They’re not going to stage a concerted campaign to bring single corp down, but if you can find a way to overlap with their efforts, they can briefly serve as a force multiplier.
