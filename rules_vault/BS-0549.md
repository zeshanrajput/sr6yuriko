## Natural Bioware

Any bioware augmentation that is eligible for critters may be grown as part of its body as a genetic augmentation. The Essence cost of the bioware is reduced to zero, but only one point of Essence (based on standard grade) may be applied in this way. Unlike the attribute augmentation described above, any attribute increases from this genetic augmentation count toward the critter’s augmented maximum.

For the purpose of determining the cost of the formula, the threshold for Biotech Extended tests, or the nuyen cost for hiring a laboratory, the rating of this genetic augmentation is ten times the standard-grade Essence cost.

For example: A standard-grade rating 2 synaptic accelerator normally costs 1 Essence, so the rating for a genetic augmentation would be 10. Standard-grade platelet factories normally cost 0.2



Essence, so the rating for the genetic enhancement would be 2.
