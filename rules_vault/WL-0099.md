## **Shadow Yak**

## **> Lyran**

## **(Bos umbras)**

**I’m not sure if this is a genemod or some quirk of evolution in an area that is rife with tribal arguments that escalate quickly and require individuals to get around quietly, but either way it’s some funny drek. For those who don’t know, yaks have been used as domesticated animals for a variety of purposes up in the mountains. They’re hardy, they’re sturdy, and they don’t taste too bad in a pinch. The thing about the shadow yak is its peculiar purpose, which is stealthily transporting metahumans from region to region in the mountains.**
