## Matrix Attribute Advancement

You have become extremely good with some aspects of the Matrix or have found unconventional ways to enhance your persona.

- **Cost:** 15 Karma/level (max level 3 per Matrix attribute)

- **Game Effect:** Choose a Matrix attribute: Attack, Sleaze, Data Processing, or Firewall. This Matrix attribute is always increased by the level of this quality. This increase applies

You may take this quality multiple times in order to advance multiple different Matrix attributes.
