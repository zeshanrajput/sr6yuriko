## Corporate Scapegoat

Corporate politics are cutthroat, and when a ladder climber makes a bad decision, someone else is going to need to take the fall. You were on the receiving side of some corp politic games that left you on the outside.

**Bonus:** 5 Karma

Game Effect: Anytime you are present for social interactions with members of your former corporation, your team is unable to gain or spend Edge. When operating in that corporation’s controlled region, treat all security zones as one level higher.
