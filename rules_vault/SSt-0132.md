## **Game Information**

## **Purification Straw**

**Using this items can flter toxins from drinking water. When liquid is sipped through this straw, the Power of non- Awakened liquid toxins is reduced by 3. Awakened toxins are unaffected. Availability: 3 Cost: 250¥**
