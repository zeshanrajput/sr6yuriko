## Horizon

Being the small fish in a big pond leaves Horizon in a precarious place, but being so (relatively) small often allows them to operate in ways the other big fish just overlook because they’re too busy battling the other predators. They’ve got an interest in Kechibi just like everyone else, but their efforts are small and extremely focused. All thanks to the Consensus. The masterwork program (or is it something more?) behind this tiny titan is after the code and doing an excellent job of pointing its agents toward possible copies but never quite hitting the mark. I got word from a chummer over in Africa that some Dawkins spook was poking around Lagos but missed his chance. Close but no reward, and they were back to the target board.
