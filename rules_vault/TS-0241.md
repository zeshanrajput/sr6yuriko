## **Tango**

**Vector: Injection Speed: Immediate Duration: 1 x 1D6 minutes Power: 13 Ef ect : +2 Reaction, +3D6 Initiative**

**In recent years, Jazz has become less and less efective at allowing an unaugmented cop to compete with augmented suspects on a somewhat even playing feld. The patrol ofcers call this experimental drug “Tango,” as it’s a faster version of Jazz. When Tango wears of, the user crashes, must resist 4S damage, and gains the Dazed, Confused I, and Fatigued I statuses (p. 52, SR6 ) for a duration equal to the duration of the initial efect. Tango makes people as jumpy and hyper as cafeinated two-year-olds with a monster sugar rush.**
