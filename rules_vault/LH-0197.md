## March 2, 2083

The area around Maruike Park didn’t pan out for finding Tosh, but we got a line on who may have taken him. The problem is our hacker did a little digging, and we’re not the team for going up against the world’s largest megacorp … yup, it’s MCT. A local gang is snagging forgettable folk for

a fee. The buyer links to a corp named Kochi Optics, which links to a corp named Kochi Industries, which is a wholly owned subsidiary of Mitsuhama Computer Technologies. Could be this guy’s personal or side gig, but we aren’t the caliber of team to go find out.
