## **What Factions (6WC, p. 190-193) are in play for Reputation and Heat?**

For Seattle missions, there are no tracked factions. These two scores are general area Reputation and

Heat.

For New Orleans, the primary factions include Aurelian Design, Epoch, Francisco Transport, Guild of Digital Killers, Kozlowski Family, League of Laveau, Order of the Golden Dawn, River People, Riverboat Alliance, Romero Family, and Zobop. Heat is not tracked by faction for New Orleans. Reputation is already factored into Mission initial offers.

Use the average Factional Reputation of the entire team for the purposes of interactions and calculations.
