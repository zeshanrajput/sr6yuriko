## Ninjutsu

Though widely known in popular culture as assassins, ninja were historically infiltrators and spies. In contrast to the samurai (and much like shadowrunners), ninjas were willing to employ any number of “dishonorable” techniques to win battles and achieve their objectives. Stealth and quickly disabling opponents were key tenets of ninjutsu (sometimes known as _taijutsu_ ). Ninja combat arts also included many weapons techniques, most notably the use of the shuriken.
