## Follow Through

[Trigger: Successful Social Skill Test, Action: Minor (Anytime)] 5 Edge

When a good point is made, it’s time to seize that moment and push for greater impact. When successful on a Social skill test, the player character may use this Edge action to take another immediate Social skill test as a Minor Action. This additional Social skill test must be a separate attempt than the initial skill test. It doesn’t allow one to simply pile on more hits from a second roll in a negotiation.

For example: If someone is successful at tricking a guard into believing they aren’t needed at a location, you can take a Follow Through Edge action to convince them they’re needed elsewhere with an Influence (Leadership) test.

Note: This Edge action counts as an Edge expenditure for the initial skill test. This means Follow Through can’t be used on another Edge boost or Edge action, unless there’s an exception that allows you to use more than one Edge boost or action on a test. If Follow Through is used, Edge can’t be gained or spent on the additional action.
