## eaten from within

When the host takes resisted damage greater than their Body, or they enter overflow, the symbiont organism turns parasitic. This can also occur during a critical glitch while actively using the benefits of the symbiont. While the symbiont is parasitic, the host suffers the loss of one box from their physical damage chart per week. Worse, this lost box cannot be healed until the parasite is removed. Removing a symbiont turned parasitic requires a successful Biotech + Logic (12, 1 hour) Extended test.
