## **Headquarters:** Humanis

- Corps all want us drinking their branded swill. New Orleans won’t have it. We’ve always been known for our fine taste, and it’s much too fine for vat-grown grapes.

New Orleans’ outskirts represent the smattering of small towns and parishes that are technically just outside of city limits but nonetheless consider themselves part of the city. Out here is where you’re going to fi nd the very best po’boys and cheap food in the entire CAS. It’s homelike, comfortable, and honestly an easier place to live daily life than in the city proper. Kids riding bikes, little cottage homes, good schools, local churches, that kind of life.
