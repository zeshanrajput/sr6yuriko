## Pathological Liar

These contacts aren’t great for getting information, but sometimes you just need them to get you stuff. These contacts are great when you don’t want others to figure out what you’re doing, and they can’t help but lie about what they’ve done for you. Did you buy that highly illegal drug? No worries, because the dealer told the faction you were there to pick up your prescription for novacoke. It can go the other way though, like you just asked them pick you up something legal, and they go and tell the cops you robbed them.

**Game Effect:** Whenever someone interrogates the contact, roll a Loyalty (1) test. If the test is successful, then the lie works in your favor. On a failure, the lie makes things worse for the team, increasing the Heat Modifier by +1, or increasing the Heat by +1 if done at the end of the run.
