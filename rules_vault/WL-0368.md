## **Forage/Hunt**

**You can train your critter to fnd its own food, which reduces the added monthly cost of feeding them. If you have trained your critter to forage or hunt, the critter makes an Outdoors or Perception + Intuition test. For every hit on this test, treat the critter’s Body as one point lower for the purpose of determining the cost of feeding them. If they score as many hits as their Body attribute, they fnd all of their own food for the month. This food is highly perishable, so extra hits (beyond their Body attribute) provide no additional beneft.**

**If the critter glitches on this test, they become injured while foraging or hunting, taking 6P damage (resisted using Body).**

**On a critical glitch, the critter becomes lost. If you can’t track them down somehow, they may be lost for good. By default, tracking your lost critter requires an Outdoors (Tracking) + Intuition Extended test (6, 1 hour).**
