## Matrix Defense Suite

Critters with wireless-enabled devices and augmentations are vulnerable to being hacked. A Matrix defense suite provides a rugged and bulky array of electronics that provide additional defenses against hostile Matrix activity.

**Game Information:** This module provides a bonus to the critter’s Firewall equal to its rating and takes up capacity equal to half its rating (round up). The maximum rating for this type of module is 6. A critter must be augmented with a critterjack and a device that provides a Firewall in order to benefit from the Matrix defense suite—it does not provide a Firewall attribute if none already exists. If the critter is protected by an external Firewall, such as from a PAN, host, or complex form, thebonus from this module only applies to its own Matrix defense tests—other devices connected to the network that are not equipped by (or implanted in) by the critter are unaffected. The critter’s Firewall may be boosted by other sources, such as the Infusion complex form, but its Firewall cannot be increased to more than double its original rating.
