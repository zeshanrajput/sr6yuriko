## Lord Vendaval

Lord Vendaval is cool and calculating sylph. He is the military commander to the queen’s army, with various detachments of elementals stationed at the Octahedron. He is skilled in some ethereal martial arts, to a level few other elementals have achieved. His fairness and courage to lead from the front has earned respect for his position.
