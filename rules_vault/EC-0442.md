## Stoker’s Coffin Motel

Barebones doesn’t begin to describe this squatter-level default, where a few nuyen buys you a spot to sleep and literally nothing else. Even the vending machines have been looted into less than scrap. But then, five nuyen for a night of almost-safety has its value.
