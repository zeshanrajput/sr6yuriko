## Cyberfins

Retractable webs and supporting struts are implanted as a pair into the user’s hands or feet. While deployed, the user gains Edge on Athletics (Swimming) Tests and increases the base swimming speed (p. 93, _SR6_ ) by 2 meters per round per pair of cyberfins. It takes a Minor Action to deploy or retract a pair of cyberfins, and they cannot be deployed while the user is wearing gloves or footwear on the relevant body parts. Note that deployed cyberfins are rigid and complicate fine motor work. The user cannot gain or spend Edge on any test when the affected body parts are involved in an Agility test other than swimming. This includes attacks made by handheld weapons, running, etc.

**Cyberware overdrive:** Reduce the threshold for an Athletics (Swimming) test by 1, to a minimum of 0. Convert one die to a wild die.

**Wireless bonus:** Gain a bonus Minor Action after spending a Minor Action to deploy or retract a pair of cyberfins.
