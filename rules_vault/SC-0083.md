## Backgrounds

The summoner who focuses solely in the realm of beasts is a unique individual. They can come to their path from many directions, but three seem to predominate the tales of most beast summoners: cultural choice, counter-cultural choice, or drawn by the spirits themselves. While these are broad strokes, they are easy starting points from which a player can build a deeper character.
