## **Resources**

**This is the fun stuff! This society has some of the widest-ranging resources of anyone I’ve poked my nose into. Their properties include chateaus in the heart of France, mines throughout Africa, entire villages in Amazonia, castles across Europe,**

**penthouses across the globe, and every other level of property one could imagine. The Echoes themselves cover a broad range of wealthy types and a few who are not as rich in wealth as they are in fanaticism, though none are hurting for cash. Their connections are just as diverse, with some sitting on boards of megas, or even more common, holding quite a bit of their stock, enough to infuence corporate values. That gives them a touch of control and connection to all the powers within those corps. That’s not even looking at the skills and wealth of knowledge possessed by the Echoes themselves. As I’ve said, it’s a large enough society that I don’t have the time to cover every individual, nor can I even claim to know them all, but I can put out a few specifcs that I’m aware of.**

**Let’s start with connections. One is a member of the fragging Seelie Court, giving him access to information beyond our own metaplane of knowledge. He’s old and has had centuries (at the least) to study and learn, but more importantly he’s had that time to make connections in the physical plane, his own plane, and whatever other metaplanes he’s visited. Wheeling and dealing for metaplanar knowledge has him owed favors by execs all over MCT and Aztechnology, not to mention both Tírs. We have political fgures who trade power and favors for access, and while the world’s big governments may not have the power of the Big Ten, they still have plenty to bargain with. The best connections come from the old-money families who’ve built up favors and friends through generations rather than just years. These people call in favors from previous centuries, and thanks to most people’s short memories or reliance on current data with so much lost in the Crash, the connections aren’t easily made, leaving runners scratching their heads on how some old-money coot in Seattle is pulling strings with a farming village in India. If you’re working for, with, or against the Echoes, don’t expect everything to make sense.**

**While I mentioned some broad-stroke locations above, let’s point out a few specifc examples. Destiny Grounds , just outside of Sacramento in Cal Free, is a sprawling estate with a massive main mansion, six private guest houses, and property that includes a small lake, several gardens, and a hedge maze that changes with each aspect of the lunar cycle. It is also the location of the Blue Moon Gala, held at each blue moon, where Echoes gather to brag and show off their latest acquisitions. It’s a popular target for info-gathering jobs but is considered neutral ground. No member of the Echoes would ever contract a job to acquire a card during it. If you’re hired for a grab at this event, it’s an outsider, and you can expect the Echoes to come after them for violating their sacred space. The Telskirtain mine in Azania is a former diamond mine that seems to belong to the Echoes as a whole. It is another neutral location, but it doesn’t host events.**
