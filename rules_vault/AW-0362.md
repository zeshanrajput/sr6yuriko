## The Lava Lake

Opposite the Great Lake, on the east side of the volcano, the mountainside glows red-orange due to the lava that flows slowly but continuously down into a smaller lava lake. The surface of the lava lake is obscured from distant observation by fog and smoke. The land beyond it is the island’s desert. The creatures that hunt near the lava lake have a very low center of gravity. They will bite your legs and try to drag you down. Some of themcan breathe fire as if they were dragons, but the resemblance only goes as far as their flammable halitosis and scaly skin.
