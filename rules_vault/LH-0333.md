## FAB I

The original version of FAB, this variety is highly sensitive to impacts from other astral forms, rupturing from almost any astral collision and emitting a chemical marker that fluoresces under ultraviolet light. This phenomenon only occurs after the bacterium is ruptured by such a collision. During the end of its life cycle, FAB does not release the marker.
