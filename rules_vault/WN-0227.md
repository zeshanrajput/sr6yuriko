## Wrapping it Up in a Bow

When presented with all the details, and another sample of the same type of fabric that Gu Guanyu can provide to them, Becky Wu Ping will have what is known by some as Mnemonic Integration, basically new memories build and call back old ones. The fabric is the final piece of the puzzle, which upon Becky’s own recovery will begin to shape and change directly into the dress for Gabrielle Spindrad. Watching it is a thing of near magic.
