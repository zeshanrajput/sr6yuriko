## Hea ling Gris-Gris

The creation of gris-gris is different from the process of artifi cing and enchantment. Instead of imbuing the object with magic, the practitioner is calling upon the spirit that resides within the object and requesting a specifi c boon. An example that I have used in the past was a healing charm created by Michael when I was recovering from a particularly nasty run. Wearing this fetish while recovering, I could tell that it was helping me heal from the wounds that I had taken. He described it as the spirit of the talisman interceding on my behalf with Papa Gede, allowing me to not feel the tug of the afterlife. He described creating the wooden amulet as beseeching the spirit to aid me, giving it both gifts of fried plantains and cigars, as well as reagents.

These charms come in a variety of forms. The most common of these are related to the lwa Ghede, which are best used for longer-term recovery and don’t provide any meaningful help on fi rst aid or initial sets of healing. Charms that assist on fi rst aid or initial healing require calling on the aid of Erzulie Mansur. When purchasing, make sure that the symbols on the charm match that of the lwa needed to intercede. If the symbol or colors are incorrect, you risk offending the lwa—or perhaps even communing with the wrong lwa—and may fi nd yourself in worse condition than before.

Healing gris-gris of Ghede provide (Force) dice as a dice pool bonus for long term recovery or natural recovery. Healing gris-gris of Erzulie Mansur provide (Force x 2) dice as assistance as teamwork on Healing tests for fi rst aid, medkit, or magic.
