## these character creation recommendations apply to all provided backgrounds

- **Attributes:** Charisma 6, Willpower 5, Magic 6

- **Skills:** Conjuring 5 or 6, Sorcery 5 or 6, Con 5, Stealth 4+

- **Knowledge skills:** Arcana, Magical Societies, Magical Traditions, Spirit Types

- **Spells:** Improved Invisibility, Levitate, Magic Fingers, Physical Barrier, Physical Mask

- **Positive qualities:** Aptitude (Con, Conjuring, or Sorcery), Astral Chameleon, Exceptional (Charisma or Willpower), First Impression, Focused Concentration, Mentor Spirit (Coyote), Shamanic Mask

- **Negative qualities:** Addiction (Novacoke, Level 1 or 2), Impaired (Body or Strength), Spirit Bane (choose a spirit type you have offended)

- **Life modules:** Artist, Astral Explorations, Charlatan, Entertainer, Magical Studies, Street Shaman, Event: You struggled through some tough times.
