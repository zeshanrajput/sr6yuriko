## Quick Slivers

The Quick Slivers are a Mafia go-gang with close ties to the Gianelli family. Each of the bikes has been chromed out, and gang members are given store credit at a few chop shops to add custom touches. They harass the competition or run down any threats to Mafia-owned property. The Gianelli family doesn’t hold the reins very tightly, so the Quick Slivers operate a lot on their own will.
