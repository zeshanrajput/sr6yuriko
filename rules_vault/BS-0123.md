## Internal Router (Rating 1-4)

Modern cybernetics rely heavily on wireless communication. Protection against hackers is a major concern on both sides of the law. Ordinarily, the best defense against exploitation is to disable wireless and operate any implants solely via neuro-mechanical control, but this tactic degrades the full effectiveness of one’s cyberware. An internal router employs the user’s neural network as “hardwired” direct connections so other cyberware do not need to be wireless-enabled to benefit from any wireless bonuses they may have. The internal router must still wirelessly connect to a communications device such as a datajack or commlink. When any of the user’s augmentations are targeted by a Matrix action or complex form, add one die equal to the internal router’s rating to defend against that attack.
