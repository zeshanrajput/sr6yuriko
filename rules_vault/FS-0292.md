**Notes:** Small blast shields provide +1 to Defense Rating for each shield installed (max +2). Large blast shields provide +2 (max +4) for each shield installed. Large blast shields decrease movement by three meters per round in all categories when one is used, five meters if two are used. Use of two large blast shields also grants the user the Cover I status, but it does not require the additional Minor Action to employ/fire the weapon. **Slot:** Barrel, Top, Underbarrel
