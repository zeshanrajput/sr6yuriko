## Laés

(Leäl, Laésal Wine)

- **Vector:** Ingestion, Inhalation, Injection

- **Speed:** 1 combat round

- **Duration:** 20 x 1D6 minutes

- **Power:** 12

- **Effect:** Stun damage, memory loss

This drug is made from the Awakened laésal fruit, which is thought to only grow in government-controlled orchards in Tir Tairngire. Upon taking a dose of laés, the user resists 12S damage. If they fail to resist all the damage, they fall unconscious for the drug’s duration while the last (12 – Body, minimum 1) hours are wiped from the user’s mind. This works backward from the point the drug was taken, changing the chemical structure of the brain and rendering it impossible to retrieve the memories using technology or magic. Laés can be ingested, injected, or even rolled into specially prepared nicsticks.

Laésal wine is a brandy made from the laésal fruit. Its effects are the same as taking laés, while leäl is a less potent version (6S damage). Leäl has the same effect as laés, but the duration is only 5 x 1D6 minutes and only erases (120 – Body, minimum 100) minutes of memories. All three drugs are not addictive.
