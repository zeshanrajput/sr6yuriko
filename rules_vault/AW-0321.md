## Pirate Island Coliseum

The largest structure on Pirate Island is a massive coliseum, roughly the size and shape of a modern sports arena. Instead of astroturf and goalposts, the playing field is a marvel of engineering that allows for dramatic shifts in the environment of the arena. It can be configured as an open field, multiple smaller arena spaces, or even a shifting maze.



Many different events happen in the arena. Some of these are sporting events—games or races. Most of the events are combats between spirits, critters, and even visiting metahumans. The Cloud Corsairs pay a high price for unusual and dangerous critters captured on one of the cloud islands, and even more for critters brought from other metaplanes. Spirits and metahumans participate in the games for a chance at getting rich—or as a consequence of not paying their debts. These combats are rarely straightforward, stand-up fights. Usually, the arena is configured to provide plenty of cover, hiding spots, and flanking opportunities. The most popular event is a free-for-all in which critters and spirits are deployed separately, and without weapons. Various weapon caches can be found throughout the arena, and the winners of such events are almost always the ones who are lucky enough to find the best caches. The spirits do not seem to have any ethical problems with the nature of these games, perhaps because any spirits killed in the arena are only disrupted instead of dying.
