## 01-A Who Is The Enemy?

To recap: Disians are a race of extra-metaplanar beings who have infiltrated our home metaplane in order to harvest magical energies (mana) by methods we don’t fully understand. To help facilitate this, Disians have forged extensive alliances throughout the Sixth World. In exchange, these Disians offer advanced magical and technological information. Disians have also directly assisted on certain projects and operations. They do not act directly; instead, they prefer to act through allies, agents, or other proxies. Whatever their endgame, we’ve confirmed that the Disians are responsible for a significant number of flashpoint threat events in recent months.
