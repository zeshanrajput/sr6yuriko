## dark currency

## Coins of Woe

In the metaplanes, shadow spirits can regurgitate collected suffering and pain into a pile of what appear to be coins— coins that can be used for services, goods, or rainy-day snacks. The coins of woe condense metahuman essence and the pain and suffering endured in the extraction. One side of the coin shows the image of the person it was taken from. Metahuman coins are gold in color, while most spirits (collected force) are silver; presumably, the metahuman essence is more valuable. Only the energy drained in the last twenty-four hours by a shadow spirit can be expelled as coins; after that, the energy has been absorbed by the spirit. A few metaplanes recognize the coins of woe as currency, though many consider them abhorrent, and possession of them is cause for punishment.
