## June 14, 2083

Tragedy at Skyhook Space Elevator. Initially reported as an accident, reports later classify it as a terrorist attack. Aztechnology president Flavia de la Rosa and two other board members are killed. Aztechnology increases its offensive operations against Defender targets in retaliation, which leads to some of the most brutal fighting of the shadow war.
