## A Rather Ghoulish Event

Evo’s gift to the Gala, a company of Ghoul Dancers from Asamando, is to be given everything they need in order to put on a show for the celebrities. What these ghouls need right now is flesh. A secret arrangement between Evo and the Yakuza led to the acquisition of seven trafficked individuals. Unfortunately, the Yakuza are on high alert in the city, due to rumors that a third party is looking to stir up trouble between the syndicates. So, instead of using their own couriers to deliver the goods, the Yakuza turn to the runners through one of their fixers.

The Yakuza are offering 10,000 nuyen. The job is simple: Pick up a truck at the docks in Battery City. It’s been coded to your CityPasses. Don’t look in the cargo area. Your code won’t grant you access, because it isn’t your business. You’ve been granted clearance to enter the Met during the Gala. Deliver the truck to a loading dock at the Met. Enter through the basement garage. Leave the truck. Walk back out of the garage where you will be met by a man with your payment.

This is a straightforward job. The key here is suspense. The ride to the Met is broken into a minimum of six segments, each defined by a roll. Each of the segments is represented on the **Drive**
