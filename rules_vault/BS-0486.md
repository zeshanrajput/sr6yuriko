## Venom Reservoir

Without a venom reservoir, the venom fangs augmentation only carries enough of a drug or toxin for a single dose. In order to carry multiple doses, a critter must be implanted with a venom reservoir.

**Game Information:** The venom reservoir can carry a number of additional doses of a single drug or toxin equal to its rating. Each venom reservoir must be loaded with one type of drug or toxin, but if multiple venom reservoir augmentations are installed, each reservoir may be loaded with a separate drug or toxin. Switching between different drugs or toxins requires a Control Device Minor Action. If the critter is implanted with a critterjack, they may be trained to activate a designated reservoir on command—though the critter is not capable of understanding which toxins or drugs are loaded in their augmentations.

**Wireless Bonus:** Gain an extra Minor Action after using a Control Device Minor Action to switch between available venom reservoirs.
