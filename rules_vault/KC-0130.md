## TerraFirst!

Okoro had a connection to this terrorist organization before she ever had the code and the money that came from it. She now moves funds to TerraFirst! for investigations into ecological issues and even to create minor incidents in order to allow other investigators onto the scene to find larger problems.

This terrorist group focuses a lot of their efforts in Lagos around two areas: trash and oil. The oil industry in the area is a massive moneymaker, but funding resources to keep it safe would cut into their massive profits, thus the industry continues to maintain low safety standards. As for trash, TerraFirst! finds the lack of government support in handling garbage a major issue. The larger problem is that it would be relatively easy for the government to handle it and it would provide decent jobs to Lagosians, replacing the slave labor being used to gather recyclables from the trash for corporations to use in cheap remanufacturing.

As an eco-terrorist organization, TerraFirst! commits illegal and dangerous acts all over Lagos. The individual members tend to act in whatever way they feel is best, meaning some care about collateral damage while others only care about pushing the agenda for a cleaner planet regardless of the damage they cause to people and structures (and even the environment) in order to stop their targets from polluting.
