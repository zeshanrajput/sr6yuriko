## Ace in the Hand

Sometimes you have just the right piece of info or leverage you can hold over someone else. That information just made you more dangerous during any negotiation, intimidation, or basic social interaction. You receive a +1 dice pool bonus per level to any Influence + Charisma or Logic test involving that person.
