## Speed Racer

You can drive fast, but it’s always faster when you have someone to beat—or catch.

- **Cost:** 8 Karma

- **Game Effect:** When you are in a chase or race, gain a point of Edge at the start of every round. This Edge can only go into your Chase pool (p. 175).

- **Game Effect:** When piloting a vehicle of that specialization, Acceleration and Speed Interval are increased by 2. At the 4 Karma level, the character only gains this benefit when they are manually driving the vehicle. At the 8 Karma level, the character gains the benefit when they are physically connected to the vehicle (manually driving or jacked in).
