## Water Jet

A water jet system includes an intake, compressor, and exhaust port all installed into a cyberlimb. A maximum of one water jet can be installed per cyberlimb. For each water jet system, a user gains one bonus hit on an Athletics (Swimming) test.

**Cyberware overdrive** : A water jet system adds +2 dice to an Athletics (Swimming) test, but one die must be a wild die. If the wild die is a 1, the water jet system will not function until repaired.
