## Optional Rule: Contact Keywords (continued)

## Scapegoat

Some contacts just look guilty, or maybe they actually did something wrong. The local law enforcement service providers know it. The local gangs know it. But somehow, they still always seem to grab the choicest gear. It seems that having some notoriety can be good because everyone knows you.

**Game Effect:** The contact can acquire information or gear as if their Connection was 1 higher except during character generation. Any time a team uses the contact and also gains Heat, increase the amount of Heat gained by 1.
