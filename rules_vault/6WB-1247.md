## Gel

These gel-ball rounds look like flattened blueberries on impact. Generally used for riot control or non-lethal subdual, they’re great for knocking a target on their hoop, though you shouldn’t expect that to work on trolls. Gel rounds inflict Stun damage. A target struck by a gel round must succeed at an immediate Agility (2) or Body (4) test (player’s choice) or gain the Prone status (p. 53). Burst Fire and Full Auto increase the threshold to avoid being knocked down by 1.
