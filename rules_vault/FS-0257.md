## Spikes, Retractable

Same concept as regular spikes, but retractable so the weapon can look less deadly if you choose, and you can switch between the types of damage you inflict.


## spikes, retractable

**Note:** Weapon commlinks have a device rating 1, Data Processing 1, and Firewall 2. Its Firewall attribute is cumulative with other devices, such as commlinks, cyberjacks, or tactical networks. If the weapon is bricked, it loses all wireless connection/bonuses but can still fire manually. Repairs for weapon commlinks are the same as for cyberdecks.
