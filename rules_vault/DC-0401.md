## Tuned Suspension

Most modern ground vehicles are designed to operate on smooth-surfaced roads while a few are designed to work well in any terrain. With a tuned suspension, a vehicle’s under chassis is modified, setting the suspension for a specific terrain, increasing the vehicle’s performance in that terrain and worsening the performance in other environments. A tuned suspension halves Speed Interval modifiers (rounding up) in either on-road or offroad environments, depending on the setup. Whiledriving in the environment the vehicle was not tuned for, the pilot may not gain Edge.
