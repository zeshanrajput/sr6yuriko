## **High Lifestyle**

**Those who make enough nuyen to support a High or Luxury lifestyle are rarely told that they can’t have what they want—provided they’re willing to pay for it. Exotic pets aren’t exactly common among the wealthy elite, but they aren’t an unusual sight or a forbidden luxury. Anything that causes too much trouble for your neighbors or disrupts the local ambiance can still get you into trou-**

**ble—your neighbors may “ooh” and “ah” over your prized purebred hell hound, but not if their rose bushes are set on fre.**
