## **How do I apply injection toxins to a weapon (** _**SR6**_ **, p. 121)?**

Injection vector toxins may be administered by a bladed weapon (p. 121, SR6W) that deals physical damage. To use, it is a Major Action (I) and requires a successful Biotech + Logic (2) test to apply a toxin to a suitable weapon in such a way that it retains its potency for 5 minutes, with an additional minute per net hit. The first attack that succeeds with at least two net hits (or one net hit if the target has no armor) will deliver the toxin as well as the damage from the weapon. If that successful attack has less net hits, the toxin is wiped off the blade without being delivered into the bloodstream and will not be added to the damage from the hit. On a glitch or crit glitch for the Biotech test, you cut yourself on the blade and must resist the toxin. Sheathing the blade does not wipe away the venom, however retracting an envenomed cyberspur or razors will require the runner to take a toxin resistance test and the toxin is now internal to the street samurai.
