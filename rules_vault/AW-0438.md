## Willpower

A Willpower mutation causes a hunger that cannot be satisfied. This may manifest as a physical hunger, or it can feel like withdrawal symptoms from an addiction.

**Game Effect:** All Edge boosts and actions cost twice as many points of Edge on tests that use Willpower. If you gain a second Willpower mutation, increase the threshold on all of tests made to heal you (first aid, medkit, Heal spell, natural healing, etc.) by 1.
