## Motivational Teacher (I)

Having the right know-how is important for teachers, but never doubt the strength of a rousing speech to really help a student believe in themselves. Spend a Major Action and make an Influence (Leadership) + Charisma (3) test. On a success, your students gain a point of Edge for use on any Memory test regarding the topic.
