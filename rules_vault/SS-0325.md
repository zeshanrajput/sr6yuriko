## Modern Druid

## (Professional Rating 3)

Despite the rapid development and integration of technology into everyday life of the Sixth World, there are some who eschew it and seek out a simpler, less complicated life in more rugged and primitive areas of the world. In the past, these individuals would have been called mountain men or women. These individuals have learned how to live in harmony with nature. And while often highly independent (bordering of stubborn), they make excellent guides when the job leaves the confines of the modern city—or when one finds themselves without a wireless signal.
