## Nanotech Kits

Like the nanites used as augmentations, nanotech kits are purchased as a single-use refillable package. Kits consist of a dedicated controller with a rating 1 nanite pilot program and contain a single volume of nanites. Unlike colony nanites, nanotech kits can never be placed into overdrive.

**Demolisher Spray:** This kit functions exactly as a single-volume use of demolisher nanites. The primary difference being that the kit is applied directly and not extruded from the skin.

**Dynamic Handprint Spray:** As with similar crossovers, this kit functions exactly as a single-volume use of dynamic handprint nanites. This kit is applied directly to the skin without the need to be extruded.

**Nanocosmetics:** This product claims to take ten years off your complexion and repair years of skin damage. Nanocosmetics actually do what they claim, and the result is that your skin has a healthy glow that lasts for eight hours or until you remove the cosmetics. While a user is wearing this nano treatment, they gain a point of Edge on Influence and Con tests, which must be used on the test or lost.

**Nanopaste Disguise:** Using the same nanites as a dynamic features colony, the nanopaste disguise follows the same rules. The paste can be programmed to copy biometric data and forms

a structure over the skin that responds to muscle movements underneath it, allowing the disguise to move more naturally with the wearer.

**Nanospy:** The nanospy spray is a fantastic addition to an infiltrator’s toolkit. When sprayed on a surface, a layer of nanites constructs a camera and microphone with a visual and audio range of five meters. The construct also can transmit its data up to one kilometer. The entire assembly takes up thirty square centimeters, appearing as a slight discoloration, which requires a Perception + Intuition (4) test with an appropriate knowledge skill to identify. The spray can be applied to any surface, from walls to cars to the family dog. The constructed sensor suite lasts for twenty-four hours before falling to dust.

**Nanotat Spray:** This kit forms a thin polymer layer on the surface of the skin that can generate a range of full-color, full-motion displays, simulating the older LED tattoos. The colors and patterns can be changed on demand with the controller. Unlike a true nanotattoo colony, the spray wears off after eight hours.

**Savior Medkit:** Small enough to carry in a pocket, the savior medkit has the diagnostic software of a rating 6 medkit. Instead of pharmaceuticals, physical sutures, and bandages, the savior medkit contains a single volume of savior nanites to treat the user’s injuries. The kit operates as a rating 6 medkit (p. 281, _SR6_ ) or as a trauma patch. Note that the savior medkit does not gain the faster treatment time of the savior system colony.

**Universal Sealant:** This nanite goo spreads quickly across an area up to one meter in diameter and can act as a seal. The primary application of this nanotech is in space or undersea, where the integrity of your atmospheric seal is critical for survival. Once the spray has been put in place, it transforms into a polymer sheet that adheres to nearby material within fifteen seconds. The kit can be set to form either a rigid plastic (Structure Rating 5) or a flexible polymer sheet (Structure Rating 4).
