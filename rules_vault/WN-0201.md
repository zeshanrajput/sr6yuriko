## Just Staying in My Pay Grade

The runners should be doing what they are supposed to be doing all the time, either working a job or looking for a job. If they just finished mission 6, **Cutting Edges** , the same Mr. Johnson will reach out to them. Otherwise, this job can be introduced by El Guapo (p. 141).

The runners are hired to investigate a disturbance occurring right now in Central Park. A

half-dozen or so confused persons wearing nothing but medical gowns have been reported wandering aimlessly in the vicinty of the Alice in Wonderland statue. None of them seem to know who they are, or how they got here.

Nothing this crazy happens in Manhattan without a reason, and the runners’ employer wants to be among the first to figure out what’s going on here. Mr. Johnson offers 1,000 nuyen to each runner if they drop whatever they’re doing now and head immediately to the park to investigate (discreetly, of course), and a 1,000 nuyen bonus if they dig up any juicy paydata. Runners can negotiate an extra 100 nuyen per het hit on an Influence + Charisma Opposed test. Mr. Johnson is paying a premium for prompty action, but the matter is time-sensitive, which gives the runners a bargaining advantage.
