## > **Tacoma Dome Hotel**

You can do worse than the Dome. Big rooms, good food, complimentary breakfast, and it’s close to all that transportation Tacoma is known and used for. Security is decent, but the determined assassin or spy can find their way in, so make sure to compensate if you stash an asset here or the J wants a meet in the restaurant.

> Traveler Jones
