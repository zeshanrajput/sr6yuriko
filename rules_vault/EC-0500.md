## Shadow Lake Correctional Facility (Maple Valley)

Shadow Lake is a medium-security facility for white-collar criminals, typically repeat Matrix violators. The new wing is entirely detention facilities for technomancers. It relies on a combination of Matrix shielding, throwback equipment for the guards, and paranormal watch-critters. The rest of the prison still relies heavily on electronic countermeasures and drones
