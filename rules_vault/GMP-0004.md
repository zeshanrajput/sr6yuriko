## First Taste 

Runners can participate in a First Taste adventure. These are introductions to the world of Shadowrun and should give the players a taste of what their character archetypes can do and let them roll some dice. These are geared to run in two hours. One option is to run Food Fight or the adventure in the Beginners Box. Another option is to create an adventure on the fly, but make sure that you are tailoring the adventure to what is at your table. If you have a rigger, allowing them to use their drones will help them experience what they need to when they get into the longer living campaign. These adventures grant the characters at most 3 Karma and 3,000 nuyen on adventure completion.
