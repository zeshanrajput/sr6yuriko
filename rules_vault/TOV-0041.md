## Qualit y Path: Priest (Houngan or Mambo)

A priesthood of Voodoo is a part of their community and acts as a guide to those around them. They honor the hidden world and call upon the lwa as their guides as they work for those around them. In the beginning of your path to priesthood, you assisted the different priests in the ceremonies and provide your help in caring for the hounfour. But over time, your responsibilities grew as you began leading the ceremonies, mentoring others, and healing those that needed it.

**Game Effect:** To take this quality, you must have a Voodoo knowledge skill. Hounsi are considered respected members of the community, granting you a point of Edge for all Social tests involving those near where your hounfour is located. You automatically gain a group contact for the local temple community (Connection 1, Loyalty 2), which can be bought to higher Connection and Loyalty with additional Karma at the time of taking the quality. Hounsi also gain a point of Edge when assisting any ritual spellcasting, which can be spent or transferred to the ritual leader on a 1-for-1 basis (instead of the usual 2-for1). Harming another member of the community will result in the permanent expulsion from the group (see Bokor) and loss of the group contact. This results in the loss of the Hounsi, Sur Pwen, and Asogwe Qualities—which cannot be bought again.
