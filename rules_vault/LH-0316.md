## Shaheed Zahir

## Posted by: Skarn Ka

Global Sandstorm’s mysterious ally is leading inquiries into Spinrad Global’s rivals. His most recent dossier contained investigations on the actual danger behind “the memo,” a threatening message that was sent to the board of directors before being somehow deleted. The teams he assembled to look into the matter fell victim to a series of deadly Matrix attacks, and Shaheed turned to the shadows to find runners willing to risk their life for a piece of info.

- Shaheed Zahir bought the service of Shahmaran, a high-profile data broker who you can only meet in their choice of secured servers within the Matrix. Their persona looks like a large snake that expands to entirely surround their interlocutors during the discussion. Null Sect already assaulted them multiple times without success as they slipped away every time, leaving nothing but empty servers. I believe they are not one individual but a collective of hackers who created this persona specifically to work with Global Sandstorm.

- Sim Eon
