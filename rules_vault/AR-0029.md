## Boys Town 

Formerly home to a social service organization for at-risk youth, Boys Town has gotten a corporate makeover. It’s also swallowed up some nearby neighborhoods—as it became more prestigious, more people wanted to claim it as an address, so they found ways to apply the label to their locales. The end result is that many areas west of downtown claim this name. Executives and managers have moved into the elegant structures while making sure the density of the neighborhood stays low. It’s the perfect place to think about how the wealthy co-opt social movements for their own gain. And then attempt to steal some of their stuff.
