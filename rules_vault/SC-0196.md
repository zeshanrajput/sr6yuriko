## Whack-a-Ganger

Sometimes, gangers get really lucky and get their hands on something whose value they don’t understand. Others, then, scramble to get it back before the gangers realize what they have.

A corporate commlink has fallen into a gang’s hands. There isn’t anything that leaps out as especially valuable on it on first glance—no files marked “top secret prototype”—but careful analysis and some knowledge about the corporation the ’link was taken from reveals there are commcodes for some high-ranking and secretive corp executives.

The executives whose numbers are on the ’link really want it back. Luckily for them, the gangers

don’t have time to analyze the device much, since they keep snatching it away from each other. The ’link is wandering its way through a local barrens, jumping from ganger to ganger as they keep stealing it from each other (because it’s a high-end Erika Elite, not because of the paydata onboard). The decker fighter is asked to follow the commlink through the barrens, tracking it and punching anyone who gets in their way in the face. Hopefully, their journey will end with the recovery of the ’link.
