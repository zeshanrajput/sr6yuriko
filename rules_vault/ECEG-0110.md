## Surfstar Marine Seacop

One other vehicle worth mentioning is going to be a sight coastal dwellers are used to, but those coming from landlocked sprawls probably have never seen. The Surfstar Marine Seacop is a harbor patrol vessel that anyone smuggling in or out of the port should be aware of. Due to nearly all the docks and warehouses that ships unload at being owned

- **Game Effect:** When operating in the bayou or swamp, reduce the cost of Edge actions and boosts by 1 when performing Outdoors, Perception, or Stealth tests.
