## Seattle Coliseum

Home to the Seattle Transonics, the Coliseum has received a generous renovation after their purchase from Federated-Boeing by the Metroplex government (with Horizon backing). The Coliseum is set to re-open within a year for sports, conventions, and concerts with state-of-the-art technology.

- Another clue about Potter’s true loyalties. Still, it’s an effective tactic to promote love of the city and distraction from all this chaos. Tribalism that can link people to their city is invaluable in promoting stability, and there aren’t many things that fit the bill that way like sports teams.

- Doc Spin
