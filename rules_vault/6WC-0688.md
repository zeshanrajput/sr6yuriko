## Suprathyroid Bonuses Stack

**Use this rule** to increase the attractiveness of using this augmentation.

A suprathyroid gland (p. 292, _SR6_ ) offers numerous bonuses, including a rare, unconditional bonus to Body. However, it also grants several other bonuses that render the augmentation incompatible with basic combat ’ware such as wired reflexes or muscle augmentation. Under this optional rule, the suprathyroid gland’s bonuses are exempted from all other augmentations’ restrictions on what they cannot stack with. Its bonuses are still subject to the augmented attribute maximum (p. 37, _SR6_ ).
