## Hotspots

The entirety of this adventure takes place within the confines of Central Park in Manhattan, primarily around the park’s east side near Conservatory Water, the Alice-in-Wonderland play area, and the Loeb Boathouse. Matrix specialists should be able to get eyes on scene and assist the other team members to remain unseen or unnoticed by NYPD and any other authorities who will be poking their noses into this.
