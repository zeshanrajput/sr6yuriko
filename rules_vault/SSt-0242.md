## **Living with Children**

## **How do you work out child care?**

**Now we get into the real adventure. You think running with a partner adds a challenge? Running the shadows while being a parent is a whole new level of diffculty. The two most common reactions when a runner has a child is to retire or abandon the kid. That tells you how rough it is. I’ve kept it going with a kid in the house, though, so let me look at some of the FAQs I hear on this subject.**

**Through a network of babysitters. We have four that we trust implicitly and, between them, can cover all hours of the day. Then we have a handful more that we know we can use if we set up the right guardrails. Life is also a** _**lot**_ **easier when Jack is in school (see next question), since both of us have an extended stretch of time where we can get things done. During non-school hours, we have the rotating babysitters at our house, and I have to tell you, those costs add up. I’m talking hundreds of nuyen a week. I’m telling you, we have every incentive in the world to look for a little extra paydata during a job.**
