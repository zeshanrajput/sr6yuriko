## Corporate Allies

Fourth, Harmonics. One popular metaplanar theory is that each metaplane has its own mana energy signature. The Disians call this “harmonics.” It’s significant because one of the issues with metaplanar travel, from a scientific perspective, is having to synchronize the harmonics and signatures so that portals or gateways remain stable and don’t damage the local manaspheres … or kill travelers. The Disians apparently have mastered their metaplane’s harmonics and metaplanar travel. It also explains why the Disians need our help, social norms aside. They need our knowledge and our technology (ironic) to ensure that the harmonics remain stable and don’t damage either metaplane, at least not before they harvest.

Can’t verify that yet, but I’ll put down good nuyen that I’m right.
