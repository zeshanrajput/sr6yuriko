## Getting Paid

After the broadcast airs, Eager heads to the runners’ apartment to celebrate. He brings a case of soybeer and cracks one open before handing each runner a certified credstick. He is clearly geeked and tells them to stay tuned because it won’t be long before he or someone he knows has more work for them.
