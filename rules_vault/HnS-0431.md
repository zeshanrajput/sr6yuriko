## ECM Integration

This codemod works though any Matrix-connected device and allows it to act as either an area jammer (p. 270, _SR6_ ) or a directional jammer, with the direction controlled by the AI as a Minor Action. This virtual jammer has a rating equal to the rating of the codemod.
