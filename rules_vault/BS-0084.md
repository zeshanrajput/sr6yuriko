## Flex Hands

The bones and connective tissues in the user’s hands are replaced with smart materials that canshift between rigid and flexible states. Flex hands allow the user to unnaturally compress their entire hand and then return it to its normal state without adverse effect or discomfort. They reduce the escape threshold (p. 93, _SR6_ ) by 4 (minimum 0) for tests involving squeezing the augmented hand into or out of a tight space, such as handcuffs. Flex hands cannot be implanted in hands that have been cybernetically replaced or have any other bone augmentations.
