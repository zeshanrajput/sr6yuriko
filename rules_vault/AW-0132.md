## Mote

## (Fire, Force 2)

Motes appear as small, ash-colored moths, barely eight centimeters in length. They flutter around looking for a flame or heat source. They are simple creatures, feeding off the ashes of whatever they find burned. When cold, they cluster together for protection from the elements.
