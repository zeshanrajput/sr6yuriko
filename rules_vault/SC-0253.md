## Matrix Distraction

A powerful and well-connected fixer hires the Dissonant technomancer and their team for a difficult and high-profile run. They must make a sufficiently large distraction to draw the attention of local security forces, and keep the focus of those security forces on them for several minutes. The distraction is part of a larger operation, and it would be best if the Grid Overwatch Division is distracted along with other security forces. The employer wishes the team to deliberately trigger a convergence as part of the distraction. If one of the runners is a GOD-slayer, this is likely an opportunity they won’t want to pass up. The very idea should scare the drek out of anyone else, and so the pay for the run should be on the high side.
