## Earth Gnome

## (Earth, Force 4)

Earth gnomes are not to be mistaken for the gnome metatype or fae gnomes, though there are enough similarities to warrant a future study. Earth gnomes appear as short metahumans with dull-colored, stone-textured skin, and familiar features shaped from clay. They are mostly bald through nature or personal grooming. Any tufts of hair are a coarse asbestos material, and their clothing is of equally dull-colored coarse material, though some add polished stone buttons or shiny accents.
