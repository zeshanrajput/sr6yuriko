## **Understanding those Gathered**

**The forum information in this chapter was put together by one of the Yakut ambassadors known as Marko on JackPoint. On the surface, it was intended to share some basic information and the encounters that other Matrix users had experienced with what may have been Psyche or one of its agents. Several individuals were invited originally, all previous guests or frequent commentators to the shadow site except for one, who was given a special guest pass for one-time-use only. Below the surface, the ambassador is working on several tasks involving the restoration of the person known to him as Atlas, a former colleague and friend. A detail the ambassador has not explained is that recently he was able to secure a copy of the Kechibi code and used it to gather detailed information on those individuals or organizations that could assist him in his task. The list of invitees was derived from the fndings of the Kechibi code during his short time possessing it.**
