## Az-Am War

## (Professional Rating 6)

The Az-Am War featured few pitched battles and a lot of guerrilla combat. The jungles of Amazonia saw plenty of fighting, and the Awakened troopers of that nation were feared for their craftiness and guile. Their time lost on a distant metaplane has not made them any less skilled at their craft, and they carry it out with a large chip on their solider. They’re suspicious of everyone they meet and determined to not let anyone get the drop on them, ever.
