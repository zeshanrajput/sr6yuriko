## Distant Vision

The soldier gains the Innate Spell critter power (p. 225, _SR6_ ) and uses it to cast Clairvoyance (p. 134, _SR6_ ). If they were not Awakened before they disappeared, they receive the Magic attribute at 3 and the Sorcery skill at 2.
