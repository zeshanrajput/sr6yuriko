## Mental Mapmaker

You’ve lived in the ever-changing Underground so long you can put in the smallest amount of mental energy in order to keep track of a mental map in your head. The same effort seems to work in the labyrinthine halls of colleges and corporate offices. During regular activities, you can keep track of where you are at and not get lost inside any confined structure or building (not out in a forest).

- **Cost:** 5 Karma

- **Game Effect:** When things are hectic (active combat, operating in Initiative, etc.) and you make a Move Action, you can spend a Minor Action alongside it to avoid getting lost when moving quickly. If actions are spent, you can follow this mental pathway back even in complete darkness or obscurity.
