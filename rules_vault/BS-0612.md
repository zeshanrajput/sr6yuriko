## Ares T-Rex

King of the dinosaurs, the great Tyrannosaurus rex is a massive killing machine with huge jaws filled with deadly teeth that more than makes up for its comically tiny forelimbs. As iconic as it is powerful, there’s little reason to wonder why Ares made this dinosaur their top priority for resurrection from extinction. Whether due to its tiny brain or aggressive nature, it is incredibly resistant to being tamed or trained.
