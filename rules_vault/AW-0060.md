## Fettering and Offerings

Air elementals appreciate the traditional offerings of candles and incense, though not for the consumption of them like fire elementals. They use the ritual release of gaseous materials. Offerings of perfumes and fragrances have been attempted, but some elementals find them artificial and offensive.

- Like earth elementals, air can appreciate natural aeromantic plants, especially those such as mint, lavender, or basil.
