## AAA-Rated Corporations

None of the Big Ten corporations are headquartered in Manhattan. However, the Corporate Court maintains their dirtside headquarters in the heart of Manhattan’s financial district, so every AAA follows suit with their own local complex— even if the presence is just for show. AAAs own the majority of the land in the city and are responsible for providing key municipal services in their districts, either directly or through their local subsidiaries.
