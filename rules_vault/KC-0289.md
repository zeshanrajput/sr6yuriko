## Loophole

## PCC shadowrunner, Technomancer

Loophole is one of the few survivors from experimentation by the Gennex corporation during 2070. While Loophole was able to survive, his sister didn’t make it. Mitsuhama Computer Technologies got away clean, while their subsidiary Gennex was hung out to dry in court—and in the public’s eye. While not seen in public often, his face is covered in complex tattoos, which he uses to cover the scarring from what the surgeons at the Denver hospital did to him before he was rescued. Since then, he has been waging a one-technomancer war against MCT for what they did, while still avoiding their hunting parties.
