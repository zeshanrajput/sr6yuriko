## **The Null Sect**

**These Deep-Foundation-dwelling xenosapients have decided that all technomancers (and other AIs) are abominations that must be destroyed. With many of the Nexus staff being Emerged, and with several AIs there as well, the Nexus has been marked for destruction by the Null Sect. The Nulls have already made one very serious attack on the main host from within the host’s Foundation, using thousands of constructs and coming very near to destroying it. While this happened more than a year ago, many believe that, with their irrational hatred of the Data Haven, it will only be a matter of time before they try again.**

- **As much as I hate to agree with this corpo dirtbag, yes, it’s only a matter of time. For the moment, the Nulls have other things on their minds, and even though MCT put them up to it the first time, they are not going to stop trying to take down the Nexus. This is just a short pause.**

- **Spyder**
