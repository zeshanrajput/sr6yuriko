## IMA: Hostage Situation

## Posted by: Colonel Cobra

I’ve been asked by the powers that be to offer a bit of an update regarding the International Mercenary Association and their current status.

As mentioned in other downloads, the Corporate Court, backed by the UN and based on a complaint filed by Aztechnology, put a freeze on all active contracts. This also prevented any new contracts from being negotiated by, well, anyone. This meant that no matter their status, any mercenary or military service provider was legally bound to continue serving their current contract or face stiff penalties, fines, censure, and blacklisting.

Of course, that didn’t sit well with the thousands of mercs out there. A protest started at the IMA HQ in Lisbon, but then all hell broke loose. Someone from the merc side tossed some ordnance, and the local security forces responded in kind. And instead of being heard, the mercs were basically trapped by what is essentially a false flag operation.
