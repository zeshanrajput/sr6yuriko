## Mana Siphon

## (Anchored)

## **Threshold:** 7

This ritual pulls mana through an astral gateway or rift to make a mana flow in the immediate vicinity. It takes four hours to perform. The area of the flow is the same as the five-meter area around the gateway that makes objects dual-natured, plus one meter per net hit on the sealing test. Those net hits also determine the strength of the mana flow, per the **Mana Ebbs and Flows** rules, below. Up to two net hits makes it a low mana flow; three or four hits make it a medium mana flow; five or more hits make a high mana flow. The mana siphon lasts for twenty-four hours. The flow is aspected for the tradition of the ritual leader.
