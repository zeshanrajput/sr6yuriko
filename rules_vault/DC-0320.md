## Off-Road Tires

When equipped and used off-road, these tires improve Acceleration by +5 and Speed Interval by +10. The downside is that when driving on pavement, those bonuses become penalties (–5 Acceleration and –10 Speed Interval). Off-road tires mayhave run-flat technology that allows them to continue after a puncture as an option for a 50 percent increase in the base price.
