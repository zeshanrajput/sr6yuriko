## Cougar Mountain Resort (Cougar Mountain)

Located on one of the largest tracts of pristine forestland left in the Metroplex, the Cougar

Mountain Resort Complex covers the east slopes of Cougar Mountain and the entirety of Squak Mountain. The east-facing resort complex offers a full range of recreation and relaxation services, with a seven-floor resort hotel, three fine restaurants, and full spa services. The Squak Mountain side of the complex features a ski lodge with a variety of slopes. During the summer months, the lodge offers hiking trails, rock climbing, and mountain bike rentals.

- Still independently owned, the resort has survived several buyout attempts, mainly by drawing rival corps into bidding against each other and driving the price past what either is willing to pay. The latest takeover attempt was from Horizon and resulted in giving the mega a long-term lease to a portion of the property, avoiding an actual hostile buyout.

- Mr. Bonds

- Fun fact: While the Renton-Bellevue border is typically shown as Route 900, the official boundary runs along the north and west property lines of the resort, and it doesn’t split the resort up into two different districts.
