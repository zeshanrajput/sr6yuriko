## Hacking the Shadowland

Inevitably, some shadowrunner is going to decide it’s a good idea to hack the host and take all that valuable data for themselves. The admins know this—they’re in the same basic business as the runners and know just how useful and abusable the data is. They’ve added security to protect the data, but they also rely on the fact that just about anyone can submit a review if they want to. This has led to zeta-pulses of data on the host, which could take hours to download, and in the end, be basically useless without the specialized processing and indexing the team does.
