## Forged in True Fire

True fire can be used to forge the focus, destroying the stone as it is thrown into an alchemical forge. With this kind of preparation, the focus cannot be bonded to anyone else after the initial bond. Additionally, the artificer can imbue one additional attribute into it, selected from the list below.




## forged in true fire

**Animated.** The focus has the ability to move as per the Animate spell (p. 139, SR6). It can be mentally commanded to do so if within (owner’s Magic Rating + [Force of focus x 2]) meters of them. The owner may also quick draw (p. 42, SR6) the focus, making it magically leap into their hand. The focus must be within 1 meter of the owner for this to happen.

**Dangerous.** The focus can “bite” anyone who wields it. Focus has innate Death Touch spell (p. 25, Street Wyrd) cast as if Magic is equal to Force, rolling (Force x 2) for the Spellcasting roll. The focus doesn’t suffer drain but can only cast it (Force) times in twenty-four hours.

**Elemental Mastery.** The focus can gain +2 dice in its use in Conjuring tests toward the related spirits of that metaplane (Earth to earth, etc.) or gain +2 dice in spell effects related to the true element.

- **Familiar.** The focus is an extension of the magician. The owner can sense where the focus is within (Magic Rating + [Force of focus x 2]) meters of them and gains Clairvoyance or Clairaudience (p.134, SR6) as a minor action from the focus when in range.
