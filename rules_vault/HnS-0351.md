## Overseer

These are the most sophisticated and dangerous golem constructs of the Null Sect. Overseers appear as a version of the Greys, but with defined, humanoid facial features. The only constructs that are not monochromatic, they have black overcoats and hats, ice-white skin, blood-red eyes without an iris or pupil, and a triangular mouth full of shark teeth like most Null constructs. Overseers are the lieutenants of the Null Sect, and their overlords often place them in charge of teams of lesser constructs, with sometimes hundreds serving under

each overseer. Like all constructs, they exist only to carry out the instructions of their overlords. Overseers will not engage in any type of communication; they cannot be negotiated with, reasoned with, or intimidated. In fact, the only way to stop them from carrying out their assigned missions is to destroy them.
