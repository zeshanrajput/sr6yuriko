## Mean Look

Perhaps most of the time you are friendly and unassuming, but when you need to intimidate someone, you know how to put them off balance with just a look.

**Cost:** 8 Karma

**Game Effect:** You must have at least 1 rank in the Con skill to take this quality. With a Minor action, you may voluntarily reduce your Social Rating (which makes you more effective at Intimidation tests) by the number of ranks you have in Con. You may also choose to negate any bonuses to your Social Rating from your clothing, accessories, and other gear. This lasts until after your next social skill test.

When a character within your line of sight makes a Composure test, you can use a Minor Anytime action to give them a mean look—in this case, they can’t gain or spend Edge on the Composure test.
