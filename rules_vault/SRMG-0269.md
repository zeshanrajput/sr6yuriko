## **The spirits in the CRB now have weaknesses, what about the ones in Street Wyrd?**



Below is the list of spirits in Street Wyrd. As a note, using a spirit’s weakness or vulnerability does bypass Immunity to Normal Weapons. The response of the spirit will be to escape, while trying to still fulfill the summoner’s basic command.

- Spirit of guardian: Allergy (Wood, Severe)

- Spirit of guidance: Allergy (Gold, Severe)

- Spirit of plant: Allergy (Fire, Vulnerability ( _SR6_ , p. 229))

- Spirit of task: Allergy (Corrosives, Severe)
