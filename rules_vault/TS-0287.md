## **Client Support**

**Uses: counseling, legal proceedings, other contacts, psychology Places to Meet: café, parks, their offce Similar Contacts : beat cop, frst responder, psychiatrist**

**This non-law-enforcement civilian support role was once known as victim services and was rebranded as client support, and it can be found at all levels of law enforcement. Their role is to provide civilian support, assistance, and resources to crime victims, family members, witnesses, or any other individuals who have experienced crime or trauma. Their goal is to minimize the immediate and long-term impact of the event by offering emotional support, information about legal proceedings, and practical aid such as referrals to counseling or subject-specifc assistance. They are taught to foster a compassionate and responsive environment to put their charges at ease, to empower those victimized to navigate the system and rebuild their lives … and not seek legal action against the law enforcement service provider.**
