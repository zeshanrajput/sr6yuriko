Thus, Eagle Security Services Inc. was born.**

**Initial duties of Eagle Security agents were largely to act as the not-Sioux-Defense-Force enforcers within the Sioux Nation’s section of the Denver Metroplex, bringing order and corporate law to all. The frm’s responsibilities expanded rapidly as the company took contract after contract in surrounding nations, seeing its proftability increase exponentially.**

**Eagle Security operatives, or simply “Eagles,” were routinely dispatched to zones that other security frms refused to patrol, at least not without heavy support in tow. Eagles patrolled these areas with minimal personnel and with a great amount of skill. Their often heavy-handed approach won them few friends but earned them a reputation as pragmatic and ruthless enforcers who pursued their enemies with endless vigor. This reputation helped ESSI cultivate an image of a dedicated frm with agents who take pride in their work. As such, they have remained proftable since their inception.**

**There’s another reputation that Eagle Security wears less proudly, however: extremely high turnover. The culture of “One Ranger, One Riot” permeates the ranks of the agency and is a source of great pride for all Eagles. It’s a double-edged sword, though. Eagle Security demands that its agents be able to operate without assistance in most, if not all, of their duties. This means that the Eagles who survive their assignments become tough and merciless opponents. But for every roughnecked Eagle who comes out of the fre as tempered steel, twenty have been turned to ash. Eagle Security doesn’t release numbers for agent fatalities, but their constant recruitment pushes are quite telling.**
