## Evidence/Clues Available

Bintana’s commlink also has a hidden file named “Tertiary Market Analysis” that contains a spreadsheet of the results of actions of the Kechibi Code. It shows transactions in market coding, times, and amounts. The file can be located by knowing the name, searching for that name, or accessing Bintana’s hidden files. Other hidden files include saved text conversations with Okoro as the pair shared in the excitement, eventual fear, and finally determination to do good with this windfall.

The commlink has contact information for Okoro, the law firms involved with Okoro, former Erika contacts, several local clients, and three Area Boy bosses in the city. The Area Boy bosses are the ones she is trying to assess whether she can sell the Code to. Her copy of the Kechibi Code is kept on an inactive Transys Avalon commlink (rating 6) hidden within one of the newly constructed walls of the office. Note that she is not aware that her copy of the code is inert and cannot be re-activated, but that’s a plot point for later.
