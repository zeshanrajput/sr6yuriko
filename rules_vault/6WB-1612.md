## Winged

## Ares Black Sky

A black project for the longest time, the Black Sky moved toward full production after field tests during the Boston lockdown were a stellar success. This drone is Ares’ latest in stealth air-to-ground support. Able to drop supplies or rain down hell with minimal chance for detection, the Black Sky will definitely be finding its way into the regular rotation for runners and smugglers alike.
