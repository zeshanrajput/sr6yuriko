## MCT

MCT is known for their harsh approach to security, establishing “zero zones” where any trespassers are terminated with extreme prejudice. This can be difficult to accomplish in underwater environments, as most traditional means are ineffective. The HK-Jaws is an effective solution, a cybered-up shark that is trained to hunt down and attack unauthorized intruders.
