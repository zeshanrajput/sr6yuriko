## Kai Takker

## **Cost:** 3 Karma

It’s all about knowing the right people and growing your own sense of _guanxi_ . As such, you can enter and leave the Kai Tak Night Market without fees, and you can bring up to your Charisma in additional friends for a nominal cost of 50 nuyen per person.

\*Wen Xun Lee is a Shapeshifter (Leopard). His enhanced senses as a leopard are available to him as a human as well, while all other abilities are only available to him in his true animal form. It takes a Major Action to change from human to leopard, or vice versa. He adjusted entirely to urban environments long before he ever came to Hong Kong, and he now fits right in in nearly all avenues of the metahuman world. He prides himself on his skills and uses them to make a way for himself in the world.
