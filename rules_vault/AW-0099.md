## Fettering and Offerings

Cauldrons of pure spring water or glacial ice have long been acceptable offerings by water elementals. But with modern times, things have evolved. Where once there was an awkward cultural greeting with an exchange of water, as the elemental would spit on the traveler with mixed results, now there are dozens of replicated tea houses and soykaf shops in the lower caste cities as magicians and elementals sit and do business. The lower caste has found carbonization and flavoring delightful if they are from real sources. Artificial ones are construed as false gestures of friendship.

- Trying to buy a used one legitimately is going to cost more than a case of Burpee cola.
