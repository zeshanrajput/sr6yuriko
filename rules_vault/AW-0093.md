## Dodecahedron 8

The vadose cities are numbered and don’t have more distinct names. It may be because the vadose are more practical and pragmatic. The undines who also swim the seas are less flattering, saying that the vadose are not very creative or imitative. In any case, homes are cubical in shape with a small entrance. The cubical homes are easily stacked next to their work area, as they never vary in construction. You can see varying columns near the great machines to know what they are working on. While their personal side is bland, the great engines themselves are beautifully crafted, resilient and easily replaceable when needed.
