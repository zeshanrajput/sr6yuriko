## Acid Jet

One of a set of three digital weapons that act as a modification to the standard Tarpit Matrix action, Acid Jet washes the target icon in corrosive code, eating away defenses. It replaces Data Processing with the Firewall attribute as the target of the Tarpit action. On a hit, the target’s Firewall attribute is reduced by 1 per net hit in addition to the normal effects.
