## **How do multiple Edge actions and Edge cost reductions interact?**

First the total cost of the Edge actions is summed together and then the discount is applied. For example, if you have a Edge cost reduction of 2, and you use three instances of +1 pip (2 Edge), it will cost you 4 Edge to use.
