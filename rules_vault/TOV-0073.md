## S pirit of Ogoun

## (Spirit of Fire)

Ogoun spirits appear as powerful warriors with large muscles and iron-hard skin. This orisha often appears as a blacksmith carrying the tools of the trade. Spirits of Ogoun are part of the Anago nanchon and represent aspects of war and metals but also are associated with transformation and meditation. Some of the manifestations of Ogoun include Ogou Badagri, Ogou Balenjo, Ogou Batala and Ogou Feray.

The colors aspected with Ogoun are red and black, and the symbolic elements are often iron, knives, and guns. The preferred ritual offerings include goat, rum, sugar, black beans, and yams. Ritual sacrifi ces must be of male animals and include the ox, billy goat, rooster, and red snakes.

**Mentor/Patron Spirit:** Wise Warrior
