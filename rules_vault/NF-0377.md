## **UNCONTAINED**

**Chicago’s Containment Zone was founded on desperation, the last hope to keep the multitude of threats inside the walls from getting out into civilization. But if you keep enough heat and pressure confined for a while, it blows up, and then all hopes of control and containment are gone.**

_**Chicago Chaos**_ **is a supplement for** _**Shadowrun: Anarchy**_ **, adding characters, contract briefs, shadow amps, and more to the Anarchy game. Whether the city crawls up from the ashes or destroys itself while chasing distant glory is something only the runners can decide. It’s a heavy burden to shoulder, but hey—it’s a chance to save lives while also earning a few nuyen. What more could you want from the shadows!**
