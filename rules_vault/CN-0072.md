## Acid Thrower “Mimung”

Invented by the GreenWar weapon designer Wieland, the portable acid thrower (nicknamed Mimung, after the sword of German mythology) is based on early design for the Shiawase Blazer but was re-engineered using materials that withstand the highly corrosive chemical mixture from the tank, allowing for an efficient acid intake and high-pressure ejection through the spray nozzle. While extremely dangerous at short ranges, it lacks accuracy at longer ranges. Each tank can be used to make up to four attacks. Successful attacks from the Mimung are treated as Chemical damage (p. 109, _SR6_ ). The acid thrower is small enough that it can be mounted with a standard weapon mount. When swapping tanks on the fly, the tank must be replaced within one combat round to make the switch seamless.

**Gamemaster note:** The value of the Corrosive status (p. 52, _SR6_ ) should be adjusted appropriately to the situation, but the recommended baseline is 4.
