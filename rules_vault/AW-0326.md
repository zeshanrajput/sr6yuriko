## Evo

Evo has a small embassy within the kindred district from which they plan and monitor a largescale exploration of the Place of Clouds. Evo’s current primary objective is to map and catalog this hyper-metaplane, a particularly difficult task due to the required geometry and the fact that the cloud islands are not stationary. Very few corporate resources have been expended on Evo’s interests here

in the Place of Clouds—most of their work here is carried out by freelancers and shadowrunners.
