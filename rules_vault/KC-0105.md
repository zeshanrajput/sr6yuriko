## Bintana Obukanwe

As an investment broker, Obukanwe was incredibly successful in Lagos, and she did well enough to be hired by Brackhaven Investments’ office in the Carib League, which pulled her from Lagos right after she initiated the code for Okoro. After the exponential growth of Okoro’s investment, Obukanwe pulled the code offline, terrified of what it could mean if she was caught with it.

She is a rule follower by nature, and the abnormal events tied to the code have her uncomfortable and on edge. She has come back to Lagos to help Okoro manage the money as well as hide from any potential repercussions of their use of the code. She has grown increasingly paranoid, which is drawing more and more attention to her as she hires security and expands her circle of suspicion.
