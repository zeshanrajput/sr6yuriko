## Earware

## Cyberears

Ordinarily, cyberears replace only the inner ear, although the whole thing can be replaced as well if you like. Cyberears offer hearing within normal ranges (like an omnidirectional microphone), a sound link, and Capacity for modifications and enhancements. All cyberears provide a bonus Edge vs. a target who has something interfering with their hearing, but the Edge point is lost if it is not used immediately.
