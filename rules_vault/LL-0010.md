## Quotes: 

“These gutterpunks and console cowpokes, they think they can just walk into my house and take what they want. They think we’re not ready for them. They think we’re predictable, static, dull. We have to show them we’re not. It’s not thugs with guns that will stop them from leaving with proprietary information—it’s me. My wits, my will, my way. The thugs with the guns? They’re just here to keep my meat safe, and mop up.” 

“Not on my watch!” 

“The intrusion is coming from inside the building!” 

“Okay, then.” Cracks knuckles. “Let’s do it the hard way!”
