## **Word on the Street**

**Shadowrunners love stories, and if you’ve been running for more than a minute, you’ve heard some of these stories. They can be a source of some good leads, but only if you listen to them properly. Remember that any story you hear should be taken with a grain of salt. Everything will be slanted to make the runners look good and the corps look bad. If the person telling the story is involved in it, they absolutely will slant things positively toward themselves.**

**This means that you should play journalist— once you hear a story that interests you, look for confrmation. Find other people who were involved in the run in question and get their side of the story. If you can fnd police records, look at them to see how their investigation shaped up.**

**> “How do I get a look at police records?” you might be about to ask, and if you are, shame on you. If you don’t have either a source in your local police department or a hacker who can go in and get some records, what are you even doing in the shadows? > Sunshine**
