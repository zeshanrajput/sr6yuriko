## **Vigilant**

**Critters with this quality are very perceptive and always on guard.**

- **Cost: 3 Karma**

- **Game Effect: Whenever this critter makes a Perception test, they will buy hits instead of rolling, scoring one hit for every three dice (instead of four). If this critter must make a surprise test, it gains a point of Edge that must be spent on the test or is lost.**
