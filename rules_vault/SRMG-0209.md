## **From Bestial Nature, does supernatural healing override damage from Stolen Gear or other sources?**

For _Shadowrun Missions_ , damage from a source which contains the allergy for that shifter does not heal at a rate of one test per minute. Instead, damage from these sources is healed at the same rate as other natural healing. In the case of damage from bounty hunters or other quality-based damage, the hunters are using weapons which use the allergen for the character.
