## Translation of Rigging on the Elemental Planes

The VCR and CC appearance translates into the elemental plane that the character traveled to—unless they have bonded a fire wire focus (p. 58), in which case the VCR has the Fire Plane characteristics regardless of the metaplane that the character arrived in. The VCR still functions normally for any drone or vehicle brought with the character, but jumping into a metaplanar vehicle requires a fire wire focus. Riggers can still manually attempt to pilot metaplanar vehicles with the appropriate skill.
