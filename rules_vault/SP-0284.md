## **The Job**

**Smuggling across NAN borders is no easy task. The runners shouldn’t attempt to sneak the shipment through a public checkpoint, as the crates will draw suspicion. An off-road or backroad smuggling route is their best option. The crates themselves are highly resistant to damage, with a structure rating of 14. They are shielded against any Matrix intrusion and protected by rating 6 maglocks and Force 6 wards. If the players open the crates, the wards will be broken, and their employers will be aware of the tampering. No external scans of the contents will yield any results.**

**The real threat to the mission (before the double-cross) is that a team of runners have been hired to intercept the shipment and destroy the cargo. This could make for an exciting chase, combat, or game of hide-and-seek, depending on how the player characters decide to handle it.**

**Assuming the player characters get past the border patrols and enemy shadowrunners, they’ll arrive at a secret construction site in the wilderness consisting of semi-trucks, mobile homes, and construction drones. When the players arrive, their contact has them unload the crates. While the crates are being unloaded, a group of corpsec goons and combat drones move in menacingly and open fre on them, backed up by a combat mage with a spirit of air.**
