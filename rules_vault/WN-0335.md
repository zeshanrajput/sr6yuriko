## Hotspots

## The Blind Pig

Restaurants in Manhattan come and go, but this one has managed to last at least a few years. It features contemporary French cuisine and is neither overly snobby nor high-priced, so it’s a good place to enjoy a white tablecloth, dim lighting, and fine food. The staff is kind and down-to-earth, which is why they are willing to serve up food to Sigrid.
