## Patrol

Patrol IC is often the first line of defense in hosts, since it doesn’t escalate any confrontations, but instead gives the host’s owners a good sense of what’s going on. When it is activated, make a Matrix Perception roll for the Patrol IC; that serves as hits on Matrix Perception actions it takes every in-game minute it is active. That means one of the Patrol IC’s Minor Actions is always given over to Matrix Perception. Reroll the Matrix Perception test once per minute.
