## Overwhelming Response

There’s nothing like a team of runners to convince someone they need to call in the guys with the big guns. To be fair, I know a few of us who easily elicit that sort of response if we’re seen on camera.

- Where am I supposed to keep my Panther XXL?

- Kane

- You don’t need to bring it everywhere you go, you know.

- /dev/grrl

- Cover

The thing you need to remember is a lot of jurisdictions classify shadowrunners as terrorists and will deal with them accordingly. It’s really on us to play nice so they don’t have to play by those rules. That said, a good trick is not to look like one. I know it sounds weird, but even dressing as gangers can lead a corp to treating you as small-time criminals.
