## **Neo-Support**

**Not an offcial name so much as a blanket term, the Neo-Support services are the resources that Neo-PD invests in that are beyond what normal law enforcement providers bother with. Psychological teams have been mentioned, but there’s a companion service, where offcials go to the homes of the elderly to check up on them, provide conversation, and direct them to aid for food or health before problems develop, as well as an education arm that teaches local merchants how to spot criminal intent in children and teens, assorted teen activity offcials to keep idle hands from turning into criminal ones, and general social work.**

**Neo-Support also works with local governments to expand services (which can be provided by Renraku at a generous rate, of course) for job placement, educational opportunities (tied in with employment options), affordable housing (often constructed by those with criminal SINs as a chance to re-enter society), and a focus on decriminalization and regulation of activities, like gambling, BTL use, and prostitution.**

**> Netcat**

**By treating BTL use as a health issue, rather than a crime, and facilitating mental or physical help, as well as removing the baseline issues of inability to afford food or shelter, and general hopelessness, Neo-PD tries to keep people from getting into situations where they need to turn to crime. At the same time, culling assorted vice operations lets offcers focus on keeping the peace. It’s a slower methodology and has detractors from those who believe that criminal tendencies are born, not made, but it shows results in the long term.**

- **For those curious, the average Neo-PD officer wears armored clothing, and while armed with a light pistol, the taser is their go-to weapon of choice. Their cars contain an armored vest and a heavy pistol if needed, but they’re reluctant to break them out.**
