## **Outliers**

**There are four other powers I haven’t covered, because we’re still not sure how to deal with them: dragons, Aztlan, Tír Tairngire, and California Free State.**

**A dragon’s plans are hard to fgure out, and it’s worse when it’s a great dragon and the land they have claimed. While Hestaby seems sympathetic to the Shasta people, Ghostwalker and Sea Dragon are less friendly, and we’ll likely end up in a confict we’re not ready for.**

**The same can be said about the Tír. They have been a thorn in the side of the SSC for decades, but for now, the elves are too rich for our blood. As for California, the PCC seems to have things in hand, so we are not particularly involved there. If they ever need a hand, we will help.**

**Aztlan is a tricky case. Sure, western Texas got handed to the PCC, but that meant dealing with the aftermath of Sirrurg and the CAS, and with our Yucatán ties, we know full well what they can do. But like the dragons and the Tír, they’re too big to tackle for now.**
