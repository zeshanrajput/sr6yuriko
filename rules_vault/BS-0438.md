## Bodyguard BTL

- **Vector:** Slot

- **Speed:** 1 combat round

- **Duration:** (Rating x 3) hours

- **Power:** 9 + (Rating)

- **Effect:** +1 Body, –1 Logic

These chips need to be programmed with a principal to protect. This takes only a moment, and exploses the user to a series of pre-rpgrammed memories that reinforce feelings of care and protection for the principal. When the chip is slotted, it also triggers the release of oxytocin. The user will then protect this person above all else. This p-fix reduces the Edge cost for Protect the Principal by 2 and Taunt by 1. Once the BTL wears off, you still have feelings that you should be protecting the subject for the next (rating) hours. You can make a Charisma + Willpower (2) test to avoid this effect.
