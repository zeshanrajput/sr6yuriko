## Additional character creation recommendations

- **Attributes:** Agility 4+, Reaction 4+

- **Skills:** Enchanting 6 with the Alchemy specialization. Sorcery and Conjuring are optional.

- **Knowledge skills:** [Area] Talismongers

- **Positive qualities:** Aptitude (Alchemy), Mentor Spirit (Protection, which is a version of Bear)

- **Negative qualities:** Gear Acquisition Syndrome, Gremlins, Indecisive, Injury Prone



- **Life module recommendations:** Artist, Mentor, Security Mage, Talismonger, Event: You became extremely interested in unusual or mysterious topics.
