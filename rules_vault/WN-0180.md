## Vera Spokane, Prometheus Engineering: Manhattan-based Project Supervisor

**Description:** Female human, auburn hair with striking professional highlights, rich caramel skin, wearing a glamorous deep red Zoé dress.

**Possible Party Missteps:** This isn’t the real Vera Spokane, but rather a social adept working for MCT trying to figure out what this little soirée is all about. She asks a lot of questions of

the other guests, doesn’t partake in any of the fun, and has an Awakened aura, which the real Vera Spokane does not.
