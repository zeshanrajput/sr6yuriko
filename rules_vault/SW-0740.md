## Nymph

Nymph spirits resemble smaller, less-developed versions of their species and will eventually grow into queens or mothers. Though they lack the size or power of a full-grown Queen, they still assist the hive with the use of their pheromones and spell-like abilities. Nymphs usually inhabit femalehosts, as shamans find they tend to be more successful that way.
