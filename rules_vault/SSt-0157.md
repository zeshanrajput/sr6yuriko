## **Response Teams**

**HTR, SWAT, FRT, SRT, Tac Team—different departments have different names for these guys, but it’s all the same job. When Offcer Schmuckatelli runs into a more dangerous problem than he can handle, these guys get called in, which is why runners see them so often. Most response teams will have on-site magical and Matrix support and be able to call up any reinforcements they need from the nearest precinct. They can also deploy armed drones, piloted by riggers.**

**Most of the time, runners will only face a re- sponse team if they’ve already made a patrol off cer believe they’re a threat (which doesn’t take actually interacting with them; Offcer Schmuckatelli might’ve just seen your street sam limbering their machine gun over their shoulder and decided not to get any closer). The exception is if you’ve made enough of a habit of going loud, and the cops recognize you and send the response team right away.**
