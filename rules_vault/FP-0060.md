## **The Archive G.E.E.D.**

**Grand Estates of the Echoes of Divination**

**This sprawling property in St. Louis holds several separate estates owned by the Echoes of Divination as well as their central membership roster and card track. The central estate is a sort of neutral territory for the cartomancers to hold parties, announcements, and other general galas. All around the grounds are other structures and compounds used and modifed by the gamblers within the membership of the Echoes to create challenges for others to try to take cards. Often bets are made with one or more cards put up as a buy-in, and the winner takes all. This means some challenges are both head-to-head and versus the house.**

- **This place is also a common target for external efforts, as cards are required to be present in order for bets and challenges to be valid. The cards that aren’t at the heart of the challenge are often protected in ways just as interesting.**

- **> Delphi**

- **One very common protection is to hire a free spirit with access to a metaplanar gateway. Trouble goes down, and they just step over. The move has its risks, as the artifacts have a habit of doing wacky drek when they cross over.**

- **> Sage**
