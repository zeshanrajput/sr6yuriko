## Yasmal and Yaggil spirits

Yasmal and Yaggil are kindred spirits, usually with a Force of 4—though some of the more powerful among them can be as high as Force 8. Their stat blocks are exactly the same as other kindred spirits, though they also possess an Influence and Firearms skill with a number of ranks equal to Force.

Magicians with the Spirit Affinity (Kin Spirits) quality may summon Yasmal or Yaggil spirits once they have traveled to the Wastes of Miggon. This requires expending a number of reagents equal to the desired Force (though you also gain a point of Edge in return for this expenditure). While they are skilled with firearms, they don’t bring their own guns or armor when summoned—be prepared to provide gear if you expect to put their Firearms skill to any use.
