## Spellcasters 

Tradition choices in the Core Rulebook are hermetic and shamanic, so either pick one of these two or work out a House Rule Agreement for something different. Initiate grade transfers directly over, and most of the spells, rituals, mentor spirits, and metamagics from _SR5_ transfer directly over too. A few spells have an Equivalent Name clarification below; for everything else, either work out an Equivalent Name, a House Rule Agreement, pick another spell / formula / mentor spirit / metamagic, or just bank the Karma refund. Quickened spells will transfer over, or not, depending on whether the spell itself transfers over or not. 

- If you have a Touch-range Combat Spell, swap it for the LOS or Area Effect version. 

- Animate is split into different spells based on the type of material; pick one. 

- Shape (material) is now limited to just four different materials; pick one.
