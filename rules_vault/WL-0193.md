## **Gamemaster Tips**

**In the jungle, gomatias are a deadly hazard. They blend in perfectly with their environment, and their bite is deadly. They aren’t likely to be a serious threat to a group of people, but they aren’t shy about grabbing an individual as they pass by. Gomatias tend to be solitary, though. A gomatia attack against one of the PCs is likely to be a memorable encounter.**

**Gomatias are often used for security, and guard gomatias are usually augmented to make them more efective. For locations that prefer to capture intruders, Gomatias are equipped with shock pads on their tongue and limbs—these deal the same damage as a shock glove [Close Combat, 4S(e), 5/—/—/—/—]. Deadly security gomatias are equipped with lockjaw and natural weapon sheathing augmentations (p. 139, Body Shop), along with wired refexes to provide the critter with enough actions to make an extra attack.**

**A tongue attack with the binding power is usually what a gomatia uses to strike frst, as their bite is their most dangerous weapon. With a base damage of 4P, even the toughest PCs will be wary of those jaws.**

**In the wild, gomatias use their tongue to strike and bind their prey. They then draw it to their waiting mouth. Security gomatias are usually trained to strike with the tongue then leap at their prey. While the sticky tongue is dangerous, the bite of a gomatia is deadly.**
