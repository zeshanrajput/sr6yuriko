## **UCAS Senator Carter Barnes (HP-Iowa)**

**Barnes has been around for a couple of decades, spewing hate in the Senate, sitting on a few committees (Agriculture from his frst term on, Oversight, and now Armed Forces) but not introducing many bills that went anywhere. But he was always there when the Arch-Conservatives got something passed. He’s adept at trading his vote when it matters most and getting something against metahumans passed in the process. He pitched the ideas of “meta-reservations” for years, and while it never caught on, he was able to work with Lone Star to get a prison with a suspiciously high meta-population placed in his state. Like many Humanis members, he’s also anti-Awakened and anti-Native Nations, if at a smaller degree, but the recent war that the UCAS lost seems to have driven him harder into that camp. More worrisome, he’s riding the anti-Seven Council Fires wave with skill and recently announced his intention to run for president. Normally, he’d not have a shot, but by handing him the war drum to bang away on, rather than just meta-hate, he’s found a much larger audience. Never forget that Brackahven came in second when Dunk won.**

- **The fact that the NAN militaries have a huge ork population leading the front, and Oceti Sakowin often used spokes-elves to get a message out, made it easy for Barnes to conflate the two. With his state now the border with Oceti Sakowin, his constituents are terrified, and the other border states think they can trust him more than someone from the safety of back east.**

- **Kay St. Irregular**

- **Bonus note: Barnes was the driving force behind last year’s bill to change Social Security, which would have removed metahumans from the rolls “until such time as their lifespans are better understood.” The first-generation dwarfs and elves started drawing at seventy under current law, but some may have decades or longer of being perfectly healthy ahead of them. The Supreme Court shut it down, 5-4, but if elected president, Barnes would be in position to nominate new judges.**

- **> Sounder**

- **Barnes has also been talking with fellow Humanis members in the California Free State about reunification with the UCAS. The CFS is frightened by rising NAN militancy, and the UCAS needs a western port with the loss of Seattle. Humanis people are a minority there, but fear is a powerful motivator.**

- **Kay St. Irregular**
