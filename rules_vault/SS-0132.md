## BAD MOJO

## Posted by: Elijah

First things first: I’m in this too deep.

Some of the things I’m going to talk about are going to seem new—new corporate efforts, new shadow activity. And a lot of it _is_ new, relatively. But I’ve been in the middle of it for years. I saw the seeds get pollinated, and I helped water some of them. They’re growing now, some taking shapes I didn’t expect.

A lot about this is complicated, but the core is simple: The corps see a potential new source of power, and they want it. That’s frequently the bottom line we deal with. The fun is the different twists they put on it.
