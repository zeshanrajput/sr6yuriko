## Dissonance and Sprite Rep

Dissonant technomancers do not gain the benefits of a positive Sprite Rep, as most sprites are uncomfortable near them. A Dissonant technomancer does not suffer the drawbacks of a negative Sprite Rep when dealing with sprites that are the preferred type for their Dissonance aptitude. For rules on Dissonant technomancer player characters, see _Shadow Cast_ (p. 79).
