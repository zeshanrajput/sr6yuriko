## **Miscellaneous Tidbits** 

## Core Rulebook damage is “less than 5th Edition” but what is it? 

## **Shadowrun, Sixth World Core Rulebook** 

The Shadowrun, Sixth World Core Rulebook (CRB-28000 for short) is a subtle weapon. It is easy to sneak into locations due to its unassuming formfactor. It has a concealability threshold of 4. Owners of the CRB-28000 can spend four hours of dedicated time to gain a point of Edge. 

**Wireless bonus:** You gain a temporary point of Edge for use on memory tests when related to a knowledge.
