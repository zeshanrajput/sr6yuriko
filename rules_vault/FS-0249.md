## Stock

## trigger removal

It may seem obvious, but sometimes just throwing something as simple as a stock on a weapon can offer stability. This isn’t anything more special than a piece of regular composite (or wood, if you’re rich) that is mounted on the back of the weapon. You can even toss them on pistols, though you still don’t get much benefit in the way of range.

- Also makes a great object to smack someone with if you run out of bullets. Not the best club in the world, but better than bending your barrel trying to pistol-whip someone.
