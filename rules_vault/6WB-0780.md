## User/Admin

You eradicate a Matrix signature that has been left by a Resonance being, such as a technomancer or a sprite. If the test is successful, the signature dissipates. You must have a Resonance rating to attempt this action. Unlike other actions that need Resonance, this one’s a real Matrix Action, and illegal to boot, so you risk Matrix damage and Overwatch Score when you use it.
