## Phantom

The sprite can conceal a persona or device in such a way as to make it invisible to Matrix searches. Concealed personas or devices can only be found with a Matrix Perception test that is specifically looking for the hidden icon; even then sprite can make an Electronics + Resonance test, with net hits becoming the threshold for the Matrix Perception test.
