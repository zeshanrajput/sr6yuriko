## **Game Effect: Counterspell Bubble Alchemical Preparations**

**When the preparation is activated, it creates a Boosted Defense counterspelling bubble, providing bonus dice to defense tests against all spells within its area equal to the number of hits rolled on activation.**

**Base Drain Value: 4 Base Cost: 900¥**
