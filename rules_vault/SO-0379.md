## Teamwork Example One

Jazz and his team have tracked down someone who may have been involved in the murder of several Halloweeners, and they need to find out what he knows. This is going to be a tough nut to crack, so Jazz brings in Rumble (another face) to back him up. Once the target has been captured and restrained, they begin their interrogation. The unfortunate grunt wakes up to Rumble’s face—and Rumble is a tough troll who knows how to look scary. Rumble gives the guy a mean look and starts going on about all the horribly painful things that are about to happen to him. He spends 4 Edge to take the Bad Cop Edge action and scores 3 net hits. Before the proposed torture can begin, Jazz enters the room. He appears to talk Rumble down for a bit, then approaches the target with a soothing tone, giving the guy a way out if he cooperates. Jazz spends 4 Edge to take the Good Cop action and gets 5 net hits. The target’s Willpower is reduced by 1 from Rumble’s action, and their Intuition is reduced by 1 from Jazz’s action—but these effects will only last a few short minutes. Next, the two faces work together on a Teamwork Influence (Intimidation) + Charisma test opposed by the target’s (now reduced) Composure. Between their combined skills and their preparatory actions, the odds are definitely stacked in their favor. The grunt soon spills his guts, providing Jazz and Rumble with the clue they need to get the Halloweeners off their back.
