## Kale Logan

## Horizon, Los Angeles

Kale is good for you and will make you strong. At least, that’s what Kale Logan will tell you. Even if his pickup lines are full of soy-cheeze, it’s easy to believe what he is telling you. He’s always had this way with people and the ability to help them see things in the right light—his light. Born to two elf parents, he didn’t have the characteristic pointed ears and found he could pass for human. While Mr. Logan is eager to help, he is also keen to climb the corporate ladder.
