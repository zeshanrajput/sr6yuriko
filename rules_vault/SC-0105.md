## The Red Dervish

The slums of Tenochtitlán are among the most brutal in the world. You were born to them, forged by their violence. They say never bring a knife to a gunfight, but you discovered at far too early an age that the old adage’s truth is simply a question of circumstances and speed. You carved your share, from the back alleys to the blood sport pits, and made a name for yourself. You also learned that a name can buy quite a bit. The fallen are often offered as sacrifices to the Bloodpanthers who patronize the fights, and while the victors always collect a purse, for the truly gifted, a great offer can be made: a future of blood and riches. An offer you snapped up without hesitation.

Melee combat and sacrificial metamagic aren’t always associated, but for you, they are two components in a dance you’ve perfected. Magic and Infection give speed and precision, and every slash, every kill, every drink is fuel to enhance the effect. The more you murder, the faster and stronger you get, a whirling storm of edges and scarlet sprays, until there’s just you and the bodies.
