## **Do: Follow Through on Your Word**

**I thought about putting this frst, but I decided to end with it, because it’s what everything comes back to. If you have promised something, do it. If you have made a claim, back it up. This is the basis of your entire reputation—can you deliver what you say you can? If you can, your reputation will be solid with the people who matter.**
