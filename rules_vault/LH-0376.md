## Effect on Dual-Natured Beings

Once the initial panic of what just happened had subsided, people started getting their drek together and looking at the big picture. While some started thinking about ways to possibly bring magic back, the rest of us started looking at how other things were affected, such as dual-natured beings and critters.

At first, we feared that the lack of magic would have been a death sentence for them. But thankfully, the Un-Awakening put the vast majority of dual-natured beings and critters into a kind of deep hibernation or torpor state. Some of my contacts reported back that there were some deaths, but most likely these were the old and sick that simply couldn’t handle the shock to their system.
