## **From No Future, how do I light a Molotov cocktail?**

To light a Molotov, you use the Ready Weapon action. This is separate from the Ready Weapon action you use to pull the thing out of the milk crate you have strapped to the back of your Dodge Scoot. So to throw one, it costs 1 Minor Action to pull the cocktail, 1 Minor Action to light the weapon, and then 1 Major Action to toss it.
