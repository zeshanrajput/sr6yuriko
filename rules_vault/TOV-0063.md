## S pirit of Damballah

## (Spirit of Beasts)

Damballah spirits appear as large, hissing snakes, languidly twisting their coils until they quickly strike. Members of Rada nanchon include Damballah Wedo, Damballah Tocan (spirit of intellect), and Ayida-Wedo (wife of Damballah). In Petwo rites, Damballah La Flambo (jealous guardian of secret knowledge) will appear. Others include Odan Missi Wedo, and Damballah Kongo

Damballah’s colors are white and green, and the ritual area must be clean, as the lwa avoids dirty or sick people. A traditional offering is chicken, eggs, milk, rice, corn syrup, white fl owers, and silver.

**Mentor/Patron Spirit:** Snake
