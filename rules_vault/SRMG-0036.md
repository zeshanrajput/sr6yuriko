## **Priority Table**

The steps in character building need to be done in order, however, picking Qualities can be done at any step, but the major point is that you need to pay for the Quality in Step Three. For instance, the player could opt to buy Aptitude prior to Step Three and then use Skill Points to move that skill to 7.

One point of confusion is whether your Edge rating starts at 0 or 1 during character generation while using the Priority Table. For the purposes of _Shadowrun Missions_ , the Edge rating starts at 1.
