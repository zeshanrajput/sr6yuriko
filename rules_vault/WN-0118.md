## The Spy Job

The meeting takes place in a sixth-floor conference room at the Goldwater Club. That space was chosen because of its discreet location. Other than catering services or security, no one has a need to wander up there. The meeting is meant to be top secret, so building security will sweep the room for bugs both before and after it takes place. There are unused offices on the wall adjacent to the conference room, a small library across the hall, and bathrooms at the end of the hall. Two security officers are stationed in the hall, directing traffic away from the room (use Lone Star Patrolman, p. 206, _SR6_ ).

The conference room itself has a large window overlooking the rear courtyard. The room contains a table to accommodate eight people on each side, though presently there are only three on each side. Only two people are talking. These key representatives—one male, one female—are negotiating a joint venture targeting Takai Development Corporation. A quick Matrix search will reveal that corporation to be a Mitsuhama subsidiary. A Memory (3) test done by someone with the Corporate Personalities Knowledge skill or something similar or a Matrix image search informs the runners that the man talking is Devassy Singh, a representative of OmniStar. The woman is Monica Ramos, a top consultant for Charisma Associates, a Horizon subsidiary.



In exchange for OmniStar agreeing to the joint venture, Horizon will formally advocate for Lone Star to receive the Transit Police Services contract, which is scheduled to be renegotiated following the next council election cycle. This is bombshell information that could potentially create problems for Horizon on the Manhattan Development Council.

The meeting lasts for one hour. At the end, the parties depart one after the other.
