## (Recording paused for 7 hours, 44 minutes; recommences in sterile corporate meeting room)

_McCann:_ I don’t want to waste your time, and I’m sure you don’t want to waste mine. What have you found?

_Bungo:_ It’s pretty simple. The people who did the attack on the bank were like a dog who caught the car they’d been chasing—they didn’t know what to do with what they had. They’d worked for a long time to pull it off and managed some pretty impressive hacking, but in the end they didn’t know what to do with it. They know what they want—basic anti-corporate stuff, bringing down the banks, power to the people, that sort of thing—but they don’t really know how to _do_ any of that. They hoped their stunt would cause a panic, and that when people panicked the whole system would come crashing down. It didn’t. They tried to keep it going, but they didn’t know how.
