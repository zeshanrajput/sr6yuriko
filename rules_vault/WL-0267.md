## **Ghost Bear**

## **(Ursus arctos mal)**

**We’d been hired to hunt down a ghost bear that had been terrorizing a corp town about eighty klicks from Anchorage. They were having trouble keeping their signal up to keep calling for help because of the bear, and when no one heard from them for four days straight, a team got hired to bring in some spare comms equipment and, for a bonus, take down the ghost bear. I got tagged as an add-on for my biological and environmental expertise.**

**Sounds like the name for a tribe that wants to be both scary and sweet. As a name for this nightmare come to life, it’s fairly soft and middleof-the-road. I personally would have gone with “Holy Frag Bear” or “Fraggin’ Run Bear,” but for an Awakened subspecies of polar bear that can be invisible like the moniker, I’ll accept it.**

**Now, why do I want a terrifying name on this wonder of paranature? Because it has a good chance of becoming the biggest mammalian threat to metahuman life on the planet (if we exclude ourselves and dogs). With 490 deaths attributed to the ghost bear last year (just behind the hippopotamus at 507) and already on track to break 700 by this year’s end, the ghost bear’s attraction to and particular dislike of Matrix signals draws it into contact with metahumanity on a regular basis. Add metahumanity’s inability to detach from their commlinks and you can see how so many get killed. If there is one positive to take from this, it’s that the ghost bears actually eat people they kill. Some may fnd that dark, but to my biologist brain it’s a better natural fow.**

**First day, no one wants to listen to me on the planning. They rely completely on their rigger, a complete and total city boy who read Matrix articles on how to mod vehicles for cold and ice, and he eats about a quarter of their ops budget adding doodads and gadgets to his copter and collection of six Evo Ballers (yup, the hamster ball again). I try to remind them of the data package we have on the ghost bear and its tech aversion, which I clarify for them is a complete and utter fragging hatred, but I get ignored. They basically tell me I’m there to ID the corpse so they can get paid.**

**We head out day two with city rigger’s bird loaded down with the modded Ballers and a tri-**
