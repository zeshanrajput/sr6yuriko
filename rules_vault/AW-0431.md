## Heavy Metal Safari

The runners are hired to provide an escort and protection for a megacorporate survey team that aims to explore the Wastes of Miggon. The survey team must visit the Yaggil enclave and will then insist on riding the Death Train to visit the Yasmal enclave. The members of the survey team are not properly prepared for the dangers of the Wastes of Miggon and are going to be a liability. No Edge may be gained or spent on Stealth tests while escorting the survey team.

At one point during the adventure, one member of the survey team goes missing—they snuck off to explore on their own for just a bit but got grabbed by a Crimson Carnage patrol. The runners must chase down the Crimson Carnage and rescue their lost lamb, or else they stand to lose a significant amount of nuyen for not keeping it safe.
