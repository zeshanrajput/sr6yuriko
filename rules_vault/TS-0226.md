## **Mk3 Deployable Barrier Shield**

**Sometimes a riot shield just isn’t enough, and you need some good solid cover. For those times, Ares has made the Mk3 Deployable Barrier Shield. In its compact form it’s used and functions just like a standard riot shield, but when activated it can expand horizontally two meters to either side, creating a barrier wall large enough to protect the user and two adjacent targets with a Structure Rating 16 wall. The barrier shield can be set in place, freeing up the user’s hands for other purposes. Deploying the barrier shield is a Major(I) action.**
