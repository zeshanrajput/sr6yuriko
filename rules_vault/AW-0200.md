## Ares Macrotechnology

Given the technology used by the late Damien Knight during the Detroit fiasco, one could assume that Ares was heavily involved with Dis. Data, however, indicate that Knight actively resisted Disian influence, but he had little issue stealing technology derived from Dis assistance. Unfortunately, his would-be successors may not share Knight’s views. Several mid- to high-level employees within Ares have been covertly approached by known Dis agents. Further investigation and monitoring is ongoing.
