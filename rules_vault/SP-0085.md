## **Current Events**

**The Third Law of Newtonian Physics says that for every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction. Same principle often applies to warfare. When you hit someone, be ready for a counterattack. It took a while for the Disians to reply, but what they lacked in urgency they made up for in sheer scale.**
