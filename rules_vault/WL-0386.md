## **Complex Orders**

**Critter commands are usually simple and straightforward (attack, use a critter power, fetch me that thing, protect that person, etc.). If you order a critter to follow multiple commands at the same time, if the critter is unable to perceive the target of the command, or if the gamemaster determines that the situation is especially confusing to the critter, the critter must make a Comprehension test.**
