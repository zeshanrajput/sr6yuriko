## Antipathy

(Health)

**==> picture [227 x 20] intentionally omitted \<==**

**----- Start of picture text -----**<br>
RANGE TYPE DURATION DV<br>Touch M S 4<br>**----- End of picture text -----**<br>

The targets of this spell are magically infused with a strong opposition to social interactions. The caster rolls a Sorcery + Magic (5 – Essence) test. While under the effects of Antipathy, a target is immune to being Suckered, Smitten, and Misdirected (p. 44) and gains a temporary Edge whenever they are resisting a Social test. However, the gained antipathy also means they cannot gain or spend Edge when initiating a Social test.
