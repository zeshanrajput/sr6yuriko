## Game Information New Metamagic: Dinosaur Summoning

## Prerequisite: Invocation

You have forged a personal connection with the Island of Tandora and its spirits. When you summon a beast spirit, you may choose to summon a dinosaur spirit. You may not choose the spirit’s force, which is determined by the type or species of dinosaur you choose to summon. Note that many dinosaur spirits are great form, which requires an additional test (and the possibility of summoning an uncontrolled, rampaging dinosaur).

If you are present on the Island of Tandora when summoning a dinosaur, gain a point of Edge on the summoning test, the invocation test, and the drain resistance roll. In each case, this point of Edge must be spent on that test or it is lost.

A dinosaur spirit that is summoned to the material plane does not have the Astral Form or Materialization powers, and instead appears as a dual-natured physical critter. Dinosaur spirits may not move freely in three dimensions (as most other spirits are capable of)—they must walk, swim, or fly using their natural means of locomotion.
