## The Harvest

While the “Art of the Deal” is a guiding principle of Disian society, the Harvest is their primary goal and sacred duty. From what we know, the Harvest started unknown ages ago when the first artificers discovered other metaplanes and how to convert and siphon their mana for consumption. Details are vague, but this new process changed the Disians. It provided them with untold power and evolved them as a species. Eventually, all they needed to survive (and wanted) was the Harvest.

To oversee the Harvest on another metaplane is considered a great honor, and Disians who accomplish this are elevated to the highest status. The Highest Council is said to be made up of only those who have Harvested multiple pre-eminent metaplanes, taking that which they simply call “life.”
