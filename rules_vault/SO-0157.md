## The Murphy Game

## Posted by: Fianchetto

This is considered one of the simplest cons because it’s been around for a long time. You know the vernacular “fell off the back of a truck?” Well, this is possibly the genesis of that statement. Pretty much you’re selling crap under the guise of a flash sale. This is a hit-and-dip kind of a con. So when the mark realizes what’s happened, you’re long gone.

- If you really want to make it a classic, use a nondescript white van.

- Traveler Jones

- With pollution the way it is, keeping a van white is never nondescript.

- ScarlettPlus
