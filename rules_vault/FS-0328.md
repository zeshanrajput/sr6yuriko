## Combat Principles and Tactics

There’s an old saying that’s become a maxim in military parlance: “no plan survives contact with the enemy.” In a nutshell, it means that extensive planning and preparation can still end up amounting to nothing once that point of no return is passed and combat actually begins. Combat is fast, chaotic, and brutal. It’s also not the time for half-measures, but I already made my feelings clear on that.
