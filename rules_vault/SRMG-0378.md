## **If the person I Squelch (p. 30) reboots, do I lose my call prevention?**

Nope. The call block continues for the number of minutes or until the attacker reboots their device. This command stops two things: calls and Send Message Matrix action. It’s not a panacea to prevent sending commands to drones or preventing a decker from hacking.
