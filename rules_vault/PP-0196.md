## Zero-Zone Mentality

Ice in your veins makes you cold to the plight of others. You have no remorse or pity for those who have been killed on corp territory—or almost anyone else for that matter. You believe whole-heartedly in the zero-zone concept and think all other corps should use it as well, seeing other corps as weak for not using the zero-zone plan.
