## **Are the Lifestyle Options allowed for** _**Missions**_ **play?**

Lifestyle Options are allowed for _Missions_ play and can be found in the _Sixth World Companion_ or _Lifestyles of the Shadowy & Infamous_ . For more information, please see that section of the _SRM Guide_ .
