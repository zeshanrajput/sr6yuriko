## The Hudson Valley

The Hudson Valley in upstate New York is an area of the physical realm that has an unusually weak partition between reality and a uniquely fractured metaplane that is also named the Hudson Valley. The physical version of the Hudson Valley has a number of astral rifts and even a few metaplanar rifts that lead to the metaplane. The thinness of the astral wall here has encouraged the construction of numerous portals—just don’t expect to get easy access to any of them.

The Hudson Valley Metaplane is a unique example of a fractured metaplane. While it primarily exists on the Plane of Kin and makes travel possible to many of the hyper-metaplanes there by simply traveling along its length, its overlap with the physical realm creates the fractures expressed as rifts. It also extends into other metaplanes, pushing into those realities as a nexus connecting all the inner metaplanes. Just as there are rifts connecting the valley to the physical realm, astral and metaplanar rifts lead to the four elemental planes and more commonly the Metaplanes of Plants and Beasts. Rumors that faerie gates also exist in the Hudson Valley Metaplane are always circulating, but if these exit, their locations are a closely guarded secret
