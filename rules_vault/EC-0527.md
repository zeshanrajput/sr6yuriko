## Good Ol’ Folks

Either you grew up in a rural setting or you’ve spent a lot of time there, so you know how to fit in with the “country folk.” You know how to dress, how to act, what to say, and what not to say to blend right in and not draw any unwanted attention from the locals.

- **Cost:** 5 Points

- **Game Effect:** You gain a point of Edge during any social interaction with people living in, or from, a rural setting.
