## The Nuyen Box

## Submitted by: Mr. Bonds

Ever wish you could print your own money? Who doesn’t, right? In days long past, when physical cash was king, money was printed by the press using exceptionally detailed plates to defy forgers whose value was beyond what they could make. Whole movies had plots about “stolen plates” or other MacGuffins that don’t make a whole lot of sense today, when the actual scrip is suspect (on the rare occasions you ever find it). But the age of digital currency hasn’t blocked the creation or use of counterfeit, only complicated the process of generating it, and that complication, much like the antique plates of old, is what separates the average SINner from economy-breaking wealth. Ask yourself what you would do with it, because the mark is asking themselves the same thing, and that means we’re really just discussing price, now.
