## Punching Above Your Weight

To be eligible for this path, the character must have either Close Combat or Firearms at 2 or higher. With this quality, you begin fighting in earnest, only to find out that other combatants aren’t going to take it easy on you. You refuse, however, to back down.

- **Karma Cost:** 4 Karma

- **Game Effect:** When you use Close Combat or Firearms against a character who has a higher level in that skill than you do, you gain a bonus point of Edge—until they do damage. After that, you cannot gain or spend Edge against that opponent.

  - You move on from this quality once you

  - have Close Combat or Firearms at 4.
