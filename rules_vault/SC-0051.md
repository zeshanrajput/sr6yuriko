## BANSHEE HITMAN

The banshee hitman is an Infected evolution of the classic elven hitman, a combination weapons specialist and covert ops infiltrator specializing in stealth takedowns, whether by close range, at distance, or by use of poisons, explosions, or other remote sabotage. There’s a lot of ways to off people, and the diversity of options can be overwhelming when deciding how to specialize in zeroing marks. The thing that sets a banshee hitman apart is that this kick artist has access to a slew of supernatural advantages—such as mist form, regeneration, and fear—counterbalanced with the hazards of sunlight vulnerability and metaphysical traces that make it harder to choose the field of battle or make a silent getaway.
