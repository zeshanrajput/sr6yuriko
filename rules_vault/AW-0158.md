## sandcasting table

- **Disruptive.** The focus can temporarily damage itself to create a low mana ebb (p. 168, Street Wyrd) centered on its location. The mana ebb is (Force x 10) meters in diameter and lasts for (Force) minutes. The focus recovers 1 point of Force every 24 hours.

- **Elemental Mastery.** The focus can gain +2 dice in its use in conjuring tests toward the related spirits of that metaplane (earth to earth, etc.) or gain +2 dice in spell effects related to the true element.

- **Familiar.** The focus is an extension of the magician. The owner can sense where the focus is within (owner’s Magic rating + [Force of focus x 2]) meters of them and gains Clairvoyance or Clairaudience (p. 134, SR6) as a minor action from the focus when in range.

- **Protective.** If the character is dying, the focus will temporarily damage itself (reducing itself to Force 0) to stabilize the character. Character is healed (Force + 1) overflow damage. The focus recovers 1 point of Force every 24 hours.
