## Game Information

## Summoning Elementals

Magicians with spirit affinity (p. 73, _SR6_ ) may conjure specific elementals from the appropriate elemental plane. Each elemental notes its type and Force. The noted Force represents the approximate size of the elemental compared to other conjured spirits and its resistant Force to summoning or banishment.
