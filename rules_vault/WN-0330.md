## 21: the seat of power

The characters have glimpsed some of the capabilities of whoever is behind the lab, but they haven’t uncovered anything concrete about its owners or sponsors. As can be expected, any group that capable has equally capable protections. The PCs need to dig deep to find information that some powerful people want to stay hidden.
