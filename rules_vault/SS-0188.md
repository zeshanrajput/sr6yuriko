## What They Want

This is simple: They want the books of Dru and Obe. Those are means to the larger end of bringing magic to the non-magical, but they are clearly focused on those means. The Society might have worked on parallel tracks in the past, looking into spirit pacts while also working on other possibilities, but these days it’s all about the tomes. That means tracking down any and all rumors of the tomes and their locations.

The Yellowstone calamity was a blessing for the Society, as it greatly expanded the reach of the searches they could conduct. They have been active in building connections to the Seelie Court, and speculation says that at least one of their new members is also a courtier. They’re also building connections to as many metaplanar entities as possible to help broaden their potential reach. It’s not clear if they have connections to Dis at this point,
