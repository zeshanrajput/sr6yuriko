## Water

The bathyscape of this elemental plane is of three distinct planar regions relating to water’s three states: liquid, solid, and gas. The plane’s cardinal poles are oriented along a gravitational axis, with the earth-water pole at the “bottom” of the plane, and the air-water pole at the “top.” The edge of the metaplane is rimmed with ice and sublimating rime frost as it borders the Astral Sea.

- I imagine the metaplane looking more like a large tequila glass, but that’s probably just me. All the elemental planes are connected by Astral Sea; however, it is a very dangerous form of travel. Few elementals even attempt crossing that way, with the cardinal poles being a safer route. It’s also why G-Nome only now mentions it for visual purposes.
