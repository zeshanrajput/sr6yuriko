## Flu (Influenza)

- **Vector:** Any

- **Speed:** (1D6 / 2) days (2)

- **Power:** 6

- **Nature:** Viral

- **Effect (Ongoing):** For 1D6+2 days, the infected individual gains the Fatigued I status condition.

- **Effect (Final):** On a glitch or critical glitch, the individual resists (Power)S damage.
