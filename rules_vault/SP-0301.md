## **Heading to the North Tower**

**Once the players reach the North Tower, they enter a large circular room at the tower’s base. Approximately ten meters in diameter, this room is just as dirty, but is relatively free of debris, except for various boxes placed along the perimeter. In the center of the room is what looks like a hurriedly drawn set of numbers consisting of a series of one and zeros. If the team has a decker or technomancer, they may immediately recognize the numbers as old binary code; however any of the player characters with the appropriate knowledge or active skills may realize the same. If they do, have them make either an Electronics or Engineering + Logic (4) test. If they succeed, they realize that ninety percent of the stream of numbers looks legit but is the equivalent of gibberish; all except for “10111”, which is the number “23.” If the players can make an Perception + Logic (3) test, they realize the room as been set up like a giant clock face and that 23 could be 2300 hours, indicating another direction toward rows of smaller warehouse buildings.**
