## implanted weapons

Corps have been implanting weapons in guard animals for decades. Shifters can have implanted weapons too, but keep in mind some just don’t work well for a particular animal. The leonine assassin may value his delta fi ngertip monowhip, but as a lion, it’s just not easy to use. It is totally appropriate for a GM to grant a situational point of Edge to a defender when the animal form isn’t well-suited to a particular implant.
