## The Bone Traders

Besides the Yaggil and Yasmal tribes of kindred spirits, the bone traders are the only other group that is unlikely to be immediately hostile to a metaplanar traveler. The bone traders are a loose association of nomadic trade caravans that travel throughout Miggon, gathering salvage, reagents, and other items of value, and using them to trade for bones. The bone traders are all gnawers. GnawWASTESers are a type of HMHVV-infected dwarf, unique among the Infected in that they require the bones of metahumans for sustenance—the bones of kindred spirits seem to work just as well for the gnawers of Miggon. The bone traders prefer to trade for their food rather than hunt and have never been known to eat anyone who was willing to make a deal with them. Be careful when trading for valuable items, as it may cost you an arm or a leg. The kindred spirits are capable of regrowing lost limbs (though it takes them a month or so to do it). They deal with the bone traders both to gain valuable items and ensure they don’t get too hungry.
