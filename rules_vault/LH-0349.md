Like the prototype power maul, a maximum of 4 charges can be set off with a single strike. The Kronos comes with a double-sided striking head, but a variant with an armor-penetrating spike on one side is offered as an option. The spiked variant has the same rules but with –1 DV and +4 AR on the spiked side.
