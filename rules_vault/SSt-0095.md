## **The Run**

**So how did this team handle the job at hand? First, thinking that was proper legwork, they went on the Matrix and discovered almost nothing about the ghost town. Just that the population dwindled over the years and that Crash 2.0 rang the end of the place. Armed with this utter lack of intel, they happily geared up, paid a coyote to fy them in his VTOL to the site. Indeed, Park Falls is now in the middle of the wilderness, and most roads leading there are no longer in use.**

**In short, their frst move was “let’s go there and start looking for the guy.” Oh, they found him all**

**right—quite easily, actually. His corpse was rotting in a fooded basement in the decaying ruins of the city center. The building itself was the remnant of an old paper mill, named Flambeau River Papers (after the river passing through the town). Once on site, they discovered that the edifce had been repurposed, back a decade or two, into some sort of church or charity organization they had never heard about. The body was next to an archive room that the guy clearly visited before being killed.**

**Guess what happened next? Yep, that’s right, as was painfully expected, a nasty bug spirit living there jumped on the team and massacred almost all of them before they managed to put it down.**

**> How was this so foreseeable?**
