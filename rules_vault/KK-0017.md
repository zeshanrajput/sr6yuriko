## Krime Soldier

The rifle that started our Real Sized Soldier™ concept! With two years of research and development involved in bringing it to market, and with interest in over a dozen militaries, the Krime Soldier combines a proper-sized rifle round with a reliable-as-anything mechanism and a standard grenade launcher secondary weapon! The Krime Soldier is a proper-sized assault rifle for a proper-sized soldier! Order now to get ahead of the rush, and get a free Krime smiley button!

**>** So, this is just an old US M14 design with a drekky barrel, chromed everything-that’s-metal, and an underbelly grenade launcher. If they had bothered with putting a proper barrel on it, it’d made a pretty decent designated marksman rifle (DMR) with a three-round burst option and a heavy weapons back-up. As it is, if I were to even want something like this, I’d get a Springfield M1A from the Gun Heaven download and slap an aftermarket grenade launcher on the underbelly.
