## WEaring Armor with your Outfit

Wearing armor over clothing overrides any bonuses or penalties to Social Rating and eliminates any special Edge-related bonuses provided by said clothing. Armor that’s designed to be concealed under clothing does not interfere with bonuses or penalties.

**Types of Concealable Armor:**

- Armor Vest

Armor Clothing

Chameleon Suit (provides no stealth benefits when worn under clothing) Urban Explorer Jumpsuit

- Other armor with a DR bonus of +3 or less, per the GM’s discretion
