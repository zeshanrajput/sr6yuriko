## Kim Dolsoon, Nagato: Peripheral

## and Lateral Asset Management Director, North American Eastern Seaboard

**Description:** Female Japanese human; long, black, silky straight hair tied back in a ponytail; wearing a bland but obviously expensive pantsuit.

**Possible Party Missteps:** None. She leaves the party shortly after the “presentation” by the twins, as she has a nose for trouble. If she encounters any runner with a Reputation or Heat higher than 5, she will recognize them and may stop for a brief introduction and hand off a business card. It’s not a positive job offer, more of a “gotcha, you need better cover” moment.
