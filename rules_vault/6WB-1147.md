## Bullwhip

The classic leather cord used for centuries to keep cattle, and other types of “livestock” in check. Brutal on bare flesh, these things are mainly a tripping hazard for anyone with armor. You gain a bonus Minor Action on a turn when you use the Trip Minor Action with this weapon.
