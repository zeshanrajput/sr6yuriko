## **New Gear**

## **Pocket Secretary ’85**

**This old-school-looking piece of modern tech pulls back from your standard commlink to remove the most overwhelming portions of accessing the Matrix. Used solely for text, voice, and screen-based Matrix site access, this is the primary communication piece for members of Asintmah. It uses their isolated network of hosts which all reboot randomly once per minute to avoid tracing, eavesdropping, and malicious access.**
