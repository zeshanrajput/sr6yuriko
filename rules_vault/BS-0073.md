## Barrel Strike

Implanted firearms were never a reality for the ancient sifus and senseis. But in the Sixth World, martial arts such as krav maga, Systema, and Wildcat were the first to recognize and adopt them. Barrel strike is a deadly technique that allows the practitioner to make a melee attack with an implanted cybergun in either the SS, SA, or BF Narrow Burst firing modes (p. 119, _SR6_ ). Roll Close Combat (Unarmed) instead of the weapon’s usual skill. The attack has the user’s unarmed Attack Rating and the weapon’s usual DV; both are modified by ammo and firing mode. **Category:** Ranged, requires an implanted firearm.
