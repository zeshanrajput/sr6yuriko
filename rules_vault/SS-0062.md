## Stored Mana

The fruits of these vines create pockets of mana. This is an enticing food for any creature with a Magic attribute, luring them in and then exposing them to astral rabies (see below). For an Awakened metahuman, each grapesized berry consumed offers a point of Edge that can be spent on any Magic-based or arcane test. Up to two berries can be eaten with a Minor Action, four with a Major Action, and gained mana must be used within 2 Combat Rounds. Berries lose their potency two weeks after harvest.
