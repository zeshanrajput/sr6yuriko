## **Can I build a character with Quality Path: Chwal (p. 15,** _**That Old Voodoo**_ **)?**

Yes, you may play a character that is a horse, channeling the divine lwa to help communicate with other devotees.

Only the Serviteur and Chwal qualities are available at character generation. The Bonded Chwal and Channeled Chwal qualities may be gained using a major downtime activity for each quality.

At the gamemaster's discretion, they can say that you are breaking the tenets of the religion, at which point you lose the highest two Chwal qualities you possess for that lwa (p. 16, _That Old Voodoo_ ). These qualities are unavailable for the rest of the mission. They can be restored with proper atonement and a Major Downtime action.
