## SMALL STAKES

## Posted by: Nguyen Rikki

If you’re like me—and you should want to be— then you’ve been hearing increasing word about a code or AI or something that is disrupting a lot of businesses and markets. It’s not surprising that these activities would make their way to some of the biggest corporate centers in the world. I have seen some things I can’t entirely explain going on in Hong Kong, but if I try to craft an explanation, this supposed code is as good a one as I can come up with.

Building an understanding of what’s happening starts with the profile of a company called Yingyong and the various parties who have become interested in what they are doing. I was one of those parties—I became involved in this by looking at some technologies Yingyong was developing. I’ll take you through some of the things I learned as I started my work.
