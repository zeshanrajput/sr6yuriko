## Augmentations 

While drake characters may have cyberware and bioware like any other metahuman, it comes with a caveat. Those mundane enhancements are worthless when transformed into their dracoform. These augmentations are not rejected by the body (you can’t shake a kink bomb loose by shifting to drake form, but neither do you need a new cyberlimb or titanium skeleton every time you change back), but they are absorbed into the body as part of the transformation and rendered inert, regaining full function only upon return to metahuman form. 

Magical elitists point to this as a sign that those blessed with sorcerous power or the abilities of an adept are best suited to be drakes, as their augmentations and abilities carry over from form to form. Others see the existence of mundane drakes at all as a sign that the species itself is evolving for the Sixth World, and point out that their fires burn just as brightly as their spell-slinging cousins.
