## Overcoming Addiction

Kicking addiction has haunted many folks. It’s not easy and oftentimes will require help from others. Once you’ve been in remission, lapsing back into addiction is that much easier to do. Sometimes getting checked into a clinic is the easiest way to recover.

To overcome the addiction, you will need to not use the substance for Addiction level x 2 days. At the end of this period, you can spend 4 Karma to reduce the Addiction by 1 level. If a runner begins using again, they take a negative dice pool modifier equal to their previous addiction level for Addiction Resistance tests. This penalty goes away after the runner has been in recovery for (maximum Addiction level) months.
