## The Job

There is no Mr. Johnson for this job. Any money the PCs pick up will be incidental sums earned along the way, but this mission sets them up for potential large paydays to follow.

Here are some of the key facts the PCs should have learned:

- Jonathan Blake, the UCAS CIA, and Mitsuhama all play leading roles in the memory-altering business of Animus Construction.

- MCT used Animus techniques to erase the memories of people working on their secret metaplanar technology in Denver.

- Aztechnology has a portal to Dis under Governors Island,

- The mana levels on Dis are off balance and wildly fluctuating.

All of this information has value, and there are several parties interested in it. In this mission, the PCs encounter some of those parties and the things they’ll do for the info.

These encounters do not have to happen in any particular order—gamemasters can make them pop up when and where they will.
