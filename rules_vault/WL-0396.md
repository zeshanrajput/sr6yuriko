## **Protect**

**With the right training, critters can make for excellent bodyguards. The protect command instructs a critter to defend a person or object. When ordered to protect a target, the critter will stay near, watch for signs of danger, and aggressively defend their charge from any identifed threats. If an individual threat fees, the critter will not pursue them beyond line of sight to their charge, returning to their side and remaining vigilant rather than becoming fxated on following one threat to leave them exposed to others.**
