## Artifact Assensing

The Enchanting skill allows you to assense a focus in a way that helps you understand its creator. Make an Opposed Test of Enchanting + Magic vs. twice the Force of the focus. You can use the net hits to learn about the aura of the artificer who crafted it (see **Assensing Table** , p. 159). This can be attempted once a day per focus.
