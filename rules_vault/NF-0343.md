## Pitchfork

Where you have torches, you gotta have pitchforks. The items are easy to find out on the farm, but sprawl-dwellers have to build their own out of meter sticks, cast-off fence parts, and so on. But it’s worth it—nothing gets the blood pumping like a good ol’ mob!
