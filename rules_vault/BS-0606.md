## Spinrad Global

Spinrad Global is the newest megacorporation to join the Big Ten, and has historically been focused on pursuits other than genetic engineering and critter augmentation. Always looking to make a strong impression, however, they have utilized recent breakthroughs in the field to create an improved version of Portugal’s most famous breed of horse: the Lusitano. Famed for its courage, agility, and good temper, the Melhor Lusitano upgrades the breed’s natural capabilities to create a truly magnificent mount. There is only a small market for genetically upgraded horses, so this product remains relatively rare—more a sign of extravagant wealth than anything practical. Only the best results are allowed to survive, and any that do not come out absolutely perfect are killed and discarded. Only about a third of the Melhor Lusitano horses are without any weaknesses, and even the most minor genetic deformity is enough to disqualify one.
