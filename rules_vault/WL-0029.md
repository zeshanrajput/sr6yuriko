## **How to Win Pets and Influence Critters**

## **Posted by: Moreau**

**I’ll step back in and take over for this next part, as I’ve been taming, training, keeping, breeding, and raising all manner of critters for many years now. Critters can make for loyal and useful companions—I rarely go anywhere without a pair of hell hounds. Canines are my favorite, but I’m happy to work with any critter—the stranger, the better. Forming a bond with a critter creates a powerful connection and helps you see the world from a different perspective. I couldn’t imagine living a life without them.**

**But there are plenty of things to consider before you go out and get yourself a hell hound puppy.**
