## Special GreenWar Weapons

GreenWar is known for unique, self-invented weaponry, some of which are presented below. While each item has availability rating, these are usually quite difficult to get (even through black markets) unless characters have a contact in GreenWar or associated direct-action green groups at the appropriate Connection Rating.
