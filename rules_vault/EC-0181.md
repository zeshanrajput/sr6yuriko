## Congo Tribal Lands

They’re usually as reclusive as they come and in fact they have no real centralized government, but an outbreak of CFD brought them to the international table looking for aid and answers. According to accounts, two tribes were completely taken over, and infections of others is suspected. While they would normally settle the issue internally, this issue is beyond the simple life they try to live among the lands of central Africa.
