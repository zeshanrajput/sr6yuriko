## **Technical Database Service**

**Similar to a knowsoft, technical databases are available for a nearly endless variety of topics. These are host-managed sets of programs that when accessed operate in the same way as having a Knowledge skill. Depending on the complexity of the problem, fnding an answer with the assistance of a technical database could take minutes, hours, or days.**
