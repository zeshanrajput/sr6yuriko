## Nano-Critter Venom Enhancement

A critter with natural venom can be enhanced to produce a more highly concentrated version

of that venom. The venom’s Power becomes 6, and its effects are the Dazed status and Physical Damage. This is normally combined with a bio-drone harness to allow critters to be used as a directed weapon, but if an individual’s genetic sample is collected, the critter can be programmed to target that person. Once the venom is used, it takes the critter one hour to produce enough venom for another dose.

If a swarm of critters attacks using their venom, make a single attack test against the target, but add +1 to the Power of the venom for every two critters in the swarm, up to a maximum Power of 12.
