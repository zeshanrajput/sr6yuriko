## **Game Effect**

**Close combat weapons constructed using GMT alloy are more fragile but can be used any number of times without wearing out. Reduce the Attack Rating of a GMT alloy weapon by 2. On any attack tests made with a GMT alloy weapon, replace one of the dice with a wild die. If the wild die rolls a 1, the weapon becomes damaged after the attack and must be repaired before it can be used again. This requires an armorer shop and an Engineering (Armorer) + Logic (6, 1 hour) Extended test.**


## **Game Effect**

**Whenever a weapon made with GMT alloy or modifed with GMT alloy coating is used to attack a Disian shell, add +4 to the Attack Rating and +2 DV. When used against a Disian chimera, add +4 to the Attack Rating, but there is no bonus to DV.**

**When used against critters with the Immunity to Normal Weapons critter power, the hardened armor granted by the immunity is counted as normal armor.**
