## **GMT Alloy**

## **Cyber Implant Weapons**

**Lacing cyber implant weapons with a newly developed proprietary metal alloy (which almost certainly includes orichalcum as well as grey manatech materials) can make them much more effective against Awakened targets that are otherwise resistant to normal weapons. These don’t quite put them on par with spells and weapon foci, but they do provide an edge against materialized spirits—with the added beneft of being particularly effective against Disian shells and their chimera. Upgrade kits are available to apply this mod to pre-existing cyber implant weapons.**
