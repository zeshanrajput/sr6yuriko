## **Disian Initiation and Metamagics**

## **A Quick Summary of Disian Magic Rules**

**==> picture [37 x 696] intentionally omitted \<==**

**All native Disians are Awakened and may take grades of Initiation, gaining access to metamagics—the other benefts do not apply. The Magic rating limit for grades of Initiation does not apply, as Disians do not have a Magic rating and either allocated units of mana or (Logic + Intuition) takes the place of a Magic rating in any tests.**

- **Disians do not have a Magic attribute and must use stored mana—measured in units of mana—for any test or ability that uses a Magic rating. Stored mana may also be used to Amp Up spells, increase the AoE, ofset Drain, set up a Boosted Defense, and to power adept abilities.**

**The following changes apply to the listed metamagics when used by initiated native Disians. All other metamagics work as described.**

- **Disians may internally store a number of units of mana equal to 50 times their (Willpower + Initiate Grade).**

- **Adept Centering: This metamagic may be gained by any Disian.**

  - **They may also draw on external mana storage devices at will and can move stored mana between their internal reserves and external devices—or between storage devices—as a Minor Action.**

- **Absorption: The mana charge generated simply merges with the pool of internally stored mana. This absorbed mana may be held even if the new total is above the normal maximum.**

  - **Disian magic only afects targets with Essence and attacks against low Essence targets have reduced net hits. In addition, it has no efect on non-living targets and cannot produce any physical efects.**

- **Channeling: As Disians are energy beings already possessing a shell, this metamagic is not available.**

  - **Drain can be ofset (reduced) with units of mana; any remaining drain is resisted by 2 x Willpower.**

- **Exorcism: Use units of mana as the skill.**

  - **Every 10 units removed from the maximum internal mana storage can be converted into one power point worth of adept abilities.**

- **Invocation: Disians may not learn the Conjuring skill, so this metamagic is not available.**

  - **Disians may not summon spirits with Conjuring. They can attempt Banishing untrained using only units of mana.**

- **Masking/Extended Masking: The Assensing test is opposed by (Logic + Intuition) + Initiate Grade.**

**to the standard uses noted above, Faustian mages are able to draw on their patrons’ stored mana and use it to augment their own magical abilities.**

- **Master Summoner: The Disian can control 4 x (Logic + Intuition) force of spirits bound with ritual magic.**

- **Paradigm Shift: Does not apply, as Disians do not use traditions.**
