## **>** X-Prime

This place isn’t for everyone. According to the local real estate guide, affordable housing starts just a shade under two million to own and 2k a month to rent. While there are areas like the Warehouse District and the Factoria micro-arcology that can be had for less, the who’s who are living like kings, stretching their legs out and partying like there is no tomorrow. If you’re looking for a playground for the rich and famous free from the constraints of a singular corporate existence, this is the place for you. Welcome to the Belle.
