## Improved APDS

**Use this rule** if you feel APDS ammo should have a stronger overall benefit.

Under this rule, APDS halves (rounded down) structure ratings (p. 113, _SR6_ ) as well as the rating of any DV reducing effects a target may have, such as hardened armor. This includes hardened armor gained via Immunity to Normal Weapons (p. 225, _SR6_ ).
