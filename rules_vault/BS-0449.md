## Optional Rule:

## Upper BTL

- **Vector:** Slot

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** (Rating x 5) minutes

- **Power:** 6 + (Rating)

- **Effect:** +1 Reaction, –1 Intuition

Act before thinking is the mantra of this BTL. This chip is like having a coach yelling in your ear to “go-go-go” instead of allowing you to get a read on the situation. When the BTL wears off, the user gains the Dazed status for the next (rating) minutes.
