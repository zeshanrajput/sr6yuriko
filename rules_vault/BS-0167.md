## Shotgun Knee

Although this implant incorporates a shotgun, it is in practice only usable in close combat. Virtually everything other than the barrel and the chamber housing a single shell are stripped away and implanted in the user’s lower leg, aligned upward. A shotgun knee has no Attack Rating and instead has the user’s unarmed attack AR. When the user connects with a knee strike, the impact triggers the shotgun to add its blast to the successful kick. A shotgun knee must be reloaded after discharge before it can be used again.
