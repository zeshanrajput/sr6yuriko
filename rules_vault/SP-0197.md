## **Africa**

**In the past, Africa has held her secrets tight, but the Disians have changed the situation. As of this posting, most of the hotspots are along the coasts or in known, developed locations. But recent intercepted data indicates that the Disians have made inroads into Africa’s heart and constructed Harvester locations there.**

- **I can confirm some of those. Associates of mine who specialize in those kinds of expeditions have been approached and offered seven-figure payouts to lead some of those expeditions. The smart ones turned it down flat, the few who took jobs haven’t been heard from in a while. Not to sound cliché, but Africa still protects her secrets.**

- **Traveler Jones**

- **And yet, the Disians have successfully pierced the continent in an effort to steal its power. I have infiltrated several satellites**



**to obtain orbital images. These indicate multiple Harvester construction sites—some completed, some still in progress. > Polaris**
