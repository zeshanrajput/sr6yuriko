## **Paid Training Services**

**If you’re going to keep a critter around, you should probably learn how to train them as well as care for them. But not everyone has the skills to train their critters on their own. You can hire someone to train your critter for you or assist you with training them.**

**If you hire someone else to train your critter, use their dice pool (Connection x 2, unless otherwise specifed) for any training tests instead of your own—you may not gain or spend Edge to infuence this test. If the trainer is one of your contacts, add your Loyalty with them to their dice pool.**

**The cost to hire a trainer to train your critter for one week is the trainer’s Connection rating x 100 nuyen—which you are expected to pay whether the training is successful or not. This price is doubled when training Awakened or Emerged critters, and critters with less than 5 Essence.**

**If you hire a trainer to assist you, add their Connection rating to your dice pool on the training test. Since you are making the test, you may gain and spend Edge on the training test as normal. The cost to hire someone to help you train a critter is their Connection rating x 50 nuyen per week—double when working with Awakened or Emerged critters, and critters with less than 5 Essence.**

**You can also purchase a critter that has already been trained with the commands and special training you require. For each command or special training, add 1 to the critter’s Body attribute for the purpose of calculating its nuyen cost (the actual Body attribute is not increased) and one week to the time it takes to acquire the critter.**
