## Magical Researcher

## (Professional Rating 3)

Ever since the Awakening, metahumanity has endeavored to learn about the ever-evolving frontier of magic. At the vanguard of this endeavor is the magical researcher. Some choose to focus on magical theory or practical applications in an academic or laboratory setting. Other researchers may prefer to get into the field to experience new magics and phenomena firsthand or explore ancient places of arcane power. No matter their methodology, the magical researcher is a trove of magical knowledge.
