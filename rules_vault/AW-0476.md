## Physical Transference into the Astral Plane

Entering the astral plane in a way that uses physical transference carries the physical body into the astral plane along with the astral form. The physical body and any equipment carried is incorporeal while in astral space and can’t be interacted with. The astral form is still used exactly as presented above, with two exceptions. First, there is no time limit for staying in the astral, since there is no separation between the body and the astral form. Second, a passageway capable of physical transference must be used to return the body to the physical realm or carry it into the Astral Sea or a metaplane. Leaving the astral plane using any form of astral passageway, while the physical body is still there in a non-corporeal state, would leave the physical body separated and lost in astral space, resulting in instant death.
