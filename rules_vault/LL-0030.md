## Latent Drake 

Surprise, chummer, you’re a drake! You’ve likely got no idea what’s going on, but you’ve got enough dragon blood in you that someday, somewhere, probably at a very bad time, it’s going to bubble up and you’re going to turn into an anthropomorphic dragon. You likely have no idea how your powers work, what’s happening, or why. 

- **Cost:** 25 Karma
