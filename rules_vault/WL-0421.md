## **Wild Soul**

**This critter was most likely captured as a wild animal, then tamed—but the taming never quite took. Or perhaps they were born in captivity but deeply feel the call of the wild nonetheless.**

- **Cost: 1 Karma**

- **Game Effect: Whenever this critter makes a skill test, add a wild die to its dice pool. If a glitch or critical glitch is rolled, the critter stops following orders and runs off for at least an hour—the critter’s handler may attempt to appease the critter as if it were a wild animal to end this effect.**
