## Virally Induced Aura Deficiency

- **Vector:** Contact

- **Speed:** 1 day (1)

- **Power:** 6

- **Nature:** Viral

- **Effect (Ongoing):** This viral infection impacts the magical aura of the disease carrier, causing nausea and disrupting the use of magic. The disease lasts (2D6) days. During this phase of the disease, the infected individual gains the Nauseated status condition, temporary Magic Resistance quality, and cannot gain or spend Edge on Magic-based tests.

- **Effect (Final):** At the end of the infection, the infected individual loses the Magic Resistance quality if they gained it due to the infection. If an Awakened subject gets a critical glitch on their Disease Resistance test, they permanently lose a point of Magic.
