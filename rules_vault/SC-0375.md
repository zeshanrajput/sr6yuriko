## Plot Hooks

Gun-fu street samurai are extremely pragmatic, always on the lookout for new ways to improve or enhance their arsenals to stay one step ahead of their adversaries and rivals. This can lead to interesting situations where they must decide if the end results are worth the effort or danger.
