## An Array of Oppositional Arsenals

Welcome to the array of shadowrunner teams (and their employers) lined up in opposition to our “noble” anti-heroes. These assets are contracted by forces in the city who have learned of this valuable item in the city. Each team is identified by who hires them, then there is a little information about them and the tactics they will use, along with statistics for each team. As usual, feel free to adjust the level of the opposition to keep it fun and challenging for your players.
