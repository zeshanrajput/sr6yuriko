## Custom-Fitted Cyberlimb

Most cyberlimbs are manufactured to a onesize-fits-all standard. The top-of-the-line models are customized specifically to the user with attention to detail worthy of the finest tailors of Paris, Milan, or Neo-Tokyo. The custom-fitted cyberlimb modification can be applied to full arm, full leg, or torso cyberlimbs (p. 289, _SR6_ ). Both synthetic and obvious cyberlimbs can be custom fitted. Instead of beginning at 2 Strength and 2 Agility, a custom-fitted cyberlimb matches the user’s unaugmented attributes and can accept further attribute increases (p. 288, _SR6_ ) but is subject to going no higher than +4 beyond the user’s unaugmented values. The custom fitting process modifies the base limb’s cost by 7,000 nuyen for each point of the user’s Strength and Agility thatis higher than 2. Used-grade cyberlimbs cannot be custom fitted.
