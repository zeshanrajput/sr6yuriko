## WASTES OF MIGGON

## Posted by: Wayfinder

Within the Plane of Kin, there are dangerous and dark places. These places are little understood and only barely explored, so there is a lot of nuyen to be made by those willing to undertake expeditions there—those who survive to cash out, at least.

Miggon is a hyper-metaplane that represents metahumanity’s fears of nuclear annihilation. It is an ash-covered wasteland marked by skeletal frames of buildings and inhabited by desperate and hardy kin spirits who barely survive a hostile land inhabited by mutant and toxic creatures, and the radioactive, glowing Kr’u’Ba. As if that wasn’t bad enough, the denizens of the wastes are threatened by ghouls and vampires from neighboring hyper-plane Carcean. Miggon also bears a thin connection to the ancient city of Kadath.
