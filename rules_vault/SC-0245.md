## GOD-slayer

The megacorporations may have convinced the masses that they created the Matrix, and they use this lie to establish their claim of ownership and control over it. You know the lie for what it is and find the very idea that the Matrix could be owned and controlled for the vulgar purpose of wringing profits from the people who use it incredibly offensive. The people who anger you the most are the ones

who enforce that blasphemous control—the Grid Overwatch Division. Everyone’s always so afraid of those fraggers, and for good reason, not the least of them being convergence. You’ve had more than a few run-ins, and you gained an intimate knowledge of their methods. Your purpose in life is very clear: you will give GOD something to fear. Others see you as reckless, but you refuse to live in fear of despotism. Someday you’ll show them all that the Matrix doesn’t belong to GOD. It doesn’t belong to anybody, and it should stay that way.
