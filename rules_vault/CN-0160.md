## Monad Matrix Attributes

Monads create something similar to the living persona of a technomancer. NPC Monads use the same mental attributes (Attack = Charisma, Sleaze = Intuition, Data Processing = Logic, Firewall = Willpower) They can boost these attributes by a number of points equal to their NV. Points may be added to a single attribute or distributed among several, and they can be reassinged with a Minor Action.
