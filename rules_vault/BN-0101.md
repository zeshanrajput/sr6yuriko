## Stun Cages

As if the manacles weren’t bad enough, they make cages specially designed to hold a shifter, too! These come standard with trackers, a biomonitor, motion detector, thermographic sensor, rating 4 maglock, lights, and a zapper. The battery holds up to 50 charges that recharge via wireless induction at a rate of one charge per hour. The cage also includes solar panels for faster charging in direct sunlight and a standard power jack that will plug into most outlets and vehicles. In direct sunlight, it regains one charge every ten minutes. When plugged into an external power source, it regains one charge per minute.

**Game information:** Stun charge does 5S(e) Damage and infl icts the Zapped status when triggered.
