## Hotspots

## Nowhere, Terminal

This rooftop club in the newly rebuilt Terminal district is on the edge of becoming a local hotspot. Terminal still has its rough edges, and the club serves as a DMZ for the Westside Kings and Slaughterhouse. Located fourteen stories up on a midblock building, the venue offers clear sightlines to Ninth Avenue below. You can often find student and undiscovered fashion designers here mixing in with locals, drawing inspiration and testing out new looks.
