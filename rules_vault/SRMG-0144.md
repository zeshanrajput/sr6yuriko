## **Do I have to end my movement when I perform another action?**

The use of a minor action to move allows you to move your full range during that combat round. This means that during your turn, you can move three meters, shoot, move five meters, and take cover. This set of



actions costs a minor to move, a major to shoot, and another minor to take cover.
