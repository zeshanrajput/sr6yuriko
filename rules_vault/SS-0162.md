## **>** Lyran

**>** I wouldn’t be so cavalier about the Codex. Yes, it’s been the subject of a ton of lies and cons, but every time I convince myself to believe it’s nothing more than a scam or a fairy tale, I receive a hint that there is something more to it.

That said, I don’t think there’s a chance in hell that this envoy got three pages of the book in such a short time. The Disians identified a weakness in him and played right into it. That’s admirable precision, as well as something to be wary of.
