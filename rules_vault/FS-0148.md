## rEVOlution Arms Steel Tiger

I respect the effort to make a rifle of this level that isn’t some bulky beast. She may lack a little grip versatility, but this class of guns isn’t designed to be versatile. It’s just supposed to drop a target from a thousand meters, then drop another target, and another target, all before anyone hears thatfirst shot coming. The Tiger is a stealth killer, and a good one at that. This weapon also breaks down easily into the lightweight case that checks in at under 10 kilos with a decent scope in the case.
