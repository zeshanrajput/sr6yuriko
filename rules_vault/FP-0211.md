## **Principals**

**==> picture [233 x 4] intentionally omitted \<==**

**The Tiding has no strict hierarchy or titles. Jobs are taken and performed by whoever is able to take and perform them. Ad hoc teams are formed all the time. But at the core of it all are six people who are, more or less, the founders of the operation. Even if they have no titles, anyone else in the Tiding will defer to them and take direction from them.
