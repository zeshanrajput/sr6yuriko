## Forget-Me-Not

- **Vector:** Ingestion

- **Speed:** 1 combat round

- **Duration:** (12 – Body) hours, minimum 1 hour

- **Power:** 6

- **Effect:** Gain Photographic Memory quality

Forget-me-not is a drug designed to prevent alterations of memories, whether caused by magic, drug, or other means. While the drug is active in the system, the user gains 2 points of situational Edge for use in resisting drugs such as laés, spells, or other means to alter the person’s memories. The use of forget-me-not with memory-altering drugs will cause some drug interactions. When the drug wears off, the user suffers from déjà vu that increases the threshold of all Memory tests by 1.
