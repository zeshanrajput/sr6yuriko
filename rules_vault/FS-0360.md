## Mobile Medic

Designed to work directly with a medkit, giving the user additional information or instruction when treating wounded. The app constantly updates with the latest emergency medical data, so if someone doesn’t know how to administer aid, this app will guide them.

**Rules:** Add half the Device Rating to the user’s First Aid dice pool, or add +1 to a medkit’s rating .
