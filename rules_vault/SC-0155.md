## Coyote Spirit

Your kindred and beast spirits are uniquely talented.

- **Cost:** 10 Karma

- **Game Effect:** Spirits of beasts that you conjure gain the Stealth skill. Kindred spirits that you conjure gain the Con and Influence skills.
