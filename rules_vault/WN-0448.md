## Piper Jane and the Independent Press

Piper Jane has been along for a lot of the ride, having the PCs dig up useful facts, and now they’ve really found some explosive, valuable information.

They could make a lot of money here, but Piper Jane wants to persuade them that they have another choice. With her help, they can expose the shenanigans that have been going on and do some damage to the powers that be. The PCs will have to forego some money, but they could improve the world a little. What’s a runner to do?

_Sends_ : Piper Jane herself.

_Resources_ : The only resources Piper Jane brings to the table are communication skills and a media megaphone that will help her share the information the PCs have with the world.

_Tactics_ : Piper Jane has given the PCs a fair amount of money during their time in Manhattan, but she can’t compete with the six-figure offers coming their way. Instead, she says that if the information is presented correctly, the landscape of Manhattan could be reshaped. If information comes out that Aztechnology had a portal to Dis right next to Manhattan, the anger of the citizenry and even other members of the MDC might be severe. She would then paint a lot of the Animus affair as a failure of NYPD, Inc., allowing inhumane, CIA-backed experimentation unchecked within city boundaries—experimentation that sometimes affected New Yorkers, as the incident in Central Park showed. If the PCs figure out the identity of “the widow” as discussed by Animus directors (see p. 115), Piper Jane will be thrilled to use that as evidence of misbehavior in the top ranks of Spinrad Global, another MDC member. This series of blows should call into question the composition of the MDC, if not its entire reason for being. That could lead to change at the top, and Piper Jane is optimistic enough to believe that any change presents a chance for improvement.

Most concretely, exposing the activities of Animus Construction will cause their work to stop. Every corp will learn that it’s potentially powerful, but they also know they never want it used against them, so it becomes like a nuclear weapon—you hold it with the hopes of never having to use it. The firm shuts down, and memory alteration (along with the flaws in Animus’ techniques) falls off as an area of pressing research. This avoids a lot of negative consequences for the people of the Sixth World.

_Offer_ : 40,000 nuyen. But Piper Jane says the runners can be involved in the work of crafting and spreading her stories and seeing what kind of change they can make in the city.

_Conditions for Attack:_ None. That’s not Piper’s way or skillset.

_Ways to Play Off Other Factions:_ None, really. Going this route means going alone, with the PCs turning their backs on money and opting for shining daylight on some dark places. The PCs may, however, spend some time fielding offers to see what secrets they might uncover. For example, if they bargain with Prometheus and OmniStar,
