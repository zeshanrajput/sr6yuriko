All of the subjects speak English. Here is their basic information, along with their real names (which they don’t know, so the PCs will not learn this mission).

- Human woman 1, “Alice.” Chinese, in her forties. Her hair is cut short, unevenly rather than stylishly. Real identity: Becky Wu Ping, fashion director with White Clouds.

- Human woman 2, “Hatter.” Latino, in his early thirties. Fair hair that’s shaved short. No scars. Real identity: Fernandina Lopez, nonprofit director.

- Human man, “Rabbit.” White, in his mid-forties. Thinning red hair, cut short. No scars. Possesses significant augmentation (use Street Samurai, p. 87, _SR6_ ). All augmentations are scrubbed of manufacturer’s information. Real identity: Brody Biggs, street name “Hipscotch,” shadowrunner.

- Elven man, “Inferiority.” Black, late forties to early fifties. Bald, medical scars show evidence of extensive augmentation removal. Real identity: Vernon Gold, former decker.

- Female valkyrie, “Damnation.” White, mid-twenties. Medical scarring shows evidence of invasive implant removal. Auburn-red hair, cut short. Real identity: Alona Baldwin, currently unemployed.

Cat calls the runners—she needs a team to break into the NYPD, Inc., Precinct 10 Lower West Side impound lot.

- Male ork, “Puppy.” Black, mid-thirties with a deep south CASian accent. Thick dark hair, dark brown eyes. Real identity: Darryl Beverly, Saeder-Krupp manager.
