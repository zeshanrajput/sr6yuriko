## **The Johnson**

**First, let’s start with the client, who a few of you may have already recognized, based on looks, the location, and when the run happened. Smartly dressed dwarf, doing business near Detroit, just before the big showdown mess of ’80. No surprise, I gave you Ringmaster, most likely doing the bidding of his true master, the dragon Arleesh. The run itself was presumably predicated on some intel provided by none other than Colonel Ravenheart and her Firewatch renegades. Just this information—Ringmaster is the client—is already a forewarning on how the drek was doomed to hit the fan.**

**Yes, protocols in the shadows are such that you may never truly know the identity of Mr. Johnson. But our biz is based on rep, so even if the fxer setting up a meeting has no idea who the Mr. Johnson was, people in the shadows may have an inkling about his identity. There may be some chatter that “Ringmaster is in town, and by the way, he is a dwarf,” for instance. Also, once you met the guy, you actually have a lot to work on to identify the client. Dwarfs are not the most common metatype, and Ringmaster was probably one of only a few Mr. Johnsons active in the whole area of Detroit at the time who happened to be one.**
