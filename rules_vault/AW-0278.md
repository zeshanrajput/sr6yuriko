**Notes:** The elder minotoad’s tongue acts as a grapple attack at range. The only action an elder minotoad can take once successfully grappling an opponent is Drag (as Restrain, but moves the target 5 meters toward the minotoad). Elder minotoads may be initiated and know metamagics heretofore unknown on the material plane.
