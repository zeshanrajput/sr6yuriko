## NYPD, Inc.

The NYPD is indistinguishable from New York City, and they’d love to keep it that way. Unfortunately, NYPD lacks the manpower to keep up with its exponential growth. The New York City contract forced them to relinquish several contracts in surrounding cities. Now, as they try to safely re-expand, the NYPD is searching for allies on and off the council to help stave off competition from Knight Errant and Winter Systems. To make matters worse, the introduction of a new player, OmniStar, is drumming up even more competition as they’re looking to get contracts in the surrounding cities and eventually in NY itself.
