## March 1, 2083

Turns out Tosh is a missing person. Mom is not who hired us, and she’s worried too. She’s a stoic old bird, and we’re all sure she’s holding back, but none of us are the torture-an-old-lady-type. She gave us some decent leads and narrowed down Tosh’s whereabouts after he did his disappearing act. That’s our next spot to dig, but momma MacDonald (she is a red-headed Scot) warned us to watch our backs. We tried to dig, and she just told us some boiler-plate garbage about folks going missing from there, even though we all think it’s something more specific to Tosh.
