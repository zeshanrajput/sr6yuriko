## Returning Warrior

A Ghost Dance War veteran has returned from Dis in a manner similar to warriors from other past conflicts appearing recently around the Sixth World. This sniper fought alongside the legendary figures Tyee and Daniel Howling Coyote, but he was presumed killed in action when he was sucked away to the metaplanes in a byproduct of that war’s earth-shaking ritual magic. Because he disappeared before the Native American Nations’ governments were formed, or before any SINs were ever issued, he could not reclaim his own identity after returning to the people and land he once fought for. Bitter and sullen, he squats in the tribal wilderness and warns away law enforcement with gunfire. A NAN ranger is intrigued by this sniper but cannot convince the hermit to permit any tribal officials to approach. Having heard the rumors about his wild claims, she reaches out to shadowrunners to identify this mysterious stranger, track him down, and convince him to agree to meet with her to finally tell his tale. If the story is proven true, she will help him reclaim his position among his people.
