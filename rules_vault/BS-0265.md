## Tusks

All the rage during the big Orksploitation craze, tusks continue to be sought by ork posers worldwide and, quietly, are purchased by orks who have lost their tusks due to mistreatment or combat. Available in small, medium, and large sizes, tusks are not as difficult to use in a fight as fangs, but most aren’t as dangerous. Tusks of medium size or smaller need no registration, but larger tusk implants—which quickly became a thing for urban ork gangs—necessitated the passing of legislation in the UCAS, CAS, and several other nations to restrict their sale to SIN-bearing customers only. Small tusks are no more useful than a standard metahuman bite, but they have become stylish. Tusks use the Close Combat (Unarmed) skill. Damage Values and Attack Ratings can be found in the Bioweapon Table on the next page. If combined with a Charge Minor (I) action, the DV and AR increase by +2.

Note that replacing a broken tusk with a medium or small bioweapon tusk costs an ork no Essence.
