## Traps

Traps are, by their nature, often difficult to detect, and they require specialized skill and knowledge to disarm safely (because you can always use a flame strike or something similar to destroy them from afar). To locate traps, it is generally best for the gamemaster to make hidden Perception + Intuition tests for the player characters, with a threshold set to reflect the difficulty of the journey through the Citadel. The Observe in Detail Major Action (p. 43, _SR6_ ) is also of tremendous use in this situation.

Upon spotting a trap, roll a simple Engineering + Logic test to disarm it (again, the threshold is set by the gamemaster based on the desired difficulty). Knowledge skills like Ancient Booby Traps are of tremendous benefit in this sort of scenario, granting a point of Edge for spotting and disarming traps.
