## **—Three Tusk Trin, Crimson Crush**

**workers and nightshift janitors, public sanitation and cube-rats have no interest in being mugged, and the knowledge there’s a corp cop on the corner means you breathe just a little easier. But for those who make their living outside the law, cops tend to represent the risk more than anything. Neoanarchists can believe in the common good of metahumanity, but it’s a sad reality that without security, it would be a bloodbath.**

**Getting a job with Ares Seattle was the best thing that ever happened to me. Sure, a third of my pay is in Ares dollars, but they can pay the rent for my spot in an enclave park. I was born in Puyallup, and I can’t remember a night I didn’t fall asleep without hearing at least one gunshot. My little girl is getting a great education in safety. I don’t worry about leaving her an orphan. I see Knight Errant and something in me relaxes, something that was tense for half my life. These people are motivated to keep my family safe, and they put their lives in danger. I don’t really care if it’s a paycheck or a good heart that makes them do it.**
