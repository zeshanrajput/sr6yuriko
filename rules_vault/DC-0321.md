## Racing Tires

The flip side of off-road tires, racing tires are designed to maximize vehicle performance on the street. When equipped and used on-road, these tires give a +5 Acceleration and +10 Speed Interval. When off-road, this changes to –5 Acceleration and a –10 to the Speed Interval. Racing tires may have run-flat technology that allows them to continue after a puncture as an option for a 50 percent increase in the base price.
