## **Can we use the Codes of Honor listed on pages 117-119?**

Yes, however we have to warn you that if you take the Code of Wuxia and accept a job with Mr. Johnson, you’ll be in violation of your code. There’s no exception… we’re not telling you that you can’t take that Code… but don’t take that code.
