## Utility and Support

The defensive decker can sometimes be like the healer of the party in a Matrix MMORPG—all teams should have one, but they sometimes don’t know what to do when the action gets out. You’ll get a boost when you remember that you’re part of a team, and that your true potential is unlocked when you help your allies get things done. Using your firewall and Matrix perception to detect and defend against Matrix threats is useful, but deckers are also really great at building up Edge quickly. Even if you don’t have the Team Player quality, that Edge can come in handy for Edge boosts and actions that help your fellow runners.
