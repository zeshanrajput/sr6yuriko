## Wrong Character for the Run 

Sometimes the run is not something that a character would do. You brought your uber black trenchcoat character that won’t hurt children, but the mission requires you to go kill the kid of a corp executive. The player isn’t really going to find out until the meet with Mr. Johnson. The player may be able to easily handle this with a “well my character happens to know a perfect replacement for me on this run.” The only caveat to this is if that character participated in the negotiation with Mr. Johnson. This would result in those successful hits helping Mr. Johnson in his negotiation as opposed to it helping the rest of the team. But the goal is for the players to have fun, so swapping out the character makes sense.
