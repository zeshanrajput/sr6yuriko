## Alt

They say change is the only constant, and so you are the very agent of constancy. Just kidding! The Matrix would be so orderly, boring, and static without people like you. Everything is much more interesting after you get the chance to alter it. You love to change things up in the Matrix, editing files, messing with databases, modifying sensor feeds, and garbling indexes. Your talents allow you to easily and quickly change files and wireless data feeds, sometimes for very subtle effects useful for misdirection or hiding information, and sometimes dramatically in order to cause confusion. Encryption is of very little consequence to someone like you. Misdirection and misinformation are your weapons of choice, and nothing is secure enough.
