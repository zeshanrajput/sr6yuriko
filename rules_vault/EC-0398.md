## Puyallup Landfill

If it got lost, it probably ended up here. Over a century of trash and personal debris has created these artificial hills on the east side of Trashtown. The ash softens the edges while toxic gasses sometimes ignite over it, giving it a beautiful if hazardous scenic landscape. The landfill isn’t as useful as Black’s Junkyard for vehicle parts, but residents can find many electronic components and antique trinket telesma. They may even dare to siphon and condense methane for fuel. The Gianelli family sanitation business uses the site to cheaply dispose of trash, and they use that term broadly. While the mob is dangerous enough, the bigger threats are the toxic elementals that reside in the hills.
