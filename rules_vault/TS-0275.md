## **K-10**

**The K- 10 is the preferred partner of a Neo-PD offcer, providing backup on site, companionship, legal advice, and extra camera angles for social media accounts. Performance is reduced by the whisper-quiet rotor-thrust system used on C3 media drones, but it was never designed as a high-speed combat drone. SOP is to equip them with tasers, not frearms,**
