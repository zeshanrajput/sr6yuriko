## Gearing Up

Eager’s limited set of contacts includes a Yakuza-run pawn shop called Hudson Pawn, which can handle the runners’ basic needs. If magical characters need gear, he directs them to Al-Hazad’s House of Wonders near Times Square (see Landmarks and Locations, p. 17). Eager will introduce the PCs to Sully (Character Trove, p. 148) if they need someone to drive them around the city.
