## **Glastonbury, England, United Kingdom**

## **Status: Contested,**

## **Known Harvester Location**

**Known for their festivals and for the famed Glastonbury Tor (where King Arthur is rumored to have been buried), this small town in Somerset was recently the victim of a “major ecological” event. During the Week of Death, a convoy of semi-truck drones transporting dangerous materials went off road a mere three kilometers from the Tor. Offcials were quick to respond to the incident, evacuating everyone and setting up a fve-kilometer safety perimeter around the small town. Since then, no one but government offcials, environmental specialists, and cleanup crews have been allowed to enter the site. Offcial reports say that while the Tor is unaffected, contaminated soil and water sources may require extensive cleanup that could take months, maybe even years.**

**Of course, this entire story is bogus and nothing but a smoke screen to cover the construction of a Harvester disguised as decontamination equipment. But government offcials (read: Disian proxies) are doing their best to keep all eyes away from Glastonbury.**

**But despite the British government spin, already anti-Disian groups, spearheaded by Celedyr’s Knights of Rage, have started reconnoitering the area and setting up watch posts.**
