## Legend and the Rookies

## Ire (elf, male, 60, grey hair, grey eyes, average build)

A legend in the shadows at surveillance and summoning, Serrin Shamander faced a difficult period and developed a bit of a mean streak. When he cleared his head, he had earned the street name Ire and has run under it since. He specializes in highend investigations and surveillance, including on targets related to dragons. And in that lay his problem. He was working in Denver when a target he was sent to surveil turned out to be none other than Ghostwalker himself. The Big G made Ire a deal: he wouldn’t eat him for dinner but he owed him a favor that could potentially get him eaten for dinner

by a different dragon, but at least that information came up front. Despite long knowing to never make a deal with a dragon, he figured it was his best option, as the alternative was being eaten.

He’s now in St. Louis keeping tabs on the operations of the Sea Dragon. He was given some more specific instructions when word of the Kechibi Code being in town for sale hit Ghostwalker’s network, and he was tasked with interfering with any efforts made by the Sea Dragon to distribute it as well as pushing to try and get the code for Ghostwalker.

Tactically, he’s a hands-off, remote operations type. He’ll make sure to use watcher spirits and regular spirits to keep an eye out and be around for him to target some ritual sorcery, but he’ll rely on the team of runners he hired to do all the closein work. He’ll still be a tough foe, but not near as tough as he would be if he just came in and went toe-to-toe with the team. Street legends are legendary for a reason.
