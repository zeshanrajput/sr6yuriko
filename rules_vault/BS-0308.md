## Magic, Resonance, and Genetics

We already know that magic and genetics have some relationship. The first expressions of _nobilis_ and _pumilionis_ just as magic was beginning to reawaken set a big blinking neon sign there. This has led to tons of nuyen spent in research in this area. First, it was all about trying to determine what was happening to our children and then later to determine why some people had access to this new and scary thing called magic.

One effect that we have seen is that any modification to the genome casts a shadow on the aura and interferes with magic use. Just like the research that has been done to determine where magic and technomantic ability comes from, we haven’t been able to determine why changes to the genome dim the aura of the patient. Corporations and even some members of our community continue our research in these avenues to hopefully get to the bottom of these vexing topics.
