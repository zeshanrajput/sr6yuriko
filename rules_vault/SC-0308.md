## Combat

If you get your meat body caught in a combat, your first instinct should be to get under full cover ASAP. Hopefully your teammates will draw fire and take out some of the combat threats so you can get out of harm’s way, because you are most likely as squishy as a decker. You should rarely be surprised, though, because you will always have tiny surveillance drones keeping an eye on everything for you. This should allow you to avoid most fights and set the terms for the ones you must face.

Many of your drones have weapon mounts capable of bringing some serious hardware to bear. But even a Steel Lynx is much more fragile than a street samurai, and you can’t fix a drone with a heal spell. Ideally you will use your combat drones for hit-and-run strikes in support of your team’s combat specialists. When it comes time to strike, you can hit very hard. Unfortunately, it gets very expensive for you when your drones get hit back. Keep in mind that if a drone gets destroyed, it can still be repaired if it is recovered. One more thing: You should always keep at least one drone with orders to keep an eye on your meat body, just in case the opposition gets the idea to send someone to sneak up on you. If you can talk your team’s mage into having a spirit watch your back, even better.
