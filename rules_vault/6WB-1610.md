## Anthro

## Ares Packmule

Jokingly referred to as the “Hauling Ass,” the Packmule is a four-legged cargo hauler intended for rough terrain where roads are rare and airspace is dangerous. Spec-ops teams will often ditch a centerline of pack boxes for a gyro arm under an HMG used as a turret by the team’s support specialist.
