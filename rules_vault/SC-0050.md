## Utility and Support

The academic magician shines best in support of a team by gathering information and intelligence, knowing important details about paranormal elements of a run, and using their spells to accomplish what skill or tech will not. Binding spirits is a powerful method to prepare for emergencies as well as aid in magical tasks. Do your best to have access to just the right knowledge skill, spell, or preparation for the situations and obstacles that your team will face. By looking for ways to advise and magically enhance your team, you amplify the effects of your magical abilities dramatically. You are a consummate team player who shines brightest when coordinating with a group. And it’s hard not to make friends when you’re known for augmenting and healing your allies.
