## The Airlock Facility

Past the door is a large (fifty by ninety meters) low ceilinged (two to four meters) cavern filled with stalagmites and stalactites. The entire area is dimly lit by electric lights and a flickering purple light source near the center. The cavern slopes away at an angle, and the far side dips into a pool of water with the ceiling eventually disappearing into the water as well. Near the shore is a small metal dock and a number of prefab buildings with electric lighting; several metahumans, some in Leopard Guard–styled body armor and carrying weapons, can be seen around the building. The light at the center of the cavern is twenty meters away from the metal door, and the rock formations provide plenty of cover for anyone sneaking closer. The light is being generated by a shimmering energy field contained in the oval opening in the center of an elaborate carved stone arch. The arch looks like a natural formation, and the carvings are time-worn. Banks of electronics and sensors surround the arch, with cables snaking across the floor leading back to the waterside buildings. While the team is watching, the light from the arch flares brightly, and a group of six metahumans steps out of the shimmering energy field and moves to the buildings. There are no wireless signals from any of the equipment in the cavern, but the arch is generating electromagnetic interference equal to 16 noise.

If the runners decide to investigate the underwater entrance from the bay, they will discover the cavern is blocked with a heavy security grate (barrier rating 18) operated by a keycard-like device built into the manipulator arm of the Launchpad submarines. A wire fence reaching the ceiling separates the water from the buildings; it can be electrified (DV 10S) at the flip of a switch. Tripod-mounted Aztechnology Strikers and Stoner-Ares M202s behind blast shields can be seen just past the fence. If necessary, use Renraku Red Samurai, p. 208, _SR6,_ for the Leopard Guard. There is a wired rating 8 host serving the Airlock research facility, and the host is protected by a security technomancer (use DocWagon HTR Support Engineer, p. 208, _SR6_ ).
