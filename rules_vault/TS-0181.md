## **Security Critter Squads (SCS)**

**These special forces comprise critters with training regarding police duties. Duties involve attack, protection, and search for drugs, corpses, refugees, etc. Sternschutz uses body-modded dogs but can also rely on barghest squads in Greater Frankfurt and Stuttgart for support.**
