## Gecko Grip

The soldier has grown small hairs over their hands and feet similar to the outer layer of gecko tape gloves. That means they can use assisted climbing (p. 93, _SR6_ ) whenever scaling a vertical surface—but only when they have no shoes on. A Called Shot (Disarm) action made against them needs three net hits to make them drop their weapon. They also have trouble putting down a weapon when they want to.
