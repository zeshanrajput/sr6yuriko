## **Resonance Wells**

## **and Dissonance Pools**

**The new Temple of the Seed under construction at the old Alpha Site is being built around one of the largest stable Resonance wells known to exist in the Sixth World. The phenomenon of a Resonance well is not exclusive to Denver—wells of various size and intensity can be found across the globe. Why Resonance wells appear at a given location is unknown. Some are temporary and only exist for a few hours before dissipating or drifting to a new location, while others have lasted for decades in the same place. While never very common, they have steadily appeared more often since 2075.**

**According to the Universal Information Theory, also called UnITy, Resonance is one pole of a universal force, generated by information processing, known as Nous. Resonance is thought of as the positive pole associated with organization, stability, and creation. Having a nearby source of Resonance can greatly enhance the abilities of a technomancer.
