## **Smoke and Mirrors**

**With Grey Cell et al. on the defensive, I have a working theory that the Disians have another objective at play here. The Week of Death is simply part of an elaborate diversion meant to further obfuscate their real intentions, which involve the implementation of this Harvester Network. With the world (again) on the edge regarding terrorist threats, they’re not paying attention to what the corps and their government lackies are doing. Additionally, any odd movement of personnel and material or increase of security can simply be explained away as “anti-terrorist operations” or measures.**

**But that’s just an** _**educated**_ **guess.**
