## **Are Quality Paths used in Missions?**

Some Quality Paths are beyond the scope of _Missions_ . For more details, please see the **Enhanced Fiction/Rules Lite** section for _Shadowcast_ or other sections to view the specific legality for specific quality paths. Note: the hooks are not available in _Missions_ in order to reduce the cost of a quality path. They always cost double Karma after character generation unless stated otherwise.
