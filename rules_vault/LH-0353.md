## Disian Linguasofts (Beta)

Developed by Transys Neuronet (with covert help from Null Sect), this translation matrix for the Disian language is still incomplete. The primary complication is that it must translate memories, visual scenes, and impressions into words. Often these translations are inexact, missing context, incomplete, or just plain wrong. At best, the user will get a general idea of what the information is, and at worst, it’s just a garbled mess. These programs are works-in-progress and constantly updated as new information comes in and new theories about Disian communications are developed.

**Developer’s Note:** This version represents what is available before July 15, 2083. At the start of 2083, this software will provide very little usable information. As the in-universe timeline progresses, the program becomes more reliable and more likely to provide a usable translation, slowly improving until the final version is released.
