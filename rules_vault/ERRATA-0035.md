## p. 195, Increased Maximum Resonance 

## _Adjust the paragraph as follows:_ 

The natural maximum for your Resonance attribute is 6 + your Submersion grade (reduced by one for every full point of Essence lost). Increasing your maximum does not automatically increase your Resonance attribute—you still have to spend Karma to increase your Resonance attribute.
