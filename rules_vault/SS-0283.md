## Brief

They know where I am. They know I know too much. They’re coming. I’ve hidden as well as I can, but it won’t last. They know. They find everything. They will find me. My position is defensible, hidden by rocks and trees, but not by myself. Be here when they arrive, help me stay alive, and I will pay you. The better condition I’m in when this is over, the better your pay.

They will want to bargain, or at least make me talk. You cannot allow that to happen.
