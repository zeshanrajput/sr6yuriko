## optional quality: dying cyberware

Tracking the age of a runner’s ware makes for a complex game, and you would need a very specific campaign concept for it to be fun for your players. In addition to this, it isn’t much fun to punish cybered characters for equipment that they have already purchased with nuyen and Essence.

Instead, a PC can choose this quality for up to three items of cyberware they have installed. For cyberware that is merely Obsolete, refer to the Cyberware chapter (p. 27).
