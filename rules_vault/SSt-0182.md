## **Get In Shape**

**One thing about media depictions of Club Fed that often rings true is that you’ll have a lot of free time to hit the yard and go jogging, lift weights, maybe even have an impromptu boxing match or two. Use this opportunity to make up for all the years where your seven-to-seven schedule didn’t give you enough time to get to the gym!**

- **DangerSensei**

**Sometimes, your worst failures can be blessings in disguise. Take me, for example. When people found out that I’d been convicted for wire fraud and would spend ten years in Club Fed, many of them thought my career was over. They might’ve been right about my law career, but I wasn’t down and out yet. In fact, my government-funded getaway gave me the chance to grow my business in ways I’d never expected. Here are some tips anyone in my circumstances can beneft from.**
