## Minidrones

## Wheeled

## GM-Nissan Flip-Flop

A tracked drone about the size of an RC car, the Flip-Flop’s tracks are larger than the body frame and the frame has a gyroscopic center that rolls to always be upright while the Flip-Flop can tumble and roll. Great for all-terrain environments, it can still get quite a bit of speed on a straight, flat stretch of land.
