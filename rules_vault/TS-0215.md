## **Neo-Nambu P126**

**The Neo-PD sidearm of choice, the Neo-Nambu P126 is a lightweight revolver chosen for being a caliber small enough that stray bullets wouldn’t be a threat to crowded urban environments, rather than a larger round with greater stopping power. While standard patrol offcers carry them, they default to their taser, using the pistol only as a last resort. Standard operating procedure is to keep a stronger pistol, and body armor, in the patrol car’s trunk, but to always carry the Neo-Nambu.**

**At the request of many offcers, Ruger also offers an off-duty version called the Lawbringer CS. The Lawbringer CS is a stripped-down version of the original and built on a smaller frame. It lacks the VAASS, and has a less robust safety system and a smaller magazine, but it’s smaller, making it easier to carry and conceal.**

**Notes: Use of the VAASS allows the offcer to make an Infuence (Intimidation) test against a target as a Minor Action.**
