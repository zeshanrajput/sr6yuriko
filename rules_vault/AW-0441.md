## Charisma

Your tongue swells with tumorous growth, your voice becomes a harsh and hollow whisper, and disgusting boils and lesions cover your skin.

**Game Effect:** All Edge boosts and actions cost twice as many points of Edge on tests using Charisma. If you gain a second Charisma mutation,

GAMEreplace one of your dice with a wild die on all Influence tests. If the wild die rolls a 1, 5, or a 6, treat it as if it had rolled a 1.
