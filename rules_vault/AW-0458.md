## Toxic Mutant Horror

A toxic mutant horror is an unrecognizable mass of nightmare fuel that resembles no natural beast. The most common form is a central blob covered in overgrown tumorous masses, spines, and bone spikes, with several mismatched limbs and tentacles with claws or barbed hooks and at least one mouth full of terribly sharp teeth.
