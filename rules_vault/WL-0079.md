## **Exoworm**

**(Vermes saxi extremus)**

**Similar to the freworm as a pseudo-extremophile, this subspecies can heat the materials through which it burrows. As it heats and digests the minerals and nutrients, its excretions are doubly unique. The cooled material forms a solid channel, and many of the metal components are separated and deposited in those tunnels, including precious gems and rare earth metals.**
