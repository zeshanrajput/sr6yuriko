## Hotspots

## NYPD, Inc., Precinct 10 (Lower West Side)

This compound takes up about half of a city block and acts as the primary checkpoint in and out of the Terminal district. The precinct building has three floors above ground, with processing and dispatch on the first floor. Training rooms, locker rooms, and the equipment armory take up the second floor, while administrative and executive offices are on the third. The basement level contains the prisoner holding cells and the impound lockers, while the sub-basement is building mechanical systems. At the rear of the building there is a garage for NYPD vehicles and a walled lot of impounded vehicles. The lot only has a single gated entrance. Currently the few officers in the building are securing the upper two floors and the vehicle garage. There are no officers on the ground floor or the basement.

**Impound lot:** There is no Roadmaster in the lot, just an assortment of local criminals attempting to break into the vehicles here.
