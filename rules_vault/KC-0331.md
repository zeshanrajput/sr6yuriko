## The Riverfront

The riverfront side of this complex spans the space between the two bridges. It is a concrete retaining wall with a walkway, and a large section of the wall has collapsed. The river has flowed in pulling out most of the soil and letting the river settle into this little alcove. The water has flooded the area to a depth of five meters as it pulled out loosened soil beneath roads and walkways. The water is generally navigable near the river, but there are many hazards beneath the surface from collapsed buildings beyond the towers. Access from the river is through a ten-meter gap in the retaining wall that allows in many small boats that pass through to make deals with the River Rat gangs.
