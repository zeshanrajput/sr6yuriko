## The Null Node

This node is the power source for the host and the gateway into the Deep Foundation. It’s also the most dangerous node to operate in. Variance created in the Null node will have two times the regular value, and attacks from a Foundation alert will cause an extra +2 points of damage. There are two stable data trails from this node: one to the Portal and one to the Primary Control.
