## **Multiple Accounts Won’t Save You**

**I know too many people who think that as long as they have different bank accounts for each SIN they have, they’ll be fne, and money can fow between accounts. No. Just no. Authorities can trace transfers easily. If they fnd an account linked to illegal activity, and you transferred money from it to fund your normal life, then they’ll fnd your normal life. That’s bad. So you need to think about this more carefully. How? Let’s look at the next point.**
