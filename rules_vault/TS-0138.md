## **The Knights**

**What you think of as police, KE calls Knights. Standard operating procedures call for fve knights per thousand people in a contracted area, but only Atlanta actually hits that target. Most are closer to seventeen or eighteen, with some as low as fourteen in a low-paying area. This is lower than most other law enforcement providers (but see below for true numbers), which is fair since Knights tend to be better armed and armored than other forces—and that costs nuyen. Knights always patrol in pairs, but with fewer cars they don’t have quite the timeto-crisis that Lone Star has.**

**Stan dard Knights come with light full body armor, heavy pistols as primary sidearms, carbines for emergencies, grenades, and tasers that can stun an ork. They don’t do stealth and bribes don’t usually work, but you can outrun them on foot and outthink ’em … just don’t try to outshoot them. That never ends well.**
