## Neighborhood Watch Headquarters/The Hall (Downtown Renton)

The Neighborhood Watch is only a decade old, and in the last few years it has grown considerably. The all-volunteer group takes in donations from district businesses and uses the money for equipment, support staff, and headquarters maintenance. The Hall, as it’s known, includes a communications center, an armory, training rooms, and offices for Watch leadership.

- Beaker
