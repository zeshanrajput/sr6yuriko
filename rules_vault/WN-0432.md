## Piper Jane Calls

This should be one of the first incidents of this mission.

Soon after the conclusion of mission 31, Piper Jane starts regularly pinging the PCs’ commlinks until they pick up, because her journalistic instincts tell her they have some good stuff. The role she wants to have is to act as broker, figuring out who wants what info and how she might be able to play interested parties off each other. She asks the PCs to keep her informed as parties interested in the information they have make themselves known. She doesn’t have cash to offer right now, but she assures the PCs that she can use her contacts and skills to either make a lot of money for them or help them use whatever they know to hurt groups they would like to target.
