## February 27, 2083

Street name’s Emaichtee, and this is the start of my shadow journal. I’m a runner with a cause, but I know the rules. We work in the shadows. No glory, all guts. I’m young, but I’ve leveraged everything I had to get some solid gear and found a mentor to guide me as a shamanic follower of Shark. I got a lot of learning to do, but I’m doing it with my combat boots on, not in some crusty classroom or ivory tower.

Enough about me, let’s talk about my first gig. My mentor knows my passions, but also knows I’m new to the work, so he lined me up with a solid squad in the Japanese Imperial State (JIS). Won’t say where we met, but it’s in Kyoto. Gig isn’t local, it’s down in Kochi. Need to find a wayward soul. Seems they went missing (or ducked out of sight) and someone wants them found. Slag’s name is Tosh MacDonald—weird for the JIS, but it’s a small globe these days.
