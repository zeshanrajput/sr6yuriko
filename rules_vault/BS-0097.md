## Vestibular Stabilizer

The inner ear is responsible for sensing motion, but it is vulnerable to false perceptions and thereby causing dizziness or motion sickness. A vestibular stabilizer regulates the biomechanics of the inner ear to nullify such sensations. Acrobats, figure skaters, sailors, and spacers commonly employ this cyberware technology. The user is immune to the Nauseated status (p. 52, _SR6_ ).

**Wireless bonus:** If the user is being subjected to centrifugal forces, a wireless vestibular stabilizer will inform the user of the number of revolutions per minute for this motion and calculate the distance between the user and the point of rotation.
