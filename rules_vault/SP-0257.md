## **Cyberzombies**

**The cyberzombies within this facility are inactive; keeping one here would create a mana ebb that would make creating further cyberzombies impossible. When in recovery, they’re unconscious and can’t be awakened. Cyberzombies in progress will die if the ritual and surgery are interrupted. The only ones that pose a danger to the players are the two prospective cyberzombies that haven’t started the cybermancy procedure. When sub-level 1 has been breached, they will join the fght. For their stats, use Street Samurai (p. 87,** _**SR6**_ **).**
