## Through the Looking Glass

We have four basic types of rifts that lead us to other places or realities when we pass across the threshold: historical reflection alchera, metaplanar gateways, astral portals, and void doors. Reports from each vary extensively but I’m happy to offer short briefs on the several I have personally experienced and, as before, I will offer the experiences of others in a minimally modified way and place my thoughts in the comments.
