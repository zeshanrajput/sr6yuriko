## **Wild Boar Hunt**

**One common element has emerged in multiple visions from Convergence diviners, and that is an odd wild boar with four tusks, one curved and one straight on each side of its mouth. It has appeared in a range of visions, but its demeanor is always the same—it is still but poised, seemingly ready to charge but never getting to the point where it does. Every vision describes a sense of threat from the boar and nervousness that it is about to charge.**

- **This is mostly true. In a few recent visions, the boar has started to charge. The vision then abruptly ends.**

**> Arete**

**Traditional symbolism of the boar says it represents courage and ferocity, and the visions make it clear that it is some sort of threat. Convergence is very interested in going beyond broad symbolism and learning what this boar specifcally represents. Since Convergence thinks the Sixth World Tarot can pull a lot of threads of their visions together, they’re looking for any cards connected to boars or pigs to help them understand this vision. The primary focus is the Page of Blades, which includes a severed pig’s head (and a severed human head, a connection that intrigues tarot afcionados). They hope the identity of the decapitated man, the woman doing the decapitating, and the pale fairy-like creature lurking in the background will help them learn more about this boar.**

- **Don’t forget the poster with the boat marked CAS in the background. That got a whole lot more interesting when the CAS gained power in the wake of the blackouts.**

- **Elijah**

**Convergence is not placing all of their eggs in the tarot basket, though, and they are pursuing other leads, no matter how esoteric. For example, Clan Campbell of Scotland has a boar’s head as its symbol, and some early Kechibi code activity took place in Scotland. Is the boar connected to the Kechibi code?**

- **But Clan Campbell is based in the west, and Edinburgh (where the Kechibi activity happened) is in the east.**

- **Winterhawk**

- **When you’re talking about broad symbolism, what’s a few dozen kilometers of distance?**

- **Fianchetto**

**If you think you can help assemble these various puzzle pieces, Convergence wants your help. They have leads they need people to track down, including looking for the Page of Blades, but they also welcome runners who have theories of their own—as long as they can support them.**
