## **Critter Housing: Separate Location**

**Whether it’s a separate outbuilding near your house, a professional kennel service, or a no-questions-asked rented room, you’ve got a separate location where you can keep your critter companions. You have a maglock on the door, basic cameras and sensors to keep an eye on your critters and their domicile, and an automated food and water supply for them—just in case you fnd yourself unable to visit for some reason. For an extra fee, you can also hire someone to keep an eye on your critters for you—they’ll take your critters for walks, make sure their food and water supply is good, and call you if there’s any trouble, but they won’t risk their life in the case of an attack.**

_**Cost**_ **: 2 LP base cost, +1 LP per mundane critter, +2 LP per Awakened or Emerged critter.**

_**Optional Cost**_ **: +1 LP to hire someone to keep an eye on your critters when you aren’t there.**
