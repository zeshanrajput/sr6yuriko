## Riviera Golf Course

This place is a battleground, but using woods, wedges, and irons instead of bullets and spells. Locals discuss deals worth more than aircraft carriers between hideously inaccurate drives and putts. They make deals on the greens and a few have made their final swings on the eighteenth hole as the assassins know they never leave a round unfinished. Getting in takes money—a lot of money. But day passes are often granted to servants to scout or check green depths or pit sand density.
