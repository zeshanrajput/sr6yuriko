## After The Storm

## Main Location: Pankow

## Notable Characters: Cashmere

In 2081, an event took place at the Caligariplatz in Pankow that is called “Netzgewitter” in Berlin’s shadows: While a major battle took place in the Matrix between at least two digital entities and dozens of technomancers, various gangs and even corporate forces descended on a heavily fortified block of old buildings inhabited by neo-anarchists. Both battles left the kiez in ruins—there are holes in the walls, fires destroyed a good part of the block, and the Matrix battle caused all the power and grid lines in the area to cook and burn.

**Original Message:** Cashmere asks if you can help her allies in the Caligarikiez in Pankow with reconstruction. Most jobs will revolve around tracking down and acquiring ancient Matrix tech from the 2050s to replace the destroyed communication links of the alternative chip phone and cable Matrix system in the anarcho zones.
