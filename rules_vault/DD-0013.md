## shadowrun

## Game Information

An assassin is only as good as their tools. Anyone with a gun can kill a person, but the right piece of specialized kit can elevate someone to the point where they might actually manage to avoid purchasing agricultural real estate. Whether it’s a particular weapon, spell, or a mindset, every professional needs their tools.
