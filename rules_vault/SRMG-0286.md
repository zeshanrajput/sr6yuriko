## **Can I improve the Conclave or Covens Loyalty?**

Yes. This can be bought during character creation by spending the full price of the group contact (as written in _Sixth World Companion_ , p. 175). They can be improved during play using Downtime Actions, Karma, and paying the additional cost. This is further elaborated in the chapter on _Sixth World Companion_ ( **What is the impact of group contact rules (6WC, p. 175-176) on existing groups?** ).
