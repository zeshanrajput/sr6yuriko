## Functional Tail (Paddle)

- **C** ost: 6 Karma

An alligator’s or dolphins’ powerful tail strokes propel them through the water faster. You gain a bonus point of Edge on all Athletics (Swimming) tests, and an additional +2 meters of movement per hit when sprinting while swimming.
