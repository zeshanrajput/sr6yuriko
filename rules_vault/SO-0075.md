## Pressing Flesh (I)

The power of touch can be highly influential. It’s long been used by politicians and seducers to form an intimate connection. Spend a Major Action and make an Influence (Etiquette) + Charisma (2) test to touch someone in a benign way: a handshake, a pat on the back, or an “accidental” bump in a crowd. On a success, you can either gain a +2 dice pool bonus to a Con (Seduction) test or a Stealth (Palming) test against the same person within 2 combat rounds.
