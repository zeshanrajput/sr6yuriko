## Evo Signal Plankton

Plankton are tiny crustaceans augmented with (some might say infected by) Matrix relay nano-critter implants. The nanite-infused solution that implants into them on contact is pumped into the ocean, where clear Matrix signals are a high priority. These are expensive to design but cheap to produce, and Evo hopes to one day make the entire ocean a place for clear and smooth surfing on the Matrix. Wherever these are in place, Matrix noise is reduced to 2.
