## **Pushing the Envelope**

**There are actually two dens of ground wolves in the area. Once the PCs capture a batch from the frst den, a group of ground wolves from the other den attacks—some of them attempt to break open the cages while others try to kill the PCs.**

**thirty-centimeter spikes rests along its spine, resembling saw teeth. Its head is very similar to the bison with horns, but instead of eyes located near the sides of the head, it has a singular eye facing forward, toward its prey.**

**The miniwashitu is exceedingly rare and to date has only been spotted in the prairies of the central UCAS around the Missouri River. There is not much known about their behavior so far just because of how rare the animal is. We do know about their hunting method, which involves setting up ambushes in tall grass and then striking forward by both blinding and paralyzing the target with its magic-enhanced howl. So far, they have only been seen as solitary hunters, but given how little we know, they could work together to bring down prey.**
