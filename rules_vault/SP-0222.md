## **Central/South America**

**Central America can easily be summed up thus: Aztechnology is in deep with the Dis, Aztechnology controls Aztlan, and Aztlan has a stranglehold on Central America. And while not under Aztlan control, almost the same can be said about most of South America as well. Disian proxies have deeply embedded in the Argentinean, Amazonian, and Peruvian governments to the point they can also be considered puppet nations. Likewise, consider the entire continent a Disian stronghold.**
