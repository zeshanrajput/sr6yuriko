## Alchemical Preps

Alchemy is a fun way for anyone to gain a magical edge; the following preparations are just a few possible concoctions your character can play with and follow the rules for commercially prepared production models (p. 69, _Street Wyrd_ ,). Alchemical preps for sustained spells remain in effect for one minute per potency after resistance (p. 151, _SR6_ ).
