## Berlin Nights (WBN)

Set across the Berlin sprawl, this series features action that is frequent and frequently real. The film crew does all of their shots on site. This means a need to maintain some level of control in a very uncontrolled environment, or they have to just go with the flow. Director Brig Koch mixes and matches both elements in the series, which features Stephan Marksman as Wolfgang Richter, a former Sonntag-Krieg executive who was pressured into selling corporate secrets to rival MTC, leading to a massive shift in corporate power. Now Richter works with members of Berlin’s robust anarchist community in order to correct his misdeed by running in the shadows for Sonntag-Krieg.

- Several teams in Berlin claim to have worked with Marksman. They all have similar tales of being hired with a caveat that Herr Schmidt sends a pair of guys along with them. One is Marksman, the other is his bodyguard. Helps Marksman make his character more real, I’m sure.
