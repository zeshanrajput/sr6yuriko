## First Nations

With the strength of the NAN practically shattering a major world power, the rise of a primarily Amerind gang was no surprise to anyone. The direction they took left their tribal leaders appalled— but what gang ever bothers to try making people happy? Pulling cultural cues from across the tribes of North America, the First Nations style can end up looking a little haphazard. While some criticize them for their cross-cultural borrowing, they learn quickly that while the gang members may mix Hopi and Cherokee with a little Seminole and Iroquois, they learn the meaning behind every style they steal. So mock their garb and see if they don’t take a piece out of you in compensation.
