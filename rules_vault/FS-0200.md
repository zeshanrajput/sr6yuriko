## Drag Handle

This cheap but effective accessory makes it easier to pull chummers or other associates out of a hazardous situation, such as gunfire. Typically, a drag handle is attached to the back of an armor system just below the neck and between the shoulders, although it has been known to be attached to the front just below the neckline to facilitate removing an individual from a vehicle.

The drag handle provides a bonus point of Edge to be used for Strength tests to pull a chummer through a hazardous situation.
