## Attributes

The first is probably the most obvious. The more innate charm and personality that a face fatale has, the better they will be. In addition to just making Charisma a priority, its potential can be raised with the Exceptional Attribute quality. Every extra bit of charm and poise will make all their social interactions that much easier. The face fatale should never skimp on Charisma.

Other helpful attributes are Willpower and Intuition. They need Willpower to resist the charms of others and to avoid losing themselves in whatever role they are playing. Intuition is also useful to help them stay aware of what is going on, especially with everyone around them. The face fatale doesn’t want to be caught off-guard; they work best when they control their environment. Reaction is never a poor choice, either, whether it’s used to take hold of a situation that is getting out of control or take the first step when it’s time to get out of Dodge.
