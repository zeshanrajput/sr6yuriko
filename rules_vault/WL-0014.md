## **Other**

**There are other entities out there that sit outside of the categories I’m using. Some may consider spirits to be critters, and from a practical standpoint they might not be wrong, but my metaplanar travels have shown me that spirits are so different from animals and metahumans that it’s not useful to lump them together. Protosapients, AIs, EIs, and other Matrix entities fall within a different scope as well—check out** _**Hack & Slash**_ **for details about them. The Infected—vampires, ghouls, banshees, and the like—are worth mentioning, especially since there are a number of other Infected critters and carriers out there. My general advice with them is that they all tend to have some specifc vulnerabilities, which you should take advantage of if you have to fght them—I never leave home without a few wood pulp rounds on me in case a certain bloodsucker decides they want another go at me.**
