## **Special Notes**

**In the aftermath, there will be a lot of “what ifs” for this mission. If the runners fee the battle without aiding the town, Ms. Johnson will be disgusted, but will still pay (business is business after all). If they fee after attempting to help, Ms. Johnson will be furious, but not at them. The player characters will still be paid and offered any medical treatment needed.**

**If the player characters fght and survive, they’ll receive an additional ten thousand nuyen bonus and be given a Grey Cell challenge coin (see adventure Breadcrumbs , p. 130, for details).**

**As for the townspeople, they will be relocated to safehouses until more permanent solutions can be made.**

**Should Calvin survive the battle, he will join the fght against his former Disian masters.**
