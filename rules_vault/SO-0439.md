## Orator

Your speeches are captivating and eloquent, but rarely concise.

**Cost:** 4 Karma

**Game Effect:** When you can take your time to speak at length, you may store up Edge over time before making a final dice roll to determine the effect. For each minute you speak, you gain a point of Edge (up to a maximum of 7). When you choose to conclude the speech, any Edge gained from this quality must be spent on your social skill test or it is lost.

Other characters may attempt to interrupt your speech if they make an Influence (Etiquette) + Charisma (3) or Composure (3) test. If you’re interrupted, you must make a Composure (3) test to continue. The threshold of the Composure test increases by 1 each time you’re interrupted. If you fail the Composure test, all Edge gained during your oration is lost.

You may voluntarily end the speech and make your final social skill test at any time—though the longer you manage to drag things out, the more Edge you’ll have available.

You can’t initiate a long speech during combat, and if combat occurs during your speech, it’s automatically interrupted with no opportunity for you to continue with a Composure test.
