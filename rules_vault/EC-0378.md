## Spanaway Speedway

For riggers and speed enthusiasts alike, the speedway is a one-kilometer track that was revitalized as another place of entertainment and gambling in Loveland. They put on full auto racing, combat biking, and even an occasional autoshow with street race competition (all supervised by Sottocapo Enzo).
