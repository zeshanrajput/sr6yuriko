## Junkyard Siege

This adventure hook is designed with the commander or scrapyard salvager background in mind, but it would be fun for any team of runners with tech skills and a willingness to help a community in the barrens. A junkyard squatter community (the one that the drone rigger grew up in, if applicable) has come under threat, and they reach out to the PCs to help them set up defenses so they can protect themselves. The nuyen payment for this job is very low, but the Karma reward is significant. The nature of the threat might be a large pack of hostile ghouls, vicious paracritters, gangers, or even vengeful spirits. Perhaps the runners might be able to find a way to deal with the source of the problem, but the squatters desperately insist that getting their defenses set up first is vital. As for the defensive designs, this is a great time to let the playersget creative. Have them draw up maps and provide them with a pool of spare parts and components they can use to build turrets, drones, and whatever traps and defenses they come up with. The pool of spare parts can be used to build up to 10,000 nuyen worth of gear for the defenses. The PCs can also spend some time scavenging the junkyard for more parts. Any player may make one Engineering + Logic test. Each hit adds the equivalent of 2,000 nuyen to the spare parts pile. Of course, any PCs who wish to donate their own funds (or equivalent value of relevant gear) may do so. Once the plans are made and the parts acquired, the PCs make a teamwork Engineering + Logic test with a threshold of 4. If 20,000 or more nuyen worth of parts has been acquired, gain a point of Edge that must be used on this test. If the test fails, the squatters will be unable to gain or spend Edge when acting in defense of their home. If the test succeeds, the squatters may gain and spend Edge as normal. Any net hits become a pool of Edge that the squatters may use when defending the junkyard. This Edge refreshes at the start of a battle.

Just as the defenses are finished, while the PCs are still there, the junkyard comes under attack. Whether or not there is any warning depends entirely on what precautions the PCs have taken. If none were taken, the attack comes as a surprise. If the Engineering test succeeded, the PCs may spend their own Edge on behalf of the squatters during the battle.

If the attack against the junkyard is repelled, the PCs all gain a new street and engineering type contact with Connection 4 and Loyalty 4, and one favor each. This contact represents the junkyard squatter community rather than an individual. The junkyard salvagers are a great resource for parts and barrens gossip, and they’re also a safe place to lie low.
