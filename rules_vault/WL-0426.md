## **Jealous**

**Some critters prefer to be the only one around and don’t play well with others.**

- **Bonus: 2 Karma**

- **Game Effect: This critter prefers to be the center of attention and doesn’t like being around other critters. When other critters are present, issuing commands to this critter takes two Minor Actions (instead of one). Additionally, the Pack Attack command and Dogpile Edge action are not available to this critter.**
