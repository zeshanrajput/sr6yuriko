## **What critter augmentations are not legal (p. 136-165)?**

All Genetic Augmentations (p. 149-151), Mundane Critter Power Augmentations (p. 151-153), Magical Genetic Augmentations (p. 153-154), and DIY Genetic Engineering (p. 154-155) are not Missionslegal.
