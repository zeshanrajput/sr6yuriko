## Clubs

## Spikes

Whether as simple as nails hammered into a baseball bat or something individually machined and welded, spikes add a lethal sting to clubs of all kinds.

- Keep in mind that law enforcement usually sees clubs as sensible self-defense not worthy of much note, but spikes draw significantly more attention.
