## Nitro Boost

Few modern vehicles still employ internal combustion, but “nitrous oxide” still carries a colloquial understanding among gearheads as a temporary acceleration booster, even if it’s actually a customindustrial capacitor. These mods are invariably installed along with drag chutes or similar drastic braking options, meaning this temporary attribute increase can be applied to both stopping and accelerating. When triggered via Minor Action, nitro boost doubles the Acceleration of a vehicle for 1D6 rounds. Vehicle tests made during this time convert one die to a Wild Die.
