## Incendiary Grenade

Made by manufacturers all over the world, the standard incendiary grenade does one job: It lights things on fire. Modern advancements have not changed the overall concept much. Instead, the updates simply make the incendiary explosive much more effective. First, the grenade sprays the area with a flammable gas, then it releases white phosphorous, igniting the gas and causing anything flammable to catch fire.

Incendiary grenades do their damage as Fire damage (p. 110, _SR6_ ).

_Gamemaster note:_ The damage and duration of the Burning status should be adjusted appropriately to the situation, but the recommended baseline value for the Burning status effect should be 4.
