## Remote Pilot

Driving is a fine thing to do—unless you’re actually in the vehicle, where all that movement is just too disorienting. You prefer to drive at a remove.

- **Bonus:** 12 Karma

- **Game Effect:** You may not gain or spend Edge when piloting a vehicle unless you are not physically present within the vehicle.
