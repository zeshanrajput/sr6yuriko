## Driving Style: Reckless Driver

You take risks whenever you get the chance, relying on the wild and chaotic nature of the chase and your own luck to get you through to the other side. There is no middle ground for you. You’re either going to win big or crash and burn. Or both!

- **Cost:** 8 Karma

- **Game Effect:** You only pay half the required Edge cost for the following uses:

  - Double Down only on Piloting tests (1,2, or 3 Edge)



- Chicken (3 Edge)

- Equalizer (1 Edge)

- Up the Ante (1,2, or 3 Edge)
