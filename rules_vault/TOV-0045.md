## Asogwe

A mambo asogwe or houngan asogwe is considered the high priest of a single temple. Only once a sur pwen has gained knowledge of all the spiritual needs and ceremonies are they able to achieve the highest rank in the religion through the initiation ceremony. Upon achieving the highest rank, they are presented with the asson of their offi ce and are expected to lead ceremonies and guide their adherents. They are the ones leading the rituals asking the lwa for help in healing others and cleansing the energy of those who worship.

**Cost:** 8 Karma

**Game Effect:** To take this quality, you must have lost the Honsi, Sur Pwen, or Asogwe quality. You gain a point of Edge when creating gris-gris or zombies, which must be spent on the ritual or is lost. If you had achieved the rank of asogwe before losing your community, you still retain your asson.
