## Perforated Firewall

Maybe it’s bad luck, or maybe it’s because you keep hitting the wrong links on the Matrix, but any firewall you touch seems to start to crumble.

- **Bonus:** 3 Karma

Simply put, no matter where you go, you stick out.

- **Bonus:** 7 Karma

- **Game Effect:** You may not gain or spend Edge on Stealth tests.
