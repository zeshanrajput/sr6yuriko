## Hyundai Shin-Hyung

If you want options, then this is the sedan for you. It’s built around a fairly powerful motor, and the tuner community has latched onto this ride like a barghest with a piasma bone. Flash and performance can all be bought with the right investment. This setup has made it a popular choice in the Asian criminal factions as well as a way to seem tough if you’re a spoiled corporate rich kid looking to slum with the street toughs.
