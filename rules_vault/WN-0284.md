## Satellite Link Host

**Theme:** A simple white room with a collection of doors spinning at the center. All the IC appears as slight variations on the men-in-dark-sunglasses motif.

| **RATING**<br>8 | **A**<br>11 | **S**<br>8 | **D**<br>9 | **F**<br>10 |

**IC:** Patrol, Acid, Blaster, Track, Binder, Black IC, Tar Baby, Sparky, Killer **Notes:** This host is the security hub to access this and other CIA safehouses. It also holds a data collection with backups of all the footage ever recorded in the safehouse and contains several references to someone named Meiers who seems to be in charge. This is dangerous data in the wrong hands, as it can be used to identify agents and those who have been taken into the safehouse, whether for protection or interrogation. This can also help identify the spot as a UCAS CIA safehouse.
