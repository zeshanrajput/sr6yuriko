## Landmine Modification

Essentially, a landmine is a grenade with a special triggering mechanism. This “modification” is just a shorthand way to describe a landmine that has the properties of a grenade. A landmine can be loaded with any of the explosives and contents of a grenade. Applied pressure arms the landmine, then the subsequent release of that pressure triggers it.
