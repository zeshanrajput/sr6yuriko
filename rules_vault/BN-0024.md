## **New Shifter Adult Life Modules for Life Path** Character Creation

## Li ve Caged

## (Shifter only)

You spent signifi cant time imprisoned and forced to labor. These were grueling times, but you made it through. Could have been forced labor in a mine, a bunraku parlor, or some other dire circumstance.

- +1 Body, Strength, or Willpower

- +1 Intuition, Agility, or Reaction

- +1 Con, Edge, or Magic

- +1 Charisma, Close Combat, or Perception

- **Contact points:** 2

- **Contact types:** Street or Criminal

- **Knowledge Skill:** Smuggling, Mining, Agriculture, or one level in any language skill.
