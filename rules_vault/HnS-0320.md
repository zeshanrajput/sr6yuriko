## Joining a Group

If an individual desires to join an existing group, and they meet with all the required restrictions and strictures, they may do so by undergoing Symmetry. This process requires the assistance of at least two other members of the group in good standing, a number of hours equal to the Resonance attriUNIONbute of the would-be member, and the expenditure of 6 Karma by the petitioner. Once completed, the member has full benefits, if any, possessed by the group.
