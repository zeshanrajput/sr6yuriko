## Lucky Fail

[Trigger: Failed Skill Test, Action: Minor (A)] 1 Edge per +2 Dice

Sometimes a failed attempt can be used to set someone else up for success. After a skill test is failed, a player character may spend a Minor Action and some Edge to help one of their allies. For every point of Edge you spend, one ally gains +2 dice pool on their next skill test. This counts as a Teamwork bonus and is limited by the number of ranks the player character has in that skill. They also gain a point of Edge that must be spent on the test, or it’s lost—but one point of Edge regardless of how much is spent on Lucky Fail.
