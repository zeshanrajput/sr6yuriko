## Kazimira “Cashmere” Burakgazi

## Female Elf

For more than fifty years, the Café Cezve at Moritzplatz has been the beating heart of the Kreuzbasar kiez. This is thanks to its founder and former operator Altug Burakgazi, who, in addition to serving genuine Turkish coffee, also gained a reputation for offering valuable information services. “Uncle Altug” also gathered a crowd of homeless children around him, whom he used as tails, spies, and messengers. Sometime in the sixties, when Altug retired, Cashmere—another child of this “Cezve clan”—took over and has continued in his spirit ever since.

Cashmere is a small, unobtrusive woman in her late thirties, with mousy hair and ever-awake eyes, who has an excellent reputation in Berlin’s shadows. Her main asset lies in her close local network and a web of relationships that has grown over many decades. Many a now-legendary shadowrunner got their first chance at Café Cezve or was found bleeding in the gutter by the Cezve kids and brought in time to Ezkibel, the elven ripperdoc at Moritzplatz, who is a close ally of Cashmere and the Cezve clan.
