## Anarchist

The authority is clearly wrong, and this NPC works against all factions they consider part of that category. They don’t care if the team is hurting the corps or the government, but if they take away the free will of the individual, they’ll make the runners’ lives hell. That said, anarchists make some of the best contacts if you want to lie low and avoid attention.

**Game Effect:** The contact gains 4 dice to resist any influence or con from authority figures.
