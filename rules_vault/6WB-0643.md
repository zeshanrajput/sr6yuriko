## Adept Powers

These are the powers adepts can use to give them the boost they need. The listings include the power point cost and the type of action needed to activate the power. Some adept powers have levels, meaning their effect can be increased as multiple levels are purchased. Others are a flat bonus—you buy them once, and you have the full thing!
