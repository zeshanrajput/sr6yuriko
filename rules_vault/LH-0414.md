## Special Interests

## Posted by: Sunshine

Despite what the corps may want us to think, even if they are keeping the whole affair a secret, the Disian Shadow War touched several other groups as well. Some of them were victims, some of them worked behind the scenes, and others fought on the front lines. While there are way too many to mention, the following either had an impact on the war itself or are poised to become power players in the near future—perhaps both.
