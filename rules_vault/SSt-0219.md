## **Make Nice with the Neighbors**

**I’m not saying you have to be their best friends— in fact, it’s probably best if you aren’t—but making some overtures to the neighbors so that they think well of you is a good idea. Leave them a cookie basket when you move in. Hold doors for them. Don’t cause disruptions, and be pleasant when you see them. Remember their names and use them, without being aggressive about it. Be kind without being pushy. All you want is them to not believe any accusations about you they might hear.**

- **Also, if you get to know them, you’ll get a sense of who else in the building might be covering up some nefarious activities, which can be valuable for a number of reasons.**

- **> Plan 9**
