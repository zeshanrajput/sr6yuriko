## Positive Quality: Essence Regrowth

Your aura is healing or adapting to a significant change. You may only take this quality if you have an Essence hole (see p. 146, Sixth World Companion), whether from a removed implant, damage from an injury or addiction, or having a body divergent from your Essence. This healing represents a natural regrowth, but also the time taken to understand and accept the changes that you are going through. After healing more than a point of Essence, your aura will seem very different to anyone who perceived it before the regrowth happened.

This quality cannot be taken at character creation.

**Cost:** 10 Karma (not subject to doubling)

- **Game effect:** At the end of each month, you may spend

- 1 Karma to regrow 0.05 Essence.

  - This quality only lasts until the Essence hole is healed, at which time the quality is lost. You may only take this quality with the explicit permission of your gamemaster.
