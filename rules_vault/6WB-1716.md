## Dog Nights

## Main Location: AztechSchönwalde

Notable Characters: Barghest (p. 216)

Animal rights activists have freed five barghests from a critter farm on the western edge of Berlin. Since killer beasts roaming freely through the district are bad PR, Aztechnology hires runners to track down the pack and either neutralize the critters or—better and with extra pay—bring them back to the critter farm unharmed.

**Original Message:** Do you like animals? Then help us save some of them. Our darlings have been forcibly freed from their familiar environment, where they were sheltered and cared for, and now pose a danger to themselves and

others. Ms. Erika Schmidt is looking forward to your call.

**Note:** “Schmidt” is the German equivalent to “Johnson.”
