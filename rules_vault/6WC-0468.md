## Metagenic <Attribute> Improvement

## **Cost:** 15 Karma

An individual with the Metagenic Attribute Improvement possesses a genetic expression that enhances the genes associated with the development of a certain mental or physical attribute. In game terms, the maximum value for one of the character’s attributes (as listed in the Metatype Attribute Table, p. 64, _SR6_ ) is raised by 1. This quality may only be taken once per attribute, and it is cumulative with the Exceptional Attribute quality (p. 71, _SR6_ ).
