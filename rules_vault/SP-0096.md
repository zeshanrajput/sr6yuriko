## **Fighting a Dirty War**

**All other problems aside, the greatest challenge Grey Cell now faces is one of ethics and conscience. So far, their two biggest allies, Evo and Renraku, have no qualms about using whatever means are at their disposal to deal with the Disian threat. This includes using or at least condoning a lot of brutal tactics, the acceptance of collateral damage, and the use of torture on prisoners to gather intelligence.**

**For people like Cole and especially McCord, who have built their reputations on foundations of professionalism and a code of honor, this may be the hardest part of the current confict. Because is the self-respect of a small number of people worth the very survival of the planet?**

- **Not just Grey Cell—a lot of people are taking hard looks in the mirror. The threat of annihilation tends to bring out the darker sides of metahuman nature.**
