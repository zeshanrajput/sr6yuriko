## Counter Peel

## Threshold: 3

This is used when withdrawing from a combat zone or moving to find better cover. The participants form a sloped or diagonal battle line, allowing each team member a clear field of fire. The team member closest to the enemy then withdraws down the line, while under supportive fire from the rest of the team, moving to a position beyond the end of the battle line. The process repeats until all team members have moved or cleared a combat zone. To perform this maneuver, each participant must have a ranged weapon. ( **Notes/Benefits:** +3 to DR.)
