## Glider Wings

Large wings extend from the exoframe when needed and fold close to the critter’s body at other times. A critter must be specially trained to trust their glider wings, or else they won’t take advantage of them.

**Game Information:** A critter must be equipped with a critterjack and receive special training in order to utilize this accessory. If the critter intentionally jumps from a height, they must use a Major Action and make an Athletics + Agility (2) test to land safely, in which case they take no damage from a fall of any height and may glide up to two meters for every meter they descend. If the test fails, the critter falls straight down but takes only half the usual damage from the fall.
