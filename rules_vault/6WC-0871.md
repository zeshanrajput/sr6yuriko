## Hotel California

This quality only applies to safehouses. You’re allowed in, but only allowed out at certain times. There are a variety of reasons for this, some of them even make sense. But the fact of the matter is, you’re locked down for a set amount of time determined by the master of the house. Inconvenient, but, hey, the cred off your stick is a bit lower. **Cost:** –3 LP
