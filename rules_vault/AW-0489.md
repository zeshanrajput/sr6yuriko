## The Mist

A phenomenon known as the Mist occasionally materializes in the forests of Brittany, France. The rolling fog that forms in the forest can cause astral rifts to appear. These rifts cause forced projection, pulling the unwary through the associated astral passageways and into the Metaplane of Kin or the Faerie Metaplane. When the fog becomes especially thick, the passageways can shift to metaplanar rifts, taking unwilling travelers physically to the metaplanes, leaving them stranded until they can find another metaplanar passageway back to the physical realm.
