## Rival Runners

Easy to forget this one, but your team ain’t the only players in the game. Dozens of other teams operate in the shadows, looking for work, same as you. Sometimes their job and your job will cross one another up, at which point you have to be ready to make some hard calls real quick. There’s nothing quite like pulling off a perfect infiltration while a little buzz in the back of your head tells you that it’s been too easy, only to find another team already in the lab, target in hand. How you handle this separates the rookies from the pros.
