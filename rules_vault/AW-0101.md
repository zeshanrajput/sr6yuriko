## Mist Queen

The Mist Queen is described many times as an alabaster beauty. Her majesty is formed from gently falling snow and mist into a larger-than-life humanoid figure four meters tall. Light gathers around her features to highlight her regal position. She could be mistaken for a giant on the material plane if not for the absence of color. She has a calm and soothing voice—no one can stay angry facing gently falling snow. She is ruler over the Plane of Water and the great arbitrator of the metaplanes.

While the stories of her negotiation of truces and treaties between metaplanes are legendary, sometimes she seems to be outmatched. Where she normally sits on a frosty throne of clouds tohear out spirits who have come to Korikyu, she is instead sidelined by interruptions or sits silently when the Dis ambassador takes center stage to issue new decrees or cite amended laws. Some of her subjects are starting to question who is in charge.
