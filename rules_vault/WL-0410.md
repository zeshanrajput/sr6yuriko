## **Critter Companion Qualities**

**Critters sometimes have positive or negative qualities, though not all of the pre-existing qualities are available to all critters. When deciding which qualities you’d like to assign to your critter, check with your gamemaster to determine whether or not they are appropriate. Regarding negative qualities, a good rule of thumb is that if the quality doesn’t affect a critter’s normal capabilities, it isn’t available—therefore, qualities affecting social or technical skills would generally be unavailable.**

**The qualities included in this chapter are specifically designed for critters and are unavailable for sapient critters and metahuman characters. These qualities are also designed to apply to tamed and trained critters.**
