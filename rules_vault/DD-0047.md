## Conceal Scent

It’s like invisibility for body odor! This spell conceals any source of scent coming from the target, whether this is from poor hygiene, tailored pheromones or chemical objects. This is handy for hunters who don’t want prey picking up their scent, or for fooling olfactory sensors, as this works on both natural and artificial
