## Nano-Critter Toxin Injector

Individual critters that are naturally equipped with a proboscis, stinger, or fangs can be implanted with a tiny toxin injector that can deliver a minute but highly concentrated dose of any desired toxin or drug. This technology was initially developed to deliver vaccines via mosquitoes in impoverished areas,

The injector only carries a single dose, after which it becomes effectively useless. This technology is relatively random unless combined with a bio-drone harness, but if a genetic sample of an individual is collected, the critter can be programmed to deliver its payload to that person.
