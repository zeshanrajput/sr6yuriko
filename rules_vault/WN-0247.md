## **Rachael Zeiher (7) [4]**

The best way to be a good pickpocket is to be hard to ID and harder to find. This one could baffle the runners enough on trying to find her that the trick might go the other way. If the runners can’t pull her ID, they can spot her when she lifts a commlink off someone near Terminal while they’re working in the area. She was originally picked up on a loitering charge after she tried to pickpocket some high-end suit and got a needle dose of neurostun instead. Corp scum left her for the rats but the cops picked her up.
