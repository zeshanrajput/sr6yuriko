## > **Learson Shipyards**

Learson has been building ships for over half a century, with clients all over the Pacific Rim. A favorite target of eco groups, violent and otherwise, for some of their leaky tanker designs, they’ve recently refocused on bids to maintain Seattle’s newfound fleet. They’ve been a target for corp-sponsored runs on their R&D, but expect that to escalate as competitors try to take them down a peg.
