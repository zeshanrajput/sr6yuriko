## Cyber-Implant Weapons Buzzsaw

A buzzsaw is an unsubtle weapon with no place in polite society. The user’s hand and forearm are replaced with a circular, spinning saw blade or a long chainsaw and a power source to run the weapon. Splatter guards are recommended, but commonly omitted. A buzzsaw doubles its DV when employed against a barrier.
