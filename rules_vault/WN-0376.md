## Special Delivery

At some point while the PCs are in the lab, a messenger arrives. They are carrying a black metal box, five centimeters by three centimeters by two centimeters. It has a biometric lock (rating 4) set to unlock via thumbprint. The metal is decently strong for a small box (Structure rating 10). The messenger has instructions to deliver it directly to Dr. Rose Salinger. The path of the box goes like this:

- The messenger (use stats for Pickpocket, p. 170, _Sixth World Companion_ ) talks to security, telling them they have a message to be delivered personally to Dr. Salinger. Security says they will give it to her, but the courier says they have to put it right in the doctor’s hands. This wrangling proceeds for two minutes.

- The messenger is sent to patient room 5, the second door on the right past the security room. They wait there for ten minutes.

- After ten minutes, Dr. Salinger’s assistant comes to pick up the box. The courier once again explains that they cannot give it to anyone besides Dr. Salinger. This conversation lasts two minutes.

- Finally, the assistant escorts the courier down the hall to the last door on the right. This is Dr. Salinger’s office. When the assistant and courier arrive at the door, it opens, and a woman in a medical coat holds out her hand. The courier gives her a once over and then places the box in her hand. The doctor goes back into her office, and the courier leaves.

If the PCs intercept and open the box, they’ll find a data chip with a message on it. It is protected by a Data Bomb (rating 6). The message on the data chip can be played and replayed without harming it or triggering the Data Bomb. The data on it is heavily encrypted (rating 10, with Data Bomb). The file is a recording of a synthesized voice expressing the desire for a meeting with some directors of Animus Construction in just a few days (see p. 114 for more details on the message). They give a secure drop address for the response.

If the PCs leave the lab with the chip, they will receive their full promised payment. Any other result leads to reduced payment. The meeting will still happen (see mission 30, p. 114), as Dr. Salinger will be informed of it through other means.
