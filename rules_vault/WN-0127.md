## Special Notes

The goal of this initial adventure is to get the runners into the city and become seen as valuable new players in the shadows. Eager, being who he is, will spread the word of the runners’ exploits and emphasize the fact that he brought them to town. This will generate more work for the runners, who will soon be contacted by other fixers looking for fresh talent.
