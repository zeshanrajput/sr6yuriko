## Wrecker

Junkyard-built, extreme close combat weapons are limited in form solely by the imaginations of those twisted creators who manufacture them. Categorically, any kind of blunt or spiked impact weapon is known on the streets as a wrecker. They don’t necessarily involve the complete replacement of a user’s forearm and hand, but they usually do. Like buzzsaws and crab claws, these weapons are crude, rude, and unwelcome in law-abiding society.
