## Meeting Ms. Johnson

The Ms. Johnson selected for this job explains that the all-important Met Gala is only a few weeks away. The theme for the Gala is Convergence: Where Magic and Technology Meet in Fashion. Designers are tight-lipped about what they have planned, and Ms. Johnson needs to know what other designers are doing.

In this mission, the runners’ fixer arranges a meeting with Ms. Johnson at Nowhere, a club in Terminal. Ms. Johnson is Becky Wu Ping, a prominent fashion designer. She asks for the runners ‘ help with a pair of smash-and-grab-style tasks. She opens negotiations at 2,000 nuyen per runner and more work if they can complete the job. She is willing to go as high as 2,500 nuyen per runner. Once the fee is set, she explains that there are two tasks she needs the runners to accomplish. For safety reasons, she will only give them one task at a time. When they finish a task, they need to return to her for the next job.
