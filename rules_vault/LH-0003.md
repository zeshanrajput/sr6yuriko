## Introduction

The Doomsday clock is ticking ever closer to the extinction of metahumanity. Across the globe, horrific machines are poised to strip the material plane of all it’s mana and leave the Earth nothing more than a lifeless husk. But as precious seconds pass and most of the world is paralyzed, there are those in the shadows who’ve banded together to defend the Sixth World against this threat.

_Lethal Harvest_ chronicles the last several months of the Disian Shadow War; the battles, the victories, the losses, and the lasting impact it will have on the Sixth World.

- **Welcome Back to the War Room** outlines the current Disian threat and brings those new to the Disian Shadow War up to date on just how dire the situation really is.

- **Opposition Report** discusses the current status of the Disians, their proxies, and their so-called allies whose loyalty may be wavering … all the while enacting plans of their own. It also talks about a new and horrific Disian weapon that could tip the balance of power in their favor.

- **Resistance At All Costs** looks at the Defenders, those who have joined forces to combat the Disian threat against all odds, their methods, their tactics, and how in some cases are barely holding on in the face of destruction.

- **When Discretion Isn’t the Better Part** shows that in every war, there are those who would sit on the sidelines, seek only the means to save their own skins, or to find a way to profit. Because after all, business must go on, even in the face of Armageddon.

- **Pocket Wars** takes this another step further and shows that not everything going is directly connected to the shadow war. Even when survival is in doubt, some are making plays to increase their own power and influence. On the off chance that the world survives that is.

- **In the Crossfire** shows how the Disian Shadow War affects the common person through the journal entries of an MCT employee who finds themselves drawn into the conflict and gets a first-hand look at what’s really going on.

- **Battles Within Shadows** dives right into the battles being fought in the shadows and across the globe, giving first-hand accounts of those fighting on the front lines of this shadow war. Here there are stories of valor and triumph as well as horror and brutality.

- **Rank and File** highlights some of the major players of the war as well as those who are poised to be major players in the future.

- **New Gear and Dark Tech** highlights new and advanced technology and weaponry developed for the final battle against the Disians. The nature of this technology and weaponry reveals the lengths some are willing to go in order to ensure their survival.

- **Aftershocks and the Disian Influence** takes

- a hard look at the results of the Disian Shadow War where nothing has remained untouched. The corporate landscape, society in general, the shadows, and even magic itself will be forever altered.
