## Balance Receptor Alteration

The process provides enhancements to various internal organs that regulate an individual’s balance, augmenting them to a more refined and reactive state. This gives the individual a bonus point of Edge on all balance tests, including falling and landing safely. Note the rules in the Preventing Edge Abuse section (p. 45, _SR6_ ). Cannot be combined with anything else that provides an edge to the Athletics skill.
