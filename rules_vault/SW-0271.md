## Mutable Form

(Free)

**==> picture [228 x 20] intentionally omitted \<==**

**----- Start of picture text -----**<br>
TYPE ACTION RANGE DURATION<br>P Auto Self Special<br>**----- End of picture text -----**<br>

Typically, spirits using the Materialization power appear with the same form each time. Mutable Form allows the spirit to change this form each time they materialize, making them look physically different whenever they want, but it does not change the spirit’s aura, leaving them unchanged on the astral plane. If the spirit also has the Realistic Form power (p. 70), they gain a point of situational Edge and a dice pool bonus equal to half the spirit’s Force on Disguise Tests (p. 94, _SR6_ ) made to impersonate someone. This power is only available to spirits with Materialization. The chosen form lasts until the spirit chooses to end the use of the Materialization power.
