## Hook

Rikki Nguyen is aware of the success the group has recently had with restoring Becky Wu Ping’s memory, and he thinks they seem the natural choice for his next job. Like a fashion trend shaping up, he wants the runners to help figure out what is going on with someone who has lost at least part of their mind.

Rikki offers the team 2,000 nuyen each for helping this poor sot out, with negotiations increasing this by 200 nuyen per hit, to a maximum of 3,000 nuyen.
