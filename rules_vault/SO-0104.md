## Scaling

The Vampire’s Invitation is generally packaged for a particular result, but gets dicey when you’re playing in corporate territory like arcologies and housing parks. The big problem is that everything is funneled through the corps, so anything like maintenance or security has to be modified and modeled after their masters’ procedure. In that case, just take the place of, say, Ares maintenance personnel or a Knight Errant. Obviously you’re going to need better fake credentials for that, so it’s more work and maybe more cash up front. Pretty much boilerplate infiltration, at that point. The more casual approaches like Missionaries or especially Hot Day work a lot better here, though, since if you’re there it’s assumed you’re meant to be there.

- For a little extra credibility on a Hot Day Play, wear branded clothing that matches the local corporate culture. Ares Hardplay jogging shorts will play a lot better in an Ares park than Shiawase Slimmers.
