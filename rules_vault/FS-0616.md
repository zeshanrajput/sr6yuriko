## Molotov

## (Professional Rating 3)

Halloweeners who survive a few burnings realize that the gang may seem like a raging wildfire to those outside their ranks, but being inside lets you see the point of the fire and the flame: purification. This isn’t theological, but simply a desire to return everything to a simpler time when fire was respected. Halloweeners who reach these ranks see people’s everyday use of fire and flame and despise how their powerful mistress is taken advantage of and disrespected. Molotovs have the discipline and experience to harness fire safely and wield it with focus and precision. Halloweeners are certainly aware of the irony of this behavior when combined with the rank’s title.
