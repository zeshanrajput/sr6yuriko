## **North America**

**While not as tightly controlled as Central and South America, North America (specifcally the eastern part of the UCAS) is considered to be enemy territory by all anti-Disian forces. Being the frst continent the Disians arrived on, North America is where they are the most entrenched. They’ve also fortifed more locations in North America than any other location on the planet. It should be noted, however, that like the corporations, not every government in North America is allied with Dis. Some are, while others are simply blissfully unaware. But there are those who have seen what’s really going on and are trying to work against Dis; they simply need a direction to focus their efforts.**

- **One such person is (interim) CIA Director Kent Burroughs. Formerly operations chief, Burroughs was put in the top spot as a placeholder while the UCAS got itself back together. But instead of simply keeping a seat warm, Burroughs became proactive and looking in places he shouldn’t have. Now he and his agency are neck deep in Dis.**

- **> Thorn**
