## Analysts

Having raw data is one thing, but often it takes specialists to sift through that data and make sense of it or find the patterns or connections that others may not. Grey Cell Analysts are responsible for given the data collected by the Watchers and giving it not only context, but when necessary determining what priority it may have; which could mean the difference between avoiding and experiencing a disaster. Analysts are also often the ones doing direct research (like myself) into new magical techniques and tactics to combat magical threats, also using what pertinent data the Watchers may uncover. And every so often, Analysts are the ones who, armed with this data, may directly investigate or confront threats (depending on the Analyst, of course …).
