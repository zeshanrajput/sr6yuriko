## Creating a Piece of Art

Creating a piece (a song, a painting, etc.) is different than performing live. Characters can specialize in Composing with the Con skill to focus on creating wiz wax. The base time to create a piece is 10 days per minute of the song or dance, or per square or cubic decimeter of canvas or other medium, while other forms of art may take even longer, at the gamemaster’s discretion. Make a Performance test with a threshold of 6, or 4 if the character has the Composing specialization, and divide the base time by the number of net hits. A failed test means the piece automatically has an Impact of 1 (see below).
