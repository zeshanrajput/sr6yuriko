## Silver Nail

(New runner, Connection Level 2)

Lex James enjoyed his life when his parents worked at Microdeck Industries. That all changed during his last year of school—he was diagnosed with a disease, then his family moved to Manhattan to start working on some project for Spinrad Global. His parents had said it was a great opportunity for the family, but Lex didn’t see how.

Using all the skills, talent, and contacts he could acquire, Lex decided he would get away for a while. He would take a walkabout and see what else there was to life. If he did it and didn’t enjoy it, he could always go back and continue the course his family had mapped out for him.

Silver Nail is the alias Lex James is using while he takes his journey. He has overheard his parents and his father’s friend talk about shadowrunners. It sounds exciting, so that’s the road he elects to travel.

Lex is 1.9 meters tall and weighs 90 kilos. He has a pale complexion, blue eyes, and red hair. He is using a sustained Physical Mask spell (5 hits) to appear as a dark-eyed white person with dark blond hair that looks silver on the end. The hair appears spiked (which is where his street name came from), and he looks similar to a popular trid character.
