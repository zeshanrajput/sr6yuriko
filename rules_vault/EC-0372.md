## Graham Cracker City Sites

## Denning Castle

You’d think that a mage group would have occupied this place first, but instead it’s a den of deckers. Who wouldn’t want a place with its own moat, dungeon, wine cellar, and medieval village? The place was worse for wear, with uneven wetlands now surrounding it. With the addition of a threemeter-tall concrete wall around most of the property, the place became well defended against trespassers. One of the towers has added satellite uplink capabilities for pirated broadcasts.
