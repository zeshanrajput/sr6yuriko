## Alternative Campaigns

The default _Shadowrun_ style is a game that follows the career of a team of shadowrunners. The Sixth World is a complex and rich world, which provides many opportunities to tell a wide range of stories. Included here are some suggestions for alternate campaign styles, along with some guidelines for how you might approach them.
