## Imitation Juggernaut

As above, but with a twist: the big game hunter has mistaken rumors about a huge, armored, and unstoppable beast in the Texas panhandle for “merely” being about a twenty-meter-long Awakened armadillo. Once they find the beast, they discover it is in fact an ornery western dragon that violently wants to be left the frag alone. This discovery only amplifies the big game hunter’s bloodlust for a trophy, rather than quenching it.
