## **Firelion**

## **(Panthera Leo infernus)**

**A modern legend of the Zulu tribe speaks of the fame-hearted lion that rises up to bring a power and unity to the tribe not known for hundreds of years, since the imperialists brought war to their tribal lands. I headed off to Azania and began looking into this species with serious precautions in place after hearing what had happened to several other research groups.**

**The frelion is physically dangerous. Besides their fre traits, they are a quarter larger than their mundane counterparts. The male variety of this**

**Awakened lion has a fame orange mane and black tuft of hair at the end of its tail that can ignite. The rest of its fur is a charred black color but often appears grayish white from smudges of ash created by their fres. They are obviously extremely resistant to fre. The female lacks the mane, but the tail tuft is still there and in place of a mane they bear thick tufts of fur over their paws.**

**The paws and mane of female and male create an extremely fammable oil that burns at an extreme heat but is quickly consumed. A portion of this is arcane and related to the frelion’s resistance. These oils will be spread in a pattern to corral prey or create traps once ignited.**

**It was a challenging study. We needed to maintain observation distance with successful tracking, full stealth protocols, and a full 360-degree security feld. Thanks to a rather generous grant by Evo and the use of some of their latest and greatest experimental tech, we were able to silently glide high above the territories of several separate prides and keep tabs.**

**That key word right there is the big hint for how I ended up trapped in a blaze, surrounded by a hunting pride that was expert at thwarting my every move.**

**It started so innocuously, with a few minor ship alarms and some platitudes by the Evo techs about nothing wrong. Then four of the drones went out in rapid succession followed by more platitudes. It was just one day. It was just a short stint. Didn’t lead to anything, but it was certainly something intentional. We snagged some footage from the node and spotted the actions of the lions. Three young males released large quantities of their oil, the vapors rising and initializing some error in the air intake systems. The same move with the drones caused them to fame out and crash.**

**Give it a few days, we see some excellent hunts, including a hunt that brings down an earthshaker, and we’re all calmed down a bit about the glitches but living on cloud nine from the research. As we are evaluating patterns in the burn corralling, the air system alarms go off again. One moment the ARO is chirping, the next we see a frefash and the whole rig is flled with smoke, lurching, then plummeting. Several pops in the distance have the rigger screaming, and then BOOM! We are dirtside.**

**Exterior footage shows the pride members oiling and popping the doors they can reach. A moment later they’re inside the craft and it’s close quarters combat with massive feline predators fashing fres on walls and along foors. Troll sec guards go hand-to-hand. One moment it’s all tooth, claws, tusks, horns, and spurs; the next it’s a fash and a fame-up, and the troll is down after being smeared in oil.**

**I’m stuck on the troll sec train wreck and Kastlana slaps the back of my head and starts dragging me back down the corridor. She’s been the expert on their hunting patterns and styles and was the only way I got dragged back out of that crashed**
