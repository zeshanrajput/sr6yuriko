## Another Job

After Mr. and Mrs. James have been saved, Silver Nail will ask that the team stay at his place. It’s a middle-class apartment in a nice area of the city. He says he’ll tell the players about the other job after the Spinrad Global event is over. The rest of the evening, he is glued to Matrix feeds until Ervine and Gail James are seen getting into their limo and driving off. At that moment, it looks like the weight of the world has been lifted.

He sadly looks up at the players and says, “I only intended to get away for a little while, but I created even bigger troubles. I believe you all know who I am.” At that point, the image of Silver Nail changes to that of Lex James. He continues, “I have no

doubt El Guapo has offered you money to bring me back. I have a counteroffer for you, though. I have to go back, I know that. But I don’t want to go back like that. Can you all help me create a good cover story? So I don’t get in as much trouble or create any other issues for my parents? I’ll give you the micro-brick with Double A’s MCT data on it, 6,500 nuyen, and the stuff I‘ve been using since I started this charade. That includes my Yamaha Nodachi (p. 24, _Double Clutch_ ), Hermes Ikon, and a Defiance Super Shock Taser.”
