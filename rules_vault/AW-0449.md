## Crimson Carnage Motorcycles

The Crimson Carnage ride motorcycles that are equivalent to the Harley-Davidson Scorpion, with two notable exceptions:

First, they are covered in nasty spikes and blades. This improves the bike’s armor by +2, and anyone attempting to make a melee attack or leap onto the bike suffers 6P damage (resisted with Body) whether the attempt is successful or not

Second, the bikes are armed with bola launchers and net guns. These are somewhat magical in nature, and function exactly like the Binding critter power. Use the rider’s attributes for the attack test.
