## Lorelei Angel

Still a stunner, the Phoenix shaman continues to do shows independently at her own pace. She doesn’t maintain a very busy schedule anymore, mostly holding secret shows or single appearances whenever and wherever she likes. Her mastery of illusion magic has only improved over the years, and her live show is still considered one of the best in the biz.
