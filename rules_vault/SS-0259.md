## Brief

I’m Lancaster Jones, and I’m looking for a group of adventure seekers to discover the true final end of the Ghost Flight. What is the Ghost Flight? Read my articles and you’ll see. What we know so far is that this is a flying and mobile alchera of World War 2-era airplanes that come in from the Pacific and fly into the Olympia region of the Salish-Shidhe Nation.

I need a team to track the flight, potentially cross into the alchera (if possible) and investigate the history of this event. The arcane nature of the event lends itself to a team of individuals with magical knowledge, but transportation and vehicular skills mean at least one technically skilled individual is necessary.
