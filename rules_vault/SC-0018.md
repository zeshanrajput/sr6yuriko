## academic mage

## Plot hooks & adventure seeds

This section is primarily intended for use by the gamemaster, but players are also encouraged to read through them. These are story prompts and adventure ideas for academic magicians. There are no spoilers here, since this is not about detailing endings, and as a player you are encouraged to recommend the ones that interest you most to your gamemaster, along with any character themes or background ideas that you want to involve or explore. Then leave the final details up to the gamemaster and the action that occurs within the game sessions.
