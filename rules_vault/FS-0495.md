## Less-Lethal is Expensive

You’ll mostly hear this argument from runners who are already stuck scraping up every last nuyen they can to try to keep a roof over their heads and food in their stomachs. I can’t really blame someone for buying cheaper ammo so they have enough money left over to eat. On the other hand, some of the people who complain about the price of less-lethal ammo only do so after they’ve spent most of their money on APDS and EX-Explosive rounds, or are just such goddamn cheapskates that they flat-out refuse to buy anything other than regular ball ammo no matter how much money they have.
