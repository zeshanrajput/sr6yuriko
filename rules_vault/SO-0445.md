## Stiff Upper Lip

Your composure is your armor, and you know how to use it as a weapon as well.

**Cost:** 9 Karma

**Game Effect:** When you succeed on a Composure test, gain a point of Edge. You may also choose to use a Major action during combat to center yourself and express your unfazed composure to those around you—make a Composure (3) test, which grants you a point of Edge if you succeed. Per the GM’s discretion, you may take a Major action to trigger a Composure test in other stressful situations besides combat.

Your composure is one of your great strengths, but it is also particularly unsettling to you when it fails. When you fail a Composure test, lose one point of Edge. If you have no Edge to lose, take one box of unresisted Stun damage instead.

You may not take this quality if you have the Brittle Composure negative quality.
