## **Wardens and Keepers**

**Everyone within the XD is either a Keeper or a Warden. Keepers, such as myself, are the covert and support side of the house. We are pilots, hackers, alchemists and enchanters, healers, mechanics, and all the other support and logistics needed for our brethren to carry out the Mission. Some are also investigators, scouts, intelligence analysts, and infltrators. These are much-needed functions, as a large part of the Mission is to investigate and identify threats so that they can be dealt with quietly. This was one of the lessons learned from the May Day Massacres—combining all the investigative and intelligence assets needed to truly deal with terrorists and subversives into the same organization that is charged with eliminating them. When it comes to magical threats, we keep watch on the toxic zones, both as places cults seem to like to hide out and as the source of the abominations that attack our people, and we use those same investigative and intelligence methods one might expect a police force to use.**
