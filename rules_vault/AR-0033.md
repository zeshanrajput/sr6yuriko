## Assensing 

While most physical goods have been removed from the base, the work of aura scrubbing is not as thorough. The direct auras of spells have faded, but some mana ebbs and flows still exist within the base, and the auras surrounding them may be worth examining. Careful assensing (3 hits on an Astral + Intuition test if the character is reasonably close to one of the listed areas) will find three spots of interest: 

- **Leadership Academy:** On the north end of the base is a nondescript two-story building (though to be honest, “nondescript” is pretty much the dominant military architectural style). The building is for leadership training, but it has a low mana ebb (–1 dice pool penalty to any tests using the Magic attribute) in a classroom on the second floor. It was used to teach specialized magic. 

- **STRATCOM building:** Toward the southern part of the building is the large strategic command building. That includes the offices of the MAGDEFCOM (magical defense command) staff, and the sheer amount of mana pumped into this area makes it a low mana flow (+1 dice pool bonus to any tests using the Magic attribute for Manipulation spells or +1 level of the Mystic Armor adept power for adepts who have it). 

- **Security building:** Not far from the east 

gate is security force headquarters, and the basement of the building has a seriously disturbed atmosphere. It has a high mana ebb, so that Edge cannot be gained or spent on tests using the Magic attribute. 

Examining these areas can provide information about what was happening there, which could plant the seeds of future adventures. Each location has information provided based on the number of hits on an Astral + Intuition test.
