## Utility and Support

Most of your support abilities are related to mundane and astral perception, as well as the use of detection spells to notice ambushes or other hidden information. It’s also a good idea to widen the scope of your abilities with a variety of spells, such as Heal, Levitate, Physical Barrier, and one or two combat spells. Unless your team is flush with mages, you’re going to be providing the primary magical support, which means dealing with anything magical standing in the way of your payday.

If you can spare the Karma or skill points, pick up an Outdoors skill with the Tracking specialization. This can be used for tracking someone within astral space as well as in the physical world and is often much faster than a spirit’s Search power.
