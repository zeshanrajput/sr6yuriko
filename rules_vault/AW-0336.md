## True Element Harvesting

Some islands contain veins of true elements. Finding such a vein requires traveling to an appropriate island and searching, which requires an Astral + Intuition (6, 1 hour) Extended test. Success reveals a vein of the true element. Harvesting the vein requires an hour of work and an Enchanting + Magic test. Replace one die with a wild die. For every two hits on the harvesting test, you acquire one unit of the true element. If the wild die rolls a 1, 5, or 6, the vein is exhausted after this test.

Areas where true elements can be found are often hotly contested by other metaplanar travelers, protected by a local guardian, or sought out by chimera employed by Disians. Each test made to locate or harvest a true element brings the possibility of a hostile encounter. The gamemaster may decide to introduce an encounter at this time, or roll 2D6—on a 5 or less, there is a hostile encounter. Possible threats include critters, airship pirates,

and Dis seekers. At the gamemaster’s discretion, a hostile encounter could be avoided with a successful Stealth test.

In addition to true elements, pure reagents can be found and harvested. These reagents are common on any island. Make an Enchanting + Intuition (2, 1 hour) Extended test on any island and gain a point of Edge that must be spent on this test or be lost. For each net hit, you find one pure reagent. The threshold of each subsequent test on the same island increases by one. The increase to the threshold goes away at the rate of one per twenty-four hours. Pure reagents can be broken down into normal reagents with an Enchanting + Magic (2) test. If successful, one pure reagent breaks down into 2D6 + net hits normal reagents.

When a pure reagent is used in the process of creating a focus, add +1 to the enchanter’s dice pool on all related tests—including drain. If only pure reagents are used to create a focus, reduce the Karma cost to create the focus by 1D6 (to a minimum of one).
