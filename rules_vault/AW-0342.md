## Airship Destruction

When an airship’s condition monitor is full, it is effectively destroyed. This doesn’t mean that it blows up or falls from the sky, but it does mean that it won’t be going anywhere soon. Most airships are built from local wood, which is naturally buoyant in the air within the Place of Clouds. Even after the engines, sails, propellers, or other systems are wrecked, the hull will remain afloat and adrift in the air. With the assistance of a spirit or another airship, it can be moved or towed.

To obliterate the wreckage of an airship requires dealing another 12 + (Vehicle’s Body/2) boxes of Physical damage, which isn’t hard to accomplish when it’s a sitting duck. Unless an airship is obliterated in this way, it can still be boarded and looted, and survivors will not fall into the sky (unless pushed).
