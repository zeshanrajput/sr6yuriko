**The obvious answer is: in the UCAS. While this is true, it’s also a pretty incomplete answer. So here’s a more complete portrait of the situation:**

**Outside of North America, the Leif-Killers are operating out of the Scandinavian Union . I’ve never been there, but I was told that they’ve set up a Lodge dedicated to Wolf on a farm near Kiruna and have really good working collaborations with the Sámi. Similarly, they have rear bases set up in Qaqortoq, Thule, in the Trans-Polar Aleut Nation , and in Conne River, Newfoundland, in the UCAS .**

**To the south, we have fallback points and staging areas in the Caribbean League and the Yucatán Autonomous State . They are mostly used to train members or to hide when things get too hairy. In both cases, we have a small, fortifed complex set up, with barracks, a fortifed bunker, training facilities and infrmary, a small helipad, a comm center, and access to a discreet pier. They are located somewhere northeast of Red Bay in the Bahamas and northwest of Cancún.**

**Obviously, we can also be found in the Native American Nations . To the west, we operate out of a nightclub in the Pueblo Corporate Council sector of Boise and out of the old Glen Lyon Inn, in Port Hardy on Vancouver Island, in the Salish-Shidhe Council . From there, we mainly strike at California and the megacorps in the Tsimshian Protectorate, respectively. We do have a fairly signifcant and active presence on Fox Island, in the Free City of Seattle , especially since a certain dragon’s arrival monkey-wrenched local plans of integrating the island to the SSC.**
