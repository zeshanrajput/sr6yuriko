## Zero Sum Mentality

There’s only so much to go around—that means if someone else makes out well, someone else is getting screwed. You either win or you get hosed. Compromise is for losers.

**Bonus:** 11 Karma

**Game Effect:** Whenever you lose an Opposed test of any kind, you lose a point of Edge and your opponent gains one point of Edge. If there are multiple opponents involved, only one of them gains a point of Edge. Per the GM’s discretion, this only applies on tests that have some impact on your character. Just playing rock, paper, scissors with another member of your team so you can transfer a point of Edge to them is not going to work.
