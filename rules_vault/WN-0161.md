## Not What it Seems

The players will need to call El Guapo and give him their findings that Lex wasn’t taken—he left on his own accord. After the players tell him, El Guapo will say “I came to the same conclusion, but I couldn’t approach his friends without creating an issue. Your funds will be sent shortly, and the offer still

stands for the bonus. So long as you remember not to talk about this, I expect I’ll need your services again.”
