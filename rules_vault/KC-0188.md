## I Told You So

## Posted by: Plan 9

I suspected something, and I can enjoy it now that I have actual proof. I was a bit curious when I noticed a lot of sudden value shifting around Yingyong and Kanabo. More so after the fallout from the financial moves between Keiretsu and Lei Yimu. So, we dug around. Turns out, they have two projects under development, not just the one R&D success that made its way to manufacturing. While the Guizhen I developments are solid, with working models under production, I found indication of a second production facility that’s also under Kanabo management. The products from this one are coming out with a lot more hush to them. I’ve also learned that it, too, originated with Gu Guanyu and Shun Yu Chen’s lab group. The money trail from those supporting this project led me to a truly ill-reputed source.

First, the technology. It seems that Shun Yu Chen is not all about optical emitters after all but is more focused upon radiometry and EM projection. Her breakthroughs included a sort of rail-gun particle-projector capable of sending a direct beam down a range up to almost 300 meters. She was able to send radio and wireless related frequencies nearly 5,000 meters. It seems that she was instrumental in creating something else with a far more sinister capacity.

Guizhen II is another form of sleeper technology, but instead of triggering biomechanical symptomology, this one directly interferes with low-wave electronics and cybertronic implantation. This one is a literally disruptive technology. Based principally on older microwave gun considerations, this one has been refined to work as a larger handheld device, about the size of an AK-47 or Yamaha Raiden. But there are larger versions that could easily be mounted onto a vehicle or installation turret. The handheld version has settings capable of causing sustained disruption of any electronic device or implanted cyberware in an individual, including hard nanotechnologies.

Yes, this is a Monad hunter. Within the focus of the emitter, the ability of devices and implants to communicate, transfer power, or even remain operational is impeded. Extreme exposure for prolonged periods of time will directly damage, brick, or even cook the devices.
