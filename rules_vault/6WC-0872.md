## Game Effect

You’re locked in your room and can’t get out until a certain amount of time decided on by the gamemaster goes by. This is typically measured by weeks but is rarely more than a whole month. Expect to get cabin fever, and hope beyond hopes that they included enough entertainment—otherwise you’ll be reading _Catcher In The Rye_ over and over again.
