## **Game Effect: GMT Bone Lacing**

**GMT can be applied to any type of bone lacing. Regardless of the bone lacing material, the effects of this upgrade are the same. The user gains +1 to their Defense Rating and dice pool for defense tests against mana spells and critter powers that are resisted by Willpower, Logic, Intuition, or Charisma. It increases the threshold by +1 to target the user with the following spells and critter powers: Analyze Truth, Mindlink, Mind Probe, Control Thoughts, Compulsion, Confusion, and Infuence. Spells and critter powers that are more emotional in nature, such as Fear and Control Emotions, are not affected by this added protection.**
