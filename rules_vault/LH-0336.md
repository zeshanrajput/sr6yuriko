## FAB III (epsilon) Infection

**Vector:** Contact (both physical and to astrally active bodies)

**Speed:** 6 Hours (8)

**Power:** 8

**Nature:** Dual-nature bacterial

**Effect (ongoing):** Carrier is contagious while the effect is ongoing. Magic Loss (if the Power is not zero at the end of a test, take two boxes of unresisted Stun damage and lose 1 point of Magic for the duration of the ongoing effect. For each resistance test, record both the current disease Power and current Magic rating at the end of each interval. The unresisted Stun damage may not be recovered / healed while the Power of the disease is above zero. For adepts, power points are lost at a 1:1 ratio with points of Magic, with corresponding ability loss chosen at random or picked by the GM. Adepts who are reduced to zero Magic but still have remaining power points through initiation may not use any abilities.)

**Effect (final):** Possible Permanent Magic Loss (Each ongoing resistance test that’s taken by the infected while their Magic rating is zero and the disease’s Power is greater than zero at the end of the test causes one point of permanent Magic loss.
