## Electronic Skin Coatings

These systems integrate the skin of the vehicle into the electronic systems and provide either a sensor input or an electromagnetic output. Most

A variety of features such as shape, coloration, and emissions baffles can be combined to make a vehicle or drone more difficult to physically detect by visual or auditory means. With these features, the threshold for an observer to detect it is increased by one. If a Stealth test is instead performed in an Opposed test format, the defender must discard one hit before tallying their total.
