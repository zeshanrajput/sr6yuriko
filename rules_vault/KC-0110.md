## Okoro Earth Law

This is Eniola Okoro’s law firm, which has taken the lead on hundreds of cases being filed against the corporate powers abusing the environment around Lagos. Okoro has a dozen staff members who are all fanatical environmentalists—love for the Earth is a requirement for being hired.

The firm is currently mostly funded through Okoro’s windfall, but a small amount of money comes in from Erika as they try to curry favor with the senior partner.

The firm is located in the same building as Gaia Investments, and it is where you will find Okoro most often.
