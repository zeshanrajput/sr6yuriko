## COYOTE SHAMAN

The Coyote shaman is a combination of magician and face who specializes in the Con skill. They use both mundane and magical trickery to sneak, disguise, impersonate, and misdirect. Those who are capable of conjuring spirits find them extremely useful for all manner of shenanigans.
