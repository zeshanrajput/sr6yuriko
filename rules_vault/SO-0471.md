## Con Artist’s Apprentice

Most con artists must learn things on their own, but not you. Someone took you under their wing and showed you the ropes. Did you part ways on amicable terms, graduating with your mentor’s blessing to pull off your own cons? Or did they ultimately use and betray you? Either way, you learned some useful and lucrative skills—if you don’t get yourself geeked trying to use them.

**Choose one:** +1 to Agility, Charisma, or Intuition +1 special attribute point **Choose one:** +1 to Con or Stealth +25,000 nuyen **Language skills:** Choose one **Contact Points:** 2

**Contact Types:** Corporate, Criminal, or Government
