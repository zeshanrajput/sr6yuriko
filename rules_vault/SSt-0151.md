## **Don’t: Cross the Streams Too Much**

**Referring some people you know to other people you know can be useful, but it also helps to remember that some parts of your life should always remain separate. Don’t connect your grandmother to any member of your shadowrunning team. Just don’t. And if you have a good law enforcement contact, be cautious about introducing them to your shadowrunner friends. Both of them would be at risk in such an introduction, and imposing that kind of risk on your contacts is usually not a good idea.**
