## Docks and Ports

This area is always at work, more so now after independence. Its sometimes easy to forget just how much business takes place at these bustling locations all along the waters. **Kondorchid** retains their quartet of automated warehouses and shipping depots in the southern Everett docks and continues to accept and deliver all kind of freight to all parts of the Pacific Rim. **Everett Marina** is now two locations, one on each side of the new **Everett Business and Economic Center (EBEC)** , all of which overlook the chase of the Snohomish River into Puget Sound. The **Everett Port Authority** has its newly expanded offices within the EBEC, making them more available to the northern and southern docks. This is speaking of course of the **Port of Everett Docks** , where nearly 20,000 people come to work round the clock.
