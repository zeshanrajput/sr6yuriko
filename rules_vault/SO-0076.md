## Rub It In (I)

You know one way to really stick it to someone is to remind them of when they fragged up badly and put them on their back foot. Spend a Major Action and make an Influence (Negotiation) + Charisma Opposed test when you have leverage over the subject. Any net hits are used to increase Ace in the Hand’s level. This status must be acted upon in this scene, and once you’ve used Ace in the Hole, it immediately changes to Suckered at the same level.
