## Kyoko

Kyoko is the smallest Tandoran city, but what its residents lack in numbers is made up in sheerdetermination. The Great Jungle is probably the most dangerous place in all of Tandora, and this draws the fiercest and most highly skilled Tandoran hunters. The city was established by Kyoko and her followers after the war. Almost all of the Tandorans who live here have vowed never to leave the island, no matter what the Great Mother and her promises or prophecies might have to say about it.
