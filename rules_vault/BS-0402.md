## Manaproof IPA

- **Vector:** Ingestion

- **Speed:** 1D6 minutes

- **Duration:** (8 – Body) hours

- **Power:** 10

- **Effect:** –1 Reaction, –1 Logic, High Pain Tolerance, Provides Boosted Defense with 1 net hit

This brewed magical compound really provides the hipster street samurai the ability to say “hold my beer” when faced with an enemy caster. Manaproof is an India pale ale made from an Awakened _humulus lupulus_ native to the Pacific Northwest in Salish territory. The brew is noted for its high alcohol content and a heavy floral head, which isn’t always appealing to everyone. The standard dose for a human is one pint but will vary based on the size of the drinker. While under the influence, the user experiences the effects of alcohol (p. 124, _SR6_ ) as well as Boosted Defense, as if an ally had 1 net hit on their Sorcery + Magic test, increasing the magic defense dice pool.
