## **Low Lifestyle**

**Low lifestyle rarely provides room for pets. You’ll either have to make special arrangements, house them in a separate location, or try to keep them a secret from your neighbors and landlord. Even with permission, only critters with a Body attribute of 2 or less can be kept in your home. Larger critters (Body 3+) must be housed in a separate location.**
