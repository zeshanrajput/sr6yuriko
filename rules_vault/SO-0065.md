## Reading Auras

A person’s aura is a key piece of information in getting a solid read on what they want. More than just their health, emotions, and attributes, the target’s astral aura reflects who they are, and it can give clues toward finding their identity. Some individuals have learned advanced metamagics to hide aspects of their aura. But for everyone else, this can be a dead giveaway if the astral perceiver is trained in reading the nuanced aspects of an aura.

Assensing can be used as a teamwork test on Judge Intentions, but only if the runner was already observing the subject astrally during the exchange. If the observer is astraly perceiving while acting on the physical plane, they take a –2 to all actions.

Keep in mind that anyone assensing will be active in the astral. This means that other people able to assense can notice if they are perceiving astrally. Reading the aura of an experienced Mr. Johnson will most certainly make the rest of the briefing or meet awkward. Detecting someone who is astrally observing requires a Perception + Intuition (2) test.
