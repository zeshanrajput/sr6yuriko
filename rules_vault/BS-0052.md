## Aztechnology

Like Evo, **Aztechnology’s** work in genotype modification and assorted animal hybridizations makes them well known, but their deep bench of agritech has always been their strength. They’ve largely recovered from the Atlacoya Blight, but the loss of NatVat continues to be a problem.
