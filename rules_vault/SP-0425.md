## **Astral Jammer**

**An alchemical preparation that takes the form of a gel, which is stored inside a small handheld pod with a single orifce. When a user sticks their fnger into the slot, contact with the gel triggers the preparation, which creates an astral static effect that inhibits mana spells and assensing tests within its area of effect.**
