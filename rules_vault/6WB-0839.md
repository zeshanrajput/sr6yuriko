## Technomancers

Since the 2050s, the world has known that there are people who interact with the Matrix in unique ways—namely, they don’t need gear. The information of the Matrix flows right into their brains, and they are able to translate it into something meaningful. What’s more, they can send information back into the Matrix, transforming and editing it the way the rest of us do with a commlink. When they were first discovered, they were called otaku, the children of the Matrix, and they often aged out of their abilities by the time they turned twenty-one.
