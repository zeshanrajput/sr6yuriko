## Direct Combat Spells

Direct Combat spells are when you shape mana in a way that it directly pummels the opposition—the magic _is_ the damage. When you cast a Direct Combat spell, roll Sorcery + Magic opposed by the Willpower + Intuition of the target. Net hits become damage and are added to any Amp Up damage the character chose. Damage from Direct Combat spells is not resisted.
