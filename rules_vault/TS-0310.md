## **Professional Rating 0: Watchguard**

**Though some use the terms “watchguard” and “security guard” interchangeably, that is not the case. A licensed security guard requires more training and has more options. They are the cheapest option when trying to make an area look and feel secure. A watchguard at most will have basic frst aid training, and their job is to watch cameras or patrol a given area on a routine schedule.**
