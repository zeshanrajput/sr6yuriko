## Points of Interest

## The Glass Desert

Close to the heart of Miggon is a desert of glass. The glass is melted into smooth, shiny, colorful blobs in places, broken into scattered and splintered shards in others, but even worse are the flowing dunes made of tiny granules of glass that seem to be a scintillating form of sand. It may be the representation of the first atomic blast, an acorn from which Miggon grew. The beauty of the location, with its sparkling dunes, is marred by its manner of creation. Do not breathe without a mask in such a place, or your lungs will be lacerated from within. No travelers or denizens of Miggon venture into the glass desert except to hunt the Kr’u’Ba. The death train crosses through the glass desert, creating haunting music as the glass tinkles against its surface.
