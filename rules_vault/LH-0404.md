## Current Status

Personally taken aback by the destruction caused by the war (a.k.a. the “natural disasters”), Empress Hitomi has publicly stated that she will be taking a more active role within the corporation and is working on several initiatives aimed at “providing relief and helping rebuild what was lost during these trying times.” Insiders speculate this could also include a complete corporate rebranding to take advantage of these new initiatives.

However, there are rumors starting to circulate that she is also speaking to her husband, the Emperor of Japan, about issues relating to both the MCT situation and Renraku’s current unofficial ascension within the corporate ranks. Details are scarce, but Empress Hitomi has already called for a meeting with Renraku and MCT leadership in Neo-Tokyo within the next three months.
