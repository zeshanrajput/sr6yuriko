## **Joining a Data Haven**

**Data havens are a special subset of group contacts** _**(**_ **p. 175,** _**Sixth World Companion**_ **). These contacts can be gained by using the free contact points in character creation or by buying the quality Data Haven (p. 81,** _**Hack and Slash**_ **) or both. Note that contacts gained by the Data Haven quality, without using any free contact points, start at a Loyalty 1. Paying Karma to gain access to a data haven group contact removes the normal cap on the Loyalty rating imposed by Charisma in the same way as joining a magical group. Joining with Karma and free contact points also grants the same +3 bonus to Loyalty.**

**In addition to the above methods, data havens as group contacts can also be gained during play in the same way as any other contact.**
