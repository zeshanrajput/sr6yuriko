## Gastric Pulverizer

Need a convenient way to dispose of something? Why not eat it! A gastric pulverizer augments the user’s stomach lining to produce and survive using more powerful acids that can even break down inorganic materials. A mechanical diamond-tipped grinder replaces the lower esophageal sphincter, reducing any matter entering the stomach into tiny and more easily dissolved chunks. It also prevents the user from experiencing any acid reflux or even being capable of regurgitation. A gastric pulverizer allows the user to break down and ultimately harmlessly pass any material they can swallow. If the user also has a tough gut (see p. 66), they can even derive nourishment from normally inedible substances.
