## Bilko Contact

## (Positive)

At some point in your past or career, you’ve made well-placed and loyal contact with one or several Bilkos at Fort Lewis. As such, gear or information is easier for you to obtain—but it still costs (just not as much).

- **Cost:** 7 or 12 Karma

- **Game Effect:** Depending on Karma cost, availability for gear obtained through contact is at –1/–2 and the contact’s connection rating is increased by +1/+2. Must create contact at character generation; all other contact rules apply. Can purchase this quality for multiple contacts.
