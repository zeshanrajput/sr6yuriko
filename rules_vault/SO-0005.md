## LET ME HAVE A WORD

## Posted by: DangerSensei

Contrary to popular opinion, not every run or problem can be solved through the appropriate application of force. Those have their time and place for sure, but some of the most challenging, gratifying, and highest-paying runs I’ve ever been a part of were accomplished without a single shot fired or any act of violence taken. Boring? Maybe, but I like quick and clean (emphasis on clean) runs. A good face is just as valuable to a team—and in some cases even more so—than the muscle, magic, or Matrix support is.

Now your mileage may vary, but in every single one of those quick and clean runs, my team’s face was the key. Because no matter the run, there will always be the metahuman factor to deal with. It’s a face’s job to deal with those factors. Sometimes it’s sweet-talking or making a deal for some vital paydata. Sometimes it’s pulling off a con. Other times, it’s an infiltration in plain sight with the face taking on the persona of another person.
