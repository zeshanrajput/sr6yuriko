## Downfall

Nothing special insofar as watering holes go, by Seattle standards, but that makes Downfall a pretty nice place in Redmond. The real draw is that it attracts a number of Awakened, who keep the place safe out of informal loyalty to their unofficial spot. Not exactly the place to scout talent, but fairly secure with non-lethals if someone gets out of line.
