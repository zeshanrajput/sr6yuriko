## Translation of Technomancers to the Elemental Planes

There is no concept of technomancers within the elemental planes, as there was no formation of a Matrix here. There is, however, the concept of sagani. While technomancers are not “one with the elements,” they will suffice. Where the noise of the Matrix would be, there is instead a subconscious feeling of being connected to living beings within the metaplane. While on the elemental planes, technomancers have a form of Animal Empathy (p.77, _Street Wyrd_ ) that they can use with elemental creatures (those that are not sentient), Danger



Sense (p. 156, _SR6_ ), and with an Observe in Detail action, they can sense spirits within (Resonance x 10) meters of the technomancer.
