## **European gargoyle**

## **(Gargoyle saxi europaeus)**

**The European gargoyle is squatter and bulkier than other gargoyles. Their dermal ridges and plates combined with their dark-grey skin give them the appearance of living stone. They are about 1.5 meters tall and weigh 160 kilograms or more and are the most aggressive and violent, which is saying something considering how aggressive the whole species is. Because of this aggression, troops are usually smaller and the general population is less than in other regions of the world.**
