## Network Connections

When you improve one part of your network, it improves the whole network.

**Cost:** 7 Karma

**Game Effect:** You must have the Contact Network quality to take this quality. Whenever you increase the Connection rating of one of the core contacts in your network, all the Connection ratings of your network contacts increase by one— up to a maximum of the Connection rating you just improved.
