## Hadrian Williams-Lee

Williams-Lee can date his family’s New York Heritage to the 1800s, and he does so regularly. He believes his destiny is to inhabit a c-suite for SpinGlobal NY. Unfortunately, all of his efforts have done little more than turn him into SpinGlobal’s local go-to for all things shady. He joins the mission because Becky Wu Ping disappears shortly after the runners have their first contact with her. When pressed on the issue, he tells the runners it is above their pay grade, and they will be dealing with him moving forward.

Williams-Lee is a white elf with black hair and an ever-present emerald earring in his left ear and a diamond earring in his right. He sees taking over this job as an opportunity to prove his value to the corporation. He appears to take an immediate liking to the runners, even suggesting that he may contact them again for future work. His stats are on p. 143.
