## **Can I create new spells using the spell ingredients?**

No. But if you have a really cool spell idea, post it in the Shadowrun Tabletop forums (https://jackpoint.live/) and it might get added to the SRM Grimoire. There are some unique crafted spells associated with the SRM specific covens and conclaves.
