## GAME MECHANICS

## Groups, a Collective Effort

Rules for Groups, Groups as Contacts, and Group Resources can be found in the _Shadowrun, Sixth World_ and the _Sixth World Companion_ , with the groups listed previously utilizing those mechanics to be useful to gamemasters and players alike. The focus here is upon the formation and utilization of an **Emergent group** , something that has not been fully detailed until now.
