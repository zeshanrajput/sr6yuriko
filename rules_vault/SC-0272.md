## Combat

No other hackers are as well-suited for Matrix combat as you are. Whatever your preferred target might be, you are very good at messing with them. When the gamemaster calls for that initiative roll, get into cover as quickly as possible, and identify devices and personas. Ideally, you should have a sprite compiled at all times, ready to come to your aid with a Minor Action. Once you’re out of the immediate line of fire, take stock of what is vulnerable to you and try to find the most effective uses of your attention. Brick weapons and cyberware, take control of drones, or edit sensor feeds to display false data, using a combination of Matrix actions and complex forms. When it comes to Matrix combat, fault sprites are the way to go.
