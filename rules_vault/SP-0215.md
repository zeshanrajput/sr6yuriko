## **A Magician’s Best Friend?**

**During the latter half of the twentieth and into the beginning of the twenty-frst century, Antwerp was known for its diamond and jewelry industry. After the Awakening, with the discovery orichalcum, Antwerp became one of the prime locations for the creation of orichalcum items, specifcally for use as magical foci. Master crafters from all over the world set up shop in Antwerp to ply their trade and create some of the fnest pieces the Sixth World ever had seen.**

**It was also another factor that drew the Disians to the city. For the past three years, Disian agents have been moving to secure as many of these pieces and material components as possible, through both legitimate and illegitimate means. This created a great demand for those with the skills to craft such items—and those who can obtain them. Conversely, anti-Disian forces are also searching for such items to use them against their enemies and to keep them out of enemy hands.**

**No matter what side one is on, there’s a lot of nuyen to be made in orichalcum in Antwerp.**
