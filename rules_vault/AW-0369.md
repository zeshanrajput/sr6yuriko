## Unique Qi Focus: Sauropod Heart Amulet

The Tandorans of the Camp do not hunt the great sauropods, but when one dies, they harvest everything that can be used. Its hide is turned into leather, which is used for clothing, armor, and shelter. The bones are prized for use in the construction of structures, vehicles, tools, weapons, and armor. The meat and organs provide food, medicine, and reagents. Everything is used, and nothing is wasted—including the heart. The heart of a sauropod spirit is specially prepared—cut into many small pieces, preserved within an amber-like substance, enchanted, and worn as protective amulets. These amulets act as a special qi focus that grants protection equivalent to a spirit’s Guard power when bonded.
