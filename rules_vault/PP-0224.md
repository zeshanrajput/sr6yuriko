## Ground Game

Adapting to local circumstances, as Renraku likes to do, requires knowledge of local circumstances. Some of this is easy to come by—holidays, customs, notable people, and the like. Other parts are trickier—which organized crime figures are serious, and which can be ignored? What’s the feng shui of the area like? Who has a secret they would die for? Who else knows about it?

All of those are things the shadows can help with. We excel at getting the information others have trouble finding, and we’re pretty good at keeping it secret. The only trouble is, if you find out some juicy secrets, do you really think the right thing to do is sell it to a megacorp?
