## Gamemaster Info

Back in early 2070, the woman now living under the name Alice Lewis (among many other alternate SINs) was working under Dr. Halberstam (or Dr. Shalbermat as she knew him then) and was assigned as head of a secondary project involving technomancers, specifically attempting to create long-lasting sprites and using them as an alternative to the far more resource-intensive intrusion countermeasures that computer security systems the world over relied upon. She was given three of the least cooperative subjects MCT had acquired, and orders that ended with, “Oh, and try not to kill them too quickly.”
