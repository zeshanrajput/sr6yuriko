## Utility and Support

Most hackers are great at information gathering, and you’re no exception to this. It’s just not your favorite thing to do. Dissonant technomancers work best when they get to mess with other people and their devices, and so you shine the most when in direct conflict. Your most supportive activity isn’t boosting the capabilities of your team—it’s disrupting the opposition. When other runners are making preparations and doing legwork, you’re most likely compiling and registering sprites and searching for vulnerabilities.
