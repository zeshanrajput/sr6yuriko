## Ozaki Nakazo, Fujiwara-gumi, New Orleans

The Sokaiya racket has just been kicked into high gear with the use of the Kechibi Code. Normally the Yakuza would buy up a sizable amount of shares in a company and then strongarm them during their shareholder meetings or start putting pressure on the staff as a large shareholder. Publicly, the Yakuza clans all denounce this behavior, but really it only stops them from hitting targets that the megacorps are after. Normally the Fujiwara-gumi deal in prostitution, but the local oyabun, Ozaki Nakazo, has been resurrecting this technique down in the Crescent City and hitting up some of the A-rated and smaller businesses. His timing on buying shares of these businesses without letting them get gobbled up by the bigger alligators in the swamp has been uncanny. It might be interesting to see if Nakazo might have an edge from a certain piece of software. If he still has it, there are some folks that will pay some serious nuyen to get their hands on it.

- With Ares moving into Atlanta, you don’t think they aren’t looking to have a sizable presence in the biggest shipping port in North America? They’ll make it hard for the Fujiwara-gumi to be able to run this practice.
