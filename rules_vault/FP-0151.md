## **Atlanta Mayor Sheila Martin**

**Less powerful than Barnes on a national level, Martin’s a big local threat. She’d like nothing more than to drive every single pointed ear out of “her” sprawl, but she doesn’t have the full backing of the governor that she’d need for that level of nasty. Instead, she works with zoning and city ordinances, as well as Lone Star, to keep the city as uncomfortable as possible. You don’t have to pass anti-troll laws when you can just refuse to change zoning and house-building regulations to take troll size into account. One hope here is that Knight Errant will get the contract if enough of Lone Star’s meta-beatings can be shown to the public.**
