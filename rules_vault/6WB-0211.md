## Cover I, II, III, IV

Whether you hide behind a wall, a dumpster, or a body, Cover is protection from hostile fire. It comes at four levels, indicating how much of a character’s body is protected (one-quarter, onehalf, three quarters, or all). A character in Cover gets a +1 bonus per Cover level to their Defense Rating and dice pool to Defense tests. Attacking from Cover requires an extra Minor Action in order to get clear enough to take a shot, imposes a –2 dice pool penalty to your attack at Cover IV, and you cannot gain Edge while attacking from Cover (you can gain it from defending).
