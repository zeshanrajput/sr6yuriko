Wingman is a specialized form of Mindlink, that allows the caster and subject to read off each other’s intents and emotions without showing any obvious communication. When attempting a social related Teamwork test, the helper gains a temporary Edge

to use on their assistance roll, the leader on the test then gains one temporary Edge for each additional die the helper provides to the test. All other limits and conditions of teamwork still apply.
