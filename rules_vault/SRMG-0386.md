## **Some commlink programs are missing prices, how much do they cost and what are their limits (p. 5657)?**

The programs, Mannequin, eParkour, and SimShare, all cost 100 nuyen. The program, Personal Assistant, is limited to the device rating of the commlink that it is running on.
