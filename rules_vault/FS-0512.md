## Psychological Injury

In a world where nearly any wound short of death can be shrugged off with enough healing magic or ’ware, the ones that stick with people are the ones that damage the mind and soul, not the body. This usually requires a closer call than threats and intimidation, but whatever the target’s worst fear is doesn’t actually have to happen; this kind of approach can be just as effective with near-misses as hits, so long as they’re close enough to make the target think they were going to happen. If you’re the kind of person who values their moral compass, be very careful when using this tactic, or avoid it entirely. Making someone think their worst fear is coming to pass can fuck them up for the rest of their lives.
