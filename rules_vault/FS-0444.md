## Bending of the Reed

Gain a bonus Edge when using the Dodge Minor Action against melee _or_ ranged attacks (choose which category when learning this technique). This technique may be taken up to twice in order to gain both the melee and the ranged Dodge bonus. **Category:** Mobility
