## **Assault**

**While the Gorilla chimera and the Patchwork chimera (p. 73,** _**Astral Ways**_ **) fall into the Assault category, this newest chimera takes the idea of Assault chimera to a new level. The recent appearance of this variety leads me to believe that the Disians are moving the confict to a new phase: allout war. These creatures are created for one simple mission: kill and destroy.**
