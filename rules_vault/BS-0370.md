## Blight

- **Vector:** Injection, Special

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** (12 – Body or Magic, whichever is higher) hours, minimum 1 hour

- **Power:** 9

- **Effect:** Magic loss, Stun damage

Blight was an accidental discovery made by the research scientists at Grey Mana production. Well, that’s the truth of what happened, but the marketing spin you get from Brothers Grey Auction is that this was a specifically targeted bit of research done to help security personnel deal with hostile or rampaging spirits. When combined with DMSO, blight gains the contact vector of transmission. When a magical entity or Awakened being is exposed to blight, they make a Drain Resistance test instead of a Toxin Resistance test versus the Power of the toxin; any unresisted damage is taken as Stun damage. All entities exposed to blight lose access to the Magic attribute. Adepts lose access to their powers. Spirits lose access to spirit powers such as Immunity to Natural Weapons. Dual-natured creatures lose their connection to the manasphere and can’t use critter powers that rely on the Magic attribute. In addition, all magical entities affected by the drug are unable to gain or spend Edge while under the effects of blight.

- **Effect:** –1 Reaction, –1 Logic, High Pain Tolerance
