## Gold Bricks

## Posted by: Mr. Bonds

While the Gold Brick is a classic on its own, it might be seen as the variation of a several other

cons, many of which will follow. The base notion is selling something of low value to get something high. Now, that sounds like the basis of all capitalism, and it is. The difference here isn’t a matter of markets, but deception. You’re not selling a speculative product or commodity or taking advantage of low-product cost and high-value markets, you’re selling garbage and claiming it’s treasure, falsely representing what it is. And only after you’ve pocketed the payday and made your exit does the mark realize that all that glitters is not gold.
