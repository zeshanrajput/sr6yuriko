## **GMT Cyberlimb Plating**

**Cyberlimbs—including cybertorsos and cyberskulls—can be upgraded with grey manatech materials infused in their casing. This takes up very little capacity and provides signifcant protection against some forms of magic. This modifcation is more effective in obvious cyberlimbs than synthetic ones. Upgrade kits are available to apply this as a modifcation to pre-existing cyberlimbs, but it is signifcantly more inexpensive when the GMT is incorporated into the cyberlimb’s original design.**
