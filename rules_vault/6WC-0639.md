## Vendetta: Lie Low

Will you lie low and hope the special attention blows over? If so, your way out is to reduce your personal Heat to zero using whatever methods are available to you. You will suffer the effects of the Bounty or Hunted quality every session until your Heat reaches zero. Once your Heat reaches zero, the Bounty or Hunted quality goes away.You maythen purchase one positive quality at its regular Karma cost (instead of double, per usual character advancement costs). The path ends here.
