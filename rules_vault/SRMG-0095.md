## **What counts as your ‘local neighborhood’ for Home Ground?**

Choose a ward or district. In Neo-Tokyo, this could be Kabukicho, Chiba, or something similar. In Seattle, districts would include Bellevue, Downtown, Redmond Barrens, and Renton.
