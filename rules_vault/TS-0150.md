## **> eRic**

**For ATOs, there’s another entry route— that of the Astral Support Team (AST). They’re exclusively those Awakened who can astrally project, and the vast majority can summon spirits, too. Offcial statistics say that 98 percent of coppers assigned to ASTs are full mages, with the others being those types of mage who can project but not summon spirits. The ASTs act as response teams, in much the same manner as ARVs, but from the astral side. We have a lot of things that go bump in the night here in Britain, so having mages capable of dealing with astral threats has literally saved lives. I ain’t no mage, but I’ve heard the ASTs are no slouches.**

- **Can confirm! The ones I tangled with were competent and initiated a few times. They’re not elite spec-ops level, but they’re better than the Knight Errant and Lone Star mages I’ve dealt with.**
