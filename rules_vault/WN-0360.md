## Get It to Go

Today, Fluffy is at 112 West 50th Street near Radio City Music Hall. As you walk up, you see Silver Nail seated at a table that has a “Reserved

VIP Meeting” ARO floating above it. He waves you over and motions for you to sit down. After you sit, Fluffy takes everyone’s orders. As you are waiting, Silver Nail says, “I just received word of an assassination contract that’s going to be carried out tonight. It’s going down at a Spinrad Global corporate dinner, and the targets are Mr. and Mrs. James, who work for SG. I want you to help me stop it. I can pay each of you 12,000 nuyen for this job.” Each net hit on an Influence + Charisma Opposed test increases that figure by 500 nuyen (maximum bonus of 2,000 additional nuyen).

If anyone questions Silver Nail about his true identity, he says he has another job imminently after this one and they can discuss everything at that time. If the party continues to question him, he will ask if they are interested in the job he has; if not, he will leave.

If the team contacts El Guapo about the assassination attempt mentioned by Silver Nail, he will calmly ask them which one, then thank them for the information and tell them he has it covered. If
