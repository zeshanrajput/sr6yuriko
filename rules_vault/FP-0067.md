## **Like Taking Candy from a Baby**

**The runners are hired by the Creagers to acquire four cards that have recently been discovered. For reasons known only the fates behind the tarot, they are all in the hands of kids. The pay is good, if slightly below average because the cards are with kids.**

**But there’s a large twist.**

**Others have already made attempts on the cards, from purchases to thefts, but none have managed to hold onto them. It seems the cards can only get thirty-one meters from the kids before appearing to fade out of existence and reform back with their owners. The Creagers know this but aren’t saying up front. They are hoping the runners will resort to kidnapping without the extra expense. If the runners discover the issue and attempt to renegotiate, the Creagers will come back to the table with an increase in pay that matches kidnapping.**

**The Six of Blades is with Enkai Sigashi, a nineyear-old elf girl living in Tacoma. She loves the card and always keeps it with her in her pocket. She fnds it fascinating how it doesn’t get damaged from being folded, even into origami shapes, and when she once sold it to a nice man who gave her a 100-nuyen credstick to buy dresses and candy, she was very sad, but then ecstatic when it returned. She hasn’t told her parents about it—or the credstick—because she thinks they’ll be mad. Her father is a low-level executive at Telestrian, and her mother is an arcane nurse for Tacoma General hospital. Enkai is usually at home with her nanny, Danella, and attends school virtually. When activated, the card summons a Force 8 spirit of beasts in the form of an origami dragon with six services.**

**The Knight of Blades is with Trager Gleason, a nine-year-old ork boy living in Puyallup. He got the card in a pack of urban brawl cards and tried to throw it away but couldn’t. He kept fnding it. Then the card disappeared from the dean’s desk, they found it on him, and he got in more trouble. He’s happy to get rid of the card but doesn’t think he can. If he bleeds on the card, he gains the armor (armor jacket) and motorcycle (Suzuki Mirage) of the fgure along with the skill to ride it with a 12-dice pool.**
