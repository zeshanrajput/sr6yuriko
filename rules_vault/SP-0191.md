## **Completing Harvester Network**

**The linchpin of the entire Disian operation, the Harvesters are large and complex devices that require tremendous amounts of logistical support to construct and later operate. Unfortunately, the Disians thus far have been extremely profcient in constructing and obfuscating already completed ones or those nearing completion. But we have learned that only a fraction of the Harvester Network has been completed. As such, the decision has been made to prioritize the prevention of new Harvester locations from going online.**

**The general plan of preventing this is based on the tactical principle of asset interdiction and location denial.**

**As one would suspect, the complex nature of Harvesters requires multiple specifc and custom components that are being produced from multiple locations worldwide, not to mention the sheer number of metahuman (pardon the term) batteries required to function. It’s estimated the interdiction and denial of these components is the best chance the material plane has for survival.**

- **The downside of this plan is that we don’t know how much of a stockpile the corps producing Harvester components have. Sure,**

**we may get one or two shipments, prevent one Harvester from being built, but I have a feeling the corps can simply outlast us logistically.**
