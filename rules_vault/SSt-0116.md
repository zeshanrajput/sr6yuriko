## **Seal the Cracks**

**The true skill of extreme conditions is exposing any weaknesses in your defenses against them. Do you have exposed skin or a break in any seal you’re using to keep the conditions out? It will be found and exploited. Did you miss a patch of skin with whatever you’re using to protect yourself from the sun? It will get burned. And if you’re in a particularly sandy desert, the sand will get. In. Everywhere.**

**Half-assed protections are basically no protections. You need to be thorough, and you need**

**to put things on appropriately. If you don’t know how to seal your weapon, pass it over to someone who does. It’s too important to do things wrong.**

- **Your rigger has a real chance to shine by making sure their vehicles stay operational in an extreme environment, but don’t assume that every rigger knows how to do this. Someone who has mainly been a getaway driver in some sprawl may know everything there is to know about urban driving, but get them in the wilds and they may be close to helpless. Do your due diligence and ask what they know before you drag them to the frozen tundra.**

- **Turbo Bunny**
