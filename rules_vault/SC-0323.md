## Toxic Cleanup

This adventure hook is designed for the Gaia first background, but any team of shadowrunners that would be motivated to take down a toxic shaman would be a good fit for this run.

Someone approaches the Eagle shaman with some terrible news. Several deaths were caused by a sudden tragedy, and there is evidence of toxic magic at the scene. They ask the Eagle shaman and their team to hunt down the source of this attack and put an end to it. Whether through astral tracking, searching, or investigating recent events for a pattern, the trail will lead to a toxic shaman. The toxic shaman is a powerful magician surrounded by minions and allies: ghouls, gangers, hardened specialists equivalent to shadowrunners, toxic spirits, and various critters. This is a fairly straightforward adventure, with little or no chance of diplomatic resolution with the target. The Eagle shaman will most likely be highly motivated to destroy the toxic shaman and end their influence. If any of the players bring up Dunkelzahn’s will, in which a one-million-nuyen bounty for the capture of a toxic magician is offered, it’s safe to assume that the majority of the funds earmarked for this bounty have been depleted by now. The Draco Foundation will pay a bounty for a captured toxic magician, but they now offer a much smaller reward, equivalent to what the PCs are used to being paid for completing a shadowrun. No bounty is awarded for killing a toxic magician, due to the difficulty in verifying that the target was actually a toxic mage.
