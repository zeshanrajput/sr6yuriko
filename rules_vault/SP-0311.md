## **Arriving in Stony Plains**

**Stony Plains is a typical agro-town with no major buildings or downtown, just a series of necessary locations such as a grocery store, a pharmacy, a small clinic, and so on. When the player characters arrive, they can at least report back to Mr. Johnson that the town itself is still there. But the player characters quickly realize that the place is a ghost town.**
