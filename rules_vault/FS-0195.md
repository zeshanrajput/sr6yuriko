## NightShade/ Moonsilver Line

While the Moonsilver line is limited to dresses with electroluminescent threading, the NightShade line offers style and class in a bullet-resistant package that wraps up even the homeliest troll and makes them fit in on the Horizon runways. The “For a killer night, wear NightShade!” slogan has kept this line from leaping into the ranks of Armanté, but for an Ares product, its militant manufacturer is often overlooked.
