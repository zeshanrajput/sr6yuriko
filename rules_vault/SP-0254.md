## **Cybermancy Magicians**

**An initiate group consisting of six blood mages (Magic rating 10) is focused on the task of cybermancy. The only exception to this duty is maintaining the astral intrusion wards. When they’re not working on producing cyberzombies, they’re resting to recover from the signifcant drain incurred through their work. When the player characters hit the Evo facility, they’ll be engaged in cybermancy, and unable to break off to assist the security forces—until the runners breach sub-level 2, that is. If a security breach reaches sub-level 2, they’ll abandon any work and defend themselves. Their talents are entirely focused on cybermancy; they’re not combat mages. For stats, use Talismonger (p. 215, S** _**R6**_ **), but with Magic 10, Sorcery 6, Conjuring 6. Spells: Clairvoyance, Confusion, Heal, Mana Barrier, Physical Barrier, Stunbolt. When encountered, each blood mage already has 3 boxes of Physical damage and 3 boxes of Stun damage from drain.**
