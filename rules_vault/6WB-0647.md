## Combat Sense

**Cost:** 0.5 PP per level **Activation:** Passive

This power gives you the ability to anticipate and avoid harm. Gain a +1 dice pool bonus per level on any defensive tests. This bonus also applies to any surprise tests (p. 108) you make.
