## Ocular drone

This enhancement only affects one eyeball per purchase. A small drone (treat it as a Horizon Flying Eye, p. 299) is placed in your ocular cavity. The spyball functions as a normal cybereye (with all the relevant enhancements) until you remove it and control it as though it were a drone. When it’s out, you have only one eye and suffer the Blindness I Status effect for all tasks. If you replace both eyes with drones, you gain the Blindness III Status Effect while those drones are operating apart from you. An ocular drone is obviously a fake eye.
