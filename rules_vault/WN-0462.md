## Background (Ghostwalker)

After the destruction caused to Denver by the metaplanar energy transfer experiments described in _The Third Parallel_ , Ghostwalker sends out his agents to find hard information about who is responsible. One trail leads to Mitsuhama’s Manhattan arcology and eventually to MCT’s use of Animus technology to cover up the events. Ghostwalker has no interest in the Animus technology— from his point of view, any effect it could generate can be easily duplicated with magic—but he understands that it could cause a great deal of mischief in the hands of the lesser races. Ghostwalker is focused on the information about Mitsuhama’s activities that Cat eventually acquires from the Null Sect, but he will also show an interest in acquiring news of what is happening on Dis. While not as concerned as Arleesh is about Dis, he believes they could eventually be a threat if left unchecked. As far as the Animus technology is concerned, Ghostwalker would like to see it destroyed and put beyond the reach of anyone.
