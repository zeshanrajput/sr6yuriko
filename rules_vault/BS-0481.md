## Gecko Pads

Gecko pads allow a critter to walk on walls and ceilings.

**Game Information:** These can be installed as a stand-alone augmentation or as a cyberlimb accessory that takes up 1 capacity. In order to use the gecko pads, a critter must have a critterjack and receive special training. A critter may not take the Sprinting action when using gecko pads. The gecko pads do not function on a wet surface.
