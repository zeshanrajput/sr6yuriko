## Biosprayer

Accompanied by its own self-filling chemical gland, the sprayer is a tubular sphincter lined with powerful muscles, allowing the user to spray a tight jet of liquid. This results in a weapon delivery system not unlike a skunk, though the chemical used can generate quite a range of results. A single dose can hit a target up to one meter away. With a stronger muscle contraction, the jet can strike as far as three meters, but this expends two doses of the chemical. The sprayer uses the Exotic Ranged Weapons (Biosprayer) skill. The biosprayer uses Spray Attack rules (p. 117, _SR6_ ). The gland holds two doses and takes twelve hours to refill a single dose.

The number of hits does not cause damage but instead conveys a status effect up to the biosprayer’s rating or the number of hits, whicheveris greater. The four types of biosprayers are Blind, Confused, Dazed, and Nauseated.
