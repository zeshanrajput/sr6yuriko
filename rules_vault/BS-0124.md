## Magnetic System

Electromagnets anchored to the skeleton generate magnetic fields that allow the user to attach ferrous metals to their body, or vice versa. Each limb is limited to one implanted magnetic system, but the torso can house two: one in the front and one in the back. While a magnetic system is active, the user is virtually incapable of dropping or being pried off of metal objects, and if the user has two or more magnetic systems they can be used sequentially to move along surfaces with iron or steel construction or reinforcement. While engaged, a magnetic system has Strength 6 for the purposes of how much weight it can bear. Note that this may well end up being more weight than the user can even lift. It takes a Minor Action to activate or deactivate a given magnetic system.

**Cyberware overdrive** : The magnetic system’s Strength is increased to 8 during one test. Convert one die to a wild die.

**Wireless bonus** : Gain a bonus Minor Action after spending one to engage or disengage a magnetic system.
