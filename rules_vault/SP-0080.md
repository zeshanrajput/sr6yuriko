## **Lynnette Falco (Horizon)**

**If any board member is going to lead the fght for the status quo, it’s Falco. She’s getting close to the end of her term with just over a year left, but not so close that she is acting like a lame duck. She knows the ins and outs of the bank and all of the players, and she knows how to work the system, so she will be a formidable opponent for anyone trying to change anything.**

**Falco’s background is as a corporate lawyer, which means she’s not going to try to get her way through inspirational speeches or soaring rhetoric. Instead, she will craft legal arguments. For any proposed change about where investments should be moved, Falco will back up a virtual dump truck of legal info on the requirements and restrictions of the proposed moves. Binding covenants, national law, Corporate Court agreements, and more— she will scour the earth for any text that says, in effect, “the thing you want can’t be done.”**

**Is she right? Frequently, but her exact batting average is tough to determine. Often, she is not worried so much about being right as she is about creating an intimidating avalanche of legalese that will make people back off simply because they don’t want to have to craft a response.**
