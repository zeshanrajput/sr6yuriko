## FAB III Epsilon

The original FAB III variant was a mutant variety of FAB II discovered through an unconventional research program (read: lab disaster). All varieties of FAB III-epsilon attach to the astral aura and drain the magical energy of the host. All known colonies of previous varieties have died out in the wild and now only exist as tightly controlled laboratory specimens.

**Game Rules:** FAB III-epsilon is the latest iteration of FAB III and reportedly the most controllable. The epsilon strain has all the properties of FAB II, but any astrally active aura generated by a living entity (including spirits, but not spells or foci) can be infected with the dual-natured bacterium. Unlike the original variants, FAB III-epsilon lacks mobility, it doesn’t move on its own and it can only infect through initiated contact. This contact may be from astrally pushing through a FAB III barrier, being in direct contact with a carrier, or by being in contact with the physical bacterium.
