## **Special Operations (SO)**

**The Special Operations unit is more like a task force, which is created on the fy. It is usually created for a specifc task and includes highly skilled members from other services who are temporarily assigned to the Special Operations unit.**

**If a ’ plex or corporation needs security services that Sternschutz does not offer, they are open to creating a new service just for that contract.**
