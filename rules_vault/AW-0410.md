## Translation to Miggon

The hypermetaplane of Miggon translates the clothing and equipment of all travelers into shabby, worn-down, rust-pocked, and dirty equivalents of their usual appearance. While your gear might look worse for wear, you needn’t be concerned about whether or not it will function, as all functionality is retained. Most of the magic is in a low mana ebb if it’s not from a toxic caster. The center area, being pre-Awakened, is a mana void.
