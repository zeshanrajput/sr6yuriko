## New Complex Forms New Fade Value (Hits)

Some of the new complex forms have a Fade Value listed as “Hits.” In this case, the Fade Value of the complex form is equal to the number of hits rolled on its related Electronics + Resonance test. This always refers to total hits, not net hits. You may intentionally reduce your total hits in order to limit fading.
