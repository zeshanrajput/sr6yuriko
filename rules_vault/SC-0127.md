## Utility and Support

If the runners you work with are open to an infected teammate, make it worth their while. After all, they’re potentially assets worth cultivating, whether for Aztechnology operations or your own down the line. With magic, you can provide any assistance any mage could, though a sacrificial bent means more death equals more power. If your teammates are willing to offer their own blood to empower your work, don’t take it for granted, or you’ll alienate them quickly. Otherwise, feed on your foes and turn that strength toward the next, like an avalanche gaining power with every centimeter. Remember that your Essence can be channeled into a number of attributes, which makes you a potential jack of all trades, proficient in activities ranging from negotiations to combat, but all of it comes at the price of feeding. A vampire without Essence to draw on is sometimes more fragile and volatile than any given metahuman.
