## broken arrows

A new movement has formed among the disenfranchised and blacklisted mercenaries. Calling themselves “Broken Arrows,” these mercenaries identify themselves by either a special broken arrow patch, tabs, or both as a means of protest, but also to show solidarity with each other. Mostly comprising individual mercs or a few smallto-medium-sized units, some Broken Arrows have already signed on with the Defender Movement, lending their support and expertise wherever they’re needed. Others are starting to form their own ad hoc units, or in some cases, sign on with razor teams or detachments.
