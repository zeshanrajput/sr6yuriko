## Knuckle Breaker

## (Any Attack)

As the name suggests, the attack is designed to neutralize the opponent’s effectiveness in combat or ability to escape. Character targets a specific limb (left leg, right arm, etc.). This called shot has a –4 dice pool penalty. If the attack is successful, that limb takes on Disabled status (see p. 106). If the net hits exceed the Body of the opponent, the limb takes on Disabled II status. The status lasts for a number of combat rounds equal to net hits.
