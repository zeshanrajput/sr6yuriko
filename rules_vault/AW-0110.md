## Borea

## (Air, Force 4)

Boreas have an almost solid, unnaturally blue humanoid form. They wear a fog like cold-weather gear. Every move they make leaves a sublimation trail. Their heads are crowned with a mane and beard of vapors. Boreas are good at tracking and hunting, as they lead a low-tech lifestyle. Their preferred weapons are spears and nets.

**Powers:** Astral Form, Energy Aura (Cold), Innate Spell (Fashion), Innate Spell (Makeover), Materialization, Movement, Sapience, Search **Weaknesses:** Allergy (Fire, Severe)
