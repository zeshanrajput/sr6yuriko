## 1. welcome to manhattan

## Hook

A powerful Mr. Johnson reaches out to the runners to get them into the city in order to clean up the mess he’s made for himself. He wants them to perform a splashy job in the middle of the city. If they can pull it off, they’ll instantly be established as NYC runners worth knowing.
