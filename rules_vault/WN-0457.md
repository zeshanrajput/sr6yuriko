## Background (MCT)

MCT began working with the Null Sect before anyone else, except maybe Global Sandstorm, helping them learn about the “outer world” and using them to accomplish their own goals. The Null Sect are information hunters second to none, and information about Animus Construction was discovered early on. The data about what it was and what it could do was passed along to a number of MCT executives, including Even Teeg. After the Aurora Event, the Null Sect was assigned by Teeg to erase all data on the Matrix related to it. He also reached out to Animus Construction to start erasing the memories of any personnel who were too valuable to just eliminate. Everyone scheduled for alterations was transferred to the Shibata Consulting home office to be near the main Animus Construction facility and basically held there until Teeg decided where to reassign them to. With the new access to MCT’s resources and industrial capacity, Animus Construction began upgrading the clinical operations facility in New Harlem to handle the influx of MCT employees and carry out Project Lazarus operations.

After the hijacking of a shipment of the specialized nano-forges being sent to Animus, Teeg overacted, sending the Null Sect to erase all the data about the hijacking and then sending in a shadowrunner team he occasionally uses for the physical retrieval. Unfortunately for Teeg, his rush to manage things left a trail leading back to MCT.

MCT’s connection to Dis is another secret they are going to great lengths to keep. The devices used in Denver were equal parts stolen Aztechnology research and magical knowledge gained from Dis. What Dis gained in return for this knowledge is another secret that Mitsuhama plans to keep.
