## **Who’s Who**

**Doctor Julia Rickman is a female elf in her late thirties and is the head cryptologist at Transys-Neuronet. She and her team have been tasked by the great dragon Celedyr himself with learning and translating the complicated Disian language. After almost a year of frustration, Dr. Rickman accidentally stumbled on a cypher that would accomplish all they’d been tasked to do. But before she could report her fndings, her lab was attacked by shadowrunners hired by Disian proxies.**
