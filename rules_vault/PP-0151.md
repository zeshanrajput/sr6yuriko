## Evo In

You have a connection in Evo that makes you one of the people they reach out to when they want to field-test new gear.

**Cost:** 4 Karma

**Game Effect:** Having an in with Evo means you can accept those lucrative (and dangerous) tech-testing jobs. This quality opens up the possibility of Evo offering the character equipment to test. What pieces are up to the gamemaster, and they should always come with some potential for failure or folly in order to keep things interesting.
