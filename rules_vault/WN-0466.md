## CHARACTER TROVE

## Abyssa

## (Young sea dragon, Connection Rating 4)

One of the “eldest” of the brood of eggs the Sea Dragon gathered, hatched, and slipped off to a metaplane with a different progression of time, Abyssa clawed her way to the top of the pool of her siblings. When she returned, she expected a seat at the right hand of her mother. Problem is, the Sea Dragon was offering no seats beside her, only places to work under her, and that rubbed Abyssa the wrong way. A few too many power moves has her on her mother’s bad side, and this little disaster is her mother’s way of attempting to put her back in her place.
