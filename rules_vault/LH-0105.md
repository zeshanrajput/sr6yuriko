## Current Sit-Rep

Now that we’re all caught up, let me tell all of you how the drek has only gotten deeper.

Hundreds of mercs from the protest are still in custody, rotting away, and out of the fight. Others have been blacklisted and sanctioned for failing to abide by the IMA decrees. Conversely, numerous lawsuits have been filed against the UN, the CC, and the IMA in an attempt to free those incarcerated, lift the sanctions, and break the deadlock by forcing some kind of resolution. And of course they’ve gone as well as one can expect, nowhere. I don’t understand the exact legalese used as a rebuttal, but I understand when someone is telling me that I’m pissing in the wind or to frag off. And a lot of my fellow mercs have realized the same.
