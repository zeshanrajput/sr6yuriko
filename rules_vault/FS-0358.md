## ECM Warrior II

Matrix asset denial and attacks are mainstays of modern combat. To assist in attacking enemy networks and defending their own, the ECM warrior is designed to give an operator extra “electronic ammo” to engage enemy hackers and help cut through any noise.

**Rules:** +2 to all offensive Cracking tests while using this app. Additionally, provides a –2 to all noise within (Device Rating x5) meters of the device running the app.
