## Negative Metagenetic Quality: Saurian

## **Bonus:** 6 Karma

These individuals have a distinctively dinosaur-like appearance. The skull is narrow and pointed, and the mouth resembles a beak—though it is softer, more flexible, and capable of speech. Along the back of the head is a crest or a row of horns. The hands and feet are tipped with stubby claws rather than fingernails. Saurians are easily mistaken for drakes. Other people get a +2 dice pool bonus when making Memory tests to recall your appearance or remember if they have seen you before.
