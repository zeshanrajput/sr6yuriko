## Bruce Lee Keychain

- **Formula:** Punch, Verbal

- **Drain Value:** 5

- **Cost:** 75¥/charge

This balled fist attached to a small chain is the tool of choice for those who like to practice the famous “one-centimeter punch” without seeking out a decade of training. Touch the keychain, touch the target, say “hoosh,” and _blam_ , a blast of telekinetic energy erupts from your fingertips. It’s a great wayto start (or end) a bar fight, especially if the person you tag has someone else to blame the hit on. Did we mention it can be a great distraction?
