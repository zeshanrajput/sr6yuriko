## Math SPU

An implanted cranial sub-processing unit (SPU) handles complicated mathematical functions sothe user doesn’t have to, such as converting imperial measurements to metric. A math SPU maintains a mental calendar and tracks multiple timers that may be of interest to the user, such as “how long has my girlfriend been asleep” and “how long until my wife gets home from work.” A Math SPU lowers the cost of Edge boosts by 1 (to a minimum of 1) for Cracking, Electronics, and Build/Repair Tests (p. 95, _SR6_ ).

**Cyberware overdrive:** A Math SPU increases the Edge boost cost reduction to 2 (retaining a minimum cost of 1), and a wild die is added to the test.

**Wireless bonus:** A Math SPU maintains an internal chronometer synced with global Matrix timekeeping.
