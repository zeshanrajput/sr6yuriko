## Ares Triceratops

Another iconic dinosaur is the triceratops, and while it may be an herbivore, that doesn’t make it any less dangerous. These four-legged beasts are protected by a thick hide and an armored crest, and they are capable of making attacks with great horns that can gore the largest predators—or tear apart heavily fortified positions or military-grade armored vehicles.
