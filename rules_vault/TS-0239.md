## **The Next Gen**

**Over the last few months, three distinct combat drugs have entered use. One is a milder yet faster-acting version of Kamikaze; another is the “modern” take on Jazz; and the third is rumored to be signifcantly more potent than anything that’s come before.**

**Also entering use is an externally worn injector unit, capable of holding four different substances. The unit is worn on the chest or thigh and requires that an access port be installed by a doctor or med-tech. The implantation process takes only one hour, as opposed to the two hours (plus implant cost) to install an autoinjector.**
