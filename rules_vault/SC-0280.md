## Additional character creation recommendations

- **Attributes:** Edge 4+

- **Skills:** Con 3+, Influence 3+

- **Knowledge skills:** Current Events, Drone Manufacturers

Your family made their living doing physical and technical labor for a megacorporation. Nearly every adult around you was employed to build structures, install systems, upgrade tech, maintain and repair equipment, or something similar. For one reason or another, you never felt comfortable with the active, physical side of the work. Perhaps you suffered an injury at a young age or had a medical condition that made labor difficult. Or your objections might be related to witnessing the results of a horrible accident, the likes of which are all too common on a dangerous work site. Still wanting to be useful, you turned your attention to learning everything you could about the higher-tech stuff. You studied hard and got involved in some engineering-related extracurricular activities. That’s when you were invited to join the drone club, which changed your life. The idea that riggers could experience senses from drones as extensions of their own fascinated you. Simsense simulations were intriguing, but you needed the real thing: a control rig. When you were old enough to apply for higher education, you gained the attention of the megacorp that you grew up within. They offered you everything you ever wanted, in exchange for a long-term employment contract. Very long-term, but what could be the harm? You’d have a steady job doing what you loved. As it happened, the work didn’t suit you very well at all. You were used to spy on people—not just enemies of the megacorp but its own workers. You were a part of the omniscient panopticon, a cadre of drone and sensors operators hired to watch personnel and report any infringements of workforce policy. One too many reprimands for not accurately reporting employees for using the restroom an unauthorized number of times was too much for you. Using your intimate and complete knowledge of the facilities, you were able to effect an escape from your old life. Fortunately, you were able to appropriate a number of your favorite drones and a few other useful toys. The paydata you carried out was enough to make sure you wouldn’t have to sleep on the streets.
