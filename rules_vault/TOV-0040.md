## Channe led Chwal

You are one of the chosen vessels of your lwa. You have seen the secrets known only to a select few. The lwa has seen that they can trust you to wield their divine power. When the lwa is rides you, they give you your lead, grant you their powers, and boost your natural abilities.

**Cost:** 15 Karma

**Game Effect:** The lwa is channeled (p. 121, _Street Wyrd_ ) into the chwal. The houngan who conjures the spirit does not control this spirit, and it does not count against any spirit limits. The channeled chwal does not require the second Conjuring + Magic + Initiate Grade vs. Force x 2 test of the channeling metamagic but can choose to attempt the test for additional services (just don’t Glitch!). The channeled chwal gains all the benefi ts of channeling. If you break the tenets of the religion, you lose both the Channeled Chwal and Bonded Chwal qualities. You can take this quality multiple times, selecting a different lwa each time.
