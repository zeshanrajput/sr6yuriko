## Hecate’s Blessing

- **Vector:** Ingestion

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** 10 x 1D6 minutes

- **Power:** 9

- **Effect:** Gain situational Edge to resist drain

Using the sap of an Awakened maple, this bioengineered drug helps magic-users channel more mana through their body. This grants them a situational Edge on tests to resist drain. When the drug wears off, the user’s Magic is reduced by 1 for twice the original duration.
