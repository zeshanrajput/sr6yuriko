## The Belle of the Ball

Decade after decade, Shiawase sits atop her throne, prim, proper, and fully in control. Nothing happens in the biotech field without her knowledge and, to a lesser extent, her blessing. Her control over type O bio-organics gives a constant source of income, her medical facilities are the envy of the world, and her own experimentation on genomes remains unequaled. True, there was a bit of a blemish on her dress after Leónization Type II was shut down, but the problems with the procedure were discovered in-house, rather than exposed, giving the queen a chance to pull it from the market herself, showcasing the trust that

the overall industry places in her wisdom. They wound up _more_ trusted! It’s good to be the queen.
