## The Purveyors of Pollution

This section covers the forces arrayed against Okoro. They are contracting runners to look into her sudden cash influx, sending their own people to check out and deal with those who may be watching her, and funding efforts to get the information by other means.

The runners should be hired by one of these groups; the others become peripheral foes and opposition who are seeking the same paydata but working for a different boss.
