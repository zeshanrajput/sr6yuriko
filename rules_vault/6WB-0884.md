## Suppression

Sprites are confusing at the best of times, but a sprite using Suppression is just bizarre, especially to hosts. If a sprite is in a host and using this power when the host launches IC, that IC is delayed from launching by (Level / 2) combat rounds. Delayed IC can’t act or be targeted.
