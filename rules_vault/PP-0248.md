## Resources

Mining is another integral part of Saeder-Krupp. To provide raw materials and fuel for its manufacturing and heavy industry operations,

the megacorp has long focused on mining, extracting, and refining biotic and abiotic resources (such as oil and minerals). As traditional mining and drilling areas either became depleted or inaccessible (like Central Africa, Yakut, or Amazonia), S-K expanded to more exotic locations, like asteroid, lunar, and underwater mining.

- S-K wasn’t quite happy when MCT’s Exo Mining Systems showed up to mine the moon as well.
