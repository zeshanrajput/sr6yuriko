## dis manasphere

The manasphere of Dis is on a frequency or “harmonic” that is out-of-phase from the mana of most other metaplanes. In addition to causing difficulty using magic, it generates a general feeling of malaise and depression that slowly increases over time. This affects Awakened and non-Awakened individuals alike, but it is most pronounced in those with high Essence; those having very low Essence are affected the least.

In game terms, this harmonic imbalance means that any time a non-native or non-chimera attempts a test that includes the Magic attribute, they must replace one die of the dice pool with a wild die. Additionally, both 1s and 2s count toward glitches. To make matters worse, the Disians have been pooling their remaining mana in the center of the city, leaving the rest of the metaplane as a high mana ebb, with drifting patches of mana voids. Near the city center, this changes to a medium mana ebb and finally a low mana ebb for non-natives of Dis (see p. 167, Street Wyrd). The native Disians and their chimeric creations may use the mana of the metaplane normally.

In addition to the harmonic imbalance problems for the Awakened, the enchantments used by the Disians inhibit translation to the reality paradigm of the metaplane, and this prevents outsiders from accessing most Disian technology. The technology brought into the metaplane by others may or may not be accessible by deckers. Technomancers, however, may be able to access a wider range of devices. See p. 174, Astral Ways, for the specific rules for operating on the metaplane of Dis.
