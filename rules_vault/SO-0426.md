## Positive Qualities

## Air of Superiority

You believe you’re the cream of the crop and you feel entitled to dominate those you see as beneath you (that’s everyone else, by the way). This wouldn’t be so extraordinary, except you can make other people see you that way as well.

**Cost:** 5 Karma

**Game Effect:** When you compare Social Ratings against a target, you always compare the positive values—even for tests when a lower Social Rating would gain an advantage, such as Intimidation tests.
