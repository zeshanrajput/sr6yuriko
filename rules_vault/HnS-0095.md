## Cases

Every cyberhack and cyberdeck needs a case to house and protect its internal components. None of these things are legal, and so most are designed to disguise their nature. Some deckers prefer to make their cyberdeck as compact as possible, while others hide them in plain sight using a case design from an RCC, a commlink, or a more creative option.

And then there are the gonzo case mods. Keytars are in high fashion for this purpose, but some go even further to add weapons and other devices. Usually the larger cases are designed to house both a cyberhack and a cyberdeck, while deckers who have the cyberjack augmentation can get away with only having to carry around the smaller device.
