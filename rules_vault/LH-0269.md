## Update Three: No Plan Survives Contact

## \<07-21-83/0844:05>

## We’re in it now!

Fire from Triple Zero combined with lightning strikes are continuing; a few more mechanized vehicles and a fireteam have taken hits. I swear this lighting is being directed! We’re getting bogged down, so Colonel Cabrera has ordered the Twenty-Second and the Sixty-First into flanking maneuvers to try and draw some fire and maybe find some holes, while MET’s remaining artillery lights the place up in preparation for a frontal assault. I was asked if I wanted to fall back with the arty slags. Frag no! So now Dash is hauling my hoop into an APC.

The things I do for a story!
