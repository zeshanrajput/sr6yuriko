## QUICK HITTERS

## Posted by: Cover

- All right, I have brought in another guest to see if they have good information. I have heard from Marko that they have a solid rep.

- Glitch

First off, thanks for the opportunity. Rather than give a bunch of information about me, I’m going to dive straight into the topic at hand: the use of substances or software to gain a temporary boost.

- I’ve worked with him in the past. While his specialization has been in cyberware, he’s been running the shadows long enough and around enough users to give you good information. He hasn’t been addicted, so you won’t get a fuzzy view from the inside of a high.

- Marko

There are several substances that you can inject, inhale, contact patch, or slot to gain a tactical benefit, or maybe just to get high. They can make you

faster, smarter, or stronger, even for just a short period of time, and that can mean the difference between life and death. Your boost or high isn’t limited to the realm of drugs.

With the realism or hyperrealism of simsense technology, better-than-life chips can give you a high just as easily as a hit of novacoke, leaving normal life just feeling flat, grey, and dull. BTLs aren’t just for overloading your senses; they can amplify neural patterns and trigger neurochemical release. BTLs can also load in memories and feelings to help alleviate psychological symptoms or even give you new skills.

Just like drugs that can act as a toxin if you take too much, BTLs cause their own set of problems. We’ve got a class of BTL called ripjacks that are coded specifically to cause targeted issues. A lot of the runners I’ve talked to are really considering taking off their trodes, but the ones with datajacks or similar are screwed. Each person needs to decide for themselves if they’re worried about ripjacks or not.
