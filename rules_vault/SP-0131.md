## **State-of-the-Art Weaponry**

**Even though Evo isn’t as well-known as other corporations, most notably Ares or Saeder-Krupp, for weapons production, that’s changed since the introduction of the rEVOlution line of weapons and frearms. Now, I won’t give a sales pitch, there are more than enough marketers for that, and the products speak for themselves. But with most weapon makers under the control or infuence of Dis-infltrated corporations, Evo has stepped up as the de facto primary source of weapons for the Disian resistance.**

- **So, we’re a “resistance?” now huh? Does that mean we get t-shirts?**

- **Haze**

- **I’m surprised she didn’t mention any of the futuristic technology some of Evo’s subsidiaries have been producing. I know a few years ago Yamatetsu Naval Technologies was experimenting with some kind of force field (or something), and they’re also working on that anti-grav tech right? Wouldn’t that be useful against Dis?**

- **X-Prime**

- **No, you see … wait, that’s actually a good question.**

- **Cayman**

- **In a conventional war, perhaps. But this is still an asymmetrical or shadows war. Such technology is still mostly in the development phases, not to mention it would attract a lot of attention. And unless Evo has secret stockpiles of this stuff, they**

**don’t have the numbers to be a decisive element in a global conflict, even one that continues to be fought in the shadows. My educated guess is that if such things are deployed, they will be saved for specific situations.**

- **Picador**

- **Not to mention that such things would instantly become a massive target. Guns are both easier to make and easier to hide.**

- **> DangerSensei**

- **True. And on that note, Buttercup isn’t 100 percent accurate here—there are lots of small-arms manufacturers that have been flying under the Disian radar. Cavalier, Colt, FN, and Ranger Arms to name a few. Not to mention a lot of independent shops and gunsmiths doing their own mods or making their own inhouse builds.**

- **Rifleman**

- **Say what you will, but rEVOlution Arms is doing quite well for itself. Their niche weapons such as bows and crossbows are decent sellers, but the jury is still out on their firearms. However, where rEVOlution seems to shine is their extensive line of blades and other close-quarter melee weapons.**

- **Hard Exit**

- **Those are no joke. I’ve got one of every blade and melee weapon from the rEVOlution catalogue, and they’ve been serving me well on the job. But I’ve also been hearing rumors about updated melee weapons, powered ones to deal with “extralarge” threats. Wonder what those possibly could be?**

- **Treadle**

- **I’ve heard rumors about some bleeding-edge combat drones. *DROOL***

- **Clockwork**

- **Interesting. I’ve been getting a lot of requests not just for a lot of these new weapons, but for a lot of advanced-grade augmentations that I’ve never heard of before. Did some digging, talked to a few contacts, and found out that Evo is going for broke. Not only are they offering some deals on weapons and ’ware, but they’re also opening their advanced research and prototype vaults and pressing those items into service against Dis.**

- **Red Anya**

- **Okay, but so what? Corps like Evo have been using runners to field-test their new toys and tech for decades now. And if they can raise the Disian body count even higher, what’s the problem?**

- **Bangswitch**

- **The problem is that we’re not just talking about advanced tech or upgrades. Some of these prototypes are dangerous or unstable. We’re talking things anti-magic or “grey” tech, things that have been in the works since the Awakening, but were never actually produced in numbers because they were too**



**fragging dangerous ! Now, it seems that Evo is handing out nukes to grade-schoolers to combat Dis! > eX-Professor**
