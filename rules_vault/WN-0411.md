## Ares After Story

King’s abrupt departure from the city and upcoming Ares leadership vote means that she needs to gather her assets and get them to a safe location. One such asset is a Dis entity named Arthur Hemming who is performing some research while in the material plane. He is presently en route to Condatis Tower (Ares’ Midtown Manhattan base) under the protection of a four-person team of Knight Errant special operators (use DocWagon HTR officer, p. 207, _SR6_ ) in an Armored Range Rover 2080 (p. 66, _Sixth World Companion_ ). Runners need to intercept him and get him to the docks on the south end of Terminal, where a boat will be waiting for a short time in order to get him out of the city.
