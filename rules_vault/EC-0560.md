## > **Lakewood Comfy Cubicle**

This is an upper-end coffin motel, but the real note of interest here is Bruce Meyrick, the manager. He’s a mage who likes breeding eyekillers for animal fights. He’s usually got one with him on duty. I honestly don’t know how this might help you, but it is pretty cool, and if you don’t find paranormal cockfights morally reprehensible, check him out for some sweet side bets at the Snohomish Coliseum.
