## Waterworks

[Trigger: You gain a negative condition, Action: Minor (A)] 1 Edge per –1 Dice

When things go poorly, some can’t, or won’t, hold back the tears. Not only do they bring catharsis, but they also motivate other people to be nicer—or at least not quite as mean. When a player character gains a negative status (which is any status that provides a disadvantage), they may spend a Minor Action and some Edge to cry or whine piteously to elicit sympathy. Anyone targeting them with an attack (including offensive spells and critter powers), or an opposed social skill test, gets a –1 dice pool penalty for each Edge you spend. This lasts until the end of the scene or encounter, an hour passes, or success on a defense test or an Opposed Social skill test (whichever comes first).
