## **Does Neijia bypass a spirit’s Immunity to Natural Weapons?**

Yes, but the martial art generates an Astral Combat attack using Close Combat (Unarmed) + Agility instead of Astral + Willpower. Weapons DVs, weapon skills, and the weapon’s AR do not factor into the attack. This uses your Unarmed AR and can be modified with cyberware. With this martial art, you can strike astral entities which include manifested mages and materialized spirits. The Damage Value is not modified by augmentations, except for magic that increases the Willpower of the martial artist.
