## Caitlyn Dawson, Trans-Orbital: Head of the Department of Administrative Aides

**Description:** Female human, fire-red hair flowing down her back, wearing a deep-cut purple dress that dips to her navel in front and even lower in the back, with silver stud and spike accessories and tall stiletto and platform heels. She is impossible to miss and draws the attention of everyone in the room.

**Possible Party Missteps:** This is not the kind of thing she is usually invited to, and she is looking to make an impression on everyone. Her outfit has very little in common with her normal professional office attire. She gets a bit deep into character starting with excessive drinking and flirting, leading to a quick but exhilarating bathroom fling with Cade Scanlon, and ends with leaving with the joygirls to keep the party going at another Manhattan club.
