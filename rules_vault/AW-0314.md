## Air Skis

These oversized skis (at least twice as thick and wide as those used to ski on snow) are popular with spirits who are incapable of flight. Paired with a kite-like sail, they remain afloat in the air and can be used to fly from one island to another. If you’re stranded on an island with local wood available, skis or a raft make for a simple and easy way to escape. Other popular designs are shaped like a giant boomerang or a surfboard. Using air skis allows a person incapable of flight to move horizontally through the air within the Place of Clouds. They can also be used to climb at a much slower rate, or resume horizontal movement through the air after falling. They are a much slower way to get around than airship travel, but many travelers prefer to hike through the skies in this manner.
