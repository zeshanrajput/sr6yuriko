## The Street Shaman

This adventure hook works best with the street shaman background, but should work well with any team with contacts in a rough part of town and at least one Awakened character.

One of the street shaman’s old friends from the barrens, ideally someone who still lives there and runs a small business or shelter, reaches out for assistance. Strange things have been happening in the neighborhood lately, and they think it involves some sort of magical or spirit activity. Strange animal sounds and other bizarre noises have been heard at night, which has everyone on edge. Just last night, some people were killed. They were locals, regular people just trying to survive in the urban wasteland. Their bodies were savagely mauled, as if by some beast. Law enforcement services are not under contract for that part of town, so the people are on their own … unless the street shaman and their friends could look into it? The nuyen gathered at short notice from the poverty-stricken residents of the barrens is definitely not even close to what the runners are used to getting paid, but the gamemaster should point out that the Karma awards for doing a good deed are higher than usual. If the runners do not get involved, more bad things will happen to the residents of that area, and it will eventually become almost completely deserted. If the runners agree to investigate, they must find out what strange threat is present. The cause could be something fairly straightforward, such as a pack of ghouls moving into the area or an escaped cybernetically modified critter, which must be tracked down and dealt with. More serious threats could include an insect shaman, toxic magician, super-secret experiment being carried out and observed by a megacorporate research group, or angry free spirits upset by the horrible vibes of the area. These could be settled with a wide variety of approaches and could become a campaign arc rather than a single adventure. If the runners are able to help their friend, in addition to at least 2 bonus Karma they should each gain any contact of the Street type that is grateful to them, with Connection 2 and Loyalty 5. Each runner may select a different contact if they choose. If any runners would like to double up on a contact, they may each choose the same contact, but with a Connection rating of 3. If more than two runners choose the same contact, the Connection rating does not increase to any higher than 3. These contacts might be loyal, friendly, and helpful, but they aren’t particularly wealthy, they lack access to the best resources, and they don’t have the clout or connections to improve their status without significant assistance that isn’t likely to come. But who knows? Maybe with the PCs’ assistance, their fortunes might improve.
