## Ascalon Rail Gun

The Ascalon is a high-power electromagnetic rail gun using a sabot designed to defeat mil-spec vehicle armor—of course, it’s also devastating to lighter targets. The Ascalon uses a proprietary turret system, and due to the massive size of the charging capacitors, it can only be mounted on the largest vehicles.
