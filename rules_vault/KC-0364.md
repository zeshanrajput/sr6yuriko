## RAM (troll, male, 27, no hair, chrome eyes, average build)

Not all deckers need to hide behind the front lines. RAM is a frontline decker. He learned his skills in a gang, but who cares, as long as he knows that getting inside the building often makes the job of hacking the target that much easier. He has no problem getting into the fray and likes to camouflage himself as a bit of street muscle while mentally sending data spikes at his opponents’ equipment.

During their initial efforts he’s going to be gathering every bit of data he can on the electronic gear of the characters so he knows what to target.
