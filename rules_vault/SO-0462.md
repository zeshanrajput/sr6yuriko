## A Clean Break

Assuming you achieve your primary goal and pull off the con, it’s time to escape, break off, and fade away. Hopefully it’s a clean break, but sometimes it gets messy. If you managed a clean break in which the mark and related authorities are not aware that you were responsible for whatever damage or loss you caused, you only gain 2 Heat. If your con was revealed in the end (or shortly after), you gain a number of Heat equal to the level of your Long Con Pool quality—hopefully it was worth it. In addition to whatever other rewards you earned in this con, you may spend Karma for nuyen at a rate of 5,000 nuyen per Karma up to a maximum of 50,000 nuyen/10 Karma. This represents something you swindled away, stole, or otherwise profited from due to the con.

Whether you succeeded or failed, and regardless of if it was clean or messy, once the con is over your Long Con Pool quality goes dormant. Don’t erase it, but until the next long con starts, it serves no purpose. Once you start a long new con, the process may begin again. You may unlock your Long Con Pool quality by performing these actions:

**Level 2:** Choose a mark and acquire a Knowledge skill related to them.

**Level 3:** Spend one week of effort and 2,000 nuyen setting the stage.

**Level 4:** Successfully set the hook with the mark.

Once you perform the required actions, the Long Con Pool quality becomes available to you at the maximum level listed above. You do not need to spend Karma to reacquire or raise the quality.



Each time you successfully complete a long con (using this quality path), your maximum level of the Long Con Pool quality increases by one, up to a maximum of 7. If you raise the Long Con Pool quality immediately after succeeding at a long con, the Karma cost is not doubled.
