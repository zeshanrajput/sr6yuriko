## Revisiting Transhumanism?

One of the more controversial aspects of Evo’s involvement in the shadow war was their use of cybermantic techniques to create living weapons known as cyberzombies. While extremely effective, how they’re created, coupled with the inherent dangers of a handler losing control of a cyberzombie, has become a point of contention between former allies. Some want assurances that Evo will destroy or at least seal all data pertaining to cybermancy, while the new Evo board sees their new knowledge as a way to perhaps propel their research into a new phase while revitalizing ideas based on transhumanism.

- It’s already happening. A lot of those prototype and upgraded augmentations Evo put out during the war have the fingerprints of cybermancy all over them. I’m not saying their intentions are inherently bad, but then the same could be said about one Victor Frankenstein.

- Butch
