## Tough Gut

## **Cost:** 8 Karma

Originally listed as “Ogre Stomach,” this quality is an adaptation the entire digestive tract has undergone enabling the individual to process a variety of substances that are considered non-standard. Only organic matter can be processed in this manner, and those with it have a –1 LP modifier applied to their Necessities costs (p. 179). FurtherEXPRESSmore, they are more resistant to ingested poisons and toxins, gaining +1 Edge on tests to resist.

Grants a +1 Edge bonus when resisting any firebased attacks. Negates the Burning status (p. 51, _SR6_ ) from all non-magical sources.
