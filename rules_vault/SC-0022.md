## Artifact Recovery

This adventure hook is designed with the chosen one background in mind, but it might work well as a shadowrun concept for any team of runners with some Awakened PCs.

The academic mage PC receives a call from one of their Awakened contacts, ideally a talismonger

or fixer. They have been tasked with acquiring a unique, pre-Awakening artifact, and they need to hire the magician’s team to get it. Unfortunately, the tip that led to the job was also picked up by other parties, and they need to get to the artifact before a rival team does. If the runner team lacks their own transportation, arrangements are provided for them.

This is a great adventure to get the runners out of their usual stomping grounds and explore a different location. However, if the chosen one is in play, they will find out that the rival team attempting to reach the artifact is working for their old master. This may lead to some conflicts of loyalty, as the former master may attempt to exert some influence over the PC. Getting to and finding the artifact should provide little challenge, but the real challenge is dealing with a rival team with capabilities equivalent to the runners. If the runners are slow in reaching the target location, their rivals will have beaten them there, and they must overtake them if the run is to be successful. If the runners reach the target swiftly, they will find themselves pursued by the rival team. And if it’s somewhere in between, have the rival team reach the target at the same time the runners do.
