## Quenched in True Water

True water can be used to quench the focus, hardening its intended purpose, destroying the stone as it is struck by a heated focus. With this kind of preparation, the focus cannot be bonded to anyone else after the initial bond. Additionally, the artificer can imbue one additional attribute into it, selected from the list below.


## quenched in true water

- **Dangerous.** The focus can “bite” anyone who wields it. Focus has innate Death Touch spell (p. 25, Street Wyrd) cast as if Magic is equal to Force, rolling (Force x 2) for the Spellcasting roll. The focus doesn’t suffer drain but can only cast it (Force) times in twenty-four hours.

- **Elemental Mastery.** The focus can gain +2 dice in its use in conjuring tests toward the related spirits of that metaplane (Earth to earth, etc.) or gains +2 dice in spell effects related to the true element.

- **Protective.** If the character is dying, the focus will temporarily damage itself (reducing itself to Force 0) to stabilize the character. Character is healed (Force + 1) overflow damage. The focus recovers 1 point of force every 24 hours.

- **Secretive.** The focus is loyal and resists giving away information. The focus makes a poor astral link (p. 161, SR6). Add (Force + 1) to Astral tracking tests to follow this focus to the owner. It also hard to assense, adding (Force + 1) to the focus to resist assensing (p. 152, SR6).
