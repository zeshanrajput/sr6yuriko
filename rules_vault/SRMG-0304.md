## **Will Bermuda Gremlins leave me stranded in Neo-Tokyo because of a wrong turn in Seattle (p. 171)?**

No. Also it won’t land you in enemy territory because that would probably set the mission back completely. Instead, if both dice are 1s, you will always double the time in the late table on p. 171.
