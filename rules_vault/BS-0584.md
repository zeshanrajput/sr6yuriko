## Ares Macrotechnology

Ares as a megacorporation is focused primarily on security and warfare applications, with little or no regard for the value of life except as a means to deal death on behalf of its customers. They make the best guns and have no apparent ethical boundaries in the pursuit of weapons design. After their experiments attempting to weaponize insect spirits, they’ve turned to more reliable and malleable baseline lifeforms for the purpose of creating powerful and expendable super-soldiers.
