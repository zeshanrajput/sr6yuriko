## Planned Encounter 1

The Central Parkers, a well-known local gang (use Eye-Fiver Go-Ganger, p. 204, SR6), show up claiming Swen owes them. Swen refuses to handle that business now.

The twelve gangers go in for a fist fight, all doing Stun damage. This is a test to see if these runners escalate violence or match it. Swen stays in motion to keep runners between him and the fray, while his security has a fake fight with one ganger.
