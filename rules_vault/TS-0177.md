## **Law Enforcement Service (LES)**

**This is the closest a Sternschutz cop will get to your typical police offcer on patrol. They are the cops in the black uniforms with their shiny silver stars you’ll see walking around public places. They manage public event security, criminal pursuits, arrests, and traffc control. Besides that, they offer police protection for objects and individuals. To do so, they use human resources, as well as drones and other technical toys.**
