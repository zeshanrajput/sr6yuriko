## Saotome Sanctuary

The Evo subsidiary has been working to transform one of its most significant projects into a massive self-contained arcology. The structure bears many similar design styles used by the now-failed Aquaticus facility that is said to have been sunk by forces of the Sea Queen back in 2074. Saotome has been pouring a lot of resources into making it sturdy, defensible, and reliable, but entirely eco-friendly. It takes shape in the Sea of Okhotsk east of the Schmidt Peninsula in about four hundred meters of frigid seawater. The facility is said to have dense concrete infused with ceramic plating for the outer casing, measuring several meters thick, thus able to survive the pressures and temperatures equally well.

The size allows it to house four thousand individuals along with supplies and support for at least five years, basing on nuclear winter projections for their modeling. Its rate of construction is unconfirmed, but records show more than fifteen hundred individuals have already been relocated there.
