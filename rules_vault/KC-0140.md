## The Watchers

Each of the major players has an agent keeping tabs on Eniola Okoro. These agents will begin monitoring the runners once they make it known they are digging into Okoro. Along with monitoring and reporting to their respective organizations they will also be looking to interfere and possibly steal any data the runners get their hands on.

Descriptions for the NPCs, possible interference techniques, and methods/resources for stealing the data are listed here. Where encounters occur is up to the gamemaster.
