## Charismatic Endurance

## **Cost:** 0.25 PP

## **Activation:** Passive

An adept possessing Charismatic Endurance is not only resistant to social manipulations, but they are also able to shake off any social status condition. The adept gains a temporary point of Edge whenever they are defending against an opposed Social test

roll. In addition, they can make a Composure (2) test to shake off any unwanted social statuses (p. 44).
