## **Allies & Contacts**

**Dadeson: How is it you fragbrains manage to continue to exist? Whose closet full of skeletons are you exploiting?**

**Sarkenbleck: Your petty insults mean nothing. We survive because we are the truth the world needs. We exist because the masses deserve to be in control of the world, not subservient to the mages and cyborgs. Our allies are abundant because we are the truth. We need nothing as petty as dirty secrets to gain support—we have the truth.**

**Dadeson: Stop talking. New question. Specifcally, who are your most infuential allies and supporters?**

**Sarkenbleck: Through placement and recruitment, we have our people around the globe, seeking ways to make our future better. We have connections in more places than even I know, including the German Catholic Church, Humanis Policlub, Partido Monarquico, Alamos 20k, the Golden Era So-**

**ciety, We the People, the governments of the UCAS, CAS, Spain, the Caliphate, and dozens of other nations, as well as intelligent individuals within every megacorporation, even Evo, Wuxing, and Manadyne. We tug strings and watch their webs unfold. Each provides sanctuaries, places to meet and plan, soldiers for the cause, and all from behind at least one more layer of anonymity. We have the backing of Winternight’s fragments. But our greatest asset is our heart, Amel Cynthia Dolordvan. She leads from the hidden depths, funds from the heavens, and guides our efforts on Earth. She is above, or better stated, around even the Seven.**

- **Dolordvan is eighth-generation money. While Dolordvan is not a household name, John Jacob Astor is. Her money is that old and has grown, quietly. After the first Crash, her birth trust mysteriously tripled in value overnight. After the second Crash, her personal worth did the same. She’s quiet, reserved, seemingly vapid, and utterly devoid of personal opinions (according to social circles), but obviously she’s just that quiet one you can’t trust.**

**Dadeson: Shut up or I swear I’ll hit your brain with more magic. I need to just get some straight answers from you, though. What are these people doing for you?**

**Sarkenbleck: From inside these powers, they plant the seeds. They fnd others who believe. They study the fles and efforts of those around them, looking for ways to rein in or remove magic from this world. They eliminate problems if the opportunity arises. They look for solutions to the mental health plague that is enhancement addiction and seek ways to eliminate the addicts and the product from our society. While genocide is not our preference, if magic cannot be taken from the world, those who touch it certainly can.**

- **There is a running contract/bounty on the Hidden Blades that’s generic and placed on any and all Awakened or Emergent animals. I checked into it because it seems a decent-paying gig and the “all” is serious—it includes metahumans, animals, whatever. It doesn’t get lots of traction with the real hitters, but young guns often pick it up to build rep and nuyen.**
