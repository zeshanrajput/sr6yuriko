## Superhuman Ego

When you can be faster, stronger, and tougher than pretty much everyone else, who’s to say you aren’t actually a superhero? Runners with superhuman abilities, such as those granted by magic or other augmentations, can start to feel as if they are above other mere metahumans.

- **Bonus:** 12 Karma

- **Game Effect:** Runners with this quality see normal metahumans as beneath them. They have absolutely no qualms about killing such lesser beings. Also, they are not too concerned when being attacked, as they feel indestructible. They do not gain Edge on Defense tests. Characters with Superhuman Ego may not take any quality that prevents them from killing another metahuman or makes them see others as anything other than lesser beings, such as Hooder or Honorbound.
