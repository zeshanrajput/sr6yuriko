## OmniStar

**Focus:** Emergency response and more—this new conglomerate includes medical service corp DocWagon, law enforcement corp Lone Star, and magical goods and services ace Manadyne.

**Headquarters:** Atlanta, CAS

**Key thing to know:** Now occupying the same city as Ares (parent of law-enforcement rival Knight Errant), OmniStar is prepared to bring competition right to Ares’ face and perhaps someday take their seat on the Corporate Court.
