## **Can I negate a Glitch or Critical Glitch with Edge?**

Only using specific types of Edge actions. The 2-Edge boost to add +1 to a single die can potentially remove a Glitch or Critical Glitch. The 3-Edge boost to buy one automatic hit can move a Critical Glitch to a Glitch but doesn’t remove a Glitch. The 4-Edge boost to add Edge to your dice pool effectively increases the number of 1s required to Glitch or Critical Glitch and can remove a Critical Glitch or a Glitch.
