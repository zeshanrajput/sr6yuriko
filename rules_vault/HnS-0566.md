## Breadcrumbs

The comatose hacker visited three places before getting lost in the Matrix: the closest to the runners’ location is Dante’s Inferno, a club he visited in real space. Next are a few virtual bars and hangouts on Hong Kong’s grid, which will lead them to a virtual brothel, Nasty Habits. The last is the VR tour of the Cathedral of Notre-Dame de Paris. Investigating each spot will give a clue to the runners about what their comrade was doing, which will lead them to the employer behind the job.
