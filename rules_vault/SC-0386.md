## Recommendations for character creation:

- **Attributes:** Willpower 6, Intuition 5, Charisma 4

- **Skills:** Firearms or Close Combat 4,

  - Influence 6, Perception 5

- **Knowledge skills:** Local Eateries, Local Gossip, Local Law Enforcement

- **Positive qualities:** Analytical Mind, Indomitable, Quick Healer

- **Negative qualities:** Addiction (level 1), Honorbound, Finesse

- **Life modules:** Agent, Detective, Investigator, Shadowrunner, Event: You struggled through some tough times
