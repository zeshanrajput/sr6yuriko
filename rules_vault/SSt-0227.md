## **When to Run**

**We all have some kind of go-bag ready, a collection of goods assembled that can help us at very least get far from trouble, and maybe even get us a few steps into whatever our next life will be. But how do you know when it’s time to use it? How do you know when your protections are gone, your safehouse is no longer safe, and your current identity needs to be forever buried? As is usually the case, there is no one absolute answer, but here are some things to watch out for.**
