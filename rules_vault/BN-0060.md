## **Supernatural Recovery**

**==> picture [137 x 12] intentionally omitted \<==**

## • **Requirement:** Essence 6

This form of supernatural healing allows grants you a Body + Body healing test once per minute. Each hit recovers one box of damage. All Overfl ow damage must be healed before any Physical, and all Physical damage must be recovered before any Stun damage. This test occurs even if you have exceeded your Overfl ow track, but if your Overfl ow track is still full after one test, you die. Track damage beyond your Overfl ow for the purpose of this test.

Damage from drain, fading, vulnerabilities, and allergies are not healed by Supernatural Recovery. Anything that modifi es a Natural Recovery roll like Quick Healing, medical attention, symbiotes, and similar effects apply to Supernatural Recovery.

Supernatural Recovery will expel any cyberware that is not installed into an Essence hole from Animalism as long as your Magic attribute remains 1 or higher.

This power is lost if your Magic attribute drops to 0 or if your Essence drops below 6.
