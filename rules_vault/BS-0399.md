## Immortal Flower

- **Vector:** Ingestion

- **Speed:** 2D6 combat rounds

- **Duration:** 1D6 hours

- **Power:** 11

This blend of mosses and grasses found in the North American Great Plains grants the user abilities identical to the Concealment and Confusion powers associated with Awakened critters and spirits. When the drug wears off, the user suffers –1 Intuition and –1 Willpower for twice the duration.

- **Effect:** Regeneration (p. 227, _SR6_ )

Immortal flower is a plant from the Mojave Desert. Users of this magical compound consume the petals of the flower and gain the Regeneration power. For every 16 boxes of damage sustained while the user is under the influence, permanently reduce the character’s Essence by 0.1. If the user has any cyberware, they resist 4 + 1D6 boxes of Physical damage when the drug wears off as the body attempts to repair the implants. When the drug wears off, the user gains the Fatigued I status.
