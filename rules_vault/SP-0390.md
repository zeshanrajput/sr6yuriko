## **Chainblade**

**The chainblade is a heavy, combat-knife-sized weapon. Instead of a cutting edge, the weapon incorporates a few dozen chain-link, mounted, mono-edged blades moving at high speed. A small high-torque motor is mounted just above the knife’s hilt, spinning the chain along the outer edge of the blade. These whirling blades can cause severe rending wounds. When powered up, these weapons create a substantial amount of noise, making their use obvious.**

**Chain weapons are powered by removable power cells held inside the hilt. Power cells are non-rechargeable and last for about one hour of constant runtime or twelve hours of intermittent operation. Once a power cell is activated it will provide power until the electrical-chemical reaction runs its course. The power cell then becomes inert, and the weapon requires a new power cell to be inserted.**
