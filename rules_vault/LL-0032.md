## shadow stock 

## Drake Rules 

## Classifications 

Drakes are metahumans who are able to transform into a metahuman-sized dragon/ human hybrid form. Unlike shapeshifters, they are fully metahuman and can be targeted with spells and effects that specifically target metahumans rather than critters (such as Slay Elf or Detect Human), but they also count as drakes, so that spells and effects tailored for drakes or dracoforms will also affect them.
