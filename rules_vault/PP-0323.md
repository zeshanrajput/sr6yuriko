## History

The cold fact of the matter is that Spinrad Global has very little history. Their AAA rating is just over a year old, and they are still learning how to use their new powers (such as feeling their way through their role on the Corporate Court). But to understand what the corp is, you have to look at the two pieces that make its foundation—Spinrad Industries and Global Sandstorm.
