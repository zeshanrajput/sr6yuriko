## **Wuxing**

**You were supposed to be the guardians, the ones who preserved the sacred blood! Yet you let the ultimate corruption seep its way into your home, infect so they can steal the very thing you swore to protect! Instead of nurturing and preserving the lifeblood, you now steal it and toss it into the abyss! Of all, you should have understood the most, fought the hardest! But instead, you handed it over like so many before you, and for what?! Of them all, you deserve the most pain, the most suffering!**
