## Racer

## (Elf, Professional Rating 3)

The masters of those hyperbikes can make them dance in the densest traffic. They’re as sleek and fast as their bikes, but all it takes is an off comment about being faster, better, or slicker than they are, and they’ll be looking to race. They’ve also been known to roll up on anything that might be fun to outrun (including cops and corpsec) and issue a challenge in the flashiest way possible. They often carry a rider into gang scuffles, and while the added weight might slow them a tick, the riders know how to operate from the back of a two-wheeled street rocket.
