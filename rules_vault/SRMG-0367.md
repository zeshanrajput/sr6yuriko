## **Can group contacts (6WC, p. 175-176) buy or fence gear and what is their maximum loyalty?**

When fencing gear through a group contact, instead of rolling the normal opposed roll, instead they will roll Connection + Connection to resist selling gear for the runners. The Loyalty is limited to 8 at character generation and in play.
