## Current Status

Almost as soon as the Disian threat was eliminated, Horizon graciously bowed out of the Defender Movement. Previously established lines of communication went silent, and information stopped flowing. No spectacle, no acknowledgement of what had just happened. They simply went back to business as normal, like the entire shadow war was simply another news day. And they’ve been operating like that ever since.

Whatever Horizon is planning for the future, they’re keeping it close to the chest.

- It’s interesting to note that Horizon is responsible for initially drafting the resolution to ban all mention of the Disians and the shadow war. Take that for what you will.

- **>** Fianchetto
