## **Game Effect: GMT Dermal Plating**

**Dermal plating with GMT adds its rating to the user’s Defense Rating against mana spells and critter powers that are resisted by Willpower, Logic, Intuition, or Charisma. Half of the rating is also added to the dice pool on defense tests against mana spells and critter powers that beneft from the DR increase.**

**Sustaining spells on any individual with grey mana cybertech is also more diffcult, increasing the sustaining penalty to –3 dice for each spell sustained on such a target. (Sustaining foci and the Focused Concentration quality are not affected.)**

**GMT is inherently unstable. After its frst exposure to magic, any subsequent test using a GMT augmentation must include a wild die.**
