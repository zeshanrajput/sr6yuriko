## **Fish and Game Warden**

**Uses: botany, hunting regulations, parazoology, pest control, tracking, zoology**

**Places to Meet: campground, back road, hunting locations, lakes, rivers, rural areas**

**Similar Contacts : campers, eco-warriors, hunters, survivors, preppers**

**Fish and game wardens are usually found in rural areas, but can be in urban environments that either have large campgrounds or natural reserves, and they’re sometimes called in when Awakened or other critters are involved. They safeguard natural resources and, when possible, ensure the responsible use of wildlife ecosystems. Their responsibilities encompass a wide range of duties, including enforcing fshing and hunting regulations, monitoring wildlife populations, and protecting habitats. They patrol natural areas and investigate violations. They enforce laws related to wildlife and environmental protection in the hopes of creating or maintaining a balance between metahuman activities and the preservation of nature.**
