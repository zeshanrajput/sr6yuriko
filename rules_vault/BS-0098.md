## Eyeware

## Eye-Light System

Eye-lights are miniaturized light emitters that are implanted into the user’s inner eye, coupled to a secondary iris and pupil that allow the light to shine outward without interfering with the regular field of view. The light allows persons with low-light vision to see for twenty meters in total darkness. The light can emit any color of light, but red is most common because it best preserves night vision.
