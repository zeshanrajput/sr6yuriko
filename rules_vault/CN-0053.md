## Group Structure: Rabid Packs

Contrary to most organizations that work in a clandestine cell structure (where cells knowing nothing about other cells in their network for full deniability), GreenWar works mostly in an organized network structure with a central and local leadership.

- While true, there are a few individuals who are too sociopathic or misanthropic to play well with others. They work better on their own.
