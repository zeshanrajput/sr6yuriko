## **Relationships**

**Saeder-Krupp has never liked letting its relationships defne it. Like a defantly single twenty-something, they emphasize doing things their own way, and they worry that any close connection with another entity will cramp their style. So they’re not big on equal partnerships, making it unlikely that an exterior partner can drag them one way or another in this confict.**

**But** _**drag**_ **is different from** _**infuence.**_ **There are some groups or individuals that Lofwyr and his company listen to because of their track records, and others who they react against due to decades (or more) of antipathy. Here are some of the relationships to keep an eye on.**
