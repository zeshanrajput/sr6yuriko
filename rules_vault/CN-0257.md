## Middle East Division

It is no coincidence that STV’s presence is majorly restricted to cities that are either great corporate hubs such as Cape Town or Dubai, have some arcane connection, or contain a great number of magical groups. Business relationships across the region are run from the Cairo head office. STV’s membership in the Apep Consortium in the past brought them many clients from the Caliphate, including a number of “djinns” (free spirits) and insect shamans/spirits, such as the **Hem’netjer** , a group of beetle magicians.



- These operations are overseen by senior partner Amenset who believes she is the reborn soul of an Egyptian priestess or vizier of Thoth that practiced law in ancient Egypt.
