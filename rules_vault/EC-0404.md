## Don Joseph Gianelli

Gianelli is grandson to consiglieri Toni Gianelli and godson to the previous Don Maurice “The Butcher” Bigio. While his house doesn’t have a seat at the table, he’s accepted Capo O’Malley and the direction she has set for the Seattle Families. Don Gianelli is focused primarily on cleanup and consolidation in the ongoing wake of the Mob Wars. In Puyallup, he keeps tighter reins on smuggling for the family across the whole network, making sure that the Yakuza don’t interrupt it. The rest of Puyallup he leaves to Sottocapo Enzo Gianelli to manage. He’s indifferent to Puyallup and really only cares that the Yakuza are not in control.

- Some think of this as a test for Enzo and how can he manage the assets of this wasteland. They forget that this could also be a test for Joey on how he runs the family business. Joey’s youth still runs counter to the old guard.

- Ethernaut
