## Ares Roadmaster

This is a long-haul commercial van built like a tank. The Roadmaster is used across the wideopen spaces around the world, which are often full of go-gangs and nomadic hunters. It’s heavily armored and designed for easy installation of a pair of remote turrets. Within the urban streets, they usually sport the logos of security firms that use them to transport valuables, both personnel and property.
