## Koshari

While small, the Koshari are still dipping their toe in the hunt for Kechibi. Their connection to the NAN and in particular the Pueblo Corporate



Council (PCC) provides them a unique position. They have many places within the NAN where they provide almost exclusive criminal access. They have enough dirt on PCC execs and politicians that they obtain resource access easily when needed, and they snag prototype code and tech for their own use on the regular. Along with that, they’re aided by the security-minded and slightly xenophobic nature of the NAN. Their Amerind heritage protects them from crackdowns that smash other syndicates before they even catch a footing. If the Kechibi Code pops up somewhere in the NAN, the Koshari will be happy to find themselves as the only crime syndicate working from those particular streets.

If they get their hands on it, you can almost guarantee they’ll sell access to the PCC and the NAN. That would fetch a high price and help them keep fighting against being forced into Business Recognition Accords. Standing on their own two feet has been a struggle, and the Koshari certainly wouldn’t mind making a massive truckload of nuyen while helping to solidify and strengthen the nations they do the bulk of their business in.
