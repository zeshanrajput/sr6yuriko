## The Italiano (Kent)

Traditional Italian food and a large dance floor with nightly live bands are the main draws of this local nightclub. It’s an open secret that the management of the Italiano recruits for the Mafia, making the place a popular hangout for would-be mademen. Directly across the street from the Murdered Mime, it’s also known for nightly brawls and gunfights in the parking lot as both the Yakuza and Mafia prospects try to prove themselves by taking on the opposition.
