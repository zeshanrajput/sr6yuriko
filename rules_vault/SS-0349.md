## Manifest Alchera

Manifest alchera are ghostly and insubstantial. The reality they overlay can be seen beneath their ghostly images, and nothing in the alchera can be interacted with in any way. Manifest alchera can be used to add to the atmosphere of a scene or to drop hints about metaplanar events that are relevant to the ongoing plot. For example, an alchera might show a location of an artifact they PCs are looking for, provide a window into the past, or provide information about spirits who are part of the job the PCs are working.
