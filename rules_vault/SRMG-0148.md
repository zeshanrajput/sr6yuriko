## **Assist and teamwork tests in initiative don’t seem to work well together?**

The Assist action is an Anytime action. When the runner declares they are assisting, they must have a Major Action left. The assister only needs to declare they are assisting before the leader acts which does not need to occur on the assisters turn. They then immediately spend the action and perform the teamwork test. The leader of the test must use the bonus dice from the teamwork test on the next action they have, or the teamwork bonus is lost. You cannot assist yourself.
