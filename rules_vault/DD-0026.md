## Part of the Family:

## Retired

With a lucrative career behind them, you managed to negotiate your release from your obligation from the order and now are free to carry on with your life. In order to maintain this status, you must never reveal information about the organization.

**Bonus:** 4 Karma

**Game Effect:** You left the organization on good terms, having sworn an oath of secrecy. Maintain the group contact, though your Loyalty rating can no longer be increased. You also lose access to the gear and services that are available to an active member of the order (including discounts). You may use the group contact as a resource, but reciprocity is expected (you pay full price and can’t just borrow gear anymore). If you ever break your vow of secrecy, this quality is replaced with Part of the Family: Deserted.
