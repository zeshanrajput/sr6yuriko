## **Initiative:** 11(13) + 3D6

## **Defense Rating:** 12

**Skills:** Astral 3, Athletics 6, Close Combat 6, Exotic Weapon (any ranged weapon) 5, Influence 3, Perception 6, Stealth 4

**Powers:** Armor 4, Astral Perception, Berserk 2, Fire Resistance (as quality), Great Leap 4, Improved Reflexes 2, Natural Weapon (claws/bite), Natural Weapon (horns), Natural Weapon (tongue), Sapience **Attacks:**

Unarmed [Close Combat, DV 2S, AR 17/—/—/—/—] Claws/bite [Close Combat, DV 4P, AR 17/—/—/—/—] Horns [Close Combat, DV 4P, AR 17/—/—/—/—] Tongue [Exotic, DV 3P, AR 17/15\*/—/—/—]

- Maximum range 15 meters.

**Notes:** The mature minotoad’s tongue acts as a grapple attack at range. The only action a mature minotoad can take once successfully grappling an opponent is Drag( as Restrain, but moves the target 5 meters toward the minotoad). Because their tongue is a natural weapon, using it during a berserk rampage is well within the capabilities of a mature minotoad.
