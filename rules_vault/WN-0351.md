## 23: freedom of the press

## Hook

Another offer of a possible job comes in from Piper Jane. She wants to meet at her office at 17:00. She apologizes for the early meet, but the job needs to get done tonight, and she wants the team to have as much time as possible.

When the runners show up, the first things they notice are a lack of Jinxy in the hall, the office door for Jon Nox is kicked open, and Piper’s door is slightly ajar. Inside Piper’s office there appears to have been a struggle, and Piper is not there. Inside Jon Nox’s office there is no sign of Nox—just a spray of bullets into the space and signs of clearing the room. It doesn’t look like Nox was present when the bullets were fired. Piper’s commlink is on the floor, and Jinxy is found in a bag under the desk.
