## Professional Rating 8: Special Forces

You’re reaching the top of the world’s elite opponents who can bring death in an eyeblink. They’re infiltrators, assassins, and the definition of surgical strikes. Some of the world’s governments and their large special operations groups such as the Navy SEALs, Marine Force Recon, and Spetsnaz all find a home in this tier of death-dealers.
