## Flynn Ames, Maersk: Senior VP of Logistics, North America

**Description:** Male human, long dark hair, wearing a classic suit accented by a collection of fancy rings and necklaces rather than a tie. He expresses himself loudly and has a large personality.

**Possible Party Missteps:** Flynn is single and rich. He barely paid attention to the invitation to this event but gladly partakes in the drugs, BTLs, and joygirls. The closest to a misstep he manages is almost getting into a fight with Cade Scanlon when he repeatedly mocks his appearance.
