## Telekinesis

The soldier can move physical objects with the power of their mind, and they do not need to be Awakened to do so. They make a Willpower + Intuition test; for each net hit, they can move a kilogram of material. They cannot fling it; it moves relative slowly, at a rate of a half meter per combat round.
