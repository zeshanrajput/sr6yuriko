## Navigating the Astral Sea

Most methods of travel into the metaplanes create a direct passageway from the traveler’s current location to the destination metaplane. While this works well for traveling to known metaplanes, or the location a portal or rift leads to, it’s not very usable for general exploration or searching for undiscovered metaplanes. Explorers searching for undiscovered lands will typically need to travel the Astral Sea to search for them.
