## Company in Focus:

## Yingyong

Is a security services company focusing on non-lethal and universally applicable appliances development still a security company? Started in 2058 by Feng Su Chow, a graduate from the University of Hong Kong, Yingyong has had many successful product lines and patents for weapon styles and forms since their inception. Their current leading research director is Gu Guanyu, a forty-three-year-old elf of indeterminate composure but unmistakable intellect and sense of purpose in his work. His track record is impressive—he has developed all-ceramic dart casters, bone-induction restraining systems that function on mundane and Awakened alike, and smart-delivery technologies that can be used for precision control of gas-deployment devices.



Yingyong’s principal offices and research facilities are located in Hong Kong, but only just. While they may reside in an A-rating security zone, they are not large enough for all their employees to benefit from secure corporate housing. Most live within five kilometers of their offices, but a few employees commute from much farther distances. In an attempt to tighten the security for their employees, it is not uncommon for Yingyong to provide PanicButton-enabled commlinks or even bonded taxi or commuter-pass benefits.
