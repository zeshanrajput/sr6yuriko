## **Attention to Detail**

**It’s standard fare to know how to extract a target in this biz, whether willing or … not so much. What isn’t standard are the environmental factors that surround the extrication of said target. You’ve got to think of everything: strength of the opposition, assets, security budgets, building plans, Matrix support, Awakened critters … what about**

**inclement weather? Or even the current state of politics in the region? Feel me, omae? A good shadowrunner can prepare for the unexpected, but a great shadowrunner** _**has**_ **to be prepared for it.**

**What is the line between kidnapping and extraction, and do you know when you’re about to cross it? What about slipping into the grey space of the person you’re removing—want to know how to get the target to trust you? Maybe most importantly, what are the typical—and atypical—complications that having a plus-one with you can bring?**

**Here are three ops that really stand out in my career—each one highlights an important piece of what it takes to understand and accomplish a successful extraction. Dig deep, fnd the faws, tear ’em up, I don’t mind. They’re done, and we did our biz.**
