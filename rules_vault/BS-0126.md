## Nutrition Storage System (Rating 1-6)

Food paste and water are stored in implanted reservoirs that release into the user’s stomach and mouth. These storage systems are refilled via external ports. Each rating point allows for a day’s worth of food and water to be consumed internally.

**Wireless bonus** : If equipped with DNI or a taste booster, you can experience the taste of the paste being pressed directly into your stomach.
