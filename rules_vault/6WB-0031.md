## Datasteal

Companies are always working on the next new prototype. It is often easier and cheaper to hire shadowrunners to steal a prototype and all related research data from a company than to invent new things themselves. Corporate redundancy and compartmentalization mean that often, the data needs to be acquired from more than one location. One team of researchers could be working on part of a prototype at one location while a separate research facility holds the rest of the device. Particularly sensitive files may be stored at yet another, air-gapped location. Occasionally this type of work is combined with extraction when an employer decides they need the scientists as well.
