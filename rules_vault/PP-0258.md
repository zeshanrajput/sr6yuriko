## Global Management

The most noticeable and rememberable is certainly **Roel Vandenhoeck** . This slick and arrogant elf is the head of public relations and S-K’s main spokesperson. **Cheyenne Galais** is a Quebecois dwarf and head of global human resources. She is also responsible for the infamous _Innenrevision_ . The Turkish-German human **Prof. Mesut Sahin** was professor for corporate finance in Greater Frankfurt before he joined S-K. Now he is head of finance. The corporation’s chief legal officer is a dryad named **Evelyn Westlake** , who originates from Portland.
