## **The False Flag, with a Twist**

**This is a classic—stage an attack against a target and make it look like someone else was responsible for the attack, so that the target gets mad and increases aggression against who they thought attacked them.**

**The Seelie Court does not traffc in physical violence much, of course, so an attack on them is likely going to come in the form of some espionage-related activities. My preferred way to do this operation is to let Court operatives hear about some ongoing intelligence operations against them and then give them a trail of breadcrumbs to lead them to this supposed operation. When they break the cell, they fnd some private intelligence (providing a private** _**coimeádaí**_ **is always a nice touch) that allows them to learn details of the planned activities, inspiring them to act. Only a foolish organization doesn’t at least look into the information they fnd—it’s a great way to spur them to action.**

- **The climate is right for something like this. The Court already knows Dis is going to act against them, so they are primed to believe any information that helps them take the initiative.**
