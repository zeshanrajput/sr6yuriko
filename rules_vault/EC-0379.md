## Route 7

Route 7 is one of the direct smuggling routes into Seattle via land. The Yakuza supply go-gangs as distractions, overwatch, and support for smugglers in exchange for a small percentage of the take. If payment is not met, those gangs can become a pack of wild dogs to harry and raid smugglers.
