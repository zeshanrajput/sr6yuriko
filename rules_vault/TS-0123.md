## **Excalibur Division**

**the Snake came, and usurped the Sovereign and desecrated the image of Excalibur. The people wept, until the Pendragon rose up to remind the Queen of Her most sacred of duties. With loyal wardens as Her blade, She cast out the Snake and his flth, banishing them from the realm! But the Snake’s spawn did not go quietly. They hid until they struck at the people, trying to destroy the peace of Her realm. But all was not lost, as loyal wardens rose up and heeded the Lady’s call, and in the Queen’s name they smote the Snake’s spawn. The Lady then bestowed Excalibur upon the rightful Queen, to be Her right hand and the instrument through which She would vanquish Her foes and maintain peace and justice throughout the realm. Only this time, the Queen was not a warrior sovereign, and so the Lady reforged the hearts of those wardens of old, that a portion of the blade rested within the bosom of the wardens. Excalibur rests in the rightful Queen’s right hand. Let none oppose the Queen’s justice or the peace of the realm.**
