## Mafia

The five families are alive and well in NYC. While each has a significant presence in the city itself, the bulk of their individual operations are outside of Manhattan. Within the city, they operate in different districts or different markets. The Carnetti family controls Battery City, and by extension the illegal trade coming in through the Ludmilla Reanka Memorial Tunnel. The Lucchese family controls the Washington Heights area. The Colombo, Genovese, and Bonnano families operate a string of businesses throughout the city. Each is careful not to step on the others’ toes, which is why the peace between them has lasted as long as it has.

- The NY Mob has a longstanding business relationship with CitiGroup. The bank serves as a laundry for the mob’s less-legal enterprises, and in exchange CitiGroup executives get access to all the black-market goods and services they desire. They even have access to muscle when needed.
