## Miscellaneous Allies

We can confirm that there are at least three Dis agents working within the Draco Foundation. Their exact roles are unknown. Likewise, we’ve learned that the Atlantean Foundation, the Astral Space Preservation Society, and The Oracle Society may have been compromised by Dis. We also know multiple fixers and Mr. Johnsons within the shadow community and around the globe have been working for Dis or Dis interests. Some knowingly, others not.
