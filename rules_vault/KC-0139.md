## Kiniun: “The Lion”

To take the name of the highest denomination of hawala marker is a sign of the value Kiniun puts into himself. The name also shows the ferocity with which he acts and the level of pride he possesses. Most of the other hawalas are quietly powerful, but the Lion is loud and brash.

This volume and brashness are all part of a longer-term plan. The Lion has no desire to stay a hawala forever. He has already completed the first two steps of his goal. The first to become a hawala, and the second is to gain a seat on the council. The third is to move from the streets of Lagos to the towers of Lagos Island and eventually to the towers of another corporate sprawl. He wants a place in the megacorporate world, and the current focus of the corps on Eniola Okoro looks to him like a perfect opportunity to move up. Some believe the Lion is trying to acquire the data needed to stop Okoro in order to curry favor with the corps, but instead he is planning to get the information and then pressure Okoro into leaving the corporation of his choice alone. That shift in focus is what he hopes to use to get in with his corporation of choice, Horizon. Because Kiniun isn’t looking for just any place in the corporate world—he wants to be a simsense star.
