## The Wu Quints

For those of you who don’t keep up with history, here’s the short version. Wu Lung-wei and Sharon Chiang-Wu have five daughters: Fo, Shui, Tou, Moak, and Gum. They were born in 2061 after Sharon received a magical artifact called the Second Coin of Luck from Dunkelzahn’s will. The girls are identical quintuplets, so people call them the Quints. If you want to know more about them as people, you can find them in pretty much any celebrity gossip rag.
