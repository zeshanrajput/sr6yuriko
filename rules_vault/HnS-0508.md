## Game Information

## Entering the Resonance Realms

There are four ways that a player could get to the Resonance Realms.

- Going through the Event Horizon is the most common route, but this way is only open to technomancers who have undergone submersion.

- Using a Resonance Rift may take anyone anywhere, bypassing the Event Horizon, but rifts are random and impossible to find on purpose. Entering one accidentally also offers no guarantee of ever getting back out.

- Bartering with a free sprite that can open a rift on demand is an option. The sprite may have some control over the rift, but it can be hard to find what a free sprite wants out of the deal.

- Entering the Deep Foundation is technically entering the realms, but it only grants you access to the Reflecting Pool. There is no pathway from here to the deeper realms.
