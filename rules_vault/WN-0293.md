## The Foundation Archive

The runners move directly from the portal to the Foundation archive node and discover the paradigm is already set. The archive is represented by a woodland glade surrounded by vine-covered trees. There are dozens of wageslaves busily working at desks organized in rows on the grassy field. As the runners enter the node, a young sarariman stands and bows slightly, then asks the team what business they have in this Foundation node. This is actually a member of the Null Sect named Acolyte 3-7-2, and it has little patience with unauthorized visitors. The xenosapient’s attitude will be condescending and arrogant, but it will listen to the runners and can be convinced to help if handled correctly. Unless attacked, Acolyte 3-7-2 will decide the runners may be useful to the Null Sect. It will reach out as if pushing a button in mid-air, making a set of elevator doors appear. This is a temporary data trail to the host’s null node and to Prelate 3-7. If the runners check the archive at some point, it is just as empty of data as the host above. If attacked, Acolyte 3-7-2 will use Fnord to disappear. As the Null Sect is thoroughly in control of the Foundation, it will set the variance score of all the runners to 80 and send the Foundation into alert, probably sending a few Nights and Greys in to attack for good measure.
