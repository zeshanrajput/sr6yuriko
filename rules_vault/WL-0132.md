## **Pushing the Envelope**

## **Gamemaster Tips**

**The toxic magician has succeeded and created toxic martichoras. They are all too happy to use the PCs as test subjects to see how dangerous the toxic beasts truly are. Increase the toxic martichoras’ Magic attribute by 2 and replace their allergy with Immunity (toxins). Their venom is now delivered by their claws and bite as well as their quills.**

**Martichoras are dangerous critters, with an efective close-combat attack and venom that causes both nausea and Physical damage. They hunt for survival, and they probably won’t attack what they aren’t going to eat. Martichoras are likely to try to pick of individuals who stray from the group. Any attack against a team of PCs is going to be**

**opinions on the validity and potential value of merlin hawks to internal arcane security forces, but since everything I found out pointed to a rudimentary sapience as well as a casting and summoning ability that would be diffcult to include in any form of training, I’d guess they put it down very quickly and moved on to something I turned in about a critter with fur, claws, and sharp teeth.**
