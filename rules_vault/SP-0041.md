## **Magical Support and Locations of Power**

**To say that Aztechnology’s magical resources are crucial to Disian plans would be a massive understatement. Blood mages especially have been working closely with the few Disians who have traveled to the material plane (such as the Auditor) to perfect the magical side of the proposed Harvester Network.**

**But despite everything that the Disians have done to Aztechnology, the Azzies still have one ace in the hole: they control several sites of power that are critical to the establishment of the Harvester network. Sources indicate that if the Disians lose access to even a few of these sites, then the Harvester network is worthless.**

**Of course, taking those locations off the board is easier said than done.**
