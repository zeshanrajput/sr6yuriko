## The Bench Clearer

- **Formula:** Stunball, Delayed Verbal (1 Combat Round), Somatic

- **Drain Value:** 7

- **Cost:** 550¥

This preparation looks like a typical baseball. Speak the command words (default: “high heat”) and toss it into an area (fast ball or underhand, whichever), and you’ve got a shot at snoozing the opposing team, or at least wearing them down enough to rethink skinning their smoke wagons when you come in guns blazing. The toss requires an Athletics + Agility (1) test. If the test fails, the ball lands somewhere other than intended (use the scatter diagram, p. 114,



_SR6_ , if desired) and fails to activate. The ball does not lose its charge on a failed activation but still must be used on the same location with a successful toss.
