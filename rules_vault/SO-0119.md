## The Strip Club

This one gets the most mileage because it has every conceivable thing you want, and it’s the classic for a reason. The whole point of a fleshpot is getting titillated, and once the mark is suitably wound up in their temptation and fantasy, you start implying they can get a little more in, oh, the Champagne Room. Sure, the prices are higher, but high-rollers get special benefits. You don’t have to outright promise sex, but you can imply anything, and who is better at implication than a stripper? Just one more bottle. Just one more show. Just one more one more until … right?
