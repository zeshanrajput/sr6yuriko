## **Fullerton Smythe**

**Smythe is a smooth operator based out of Lagos. He made his money the old-fashioned way— he inherited it. He has zero concept of real-world economics, which means he’ll often pay well above market value for cards, but he also understands his inheritance is not infnite, so screwing people over occurs often enough to give him a reputation. He has been known to activate cards when still in the presence of the team that brought it and has dragged at least two runner teams on some metaplanar jaunts they’ll never forget.**
