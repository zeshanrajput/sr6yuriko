## Randalls Island and Wards Island

These two islands are often thought of as a single landmass due to the shared purpose of the space. The islands are designed to isolate dangerous individuals. Randalls is a prison, plain and simple, and Wards is branded as a rehabilitation zone, though the truth of what goes on there is far worse. Randalls and Wards share a dark history. They housed everything from prisons to orphanages, and even held insane asylums used for psychological experimentation. With the shift of high-risk prisoners to Roosevelt, the five prisons on Randalls range from minimum to moderate security. The prisons here are primarily used to hold low-escape-risk prisoners, prisoners awaiting trial, or prisoners who will be transported to long-term corporate facilities outside the city.

The people on Wards Island are called patients, but they are no more free to leave than their counterparts in the prisons next door. The main facility on the island is Horizon’s Rehabilitation and Psychiatric Center, which happens to be a global leader in the research of CFD.

- Like with Roosevelt, the mana ebbs on these islands can be pretty severe. The DIMR maintains a research facility here dedicated to studying the long-term impact of that mana ebb on the prisoners who reside here.

- Elijah

The only entry/exit point by land is the Sony Bridge, which passes into neighboring Queens City. Travel across the bridge is slow due to NYPD checkpoints at either end.
