## Tamanous

Globally famous for their connection to organ-legging, in Lagos they have an interesting reputation. Most places see Tamanous as monsters who sell second-hand wares and body parts to criminals, but in Lagos Tamanous is almost like the local funeral home or a pawn shop for corpses. They sell organs and secondhand cyber on the black market, but they also sell the other bodily remains to connections in Asamando for the ghoul population there.

Not all of their sales and body-gathering are on the up-and-up, however. They still kidnap and murder for specific parts or to have fresher materials for higher-end clients.

Okoro used Tamanous in her early days because they were among the cheapest illegal assistance she could find—they always supplemented their pay with resources they took during the job. She hasn’t contacted them since her windfall, which was not a big issue at first, but they have recently become aware of her growth and are making efforts to contact her again in order to get more work from her or blackmail her about the earlier partnership.
