## **Teamwork (Specify a Skill)**

**Sometimes you need a little help from your furry friends. You can train a critter to provide assistance in the form of a Teamwork test for any skill the critter knows. A separate command must be learned for each skill. Emerged critters can be trained to assist with Matrix actions. This command only allows a critter to assist when a Teamwork test would normally be allowed—it does not apply to Close Combat or Sorcery tests, or any test made to attack.**
