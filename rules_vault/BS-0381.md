## Hurlg

## (Fomorian Usquebaugh, Orkstaff’s XXX)

- **Vector:** Ingestion

- **Speed:** (12 – Body) x 2 minutes

- **Duration:** (12 – Body) hours, minimum 1 hour

- **Power:** 7

- **Effect:** –1 Reaction, +1 Willpower, –1 Logic, High Pain Tolerance (p. 72, _SR6_ ), Confused 2

Hurlg is a real ork’s drink, not some milquetoast stuff that breeders drink. It was developed by orks for orks and is a dark, soup-like ale swimming with hops and nutmeg. The mildly hallucinogenic properties of nutmeg combined with the 180-proof liquor create a terrific buzz that will appeal to orks, trolls, and even dwarfs. When the drink wears off, the user crashes and resists (Power)S damage. If the user is human or elf, they also gain the from Confused 2 status.
