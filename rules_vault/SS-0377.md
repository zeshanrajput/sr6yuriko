## Memory Hole

The soldier has a specific part of their past that they cannot recall, even though other memoriesare intact. They may remember their childhood home but not a best friend they had while living there, or they may remember all of their siblings or unit members except one, and so on. A Mind Probe spell with 7 or more hits can access the memories that have somehow been locked off to the individual.
