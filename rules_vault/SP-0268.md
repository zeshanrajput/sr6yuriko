## **The Hook**

**The players receive an urgent message for a job—Mr. Johnson doesn’t have time for a proper meet and briefs them over the phone. “Is this line secure? Well, drek, even if it isn’t, there’s no time …” Mr. Johnson tells the runners that a busload of Awakened children has been abducted, and time is of the essence. He needs to track down the missing children ASAP and has a t-bird powered up and ready to pick them up. He’s offering a lot of nuyen for the job, plus the use of an Ares Venture V/TOL. The player characters can utilize the provided pilot or, if they prefer, pilot it themselves. If the player characters have been working toward acquiring an aircraft, the Venture could be used as payment instead of nuyen.**
