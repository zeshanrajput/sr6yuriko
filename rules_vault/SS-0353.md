## The Sears Tower

Perhaps one of the grimmest regularly recurring alchera in the world, this manifest alchera appears on the anniversary of the collapse of what was once the world’s tallest building. The blocky, black-steel building is imposing, a ghostly reminder of what downtown Chicago used to be while looking over the Shattergraves, the area it destroyed in its collapse. Recent attempts to reclaim the core of Chicago led to an uptick in tourism for the site, but the return of chaos to the area has seen that tail off.
