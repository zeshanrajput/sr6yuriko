## Powers 

In addition to gaining dracoform adjustment points, every drake gains access to certain magical powers when in their drake form. 

Universally, these are Armor, Dual-Natured, Elemental Attack, Natural Weapons, the flaw Unsteady Hands, and a movement power—either Vestigial Wings or Underwater Adaptation. As they grow more attuned to their draconic form, drakes may purchase additional powers (see Dracogenesis, below). 

Any time a drake power, or a critter power it references, is calculated based on Magic, use the drake’s Edge attribute, instead (for a way to modify this, see Dracogenesis below). Not every drake is a magic-user, and natural talent coupled with determination—represented by Edge—are the keys to harnessing the full power of one’s dracoform. 

_Example_ : Our eastern drake from before has an Edge of 4. When she’s in her Dracoform, she gains 4 points of Armor (her Edge attribute), she is Dual-Natured, she can breathe fire by rolling her Edge + Agility with a DV of (Edge)P (making it 4P), and with ranges and attack values based on her Edge of 4 (giving her 8/6/— /—/—), she gains Natural Weapons that do Str/2 with Attack ratings of (Str + Rea), her claws are brutal but clumsy enough she has the Unsteady Hands quality applied, and finally, she has a flight speed equal to her normal movement speed.
