## Pure Reagents

The reagents harvested on cloud islands are particularly pure, making them especially useful to alchemists. A pure reagent may be used to increase the duration of an alchemical preparation. For each pure reagent used, the amount of time that the preparation remains at full power is extended by twelve hours. Once the preparation begins to lose potency, it does so at the normal rate.
