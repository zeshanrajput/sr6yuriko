## No Resonance Critter Power Augmentations

Technocritters are a relatively new phenomenon, and while you can bet that most megacorporations are pouring piles of nuyen into R&D to discover how to reliably enhance critters with Resonance abilities, no one has succeeded. It’s probably only a matter of time, but that time is not yet here.

Emerged critters can receive cyberware, bioware, and even genetic augmentations. Note that Essence loss has a negative impact on their Resonance attribute, but that doesn’t stop everyone from trying. Maybe someday there will even be the critter equivalent of a cyber-adept.
