## The Circle is Closed

## \<07-23-83/0901:05>

I awoke back on the _Iwo Jima_ . Next to me, sleeping peacefully, was Hanna, heavily bandaged and thankfully sedated. I laid there for a few moments, listening to the sounds of the sickbay before hearing gentle words in my mind. “The circles are closed, especially yours. Our bargain is fulfilled, your task is complete. We leave you in peace.”

And with that, I felt alone in my own thoughts again and started bawling my eyes out.

This is going to be my last message for a while. There are things I need to deal with, like Hanna.
