## Free Dive Deep Breath Mask

This full-face mask has a series of slots to hold deep breath disks and a small lever to slide them out toward your mouth. The mask is purchased with a single deep breath disk; additional disks are extra. It allows for the equivalent of scuba diving without the pesky risks. **Cost:** 150¥ **Availability:** 4
