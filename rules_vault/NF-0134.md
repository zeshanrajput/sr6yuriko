## Royalties

Royalties is the amount an artist of this Status gets paid after Matrix distribution happens. It is paid three months after the performance ends. Payment is based on roughly an album’s worth (one hour) of music. Multiply or divide accordingly, based on length of performance. As above, a group performance does not increase payout. Pay is split between group members.
