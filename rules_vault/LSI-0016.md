## Necessities

Necessities cover those aspects of life every metahuman needs to survive, namely food, water, and shelter. Do you fend for yourself, searching through dumpsters for scraps, or do you have the means to get soystuff and more? Note that if you don’t spend two LP on the Living Expenses asset, you’re going to go hungry.
