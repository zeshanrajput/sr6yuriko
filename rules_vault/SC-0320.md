## Additional character creation recommendations:

- **Adept or Mystic Adept**

- **Attributes:** Agility 6+, Reaction 4+, Logic 1

- **Skills:** Athletics 3+, Firearms 6 (Specialization: Pistols), Stealth 3+

- **Knowledge skills:** Puns, Small Unit Tactics

- **Positive qualities:** Ambidextrous, Bravado, Shamanic Mask, Team Player

- **Negative qualities:** Astral Beacon, Combat Junkie, Distinctive Style, Killer, Twitchy

- **Life modules:** Adept Training, Bounty Hunter, Hunter, Martial Arts Training, Military, Shadowrunner, Event: You were overwhelmed, but things worked out

- **Adept powers:** Adrenaline Boost, Attribute Boost (Agility), Combat Sense (plus 1 free level from Eagle), Danger Sense, Enhanced Accuracy, Improved Ability (Firearms), Improved Reflexes
