## Obstacles

The first and most obvious problem is that if this MacGuffin works as intended, literally spawning wealth, why would anyone part with it? Even more suspect is, why would you sell something that makes money for money? That makes no sense and screams a catch at best, a scam at worst. That’s why the sale is the most important part of this con because doubt is unavoidably built in when trading theoretical infinite value for limited value.

This component is less of an issue when passing off something other than a Nuyen Box, but in some cases that’s a whole other con, and in others it’s just an extra step from the infinite value flaw.
