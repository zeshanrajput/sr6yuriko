## Vault Raiding

That trip we opened a further three vaults—we each chose one and then worked on the puzzles together. In one of the vaults, we found a spirit protecting the scroll case, the very same spirit that was pictured on the scroll alongside its true name. I decided to keep that one, as the spirit we’d fought guarding the vault had some disturbing characteristics and qualities (that made me regret taking my underage daughter with me on this quest). If you find the scroll case yourself, you’ll see it now says “destrui” on the scroll. Some things are abominations that cannot be allowed to continue existing—things like bugs and blood spirits and shedim. This spirit was definitely in that category, as far as I’m concerned.

- That hunt was a fun field trip. It made up one hundred percent of the mark for those who volunteered out of one of the advanced postgraduate combat magic courses. We stood watch as a score of second-year postgrad students went ham on the wretched thing.

- Exile

- It had figured out how to get to our plane after we opened that vault. The class learned two valuable lessons from that hunt: take responsibility for your actions, and some things are too dangerous to allow to live.
