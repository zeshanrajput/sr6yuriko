## Aztechnology

Currently, Aztechnology is one of the most involved with Dis. As a result, Aztechnology magical research has outpaced most of the other Big Ten over the last fifteen years. There are signs of strain, however. With the breach of their data vaults, the Disian known as the Auditor recently decided to make an example of Aztechnology and forced them to pay a penance by sacrificing one of their most powerful blood mages in one of the “Devices.” This angered many people within the Aztechnology magical ranks, primarily Director Salazar of Special Projects. But how this will ultimately



play out remains to be seen, as President de la Rosa is still considered a Dis ally.

- Don’t forget Aztechnology still controls Antarctica, which may be key to Dis’ plans.
