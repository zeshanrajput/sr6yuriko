## playing Advice

The cyber ninja is not a tank happy to absorb a ton of incoming damage or a spray-and-pray shooter who fills the air with lead. They are about speed, precision, and silence. They frequently spend more time setting up the right combat position than actually fighting, because their entire goal is to get in a spot where they can end the fight quickly. They know the right weapon to use in any given situation, and they build a plan around how best to use it.

The physical abilities of the cyber ninja often get a lot of focus, with good reason. They are blindingly fast and smoothly graceful. The put a premium of good aim so that they can land their blows (or shots) where they want. They are often not the strongest fighter around, but they don’t need to be. Their speed and precision more than make up for any relative lack of strength.

Their secret, though, is their planning. Cyber ninjas are dedicated to knowing every entrance and exit of any place they’re going to. The more they know, the easier it is to adjust when things go sideways. Some of them plan specific movements so that their attacks are closer to choreographed ballet than violent combat.

Don’t make the mistake of thinking that this planning means the cyber ninja can’t improvise. Sometimes the point of having a plan is to provide a structure from which you can diverge. Take a few steps away from the planned path, execute some alternate actions based on changing circumstances, then find your way back to your pre-planned path.

If the cyber ninja is to accomplish this level of planning, they need good legwork. Hackers and conjurers can help develop a picture of where they’ll be going, but the cyber ninja tends to only be happy with observations gleaned from legwork when they have done some of the observing themselves. They like to know not just how a place looks, but how it feels when you’re there.

Cyber ninjas also tend to be good trackers, because sometimes they need to trail people to a place where they are trying to not be found. This is about more than just following someone’s footprints (though it can be that). It’s about understanding the movement patterns of a particular place, the tendencies of their quarry, and how those two things combined can predict a course they should follow. To provide extra help, many cyber ninjas have hacker contacts who can get them into databases where they can find critical info on their targets and use it to track them.

Their planning, combat, tracking and stealth abilities mean the cyber ninja is rarely at a loss of things to do on a run. If there’s no one to follow or nothing to sneak by, there usually is someone who needs to be punched, so the cyber ninja can help there. But really, if a team is not using the full range of abilities of the cyber ninja, why have them on the team? If you have them, set them up to be their sneaky, speedy best.
