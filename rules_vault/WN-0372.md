## Hotspot

## Commerzbank Café

This setting looks slick and modern, with contoured plastic seating and designer lighting. It’s bright, friendly, and vacant, because no one likes to have soykaf while an AR program gently asks if they’ve heard about the latest interest rates. There are two hosts here, a rating 6 host to run the ads and a rating 9 host for financial data. A remote spider (use Seal Team 6 Counter-Electronics Commando, p. 209, _SR6_ ) watches over six different Commerzbank Cafés in the area.
