## Off Road Chassis

Sometimes you just need a vehicle that is built to go where the roads don’t. Move one point of on-road handling to off-road. Example: A base truck has a Handling of 4, so an off-road truck has a handling of 5/3, but reduces Top Speed by 10. Cost: 5
