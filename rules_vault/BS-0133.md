## Additional Cyberarms

Standard cyberarms are built to mimic natural biological function, but there are also limbs designed to push the body beyond what evolution designed. Additional cyberarms do not replace the user’s natural arms but reinforce the torso to accept more mechanical arms than standard for their metatype.
