## Pine

(Noupep, Clarity)

- **Vector:** Ingested

- **Speed:** 1D6 combat rounds

- **Duration:** 10 x 1D6 minutes

- **Power:** 11

- **Effect:** –1 Reaction, –1 Strength, –2 Willpower, +4 Logic, +3 Intuition

Pine is typically sold as a stick or pine needle that is coated in a bitter resin. Users of the drug have reported feeling lethargy in their muscles but have shown increased scores for mental clarity and agility. Based on phenylpiracetam and bacopa monnieri, which both have a longer duration, this potent derivative has a short time in the body and is a favorite for deckers who measure time in picoseconds. Once the drug wears off, the user resists (Power)S damage and gains the Off-Balance (p. 106, _Firing Squad_ ) status for the same duration as the high.
