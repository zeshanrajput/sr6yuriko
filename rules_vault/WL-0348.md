## **Tanuki**

## **(Nyctereutes procyonoides)**

**In Japanese folklore, tanuki are said to have supernatural abilities. Because of their mythical ability to attract luck and fortune, tanuki are frequently found around bars, restaurants, and stores. For some reason, the Japanese have always seemed to fnd them irresistible. Tanuki spread worldwide due to fur trades, but the rest of the world considers them nuisances and pests. By 2056, the rest of the world considered the tanuki to be a harmful and invasive species, where they could be hunted openly and without limit. They are still hunted in Japan, but only when they are damaging crops.**
