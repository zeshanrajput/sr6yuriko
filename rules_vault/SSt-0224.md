## **Pay Your Taxes**

**Speaking of things to lull you to sleep: taxes. I could go onto a whole thing about how taxes are the cost we pay for living in a society or whatever, but also can fuel all sorts of fraud, but let’s get to a point closer to the theme of this post: Paying taxes keeps people off your back. If Al Capone went down for tax fraud, so can you. Don’t give ’em an excuse. Enough said.**
