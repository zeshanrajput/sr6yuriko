## Firn

## (Water, Force 4)

Firns are squat, blue-tinted creatures, like cubes of crushed ice piled into a somewhat humanoid shape. Limbs are not designated as arms or legs, nor are they required to bend a certain way. This gives them an advantage in working in various conditions. Firns are the architects of the various ice structures and steam generators for the cloud palaces. As part of the middle caste of water elementals, they know how to take and give orders.
