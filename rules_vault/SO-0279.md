## Game Information

Casual wear provides no bonus or penalty to Social Rating. It’s provided with a lifestyle category of Low or better.

Casual wear has Capacity 2 for fashion accessories.


## Game Information

Business casual clothing provides a point of Edge on Con or Stealth tests to blend in within a corporate environment. It’s provided with a lifestyle category of Medium or better.

Business casual has two capacity for fashion accessories.
