## **Spell Generator**

**While the magic used by the native Disians is Essence-based and is unable to affect non-living material, the magic used by many of their subjugated servants has no such limitations. Spell generators are small devices physically similar to a mana battery. They are designed to capture, hold, and recast a spell initially cast by a non-Disian. These devices operate in a way similar to the Refection metamagic, but they require a willing initial caster. This gives Disians limited access to magic that can produce physical effects.**

**Typical spells stored in a generator include physical illusions such as Improved Invisibility, physical manipulation spells like Physical Barrier or Shape Stone, and of course physical combat spells like Lightning Bolt that can damage drones and low-Essence street samurai—the sorts of things normally mostly unaffected by Disian magic.**
