## Code Eco

As a modern hacker/hacktivist, Code Eco (pronounced like “kodeko”) had erased almost all of his past. Several weeks prior to the start of this mission, his life was cut short in a random act of street violence (or at least, that’s what the perpetrators wanted it to look like). He was an elf and lived all over the world. He was well-known and well-liked by the Lagos community when he came down after meeting Eniola Okoro at university.

He was working as a runner in Lagos, usually contracting with out-of-town talent that needed a hacker or local talent when going after ecological offenders. Most local runners appreciated him because he took his normal cut and always shared any paydata with his team.

Before his death, Code Eco had been on the radar of several local powers for his connection to Okoro after her sudden influx of cash. He is still a good source, but finding him is going to be a little more challenging, as after his death he went to Tamanous as parts and ghoul chow. Before that, though, they stripped him of his cyberware, including his headware, which has some solid paydata on a locked drive. Sadly, it’s currently in the possession of Tamanous. A local cell leader has been working to unlock it.
