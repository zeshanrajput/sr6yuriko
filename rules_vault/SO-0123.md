## Scaling

Size-wise, scale is just that: scale. At its smallest, you’re a stringer of a date or picking up Cubans for a friend and you collect too much cash or a free ritzy dinner. At the high-end, you’re peddling slowcooked devil rats to high-society connoisseurs as, I dunno, roast orphan or some other nonsense they get into. However it dices, the point is making where it’s more trouble than it’s worth for them to protest. You can do that from street corners or skyraker boardrooms. And if anything, it’s the cornerstone of modern capitalism.
