## Who to Talk to

After the “where” comes the “who,” as in who you are going to get your information from. Contacts don’t just happen; they take time and effort to cultivate. But in this biz, there are some solid places to start. Gangs, companies, and organizations are going to look strong from the outside, but they’re still run by individuals who have their own desires and vices. And who and what they’re part of will determine the best way to approach them.
