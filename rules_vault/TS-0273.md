## **Nissan “Johnny” Patroller**

**Marketed as an upgrade to the Doberman, the Nissan Patroller is a relatively cheap and generic patrol drone. Its enhanced sensor package has certainly earned it a place in the LESP industry, where it makes more sense to patrol an area with a drone rather than invest in metahuman training. The initial release in the CAS, combined with its uninventive name by Nissan, earned it the Johnny moniker.**
