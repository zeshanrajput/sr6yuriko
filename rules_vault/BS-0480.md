## Enhanced Movement

This is an additional accessory that is available as an option for augmented cyberlimbs for critters.

**Game Information:** Each rating of enhanced movement takes up 1 capacity and adds +1 meter to walking speed and +2 meters to running speed.



When a critter with this accessory takes the Sprint action, they gain a bonus to their Athletics dice pool equal to the level of the enhanced movement accessory. This accessory can be applied to cyberwings, in which case it increases flight speed instead of walking and running speed, providing the same bonuses to movement speeds as the groundbased equivalent. A critter with an aquatic cybertail improves its swimming speed with the same bonuses as the land equivalent.
