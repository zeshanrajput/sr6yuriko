## **Northru p Thunderhawk**

**When Lone Star requested proposals for a new air patrol craft, Northrup rose to the challenge. The Thunderhawk is a helicopter that offers more versatility than the Wasp but is still not a gunship like the Yellowjacket or Hornet. The Thunderhawk can be outftted with customizable options that allow it to be assigned to specifc unit roles, and its base model functions well as a general-purpose patrol and surveillance craft.**
