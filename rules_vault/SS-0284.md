## inside info

## Resonance Exploration

**Location:** DeeCee

Mr. Johnson is a relic hunter who learned too much while looking for a Sioux artifact, a ghost shirt embued with protective magic. Specifically, he learned that one of the people interested in the shirt was a member of both the Sioux Nation’s Council of Chiefs and the Black Lodge. The Lodge does not like their secrets exposed, so they want to take out the relic hunter before he can tell anyone else.
