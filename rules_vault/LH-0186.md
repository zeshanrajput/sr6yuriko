## **The Eclipse**

All the major arcana are valued at six-digit prices at the very least, but this card (depicting the Sun from a traditional tarot obscured in shadow) has been driven up to a full million nuyen. I’m not sure why this card is valued so highly. The last sighting was a year ago, when it was wagered in a high stakes game at the Sky Casino outside Kuala Lumpur. Those involved were anonymous, but it’s likely management still kept records.
