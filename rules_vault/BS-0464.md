## Special Training and Commands

Some augmentations or specific behaviors require a critter to receive special training. This requires one week of effort and a successful Training test. A critter may acquire a number of instances of special training equal to their Intuition x 2.

Training a critter to perform an action upon command requires a successful Training test and one week of effort. When teaching a critter a command, you must specify an action and a trigger. The trigger may be any stimuli that the critter is capable of perceiving, such as a spoken word, a gesture, or even an AR object. Whatever form it takes, triggering a command takes a Minor Action. A critter may learn a number of commands equal to their Logic x 2.
