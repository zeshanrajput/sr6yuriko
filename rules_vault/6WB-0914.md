## drone combat example

Emu has a roto-drone with an HK-227 mounted on it, providing backup for her escape. As she dashes out of a corporate research facility, contraband in hand, she takes a Command Drone action to order the drone to fire on a pursuing guard. The drone is at medium range to the guard, giving it an Attack Rating of 8. The guard has a Defense Rating of 6, so no Edge is given out at the start of the attack. The drone is being controlled remotely, so it fires with its HK-227 Targeting autosoft 3 + Sensor rating 3, meaning that it rolls 6 dice. The defender rolls 7, so the odds are against it. The drone rolls 2 hits while the defender rolls 3. The drone misses, but the bullets whiz by close enough to make the guard think about whether he really wants to continue this pursuit.
