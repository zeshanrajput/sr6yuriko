## The Immortal Flower

Every two years, Tandorans from all over the island gather at the edge of the Great Desert to search for the immortal flower. This incredibly rare flower is made even more rare due to a magical property that conceals it even when in full bloom, which only occurs once every two years on the island. The Tandoran healers use it to make rare and powerful medicines (including their equivalent of a trauma patch), but it can also be used to make an Awakened drug that makes PCP seem like a sugar high in comparison.
