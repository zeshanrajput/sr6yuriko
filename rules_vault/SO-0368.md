## Bad Cop

## \[Influence (Intimidation), Major\]: 4 Edge

To attempt this action, the target must be restrained or otherwise at someone’s mercy. Prior to attempting an interrogation, player characters can attempt to break the target’s will by convincing them they’re ruthless and cruel, and that unless they give up the information, things will go very badly for them. Make an Influence (Intimidation) + Charisma test opposed by the target’s Judge Intentions. If successful, player characters can reduce the target’s Willpower by one (to a minimum of 1). This reduction of Willpower lasts for one minute, plus one minute per net hit. Multiple Bad Cop actions extend the duration by one minute per net hit but don’t further reduce Willpower.
