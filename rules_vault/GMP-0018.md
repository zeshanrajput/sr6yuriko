## **Astral Beacon** 

Mages can have it. Mundanes can have it. This definitely will have an impact on the runs for any of the astral security. It makes it easier for them to track and follow the runner team. Fun time! For the exact list of game effects, see pg. 75, _6WE_ . 

For the purpose of teams wanting to sneak or hide, the character will always need to roll Stealth twice. For the physical space, they can roll trained if they are trained in it. But they will always roll Stealth untrained for astral sneaking. So Hardwire the decker has Astral Beacon and is trying to sneak into the corp facility. They have 4 ranks in Stealth and 4 in Agility. They will need to roll both trained (8 dice) and untrained (3 dice) to keep hidden.
