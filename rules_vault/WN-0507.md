## Resculpt

This ability allows the Monad to change their skin color, voice, and facial characteristics. Usually used to make a permanent change, the ability can be used on the fly as a disguise, but it doesn’t change hair color, though the cut can be modified shorter (but not longer). The ability can’t modify body size.

Monads can use a series of Minor Actions to make an Intelligence + Con (16, 1 Combat Round) Extended test to change their facial appearance and skin tone. The character then gets +2 dice on any Disguise (Con + Charisma) tests.
