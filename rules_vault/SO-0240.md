## Nudge

## (Manipulation)

Similar to Control Thoughts but more subtle and less controlling, this puts the spellcaster inside

the head of the target, making them very susceptible to what the spellcaster suggests. Roll Sorcery + Magic vs. Willpower + Logic; net hits give the maximum duration of the spell in minutes.

Unlike Control Thoughts, the target’ is often unaware that this spell was used on them. At the gamemaster’s discretion, the target can make a Logic + Intuition test after the spell wears off to recognize the spell’s effect on them with a threshold based on how different from normal their thoughts were made to be.

Nudge is also less controlling than Control Thoughts. Instead of absolute control of the target’s thoughts, the spellcaster gains +1 to all Social skill tests per net hit, to a max of +4 while the effect lasts. The target is also prevented from spending Edge to resist these tests.
