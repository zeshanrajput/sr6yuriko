## inside info

Corporal Patton returned with significant alterations (see options on p. 141). Opportunistic runners in Motor City have seen his potential as one of them, and they’ve been helping hide him. A number of parties are seeking him besides military intelligence—the Mafia, Evo, the DIMR, the Black Lodge, and possibly more. Finding him will be difficult enough; keeping him will be even harder.
