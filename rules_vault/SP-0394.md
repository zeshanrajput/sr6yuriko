## **Chain-Glaive**

**Essentially a chain-sword with the standard hilt replaced with a one-meter-long shaft, allowing greater force to be applied in an attack. This makes the overall length two meters and requires two hands to wield.**

**Vibro-weapons are powered with the same removable power cells as chain weapons.**
