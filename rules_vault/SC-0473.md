## Ja’nae Collinwood

Raised as an orphan and destined for a hard life until she Awakened, Collinwood fell in love with magic because it saved her. She feels her continual learning is a way of giving back to it.

Academic Mage

**GEAR:** Armor jacket, contacts (flare comp, image link), micro-transceiver, reagents(100), telescoping staff **SPELLS:** Clout, detect life, fireball, heal, increase reflexes, overclock, physical barrier, trid phantasm

**QUALITIES:** Focused concentration, spell components, talismonger’s assistance/assistant
