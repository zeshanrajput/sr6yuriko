## OmniStar

While Lone Star, DocWagon, and Manadyne are not individually known for their specific tech departments, when the three combined, assets from each of their internal Matrix security departments formed a new Matrix security division. If they have any internal efforts on the trail, they’ll come from OM Systems (OmniStar Matrix). They’ll still be backed by extraterritoriality, and you can guarantee they’ll slip on the disguises of their subsidiaries in order to get to places they don’t belong.

- OmniStar is developing quite the reputation for corporate shadow ops using the cover and access provided by their various parts. I know several runners who have sold out to the man in order to get to play under their umbrella.

- Balladeer

If they get their hands on it, there’s no telling what they’ll do, but you can guarantee it’s going to add to their internal strife before it gets put to anything beneficial for the massive AA. They have plenty of opposition across their diverse markets to hit, and when they do, you can guarantee they’ll have shadow assets making noise for them in order to demonstrate why their stock values are weakening and why a new security, health services, or arcane services provider should be chosen. I don’t see OmniStar knocking out competitors completely—they need someone to take the low-profit contracts—but I definitely see them manipulating things in order to pull more high-value contracts their way.

- The OmniStar name is helping them on the global markets. Lone Star, DocWagon, and Manadyne all have strong North American roots that chase off some international clients, but OmniStar is a fresh(ish) face. Despite the megacorp forming in order to protect the ratings of its three primary subsidiaries, it owns a lot more, and even though they initially said the overarching corp was going to be just a paper umbrella, the OmniStar brand is starting to get used more and more.

- Orbital DK

- As if three major factions weren’t enough, a fourth has formed with allegiances to the umbrella. Their main focus appears to be peacekeeping between the other factions on the surface, but behind the scenes they’re promoting the parent name and developing divisions, new subsidiaries, and unique OmniStarbranded lines.
