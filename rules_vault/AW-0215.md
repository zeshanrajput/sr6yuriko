## Game Information Disians

Disians represent a tremendous threat to the Sixth World. Their ultimate goal is to strip the Sixth World of all its magical energy (mana), leaving it a dead wasteland. To accomplish this, they have spent decades making alliances and placing agents in key positions of power to facilitate their goal.
