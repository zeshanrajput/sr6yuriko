## New Bioware for Critters Hound’s Nose

The critter’s olfactory senses are improved, both by adding sensitivity to its olfactory receptors and by enhancing the synapses that process olfactory information.

**Game Information:** The critter gains the Enhanced Senses (Smell) critter power. This provides the critter with a point of Edge on Perception tests that use their sense of smell; that point must be used on the test or is lost. If the critter normally does not have an Edge attribute, the Edge is used to re-roll a single failed die on the test.
