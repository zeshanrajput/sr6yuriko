## Wudu’aku

- **Vector:** Ingestion, Inhalation

- **Speed:** 2D6 minutes

- **Duration:** 1D6 hours

- **Power:** 7

- **Effect:** Gain situational Edge on Conjuring tests for spirits of kin



Using powdered fossils from the Australian Outback, this drug aids in the summoning of kindred spirits. Once ingested, along with the listed benefit, non-kindred spirits gain a point of Edge to resist summoning attempts by the user for twenty-four hours.
