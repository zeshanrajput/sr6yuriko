## Kingdoms of Nigeria

Always active in trade, the various kingdoms have many global deals they need to make, and they use CI as a contact point for other important nations. It helps that they really enjoy the nightlife in Seattle, and when they come to town, they slip over the bridge and find plenty of activity in the city proper.
