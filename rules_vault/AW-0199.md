## The Disian “Matrix,” by Prof. Hanna Pierce

A recent find in the AZS files is a report from the Special Projects Division director Armando Salazar.

According to this report, Dis didn’t have a data-storage system analogous to our Matrix. This was perplexing, because how can such an advanced civilization have no advanced data storage? Turns out they do.

The Disians use “memory crystals.” In layman’s terms, actual memories are imprinted on these crystals along with the data. Some require the user to know and generate a specific harmonic or energy frequency in order to access said crystal. Likewise, with the right frequency, multiple crystals can be linked, with information transferred and their (for the lack of another term) processing abilities combined.

If this is true, it represents a quantum leap in technological processing ability and a foundation from which to merge technology and magic.

Damn. I’m starting to see why some people would side with Dis …

As the Big Ten are the predominant powers in the Sixth World, it’s little surprise that Dis has cultivated allies among the corporations. Data is still forthcoming, but this is what is currently known.
