## Manufacturing Your Fix

The billions of nuyen poured into research by the pharmaceutical companies for drug formulation, testing, and production can somewhat be replicated in your home pharmaforge. That is, if you’re ready to deal with some potentially deadly side effects, weird durations, and other things that come with little time to research and good-old guess and check. To get started, you need to have the right skills and the right equipment to create drugs, toxins, BTLs, and ripjacks.
