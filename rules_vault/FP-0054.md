## **ELIS**

**With at least one of the major card prophesies involving the monads, having one member join their ranks can’t be surprising. The surprising part is that ELIS was a hardliner. They wanted their kind to pull the trigger on the Thor shots they controlled. From everything I’ve discovered, they’re now all about fnding a way to use the tarot to make that a reality. They aren’t the wealthiest of the Echoes, but they have an extensive network of close monad allies to call upon in the hunt for cards.**
