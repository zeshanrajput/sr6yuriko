## Allies and Enemies

## Enemies The Cops

It should go without saying, but the number-one enemy in Downtown is law enforcement. Downtown is where the big money is and, moreover, where the most visibility is. They make more money patrolling the homes of the wealthy elite in Bellevue, but screwups there stay quiet. Screw up in downtown, and the governor will lose her _mind_ . The Star will call out the heavies and you _will_ go down, full stop. Downtown can’t risk high-speed car chases or tank-like drones unloading heavy ordnance, so all vehicles here have to be blaring their VIN at all times, locking into the assorted Guides, and giving override permission to law enforcement, who will use it to stop you dead in your tracks ... at least during the day. At night, the rules relax a bit (the corps need runners to get around and do missions, after all), but big, loud exchanges of firepower get shut down _hard_ . You want to unload chainguns and mini-rockets? Take that drek to Redmond.
