## Negative Quality: Permanent Damage

Your connection between your body and Essence is incomplete or damaged. This quality cannot be taken by a runner who has 6 Essence. This quality can only be bought off when a runner has 6 Essence. This quality can be taken after character creation when Essence is lost with the agreement of the GM.
