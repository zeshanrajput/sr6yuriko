## Australia: Sigh of Relief

While the rest of the world freaked out during the Un-Awakening, Australia considered it a godsend. For the first time in decades, all manastorms ceased, allowing rescuers and aid to reach the embittered continent. Hundreds of cargo and transport aircraft swarmed the continent in a way not seen since Berlin in 1948. At the same time, all nearby ships offered as much assistance as they could. Communications were also re-established, and while exact casualty figures are still being calculated, the numbers are far below estimates.

While the reprieve was brief and the storms started up again when magic returned after the Un-Awakening, they were noticeably different in both their intensity and frequency. No longer did they swiftly ravage the countryside, but instead they moved along with the pace of a more traditional storm, which will allow locals to find shelter much more quickly. Overall, the continent and its people are recovering, but it will take years, if not decades, to fully recover.
