## Horizon Africa

Usually all about the PR, Horizon Africa is one of the few organizations that actually gets their hands dirty—sometimes literally. They are part of the production operation for Horizon-branded beauty products, electronics, clothing, and “health” foods. All of these items are made around Africa under the supervision of this division. Horizon is not as large as some of their AAA counterparts and thus do not have near as many facilities or the ability to defer costs through bulk production. Instead, they defer costs by doing things cheaply. In Lagos, that cheap production means cheap labor and dirty resource acquisition. This puts them in the crosshairs

of Eniola Okoro. They are particularly vulnerable as a smaller operation, and they believe the threat of Okoro is quite real. They need to find out how she gained so much influence so quickly. While some of the other corporations are looking into this in order to find out if she did anything illegal and bring her down that way, Horizon (and their Dawkins Group operative, Geoffrey Dakins) just wants to know who she is connected to and whether removing her from the picture will solve their problem or bring more trouble down on them.
