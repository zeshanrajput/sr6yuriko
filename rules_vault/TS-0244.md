## **External Injector Rig**

**For those who do not have/want auto-injectors, or need access to more drugs than they can store, the external injector rig is the device for them. Worn around the thigh or torso (determined when purchased), the injector rig has four reservoirs, and each substance can be injected at the push of a button, or if wirelessly enabled, with a remote Matrix signal. Each reservoir can hold Rating doses of that substance, though only one dose can be administered at a time. An access port is needed to use the injector rig. Creating one is a Logic + Biotech (Medicine) (5, 1 hour) Extended test, or an appropriate contact can perform the procedure for 500¥.**
