## **Do: Facilitate Connections**

**This is a great way to build goodwill without costing you much. Get in the habit of reviewing who you know and what they’re good at whenever someone expresses a need. Did someone’s car break down? See if they need a connection to your rigger friend. The old lady across the street feeling unsafe? Introduce her to some big people who might be able to hang out at her place for a bit. Friendly neighborhood gang boss get hacked? Refer them to a quality hacker. Both sides beneft from the introduction, both sides will be grateful, and all it cost you was dropping a name.**
