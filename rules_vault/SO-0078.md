## Shake It (I)

You know how to move your body and give just the right facial expression that seems to promise more good things to come. Spend a Major Action and make a Con (Seduction) + Charisma (3) test. On a success, those observing you gain a +2 dice pool bonus to Perception tests against you but take a –2 dice pool penalty on Perception tests against others in the vicinity as you hold their attention.
