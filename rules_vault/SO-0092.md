## Obstacles

Passcodes tend to change regularly, and anomalous activity is monitored by the powers that be for the exact unauthorized activity you’re attempting. Generally speaking, the clock is ticking on your access, so use it quickly and ditch it before investigators catch up.

People have a natural tendency to hesitate before giving over their authorizations, so play up the professional angle, maybe add a hint of boredom. It should be routine for you. The more casual it is for you, the more they’ll buy that this is legit.
