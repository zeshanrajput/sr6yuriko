## Incorporated Islands

When Manhattan sued to formally declare itself a city, the MDC members also moved to buy up the islands surrounding their new city. Six localized islands are officially part of NYC or the wholly extraterritorial property of major corporations. The other nearby islands belong to the cities surrounding New York.
