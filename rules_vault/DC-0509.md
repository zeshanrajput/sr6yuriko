## Junkyard Frankenstein (x0.5 BP)

The opposite of the one-off custom but yet just as unique, the junkyard frankenstein is rarely the prettiest girl at the ball but still very memorable. Built from whatever leftover scraps you can find this vehicle will cost you less but function just as well if you know what you’re doing. Cost Multiplier: x0.5
