## Neighborhood

This is the area that your neighbors and you live in, and the type of place that attracts certain types of people. It is also an indication of police contractor presence, along with response requirements, time to respond, or even if they respond in the first place. Also, for most places, “Neighborhood Watch” means folks are using sensors on the outside of their buildings to keep track of the firefight going on in the street while they lie flat on the floor and try not to catch a stray bullet. Security Ratings are detailed on p. 239, _SR6_ .
