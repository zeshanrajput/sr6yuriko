The Condor is a simple two-man helicopter with openable windows and an upgraded sensor suite to allow all manner of camera updates. Ordinarily, it’s crewed by one pilot, while the co-pilot’s job is simply to keep an eye on what’s going on while shouting into a mic on live TV. Not a combat-worthy machine by any stretch, most corporate facilities tolerate the buzzing gnats in their airspace for a few minutes before waving them off.
