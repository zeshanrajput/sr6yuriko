## Combat Commander

When the drek hits the fan, you rise to the challenge and coordinate your team with tactical precision.

**Cost:** 8 Karma

Game Effect: You must have the Small Unit Tactics knowledge skill to take this quality.

You may use Influence (Leadership) + Charisma as your dice pool when leading combat maneuvers instead of Logic. Additionally, leading and participating in a combat maneuver only requires a Minor Action—even if the other participants don’t have the Small Unit Tactics knowledge skill.

If you successfully lead a combat maneuver (p. 86, Firing Squad), choose one of the following additional benefits for every two hits scored on the maneuver test. These are applied to all characters participating in the combat maneuver. If you get enough hits to receive multiple bonuses (four, six, etc.), you can choose multiple options or stack the same benefit multiple times.



+1 to Initiative score

+1 to Defense Rating

+1 to Attack Rating

These bonuses apply until the end of the scene, the next time Initiative is rolled, when the combat maneuver is no longer in effect, or when a new combat maneuver is initiated (whichever comes first). Multiple uses of Combat Commander do not stack. The team receives bonus Edge if they exceed the maneuver threshold by 2 or more, in addition to the bonuses provided by this quality.
