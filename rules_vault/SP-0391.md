**Notes : The chain-sword and chain-glaive use the same basic rules as a chainblade with the following exceptions. A chainblade is always a single-handed weapon.**

**A chain-sword must be used two-handed unless the user has Strength 3 or higher; then it may be used single-handed. A chain-glaive can only be used as a two-handed weapon.**

**Powering up or turning of a chain weapon is a Minor Action.**

**If an unpowered chain weapon is used in melee, reduce the profle by -2 DV and -2 AR. On a glitch, the chain becomes jammed and stops rotating, with the same result as if the weapon was unpowered. A jam requires an Engineering (Armorer) + Logic (3) test to clear.**
