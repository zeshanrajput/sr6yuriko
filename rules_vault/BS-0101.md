## Single Cybereye

Cybereye basic systems (p. 285, _SR6_ ) are normally sold and implanted in pairs, but not everyone wants to replace a perfectly good eye just because the other one was diseased or damaged. A single eye can be replaced for one half the Essence and nuyen cost, leaving the other biological eye intact. A single cybereye only has one half the capacity of a matched pair.
