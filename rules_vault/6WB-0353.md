## Face

Most faces hate being called a face, because they are so much more than that. They deploy personality, charm, wit, humor, well-timed compliments, stern reproaches, and a million other tactics selected for the situation to get their desired outcome. Not everyone falls for a pretty face, especially one with tusks. But a bit of humor or a subtle reminder about stereotypes can go a long way to allow the face to get exactly what they want without ever firing a shot or even making a threat of violence. They still might break out a threat from time to time, and when they do, they love to make it with authority they don’t always have. They know that a little attitude, applied properly, goes a long way.
