## Limb Dropping

Autotomy is the technical term for self-amputation and describes the behavior that some critters employ as a survival tactic. Just as many lizards can detach their tail to escape from a predator, a martial artist with a cyberlimb can sacrifice the metal to preserve their meat. When suffering damage from an attack or indirect combat spell, this technique allows the user to forfeit the damage resistance test to ignore all damage entirely by intercepting the hit with their cyberlimb. The limb becomes nonfunctional and must be repaired using the Biotech (Cybertechnology) skill before it can be used again.
