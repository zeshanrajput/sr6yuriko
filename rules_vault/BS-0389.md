## Red Mescaline

(Manashrooms, Vertigo)

- **Vector:** Ingested

- **Speed:** 1 hour

- **Duration:** (18 – Body) hours, minimum 1 hour

- **Power:** 8

- **Effect:** –2 Reaction, +1 Willpower, +1 Charisma, Confused 4

Red mescaline is a powerful psychoactive substance ten times more potent than naturally occurring peyote. This drug is popular with musicians, technicians, and magicians for its hallucinogenic properties. Redmesc users compare the trip to a religious experience, suffused in a god-like heightened awareness. The amazing highs are brought low when the drug wears off, and the user suffers –1 Willpower and –1 Charisma for the same duration as the high.
