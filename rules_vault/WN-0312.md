## Ahma Yoshida

Ahma Yoshida is here to serve two purposes: First, to protect the interests of the Nagato Corporation in their dealings with Evo. Second, to represent the Yakuza in pursuit of an alliance against MCT following their ouster of the Four Oyabun.

Yoshida formally trained with the Green Serpent Guard in her youth before it was discovered her talents were wasted on security work. For a time, she ran the shadows as a Yakuza assassin by the name of Rain until she was sent to Japan to study to be komon for the Nagato Combine. As her talents matured, she became invaluable to the corporation as a bridge between their formal business interests and the interests of the Yakuza. Now she serves as Nagato’s representative on the Corporate Council. She’s a talented fixer and mage, a pale Japanese elf with long black hair. She dresses primarily in black business attire. Use Tir Ghost (p. 209, _SR6_ ) for her statistics.
