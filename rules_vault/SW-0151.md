## Designing a Spell

The structure of spells is based on the spell type, with each spell ingredient adding different effectsto what the spell does. The ingredients bring a set amount of drain to the spell—the more ingredients are chosen, the higher the drain will be. Once the ingredients are selected, players and gamemasters should work together to define the full effects of the spell, including the function of net hits on the Sorcery test.

That’s the basic process—now let’s break it down step by step.
