## Swimming

The base swimming speed for characters is 3 meters per combat round with a Minor Action (gear and augmentations may increase this). By making a Swimming test, which is Athletics + Agility (2), you add (net hits/2, rounded up) to the distance in meters. The threshold can be increased for choppy or otherwise difficult waters. Swimming is tiring, and the threshold should increase by 1 every fifteen minutes.
