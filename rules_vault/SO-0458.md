## Long Con Pool

You’ve spent time, effort, and resources studying your mark, building a profile, and getting inside their head.

**Cost:** 3 Karma per level (max level 2–for now) **Game Effect:** To gain this quality, you must choose a mark (the target of your long con) and acquire a specific Knowledge skill that is all about the mark. This quality establishes a Con Pool—a special pool of Edge that can only be used to benefit social tests that target the mark. The maximum Edge that can be stored in this pool is equal to the level of this quality. This Edge pool doesn’t reset or refresh at the start of each session. When you first purchase this quality (and every time you increase its level), its Edge refreshes to its maximum. You can reload the Con Pool by giving up two points of your own Edge for every one point you wish to add to the pool, but you can only do so at the beginning of a session when your Edge refreshes. This represents your focus on this con, which may be distracting you from what’s right in front of you—a small price to pay for the advantage it will provide when you need it most.

Edge from the Long Con Pool may only be spent on Edge boosts and Edge actions to affect Con skill tests opposed by the mark.
