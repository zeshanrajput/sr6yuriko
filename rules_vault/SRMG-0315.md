## **If I buy Living Space, then I don’t need to pay for my lifestyle?**

Unfortunately, no. You still need to buy food, pay for incidentals like your trid subscriptions, and also docking or camping fees. Nothing is free in this life, chummer. Living in your vehicle rather than a structure still incurs the same monthly lifestyle cost.
