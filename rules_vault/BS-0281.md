## Hard vs. Soft Nanite Technology

Just as a refresher, hard nanites are nanomachines built from carbon or silicon lattice structures, while soft nanites are biological in nature and built as protein- and enzyme-based structures. When the industry was retooled to deal with the CFD threat, the overall control of nanite colonies was moved to the external devices, and simplified hard nanites became the rule for almost all applications. All of the nanite colonies and systems presented here use hard nanites.
