## The Kingdom of Hawai’i

The Yakuza have been invested here for over a century and have no intention of leaving. They’re a huge source of income, thanks to Japanese tourism, and the Yakuza seem content to use the islands more as a vacation spot than a base of operations. Mind you, they own every single oceanside hotel and casino, and they also own the entertainment industry from top to bottom, but they’ve never pushed to mold nation into “Japan East” like they have conquered lands.
