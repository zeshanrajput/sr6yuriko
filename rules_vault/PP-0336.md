## Mohammad Sudayrin

On the Global Sandstorm side of the merger, perhaps no one has elevated his profile as much as Mohammad Sudayrin. A financial wizard, Sudayrin added a ton of assets to his purview in the merger while also learning a few secrets. If anyone besides Johnny Spinrad knows what it took to pull off the buyouts Spinrad made to rapidly grow his company, it’s Sudayrin. He doesn’t seem to be the type who would use this knowledge directly against Spinrad for blackmail purposes, but he may well know which way the wind will be blowing—and as any financier can tell you, that is a considerable advantage.
