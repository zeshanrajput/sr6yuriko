## Krime “Arson” Rounds

Only available in shotgun shells—as other types of rounds lack the required volume to be effective—the arson round, made by Krime Industries, is a slug of jellied white phosphorus contained in a hard case that breaks open on impact, igniting the phosphorus as soon as it is exposed to the air and setting everything it touches on fire.

**Game Rules:** These shells have the same effect as Incendiary archery points, with the weapon gaining a -2 DV to its profile but the target gaining the Burning 1 status from a successful hit with a shell. Successful BF or FA attacks assume multiple shells hit and cause the Burning 2 status instead. Additional strikes on the same target in subsequent combat rounds increase the Burning status by 1 (or 2 for BF and FA), up to a maximum of Burning 6.

On a critical glitch, an arson round will detonate inside the weapon, causing the Burning 1 status for the wielder.
