## To xin Resistance

You are incredibly resistant to toxins. Each level of this power reduces the power of toxins by 1. Drugs have no effect on you, but you may choose to intentionally lower your resistance in order to be affected by them. While you are willingly under the infl uence, this power has no effect—even against toxins. Be careful what you wish for.
