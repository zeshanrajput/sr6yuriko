## Financial Basics

Money is core to any corporate fight, but there is also a central truth we need to keep in mind: Money is imaginary and has no intrinsic worth. People make as much money or more predicting and anticipating the flow of money as they do making and selling tangible objects. You can make good money by picking the right financial assets at the right time, but trying to succeed that way is a lot like trying to get rich by playing poker—your odds increase as you gain experience and knowledge, but they never rise to the point of becoming very likely.

The real way to gain wealth through financial tools is not to pick the magic stock for yourself, but to convince others you can make such a pick for them.

Let’s say you have 10,000 nuyen, and you invest it in a collection of stocks and bonds that net a seven percent return in a year. You would have earned 700 nuyen in that year. Good for you! But let’s instead say you started an investment fund for your friends and family and managed to get ten people to invest 10,000 nuyen each. They each get 700 nuyen, but you charge a one percent fee. There is 100,000 nuyen invested in the fund, so you get 1,000 nuyen, which is better than your 700 nuyen return if you invested your own money. Your investors only net 600 nuyen instead of 700 nuyen because of your fee, but they still come out well ahead of their starting position. Everyone is happy—and you earned more than they did. If the next year only brings them a four percent return, they net 300 nuyen apiece. And you still get 1,000.

These numbers only get larger in the real world. I spoke recently to a fund manager at an investment branch of the Frankfurt Bank Association. She was proud that she had beat returns of stock market index funds, giving her clients an eleven percent return—which, to be clear, is quite good. She made more than ten million nuyen from her clients that year. Only her clients with more than 90 million or so nuyen invested with her would have made as much as she did. How many people do you know that invest that much money?

If money is an illusion, then it flows to those who put on the most persuasive magic show.

With that established, we will move to discussing trading.
