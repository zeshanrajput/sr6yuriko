## Salamander

## (Fire, Force 4)

The salamander resembles a small, swirling ball of flame hovering and dancing in mid-air. It is about twelve centimeters in diameter, not much bigger than a fist. Sometimes it cloaks itself in diaphanous veils of flame or will transform its appearance to that of a normal salamander. Salamanders are known to travel to the material plane during natural disasters such as forest fires or large conflicts that include fire damage. These packs feed on the still-warm ash and charcoal before returning home. How they know how and where to travel makes them great companions when it comes to navigating the elemental planes. Salamanders can be found throughout the Plane of Fire. The sapient elementals call them nuisances or pests, as theywill find their way into storage or fuel tanks and quietly consume the contents.
