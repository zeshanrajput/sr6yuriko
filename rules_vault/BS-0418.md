## I See Dead Icons

- **Vector:** Injection

- **Speed:** 3 combat rounds

- **Duration:** Willpower hours, minimum 1 hour

- **Power:** 6

This drug infuses a hacker and their Matrix-connected device with Resonance. This allows the user and their equipment to perceive in the way that technomancers can. This provides +2 dice and a wild die for normal Matrix Perception checks and allows a non-technomancer to identify icons more easily with Resonance.

While this is active, the signature of the hacker is very noticeable. Each person or icon attempting to Trace Icon on you gains a point of Edge on each roll. The threshold to identify a Resonance being from a standard Matrix icon is reduced from 5 to 3.

When the effect ends, your Perception actions have –2 dice for the same period that your resonance vision was active for, and anyone attempting to deceive you receives Edge. When the effect ends, the Resonance built up in your deck and icon is suddenly discharged, disrupting the efforts of anyone attempting to Trace Icon or Snoop on you. Your Overwatch Score is also reduced to zero. You and your deck take unresisted damage. You take 2 Physical damage, and your device takes 2 Matrix damage.
