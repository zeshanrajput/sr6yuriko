**Patrol cars come in many shapes, sizes, and brands, with every manufacturer producing some variation or option. The Ford Stinger is just the most common example in the UCAS and can be found in almost any community in both marked and unmarked versions. Based on the Americar model that’s been outftted with a law enforcement equipment package and performance upgrades, the Stinger typically serves the role of general-purpose patrol and general-duty units.**
