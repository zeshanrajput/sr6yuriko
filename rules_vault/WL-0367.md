## **Exotic**

**Critters with the Dietary Requirement weakness (p. 229,** _**SR6**_ **) are among the most challenging to care for. If your lifestyle category is High or Luxury, the costs are covered under your lifestyle payment. At Medium lifestyle or lower, you must make special arrangements to feed your critter, which costs you (critter’s Body x 250) nuyen per month to keep them well-fed.**
