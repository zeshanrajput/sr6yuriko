## Augmentations

Tailored pheromones are a must. Every advantage to increase your Charisma should be your primary goal. Be aware of the pheromones’ limBUILDINGitations—the face fatale needs to be close to their target, and the mark shouldn’t be using a filtration or breathing apparatus. So meet your target on the beach, not after they’ve put on their scuba gear. The face fatale should be sure to get the best version they can afford and upgrade often.

Face fatales of the twentieth century had to rely heavily on natural beauty. The modern face fatale can pop down to a street doc and look totally different a few hours later. Nanopaste disguises can give quick and easy appearance changes to the face fatale, allowing them to look like whatever the job requires (+2 dice pool bonus to Con tests involving Disguise; cost: 500 nuyen, Availability 4).

In addition to tailored pheromones, some other cyber and bioware upgrades can be helpful. Voice modulation can allow the face fatale to easily adopt a voice that will be pleasing to the target. A simrig is a great tool for blackmail, either to get the job done or to ensure the face fatale’s safety after the job is done. A skilljack or skillwires can help sell the role when it comes to languages or adding a special skill to seduce the victim, such as becoming a skilled dancer for someone who loves to cut a rug. A toxin extractor is a good investment—keeping a level head when drinking (or indulging in whatever vice is best for the job) can be a lifesaver.
