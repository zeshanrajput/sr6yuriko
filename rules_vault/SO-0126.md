## Effects

Obviously giving away free tips isn’t going to get you anywhere by itself. After all, nothing stops them from just continuing to listen and place their own bets with their own bookie or broker. So, you need to offer something that puts their cred in your pocket. Offer better odds than they can find elsewhere, or tell them that while this one is free, many other picks are available, and this is simply one of the smallest ones as a free taste. Offer a better way at every step to profit from this knowledge. As you build trust through survivorship and selection bias, some of them will trust you enough to try for the big payoffs you promise. Pocket the cred then remove them from your list, continuing to work the survivors until you run out.
