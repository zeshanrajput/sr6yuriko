## Soul Splice

- **Vector:** Injection

- **Speed:** 2 combat rounds

- **Duration:** 1 hour

- **Power:** 11

One of the most demanded drugs around, this is a booster for technomancers. When this is injected into a technomancer, Resonance flows through the physical body and into the living persona. When this drug is active, the technomancer gains 1 point of Resonance and adds a wild die to every test they make while in the Matrix. Anyone who can identify that the technomancer’s icon is a living persona can see it pulsing, as if it can barely contain the amount of Resonance flowing through it. It’s not unusual for people using this drug to take on personality traits that they don’t normally have, hear voices that no one else can detect, or experience paranoia. The drug is a strong euphoric, and it’s easy to become addicted.

When the effect of the drug ends, the technomancer takes 3 points of unresisted Matrix damage and reduces their Resonance by half for six hours.

If more than one dose is taken at once, the Resonance of the technomancer increases by one for each additional unresisted dose, and for each additional dose taken, the technomancer takes 2 more points of unresisted Physical damage when the effect ends.

Several users have become convinced that theyare injecting stolen Resonance into them, and that the Resonance contains the souls of technomancers killed to make the drug. There is no evidence that this is true, but it’s a creepy thought.
