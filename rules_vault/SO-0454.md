## Teetotaler

Drugs are bad, okay? Don’t do drugs. **Bonus:** 5 Karma

**Game Effect:** Whenever you’re under the influence of a drug or toxin that you intentionally took, you may not gain or spend Edge. If you don’t partake while in social situations in which alcohol or drug use is expected, make an Influence or Con test (3)—if you fail, your standoffishness is highly noticeable and other characters gain a point of Edge when making social skill, Composure, or Judge Intentions tests that affect you.
