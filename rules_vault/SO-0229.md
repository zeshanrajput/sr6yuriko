## Playboi

Playbois are charismatic individuals who navigate the intricate webs of society with finesse … and magic. Their abilities, primarily centered around manipulation or illusion spells, empower them to influence minds, deceive adversaries, and forge alliances. Moreover, their expertise in social skills such as negotiation, etiquette, and persuasion amplifies there effectiveness in various situations.

It’s not uncommon for a playboi to employ a multitude of spells and magical techniques. Manipulation spells that allow them to control perceptions and emotions, even going so far as employing spells such as Control Thoughts to subtly sway the decisions of others. Another option is the use of detection spells to perceive and identify potential threats or allies. Having insight as to how others operate, the playboi will also have some skill to counter these types of social magical threats. They can also use those same skills in countering magical threats directed at their team. Or they can nullify enemy spells with counterspelling techniques to ensure the safety of their allies. Playbois can also create protective wards, safeguarding their team from hostile magic and spirits. Even the use of abilities such as astral perception is an asset that can be better leveraged by a mage face to read the mood of a client.

Like most other faces, a playboi typically possesses high charisma, allowing them to charm, persuade, and lead others effectively. Their magnetic personalities make them naturally liked, both within their teams and in social interactions with influential contacts. Mastering various etiquettes, from corporate to street, a playboi can magically alter their attire to navigate different social environments seamlessly.

For these same reasons, a playboi excels in negotiation employing their magical abilities and social skills to broker deals and resolve conflicts. Their ability to read people and auras helps themto anticipate others’ reactions and gives them a significant advantage in high-stakes negotiations, ensuring favorable outcomes for their teams.

It may seem like a playboi can do just about anything, but developing magical abilities and social skills requires significant investment in terms of time and resources. Playbois will have fewer resources to invest in equipment, making them rely heavily on their magical abilities or negotiation skills. They may also try to diversify too much and become more of a jack-of- all-trades type and not perform well in their originally intended face role.

Another disadvantage is often prioritizing mental attributes over physical ones, making them physically vulnerable compared to street samurai or riggers. They may lack combat skills and physical resilience, making them reliant on others for protection in dangerous situations.

Finally, the use of mind control and manipulation spells raises ethical concerns. Some might struggle with moral dilemmas, questioning the ethicality of their actions, especially when using spells to deceive others or influence them against their will. For a playboi, it’s just another day on the job.
