## Working Cattle

## Main Location: AGC-Siemensstadt

Notable Characters: Arbeiter Policlub Members (use Sons of Sauron Brute, p. 205), AG Chemie Guards (use Lone Star Patrolman, p. 206)

Berlin’s fall into anarchy provided excellent opportunities for chemical giant AG Chemie. With no need to comply with regulations on environmental protection, occupational safety, and building codes, AGC converted its sector into an industrial hellhole. And since human labor is cheaper than robots when paid only with chicken coop housing and canteen sludge, thousands of workers toil under slave-like conditions in the lightless tunnels of this decaying factory labyrinth.

One obstacle is that the union-like Arbeiter policlub occasionally disrupts the operations of AGC’s profit machine. The runners are to locate the Arbeiters’ meeting rooms hidden in the district. But once they find them—will they pass the info on to the AG Chemie guards, notorious for their brutality?

**Original Message:** Team wanted to investigate terrorist threats in the AGC Siemensstadt area. Expressions of interest are being accepted by Gunter Maasen of Plant Security at AGC Tower.
