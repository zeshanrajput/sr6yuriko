## Professional Rating 10: Elite Special Forces

Usually one of these guys is all it takes to handle most problems, but they still operate in teams. Here at the top, we find names like the Sioux Wildcats. Considered the best in the world, they combine intense training, fierce loyalty, precision discipline, and bat-drek insanity to make up the world’s most feared fighting force.
