## Armor Plating Module, Hardened

Hardened armor plating consists of thick and heavy metallic plates that somewhat resemble a

high-tech version of medieval plate armor. It might look a little strange, but it’s also very effective— and highly intimidating.

**Game Information:** A hardened armor plating module provides a bonus to DR equal to its rating, which stacks with all other forms of protection. Additionally, the DR increase counts as hardened armor, per the critter power (p. 224, _SR6_ ).

The hardened armor plating module takes up capacity equal to double its rating. It has no capacity of its own for further armor mods.

In addition to its increased capacity cost, a hardened armor plating module can only be used with a titanium exoframe. If it is attached to a plastic or aluminum exoframe, it causes the critter great pain, and the Immobilized status until it is removed. Additionally, if the critter has a Strength attribute of less than 6, it gains the Hobbled status. A hardened armor plating module also requires special training, without which the critter suffers a –2 dice pool penalty to all actions. Even after the critter becomes used to wearing the module, it suffers a –1 dice pool penalty to all actions that use Agility, Reaction, or Strength. This penalty also applies to its defense pool against ranged and close combat attacks.
