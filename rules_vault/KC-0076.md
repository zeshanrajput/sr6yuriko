## _Salem:_ Perfection.

## (Recording paused for 22 minutes; recommences in sedan)

_Bungo:_ Stating, for the record, that this is all bulldrek and will continue to be bulldrek for who knows how long.

_Jalky:_ Which part?

_Bungo:_ All of it. The job is bulldrek—who knows what they’re trying to accomplish? The story is bulldrek—some of it is a lie. Maybe all of it. The fact that a peacock like Salem is probably paid way more than us is _definitely_ bulldrek.

_Jalky:_ So what do we do about it?

_Bungo:_ We steer clear. Really clear. I don’t want anything to do with any of this anymore.

_Bungo:_ And we close any accounts in Scotsprawl banks.
