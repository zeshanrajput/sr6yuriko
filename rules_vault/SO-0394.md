## How Dare You!

[Trigger: Failed Social Skill Test, Action: Major (A)] 1 Edge per –2 Social Rating

How dare they show you up! When a player character fails an Opposed Social skill test, regardless of who initiated the test, they may play the victim to gain a future advantage. For each point of Edge spent, the opponent’s Social Rating is reduced by 2. This lasts until the end of the scene

or encounter, an hour passes, or until the player character succeeds at a Social skill test (whichever comes first).

When having a lower Social Rating provides an advantage, each point of Edge spent increases the target’s Social Rating. This increase in Social Rating does not apply to tests in which a higher Social Rating provides an advantage.
