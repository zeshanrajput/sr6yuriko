## Dependents (1 to 4)

The contact has someone who depends on them and they feel beholden to. Maybe this is their kid, their lover, or maybe a sick parent. Regardless, the contact is going to do whatever it takes to protect that person. While that’s an admirable trait, it also means they’re an increased risk whenever you call on them for help.

**Game Effect:** When someone tries to con or influence the NPC and uses the dependent as leverage, the NPC loses (Level) dice on the Resistance test.
