## Bone Density Augmentation

Inexpensive and highly effective, bone density augmentation is the most common application of bioware when it comes to critters. In addition to making the critter tougher and more resilient, this augmentation improves natural weapons.

**Game Information:** Bone density increases the critter’s Body attribute for the purpose of Damage Resistance tests by its rating. The DV and AR of the critter’s natural weapons are also increased by the rating of the bone density augmentation. This increase is not compatible with any other augmentations that increase the effectiveness of natural weapons.
