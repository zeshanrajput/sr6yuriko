## Nocturnal

## **Bonus** _:_ 8 Karma

An altered circadian rhythm makes the individual active during the night and asleep during the day. If forced to be active outside their normal hours, they suffer the Confused (2) status (p. 51, _SR6_ ). If active in full daylight, all mental attributes are reduced by –1 point.
