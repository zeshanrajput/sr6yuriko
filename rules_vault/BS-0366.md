## Anabolic Steroids

(Ripper, J-H, Roidpatch)

- **Vector:** Contact, Injection

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** 10 x 1D6 minutes

- **Power:** 9

- **Effect:** +1 Strength, –1 Willpower

This powerful combination of hormones (natural and synthetic), pseudolipids, sugars, proteins, and amphetamines causes a burst in muscle effectiveness, granting a temporary boost in strength and energy. Many bodybuilders and professional trainers rely on these drugs to provide an edge in their workout regimen. Regular, daily use of the drug for the duration of training reduces the Karma cost of improving the Strength attribute by 1 per target rank. This means that the user is constantly at risk of becoming addicted to steroids.

Once the drug’s initial duration ends, the user takes 2 boxes of unresisted Stun damage. Prolonged usage of steroids may result in adverse effects, such as acne, aggressive behavior, hair loss, impotence, and muscle spasms. Users who are addicted to these substances grant Edge on Social tests, and if the addiction is at level 5 or 6 ( _SR6_ , p. 74) must make a Body + Willpower (2) when doing physical activity or suffer from –1 Agility and –1 Strength for ten minutes.
