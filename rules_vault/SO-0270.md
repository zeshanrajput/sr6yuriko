## Fear of God!

**Formula:** Evil Eye, Material (Saliva) **Drain Value:** 6

**Cost:** 450¥/charge

This alchemical preparation is becoming common in some areas as a form of acquiring information from prisoners instead of having to actually do the dirty work of torturing them. In some cases, corporations have been using a modified version when spraying protesters.
