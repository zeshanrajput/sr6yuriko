## **Arcanus Gorilla**

## **(Gorilla arcanus)**

**I always enjoyed the work of Dian Fossey. It’s inspirational to young scientists and adds some adventure and intrigue to the feld that boring labs are never going to give you. This, chummers, is how I ended up living among a family of mountain gorillas doing research on their social bonding rituals. Thanks to my arcane abilities, I was often able to hold my own when it came to physical contests, but any lapse in my focus generally led to bruises and a few broken bones.**

**It took well over six months to get myself accepted, and even after I was in, I was often treated as a younger gorilla and defnitely treated by my size more than what I provided for the troop. It was humbling for sure and slightly frustrating, as I was rarely able to interact with the group I was actually there to study. Oh, yeah, I didn’t clarify that—the arcanus gorilla lives among the common mountain gorilla as a regular member of the troop. The difference is in the name, as the arcanus gorilla is magically active. From my initial distant observations, I collected data on powers it possessed and logged them as compared to other species displaying similar abilities.**

**Then, it happened. I’m not sure why, but one of the young female gorillas took an interest in me. While nothing happened to constitute bestiality, I utilized a Mask spell to interact more personally in a matched form.**

**This was unwise. Enjoyable, but unwise.**

**The troop’s lead silverback felt I was a threat to his harem control (technically, I caused some abnormalities), and he challenged me. I wasn’t keen on getting kicked out because I’d had some stupid youthful fun. To balance the scales and make my best effort to not throw off the hierarchy while also preserving my own life, I used more of my arcane talents than at any time up to this.**

**In the midst of the fght, a pair of burly, all golden-furred females barreled into the middle of the fght and used some kind of gorilla-fu to break us apart only a moment before my little gnome skull got crushed. They pinned us down as Ohtuktuk, the arcanus gorilla, moved in among us and pulled me from the fght.**

**After that, my life spun 180 degrees. I moved in among the arcanus gorillas and kept distance from the others. I learned of an unspoken hierarchy, where the physically strongest lead the mundane gorillas and the most arcanely powerful control the arcanus gorillas, who in turn lead all the gorillas of their troop.**

**It wasn’t long on the inside that I discovered their abilities were not traditional paracritter powers, but instead the actual ability to intentionally manipulate mana, which even included the summoning of spirits, and the development of their own arcane tradition.**

**Communication was diffcult at frst, until I realized the spirits they summoned could easily act as interpreters. Understanding exploded after that, which is a paper for another time and place.**

**Here, just know that they consider many of the ruins of the old cultures as theirs to protect and preserve, riches and all.**
