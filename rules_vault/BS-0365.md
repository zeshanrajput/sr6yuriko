## Drugs and Toxins

## Aisa

(Eau de Vivre, Tex-Mex Tea)

- **Vector:** Ingestion

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** 5 x 1D6 minutes

- **Power:** 12

- **Effect:** Confused, Fatigued I

Aisa is a popular party drug derived from atropine, which is generally taken as a blot or sometimes dropped into drinks. Users of the drug experience mild euphoria, fatigue, and hallucinations resulting in gaining the Confused 3 status condition. When the drug wears off, the user gains the Fatigued I status.
