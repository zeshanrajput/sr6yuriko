## Household Gremlins

Paranormal critters are prevalent in your neck of the woods. And as fate would have it, they keep crossing paths with you. Some love shiny toys, others love flittering astral things, and some are spiteful critters who find your home offensive. The GM has free rein to introduce critter complications into your life— especially when you glitch. If this quality is triggered, some piece of your gear goes missing or becomes damaged as if it was chewed on, kicked, or worse (what’s that yellow stain on your cyberdeck, chummer?). Missing gear can be recovered with a Outdoors (Tracking) or Perception (6, 10 minutes) Extended test. Damaged (or defiled) objects need to be repaired or cleaned up with an Engineering + Logic (6, 10 minutes) Extended test.

**Bonus: 1 LP**
