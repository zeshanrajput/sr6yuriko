## Driving Style: Getaway Driver

You know a few maneuvers that are handy when evading pursuit, which come in handy more often than you’d probably like.

- **Cost:** 10 Karma

- **Game Effect:** You only pay half the required Edge cost for the following uses of Edge:

  - Evade Pursuit (2 or 4 Edge)

  - Evasive Action (2 Edge)

  - Escape! (2 Edge)

  - Double Down (1,2, or 3 Edge) only on defense tests when piloting a vehicle
