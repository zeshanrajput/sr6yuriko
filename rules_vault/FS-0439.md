## Sangre y Acero

After the invention of functional cybernetic limbs in the 2030s, it was only a matter of time before someone designed cyberlimbs that were optimized for combat. Originating in the gladiator pits of Tenochtitlán, Sangre y Acero (also known as Etzilitzli and Ars Cybernetica) makes the best possible use of the advantages granted by cyberlimbs, especially against other melee weapons.

_Categories:_ Striking, Weapon _Signature Technique_ : Iron Limbs
