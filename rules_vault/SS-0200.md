## What They Want

The Children of the Dragon want what most religious orders want: peace, harmony, and a brotherhood of (meta)humanity. They also want relics and artifacts of dragons, which they believe can be used to bring themselves closer to the Great Dragon. Whenever one of these rare antiquities resurfaces, count on one of the Children to be around to try to acquire it. They are not above using their funds to hire disposable assets to help recover these relics. With more rifts opening up, they’d be very interested in discovering whether these rifts could connect them to artifacts—or even to Ghostwalker’s point of origin.
