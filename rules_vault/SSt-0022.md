## **Connection Rating: 6**

**Role: Armorer, Weapons Fence, Mercenary Location: Detroit, MI UCAS**

**Owner and operator of GI Military Surplus, First Sergeant Arnie Fitz (75th Ranger Regiment, UCAS Army, Ret) was one of many who fought in Detroit during the Ares bug fasco in July 2080. When the fghting was done, “Sarge” (as he prefers to be called) retired from the military and later mercenary life to open GI Surplus. With numerous contacts in the military, merc, and shadow circles, Sarge quickly became one of the main go-to guys in Motor City for acquiring anything combat related, be it basic supplies, gear, or hard-to-get weapons and munitions. It’s also an open secret that Sarge pays pretty good nuyen for any recovered Ares military or related tech or gear that still litters the city. What Sarge does with those recovered items is a closely guarded secret. Often gruf and sometimes stand-ofsh, Sarge has a strong personal code but is known to occasionally mentor up-and-coming mercs or runners in the Motor City area. But get on his bad side, and Sarge will easily demonstrate why people came to him for over thirty years to make problems disappear.**

**> Something to consider when choosing and customizing firearms is that it’s all well and good to have something distinct, but that also makes it easier for you to be identified. And if you think that corps or security providers don’t keep track of that drek, then you’d better hang it up now. I knew someone from the Underground who liked to engrave specific runes on the sides of his weapons—considered himself a real “modern berserker-type Viking.” After one of their sweeps two years ago, KE was able to tie him to some runs in Everett from some video footage. Seems they got some nice images of those exact runes and that exact firearm, a modified Ares Alpha. So, guess what, they snatched him up and he quickly rolled on his entire team to save his rat hoop. All of his former mates are either dead or in a corporate prison, wishing they were dead.**
