**Notes : The vibro-sword and vibro-katana use the same basic rules as a vibroblade with the following diference. A vibroblade is always used single-handed, a vibro-sword may be used one or two-handed, and a vibro-katana must always be used as a two-handed weapon.**

**Powering up or turning of the vibro-weapon is a Minor Action.**

**If the unpowered blade is used in melee, reduce the AR by 4.**

**On a glitch when using a vibro-weapon, a section of the blade breaks, and the DV and AR of the weapon are both reduced by 2. Repairing a broken blade on a vibroblade requires an extended Engineering (Armorer) + Logic (6, 1 hour) test. A replacement blade for a vibroblade costs 300 nuyen, a vibro-sword 600 nuyen, and a vibro-katana 900 nuyen. All have the same availability as the base weapon.**
