## Berserker BTL

- **Vector:** Slot

- **Speed:** 1 combat round

- **Duration:** (Rating) minutes

- **Power:** 9 + (Rating)

- **Effect:** +1 Body, +1 Strength, –1 Willpower, –1 Logic

This p-fix activates the centers of the brain controlling the release of epinephrine while subjecting the user to memories of being bullied. Once the rage kicks in, the BTL supplies a continuous track of heavy metal while the user dishes out damage. The BTL reduces the cost for the Charge Edge action by 2. When you take Physical damage, you may go berserk. Make a Charisma + Willpower (3) test; if you fail, you go into rage. While in a berserker rage, you go after your attackers without regard for your own safety. You can spend a Minor Action to attempt to dissipate your rage using another Charisma + WillQUICKpower (3) test. This rage dissipates at the end of the BTL’s duration. Once the BTL wears off, you gain the Fatigued I status for the next (rating) hours.
