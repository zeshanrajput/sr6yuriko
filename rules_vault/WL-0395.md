## **Patrol (Attack or Alarm)**

**Critters can be trained to patrol an area. Once trained to patrol, the critter must be familiarized with a particular patrol pattern. This process takes an hour and requires an Infuence (Critters) + Charisma (2) test. Once a patrol pattern has been learned, the critter can be commanded to patrol the specifed area. If you need the critter to patrol a different area, this requires another hour of training and an Infuence test. A critter can learn a number of patrol patterns equal to its Logic attribute. If you teach a critter a new patrol pattern when they have already learned their maximum number of them, they will forget an older pattern to make room for the new one (gamemaster choice of which one is forgotten).**

**When you train a critter to patrol, you must choose whether they will attack or raise an alarm. Raising an alarm usually involves making loud noises, though a critter may be trained to return to their handler, press a button, pull a lever, or—if properly augmented and equipped—send a message via the Matrix. The trigger for a patrol command is usually verbal, though augmented critters may be provided with AR instructions.**

**If a critter has been trained to attack instead of raising an alarm, they will still be as loud as possible about it—barking, growling, howling, screeching, etc.**
