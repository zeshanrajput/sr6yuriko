## Purchasing a Data Structure

The market price for a data structure is usually its level x 5,000 nuyen, but finding one for sale is difficult. You must have a contact with the Matrix and Resonance types with a Connection and Loyalty rating equal to or greater than the level of the data structure you wish to purchase. In order to integrate the data structure, you must also pay its Karma cost.
