## Tools

## Graffiti Kit

While anyone can grab a can of spray paint and tag a wall, a Graffiti Kit has a selection of colors, several stencils, and quick-cleaning solvents, letting a young artisan quickly assemble an impressive display, remove the evidence from their own hands, then store the lot for a rapid escape. Protective goggles and a rebreather are highly recommended.
