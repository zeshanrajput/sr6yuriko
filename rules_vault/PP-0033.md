## Residents

Very few people live on Z-O anymore. Taiga Mitsuhama was the last one of any consequence because, in case you were in a coma at the time, his death led to MCT’s little ownership clusterfrag that was compounded by Korin Yamana’s untimely demise. The fact is that there are bigger and better places for those hoping to escape mortality among the stars than a cramped set of tubes.
