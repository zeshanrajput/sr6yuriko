## Metahuman Glamor

A favorite of Nashville starlets and Horizon execs, silky skin is a simple two-stage process that can be completed in a long afternoon and is included in many spa-day packages. This is a very expensive process that takes place in two steps. The first part is the removal of old dermal layers and scars via “soft” laser treatment or, in Renraku facilities, a small cadre of human scrubbers. The patient is then immersed in a tank where the body receives a wide range of minor modifications affecting their muscle, tendons, joints, and vocal cords. The effect is a metahuman who moves with unearthly grace. Their countenance always seems radiant, and their voice has a natural quality that also grabs the attention of those around them. Those they speak to feel moved and inspired.

This is reflected with a bonus of +1 Edge for all social actions taken that are not aggressive. Those with this quality are particularly memorable, equating to an additional +1 Edge for anyone attempting to make a Memory test to recall the individual or the actions they’ve taken.
