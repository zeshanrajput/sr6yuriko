## Enhanced Movement Speed (1 to 4)

- **Cost:** 1 Karma per level.

Your animal form gains +5 meters of movement per level when taking the Sprint (I) Major Action (p. 44, _SR6_ ). This increase affects all movement rates for shifters with a fl y or swim speed. It does not affect the speed of your metahuman form.
