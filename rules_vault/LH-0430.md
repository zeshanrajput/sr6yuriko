## November 23, 2082

Officially recognized as the birth of the Defender Movement and the implementation of _Project Nemesis_ , although some sources report groups already calling themselves “Defenders” operating in multiple regions worldwide before this date. Pro-Disian forces transition to an overall defensive posture in the wake of Defender operations, focusing on securing the Harvester Network. Although some pro-Disian groups and units remain on the offensive and continue to actively hunt Defender forces and allies.
