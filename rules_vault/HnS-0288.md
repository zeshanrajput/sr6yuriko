Resonance has helped you achieve what technology can only hope to unlock one day: gamma grade augmentations.

- **Cost:** 25 Karma

- **Game Effect:** After you have lost 4 Essence, you may take this quality at its listed Karma cost (not double). You must have Cyberadept Master and its prerequisites in order to take this quality. All of your augmentations become gamma grade, regardless of their current
