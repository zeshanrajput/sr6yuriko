## The Cube

The portal device known simply as the Cube is currently located in Neo-Tokyo in the possession of MCT, and it’s the backbone of their exo-exploration research. The featureless three-meter black cube is made of an unknown material, and its origin is a closely guarded secret. The device opens a metaplanar passage on one of its six sides in response to a verbal trigger. There are currently 1,130 known trigger words that each open a physical passageway to a different metaplane. The Cube is in constant use, moving teams in and out of known metaplanes, while researchers test an endless stream of randomly generated syllables, searching for new trigger words.
