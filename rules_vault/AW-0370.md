## Sauropod Heart Amulet

When activated, a sauropod heart amulet protects its wearer from glitches (and downgrades critical glitches to regular glitches) a number of times equal to its Force before it deactivates. Once deactivated, the focus cannot be reactivated again until twenty-four hours pass. The cost to bond this amulet is (Force x 3) Karma.
