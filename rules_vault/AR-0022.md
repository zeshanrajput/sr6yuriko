## Government 

Like most UCAS cities, Omaha has a mayor and city council. The balance of power tilts toward the mayor. The current mayor is Joyce Parish, a lifelong Omaha resident. Everything about her says “caretaker”—she talks a whole lot more about keeping things going than about making any sort of broad changes. The chaos of the past year has been a challenge, but it helps that she could just focus on trying to maintain city services without instituting any kind of sweeping change. There is only so much she can do—the government’s main job is to maintain the streets, clean the parks, and offer what few services their meager budgets can afford. That budget is going to be dropping, so a lot of her work will be watching the city decline while she keeps parts of it looking nice. When the budget gets to the point where they might not even be able to fulfill their law-enforcement contract, things will get especially interesting.
