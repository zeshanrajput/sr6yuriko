## **1. Identifying Who Can Use the Information**

**Some parties are easy to identify—if you have found an infltrator, the people who can act are the ones above them (this, of course, becomes more complicated if the infltrator is at the top of an organization, but I expect that to be rare so I’ll leave it aside for now). The more complicated an operation becomes, though, the more diffcult it is to fnd the right people. If you fnd, say, an infltrator in Global Sandstorm who is collecting a variety of petroleum samples, what do you do about it? Who could help you understand the purpose of the samples? That’s a tricky question. You probably want someone who has good knowledge of both chemicals and magic, and that’s not a large pool of people. And you can’t just fnd someone who has the knowledge—you need someone who will work with you and any others you enlist in the effort. That’s when things get quite complicated.**
