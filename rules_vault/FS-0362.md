## Team Leader

Leading a combat team during combat is a difficult and often chaotic situation. The Team Leader app presents the designated leader with data projections on current tactical situations based on sensor data, team biometrics, weapon status, and other environmental factors in order to assist with decision-making and team coordination.

**Rules:** This app provides a pool of bonus dice equal to the M-TOC’s device rating for use on the following tests: teamwork, navigation, perception, and maneuver. This dice pool can be broken up and assigned as needed but can only be used once per combat engagement.
