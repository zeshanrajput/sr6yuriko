## Combat

You are not well-suited for direct combat engagements. One of the first things you should do when the bullets start flying is get under cover and concealment. Once you’re relatively protected from attacks, call in your spirits and have them do the fighting for you. You should have a few useful spells, but try to avoid using obvious combat magic except in the most desperate or opportune moments. Use your magic to misdirect your opponents and shield or conceal your allies.
