## Ex (Eros, Galak)

- **Vector:** Ingestion

- **Speed:** 1D6 minutes

**==> picture [288 x 765] intentionally omitted \<==**



- **Duration:** (8 – Body) hours, minimum 1 hour

- **Power:** 10

- **Effect:** –1 Willpower, –1 Logic, +1 Charisma

This party drug acts as a mild stimulant and aphrodisiac, leaving users energetic and easier to arouse. The drug was originally designed to reduce sexual inhibitions and treat some social anxieties but has since been used in unscrupulous ways as a date rape drug. Users of the drug grant 2 Edge when resisting a Con (Seduction) test.

_Note:_ Gamemasters should be careful about introducing this element into the game and should only do so with the awareness and approval of the players.

Galak is an Awakened version of the drug made from the pollen of an Awakened orchid. Increase the Power by 1, and the minimum Duration is three hours.
