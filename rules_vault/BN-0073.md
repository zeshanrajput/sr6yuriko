## Shifter Modesty

- **Bonus:** 8 Karma

- **Bonus:** 12 Karma

Some shifters have a strong preference for which form they are in to the extent that being in their other form “feels wrong.” When taking this add-on, choose either your animal or metahuman form as your preferred form. When not in your preferred form, you may not gain or spend Edge.
