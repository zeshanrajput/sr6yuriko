## Vibe

New York clubs tend to be notable either for where they are or for who shows up. Vibe has the luxury of both. The club is located in an old bank on 30[th] and Park Avenue, where it occupies the ground floor and sub-basements. The three-level club includes two separate vaults, which are used as VIP lounges.
