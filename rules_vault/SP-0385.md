## **The Ares Devices: Game Rules**

**Each of the Ares devices must be attuned to afect a specifc metaplane—originally, this was the Hive. When detonated, they are equal to several kilograms of plastic explosive, but the primary efect is in the astral plane. These devices create a mana void in the area of the detonation for a ffty-meter radius and cause a greater metaplanar eclipse (see p. 167, Astral Ways ) between the two metaplanar junction points, inhibiting normal travel for a few decades across that point.**
