## Welcome to the After parties

By this point in the evening, the runners have manipulated a number of events surrounding the city’s biggest social affair. The after-party section resolves the actions started throughout the day. The ending is based on what the runners accomplished leading up to the moment. They may only complete adventures they were initially involved in.
