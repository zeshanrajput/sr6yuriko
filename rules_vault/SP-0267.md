## **Mana Camp**

**A busload of recently Awakened children is on their way to Mana Camp, a joint megacorp site where Awakened kids learn some of their frst magical lessons (and the corps get their initial claws in them). MCT is one of the sponsors and participants in Mana Camp, and they have chosen to intercept the bus to capture the children to use them as Harvester fuel. The runners have little time to track down and rescue the children before the trail goes cold.**
