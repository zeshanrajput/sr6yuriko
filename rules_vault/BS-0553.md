## Awakened Genetic Augmentation

(Ritual Sorcery)

**Threshold:** 2 + the total number of Awakened genetic augmentations applied

This ritual is necessary to complete every day during the gestation of an embryo engineered to receive Awakened genetic augmentations. It must be performed once per day, and if it ever fails—or if you skip a day—the embryo dies immediately.
