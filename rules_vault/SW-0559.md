## Sympathetic Linking

Every runner should know about material links. Period. For those who slept through Magic for Mundanes 101, I’ll elaborate.

Material links are tissue samples that can be used to target you with ritual magic. Usually these are blood samples, but it can be any bodily fluid except sweat. (And I do mean _any_ fluid, so alwaysput your gloves on before hooking up with that novahot whoever in the bar).
