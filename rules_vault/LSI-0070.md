If you have the Affinity for Transit quality (p. 167, _DC_ ), your wait for a ride is only 10 minutes, and you may roll twice and choose the best result to determine your travel time. Public Transportation is not available in areas with a Security rating of E or lower. If you want to be able to access Public Transportation near your home, you must spend at least one LP on the Neighborhood Rating asset.
