## Uncontrolled Metastasis

The drugs that keep a heavily cybered individual from rejecting the massive amounts of implants they have can keep tumors alive too, not just their subject.

- **Bonus:** 10 Karma per level (Up to 2)

- **Game Effect:** A character with uncontrolled metastasis develops a lot of cancer. At 10 Karma, tumors grow at a steady but slow rate, and the patient must undergo regular surgery or nanotech treatment every month to remove cancerour growths or become violently ill (which effectively incapacitates them. This adds 1,000¥ per month to their lifestyle costs. At 20 Karma, the metastasis is such that tumors and cancerous tissues are simply impossible to eradicate entirely with the requisite monthly surgeries (though these must still be performed).
