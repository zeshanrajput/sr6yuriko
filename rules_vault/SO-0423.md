## Other Angles of the Con

When assisting with a Con test, player characters could opt for one of the following instead of using Con.

- **Provide the leader with useful details gleaned from social media apps on the Matrix:** Electronics (Computer) + Intuition

- **Assense the target to read their aura for emotional reactions:** Astral + Intuition

- **Observe the target’s body language and facial expressions carefully:** Judge Intentions

- **Look good and keep your cool:** Composure

- **Play Good Cop to the leader’s Bad Cop:** Con (Acting) + Charisma or Influence (Negotiation) + Charisma

- **Look mean, tough, and scary:** Body + Strength

- **Assense the target’s aura for emotional reactions:** Astral + Intuition

- **Provide the leader with suggestions for specific approaches or techniques:** Memory or Perception + Intuition test or a related knowledge skill, such as Interrogation Techniques or Psychology
