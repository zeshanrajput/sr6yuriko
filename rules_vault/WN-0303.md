## The Job

## Johnson in Need Indeed

Eager reaches out to the runners. He tells them he has a client who needs them to retrieve an item from a safe and data from an isolated computer at a local social club. There is a party being held at the club that evening. Ms. Johnson arranged clearances for the runners to get in. All they need to do is get the goods and get out undetected. The

17: ALLproblem is, this is a secure club. That means sensors at the door and no weapons allowed. Subterfuge is key. Eager says that Ms. Johnson stressed that they cannot go in loud or else it would trigger a security cascade that could cause problems throughout the city.

Ms. Johnson offers a lot of money for the job— 6,000 nuyen per person.
