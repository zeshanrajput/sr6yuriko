## Spell focus

Spell foci fall into different sub-categories, namely counterspelling, spellcasting, ritual spellcasting, and sustaining. These functions are described on p. 155. A different focus needs to be purchased for each category of spell (Health, Combat, Illusion, etc.), and each focus only provides the functions of its sub-category; there are no spell foci that cross all categories.
