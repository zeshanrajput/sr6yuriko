## Currency of the Elemental Planes

## Local Currency

Local currency is a bit more volatile than true elements and can vary within the plane itself. Currency is the processed part of some material related to the plane (for example, compressed gas, errhine hide cut into rectangles, unique earth crystals, or snowflake patterns). While it has value in the metaplane, it has to be exchanged at one of the cardinal poles for the other element. Exchange value fluctuates, so the poles hold large currency houses said to contain enough currency to endure any large shifts. Interestingly enough, the exchange rates often tie in with environmental effects on the material plane (typhoons increase the value of water and air, droughts and forest fires increase the value of fire but reduce water or air, etc.). They also carry other planar currency (fae, kindred, plant), but not as much. The complexities of exchange are challenging to keep up with, so most travelers stick with a barter system.

- True elements are a much more stable currency, but they’re rarely handled in small transactions. Corporate-sponsored Awakened people have tried to purchase True elements from all of the elemental planar governments, with no success.
