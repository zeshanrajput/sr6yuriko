## Temporary Skills

Sometimes a team member will need to give just enough information to a teammate so they can handle a situation without going through the full process of getting them up to the next skill rank. As part of preparing for a run, a runner can teach one of their teammates just enough information on a topic to give them a one-time Edge cost reduction as part of the run.

This can be used for either knowledge skills or active skills. For knowledge skills, the instructor needs to have the skill they’re teaching. For active skills, the instructor must have the skill at a higher rank than the student.

Teaching a primer to another takes one hour of time and requires a single hit Influence (Instruction) + Charisma or Logic test. On a success, the student gains a one-time Edge boost cost reduction of 1 for use with the skill. On a glitch, the student can’t gain or spend Edge on any test that relates to the skill they tried to learn for the duration of the run. On a critical glitch, the student gains the Confused 4 status condition, which lasts for the duration of the run.

A runner can only have access to one temporary skill at a time, but a single instructor can teach multiple runners at the same time.
