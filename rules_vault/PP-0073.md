## The Board of Directors

A last mention about the board itself. As it currently stands, there are no major investors in Ares Macrotechnology. Shiawase-Yamana’s the current largest shareholder with three percent (2.9743 percent, to be precise) of the outstanding shares, but no one person has the ten percent that qualifies as a Major Shareholder under Ares rules. Ideally, the board wants to have more control, to prevent another Damien Knight, but in practice, with only small voices, the eventual CEO is going to absolutely dominate the table.
