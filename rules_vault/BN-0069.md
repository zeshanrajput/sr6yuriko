## Pack Animal

## • **Bonus:** 7 Karma

You’re a pack animal—you work best as part of a team. Whenever you are alone, you may only gain a maximum of one Edge per combat round. If you are in astral space, only astrally active allies are considered company for the purpose of this quality, but a summoned spirit is all you need to prevent the penalty. If you are in VR mode, only allies in the Matrix with you make you feel less alone, but having an agent program or sprite nearby, or even a SimShare hitchhiker is enough to prevent the penalty. You may not take both Apex Predator and Pack Animal.
