## **Improvised Command**

## **(Use Skill (Influence), Major Action)**

**You may issue a command to a critter that it has not been trained for. This requires a Major Action and an Infuence (Critters) + Charisma (3) test—and one of the dice is replaced with a wild die. If the critter has any Essence loss, you suffer a –1 dice pool penalty for every point of Essence loss—round up any fractions. If the critter has a critterjack augmentation, the Essence loss penalty is reduced by its rating.**

- **Cost: 2 Edge**
