## Spirit-called

Some of the purest of the beast summoners were drawn by the spirits themselves. They were haunted by visions and dreams to eventually follow the path of a single beast or embrace the culture of the spirits that has become a cornerstone of the Sixth World. Whether they follow a single spirit animal totem or seek to follow the laws of nature itself, they hone their talents and skills to be the best summoners around, and in so doing work to keep their favor with the spirits they summon and encounter.

Those drawn by the spirits are the most likely to learn the ways to break the bonds holding spirits summoned by others who are less enlightened, rather than simply attacking them to disrupt them. They often gain extra favor with the spirits they summon from their reputation on the spirit planes. As time passes, these are the most likely beast summoners to keep focusing on the world of the spirits, the astral planes, and aiding in the plights of spirits being abused or mistreated on the physical plane.

Backstories in this area often carry some debt to a local spirit or some token given to the character as they began their journey that can develop into a plot hook down the road. These spirits can also reflect a mentor spirit and be a source of story and guidance for the character as they progress. As they are solely focused on summoning skills, the character can advance into the shadows with not only their team of running mates by their side, but also their cadre of spiritual friends that they’ve brought over to help them on our plane.
