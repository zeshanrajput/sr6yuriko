## **Conclave: Guild of Freelance Assets**

**Size and Location:** Dozens of mages and shamans in cities with a Shadow Chapters presence including Seattle.

**Connection:** 7

**Connection Type:** Magical

**SRM Contact:** Donovan Pyke, Whiskey

**Goals:** Support the Shadow Chapters resources and train magical talent that is focused on the goals of the mission without unintended loss, casualties, or victims.
