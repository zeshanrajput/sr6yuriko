## **Gungir Whale**

**Gungir whales (** _**Monodon ferrumceros**_ **) are the Awakened form of the narwhal. They range in size from eight meters to twelve meters. Like their mundane cousins, they lack a dorsal fn, instead having a dorsal ridge that extends along the length of their bodies. Gungir whales have a pronounced melon (sensory organ), far more pronounced than their mundane relatives the narwhal and beluga whale. They are mottled brown and black on a white background, and they turn paler as they age. The tusk of the gungir whale is made of an exceptionally tough material that rivals titanium/steel alloys. The tusk is hollow and forms a left-handed helical spiral. Along with being used as a sensory organ, it is the means by which gungir whales project their sonic stunning ability into the ocean. The tusk, which is seen on both sexes of gungir whale, is between 2.5 and 4 meters for females and 3.5 and 5.5 meters for males. The fukes of the tail have front edges that are more concave and lack a sweep-back, and are positioned horizontally like all marine mammals.**
