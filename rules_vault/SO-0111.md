## Scaling

The long-term version of this is also one way to plant yourself deep in someone’s life. It’s like the Plant, but more intimate. This is where the Honeypot becomes a spy game, using someone else to lend legitimacy to a false identity. I get the impression Saturday Jones might have something to say about that.

- I do. I don’t recommend the Parasite Play. There’s a high degree of getting caught or entangled in real feelings for the mark. That said, I’ve seen a few vanishing acts that found new love to go with a new life, and you don’t really have a choice but to lie to them or put them at greater risk. But whole secondary families have been raised in the name of plausible covers by those with too much conviction and not enough conscience.

- Saturday Jones
