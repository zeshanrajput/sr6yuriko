## Specialized Function

Vehicles with a specialized function gain all of the modifications required to perform that function for free. This usually means the Portable Facility mod, but could be other things such as specific hardpoint mods, living quarters, or specialized cargo environments. Typical specialized functions include but are not limited to: medical/ambulance, fire response, EMT, tow trucks, construction vehicles, recreational vehicles, sky cranes, and research vessels. Cost Multiplier: x2
