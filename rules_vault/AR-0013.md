## Item 4: Surveillance intercept from two runners talking at The Sodfather (Omaha tavern) 

**Runner 1** : … just don’t take one of those fraggin’ vans. 

**Runner 2** : Vans? 

**Runner 1** : The new ones. White, black, and red color scheme. 

**Runner 2** : What’s wrong with them? 

**Runner 1** : Not entirely sure, but <3.2 seconds unintelligible> him since. Can’t even pick him up with the help of a material link. Wherever he went, he went far. 

**Runner 2** : How do you know it’s connected to the vans? 

**Runner 1** : It’s a hunch. But there’s someone new on the streets, hiring people. Calls herself Corvus. 

**Runner 2** : So? 

**Runner 1** : It’s a bird. And a buzzard is a bird. 

**Runner 2** : Are they the same bird? 

**Runner 1** : No. 

**Runner 2** : So how is that a connection? Do you think Roger Soaring-Owl is in on this, too? An owl is a bird. **Runner 1** : No, but … 

**Runner 2** : How about Anne Ravenheart? 

**Runner 1** : Look, shut up! 

**Runner 2** : It’s just that … 

**Runner 1** : I know, I get it. I’ve heard things, but I need more evidence. Fine. I’ll find it. But then you better listen.
