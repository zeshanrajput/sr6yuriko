## **2. Convince All the Parties Who Might Be Helpful to Work Together**

**Do you have the secret to convince Wuxing geomancers to cooperate with MCT wagemages? Or to get the Draco Foundation to share anything? Or to reach anyone in the Black Lodge? If so, Horizon would like to hear from you. Their powers of persuasion are impressive, but these (and tasks like them) will test the full extent of their abilities. Very little of how the Sixth World operates is built on trust, which makes forging relationships of the type Horizon wants a very diffcult challenge.**
