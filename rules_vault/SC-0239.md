## Recommendations for character creation

- **Attributes:** Logic 6, Intuition 4+, Resonance 6

- **Skills:** Cracking, Electronics, Tasking, each as high as possible.

- **Knowledge skills:** Data Havens, The Dissonance, Matrix Entities

- **Complex forms:** Diffusion (Firewall), Infusion (Attack), Pulse Storm, Resonance Spike, Stitches

- **Positive qualities:** Analytical Mind, Aptitude (Cracking), Focused Concentration

- **Negative qualities:** Bad Luck, Bad Rep, Bounty, Gremlins, Hunted, Impaired (Strength), Social Stress, Weak Immune System

- **Life modules:** Activist, IT Support, Hacker, Technical School, Technomancer, Event: You became extremely interested in unusual or mysterious topics.
