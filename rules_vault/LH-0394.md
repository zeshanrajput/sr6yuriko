## The Sling

MCT didn’t go down easily. It was a knockdown, drag-out, street brawl, and it wasn’t until after a rapid-fire combo that they finally went down. The Defenders landed the final shot, but jabs from Toshiro Mitsuhama created openings toward the end. And while the Defenders were hitting MCT hard from the shadows, Toshiro opened MCT up for a staggering body blow after the final battle. Soon after, world markets saw MCT stock dip in value and a massive global sell-off followed.

This plummeted stock values and opened MCT up for the megacorporate haymaker that was thrown by other triple- and double-As working to knock them to the bottom of the corp rankings through boardroom and backstreet dealings. As a result, MCT is going to be forced to restructure, rename, and retreat to lick their wounds while they clutch their golden ticket and hope the efforts of their rivals don’t turn that ticket to pyrite.

But I’m also getting ahead of myself.
