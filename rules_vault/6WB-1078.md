## Characters need to do something significant to earn a Contact

Earning a contact is not just about introducing yourself, or slipping a bartender twenty nuyen. Characters need to do something of value—completing a run successfully and honestly, preventing serious harm to a person or someone they care about, or showing significant potential as a business partner are the sorts of activities that could earn a contact. The higher the Connection rating of the contact, the more the character should be doing to earn them.
