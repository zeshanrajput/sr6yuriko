## **Fleet Manager**

**Uses: cost management, drone/vehicle parts and repairs, resource allocations**

**Places to Meet: car racing, drone racing, garage Similar Contacts : chop shop owner, mechanic**

**A police feet manager oversees and maintains the operation and effciency of their law enforcement provider’s vehicle feets. This role involves strategic planning of new equipment to stay within budget and maintenance to ensure that police vehicles, drones, and other equipment are in good workable condition. From scheduling routine maintenance to managing acquisitions and disposals, the feet manager’s responsibilities support the mobility of their law enforcement offcers. They balance budget considerations, implement cost-effective solutions, and stay abreast of technological advancements that contribute to the overall success of police operations. In essence, the police feet manager is a key fgure in ensuring that law enforcement agencies are reliable and well-equipped to carry out their duties.**

**Whether in a military, law enforcement, or emergency services context, a feet manager is responsible for managing and distributing essential equipment and supplies. Having a quartermaster as a contact can facilitate access to critical information**
