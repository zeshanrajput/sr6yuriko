## The Dark City

While characteristics of Dis vary, the one constant is the sprawling city. Disians say that Dis _is_ the City, and the City is Dis. Although it’s sometimes called “The Dark City.”



Dis is a never-ending metropolis full of open courts, choking back alleys, and massive towers. At the center is the Council Tower, the seat of Disian power and home to the High and Highest Councils. Leaving the city is impossible, as the Dark City is the metaplane. Anyone who reaches a border finds themselves looping to another edge of the city.

Another aspect of Dis is the omnipresent negativity. Depression, dread, anxiety, all are common for travelers to feel during prolonged stays. Reports also state that visitors felt as if their life force were being slowly eaten away the longer they stayed.

- Even without words, the sad song of the dying can still be heard.
