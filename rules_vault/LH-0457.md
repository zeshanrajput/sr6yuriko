## Disians, Chimeras, and Fae

Chimeras originating from the material plane (Earth) are affected similarly to other sapient dual-natured individuals, but those from other metaplanes (this includes Faustians) die between five and ten days after the portal at Triple-Zero closes. Any fae who are on Earth from July 25 to August 5 fall into a sudden torpor.
