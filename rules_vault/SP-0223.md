## **Bogotá, Aztlan**

## **Status: Disian Stronghold**

**After the Az-Am War, Bogotá was offcially recognized as Aztlan property. But the locals never really took to that, and for the past six years the former free city has been a constant pain in Aztlan’s hoop. Multiple insurgencies, typically spearheaded by the Bright Star movement and supported by the reformed Black Star mercenary company, continued to spring up and challenge Aztlan sovereignty at every turn.**

**That was, of course, like many other places, before the Week of Death happened.**
