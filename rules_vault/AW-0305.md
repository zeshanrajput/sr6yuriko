## Translation in the Place of Clouds

When entering the Place of Clouds, your equipment and augmentations will remain unchanged and will function normally. If you enter the Spire, you are translated as if you were visiting the metaplane that matches the district you are in.

Most cloud islands follow the same rules, but some of the larger ones are capable of causing metaplanar translation to affect any visitors. These translation effects generally kick in within a kilometer of the cloud island. Most of these are large cloud islands with environments or biomes that contrast greatly to the elemental Planes of Air or Water. These islands bring to life the aspects of other metaplanes, the most common among them resembling Faerie. This sometimes makes exploring and gathering resources on certain cloud islands more difficult, but the effects are usually easy to adapt to.
