## **Threshold:** 4

A Spell Ward is a specialized type of Ward (p. 145, SR6) It creates a mana barrier that is designed to only target certain categories of spells. The ritual leader can choose up to two spell categories when sealing the ward, The Spell Ward functions as a normal mana barrier in all ways when interacting with the chosen spell types but ignores all other magic and astral forms. These wards are most often used by people who want to prevent the use of Manipulation or Illusion spells, but really have no concerns with other magic.

MANAsocial skills is never a bad idea. **Kinesics** is a staple power chosen by most social adepts since it is very helpful in concealing one’s true intentions when working a target. **Vocal Control** is a useful ability whenever the social adept needs to talk their way out of a situation or past an obstacle. **Attribute Boost (Mental)** and **Improved Mental Attribute** are both easy ways to maximize a social adept’s Charisma or Logic potential. **Commanding Voice** is a classic mainstay power of any social adept who wants to order people around. **Cool Resolve** is a must-have for any adept who is looking to bolster their defense against being socially manipulated. **Cosmetic Control** is the ultimate power for any master of disguise. As an alternative form of assensing, **Empathic Sense** is another useful power for those adepts looking for a way to get a read on a target. **Enthralling Performance** is an option when an adept needs to be a distraction. **Kinesics Mastery** is just a useful power to expand communication methods as well as reinforcing the basic **Kinesics** power. **Linguistics** is useful for those social adepts who might find themselves in a foreign environment and need to quickly adapt to a new language.
