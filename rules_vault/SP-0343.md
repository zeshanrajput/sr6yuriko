## **Changeling Chimera**

**These chimeras were frst identifed after one was captured attempting to enter a high-security corporate facility. Changeling chimeras specialize in short-term covert operations. Documented operations have included assassinations, extractions, sabotage, and theft of various materials.**

**As skilled shapeshifters, they can alter their form like a metahuman changes clothing, appearing as anything from a female dwarf child to a full-grown male troll while carrying out a mission. While they typically carry actual gear, if needed the bodies of these creatures can form passable copies of clothing, weapons, and other equipment, although imitated frearms and electronics are non-functional.**

**They show a great deal of skill and intelligence while carrying out their assigned tasks, and their ability to blend in and gain access to sensitive information and enter secure areas is unparalleled. If a Changeling chimera is discovered during a mission, they’ll attempt to escape capture by changing their appearance, then either return to the mission or attempt to escape the area entirely and try again later.**

**The primary disadvantage of Changeling chimeras is their inability to alter their astral signatures. This makes it diffcult for them to impersonate an Awakened individual or hide their true nature from astral security.**
