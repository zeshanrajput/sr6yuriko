## Foehn

The earth-air cardinal pole is marked by a floating island of eluvium, blown in from the elemental Plane of Earth. The island is semi-spherical with a slight wobble. The topside has flowing grey towers; the tops of them waver as the island wobbles, like columns of steam. The bottom of the island holds a collection of spheres, much like the homes of the Drumlin, only made of lighter material, giving them a fuzzy appearance. Across the diameter of the island is a no-man’s-land trench with a few bridges. Air elemental sentries keep track of those crossing from top to bottom and prevent any earth elementals from crossing. This makes trade and travel a bit awkward for those coming from the earth metaplane and crossing over to the air metaplane. Such travelers are subjected to rapid interrogation and harassment, much to the amusement of the sentries, rather than actual laws. The hours of inspections and insults can be shorted with a “misplaced” vial of fragrant oil.

**>** One guard is always asking me if I’m bringing any fruit, because pests can hide in fruit crossing the border and must be discarded or quarantined. Like, I always astral travel with a banana.

**>** Lyran
