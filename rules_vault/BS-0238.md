## Wings

This process is technically a combination of bioware and transgenic. The process starts with amore aggressive version of skeletal pneumaticity therapy. This augmentation causes the formation of hollow bones that are nearly as strong yet much lighter than the normal metahuman skeleton. In the later stages, the wings are grafted onto the back. The final phase is the addition of special air and gland sacs under the lungs. Once the procedure is completed, the character doesn’t gain true flight but something similar to hang gliding. They also weigh twenty percent less.

When the wings are in use, the user has a glide ratio of 3:1. For example, if the user launches from a ten-meter-high rooftop, they can land up to thirty meters away. If the user spends a Minor Action, they can direct their glide for up to fifty meters of horizontal movement. If they spend a Major Action, they can move in the same way but also extend the distance of their total glide by +3 meters per hit on an Athletics (Flight) + Strength test. Although the hollow bones are reinforced, the user still loses 1 die for any Damage Resistance tests. A user with wings cannot also have bone lacing cyberware or bone density bioware.

The wings bioware is incompatible with skeletal pneumaticity therapy.
