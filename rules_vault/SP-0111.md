## **Taking in Strays**

**Among those also targeted during the Week of Death was the Atlantean Foundation and the Astral Space Preservation Society . I’ve been asked to mention them here because, to be frank, they’ve both been decimated, and survivors have scattered.**

**Having both been founded by mandate from Dunkelzahn’s will, the Draco Foundation and ASPS share a common lineage, even though both organizations diverged from each other some years ago. But when their offces and researchers were attacked, the survivors managed to contact foundation representatives, who facilitated transfers to safehouses and ongoing security. Debriefngs of the survivors are still ongoing, but we’re confdent they were targeted because of their recent discoveries regarding regional manasphere changes as well as their general knowledge on the subject. The idea was to prevent anyone from using the ASPS’ knowledge against Disian interests. Thankfully, enough of the scientists, researchers, and data has been saved.**
