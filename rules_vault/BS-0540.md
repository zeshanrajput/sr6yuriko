## Instinctive Skills Enhancement

This genetic enhancement improves a critter’s instinctive behavior to advance one of its natural skills. Only Athletics, Close Combat, Outdoors, Perception, and Stealth can be increased in this way. Increase the ranks of one specified skill by the rating of this enhancement, up to a maximum of +2.
