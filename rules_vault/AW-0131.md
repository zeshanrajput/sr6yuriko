## Midden

## (Earth, Force 3)

As if a child made a sheep out of mud in the backyard, middens are a chaotic mixture of dirt, stone, and clay, with droopy eyes that make you feel sorry for them. These quadrupeds wander around the landscape, grazing on what constitutes as vegetation or crunching down on gemstone fruit. Middens grow asbestos-like wool, and they are actually resistant to electricity. Middens are not aggressive beasts, but once they find something they like to eat, it’s hard to get them to move on until they finish their meal.
