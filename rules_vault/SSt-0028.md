## Basic Stealth

The basic concept of stealth is pretty obvious— don’t be seen or heard. Being able to move about or

**hide without getting spotted, heard, or tagged by sensors is what it’s all about. Every runner, even the ones who specialize in overt confrontations, needs to learn the basics. That means practicing how to get around without making too much noise, taking advantage of cover, shadows, and blind spots in security sensor arrays. There are different techniques required based on who—or what—you’re trying to sneak past.**
