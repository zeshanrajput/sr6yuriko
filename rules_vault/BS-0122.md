## Grey Mana Armor (Rating 1-4)

The Grey Brothers’ marvel of technology has entered the realm of applied cybernetics. A proprietary blend of orichalcum, rare minerals, and oils is available as an aftermarket runic modification to existing dermal plating. Each rating point of Grey Mana armor grants one boosted spell defense die (p. 143, _SR6_ ) against any spell, power, or other magical effect that includes the user as a target. Even beneficial effects are automatically resisted, with the user rolling Body + Willpower + boosted defense dice if no resistance test is given. Any hits on this test deduct the number of hits generated by the caster. Grey Mana armor rating cannot exceed the user’s dermal plating rating and must match the same grade as the dermal plating.
