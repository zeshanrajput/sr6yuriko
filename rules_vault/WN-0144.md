## Who’s Who

Swen Rellik is a low-level fixer in Manhattan who focuses mostly on screamsheet-related gigs. He hires runners for data snooping, data thefts, and compromising vids and photos mostly. He got tapped for this gig because it’s a data theft/swap, but he doesn’t know it’s a setup.

He’s human, average height, a little overweight, and hides a balding head of brown hair with a snappy flat cap. He has a nose that’s been broken a few times, wears a long tan trenchcoat that’s a little on the dirty side over a Vashon Island suit about ten seasons old, and talks with a touch of New York accent. He smells of stale cigar smoke.
