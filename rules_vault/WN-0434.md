## Intimidation

Unlike the Disians, Jonathan Blake (p. 143) is not interested in co-opting the PCs at this point. While he likely does not know the full extent of what the PCs know (unless the PCs have been quite reckless), he knows they have their fingers in many pies, and he’s likely run into them enough to know they’ve had chances to gather some significant secrets. He doesn’t want them dead, as they may still be useful to him, but he wants them to know to keep their mouths shut.

At some public place, four Professional Rating 7 Elite Corporate Security personnel (p. 208, _SR6_ ; mix the two types at will) try to ambush the PCs with gel rounds and punches. At some point, they deliver their message, which is “Don’t do jobs you’re not paid to do.” NYPD, Inc., response time is quick in public places in Manhattan, so they’ll take off after attempting a few blows, because they don’t want to be around when the cops show up.
