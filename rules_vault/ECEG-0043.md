## Posted by: Lanyap

New Orleans magic is often thought only as Vodou. And while there is a rich tapestry of magical traditions that have found their home in the Crescent City, Vodou is deeply tied into the city’s history. There are many groups throughout the city that aid practitioners; it is up to the practitioners to fi nd the group whose strictures and goals match their own.

- **Free the enslaved:** When someone seeks to escape an oppressor, you must help them be free.
