**lin’s stomach. All dangerous magical groups are referred to as “red” sects, but one stands** out as worse than the rest.

**Eliza wasted no time in reorganizing, and soon a small following in the city turned into** what is now a real threat. The city has never been safe outdoors at night, especially near graveyards or in the slums, but now there are threats popping up in other places. This Red Tide is proving just as dangerous as its name implies. Sometimes it’s people in crowded, well-lit areas are lured off, never to be seen again, sometimes it’s someone in the middle of a shootout, grabbed from behind and taken, just like that. I’m not sure what the goal with abductions is, but it can’t be good, and the target seems to be mainly the healthy and whole.

If you are thinking of dangerous magic in New Orleans, you are probably thinking Vodou, but that’s an oversimplifi cation. All through the southern part of the CAS and the Carib League, Vodou has followers everywhere. New Orleans reportedly has twenty percent of the populace who practice in some way. Now, if you’re from New Orleans, then you know that Vodou isn’t evil. It’s picked up a bit of reputation from years of misunderstanding and the occasional over-the-line practitioner. For those in the know, most of Vodou is benign, and much the small portion that isn’t tends to deal with Petro loa. Not all Petro followers are a problem and not all problems are Petro, but there is a connection. Ultimately, the truth is more complicated, but knowing who’s more likely to be a danger is helpful.

The Zobop doesn’t publicly allow Petro followers, but they have a backside to the organization that forms a socalled “Red College” led by their Black Pope. They have been a minor player in New Orleans for a long time, but in recent years, Mama Eliza took charge of the _Marée Rouge_ , a faction in New Orleans that deals with the worst of the Petro as well as death magic and raised it up to be a full chapter in the Red College, gaining a title for them: House of the Tide. Mama Eliza has been granted a seat there, and she supposedly has the ear of their leader directly.
