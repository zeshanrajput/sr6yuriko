## **Offensive Capabilities**

**Aztechnology and their puppet nation Aztlan have one of the strongest militaries and covert ops/ shadow networks on the planet, and they haven’t been afraid to use them. Before and during the Week of Death, Aztechnology’s elite Guerreros and Blood Daggers special forces units were deployed against high-value or diffcult targets in the Western Hemisphere, as well as select parts of Africa and the Mediterranean.**
