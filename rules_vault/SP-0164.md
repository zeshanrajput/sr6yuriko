## **MET2000**

**Unlike most other mercenary units, MET2000 has enough political and legal power to avoid the current contract snafus. This leaves them free to take on whatever contract they wish. But the thing is, they haven’t taken any new contracts in the last ten months and are letting all current contracts lapse.**

**Before the Aztlan-Amazonia (Az-Am) War, MET2000 (or** _**Mobile Eingreiftruppe 2000**_ **if you’re in Europe) was the apex of mercenary commands. They literally had it all: contracts spanning the globe, access to bleeding-edge weapons and gear, and troops in numbers that rivaled those of most countries and corporations.**

**Rumors persist that both Aztechnology and S-K have offered very lucrative garrison deals (which is frankly overkill for a unit like MET) but so far have been turned down. Likewise, MET2000 has been reportedly courted by several private individuals who are negotiating hard for “unspecifed duties.”**

**But during that confict, something happened. A lot of bad decisions were made by those in command, which effectively wrecked the entire unit. After the war, most insiders considered the unit shattered and were ready to write them off.**

**That was until General Angela Schmidt wrangled the unit’s copyright (yes, that is a thing) and managed to reunite most of the shattered pieces in 2074. Since then, MET2000 has risen like a phoenix and reclaimed the top merc spot once again; punctuated by their spectacular airborne assault on Ares Tower which helped end the Battle of Detroit in late 2080.**

**So far, MET2000 has refused them all and is for the most part standing down.**
