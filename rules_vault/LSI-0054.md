## Escape Route

There’s always a way out—you made sure of that. An escape route sets up a dramatic exit from your residence, hangout, shop, or other specified location. Discuss with the GM where these escape locations are and what their nature is (hidden tunnel, secret door, trap door, etc.). If you are near your escape route, you may spend five Edge to escape along with any allies who wish to join you. Unless you are grappled, even if you are surrounded or in the midst of combat, you and your team get away clean.

**Cost: 1 LP each**
