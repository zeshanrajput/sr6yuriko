## Kensai Okama, Sony: Director of

## Game Innovation, Simsense Division

**Description:** Male Japanese human, slicked-back grey hair, perfectly tailored Berwick suit that highlights his angular frame. He speaks quietly but has a powerful presence.

**Possible Party Missteps:** He is very professional, but he is recording everything via his internal simrig. The footage is going to his internal commlink (rating 6). He is exceptionally observant and manages to move around the room and be in the right place at the right time a lot. He plans to use the footage to accurately portray executives in upcoming productions, but he also keeps the “special” data on other powerful figures, just in case.
