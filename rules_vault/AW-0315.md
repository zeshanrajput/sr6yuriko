## Getting Around

Air spirits have an especially easy time getting around in the Place of Clouds, as they are naturally capable of flight. Most other visitors are subject to something similar to the laws of gravity, and they require some other form of transportation to get from one island to another. Single passengers often hire air spirits to carry them across the skies, but larger groups (or travelers with significant amounts of luggage or cargo) prefer to travel by airship.
