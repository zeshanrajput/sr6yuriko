## Blubber

Thick layers of insulating fat and vascularized tissues are inserted or grown, and they act as thermal insulation. Grants a +1 Edge bonus when resisting any cold-based attacks or damage. Negates the Chilled status (p. 51, _SR6_ ) from all non-magical causes.
