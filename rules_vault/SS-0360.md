## Returned Soldiers

The only clear information about the soldiers returning to Earth, including those from III Corps, is they spent some time in the metaplane of Dis, and it generally altered them in some way. How they got to Dis and why they were there remain unclear.

There is significant disparity in these soldiers. Some of them have been gone for a long time, others for mere months. Some have intense memories of their time away, others have barely any memories of all. Some come back barely altered, while others are drastically changed. But we’ll start with what they have in common.
