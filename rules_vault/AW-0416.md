## The Wilderness Of Miggon

Outside the city of Hubris is a vast wilderness. Some of it resembles the blasted deserts of Nevada and New Mexico. Very little cover or plant life is available, and you can see for kilometers. Take care to avoid being noticed by the many strange toxic and mutated creatures that dwell here, but be most vigilant for the sounds of roaring engines, as these mark the approach of the Crimson Carnage.
