## **Destroyer Chimera**

**I am unsure what creature was used as a template for the Destroyers, as they resemble nothing**

**so much as a Tyrannosaurus Rex from Earth’s ancient past. Perhaps Dis acquired the results of some project to reconstruct fossilized DNA or these could be created from some type of similar creature native to another metaplane. Another source that crosses my mind is draconic, but I shudder to think of that possibility.**

**Regardless of the source, the smallest—or lesser—Destroyer chimeras are the size and mass of a car, with the largest roughly on par with a three-story brownstone. The size, toughness, and raw strength of even the smaller constructs is more than a match for a full squad of all but the most elite operators. Making the situation worse, these creatures are highly intelligent and often hunt in packs, communicating through Mindspeech and employing tactics typically seen in military units.**
