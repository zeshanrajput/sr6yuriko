## **Special Notes**

**This is a straightforward, feel-good run in which the players get to be good guys. If they fail to locate the kids in time, they risk running into MCT forces, who show up in a pair of Ares Drag-**

**ons to extract the children. If they take longer than an hour, the kids will be gone with little hope of rescue, as they’ll be brought to an MCT holding facility.**
