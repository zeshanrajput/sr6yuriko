## **Evo Security Mage**

**Only one magician is on-site, and they leave most of the work to their conjured spirits. In an emergency they’ll move to the security station on sub-level 1 to coordinate a security response. If their spirits are disrupted, they’ll conjure replacements. For their stats, use Street Shaman (p. 88,** _**SR6**_ **), but as aspected conjurer with no Sorcery skill or spells.**

**Security mages maintain spirits of air and beasts to watch for signs of intruders outside of the buildings. The spirits will not willingly enter the buildings unless the security mage explicitly orders them to. Except in combat, the beast and air spirits normally buy hits on Astral and Perception tests (beast spirit buys 3 hits, air buys 2).**

**The spirits are unlikely to react to mundane intruders, though they will assense anyone approaching the facility to detect any sustained spells on them—particularly Illusion spells. They’ll report any detected Awakened threats to the security mage, including astrally perceiving or projecting magicians, spirits, dual-natured beings, sustained spells, and foci. The security mage then reports this to the supervisor, then orders the spirits to attack the intruders. If the security mage suffers 6 or more boxes of drain, they’ll remove themselves from the action and remain in the security monitoring station. Unless the player characters enter the security station, the security mage won’t interfere with them.**
