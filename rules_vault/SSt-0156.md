## **Know Your Enemy**

## **Patrol Officers**

**The most common type of cop for runners to encounter is a patrol offcer (or “beat cop”). For budget reasons, they’ll usually be alone or have a drone following them around; if you see two patrol offcers in the same car, one’s probably a trainee. To a runner, these guys are the padlocks of the police world. They exist more to give the general public reassurance that someone’s “looking out for their safety” than to be a meaningful barrier to us doing our jobs, because nobody who stops to think about it really believes that Offcer Schmuckatelli with his armor vest and small-caliber pistol is going to be a match for a team of heavily armed “domestic terrorists” (which sounds a lot better on the news than “shadowrunners”).**

**Patrol offcers are assigned to a specifc area of town, where they patrol (hence the name) and respond to any suspicious activities, including emergency calls from the public. That makes them the most common kind of cop for runners to see, if not actually interact with.**
