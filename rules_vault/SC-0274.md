## DRONE RIGGER

The drone rigger prefers to stay in the van, but not exactly. Their spy drones provide vital information, and their combat drones are always just a moment away in case things go loud. Drones are expensive, so most prefer not to use them for frontline combat. Supporting a team of shadowrunners and being the one with the big-picture perspective are what the drone rigger does best. When it does come to combat, they are capable of bringing to bear a staggering armory of weapons.

Plot hooks involve acquiring and upgrading drones, finding a place to set up a shop, gaining contacts, taking on an assistant or apprentice to watch the shop, searching in a dangerous salvage yard for parts.

Quality paths focus on using drones, both by remote piloting and also by integrating them into an overall network, making them work better without jumping into them.
