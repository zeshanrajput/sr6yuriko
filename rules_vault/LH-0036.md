## Current Disian Operations

In keeping with their current defensive posture, the Disians appear to be fortifying their most important assets, such as the Harvester Network, the Antarctica facility, and their main metaplanar gateway facility (known as Site-000) in the former town of Dutchville, Pennsylvania, UCAS. And while these operations have been largely relegated to proxies in the law enforcement and security sectors, the Disians are continuing to target Defenders and decrease our numbers even further, or at least disrupt our operations against the Harvester Network and other high-value targets.
