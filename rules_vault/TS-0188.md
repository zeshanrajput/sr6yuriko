## **Ambitions**

**Some contracts are being negotiated now or will be in the upcoming years. In 2084, the contract for Stuttgart will be negotiated again, and Sternschutz doesn’t want to lose their home turf to some rival. It’s questionable if they can manage to come to good terms with Horizon and the artist quarters in the city, though.**

**Within the AGS, they also have ambitions to secure contracts for Cologne, Bremen, and Dresden. Time will tell if they manage to gain a foothold there and expand their infuence farther to the north.**

**When it comes to international contracts, Sternschutz applied for the Barcelona contract and is negotiating two additional contracts within Switzerland. Bern and Basel would expand their infuence over the small country.**
