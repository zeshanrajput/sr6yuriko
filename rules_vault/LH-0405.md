## Wuxing: Bloodied Flower

Wuxing, along with Aztechnology and MCT, was part of the triumvirate of pro-Disian corporations when the Week of Death kicked off and officially started the Disian Shadow War. They were the ones who created the Divine Map, which detailed the ley lines across the globe and indicated where exactly the Harvesters should be placed for maximum effect. Being experts in geomancy, they were uniquely suited for this task. The problem was, this task and their alliance with the Disians, went against everything the corporation stood for.
