## **Bazel**

**This free spirit of earth is a few trees short of a forest. They got hooked on the tarot early on and have been part of several card-hunting coteries. They’ve endured better than many of their peers and now hold high esteem within the Echoes. Word on the street is they have some of the best connections and have claimed and utilized the most cards, but their impatience in gathering them in conjunction rather than using them individually has kept them from doing anything world-bending. Thing is, they have had the most cards stolen from them as well, so work for and against this nutter is on the table.**
