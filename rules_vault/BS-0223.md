## Rhino

This is derived from a mutated version of the Epidermodysplasia verruciformis (EV) disease, or bark treatment. It is thicker and more durable than the bark dermal alteration. The character’s leather-like skin grants a +3 Defense Rating bonus cumulative with any worn armor. Cannot be taken with bark.
