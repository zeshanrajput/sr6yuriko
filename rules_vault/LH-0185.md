## **Set of Four Tens:**

While all minor arcana have prices on them (between 10,000 and 30,000 nuyen), there’s a bonus offered for all four Tens together. They’re listed at 25,000 nuyen each, but a full set payout is 150,000 nuyen: well worth the effort to track them all down. The problem is these cards are already being hunted by runners; it seems that the four cards keep

changing hands and nobody wants to turn any in until they can acquire the other three. So far, the most anyone has gotten at the same time is two.
