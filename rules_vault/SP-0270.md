## **Who’s Who**

**The enemy shadowrunning team is heavy on muscle but has at least one mage and two riggers. For the two trolls, use the street samurai archetype (p. 87,** _**SR6**_ **). For the riggers, use Humanis Paramilitary Rigger (p. 143,** _**Firing Squad**_ **). For the other muscle, use Humanis Paramilitary (p. 142,** _**Firing Squad**_ **). And for the mage, use the Combat Mage archetype (p. 83,** _**SR6**_ **). Challenge can be adjusted by adding or subtracting NPCs.**
