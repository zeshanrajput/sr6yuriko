## Ares Antioch II

The Antioch II is the fanciest tube with a trigger around, complete with an integral smartgun system. A secondary trigger setting allows for wireless activation of launched projectiles.

**Wireless bonus:** You can use the wireless link trigger for your grenades, even if you don’t have DNI.
