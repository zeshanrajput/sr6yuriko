## **Is the Vault of Ages (p. 165) legal for** _**SRM**_ **?**

- Yes. For _SRM_ , we only track the potency column and ignore the total column.

- **Are there modifications to the adept powers (p. 170-172) to make them legal for** _**SRM**_ **?** Motion Sense requires an Observe in Detail test to perceive.

Multi-Targeting adds it’s dice pool before splitting. It doesn’t stack with Anticipation.
