## Dikote

Through a process that deposits a thin diamond coat on a sharp surface such as a blade or arrowhead, the cutting and edge-retention abilities of an item can be enhanced. The process to apply dikote involves brief exposure of the base material to a plasma furnace, which limits the process to sufficiently durable materials like steel and ceramic.
