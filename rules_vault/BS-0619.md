## Ares Deinonychus Bio-Drone Prototype

These bio-drones are designed to operate in packs for reconnaissance and surgical strikes. They forego the exoframe augmentations used in their larger cousins in favor of muscle replacement that improves their physical prowess to make up for their smaller size.
