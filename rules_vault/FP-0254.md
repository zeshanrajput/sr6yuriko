## **Eagle Security**

**This Oceti Sakowin-based security organization, which also operates in the CAS and UCAS, may seem like trouble for us at frst glance, but we’ve pushed a lot of money their way and they usually look the other way. Obviously, we need to be careful when dealing with them, but overall, they are fairly trustworthy.**
