## **Activation:** Passive

**Prerequisite:** Kinesics (p. 157, _SR6_ )

An adept with Kinesics Mastery is a master of body language, nonverbal communication, and both sensing tension in others and hiding their own. This power provides two benefits: First, it allows two adepts with Kinesics Mastery and line of sight to one another to relay complicated, detailed information to one another without outsiders noticing (essentially holding a full conversation in secret). Second, every level of Kinesics Mastery increases the number of times per encounter that the Kinesics power grants Edge (p. 157, _SR6_ ).
