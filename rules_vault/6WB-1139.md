## Extendable baton

This telescoping club can be extended with a wrist flick or a wireless signal. In its retracted form, it’s easier to conceal (Concealability threshold 4 when retracted, 2 when extended). Retracting or extending it is a Minor Action.

**Wireless bonus:** You gain a bonus Minor Action on a turn when you retract or extend the baton.
