## social test edge general

**EDGE TO TALKER**

Talker is introduced by trusted person Listener is disposed to listen to people like the talker Talker’s appearance is perfectly suited for their role Listener has romantic attraction to talker Listener sees clear gain from what talker is saying Listener’s thoughts and/or emotions are being controlled by magic
