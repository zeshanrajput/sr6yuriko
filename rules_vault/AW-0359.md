## Divining Lotus

When we first arrived at the Great Lake, our shaman Bandit flipped out. When she calmed down enough to be comprehensible, she pointed out that the flowers growing near the shores were divining lotus flowers. This rare plant can be found on Earth, but the sheer abundance of them here is unprecedented. This plant is highly valued by shamanic and hermetic magicians alike for its properties as a reagent and its usefulness to diviners (initiates with divining metamagic).
