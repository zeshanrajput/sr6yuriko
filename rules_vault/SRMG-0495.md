## **APPENDIX D: SRM ERRATA**

## **How will the SRM Guide Committee handle the errata issued from the Errata Team?**

To maintain continuity across Missions events, the SRM Guide team will sometimes clarify rules prior to the SR6 Errata Team issuing a ruling on them. Sometimes these are rules that the Errata Team has no intention of changing or clarifying since any incongruencies are meant to be handled by GMs at their respective tables. Any Missions ruling in the Guide is not meant to be seen as Official Errata, though GMs are welcome to make use of them for their home tables.
