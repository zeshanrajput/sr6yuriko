## **How does Symbiosis (6WC, p. 128) interact with SRM play?**

In _SRM_ , Symbiosis does not automatically grant the Home Ground positive quality. Your neighborhood is considered healthy while you maintain a Lifestyle of Middle or higher. If you are using the expanded Lifestyle rules, then Neighborhood and Security need to be at Middle or higher.
