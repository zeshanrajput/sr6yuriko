## Security devices

## Key Lock

Metal keys, tumblers, and a whole lot of confusion for that hacker holding a sequencer in their hand. Key and combination locks are old-fashioned and used where tech hasn’t advanced or the bosses are penny pinchers, and they are a relic of the past kept around because they’re cheap. To bypass a key lock, make an Engineering + Agility (Rating) test. They have a rating between 1 and 6.
