## Override

This power allows the sprite to override control of a device operating on autopilot. The sprite targets a device, making an Electronics + Level vs. Device Rating + Firewall test. If the sprite succeeds, the sprite gains total control of the device and can substitute its Level for the Pilot rating of the device.
