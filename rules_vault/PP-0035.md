## The Future

In a word: expansion. With both the space elevator and the mass driver up and functioning, and interest in Mars and the moon growing, the Habitat has a role to play as a waystation for visitors going to and from those areas. At its present size, it can only serve that function in limited form, but adding more residence space could make it a popular hotel in space. Security questions remain, as many high-ranking corporate executives have no desire to see a significant volume of people so close to the heart of the Z-OGB and the world’s currency.
