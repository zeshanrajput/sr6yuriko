## Two-Faced

Sometimes loyalty means a lot less to some people than to others. They trust you to do business, but when the deal is done, it’s back to just biz. If there’s a chance to make some extra nuyen off selling you out, they may think about it for a second, but they’re probably going to take that money and run. Worse yet, they won’t regret it.

**Game Effect:** The contact acts as if their Loyalty is 2 lower when resisting an attempt to influence or con them into giving up information on those they’re connected with.
