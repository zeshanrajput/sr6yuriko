## Password Sequencer

(Illegal) Cracking + Logic vs Sleaze + Firewall (Minor)

You are a master of preventing your opposition from taking actions. On a successful test, the opposition’s screen or view is completely occluded by a giant animated ASCII skull and crossbones. The target cannot take any action until they have successfully completed an Electronics + Logic test where the threshold is equal to the number of net hits from the Skull and Crossbones Attack action.
