## Pantophobia

- **Vector:** Slot, Wireless

- **Speed:** 1 combat round

- **Duration:** (Rating) minutes

- **Power:** 10

- **Effect:** +1 Reaction, –1 Intuition, Panicked

This ripjack sends images of all common phobias as well as neural signals to trigger a flight response. This inflicts the Panicked status for the duration of the attack and (rating) minutes after the ripjack has stopped.
