## Utility and Support

In addition to your drones, you most likely have a relatively large vehicle to carry them around in. Just because you’re the drone rigger doesn’t mean you can’t provide transportation for your team. You are very good at piloting almost anything, you just think of your vehicle as another one of your drones.

Besides playing chauffeur, your most important role during a run is having eyes (and all kinds of other sensors) everywhere. Your Perception skill is almost as important as Piloting or Engineering. You can scout locations, watch for signs of trouble, discreetly tail a target, and search a large area with unmatched thoroughness and speed. If you take the Outdoors skill with the Tracking specialization, it will help you solve many problems that arise on a typical shadowrun.

Additionally, your technical expertise is an incredible asset. If you need to build, modify, or even subvert some tech to achieve a run objective, you’ve got the tools and skills to pull it off.
