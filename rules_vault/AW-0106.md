## Bise

## (Air, Force 5)

Bises are gentle, fluffy beasts looking like tardigrades made of clouds. They waddle their six legs across the dense flatus fields, sipping on choice gas combinations. They are not without defenses, as a grumpy bise can go from happy cloud to thunder cloud. This is mostly as a deterrent against opportunistic predators.
