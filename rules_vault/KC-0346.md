## Sliver (human, female, 26, black hair, blue eyes, lean build)

“Meticulous violence” is not a commonly used phrase, but it is an excellent description of Sliver’s style. She came over to the shadows after a short stint working solo as an assassin but found the level of stress and perpetual isolation not to her liking. She brought her precision killing skills over and avoids collateral damage when possible.

Sliver just backs Silver’s play but from a bit closer. It’s not her favorite place, as her expertise is at range, but if she needs to pull the trigger up close, she will.

When the fight comes, she kills. With precision. She’ll make sure to find a pleasant vantage and make use of that amazing PSG range. She kills directly and precisely.
