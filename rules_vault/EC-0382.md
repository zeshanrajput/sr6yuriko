## Neon K(illing) City Sites

## Master Blaster TridArcade

A huge, converted warehouse for larger-than-life trid entertainment complete with hot-sim support. It’s the jewel of Salvadore’s businesses, which he boasts is independently powered by a methane biofuel produced by his own herd of wild swine. Wild pulled pork sandwiches, a local delicacy, are available with drink purchase.
