## Game Information: Atavism and Transgenics

Transgenic infusions are available at character creation as well as throughout a character’s career.

To take a transgenic infusion, player characters may only select groups of modifications from one type of animal. The exception to this is if you have taken the positive quality Chimeric Soul, which allows characters to take a group of modifications from a second species (bat ears and fish gills, anyone?).

When characters take an initial genetic infusion, they must also select the negative quality Inhuman

and at least one other negative quality. These flaws can be found listed with specific infusions, but an additional suggested list of new qualities is available on p. 104. Negative qualities from the _SR6_ core rulebook can also be taken (p. 74). Talk with your gamemaster to determine what negative qualities would be most appropriate for your character. Note that the limit of six qualities at character creation still applies. Transgenic infusion should not be taken lightly, as it may involve roleplaying mental and physical health issues (like cancer). We recommend that players talk about these decisions with their gamemaster and group during character creation to determine what would be the right fit for your play style.

A character may only have four transgenic infusions in total, including repeated infusions of the same type.
