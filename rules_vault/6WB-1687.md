## On Sokarenallee (B1) Just Before Kreuzhain, 23:22:06

Somewhere in the hail of gunfire and Molotovs in Marzahn, Zoé had lost her pursuers. At least for the moment. She put her cab on automatic while

she crawled into the back seat to scan the small cargo box with a chemsniffer. “According to initial investigations, the bomb that destroyed the traveler’s hotel “euleXia” on Kurfürstendamm was delivered by a SINless from the former East Zone” was not a newsticker message she was keen on.

The scanner beeped green. “Nothing found, I swear, wallah, habibi, trust me.” She scrambled back into the driver’s seat and tightened the shockpad safety belts across her chest as the two striking brick towers of the Oberbaum Bridge raced by outside. Arrival at the shadow harbor and meetup with whomever Blitz could muster in eleven minutes.
