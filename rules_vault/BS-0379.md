## Grey’s Resistor

- **Vector:** Injection

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** (12 – Body) minutes, minimum 1 minute

- **Power:** 10

- **Effect:** Temporarily grants Magic Resistance ( _SR6_ , p. 72) positive quality

Where blight was an accidental discovery, Grey’s resistor was intentionally created based on how effective blight was in combat situations. The research involved many test subjects, and luckily for those on the street, the lethality of the drug has been reduced through refinement. Users gain the Magic Resistance positive quality (p. 72, _SR6_ ) for the duration. When the drug wears off, the user resists (Power) S damage and gains the Nauseated status for the same duration. If the user has the Magic attribute, their Magic is reduced to 0 for (12 – Body) hours, minimum 1 hour on crash.
