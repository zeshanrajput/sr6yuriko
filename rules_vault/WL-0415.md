## **Eager to Please**

**This quality is a very common trait among domesticated canines. The critter enjoys spending time around their metahuman handlers and is highly motivated by praise and treats.**

- **Cost: 2 Karma (1 Karma for dogs)**

- **Game Effect: When this critter rolls Willpower + Intuition to oppose a training test, only 6s count as hits. On any Infuence test made to issue commands (including complex orders) to this critter, reduce the threshold by 1.**
