## Spiritual Guides and Characters

## Lord Surt

Surt is the lord over Tramontane and the longest-reigning ruler so far in the Fire Metaplane. As a khamsin, he is stereotyped as a barbarian and nomadic thief. But keeping the position he has for so long speaks to his mental shrewdness. Like Genghis Kahn, Lord Surt is educated in the social nuances of some of the other metaplanes so that if he wanted to, he could partake in his own diplomacy in Korikyu. Lord Surt has instead been working on reinforcing his own kingdom and territory.

- His ego continues to build. There are rumors that he’s been looking to harness astral energies to put Tramontane into a larger independent metaplane beyond a cardinal pole. While the brothers were successful in creating their own moons in the shadow metaplane, they are the exception to the rule.

- Glitch

- There have been a few attempts at assassinating Lord Surt. While both air and fire elementals have been trying to deal with the thefts around Tramontane, neither sound like they would take such drastic measures.
