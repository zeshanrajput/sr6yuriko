## Replacement Limb

For those who lose a finger, a foot, or an arm, modern biotechnology can allow for the growth and natural replacement of the limb without the deep dehumanizing cost that comes from a cybernetic arm. Type-O replacement is the norm, but a more expensive and less disruptive cultured version is available for those willing and able to pay the price.
