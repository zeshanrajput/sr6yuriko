## Facility

A facility needs a building and is immobile because of the bulky and heavy machines involved, but it can be used for very advanced constructions and modifications. A facility offers the same bonus and parts as a shop, as well as a bonus Edge, and Edge can be used for Extended tests within the appropriate facility.
