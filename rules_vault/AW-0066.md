## True Air

True Air, or longfeng (dragon wind), is the rarest of the true elements. The only con firmed location on Earth was in the chaos of the mana storm surrounding Tibet. Longfeng has the appearance of a smooth-shaped, aerogel-like material

With pieces no bigger than a couple hundred cubic centimeters that only weigh a gram, Longfeng is translucent white and exceptionally cold to the touch. In the elemental Plane of Air, it is impossible to find such material naturally. Unfortunately, the only known location for longfeng is within the heart of the Octahedron, where her majesty’s wrathful tantrums sometimes coalesce into strands of the material. You’d just have to survive long enough and tactfully snatch it before some royal punkah does.

- Such manifestations are like royal jelly and punishable if found in possession.
