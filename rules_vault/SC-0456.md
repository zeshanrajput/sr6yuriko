## Organized Crime

- **Common life modules:** Adept Training, Blue Collar Laborer, Bodyguard, Courier, Entrepreneur, Fixer, Investigator, Lawyer, Magical Studies, Matrix Security, Medical Doctor, Nurse, Organized Crime, Pragmatic Magician, Security Guard, Servant, Smuggler

The largest organized crime syndicates are not merely multinational but transnational. Their interests and activities exist regardless of borders and any defined legal delineations. At their most base level, syndicates still rely on the personal touch you only get with honor-bound goons using physical or other violence to achieve their goals. The social adept is likely to have wielded force—from intimidation and coercion to trickery and diplomacy—to establish and maintain relationships and activities. Eventually, however, violence is required.

The syndicates are very traditional, even if that tradition is founded on the rejection of “legitimate” society. Over time, elements within the syndicates have incorporated or outsourced magical activities. As a rule, they are not generally tolerant of magic any more than most are of metahumans or anything other than male humans.
