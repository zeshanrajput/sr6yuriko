## Standard Skills, Training, and Experiences

The social adept usually has some or all of these as base abilities: Cool Resolve, Improved Ability

(Social), Improved Mental Attribute, and Kinesics/ Kinesics Mastery. Initiates will have adept centering and will probably invest in Infusion for additional Edge.

Social adepts are typically considered to be the liars and schemers who use their high skills in Con and Influence along with abilities like Kinesics and Cool Resolve to deceive and manipulate targets into acting against their interests. However, there are many social adepts who work as investigators hunting down spies and infiltrators, performing pen testing to identify and neutralize social engineering weaknesses, and making other social adepts’ work more difficult. These adepts will have Empathic Sensing, Iron Will, and even Astral Perception in addition to their other abilities. The counterspy social adept has an extensive skillset and training that has led more than one to excel and ascend to the heights of megacorporate and state powers.

This gets to the training and experiences at the heart of most social adept backgrounds. The younger the character, the more likely they are to have professional training to hone their adept abilSOCIALities, as that training has improved steadily over the past few decades.

Beyond their abilities, the standard training and experience for a social adept will focus on attributes of Charisma, Willpower, Intuition, and Logic, along with Con and Influence skills.
