## **I really like the idea of a kama adept but kamas aren’t listed in any sourcebook, is there a way to get them?**

With GM approval, you can ‘reskin’ a simple melee weapon. Nothing with incorporated electronics or sophisticated mechanics are valid for this purpose. For example, you can purchase a combat knife and describe it as a kama while keeping all of the stats for the combat knife.
