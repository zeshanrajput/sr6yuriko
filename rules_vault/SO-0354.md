## Gear and Qualities

If a player character has gear or qualities that specifically grant them Edge on a particular test, don’t forget about it! In most cases, these are very specific Edge awards. Sometimes the Edge must be spent on the test that generated it, though sometimes it may be banked for later use. Whatever the case, pay close attention to what gear and qualities grant Edge. It may make the difference between success or a critical glitch.
