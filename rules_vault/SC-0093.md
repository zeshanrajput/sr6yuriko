## Home Ground

The character moves to this part of the character path after living in the community for three ingame months and earning at least 8 Karma defending the community from various threats. In this time, they should be deepening their relationship with their mentor spirit and with the community. They may purchase the Home Ground quality (p. 72, _SR6_ ) for only 6 Karma. The quality applies to the community where they are now living.
