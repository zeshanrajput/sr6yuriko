## **The Dis Situation**

**We’re all aware of how many people are looking for information about what likely will soon be known as the Dis Crisis instead of the Dis Situation. Some of this is more about accounting than the sort of information gathering the Tiding specializes in. Determining the number of functioning astral gateways and their locations could be useful, but that’s more a matter of boots-on-theground legwork than the type of deep dive that is the Tiding’s specialty. The Tiding would be far more interested in solving mysteries such as: What exactly was the price Dis negotiated to make III Corps disappear? Who did they negotiate that price with? What are the levers that can be used to push change within Dis? Can they be persuaded to leave our plane alone, or do they have to be defeated?**

**Those are big questions to ask, and extreme options may be taken to protect some of the answers. It’s risky work for the Tiding but also quite lucrative, which to me means there is no way they** _**won’t**_ **be involved in it.**
