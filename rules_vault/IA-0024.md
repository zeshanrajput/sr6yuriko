## Fomorian 

The most elegant of the troll metavariants, fomorians are still not at all petite. Most measure within ten centimeters, plus or minus, of 2.35 meters, and they lack dermal deposits. They have tusks and horns, though their horns tend to be more slender than those of the other troll variants. Their higher level of acceptance by other metatypes leads to fewer isolationist tendencies.
