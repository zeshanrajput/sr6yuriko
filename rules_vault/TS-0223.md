## **Parashield Zapgun**

**Paras hield has long been a leader in non-lethal weaponry, and their dart guns loaded with Narcoject can quickly incapacitate most targets. There are times, however, when you need a nonchemical option for a nonlethal capture. The Zapgun utilizes a pulsed energy projectile that vaporizes inanimate material it strikes, creating a plasma-burst fashbang designed to stun the target. The Zapgun uses a compact rechargeable battery pack that holds 10 charges; when connected to a power source the battery pack regains one charge per minute.**

**Wireless bonus: The Zapgun recharges by induction, regaining one charge per hour.**
