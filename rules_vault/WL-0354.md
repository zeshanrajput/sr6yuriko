**If you are attacked by these things, you’re going to get bitten, so be ready for it. If you get swarmed, I hope you or your buddies brought a lot of stun grenades or fame throwers—otherwise, you’re going to be dead.**

**(Threskiornis molucca quisquiliae )**

**Life survives in harsh environments all over the planet. These environments have spawned whole new sub-species. I don’t believe any of them are as hated as the Australian trash chicken. The trash chicken is an Emerged version of the bin chicken, which is a very adapted scavenger that evolved from a white ibis. Despite their somewhat unappealing scavenging habits, trash chickens are a common sight in many Australian cities, particularly around parks and other public areas where they can fnd food scraps or large groups of people.**
