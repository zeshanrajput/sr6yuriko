## Trading in Miggon

Bones and scrap are the most common currency within the Wastes of Miggon. Each bone or unit of scrap can be traded for equipment or services with a value approximately equivalent to 100 nuyen. These are considered worthless outside of Miggon. If scrap or bones is offered as a bribe or a gift as part of a social skill test, it grants you a situational point of Edge. Only one Edge can be gained per test in this way, but multiple gifts or bribes can be used if more than one social skill test is required.

If you are trying to purchase a specific item from a trader, make a Charisma + Edge test with

a threshold equal to the item’s Availability. If the test succeeds, the item is available and may be purchased with bones or scrap equivalent to its nuyen value (one bone or unit of scrap = 100 nuyen). A Kr’u’Ba heart is worth 1,000 nuyen, though the True Fire it contains is worth much more to enchanters.

The bone traders accept bones at their full value, and the Yaggil and Yasmal prefer scrap. If you trade bones to a Yaggil or Yasmal, two bones are valued the same as one unit of scrap. If you trade scrap to the bone traders, two units of scrap are valued the same as one bone.

If a character wishes to barter with some equipment that they brought, it is valued at 1 bone or unit of scrap for every 200 nuyen of its listed cost.
