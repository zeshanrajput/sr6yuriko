## Ill Tidings

The PCs are not the only people interested in DJ’s actions and what made him act the way he did. A group known as the Tiding (see **The Tiding** sidebar) has heard about DJ’s day, and it fits with some other information they are gathering, so they would very much like to have a word in private with him. Tiding magpies are aware of Rikki’s reputation as a voracious data tracker, so they have been watching him to see what he knows, which led them to his safehouse. Tiding magpies are preparing to quickly and silently extract DJ.
