## **Closing the Noose**

**When a police offcer contact for the runners decides to issue a be-on-the-lookout (BOLO) call, they have a reach and infuence equal to their Connection. This will temporarily increase the Heat for the entire team by their Connection, which will last for a number of days equal to their Connection. The offcer can only issue this once per month. Any effects of the increased Heat take effect at the end of the active period of the temporary increase. For instance, if the Heat hits 10 because of this, the runner’s lowest-rated SIN is burned.**

**A detective who’s more interested in gaining the person as an informant is less likely to take away some of their gear, specifcally the ones that prevent the Emotional Analyzer from working. A runner with a knowledge skill in Police Procedures is able to use this to their advantage during any attempt by the law enforcement offcer to bring them over to their side, gaining a temporary point of Edge for use on those tests.**
