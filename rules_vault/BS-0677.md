## **>** Bull

The final area of growth in gene tailoring is the reconstructive efforts being led by Aztechnology to cherry-pick traits from non-human species and install those traits in human hosts via transgenics. This is not new science. We’ve seen commercialization of claws and tails and other such extremities. What we have yet to see and will see in the coming decades are humans with traits from emergent species such as drakes and nagas. The technology to make this happen is presently under development, though there are barriers due to the peculiar nature of these organisms.
