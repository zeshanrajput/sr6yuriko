## **War on Several Fronts**

**Despite their recent losses, Grey Cell continues to move forward with their worldwide anti-Disian operations, albeit with a bit more caution and moderation.**

**Even with the patronage of a great dragon and all that entails, Grey Cell always suffered from manpower shortages. Even with the creation of the cell network and subcontracting through mercenaries and shadowrunners, Grey Cell could only hope to successfully engage the Disians on a limited number of fronts. This meant that Grey Cell had to prioritize what threats and fashpoints they would deal with frst. And given the nature of some of the Disians’ operations, I’m guessing that was a tough call to make.**

- **Do we use our strike options to take out a high-value Faustian scientist or do we continue to let hundreds of people in the same area be kidnapped for Disian experimentation? The latter seems obvious until you realize that if you take out the former, it may lead to ultimate success sooner.**

- **Those are always some of the hardest calls to make.**
