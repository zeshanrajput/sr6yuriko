## Awakened New York

I’m no magic-user. Truth be told, those types give me a strange feeling inside. If anyone like me is walking around the streets of New York, they’re bound to be feeling particularly strange right now. New York is becoming a flashpoint for all types of weird. If what I hear is true, that weird might be pouring in from another dimension.

New York is home to several magical societies. Some, like the Union Club, masquerade as social clubs. Others are more upfront about their nature. Just because a society exists in the city or holds real estate doesn’t mean the group is openly accepted. Thunderbird Vanguard, an offshoot of the New Revolution, are considered a criminal organization and are being sought by NYPD for questioning. The Magical Investors Group has avoided NYPD’s notice, but association with the group is seen as a quick way to lose your job in the corporate world. Meanwhile, other groups like the Dunkelzahn Institute of Magical Research are widely accepted and integrated into the social structure of the city.
