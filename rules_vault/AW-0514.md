## Terrain

**Open Space** : Typically an open void without any visible ground or an ocean without surface or floor. Residents either fly, float, or swim, and any structures or other elements of the environment must do the same.

**Scattered** : Inhabited or usable areas are scattered and disconnected from each other. These may be isolated mountains floating in the void, cities built on the backs of gigantic ocean-going beasts, or huge fungi-lit caverns connected by long, twisting passageways.

**Plains** : The terrain of this type of metaplane is open and flat. It could be an endless grass-covered plain, a mirror-smooth waveless sea, or a flat expanse of smoking lava and barely solidified stone.

**Rolling Hills** : This type of gently undulating terrain may be enormous swelling waves that never seem to crest, idyllic forest land spread across gentle hills, or an ancient city-state built on an endless number of low hills with wide valleys between.
