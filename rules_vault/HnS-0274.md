## Data Liberator

You have a strong, driving personal value that data should be made public, or at least the data that powerful people want to keep secret.

- **Bonus:** 2 Karma

- **Game Effect:** You may not gain or spend Edge on any test related to selling valuable data, and you may not participate in a Teamwork test for the same purpose. If you release valuable data with the approval of anyone who participated in the run in which you acquired it (which usually involves compensating them with nuyen or favors), you and everyone on your team who did not request compensation gains one bonus Karma. This

Karma award only applies once per run, and only if the value of the data is worth at least 5,000 nuyen.
