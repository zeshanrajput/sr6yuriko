## Crossed Physics

It is natural to wonder why physical descriptors are, or even can be, used for a place which is non-physical (at least in terms of how we recognize physical mass). For the mundanes in the group: the metaplanes are as physical as the astral, or to use something you’re more familiar with, the Matrix. It’s incorporeal, insubstantial, impossible to touch with your hands. It has to manifest through the transfer of energy from one dimension to another, and it’s rare that it takes a lasting physical form.

- Well, not exactly, since you can’t channel energy from the Matrix into physical space, but that’s a whole other kettle of kelp. Let’s just say that the theoretical weight of any metaplane cannot be measured by even the finest scale.

- Ethernaut

Now, your standard spirit can manifest by virtue of energy expenditure, and a mage can visit their home plane by astral projection and a metaplanar journey. While there, time flows differently and reality conforms to the rules of the land, like a perceptual filter in the Matrix. You might be a raging knight in one plane, a cute stuffed caterpillar in another, and so on. There’s a lot to play with. metaplanar travel, much like easier astral travel, experiences a time dilation effect. That is to say, in the astral, you’re moving much faster than in meat space. In the metaplanes, you’re moving at some

extraordinary speeds. You pack more journey and experience into the physical meat-time you were away. Moreover, the tangible nature of the planes is only thus because you’ve become energy, as well. Your body is still sleeping in a coma-like state back home on Earth.

The exception is traveling through a metaplanar rift. Ares’ portal tech isn’t the first time we’ve encountered them; you may well remember the DC Rift after Dunkelzahn’s assassination, and certain greater guidance spirits or even free spirits have been known to possess the ability to open a rift to alternate planes. In these circumstances, the person physically steps through the rift, which effectively converts their entire being into spiritual energy compatible with the destination.

- You realize how incredibly far-reaching the implications of this are? The concept of matter transmission or transformation is limitless!

- Plan 9

- Ares for sure knows.

- Elijah

- And look where it’s gotten us.

- Nightfire

The Hive (and we can theorize other deep metaplanes share this quality) remains so intensely distant that the conversion process of the portal simply isn’t sufficient to provide a total transformative experience. That is to say, the rift cannot attune a metahuman to the Hive so completely that it is no longer a spiritually hostile environment. It may be that the realm is simply too alien, or the distance so great there isn’t enough power left over for complete attunement. That being said, this is good news: if it were possible to open such a matter/energy stable rift, the bugs would simply flood into our world unabated, no need for hosts. That which slows us stops them, more or less.

- I’d like to say right now how intensely fascinating this all is. For a very long time, I’ve been happy to marvel at technological innovation but have continuously found myself tried for patience in regard to metahuman understanding of magic. This, on the other hand, really is where none of us have gone before. You should all be proud.
