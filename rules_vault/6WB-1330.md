## Datasofts

Datasofts are like mini-library sections. Each holds a database on a specific subject (i.e., a Knowledge skill). An appropriate datasoft provides you with a narrow version of a Knowledge skill—for example, since Seattle Gangs is a Knowledge skill, a datasoft might cover the Ancients or the Halloweeners, or might just focus on Puyallup gangs. The datasoft is then used the same as a Knowledge skill (p. 97).
