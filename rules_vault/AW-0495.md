## Shadow Amulets (Amulet of Zheng He)

Another magical device that has its origin as a copy of an ancient artifact, shadow amulets are said to be patterned after the Amulet of Zheng He. These devices hide the wearer in a way that works exactly like the Conceal power of a spirit, but the effect only seems to work while on the Astral Sea and only benefits the wearer. As with other items of this type, shadow amulets have an astral form that can be carried into the astral plane. It does not require bonding to work and only needs to be worn to provide its benefit to any user, even the mundane.
