## Horizon

Horizon doesn’t just chase the latest trends—it shapes culture for its own ends. This requires an immense amount of data gathered on an unimaginable scale, and that information is considered more accurate when obtained candidly. Pigeons are a common sight in most urban areas, and so Horizon has developed the Paparazzi Pigeon, which is capable of gathering sensor data and transmitting to its hosts. Despite the name, these augmentations are also applied to other common birds, including songbirds and crows. Smile for the camera!

In most cases, a nano-critter sensor array is applied to thousands of birds, which are released to go about their business, transmitting their gathered data once per minute.

The upgraded version includes a nano-critter bio-drone harness, which allows for directly controlled flocks of feathered spies.
