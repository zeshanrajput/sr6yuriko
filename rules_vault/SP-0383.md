## **Portal Generator: Game Rules**

**Portal generators will open a two-meter-diameter portal between two metaplanes that allows physical travel in both directions. Opening a portal requires 6 Essence from an Awakened metahuman, but the portal will only stay open a few seconds. Using additional Essence will keep a portal open for one minute per point of Essence used.**
