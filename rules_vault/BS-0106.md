## Cranial Sculpting

Aural Helix Dysmorphia (AHD) is recognized as a metagenic expression, but those who accept this augmentation gain anthropomorphic facial features through cybernetics rather than SURGE. Avian, bovine, canine, caprine, equine, feline, leporine, and vulpine features are all common examples among cosplay and body modification enthusiasts. Even more extreme sculpts, such as cephalopodan and insectoid features are also possible, but users should be wary of being mistaken for hybrid-form

insect spirits. The unusual anatomy resulting from this augmentation places a ten percent markup on any worn face or headgear (see **Using Unadapted Gear** , p. 247, _SR6_ ).
