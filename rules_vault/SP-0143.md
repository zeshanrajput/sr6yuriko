Despite being utterly disgusted by the fact that Aztechnology knew about the Disians for so long, Renraku has decided to focus their energy (for the time being) against MCT and Shiawase, considering it a “matter of honor.” But Nakatomi is determined to “remove all Disian infuence” from the material plane, which means that Aztechnology only has a temporary reprieve.**
