## Terms and conditions

The use of Fast should be carefully supervised, as death from starvation or dehydration is possible without the subject recognizing the symptoms.

Successful casting of the spell removes the feeling of hunger and thirst for twenty-four hours. Each additional hit reduces the Fatigued status caused by starvation and thirst by one level (see below).
