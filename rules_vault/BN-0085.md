## Ma sterful Pounce

**Requirement:** You must have at least fi ve Minor Actions per round.

You are capable of making a sudden pouncing attack that takes your opponent by surprise. First, you must spend a Major Action to attempt an opposed Agility + Stealth vs. Intuition + Perception test. If this test succeeds, you may spend an additional fi ve Minor Actions to move up to ten meters to your target and make a close combat attack, either to grapple or using your natural weapon. The target may not gain or spend Edge on the defense or soak tests against your attack.
