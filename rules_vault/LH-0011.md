## Meet Our Enemy

Here’s a quick brief for the new people about who we’re facing.

Reports indicate that for the past several decades, a race of extraplanar beings known as Disians have covertly infiltrated our world for the purpose of stripping the life-giving energy (a.k.a. mana) from it. Composed of pure energy, they use bio-engineered bodies when away from their home metaplane. By our standards, they’re considered Awakened, and our manasphere heightens their magical abilities because it’s apparently more potent than other metaplanes.

Their primary modus operandi is convincing denizens of the target metaplane who have the right skill sets or are in positions of power to assist them. To accomplish this, the Disians offer the thing every being seems to want: power. Knownas a “boon,” this power is sometimes metaphorical, such as money or influence, but typically it’s literal power. Disians offer these boons to those deemed worthy, who in turn pledge their loyalty and become bound to a Disian patron. These acolytes, known as “Faustians,” have a special crystal embedded somewhere on their bodies that allows them to become conduits of their patron’s power.

Through these boons (or the promise thereof), the Disians create networks of Faustians and allies to create or adapt existing technologies from the targeted spot to siphon the mana back to their home metaplane. It’s a textbook example of a dark cabal and conspiracy rolled into one.

When guile and manipulation don’t work, or the opposition simply needs to be eliminated, however, the Disians deploy their most potent weapon: the chimera. These literal monsters are beings taken from targeted metaplanes and … altered using Disian technology and metaphysics. Some chimeras are deadly and grotesque shock troops, designed for indiscriminate destruction and slaughter, while others are sleeper agents that look like completely normal metahumans.
