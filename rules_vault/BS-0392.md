## Soothsayer

- **Vector:** Contact

- **Speed:** 1 minute

- **Duration:** (12 – Body) hours, minimum 1 hour

- **Power:** 8

- **Effect:** Stun damage, –3 Willpower

Soothsayer is a decade-old interrogation drug that mixes several different types of plants, including the gympie-gympie ( _Dendrocnide moroides_ ), which uses tiny nettles to inject the drug through the skin. This causes excruciating pain, making the subject more susceptible to interrogation. The subject resists (Power)S damage. If they fail to resist all the damage, they also suffer from –3 Willpower. The major drawback to this toxin is that the subject builds a tolerance to the pain, reducing the DV by 1 each time it is used.

Spell stripper is a new toxin derived from _nerium oleander magus_ , an Awakened variant of the oleander plant. Initial research on the chemical compounds did not show any practical impact other than causing nausea. Later, researchers observed that the plants were avoided by Awakened animals and spirits. Individuals affected by this toxin may find their sustained or quickened spells canceled. Spirits hit by it may be disrupted. All quickened spells or sustained spells on the victim must resist toxin Power vs. Drain Value of the spell x 2 as defined for Dispelling (p. 147, _SR6_ ). Spirits hit by this drug need to resist banishing of toxin Power vs. (spirit’s Force x 2) as defined for Banishing (p. 147, _SR6_ ).
