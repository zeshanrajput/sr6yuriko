## **Weapons:**

**Bite [Close Combat, DV 5P, 12/—/—/—/—]**

**Quills [Exotic Weapons (Quills), DV 3P 6/10/—/—/—]**

**Note: When a traptor hunts, anything passing within ten meters of a burrow must pass a Stealth + Agility (3) test or it will be detected (not necessarily attacked) by the traptor. Toxic traptors also have quills. The quills are generally shorter than thirty centimeters. They increase the individual’s Defense Rating by +2 in any form of Close Combat. Additionally, when grappling or subduing a toxic traptor, the attacker must resist DV 2P.**
