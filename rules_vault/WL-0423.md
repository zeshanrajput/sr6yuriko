## **Distrustful**

**Critters are often very wary of metahumans, especially when they haven’t been raised in captivity. • Bonus: 3 Karma**

- **Game Effect: You must pay an additional point of Edge whenever you spend Edge on behalf of this critter’s tests or use Edge actions that affect this critter.**
