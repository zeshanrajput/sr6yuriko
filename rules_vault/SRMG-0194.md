## **From Power Plays, I am a Fashion Influencer, can I buy gear for all my friends (p. 158)?**

Nope. The point of being a Fashion Influencer is that it is special because you are wearing it. These outfits are tailor made for you and the brands want to see you in it. You can’t buy for others or share your wardrobe with others.
