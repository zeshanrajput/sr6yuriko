## Goals 

The Security Special Group is, on paper, a magical special forces and an initiatory group working for Saeder-Krupp Prime’s troubleshooting and security wing. In practice, they are a force composed entirely of true or bred drakes, trained by Scale to bring overwhelming might to bear wherever Lofwyr needs or wants them.
