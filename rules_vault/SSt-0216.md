## **Exterior Doors Are Great**

**The more exterior doors your building and your unit has, the better. If things go sideways, you do not want to be trapped inside a structure—you want to get to a spot where there are lots of directions you could go, making pursuit more diffcult. So while taking an elevator to your nice enclosed hallway feels luxurious, the ground-foor apartment with a front and rear door can provide a lot easier escape. If you really want to live in a taller building, make sure there are multiple easily accessible staircases, or better yet, a fre escape you can access from your unit.**

- **Remember to check whether an alarm goes off if you use any stairwell doors. You don’t want that. If such an alarm is in place, have a hacker who likes you put in a bypass for you.**

- **> Netcat**

- **A fire escape with a flying drone that can carry you, or a jet pack, or a flight suit, are great for a fast getaway. Just try to get something like another building between you and your unit, or you might get snipers taking potshots.**

- **> Pistons**
