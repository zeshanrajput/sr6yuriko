## Flashlight, Low-Light

## Extended Clip

Extended clips are meant to be the balance between having extra ammo on hand and not completely blowing your stealth profile by using drum mags—although larger extended clips stand out just as much as drum magazines, if not more so. Extended clips come in two styles: rating 1 and rating 2. Rating 1 extended clips offer a fiveround increase in ammo capacity, while rating 2 clips offer a ten-round increase. But like with any non-standard weapon clip, extended clips are more difficult to conceal, especially when loaded into a holstered weapon.
