## Key to Grunt Stat Abbreviations

**B** Body **A** Agility **R** Reaction **S** Strength **W** Willpower **L** Logic **I** Intuition **C** Charisma **M/Rs** Magic/Resonance (not included for any NPC that doesn’t have one of these attributes) **ESS** Essence **DR** Defense Rating **I/ID** Initiative rank/Initiative Dice (the base Initiative rank and the number of Initiative Dice they have to roll) **AC** Action allotment (the actions they have available. A: Major Actions; I: Minor Actions) **CM** Condition Monitor (the number of boxes in their Condition Monitor) **Move** Movement (distances for the Move and Sprint actions, along with the per-hit increase for the Sprint test)

the ranks so gamemasters have a few pre-built NPCs with magic or tech skills.
