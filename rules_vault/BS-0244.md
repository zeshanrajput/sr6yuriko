## Hearing Enhancement

This is a basic enhancement of the metahuman ear’s accuracy, sharpening hearing that has lost some fidelity due to damage and possibly restoring it to better than it was before. The user receives 1 point of situational Edge to their Perception + Intuition test for hearing. If the point is not used on the Perception test, it is lost.

This modification is often paired with the biosculpture moderate modification to give the recipient animal-looking, functional ears.
