## Short Barrel

Chopping down the barrel sacrifices some distance shooting, but it makes tucking that beast in around corners a whole lot easier. Not to mention tucking it under your coat. This mod works best with shotguns and assault rifles, but you can feel free to chop the end off that assault cannon if you want!

- Pretty solid mod for jungle work, too. Long barrels get snagged on trees in the heat of things. If you have that kind of LOS on a target, you’re bound to have a legit sniper on deck anyway.
