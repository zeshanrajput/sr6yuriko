## **Do I need to make an Addiction test (p. 109) each time I use my cyberware or bioware?**

For _Missions_ , you don’t need to make the Addiction test for foci, bioware, cyberware, nanoware, or geneware.

**How do I make a Drain Resistance test for Blight (p. 113) if I don’t have a tradition?**

For magicians or adepts that have an adept way and a tradition, they roll their drain as normal. Critters and anything without a magical tradition roll Body + Willpower test as their drain resistance test. Centering is not applicable as this is not a magic test. Magical augmentation to attributes also cannot be applied to this test as the toxin interferes with them.
