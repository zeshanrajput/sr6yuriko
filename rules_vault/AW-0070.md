## **>** Mika

Tramontane has the most secure vaults for a traveler’s goods, with khamsin warriors guarding them. Khamsin warriors are reputed to shun any magician trying to summon one. And woe to the magician who even attempts to bind one; they’ll face the true fire blades of khamsin skirmishers. Speaking of the khamsin, they are an “evolved” form of elemental spirit, as they take aspects of both air and fire. You find them here as honorable guards and protectors of the city. Outside, they are bands of skirmishers who raid and pillage pretty much anyone except their own. Since neither air nor fire will take responsibility, any disputes over stolen goods are difficult to rectify.

- You think wolves of Wall Street were dangerous? Here you

have currency brokers with spiritual insight (both fire and air) fighting over gains.
