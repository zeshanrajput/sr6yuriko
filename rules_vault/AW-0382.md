## Tandoran Intrigue

If the runners are spending some time on the Island of Tandora, some local Tandorans might choose to involve them in their political intrigue. The runners can go about these much like any other shadowrun back home. These runs should be on the simpler side, but they are made more complicated by the unfamiliar environment and culture, the relative lack of modern technology, and thelikelihood that none of the runners will be able to rely upon their contacts for assistance.

Tandoran intrigue runs can also be used as a stepping stone for a larger run. If the runners need assistance with finding dinosaur eggs or a rare plant, they won’t be able to pay with nuyen. Unless they have something of value they’re willing to part with, a Tandoran is likely to barter by requesting the runners’ services in return for their assistance.
