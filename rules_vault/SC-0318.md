## Additional character creation recommendations:

- **Attributes:** Charisma 5+

- **Skills:** Athletics 3+, Influence 3+, Outdoors 3+, Stealth 3+

- **Knowledge skills:** Critter Psychology, Paranormal Animals, (Wilderness Area) Knowledge

- **Positive qualities:** Critter Dominator, Critter Trainer, Home Ground, Resistance to Pathogens, Will to Live

- **Negative qualities:** Impaired (Logic), Social Stress, Trust Issues, Uneducated

- **Life modules:** Guide, Hunter, Mentor, Traveler, Event: You struggled through some tough times
