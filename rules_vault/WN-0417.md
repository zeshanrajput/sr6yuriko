## Aftermath

The key pieces of information from the meeting are the names of the participants and the affiliations of Bellflower and Driess (note that Blake is there to represent both his own interests and those of his partner, Miles Lanier, but he will say nothing to reveal the identity of his partner, or even that he has one). If the runners did not figure out the clues from the end of the meeting, a contact with Government and Corporate knowledge can clue them in. If they did not catch the exchange between the two, they have a harder road ahead of them, as they will have to dig around to find out their affiliations. This should require significant asking around and the outlay of a large sum of money, as those are not secrets that come cheaply.

If the PCs hear the final exchange between Bellflower and Driess, they receive a 15,000 nuyen bonus from the Mr. Johnson who hired them.

Once the PCs know that the CIA and Mitsuhama play a substantial role in overseeing Animus, the stage is set for this campaign’s endgame.
