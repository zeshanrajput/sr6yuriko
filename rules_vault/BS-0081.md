## Cosmetic Implants

Some of the very first implants ever created were invented to enhance a user’s physical appearance. Gel implants have enhanced breasts, biceps, buttocks, and many other anatomical regions for over one hundred years. Modern cybernetic implants are capable of dialing the physical enhancement of one area of the body up or down as the user desires. In addition to its obvious advantages in getting dates, a cosmetic implant system grants bonus Edge for tests made to disguise oneself, or to impersonate another individual. It takes a Minor Action to alter the physical size of the augmented region or body part.

**Wireless bonus:** Gain a bonus Minor Action after spending a Minor Action to alter the augmented body part’s size.
