## Vomit Comet

- **Vector:** Slot, Wireless

- **Speed:** 1 combat round

- **Duration:** (Rating) hours

- **Power:** 9

- **Effect:** –1 Body, –1 Reaction, Nauseated

The victim of this ripjack is assaulted with the worst sights, smells, and tastes in a blitz of sensory overload. The target receives the Nauseated status for the duration of the ripjack.
