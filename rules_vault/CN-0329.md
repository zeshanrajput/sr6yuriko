## The Free State

## of California

Interestingly, Wuxing has moved in to fill the vacuum, with a major inYAKUZAvestment in the city, pushing nuyen around to win the populace over. I don’t think that the Yakuza will react well to Wuxing on “their” turf, so this could get ugly.
