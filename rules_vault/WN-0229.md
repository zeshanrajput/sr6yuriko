## 11: Who’s to Blame

## Hook

Piper Jane reaches out to the runners to do some digging that might require a little more finesse and muscle than she can manage alone.

Precinct 10 on the Lower West Side had some recent troubles (see mission 8, **Get Out of Jail Free Card** , p. 60), and rivals from Lone Star want some info on the folks who skated past justice. Some of those folks could be runners, and asking runners questions is a delicate task. But it’s better if questions come from another shadow denizen than from some blue suit or corp cop.

Piper offers the runners 10,000 nuyen to hit the streets and get all the footage they can on Precinct 10 and those who walked. She wants the runners to take that footage and track down some of those folks and see what they did to get snagged in the first place. She’s also interested in anything they may have been up to since they got away.

Someone is looking for a connections between those who benefitted, but discovering that connection is not going to be easy.

Time to reach out to some contacts.
