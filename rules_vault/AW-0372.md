## Black Mana Orchid

The legendary Black Mana Orchid has only been found within the jungles of Central America on Earth, but there are places within the Great Jungle where it grows in abundance. The orchid’s sap is widely used by Tandorans to create powerful stimulants, and the petals can be used as reagents.
