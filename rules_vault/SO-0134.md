## Scaling

The Badger works at any level, and it’s been used as such. Street gangers and cubicle rats have just as much to lose as politicians and CEOs. The difference is in what they have to offer and the kind of security you have to overcome and reprisals you have to escape. Risk tends to equal reward.

- Might want to bear in mind that scale includes time. Longerterm blackmail is dangerous as the mark’s stress increases. But if you apply pressure the right way over an extended period, and if you’re lucky, you may get some additional information to sell. As long as you seem reasonable and don’t push it too far, you can stretch out a useful degree of money or influence. Politicians are known to be fantastic targets for this.
