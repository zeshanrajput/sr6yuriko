## **Psyche as an Avatar (Kokopelli)**

**In many ways, an Avatar is to an initiate what a Paragon is to a submerged. They are a strengthening of the ties the Awakened has with their magical paradigm and tradition. Even adepts can have an Avatar that often serves as a guiding force or voice in their journey of self-development.**

**In terms of game mechanics, Psyche (Kokopelli) can be chosen as a Mentor Spirit, with distinct similarity to Fire-Bringer (p. 164,** _**SR6**_ **). As Kokopelli, Psyche is the bearer of songs, language, and seeds by which the valleys of the world may be made fertile and prosperous. This goes so far as to include the seeds of knowledge that help bring inspiration, drive us to perfect our pursuits, and motivate our-**

## **Advantages**

**All: Reduce the cost of learning a language by 1 Karma point for each level of aptitude.**

**Magicians: +1 Edge when casting any spell that increases a mental attribute. +1 Edge when conjuring a spirit to use their Aid Alchemy or Sorcery abilities. At the completion of that service, the spirit is released regardless of additional services or tasks it may owe.**

**Adepts: +1 Edge for any test involving the Logic or Intuition attribute, chosen when the quality is selected. For those adepts who have developed an adept way (p. 76,** _**Street Wyrd**_ **), they gain an additional +1 die to their pool when performing any test associated with their Spark of Brilliance beneft. This does not include drain tests.**

## **Disadvantages**

**Those who have chosen Psyche will not utilize Intimidation or seek to destroy anything of an intellectual nature, requiring a Composure (3) test to be involved in any such action.**
