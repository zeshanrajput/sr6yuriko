## 03-A Metaplane Features

Dis is located near what metaplanar cartographers call the Plane of Shadow, although the Earth Elemental Observatory and Water Elemental mapping of Dis show different locations. Accounts vary when describing Dis—some describe a world of shadows, others a red-hued sky with no night or day, others a neverending sprawling black or turquoise metropolis with a black moon always hanging above. When asked where specific things or locations are, Disians will simply say things like “here” or “there,” as if that’s the only explanation necessary.

While not outright hostile, Disians aren’t welcoming either. Travelers to Dis are greeted with suspicion. If they pose no threat, then the deals start. The natives do everything they can to take advantage of newcomers with hoaxes and scams galore. Nothing is free, and don’t expect charity— everything must be paid or bartered for.

- Also, make sure you bring your own provisions. There’s little on Dis that metahumans can sustain themselves on.
