## Conjunctions and Eclipses

The metaplanes are not static in their relationship to one another other or the physical realm. The interaction between the shifting energies creates events known as conjunctions and grand conjunctions that can occasionally make some metaplanes easier to travel to. Apply a –2 modifier to the distance number during a conjunction or –4 modifier to the distance during a grand conjunction. As an example, the Hive chapter (p. 76) notes that there is currently a grand conjunction between the physical realm and the Hive. On the other side of the scale, events known as eclipses and grand eclipses temporarily obstruct travel to some metaplanes, making it more difficult by adding +2 or +4, respectively, to the distance number. What metaplanes these events affect, when they occur, and how long they last, are at the complete discretion of the gamemaster.
