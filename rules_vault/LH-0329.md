## dmso

Well-known as the compound that makes the Ares Super Squirt line of weapons effective, dimethyl sulfoxide (DMSO)—toxin combos can also be used in a few other weapon systems, specifically vapor guns and aerosol grenades.

**Game Rules:** When mixed with a drug or toxin, DMSO adds the Contact vector to that drug or toxin. DMSO mixtures require time to penetrate the skin and any substance that normally has an Immediate Speed instead has a Speed of 1 Combat Round. There is no additional time added to the Speed of slower acting drugs or toxins.

DMSO gel pack ammo used in the Ares Super Squirt contains one dose of DMSO each and are designed to have one dose of a drug or toxin added to each gel pack. This requires an Engineering + Logic (4, 10 minutes) test per 10 gel packs prepared. Mixing and loading DMSO / toxin combos for other types of systems requires the same test, so loading and pressurizing one aerosol grenade (20 doses) or a vapor gun tank (40 doses) with a DMSO / toxin mix is the same test as prepping 10 gel packs. Loading aerosol grenades or vapor gun tanks requires a chemical engineering tool kit.
