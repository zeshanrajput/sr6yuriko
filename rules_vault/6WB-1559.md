out a rider. The rigger interface is what allows a rigger to jump in and actually feel as if they are the vehicle instead of a metahuman. Hands and feet become wheels, wings, or rotors; sight, hearing, touch, and taste become sensors; a revving motor is a racing heart. All drones come standard with a rigger interface, but vehicles need them installed unless listed. Without the rigger interface, a rigger can control all vehicles from their captain’s chair, but they can’t really feel them.
