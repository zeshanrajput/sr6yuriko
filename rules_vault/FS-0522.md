## The Soldier’s Code

- Must obey orders from superiors

- May not loot the dead or allow them to be looted

- May not lie to superiors

- Must maintain honorable conduct and obey the rules of war

- May not harm or torture prisoners

I’ve had the pleasure of serving with a number of fine soldiers in my career—and many others, at least one of whom lurks here on JackPoint. (Nah, I’m kidding. Rifleman’s good people, he just never takes me anywhere nice.)
