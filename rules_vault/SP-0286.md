## **Special Notes**

**Once the double-cross begins, a team of NPC runners joins the fght against the MCT forces after two combat rounds. Their primary mission is to place explosive charges on the crates, but they’ll also aid the players in escaping unless the players decide to try to kill them.**

**After the frst group of MCT enemies are defeated, more will start to pour out of their barracks, with more combat drones as support. It should be clear that the best course of action is to make a run for it, but a particularly tenacious team of runners might be able to take down the site as revenge for double-crossing them.**
