## Built-In Utility Kit

A cyberlimb can house any assortment of specialty tools. A kit can include tools such as drills, clamps, saws, or virtually anything else required for technical tasks. A built-in utility kit must be selected for a specific skill and functions as a cybernetically controlled tool kit (p. 273, _SR6_ ).

**Wireless bonus** : The user’s mental control of tools for delicate work is superior to manual manipulation. Reduce the cost of an Edge Boost by 1 (to a minimum of 1) for a test involving the builtin utility kit.
