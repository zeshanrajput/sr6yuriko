## Ghost Town

Things went wrong in your neighborhood. It’s just not the same, and no one even tries to live there anymore—except maybe you.

- **Cost:** Special

- **Game Effect:** If something terrible happens that leads to the desertion of your neighborhood, take this quality. You lose the Local Hero and My Neighborhood qualities. You may reduce your Hooder quality (p. 137, _Sixth World Companion_ ) by 1 level for free.
