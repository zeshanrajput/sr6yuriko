## **Power Fist**

**The smallest of the power-actuated weapons, the power fst is built into a vambrace that fastens to the lower arm. A small strike plate sits above that back of the hand and extends out just past the knuckles. When a fst is made, the strike plate is able to make contact with the target instead of the fst. The power fst is only large enough to accept four charges in a clip, and like the power hammer, only one or two charges may be set to detonate on a single impact. While there is no double-sided version available, some users have been known to take a two-fsted approach and use a power fst on each arm.**
