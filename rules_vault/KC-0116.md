## Behind the Scenes

According to Holmes’ on-site records, the monetary cost to retain his services is low but all of these eco-warriors are contracted to work to pay off their debt. These jobs connect directly to notes from Okoro’s law offices and make for some incriminating stuff. They also have a metric fragton of paydata on toxic dumping, labor abuse, materials fraud, murder for hire, and a myriad of other corporate crimes that the perpetrators would likely pay to see erased or turned over to make for an easier defense. All of this is located on Holmes’ commlink (rating 6).
