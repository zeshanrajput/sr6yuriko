## **Anti-Mana Grenades**

**An alchemical preparation that takes the form of a liquid that is often stored inside a specially designed grenade. When the grenade explodes, the liquid saturates the area. If it comes into contact with any living thing—including materialized spirits or Disian vessels—the alchemical preparation is triggered.**
