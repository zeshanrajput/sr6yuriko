## Optional Rules: The Un-Awakening Time of No Magic

July 26 through August 3, all Awakened beings lose access to their Magic attribute and all associated powers. Most suffer from one or more statuses: Confused, Dazed, and Fatigued (p. 51–53, _SR6_ ), with Dazed being the most common but varying over time and other conditions.

There are additional effects that a gamemaster might choose to include in this time period. They are presented at the end of this chapter.
