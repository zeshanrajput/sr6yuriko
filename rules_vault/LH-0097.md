## The Naga Kingdom

Sources I normally rely on for telesma and reagents in the Laotian and Cambodian jungles have confirmed that the Naga Raja and all their advisors have ordered the construction and reinforcement of vast networks of tunnels and mazes within the jungle-covered mountains that are their homes. As part of this command, all their emeralds and clutches have been relocated far away from anything ever seen by an outsider’s eyes. I’ve heard talk that the Disians had dispatched scores of Slither chimera to extract the unhatched, or plant weaknesses in the magical defenses there.

- Resources from Saotome and MetaErgonomics have appeared numerous times in the SAW nations. Ysil seems to have ensured her people are getting priority treatment.
