## Jonathan Blake

Blake has the classic stony look of an S-K Brackhaus, because he was one for several decades. After leaving Lofwyr’s employ, he went on to launch his own corporation, Centurion Services International. Blake’s corporation is stuck in neutral. He lacks both the physical assets and political leverage to be named an AA, and without that distinction, the growth of his corporation will always remain limited.

Blake is a social adept. His ability to manipulate people makes him a valuable asset in the corporate world. He is in New York at the behest of a new ally, Miles Lanier, who is concerned about the emergence of a new threat to this world. After being briefed on the threat, Blake agreed to assist Lanier. The price of that assistance is help from Lanier in getting CSI an AA corporate rating.

Statistics and additional information about Jonathan Blake can be found in the Character Trove, p 143.
