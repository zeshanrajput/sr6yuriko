## **Training a Critter**

**Training a critter takes some specialized skills and the right attitude. The frst mistake that most people make when working with critters is to anthropomorphize them. It’s easy to fall into, even for an experienced trainer such as myself, but do your best to be avoid doing this. Assuming that critters think and act like you do is natural, but it’s incorrect and places a barrier of misunderstanding between you and your critter. If you can get a better sense of what a critter is really like and what motivates them, your attempts to form a bond with and train them will be stronger.**
