**Matrix hares do not interact with the Matrix as personas but instead operate as hosts. When many (ten or so) of them get together, they act as a series of networked hosts and form a system of their own. At twenty hares, those systems merge. At ffty, those systems form an entire subMatrix with hidden gateways to select Resonance realms. No part of accessing the system is easy, and there are extreme dangers.**
