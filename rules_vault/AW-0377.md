## raptor skull game rules

When activated, the raptor skull provides magical concealment to its wearer equivalent to that of a critter with a Magic rating equal to the Force of the focus. When the concealment is broken, the focus deactivates. If the focus is deactivated, it cannot be reactivated again for a period of twenty-four hours. The cost to bond this skull is (Force x 3) Karma.
