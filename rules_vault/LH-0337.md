## medical treatment for diseases

Any disease resistance test may benefit from active medical treatment as long as the practitioner has access to proper equipment and medications (GM’s discretion). To treat a disease, use the Biotech (medical) + Logic as a teamwork-style test, adding a number of dice equal to hits on the Biotech test to the current interval resistance test. Only one Biotech teamwork test may be made per Speed interval.

Some types of infections are easier to treat than others. Apply a –3 dice pool modifier to the Biotech (medical) + Logic test vs Viral infections, and a –6 dice pool modifier vs Retroviral infections. Bacterial infections have no modifier.
