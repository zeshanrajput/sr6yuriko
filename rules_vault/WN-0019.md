## Public Transportation

Few places in the world top Manhattan for public transit access. In addition to thousands of ground cabs (and Jitnee service) and air taxis crisscrossing the city, it provides train and bus service to most points on the island. Danchekker’s Guide to the Sixth World gives New York a Transit Friendly score of 96—highest in the UCAS, along with a walk score of 90 and a bike score of 79.
