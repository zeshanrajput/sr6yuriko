## Operational Tempo

Despite all the losses and inter-alliance drama, Grey Cell has continued to conduct operations at a breakneck pace. Most of the razor teams and operational detachments come from Grey Cell ranks or were trained by them. They’re also Ringmaster’s go-to units for the most difficult, bordering on suicidal, missions.



- It’s worth mentioning that Grey Cell is an umbrella organization. There are several individual units that make up their military, para-military, and irregular forces. Each of these have their own specialties and capabilities. For example, the already-mentioned Sixty-First Independent Rangers are more akin to Delta Force and the Wildcats mixed with the Green Berets, while the Seventy-Seventh IR is more like a traditional Ranger regiment that specializes in direct assault. The point is, while they all fall under the Grey Cell umbrella, they are a diverse group.

- Colonel Cobra
