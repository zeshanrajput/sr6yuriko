## MCT Fox

A pickup truck that’s been made for decades now, the Fox is designed for the back country and areas that never expect to see paved streets. Unlike typical pickup trucks, the Fox is tracked for additional traction and spreading out the weight of its cargo. Its open bed can easily be converted to a covered storage area as well, which is a common sight. To deal with the harsh everyday conditions the truck is designed for, it has a factory-installed mid-grade pilot program and environmental modifi cations based on what market it is designed for. In the bayou, most folks have the water-sealed package to deal with deep mud and shallow water.
