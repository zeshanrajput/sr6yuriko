## The Job

The goal is simple: Deliver the data packet, untampered with, to the Animus Construction lab the players learned about in mission 21, **The Seat of Power** (p. 91). As El Guapo explained, this is a clinic and lab, but it gets a fair amount of data going in and out via delivery. Dropping the data off is simple—they can tell the receptionist they have a message, and they will be told to wait. Af26: OFFter about ten minutes, someone will come get the data packet from them. The real job is to use the time to get more paydata while they’re in the lab. Use the details in the **Hotspots** section to shape how the PCs function in this facility.

The first job of the PCs will be getting past security. The PCs can try stealth, fast talking, force, or whatever else they come up with, though anything too obvious (such as force) has significant drawbacks thanks to the rapid police response time. Poking around the labs, clinic rooms, and offices of the small facility will draw attention, so the PCs will have to be careful. The most relevant information they will find—new info that differs from what they found in the Octagon—will come in the **Special Delivery** section below.

If the PCs are detected and captured, they are in trouble, because they are in a bad place to not be in control of their own bodies. They will quickly be sedated and “adjusted,” meaning their memories of the past few hours will be erased, and they will be dumped in Central Park.
