## Asphyxia Spores

Spores of this genetically modified giant puffball aggressively infest the lungs, making it difficult to breathe and speak.

- **Vector:** Inhalation

- **Speed:** 4 combat rounds

- **Duration:** (6 – Body) hours, minimum 1 hour

- **Power:** 10

- **Effect:** Dazed status, Fatigued II status, Muted status
