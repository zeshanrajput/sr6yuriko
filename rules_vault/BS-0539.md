## Mental Attribute Enhancement

Improving a critter’s mental attributes is much more difficult than enhancing its physical capabilities, but it can be done. Each mental attribute—Charisma, Intuition, Logic, and Willpower—must be improved with a separate augmentation. The critter’s natural attribute is increased by the rating of this augmentation, with a maximum increase of either +2 or double its original natural attribute (whichever is lower). This does not count toward the augmented maximum, allowing the critter to be further enhanced by other methods and augmentations.
