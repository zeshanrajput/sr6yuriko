## Intuition

Your senses begin to fail you as your eyes become swollen, tumors grow over your ears, and your nostrils becomes packed with mucus or pus.

**Game Effect:** All Edge boosts and actions cost twice as many points of Edge on tests using Intuition. If you gain a second Intuition mutation, you always glitch on all Perception tests (in addition to any other results).
