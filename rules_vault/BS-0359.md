## Overdosing

The use of drugs can lead to overdosing, while other addictive behavior like gambling or use of addictive tools like foci or cyberware does not cause overdoses. In higher doses, drugs and other chemicals can reach a level of toxicity dependent on the substance. For each consumption of the same drug while still under the effect of the drug, make a Body (number of doses taken) test. Failing the test, you take unresisted Stun damage equal to the Power of the drug increased by the number of doses taken.

Using drugs to avoid a crash just doesn’t work for these potent drugs. For example, taking a second dose of novacoke while still on the first dose or crashing from the first dose doesn’t increase the duration of the effects unless the drug effects specifically mention it. For drugs that don’t list a duration for the crash effect, they are considered still crashing until they have removed the damage and any statuses caused by the drug.
