## Contact Network

You’ve established a core group of contacts that form the basis of your network.

**Cost:** 9 Karma

**Game Effect:** Choose three contacts with Connection and Loyalty ratings of at least 3 each. These contacts form the core of your network. Whenever you spend Favor Points with one of your network contacts, it costs you one less Favor Point than the listed cost (to a minimum of 1 Favor Point—nothing’s free, chummer). This discount also applies when spending Favor Points to increase a network contact’s Loyalty rating.
