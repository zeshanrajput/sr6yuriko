## Spikes Module

An exterior frame covered in spikes, rotary blades, or even a chainsaw-like apparatus provides a deadly deterrent to anyone who might otherwise want to get close to the critter.

**Game Information:** The spikes module deals damage to anything that touches the critter, and a critter with the critter comm augmentation can be trained to attack using the module in place of its natural weapons. Touching or grappling the critter inflicts its rating in Physical damage, and if the critter makes an attack, the DV is increased by +1 per net hit. Close combat attacks against the critter using a melee weapon with a base DV equal to or lower than the spikes module DV result in a broken weapon. Attacks using cyberlimb close combat weapons deal base damage to the attacker.
