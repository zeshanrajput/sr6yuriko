## Drone Rack

Large cyberlimbs can serve as a launching point for smaller drones. Like vehicular drone racks (p. 143, _Double Clutch_ ), cyberarm versions allow

storage and rapid deployment of a drone, but they can only house one drone at a time. It takes a Minor Action to deploy a drone from an implanted drone rack, but it requires a Major Action to recover and stow it.
