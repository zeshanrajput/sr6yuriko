## Environment

The entirety of the Citadel is an enclosed, welllit, and pleasant environment. In some parts of the keep, and most of the catacombs, it is expected to encounter decades worth of dust. But otherwise, no other protective or environmental equipment should be needed when exploring or making use of the Citadel.

Time within astral quests is always fluid, with no rhyme or reason to whether time passes at the same rate as on the material plane or whether it is slower, and by how much.
