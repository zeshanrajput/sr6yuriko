## **Street and Squatter Lifestyle**

**Living on the streets or squatting in abandoned buildings is a tough, dangerous, and uncomfortable lifestyle. The lack of a landlord provides an advantage when it comes to having pets, though. And most critters don’t mind roughing it—it’s what they’re most likely used to, after all. At the Street and Squatter lifestyle category, there are no particular restrictions or extra costs invoked by having critters live with you. The main drawback is that you don’t have a secure place to lock them in to keep them safe while you’re away.**
