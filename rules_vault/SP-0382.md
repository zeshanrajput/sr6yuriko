## **Portal Generator**

**The original Disian device introduced on the material plane, these machines require vast quantities of mana to open a metaplanar portal, even for a short time. The most readily available source for this quantity of mana is the life energy of an Awakened metahuman. The portal generator will have**

**piping and cabling that connects it to a number of stasis tubes containing Awakened metahumans.**

**These devices operate by creating a bridge that aligns the harmonics of two separate metaplanes. It requires the full Essence from an Awakened metahuman to open a small two-meter portal for just a few seconds. Generally, two or more stasis tubes will be connected to a portal generator, with the additional ones keeping the portal open longer. The Essence from the occupant of a second tube will hold a portal open for about six minutes.**

**There also seem to be new hybrid styles of portal generators being created. These utilize a great deal of Sixth World computer technology in their construction, augmenting the magical Disian technology. These hybrid devices appear to be twice as effcient, allowing the Essence of a single metahuman to both open and maintain a portal.**
