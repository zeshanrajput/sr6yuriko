## **If I am a rigger and I jump into a drone, can I use a Martial Art?**

Yes, so long as it is an anthropomorphic drone which mimics that of a metahuman using Close Combat skill or Engineering (Gunnery) dependent on the martial art. Sorry, but your dog drone doesn’t actually know kung fu. The drones that you can use martial arts with are the Nissan Samurai, Nissan Oni, Shiawase Bi-Drone



Butler, and Shiawase Bi-Drone Man-at-Arms.
