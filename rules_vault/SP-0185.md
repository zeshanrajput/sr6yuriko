## **Confederation of American States**

**To sum up: buyer’s remorse.**

**At the time, having Ares move to the CAS’ capital of Atlanta seemed like the perfect governmental and business coup. The CAS gets the prestige of being home to one of the Big Ten** _**and**_ **would reap the fnancial benefts of such a partnership.**

**The CAS even went so far as to foot seventy**

**percent of Ares’ new HQ building in downtown Atlanta as a way to sweeten the deal.**

**Except that since Ares moved in, they’ve done relatively frag all for Atlanta or the CAS. In actuality, they’ve become more akin to moochers and squatters, taking more than they’re giving back. This, of course, has caused a lot of friction within the CAS government between those who feel bamboozled (love that word) and those getting kickbacks from Ares, who are calling for patience while the corporation fnds its footing.**

**Ironically, this caused the creation of grassroots anti-corporation groups in the country. At the same time, other groups (mostly military leaning) have formed to reaffrm their support of Ares. Before the Week of Death, there were a few minor skirmishes between these groups, mostly shouting matches. But after, the confrontations became bloody, with the anti-corp factions accusing the pro-Ares people of using this situation as a means to take control and turn the CAS into just another corporate puppet-state like Aztlan.**

**And thus, chaos and discord continue.**
