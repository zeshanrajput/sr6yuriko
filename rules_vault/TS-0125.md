## **Reforging Excalibur**

**The Excalibur Division, known to many as the XD, was formed in the wake of the May Day Massacres, drawing together Task Force Excalibur and parts of the National Crime Agency, as well as elements of MI5, GCHQ, SCO19, other police forces, UK Special Forces Command, and other agencies and groups which offcially do not exist. It was a rough beginning, drawing together military, police, and civilian personnel into a single agency. Supply and logistics issues caused tremendous problems initially, and the “corporate cultures” of the disparate groups were markedly different.**
