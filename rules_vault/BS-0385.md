## Nightwatch

(Animu, Beggar’s Gaze)

- **Vector:** Contact

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** 10 x 1D6 minutes

- **Power:** 7

- **Effect:** Grants Low-Light Vision quality

These eye drops were developed through years of research of deep-sea fish and other creatures that live perpetually in low-light environments. The drops grant the user low-light vision and arefrequently used by budget security guards and urban scavengers. The drug can’t be turned off, so exposure to bright lights causes the Blinded II status if the drug is active.
