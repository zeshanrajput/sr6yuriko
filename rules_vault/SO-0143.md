## Effects

The point is, ultimately, to sell something worthless for a bundle. Once they’ve given you what you want, get the absolute hell out of there before they get wise. Depending on the extra caveats you’ve named (“Access to the Akashic Library only happens during the new moon, and our demonstration last night means you’ll have to wait a month …”) you might have plenty of time to break down your IDs and vanish into the night. I suggest something that both limits the MacGuffin’s supposed use and/ or the timetable of its use. Digital devices may have a limit to their hardware, or overuse might invite the scrutiny of GOD, the IRS, or other interestedparties, for example. Magical MacGuffins you can pretty much make up whatever you want.

- It’s not the worst way to set up small-timers who are in the way, either.
