## **Kinder Chimera**

**First observed in Germany, Kinder chimera stand 90 to 140 centimeters tall and appear to be adolescent metahumans. While operating, they use their child-like appearance to blend in. The theory is that the Disians realized metahumanity’s innate bias toward children and engineered an infltration tool to leverage that weakness. This has created a successful tactic for gaining access to secure locations by pretending to be a lost child. Kinder are**
