## The Vibe

Auburn is the very definition of working class. People there take pride in working hard and putting food on the table, providing for loved ones, and being part of a community; after all, getting the job done means everyone has to do their part. Free time is spent on classic working-class activities, like local sports teams, rather than a fancy life. There’s plenty of consumption, of course, but those big trucks get used and abused.
