## Path of the Juggernaut

A corporate hotshot with more ego than common sense fancies themself a big game hunter. They’ve hunted many of the Sixth World’s most dangerous beasts, including piasma, martichorases, and wyverns, and they’ve made trophies out of them all. Unable to simply rest on their successes, they wish to bag North America’s most notorious prize—a ground-shaking juggernaut. Although skilled enough as a rifleman, the would-be hunter will require guides to track down and find their prey for them on the dusty plains of Texas, and act as go-betweens to shield them from desperatelocals who are (rightfully) concerned that attacking the beast will cause more damage than simply letting it bumble about. And, without a doubt, the would-be big game hunter will require help bringing the beast down and surviving its rampage after provoking it.
