## **The Revving Injuns**

**Kerrigan “Mustang” Blue-River-Deer , the leader of the Revving Injuns on Fox Island in Seattle, has been a solid ally for years. He and his gang have been a strong voice for the annexation of the Island by the Salish-Shidhe, pushing for a certain degree of respect of our ancestral traditions and values from the island’s inhabitants, more than the First Nations or the Sly Devils did.**

**Sadly for us, this kind of stopped when Sea Dragon set up shop and they started working for her rather than becoming dragon bait … can’t exactly blame them, and they still discreetly help us as much as they can.**
