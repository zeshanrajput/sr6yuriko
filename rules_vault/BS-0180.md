## White Biotech

This is something I expect you all have some familiarity with—white biotech deals with the industrial world. This field touches a wide assortment of things, including traditional skills like leatherworking, construction material, and the time-honored glue factory to more modern approaches such as petroleum-eating bacteria, mineral-leaching, and air-scrubbing.

This is more the work of heavyweights, like MCT and Saeder-Krupp, and tends to happen on an industrial scale. By and large, we’ll never interact with things such as an acid process that can eradicate granite while leaving precious minerals untouched, so there’s not much here for us to talk about, other than acknowledging A) it exists and B) this is where material for all our stuff comes from.
