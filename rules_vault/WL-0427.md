## **Lover, Not a Fighter**

**This critter has a soft or timid demeanor, which makes them adorable and safe around children— but it also means that they are not very effective when the time comes to fght.**

- **Bonus: 2 Karma**

- **Game Effect: No Edge may be gained or spent on behalf of any test this critter makes in combat. This quality is only available for critters with a natural weapon or a critter power that deals damage.**
