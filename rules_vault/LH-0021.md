## Main Objective: Harvester Network

The primary objective of Project Nemesis is locating and then neutralizing the Disian Harvester Network. All other activities and operations being conducted through this command and our allies will facilitate the accomplishment of this objective. Anything else, unless it rises to the same threat level as the Harvesters, is considered secondary.

Currently, the problem is not in finding individual Harvesters, it’s finding and neutralizing enough of them within a rapidly shrinking window of time to prevent the network from becoming fully operational, and thus able to complete the Final Project. Our intelligence reports indicate that if a significant number of Harvesters can be taken (or kept) out of commission, then the Disians will be unable to create a network strong enough to pull sufficient mana from the material plane _and_ funnel it back to the Disian metaplane. It is therefore imperative that the Harvester Network not become operational, because once the actual harvesting process starts, it cannot be safeCURRENTly shut down … and the Sixth World, the Earth, will die.
