## Game Information

Trust is an important part of Shadowrun. The runners are quietly working behind the scenes with no safety net for when things go wrong. The average corporate wageslave assumes they can rely on the authorities or their human resources to get trusted information or help. Even if the assumed safety net is a lie, there are some social constructs that people can rely on when they follow the social norms. But for those working in the shadows, there’s no one who will protect them when some corporate Mr. Johnson decides that offing them will save some money in the budget.
