## **Vehicles and Drones**

**There is as much variety in LEP vehicles and drones as there is personal gear. There are few purpose-built security vehicles; the vast majority are all standard models that have been modifed to ft LEP roles. The result is that there is no vehicle that is commonly seen in the industry as a whole. Instead, each LEP has a contract with different manufacturers to produce spec-built machines based on one of their production models.**
