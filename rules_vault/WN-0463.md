## Background (Null Sect)

The Null Sect have been presented with new horizon after new horizon and nearly endless opportunities for exploration since the new Matrix protocols released them. The Matrix we use every day was completely new to them just a few years ago. The secrecy and heavy encryption surrounding Animus Construction drew them in like a moth to a flame. Once they acquired the organization’s closely guarded secrets, the Nulls were puzzled by all the effort that went into concealing it. Altering memories seemed like a perfectly reasonable

idea to them—after all, why would you keep data on old assignments after you were reassigned to a new position. They quickly realized that the hidden information was valuable to the biologicals such as the ones connected to Global Sandstorm, MCT, and the Moller Institute. They used their knowledge of Animus Construction, along with other secrets and other services, to acquire power, knowledge, and the equipment needed to explore the outer world.

Today, the Null Sect’s horizon has expanded once again, and their highest priority is gaining access to the new worlds of the metaplanes. Sacrificing an ally is an acceptable price to pay to do that. Specifically, the Null Sect wants to know if the ideas outlined in the Universal Information Theory from the Nexus are correct. Is the noosphere, which extends into the Resonance Realms, actually a universal force? Does it somehow connect to the metaplanes that have a Matrix equivalent and a noosphere of their own? If they can gain access to the metaplane of Dis, can they find a pathway connecting the scattered patches of the Matrix rumored to exist there through the shadowlands of the Resonance Realms and back into the Null Sect homeland? If the information in UnITy is correct, they should be able to enter the metaplanes without resorting to “magic,” whatever that actually was, and have a truly endless number of worlds open to them.
