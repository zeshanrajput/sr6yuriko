## Horizon: Knowing All, Seeing All

During the Disian Shadow War, Horizon was almost as silent as Ares was. We know they were there, and that they assisted, but it’s hard to pin down exactly how. And of course, they’re not saying a fragging thing about it.

The easiest way to describe their involvement is they were the cornerstone of all information gathering and intelligence, which is both simple in concept and extremely complex in its execution. We know they were instrumental in rallying people to the cause that became known as the Defender Movement. We know they helped reinforce intelligence-gathering cells across the globe after the Week of Death. We also know that it was Horizon that used their vast social media presence to monitor enemy activity, counter the Disians’ misinformation, and direct people where they needed to go.

If most of what Horizon did during the war ever comes out, it needs to be studied as a masterclass in informational warfare. But that’s not going to happen anytime soon.
