## Environment

The entire plane is hot, fluctuating between 85 and 115 degrees Fahrenheit (30 and 45 degrees Celsius). There is no actual night, and no sun either, so there is no day/night cycle to influence temperatures. Because of that, at any given time, the temperature could change. Any character physically present must make a Fatigue Resistance test [Outdoors + Intuition (3)] every 6 hours due to the heat. This is not required when in combat.

Due to the volcanic ash that is constantly in the air, everywhere is considered Dim Light.

Time passes at a different rate on the Plane of Battle compared to the material plane. When on an astral quest, each day on the Plane of Battle should take (quest rating) hours. If another method is used, then one material plane day is one and a half days on the Plane of Battle. It should be noted that because there is never a clear sky, it is unknown whether there are any objects passing in the sky. Due to that, in order to tell time, characters may make an Outdoors + Intuition (4) test or Astral + Intuition (3) test to determine how many days they have spent on the Plane of Battle.
