## Glitchy

Unexpected, unlikely, and unfortunate things happen to you on a regular basis.

- **Bonus:** 12 Karma

- **Game Effect:** Whenever you glitch, it counts as a critical glitch, with no hits, regardless of how many hits you may have rolled.
