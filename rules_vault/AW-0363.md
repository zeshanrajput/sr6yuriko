## The Great Forest

Roughly a quarter of the Island of Tandora is a great forest of massive trees, ferns, and fungi that provide easy food and shelter to creatures and travelers alike. The foliage is rarely thick enough to prevent easy passage, even for some of the largest dinosaurs. Sauropods do not venture far into the forest but graze along the edges. Carnosaurs hunt deep within the forest, and most Tandorans use the branches of the great trees to get around rather than risk an encounter with a hungry predator. In general, the dinosaur spirits of Tandora do not eat them, though this might have more to do with the care that Tandorans take in avoiding giving them the opportunity than any inclination on the part of the dinosaurs. Though it was harrowing at times, we stuck to the same method of travel when we ventured into the Great Forest.
