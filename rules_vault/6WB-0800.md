## Admin

Swap the base ratings of two non-zero attributes of your Matrix Persona, even if the numbers are rooted in different devices. See **Personas and Attributes** (p. 174) for more information on Matrix Attributes, and the **Matrix Attribute Adjustments** sidebar (p. 178) for an example.
