## Heritability

The main thing to know is that modern genetech is not going to be inheritable, ever. Much of the therapeutic category of geneware is based on mitigating damage to the genome that occurs over time, correcting copy errors that could occur anytime cells divide. Even treatments that correct inheritable genetic diseases don’t carry over to offspring. The genetic changes caused by augmentics and transgenics are not going to be inheritable by your offspring, mainly because it just isn’t in the best interest of the corporation’s bottom lines.
