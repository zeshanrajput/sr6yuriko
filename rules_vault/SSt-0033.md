## **Detectors**

**Weapons detectors, chem sniffers, cyberware scanners, and other similar sensors can cause problems for you if they pick up your illegal drek. The good news is that they have an even shorter range than motion sensors. The bad news is they’re likely to be installed in the most secure locations, which generally is where you need to go. Bioware skin pockets, smuggling compartments, and shielded containers might get you past them, but they also make your necessary gear less accessible. The best way to deal with these sensors is to either avoid or hack them. I don’t have any other fancy tricks to suggest getting past these, but in my experience those two tactics are usually enough. The most annoying thing about these scanners is that they’re often concealed inside doorframes or the housing of another sensor. There’s no special feld you can detect in order to spot them using sensors, such as with motion sensors and ultrasound—at least, not that I’m aware of. As far as I know, the only way to reliably detect these buggers is from the Matrix—usually by accessing the security network.**
