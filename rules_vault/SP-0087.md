## **Synonymous with “Terrorist”**

**But direct attacks aren’t the only thing Grey Cell has to worry about; they’re also being attacked on other fronts.**

**People tend to forget that Aztechnology is also one of the biggest media conglomerates out there. Since the violence kicked off, Aztechnology affliates have done their best to frame the narrative**

**that this whole mess was a conspiracy of multiple terrorist organizations, striking out simultaneously for maximum effect and confusion. Grey Cell in particular was named as the “ringleaders” of this (and this is an actual quote) “campaign of death and destruction.” Individuals and groups suspected of having links to these terrorists were likewise targeted or are under investigation.**

- **My unit, the 77[th] Independent Rangers, along with our sister unit, the 61[st] , have “officially” been placed under censure and are being investigated by the IMA for allegedly “committing military/paramilitary acts that are in direct violation of the IMA charter.” Other mercenary units and individual contractors are currently in the same situation.**
