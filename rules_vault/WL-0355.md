**From any elevated point, the trash chicken has adapted to feed on the two never-endless food sources that any urban area creates: normal rubbish within the city and people’s recycle bins or trash cans on their commlinks. Trash chickens will work as a team using Back Door Entry to gain access to a commlink. Once access is granted, illegal access will be shared among the group.**

**Skills: Athletics 5 (Flying +3), Close Combat 3, Outdoors 3, Perception 3, Stealth 4**

**Adept Power: Improved Refexes 2**

**Powers: Armor 1, Enhanced Senses (Smell), Essence Drain, Immunity (Toxins), Gestalt Consciousness (10 meters), Natural Weapons (Bite/Claws) Weakness: Dietary Requirements (Mammal Blood/Flesh) Fragile 7, Vulnerability (Fire)**

**Weapons:**

**Bite [Close Combat, DV 1P, 5/—/—/—/—] Claws [Close Combat, DV 2P, 5/—/—/—/—]**

**A trash chicken has a distinctive appearance, with a long, curved beak that has evolved so it can pierce tough plastic and frewalls alike. It has a bald, featherless head, but its body feathers are**



**==> picture [19 x 765] intentionally omitted \<==**

**mostly white, with dark green or orange showing on its wingtips and tail. They are fairly large birds, measuring roughly 0.75 meters and weighing around 1.2 kilograms. They possess the ability to mimic normal urban sounds such as alarms or heavy foot traffc, but their favorite is that of honking vehicles. When feeding offspring, they regurgitate a slurry of physical and Matrix trash.**

**Many think that if any major natural or metahuman-made catastrophe happens, the trash chicken will be well situated to be a dominant species. Its excellent scavenging ability means that our polluted and trash-flled landscape will provide a never-ending food supply for it.**
