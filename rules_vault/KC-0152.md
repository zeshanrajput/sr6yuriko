## At the Investment Firm

Gaia Investments is at the heart of this investigation but does not contain nearly as much evidence as one would suspect. The reason is obvious: no one wants a single location to target if the darker forces of the world come crashing down to snuff out the flames of hope. It does, however, include a wealth of clues and directions for the investigation to head.
