## Larger Tusks

## Functional Tail (Whip)

## **Cost:** 10 Karma

A tail of this nature is usually three meters long and fully prehensile. It stands out for its ability to be used as a Bullwhip, p. 249, _SR6_ . It can perform motor skills in the same way as a Prehensile Tail.
