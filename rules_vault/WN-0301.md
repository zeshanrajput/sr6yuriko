## Who’s Who

## Cheli

Cheli is a Hispanic dwarf in her mid-thirties. She was left on her own at the age of thirteen when her father Klubbs was killed. It took her several years to gather all the details, but she came to grips with what happened. Having no other choice, she took over the family business and has grown it from a lackluster operation into an organization respected within the city. Her father’s death has taught her not to be weak, and she has spent a considerable amount of nuyen on enhancements and training. Use Store Owner, p. 172, _Sixth World Companion_ .
