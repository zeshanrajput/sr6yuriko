## Ticker (dwarf, male, 32, black hair, brown eyes, lean build)

Focusing his arcane talent both inward and outward has left Ticker with a bit of a split personality. The signal for a shift in his states of being is a small facial tic, followed by him making a clicking noise with his tongue. It’s a subconscious indicator that he’s flipping from his mild-mannered monk mentality over to a rather violent madman who makes Heller look peaceful.

While a fight starts, Ticker will be calm and focused, looking for the best opportunity to use his arcane talents. He will summon a spirit to help by using its powers on his opponents, rather than sending it to fight directly. This spirit will be kept in reserve to assist the team in getting away as well. If any member of the team is seriously injured, Ticker flips to a more intense, decidedly less rationale state and begins tearing into his opponents with amped-up spells or bullets. Or both.
