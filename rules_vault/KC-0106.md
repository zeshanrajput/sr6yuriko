## Ayeowo

Known as Hana Orobuku only a few months ago, she has fully embraced her new role as Ayeowo, a prominent hawala in Lagos. She’s in control of the network funded by Okoro and enjoys the adoration and favor she is gaining with the people of Lagos.

As Hana, she was a burgeoning hawala slowly growing more and more indebted to Erika as they curried favor in Lagos via back channels, such as Hana’s old network. Even though she is free of the corporate strings today, Ayeowo is still in favor with Erika, assisting in building the Matrix infrastructure of Lagos. Ayeowo always imagined a hawala network that operated via the Matrix rather than with physical components. She isn’t focused on the fact that a network like that would allow nuyen and other electronic currency to dominate, only that it would make Lagos feel more like the rest of the world.

Ayeowo uses her considerable influence to make this happen, much to the chagrin of her fellow hawalas, who know that a change in the system would devastate the power they hold.
