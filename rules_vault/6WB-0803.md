## Outsider/User/Admin

You probe a network for weaknesses, looking to gain access. By taking your time not to alert any security to your presence, you can create an exploit that may last until you are ready to use it. While not as fast as using Brute Force, Probing a network does not raise an alarm automatically. Even if your attempt initially fails, it will not trigger an alarm unless you glitch. A successful Probe creates a backdoor in the system to exploit.

Once you create the exploit, you may then use the Backdoor Entry action at a later time. Net hits on this test count as a dice pool bonus on your future Backdoor Entry test. The duration of these backdoors depends on the PAN or host—most systems create a changelog and will automatically correct and report differences to their configurations caused by the presence of these exploits. Generally speaking, the backdoor lasts for [10 – Host/Device Rating] hours or until you go offline. This action is linked to the Sleaze attribute.
