## Evo Sonic Disintegrator

## **One Evo’s newest gadgets, the Sonic Disingegrator is a briefcase-sized device designed to attack Faustian crystals.**

**Game Rules:** The sonic disintegrator can be “fired” four times before it needs to be recharged (by connecting it to a standard power supply for ten minutes). Focusing the Sonic Disintegrator on a single object (crystal) is a Major Action that requires an opposed Engineering + Logic test vs. 2 x the rating of the target crystal. If the net hits exceed the rating of the Disian crystal, the target crystal shatters. Damage is cumulative, so several attempts can be made on a single crystal while adding net hits from each test. If the target crystal can’t be seen (for example if it’s inside a Faustian’s body), it’s up to the GM to decide how effective the focusing attempt may be.

Anyone within five meters without hearing protection gains the Deafened status for one minute.
