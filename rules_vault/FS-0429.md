## Parkour

Not strictly a martial art, parkour was developed as a form of quick and effective movement through dense urban environments. Many of the techniques used in parkour can be adapted to martial arts practice, whether to gain a tactical advantage over an opponent or just run the hell away from a fight you shouldn’t have picked.

_Categories_ : Mobility _Signature Technique:_ Rolling Clouds
