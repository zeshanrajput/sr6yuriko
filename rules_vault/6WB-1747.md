## Junk Dog

## (Medium Drone)

Dogs that bark don’t bite. Junkdogs don’t bark—so _run_ ! Most Junk Dogs are patched-together nightmares from the metallic carcasses of downed drones, metal scrap, and all kinds of

unsightly factory and weapon parts. Originally developed for spectacular drone pitfights, Junk Dogs have long since found their place as guard dogs, couriers, or faithful companions for critter and bounty hunters in Berlin. And if one of these rattling rabble-rousers gets you, a rusty bite in the butt is still the least of your worries. Cyberspurs, poisoned fangs, gas canisters, tasers, even submachine guns are possible features. Packs of three to eight “animals” have been encountered in the streets of Berlin. As a rule, they do not move very far from their territory and their owners. Every now and then, however, such an urban predator seems to go off on its own and causes a commotion in the neighborhood.
