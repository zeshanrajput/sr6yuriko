## > Palace of China

A taste of cultural nightclub life, the Palace is done up in medieval Chinese themes but pumps out the latest in HK dance and electronica with imported celebrity DJs. The entertainment includes magical illusions, and they’re not to be missed. The Octagon Triad runs the place, vice both above and below board, mainly BTLs and prostitution, so you know who you’re dealing with. > Red
