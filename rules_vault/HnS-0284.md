## Risking Burnout

If your Resonance ever gets to zero, you cease to become a technomancer forever. The grade improvements from this quality path remain, as does the grade requirement for any further augmentations, but you may not take any more qualities in the cyber-adept path. Many such burnouts turn to transhumanism (p. 146, _Sixth World Companion_ ).
