## **Professional Rating 5: Personal BodyguarD**

**Operatives who prove their ability to expect the unexpected receive additional training and are assigned individually to the company’s most important customers and contracts. In addition to fending off attackers, their job is to see them coming. Their surveillance devices let them scrutinize all the places their clients go, keeping an eye on the strangers they meet and an even closer eye on their assistants. Bodyguards can request a budget to hire extra help, usually shadowrunners, if they feel that their client is threatened by something beyond their ability.**
