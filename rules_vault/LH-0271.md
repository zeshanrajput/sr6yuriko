## Fragging-A!

We’re inside Triple Zero’s perimeter and all hell is breaking loose! MET Behemoth tanks and heavy combat drones took point and literally punched their way through a weak spot in the perimeter fences created by the arty! The rest of us streamed in behind with APCs and other armored vehicles, shooting anything and everything that shot back! We almost got bogged down again, but the Sixty-First managed to make entry and took out several weapon emplacements before linking up with the main force! The Twenty-First _did_ get bogged down on their flanking maneuver by a group of twelve chimeras, but apparently, they turned that into an advantage by creating an actual phalanx and simply slaughtered damn near everything that came their way!

And the fragging lightning has stopped!

Downside is that we’ve lost approximately twenty-five percent of our armored vehicles, and casualties are mounting! We’re … wait … _contact right!_
