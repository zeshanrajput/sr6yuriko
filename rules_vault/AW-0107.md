## Bole

## (Earth, Force 4)

Boles are natural predators from the Earth metaplane. Their appearance is a serpentine form of sand. Their bodies make a hissing sound as they rub their coils against the ground. Unlike other earth elementals, they don’t attempt to engulf, but instead try to squeeze and grind down their victims as they petrify them.
