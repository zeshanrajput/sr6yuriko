## **Ghostwalker: A Dragon Distracted**

**==> picture [233 x 4] intentionally omitted \<==**

## **Posted by: Peregrine**

**There’s been a lot of talk lately about where the various great dragons sit on the Dis issue, but one name is mostly absent from that discussion: Ghostwalker. While I can’t claim to know what a great dragon is thinking, I have been gathering what information I can on his activities. And while I can make some educated guesses, please keep in mind that this is all speculation based on secondhand and sometimes third-hand sources. So don’t bet your life on anything you read here.**

**The fact is that Ghostwalker has his attention on something other than the here and now. Events happening in this world appear to take a distant second—or lower—place in the dragon’s priority scheme. Anyone who’s spent time in Denver or the surrounding Front Range Free Zone during the last few years can tell you that Pale Scales has loosened his taloned grip on the city. The dragon is rarely seen in the territory and seems to have left the day-to-day administration of the fefdom to his underlings. I hear that since the spring of 2079, he has been holed up in his lair, or in the Mile-High Metaplane, or out in more distant metaplanes searching for something. For most Denverites, the absence hasn’t affected their daily lives much, but to the megacorps it was a green light to move in and do whatever they wanted in the territory. Numerous rumors say that MCT took full advantage of Ghostwalker’s absence. Back in ’81, they supposedly set up a series of experiments to move mana in and out of other metaplanes (sound familiar?). Rumors also say that it blew up in their faces—along with a big chunk of the Aurora Warrens. Apparently all that was enough to get Ghostwalker’s full attention, at least for a while. I’ve also heard that Ghostwalker eventually got his claws on all of MCT’s data regarding the metaplanar mana transfer experiments, but more on that later.**

- **I find it hard to believe Ghostwalker would get that distracted. It’s more likely that he faked his absence and leaked the information that got MCT there, simply so he could get his hands on their tech.**

**So, what has Ghostwalker so distracted that he stopped paying attention to the kingdom he worked so hard to build? While I can’t say this for a fact, I believe it’s Zebulon, or at least her memory. And that leads to this question—why the long-running obsession with the former spirit of Denver? I’ve heard a lot of wild stories, and I don’t know if any of them are true—but one of the wildest seems to ft the facts. It goes something like Zebulon was actually a great dragon in the distant past, and she and Ghostwalker were life mates. She came to an untimely end, and Ghostwalker, unable to accept the loss, somehow collected her essence together and bound it into the form of a great spirit, anchoring it to a metaplanar refection of a city near his lair. When magic started fowing back into the world, the metaplane reconnected with the new city built on the site of the ancient one, becoming the Mile-High Metaplane, and Zebulon became the spirit of Denver.**

**Regardless of whether or not any of that is true, it’s undeniable that Zebulon was very important to the dragon. I won’t go through the entire crisscrossing history of Ghostwalker, Denver, the MileHigh Metaplane, and Zebulon, but the city, the metaplane, and the spirit were all fractured into multiple pieces over many years. Ghostwalker constantly attempted to undo the damage—and sometimes he even succeeded for a little while. But ultimately it all proved fruitless; in the spring of 2079 Zebulon died on the Mile-High Metaplane despite Ghostwalker’s best efforts to save her. Ire posted a good summary of events in the** _**Street Wyrd**_ **drop a while back if you want more info.**

**After her death, Ghostwalker spent more and more time in the Mile-High Metaplane trying to stabilize it—or maybe searching for any remaining fragments of Zebulon’s essence. It’s also known that he was searching distant metaplanes, looking for something. I believe he was looking for an artifact or a ritual that would allow him to revive her once more, this time with her essence intact and unfragmented. And then there’s the MCT data on metaplanar mana transfer, which could be the reason his metaplanar searching has stopped recently. The wyrm has been holed up in his lair for months,**



**and the Denver shadows have also been very busy extracting all types of specialists, rare tech, and exotic materials. It all seems to be fowing straight into Ghostwalker’s clutches. I think this is evidence that he has the MCT data and is trying to duplicate the experiments, avoiding their failures, hoping to set up the structures to pull mana into the Mile-High Metaplane.**

**And this is where the subject of Dis comes in. I believe that while Ghostwalker has been distracted enough to ignore the situation up till now, he will soon be coming out on one side or the other. If he believes that Dis has the magical lore to fll the gaps in his knowledge, allowing him to resurrect Zebulon, he has one of two choices: Make an arrangement with Dis and trade his support for the knowledge, or go to war with them and take what he wants by force. Word on the street is that his fxers are hiring shadow assets to collect information on the Disians through datasteals and physical location hits against anyone who has been collecting information on them. There are even offers for teams to go to the metaplane of Dis itself and collect data. I suppose that sooner or later, some key piece of information will come into his claws that will push him one way or the other.**
