## Obvious

(Cost: 350,000, Availability: 6)

| **BOD** | **STR** | **AGI** | **REA** | **ARM** | **CM** | **CAP** |
| ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------- | ------ | ------- |
| 8       | 6       | 6       | 4       | 10      | 16     | 120\*   |

Troll-sized shells in either type cost an additional 50,000 nuyen and get +2 Body and Strength (increasing both their starting value and the augmented maximum), and +4 boxes to their Condition Monitor. They also have an additional 20 points of Capacity.

\*Cybernetic or nanotech augmentations that cost Essence but don’t have a capacity cost can be installed in a shell, using their essence rounded up as their capacity cost.
