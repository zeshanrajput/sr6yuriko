## Informant

Sometimes you have to make a deal just to make sure you get yours, and the powers that be will let you live your life. That deal might be dishing out information to make sure you’re always protected. What harm is it to snitch once in a while if it means carving out your own space and not getting hauled off or killed?

**Game Effect:** If the team reached out to the contact and they gain a Heat Modifier in the related faction’s territory, increase the Heat Modifier by +1.
