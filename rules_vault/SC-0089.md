## Home Sweet Home

The character feels spiritually drawn to a oneblock area that is claimed by a local gang, the Azure Flames. When the character is in the area, they can feel the flow of mana and reach across the bridge between worlds with more ease (+1 Magic, +1 Edge on Magic actions, +1 service on summoned beast spirits). This isn’t specific to them, and the gang has at least 6 Awakened members (Magic range from 2 to 7) who can all feel this power and hence have convinced their mundane leaders to claim the turf.

The Awakened gangers are all feeling the power of the area, but none have explored why the area has such powerful mojo.

The character is immediately drawn to settle in the area and can choose to do so without conflict, but as time passes (each lunar cycle) the power in the area starts to fade, losing one of the listed bonuses each month. All bonuses will be gone after three months if the characters don’t intervene.

Behind the scenes there is a powerful free spirit of beasts that is bound to the region. The spirit is currently trapped in the form of a dog thanks to a powerful artifact (currently in the form of a collar) that can be used to trap spirits and drain their power. The spirit was leashed by a former member of the gang who died shortly after from wounds suffered while binding the spirit.

The gang considers their fallen sister a hero and are enjoying the power from her sacrifice, but they don’t realize how they gained it. As it drops off, they get themselves in deeper and deeper trouble with surrounding gangs as they push their territory but start losing power.

This leaves several avenues for the character to interact.

1. They can meet the spirit, hear its plight, and seek out a way to help it.

1. They can be asked by the gang to help them figure out why they’re losing these gifts.

1. They can seek the source and look for a way to preserve this power over time.

The spirit’s plight powers the mana benefits in the area. Its collar feeds the spirit’s energy into the surrounding mana and provides the benefits listed above. The artifact is draining the spirit each month, and in its current form it has no way of building its strength.

Sadly, the collar artifact used blood magic to link it to four people in the neighborhood. In order to remove the collar, blood needs to be taken from all four people and then poured on the collar. It requires at least a pint from each person, so no little scrape or scrap will do.

Person one is a fifteen-year-old ork girl who walks past the block to go to her school. She’s become skittish of the area after she was drugged and left in an alley a few weeks ago but has no other route. She recently got a rather bulky and old Ares Predator I that she carries tucked in hercoat when she walks through the area, keeping her distance from anyone. If anyone approaches her, she’ll draw the pistol and shoot them, as she also got a handful of Stick-n-Shock rounds to go with it. To add a little flair, the bullets are old and the capacitors are wonky. Include a wild die in any attack using these rounds. She has five of these flawed rounds that can be used in any heavy pistol.

Person two is a joyboy who works the area most nights. He can be found on the corner of the block looking for work. He actually sold his blood to the shaman who bound the spirit and would sell more if offered the right price (2,500 nuyen).

Person three is the Awakened ganger with the Magic attribute of 4. While the spirit can identify all of the others, he can’t specify which ganger without getting a taste of their blood. He hasn’t been able to bite any of them yet but could be persuaded to play the part of an angry dog if the character needed them to test who was the right target.

Person four is a sixty-year-old elf who lives in the garden apartment of one of the buildings on the block. He’s quiet and keeps to himself, but he’s also a retired shadowrunner. His blood was actually stolen from a donation truck that frequents the neighborhood.

If the character can free the spirit from the collar, the spirit will stay in the area and keep fueling the manasphere at the cost of 1 Karma per month. The benefits it provides return at a rate of one per month until all benefits have returned. These bonuses only apply while within that home block.
