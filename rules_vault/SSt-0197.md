## **Where to Look**

**==> picture [233 x 4] intentionally omitted \<==**

**Now that I’ve covered some of how to look for people, let’s talk for a little about where. Finding members of a shadowrunning team is not as simple as putting an ad in a screamsheet and reviewing résumés. The key problem is this: The economic system is designed by people who** _**want**_ **corporations to fnd the workers they need but** _**don’t**_ **want good shadowrunners to fnd each other, as that can make their lives more diffcult. If we develop channels that are too regular and offcial, the cops start watching it, which is why it seems like just about every Technicolor Wings franchise has a plainclothes Lone Star offcer hanging out there and pretending they’re not obviously a cop. So our networks need to be ad hoc, and they need to keep evolving.**

**That said, let’s look at a few of these ad hoc solutions we have devised.**
