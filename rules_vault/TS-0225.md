## **Mk1 Tactical Shield and Mk2 Riot Shield**

**A smaller, more maneuverabl e version of the full-sized ballistic/riot shield (p. 267,** _**SR6**_ **), these 60-centimeter shields are used by security teams for general duty and light crowd control. They are available in two versions: a tactical version that can accept minor customization and a riot version that flls the customization space with a surface discharge taser. The riot shield taser holds 10 charges; when attached to a power source, it regains one charge per minute. Both versions can be purchased in matte black, clear plasteel, or custom colors with a corporate logo.**

**Wireless bonus: The riot sh ield recharges by induction, regaining one charge per hour.**
