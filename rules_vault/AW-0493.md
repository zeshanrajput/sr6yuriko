## Sextant of Worlds

Although the location of the original Sextant of Worlds is unknown, a number of different copies have been made. Some claim to be duplicates of the original while others trace their origin to information in the pages of the Al’Azief. The one thing they all have in common—at least the ones that have real enchantments—is an ability to read the stars over the Astral Sea. When combined with the information found in maps, travelers can obtain their location and direction of travel relative to various metaplanes.

Copies of the Sextant of Worlds are focus-like devices similar to shadow cloaks, and they have astral forms that can be carried into the astral plane and materialize a metaplanar form. Like the shadow cloaks, users do not need to bond with these items to use them in the Astral Sea.
