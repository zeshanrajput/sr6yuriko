## Passive Stealth Features

## Electronics Mods

After the rise of optical processors, the term “electronics” expanded to include any type of data-processing device. The electronics in a vehicle include any devices or hardwired programs that manipulate information, including the input and output systems. Statlines that fall under electronics are Pilot and Sensor. Other non-statline items included are the input/output systems for camouflage, communications, defense, and stealth.
