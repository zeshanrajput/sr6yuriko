## 13: i did what?

We’ve all done it. Walked from one room to another, crossed the threshold, and completely forgot what it was were going to do, get, make, etc. Now imagine talking with someone who did some very bad things and has no recollection of doing them at all. Luckily, the player characters now get to live that scenario.
