## Missing Features

The character might have parts of them that did not survive the transition to their current state of existence. It could be an earlobe, an entire ear, and eye, a finger, or a chunk out of their arm or leg bone. The change is cosmetic and does not affect their attributes or skills, and they can replace the absence with the help of modern medicine if they so choose.
