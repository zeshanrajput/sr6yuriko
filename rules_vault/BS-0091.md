## Earware

## Antennae

Antennae grant their user enhanced Matrix reception and broadcast range. They are implanted as a pair on either side of the head and can retract behind the ears when not in use. They are available in a wide variety of shapes and colors, many of which are branded tie-ins to popular trideo franTHEchises. It takes a Minor Action to deploy or retract the antennae, and while they are deployed, the user reduces the total noise affecting them by 2. A user can only have one set of implanted antennae.

**Cyberware overdrive:** Reduce noise by 4 instead of 2 during a single Matrix action. Convert one die to a wild die for that test.

**Wireless bonus:** The antennae must be wireless enabled to function at all.
