## Plot Hooks & adventure seeds Enter and Break

Some shadowruns are precision jobs, where the team wants to leave as little a trace of their work as possible. Other jobs are about making sure you leave your mark.

The decker fighter needs to get into a place with lots of fragile things and break most of them. Mr. Johnson is clear that the spot can’t just be blown up—it’s too close to too many people, and Mr. Johnson does not have the stomach for that level of collateral damage. The instructions are to break in, preferably using hacking skills, then break things up. The place to attack could be a research lab, a drug lab, an enchanting facility, or any other location with lots of breakable things. There should also be some living security on the other side of the doors, so that the decker fighter can break some bones along with the equipment.
