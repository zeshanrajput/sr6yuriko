## The Hive: A Primer

One of the deep metaplanes of which mankind is aware, the Hive is the origin of the _invae,_ a socalled Atlantean term for insect spirits. The basic foundation to begin understanding it is through the examination of its native population. Bug spirits cannot exist in the physical plane without a host to inhabit, as our manasphere is extremely alien to them. Think of it like this: If I somehow teleported you to Venus, you’d be killed by extraordinary heat, and the ashes polished off by sulphuric acid. A Venusian, if such a thing exists, would find our world unlivable in turn. It’s just too alien to bear when you’re adapted to home. You’d need some kind of encounter suit to survive there.
