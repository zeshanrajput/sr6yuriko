## Faux Pas (A)

Sometimes the right move is to play to the room and make the other person look nasty and boorish. Spend a Major Action and succeed at an Influence (Etiquette) + Charisma vs. Intuition + Charisma Opposed test to give them the Fish Out of Water status.
