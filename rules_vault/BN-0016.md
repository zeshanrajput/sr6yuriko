## Shifters Are People, Too, Just Like You!

So hopefully you’ve picked up on the idea that shifters run the full gamut of metahuman complexities. You can’t say all X are Y with shifters anymore than you can with metahumans. I’ve met hardcore assassins and fl akey cosplayers. Some like J-Pop, others have taste. It’s a spectrum. There are corporate wageslaves and c-suite executives, as well as some who will never learn a spoken word.

Just like metahumanity, shared behavior is usually due to cultural heritage or part of a shared background. You do things a certain way because it’s how everybody around you does it, and it’s how you were taught. But there are always rebels and non-conformists looking for changes.

To my fellow shifters, get out there and be you! To everyone else, seriously, we’re just people trying to get by as best we can. Chill out, don’t be an asshole, and we won’t eat you. Probably.
