## **Mount Fuji,**

## **Japanese Imperial State**

## **Status: Friendly**

**The status of Mt. Fuji appears to be a proxy for the state of the Imperial Japanese royal family.**

**We know that Shiawase corporation has been central in Harvester research, but the corporation itself has not fully embraced or thrown their lot in with the Dis. Conversely, this sentiment of “wait and see” has transferred over to the royal family as well, at least in part.**

**We have credible reports of at least one assassination attempt on the royal family, which prompted their current course of action. We’ve received additional data, however, indicating a possible schism within the royal family. Lady Hitomi herself has been extremely vocal about her anti-Disian position and has tried, but failed, on many occasions to urge the emperor into action against Dis. This schism ultimately led Empress Hitomi to leave the royal palace to work against the Disians and Emperor Yasuhito to have her replaced with a body double to hide this fact.**

**Since leaving, Empress Hitomi has gathered a sizable cadre of operatives to oppose Disian interests and forces on the Japanese mainland. Their most successful operation to date was preventing Disian forces from gaining access to the ley lines and (at least currently) deny them access to the potent magical energies found there.**

**Fearful of a counter-offensive by Disian forces or their proxies, Empress Hitomi is currently working to expand her forces to hold the line and eventually drive Disian infuence from the Japanese Empire.**
