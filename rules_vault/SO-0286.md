## Game Information

When purchasing très chic clothing, specify a social environment. When wearing the outfit in a matching environment, très chic provides a bonus to your Social Rating equal to its rating. In other social environments, très chic only provides half its rating (round up) as a bonus to your Social Rating. Per the GM’s discretion, in an opposing environment, this outfit subtracts its rating from your Social Rating.

Très chic outfits have rating/2 (round up) capacity for fashion accessories.
