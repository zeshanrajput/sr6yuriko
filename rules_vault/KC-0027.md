## Plug-and-Play Social Engineering

## Posted by: Thorn

The greatest danger isn’t just that there’s an automated process that could undermine or elevate any business or legal entities at will. Stories of algorithms, worms, programs, and AI that are sophisticated enough to pull off magic tricks like crashing a corporation or a country are as old as computing itself. It’s that code could move beyond the Matrix and arrange elements of all our lives, thereby amplifying whatever it’s doing on the Matrix. Why is this a concern? Because code is involved in social engineering all the time. Social engineering has been automated to a degree that should not be possible for even the best social adepts or cyber rogues.
