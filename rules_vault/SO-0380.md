## Teamwork Example two

Lady Greenfire is going to need to con her way past some guards to get access to a secured storage room, and Ruby (the team’s face) won’t be able to join her to take care of that (they were spotted during an important part of the legwork). However, Ruby does have one way to help. She spends 6 Edge to take the Share Expertise Edge action and scores 5 hits. For the next five hours, Lady Greenfire now has two ranks in the Con skill. With some Edge of her own at the ready, Lady Greenfire approaches the guards and tries to keep Ruby’s tips in mind.
