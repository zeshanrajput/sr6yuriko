## Close Combat

**Specialization:** Blades, Clubs, Unarmed Combat **Untrained:** Yes

**Linked Attribute:** Agility

Are you near someone? Are you trying to hurt them? Then use this skill! Whether you’re punching, stabbing, clubbing, or whatever, roll Close Combat + Agility vs. Reaction + Intuition (Athletics can be added for Dodge; see p. 43)

Base Unarmed damage is 2S. Full details on Combat are in the **Combat** chapter (p. 104).
