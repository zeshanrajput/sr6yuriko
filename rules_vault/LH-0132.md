## Next Steps and New Allies

The Four Oyabun Rengo had the motivation, the nuyen, and now the information. But they needed someone that could do the things they couldn’t. The problem was they didn’t know who they could trust within the Yakuza. In a move that no one expected, they agreed that Novikov would be their new partner. He was more than willing to do things that decent businesspeople, like the Four Oyabun Rengo, couldn’t.

The Four Oyabun Rengo used their business contacts and started flying around the world spreading this information. The only thing they asked was that certain companies under MCT not be targeted, only the Rengo would move against them. Most within the business world were fine with this, because all the companies they selected were all A-rated.

While the Four Oyabun Rengo traveled the world, so did Novikov. While the Oyabun were meeting in five-star hotels, Novikov was moving against MCT’s nuyen cows. And because most of their highest profitable entities were those using Dis technology, for Novikov, it was a win-win.
