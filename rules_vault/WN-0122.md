## Hudson Pawn

By all appearances, Hudson is your average pawn shop hawking outdated secondhand goods at ten percent below listed value. The store doubles as a fence location for the Toki-gumi Yakuza. A hidden door in the back room leads to a basement space where they offer runner-centric goods to select customers. Hudson Pawn has access to most weapons and gear up to Availability (3), which they offer at a ten-percent markup. Availability (4) items can be procured within forty-eight hours upon request.
