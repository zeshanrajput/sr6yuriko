## Magical Research Heist

This adventure hook works best with the criminal professor background, but it could be used as a run idea for any shadowrun team with some magical support.

A prominent crime boss or fixer contact of the academic magician wants to hire the team for a special job. They received a tip that a local megacorporate magical research and development facility has made some significant breakthrough. The team is hired to break into the facility, steal the research data and any available prototypes (if applicable), and delete or sabotage what they leave behind. The facility has excellent magical security: security mages, wards, patrolling spirits, perhaps even dual-natured guard critters. Only a team with solid magic support could hope to pull this off. However, Matrix, tech, and personnel-based security are lighter than usual. This is a classic shadowrun with a magic-heavy theme, so how the team goes about pulling off the run is ultimately up to them. If they fail, the contact’s Connection and Loyalty ratings will both go down by 1. If they succeed, the contact’s Connection and Loyalty ratings both go up by 1, and everyone on the team gains the contact with a Loyalty rating one lower than the character who originally had the contact.

Note: If any of the runners take physical damage during the run, be sure to point out that their blood is on the ground. Unless they destroy or clean up any such remains, there is a greater risk of identification or even retaliation through ritual sorcery. Increase the team’s Heat modifier by +4 at the end of the run.
