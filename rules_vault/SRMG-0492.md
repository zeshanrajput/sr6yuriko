## **APPENDIX C: CONTACTS**

This section lists the contacts currently available for SRM 2081, their typical “job” (i.e. fixer, talismonger, etc.), and the SRM/CMP/PM in which the contact appears. As a reminder, some Missions contacts are above the character creation limits. Characters are still limited to Connection/Loyalty 6 at character creation, which makes some Missions contacts illegal to have at character creation.
