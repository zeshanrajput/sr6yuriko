## The Job

The social adept has fallen into the shadows after being “burned” by their former employer, a corporate security and intelligence service. They have lots of offers to continue manipulating people—but can they use their skills for something better?

A social adept who falls out of an intelligence service might find life is not as difficult as they thought it would be. Sure, it’s not the posh stability of a corporate intelligence job, but lots of people have an interest in someone with good contacts and trained social skills. And a lot of the jobs they offer are about manipulation. Convince one corporate suit to take a worse deal than they had originally agreed to. Use smooth talk to help someone get into a relationship, a la _Cyrano de Bergerac_ . Stir up two street gangs so that they attack each other.

But then a few people reach up from the streets with a different offer. A small store in the barrens, one of the few honest businesses, is being shaken down by the local Mafia. Can the adept talk the mob into backing down? This job is way less prominent and pays almost nothing, but it could set the course the adept takes in the shadows. Will their shadowrunning career be more of the same, or will they use their new life to start a new path?
