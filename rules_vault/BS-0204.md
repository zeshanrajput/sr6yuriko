## Spidersilk Gland

Spidersilk is renowned for its strength concerning its weight, so engineers have long looked for ways to harness its abilities. By implanting glands that shoot a reasonable facsimile of spidersilk from the human body, people get the opportunity to trip up opponents, or even rappel down a building. Don’t try swinging from it, though—this is not a comic book. Glands are often implanted in people’s wrists; those hold two charges, with each charge providing about twenty meters of spidersilk. Glands implanted in the abdomen or lower back can hold five charges. When the silk shoots out, the user can choose to keep it stuck to the gland, or they can snip it off. Aiming spidersilk at a target requires an Exotic Ranged Weapon (Spidersilk) attack.

A successful hit applies the Immobilized status. Breaking free requires a Major Action. Roll a Strength + Body (3) test to escape. If they fail, the Immobilized status remains until the next time they can attempt an escape. Someone else can assist them with a teamwork test. Additionally, someone

beside the Immobilized metahuman can use a Major Action to cut them out.

When used in rappelling or climbing situations, the player gets one additional hit on any Athletics (Climbing) test where the silk rope is used.
