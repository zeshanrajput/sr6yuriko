## Artist Status

Whether you’re in this for the money or not, money is always part of the wax biz. During character creation, artists may purchase Status with their starting gear nuyen. Costs for each Status is listed under **Cost at Character Creation** . If no status is bought, the artist is considered a Newb. Provided the artist makes at least one performance per month, lifestyle costs associated with each Status are considered paid.
