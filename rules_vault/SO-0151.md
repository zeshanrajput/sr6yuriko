## Scaling

This can be scaled in multiple ways. If the venture is going well, you may want to scale up your area of influence. Neighborhood to citywide. But make sure you can handle the increase in production before you do. I wouldn’t recommend going larger than a city, because that may bring unwanted attention. Or you can scale up your clientele. Everybody knows somebody who has a rich eccentric friend. If you’ve gained a loyal base, you may have an in with said rich friend. And from there you have a brand-new client, with deep pockets.
