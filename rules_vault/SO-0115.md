## The Clip Joint

## Posted by: Carbon Score

## Obstacles

While the target fighting back is so obvious a point as to make further discussion moot, the real problems have already been mentioned. If they’re connected, or they decide to get some revenge if you let them live, they or their survivors will start to investigate the disturbances to their operations. Even small-timers play into the criminal ecosystem, and disruption of the chain provokes a response from those who get shaken up by it. The higher the shake, the bigger the response. While it might seem like a jackpot when you intercept someone carrying a neighborhood’s protection money or some other special cargo, you’ve also just provoked the kind of investigation that means spending that money to get out of town. At that point, you’re either going to have to cut the head off the snake or, far more likely, run fast before they start looking for you.
