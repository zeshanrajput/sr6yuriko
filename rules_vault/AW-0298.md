## Puzzles

The puzzles to open the vaults in the catacombs can be literally anything, from riddles to crosswords or sudoku puzzles. Perhaps they could be a sliding tile puzzle or a math puzzle. Anything and everything the gamemaster wishes to use will easily work with the strange magic of the vault doors.

If players are struggling with puzzles, as they are not everyone’s thing, then consider allowing an appropriate skill test, or even an Intuition + Logic test, to bypass the puzzle should the players become stuck.
