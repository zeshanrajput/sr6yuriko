## Ayeowo

This new hawala is completely funded by Eniola. She grew rapidly to be one of the most influential due to abundant resources provided by Okoro and support from the poorer side of town. She offers great exchange rates and very low interest loans to the struggling of Lagos. Her network is also involved in fencing, and she offers rates above the market norm (25 percent rather than 10 percent) because she does not need a large profit thanks to Eniola’s investment.
