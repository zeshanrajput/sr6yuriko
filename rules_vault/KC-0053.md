## Scotsprawl Basics

The Scotsprawl is two distinct cities whose urbanized areas have blended into one long band of development, stretching across most of the narrow neck of Scotland. The two cities that combined are Edinburgh on the east, which holds financial and government prowess, and Glasgow, which is a hotbed of industry and manufacturing. The relationship between the two major sections of the sprawl is sometimes strained due to these differences, but their Scottishness and distaste for the crown often bring them back together.

Be aware, though, that there are multiple toxic zones in Scotland for a reason, and it isn’t just because England sends a bunch of waste up north (though that is, in fact, part of the problem). The Scotsprawl holds a lot of industry, and most of it is pretty dirty. Be prepared for smog and chunky tap water.
