## Compulsion

## (Behavior, 1 to 6)

Some people alleviate their suffering by stuffing themselves to the gills with illicit substances. You’ve chosen another way: a behavior you can’t help but indulge in, that’s less poisonous but equally toxic. This can represent anything from psychological addictions like gambling or Matrix gaming to “obsessive-compulsive” behaviors like arranging items in a certain order or repetitive handwashing.

**Bonus:** 2 Karma per level

**Game Effect:** You must indulge in your chosen activity for a specified amount of time per day, depending on the level of the quality. Failing to do so results in you suffering withdrawal, inflicting a –2 dice pool penalty on all tests, increasing by 1 with each withdrawal period that elapses.
