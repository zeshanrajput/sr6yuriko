## **Cyberspace Designs Terrier**

**The Terrier began as a multi-purpose industrial drone, and still sees a lot of use in that market, but its fexibility and easy customization also make it a great choice for LESP special purpose units. Even without any further customization the Terrier makes a great little drone, but it can be modifed so easily that many departments have them on their bomb disposal, hazmat, and emergency response units. The base model comes with a factory-installed autosofts (Engineering/Industrial) and generalpurpose toolkit, but by simply swapping out the Autosoft and upgrading the toolkit, the Terrier can be adapted for any role.**
