## Collins Law

Operated by Seamus Collins, this is the shadowiest member of this list of legal firms. Collins came to Lagos as a runner going by the handle “Eagle.” He got his wings clipped, losing his lower leg and arm on a run, and while recovering in a Lagosian clinic, he helped the owner deal with some legal troubles. The clinic recognized the value of his expertise (gained through an education in the CAS before he was a runner) and convinced him to stick around.

His mix of legal knowledge and shadowrun experience earned him a great niche. He has faced Eniola Okoro across a courtroom and respects her talents. He also found her intriguing and tried to ask her on a date. The courting efforts were a disaster, but the friendship they formed is for a lifetime.

Okoro has often needed hard-to-find information, and Collins’ shadow connections are quite useful for that. He has been one of her key allies for years.

This firm is located in the same building as Gaia Investments and Okoro Earth Law.
