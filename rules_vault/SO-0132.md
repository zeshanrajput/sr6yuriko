## Effects

Ideally, you’ve got leverage on your mark, which you can now use against them. The benefit of the Badger is that you have actual evidence on hand, ideally crafted and at your fingertips.

The alternative is pulling it off on-the-spot rather than holding it as leverage. This isn’t when you need to coerce the mark but discredit them. You could use your evidence when it’s most advantageous to do so, or you might even arrange for them to be caught in the act by the appropriate parties (have the spouse walk in mid-flagrante delicto, for example). In these cases, it’s important to make sure you have deniability or a good escape route so you or your proxy isn’t caught up in the consequences.
