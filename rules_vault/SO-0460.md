## Setting the Hook

Once you’ve done your research and set the stage, it’s time to make contact with the mark and

pull them into your narrative. This part is risky and requires you to roll your Con (Acting) + Charisma vs. the mark’s Judge Intentions in an Opposed test. If you use the Long Con Pool to spend Edge on this action and succeed, you may improve the level of your Long Con Pool quality up to a maximum of level 4 at its normal Karma cost (not doubled). If this test fails, the long con fails and must be aborted.
