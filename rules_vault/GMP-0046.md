## **Using Knowledge Skills during Legwork** 

**Step One:** Check to see if the runner could possibly know the information they are after. 

**Step Two:** The runner performs a Memory Test and compares the number of successes against the modified threshold of the different lines of the Legwork Block, this is what the runner remembers. In general Edge may be used for this test, as it is made by the runner, not a secondary party.
