## Mitsuhama Computer Technologies: Tripling Down on Destruction

Of all the corporations aligned with the Disians, MCT has fallen the farthest to them.

Initial reports indicate that the head of the top AAA-rated megacorporation honestly felt they could “handle” whatever the Disians were planning and turn the situation to their benefit. The end result is that MCT currently has the highest level of Disian infiltration and influence of _any_ corporation or government organization. And to make matters worse, they essentially welcomed the Disians with open arms.

Let me start by keeping the focus where it needs to be: Mitsuhama. Why I got involved here is not part of the discussion, so please leave it alone to avoid distraction from the real target.

MCT has solidly cemented itself as an evil far greater than even Aztechnology and their blood magic habits. Since they first climbed atop the megacorporate pile, they’ve kept growing and gobbling and doing typical dirty corporate drek. This was on the surface, and everyone saw it as normal. Behind the scenes, far worse drek was going on. This rise was fueled by heavy growth in resource acquisition, particularly metaplanar resource acquisition. Most just thought it was good old-fashioned corporate greed strip-mining somewhere other than the AMC. I’m here to bring the truth to light. Not just the truth about what they were doing, but how and why, which plays into MCT’s status as Number One on my drek list.

MCT enhanced their metaplanar access through a deal with the Disians. The threat of the Disians wasn’t fully realized yet, so MCT thought they were just making a strong-arm deal with an ignorant metaplanar operator. Little did they know that the Disians used their deal to weasel their way into the ranks of MCT. From within, they came together and progressed like a cancer rotting its host from the inside out. It wasn’t fast, but it was targeted and thorough. They slowly replaced anyone in the corp with an inkling of control and used their vast resources for their own designs.

To further realize their goals, the Disians manipulated and pushed the Megacorporate Audit. While that same event may play out differently in the long run, it has, for now, worked out in spades for MCT—all with the Disians’ help.

Existing ownership and top management didn’t need to be influenced a lot, as they were all blinded by the meteoric rise in stock value. The Disian element within MCT has kept everyone cash-fat and happy while pushing billions in slush fund money and black ops budgets over to operations that have helped the Disians build their initial foothold on our plane. And now that foothold has turned into a boot on our throats.

But, MCT being MCT, they were quick to see the benefits of being the favored puppet—I mean **ally** —and quickly went to work, eventually supplanting Aztechnology in the eyes of the Disians. Which has influenced our current situation.
