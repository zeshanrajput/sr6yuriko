## Bark

This process involves harnessing the Epidermodysplasia verruciformis (EV) disease, commonly called the “tree-man disease.” This process is one of the less traumatic dermal alteration processes.

The character’s skin takes on the appearance

of rough tree bark. This quality provides a +2 DeBUILDINGfense Rating bonus, cumulative with armor, but increases the cost of all armor and clothing by ten percent. Cannot be taken with Rhino.
