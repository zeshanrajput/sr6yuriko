## Obstacles

The obvious problem is when they kick up a fuss and have power enough to back it up. You’re running your standard and someone who knows better from experience is willing to make trouble. It takes money and power to get that experience,

SNARESand money and power are probably going to do better by way of violence than your own. So how do you get your way out of it? Bottle Service is easy, just make sure you have something real on the shelf to offer by way of an apology. If it’s joy toys and you have a conscience, you chop ‘em up and close up shop rather than let them put one finger where there’s no permission. The softer option is if you’re working with pros who don’t mind going all the way, in which case you’re just running a brother with extra markups. In other words, when a Clip Joint John gets wise, break them or make them. And if you need to, pack it up and move on.
