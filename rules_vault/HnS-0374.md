## Final Calculations

Once you have completed all your purchases and adjustments, there are a few calculations to finish your character. Attack rating for an AI will come from their Attack + Sleaze. An AI’s Defense rating is Data Processing + Firewall. Initiative for an AI is always based on hot-sim VR, so it will be Intuition + Data Processing + 3d6. For more details on this, please see p. 179, _SR6_ .

An AI has a single Matrix Condition Monitor equal to [(Firewall / 2, rounded up) + 8]. When the Matrix Condition Monitor is filled, the AI is

disrupted. An AI has no limit on Matrix damage overflow.
