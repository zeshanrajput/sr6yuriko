## **Game Rules for Disian Magic Measuring Mana**

**The core requirement of Disian magic is that something other than a natural Magic rating must be used to accomplish magical tasks. We will use the term “unit of mana” or simply “unit” as a replacement for a Magic rating.**

**When using units of mana to cast a spell, one unit provides one die toward the test’s dice pool as a replacement for a Magic rating. As a comparison, a normal metahuman mage with Magic 5 would contribute 5 dice toward the test from their Magic rating—a Disian would have to use 5 units of mana to produce the same result.**
