## What Doesn’t Kill You

Many anarcho zones haven’t had a working waste disposal system for decades, and access to clean food and water is rarely given. Growing up on toxic soil, living in roach-infested ruins, eating moldy leftovers, and being exposed to diseases and bacterial infections with no access to medicine kills many, but it has made you stronger.

- Districts: Falkensee, Gropiusstadt, Marzahn, Pankow, Reinickendorf, Strausberg

- Bonus: 3 Karma

- Game Effect: Replace one die in your pool with a wild die when rolling any disease or toxin test.
