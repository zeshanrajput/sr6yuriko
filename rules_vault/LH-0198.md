## March 4, 2083

Got a half stake on the last gig but it’s better than getting shredded in a zero-zone. I made a personal visit to momma MacDonald, felt she deserved to know, and got quite the shocking reward in return. Turns out back in the ’60s momma Mac made the acquaintance of my personal hero Toshiro Mitsuhama. Despite a healthy age difference, the pair had a short relationship that ended with a long commitment. Momma Mac got baby Tosh but didn’t want any of the megacorporate limelight. She fell for a sweet man, not a corporate shark. They parted amicably and Toshiro provided subtle support for momma Mac and Tosh. That was why she warned us, because someone may have found out about the connection and wanted to leverage it against Toshiro. Wish I could dig further, but I was in total agreement with the team I worked with that we’re too small of fish to swim in deep megacorporate waters. Maybe someday.
