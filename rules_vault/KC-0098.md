## Communication/Limited Matrix

The Matrix connection in Lagos is terrible. They barely have a local grid, and trying to do much on a national or inSUDDENternational level is nearly impossible. There are a few areas with consistent wireless coverage, such as Lagos Island and its immediate vicinity. Since the majority of the population has commlinks (although most are scavenged and built from scraps of poor-quality gear), a viable mesh network exists. The network is subject to the flows of metahuman traffic, however, and coverage could go from strong to non-existent within any given handful of minutes. The device situation leaves the area with a flexible Noise rating from 0 to 5 (0 in the throngs of Lagosians, 5 in the dead of night when the runners walk the streets alone), the exceptions being Lagos Island and Victoria Island and the 100-meter perimeter where the local grid is accessible.
