## M-TOC Mark II

Designed for front-line military units, the Mark II has some of the same capabilities as the Mark I but is more specialized toward combat operations rather than law-enforcement/security duties. The Mark II is also known for being physically resistant to external and Matrix damage. And thanks to modular components, it is easier to repair in the field.
