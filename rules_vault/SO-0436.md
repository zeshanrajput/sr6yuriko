## Interrogator

You know how to get people to talk. They may not like it, but they’re going to share what they know anyway.

**Cost:** 6 Karma

**Game Effect:** When you make a social skill test to question, interrogate, interview, or otherwise convince a target to reveal information to you, gain a point of Edge that must be used on the test, or it’s lost. The target of your interrogation may not gain or spend Edge on their opposed test to resist your interrogation.

This requires an unfriendly (or even hostile) approach. If you choose to employ this quality toward an NPC, they’ll regard you with fear or hostility in the future—except for further interrogation. You may not gain or spend Edge on social skill tests that affect that NPC for at least one month. If you interrogate a contact with this quality, it costs you a Favor Point—if you have no Favor Points with that contact, their Loyalty rating toward you is reduced by one.

Note that using this quality does not require extreme measures such as torture. An interrogation requires a conversation—though some leverage or threats are likely to help, resorting to torture is a fast way to earn a bad rep.
