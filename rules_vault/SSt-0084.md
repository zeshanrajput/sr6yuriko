## **Tactical Coordination**

**In addition to gathering intel and watching a place, having eyes in the sky is also handy during an op. When your drones are in position to watch for trouble, you are in an ideal position to coordinate your team and help them react to what’s going down. Sometimes that’s all you need—who needs to risk drones in combat when a street sam has your back? Just don’t forget to have at least one drone watching your own back, as it’s very easy to get so distracted by everything else that you forget to look out for trouble creeping up on you. If an enemy technomancer traces your position, you might end up with a visit from a street sam who’s playing for the other team—and that’s always a bad day. If you can afford an M-TOC and a few combat apps for it, I highly recommend it. Not only can it help you coordinate your team and drones better, it can also up your data processing—which lets you run more programs and autosofts on your deck—or buff your frewall so you can survive a few extra seconds when the technos come knocking.**
