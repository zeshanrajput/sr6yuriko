## General Malaise

On some fundamental and instinctive level, the mind, spirit, and body rail against their unnatural existence and seek to bring an end to it. In this, cyberzombies are as tragic as they are inhuman. Every month, a cyberzombie must perform a Willpower + Intuition (2) test. The threshold increases by 1 for every week without medication since the last test and for every year in service. Failure in the test burdens the character with a 5 Karma negative quality or increases an existing one to a value 5 Karma higher. Alternately, the character may choose to permanently lower an attribute and respective maximums by 1 point instead. There need not be a logical progression of increased severity with a specific disorder. Instead, a new and more serious one may simply supplant an existing one.
