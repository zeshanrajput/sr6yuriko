## Main Location: Strausberg

## Notable Characters: Demon Rats, Devil Rats (p. 217)

In 2067, Shiawase City Services put the alternative waste disposal syndicate Katschmarek out of business, causing the structure the Katschmareks had created not only to collapse but also to turn into a collective endeavor directed against Shiawase and their advertised promises. One can argue whether, even without active sabotage, Shiawase would ever have had a chance to dispose of the mountains of garbage piled up since the 2030s as promised through new state-of-the-art sorting, recycling, and garbage disposal facilities. The fact remains that Shiawase failed, causing the biggest waste scandal in Europe, after which the megacorp largely withdrew from Berlin.

**Original Message:** Dismantling the landfills south of Strausberg is a generational task. But the Katschmareks need a success story _now_ to position themselves for the inevitable pitch for BERVAG’s new waste-disposal contract. They want you to kill as many demon and devil rats as possible in the vicinity of waste incineration plant SCS-II. Pay is 100 nuyen per beast, and the thousands of rats there are more than enough to make you very rich. Or very dead.
