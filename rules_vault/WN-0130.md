## The Job

Cat contacts the runners about doing some work for her and sends along a Washington Heights address, a time (11:00 p.m.), and a password: “Rosebud.” The location is a mob-owned club called Tough Tony’s, located in a repurposed meat-packing plant. After being asked for the password through a slot in the door, the team is escorted to a large circular booth at the back of the club. Cat, a well-dressed and attractive female ork who looks to be in her thirties, is in the booth with an older, obviously cybered-up human male. She motions for the team to sit down and introduces her associate as Spyder, who nods and crushes out a cigarette butt, adding it to the overflowing ashtray in front him.

Cat orders a round of drinks for everyone. If she doesn’t already know the runners, she spends some time getting to know who she’s hiring. After

pleasantries and a few more drinks, she gets to the job. Cat explains that she is looking for intel on three targets. The offer is 4,000 nuyen per runner, and any paydata liberated will earn a premium bonus. After an agreement, she sends a dossier with pictures, names, and the employer—Shibata Consulting. It’s also assumed they live in the arcology, but no home addresses are listed. Cat wants to know every detail about what these three, and by extension MCT, were doing in Denver. This can be from company files or direct interrogation, preferably both.

Spyder tells the team a Matrix search shows Shibata is an AA-rated subsidiary of MCT. Information past that is scarce. The host address is on the twenty-ninth floor of the Mitsuhama Arcology and has a directory of office numbers without any names. There doesn’t seem to be any other shell hosts attached, so Spyder is convinced that they are using an off-Matrix framework host located inside the offices. Cat finishes up by explaining a plan for getting the team into the arcology—she has made arrangements with a local smuggler named Ratman to take the team through underground tunnels into the arcology. The team can call Ratman and leave anytime they want. The smuggler leads the team from Tough Tony’s, through the tunnels, to a utility hatch in a sub-basement mechanical room. He will wait there for the team to return.
