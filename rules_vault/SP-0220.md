## **Prague, Czech Republic**

## **Status: Friendly**

**Prague and the Czech Republic have managed to accomplish something that few nations and cities have: resist Disian incursion and infltration on all fronts and at all levels of government.**

**The majority of the credit for this goes to the great western dragon Schwarzkopf . For the past several decades, he’s pursued his advanced magical studies at the** _**Universitas Carolina Pragensis**_ **. He’s also used his position there to create an extensive network of contacts (many of whom are former or current students) that includes not only academia but people within the local government as well. These relationships allowed him to alert the Czech government about the Disian threat two years ago, and in a rare example of common sense, they believed him and took proper precautions. There was, of course, the need to remove Disian elements already in place, but the process didn’t take long. This also spared the Czech Republic from the trouble of the Week of Death.**

**As such, the Czech Republic and Prague specifcally are seen by anti-Disian forces as the one true safe haven left in the Sixth World, serving a vital function in the confict against Dis: that of safe haven.**

- **Disian proxies may have been removed from positions of power within the Czech Republic, but that doesn’t mean they’re gone. They’re more likely waiting for the opportune time to strike.**
