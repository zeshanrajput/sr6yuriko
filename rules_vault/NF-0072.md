## Complectogram

The brainchild of Carrie Highland, Kimama Edmo, and Eddie Brighton, this band walks the lines between a few different genres, changingtrack to track from haunting ballads to dancehall floorbreakers. While their performance is entirely mundane, it is informed by their individual worldviews and experiences as mages, with each hailing from a different tradition (Highland is a Kabbalist, Edmo is a Shoshone shaman of Dog, and Brighton is a follower of Dragon, and is notably a grandson of Holly Brighton, the reporter who first interviewed Dunklezahn). Any attending mage can perceive that they do not utilize any magic in their shows, but many enjoy the inspirations they use.
