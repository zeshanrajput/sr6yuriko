## It’s Not So Bad Example

Jazz and Rumble tracked down the team responsible for killing those Halloweeners, and things quickly escalated into a firefight. During the firefight, Jazz is caught in the blast radius of a frag grenade and suffers 9 boxes of Physical damage. He only has 9 boxes in his Physical Condition Monitor, so that’s not acceptable. However, Jazz has a little bit of Edge left. He spends 2 Edge to take the It’s Not So Bad Edge action, reducing the Physical damage by one box. Jazz may not be feeling very good after that, but he’s still in the fight!
