## Tai-sa Minoru Kenji

## Posted by: Sam-R-Eye

Among the Red Samurai, Tai-sa Minoru Kenji has the distinction of being the only Red Samurai in history to make it to retirement. And he did it not by being careful or reserved, but by slaying all enemies who dared challenge him. As such, he embodies the standard of what all Red Samurai aspire to be: stoic, focused, lethal, and most of all, loyal to Renraku.

Having served as a Red Samurai for over forty years, Kenji has seen a lot of action. He’s personally claimed the lives of 101 enemies in Renraku’s name, and served in the field for all but five years of his career, when he was finally ordered to take an instructor’s position. And even then, he was a harsh taskmaster who had no problems with removing body parts from students who became too careless or arrogant.

- There’s an urban legend within Renraku that Kenji beheaded one of his students because the student insulted him—he claimed the old samurai and his teachings were “obsolete” and challenged him to a duel. Turns out this student was the son of a board member, who demanded Kenji’s life. Instead, the board unanimously demanded the board member’s life for raising such a disrespectful offspring. If that doesn’t say how valued Minoru Kenji was, I don’t know what will.

- Baka Dabora

The only stain on his record, at least in his eyes, is the Renraku Arcology incident of 2059. Kenji still carries the personal shame of not being able to retake the arcology, defeat Deus, and restore Renraku’s honor. Note, there’s a reason I didn’t say anything about the victims of that incident.

Kenji had been in retirement for less than two years when he was brought back out, and his augmentations were upgraded to delta-grade in order to lead the Red Samurai against the newly discovered Disian threat. His orders were simple: train up the Red Samurai to combat the Disians, find them, and kill them wherever they are, no matter who or what gets in their way.

Once the counter-offensive against the Disians began, Kenji took those orders to heart, and the Red



Samurai became a force of nature. And true to his orders, he let nothing, and no one, stand in his way. His final battle occurred on the island of Luzon, when his personal detachment learned that three Disians in their famous war forms, commanding dozens of chimeras, had taken up position within a Harvester there. Airdropping directly onto the Harvester, Kenji and his samurai cut a bloody swath through the Disian ranks. Before he could face one of the Disians, however, Kenji fell to an onslaught of five large destroyer chimeras, killing four in the process.

- Minoru Kenji was so beloved by his samurai that those who accompanied him on that ill-fated mission to Luzon refused all recall orders from Renraku. Instead, they joined Operation: Cold Fury, where they fought right into the heart of the Antarctic stronghold and confronted the Auditor himself. After the battle was won and they avenged their master, those remaining Red Samurai committed seppuku on the icy Antarctic shores to atone for disobeying Renraku.

- Sam-R-Eye
