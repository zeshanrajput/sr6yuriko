## **Low Maintenance**

**Whether due to a lighter appetite or a knack for scrounging for their own food, this critter doesn’t go through food and other supplies as quickly as other similar critters.**

- **Cost: 1 Karma**

- **Game Effect: When determining monthly costs to feed and house this critter, count their Body attribute as half its value (round up).**
