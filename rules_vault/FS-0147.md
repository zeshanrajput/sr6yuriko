Like its namesake, the Cheetah isn’t about power, but makes a fine demonstration of how speed kills. Designed with a specialized firing system that offers an unmatched rate of fire, the Cheetah offers burst options that stack bullets within centimeters of one another. Sustained fire isn’t an option yet, but rEVOlution is looking to change that.
