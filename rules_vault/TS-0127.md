## **Organization**

**Our org chart is fairly simple in principle, but devilishly complex in some areas. At the head is the Steward. Through them we report to Her Majesty and the Home Secretary, and thus Parliament. So far, aside from accepting the expansion of our remit to include magical threats, and some minor details which are of no importance, Parliament has thus far stayed out of our business. They do have the power to get involved, and I suspect the morons—sorry, I meant honorable members of Parliament—will stick their noses in at some point. Thus far we’ve been allowed to get on with our mission as we see ft. The Steward reports regularly to Her Majesty and the Home Secretary, so I can only conclude that they are happy with our efforts. I certainly hope so. Like all my brethren, the very thought of letting the Queen or the nation down makes me sick to my stomach!**
