## Extra Mouths to Feed

For each two LP spent, your Living Expenses asset covers the basic survival requirements of another roommate or a dependent (this asset is not available if you do not also purchase the Living Expenses asset). Extra Mouths to Feed reduces the additional financial expenses incurred by the Dependents quality by 200 nuyen.

**Cost: 2 LP**
