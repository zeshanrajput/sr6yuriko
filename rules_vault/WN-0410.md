## A Polite Dust Up

After a sit-down conversation with Jonathan Blake and Miranda King, Gabrielle Spinrad understands what almost happened. She wants payback against Ramos for what he set in motion. While she is unwilling to act against Aztechnology directly, she believes Ramos has to be vulnerable in other ways. Al Thani tasks the runners with finding out all they can about Ramos’ operations in the city. She offers 10,000 nuyen for the research. Doing this research means reaching out to contacts and perhaps even hacking personnel files at Aztechnology’s HQ in order to discover that he has a brownstone in Stuyvesant town with a secured host. She then offers an additional 10,000 nuyen if the runners will break into the house and host and grab whatever paydata they can find.
