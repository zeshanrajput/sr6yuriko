## Komon surgeon

## (Professional Rating 4)

Any organization that spends a fair amount of time brawling with others needs someone who can patch up wounds when the fight is over. The komon surgeon is perpetually exasperated at his people for getting hurt, but he fixes them with grumpy efficiency.
