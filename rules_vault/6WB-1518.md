## Bone density augmentation

The molecular structure of your bones is altered to increase density and tensile strength. Ligaments are strengthened, and the greater bone density increases your weight by fifteen percent. You gain its rating as extra Body when resisting physical damage. Your unarmed combat damage becomes Physical and your Damage Value and Attack Rating increase per the table below. Bone density augmentation is incompatible with other augmentations to bones, including bone lacing cyberware.
