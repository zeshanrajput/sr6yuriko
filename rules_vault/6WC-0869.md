## Cursed Utilities

No matter what your landlord and you do, the place just doesn’t work half the time. Power goes out, water comes out full of algae, the food printer is stuck on the flavor you absolutely hate, and the toilet flushes in the opposite direction than it should. And that doesn’t even include the water

pipes threatening to kill you and eat your soul. At least, you hope it’s the water pipes. **Cost:** –2 LP
