## What They Want

The UTA are known primarily as talismongers, but the sudden and more frequent appearance of alchera has put information about magical phenomena in high demand. By pursuing its interests in talismongering, the UTA has found itself in a position to acquire and trade that information. Those who have already become accustomed to trading in secrets and favors in their dealings with the Seelie Court find their expertise and knowledge in very high demand. This has elevated UTA’s importance in many circles, but most especially shadowrunners. The relatively lucrative talismongering trade—much of it off-the-books—allows the UTA the ability to hire off-the-books professionals whenever they are needed. Being a provider of offthe-books magical goods makes them generally popular among Awakened shadowrunners.
