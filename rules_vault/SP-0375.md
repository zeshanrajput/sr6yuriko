## **Memory Crystal**

**While all Disians have the ability to use Mindspeech, this power has a relatively short range. To solve this limitation, Disians use devices called memory crystals. Like mana batteries, they are crystals of various sizes and are often worked into jewelry. They function in a way similar to the commlinks we use on a daily basis. They can record and transmit “thought” messages, saving them for later retrieval, with the ability to search old “fles” for a specifc keyword or image. The crystals can make full sensory recording and share these recordings with other crystals. This also includes “downloading” and saving the memories of chimeras under their control. There are also reports of crystals that duplicate the full functions of a cyberdeck, usable by some of the servant creatures under control of the Dis.**

**On metaplanes that create a translation of reality, this type of device and the languages contained within it would be in sync with the native technology—or vice versa—and be hackable by deckers, but both Dis and the material plane prevent this type of translation. Being an untranslated device processing information with magic instead of science or technology, memory crystals are not accessible by any standard Matrix devices. That limitation is only for deckers, though. As these are data processing devices, they generate nous, and this allows technomancers to see them in the noosphere and manipulate the copies of the data that exist as nous-felds. A technomancer may access a memory crystal and manipulate its data in the same way as any other device. However, at present the Disian language is**

**beyond our understanding, and any memories extracted from the crystals are untranslatable.**
