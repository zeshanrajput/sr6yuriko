## Effects

Unsurprisingly, you have a spray-painted brick (or, hopefully for you, something with a little more panache). You’ve got your mark lined up. You make the handoff. You drive off into the sunset with money, and they go home to discover they’ve paid for garbage and feel like an idiot for the rest of their life. I’d love to say there’s more to it, but this is a simple transaction, easy to do, and easy to get caught.
