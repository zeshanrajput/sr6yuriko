## **High Maintenance**

**This critter eats more food, requires special food and medicine, or perhaps wrecks your furniture and chews on your electronics. Whatever the cause, it’s much more expensive than usual to feed and care for this critter.**

- **Bonus: 1 Karma/Level (max level 3)**

- **Game Effect: Add the level of this quality to the critter’s Body attribute for the purpose of determining the monthly cost to feed and house this critter.**
