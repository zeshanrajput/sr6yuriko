## Errhine

## (Fire, Force 7)

Errhine are large buffalo with bodies of jagged coal and horns like pokers pulled from the fire. Every step they take sends sparks, and when a herd stampedes, a great veil of smoke rises in their wake. While one may be tough, a group can be formidable.
