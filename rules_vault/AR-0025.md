## Travel 

Entry to Omaha is fairly simple, as there are few natural boundaries keeping people out. The Missouri River runs by the east side of town, a few hundred meters wide and not overly deep—so if you want to arrive by boat, make it something with minimal draft. Interstates 80 and 29 bring you to the area, with state routes and spurs giving all the coverage you need. The cross-continental train stops downtown, and Eppley Airfield will bring you in northeast of downtown. Want a sub-orbital flight? Get yourself up to Minneapolis.
