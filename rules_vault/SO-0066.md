## Assensing to Get a Read

In addition to the information learned on the assensing table (p. 159, _SR6_ ), one additional row have been added to the table. With 3 hits, the astral perceiver would get a sense of the source of their current emotions. In terms of Getting a Read, this would allow the perceiver to understand if this is something the target is manipulating about themselves. With either of these, the Masking metamagic can still be used to increase the threshold.
