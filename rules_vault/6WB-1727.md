## Les Fleurs Du Mal Main Location: Potsdam

Notable Characters: Vampire (p. 218)

From humble beginnings as an actor in smut sims and as an over-sexualized host of a HorrorTrid show, Bella Lugosi has worked her way up to being a millionaire simsense producer with her own company. After the introduction of new laws that turned ghouls and vampires from hunted monsters into recognized citizens with a notifiable condition, Bella not only came out as a true vampire but joined the Partei der Neuen Ordnung (PNO) that seeks to collect all these tasty votes from the newly eligible (especially ghoul) voters.
