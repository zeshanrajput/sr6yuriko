## The Goldwater Club

The Goldwater Club shares their mixed-use block with several residential buildings between fifteen and fifty stories in height. The eight-story building the club calls home is located in East Parkside on 79th between Madison and Park. It used to serve as the Consulate of Greece and maintains the same dedication to security as its former inhabitants. Two security officers dressed in black suits stand outside the front entrance, while a third seated inside doubles as the concierge (use Minuteman Security Rigger, p. 206, _SR6_ ).

The building is used primarily for meetings and as an event space. There is a rooftop garden as well as a courtyard in the rear of the building surrounded by the other buildings on the block. The building does not have a garage. Cars are parked in a facility a block away from the building. It has multiple basement levels that are used for storage and emergency shelter.
