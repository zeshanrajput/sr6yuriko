## The Orphan

You grew up within an institution, perhaps an orphanage or foster care system. You never even knew who your parents were, and no one ever seemed to have answers for you. Until the day you tested positive for Awakened potential, it seemed that no one cared much about you at all. That moment changed everything, and from then on you were treated like someone of real value. Your education was intense and highly competitive, and you were raised in a new kind of program. It didn’t take long for you and your fellow students to discover the shared thread among your stories: None of you had ever known anything about your parents. As the years passed and you graduated to pursue further studies, you found no door closed to you. One day, you gained a hint of a powerful secret. Not enough to present as evidence to a court, but enough to confirm suspicions you hadn’t even realized that you harbored. Your life had been planned from the beginning by the organization that funded your employment. All the scholarships and financial support came with hidden strings attached. It soon became clear that you were part of a vast program to locate and recruit vulnerable and marginalized Awakened individuals, and groom them to be loyal to the organization. When the time came for you to graduate, you found all of your options locked down under contractual obligations. You were immediately put to work in research and development, magical security, or magical support to a team of special operatives. It didn’t take long for you to find the situation untenable. This is not the purpose you imagined your education would be put to. Both time and careful planning preceded your retirement, and you were able to break free from your bondage by becoming SINless. You have no family to turn to, and the megacorporation or organization that was your home is the very prison you escaped from. So, what now, chummer?
