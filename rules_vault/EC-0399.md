## Kenston Aircraft Interiors

This massive factory complex manufactures interior aircraft sections before delivering the components to an offsite Fed-Boeing location for final assembly. Almost everyone living in Trashtown works here. The Trashtown residents fight for as many sweatshop hours as possible, attempting to achieve living wages for their families. Residences are basically shanties, with their own sprawl of squatters looking for employment.
