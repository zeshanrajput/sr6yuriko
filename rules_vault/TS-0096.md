## **The Crime**

**One of the simple ways to approach any given crime is to take inspiration from the runners. Their job is really built around committing some crime against the corps. Who’s your criminal? Is it an investigation into corporate malfeasance or some murders committed in the community? Setting the core of your investigation is the frst step in setting up the path to solving it.**

**When planning the crime, everything leaves behind sets of clues and information that will point the investigators on the right track. There are tons of stories about dumb criminals who leave behind stupid clues, but even the best-planned crimes leave a trail. They talked with people, left physical evidence, or left a Matrix trail that is just impossible to fully remove, especially when time is of the essence or when things just go wrong.**
