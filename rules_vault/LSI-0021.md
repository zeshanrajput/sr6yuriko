## Survival Assets

Tending to a garden, orchard, mushroom farm, chicken/cricket coop, or other similar alternative provides an alternative source for the basics you need in order to survive. Spending one LP on Survival Assets provides a bonus Edge to Outdoors (Survival) tests. Spending two or more LP on Survival Assets is equivalent to purchasing Living Expenses. If you have both Living Expenses and at least one LP invested in Survival Assets, you gain a +1 dice pool modifier per LP spent on Survival Assets on natural recovery tests (p. 120, _SR6_ ).

**Cost: 1 or more LP**
