## Basic Overview of a Vehicle

Thanks TB, and I’ve only had three pots so far, so null sheen! So let’s start this off by going through some basics of vehicle design. While I know most of you aren’t full on engineers, I also know you wouldn’t be reading this if you didn’t have at least some interest in the technical side of building avehicle. However, just so we don’t have any confusion going forward, I don’t want to get bogged down in too much technical jargon. Instead, I’ll set some expectations about what I mean when I’m talking shop.

It is also worth noting again that while all drones are vehicles, not all vehicles are drones, so that means when I am talking about vehicles here, what I say applies equally to both unless I specifically say drones.
