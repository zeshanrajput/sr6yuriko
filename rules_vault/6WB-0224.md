## Poisoned

Something wrong has gotten into the character’s body, and it’s going to mess them up until it works its way through. This status has a damage code after it, with a number and an indication for either Physical or Stun damage. At the end of each combat round when this status exists, the character must resist the indicated damage by rolling Body. The damage decreases by 1 each round.

Antidotes, both physical and magical, can remove this status.
