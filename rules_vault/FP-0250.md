## **Our Sympathizers**

**They are key to our success. Regardless of who they are or their background, they have been beside us since day one. While many are average Joes, they can also be found in the ranks of corporations (not just Gaeatronics or Universal Omnitech), within governments, law enforcement, and military spheres (not only in the NAN, either). They are those who, like us, feel that we were robbed by the First Treaty of Denver. They are the people who provided us, and still do, with money, gear, sustenance, information, and, most importantly, a place to hide and disappear when warranted.**

**> Funny enough, it’s easier for Native Americans working for Ares to support Warpath, because it won’t be anyone’s first guess that this weapon shipment for North California has actually been hijacked by runners working for Warpath, using intel provided from within the corp.**

- **Sim-Eon**

**We also have skilled sympathizers: smugglers, arms dealers, runners, hackers, and gangers. People who not only can provide the usual help, but also, for the right price, will be more than willing to do a few jobs to aid us. The most valuable of these often end up being recruited as full members.**

- **It’s taboo to mention it, but Warpath has sympathizers and people in their debt working the known smuggling routes from western North America. I know more than one coyote, even Anglo ones, who confessed to having knowingly helped a Warpath op either to pay a debt of honor or just be sure they would still be able to do their usual run.**

- **Turbo Bunny**
