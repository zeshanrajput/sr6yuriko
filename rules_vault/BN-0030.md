## Ev ent Life Modules

## Ev ent: Captured!

## (Shifter only)

You were captured and imprisoned against your will. Your captors could have been traffi ckers, law enforcement, a hostile tribe or gang, or even a private collector. But you persevered through the experience and came out stronger.

- +1 to Agility, Body, Reaction or Strength

- +1 to Charisma, Intuition, Logic, or Willpower

- +1 to Edge or Magic

- **+1 Strength, +1 Engineering, or +1 Intuition**

- **Contact points:** 4 (any type)

You may take one negative quality to represent the consequences of your escape. Or you may take one positive quality to show your ingenuity and resourcefulness in your getaway.
