## Law Enforcement

You’re a bad-ass runner. You’re tough and independent. You know law enforcement hates you, and that’s the way it should be. Why try to change it?

But that’s the wrong way to think. We sometimes call ourselves criminals, but more properly we reside in the grey areas of the world, not the black-and-white of cops versus criminals. Sometimes, cops are chasing us, while sometimes our interest lines up with theirs. Looking for a missing person? So are the cops.
