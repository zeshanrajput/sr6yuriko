## Report 1: Audio recording made at the Scrap and Tug 

_Deerslayer (female troll, known member of Trip Set gang):_ The simple fact is that when the dust settles, Vicar will be one of the richest people in town. 

_Scope (male human, freelance operative):_ And they say crime doesn’t pay. 

_Deerslayer:_ Because they’re moralistic prudes. With no vision. 

_Scope:_ Don’t give your guy too much credit. If everyone richer than me left town, I’d be the richest person in town, too. 

_Deerslayer:_ Yeah, but you’d need a hell of a lot more people to leave. But this isn’t about that. I’m not looking for credit or anything. I’m describing a fact. 

_Scope:_ What do you want me to do with that fact? 

_Deerslayer:_ Understand which way the wind is blowing. The governor doesn’t matter anymore. He’s fled to Iowa, and he’s not coming back. Mayor Parish is hanging on, but with pretty much no revenue coming from the feds or state, she’s not going to have much power. So the government is a non-entity. The military’s gone. No AAA in sight. The old rules are gone. Vicar’s going to make new ones. 

_Scope:_ Vicar’s a drug dealer.
