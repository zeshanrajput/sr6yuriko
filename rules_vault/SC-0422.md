## The Drone Factory

There is a massive factory in the middle of a sprawl that uses drones to make drones. Both

aerial and ground drones move here and there, carrying parts, affixing parts to other parts, and doing all the work needed to assemble drones. Multiple models are made here, since common parts can be shared between models. The factory is hundreds of meters long and wide and without interior walls, though there are several conveyer belts and large pieces of machinery that serve to break up the factory floor.

A flying drone has entered the factory, carrying a vital piece of information. This could be whatever the gamemaster wants it to be—a commlink with valuable commcodes or design schematics, a packet of reagents, valuable gems, whatever. The drone enters the factory to shake any tail, or perhaps injure anyone following it. This should become a mad chase through the large space. Drone after drone will present themselves as an obstacle, including the inevitable security drones firing Stick-n-Shock ammo. The motion in the factory is constant, so the racing rigger needs to stay in motion too, in order to avoid the obstacles and eventually catch the target drone.
