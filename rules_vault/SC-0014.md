## The Chosen One

When you tested positive as Awakened, it was initially exciting. Corporate scholarships suddenly became available, and the thought of wielding special powers was thrilling. The strings that were subtly attached to you went unnoticed for many years. After graduation, one employment offer stood out among the rest, matching your interests and talents perfectly and offering an astonishingly good salary and benefits. As you considered your options carefully, every other possibility was eventually withdrawn. This may have been a warning sign to you, but you were going to accept the best job anyway, right? How could you have possibly known that your employer was a dragon and that you would be brought into their direct service under great secrecy? You received further special training to prepare you to act as an agent of this dragon, but you eventually sought a way to break free. Things got messy fast, and you got out. Somehow you feel that your “escape” was the dragon’s true design. Are you truly free, or are you still a pawn on the unseen chessboard of draconic machinations?

This background is easily adapted for a number of alternative variations. Instead of a dragon, your mysterious master might be a vampire who sought to groom you for Infection. Another option might be a powerful toxic magician who saw in you the potential to become an ally. Insect shamans, blood mages, shedim, and even cultists of the crystal entity all provide interesting alternatives as well.
