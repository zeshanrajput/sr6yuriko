## Silver Golem

I began calling them silver golems because they look like they are made of silver, but I can assure you they are much more durable than silver ever has been. Like their steel cousins, we do not fully know what metal they are actually made of.

Whereas steel golems are brutes, silver golems are agile and skilled fighters. Physically comparable to an augmented elf, their strikes come from unexpected angles and with a level of skill far in excess of their cousins. Due to them being physically about the same size as your average human, they seem a little less intimidating. Until they move, that is, because these things are capable of bursts of incredible speed when they need it. Silver golems are also not as susceptible to illusion spells as steel golems have proven to be.

Silver golems are typically armed with a pair of short swords, but it is not uncommon to find them carrying a bow or crossbow.
