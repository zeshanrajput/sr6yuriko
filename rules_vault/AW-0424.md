## Transportation

Travel through Miggon is like any post-apocalyptic trid. Sometimes you walk, sometimes you scrounge enough parts and go-go juice to drive. This is a common mode for mutant go-gangers in the area. Then you have some legendary engineers who can fly.

If none of these are of your speed, there’s always the death train. The death train is a Kin-created bullet train that weaves its way through Miggon’s points of interest. While faster and more efficient, they don’t call it a death train for marketing purposes. Remember those shadows on the walls? Those lost spirits try to escape their fate in Miggon by taking the train. They may tell a heartfelt story, but don’t agree to any of their requests, or it may be your shadow that gets stuck in Miggon.
