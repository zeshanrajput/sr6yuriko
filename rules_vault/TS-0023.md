## **Game Information**

**While these lesser talents may not be of great use to most players, the fact is that the majority of Awakened individuals encountered in the Sixth World will actually be one of these two types. Any standard law enforcement NPC (or any other NPC) can be given one of these talents to provide an unexpected twist for the players.**
