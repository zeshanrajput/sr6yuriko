## Social Style

You have a unique set of social talents that form a signature style of yours. It may make you a bit more predictable than you’d like, but the advantages far outweigh the cost.

**Cost:** 7 Karma per level (1 to Charisma attribute) **Game Effect:** For each level of this quality, choose an Edge action that involves a Con or Influence skill test. The cost of these actions is reduced by one point of Edge.
