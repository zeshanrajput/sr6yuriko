## **Availability:** 5

This treatment will restore lost Magic to anyone who has at least 1 Magic remaining. This takes longer and is more difficult the more Magic has been lost. As with Revitalization, Essence loss from augmentation can only be repaired once the augmentation has been removed. This treatment also increases Essence in the same way that Revitalization does. Magic is restored faster than Essence.

| **MAGIC LOST**<br>4+<br>3 | **TIME TO RECOVER 1 POINT OF MAGIC**<br>3 months<br>2 months |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 1 or 2                    | 1 month                                                      |
