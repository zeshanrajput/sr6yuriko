## Myo meric Smart Harness

This item was originally marketed to elder care or as mobility assistance. With a DNI command this simple harness will move on its own, put itself on, and reach out to grab and carry small items. It’s not fast, but it’ll cross a room by itself if you tell it to. It can grab several small items and keep them well secured.

**Wireless Bonus:** The wearer can perform a Pick Up/Put Down Object(I) Major Action for small objects in close range even if they have no free hands—or no hands at all. The myomeric harness can move fi ve meters per turn with a wireless command. It takes a Major Action to Ready Weapon or Put Down an object stored in the harness. The harness is unable to perform any kind of attack action.
