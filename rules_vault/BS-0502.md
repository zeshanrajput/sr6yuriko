## Plastic Exoframe

A plastic-reinforced skeleton and exoframe is sufficient for lighter-weight modular components and also is the most inexpensive option. Modules designed for a plastic exoframe are rarely designed for combat or heavy-duty tasks, but they are ideal for carrying electronic systems. A plastic exoframe has a maximum capacity of 4.

A plastic exoframe includes the plastic bone lacing augmentation.
