## **>** Kane

The Cutty Sark II is 85 meters long and 11 meters wide with a draft of 6 meters, and it is a full rigged clipper. This means she has three masts, all with square type sails (I’m sure we’ve all seen the pictures). She’s not the fastest vessel out there nowadays, but she truly is a majestic thing that’s utterly at home on the water. She sails with a permanent crew of 30, buthas berths for an additional 12 guest crew—usually students or paying passengers. Each crew is specially certified, and the wait list for spots on the permanent crew is approaching three years right now. The wait list for guest crew is double that!
