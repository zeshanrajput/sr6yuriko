## The Watchers

There are numerous parties who have a particular interest in these algorithms, expert systems, networks, and intelligences that are or could be capable of performing these operations. The fact remains that these all exist across the Matrix and physical world, and they perform their intended operations all of the time. Some of them are

legitimate and regulated by financial exchanges or other regulatory bodies (and even by some governments!). These tend to carry out pre-programmed tasks and are restricted in their scope to prevent them from causing too much damage. There are others that aren’t regulated and operate on exchanges and out in the real world performing automated tasks on behalf of some nefarious purpose or entity. There are also free-floating entities that exist without known purposes or benefactors. These are the ones treated as inherently dangerous and the ones that are the most likely to get the attention of the biggest players.

This is where things get damned serious for everyone involved. However, it’s serious in different ways, because the motives and purposes of these things are unclear, which means anticipating their actions is not easy. There are plenty of groups who try, and they are worth a look.
