## **Maersk**

**Maersk and Wuxing have been going at each other for supremacy in the shipping sector, and**

**their jockeying for position won’t stop just because the world might be in danger. Maersk is not run by nihilists—if they are presented with a choice between exercising their rivalry and preserving life as we know it, they’ll choose the latter. Up until they have to make that choice, though, they’ll proceed as if both options are viable.**

**While the public likes to think that competition between shippers involves regularly raiding each other’s ships, it tends to be more sedate than that.**

- **Most of the time.**
