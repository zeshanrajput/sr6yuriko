## Cyberzombies and Conjuration

Cyberzombies have some unique interactions with spirits and Conjuring. While cyberzombies could be looked at as “possessed” by their own spirit, they are not vulnerable to any form of Banishing. The ritual that creates a cyberzombie creates a toxic environment for spirits. Most spirits tend not to go near a cyberzombie unless ordered to, and even then, it may take some coaxing.

Should a spirit attempt to possess a cyberzombie, they do not receive any Edge on the attempt to do so, even though the cyberzombie has been prepared as a vessel.

If a spirit successfully possesses a cyberzombie, they will soon find out it was a major mistake, as the dark magic of the blood ritual traps the invading spirit within its own spiritual net. To escape, the spirit must be Banished from the cyberzombie.

Further, inhabiting a body that should not exist within the natural order of the universe saps the strength of the invader, reducing its Force by 1 for every minute it continues possessing the cyberzombie until it is destroyed. Being devoured by dark magics in this way is an extremely painful and horrifying way for a spirit to die.
