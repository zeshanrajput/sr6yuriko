## Council of Hunters

The Hunters are the ruling guild of Kyoko. The Hunters are a large magical group composed entirely of adepts. They prefer adept powers that increase their combat and stealth prowess, and they have a special knack for tracking. If you’re looking to make any deals for reagents in Kyoko, you’ll need special permission from the Council of Hunters. The highest-ranking Hunters are bonded with a pterosaur spirit, which make for excellent scouts as well as aerial mounts.
