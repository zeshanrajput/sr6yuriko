## **Starving Critters**

**If your critter goes hungry, they aren’t going to be around much longer. For each month that you fail to feed your critter, their Body attribute is re-**

**For critters with the Dietary Requirement weakness, lack of food is a much more immediate and dire problem. If you don’t feed your critter with a Dietary Requirement, they die. They can’t last until next month, because they will wither away in a matter of days.**
