## FAB II Gamma

The variety of FAB II currently in use, the gamma strain may either be held in a fluid nutrient solution and circulated between two plates, or an aerosolized solution sprayed across an open area. Both applications are used to detect and prevent astral intruders. This strain is very resistant to astral collisions and is very difficult to move through when in astral space, effectively creating a non-magical ward.

**Game Rules:** FAB II acts as FAB I in that it releases a fluorescent marker after an astral impact, however it is much more resilient. FAB II acts as a dual-natured mana barrier with 1 cm depth of fluid, or 50 cm depth of aerosolized mist, equaling 1 point of Barrier Rating, with no creator (so equally affecting everyone) and a maximum of Rating 6. This FAB II barrier may be pushed through or destroyed in the same way as a normal barrier. Both pushing through and breaking through the barrier kills the bacteria and releases the fluorescent marker.
