## **Anansi Chimera**

**Anansi chimera were frst found in Pretoria. Their physiology is similar to the giant huntsman spider, but larger with a variety of colors, patterns, and body shapes and a leg span of about 50 centimeters.**

**Deployed to an area ahead of an attack, Anansi chimera can infltrate facilities and have an innate ability to sense electronic devices. They will move to the highest-rated active device, latch onto it, and deliver an electromagnetic pulse causing Matrix damage to electronics. Although the weapon has a recharge time measured in days—effectively making it single use—these creatures can be a very effective tool for knocking out communication or security equipment in a targeted facility.**

**In addition to their vicious bite and bio-electric weapon, they will also use their spidersilk to cover system controls or even entire rooms in webbing, slowing down security responses and hindering repairs.**
