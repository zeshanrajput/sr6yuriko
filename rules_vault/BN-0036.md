## Sh ifter (Scaly)

This sub-category is for reptiles and amphibian shifters, including alligators, crocodiles, anacondas, and other snakes. Some have legs, some don’t. Venomous or aquatic adaptations are also options. Alligator shifters are found naturally in the rivers of the CAS and Amazonia. Crocodile shifters are most common in the Nile River Valley although some have been reported in Australia. Anaconda shifters are known to inhabit South America, and other species of snake shifters have been reported in deserts and rainforests all over the world. A family of cobra shifters has been documented among the Angkor Awakened and the naga community there. A coral snake shifter lives in the Amazonian shifter community after escaping captivity as a pet from the CAS. Rattlesnake shifters are rumored to exist in several NAN states. A community of poisonous tree frogs lives in Amazonia as well.

- **Minimum Base Cost:** 15 Karma

- **Gained Powers:** Dual Natured, Natural Weapon, Supernatural Recovery, Shift.

- **Gained Weakness:** Allergy (Silver, Extreme) or Allergy (Gold, Extreme)

- **Attack:** Bite.
