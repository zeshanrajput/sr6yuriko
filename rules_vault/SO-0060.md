## Follower

Maybe someone’s giving commands and you can’t help but follow along. You find it difficult to take the initiative and make your own decisions. This status applies a –2 dice pool penalty to any action you take if it requires a decision. Conversely, you gain a +1 dice pool bonus to actions in which you assist the leader giving you orders.
