## Tech Blast

Monads can use Tech Blast, which is similar to the Data Spike Matrix Action. They use a Major Action to attack devices within 10 meters by rolling NV + Logic vs. Data Processing + Firewall. The Damage Value is equal to their Attack Rating/2, rounded up, plus 1 additional box per net hit.
