## **How to use Explorer TU625 (2B5)**

**Many players and gamemasters have become somewhat familiar with the Null Sect, which has been introduced, developed, and expanded upon in numerous previous books. With the events of** _**Whisper Nets**_ **, the consideration of some kind of workable relationship was only a matter of time. But make no mistake—unless the encounter is with a rogue member of the Null, their ultimate agenda has not changed. They have, however, discovered it is possible to have a working relationship with the residents of the Matrix, and the strange world it shares an existence with. The Explorer class of Null are best suited for this relationship to be expanded upon, and what takes place during the forum at the beginning of this chapter is perhaps the frst open-table example of such.**

**Explorer TU625, who will respond to the phrase two-bee-fve, or 2B5, has been researching the entity labeled as Psyche, and by extension, the Kechibi code that Psyche commonly uses. Among the Null, Psyche is considered an elite intellect. 2B5 has been charged with creating an in-depth dossier the Null can use to communicate directly with Psyche whenever they wish to do so.**

**To that end, 2B5 has decided to engage with shadowrunners as a Mr. Johnson. This means the runners could fnd themselves pursuing leads that go nowhere or might put them on a direct track to getting the attention of Psyche. 2B5 has different methods of payment for services rendered that may be of interest to characters, including connecting an AI or EI with a persona that is capable of installing, upgrading, or removing codemods. 2BS will also compensate Matrix users with new cyber utilities or improve the attributes of their current hardware through customization, including similar options for rigger-related utilities and improvements. Finally, 2B5 can also offer payment for runs through virtual services such as establishing or re-establishing SINs and IDs for individuals or licensing for new or current hardware.**
