## **Additional Personnel**

**There are 20 members of the support staff (cooks, maintenance techs, personal assistants, and admin). They will stay out of the way during an emergency, following protocols to remain under cover. If encountered, they’ll set off an alarm, but otherwise they pose no threat.**
