## Guizhen II Particle Projection Guns

Second-generation Guizhen technologies are harder to locate, let alone obtain. Produced at the second Kanabo facility, its existence was a closely guarded secret until recently, hidden behind the more prominent and less dangerous Guizhen I. It directly impedes the operation of electronic appliances, implanted cybernetics, and operating nanotechnology, but not items or materials manufactured using nanotechnology. There are three principal formats ready to deploy: the Fángfeng zhào (Storm Shield), the Màichong chuí (Pulse Hammer), and the Yanlèi guan (Hall of Tears). The Storm Shield looks like an opaque riot shield lacking a viewfinder window. The Pulse Hammer resembles a heavier-barreled variation of a Yamaha Raiden. It’s capable of unleashing a sustained beam of charge particles that impedes all forms of electronics, cybernetics, and active nanotechnologies, causing them to cease function or perhaps even take direct damage. The third form of the technology is the Hall of Tears, which is non-mobile and can be implemented as part of an accessway or airlock, which upon command can flood the chamber with pulsed emissions that will do the same thing as the Pulse Hammer.
