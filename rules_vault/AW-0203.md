## Horizon

If Evo was snubbed, Horizon has been ignored. By all accounts, Horizon has nothing of interest to the Disians and is considered a non-entity. Given Horizon’s specific resources, they’ve been extremely active in trying to ally with Dis, with no luck. But given how unpredictable Horizon can be, they’re definitely a wild card.
