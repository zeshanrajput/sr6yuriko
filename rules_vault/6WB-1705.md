## Under the Radar

Berlin’s alternatives are paranoid by nature, and with good reason: For decades, the megacorporations of the city have tried everything to tighten their grasp, identify threats, and closely monitor those who dare to decline their generous offerings. Growing up in one of Berlin’s anarcho zones, you have not only gained a general understanding of likely places where cameras or sensors will be placed in the tightly surveilled parts of the city, but you also developed an uncanny instinct to feel which areas to avoid, where to turn away from a possible sensor, and how to use a passing drone to block the line of sight of a camera when crossing in front of it.

- Districts: AGC Siemensstadt, Chawi, Mitte, Renrakusan, S-K Tempelhof, Z-IC Tegel

- Cost: 15 Karma

- Game Effect: You gain one bonus point of Edge on Perception tests to find hidden cameras, drones, bugs, or other surveillance tech, and also on Stealth tests when evading technological surveillance by drones, cameras, or other sensors. This bonus Edge is lost if not spent on that test. This quality can be combined with the Crowd Ghost quality.
