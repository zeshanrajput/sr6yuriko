## Sh ifter Martial Arts

You may be a crane, that doesn’t mean you should try a Crane Kick. Martial arts styles and techniques are learned for each form by a shifter. Most shifters learn Drachenklaue (p. 25, Lofwyr’s Legions) or something similar for their animal form. Rake is the only technique that increases the Damage Value of a shifter’s natural weapons. Rolling Clouds and Threatening Edge are the only weapon techniques that work with a shifter’s natural weapons.
