## Swordbreaker

The last of three digital weapons binds up or outright breaks the protocols that the target icon uses for attacks. It acts as a modification to the standard Tarpit Matrix Action. Swordbreaker inhibits the Attack attribute instead of Data Processing in the Tarpit action. On a hit, the target’s Attack attribute is reduced by 1 per net hit in addition to the normal effects.
