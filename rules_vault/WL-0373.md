## **Medium Lifestyle**

**Medium lifestyle usually allows for enough room to house critters, and pets are relatively common at this lifestyle category. Dangerous predators and Awakened or Emerged critters are usually not allowed, nor are particularly large critters (Body 5+)— these will need to be housed in a separate location.**
