## **Corporate**

**A fact for most commercial police forces is that the fees paid on municipal contracts rarely cover the cost of overhead. As a result, almost every commercial force has developed ways to earn a little extra nuyen from their territories.**

**Corrections is a very lucrative operation that some commercial forces maintain. In addition to fees from a municipality for housing inmates, there is money to be made in commissary sales, fees for parole maintenance, halfway houses, work programs for the inmates, and income generated by providing “volunteers” to research programs. Some corrections also run programs to hire out inmates with special skill sets, typically shadowrunners, for corporate black ops. Highly proftable for the corporations, as the only real cost is an offer of reduced sentences for these deniable assets.**
