## **Are there ways to increase Social Rating for Matrix meets?**

Runners can pay for sculpted Matrix icons which increase Social Rating. Runners can buy these off the shelf at some Matrix shops or pay to have something completely custom. For each 100 nuyen spent, the runner gets a +1 to Social Rating. Due to the fast-moving nature of the Matrix culture, a custom persona only grant a Social Rating bonus for the duration of a single _Mission_ . In addition, the persona should be one that matches the type of meet and who you are meeting with. It doesn’t matter how much you spent on your digitamagotchi-poke-pet skin, if Mr. Johnson doesn’t like it, it’s not going to help you.
