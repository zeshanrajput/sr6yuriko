## Earner

Organized crime means organized profit, and you’ve made enough connections to make the most of that organization to maximize profit between you and a mob of your choice. Of course, caveat emptor—strengthen those connections too much, and you may end up Connected.

- **Cost:** 5 Karma

- **Game Effect:** You gain a Mob (Fixer) contact with the syndicate of your choice, with Loyalty 1 and Connection 3. This contact can buy and sell goods as well as potentially line up work for you.
