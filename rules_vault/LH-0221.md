## Mount Fuji: Japanese Imperial State

\<05-28-83/1649:13>

Reports are incomplete, but multiple Disian-backed mercenary units hired as “special security” by Faustians within Shiawase have conducted attacks against the Seisi No Te in an effort to take the area and capture Empress Hitomi. All of which have failed, but reports show that Empress Hitomi was injured in one of the raids and may have secretly returned to Neo-Tokyo—though there’s no information to corroborate this. As of this post, her group is still holding the line.
