## **Records Clerk**

**Uses: case information, department politics, legal document**

**Places to Meet: breakroom, restaurant, water cooler**

**Similar Contacts : corporate secretary, paralegal, spider**

**A records clerk will be used to working in a stressful environment and being under pressure. Their main responsibility is to know all the current information about crimes and accidents and respond to public inquiries. Records clerks ensure the effcient operation of a police department by continually managing and organizing various kinds of physcial and digigtal information. Their responsibilities include accurately maintaining and updating records related to criminal activities, incidents, and investigations. Through data entry and retrieval, they support law enforcement offcers and other personnel in promptly accessing essential information. The records clerk contributes signifcantly to the transparency and accountability of the police force by ensuring that information is**

**well documented, secure, and easily accessible. In essence, their work helps streamline administrative processes, enabling the police department to function effectively and make informed decisions based on accurate and up-to-date records.**

**Though not tasked with analyzing or gathering the information, a records clerk will have access to most information within the police department.**
