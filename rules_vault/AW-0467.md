## Salvaged Loot

When an encounter is defeated, the survivors may search their victims for salvageable loot and other valuables. Make a teamwork Perception test. Roll 2D6 on the Salvaged Loot Table, and add the number of hits scored on the Perception test to the result of the dice roll.
