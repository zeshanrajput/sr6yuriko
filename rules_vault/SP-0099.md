## **Political Update: Oceti Sakowin**

**For those of you who may not be hanging on current events, it’s time to update your mapsofts because the place formerly known as the Sioux Nation has a new name: Oceti Sakowin, or Seven Council Fires. Here’s an excerpt from their formal announcement:**

**“We founded our nation as part of an efort to reclaim what was ours. That efort continues today. The founders of our nation used the name that many people know us by as a way of ensuring that neighboring nations knew who we were and who the land now belonged to. The time has come, though, to reclaim our name, to label our nation with our own language, not the language of others. Efective immediately, our nation shall be called Oceti Sakowin, in honor of our heritage and our people.”**
