## Ade Aabo

As part of the ritual process for calling upon the lwa, practitioners have created methods to help reduce the potential for the spirit to possess someone other than the intended. The houngan or mambo may have used all the favorite spiritual offerings and dressed the chwal in the forms most pleasing to the lwa, but the spirit may still choose someone else. One of the methods is the creation of a special cap with an inset ring of specially prepared wax. For this to work, the wax must maintain pressure on the head of the wearer. This means that any damage done requires the purchase of a new Ade Aabo.
