## Sigh-on-Wind

Sigh-on-Wind is considered the voice of reason for the queen. He is the lead diplomat in many negotiations with other metaplanes on behalf of the air elemental plane. Sigh-on-Wind is very charismatic and does feel sympathy for the wayward magician. Though not everyone thinks he’s qualified. For one thing, he’s a shaitan, an air djinn rather than an air elemental. There are several spiritual organizations that do not believe a foreigner should be in such a position of power. No outright rebellions thus far, but passive-aggressive insubordination has been known to occur. Second, of all the djinn, the shaitan is one of the tribes who favored demons over humanity.

Sigh-on-Wind’s appearance is of a humanoid black sandstorm, complete with intimidating horns and fiery eyes, a contrast to his actual demeanor. With that alone, few would directly confront Sigh-on-Wind.
