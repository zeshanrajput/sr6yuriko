## New Positive Quality: Façade

Finally, creating a façade you can truly inhabit, and that feels like a core part of you, can help hide one’s true intentions. When this role is taken on, behavior and goals shift along with it, making it difficult for someone to see what an individual’s true goals or motives are.

**Cost:** 5 Karma

Your carefully crafted public face helps you prevent others from seeing your true ambitions and goals. While you’re in the façade, Edge actions used to Judge Intentions against you cost one higher.
