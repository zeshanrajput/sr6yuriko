## Simoon

## (Air, Force 8)

The simoon is a great living fog. Ordinary ones can be the size of house, while legendary ones might be the size of a football field. It sneaks up on its prey, hiding within other dense clouds so that travelers wouldn’t suspect they walked into its belly. It tries to distract and confuse its food from leaving as its digestive gases slowly dissolve them.
