## Maps of the Astral Ways

Information gained from the Piri Reis map, the Al’Azief, the Phaistos Disk, and the Tome of Penticlese, as well as numerous other artifacts and sources, has made its way into an assortment of maps that detail the astral ways and are essential for navigating the Astral Sea. Just as with the Sextant of Worlds, these items are focus-like devices that contain their information in both physical and astral forms. The simplest maps are rolled parchment scrolls, while those with more information may be numerous map sheets bound together into massive tomes with covers of exotic materials. These items can also be used to learn the location of metaplanes for ritual gateways, but mundane versions of that information can be found for lower prices.

Maps of the astral ways are rated with a Force from 1 to 9, and this number is both the power of the enchantments on them and the usefulness/accuracy of the information they contain. These items will typically match up to a known metaplane or a specific area of the Astral Sea, and any one item may or may not be useful in a specific location. The gamemaster has full discretion over whether a specific map or tome actually has any information that pertains to where the players want to go.
