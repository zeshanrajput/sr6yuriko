## Do-Gooder

Most folks have some code of honor, something they will stick to out of principle. But how much a contact holds to that code is going to vary. The do-gooder is one who holds strongly to that code and will go to extremes to protect whoever they feel is innocent. If they find out that a team they work with is doing something to hurt the group they care about, they’ll first try to convince them not to do it. If that doesn’t work, then they’ll potentially turn on them.

**Game Effect:** Determine the group the NPC is protecting. If the contact learns that the runner team has negatively impacted the protected group, they’ll first try to convince the team not to do it. If that fails, they’ll turn them in to the local faction and/ or law enforcement, increasing the team’s Heat by 1.
