## Pirate Island

The Spire isn’t the only city in the Place of Clouds. One island approximately one hundred kilometers from the Spire is covered by asphalt streets, sidewalks, and urban buildings—it evenhas a subway system. The island is roughly half the size of Manhattan and is shaped vaguely like a skull. The city itself is a vibrant place, with neon signs, shopping centers, restaurants, hotels, and casinos. Despite the danger (or perhaps because of the thrill of it), spirits from all over the nearest metaplanes risk coming here to gamble and indulge their darker desires.

This island is Pirate Island, and it’s a hive of scum and villainy. Not a literal hive, of course— not even pirate spirits are crazy enough to allow insect spirits on their turf. This island city is owned and operated by the most powerful airship pirates in the Place of Clouds. The Cloud Corsairs are a loose alliance of the ten most feared pirate ships. Their main base of operations is Pirate Island, though they also have a massive dirigible-style ship called the _Skull Cloud_ that is usually stationed directly above Pirate Island. The _Skull Cloud_ is a flying fortress capable of carrying a full complement of smaller attack ships. The Corsairs have become so powerful and wealthy that they rarely need to engage in direct piracy themselves. Instead, they collect tribute from other pirates and gain wealth from the businesses and casinos of Pirate Island. Any pirates who operate within the Place of Clouds are expected to offer at least twenty-five percent of their take to the Cloud Corsairs—those who do not become the Corsairs’ preferred target for piracy.

The Cloud Corsairs also patrol and protect the airspace surrounding Pirate Island, and they always move to intercept any airship that approaches the island. Instead of preying on them, however, they collect docking fees and provide a safe escort to the docks—provided that you are willing and able to pay the fees, which are not exorbitant. Any airship captains who refuse to pay are turned away and escorted at least five kilometers from Pirate Island, but they are not otherwise molested unless they attempt to force their way past the patrols. The Cloud Corsairs largely ensure that Pirate Island is safe to approach, and they enforce this by making brutal examples of anyone who attacks another ship for any reason within their airspace. That doesn’t mean that it’s completely safe to travel to Pirate Island, as most of the airships in the surrounding areas are pirate vessels that are eager to prey on other airships (including other pirates). The pirates invariably break off if their prey gets close enough to Pirate Island to fall within the Cloud Corsairs’ bailiwick—there is very little that the airship pirates fear more than the wrath of the Corsairs. In turn, the Cloud Corsairs rarely get involved in conflicts that occur outside their airspace.

Three megacorporations have established their own outposts on Pirate Island: Ares, Aztechnology, and Spinrad. Ares has begun importing weapons-manufacturing tools in an effort to establish a

local factory to produce firearms, rocket and missile launchers, and cannons from local raw materials. Thus far they have only sold Ares products that have been imported, as the factory is not yet operational. Once they become established, there will be a new arms race among the airships of the Place of Clouds.

Aztechnology has secured several prominent buildings on Pirate Island and is in the process of building a corporate outpost in the shape of a ziggurat. When complete, this building will be the largest hotel and casino on Pirate Island. In addition to this, Aztechnology has secured the equivalent of a security contract with the Cloud Corsairs. Their jurisdiction is Pirate Island itself. You might think of them as police, but their only mandate is to protect the Corsairs’ interests. This includes making collection runs for the businesses and casinos on Pirate Island and ensuring that all of these taxes and profits find their way to the Corsairs. They don’t get involved in personal disputes and squabbles unless these events escalate to threaten the peace. If they do get involved, they don’t make arrests—they shoot first and don’t bother asking questions.

Spinrad has acquired a local airship docking and repair center, but they do not provide services for customers here. The Spinrad hangar structure is capable of repairing and constructing airships, and its entire purpose is to support Spinrad’s airship pirate crews. They currently boast a fleet of six airships, with the largest of them named _Johnny’s Revenge_ . The pirate Spinrad airships travel the Place of Clouds in groups of three and are not shy about attacking other airships. They currently have an arrangement with both Ares and Aztechnology and do not prey upon their vessels. Everyone else is fair game. For what it’s worth, the Spinrad pirates (most of whom are metahuman Spinrad employees or hired shadowrunners) are not particularly bloodthirsty and have earned a reputation for treating the crew of anyone they attack well. For this reason, some airships that are assaulted by Spinrad pirates surrender without a fight when it becomes clear that they can’t escape. Those who surrender are plundered, but the crew and ship are left unharmed. If Spinrad pirates are forced to disable an airship, their policy is to tow the wreck and its crew to a nearby cloud island instead of leaving them stranded in the sky.
