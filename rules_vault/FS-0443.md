## Baton Lock

In the hands of a trained fighter, sticks of various lengths can be used as grappling aids. When using a blunt weapon, you may use any technique from the Grappling category as though you were unarmed, with the option of using the weapon’s damage (including the bonus from Precision Strikes, if applicable) in place of your base unarmed damage. **Category:** Weapon
