## The Job

A player’s commlink chirps that they have a new message from their fixer. He has a job for you, or that is what you assume from the message header, which says “Carson Place Hotel.”

The rest of the message states, “Meet El Guapo at 12:15 p.m. in conference room 6 on the



second floor. He has another business proposal for you.”
