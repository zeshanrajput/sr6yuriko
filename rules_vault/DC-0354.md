## Living Space (CF Mod)

Unlike simply having amenities, this mod converts CF into actual full-time living quarters. This mod can range from a bare squatter-level sleeping bunk on a cargo freighter to a middle class overthe-road RV to the finest amenities in the most luxurious yacht. The interior space needed and the cost of the conversion depends on the lifestyle represented in the quarters. By the way, if you’re sleeping in your unmodified car seat, with a pile of McHugh’s wrappers for a pillow, that’s a Street lifestyle. The CF needed for this mod is equal to [2 CF x number of people x Lifestyle (1=Squatter, 2=Low, 3=Middle, 4=High, 5=Luxury)]. The cost is equal to one month of living expenses at the desired Lifestyle per occupant. If this space is used in conjunction with an actual lifestyle, the monthly cost is still the normal lifestyle expenses for that lifestyle—it functions just like an apartMAKINGment on wheels. The rating for this CF mod is the Lifestyle rating.
