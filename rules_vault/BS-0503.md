## Aluminum Exoframe

An aluminum-reinforced skeleton and exoframe is capable of handling heavier modules, including weapon systems, armor plating, and moderately heavy-duty utility functions. This augmentation has a maximum capacity of 8. An aluminum exoframe includes the aluminum bone lacing augmentation.
