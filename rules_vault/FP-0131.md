## **Goals**

**> €spion**

**Given its origins, the question is: has the purpose of the French Touch evolved or is it still about mere survival? The right answer is … both.**

**One would expect this safety blanket for French corporate powers to be cast aside once the audit passed. However, in the chaos that preceded the Néo-Révolution, the safety blanket remained to keep these French corps warm and cozy. Then the cyber-republic arose in 2079. Most recently, blackouts struck the UCAS in 2080. They couldn’t just disband the French Touch with all of this happening, right? So now it’s fve years later, and the temporary and circumstantial alliance has become a permanent fxture of the corporate landscape.**

**If targeted by “a foreign [not from France] corporate entity, nation, or organization,” any member can request help and support from the consortium. This often ends up as legal, PR, or security help. And such requests are still made regularly, although not as frequently as they were during the Megacorporate Audit. It seems to average roughly every other month.**

- **Note that the verbiage includes “organization” on purpose. I know that members of the FT have, more than once, requested help to deal with organizations such as the Ordo Maximus or even, ironically, some maneuvers from STV. Well, this particular happenstance was back when Fauchon was still in jail, in 2078, so maybe this explains that.**

- **While the change of regime in France made our corporate overlords worry for a little while, the fact is that the cyberrepublic quickly became a (discreet) sympathizer of the corporations (see Marianne’s dossier in the Null Value upload for more details) and even more a friend of the French Touch. But only the French Touch. No member gets support from**

- **the government unless the request comes from the consortium. They learned to play ball and now they all route every demand**
