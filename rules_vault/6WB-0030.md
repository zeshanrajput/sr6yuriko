## Straight Thievery

We live in a world of increasingly rare and unique items. From time to time, runners are hired to steal some of these items. Occasionally they will be hired by the victim to steal the item back. Theft is a large part of working with organized crime syndicates. While bank robbery is rare, syndicates hire runners to hijack automated cargo trucks, disappear shipments of goods for the insurance, and rob stores who refuse to pay protection money. Thievery is also a common part of Matrix shadowruns. A hacker turned loose in a datastore to steal a specific file might find several other files they can sell on the black market. Taking more than you were asked to steal is frowned on by employers, but it’s incredibly hard to track or prove. Most runners view taking extra stuff as valuable side income.
