## Training Camp

In your younger years, you were trained to be an operative by an organization. Whether it was as a soldier of fortune, specialized assassin or deep cover spy, the lessons you learned have stuck ever since.

- **Choose one:** +1 to any Body, Agility or Strength

- **Choose one:** +1 to any Willpower, Charisma or Intuition

- **Choose one:** +2 to Firearms, Close Combat or Con

- **Choose one:** +1 to Firearms, Close Combat or Con

- **Knowledge Skill (choose up to two):** Academic Knowledge Skills, Security Knowledge Skills, Seduction, or a level in any language.
