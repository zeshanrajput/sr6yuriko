## The Magical Investors Group

Talk about your outside chance—this small group of money-obsessed mages seems to have found out about the code in the last few weeks, and they have become _obsessed_ with it. The group has always been trying a bit too hard to make it big, but they must have picked up on rumors about what it does and how it could be used to make fortunes. In any case, they are fast, nimble, obsessed with money, and pack some pretty powerful spells. They seem to specialize in using magic to enhance the abilities of people, and their agents in the field apparently fairly glow with enchantments and spells. Some of their agents are magically enhanced

for combat, but the most effective ones have magic-sharpened cunning, intuition, and persuasiveness, and the group itself is spending nuyen like it’s water in the hopes of tracking down this holy grail of the finance world.

They have held their own as well. More agile than the big players, and more powerful than the individual cranks, there is a fair chance that they will eventually lay their hands on the code. Of course, they probably haven’t planned what they are going to do when they get that far. I’m betting they will destroy themselves with bickering, but there is also a good chance that one of the big players will put the boot in and take the code after the group has done its work.

If you want to make money and have magical backup, they will be good to work for, but be aware that they are likely to push you to go for impossible situations eventually, or they will attract the hostility of a megacorp, and the gravy train will come off the tracks. Still, good money, right?

- They are actively recruiting as well, if you want to join a group and can think like an entrepreneur, then you could probably join their little initiation circle. So far, I’ve had three of them try to recruit me.
