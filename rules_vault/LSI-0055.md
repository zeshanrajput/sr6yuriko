## Warranty Voider (Home Edition)

As per the quality Voider of Warranties (p. 84, _H&S_ ), you’ve tinkered with a few devices around the home to make them more secure. Add one to the rating of all of the devices in your home, which have been customized to a degree that most would consider eccentric. Whenever you perform an Extended test from within your home, gain a point of Edge that must be used on that test or lost. You must have access to the appropriate tools in order to gain this benefit. Additionally, if you have the Voider of Warranties quality (p. 84, _H&S_ ), you gain a number of bonus upgrade points equal to your lifestyle category: none for Street, 1 for Squatter, 2 for Low, 3 for Middle, 4 for High, and 5 for Luxury). These can be used on any of your upgraded devices.

**Cost: 2 LP**
