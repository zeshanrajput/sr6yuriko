## Evo

The most diverse duchess, **Evo** is best known for their lines of radical biomorphs, rebuilding the metahuman body’s genotype in entertaining ways, but the real money comes from their work in foodstuffs; Evo’s reach into Russian grain, Indian rice, and Japanese fish is second only to Shiawase. They’re lacking in grey and blue bioware, however.
