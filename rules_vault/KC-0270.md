## Agano Kuniko, Kawaru-gumi, Australia

Normally you wouldn’t think of using an algorithm that does data analysis on Awakened drugs. And if you do think to use it, it’s probably to track supply and demand or to disrupt your nearest rival. Agano Kuniko has a different use in mind. She is looking to increase the potency and yields of her crops. Kuniko has been tracking the lineage, output, and potency of the last thirty generations of Crimson Orchid across hundreds of fields and now is letting the Kechibi Code crunch the numbers to tell her where her best plants are growing and help her gain an edge in the marketplace. Now if she can only get her own copy of the code. It would be a shame if anyone happened to come across this data. I imagine it’s worth a pretty nuyen.
