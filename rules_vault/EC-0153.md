## Faces in the Crowd

Bellevue is about being noticed. Generally speaking, that’s a problem for people like you and I, but here it feels more like an asset. No matter who you are or what you do, the goal in the Belle is to bring something different to the table. That’swhat makes you noteworthy. That’s why these individuals stand out among the rest.
