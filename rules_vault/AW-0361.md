## The Great Lake

Nestled up against the volcano is a freshwater lake teeming with fish and other aquatic creatures. Two wide and deep rivers—the East River and the West River—flow out from the lake toward either end of the island. A thick and ever-present fog shrouds the shore where the lake meets the volcano, forming a dense ring that surrounds the entire base of the mountain. A number of small villages dot the lake’s shore, and at its center is Tandora City.
