## Gold Coast 

I know, I know—what coast? But logical or not, this area west of downtown bears the name, and at least the first part of it is accurate. Some stunning mansions sit in this district, and this is where you live if you want to make it clear you’re on the top of the Omaha heap. **Joslyn Castle** isn’t even two hundred years old—it would be considered quite new if it were in Europe—but its grandeur makes it feel older. For a while it was turned over to government and nonprofit uses, but in the ’30s, treasures like this were reclaimed by the rich, as the idea of “public goods” faded in prominence. It’s supposedly haunted, because every somewhat old, prominent building in North America is supposedly haunted, but more interesting are the tales of a group of Infected that supposedly gather at the castle every month. What they’re doing and why the owner (Stabler NanoGro CEO Edwina Cunningham) invites them is unknown.
