## Airships

Airships are common within the Place of Clouds and come in a wide variety of shapes, sizes, designs, and methods of propulsion. Most airships are built of wood that has been harvested from one of the forest islands in the Place of Clouds. Vessels built from local wood are naturally buoyant in the air.
