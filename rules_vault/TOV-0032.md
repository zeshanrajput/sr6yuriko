## Grande Zombies

**B A R S W L I C ESS M** * * * * F F F F F F **DR I/ID AC CM MOVE** * F + \*/1 A1, I2 B / 2 + 8 10/15/+1

When a zombie escapes from the control of its summoner, it becomes a free spirit (p. 56, _Street Wyrd_ ) that is still bound to the body. In most cases, the zombie fl ies into a rage and quickly deteriorates. But on a rare occasion, the freed spirit is stable and becomes known as a grande zombie. Once the zombie has successfully escaped from the practitioner and managed to retain its intelligence, it will often seek to escape the hounfour. If the grande zombie is especially upset with the summoner, it will immediately attempt to kill them—and the chances are high that the unfortunate magician is helpless.

**Skills:** Athletics F, Close Combat F, Perception F, Sorcery F **Powers:** Dual Natured, Essence Drain, Fear **Weaknesses:** Essence Loss (1 point per month)

**Spells:** The grande zombie knows all the spells of the summoner. **Weapons:**

Unarmed Strike [Close Combat, DV 2S, F\*/—/—/—/—]

**Notes:** The grande zombie’s physical attributes are half of what the corpse’s attributes were when alive. The Defense Rating is based on the body of the zombie as well as any worn armor. The grande zombie’s Force is equal to its Essence, so as the Essence increases and decreases, so does the Force of the grande zombie.
