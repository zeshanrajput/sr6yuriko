## **Game Effect: GMT Alloy Cyber Implant Weapons**

**For additional expense and capacity, any close combat cyber implant weapon that deals purely physical damage can be upgraded with the GMT alloy. When attacking any target that has the Immunity to Normal Weapons critter power, any armor gained from the immunity counts as normal armor instead of hardened armor.**

**GMT-laced cyberweapons are somewhat less durable than the original version, reducing AR by –2.**

**When attacking a Disian chimera, GMT-upgraded cyber implant weapons also gain +4 AR.**

**When attacking a Disian shell, GMT-upgraded cyber implant weapons also gain +4 AR and +2 DV.**

**On any attack test made using a GMT alloy cyber implant weapon, replace one die with a wild die. If the wild die rolls a 1, the weapon is damaged and may not be used or retracted until repaired. This requires an armorer shop and an extended Engineering (Armorer) test (6, 1 hour).**
