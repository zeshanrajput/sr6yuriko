## Swindler Stick

One of the oldest tricks in the book is handing off a certified credstick that has been illegally modified to misrepresent the amount of nuyen it holds. This scam rarely works on its own, but that doesn’t stop some people from using it. More sophisticated con artists may find more subtle and clever uses for such a device.
