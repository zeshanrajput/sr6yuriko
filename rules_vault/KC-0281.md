## G04tsucker

## Reality Hackers, Combat Decker

G04tsucker is one of the lieutenants for the Reality Hackers down in the Puyallup Barrens and is a human of Korean descent. While not the leader of the gang, his flair for words allows him to help with all the Reality Hackers’ negotiations. His reputation with the Hackers rose when he brought in work from Chosun Ring a couple of years ago. His current play is needling the Yakuza at every turn. He is easy to spot with a faux-hawk and an armor jacket with vertical, bright neon stripes. G04tsucker brings some welcome face skills to the chaotic hacker gang, but he definitely has some skills in the Matrix to go with his charm.
