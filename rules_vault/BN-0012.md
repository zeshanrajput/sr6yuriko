## Th e Wild Hunt

As any of the JackPointers who make a living outside the sprawl will tell you, you’ll never see a shifter in the wild unless they want you to. Natural shifters spend almost no time in their metahuman form. If they’re un-contacted, you’ll need a language specialist to even start to communicate—and that usually takes months or years.

Any interaction with shifter tribes that a runner will have outside the sprawls will most likely be with one of the many shifter communities that sporadically interacts with metahumanity. They may be smugglers, long-distance transporters, or just happen to have a job in their territory. These tribal communities don’t trust outsiders, don’t have much use for nuyen, and—trust me on this—some of them will eat you.
