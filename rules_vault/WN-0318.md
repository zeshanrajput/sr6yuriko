## 19: digging for the truth

## Hook

It’s “down the road” time, and the neo-anarchist Storm Raven Sect is ready to slip the team another tip on newly accessible locations. They claim it’s a reward for the runners’ good work. The truth is, this one is rumored to hold a pre-quake secret research lab related to some of the hot jobs that have been flying around town. This job comes on the heels of the exploration of the Arsenal basement in the previous mission (p. 85). There’s no upfront pay offer, just a free tip for a job well done and maybe a chance to make the first offer on the paydata if they find any.

The location is the 18th Street subway station, or more accurately, the access tunnel that extends below the former Yves Condominium Complex, now the home of a 500 room Quik-E-Nap coffin hotel.

Access to the station was lost in the earthquake, along with a large portion of the Yves complex. Yves was later fully demolished and the Quik-ENap built, but the damaged sections of the station below were simply closed off.
