## **Making a Connection**

**==> picture [233 x 4] intentionally omitted \<==**

**Dadeson : How can one get through a frst encounter with your group in order to work up an invitation?**

**Sarkenbleck: Honesty. Integrity. True belief. We always talk around the important subjects at frst and then get to being a little more direct down the road. It is never after a frst encounter. Invitations to lesser organizations may be issued in order to allow us to vet potential members, but no one rises to the Asintmah Alliance after only a chance encounter.**

**Dadeson: What does joining entail?**

**Sarkenbleck: A personal invitation from one of the Seven. While the everyday membership can make suggestions and introductions, only the Seven can make the formal offer. This doesn’t mean those with potential can’t join the peripheral ranks of other organizations, whether to infltrate or to elevate their chances of being brought into the Alliance. We are leery of strangers and always background-check potential candidates. One of the reasons we are so successful at avoiding infltration and discovery is our policy of perfect backgrounds. Missing chunks, a history of lying, false records, and extensive periods of incarceration will keep anyone out.**

**After the invitation is extended, the true tests begin. Loyalty tests. Integrity tests. Morality tests. Failure anywhere along the line isn’t a rejection, but a death sentence. It is a harsh reality if you do not make it into the Alliance once it has been revealed to you.**

**Dadeson: What’s your view on mundanes who aren’t part of the Alliance?**

**Sarkenbleck: Potential. They are neutral entities until we can meet and evaluate whether they will understand the truth. This is not necessarily the viewpoint of everyone in the Alliance. Some are more specifc about who they consider ft. A certain level of mastery in a feld is usually a good basis for whether they have the right mentality or mental fortitude for the Alliance.**
