## Biospikes

This augmentation was developed after a rare genetic pattern was identified following a standard bone lacing procedure. This process grows dense bone spikes in the subject, with attached musculature to extrude or retract the bone. The process includes a procedure to create a channel within the body and an aperture in the skin that prevents damage to the subject when the bone is extended. There are two versions, both retractable. Claws are placed in the hand or feet under the nails, and blades are placed in the forearms or shins.

If a character has the bone density augmentation or bone lacing, they can use the DV of that augmentation instead of the biospike DV. Unless modified by bone density augmentation or bone lacing, biospikes have a concealability modifier of –10.
