## Becky Wu Ping

(Fashion Director and Marketing Lead, Light Clouds)

Currently the line developer and VP of Light Clouds, Becky recognizes her humanity while still reaching out to everyone to help them look and feel their best. A consummate fashionista, Becky is known for attending every glitzy event along the Chinese coastline she can fit into her busy schedule. Her mother is alive and her father passed away in the hospital due to an accident from Crash 2.0. She believes she has a very strong connection with Gu Guanyu even to the point that she is eyeing an extraction from his current situation to a larger entity that would give him even greater access to develop whatever research he happens to be taking on at the time.
