## Adept Powers

The social adept is a master of social manipulations. Whether they’re a fast-talking con artist, a charming playgirl, a negotiator, or an impersonator, there are many paths and powers to choose from that allow them to make the most of almost any social situation. **Astral Perception** can be used to read a person’s aura, allowing the adept to get a read on them and potentially giving the social adept the edge they need.
