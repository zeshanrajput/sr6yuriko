## Digging Deeper into DJ

One starting point is a complete bioscan or Assensing of DJ and his augmentations. The information found in such a scan is in his profile in the Character Trove (p. 140). DJ also has a cellular mark like those found on Becky Wu Ping and others who were found confused in Central Park (missions 7 and 10, pp. 58 and 64).

Questioning DJ will get little information, as his memories of the day are fragmented and confused. However, an Influence + Charisma (3) test will help him remember some incidents before, namely some vague memories of riding in an ambulance across a bridge and being rushed into some facility. He doesn’t know why he would be rushed into a facility like that, as he had no procedures scheduled and no memories of any sort of medical emergency. He has no conscious memories of the day where he was tailed by shadowrunners, and showing him video footage of the day will confuse and frighten him.

Let the efforts of the PCs go on for long enough that they are rewarded with some hard-earned information but not long enough for them to be frustrated. If they don’t succeed on the Influence + Charisma test, feel free to move to the next scene with reasonable speed.
