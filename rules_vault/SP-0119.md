## **Corporate Activity (Part Two)**

- **We tend to think of megacorporations as soulless entities seeking nothing more than profit and power. And while that’s true to a point, not all corps, it seems, are stupid enough to ally with beings who want to destroy the very world they get their profit and power from. At the end of the day, these corporations are still what they are, but at least they’re (nominally) working for the greater good. So, here is the second part on corporate activity for this download.**

- **Glitch**

- **What I find interesting is that corporate activity is still business as usual, even with everything going on. So surreal to hear the normies worrying about mundane things like when the next**

**Game Disk or installment of Karl Kombatmage is going to come out. Meanwhile, those of us in the shadows are literally staring down the end of the world.**

- **Bangswitch**

- **Look at it this way—even if word got out, a global panic won’t do us any good. Disians have too many spin-doctors in place, so no one would believe it anyway. Besides, those corps that are mostly on our side still need their revenues to keep fighting.**

- **Thorn**

- **Wars, even ones in the shadows, are not cheap.**

- **Red Anya**
