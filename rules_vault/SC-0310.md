## EAGLE SHAMAN

Shamanic magicians who follow the Eagle mentor spirit miss very little. These perceptive magicians are able to watch astral space not just for signs of trouble but also for clues and valuable information. Their spells and spirits are a useful addition to any shadowrunning team, but their tendencies toward higher moral codes and fighting corruption can sometimes make jobs more complicated than they might otherwise be.
