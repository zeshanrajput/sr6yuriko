## **Blindness**

**----- Start of picture text -----**<br>
TYPE ACTION RANGE DURATION<br>P Major LOS Sustained<br>**----- End of picture text -----**<br>

**The creature is able to cause blindness either through brilliant fashes of light or as a magical effect. With a Major Action, the creature can make an Agility + Magic vs. Willpower + Logic Opposed test. On a success, the target is afficted with the Blindness I status. The effect intensifes to Blindness II with two net hits, Blindness III with four net hits (p. 51,** _**SR6**_ **).**
