## Social Adept

## Backgrounds

The social adept is an unusual archetype. They are among the most open-ended archetypes because the base skills and backgrounds can perform many roles directly or complement the rest of the team. Their magical abilities define the archetype, but without skills, experience, or context, they would just be the most popular person at the office. Whether they are a fixer, face, covert ops specialist, investigator, rocker, snoop, or gang leader, the social adept has a diverse set of experiences, training, skills, and contacts to perform their role as a team member.
