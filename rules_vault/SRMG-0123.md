## **What isn’t subject to Augmented maximums?**

Specializations/expertises, and other bonuses that give themselves exceptions to augmented maximums, such as teamwork (which limits at the leader’s skill ranks), minor actions that don’t add a skill (Hit the Dirt, Take Aim), or dice that are added to a test by spending an action.
