## Rotor

## Cyberspace Designs Dalmatian

Able to be folded down to lawn-mower size, this VTOL recon drone is easy to haul behind enemy

lines. It deploys to hang-glider size and is frequently spotted over urban and rural locations that have contracts with Lone Star or Knight Errant, as they use them for patrol and surveillance.
