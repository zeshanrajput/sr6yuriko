**Then in 2061, when Halley’s Comet appeared, the entire species Awakened. The old folklore about them having supernatural abilities was correct. The whole species now has the rest of the world treating them like they do in Japan, meaning people will just seem to love them and accept whatever they are doing. When the local tanuki population gets to a point where they have to be dealt with or one happens to attack someone, it still isn’t easy to remove the critter. They aren’t tough or aggressive, but they are the luckiest critters I have ever seen in my life. I have seen one that was about to be shot stumble off a wall at the last second, and another stop to grab a piece of discarded bread making a perfectly aligned shot miss. The oddest thing was when one turned and looked at another member of the team. The guy lowered his gun and said he couldn’t take part in killing something that cute and innocent.**
