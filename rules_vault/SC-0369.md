## Skills

Any face fatale worth their salt will work hard to master the skills of Con and Influence. Con is very useful for convincing marks that the face fatale is who they say they are and that their appeal

and feelings are real (when of course they are not). Influence can be just as important for negotiations and displays of etiquette in a variety of circumstances. It can also be used for intimidation, which the face fatale wields through logic or the sheer heat of their physical presence, rather than through the threat of harm. A third priority skill is Perception, important for keeping awareness of the situation around the face fatale and staying on top of new threats before they can develop into danger. They live their life in a lie, often walking a fine line, and they need to be able to duck out quickly when things are about to go south.

The face fatale needs some other skills to complement their core. Working in the shadows is a life unto itself, and there will come a time when diplomacy and communication fail and the bullets start flying. The face fatale should be able to help their teammates when the drek hits the fan—most likely with a firearm of some sort, as ranged weapons allow for the sort of getaway the face fatale would prefer. There are other skills to consider—having a little Stealth can be useful in an infiltration or just getting out of the house without the mark noticing. A few points spent on Electronics, Driving, or similar skills can come in handy from time to time.
