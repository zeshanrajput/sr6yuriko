## Feng Shui of the FEZ

Each of the ley lines that traverse the HKFEZ wields influence in a variety of ways. They can serve as low or medium mana ebbs and flows, often aspected to particular traditions. Earth is a restorative power and impacts not only health magic, but also spirits of the same and actions seeking to heal or rejuvenate tangible life, and it is a common aspect for these lines to take.
