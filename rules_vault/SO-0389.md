## Suggestive Gesture

## [Con (Seduction), Major Action] 5 Edge

You make an inviting gesture or suggestive remark designed to spark romantic—or purely sexual—interest. Make a Con (Seduction) + Charisma test opposed by the target’s Con (Seduction) + Charisma or Composure (whichever is higher). If successful, the target gains the Smitten condition toward you. This lasts for one hour per net hit or until an action is taken by the seducer that would be against the target’s best interests. If the person attempting this does not match the target’s preferences for a romantic or sexual partner, they still gain the Smitten status—it just represents an aromantic or asexual interest or temporary fixation instead. They may not want to date or have sex, but the game mechanics of the condition still apply.
