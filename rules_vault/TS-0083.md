## **Kick Off the Case**

**Stop me if you recognize this trideo trope: The rookie chases a petty criminal into a decrepit old warehouse, and the old-offcer-a-few-monthsfrom-retirement drives around the building. After securing the suspect, the dashing young cop glances around and discovers, instead, a decomposing body. Fade to black. Next scene is back at the station where the sergeant introduces the case and is probably shouting that it has to be solved immediately because he’s got someone on his back.**
