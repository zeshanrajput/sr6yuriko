## Chases: Step by Step

A chase begins when someone is trying to outrun someone else. Usually this happens when vehicles are involved, but you can use these rules to resolve chases on foot as well.

Tracking exact positions with precise measurements is sometimes useful in a mixed combat, but high-speed chases cover extended distances and shifting environments. Instead of mapping out everyone’s exact positions, it’s better to move to something a bit more flexible.

At the start of a chase, you need to define two things: the type of environment and the relative positions of everyone involved.
