## TECHNIQUES OF TRAVEL

## Pathways to the Metaplanes

It may seem like there are a bewildering number of ways to travel to the metaplanes, but they can be broken down into a few simple categories. First, you need to know if you are leaving your body behind or taking it with you. The methods of travel that start with “astral” are all some type of astral projection, meaning your body stays behind. This category includes astral quests, the original method of traveling to the metaplanes. On the flipside, anything that starts with “metaplanar” allows you to physically travel to your destination.

The astral and metaplanar qualifiers are usually listed in front of one of the three types of passageways. **Gateways** are temporary passageways created with either ritual magic or by a critter power. **Portals** are passageways opened by a physical object and anchored to that object; the portal itself may be an ancient artifact or site, a metaplanar device, or an item created with enchanting. Anything that doesn’t fall into one of the other two categories is a **rift** . These are typically natural phenomena generated by a variety of forces, including things like the Yellowstone Anomaly, the Watergate Rift, and some rare types of alchera.
