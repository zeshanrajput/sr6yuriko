## **From Astral Ways, can I buy shade (p. 164)?**

No, use the one from _Body Shop_ instead.

**From Astral Ways, can I buy a shadow cloak (p. 164)?**

Yes.

**From Astral Ways, can I buy the sextant of worlds, maps of the astral ways, or shadow amulets (p. 170)?** Yes, but in order to use them, you will need to bind them.
