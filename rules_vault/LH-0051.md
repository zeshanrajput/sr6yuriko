## Current Wuxing Operations

On the strategic front, Wuxing is significantly lower than Aztechnology and MCT. While they have a few agents and Johnsons who continue to focus on locating Defender cells and agents, as well as continuously update the Divine Map, Wuxing is currently more concerned with dealing with in-house matters—President Chiang-Wu and her agents are constantly coming up with new and creative ways to keep the Disians supporters paranoid and ultimately paralyzed with fear.

- The one I love the most, which I heard about just last week, is Chiang-Wu calling for a mole-hunt to track down whomever assisted the defector in accomplishing their mission in the first place. Ghost, the number of red herrings and misdirection she’s put into play … I think she missed her calling as an intelligence director.
