## Deep Impact

- **Vector:** Injection

- **Speed:** 1D6 combat rounds

- **Duration:** (4 – Body) hours, minimum 1 hour

- **Power:** 6

This drug allows non-Resonant characters to attach themselves to the Resonance being used by a sprite or technomancer. This is noticeable to anyone who can perceive Resonance and who makes a Matrix Perception test but has only one effect: Someone under the effect of this drug can accompany a Resonant entity to the Resonance Realms. To do so, the user of the drug must be using hot sim VR and be in physical contact with a technomancer or a digital device currently inhabited by a sprite. They then must go online using any mundane method. Once they do so, they notice the Resonance pulsing behind the Matrix host they are in.



When the users are in the same host as the entity going on the Resonance trip, they are pulled along, their icons disappearing from the host, and moving to the Resonance Realms. The users experience the trip as standard VR, but they are uniquely vulnerable while they are connected. Users no longer have any interaction with their physical body and cannot pull the plug or disengage until the Resonant entity returns. Users whose plugs are pulled (either by disconnecting the device from the Matrix or the user from the device) suffer 6P unresisted damage. Anyone taking deep impact who doesn’t return to their body by the time their dose wears off suffers 1P unresisted damage every minute until they return or die.

A wild die is added to any roll undertaken in a Resonance Realm, and this drug doesn’t allow a non-technomancer to attempt Resonance actions.
