## **Access Commandos (AC)**

**The most common comparison for these would be SWAT/HTR teams. Access Commandos are well trained, heavily gunned, and have the kind of SOTA cyberware a street samurai can only dream of. They handle cases involving kidnapping, paramilitary groups, or terrorism (like shadowrunners).**
