## **Gargoyles**

## **(Gargoyle)**

**Gargoyles are winged humanoids, nocturnal, and share a weakness for sunlight and iron. They were one of the earliest species discovered after the Awakening, due to migration, zoos, and wealthy collectors. They have since spread to most of the world and adapted to become a more effcient predator in those areas. The ones I cover will be those commonly found within urban environments.**

**When hunting, they prefer to follow their target from a safe height before launching a dive bomb-type attack with their razor-sharp claws. They often hunt down prey and carry it back to their dens. Gargoyle packs have been known to attack larger prey, but it is rare. A troop of gargoyles consists of two to fve adults, and their den will periodically move. If the gargoyle population becomes too dense, it will result in confict, with one den destroying the other.**
