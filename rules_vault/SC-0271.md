## Strategies

This is an excellent archetype to play up the lone wolf trope. When in the Matrix, you actually are most likely on your own, as most shadowrun hackers tend to be. But the Matrix is only one aspect of the world. You are not capable of handling everything yourself, and you know it. Lean into the loner nature if it suits you, but also lean on your team. The street sam will keep you from getting shot, the mage will hack astral space (or whatever it is they do), and the face can hack the conversations. You might not ever acknowledge it to their faces, but you need them, and you’re glad they’ve got your back.

Your Dissonance qualities provide you with bonuses for certain kinds of actions or targets. Make those the focus of your tactics whenever possible, because you can leverage some serious clout in that regard. Keep in mind which drawbacks affect your capabilities as a hacker, and do your best to avoid or mitigate the effects of those penalties.
