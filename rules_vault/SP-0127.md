## **Saeder-Krupp**

**Oh, such pride before the fall. How much further will you go, Golden One? Were you so sure of your power, so secure in your infnite wisdom, that you failed to see the darkness creeping up along your own feet, in your own house? Or is this a game for you? Do you think you have the moves all planned out? Are you merely positioning yourself for the fnal blow? More likely, you have already lost and you know it, but your pride will not let you admit any kind of defeat. So now I ask, how many will you take with you to the grave? Or do you even care?**
