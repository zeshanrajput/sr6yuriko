## Good Cop

## \[Influence (Negotiation), Major\]: 4 Edge

To attempt this action, a target must be restrained or otherwise at the player character’s mercy. Prior to an interrogation attempt, player characters must attempt to erode the target’s resistance by convincing them they aren’t so bad, and if they just listen and do what they’re told, things will go better for them. Make an Influence (Negotiation) + Charisma test opposed by the target’s Judge Intentions. If successful, the target’s Intuition is reduced by one (to a minimum of 1). This reduction of Intuition lasts for one minute, plus one minute per net hit.

Multiple Good Cop actions extend the duration by one minute per net hit but don’t further reduce Intuition.

A target can be affected by both Bad Cop and Good Cop, but the actions need to be used by separate characters. One person playing both Good Cop and Bad Cop risks spoiling the effect.
