## River Boats

The other major krewe to be aware of is the Moonshiner’s Union. They’ve been around in some form or another for probably 100 years at this point, although I don’t think anyone has kept track of their history well enough to be sure exactly when they came to be. Grown out of swamp moonshiners working together, the Union continues to work closely with the local population, both in the city and in the region nearby. If you need to know the word on the street, fi nd some swamp reagents or just the strongest moonshine around. The Union can get it for you.
