## **Negative Qualities**

## **Disobedient**

**This critter has an independent spirit, and sometimes balks at being ordered around.**

- **Bonus: 4 Karma**

- **Game Effect: You may not gain or spend Edge on any Infuence tests to interact with this critter.**
