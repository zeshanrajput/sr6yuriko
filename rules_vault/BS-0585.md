## Ares Warhound

Exhibit A of their current efforts is the Ares Warhound: a genetically enhanced hellhound variant that is easily trained and more than eager to set your enemies alight with its improved flaming halitosis. This beastie is free from Essence-reducing augmentations that would dampen its fire, relying entirely on embryonic genetic engineering techniques that enhance its obedience, intelligence, and Awakened talents.
