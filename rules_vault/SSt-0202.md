## **How It Works**

## **Posted by: Bull**

**Look, I don’t mean to tell you your business. No, wait; frag that. Actually, I do, since that’s why you’re here. If the topic is teamwork, then yeah, you could say I have a fair bit of experience. I’ve been there when a shadowrun’s gone golden and I’ve been there when it’s all gone to drek. Buckle up, kids, and I’ll tell you about one of those.**

**Hell, call it bonus hour. I’ll tell you about both, since it’s the same damn run.**

**Anyway, anybody who knows me knows I don’t like to talk about myself. That’s just not what I do. Lots of reasons for it, I suppose, but the biggest one is “I don’t know you, so why would I tell you anything about me?” That’s why what I’m about to tell you isn’t about me being in charge. I’ve led a lot of runs, but you’ll learn more if I talk about one where I was just a part of the team.**

**I am going to tell you about one of the best and worst shadowruns I ever did. Those of us who survived call it “The Trolls and the Taco Truck,” and it all starts with Lesson One.**
