## Skillsoft Subscriptions

Skill hardwires are great if you’re looking to save some nuyen and pick up very specific skills. But if you need flexibility, skillsofts and skillwires are the way to go. There is a huge advantage to buying the skillsofts you need since you can run with wireless off, but those skillsofts are pretty expensive, so picking up a couple of them may break the bank. Luckily for you, the big megacorps offer a subscription service that gives you access to anything in their library!

The subscription to the network allows access to a single skillsoft (p. 271, SR6) at a time while their skilljack remains wireless enabled. The monthly fee for the subscription is based on the level of the subscription and is added to the cost of the user’s lifestyle. It is a Minor Action to switch between skillsofts. The maximum skill rating cannot be higher than your skilljack and skillwires rating. Knowsofts have no rating and are available at any membership tier.
