## Lifestyle Category Details

Street/Squatter: You regularly make small donations to support local artists or a sports team

Low/Medium: You have season tickets to a sports arena, theater, or museum, you maintain premium super-fan subscription access to a band or solo musician

High/Luxury: You’re recognized as a significant sponsor of your local sports team, theater, or museum, and the bands you’re into know you by name because of how much and how frequently you donate
