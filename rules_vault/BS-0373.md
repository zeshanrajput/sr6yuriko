## Dopadrine

(Bitter, Werden)

- **Vector:** Contact

- **Speed:** Immediate

- **Duration:** 1 hour

- **Power:** 7

- **Effect:** Stops berserker rage, cannot spend Edge on tests involving Agility, Reaction, or Strength

Originally was designed to counteract the effects of kamikaze and nitro, it was used by law enforcement on strung-out gangers. The drug eventually found its way to the streets and into the hands of runners. The drug immediately interrupts any berserker rage the recipient might be experiencing and prevents it from occurring for the duration of the effect. Users of the drug experience a disconnectedness from those around them and gain a point of Edge on all Social tests. When the drug wears off, the user is more susceptible to heightened emotional states that would cause the Frightened or Panicked statuses and automatically fails tests to avoid those conditions for the next hour.
