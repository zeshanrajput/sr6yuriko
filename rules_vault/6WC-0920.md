## GAME RULES

Now it’s the gangers’ turn. They fire their Beretta 101Ts as one. The Attack Rating at Near range would normally be 8, but the four extra gangers boost that to 12 (more bullets are harder to avoid). Still, that 12 is well under Claret’s Defense Rating of 16–she gets another point of Edge!

The third and the fifth ganger add attack dice according to the Grunt Group rules (p. 114, _SR6_ ), so the gangers will get 2 extra dice. Their pool for the attack is now 7, while Claret rolls 8 dice in defense. The attackers get 2 hits, while Claret gets none—and she critically glitches! That won’t do at all. She had 1 Edge left after her attack, and she gained one more thanks to her superior Defense Rating. She decides to spend those two Edge to add one to a die roll. She makes a 1 into a 2–thereby avoiding the glitch!

Still, she has damage coming in. The base damage is 2P, raised to 4P by the 2 net hits. She rolls her Body of 7 and comes away with 2 hits—reducing the damage to 2P. She marks that off in her condition monitor.
