## The Highest Council

We know even less about the Highest Council than any other caste. They are spoken of among the Dis castes with a combination of reverence and fear. It’s said among Disians that the Highest Council truly rules Dis, that “they keep Dis pure and alive so Dis will continue until the end of time.”
