## Negative Quality: Divergent Essence (1 to 3)

Your body doesn’t match your aura. This includes people who are assigned the wrong gender or metatype at birth, as well as more obscure divergences. While this has led you to explore new and different areas of your life, your aura is noticeably dimmed, but is stable. This can be taken alongside the Leaking Essence quality.

**Bonus:** 5 Karma per level

- The player must describe the nature of the divergence.

- **Game effect:** Your starting Essence is reduced by the

- level of the quality.

  - In addition to paying the Karma cost of buying off this quality to restore the lost Essence, the character must undergo adjustments to their body to resolve the divergence. When using cyberware or

bioware to make these adjustments, there is no Essence cost for the specific implants used to correct the divergence. Once both conditions are met, any Essence lost due to Divergent Essence is returned.
