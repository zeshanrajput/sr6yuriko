## Utility And Support

Professional wetwork artists aren’t spontaneous—they’re meticulous. You share ground with the face in that regard, since a lot of research and planning requires social engineering. If the team has a face, you’re an excellent backup and can often make an effective alternate or secondary angle for all kinds of teamed plays. If they don’t, you’re it, but with a killer’s edge. The challenge is to remember to value your teammates. Your instinct as an assassin is that they are patsies. Your instinct as a monster is that they are food. Don’t think they’ve forgotten either. But a team makes things easier, lightens the load, and provides the support you can’t get from an agency or client. If you can suppress what you are, channel and use it to your collective good, you’ll be their ace in the hole, and they won’t betray you.
