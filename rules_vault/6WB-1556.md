## Magical Lodge Materials

Used to create a lodge for shamans or a hermetic circle for hermetics, the gear is sold with a bag that holds all the components. Once used, some items are reusable. To reuse parts, it takes a number of hours equal to the rating of the circle or lodge to break down properly, and purchasing the new missing components cuts the cost in half.
