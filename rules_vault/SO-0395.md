## It’s Not So Bad

## [Trigger: Damage, Action: Minor (A)] 1 Edge per box of Stun, 2 Edge per box of Physical

Sometimes a hit can’t be avoided, and in those times it’s helpful to know how to take that hit without going down. You can take this action when a player character takes damage, after the soak roll, and any other mitigation has been applied. This involves rolling with the punch and angling the body so it doesn’t take as much punishment as it should. Spend Edge to further reduce damage, 1 Edge per box of Stun damage, and 2 Edge per box of Physical damage. No matter how much Edge is spent, the damage can’t be reduced to less than one box.
