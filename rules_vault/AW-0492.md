## The Al’Azief

It’s an open secret that the Draco Foundation thoroughly studies every artifact that passes through their hands, so it was widely assumed that the Al’Azief was copied before it was handed over to Dr. Gordon. That assumption has fueled rumors about a copy of the book being stolen from the Draco Foundation for at least a decade. While an entire intact copy has never surfaced, pages reported to be from the Al’Azief occasionally turn up. The fact that a working duplicate of the Sextant of Worlds and numerous maps detailing the Astral Sea and various metaplanes that can be traced back to some of these pages lends weight to the idea that there are indeed accurate copies in existence.
