## Unplanned Encounter 1

Pickpocket (use Covert-Ops Specialist, p. 83, SR6) hits Swen, but he notices quickly after a spirit (use Force 6 spirit of air, p. 147, SR6; add Fear, Psychokinesis) in the area trips up the pickpocket with the Accident power. The pickpocket is a serious opponent, but he will try to run, negotiate, or fight (in that order).
