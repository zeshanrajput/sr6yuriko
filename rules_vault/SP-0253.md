## **Evo Security Guards**

**The twelve security guards are a cut above typical corpsec. Six are always on-duty, with six off-duty. This does not include the two security supervisors. Despite the long hours and isolation, they consider this position highly important and maintain a high degree of professionalism and alertness. For their stats, use Centurion (p. 140,** _**Firing Squad**_ **). They have a dice pool of 8 for Perception tests and will normally buy 2 hits.**

**Two of the guards are stationed in the security monitoring station, monitoring cameras. Two more are stationed in the guard tower attached to the drone building, while the remaining guards patrol in a Gopher. They’ll respond to any intruders with lethal force while calling for backup. Once an alarm is raised, the six off-duty guards will gear up and be ready for action in 1D6 rounds; their barracks and armory are located on the ground foor of the main building.**
