## **Milk Run**

**The target is going on a school trip, which will take them outside of their more highly secured environment. On the trip, the target is permitted to have only one gomatia with them, and their security team will be trailing nearby.**

**Taking out a gomatia is best done at range, out of the reach of their tongue and jaws. Don’t bother putting Narcoject in their food, because gomatia are immune to toxins. If you need to take one down without killing it, use gel rounds instead of tranq darts. Gomatia have no particular vulnerabilities or resistances. Just remember the rule, and stay away from those jaws.**
