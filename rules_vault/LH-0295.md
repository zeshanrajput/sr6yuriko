## By Lew Steinbush,

## NewsPulse LIVE!

After almost fifty hours, the largest surge of manastorms the world has ever seen has finally dissipated. Reports are coming in from all over the world telling tales of devastation and loss of life. Witnesses from across the globe report strange, swirling colors in the sky accompanied by eerie silent lightning. Then, just minutes before the storms dissipated, a wave of what most called “magical energy” surged toward the southern hemisphere.

A special session of the UN and the Corporate Court is scheduled for later this week to establish a response plan, as well as determine the cause of this magical phenomenon, and how to prevent it in the future.
