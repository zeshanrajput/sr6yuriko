## It’s Just Business

Given enough time, the players will get to speak to Anna. She is friendly but always keeps an eye on any

other customers. After a little persuasion (meaning a payment of at least 50 nuyen) she will remember the car and the driver. She remembers him because he has been one of her best customers lately. She has no issues telling the players what he purchased but will not give away all his secrets. Depending on what the players offer her, she is willing to tell the players that the driver’s name is Silver Nail, and that he bought magic reagents, magical lodge material, and two spell formulas (Flamestrike and Fireball).
