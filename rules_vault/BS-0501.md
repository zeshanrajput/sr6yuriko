## Critter Exoframes

An exoframe is a skeletal modification that adds a cybernetic framework with external modular attachment ports to allow for a wide range of augmentations that can be swapped out or replaced as needed. Essentially an adaptation of the bone lacing augmentation, this includes an exterior frame that extends from the spine, skull, and limbs. With no modular components in place, it has the appearance of a grotesque external wireframe. With one or more exoframe modules in place, it looks even stranger.
