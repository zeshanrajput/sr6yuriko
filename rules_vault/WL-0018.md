## **Research and Experimentation**

**Megacorporations as well as many smaller organizations also perform research and experiments on critters, seeking to understand their unique nature and powers—or to test how toxic something is. Some bioware implants have been developed by analyzing and modifying organs that some critters have (and metahumans don’t).**

**Due to the extraterritorial nature of most corporate facilities, there is very little oversight into the fair and ethical treatment of animals as test subjects. Megacorps are not a fan of bad press, however, so they tend to do their most egregious work in secret facilities, often using subsidiaries that provide them with a measure of deniability.**

**The most dangerous such places are pushing the envelope on what is possible regarding the augmentation or engineering of critters. I trust everyone’s seen that footage of the team that managed to break into an Ares facility that was making cybered-up dinosaurs. Don’t end up like them.**

**If you can score some solid evidence of mistreatment of animals, you can usually get a fair amount of nuyen from their competitors for the paydata.**
