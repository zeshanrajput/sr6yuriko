## Underdog

You’re not a front-runner. When you’re behind, you feel inspired to do your best work. Or go out in a blaze of glory.

- **Cost:** 7 Karma

- **Game Effect:** Whenever anyone has a position advantage against you in a chase (see p. 175), add a wild die to all of your Piloting tests.
