## Augmentations

## Grades

Cyberware and bioware implants are available in five distinct grades: standard, alphaware, betaware, deltaware, and used. The prices for cyberware and bioware presented in this chapter are for standard ’wares. When purchasing implants of other grades, apply the Essence cost, cost, and Availability adjustments as noted on the Implant Grades table. All accessories and add-ons must be of the same grade as the implant to which they are added.
