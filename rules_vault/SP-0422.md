## **New Alchemical Preparations**

**New innovations in alchemical research aimed toward fghting and defending against Disians has produced useful new formulas for preparations. Each one requires a specifc formula. Unless otherwise noted, these have an area of effect with a 2-meter radius, which can be boosted by +2 meters per +1 drain value during the preparation’s creation.**

**Commercial versions of these preparations can also be purchased following the rules on p. 89,** _**Street Wyrd**_ **. In addition to the standard options available there, one increase in area may also be purchased for commercial preparations (to go from a 2-meter radius to a 4-meter radius) at an additional cost of 500 nuyen.**
