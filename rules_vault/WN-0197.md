## Who’s Who Becky Wu Ping

This is the first time Gabrielle Al Thani Spinrad will be in attendance at a major social event since the death of her husband, so her look needs to steal the show and the hearts of everyone watching. As fashion lead of SpinGlobal subsidiary White Clouds, Becky Wu Ping was tapped to design her look. This is a tremendous career opportunity for Wu Ping. She isn’t going to chance getting it wrong. That means having advanced intel on what all the other major designers are doing.
