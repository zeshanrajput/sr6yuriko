## SOCIAL SHOPPING

## Outfitting the Runner: Clothing

It’s often said that “the clothes make the metahuman.” And when it comes to social expertise, it’s true. A wardrobe is a collection of specialized tools, just like a samurai’s weapons, a decker’s cyberdeck, and a mage’s grimoire. How a face chooses to present themselves is often a key factor in either their success … or failure.
