## Shooting a Barrier: Full Auto and Metal Door example

Through the first door and running fast up on another, Grizzly raises the AK and sends another Full Auto blast at the same spot. The AK has a DV of 5P, but Grizzly doesn’t realize this next door has a metal core, bumping its Structure rating to 11. The DV of the attack doesn’t meet or exceed half the Structure rating, giving Grizzly a whole lot of ricocheting rounds and a door that is still quite operational as they charge forward into it.
