**Having Awakened from the green iguana, no one expected this to be a ferocious predator, but hiding somewhere in there was the DNA of a ferce raptor. The novaraptor looks very little like the iguana—in fact, it actually has more in common with the extinct velociraptor. With bipedal movement as their primary form of locomotion, the novaraptor is a fast and agile runner. They have the same large dewclaws on the hind legs and equally sharp and deadly foreclaws. All of which can ignite in order to attack prey with both razors and fre. They can also produce a fame from their mouth. This isn’t like dragon’s breath but more of an additional bit of searing pain on top of the already excruciating pain of their sharp teeth and powerful jaw.**

**Now the novaraptor is burning it all down again. Along with using fre as a hunting weapon, the novaraptor has been regularly burning massive sections of the jungle. It isn’t for food or marking its territory, it’s just burning things to burn them. My limited research didn’t discover whether this is a toxic behavior and the novaraptor is somehow twisted or if it’s just Mother Nature balancing things out. Maybe the arcane enhancement of the jungle’s growth is too much.**

**No matter the answer, it doesn’t change the fact that I got trapped in the middle of one of these burns. I was keeping tabs on a mated pair of novaraptors deep in the jungle when I caught the frst hints of smoke. It wasn’t my pair, but a third that had started its burn behind my ingress trail. I considered levitating, but I didn’t know what would meet me in the canopy. I should have risked the levitation. It wasn’t more than a minute later that the whole jungle around me was flled with smoke and I couldn’t see anything. I worried it was a hunting tactic and the novaraptor was coming into the smoke to hunt, but I didn’t encounter the actual raptor until after I found a ravine to duck into and follow out. It was deep enough the smoke hadn’t flled it.**

Its hide is thicker and tougher than one would expect and the creature is immune to fre (no surprise there).**
