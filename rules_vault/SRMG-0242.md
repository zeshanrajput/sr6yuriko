## **How often will the Compulsion quality affect me if I take the Compulsion quality?**

We encourage you to role play this whenever you can. However, every Mission there is a chance your Compulsion will be triggered. If you do not indulge it, you start accruing the withdrawal penalty until you go indulge in your compulsion or the Mission ends.
