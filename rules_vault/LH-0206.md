## Russia: Cloudy Clearwater

Posted by: Gramius \<06-22-2083/1313:45>

This is an update regarding the escalation against local Yakut shapeshifters throughout the Lake Baikal region.

Until the fall of 2082, an organization known as the Saban Zaga had control over the area. This dominance was originally established in the ’70s, with help from three principal groups.

The first are metahuman dissidents disillusioned with the governing powers within Yakut, including Evenk tribals (fishing communities surrounding the lake and up the Ulan-Ude River), and pro-Russian factions in the southern region. They’d been unable to pursue their agendas until the arrival of someone I am calling, “the White Wizard.” More on them later. The Saban Zaga and the White Wizard successfully pushed back against the Yakutian forces.

The second was the Vory-dominated Russian military presence in Irkutsk, which was tasked with maintaining the security of the water-enrichment facilities there. These forces included an aging armored brigade and multiple satellite relays.

The third and final group is the Amur Tigers, a mercenary company that made its reputation in part by their officer training initiation that involved penetrating Yakut territories, then locating, engaging, and skinning a shapeshifter—supposedly using close combat methods. As expected, wolves, bears, and great cats are their preferred targets.
