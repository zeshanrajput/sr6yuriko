## Site 000 Security Trooper: Standard

## (Professional Rating 5)

Responsible for basic and day-to-day security functions, these troops are the first line of defense should anyone be foolish enough to try and breach security. They may be the grunts of the site, but they’re also more skilled, trained, and better equipped than the average security guard or military trooper.
