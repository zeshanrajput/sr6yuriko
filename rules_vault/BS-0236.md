## Tough Gut

The tough gut bioware enables the individual to process a variety of substances that are considered non-standard. The variety of substances that can be processed must be organic matter. Those with it have a –1 LP modifier applied to their Necessities costs (p. 179, _Sixth World Companion_ ). Furthermore, they are more resistant to ingested poisons and toxins, gaining +1 Edge on Toxin Resistance tests.
