## Reflex recorder

This system uses extra nervous tissue linked to specific areas of the body to improve muscle memory. The reflex recorder adds 1 to the rating of a skill linked to a Physical attribute. Multiple recorders may be taken for multiple skills, but you can’t implant two reflex recorders for the same skill.
