## laser sight, low power

## Guncam

| **AVAIL** | **COST** |
| --------- | -------- |
| 4(L)      | 350¥     |

**Notes:** When used as part of a Perception test before combat begins or when ambushing, guncams grant one point of Edge if the target is not concealed. If a shooter initially misses with a guncam-equipped weapon, they gain a +1 dice pool modifier to an attack roll if a Take Aim action is performed on the second shot. Guncams have a capacity rating of 2 for visual enhancements.

**Slot:** Any
