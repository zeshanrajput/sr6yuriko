## **Tactics**

**Dadeson: For such a small organization, how do you manage to make any signifcant change?**

**Sarkenbleck:** _**All**_ **change is signifcant. Its cumulative effect is what will reshape the world. None of Asintmah’s faithful would hold back or fall short in their efforts to bring order to the entropy of this world that is caused by its greatest cancers. Those who believe are not tempted by the easy road; they see the path ahead. While some among us value peaceful discourse, many know that more extreme measures are going to be required at times.**

**We are the disciplined few with the clarity of thought to know that such goals are only achieved through pure human skill, honed through arduous practice that leaves those without the iron will of Asintmah panting in the cloud of dust.**

**Asintmah is not new. She has grown. One of the earliest to see and feel the value of Asintmah was Michael Jordan. He was no adept, just a man with Asintmah’s will. He dedicated himself to practice at levels no one else could achieve. He was the greatest of all time in basketball and on a whim went to play baseball and again, rose ranks faster than any other player of his age.**

**That personifes Asintmah.**

**But you want to know what we will do. How far we will go. Among us, that varies widely, but the glory of Asintmah is independence. Your ac-**



**tions are yours alone from which to reap beneft or reward. We are everywhere, we see the moving pieces of history. How far are we willing to go? A code to cripple the Matrix, dangerous to all, but none of us tried to stop Winternight.**

**Dadeson: Winternight?! You claim the Crash? Sarkenbleck: No, they do, but were it not for the determination of Asintmah among their ranks, they would have failed before they started. And had they listened more, the threats that harmed them most could have been dealt with frst. Now, they reinforce and seek to build their own refuges that we must root out.**

**But excuse me, you interrupt, which is something that does not occur in Asintmah discourse. We have members who seek arcanogenocide, alongside others who see the source as the problem and seek to void the world of mana. Others see the rips and rifts to other realms as a wealth of opportunity to allow them to pull through the magic that has fooded our world and corrupted us.**

**We have not always had success. We made contact with the monads, seeing their ability to show tech as the fawed system of artifcial enhancement that it is, but their alien minds rebuked us. Despite the threats they made, they never seemed willing to execute their efforts. Tech stolen from them is still in our arsenal, and we fully intend to use it when the time is right.**

**We know this battle cannot be won as a headon onslaught. It will come in chips and pricks until all the tiny wounds have undermined the structure and bled out the target before a fnal strike is made. Then, nothing will ever be the same again.**

**From us, expect subterfuge when the mission calls, precise violence when something specifc needs to be removed from the road, and no less than wanton destruction at the hands of professional murderers when a message needs to be sent to both the attacked and the allies of the attackers.**

**A tool can be used until the job is done and then destroyed or repurposed.**

**Dadeson : This is all vague, Joe. How do you all operate? What are your plans?**

**Sarkenbleck: One, we each tend to operate independently. We come together and chat to make sure efforts aren’t countering each other or, if they’re getting close, how we can best work together.**

**What we want is also simple. Augmentations are a plague. Repair and replace is fne but enhancement is no longer fair to humanity. Its effect on the human mind and the damage it does to the overall structural integrity of the human form is a travesty. We have started with social and cultural efforts that counter the transhumanist movements and promote a pure form, pointing out the hazards of augmentation from rejection to cyberpsychosis. We also point out the debt it creates and the need to be forever beholden to your installer for maintenance and**

**updates. The sims and trid shows rarely show this aspect when they glorify these chromed-up street warriors, hiding their need to visit a street doc or pay a favor to a megacorp in order to get anti-rejection drugs. And while cultural change is great, we know the true way to stop a problem is to remove it completely. We have no problem being labeled saboteurs and terrorists if we can help the world break its addiction to this technology. Manufacturing plants, datafle storage for blueprints, viral code insertion—all of these are actions we take.**

**The Matrix seems a place to advance knowledge and make man better, but instead it has become a giant video game where humanity goes to hide and be coddled after being stomped on by their corporate masters. Sadly, the only way to halt this blight is to destroy it. Winternight had the right idea. Bring down the Matrix in order to bring man back in line with nature. We allow escapes to false green places while we burn the real ones, and we let everyone hide behind funny or sculpted forms rather than their real selves. This has led to a society that feels it can freely attack with impunity from behind a mask of anonymity. We have our own alternative that helps our people see a world without this crippling addiction while keeping connection alive.**

**To bring down this monster, we look to the past and to the present. Every mega has a way to take down their rival’s Matrix. Look at the blackouts. One city at a time wiping the slate clean. We gained so much support during those times. We saved lives, we saved families, and we showed people the true evil in the world. We will fnd all the corporate secrets one day and unleash them all at once while placing our safe alternative into the hands of the masses.**

**As for magic, it is an abomination that we know nothing about, but we play with it like nuclear fssion. It’s a danger we think we can control because we can make it sling some spells or call over some spirits, but we know that’s not the limit. We’ve seen bigger, and we don’t need that kind of trouble. The bulk of this world is mundane, and we don’t have defenses against the dark arts. In a war of magic, we are fodder to be thrown out to tire an opponent while those who are magically enhanced perform important missions. Asintmah looks to every culture and R&D program that seeks to separate itself from magic or for a way to remove magic from our world. Much of our work is a reversal of corp techniques. While most corps want to steal mana from other realms, we want to drain this place of it and return our planet to its mundane and pure existence.**

**We have gained access to the tech used in Denver that siphons mana. We know the siphon can go either direction. We have leads on places where our mana could easily be dumped to rid our world of that problem once and for all.**
