## Motecuhzoma

Motecuhzoma is an enigma within the corporation. Rumors and speculation have abounded for years as to whom, or more precisely _what_ Motecuhzoma is **\<<\<1.7 MP removed/archived by SysOp-01>>>** indicates that he is most likely a free spirit or at least a metaplanar creature of some kind. Regardless of his true identity, for many years Motecuhzoma has been the main driving force behind the corporation’s magical R&D initiatives. Normally quiet and reserved, he is known for having a ruthless temper toward anyone who interferes with his work. And while he has relinquished most of his operational and logistical control to the Special Projects Division in order to concentrate on research, Motecuhzoma maintains a solid working relationship with Director Salazar, to the point where the two are _almost_ considered friends, if not allies.
