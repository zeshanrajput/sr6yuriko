## **Map Madness**

**The magic of the Sixth World is a funny thing. Sometimes items are a mystery, and then over time, something appears that could break the balance of power. That is what the Etteilla Map, named for John-Baptiste Alliette (an eighteenth-century French occultist and tarot scholar), is going to do with the tarot cards. According to the early street**

**rumors, the map shows the location of any tarot card that has reached our plane.**

**Thus far, the map is the stuff of rumors and legends, but too many people are talking about it. They’re seeing it in dreams and visions then describing it the exact same way. These viewings are the best clues to its location, and one Echo is hot on its trail.**

**Bazel is one of the best at fnding cards. As a free spirit, they don’t need sleep and have extra time and extra abilities to help them in their search. They have a few good leads but need some quiet runners to look into them.**

**This team is sent to Vashon Island, where the map is supposedly hidden among a private collection of cartography works by Amerigo Vespucci. The owner, Kris Rain, was a one-hit-wonder trid star who turned recluse after his massive success. The real truth is he got Infected with HMHVV and is a vampire. He invested his massive fortune and has been able to live his reclusive life without trouble for over two decades. He has become slightly more aggressive of late, and his “service” for delivering and disposing of food is not happy with him.**

**The house is well kept but deserted and creepy. Rain doesn’t have security, but he is formidable and operating on his home turf. He targets individuals and then focuses on evasion and escape before he can be overwhelmed by numbers.**

**The map is in his collection and can be located by assensing for it. The map looks relatively normal until a tarot card is brought near, at which point symbols for all the tarot cards form along the border. When a symbol is touched, it turns red if the card is not currently on the physical plane or blue if it is, while at the same time a dot appears on the map and the compass rose animates and points toward the card. This makes the map great for narrowing down a city or region but then needs to be brought to that place to use the animated compass rose to fnd the card.**
