## Headjammer

The headjammer is used to neutralize implanted commlinks and cyberdecks. Once attached to the location of the device, it works in the same way as any other jammer but limits its effect to your implanted device. Removing a headjammer from someone without the proper key requires an Electronics + Logic or an Engineering + Agility (10, 1 Major Action) Extended test. Removing aheadjammer from yourself is an Athletics + Agility (5) test, requiring a Major Action. Failure to succeed in any of these actions could activate any additional anti-tamper devices this jammer may be attached to.

Headjammers are available with ratings from 1 to 6.
