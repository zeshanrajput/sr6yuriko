## Odds and Ends

Expanded Teamwork tests don’t have to be limited to social skill tests, either. There are a few additional examples below that involve non-social skills.

Assisting a combat skill test (Major Anytime action):

- **Distract the target:** Con + Charisma

- **Spot weaknesses:** Perception + Intuition

- **Assist diagnosis with a Matrix Search:** Computer + Intuition

- **Help the medic focus, reduce distractions:** In-

  - fluence (Leadership) + Charisma or Composure

- **Restrain the wounded patient:** Body + Strength

- **Look for details or clues to point out:** Perception + Intuition

- **Anticipate the target’s path or actions:** Judge Intentions

- **Check the Matrix for signs of the target’s passage:** Computer + Intuition
