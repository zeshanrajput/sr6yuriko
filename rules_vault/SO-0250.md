## Calming Voice

## **Cost:** 0.5 PP per level

## **Activation:** Major Action

The adept is able to enforce a calming emotional response from any targets within range. The adept gains +1 die per level to all Influence tests used to calm people down or defuse a situation. In addition, the adept may make a Charisma + Influence (3) test to remove the Confused, Frightened, Panicked, Misdirected, Smitten, or Suckered status from a target.
