## Encounters

Traveling the Wastes of Miggon is always dangerous and rarely uneventful. Any time a group of characters attempts to travel through them, they are likely to encounter a hazard or threat. The exact distance or time between encounters will vary depending on the region the characters are traveling through, as well as how they are traveling. A good general rule of thumb is to have 1 to 3 hazards or encounters per trip from one location to another. The random and chaotic hazards and encounters can always be avoided if you’re willing to ride the Death Train instead. This carries its own risks, of course.
