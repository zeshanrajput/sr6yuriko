## **Don’t: Be too Humble**

**At the same time, walking into a meet and telling Mr. Johnson that maybe they shouldn’t hire you, as you’re not that good, is a bad look. Wherever you go—the meet with Mr. Johnson, a corporate suite, a ganger hideout, a government offcial’s offce, whatever—walk in like you belong there. The more you act like you might not belong in a certain spot, the more others will start to believe that, and you don’t want that. Quiet confdence goes a long way in a lot of situations.**
