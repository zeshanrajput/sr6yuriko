## Skills

One of the advantages of cyborgs over normal drones is skill learning and use. Instead of autosofts, some jarheads can possess and develop their own skill sets and may complement these with skillsofts. Cyborg-adaptation includes cybernetic skillwires that can run normal skillsofts accessed through the CCU’s onboard processors. Many cyborgs rely on skillsofts extensively, as they have had limited opportunity to learn how to do things via traditional training before being deployed. Full-grown adult brains retain their own skills, and after a transition and adaptation period (a few months), they may use them at their normal levels.
