## **Opposition Force Update: Guerreros and Blood Daggers**

**For those of you who don’t know, the Guerreros are Aztechnology’s answer to units such as UCAS Delta Force and SEAL Team Six, CAS Marines, British SAS and Royal Marines, Sioux Wildcats, and Renraku’s Red Samurai. The unit comprises four distinct branches called “warrior orders”; each one focuses on a particular warfare doctrine or specialization. While normally kept separate, these orders will often work together in task forces whenever necessary.**

**Ocelomeh (a.k.a. the Jaguars, Jaguar Guard) are primarily a defensive and protection detail order most often deployed within Aztlan borders or on high-value Aztechnology corporate property. However, Jaguars have been recently observed operating with Guerrero Task Forces outside Aztechnology borders. Additionally, Jaguars have been observed securing several locations that are rumored to be current or future Harvester sites. Outftted with the latest weapons, augmentations, and gear Aztechnology can provide, the Jaguars also have some of the most powerful magicians and adepts within their ranks. Jaguars can usually be identifed by either leopard pelts integrated into their uniforms or similar tattoos.**

**Cuacuahtin (a.k.a.Eagles) consist entirely of Awakened individuals. Eagles most often act as generals or feld commanders of Aztlan or Aztechnology forces. They use their magical abilities to coordinate and enhance the performance of their troops. They are typically identifed by an eagle patch on their feld uniforms. In recent months, Eagles have either planned or directly led task forces in the feld.**

**Otonin (a.k.a. the Brave Ones) are experts in deep infltration, assassination, and asymmetrical warfare behind enemy lines. Brave Ones are not a unit per se, but more a designation. They operate mostly as single individuals but were recently discovered to be operating in three- to four-person cells. When selected as an Otonin, the operator’s past is erased. During the Week of Death, the Brave Ones are known to have conducted (at least) thirty high-value assassinations.**

**Cuachicqueh (a.k.a. the Shorn Ones) are heavy-hitting, paramilitary black-ops shock troops that perform all the missions that require certain combinations of brutality, terror, carnage, or destruction. Like the Jaguars, they’re often given access to state-of-the-art weapons, augmentations, and gear, but most prefer to customize their own kits. Shorn Ones are so named because of their shaved heads, Mohawk-style haircuts, or single braids. Recent Shorn Ones operations have been difcult to confrm, as they seldom leave much standing or any witnesses.**

**Blood Daggers are a recent addition to the Guerreros. Technically an Aztlan Military unit, the Blood Daggers have been conducting joint operations with the Guerreros since the Aztlan-Amazonian War. Composed exclusively of blood magicians, the Blood Daggers are sent in to deal with higher-level threats or those specifcally suited to their abilities. In recent months, Blood Dagger magicians have been embedded with Guerrero task forces as specialized magical support and force multipliers.**
