## Manhattan’s Magical Societies

- Illuminates of the New Dawn

- Magical Investors Group

- Ordo Maximus

- Dunkelzahn Institute of Magical Research

- Society of Hawks

- The Holy Order of St. Francis



Groups are only one-half of the strangeness that is the New York magical landscape. The city is crawling with magical hotspots, none more magic than Central Park itself. Following the Awakening, the park experienced a surge of ecological growth. Fauna and flora that hadn’t been seen for years were suddenly commonplace throughout the park. Shiawase established a team to research these changes, and a hundred academic teams, including MIT&T, have followed. What remains clear is that the Awakening shifted the manascape for the city, and that shift has attracted things that were not here before.
