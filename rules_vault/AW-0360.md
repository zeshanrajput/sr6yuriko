## Tandora City

The Tandorans have established a floating city, though in truth it surrounds a small island nearly at the center of the lake. Much of the city floats, but its center is on solid ground so covered by docks that you might not notice your arrival on dry land until you have gone far beyond its threshold. The guardian spirit of Tandora City resembles a huge plesiosaur—for all I know, she is a close relative of Nessie. She is rarely seen above the lake’s surface but is always near.

At the center of the city is a temple that was built to honor Miko. Her remains are kept here, and many Tandorans make a yearly pilgrimage to pay their respects on the anniversary of her arrival on Tandora.
