## **Brashton Seakeller**

**I’m not here to cause trouble … haha, yes I am. Seakeller is an active hunter but doesn’t have a resource base to pull off the moves he has. While others don’t care as long as he keeps paying dues and providing tales and the occasional card, I chose to dig a little deeper. I can’t be entirely sure, but a job in Africa that was paid with gold doubloons defnitely points to Seakeller working for the Sea Dragon. Nothing inside the society has been paid for with anything odd, but this job seemed like a rush and Brashton paid with what was on hand. This could have been a ploy to point that way while the truth is something else, but that’s a dangerous patsy to play with.**
