## **>** Winterhawk

When speaking with the sapient spirits of fire, it is difficult to keep up, as they can change discussions or decisions as quickly as we blink. They are not irrational—far from it. Their logic follows the path of least resistance as long as there is conversation to discuss. Fire elementals accept death as part of the cycle of life and may briefly mourn a fellow spirit. They don’t think that far ahead. If they had a motto, it would be “ _carpe diem quam minimum credula postero_ ”—pluck the day, trusting as little as possible in the next one.

- If you ever wanted a spontaneous adventure, bring a fire elemental along.

- Ethernaut
