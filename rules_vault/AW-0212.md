## Fausteans

Of all the Disian allies, Fausteans represent the greatest threat. They have actively pledged their literal mind, body, and soul to a Dis patron in exchange for a boon that grants actual power by yet-unknown means. In exchange, Fausteans are willing puppets for the rest of their days, actively reporting back to the patrons, acting as eyes and ears on this metaplane for Disian interests.

We’re still working on an active list, but so far, we have identified Fausteans in multiple corporations and governments, as well as a few in various societies and independent operators. I’d like nothing more than to expose all of them, but I can’t risk them finding out. It would jeopardize too many operations and put too many lives in danger.
