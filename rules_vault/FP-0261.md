## **The CAS**

**Since the CAS is more stable than the UCAS, we need to tread more carefully. Thankfully, we were never as active there, mainly operating in the Oklahoma area. Still, it didn’t take long for the Department of Domestic Investigations (DDI) to have their own Warpath-focused team. Because our scope was more limited, they initially had greater success in their operation, but we quickly wised up and their degree of success dropped quickly. After Crash 2.0, a lot of the surviving members were in the CAS and many hid in plain sight under false identities.**

**Today, our CAS operations are still limited compared to those in the UCAS, and we have so far avoided any major infltration or crackdown, although they managed to successfully catch one of our leaders last year. Thankfully, we are not the only terrorist organization they have to deal with, and Aztlan’s action in Texas, as well as the annexation of South Florida, has provided us with plenty of opportunities to strike.**
