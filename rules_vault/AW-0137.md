## (Air, Force 4)

A punkah resembles a humanoid cloud formation, slowly shifting within their shape. Their heads can be shaped like a rabbit, a lion, or even an elephant’s head. Most punkahs are servants at the palace and are familiar with cleaning and organizing a household. They mostly float around quietly, getting things done without being seen or becoming a nuisance to those around them.
