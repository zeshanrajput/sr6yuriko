## Capoeira

Developed in Amazonia (then known as Brazil) by slaves of African heritage, capoeira has practitioners who avoid detection by disguising their art as a dance. Since the practitioners’ hands were often shackled, preventing them from attacking or

defending effectively with their arms, capoeira emphasizes highly evasive movements and leg techniques like kicks and sweeping the legs.

_Similar Styles_ : Engolo (Angola) _Categories:_ Mobility, Striking _Signature Technique_ : Feint
