## Hotspot

## Dump Sh0ck

The club has many locations across North America—this one is located at 50 Meadowlands Parkway. After the blackouts, this site was transformed and has grown both figuratively and literally into what it is today—a Mecca for any upand-coming sports streamer or hacker. In AR, VR, or the boring real world, this place flashes, music bumps, and the sounds of games being played can be heard all the time, day and night. It has a reputation as a no hack zone. The Dump Sh0ck collective (owners and fellow customers) will take action against anyone breaking the rules. The crowd has a long memory—if you break the rules, don’t expect to be able to walk around for at least a month or so without your commlinks getting randomly bricked—or worse.
