## Ward

## (Anchored)

## **Threshold:** 6

Ward is a ritual taught to many magicians. It creates a mana barrier (p. 161) with a rating equal to the leader’s magic plus net hits on the Sorcery test in Step 6 of the ritual. The ward may encompass a volume of up to fifty cubic meters times the sum of the participants’ Magic ratings.

This ritual takes four hours to complete. The ward lasts for a number of weeks equal to the net hits from the sealing step of the ritual. If the leader spends 6 Karma, the ward is permanent.
