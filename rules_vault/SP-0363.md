## **Example of Disian Sorcery**

**A native Disian is attacking a team of runners by casting a Fireball spell into the group before they can get into close combat with his chimera bodyguards. The base number of units chosen for the spell is 12, and this gives the attack 20 dice (12 Magic + 8 Sorcery) and an AR of 18 (12 Magic + 6 Willpower). The base DV is 6 (1/2 units used) and the starting area is a two-meter radius. The Disian decides to Amp Up the spell by 1 more DV point and increase the area of efect by another +2 meters, increasing the units used to 16, the DV to 7, AOE to a four-meter radius, and making the drain 8. For the roll to resist the drain, 4 additional units of mana are expended (making the total cost of the spell 20 units of mana) reducing the drain from 8 to 4. The Disian rolls 12 dice (two times Willpower) to resist the adjusted drain of 4. As the reduced Drain Value is less than the “caster’s Magic” (the units used), any damage taken will be Stun.**

**The Disian has made a rather average roll of 5 hits on the spellcasting test, but the actual number of hits resisted by the team will depend on their Essence. The mages and adepts with a full 6 Essence have to oppose all 5 hits. The face with some bioware and 5.6 Essence only has to oppose 3 hits, the rigger with 3.2 Essence only has 2 hits to oppose, plus they gain a point of Edge, while the street samurai with less than 2 Essence only has 1 hit to resist and they gain 2 Edge while doing it. The drones operated by the rigger are also in the area of the freball but are unafected by the Disian fre.**
