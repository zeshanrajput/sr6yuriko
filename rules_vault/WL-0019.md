## **Specialized tasks**

**Critters have unique capabilities, such as the enhanced sense of smell that makes canines excellent trackers, or a horse’s frame which makes them ideal as a mount, or something as simple as a bird’s ability to fy. These critters may be used for specialized jobs that take advantage of their natural capabilities. If a critter has a unique ability or even an Awakened or Emerged power, you can train them to use that power on command.
