## **restore spark**

**This powerful ability allows an individual to restore lost points of Spark. This includes points lost through a Munge Spark attack (p. 115, Hack & Slash) or to those who have previously had codemods (p. 125, “Hack & Slash”), which are then removed. When used to recover lost Spark from a Munge attack, the action takes one hour per point lost. When used to recover lost Spark from codemods integration, an amount of Karma equal to the restored Spark attribute as if raising an attribute (p. 68, SR6) is needed during the action and an amount of time equal to twice the restored rating in hours.**
