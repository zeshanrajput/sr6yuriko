## Grenade Barrage Roto-Drone PACK

## (15,100 nuyen)

If you like the idea of launching rockets from a roto-drone, but don’t have _that_ much nuyen to burn, I suggest you turn your attention to grenade launchers! They have the added benefit of being able to use smoke and stun grenades, instead ofonly being able to blow things to pieces. They can do that too, though!

**MCT-Nissan Roto-drone** [w/ heavy weapon mount: ArmTech MGL-12 w/ airburst link, 6 stun grenades, 6 fragmentation grenades, 6 high explosive grenades, 6 smoke grenades]

**Suggested Autosofts:** Clearsight, Maneuvering, Stealth, [ArmTech MGL-12] Targeting
