## Hyperallergenic

This mixture is a unique cocktail of biochemical cytokine release triggers (and other stimulants) that can cause anaphylactic shock even in targets without allergies. Those who have allergies are more susceptible to the effects, so if a character already possesses the Allergy quality (p. 75, _SR6_ ), increase the power of the toxin by 1 per severity level (mild, moderate, severe, extreme).

- **Vector:** Contact, Inhalation

- **Speed:** 1 Combat Round

- **Duration:** (6 – Body) x 10 minutes, minimum 5 minutes

- **Power:** 9

- **Effect:** Special\*

\*Treat this as if the character would have extreme allergies and is exposed to the allergen (see p. 74-75, _SR6_ ; take a –6 dice pool modifier to all actions and 1 box of unresisted Physical Damage for every 30 seconds of exposure to the allergen; cannot spend or gain Edge while exposed to the allergen).
