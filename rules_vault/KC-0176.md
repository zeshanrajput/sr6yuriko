## Bigger Guns

The work being done by Yingyong is getting a lot of attention; here are some of the significant corps keeping a close eye on the unfolding story.

**Bach Mai Medical** starts the list and makes this an unusual situation in which we get to watch what happens when two arms of one megacorporation are after the same thing. You might wonder if they would join forces, and maybe on some back-channel level, they do. Bach Mai, however, is looking at the Guizhen R&D for non-security purposes, mostly due to its ability to immediately and non-aggressively put an individual to sleep without the need for drugs, simsense, or magic. The technology could theoretically help to fight an individual’s insomnia or other related conditions. This means a medical services corporation would have significant interests in their work. It’s surprising that at the time of this compilation, other medical groups such as OmniStar and CrashCart weren’t also investigating the tech.

- I feel I should mention something here. OmniStar is certainly exploring the details, but they do not seem to be taking an active role in reaching out or extracting data or personnel. At least, not yet. Kimberly Ronson is the inspector who normally handles Omni’s market exploration and expansion for Asia, but either this has not been brought to her attention, or she and her team have decided to not get involved.

- Pistons

**Nemesis Arms** is one of the divisions of weapons and armaments under the Shiawase family of properties. The word got out recently about the Guizhen R&D, and Nemesis sent Tarou “Tommy” Shoehi to investigate the validity of the technology and, if possible, buy up the designs and technologies of the entire line. He and Gu Guanyu met virtually several times and supposedly had an in-person business luncheon along with Yingyong’s chief financial officer, Yan Xun. Certain financial offers are rumored to have been made, with the possibility of an entire corporate embrace.

- This caught my attention, and after a quick search, I found some memos passed down from the desk of the leading shareholder, Reiko Shiawase, regarding the exploration. Lady Reiko could be making maneuvers of her own—not only cement her position on the board, but also to point out that she is ready to make a decision regarding the damage that the Shinto Advisory Board has, in her eyes, inflicted upon the Shiawase name and brand.

**rEVOlution Arms** has obvious interests in developing weapon technologies put forward by Yingyong, with opportunities that AI and Monad alike see as useful without being absolutely destructive. They have field observers all over and above the world, constantly sampling companies in search of existing technologies that can be incorporated and advanced. The perspectives of Gu Guanyu caught their attention before, and they have extended offers for partnerships, seeking to shut out competitors such as Ares Arms and Yakashima, who retain large presences in Hong Kong.

- I’m guessing that this is the primary source for Rikki’s involvement in this.

- Old Crow

**Wuxing Security** was discovered to also be investigating Yingyong for opportunities, especially with regard to the Guizhen R&D materials. Breakthrough technology in pulse-microwave energy weapons that are easy to carry, concealable, and ready to be integrated into pre-existing small arms platforms has their full attention. Sharon Chiang-Wu has sent financial investigators from the Wuxing Financial Services division to investigate their profit potential.
