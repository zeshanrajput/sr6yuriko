## Resonant Drugs

## Baud

- **Vector:** Injection

- **Speed:** 3 combat rounds

- **Duration:** (6 – Body) hours, minimum 1 hour

- **Power:** 9

Some technomancers, when they submerge, can form a Resonance link with another technomancer. This allows them to talk and communicate emotions to each other. This drug has a similar effect: Someone who takes it can build a Resonance link to a willing technomancer, sprite, or someone else who is taking this drug. The link, like the Resonance link, allows communication, although with the drug it is two-way communication. A device is not required to create the link, but the link won’t be very useful without one. The drug also allows a distant person to help with teamwork tests. Whenever one half of the link is attempting a task and the other end is paying attention, a wild die is added to the link, and the person on the other end can spend Edge on the test. A technomancer can assign a sprite to connect with someone using baud for teamwork purposes.

Any Matrix damage or negative statuses suffered by someone who is linked by baud is also suffered by the person on the other end of the link. This includes events such as icon tracing, which will reveal the physical location of both people.

When the effect of the drug ends, so does the link. Each user of baud takes 2 unresisted Physical damage, and the Willpower, Logic, Intuition, and Charisma of the user are reduced by half (round up, minimum of 1). The link can be continued if more of the drug is taken, with the damage being cumulative, although postponed until the link is broken.

Baud is addictive, and deeply personal, as emotions are shared through the link. It is almost impossible for one half of the link to lie to the other half, and first-time users are often unprepared for how emotionally exposed they become.

Technomancers can use this drug as well.
