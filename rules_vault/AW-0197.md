## Section 04: Threat Assessment, Disian Allies

## Posted by: Ringmaster

As much a threat as the Disians represent, I’m just as concerned about their allies. Dis would not be the threat they are if they did not have them. Therefore, it’s important to determine just how deep Disian influence goes. Because when the time comes, we won’t be fighting _just_ Dis.
