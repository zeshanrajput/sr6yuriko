## Yakuza

## Kenran-Kai Yakuza

Cobbled together from survivors of the Nishidon- _gumi,_ undesirables from the Shigeda- _gumi,_ and a diverse recruitment off the streets of Puyallup, the Kenran-Kai Yakuza have control mostly to the south and west of the Puyallup district. Their main controlled smuggling routes are via Route 7 with their collection of go-gangs policing it. Other than the smuggling routes, they have some success with a collection of bunraku parlors, gambling, and narcotics across all of the district, though it’s minor compared to the other Yakuza.
