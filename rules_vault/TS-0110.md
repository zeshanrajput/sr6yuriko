## **You Can Trust me**

**Remember, we have attorney-client privilege here. What you’ve told me in the past remains between us, so if you need me to make a few connections for you on the outside, that is something I can do as your representative. You need me to get in touch with one of your friends who knows how to get things easily? I can do that. You have buddies you’ve worked with in the past who can help out? I can help out there as well. Even if you ended up hung out to dry and everyone you knew has abandoned you, I can probably fnd someone who can help.**

**Think about it. I know who these judges are, and these district attorneys. I know where they work, and what cars they drive. I know the clerks at the courthouse, and I can get on the inside very easily. That judge giving you a hard time? Wouldn’t someone like him change his tune if, say, compromising trid captures of him in an adulterous affair come out? Maybe arrange a conversation with the overzealous DA or corporate equivalent on their way back home. Or maybe key evidence for the case can simply vanish when it is needed. After all, those lockers are secure, but they aren’t foolproof. All of this costs money, but in the end, it may cost you less than what it would be to see a case go to trial, not to mention what happens afterward should the case not go your way.**
