## Sixth World Martial Arts

Developing new ways to hit each other with fists, sticks, and sharp objects didn’t stop just because people started growing horns or shooting lightning from their fingers. The Sixth World has spawned its own crop of new combat techniques, many of which have made their way into the shadows.
