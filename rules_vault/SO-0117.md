## Elements

So, what makes this fundamentally different from forgery or any other scam? Easy. You’re offering something they can’t take home with them. No proof, no evidence, and hopefully something so questionable in legality (or potentially scandalous) that they can’t go to the law for resolution.

The Clip Joint doesn’t have to be a business, per se, with a storefront or anything. You can run it out the back of a van. Plenty of pimps have their own mobile version of this scam. But what you’re selling works best when you control the field, just like any game.
