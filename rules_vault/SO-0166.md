## Scaling

You’re likely going to try to move as much currency as possible. As ever, the bigger the mark, the better informed or protected they are. When you start moving in the five to six figure range, they’re doing their own research or even running it past their own actuaries, digital or flesh and blood. That means you either need to get close to them to earn their trust over their normal channels of proof, or your proposals need to be far more convincing to win over the whole team. The latter is much harder to do. The upshot is that you’re more likely to be seen as a quack with a junk investment proposal rather than a malicious actor, so there’s a lot less risk unless they uncover that your reports are bogus. The best way to cover for that is to salt the Matrix with your fake speculation over a longer period of time,

building a kind of con credit that lets it integrate into the zeitgeist, which can still look like a bad investment, but probably not a con on the surface.
