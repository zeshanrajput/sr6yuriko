## Out of Favor

You always seem to owe something to somebody, and whenever you try to settle your debts or do something that should net you a favor or two, it just doesn’t seem to work out that well.

**Bonus:** 5 Karma

**Game Effect:** When you gain Favor Points, you gain one less (to a minimum of one). Whenever you spend Favor Points, it always costs you an extra Favor Point.
