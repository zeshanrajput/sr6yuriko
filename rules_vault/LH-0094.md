## Operation: Ark

## Posted by: Frosty

To be blunt, not everyone who’s aware of what’s happening believes we’ll win. Some are taking measures to secure safe havens for themselves and others they connect strongly with. I learned of these measures while tracking down some leads on other matters.

Now, I’ve found no global project coordination. There’s been evidence of similar designs taking shape by different parties. Amazonia, Azania, Yakut, the Naga Kingdom, the Kingdom of Hawai’i, and both Tir governments, as it turns out, have been working on this the longest. There are others, even some of the megas, but it seems that’s not where most of their resources are being put to work.
