## **Game Information**

## **Speed: Immediate+**

**When a substance has a Speed of Immediate+, the substance’s efects kick in after the action used to take the drug. The downside is the Power of the substance for purposes of Addiction Resistance tests is +4, and any action that benefts from the efects of the substance gains the wild die for the duration.**
