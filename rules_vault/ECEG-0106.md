## Gaeatronics Cypruss and Kneader drones

Of course, it isn’t only residents that make use of the swampy waterways. There are any number of corporate facilities out in the bayous, and some of them become targets for groups of ne’er-do-wells. Gaeatronics in particular has had trouble with ecological crusaders attempting to remove their power substations and pipelines and designed a form of defense drone to help raise alerts and protect unmanned locations. The Cypruss drone is technically one of the largest drones on the market, but most of that bulk is simply a shell to better disguise the drone among the native cypress trees. The drone’s realistic outer features prevent observers from noticing anything beyond “yet another tree.” Inside the drone there is a high-end sensor suite allowing the operator to keep an eye on the surrounding area. In addition to that, each drone comes in one of two variants, bearing either a hidden gun mount or a rack of drones it can let loose into the swamp.
