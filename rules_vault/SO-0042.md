## Feed on Desire

Understanding is gained by learning about who you’re seeking to exploit. Then comes the critical step of using what you know against them. Everyone has vices, and when we play into those vices, we play into their desires. This represents a fraction of the available paths and their responses. To break it down into a dramatic device that’s easier to remember, let’s use the Seven Deadly Sins as a framework.
