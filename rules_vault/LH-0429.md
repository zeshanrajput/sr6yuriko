## Game Information for Gamemasters and Players

## The Disian Shadow War Timeline (Abridged)

## November 02, 2082

Week of Death begins, and the Disian Shadow War officially starts. The next four months are a series of engagements and skirmishes between Disian and Defender forces within the shadows, with the Disians gaining a moderate strategic advantage. ( **Developer’s Note:** For background information regarding events before this date and relevant to the Disian Shadow War, see the following in this order: _Cutting Black, Slip Streams, Collapting Now, Third Parrallel, Scotophobia, Needle’s Eye, and Final Bets_
