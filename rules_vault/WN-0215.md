## The Garage

Silver Nail will punch in a code to open the chain-link gate. He backs the car into one of the rental units and rides out and down the street on a Yamaha Nodachi (p. 24, _Double Clutch_ ).

The runners must get into the chain-linked area and bypass the mechanical key lock (rating 4) without being seen by the security cameras or the guard (see the key locks section, p. 241, _SR6_ , for info on how to pick locks). Silver Nail has hidden three MCT Gnat micro drones and a disposable commlink in the car for tracking. Additionally, a Force 4 spirit of fire is watching and will follow the car. As soon as the car moves, Silver Nail will follow in the astral to see what he can find out.

Once the players have the car, all they have to do is call El Guapo to arrange a meeting time and place.
