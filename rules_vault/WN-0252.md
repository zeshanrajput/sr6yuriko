## Part Two

Any runner who keeps track of the news after the heinous acts of their target are shocked to see the events reported on, but several different faces have been edited over their target. The clothes are the same, the footage is all the same angles, but several different other people are taking the blame for the target’s actions. The faces are people the runners recognize from the job at the c-suite ( **All the News that Fits** , p. 52).

This part can start with the runners reaching out to Piper to see if she knows anything or Piper reaching out to the team, asking them to dig into some strange contradictions with the news and info from her own contacts.

There is nothing Piper hates more than fake news or loves more than uncovering fake news.

No matter which way the contact is initiated, Piper offers the runners a job: Find out who was behind this and why. The pay for the gig has one big variable. Piper’s basic offer to the team is a 10,000 nuyen lump sum. If the runners reveal the part they played in the fake news, this job might require the runners break a little shadow etiquette. To compensate, Piper ups the offer to 15,000 nuyen. Negotiations run as per standard, with a boost of 1,000¥ per net hit.
