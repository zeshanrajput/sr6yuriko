## **Griffon**

## **(Alatusleo Aquila)**

**The basic namesake, pulled from Greek mythology, fts the original physical bill. It has a large feline body, powerful eagle-like wings, sharpened talons at the end of its birdlike forelimbs, and a raptor’s beaked head.**

**This was my third near-death experience when the wily critter dropped me at the top of a dense fr. Hitting the branches kept me disoriented enough that I couldn’t get a spell off to slow me down. On the negative side, the fall didn’t kill me, and I wasn’t going down gently. That critter paid a healthy percentage of my medical bill after I sold its bits to my favorite talismonger.**
