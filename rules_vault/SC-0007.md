## these character creation recommendations apply to all provided backgrounds

Attributes: Logic 6, Willpower 5, Magic 6

- **Skills:** Conjuring, Enchanting, and Sorcery. All of these skills should start as high as possible, though only one of them may start at rank 6 (with some exceptions provided by certain qualities). You may have only one skill specialization at character creation, which might be best taken as Conjuring (Binding), Enchanting (Alchemy), or Sorcery (Spellcasting). The Astral skill is also important but not as vital as the other three mentioned above.

- **Knowledge skills:** Arcana, Magical Societies, Magical Traditions, Spirit Types

- **Positive qualities:** Analytical Mind, Aptitude (Conjuring, Enchanting, or Sorcery), Exceptional (Logic or Willpower), Focused Concentration, Rote Alchemy, Spell Components

- **Negative qualities:** All Business, Combat Paralysis, Glass Jaw, Honorbound, Impaired (Body or Strength)

- **Life modules:** Alchemist, Astral Explorations, Clairvoyant, College/ University, Magical Studies, Event: You became extremely interested in unusual or mysterious topics
