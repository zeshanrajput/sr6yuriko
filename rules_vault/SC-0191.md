## Additional character creation recommendations

- **Attributes:** Agility 3, Strength 4

- **Skills:** Athletics 4, Close Combat or Firearms Specialization

- **Knowledge skills:** Bodybuilding, Rehabilitation Therapy

- **Positive qualities:** Will to Live

- **Negative qualities:** Loss of Confidence

- **Life modules:** Ganger, Event: You struggled through some tough times
