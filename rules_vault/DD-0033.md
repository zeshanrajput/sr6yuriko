## Event:

## Brush With Death

You really managed to piss someone off, chummer. Someone took a crack at you and the assassination attempt was very nearly successful. If it weren’t for the quick reactions of a friend, you wouldn’t be standing here today. You’ve got some scars that will never let you forget.

- **Choose one:** +1 to any Body, Strength or Willpower

- **Choose one:** +1 to Edge

- **Resources:** +50,000 nuyen to be spent on augmentations you acquired because of the attempt on your life

- **Contact Points:** 4

You gain one quality, positive or negative, that arose from your brush with your own mortality.
