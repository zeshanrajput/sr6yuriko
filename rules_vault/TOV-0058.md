## **(** Spirit of Earth **)**

Azaca spirits take the form of an exuberant youth, eager to act. The lwa of this family is generally associated with the harvest and is part of the Rada nanchon without any Petwo counterpart. Kouzen Zaka plays both the role of good-natured peasant but can be a bit of a trickster and gossiper. In addition, there are Minis Azaka, Zaka Mede, Zaka Krebe, Kouzinne, and Orisha Oko.

At the gamemaster’s discretion, when the ritual to summon the lwa is performed with exceptional diligence or the goals of the summoner and that of the lwa align, an additional service request may be granted, and even some requests may not cost a service.
