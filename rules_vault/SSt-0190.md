## **Make Sure You Listen to Each Other**

**Communication between team members is super-important, and if there’s one aspect of communication we need to do better, it’s the listening part. Lots of runners are perfectly happy to let other members of their team know what they are thinking, but we’re not always great at responding well when others do the same thing.**

- **Hard Exit**


**different approaches, and if you don’t know which one your team is using and you, say, turn off your ’link while everyone else is getting ready to receive tracking agents, you’ll mess up group protocol. That’s a mistake that could be avoided if the person was prepped properly. > 0rkCE0**
