## Karate

One of the first Asian martial arts introduced to the Western world, karate originates from Okinawa and focuses primarily on striking, with particular emphasis on the fists and other parts of the hand (“karate” translates as “empty hand”). Kicking techniques are relatively common in karate, while grappling and weapon use is more rare. _Similar Styles_ : Tang Soo Do (Korea) _Category:_ Striking _Signature Technique_ : Tameshiwari
