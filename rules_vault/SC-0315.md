## Gaia First

You grew up in a megacorporate institution, raised among batches of other orphaned children. Like many of your fellow kids, you never had any memory of your parents. Your childhood was highly regimented, filled with propaganda designed to prepare you for a productive life as a loyal employee. The visions began shortly after your eleventh or twelfth birthday—painful and distressing but impossible to fully comprehend. The official response was to medicate you heavily, which left you in an awful fog. Not long after, you projected into astral space. There you were greeted by a great eagle spirit, who guided you into the metaplanes and showed you many things.

Your Awakening had come, but it brought no greater understanding of the visions. You knew they meant something important, and the psychologists and teachers didn’t have the answers you needed. So you ran away. Most runaways are quickly recovered due to implanted tracking devices, but spirits came to your aid. You were concealed when you needed to hide and moved swiftly when you needed to run. Out in the open air, you felt a sense of freedom you had never experienced. You’ve never looked back. Your early visions remain as incomprehensible to you as ever. They are flashes of memory, perhaps your earliest memories as a baby, or perhaps from someone else. Violent memories, with the impression of pain, fear, and fire.

Your present vision is clear, however. From the outside, you see how the megacorporations try to dominate both people and nature, and you are driven to undermine that domination. For some, that only requires giving them a taste of real freedom. Others might need a stronger message. When you began to make friends in your new life, you were drawn to a clique of people who seemed to have similar ideas. They turned out to be TerraFirst! activists who were thrilled to have a shaman join their ranks. Most of them are dead now, killedin retaliation for damaging megacorporate property that was harming the environment. Maybe it’s time to focus on just earning a living for a while, and running the shadows might give you some chances to frag up some polluting megacorp assets on the job. Who knows? Maybe the people you run with will help you with some ideologically motivated property damage!
