## asson

**Cost:** 15 Karma

**Game Effect:** To purchase this quality, you must have the Divination metamagic and a Voodoo Ceremonies knowledge skill. You gain an asson, which is bonded as part of gaining the Asogwe quality. You can call upon assistance from six members of the congregation in rituals once per week. These members are assumed to each have a dice pool of 6 in Sorcery and Conjuring. Harming another member of the community will result in the permanent expulsion from the group (see Bokor). This results in the loss of the Hounsi, Sur Pwen, and Asogwe qualities—which cannot be bought again.

The asson is an essential and sacred Voodoo rattle traditionally made using a dried gourd or coconut decorated with beads of glass and snake bones. The asson is given to houngan and mambos when they achieve the rank of asogwe. The asson is used to create a welcoming area for the lwa. The asogwe stands at the poto mitan and salutes the four directions with song, rhythm, and dance. This use of the asson to communicate via the sacred rattle is known as “talking asson.” Talking asson also refers to the secret language of initiates.

The asson is a Summoning focus (Force 4) that is associated with a specifi c nanchon. This allows it to work for any of the lwa in that nanchon. If the focus is stolen, it cannot be bonded by another.
