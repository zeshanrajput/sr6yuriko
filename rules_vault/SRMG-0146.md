## **Can I stack spells and complex forms?**

Casting the same spell or complex form against the same target does not stack the results unless the spell or complex form specifically says that it does. For instance, if you use the Diffusion (Firewall) complex form and get 2 net hits, and then use Diffusion (Firewall) a second time and get 3 net hits while sustaining the first complex form; the Firewall of the target is reduced by 3 and not 5. If you and a friend both cast Increased Reflexes on you, where you get 3 net hits and your friend gets 2 net hits, your Reaction and Initiative Dice increase by 3.
