## **Aztechnology**

## **Posted by: Rifleman**

**It’s well-known that Aztechnology has a particular lust for magical power that** ~~**sometimes**~~ **often borders on suicidal. There isn’t a risk they won’t take to gain** _**any**_ **kind of next-level power; frag any and everyone else in the process or who gets in the way. Combined with a massive superiority complex that permeates the corporate culture at the highest levels and a greed-streak hundreds of kilometers long, it shouldn’t shock anyone that they signed on with the Dis despite the latter’s endgame.**
