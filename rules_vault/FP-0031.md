## **Matrix Mayhem**

**Mr. Johnson needs the team on a fight to Chicago ASAP, details to be given during the fight. A copy of the Jormungand virus has surfaced within a closed-up system on an old Truman Tech host. The Asintmah want it before it falls into the wrong hands. They aren’t the only ones after it, and the runners discover they are going up against some of JackPoint’s big names as they try to get the program before it vanishes out of their reach.**
