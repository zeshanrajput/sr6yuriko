## **How to Deal**

**If you are going to be working in an area with a herd of elephants, take extra precautions to avoid them if you can. Most elephants will avoid you, and even bullyphants aren’t likely to be a problem unless you get within rife range of the herd. A Grimm’s elephant might represent a bigger problem, but if you do your legwork, you’ll at least be aware that one’s out there—they always leave a trail of destruction. If you fnd yourself under attack by a bullyphant or Grimm’s elephant, your best (and likely only) chance of survival is to get in a vehicle and leave.**

**If you need to take a bullyphant or Grimm’s elephant down, be aware that even a direct hit from a Panther cannon is not likely to do much—if anything. Your best bet to get around their hardened armor is mana-based direct combat spells, though it will still take quite a few mana bolts to drop one.**

**Between the two, the bullyphant is much more dangerous. Not only do they have more armor and a higher Body attribute, but they are very difcult to sneak around. If you need to sneak past a bullyphant, make sure to have an extra advantage such as concealment from a spirit or an invisibility spell. Neither Grimm’s elephants or bullyphants are dual natured, so they won’t detect any astral activity.**

**By comparison to the bullyphant, what the Grimm’s lacks in armor and enhanced perception, they make up for in attitude, and their close combat attack deals incredible damage. When dealing with either of these critters, avoid close combat at all costs—it takes only one hit from one of these to kill even the toughest individuals.**
