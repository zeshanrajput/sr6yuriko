## **When I purchase gear with modification, what is the Availability?**

If the modification is a flat Availability and not a cumulative Availability, then use the higher of the Availability values. If there are multiple modifications with a mixture of cumulative and flat Availability, add the cumulative value after taking the maximum of flat Availability values.
