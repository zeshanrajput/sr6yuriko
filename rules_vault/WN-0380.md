## 27: below the barracks

After analyzing the Aztechnology data, Mr. Seven believes the location of “Airlock,” the Dis gateway mentioned in mission 22, **Enemy’s Enemy** (p. 95) is under Governors Island. The team is hired to confirm that theory.
