## Astral Gateway

## (Anchored)

## **Threshold:** 9

This ritual opens a temporary gateway to a metaplane selected by the ritual leader. The leader must have visited that metaplane or met a spirit or creature from that plane in order to open a gateway to it. The gateway must remain in the place where it is opened. Any objects within five meters of the gateway become dual-natured, and anyone, even non-Awakened individuals, can astrally project in that same range. The ritual takes six hours. The gateway lasts for a number of days equal to the net hits on the sealing test.
