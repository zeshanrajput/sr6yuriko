## **Can I use an RCC and a Cyberdeck together for my hacking?**

No. The cyberdeck must be used in conjunction with either a commlink or a cyberjack. An RCC and a cyberdeck can be on the same PAN, but the ASDF used is from the commlink or cyberjack plus the cyberdeck.
