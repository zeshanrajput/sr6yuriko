## **Game Effect: Anti-Mana Grenades**

**When the preparation is activated, it creates a mana ebb within its area of efect, reducing the efectiveness of spells and other Awakened abilities. With one hit, it creates a low mana ebb. Two or more hits creates a medium mana ebb, and six or more hits creates a high mana ebb.**

**Base Drain Value: 6 Base Cost: 800¥**
