## Breath of Binding

Air spirits seem eager to be bound to your service.

- **Cost:** 10 Karma

- **Game Effect:** First, you must have the Fresh Air Spirit quality, and then you must successfully bind a spirit of air in order to

take this quality. If you offer twice as many drams of reagents as required when you attempt to bind a spirit of air, the spirit does not roll to resist the binding. Instead, it buys one hit for every full four dice it would have rolled.
