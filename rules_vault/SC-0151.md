## A Show to Remember

This adventure hook works best with the illusionist background, but any team with illusion magic and a flair for the dramatic will enjoy it as well.

The Coyote shaman is contacted by a fixer who has ties to the entertainment industry. They’ve been hired by the manager of a hot and upcoming rock band to produce a stunning live show at a prominent local underground venue (Underworld 93, for example). The band wants illusion magic to help them put on an amazing show, but access to the licenses and insurance to do it legally has proven to be too costly. Unwilling to compromise their artistic vision, the band has insisted on finding some unlicensed talent for the job. Oh, and a former band member who was recently kicked out happens to be part of a local gang, so the runners are also hired for additional security. The show is in three days, so the runners will have some time to meet the band and plan out the show, as well as do some legwork on the potential security threat. Unless they do something proactive about it, the former band member will arrive at the show with their fellow gangers and create a violent scene.

Another problem is that among the crowd is an official of some kind. About halfway through the show, they will call in the police to shut the show down and arrest the band and runners. The team will need to help the band escape or somehow deter the police from interfering. Depending on the preferences of the players and gamemaster, this could be a very technical adventure involving careful security preparations and vigilant reconnaissance and overwatch, or it could be a crazy, wild night of partying and social encounters, but it’s probably the most fun to include elements of both. If the run goes well, the team gains the band as a contact with Connection and Loyalty ratings of 4 and the Media and Street types. The pay should be pretty decent as well.
