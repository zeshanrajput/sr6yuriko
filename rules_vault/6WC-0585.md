## Maximum Overdrive

- **Cost:** 7 Karma

- **Game Effect:** When you overdrive your cyberware (p. 282, _SR6_ ), gain a point of Edge. If you overstress your augmentation with a glitch or critical glitch, reduce the amount of time your ’ware is affected by one round.

- **Cost:** 18 Karma

- **Game Effect:** This quality may only be taken at character creation. You may have up to two skills at rank 6.
