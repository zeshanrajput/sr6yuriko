## **Can I create my own Honorbound code of honor?**

You can use the codes of honor listed in the sidebar on page 77 and any that are allowed from further sourcebooks in their respective sections of the Guide. Additionally, if your code of honor is an attributable cause to a loss of Reputation for the team, you lose an additional point of Reputation (this represents a general sense that your character may be more difficult to work with than others).
