## Horizon

Of all the megacorporations operating within the Place of Clouds, Horizon has had the most success negotiating with the bureaucracy. Their embassy on the Spire is large enough to be called a skyscraper, and it is used as both an office building and hotel.

Instead of jumping on the resource mining bandwagon, Horizon has chosen to build a metaplanar resort on one of the largest and most beautiful cloud islands. This island is now called Horizon Island. Its surface is covered by a small saltwater sea that draws water up from the Plane of Water below via a reverse-waterfall on one side, and a proper waterfall on its other side returns water to the depths. At the center of the island is an island of land that can only be described as a tropical paradise. It is roughly a quarter of the size of the island of Hawai’i. The waters of Horizon Island are a popular place for visiting water spirits, but the land at its center has largely been ignored by spirit vacationers. It is still mostly a wild place, though Horizon has already built some structures on one side. When the resort is complete, Horizon intendsto offer metaplanar travel packages at exorbitant rates, which will make Horizon Island a tourist destination for wealthy metahumans.
