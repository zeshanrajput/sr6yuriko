## **Discard after Use**

**secret meetings to negotiate her extraction to work for L’Oréal!**

**The Section 89 cell responsible for the bombing at Europort has retreated to the corporate enclave of Lille, hiding in the massive construction site that is Dunkirk. They know they became a loose end, but they have nowhere to go. One of them calls in a favor from the runners. The job is diffcult, as the runners have to infltrate the port, save the cell from the Neo-PD HTR team closing on their position, and then use their own connections in the underworld to meet with Vescheau on their behalf, in order to negotiate a way out.**
