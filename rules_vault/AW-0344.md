## Cannons

Airship cannons are large guns designed to fire an exploding payload over long distances. These cannons use encased shells, similar to artillery rounds. Cannons are extremely effective against airships and their crews but are notoriously difficult to aim. They are fired using the scatter rules for blast attacks with rockets (p. 115, _SR6_ ). The DV is increased by +2 on a direct hit. Characters and items below decks on an airship that is hit by cannon fire are unharmed by the explosion.
