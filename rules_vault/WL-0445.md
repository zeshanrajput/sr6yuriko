**Creatures with the Hypnotic Song power can infuence others who hear it. The creature makes a Magic + Magic vs. Willpower + Logic Opposed test. If Hypnotic Song is successful, for every 2 net hits, the targets’ minds are adjusted from their current attitudes toward a subject chosen by the creature (including themselves) in the direction the creature wants. There are four basic attitude levels: Hostile, Dislike, Neutral, and Friendly. The creature can adjust the targets’ attitude toward a spe-**

**cifc subject, or more than one if they got enough net hits. This new attitude will affect social Edge (p. 98,** _**SR6**_ **) while the power is active. The critter has no control over what actions the subjects take with their new mentality.**
