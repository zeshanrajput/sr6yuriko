## Gear

While the face fatale doesn’t need a bevy of electronics or stockpile of firearms, they still have material needs. Perhaps the most important is clothing. A good wardrobe is essential for all the roles the face fatale will play. A helpful shortcut is a few outfits with electrochromic threads, allowing them to quickly change colors and designs, not just to cut down on the volume but also in case quick change is needed. Actioneer Business Clothes allow a combination of style and protection, but the truly stylish face fatale will opt for something like Armanté or Nightshade clothing. In addition to a nice wardrobe, the face fatale should have a good commlink. They need to be able to connect frequently with their contacts and sources.
