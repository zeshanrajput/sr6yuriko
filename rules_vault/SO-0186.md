This was the Matrix home to the full community of shadowrunners, and it was run by the legendary Captain Chaos and frequented by such icons as FastJack and Bull. But when Crash 2.0 happened in 2064, we lost Captain Chaos, and the BBS went offline with all data lost. This led to our beloved JackPoint.

I figure I should start with what Shadowland BBS was before talking about what it is now. Anyone who’s been using JackPoint, or some of the other shadow community hosts, can see the blueprint of what Shadowland originally created. It was meant to be a semi-open forum where runners, Johnsons, and others in our community could post about topics, and comment on those posts. They had a limited number of moderators to keep chatter on topic and weren’t afraid to wield the banhammer. But they kept their eyes on the things that mattered, helped newbies, and kept up on the big shifts that everyone needed to be aware of.

Shadowland helped break real stories like the Universal Brotherhood, that an AI was behind the arcology lockdown, and the lead-up to the plans behind Crash 2.0. Looking back, it was shadowrunners who first identified the issues, and then it was up to us to solve them because the corps and governments weren’t looking out for any of us. It’s all profit or reelection concerns.

So, in that spirit of truth and transparency, we now carry that torch today.

- Alright, for the kids in the audience, I know you can go search this stuff up yourself but let me give you the short version.
