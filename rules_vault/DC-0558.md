## driving styles

Driving styles are like martial arts, but for pilots and drivers. There are a few new Edge boosts, and a lot of new Edge actions, presented in this book. It’s a lot to learn all at once. Picking a driving style gives you a 50 percent discount on Edge for a set of specific uses of Edge. Get to know those ones first and use them frequently. They’re a great way to express your character’s personal style and attitude.
