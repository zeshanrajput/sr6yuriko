## Ways to Play and the Heavily Cybered

Several optional rules and qualities presented in the Sixth World Companion lend themselves to dealing with players who wish to run chromed-out characters.

The following optional rules offer appropriate additional challenges and benefits for these types of runners:

- First Aid for the Augmented (p. 145)

- Bioware Heals Naturally (p. 145)

- Transhumanism (p. 146)

- Suprathyroid Bonuses Stack (p. 149)
