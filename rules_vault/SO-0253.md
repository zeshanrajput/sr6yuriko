## Lie Detector

**Cost:** 0.25 PP per level **Activation: Passive**

You can always spot a liar. Gain +1 per level to Judge Intentions tests, assensing for emotional states, or tests to determine if someone is telling the truth. Adepts with this power are highly sought after by corporations for their ability to effectively counter other adepts using Kinesics.
