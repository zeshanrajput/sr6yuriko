## **The MCT Devices**

**Building on the metaplanar research originally “acquired” from Aztechnology and later refned using information gained from Dis, these devices can open small metaplanar portals and move quantities of mana across these openings. While MCT’s early devices used Essence as a power source, MCT’s researchers soon discovered that the portals could be opened with a fraction of the energy if all the variables involved could be calculated and controlled with precision. While viable for small quantities of mana, MCT’s experiments along this line failed spectacularly in Denver when they were scaled up. The number of variables proved insurmountable, despite utilizing the Null Sect to create an immensely powerful host to handle the data processing of the project.**

**MCT seems to be creating the new hybrid technology, blending cutting-edge data processing with Essence-powered Disian technology. The Disians have been pouring resources into MCT, and the evidence of their deepening partnership can be found in the new hybrid devices including the Harvesters currently under construction at several spots around the world.**
