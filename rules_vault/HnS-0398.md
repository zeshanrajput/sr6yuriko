## Real World Naïveté

## • **Bonus:** 8 Karma

As creatures of the Matrix (and as critters of limited understanding), virtual lifeforms are at best ignorant of the defining aspects of the physical world. Things like gravity, friction, and inertia have no meaning to them. Even if their original programming involved interaction with the physical world in some way, they may not fully grasp the entirety of it. As a result, virtual lifeforms have little knowledge of the real world and grant opponents a point of Edge when interacting with it or otherwise exercising knowledge about it.
