## **>** Baka Dabora

They have every type of games imaginable. AR games, VR games, shoot-’em-ups, sports sims, puzzle games, you name it. Their business model is simple: Get as many players as possible and give them incentives to keep spending. It’s the rare Sony game that doesn’t give you multiple options to drop a few nuyen to give you an edge, even if it’s a slight one, over the competition.
