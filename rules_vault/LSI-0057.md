## optional rules

knowledge and language skills that are directly related to your entertainment interests are reduced by one. You also gain a bonus Edge on any Memory or Perception tests that relate to your entertainment interests. If you don’t invest any LP in Entertainment, you no longer get the Karma discount or gain Edge from this element—though you still get to keep any knowledge skills (including specialization and expertise) that you acquired.
