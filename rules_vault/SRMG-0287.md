## **What Mentor Spirit do I gain for joining the Missions-legal magical group?**



The Missions-legal magical groups are more open than most and do not have a specific mentor spirit for the group and does not require you to change your mentor spirit to join. As such, you do not gain a mentor spirit by joining any of these groups.
