## Life Path Modules

## Event: Victim of a Con

Someone gained your trust, then used it to take advantage of you. The money they stole was a hardship, but the trauma of betrayal was even worse. To this day you find it difficult to trust anyone—maybe it’s better to not let anyone get close again than risk suffering another betrayal.

**Choose one:** +1 to Charisma or Intuition

**Choose one:** +1 to Logic or Willpower **Choose one:** +1 to Con, Perception, or Stealth **Choose one:** +1 to Athletics, Close Combat, Exotic Weapons, or Firearms

**Knowledge/Language skills (choose two):** Current Events, Espionage Techniques, Scams, or one level in any language skill.

This experience hurt you badly, but it also drove you to find new ways to deal with the untrustworthy nature of your fellow sapients. Choose a negative quality that represents the damage done, and a positive quality that represents your resilience or altered perspective on matters.
