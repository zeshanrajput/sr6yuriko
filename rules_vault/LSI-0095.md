The community expects you to participate in keeping the neighborhood appearance clean and occasionally festive, but most importantly you’re expected to conform with corporate standards. Your comings and goings are observed and scrutinized, and any unusual guests or behavior becomes hot gossip in your neighborhood. When you are in or near your home, you may not lie low to reduce Heat, and you may not gain or spend Edge on Stealth tests. If you flagrantly violate corporate norms, your personal Heat modifier increases by +1. Security response times to your home are the minimum for your neighborhood security rating, for good and ill.
