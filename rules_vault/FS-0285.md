## Laser Sight, Low Power

When operating in tight quarters, no one wants an errant beam warning the place down the street. For that, they use this laser option that only projects a relatively short distance.

- No one appreciates choosing this option until you’re trying to keep an operation from spilling out of a corporate high-rise made mostly of glass.

- Mika
