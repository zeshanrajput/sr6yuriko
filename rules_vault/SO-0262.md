## Mentor Spirits

In addition to the new mentor spirits present below, there are several that synchronize quite well with a socially aspected Awakened character. These include Coyote, Dragonslayer, Seducer, Thunderbird, Wise Warrior, and all their similar archetypes. More often than not, though, it’s about matching the personality of the mentor to a player’s goals as a character. So, they must make sure they evaluate the traits and disadvantages of the entor to the role that’s looking to be fulfilled before selecting. Several mentor spirits offer very similar if not overlapping benefits for a character but have very different philosophies that govern their motivations and behavior.
