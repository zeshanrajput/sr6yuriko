## Anthrax

- **Vector:** Inhalation

- **Speed:** 1 day (1)

- **Power:** 8

- **Nature:** Bacterial

- **Effect (Ongoing):** Not usually contagious to metahumans in its natural form, anthrax spores can be processed and turned into a powder that is, in effect, a bacterial toxin. It causes flu-like symptoms within a day, causing (Power)S damage each day, resisted with a Disease Resistance test. Treatment of anthrax requires the use of antibiotics (Availability 1, 80 nuyen), which provide a +4 dice pool bonus. Successfully resisting the damage results in overcoming the disease.

- **Effect (Final):** On a glitch or critical glitch, the



anthrax bacteria produce a high amount of toxins. A glitch causes (Power)P damage, and a critical glitch causes (Power x 2)P damage.
