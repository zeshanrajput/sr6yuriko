## **How to Deal**

**Unfortunately you can’t just avoid gomatia by staying out of the jungle. Running the shadows in modern times means you’ll eventually have to deal with them as security critters. Easily trained, very loyal, and far less prone to arson than hell hounds, they’ve become downright common—especially with Petrovski security.**

**The cardinal rule when dealing with a gomatia is “don’t get bitten.” Their jaws are incredibly strong, capable of severing a limb—or killing a metahuman outright with a single bite, as they love to go for the head. Not getting bitten by a gomatia might be tricky, though, as they’re incredibly stealthy and aren’t likely to give away their position before they strike. The key is to spot them, which is difcult but more easily achieved using thermographic vision or astral perception. If you do your legwork, you should be able to ascertain whether or not your target is protected by gomatia.**
