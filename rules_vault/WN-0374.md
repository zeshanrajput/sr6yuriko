## Hook

The characters are in possession of hot information. El Guapo has been offered a new contract due to the recent bump in his reputation in the Manhattan shadows. This time he’ll invite the PCs to meet him at the sushi bar **Mamasushi** between New Harlem and Washington Heights. He’s got a booth with privacy dividers (built-in white noise generator rating 3) and will greet the PCs as friends offering dinner on him. Once people get started eating, he’ll start his approach like this:

“I’ll just get down to it, if you don’t mind. It seems our past efforts have led to some successes for us, and a new client is asking for your services. They want you to deliver a data packet to a place I believe you already have some knowledge of. The data is on a secure RFID chip, thoroughly encrypted.” The offer is a 10,000 nuyen lump sum upfront, then another 30,000 nuyen once the job is done, though the exact total depends on what the PCs manage to learn while in the lab. They’re advised

to look out for other deliveries that might contain useful info. The PCs will have seventy-two hours to deliver the packet once it’s in their hands. After that, the chip will erase itself and be no good to anyone, so they’ll get no more payment.

El Guapo can answer some basic questions. No, the job doesn’t have anything to do with Silver Nail. The client does not want the PCs to harm anyone in the course of this work. Should players want to haggle, make an Influence (Negotiations) + Charisma vs. Willpower + Intuition Opposed test, with each hit netting a bonus 2,000 nuyen to a maximum bonus of 10,000 nuyen, but the runners owe El Guapo two favors. If they don’t agree to the favors owed, he can’t offer them the bonus regardless of the negotiation outcome.
