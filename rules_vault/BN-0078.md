## Animalism Ritual Groups

An animalism ritual group is created using the group contact rules (p. 175, Sixth World Companion). The character must spend 6 Karma to join the group. The Connection rating is equal to the sum of Membership, Scope, Finances, Magic, and Matrix, with a maximum of 12. Loyalty begins at 3 but can be increased as normal using Karma. The maximum grade of animalism is equal to a character’s Connection plus Loyalty with their animalism ritual group.
