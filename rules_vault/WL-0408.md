## **Look Out!**

## **(Minor Anytime Action)**

**When your critter is the target of an attack, you may issue a timely warning that prompts the critter to take evasive action. Add the critter’s ranks in Athletics to their dice pool on their defense test.**

**• Cost: 2 Edge**
