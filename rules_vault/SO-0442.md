## Scathing Rebuke

Sticks and stones may break their bones, but your words can really hurt them.

**Cost:** 13 Karma

**Game Effect:** Your vicious insults bite deeply. You may make an insulting tirade against a single target with a Major action. Make an Opposed Influence (Intimidation) + Charisma vs. the target’s Composure test. For every net hit, you may spend a point of Edge to deal one box of Stun damage to the target. This doesn’t count as your one available Edge expenditure on the test, so you may also use Edge to affect the dice roll if you choose. Other than by rolling well on the Composure test, Stun damage from a Scathing Rebuke may not be soaked or reduced.

If you fill your target’s Stun damage condition monitor using a Scathing Rebuke, excess damage does not overflow into Physical damage. If you render a target unconscious from a Scathing Rebuke, they recover one box of Stun damage after one minute.

This is a hostile action—even if you fail, your target will consider you to be a hated enemy. You may not gain or spend Edge on any further Influence skill tests that affect someone you targeted using this quality—except for Intimidation tests. If you use this quality against a contact, you lose that contact and all Favor Points you had with them—just as if you had violently assaulted them with a weapon. They now regard you as an enemy.
