## **Connection Rating: 8**

**Role: Arms Dealer Location: In and around Hong Kong Free Enterprise Zone**

**No one knows exactly where Darlene Chang came from, but in 2076 she showed up in Hong Kong with a cargo container full of high-end frearms, multiple bodyguards, and a knack for making deals. It wasn’t long before she ofoaded that frst container and used her newfound capital to bring two more full containers into Hong Kong, and so on. The rest is history. While rumors abound as to Chang’s background and how she’s constantly able to get her hands on such highend inventory, she’s earned a solid reputation for being able to score just about any kind of weapon short of an ICBM. In the past, Chang sold directly, but recent attempts by rivals to pilfer her inventory have forced her to take extreme security measures and store her merchandise in secret caches scattered throughout the region. Now, she holds monthly private auctions for select clients, but she occasionally does special orders for clients lucky enough to make her “Gold List,” flled with people who have served her well in the past. She’s also known to pay for services in merchandise alone. She’s known to be a party girl, and it’s not uncommon for Chang to arrange meets at some of the most exclusive clubs and nightspots in Hong Kong or on her personal yacht, the Fùshang or “Wealthy Trader.”**

- **During the Az-Am War, certain units working for Aztlan were known for allowing opposition forces or civilians to find specific caches of booby-trapped weapons and ammo. The locals became so paranoid, they’d go out of their way to avoid any cache the Azzie mercs would set up, sometimes even abandoning the area just to be safe. Still, any good armorer or EOD tech should be able to disarm any rigged weapons.
