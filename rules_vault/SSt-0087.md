## **Recover the Dead**

**When their drones get taken out, many riggers count them as a loss. Sometimes, depending on where and how they’re lost, this is the best outlook. But giving up on your dead drones every time is just plain wasteful. Most of the time, a recovered drone can be repaired. There are some exceptions, but even the messiest drone corpses often have some recoverable parts. Just don’t put your own life at risk or waste more drones trying to recover a lost cause. If you lose a few drones in a zero zone, just write them off as a business expense and move on with your life.**
