## Cavilard Research Center

Once considered the premier experimental research facility in the NeoNET Seattle cluster, Cavilard was bought out by MCT and billed as an informal research relationship between the corporation and the NAN. During the handover period, the location came under attack from a number of shadowrunner teams looking to extract data and prototypes. Some of those runs were successful, prompting Cavilard to upgrade its security from KE to Petrovski.
