## Extremely Local Forecast

In the new political climate, Vashon wants to keep Outremer out of the public eye. They see the issues on other islands, and they’re trying hard to avoid having those same issues boil over to them. Problem is, they share political power with their fellow islands and can’t avoid the entanglements. Instead, they are looking to use their population and social networking advantage to provide them with more influence via political gains in their own local political environment.
