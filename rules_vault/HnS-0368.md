## Special Protosapient Attack: Noisestorm

As a Major Action, a protosapient using a noisestorm can make an Electronics + Logic test where net hits increase the amount of noise in the area. This can either target a network and affect all devices connected to it or target a specific device and use it as ground zero to affect all devices within (Spark x 10)-meter radius of its physical location. The protosapient is immune to this noise.
