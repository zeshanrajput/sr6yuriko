## AURIC THERAPY/ RESTORING MAGIC

This improves as you regain your Essence, so if you’ve lost 3 points of Magic, it will take two months to recover your first point, and then one month each for the next two, for a total recovery time of four months.

Note that Magic is restored faster than Essence but still follows the normal rules whereby Magic cannot exceed Essence + initiation grade. This is tested at the end of each period of treatment, so if you have 4.9 Essence, and 4 Magic, you can increase them both in the same month.
