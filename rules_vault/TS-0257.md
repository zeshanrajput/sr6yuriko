## **Knight Errant Victory**

**New on the market and currently used exclusively by Knight Errant patrols is the new Victory cruiser. This high-performance machine is a hybrid sports car loaded with security options. The Victory so far has been issued only to highprofle precincts where KE wants extra visibility and hasn’t revealed much about what it can do beyond looking good. It does offer one exclusive feature among patrol cars: Its rear passenger area is fully separated from the operator cabin area and accessible via a rear hatch, making it a secure yet small prisoner transport.**
