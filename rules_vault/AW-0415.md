## The Metro

What would have been underground public transportation in Hubris has been claimed by the Yaggil as their home. For the most part, the stone

and concrete protect them from radiation while also making the spot defensible from marauders and mutants. Power is haphazardly connected throughout the Metro. While the few stations are used for storage and sleeping quarters, the Dakar station is one of the stops for the death train.
