## Critterjack

The Evo critterjack is headware that establishes a direct neural interface with a critter’s brain for the purpose of calibrating it to accept and utilize implanted augmentations as if they were a natural part of its body.

**Game Information:** Reduce the increased threshold for interacting with the critter due to Essence loss by the critterjack’s rating. The critterjack also functions as an implanted bio-monitor. A critterjack can also paralyze the critter with a wireless command by putting them into full VR mode—though this feature is often disabled once a critter’s training is complete.
