## Augmentation Grades and Critters

Just as with augmentations for metahumans, there are different grades available for critter augmentations. The nuyen and Essence costs are exactly the same. Used, standard, alpha, beta, and deltaware are all available options for critter augmentations—if you are willing to pay the cost.

**Game Information:** A critter must be specially trained and augmented with a critterjack in order to make an attack with a venom sprayer. When used, the critter makes a Close Combat + Agility test for the attack against a target within a limited range: two meters for inhalation vectors or up to five meters for contact vectors. Successfully dosing the target requires at least one net hit. The power of the drug or toxin is increased by one for every two net hits. A gas mask provides immunity
