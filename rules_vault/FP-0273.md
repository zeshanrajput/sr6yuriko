## **Take Runners on Tour**

**There are great individual settings in the Sixth World—Seattle, Neo-Tokyo, Berlin, and more— and running in those settings can provide fun over a whole campaign. But there is a whole world out there to discover, and** _**Falling Point**_ **can help teams visit new places. Whether it’s France using information from the French Touch chapter or Denver using Denver Data Haven , many chapters here provide hooks for quick runs in places across the globe. If the runners get the right Mr. Johnson, they might be able to do a globe-trotting job, spending a day or two in one city and then jetting off to the next, all with the help of Mr. Johnson’s bank account. This gives the gamemaster the chance to show different pieces of the world to the PCs and have a range of experiences within a single job.**
