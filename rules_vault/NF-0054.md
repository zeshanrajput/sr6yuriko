## Bugstomp

This Chicago-born style is pure street tribe. Imagine the sounds of industry and bucket drummers mixed with inhuman chitters and the screech of tearing metal, and you’re approaching the sheer chaos of bugstomp. Originating among the shellshocked youth of the survivors of Bug City, bugstomp might be seen as a coping mechanism for their PTSD. The music is primal and chaotic, always active, a mixture of tribal influences and the sound of war. While this is still more street than mainstream, some DJs looking for a fresh new sound have begun to incorporate elements into their sets, while others are just recording it and spinning it. I’m told it’s the kind of thing you’d need K-10 to really get into. Personally, I don’t like remembering bugs when I’m trying to have a good time.
