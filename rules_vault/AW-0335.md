## Riggers in the Place of Clouds

Individuals who are augmented with a control rig will find that they are capable of controlling airships with ease. Technomancers with an echo that provides them with the equivalent of a control rig also gain this ability. When a rigger (or technomancer equivalent) first touches an airship with their bare skin, they feel a connection to the vessel. No skinlink echo or datajack connection is required for this—it just happens. The connected airship will respond to the rigger’s commands. The rigger doesn’t jump into the airship as they might a drone or other vehicle, but they gain the benefits of their control rig as if jacked in. An airship will only respond to one rigger at a time. If a rigger attempts to connect to an airship that is already connected to another rigger, they may attempt to take control with a Major Action. Make a Piloting + Willpower Opposed test. If successful, the new rigger takes control of the airship. Whichever side fails takes Stun damage with a DV equal to their opponent’s net hits, and their control rig (or equivalent technomancer echo) is deactivated for one minute.
