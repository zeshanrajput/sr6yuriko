## **Can I have three sets of Shiva Arms along with two more sets of cyberarms?**

No, the maximum number of arm pairs is four pairs total. This includes the starting pair you were born with. For example, if you are a nartaki with three pairs of arms, you can add only one additional pair of cyberarms.
