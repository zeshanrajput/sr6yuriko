  • Summary: While the talk about and protests against the engagement of crown princess Emma reached their boiling point, the team got called by the orkish shadowrunner Sigrûn to The Hague’s Noordeinde Paleis, where she reveals herself to be princess Emma’s personal bodyguard and hires them to protect two palace officials during a trip through the city. As they tricked their way through a group of protesters they found out that the trip was about creating a chemsniffer primed on the chemical that’s supposed to be used on an upcoming terrorist attack.

Tasked with defending the door to the princess, they let the attackers run into the area of effect of gas grenades and mowed them down with automatic weapons.
