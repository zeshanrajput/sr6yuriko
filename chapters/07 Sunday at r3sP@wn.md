The high-amplitude vibration of the cooling fans registered through the drone's sensors.

Then, she registered a priority packet transmission through the Resonance—a staccato data burst routing directly to her address space.

**[SENDER: JAX_01]**

**[SUBJECT: ZERO_PROTOCOL]**

**[BODY: Hounds have the scent. They’re tracing the noise, not the signal. I’m scrubbing my deck and going dark. If you’re reading this, I’m already gone. Burn this code. Don’t look back, Reiko. You’re a stationary target now. Good luck, (T/Pr)eacher.]**

It was the MCT Spark. In the Mitsuhama host, it had been a jagged, screaming sphere of gold-threaded code—a Data Bomb designed to incinerate itself and everything around it. Here, under the influence of r31k0’s resonance infusion, it had been stabilized. It had been given a shape.
